930726 FT 26 JUL 93 / Construction Contracts: Refurbishment LAING LONDON AND SOUTH EAST is carrying out a major facelift of a tower office block in Chiswick, west London, for the British Standards Institution. The contract, worth around Pounds 14m, involves demolition of the canopy, construction of a two-storey extension and recladding of the whole building. The project is due for completion in July 1994.
