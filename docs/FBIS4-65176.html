BFN [Text] Munich (DPA) -- At its meeting in Munich on 7 May, the Bavarian Social Democratic Party of Germany [SPD] nominated Renate Schmidt almost unanimously as its top candidate for the Landtag elections on 25 September. In a secret ballot, the SPD land chairperson got 244 of 246 valid votes. There was one abstention and one no-vote. It is the SPD's election goal this fall to break the absolute majority of the Christian Social Union.
