940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Gardner's Pounds 240m package Gardner Merchant, Europe's largest contract caterer, has completed a Pounds 240m financing package, part of which will be used to finance its recent Dollars 100m (Pounds 65m) acquisition of Morrison's Hospitality Group from Morrison Restaurants of the US, writes Tracy Corrigan. The package includes a Pounds 155m five-year loan which will be used to refinance existing bank borrowings, left over from the Pounds 402m management buy-out of the company from Forte in December 1992; a Dollars 100m five-year loan to fund the Morrison's acquisition and a Pounds 20m multi-currency working capital and guarantee facility, again over five years. The terms of the loans were not disclosed. The facilities were arranged by the Royal Bank of Scotland, with five other participating banks.
