March 10, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

CHAPMAN NAMES CAHILL WOMEN'S VOLLEYBALL COACH

Mary Cahill, a former Chapman College volleyball player, has been named coach of the women's team, Athletic Director Walt Bowman said Thursday.

Cahill, a 1987 graduate of Chapman, was an assistant volleyball coach at El Modena High School the past two years. She is a substitute teacher in the Orange Unified School District.
