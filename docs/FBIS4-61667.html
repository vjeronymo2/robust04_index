BFN [Text] The president of the Kyrgyz Republic has issued the following decree establishing the Kyrgyz State National Military Academy. To meet the needs of national military cadres [words indistinct] and with the aim of developing patriotism and preparing our young people for military schools, I decree: 1. The Kyrgyz State National Military Academy shall be established on the basis of the Russian language comprehensive school named after the 60th anniversary of the Communist Party of Kyrgyzstan and at the dormitory of the republican special boarding school of [word indistinct] physical training in the city of Bishkek. 2. The government of the Kyrgyz Republic shall examine the organizational and financial matters connected with the establishment of the Kyrgyz State National Military Academy by 1 July 1994. Signed by Askar Akayev, President of the Kyrgyz Republic, on 6 May 1994 at Government House in the city of Bishkek.
