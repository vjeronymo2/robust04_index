BFN [Text] Pietersburg May 19 SAPA -- Northern Transvaal Premier Mr Ngoake Ramatlhodi has announced the names of the province's senators, a SAPA correspondent reported on Thursday. They are George Mashamba, Irene Mutshula, Nelson Diale, Bishop Dolo, Sello Moloto, Sentle Fenyane, Alpheus Mogane, Chief Mashile, King Kennedy Tshivase and Lawrence Mushuna.
