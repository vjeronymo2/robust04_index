Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Prague, February 6 (CTK) -- Czech Premier Vaclav Klaus today expressed hope that yesterday's massacre in a Sarajevo marketplace would provoke a tougher reaction from the international community. "However, I hope the reaction will not only be from the international community," Klaus said in an interview for Czech Television this evening. He said the massacre should be the "last straw" which should force the enemy parties in the former Yugoslavia to sit down at the negotiating table and "stop shooting." [passage omitted]
