930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Exceptionals knock DBS Management DBS Management, the financial services group, turned in a pre-tax loss of Pounds 518,000 for the year to March 31, against a Pounds 1.46m profit last time. The figure was struck from increased operating profits of Pounds 2m (Pounds 1.71m) and after exceptionals of Pounds 2.52m (Pounds 250,000). Turnover was ahead at Pounds 37.6m (Pounds 25.6m). Losses per share were 19.5p against earnings of 40.3p last time, but the dividend is maintained at 8p. DBS was fined Pounds 60,000 by Fimbra in June over its home income plans. Its shares are traded on a matched bargain basis under Rule 535(2).
