Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Kuwait Denies Freezing PLO Assets"] [Text] Moscow, AL-HAYAH -- The Kuwaiti Embassy in Moscow has expressed "regret" at the report carried by the official news agency ITAR-TASS claiming that Kuwait had frozen the PLO's assets in Kuwait. A statement issued by the embassy said that the PLO had transferred tens of millions of dollars since July 1991 from its accounts in Kuwait to Jordan and Switzerland without any Kuwaiti objection. The statement emphasized that "the people and Government of the state of Kuwait adopt a principled stance in support of the Palestinian people's just cause and legitimate rights."
