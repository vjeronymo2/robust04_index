September 30, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

CITY SECTION ROUNDUP

Sylmar 38, Lincoln 8 -- Jerome Casey ran for 207 yards and four touchdowns and caught a pass for another score as the Spartans rolled at home.

Sylmar increased its lead to 24-0 at the half when it scored on a 59-yard pass from Daniel Terry to Casey with no time remaining.

Three plays after Sylmar's London Woodfin recovered a fumble at the Lincoln 33-yard line, Casey ran nine yards for the first of his touchdowns. He capped the night with a 38-yard touchdown run in the fourth quarter. RON PIVO
