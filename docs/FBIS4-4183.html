BFN [Text] (Karki Shah), chairman of Nepal's National Council, and his wife and party, who are currently on a visit to China at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC], arrived in Xian on 13 April for sightseeing and a visit. Zhou Yaguang, chairman of the CPPCC provincial committee, met and feted Chairman (Karki) and his wife and party at the Xian Guest House yesterday evening. Chairman Zhou briefed the guests on Shaanxi's long cultural history, its natural resources, and its achievements in economic construction since the drive for reform and opening up to the outside world was launched. He hoped that China and Nepal would conduct more frequent contacts in the days to come so as to promote understanding, and wished that the chairman's current visit to Xian would help deepen cooperation between Shaanxi and Nepal. Chairman (Karki) said: The unbreakable friendship between China and Nepal has lasted for several thousand years. China's aid to Nepal is there for all to see. The Nepalese people cherish deep feelings for China. He expressed his belief that the friendship between the two countries would last for generation after generation. During their stay in Xian, Chairman (Karki) and his party visited scenic spots and historical sites, such as the site of the terra- cotta warriors and horses of the Qing Dynasty, the (Dayan) Temple, and the Xian Satellite Control Center.
