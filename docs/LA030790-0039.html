March 7, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Sizzling Ticket Price for 'Miss Saigon': "Miss Saigon" promises to be a megaproduction when it opens at the Broadway Theater in New York next March.

There'll be a helicopter landing on stage, a huge statue of Ho Chi Minh . . . and extra high ticket prices. The top ticket price for the best seats will be $100. Compare that to highest price at the Los Angeles Music Center Opera, $75; the top ticket (unscalped) for L.A "Phantom of the Opera," $50. BETH KLEID
