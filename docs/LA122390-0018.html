December 23, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

TAINTED DESERT

Regarding "The Tainted Desert" (by Sean Elder, Nov. 4): In the name of military preparedness, destroy an island; destroy a desert; destroy the plants and animals that make these places their homes; destroy people.

Will the visions someday be of all of our lives finished, lying in the poisoned desert beside bombs and military debris?

Will Richard Misrach record this?

PAMELA GHALEB

Santa Monica
