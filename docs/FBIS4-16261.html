BFN [Text] If the 200-million-dollar gas debt to Turkmenistan is not repaid within days, gas supplies to Georgia will be suspended on 1 May. A spokesman for the Gruzgaz department told INTERFAX the debt to Turkmenistan is unlikely to be paid-off before the end of April. The gas consumers in the republic -- enterprises and the population-- pay practically nothing for the use of fuel. They owe the department over 5 trillion Georgian coupons.
