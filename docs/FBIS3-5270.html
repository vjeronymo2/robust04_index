Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] This afternoon at the National Assembly Hall in Vientiane, His Excellency [H.E.] Saman Vi-gnaket, chairman of the National Assembly of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR], held a meeting with Samdech Chea Sim, chairman of the National Assembly of the Kingdom of Cambodia. The Lao delegation attending this meeting included H.E. Khambou Sounisai, deputy chairman of the National Assembly, H.E. Dr. Khamliang Phonsena, chief of the Commission for Foreign Affairs of the National Assembly, and other members of the National Assembly. The Cambodian national assembly delegation included H.E. (Chan Ven), chairman of the Interior, National Defense, Investigation, and Suppression Commission; H.E. Pen Thol, vice chairman of the Foreign Affairs, International Cooperation, Information, and Press Commission, and 12 other members. The meeting lasted two hours with an atmosphere of intimacy and good understanding from start to finish. After that, at 1100 the National Assembly delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia led by Samdech Chia Sim paid a courtesy call on H.E. Khamtai Siphandon, prime minister of the LPDR. This evening at the National Assembly Hall, H.E. Saman Vi-gnaket, chairman of the LPDR National Assembly, will host a reception dinner in honor of visiting Samdech Chia Sim, National Assembly chairman of the Kingdom of Cambodia and his party.
