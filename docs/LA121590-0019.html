December 15, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

RAMS, ROBINSON TAKE HITS FOR THEIR STRATEGY ON AND OFF THE FIELD

Missed opportunities? Fumbles? Interceptions? Give me a break! The real reason the Rams lost to New Orleans is because John Robinson doesn't have enough faith in his offensive line to go for a touchdown on fourth and goal from the half-yard line.

Sorry, Lambs. No guts, no glory.

NEIL R. SHAMBAUGH

Monterey Park
