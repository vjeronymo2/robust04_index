December 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

THE NEW LEAR

Does Norman Lear believe he can absolve his guilt for producing "All in the Family," which was the beginning of the sleaze on TV, by his new show that could be called "Look Who's Coming to Sunday Dinner"?

It isn't possible that bigoted (lovable?) Archie Bunker ever caused anyone to look at their prejudices. It only excused their own hatreds.

Perhaps that also is the reason Carroll O'Connor is doing "In the Heat of the Night" -- guilt. Why Jean Stapleton allowed her character to be subjected to such ridicule is not understandable.

MARGARET G. ROLLINS

Glendale
