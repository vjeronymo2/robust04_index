Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "ACIR Radio News" newscast] [Text] Diego Fernandez de Zevallos, PAN [National Action Party] presidential candidate, has resumed his political campaign and said he is willing to fight for the Presidency with Ernesto Zedillo: [Begin Fernandez recording] Because of the public offices that Dr. Zedillo and I have held, I have had direct contact with him. He is a respectable man and politician. With his nomination by the PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party] to replace Luis Donaldo Colosio -- rest in peace -- I again have an adversary, and I will fight for the Presidency of the republic. [end recording]
