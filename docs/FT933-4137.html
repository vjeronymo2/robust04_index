930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / Aid worth Dollars 119m for Cambodia International donor countries pledged Dollars 119m (Pounds 77m) in new financial aid to Cambodia to help Phnom Penh rebuild its war-torn economy, Reuter reports from Paris. The aid package was announced yesterday at the end of a two-day meeting of the International Committee for the Reconstruction of Cambodia which includes 31 donor countries and 11 international organisations. The assistance came on top of Dollars 880m granted to Phnom Penh in June 1992 at a ministerial conference in Tokyo. The fresh donations included Dollars 20m from Japan and FF62m (Pounds 7.08m) France pledged at the start of conference on Wednesday.
