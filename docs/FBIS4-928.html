BFN [Text] The AWB [Afrikaner Resistance Movement] says that despite the IFP's [Inkatha Freedom Party] decision to take part in the election, conflict between Zulus and Xhosas is inevitable, which will cause South Africa to become another Rwanda. The organization said it was not surprised at what it called the Zulu leadership's pathetic capitulation to the Xhosa-led ANC in view of its betrayal by the government.
