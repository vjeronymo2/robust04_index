910427 FT 27 APR 91 / UK Company News: Continuing activities buoy Dean & Bowes Dean & Bowes Group reported lower pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.68m for 1990, against Pounds 2.85m. However, on continuing activities the result was Pounds 2.88m compared with Pounds 1.06m. The company, which refurbishes leisure properties, said that inquiries, quotations and orders were significantly higher than at the same time last year and the present order book covered 60 per cent of the expected turnover for the year. Turnover was Pounds 48.81m (Pounds 26.72m) of which Pounds 34.83m (Pounds 14.34m) related to continuing activities. Earnings per share came out at 10.5p (14.2p) and the directors are proposing a maintained final dividend of 3.25p making an unchanged total of 6p. On a pro forma basis earnings for continuing activities were 13p (6.2p).
