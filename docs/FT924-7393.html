921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / Regulator defies British Gas SIR JAMES McKINNON, the gas industry regulator, said yesterday he had told the Monopolies and Mergers Commission he rejected British Gas's demands on the rate of return it should earn from its pipeline and storage network. He also questioned the extent of the company's future investment in the network. Sir James gave a clear indication at a conference in London that he was taking a tough line in the MMC's wide-ranging investigation into the UK gas market. He said two 'highly experienced' companies had attempted to enter the pipeline market but their efforts had 'come to nought in the face of the overwhelming market power of British Gas'.
