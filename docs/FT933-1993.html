930921 FT 21 SEP 93 / International Company News: Computer group completes deal HEWLETT-PACKARD, the US electronics group, yesterday completed its acquisition of BT&D Technologies, a joint venture between BT and DuPont specialising in fibre optics, writes Alan Cane. According to Mr William Craven, general manager of H-P's components group, the deal will make H-P the world's largest independent supplier of optoelectronic components. Agreement in principal to the purchase had been reached in May, but negotiations between the three parties delayed completion until yesterday.
