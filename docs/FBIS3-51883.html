Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Panamanian economic developments monitored through 25 January. Source is cited in parentheses following each item. Roy Cardoze, director general of Marine Resources at the Commerce and Industries Ministry, said shrimp exports increased 25 percent in 1993, meaning a boost of $50 million to the national economy. Cardoze also reported that fish exports have risen 35 percent. (Panama City LA PRENSA in Spanish 19 Jan 94 p 11a) Roberto Alfaro, commerce and industries minister, announced on 21 January that the price of gasoline went up 5 cents due to an increase on the international market. (Panama City LA ESTRELLA DE PANAMA in Spanish 22 Jan 94 p a1)
