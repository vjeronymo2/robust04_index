Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The Federal Police arrested former Bolivian President Luis Garcia Meza in Sao Paulo this morning. The former president was hiding in an apartment located in a neighborhood close to the airport and carried a false Brazilian passport. He was taken into custody together with his aide-de-camp, Colonel Roberto Rico. Both of them had been charged with being implicated in an international drug trafficking network. Garcia Meza had been sentenced to 30 years' imprisonment and was a fugitive from Bolivian justice.
