BFN [Text] Early this morning, security forces caught four people who infiltrated from Jordan into Israeli territory near the Hammat Gader area. The four were unarmed, and they may be Iraqi citizens. Our correspondent in the north Yo'av Aviv reports that large IDF [Israel Defense Forces] forces are searching the area and have declared it a closed military zone. The road from Ma'agan Junction to Hammat Gader and Mevo Hama has been closed to traffic.
