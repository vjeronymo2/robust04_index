921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / Japanese machine orders fall 28.6% Japanese capital investment is continuing to fall steeply, according to government figures published yesterday which show machinery orders in October fell by 28.6 per cent from the month before, Charles Leadbeater reports from Tokyo. The government's Economic Planning Agency said that orders received by Japan's leading machinery makers were 30.7 per cent down on the same month last year.
