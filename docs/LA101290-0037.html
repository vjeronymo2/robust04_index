October 12, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL VOLLEYBALL MATCH RESCHEDULED

A girls' high school volleyball match between El Camino Real and Chatsworth, originally scheduled for Oct. 4, will be played Wednesday.

The match was postponed when a conflict arose over the site for the match. It was scheduled to be played in the small gym at Chatsworth rather than the main gym. The match now will take place in the main gym at Chatsworth. It will be preceded by a junior varsity match beginning at 6 p.m.
