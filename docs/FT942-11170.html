940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / International Company News: CAE reports small gain in fourth quarter CAE, the international commercial flight simulator and military training group, reported fourth-quarter net profit of CDollars 10.7m (Dollars 8.1m), or 10 cents a share, up slightly from CDollars 8.6m, or 8 cents, a year earlier. Sales were CDollars 259m against CDollars 268m. For the full year ended March 31, operating net profit was CDollars 34.6m, or 32 cents, against CDollars 32.2m, or 30 cents, on revenues of CDollars 1.03bn, little changed. However, after writing off CDollars 396m goodwill for the acquisition of CAE Link several years ago and providing a further CDollars 33m for Link's restructuring, CAE's final loss for 1993-94 was CDollars 395m or CDollars 3.96 a share.
