Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ukrainian Acting Prime Minister Yukhym Zvyahilskyy and Georgian Prime Minister Otar Patsatsia today signed an agreement on the development of trade and economic cooperation between the Ukrainian and Georgian Governments [words indistinct]. The agreement takes effect as of the day of its signing. In a short interview with a UKINFORM correspondent, Otar Patsatsia stressed that Georgia is ready for cooperation beginning today. Mr. Patsatsia stated: We already have a list of goods which can be traded within the framework of this mutually favorable agreement; I am very glad that we have signed this protocol. The Georgian prime minister added: Now is the time for work.
