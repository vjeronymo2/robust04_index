941124 FT 24 NOV 94 / Polly Peck subsidiary sold Vestel, the listed Turkish consumer electronics subsidiary which is 88 per cent owned by the Polly Peck group, has been sold for an undisclosed sum, administrators to Polly Peck International said yesterday. Vestel's new owner is Mr Ahmet Nazif Zorlu, chairman of Zorlu Holdings, a textile manufacturer. Mr Chris Barlow, lead administrator to PPI and a partner with Coopers & Lybrand, said: 'The proceeds of the sale will be divisible amongst the three creditor groups of PPI Holdings BV, one of which is ultimately Polly Peck International.'
