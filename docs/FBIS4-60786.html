BFN [Text] Industrialists from Belarus and Ukraine do not raise the issue of restoring the former political union. However, to normalize the (?functioning) of the two states' economies, industrialists consider it necessary to restore the former economic relations. The monetary merger agreement between Belarus and Russia is a key factor for the establishment of our relations with Ukraine. In this case, Belarusian enterprises will receive the opportunity to settle their accounts with their Ukrainian partners -- who buy raw materials from Russia -- in Russian rubles, which will naturally have a positive impact on the work of Belarusian enterprises. In general, in the opinion of experts, the preservation of close partnership-like relations will impart a vital impulse to the stabilization of the economic situation.
