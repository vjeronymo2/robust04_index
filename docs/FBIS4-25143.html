BFN [Text] Beijing, June 6 (XINHUA) -- Chen Xitong, member of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China (CPC) Central Committee and secretary of the CPC Beijing Municipal Committee, met here today with a delegation from the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party (MPRP). The Mongolian visitors, led by G. Ulzii, a MPRP leading member and secretary of the MPRP Ulaanbaatar Municipal Committee, arrived here last Friday on a 10-day official good-will visit to Beijing and Hohhot, capital of China's Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region, as guests of the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee.
