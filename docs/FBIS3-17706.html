Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow, 29 Mar -- Vladimir Zhirinovskiy does not consider that Aleksandr Rutskoy will pose any competition to him in future presidential elections, the LDPR [Liberal Democratic Party of Russia] leader told a press conference at the State Duma today. He is convinced that "the electorate will not vote for Rutskoy." According to Zhirinovskiy, the State Duma faction led by him "was the main instrument is getting adopted the political amnesty" which freed A. Rutskoy and other organizers of the October events in Moscow. However, the LDPR leader complained that "not all of those who got out of jail have shown themselves grateful to the LDPR." Vladimir Zhirinovskiy said that the LDPR's 5th congress starts on 2 April, with representatives of 20 Slav states taking part. He claimed that on its second day, the congress "will be turned into a congress of the Slav peoples."
