BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Mikhail Shevtsov and Andrey Palariya] [Text] Moscow, 23 May -- This morning Internal Affairs Minister Valeriy Kuzentsov announced that the situation in Crimea is being monitored. At the same time, he said, yesterday the Ukrainian minister of internal affairs sent all town and rayon departments telegrams with orders to obey instructions from Kiev only. Valeriy Kuznetsov assessed this step by the Ukrainian leadership as a provocation aimed at destabilizing the situation on the peninsula. The duty officer of the Black Sea Fleet staff told TASS that, following the completion of routine exercise, all units and subunits of the fleet are in their permanent places of deployment. They are not carrying out any maneuvers at present.
