BFN [Text] Al-Shati' refugee camp, Occupied Gaza Strip, April 7 (AFP) -- Islamic Jihad claimed responsibility for shooting dead an Israeli and wounding four others on Wednesday [as received]. "The Islamic Jihad movement mourns with dignity and pride our martyr 'Ali Talib al-'Ammawi, hero of the Ashdod crossing operation," the fundamentalist group said in a statement. The family live in al-Shati' refugee camp on the edge of Gaza City and his father Muhammad told AFP the boy was 19 years old and in his last year at high school. He said his son had supported the fundamentalist movement Islamic Jihad. Al-'Ammawi stepped off a bus at the port city of Ashdod and opened fire on a group of Israelis with an Uzi sub-machinegun, police said.
