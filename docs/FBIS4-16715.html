BFN [Report from roundup of ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA and ITAR-TASS reports: "`Niva' With Altay Engine"] [Text] The "Rostselmash" plant has completed the readjustment of production for the output of "Niva" grain harvesting combines with engines from the Barnaul "Altaydizel" joint-stock company. Previously the "Niva" was produced only with engines from the Kharkov "Hammer and Sickle" plant. But the Ukrainian enterprise has shown itself to be a not entirely reliable partner and the complication of tax procedures when crossing the border between Russia and Ukraine has aggravated the problem.
