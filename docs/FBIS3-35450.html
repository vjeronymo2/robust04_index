Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Dhirke -- A dangerous agent of the Indian terrorist organization, "Research and Analysis Wing [RAW]," was arrested as soon as he crossed over into Pakistani territory. It has been learned that Lakshman Das, a dangerous "RAW" agent, was arrested by a Pakistani secret investigation agency when he was entering Pakistani territory near Dhirke from India's Jaisalmer area. A large amount of Indian currency and some important documents have been recovered from the arrested terrorist. It has also been learned that Lakshman Das was coming to Pakistan after receiving training at a RAW camp in India's Bhilwara District to carry out terroristic activities in Sindh Province. During the interrogation, he identified certain important personalities of Pakistan, especially an influential Hindu businessmen from Sindh Province, who provided assistance, Pakistani currency, and arms to RAW agents to carry out acts of terrorism inside Pakistan.
