BFN [Text] The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] withdrawal from the Gaza Strip and Jericho will begin immediately after the 4 May signing ceremony with the Palestinians in Cairo. The agreement is to be signed by Yitzhaq Rabin and Yasir 'Arafat. Our Army affairs correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that the IDF has already evacuated 95 percent of the equipment it plans to take out. The redeployment is to continue for 10 to 14 days.
