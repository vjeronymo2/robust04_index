April 30, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

CREDIT UNION RISING ON NIGHTWATCH SITE

The Los Angeles Firemen's Credit Union is building a $2.5-million headquarters building on the former site of the Nightwatch restaurant on Colorado Boulevard in Pasadena near the Pasadena-Los Angeles border.

CHCG Architects Inc., Los Angeles and Montclair, is designing the two-story, 24,000-square-foot facility that will replace the credit union's 20-year-old facility at 2900 W. Temple St., Los Angeles.
