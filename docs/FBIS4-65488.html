BFN [Unattributed article: "Greece/Italy: Choice of Martino Praised"] [Text] (ANSA) Athens, May 13 -- The appointment of Antonio Martino as Italy's foreign minister "was the best choice that could be made," assistant Greek Foreign Minister, responsible for European affairs, Theodhoros Pangalos said to the press here on Friday [13 May]. The Greek diplomat went on to add "Mr. Martino is a person who stands out for his sincerity." Following the results of the election in Italy at the end of March, the Greek minister for European affairs had voiced his reservations on Silvio Berlusconi as premier, opinions which were amplified by the Italian press. Greece currently holds the rotating European Union presidency.
