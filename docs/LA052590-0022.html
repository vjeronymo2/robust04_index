May 25, 1990, Friday, South Bay Edition

SOUTH BAY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: PAY RAISE FOR NON-TEACHERS APPROVED

Non-teaching employees in the Inglewood Unified School District will receive the same salary increase as teachers under a tentative agreement reached this week.

The agreement, approved by the school board Wednesday night, calls for a retroactive 2% salary increase for last school year and 5.4% for the current year, which ends June 30. The contract for the 1990-91 school year is still up for negotiation.

The agreement will become final if approved by members of California Professional Education Employees on Wednesday.
