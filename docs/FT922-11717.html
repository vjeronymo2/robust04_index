920424 FT 24 APR 92 / Heath made Knight of Garter MR EDWARD HEATH, the prime minister who led Britain into the European Community, is to join Britain's highest order of chivalry. Buckingham Palace announced yesterday that the Queen had appointed the MP for Old Bexley and Sidcup and father of the House of Commons to be one of three new Knights Companion of the Most Noble Order of the Garter. The other two appointees are Lord Sainsbury of Preston Candover - who will retire as chairman of Sainsbury, the food retailer, in the next 12 months after 23 years in the post - and Viscount Ridley, the Lord Lieutenant of Northumberland.
