BFN [Text] Beijing, 30 May (XINHUA) -- A Foreign Ministry spokesman answered reporters' questions here today. Question [Q]: What are your comments on the victory of the Hungarian Socialist Party in the national parliamentary election? Answer [A]: The Hungarian election is an internal affair of Hungary. We respect the choice made by the Hungarian people. We hope and believe that relations between China and Hungary in the political, economic, and cultural fields will develop in a sustained, steady, and all-around manner on the basis of the five principles of peaceful co-existence. Q: What are your comments on the round-table talks on peace and national reconciliation in Cambodia held in Pyonyang? A: The holding of the Cambodia round-table talks is conducive to peace and national reconciliation in Cambodia. We appreciate and support the efforts made by King Sihanouk to this end and sincerely hope that the Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia and the Democratic Kampuchea side will set store by the overall national interests and continue to seek a proper settlement of their internal dispute through consultations.
