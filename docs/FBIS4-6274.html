BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 18 (KCNA) -- Prize diplomas, money and articles were awarded to the art groups and artistes of different countries who displayed excellent skills of art at the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival, which took place here from April 9 to 18. In the team event, the diploma and cup were awarded to the Art Group of China, the Russian Folk Song and Dance Group, the Kazakh Art Group, the Mongolian Art Group and the Art Group of Koreans in Japan. The Donbas Dance Group of Ukraine was awarded a special prize. In the individual event, diplomas, money and articles were given to 30 singers from Russia, China, Yugoslavia, Malaysia, Romania, Cuba, Syria, the Netherlands, etc., to nine instrumental musicians from Mongolia, Moldova, etc., to five dancers from the Art Group of Koreans in Japan, the Art Group of the International Federation of Koreans for Reunification, etc., and to three conductors from Colombia, etc. The prizes were also awarded to 16 pieces of songs sung by artistes from Vietnam, Belarus, Italy, the art group of Koreans in China, etc., to five pieces of instrumental music performed by French, Dutch and other art groups and 14 items of dances performed by Nigerian, Egyptian, Cambodian and other art groups. In the circus event, eight items performed by Chinese, Moroccan, Bulgarian and other acrobats received prizes. In the creation event, diplomas and articles were awarded to the artiste of Bangladesh who wrote and composed the song "Ode to the Great Leader," the artiste of the trio of the Congo who wrote and composed the song "Happiness of Mankind" and the artiste of the Kazakh art group who wrote and composed the song "Thinking of Comrade Kim Il-song."
