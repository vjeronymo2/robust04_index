BFN [Text] Moscow, May 30, RIA -- Russia holds a special place among the foreign policy priorities of Uzbekistan. This was stated at a press conference at the Uzbek Embassy in Moscow today to mark the 2nd anniversary of the treaty on the fundamental principles of relations between the two countries. Addressing journalists, Vice- President of Uzbekistan Bakhtiyar Khamidov said that Russia accounted for 45 [per]cent of his country's annual foreign trade turnover, of which more than half are direct links between goods manufacturers. According to the vice-president, the prospects for all-round cooperation between Uzbekistan and Russia are "very favourable".
