930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Sheldon Jones sells mill in Somerset Sheldon Jones, the USM-traded pet food manufacturer, has sold for Pounds 460,000 cash an industrial mill at Wells, Somerset. Proceeds will be used to reduce term debt. The mill has not been used by the company since 1989 and is currently let out. The sale is in line with the new management's objective to reorganise and re-focus the assets and business.
