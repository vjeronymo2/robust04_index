December 7, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE FOOTBALL STARS TO BE NAMED

The 1989 Times All-Westside football team will be announced in Thursday's edition.

The first and second squads, 23 members each, were selected by sportswriters based on a poll of coaches and discussions with scouts. First-team players will be honored with a breakfast and awards ceremony at 9 a.m. Dec. 17 at the Anaheim Hilton, 777 Convention Way.

All-stars from 12 Times circulation areas will be honored.

Featured speaker will be Coach Dennis Green of Stanford.
