920807 FT 07 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Assets decline at Anglo & Overseas At June 30 1992, net asset value at Anglo & Overseas Trust came to 311.1p, compared with 328.2p a year earlier. In the six months ended that date, gross revenue fell to Pounds 7.89m (Pounds 8.37m), leading to earnings per share of 3.29p (3.39p). The interim dividend is unchanged at 1.7p.
