910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / World Trade News: US company in Polish telecom race AMERITECH, a US telecommunications company, and France Telecom yesterday emerged as front-runners in a race to supply Poland with a cellular telephone system worth Dollars 150m (Pounds 88.2m), Christopher Bobinski reports from Warsaw. The two, working in partnership, came ahead of Crowley Cellular Telecommunications, and BMTS, a venture grouping British Telecom, Bell of the US, and Swedish and Finnish telecommunications groups on a final-round short list. PPTT, Poland's telecommunications company, is to run the cellular system in partnership with the winner. The final three groups must now negotiate the terms of the joint company with PPTT.
