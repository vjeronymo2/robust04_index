940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Pacific Assets expansion Net asset value per share of Pacific Assets Trust jumped by 94 per cent - from 350.8p to 681.1p - over the 12 months to January 31 1994. Available revenue for the year was higher at Pounds 408,000 against Pounds 262,000, after a tax charge of Pounds 198,000, up from Pounds 137,000, giving a per share value of 2.07p, compared with 1.34p last time. The single distribution to shareholders is lifted to 1.2p (1.1p).
