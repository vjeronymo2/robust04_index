920729 FT 29 JUL 92 / The Barcelona Olympics: Judo silver for Britain Ray Stevens was the toast of the British Olympic team in Barcelona after finally ending their medal famine with a judo silver. The 29-year-old fitness coach from South London was just one fight away from becoming the first British judo player ever to win Olympic gold, but had to concede the gold in the light-heavyweight final to Hungarian Antal Kovacs. Theo Meijer of the Netherlands and Dmitri Sergeev of the Unified Team won the bronze medals. Kim Mi-Jung of South Korea won the women's half-heavyweight (72kg) class judo gold meda, beating Yoko Tanabe of Japan.
