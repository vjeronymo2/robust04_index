BFN [Text] United Nations, April 6 (AFP) -- Bosnian Serbs besieging the Moslem enclave of Gorazde in Bosnia-Herzegovina stopped their shelling late Wednesday [6 April], a UN spokesman said Wednesday. "Gorazde is no longer under attack since 1800 local time," the spokesman, Joe Sills said. Sills said that the UN representative in Bosnia Yasushi Akashi had received assurance from the Bosnian Serbs that the attacks would not resume.
