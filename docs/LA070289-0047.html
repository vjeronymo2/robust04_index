July 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

PROTEST ON PIGGING

I was aghast that Bill Marriott described families as "pigging out" (The Savvy Traveler, June 11) in his hotels. First, the condescension toward families is evident in his terminology, and secondly, Marriott hotels should not have offered the bargain to families if it did not want their business.

SALLY MONROE

Los Angeles
