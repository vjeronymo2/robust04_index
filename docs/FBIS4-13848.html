BFN [Text] Mr. 'Ali Akbar Velayati, our country's foreign minister, who has gone to Romania in order to attend the general session of the World Forum Foundation, has met that country's president and, as well as conveying the warm greetings of President Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani, has expressed the anxiety felt by Islamic countries about the situation of Bosnian Muslims. In the meeting, Romanian President Ion Iliescu spoke of the bilateral relations between Iran and Romania and called for the expansion of political and economic ties between the two countries. Also today, on the third day of the current session of the World Forum Foundation, Mr. Velayati, who was in the chair, spoke about our country's economic capabilities and about those of Central Asian countries. In the meeting, which was attended by the representatives of most countries, discussions were held about the pipeline for the transfer of gas and oil from Turkmenistan and Kazakhstan to Europe via Iran.
