May 24, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

IN BRIEF;

CZECHS TIE SOVIETS IN ICE HOCKEY

Rostislav Vlach's goal in the last two minutes gave Czechoslovakia a 3-3 tie against the world champion Soviet ice hockey team today in a three-nation double round-robin tournament.

The Soviet Union led 2-1 in the first period on goals by Alexei Kasatonov and Andrei Kovalev and boosted the lead to 3-1 in the second period.

But Czechoslovakia, which finished third after Canada in the World Championships in Stockholm in May, fought back with two third-period goals, including Vlach's shot from short range.
