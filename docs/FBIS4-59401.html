BFN [Text] At 1830 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received Salih Abu-Bakr Bin-Husaynun, Yemeni minister of oil and mineral wealth. The meeting was attended by Faruq al-Shar', foreign minister; Dr. Nadir Nabulsi, minister of oil and and mineral resources; and 'Abduh Shatfah, the Yemeni ambassador in Damascus. Bin-Husaynun conveyed to his excellency the president a message from Yemeni Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid. Discussions dealt with the situation in Yemen. His excellency the president stressed Syria's extreme interest in the ongoing events in the Yemeni arena. He reiterated the call for the Yemeni brothers to resolve their differences through dialogue, away from fighting, with a spirit of brotherhood, and with a view to safeguarding the homeland's unity and the safety of its children.
