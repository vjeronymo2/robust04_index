BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent Viktor Kovalenko for TASS] [Text] Kiev, 23 Jun -- Ukrainian Supreme Council Chairman Aleksandr Moroz has said that he sees a solution to the problem of the Black Sea Fleet in an arrangement whereby Russia and Ukraine will execute joint command of the fleet but each side would be responsible for the financial and organizational aspects of running its share of the fleet. He said so at a news conference today. The Supreme Council head said that Ukraine's agreements with the CIS countries ought to be deepened because they are not working at the moment and no one is trying to make them work. He said he was concerned with the fact that the world community was attempting to turn Ukraine into an appendage supplying raw resources, and spoke in favor of setting up and developing self-contained production cycles in the state, which would meet Ukraine's demand for the main types of industrial products.
