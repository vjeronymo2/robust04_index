930201 FT 01 FEB 93 / International Company News: Provision hits Ensor Ensor Holdings, the USM-quoted group with interest in building materials and motor vehicles, incurred a loss of Pounds 2.1m pre-tax for the half year ended September 30. That was well up on the adjusted deficit of Pounds 294,000 recorded for the same period of the previous year, but significantly down on the Pounds 2.74m loss reported for the second half of the 1991-92 year. The first half loss was struck after taking account of an exceptional provision of Pounds 1.57m which related to the disposal of discontinued activities. Interest charges rose to Pounds 510,000 (Pounds 447,000). Turnover of continuing activities totalled Pounds 11.86m (Pounds 12.16m). Losses per share emerged at 18.1p (1.4p). Comparative figures have been adjusted to accord with new accounting policies.
