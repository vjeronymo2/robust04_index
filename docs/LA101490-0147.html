October 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

INFANT AND GIRL, 5, KILLED IN CAR CRASH

A 7-week-old infant and a 5-year-old girl were killed Saturday in a car crash at 109th Street and Crenshaw Boulevard in Inglewood, police said.

The children, whose names were not released, were passengers in the same car when the mid-afternoon accident occurred, a police spokesman said. Details of the incident -- which may have involved a single car -- were still being investigated, the spokesman added.
