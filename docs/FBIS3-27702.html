Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Osaka, February 27 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice Premier Zhu Rongji arrived in Osaka this evening on the second leg of a nine-day visit which takes in a first-hand look at corporate activities in Japan's western and southwestern areas. During his two-day stay in the industrial hub, Zhu will meet a group of business leaders including Toyota Motor Corp. President Tatsuro Toyoda. He is expected to brief them about the Chinese reform on a shift to a market economy and urge them to expand trade and economic cooperation with China. He is scheduled to visit the recently completed Kansai New International Airport and a clothing wholesale market on Monday. The vice premier will also travel to Kagawa and Fukuoka Prefectures and leaves for home on March 4.
