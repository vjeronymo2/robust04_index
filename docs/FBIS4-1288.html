BFN [Text] The police in the Western Transvaal say that damage of about 25,000 rands has been caused by an explosion at the Vaal Reef Mine in Klerskdorp. However, reports say that no one was injured. The incident occurred at a parking garage of the mine's number five shaft early this morning. The police said that security guards had seen an unidentified man in the area before the blast. Industrial explosives are believed to have been used.
