BFN [Text] The NTV television company said it had in its possession a document signed by former Vice President Aleksandr Rutskoy during the days of the October crisis in 1993. This is an edict instructing state bodies and officials to stop members of Yeltsin's immediate entourage from going abroad. In an NTV interview, Rutskoy categorically refuted the allegations that he was the author of this document, saying that this edict was forged.
