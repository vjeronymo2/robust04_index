September 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: SANTA MONICA;

PERMIT PARKING APPROVED

The City Council has adopted an ordinance establishing a preferential-parking zone on 16th and 17th streets between Montana and Alta avenues.

The zone establishes one-hour parking between 9 a.m. and 9 p.m. Monday through Saturday, except by permit.

Merchants who have businesses, including haircutting and retail sales, on Montana Avenue complained that a one-hour limit would be insufficient for their customers. But the council rejected the merchants' request for two-hour or 90-minute parking.

The council also adopted an ordinance that establishes a preferential-parking zone on 25th Street between Santa Monica Boulevard and Broadway. Parking will be by permit only.
