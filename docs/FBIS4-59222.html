BFN [Text] Manama, 10 May (WAKH) -- The amir of Bahrain, His Highness Shaykh 'Isa Bin-Salman Al Khalifah, this morning received Robert Pelletreau, U.S. assistant secretary of state for Near East and North African affairs, and his delegation currently visiting Bahrain. During the meeting, his highness the amir praised the deep, close relations between Bahrain and the United States. He expressed satisfaction with the level of the existing bilateral relations of friendship and cooperation and their importance in maintaining the Gulf region's security and stability. Mr. Pelletreau briefed his Highness Amir 'Isa on the latest developments in Yemen and the importance of containing the current crisis to prevent it from being exacerbated [words indistinct] dangerous repercussions on the future of Yemen and regional security and stability. The two sides reviewed bilateral relations and ways to bolster them in various domains. They also reviewed the latest [words indistinct] Arab, and international developments. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Shaykh Muhammad Bin- Mubarak Al Khalifah, Interior Minister Muhammad Bin-Khalifah Al Khalifah, Communications Minister 'Ali Bin-Khalifah Al Khalifah, Foreign Ministry Under Secretary Ghazi Muhammad al-Qusaybi, and the U.S. charge d'affaires in Bahrain.
