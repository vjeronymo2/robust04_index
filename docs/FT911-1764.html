910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / Waterford sees signs of recovery WATERFORD WEDGWOOD, the international luxury crystal and china company, says it has turned the corner and is on the way to recovery after the near-disastrous performance of its crystal division in recent years. The group announced pre-tax losses for last year of IPounds 21.4m (Pounds 19.4m) compared with losses of IPounds 20.6m in 1989. However, it is encouraged by ahigher operating profits in the second half of 1990 and a large-scale reduction in debt levels. Dublin analysts gave a mixed reception to the results. One said it was clear that 'a disaster was now turning itself around'. But others said the group still had problems at its crystal division Details, Page 20
