BFN [Text] Beijing, May 10 (CNS) -- Minister of Internal Trade, Mr. Zhang Haoruo, speaking at a meeting recently held by the ministry on an analysis of the economic situation in the internal trade sector in the country, stressed that the key task at present facing the ministry was to stabilize markets and prices in the country. All departments and units engaging in commodity circulation must take an earnest attitude to the inflation problem with special attention being paid to the stability of prices of important goods such as grain, edible oil, meat, sugar and chemical fertilizers. The minister said that the national economic operations in the country in the first quarter this year was on the whole good. Some problems and difficulties, however, still appeared including the unsatisfactory sales of production means and a drop in economic results in enterprises specializing in commodity circulation. Some of these problems and difficulties resulted from certain temporary factors and were undergoing a solution through measures taken by relevant state departments. Mr. Zhang called for departments and units under the ministry to keep a close eye on and analyze market changes and trends especially for important goods related to people's livelihood and at the same time adopt respective measures to control and regulate such changes and trends. Mr. Zhang pointed out that the current work for the ministry was the purchase of summer grain and edible oil, the easing of supply of these products, the supply of chemical fertilizers, the earnest implementation of state price policies, prevention increased burdens imposed on farmers and the rectification of the market order of agricultural production means.
