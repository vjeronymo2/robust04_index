January 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

REAL FOOTBALL IS PLAYED AT BOWLING GREEN, TOO

Perhaps West Virginia Coach Don Nehlen should learn a little history before he opens his mouth again in front of 200 reporters. In the Bowl Notebook on Dec. 30, Nehlen said he "thanked goodness (he) had a chance to go where they play real football," while commenting on his departure from Bowling Green State University.

Just to refresh Nehlen's memory, Bowling Green earned a bowl bid and a national ranking with a 10-0 record 3 years before the Mountaineers did it for the first time in their 97-year history.

KURT KEMPER

Los Angeles
