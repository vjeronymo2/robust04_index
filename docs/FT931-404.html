930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Kewill Systems sells German subsidiary Kewill Systems is selling Weigang, its German manufacturing and commercial systems subsidiary, to Seitz Industrie Loesungen, for DM1 and profit-related payments to a maximum of DM1.5m (Pounds 600,000). Kewill is retaining bank and trade liabilities, which it has guaranteed totalling DM5.6m. It will be relieved of DM2.5m of other trade liabilities. Kewill has also agreed to provide an interest-free loan of DM500,000 to Seitz, which is owned by one of the former vendors of Weigang, repayable within 18 months.
