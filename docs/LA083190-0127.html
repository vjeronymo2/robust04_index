August 31, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

HEAD PEOPLE

Stor Furnishings International Inc., Industry, has named William P. Hayes to the newly created post of executive vice president-marketing.

Hayes, 42, has served as vice president-home furnishings for the Laura Ashley chain. Richard C. St. Peter, vice president, chief financial officer and secretary, has resigned to take a job at another company. His duties will be filled temporarily by Gary L. Schaugaard, a director and former chief financial officer for Long's Drugstores.
