BFN [Text] Recently appointed Georgian Defense Minister Varden Nadabaidze has issued a number of appointment orders, the Ministry's press center told Interfax. Major General Paata Datuashvili was relieved of his duties as first deputy defense minister to be replaced by Major General Guram Nikolaishvili who had been chief of the Georgian Army's General Staff. Major General Nadar Tatarashvili was appointed as the chief of the general staff. Until recently, he was in charge of the Georgian peacekeeping forces in South Ossetia.
