Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Jose Luis Armenta from the "ACIR Radio News" newscast] [Text] Deputy Maria de los Angeles Moreno, president of the Congressional Permanent Commission, issued a communique which states the following: Based on Article 112 of the basic law of the Mexican Congress, we convene all members of the Permanent Commission to sessions that will be held in the Legislators Room of the San Lazaro Legislative Palace at 1700 on Saturday 26 March. The agenda for this special session has not been announced. However, it has been unofficially mentioned or speculated that the Permanent Commission could convene a special session of Congress. The purpose of this special session could be to reform the electoral law. That is what we have for now.
