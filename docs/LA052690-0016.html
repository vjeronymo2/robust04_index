May 26, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CELEBRITY DEATHS: DAVIS, HENSON AND JILL IRELAND

It was in 1967 when Sammy shook hands with my heart.

My husband Ed and I were visiting Jerry Lewis, the fine comedian, as he was making a movie at the Shepperton Studios just outside of London. Jerry was busy -- so he told us to go to the commissary on our own.

Sammy was there making a movie too -- and noticing that we were strangers, came to our table, welcomed us as he introduced himself, and as he shook our hands, said, "We were strangers no more." Shalom, Sammy -- you'll make friends wherever you are. We'll miss you, we'll miss you.

ELEANOR N. JACOBS

Carlsbad
