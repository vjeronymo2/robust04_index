931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / International Company News: Mattel set for another year of record results MATTEL, the leading US toymaker best known for its Barbie dolls, has posted record third-quarter and nine-month earnings and said its performance for the first nine months of 1993 showed momentum for achieving another year of record sales and profits, Reuter reports from Los Angeles. Third-quarter earnings rose to Dollars 83.9m, or 86 cents a share, from Dollars 70.1m, or 71 cents. Sales rose to Dollars 671.1m from Dollars 606.5m. Nine-month earnings rose to Dollars 150.8m, or Dollars 1.52 a share, from Dollars 108.3m, or Dollars 1.07, in the corresponding 1992 period. The 1993 results include a gain on changes in accounting of Dollars 14.6m. The 1992 results include a pre-tax charge of Dollars 10m. Sales for the nine months rose to Dollars 1.43bn from Dollars 1.34bn. 'Our growth is particularly strong, recognising the negative impact of foreign exchange,' chairman Mr John Amerman said. He noted that on a constant dollar basis third-quarter sales rose 17 per cent.
