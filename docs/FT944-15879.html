941015 FT 15 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico goes higher Mexican share prices closed moderately higher in featureless trading as investors wait to see if third quarter company results confirm an upturn in the economy. The IPC index of 37 leading shares gained 18.08, or 0.66 per cent, at 2,777.69. A trader said: 'I think the market has already partially discounted the company reports but is waiting to see if they match expectations.' Brazil Shares closed off 2.2 per cent in volatile trade on the Sao Paulo bourse on speculation ahead of the options settlements on Monday and widespread concern over rising inflation in the current quarter. The Bovespa index of the 55 most-active shares ended down 1,108 at 48,338 points.
