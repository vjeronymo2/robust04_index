940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Citic Pacific tipped for 30% interim rise - Other companies Citic Pacific, the Hong Kong-listed arm of the Beijing foreign investment company, reports its interim results today, kicking off the week's corporate announcements. Analysts are looking for a rise of more than 30 per cent at the interim stage, giving a net income figure of HKDollars 1.04bn (Dollars 135m). Earnings will be boosted by a full six-months' contribution from Hongkong Telecom, in which it has a 12 per cent stake. On Thursday it is the turn of Cheung Kong, the property developer controlled by Li Ka-shing, to release its interims. The results will provide a pointer for the developers which follow.
