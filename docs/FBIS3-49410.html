Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] President Michal Kovac is prepared to receive information about the Brussels NATO meeting, which is to be given by President Clinton to the top officials of the Visegrad Four states during their meeting in Prague. Answering a question from TASR [Slovak news agency] concerning his opinion on the cooperation of Visegrad Four countries in their effort to join NATO, Michal Kovac said he personally believes coordination and cooperation will help the countries involved get incorporated into this security structure, although Partnership for Peace is a project to be concluded with the alliance by each individual country separately.
