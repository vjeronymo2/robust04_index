Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] A Georgian state delegation led by Minister of Foreign Affairs Aleksandre Chikvaidze, on its way to the Islamic Republic of Iran for an official visit, made a stopover in Baku, capital of Azerbaijan on 14 February. The leader of the delegation was received by the country's president, Geydar Aliyev. A meeting between the two countries' foreign ministers, Hasan Hasanov and Aleksandre Chikvaidze, took place at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Azerbaijan. In the course of the two-hour meeting, the ministers discussed a wide range of political and economic issues relating to cooperation between Georgia and Azerbaijan. The treaty on collective security among the CIS member- states and [words indistinct] were given special attention at the meeting. The ministers also discussed issues relating to the opening of the Georgian embassy in Baku and the Azerbaijani embassy in Tbilisi. A member of the Georgian delegation, Industry Minister Vladimer Kereselidze, visited the Ministry of Light Industry of Azerbaijan, where he met its (?leadership). Issues of mutually beneficial cooperation between the two neighboring countries, prospects for setting up joint enterprises, and possibilities for the delivery of raw materials were discussed. The Georgian state delegation left Baku for Tehran on the same day.
