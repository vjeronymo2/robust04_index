Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 4 Jan (INA) -- Iraq has informed the Arab League that the rulers of Saudi Arabia and Kuwait have continued to provide facilities for U.S., British, and French aircraft violating Iraqi airspace, noting that this poses a serious threat to Iraq's security and sovereignty. Iraq's Arab League representative said that during 27-31 December these aircraft continued to violate Iraqi airspace, taking off from bases in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait, and conducted 258 aggressive sorties. Iraq's representative enclosed a list detailing these violations to inform the Arab League member states of these regrettable facts.
