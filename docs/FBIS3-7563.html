Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [From MAINICHI SHIMBUN 30 March Morning Edition] [Text] Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata telephoned Vice President of the European Union [EU] Leon Brittan on the evening of 29 March, to explain the substance of the external economic policy reform outline announced by the government on the same day. Regarding the economic package, Hata stressed this does not discriminate against any country in favor of the United States, and the economic measures were decided upon by the Japanese government on its own initiative to improve the people's quality of life. He is believed to have explained that the economic package is not for the sake of the United States, and sought the EU's understanding on this.
