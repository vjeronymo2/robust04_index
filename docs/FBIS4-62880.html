BFN [Decrees by the Georgian Parliament on the appointment of Parliament commission chairmen issued in Tbilisi on 10 May] [Text] Decree of the Georgian Parliament on the Appointment of Teimuraz Kopaleishvili as Chairman of the Georgian Parliament Finance and Budget Commission The Georgian Parliament decrees: To appoint Teimuraz Kopaleishvili as chairman of the Georgian Parliament Finance and Budget Commission. [Signed] Rusudan Beridze, deputy speaker of the Georgian Parliament Tbilisi, 10 May 1994. [Signed] Rusudan Beridze, deputy speaker of the Georgian Parliament Tbilisi, 10 May 1994.
