BFN [Summary] The third investment privatization auction was held by the Kazakh State Property Committee on 7 June. Forty-eight enterprises were to be privatized. Representatives of 113 investment privatization funds participated in the auction. The minimum rate of stocks was 10 tenge for two coupons at the start of the auction, and by its end the rate increased by a factor of 3.5 and equaled 6.84 coupons. About 80 funds managed to buy at least one share. Eighty-four percent of stocks were sold as a result of the auction; 33 enterprises were completely sold out; one enterprise was not popular at all and nobody claimed its stocks. The organizers think this auction has been the largest and most successful.
