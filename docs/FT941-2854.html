940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Cortecs reduces interim losses Cortecs International, the pharmaceuticals company which has announced plans to raise Pounds 15m through a London flotation, yesterday reported slightly reduced interim losses. Net losses were cut from ADollars 4.32m to ADollars 4.24m (Pounds 2.08m) in the six months to December 31, on sales of ADollars 5.4m (ADollars 5.14m). Losses per share were unchanged at 5.6 cents. The company, incorporated in Australia, but with research, development, manufacturing and sales in the UK, already has an Australian listing.
