Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Manana, Zviad Gamsakhurdia's widow, has sent a telegram to Russian President Boris Yeltsin and the Russian Federal Assembly asking for help in having her husband's body buried in Chechnya. She informed the Chechenpress agency about this today. The widow of Georgia's former president, who committed suicide, said she was categorically against representatives of Tbilisi officialdom carrying out forensic tests on her husband's body. Her view is that they would be used for political purposes by Zviad Gamsakhurdia's enemies.
