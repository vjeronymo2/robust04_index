BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres has said that Israel is not prepared to accept Syria's proposal to first cede the Golan Heights and uproot settlements there and only then to enter negotiations. Minister Peres noted that Israel is conducting talks with the Syrians on three levels: territory, the nature of peace, and security arrangements. The foreign minister stressed that had the Syrian president signed a peace treaty with Israel together with President al-Sadat, he would have received everything the Egyptian president did. Our correspondent Meron Tzur reports that Mr. Peres was speaking in a rally of Army veterans in Tel Aviv.
