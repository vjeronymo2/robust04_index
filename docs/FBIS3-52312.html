Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Deputy Defense Minister Mordekhay Gur visited the settlement of Na'ama north of Jericho this afternoon, and promised inhabitants of the Jordan Valley there will be territorial contiguity along Israel's eastern border. Our settlement affairs correspondent Gil Litman reports that Gur's meeting with the settlers was unpleasant and raucous and that he could barely make himself heard. They leveled bitter complaints against the government and voiced anxiety over their personal safety if the village of al-'Awja is included in the future autonomy.
