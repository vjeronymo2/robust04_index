BFN [Text] Prime Minister Goh Chok Tong is in Germany for a four-day official visit. Mr. Goh, who is making his first trip to Germany as prime minister, is accompanied by three cabinet ministers and 14 high-ranking businessmen. Tomorrow, Mr. Goh will hold talks with Chancellor Helmut Kohl. The two leaders will also witness the signing of the German-Singapore Business Forum by private sectors from both sides. The forum is intended to promote business tie-ups in the Asia-Pacific region. Mr. Goh's trip, which will also take him to Britain next week, is seeking to sell Singapore's new economic strategy to European businessmen.
