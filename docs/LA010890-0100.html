January 8, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

NATION;

ALASKA REDOUBT VOLCANO ERUPTS

Redoubt Volcano erupted today with no warning, shooting an ash plume at least 30,000 feet into the air that was visible 110 miles away in Anchorage, a scientist said.

Dan Miller of the Alaska Volcano Observatory said: "We're having an eruption right now. It looks like a major one. It does contain a lot of ash. It didn't give any immediate warning."
