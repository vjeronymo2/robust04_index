930907 FT 07 SEP 93 / International Company News: Mediobanca plans issue MEDIOBANCA, the Italian merchant bank which was a big shareholder in the collapsed Ferruzzi Finanziaria group, yesterday announced lower profits and plans for a rights issue, Reuter reports from Milan. Net profits for the year ended June were L200.1bn (Dollars 126m), compared with 262.1bn a year earlier. Bad debt provision was L275.2bn, up from L153.1bn. The dividend is being increased by 10 per cent to L200 a share. The bank is proposing a scrip and rights issue to increase nominal share capital to L476bn from L340bn. There is to be a one-for-10 scrip issue and a three-for-10 rights offer at L10,000 a share. A total of 102m new shares will be issued in this way.
