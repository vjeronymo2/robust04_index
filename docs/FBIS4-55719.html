BFN [BETA attributed report: "General Domazetovic -- Special Adviser to Martic"] [Text] Knin -- At its session yesterday, the supreme defense command of the republic of Serbian Krajina appointed Colonel-General Ljubomir Domazetovic as special defense adviser to the supreme commander and president of the republic of Serbian Krajina, Milan Martic. Colonel-General Ljubomir Domazetovic was a prominent, high-ranking officer in the Yugoslav Army until he was retired in the summer of 1993.
