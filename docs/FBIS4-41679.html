BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Andrey Kirillov] [Text] Moscow, 28 Jun -- Talks have started between Russian and Chinese Foreign Ministers Andrey Kozyrev and Qian Qichen at the Russian Foreign Ministry's mansion. "Communication between us is becoming closer and more regular," the Russian foreign minister said at the start of the meeting. He expressed his sympathy over the damaging floods in southern China, which have led to the loss of many lives. Qian Qichen said the September meeting between PRC President Jiang Zemin and Russian President Boris Yeltsin, preparations for which are the center of these talks, "has great significance."
