920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / International Company News: LTV and union close to settling contract dispute LTV and the United Steelworkers Union have reached a tentative agreement to settle a contract dispute, officials from the company and the union said yesterday, AP-DJ reports. LTV, the US's second largest steel company, had sought to nullify its contract with the United Steelworkers of America and to change the benefits of its 47,000 retirees. The union said it would strike if the contract were scrapped. However, LTV said the company agreed to withdraw a court petition seeking to nullify the contract. Details of the agreement will be presented to steelworkers before general release.
