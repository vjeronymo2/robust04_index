Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] An extremely tense situation exists at the Karabakh battlefronts. Fierce battles are continuing along almost all the front lines. Nagorno-Karabakh Republic Defense Army headquarters reported that Azerbaijani troops resumed the offensive on the southern and southeastern sector of the battlefront this morning. By midday the Karabakh formations were still managing to halt the enemy's attacks. A large attack is also expected on the other sectors of the front. This is confirmed by reconnaissance data. Positional battles are continuing in the north of Kelbadzharskiy Rayon. According to confirmed data, 70 Azerbaijani soldiers were killed and seven tanks were hit in this sector on 25 January. Four fighters were killed and 11 were injured on the Karabakh side. The Armenian Defense Ministry press service reported at 1100 that there has been relative calm in the border regions of Armenia.
