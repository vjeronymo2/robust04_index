940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / World Trade News: US and France in plastics venture Union Carbide of the US and Elf-Atochem of France, part of Elf Aquitaine, are to create a joint venture to manufacture specialist plastics. The move is the latest in a series of consolidations in the European petrochemicals industry in response to low prices and competition from the Middle East and Asia. The venture will market polyethylene resins and compounds for the wire and cable, pipe and other industries in Europe. It will take over Elf-Atochem's the gas phase polyethylene reactor at Gonfreville, France, and Union Carbide will license its Unipol process technology to the venture.
