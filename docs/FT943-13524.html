940719 FT 19 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Amicable Smaller net asset value dips Amicable Smaller Enterprises Trust reported a net asset value of 137.84p as at June 30, a decline of 3.3 per cent since the trust's December year end. The benchmark Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index fell 4.8 per cent during the same period. Net revenue for the six months to end-June amounted to Pounds 605,000 (Pounds 356,000). The interim dividend is held at 1.7p, payable, after a Pounds 77,000 transfer from revenue reserves, from lower earnings of 1.51p (1.71p) reflecting increased capital after a Pounds 29m placing late last year.
