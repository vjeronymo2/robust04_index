BFN [Text] Cairo, 17 Apr (MENA)--Dr. Yusuf Wali, deputy prime minister and minister of agriculture and land reclamation, met today with Ugandan Vice President Dr. Samson Kisekka and the delegation accompanying him who are currently visiting Cairo. They reviewed a number of issues of mutual interest, primarily the current situation in Africa. During the meeting, Dr. Wali emphasized the firm relations between the two countries and Egypt's readiness to supply Uganda with Egyptian agricultural products, especially cotton and oil products. Wali also agreed to increase scholarships for Ugandans at the Egyptian international agricultural center. The Ugandan official said his country looks forward to enhancing bilateral ties and economic and agricultural cooperation with Egypt. The Ugandan official is expected to meet several officials and visit major agricultural and industrial projects.
