Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 12 (KCNA) -- The U.S. imperialists on February 10 staged a provocative surprise bombing exercise against the North with the mobilization of formations of overseas- based assault planes and an early warning plane, according to military sources. Formations of Japan-based assault and pursuit-assault planes flew into the skies above Nyongwol and Kunwi, South Korea that day and held a frantic war exercise of surprise strikes at selected targets and return to their base under the command of the "E-3" early warning plane. The ceaseless war maneuvers of the U.S. imperialists are further straining the situation on the Korean peninsula.
