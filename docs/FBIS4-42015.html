BFN [Text] On 29 June Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk received Lionel Soto, vice president of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Cuba, who paid an official visit to our country as head of a Cuban governmental delegation. There was a conversation during which various aspects of Ukrainian-Cuban cooperation were touched upon. We highly assess the all-round development of such relations and strive for their successful development in this direction, Leonid Kravchuk said. Our people, he stressed, have always respected the Cuban people. In the conversation they discussed a number of long-term trends in Ukrainian-Cuban cooperation. After the meeting Lionel Soto replied to a number of questions from journalists. Dwelling on his conversation with the president he referred to it as very important. We had an opportunity to exchange opinions with the president on restoring relations between the two countries and their further development, already in new conditions, when Ukraine acts as an independent state. For a certain period of time these relations were weakened, however at present prospects have become available for restoring and deepening these relations.
