920207 FT 07 FEB 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Cape plums are superb this week at 75-90p a lb (85-95p), along with grapefruit at 20-30p each (19-30p) for white varieties reports the FFVIB. Grapes remain a good buy with Cape Thompson seedless at Pounds 1.20-1.45 a lb (Pounds 1.00-1.25) and Cape Sultana seedless at Pounds 1.10-1.30 a lb (Pounds 1.30-1.40). All cabbage varieties are superb with lots of varieties to choose from: Spring greens at 25-35p a lb (35-45p), Celtic and Primo at 25-30p a lb (25-30p), and red and white varieties 15-25p a lb (15-25p). Large heads of white celery at 55-65p a head (55-65p) continue to be a best salad buy. Another notable salad buy are tomatoes at 60-70p a lb (50-80p).
