Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] Within the framework of reducing debts owed to Slovakia by the former Soviet Union, Slovakia will purchase military equipment in Russia to the tune of between $80 and $90 million. This was stated by Igor Urban, state secretary of the Slovak Defense Ministry, following his talks with Russian officials in Moscow. According to him, experts from both countries will discuss in the weeks to follow the precise amount and kind of equipment to be purchased. Igor Urban, who signed a plan for military cooperation between the Slovak and Russian Defense Ministries in Moscow today, remarked that at issue was equipment that the Slovak Armed Forces will use in the current year.
