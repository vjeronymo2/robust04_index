920226 FT 26 FEB 92 / UK Company News: South West Water joint waste venture South West Water has formed a joint venture company with Weir Group to provide waste treatment equipment. The new company - Rugged Environmental Technology Systems - will exploit a market for the treatment and disposal of industrial waste, sewage sludge and domestic refuse worth Pounds 2bn a year in the UK alone. Strachan & Henshaw, a Weir subsidiary, and Peninsula Waste Technology, a South West Water offshoot, will each have a 50 per cent stake.
