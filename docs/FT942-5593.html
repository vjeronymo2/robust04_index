940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / UK Company News: All-round growth lifts Carr's Milling Carr's Milling Industries said all areas of its business had contributed to an 85 per cent rise in pre-tax profits during the six months to February 26. The group, which has interests in agribusiness, flour milling, baking and engineering, increased profits to Pounds 1.13m (Pounds 610,000) on sales from continuing businesses of Pounds 37.9m (Pounds 35m). The interim dividend is 1.2p (1p) on earnings of 10.5p (5.4p).
