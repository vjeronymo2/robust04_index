Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Caracol 0600 to 0900" newscast] [Text] Santa Fe de Bogota -- Government Minister Fabio Villegas Ramirez, in pointing out his concern for the escalation of terrorism over the weekend, when subversives perpetrated a number of actions in Antioquia, Meta, Arauca, Cundinamarca, and Tolima Departments, has said the government has not been weak in maintaining public order in the country but that it is implementing measures to give adequate protection to those are working in the current electoral process. Villegas reported on the protection provided for candidates to government positions but admitted that many of them do not want to be subjected to this type of control.
