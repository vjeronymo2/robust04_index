Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow March 4 TASS -- A Department of Information Provision will be set up under the presidential administration on today's decree of President Yeltsin, the presidential press service reports. The department will provide for timely and objective informing Russian and foreign citizens of the Russian state policy, activities of the Russian president and his administration, major developments and their estimation. Under the decree, the department will have the following tasks: -- provision of mass media bodies with objective and reliable materials on policy of the president and the government, major social, economic and political problems in reforms of Russian society and measures taken for their settlement; -- assistance to mass media bodies in broad circulation of forecasts of Russian developments in accordance with resolutions of Russian state power bodies; -- mass media coverage of arrangements taken for the implementation of purpose-oriented programs on major social and economic issues as well as Russian political processes. Petr Filippov has been nominated head of the new department.
