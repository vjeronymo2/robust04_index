Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] President Suharto received a courtesy call from visiting Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina, who was accompanied by Foreign Minister Ali Alatas. During the occasion, Robaina conveyed Cuban President Fidel Castro's written message to President Suharto. According to Alatas, the written message, among other things, clarified the itinerary of the minister's visit to Indonesia and other countries such as Vietnam, Laos, Cambodia and Malaysia. During the meeting, Suharto briefed his guest on Indonesia's development program. On bilateral relations, Suharto said the Indonesian Government observes the principles of mutual respect of sovereignty, territorial integrity and non-interference in internal affairs. He said bilateral cooperation based on mutual benefit could be developed through a desirable manner. Meanwhile, in his clarification to newsmen, Robaina said that cooperation between the two countries will be upgraded. As such, it would be necessary to study sectors which need to be developed. Robaina said the Cuban Government was satisfied with Indonesia's leadership as chairman of the Nonaligned Movement. [passage omitted]
