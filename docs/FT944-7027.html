941126 FT 26 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Dart rises 15% Interim pre tax profits at Dart Group, the distribution and aviation services company, advanced 15 per cent from Pounds 1.25m to Pounds 1.44m. Sales for the half year to September 30 edged ahead from Pounds 27m to Pounds 27.5m. Last year's figure included turnover from Dart's holding in Benair Hong Kong, sold in December. The rise in earnings per share from 5.8 to 6.5p lifts the dividend to 1.5p (1.3p).
