Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] New Delhi says that it entirely shares the growing global interests on the strict observation of human rights. An External Affairs Ministry spokesman said that India is committed to strengthening the institutions relevant to the upholding of human rights in India. Commenting on a reported letter from the U.S. President, Mr. Bill Clinton, to one Dr. Ghulam Ali Fai of New York, the spokesman said that India trusts that when Mr. Clinton referred to respect for human rights, it was in the context of gross violation of human rights by militants and terrorists, aided and abetted from outside India.
