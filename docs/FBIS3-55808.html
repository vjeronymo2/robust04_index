Language: Estonian Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Mart Laar stated at a news conference today that within the next couple of days the government will shape an opinion about planned cooperation with NATO. According to Laar, a statement from Estonia to NATO has been drawn up and should logically bear signatures of both the president and the prime minister. Later, once a decision is taken, Estonia will be ready immediately to start talks so as to (?fulfill) a Partnership for Peace agreement with a spcific content, Laar added. According to Laar, the statement under discussion would (?serve) as a starting point for the talks. Estonia wishes to be one of the first countries to open talks with NATO with quite a specific program, art Laar added.
