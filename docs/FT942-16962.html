940407 FT 07 APR 94 / UK Company News: Cookson to buy Union Carbide arm Cookson, the industrial materials group, has signed a letter of intent to buy the electronic materials division of Union Carbide, the US chemicals manufacturer. The final consideration for the businesses, which had sales of Dollars 28m (Pounds 19m) last year, was not disclosed. The acquisition will add protective coatings to the range of printed circuit board chemicals which Cookson supplies. The deal covers three Union Carbide subsidiaries - London Chemical and Specialty Coating Systems of the US and Nova Tran, a UK company. They will join Cookson's electronic materials division which had sales of Pounds 400m last year.
