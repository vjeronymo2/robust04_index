BFN [Text] Havana, 13 Jun (NOTIMEX) -- Today, the Cuban Foreign Ministry confirmed a group of people's forceful entry into the German Embassy in Havana and described the action as an "obvious provocation." Miguel Alfonso, official spokesman of the Cuban Foreign Ministry, has told NOTIMEX: "We want to point out that this action has taken place on the very day the president of Mexico arrives in Havana and on the eve of the 4th Ibero-American summit." The spokesman said the forceful entry of these people into the German Embassy "is additional evidence of the harmful results of the U.S. policy" of denying visas to those who request visas at the U.S. Interests Office here.
