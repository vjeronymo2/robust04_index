July 10, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

THE WEEK AHEAD: PARIS;

FOCUS ON CAMBODIA

The five permanent members of the U.N. Security Council are to meet in the French capital Monday in another effort to end the 10-year-old Cambodian civil war.

It will be the first such attempt since three of the four feuding Cambodian factions signed an agreement in Tokyo last month calling for a cease-fire and establishment of a de facto temporary government prior to free elections.

But the warring factions still can't agree on fundamental issues and are to be excluded from the Paris meeting.
