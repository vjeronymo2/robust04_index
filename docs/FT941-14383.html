940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / International Capital Markets: Fund manager links with CIBC TAL Investment Counsel, Canada's second-biggest pension fund manager, is joining forces with the Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce to create a money management group administering CDollars 20bn (USDollars 15.2m) of assets, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. CIBC will contribute CDollars 7bn in assets under administration and will hold 55 per cent of the new company. TAL will contribute CDollars 13bn and hold 45 per cent with equal voting rights.
