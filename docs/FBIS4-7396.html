BFN [Text] The State Commission for Cooperation and Investment and the Overseas Vietnamese Central Department has just issued a circular to encourage more overseas Vietnamese investment. According to the circular, all economic units with more than half of their capital coming from overseas Vietnamese will enjoy a 20 percent reduction in income tax, except for those units already taxed at the concessional rate of only 10 percent. When their concessional tax has expired, they will pay income tax at the 20 percent rate. When transferring profits abroad, the economic units of overseas Vietnamese will enjoy the lowest tax rate for profit transfers, 5 percent, as stipulated by the Investment Law.
