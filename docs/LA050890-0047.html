May 8, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Arnold Raymon has been named vice president of finance for Toshiba America Medical Systems Inc., a Tustin-based manufacturer of diagnostic imaging systems. Raymon was previously director of financial planning for McDonnell Douglas Information Systems in Newport Beach and McDonnell Douglas Finance Corp. in Long Beach. Before joining McDonnell Douglas in 1982, he was a corporate auditor for 20th Century-Fox Corp., an independent financial consultant and an accountant with J.K. Lasser & Co.
