January 27, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

SEA VIEW LEAGUE ROUNDUP

In other league games:

Saddleback 78, Estancia 54 -- Andre McFarlane scored 20 points and Jason Moore 15 to lead Saddleback (14-9, 3-3) at Estancia.

The score was tied, 12-12, after the first quarter, but Saddleback outscored Estancia, 23-13, in the second quarter to take control. Matt Fuerbringer scored 16 for Estancia (12-8, 3-3).

Tustin 78, University 68 -- David Beilstein had 25 points and Gentry Moore has 12 to lead Tustin (17-4, 4-2). Donnell Dowdy had 22 points for University (5-14, 0-6).
