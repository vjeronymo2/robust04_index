BFN [Text] [Yemeni] Foreign Minister brother Muhammad Salim Basindwah yesterday met with the Danish foreign minister [Niels Helveg Petersen] in the Danish capital, Copenhagen. The brother minister briefed his Danish counterpart on the latest developments in the Yemeni arena and our country's abidance by the UN Security Council resolution and its repeated appeals, which were not heeded by the rebels. For his part, the Danish foreign minister expressed his country's support for unity and democracy in the Republic of Yemen. He said he hopes the fighting will stop and that the problems can be resolved through dialogue.
