BFN [Text] Dr. Nabil Sha'th, minister of planning and international cooperation in the Palestinian National Authority, will arrive in Cairo later tonight. Dr. Sha'th launched a new round of Palestinian-Israeli talks with a meeting with Israeli Environment Minister Yosi Sarid today. The Palestinian minister said that today's talks dealt with the steps taken to implement the self-rule agreement. These, he added, include the release of Palestinian detainees, the transfer of civilian authority to Palestinians, the withdrawal of Israeli forces, the holding of legislative elections before the end of September, and the date and venue of subsequent talks.
