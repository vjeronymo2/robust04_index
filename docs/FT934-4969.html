931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Enviromed beats forecast with Pounds 0.55m Enviromed, the biotechnology and healthcare group, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 546,000 for the year to September 30, beating its forecast of not less than Pounds 500,000 made at the time of its joining the market in June. Turnover was Pounds 5.3m. In the four months to September 30 1992 there were losses of Pounds 432,000 on turnover of Pounds 669,000. Earnings per share amounted to 7.13p (32.16p losses for four months).
