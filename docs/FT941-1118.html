940326 FT 26 MAR 94 / International Company News: UAL in buy-out deal with unions UAL, the parent company of United Airlines, reached a final agreement with its unions on a sale of 53 per cent of the company's stock to employees, writes Martin Dickson in New York. The Dollars 5bn buy-out, which makes UAL the largest employee-owned company in the US, was agreed in principle last December, but hit a problem when the company and unions failed to meet a deadline of March 15 for employee work rule concessions.
