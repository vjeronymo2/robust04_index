940815 FT 15 AUG 94 / Search for Guppy gems fraud money The government has appointed Mr John Alexander, chairman of the corporate recovery section at accountants Pannell Kerr Forster, to search for money under the control of Mr Darius Guppy. Mr Guppy was jailed for five in March last year after being convicted of a Pounds 1.8m insurance fraud involving a bogus gems theft at a New York hotel.
