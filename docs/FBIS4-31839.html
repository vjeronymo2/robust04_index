BFN [Report by Hamiz Deronjic and Nihad Salihovic] [Text] The Serbian aggressor stepped up provocative attacks on the Brcko front last night. At about 2000 last night, in addition to strong small arms fire, the enemy also targeted the Vranovaca-Bodariste area with 60 mm mortars. During this period, the chetniks were also observed to be amassing forces on that part of the front. In addition to firing Browings and machine guns from Lipovac, the enemy also fired over 20 grenades on the central part of the defense line. At about 0130 this morning, the chetniks again fired on the central part of the front with Browings and portable mortars. The activities of the enemy's reconnaissance units also intensified on the Brcko front overnight, especially around Djukici and (?Pekici). Members of the 108th Motorized and the 108th Infantry Brigades, as well as the support units from the 2nd Corps, were not involved in any combat last night.
