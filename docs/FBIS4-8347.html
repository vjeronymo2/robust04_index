BFN [Text] After repeated queries on our part, a few minutes ago Raycho Raykov, head of the government Press Center, issued the following announcement: Having recuperated from his cardiac bypass operation on 12 March, Professor Berov began taking an active part in the work of the Council of Ministers on 7, 8, and 10 April. According to information received at 0900 on 13 April from Dr. Todor Gerasimov, head of Lozenets Hospital, the condition of the prime minister's cardiovascular system and blood circulation continues to be stable. However, on 11 April a change in Prof. Berov's health condition was noted. The clinical conclusion was that there was the possibility of hepatitis. Specialized laboratory tests are under way and the results will be published in a day or two. According to Dr. Gerasimov, because of the development of a clinical picture of the hepatitis virus, Prof. Berov is in quarantine. He will have no personal contacts in the next few days.
