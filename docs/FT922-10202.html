920502 FT 02 MAY 92 / Guadalajara governor goes The governor for the Mexican state of Jalisco has taken a year's leave in an effort to stem criticism of his conduct before and after the explosion at Guadalajara, the state's capital, which killed nearly 200 people, writes Damian Fraser in Mexico City. Governor Guillermo Cosio Vidaurri has been accused of ignoring warnings of the danger, and of presiding over a corrupt government.
