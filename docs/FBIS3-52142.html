Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] According to reports government forces have foiled a big attack on Kabul by forces of General Dostam and Mahmud Barialay and as a result the situation in the city have now relatively eased. A spokesman of the Afghan Embassy in Islamabad in a press conference today claimed that the Islamic Army of Afghanistan have captured many areas including (Pol-e Mahmud Khan), Chaman Hozuri, Khawaja Rawash Airport, Bala Hesar, (Chapaibibi Mehroh), and Mazar-e-Sharif Airport. He said the enemy forces have suffered heavy losses. About 200 persons have been killed during fighting and at least 12 tanks captured. He said fierce fighting is still going on in Faryab Province, as well as, in the Province of (Shabergam).
