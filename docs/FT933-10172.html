930805 FT 05 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Lazard High Income offer raises Pounds 22.7m Lazard High Income Trust, an investment trust specialising in convertible stocks, has raised Pounds 22.7m via an offer for subscription. Together with a bank facility organised with Midland Bank, the trust will have available Pounds 32.4m, before expenses, for investment. Dealings in the shares are expected to start on August 10.
