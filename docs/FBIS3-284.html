Language: French Article Type:BFN [Article by Diaby Salif] [Excerpt] Where is the Democratic Party of Cote d'Ivoire- African Democratic Rally [PDCI-RDA] heading to? What will its activists do with the legacy of their founding father, President Houphouet-Boigny? These crucial questions were raised by PDCI-RDA Secretary General Laurent Dona-Fologo at a meeting with members of the party's political bureau at the party house in Cocody yesterday afternoon. This is the party's first meeting since the death of President Houphouet-Boigny. On the occasion, Mr. Dona-Fologo announced that the PDCI-RDA will hold its extraordinary congress in April as stipulated in Article 25 of the standing rules. The congress will focus on the vacant party chairmanship post and the need to fill that prestigious post. [passage omitted]
