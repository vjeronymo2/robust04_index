BFN ["News Release"] [Text] Chairman of the Estonian Liberal-Democratic Party (ELDP) Paul-Eerik Rummo and chairman of the right-wing Respublica youth movement Meelis Kitsing signed, on Monday [9 May], a cooperation agreement, which prescribes cooperation on all areas of politics and gives Respublica members a right to be put up for Riigikogu [parliament] elections in ELDP list. Rummo told a press conference that Estonia's future demands new educated and right-wing people both among liberals and the whole Estonian society and expressed hope that young politicians' representation in the next parliament would be bigger than now. Respublica has concluded a similar agreement with the ruling Fatherland Party.
