930511 FT 11 MAY 93 / Army radio reports on plans to reshuffle Israeli cabinet Israel's army radio said yesterday Mr Yitzhak Rabin, prime minister, was planning to reshuffle his cabinet in order to defuse a coalition crisis, Reuter adds from Jerusalem. The radio said Mr Rabin had agreed to the ultra-religious Shas party's demand to move outspokenly secular leftist Mrs Shulamit Aloni from the Education Ministry.
