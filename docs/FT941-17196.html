940106 FT 06 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: Brian Johnston, voice of cricket, dies The prime minister and leading cricketers paid tribute to commentator Brian Johnston, who died at the age of 81. Mr Johnston, known as 'Johnners' and the BBC's voice of cricket, recently suffered a heart attack. His conversational commentaries for BBC Radio's Test Match Special enabled him to establish a special rapport with his audience. John Major said his death meant summers would not be the same again.
