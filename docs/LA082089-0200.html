August 20, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

SHEFF HAS TWO HITS AS U.S. DEFEATS AUSTRALIA

Chris Sheff had two hits, scored three runs and stole two bases to help the United States defeat Australia, 12-1, Saturday in the World Junior Baseball tournament in Trois-Rivieres, Canada.

The United States advanced to the gold medal game today against Cuba. Sheff, a 1989 graduate of Laguna Hills High School, is hitting .523 (11 for 21) in the tournament.
