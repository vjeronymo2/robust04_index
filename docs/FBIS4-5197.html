BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 7 -- Documents on economic, cultural and scientific and technological cooperation between Vietnam and Laos was signed in Vientiane this morning. These documents include an agreement on economic, cultural, scientific and technological cooperation for 1994 between the Vietnamese and Lao Government, an agreement on general mechanism of economic, cultural, scientific and technological cooperation between the two governments and an agreement relating to sending and receiving Vietnamese experts to work in Laos. The signing took place at the end of the 16th session of the Vietnam-Laos intergovernmental commission on economic, cultural, scientific and technological cooperation opened in Vientiane on April 4. During its stay in Laos, the Vietnamese delegation to the meeting headed by Vice Prime Minister Phan Van Khai visited Luang Prabang Province. This afternoon the Vietnamese delegation left Vientiane for home.
