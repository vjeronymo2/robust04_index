February 14, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION BOYS' BASKETBALL PLAYOFF ROUNDUP

Fountain Valley 70, Perris 69 -- Fountain Valley rallied from a 13-point deficit in the third quarter at Perris. Derrick Corcoran scored 27 points to lead the Barons (12-13).

Corcoran gave Fountain Valley a 68-66 lead with a three-point field goal. Perris ended at 18-7.

Hawthorne 71, Santa Ana 55 -- Santa Ana lost despite a game-high 21 points from forward Chauncey Woolridge. Hawthorne (19-8) was led by guard Tremayne Gauthier's 17 points. The Cougars trailed, 14-13, with 2:01 left in the first quarter, but scored seven consecutive baskets to take a 13-point lead with 6:03 left in the second quarter.

The Saints (10-14) got no closer than 10 the rest of the way.

Guard Octavio Medina scored 12 points on four three-point baskets and center Oscar Wilson added nine points. DAVID RAATZ
