Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Twelve soldiers had their throats cut. An army captain, a [company] director, and a prison warden were shot dead in the last few days, according to Saturday's [5 March] editions of the Algerian press. These murders have not yet been announced by official sources. According to L'INDEPENDENT the 12 soldiers were killed in the vicinity of Algiers. EL WATAN reported that the army captain was murdered near Algiers airport, while the director and the prison warden were killed at Laghouat market [about 200 miles south of Algiers].
