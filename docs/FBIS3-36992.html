Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 13 Feb (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher held a telephone conversation on Saturday [12 February] at the U.S. initiative, according to a report by the Department of Information and Press of the Russian Foreign Ministry, which ITAR-TASS has just received. The document stresses that during the conversation "there was a frank exchange of opinions on the latest development of events on the Bosnian conflict and on seeking ways to unite efforts with a view to achieving a political settlement both around Sarajevo and in Bosnia as a whole. Particular attention was devoted to the session of the UN Security Council on the Bosnian question on 14 February."
