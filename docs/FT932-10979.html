930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Insider dealing proposals GOVERNMENT proposals on insider dealing will 'seriously undermine the flow of communication between companies and brokers and analysts and journalists', Mr Davies said yesterday, writes Ian Hamilton Fazey. The government is expected to publish the final wording of the insider dealing clauses in the Criminal Justice Bill this week, but Mr Davies urged more time to think again. 'Nothing could be worse for the improved understanding of companies' strategy by investors than a fear that practices now considered legitimate might be a matter for prosecution,' he told members of the British Venture Capital Association.
