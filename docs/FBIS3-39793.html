Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Recep Koc, ANAP [Motherland Party] mayor of Adalar, has been killed in an armed attack. According to information obtained by a TRT correspondent, Koc and a citizen named Osman Ozen had an argument at the Buyukada marketplace. Ozen pulled his gun and fired twice at Koc. Koc, who was wounded in his stomach and leg, was taken to the Kartal State Hospital, where he died during surgery. Eyewitnesses reported that Mehmet Ozkorkmaz and Zekariya Orali, who were accompanying Koc, were also wounded following the armed attack by Ozen. The assailant Ozen was later taken into custody. Officials from the Kartal State Hospital stated that Koc died as a result of heavy loss of blood. The wounded are in good condition. ANAP leader Mesut Yilmaz, who was in Istanbul at the time of the incident, expressed his sorrow over the loss of the mayor of Adalar.
