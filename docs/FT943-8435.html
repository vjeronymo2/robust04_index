940817 FT 17 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Cater Allen In a deal foreshadowed in June, Cater Allen Holdings, the discount house and banking group, has bought Compass Trust Group, a Jersey-based trust, for Pounds 8.11m cash. The trust had net tangible assets at date of purchase of about Pounds 275,000. Adjusted pre-tax profits for the year to April 30 were Pounds 1.2m.
