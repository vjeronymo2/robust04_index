Statement of Organization, Functions, and Delegations of Authority Section F.50 of the Statement of Organization, Functions, and Delegations of Authority for the Department of Health and Human Services, Health Care Financing Administration (HCFA) (49 FR 35247, dated September 6, 1984) is hereby amended to add a new paragraph, F.50.2.g., to indicate that the authorities under Section 1928 of Title XIX of the Social Security Act as added by Section 13631 of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1993 (P.L. 103&hyph;66), as amended hereafter, excluding subsections 1928(a)(2)(B) and 1928(c)(2)(c)(ii) have been delegated to the Assistant Secretary for Health. The new paragraph reads as follows: g. The Assistant Secretary for Health shall exercise all the authorities under Section 1928 of Title XIX of the Social Security Act as added by Section 13631 of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1993 (P.L. 100&hyph;66), as amended hereafter, excluding subsections 1928(a)(2)(B) and 1928(c)(2)(c)(ii).
