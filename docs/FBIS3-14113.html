Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Iraq has informed the Arab League that U.S., British, and French aircraft continue to violate its airspace, noting that this seriously jeopardizes its security and sovereignty. In a letter addressed to the Arab League secretary general yesterday, the Iraqi permanent representative to the Arab League said that these aircraft continued on 8-16 March to violate Iraqi airspace, flying from bases in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait. They carried out 321 hostile air sorties during the aforementioned period. A list attached to the letter detailed these airspace violations.
