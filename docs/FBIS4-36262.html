BFN [Text] The water commissioner has warned that the border adjustments demanded by Jordan in the 'Arava could lead to the loss of water sources vital to Israel. He told our correspondent Shula Schmerling that the reservoir of ground water in the 'Arava meets the consumption needs of the settlements in the area, which amount to approximately 40 million cubic meters per year. The water commissioner clarified that at the Washington talks, Israel will demand that all the outstanding water problems with Jordan, including the question of the al-Yarmuk River, the Jordan River, and the 'Arava, be discussed as one package.
