Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Jumaboi, field commander of the Tajik Islamic opposition, plans an attack on a Russian border post of the Khorog unit, a Tajik Foreign Ministry spokesman told Interfax, quoting General Anatoliy Chechulin, commander of Russian border troops stationed in Tajikistan, as his source. The goal of the attack is to provoke a confrontation between Russian border guards and the Badakhshan defense force. According to Chechulin, most defense commanders had said that they would support the Russian border guards if they are attacked. The border guards, local authorities, and Badakhshan defense commanders are taking steps to counter such activities aimed at destabilizing the region, the Foreign Ministry spokesman said.
