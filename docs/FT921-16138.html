920106 FT 06 JAN 92 / Construction Contracts: Rio de Janeiro hotel project Another continent - South America - has been added to BOVIS INTERNATIONAL'S site list with the company's new project management contract to renovate the listed Copacabana Palace Hotel (pictured) in Rio de Janeiro for Companhia Hoteis Palace, a subsidiary of Sea Containers. A team from Bovis International has already spent two months in Rio carrying out a technical audit on the existing buildings and preparing various different options for the planned USDollars 25m (Pounds 13.6m) renovation. The renovation programme will increase the number of rooms in the main building by 15 and the Towers by 12, together with the complete refurbishment of all front and back-of-house facilities, and the addition of a health club and roof-top tennis courts.
