930306 FT 06 MAR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Cochin ginger prices were easier at USDollars 1,025 a tonne cif owing to good crop forecasts and the change in India's currency policy, reports Man-producten. Mace and nutmegs: Grenada no change; Indonesia siauw/ambon bwp Dollars 800 spot, shipment Dollars 750; shrivelled nutmegs spot Dollars 1,295, shipment Dollars 1,250; mace broken 2 spot Dollars 1,400, shipment Dollars 1,375. Pimento: Mexico slightly easier at Dollars 1,725 spot, shipment Dollars 1,700 cif; Jamaica unchanged; Guatemala spot Dollars 1,900. Cassia: stabilising; Seychelles, Madagascan, Chinese and Vietnamese deliveries relieving the spot markets.
