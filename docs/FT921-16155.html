920106 FT 06 JAN 92 / International Company News: Shanghai advance TURNOVER on the Shanghai securities market, the main experiment by the People's Republic of China with capitalist-style reforms, hit Yn11bn (Dollars 2.02bn) in 1991 - about three times the total for the previous five years combined, Reuter reports from Beijing. The official Shanghai Securities Exchange accounted for about Yn8bn of the turnover, while the rest was made up by smaller bond trading agencies.
