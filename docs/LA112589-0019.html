November 25, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

USC TOUCHDOWN CONJURES UP BITTER MEMORIES

Why can't USC win the big games?

1. No discipline. When Notre Dame has a dumb, stupid penalty, Lou Holtz pulls the player and chews his butt on the sidelines. Why doesn't Larry (Smith) do the same?

2. No replacement for the quarterback. When will USC have a Nelson-Beathard combo again? In baseball, pitchers are recognized as having bad days requiring relief. Why not with USC football?

V. B. KNIGHT, South Laguna
