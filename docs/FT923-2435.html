920917 FT 17 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Costs of failed buy depress Quarto The Pounds 160,000 costs of an aborted acquisition resulted in reduced profits of Pounds 1.06m at Quarto Group in the six months to June 30. This compared to Pounds 1.21m in the previous first half. Turnover rose 8 per cent to Pounds 15.8m (Pounds 14.6m). The US-registered publishing, marketing support and production services group is maintaining the interim dividend at 1.6125p. Earnings per share came through at 3.3p (4.5p) basic or 4.1p (5p) diluted.
