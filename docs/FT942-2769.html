940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Chester Water Chester Water, the statutory water company, reported a 6 per cent decline - from Pounds 2.17m to Pounds 2.03m - in pre-tax profits in the 12 months to March 31. The outcome, which came on turnover of Pounds 6.02m (Pounds 5.64m), was struck after restructuring costs of Pounds 159,008 and Pounds 40,868 in connection with the periodic review instigated by Ofwat. Earnings per share dipped to 600.5p (617.7p); a final dividend of 170p brings the total for the year to 255p (225p).
