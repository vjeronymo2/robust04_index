BFN [Text] We have just received the following report from the news desk: A Lebanese Army force of four troop carriers, four military vehicles, several Jeep-type vehicles, and about 75 soldiers raided a camp belonging to the Fatah Revolutionary Council [FRC] located 1 km north of 'Ayta al-Fakhkhar, near Rashayya al-Wadi. The Army confiscated the contents of the 20-room camp: four truckloads of cabinets, beds, military hardware, an automobile, and a water tanker. The Army took a number of FRC personnel into custody and closed down the camp.
