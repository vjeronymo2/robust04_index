Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn -- Free Democratic Party of Germany [FDP] Secretary General Werner Hoyer has rejected Christian Social Union [CSU] Chairman Waigel's claim to his party's participation in the shaping of foreign policies by calling it incredible. The CSU is a party that is, for example, deeply at variance over European policies and takes complete contrary positions to the outside. Klaus Kinkel is a good foreign political representative, the FDP politician stressed.
