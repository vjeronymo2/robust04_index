930114 FT 14 JAN 93 / Japan and Russian to hold trade and economic talks Japan and Russia will hold trade and economic talks in Tokyo next week, the foreign ministry said yesterday, Reuter reports from Tokyo. It will be the first such meeting since Japan and the former Soviet Union held similar talks in Moscow in October 1990. Kyodo news agency quoted officials as saying that topics to be discussedinclude Russia's trade debt to Japan and loans to Russia through the Export-Import Bank of Japan.
