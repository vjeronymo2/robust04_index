930528 FT 28 MAY 93 / Observer: Showing off It wasn't only Sir Leon Brittan's passionate espousal of European integration which wowed his German audience at the Konrad Adenauer institute in Bonn yesterday. It was his ability to deliver an almost hour-long speech in impeccable German. His performance clearly delighted the many campaigners in Bonn trying to get German promoted to a full working language in Brussels. If it's true that Sir Leon has ambitions to succeed Jacques Delors at the top of the Commission, then his tour-de-force in front of Chancellor Helmut Kohl can't have done him any harm.
