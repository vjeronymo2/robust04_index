March 19, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

IN COURT: A SUMMARY OF SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA-RELATED BUSINESS LITIGATION DEVELOPMENTS DURING THE PAST WEEK.

Vuarnet Trademark Suit Filed: Allegra Inc. filed suit in U.S. District Court in Los Angeles on Wednesday against two companies that it claims are infringing on its Vuarnet logo. The suit asks the court to stop the defendants, Crazy Shirts Inc. of Aiea, Hawaii, and Mainland Co. of Tustin, from using the Vuarnet and Vuarnet France marks. (Filed March 14, 1990, Case No. CV90-1248 WMB)
