BFN [Text] According to our special correspondent in Chisinau, Belarusian Supreme Soviet Chairman Mechyslaw Hryb and Prime Minister Vyacheslaw Kebich have today begun a visit to Moldova. The Belarusian parliament speaker and the Cabinet of Ministers chairman went to Moldova at the invitation of that country's leaders. Strengthening and developing economic and cultural ties between the two republics is the main goal of the visit to Chisinau by Mechyslaw Hryb and Vyacheslaw Kebich. In the morning, the delegation met with Moldovan President Mircea Snegur, signed documents on bilateral cooperation, and held a news conference with journalists. At the news conference, the Belarusian and Moldovan leaders emphasized that such cooperation is in the interests of both states and people. The visit by Mechyslaw Hryb and Vyacheslaw Kebich will last two days.
