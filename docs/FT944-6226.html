941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Budget 94 (Their Budget): Electronics expert Robin Saxby, 47. Chief executive of Advanced Risc Machines of Cambridge. Married with two children. Politics: Voted Tory at last election Wanted: Imaginative measures to promote wealth creation, lower corporation tax. Said yesterday: 'This is a minor version of a step in the right direction. My mother is a pensioner and I would like to know how the measures to cushion the increase in VAT on fuel will affect her. At a business level, it is unexciting.'
