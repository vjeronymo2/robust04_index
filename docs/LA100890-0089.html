October 8, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

LAKER ROOKIE SMITH GOES BACK TO L.A.

Laker rookie Tony Smith flew home Sunday, where he will undergo tests for an irregular heartbeat.

The Lakers continued practicing twice a day.

Coach Mike Dunleavy was asked about the new rule against hard fouling. "The (Charles) Barkley rule?" he said, laughing. "We're not going to coach it, so it won't be a problem. Detroit might have a problem, though." MARK HEISLER
