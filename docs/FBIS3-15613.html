Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Report on the "Statement by the Crimean Presidential Press Service"] [Text] The Crimean Presidential Press Service has published a statement in connection with the Ukraine Supreme Council decree of 24 February 1994 "On the Status of the Crimean Autonomous Republic in Accordance With Ukraine's Current Constitution and Legislation." The statement reads that from the point of view of law, this decree "obviously does not stand up to criticism." The statement emphasizes that the president [Yuriy Meshkov] sees the practice of relations between sovereignties and many other issues worked out in the Russian Federation as the best substantiated and legal. The agreement between Russia and Tatarstan is quoted as an example.
