BFN [Text] Sanaa, June 6 (AFP) -- The Yemeni Government has agreed to observe a ceasefire from midnight (2100 GMT) on Monday [6 June], Foreign Minister Muhammad Basindwah announced. He told reporters the government had informed UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali and Arab League Chief 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid that "a ceasefire will start from midnight tonight."
