940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / International Company News: Independent Newspapers expands Mr Tony O'Reilly's Independent Newspapers of Ireland has expanded its international newspaper investments by taking a small stake in Jornalgeste. The publicly quoted Portuguese group accounts for half of Portugal's newspaper sales through its two national titles Jornal de Noticias and Diario de Noticias. The Irish group is taking a 6.6 per cent stake, which is being paid for with new shares in Independent Newspapers worth IRPounds 5.9m. Mr Liam Healey, chief executive of Independent Newspapers, said the company had made the investment because it believed that the Portuguese economy was strengthening.
