921207 FT 07 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Wiltshire Brewery reduces losses Wiltshire Brewery's capital restructuring has been approved at an extraordinary meeting, placing UB Group, India's largest brewer, in management control. Mr Vijay Mallya, chairman of UB, becomes chairman of Wiltshire and Mr O'Neal Nalavadi, chief operating executive. His group and his family will take about 16 per cent of the enlarged group. Wiltshire reported a reduced pre-tax loss for the six months to May 31 down from Pounds 324,851 to Pounds 283,607 on sales doubled to Pounds 1.33m. Wiltshire's tenanted estate grew from 26 to 40 during the period and has expanded further to 78 as a result of the restructuring.
