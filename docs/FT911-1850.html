910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Sharp fall at Jerome after textiles setback Taxable profits at S Jerome, the yarn and electronics group, slumped from Pounds 1.58m to Pounds 105,000 in 1990. The final dividend is passed leaving a pay-out of 2.6p (8.4p) for the year. The electronics division increased operating profits by 38 per cent to Pounds 647,000 (Pounds 469,000) on turnover of Pounds 12.1m (Pounds 7.84m). In contrast, the textile side saw operating profits tumble from Pounds 1.8m to Pounds 779,000 on turnover marginally down at Pounds 25.72m (Pounds 26.29m). Earnings were 1.1p (14.1p).
