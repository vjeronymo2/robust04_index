940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Bletchley Motor advances to Pounds 1.13m Bletchley Motor Group continued to improve with a 44 per cent increase in pre-tax profit for the six months ended June 30, up from Pounds 785,000 to Pounds 1.13m. Turnover was ahead at Pounds 52.7m (Pounds 39.6m). Contract hire and dealership showed good growth, although the self drive rental side was disappointing, the company said. Earnings per share came out at 13p (10.9p) and the dividend is raised from 4.75p to 5.375p.
