Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] According to a cabinet decision today, the Islamic Republic of Iran will be establishing diplomatic relations with the Government of South Africa. According to our correspondent, the decision was made after the resolution of the racial discrimination problem in South Africa, which was realized as a result of the indefatigable struggles by that country's blacks as well as a decision by the African Unity Conference and requests by South African Muslims and political groups, especially Nelson Mandela, and the Front Line states. The cabinet has instructed the Foreign Ministry to pave the way for lifting the trade embargo against that country and regulating relations between the two countries. [passage omitted]
