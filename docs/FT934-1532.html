931218 FT 18 DEC 93 / Bankers to visit south-east Asia Mr Kenneth Clarke, the chancellor, is to lead a party of top UK bankers to south-east Asia later this month to try to help the financial services industry expand its presence in the region. The tour of about two weeks, with about eight bankers, will start the week after Christmas and take in Hong Kong, the Philippines and Indonesia. A Treasury official said the countries had been chosen because of the 'good prospects' for doing business there.
