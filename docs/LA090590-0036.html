September 5, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

DIGEST /LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: HOLLYWOOD FREEWAY LANES TO BE CLOSED

The California Department of Transportation began a three-month road rebuilding project Tuesday night that will require shutting down various lanes of the Hollywood Freeway between Chandler and Victory boulevards in North Hollywood.

Caltrans plans the work between 8 p.m. and 5 a.m. weekdays. After mid-September, the project will also involve work on the weekends.

Also Tuesday night, Caltrans began reconstructing the median guardrail on a stretch of the Ventura Freeway between the Hollywood Freeway and Pass Avenue in Burbank.
