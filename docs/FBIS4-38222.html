BFN [Text] Tehran, June 28, IRNA -- No one has gone on trial for expressing his thoughts and ideas in the Islamic Republic of Iran, Iran's Attorney General Ayatollah Seyyed Abolfazl Musavi-Tabrizi reiterated here Tuesday [28 June]. Calling as fabricated the claims on human rights violation by certain international circles Musavi-Tabrizi declared that the most progressive types of human rights were present in the Islamic Republic of Iran. Addressing the concluding session of the nationwide seminar on judiciary week, Musavi-Tabrizi said that the Islamic system will not put aside due punishment as decreed by Islam for criminals.
