February 23, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

UC IRVINE PLAYS HOST TO CS LONG BEACH

UC Irvine has won five of its last six basketball games and will finish the regular season with a four-game home stand that begins tonight at 7:30 when the Anteaters play host to Cal State Long Beach.

A sweep would guarantee Irvine at least a tie for second in the Big West Conference, which would be a remarkable feat considering Irvine's 3-5 start in conference play. The Anteaters are 11-12 overall and 8-6 in the Big West.

Long Beach (12-12, 9-6) had won four in a row before getting routed at Las Vegas Monday night by 18th-ranked UNLV, 104-74. In the first meeting this season, Long Beach won, 75-70. JOHN WEYLER
