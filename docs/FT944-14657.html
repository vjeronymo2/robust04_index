941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / International Company News: Solid advances at GM divisions Revenues at EDS, the computer services subsidiary of General Motors, climbed 23 per cent in its third quarter from a year ago, to Dollars 2.56bn, writes Richard Waters. Mr Les Alberthal, chairman and chief executive, attributed the advance in sales to strong growth in Europe and Asia. The growth supported a 13 per cent increase in after-tax profits to Dollars 216.4m. GM Acceptance Corporation, the financial services arm, reported after-tax profits of Dollars 245m, up from Dollars 205m.
