940511 FT 11 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Slight fall at Drayton Blue Chip Drayton Blue Chip Trust reported a slight fall in net asset value per share to 80.7p at March 31 1994, against 81.8p a year earlier. Calculated value per preferred growth share, however, rose from 124.8p to 139.4p. Net revenue slipped from Pounds 1.14m to Pounds 1.09m in the year and earnings per share came to 8.59p (9p). The final dividend is unchanged at 5.495p for a same-again total of 8.4p.
