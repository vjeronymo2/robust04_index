BFN [Text] Guangdong, April 20 (CNS) -- Starting yesterday, Guangdong Province has launched a unified campaign to fight against the illegal copying of video and audio products such as compact and laser discs. The large-scale campaign has 891 people taking part in it in Guangzhou alone. Checks have already been made on seven entities and street peddlers found to be engaging in such illegal activities in the city and confiscated 50,338 compact discs, 1,111 audio tapes, 2,765 video tapes and 90 laser discs, all found to have been illegally copied.
