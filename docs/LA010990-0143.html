January 9, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Electrical grounding -- A story in Sunday's Real Estate section on replacing two-prong electrical outlets with three-prong receptacles is applicable only for a grounded electrical system. Many older homes do not have a grounded system and installing three-prong receptacles would create a potentially hazardous situation. If you are considering such a conversion and don't know if you have a grounded system, contact a licensed electrician.
