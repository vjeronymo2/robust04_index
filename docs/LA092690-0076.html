September 26, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

John (Hot Rod) Williams said he turned down a trade from the Cleveland Cavaliers to the Seattle SuperSonics because he does not want to disrupt his family.

"I said I wasn't about to move my kids around like checkers on a board, and I'm not going to do it," Williams told the Akron Beacon Journal. If Williams had approved the trade, he could have received a penalty payment of up to 15% of the remainder of his contract -- more than $3 million.
