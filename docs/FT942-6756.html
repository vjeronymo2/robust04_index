940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Villiers Villiers Group carried a provision of Pounds 1.52m in its first half accounts related to its withdrawal from the oil industry. The USM-traded group reported a pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.86m (Pounds 1.15m) for the six months to January 31. Losses per share were 1.83p (2.25p).
