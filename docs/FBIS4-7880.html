BFN [Text] The official part of the visit by Willy Claes, Belgium's deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs, who arrived in Bulgaria on 3 April, began today. After a meeting and talks at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs with Foreign Minister Stanislav Daskalov, a joint political declaration was signed. The two sides stressed the decisive role that the CSCE process could play in strengthening European security stability and emphasized the importance of the Helsinki conference final document, the Paris Charter on New Europe, the Helsinki document of 1992, and other official documents, the declaration reads.
