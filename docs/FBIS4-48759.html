BFN [Text] Cape Town May 26 SAPA -- President Nelson Mandela held discussions over breakfast with acting Democratic Party [DP] leader Mr Tony Leon at Mr Mandela's official residence Westbrooke on Thursday. Mr Mandela's spokesman, Mr Joel Netshitenzhe, described the meeting a "courtesy call". Mr Leon said they discussed the DP's role as an opposition party and its future relationship with the government. He said the talks were "friendly". Mr Netshitenzhe said Mr Mandela would also be meeting with Pan Africanist Congress leader, Mr Clarence Makwetu, some time on Thursday.
