Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Islamic Group in Egypt has claimed responsibility for detonating a bomb in front of a Cairo bank yesterday, which wounded one person and caused severe property damage to the building. The Islamic Group has launched more than nine bomb attacks against Egyptian and foreign banks. In a statement issued today, it warned people who frequent banks of similar attacks. The statement added that yesterday's explosion in front of a building housing the Misr-America International Bank had come in revenge for the execution of two [Army] officers on Thursday on charges of attempting to assassinate Egyptian President Husni Mubarak during a visit to the Sidi Barrani base at the Egyptian-Libyan border in November.
