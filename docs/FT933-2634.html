930917 FT 17 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Folkes falls to Pounds 200,000 THE effects of the recession continued in the first half at Folkes Group, with ongoing pressures on margins and volumes pushing pre-tax profits from Pounds 650,000 to Pounds 200,000. Mr Constantine J Folkes, the chairman and chief executive, pointed out that profits in the property division were marginally higher, but both the engineering and building products sectors showed a downturn compared with last time. The interim dividend, however, is maintained at 0.575p. Earnings per share declined from 1.25p to 0.28p. Turnover for the six months to June 30 was virtually unchanged at Pounds 20.1m (Pounds 20.4m) giving a trading profit of Pounds 312,000 against Pounds 723,000. Interest took Pounds 112,000 (Pounds 73,000).
