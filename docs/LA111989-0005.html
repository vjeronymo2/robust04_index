November 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ERNEST FLEISCHMANN

History tell us that the pen is mightier than the sword. Martin Bernheimer has a pen that he brandishes like a rapier and sometimes like a meat cleaver. The headline writers followed Bernheimer's lead and selected an equally derogatory title for the article, "The Tyrant of the Philharmonic."

Would you be kind enough to grant Fleischmann eight pages to respond to Bernheimer? If not, then it is time for the Los Angeles Times to hire a critics' critic to review this famous self-proclaimed operatic authority.

JOHN CLEARY, Panorama City
