940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Control Techs order book ahead Mr Trevor Wheatley, chairman of Control Techniques, told the annual meeting that the company had witnessed an 'encouraging' 12 per cent increase in order bookings during the first quarter. He said several large orders had been received from the US for delivery in the second half 'confirming emergence from recession'. Growth in the Asia Pacific division had continued while some parts of Europe were beginning to improve. The company makes electronic drives and controls. Its shares closed 18p up at 461p.
