Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Jeddah, 29 Jan -- This evening at the palace of conferences in Jeddah, Dr. Hamid al-Ghabid, secretary general of the Islamic Conference Organization [ICO], received a delegation of Muslims from the Russian Federation, currently visiting the Kingdom. During the meeting he discussed with the delegation members the possibility of the Islamic republics joining the ICO and also the Russian delegation's invitation to the Islamic States to invest in the Islamic republics. The meeting was attended by Ambassador Ibrahim al-Bakr, the ICO assistant secretary general for political affairs.
