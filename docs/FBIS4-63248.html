BFN [Text] With the aim of increasing effectiveness in performing the missions that are entrusted to the Security Service of Ukraine, improving coordination in carrying out the operative and investigative operations to ensure security, and in accordance with Paragraphs 3 (?and) 7.1 of Article 114.5 in our state's constitution, Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk by his directive has reorganized the Security Service of the Republic of Crimea into the Security Service of Ukraine Main Directorate in Crimea, subordinate directly to the Security Service of Ukraine chairman, with the administrative and logistical facilities, weapons, transportation means and property, that are currently being used by the Security Service of the Republic of Crimea, transferred to his use. The directive introduces the post of chief of the Security Service of Ukraine Main Directorate in Crimea and appoints Maj-Gen Ivan Kolomyytsev to that post. The chairman of the Security Service of Ukraine is to approve within the term of 10 days the complement of the Security Service of Ukraine Main Directorate in Crimea. The directive is effective as of the day of issue.
