Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 15 KYODO -- Japan's Foreign Ministry has not yet confirmed a reported visit to Japan by U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher for next month, a ministry spokesman said Tuesday [15 February]. Foreign Ministry Spokesman Terusuke Terada said he is aware of some reports saying Christopher is considering an Asian trip in March, but that the ministry has not yet been informed about the tour by the U.S. Government. Christopher is reportedly planning to visit several Asian countries, including Indonesia, China, Japan and Vietnam, after a scheduled trip to Australia on March 8 to 9.
