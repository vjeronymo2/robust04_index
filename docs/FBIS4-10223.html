BFN [Text] In view of the deaths of Cyprian Ntaryamira, president of the Republic of Burundi; and Juvenal Habyarimana, president of the Republic of Rwanda, the Council of State has decreed an official period of mourning beginning at 0600 on 11 April until 1800 on 13 April. During this period, the Cuban flag will be flown at half mast in public buildings and military facilities.
