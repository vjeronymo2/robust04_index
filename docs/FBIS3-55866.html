Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] President Geydar Aliyev has signed a decree on the measures to be taken to promote foreign trade in the republic. The decree stresses that the republic's state establishments and organizations can sell commodities or provide services to foreign countries within the framework of their foreign trade relations only on the basis of letters of credit or advance payments until the legal formalities are concluded. In addition, advance payment of dues for commodities and services from foreign countries is banned. Imports of foreign products and services by foreign countries will be paid through letters of credit. The decree says that registering barter trade transactions has been deemed appropriate. Meanwhile, it approves the new list of the strategically important items for the republic, reducing their number of 17. The full text of the decree and the list of the strategically important items, which has been drawn up with the president's consent, will be published in the press.
