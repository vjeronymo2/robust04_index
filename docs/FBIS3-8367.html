Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] A Spanish plane of the Aviocar type, which was on a mission controlling the airspace in Bosnia-Herzegovina, has been hit by antiaircraft fire. Three of its nine passengers, one British, one French, and one American, were slightly wounded by shrapnel. The planes' crew of three pilots and a Spanish mechanic were not hurt, although it had to make an emergency landing at the Rijeka airport in Croatia. The plane was transporting personnel between Split and Zagreb, in Croatian territory. It was flying over Krajina where there is often fighting between Serbs and Croats. So far, the origin of the antiaircraft fire has not been determined.
