BFN [Report by Ahmad Birru from Sanaa] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Informed sources in Sanaa have announced that Saudi troops are being massed near the common border [with Yemen]. The sources noted that more than 150 Saudi tanks have been moved toward the border, near the Yemeni district of Maydi, adding that these tanks have taken up combat positions. The sources said Sanaa has officially asked the United States, Britain, and France, which have satellites, to verify these moves. No official sources have confirmed or denied this information which has not reported by independent sources. [passage omitted]
