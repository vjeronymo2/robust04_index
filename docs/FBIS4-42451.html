BFN [Text] The issue of Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM], and specifically the appeal against Greece at the European Court on 14 June for the embargo against Skopje, is not on the agenda of the EU [European Union] Summit in Corfu. This was stated today by Government Spokesman Evangelos Venizelos, who clarified that from the moment the EU Commission chose to deal with the issue as a legal one, it has lost its political character. As a result, he said, it does not relate to the EU Summit. Matthew Nimetz, U.S. special envoy on the Skopje issue, will come to Athens next week for contacts with the foreign ministry's political leadership.
