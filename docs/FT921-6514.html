920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / International Company News: Departures Peter McNally, group finance director of LWT, will retire in March 1993 and not 1992 as reported yesterday. ***** John St Lawrence is retiring from RECKITT & COLMAN but remains a non-executive until the end of May. ***** John Cearns, a non-executive director of SOUTHEND PROPERTY HOLDINGS, died on February 8. ***** Robert Paine has resigned from TILBURY DOUGLAS. ***** Ill health has caused Nicholas Roditi to resign from ST JAMES'S PLACE CAPITAL. ***** David Allison, company secretary since 1984 of CHRISTIES INTERNATIONAL, died on February 25.
