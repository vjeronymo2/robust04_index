Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 17/3/1994 -- Invited by the Moldovan Ministry of Defence, an official delegation of Denmark arrived yesterday in Chisinau. It is led by Army General Iorgen Lyng, supreme commander of the Danish Armed Forces, the Ministry of Defence press secretariat reports. The schedule includes meetings with Moldovan Minister of Defence Pavel Creanga, General Headquarters Chief Pavel Chirau, Premier Andrei Sangheli, acting Minister of Foreign Affairs Ion Botnaru. A series of common interest problems will be discussed. According to the quoted source, Moldovan officials will ask for the support of Danish representatives within NATO, especially in the problem of the 14th Army withdrawal. Tomorrow, the military delegation is to return to Denmark, the press secretariat noted.
