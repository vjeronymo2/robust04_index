BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Jun (MENA) -- Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has emphasized that nothing is impeding a meeting with his Iranian counterpart, 'Ali Akbar Velayati, who is taking part in the Nonaligned Movement Conference being held in Cairo. In a statement to MENA, Velayati said that he does not rule out a meeting with 'Amr Musa. Velayati refused to either deny or confirm the reports that a meeting will be held with 'Amr Musa today. The Iranian foreign minister declined to comment whether the talks will deal with relations between Egypt and Iran. Diplomatic Egyptian sources said that Egypt is ready to hold any meeting with the heads of delegations participating in the conference.
