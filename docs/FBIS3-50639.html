Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] San Cristobal de las Casas, Mexico, 4 Jan (AFP) -- Three airplanes of the Mexican Air Force unexpectedly bombed a hill in the outskirts of San Cristobal de las Casas during a clash with members of the Zapatista National Liberation Army (EZLN) in a military spectacle that was watched by residents of San Juan de los Lagos neighborhood. The residents climbed on rooftops and other high places to follow the actions of the three airplanes and a helicopter in this location where the presence of guerrillas had been unknown. The rebels responded to the bombing with submachine-gun fire. The number of casualties is unknown. The action occurred about 10 km from the center of San Cristobal, a town which the EZLN members maintained under their control on 1 January. The battle site is alongside the highway linking San Cristobal with Ocosingo, toward which the guerrilla forces are reportedly pulling back after issuing a "declaration of war" against the Mexican Government and Army on 1 January.
