BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 23 (KCNA) -- The military attaches corps here Friday visited the Kang Kon Military School on the occasion of the 62nd anniversary of the founding of the Korean People's Army. Military attaches of foreign embassies went round the educational facilities of the school, being briefed on the proud course traversed by it under the wise guidance of the great leader President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army.
