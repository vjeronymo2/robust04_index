BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, chairman of the Presidential Council, today issued Republican Decree No. 5 for 1994 concerning a general amnesty for the misled ones [al-mugharrari bihim]. The first article of the decree provides for a general amnesty for all those who have been misled, and who have participated, directly or indirectly, in exploding the situation militarily, and in committing aggression against the Democratic Republic of Yemen. It also amnesties the sons of the areas against which aggression has been committed, who were forced by circumstances or concern about their own safety and lives to work against the sons of their own areas, their tribes, and their state. The second article of the decree provides for the regularization of the status of those mentioned in the above-mentioned first article, if they join in the defense of the Democratic Republic of Yemen, in emulation of their colleagues. The third article of the decree states that the decree takes effect from the date of its issuance, and that it shall be published in the official gazette.
