BFN [Unattributed report: "Albanian Parties Meeting on Census"] [Text] The leadership of PDP [the Party of Democratic Prosperity], i.e. presidents of the Tetovo party branch along with Albanian ministers and parliament deputies held a meeting behind closed doors to discuss the party's view on the census. They also informed reporters of the essence of recent talks with representatives of the NDP [the Peoples' Democratic Party], Albanian Democratic Alliance--Liberal Party, and SDA [Party of Democratic Action]-Islamic Road. Meantime, the Parliament Press Service informed yesterday of a proposal by 6 PDP deputies for urgent changes in the census. They suggested a six-months delay of the census, i.e. proposed for the census to being December 1, 1994 and to end January 7, 1995.
