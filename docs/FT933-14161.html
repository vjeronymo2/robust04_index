930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / International Company News in Brief: Viag Viag, the diversified utility group, will ask shareholders to authorise capital increases of up to DM150m (Dollars 87m), Reuter reports from Bonn. Mr Alfred Pfeiffer, the management board chairman, said that it was necessary to finance expansion measures to the fullest. He said the authorisation would allow Viag to issue warrants if necessary. Viag would sell operations such as its North American standard metallurgy business, the technical ceramics operations of Didierwerke and the secondary activities of VAW Aluminium Werke.
