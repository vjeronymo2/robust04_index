930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Aitken Hume turns in Pounds 2.69m Aitken Hume International, the financial services and banking group, made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 2.69m for the year to March 31, compared with Pounds 2.91m last time which included Pounds 2.02m from the Bachmann Group to its disposal on March 6 1992. Earnings per share were 1.04p, compared with 2.12p before extraordinary items and 19.84p losses after. The dividend is 1p (0.5p).
