Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] On the afternoon of 29 January, Japanese Trade Union Confederation [Rengo] chairman Akira Yamagishi held a news conference at Rengo headquarters. Following enactment of the political reform bills, Yamagishi noted the resignation of chairman Tomiichi Murayama and other executives of the Social Democratic Party of Japan [SDPJ] is unnecessary. He said: "The responsibility (for the upper house's rejection of the bills) should be assumed by SDPJ rebels, but not the party leadership."
