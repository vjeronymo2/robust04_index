930327 FT 27 MAR 93 / Finance and the Family: BT shares on shopping list - At a Glance The government will use its third sale of shares in British Telecom, known as BT III, to promote its share shop idea. Around 100 intermediaries met with the Treasury this week to discuss the concept; they will be entitled to display a new share shop logo and investors who apply through share shops will receive preference in allocation. The earliest date for BT III would be mid-July.
