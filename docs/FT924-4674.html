921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Sharp recovery at European Colour A sharp recovery in pre-tax profits is reported by European Colour, the manufacturer of chemical colours more than 95 per cent of which are industrial pigments used predominately in printing inks. Pre-tax profits for the six months to September 30 are up from Pounds 26,000 to Pounds 265,000 from sales which were marginally higher at Pounds 6.98m (6.92m). The interim dividend is increased from 0.25p to 0.275 payable from earnings per share of 0.59p (0.06p).
