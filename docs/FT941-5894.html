940304 FT 04 MAR 94 / Sales of books rise by up to 10% The number of books sold has increased by between 5 per cent and 10 per cent over the past 18 months, a survey by Book Marketing, the book industry research group, has found. About 300m books are sold each year with more than a third of all buyers purchasing at least 16 books a year. Over the past five years puzzle and quiz books have overtaken romantic fiction as the largest category of books sold.
