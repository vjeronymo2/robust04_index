BFN [Text] INTERFAX has reported that President Boris Yeltsin had a meeting with Deputy Prime Minister Sergey Shakhray in the Kremlin today. The head of state once again objected to Shakhray's resignation from the post of deputy chairman of the government. Sources close to Sergey Shakhray's circle have said that this problem no longer exists. During their conversation the president and the deputy prime minister also discussed issues relating to regional policy, questions of the implementation of the bilateral treaty with Tatarstan, and relations with Chechnya.
