940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Flogas rises to IPounds 3.2m Flogas, the quoted Irish supplier of liquefied petroleum gas, has raised interim pre-tax profits by 6 per cent, from IPounds 3.2m to IPounds 3.38m (Pounds 3.25m). Turnover in the six months to December 31 rose by 21 per cent to IPounds 27.4m. The interim dividend is raised to 3.24p (2.94p) on earnings per share of 11.58p (10.93p). Borrowings at the end of the period had been cut to IPounds 2.09m (IPounds 4.83m).
