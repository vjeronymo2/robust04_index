Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Juan Carlos Salina] [Excerpt] Tavapy 1, Alto Parana -- The police reacted violently against the some 400 peasants from Tavapy 1 and 2 who partially occupied Route No. 6, nearby km 22, for a short while in protest over the serious economic hardships they are experiencing and to demand at least 1,000 Guaranies per kilogram of cotton. Almost 100 people were injured as a result of the police repression. One person was seriously injured by a tear gas canister. The long history of struggle by Tavapy peasants was revived yesterday when about 400 of them temporarily occupied the road despite a strong police contingent. They refused to withdraw and sustained beatings and were the target of tear gas. [passage omitted]
