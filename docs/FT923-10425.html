920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / Observer: Sucker Ugh] - the indefatigable Hurd strikes again. Hardly had Britain's globe-trotting foreign secretary hit Kuala Lumpur after taking in a Philippines conference, than he bade the UK high commissioner there to organise a jungle walk. Off trooped the party, Hurd doing a David Attenborough in the van, our man in Malaysia to the rear. The foreign secretary came back exuding enjoyment, and the high commissioner with a leech up his trousers.
