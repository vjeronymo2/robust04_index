920309 FT 09 MAR 92 / UK News (Employment): Bearings maker extends holidays NSK, the Japanese bearings manufacturer, intends to extend this year's Easter and August holidays at its biggest plant in Peterlee, Co Durham, in order to cope without job losses with the effects of low levels of orders. The company is negotiating with the AEU engineering workers' union to lengthen the Easter holiday by eight days and the summer break by five. The proposals affect the 578 hourly-paid employees.
