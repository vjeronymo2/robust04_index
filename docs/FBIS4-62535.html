BFN [Untitled INTERFAX report under the "Visits" rubric] [Text] Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin's visit to Latvia is to take place before the end of May. This opinion was expressed in the course of a conversation in Riga between Latvian President Guntis Ulmanis and Aleksandr Rannikh, Russian ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to Latvia, INTERFAX was told in the office of the Latvian president.
