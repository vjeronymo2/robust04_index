920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / International Company News: Canadian gold merger nearer THE merger of Homestake Mining with Canada's International Corona to form North America's largest gold producer has only one step to go, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Homestake's shareholders in San Francisco approved the USDollars 400m share exchange offer. It requires approval of 90 per cent of Corona's shareholders by July 22.
