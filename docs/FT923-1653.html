920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Slight advance to IPounds 2.45m at Unidare Unidare, the Dublin-based maker and distributor of heating, electrical and welding goods, reported pre-tax profits slightly ahead at IPounds 2.45m (Pounds 2.52m) for the first half of 1992, against IPounds 2.35m which included an exceptional credit of IPounds 500,000. Sales, helped by acquisitions, rose 21 per cent to IPounds 59.9m (IPounds 49.6m). The company said underlying growth was 8 per cent. Earnings per share were 12.64p (11.46p) and the interim dividend is raised to 4.3p (4.1p).
