DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES Agency for Health Care Policy and Research Meeting of the National Advisory Council for Health Care Policy, Research, and Evaluation AGENCY: Agency for Health Care Policy and Research. ACTION: Change in the notice of public meeting.

SUMMARY: The meeting of the National Advisory Council for Health Care Policy, Research, and Evaluation will not be held in Washington, D.C. as published in the Federal Register of December 27, 1993, vol. 58, no. 246, page 68418. The primary speakers for the open portion of the meeting are unable to attend due to scheduling conflicts. DATES: The open meeting was scheduled for Monday, January 24, 1994, from 9 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. A closed portion of the meeting to review grant applications will be conducted through a telephone conference call on Tuesday, January 25, 1994, 12 noon.
