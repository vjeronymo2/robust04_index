BFN [Text] King Norodom Sihanouk issued a statement on 17 May pointing out that the situation in Cambodia is deteriorating and is leading the nation toward a collapse with each passing day. In the face of this situation, the king proposed a roundtable meeting of all Cambodian parties without preconditions in Pyongyang, North Korea on Friday, 27 May 1994. Answering the proposal, His Excellency Khieu Samphan, chairman of the Party of Democratic Kampuchea, sent the king a telegram dated 18 May expressing his agreement with a roundtable meeting in Pyongyang or in any other neutral place.
