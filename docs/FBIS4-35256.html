BFN [Text] The Saudi Arabian Government has opposed a visit by Hojjat ol-Eslam val-Moslemin Reyshahri, the representative of the supreme jurisconsult [Khamene'i] and the supervisor of the Iranian hajj pilgrims, from holy Mecca to holy Medina. In a written message, Hojjat ol-Eslam val-Moslemin Reyshahri has appointed Ayatollah Ahmad Jannati as deputy supervisor of the Iranian hajj pilgrims and official in charge of the exalted leadership's mission in Mecca. Earlier the Arabian hajj minister, in a letter addressed to Hojjat ol-Eslam val-Moslemin Reyshahri, announced the Saudi Government's opposition to a visit from Mecca to Medina by the representative of the supreme jurisconsult. The Saudi Government agreed to the presence of the representative of the supreme jurisconsult and supervisor of the the Iranian hajj pilgrims until the end of the hajj rites, as well as the presence of Iranians in Arabia, in its earlier agreements with Iran.
