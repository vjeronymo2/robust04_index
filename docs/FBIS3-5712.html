Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 10 (KCNA) -- Foreign news media dedicated special write-ups to the birthday of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. The Tanzanian paper MOTOMOTO said in an article titled "Outstanding Leader of Socialist Movement": Comrade Kim Chong-il, today when the socialist movement of the world is temporarily going through ordeals, scientifically clarified the purpose of the socialist movement and the road of its victory on the basis of the chuche idea founded by President Kim Il-song. He made immortal contributions to the development of the socialist movement by clarifying the reactionary essence and harm of modern social democracy and giving correct answers to the question of achieving unity of communist political parties and many other questions. Special write-ups with portraits of Comrade Kim Chong-il were carried by the Thai papers THE NATION, SIN CHUNG YUAN PAO and XING SAN RIBAO, the British paper ASIAN TIMES, the Mongolian paper UNEN, the Zambian paper ZAMBIA DAILY MAIL, the Nepalese paper JAN MABHOOMI, the Romanin paper EUROPA, the Indian paper DELHI KIGHALA, the Pakistani paper ITTIHAD, the Nicaraguan paper BARRICADA and the Tanzanian paper DAILY NEWS. Commemorative articles were aired by Guyana state radio, Radio Mali, Russian television, Radio Malta, Madagascan News Agency, radio and television, Togolese radio and television and Yugoslav Belgrade television.
