BFN [Unattributed report: "Inspection Carried Out"] [Text] On 2 May, the Government of Finland turned to the Government of the Russian Federation with a request, in line with the provision of the Vienna 1992 document on confidence-building measures and security, to carry out an inspection on the territory of Leningrad Military District in the area of Alakurtti, Plyarnyye Zori, Primorskiy, and Selkyalya. The Russian Federation Defense Ministry Information and Press Directorate reports that the Finnish side's request has been met. On 4 May, a Finnish inspection group comprising four representatives of Finland's Armed Forces arrived in the designated area where it conducted an inspection over 48 hours.
