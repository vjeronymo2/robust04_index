September 23, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

GUN CONTROL AND '22 COWARDLY ACTS'

Your editorial explains it all. The only time these so-called representatives would change their minds is if they could have witnessed the rampage of these gun-toting individuals, or if one of their loved ones would be affected. Do I hear the slogan "Guns don't kill, people do?" You couldn't kill seven, 10 or 15 people with a knife or ax in two minutes, could you? LEO ANSCHEL

Newport Beach
