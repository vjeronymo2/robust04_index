BFN [Text] Turkmenistan's Vice President Shikhmuradov, who arrived in Tehran today, was received by President Hashemi-Rafsanjani and presented the latter with a written message from Turkmenistan's President Mr. Niyazov. In the meeting, the president expressed satisfaction over the rate of expansion in friendly relations between Iran and Turkmenistan. He said the connection of the two countries' railways is of great significance for the increase of trade and economic exchanges as well as cargo transit between the countries of the European region and China. Referring to plans to build a pipeline for the transfer of Turkmenistan's gas to Turkey through Iran, Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani announced that the Islamic Republic of Iran is ready to participate in the speedy implementation of the plan. According to the Central News Unit, for his part the Turkmenistan vice president reported on the agreements reached between the two countries and called for the ever increasing expansion of bilateral relations on the regional and international levels and cooperation in the field of gas transfer. Referring to the Afghanistan problem, he expressed support for Iran's humanitarian policy in solving the existing difficulties in that country.
