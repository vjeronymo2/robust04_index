BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Today in the Foreign Ministry building on Smolensk Square, Andrey Kozyrev, head of Russia's foreign policy department, met his Greek colleague Karolos Papoulias. During the meeting, the sides discussed a number of questions of Russian-Greek relations and some details regarding the coming visit of Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin to Greece, which is to take part in the session of the heads of state and government of the European Union.
