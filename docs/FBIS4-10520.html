BFN [Text] A valuable cargo of approximately 50 garbage trucks arrived from several regions of Spain, mostly from Valencia, which donated 35 units. Upon the official delivery of these vehicles, among them garbage collectors, trucks, water tanks, and 612 containers intended for the collection of solid wastes, special expressions of gratitude were extended to the people of Valencia for their gesture of solidarity. State employees were also urged to take special care of these vehicles. Some 30 additional vehicles and more than 4,000 containers are expected to arrive from Spain by 30 April. These units will be put into operation immediately. Cesar Romero, Havana City Council provincial director, expressed thanks for the donation received from Valencia. He said that these trucks arrived at the most critical moment for sanitation in the capital.
