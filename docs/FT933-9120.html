930812 FT 12 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: More countries offer to evacuate Sarajevo wounded Several countries yesterday offered transport and hospital places for the seriously ill and wounded from Sarajevo. Britain, Sweden and Ireland said they would arrange for the evacuation of 41 emergency cases within the next two days, and other offers have come from nations such as Italy and the US. In Bosnia, Serb forces were reported late yesterday to be withdrawing in large numbers from their disputed positions on the Bjelasnica and Igman mountains overlooking Sarajevo.
