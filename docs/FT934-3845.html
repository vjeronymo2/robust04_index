931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Motor side boosts DC Cook After the motor retailing division produced its best results for five years, DC Cook swung from losses of Pounds 144,000 to profits of Pounds 1m pre-tax for the half year to October 31. Interim dividends are restored via a O3p payment. Earnings emerged at 1.71p (losses 0.34p). Turnover of the USM-quoted group rose from Pounds 52.3m to Pounds 75.1m. Its other activity, property, produced 'poor' results.
