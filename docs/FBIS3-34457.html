Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Fourteen people were wounded in incidents with the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] in the West Bank and the Gaza Strip today. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that nine people were injured in the Jabalyah refugee camp and three in al-Shati'. Palestinian sources say that five of the wounded are children under 12. Our correspondent Gil Litman reports that a youth was wounded by IDF gunfire in Janin after he threw metal bars and stones at soldiers. A Hebron resident was slightly injured by a petrol bomb which was thrown at an IDF patrol.
