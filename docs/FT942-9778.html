940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / International Company News: Ford in talks on Chinese joint venture Ford Motor chairman Mr Alexander Trotman said the company was in talks that could lead to a joint venture to assemble vehicles in China, writes our Financial Staff. Mr Trotman, speaking after the company's annual shareholders meeting, said the venture could involve one of several types of vehicles, including a small saloon or mini-van. He declined to identify who Ford was in talks with, but said a decision on the facility could be made by the end of the year. Ford earlier this year signed a deal with Shanghai Automotive Industry Corporation (SAIC) to form a joint venture to manufacture plastic automotive components in China.
