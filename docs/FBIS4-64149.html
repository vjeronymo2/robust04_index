BFN [Unattributed report: "The Federal Law `On the Procedure for the Publication and Enactment of Federal Constitutional Laws, Federal Laws, and Acts of Chambers of the Federal Assembly' Was Adopted 25 May by the State Duma"] [Text] As we know, on 25 May the Duma had already adopted this law, but it had not been approved by the Federation Council. In this connection, a coordination commission was created, made up of representatives of both chambers of the Federal Assembly and the Russian Federation president. It prepared a new single text of the law. The adopted document envisages that the day of the adoption of a federal law by the State Duma in its final wording is considered to be the date of its adoption. The date of the adoption of a federal constitutional law is the day when it is approved by the Federal Assembly chambers according to the procedure established by the Russian Federation Constitution. According to the document, the first publication of a federal constitutional law, a federal law, or an act of a Federal Assembly chamber in its full text in ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA or SOBRANIYE ZAKONODATELSTVA RF is considered to be the official publication.
