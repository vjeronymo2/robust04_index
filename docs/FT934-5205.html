931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / Move to cut theft of building machinery A national register of construction plant and machinery was launched yesterday to combat theft, which has cost industry Pounds 500m over the past year. The register is designed to help police trace stolen goods - often taken to order for sale overseas. This year a Pounds 240,000 mobile crane was stolen from a lay-by west of London and sold in Portugal for Pounds 120,000. Shipping documents had been prepared four days earlier.
