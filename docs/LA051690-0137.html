May 16, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ROLLINS BEATS CHAPMAN IN DIVISION II TENNIS

David Seline won at No. 2 singles and Laurent Dropsy won at No. 3 but Chapman College lost to Rollins College, 5-2, Tuesday in the second round of the NCAA Division II tennis finals at the Davis Center in St. Louis.

Rollins led, 4-2, after singles, and after winning one doubles match, the contest was stopped.

Chapman (11-11) plays Abilene Christian today for seventh place.
