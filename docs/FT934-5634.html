931129 FT 29 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Cambridge Water Mr Peter Shaw, chairman of Cambridge Water, said that water resources, much depleted in the four-year 'drought', had now recovered to near-average levels. The statutory water company announced profits ahead to Pounds 1.77m pre-tax for the six months to September 30, against Pounds 1.69m last time. Turnover improved to Pounds 7.63m (Pounds 7.23m). Earnings per Pounds 1 share worked through at 394p (410p); the interim dividend goes up 10p to 65p.
