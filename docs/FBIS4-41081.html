BFN [Text] Russian troops finished the removal of mines from the region adjacent to the village of Rukhi near the bridge across the River Inguri, the press center of the Russian troops in the Transcaucasus told Interfax. Control posts are being built on both banks of the river, after which the work to restore the Inguri bridge will begin. Deputy Commander of the Russian troops in the Transcaucasus Major-General Vassiliy Belchenko told Interfax that two battalions were ready to take part in the peacekeeping mission in Abkhazia. He said that these battalions' bases will be determined by Deputy Defense Minister Georgiy Kondratyev who is in charge of the peacekeeping operation. The operation itself is to begin during the next two days, said Belchenko. The Georgian Defense Ministry's press center informed Interfax that the Georgian troops and heavy equipment had been removed from the security zone in the region of the peacekeeping operation over to the Senaki Region. Only local police units still remain in the 12-kilometer zone in the Zugdidi and the Tsalendzhikha regions, said the Georgian Defense Ministry's spokesman.
