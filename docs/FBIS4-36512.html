BFN [Text] Jordanian Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali has denied the statements attributed to him regarding the occupied Syrian Golan, published by the London-based newspaper AL-HAYAH yesterday [as heard], saying that these statements were untrue and distorted. In a statement to the Jordanian News Agency yesterday, Dr. al-Majali added: Jordan rejects any attempts to confuse its strategic relations with sisterly Syria. Jordan regards Syria with absolute faith in common objectives and destiny to reach a just and comprehensive peace that will safeguard the rights of all of us, and ensure the implementation of UN Security Council Resolutions 242 and 338, on which the peace process is based.
