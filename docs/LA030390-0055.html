March 3, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Jon R. Haddan has joined the law firm of Cooley Godward Castro Huddleson & Tatum as partner in the Newport Beach office. He was formerly managing partner of the Newport Beach office of Hufstedler Miller Kaus & Beardsley. Before that, he was a partner at Wenke, Evans & Ikola.
