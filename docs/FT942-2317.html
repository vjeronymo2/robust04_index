940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / Company News (This Week): Hazlewood Foods - Other companies Hazlewood Foods: The food manufacturer is expected to announce an annual pre-tax profit of up to Pounds 50m tomorrow, down from the previous year's Pounds 55m on turnover of Pounds 761.8m. Last year's figure is unlikely to be matched even after taking out exceptionals related to restructuring and the sale of a Dutch subsidiary.
