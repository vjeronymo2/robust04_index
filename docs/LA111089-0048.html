November 10, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NBA ROUNDUP;

OAKLEY LEADS KNICKS' WIN

Charles Oakley did the dirty work in the second half for the New York Knicks as they defeated the New Jersey Nets, 107-105, Thursday night at New York.

Oakley had 17 of his 22 points and 11 of his 18 rebounds in the second half and Patrick Ewing's dunk with 1:22 left gave the Knicks their first lead of the game.

Denver 128, Houston 127 -- Alex English hit an eight-foot jumper with four seconds left in overtime to give the Nuggets a victory at Houston.
