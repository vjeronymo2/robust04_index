940820 FT 20 AUG 94 / UK Company News: English and Scottish Investors assets fall Net asset value at English and Scottish Investors, the investment trust, fell 7 per cent from 144.4p to 134.4p over the six months to July 31. The fully diluted figure showed a fall from 135.4p to 127.1p. However, on the back of an increase in income from investments of Pounds 4.63m (Pounds 3.58m) net revenue for the half year to the end of July came out 74 per cent ahead at Pounds 2.59m (Pounds 1.49m). Earnings per share were 1.6p (0.92p) and the interim dividend is 0.55p (0.5p).
