941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / World News in Brief: Blast on jumbo A mid-air explosion caused by a device under a seat on a Philippine Airlines Boeing 747 killed a Japanese engineer and injured 10 other Japanese passengers, forcing the aircraft to land at Okinawa. There were 287 passengers and 20 crew on the Manila-Tokyo flight. A Filipino Moslem separatist group, Abu Sayyaf, claimed responsibility.
