BFN [Text] Krahn and Mandingo frontline commanders of ULIMO [United Liberation Movement for Democracy in Liberia] have agreed to cease fire [words indistinct]. They have also agreed that all roads within ULIMO-controlled areas be under the control of ECOMOG [Economic Community of West African States Cease-Fire Monitoring Group] and be open for free passage for all persons. These agreements were reached yesterday at the end of a meeting between the two groups at ECOMOG headquarters in Monrovia. The joint communique, signed by both groups, also says that the hierarchy of ULIMO leadership will meet in Monrovia Monday [9 May] under the auspices of ECOMOG and UNOMIL [UN Observer Mission in Liberia] to effectively reunite the ULIMO family.
