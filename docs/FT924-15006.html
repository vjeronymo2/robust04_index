921007 FT 07 OCT 92 / VAT ruling BUILDERS and developers will be able to claim back value added tax on that proportion of their property portfolio which is prepared for sale, but not on those for which they levy rental income, the Customs & Excise department has ruled. The ruling follows decisions by the High Court to uphold the decision of VAT Tribunals in cases brought by two companies, Briararch and Curtis Henderson.
