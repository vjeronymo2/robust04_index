Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Veronika Romanenkova] [Text] Moscow January 14 TASS -- The Soyuz TM-17 spaceship with cosmonauts Vasiliy Tsibliyev and Aleksandr Serebrov aboard safely landed, as planned, on Friday at 11 hr 18 min 20 sec, Moscow time, ITAR-TASS learnt at the press centre of the Russian Space-Military Forces. The spaceship safely landed (despite a collision with the Mir space station reported by the Ostankino radio station) 215 kilometers west of the city of Karaganda, Kazakhstan. The crew is planned to be brought to Moscow later in the day.
