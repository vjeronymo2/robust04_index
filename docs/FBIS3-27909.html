Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Agricultural & Fishery Marketing Corp. yesterday agreed with the Australian state of New South Wales to promote bilateral cooperation on trade of agricultural and fishery products between Korea and Australia, a corporation spokesman said. For this, Sin Tae-chin, the corporation's president, signed a contract with Ian Causley, agriculture minister of the Australian state, to establish a collaboration body between food institutions of the two sides, he said. Last year, meanwhile, the state-run corporation's exports of agricultural, livestock, forestry and fishery products rose sharply 124 percent to $305 million from a year earlier, according to the spokesman. By field, shipments of agricultural and livestock products soared 148 percent to $101 million in 1993 from the year before, while exports of fishery products skyrocketed 212 percent to $92 million, he said.
