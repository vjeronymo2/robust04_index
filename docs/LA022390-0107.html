February 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

UPJOHN CO. APPROVES NEW CORPORATE ENTITY

The board of directors of the Upjohn Co. has approved formation of Upjohn Laboratories, a new corporate entity that combines the company's pharmaceutical research and medical functions.

The board has also elected Jerry R. Mitchell, formerly vice president for pharmaceutical research, to be senior vice president and president of Upjohn Laboratories.
