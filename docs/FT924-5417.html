921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / International Company News: Philip Morris in Dollars 3bn offering PHILIP Morris, the tobacco, food and beer group, has filed a Dollars 3bn 'shelf' offering of debt securities with the US Securities and Exchange Commission, plus warrants to buy the securities. It said proceeds from the offering would be used 'for general corporate purposes' and to refinance short-term and long-term borrowings. It added that, when combined with securities already registered, the group had Dollars 3.315bn principal amount of debt securities available for issue.
