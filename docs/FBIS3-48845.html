Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Present System of South Korea is Anti-national System of Confrontation" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, January 29 (KCNA) -- The confrontation atmosphere persists on the Korean peninsula because the traitorous Kim Yong-sam group of South Korea under the "civilian" veil is committing anti-national, anti-reunification crimes, says NODONG SINMUN in an article today. The author of the article notes: If the Kim Yong-sam group truly wanted national reconciliation and unity and reunification, it, first of all, must have reformed the anti-national, anti-reunification ruling system set up by the successive military dictatorial "regimes" in South Korea. By invoking the "National Security Law" the successive rulers of South Korea barred travel and contact between North and South, blocked national reconciliation and unity and tied fast the patriotic pro-reunification democratic figures in South Korea who hoped for reconciliation and unity with the North and harshly persecuted them, incriminating their slightest move. It is preposterous to talk about reunification, while leaving intact in South Korea the confrontation system with legal and institutional mechanisms which totally negate and block national reconciliation, unity and reunification itself. Nevertheless, the Kim Yong-sam puppet clique, while advertising "reform", is keeping the whole of the anti-communist confrontation system of the successive military dictatorial "regimes." Worse still the Kim Yong-sam puppet clique is on the run day in and day out to beat fellow countrymen on the back with the help of outside forces, crying for "international cooperation system" and "sanctions" over the "nuclear problem". The anti-national acts of the traitorous Kim Yong-sam clique strip bare the anti-national color of the "civilian government" pursuing North-South confrontation. As long as the treacherous North-South confrontation system against national reconciliation and unity is kept in South Korea and such a separatist as the traitor Kim Yong-sam is left alone, it is only too clear that one grave obstacle after another will be laid in the way of the struggle for a great unity of our nation and national reunification. The situation gives a serious lesson that the South Korean people and democratic forces must not place hope on the "civilian government" but smash the anti-reunification moves of the Kim Yong-sam traitor clique with a massive struggle.
