921120 FT 20 NOV 92 / Russian private sector grows ABOUT 10 per cent of small business in Russia is now in private hands, according to experts from the International Finance Corporation, the World Bank division which is assisting the state privatisation committee, writes John Lloyd in Moscow. Of the 14,222 shops and businesses now owned by the new merchant class, 8,499 were sold at auction. Many of the rest have been given away to the workers and managers.
