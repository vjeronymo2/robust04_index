Now the most important technique underlying biotechnology. Greatest expertise remains in US, with many European drug companies tapping into US biotech skills. Euro prospects: Fair ----------------------------------------------------------------------- MAGNETIC RESONANCE IMAGING Discovered in the US but key developments made in UK during 1970s. Medical equipment companies in Europe, Japan and the US now manufacturing MRI body scanners. Technology advancing rapidly; new machines will show not only images but also biochemical reactions inside the body. Euro prospects: Good ----------------------------------------------------------------------- HIGH-TEMPERATURE Discovered 1986 in Switzerland at SUPERCONDUCTIVITY IBM's Zurich research centre. Academic and industrial laboratories in many countries racing to exploit new ceramic materials which lose all electrical resistance at industrially useful temperature of liquid nitrogen. US and Japan ahead of Europe. Euro prospects: Fair ----------------------------------------------------------------------- PERSONAL COMPUTERS Invented and developed largely in US during 1970s. American companies lead in PC hardware and software. But Japan and other Asian countries making useful contributions, especially to the booming area of portable computers. European niche players at best. Euro prospects: Poor ----------------------------------------------------------------------- NEURAL NETWORKS Main development work done in US during 1980s. So far neural networks used mostly for military purposes and research but becoming more widespread in financial and industrial sectors for pattern recognition and forecasting. Though leaders are US & Japanese, Europe is catching up. Euro prospects: Fair ----------------------------------------------------------------------- COMMUNICATIONS SATELLITES Spin-off from US and Soviet space exploration and defence programmes starting in 1950s. Industry is divided between Japan (dominant in ground equipment), US (satellite construction) and Europe (launching). Euro prospects: Fair ----------------------------------------------------------------------- Sources: OECD, DTI R&D Scoreboard, European, Japanese and US patent offices -----------------------------------------------------------------------
