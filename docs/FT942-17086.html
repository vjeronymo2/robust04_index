940407 FT 07 APR 94 / Ecuador debt payment Ecuador yesterday announced it would resume interest payment on its Dollars 7bn (Pounds 4.7bn) foreign commercial debt, which it stopped servicing in September 1992, Raymond Colitt writes from Quito. Though the initial payment of Dollars 5m towards interest in arrears is only symbolic, Finance Minister Cesar Robalino said it would allow negotiations with the commercial banks to move ahead.
