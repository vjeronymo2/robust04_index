BFN [Text] Crimea must be given the maximum of economic independence. This is a condition for settlement of the Crimean issue, said Vladimir Lanovoy, who is a candidate for the Ukrainian presidency. Lanovoy said during a meeting with the Economic Council under the Crimean president that most tax revenues must remain in Crimea and be used for local needs. During his visit to Crimea Lanovoy met with representatives of the Crimean-Tatar parliament and discussed the issue concerning compensation for the damage caused by the state to the deported and oppressed peoples returning to Crimea.
