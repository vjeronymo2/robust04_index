BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 18. -- A delegation of the Ministry for Foreign Affairs led by Deputy Minister Nguyen Dy Nien has paid a visit to the Islamic Republic of Iran. While in Iran, the delegation was received by Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati, held by Deputy Minister (Mahdi Mostafavi). [sentence as received] The two sides exchanged views on orientation and measures to promote the cooperative relations between the two countries, the two ministries and on international matters of mutual concern. They noted with pleasure at the increasing consolidation and development of the two countries. The Iranian side highly valued Vietnam's renovation process and expressed their wish to contribute to the economic development in Vietnam. Deputy Foreign Minister Nguyen Dy Nien also met with leading officials of the Ministries of Trade, Oil and Gas, Light Industry, and Construction of Iran, the two sides discussed the orientation of cooperation development in the above said fields and shared the views that the two countries are capable to further develop their economic cooperation to higher level.
