920421 FT 21 APR 92 / Nigeria imports petrol NIGERIA, a leading member of the Organisation of Petroleum Exporting Countries, has been forced to import petrol following shortages caused by smuggling, hoarding and refinery stoppages, Reuter reports from Lagos. 'We imported 45m litres (12m gallons) of petrol products from Europe last week at a cost of about Dollars 9m (Pounds 5m),' an official said. But the imports did not prevent frustrated motorists forming long queues at garages over the Easter weekend.
