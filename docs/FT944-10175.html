941111 FT 11 NOV 94 / International Capital Markets: IFC guarantees Russian facility for first time The International Finance Corporation yesterday approved a guarantee of up to Dollars 10m for the Russian Trade Enhancement Facility, a new facility that will partially guarantee the credit risk of Russian banks engaged in trade finance. The facility, the first of its type to be supported by the International Finance Corporation is expected to guarantee up to Dollars 2.4bn of Russian trade. ABN AMRO, the Dutch bank, is co-guaranteeing the facility and will match, dollar for dollar, the corporation's exposure on each transaction.
