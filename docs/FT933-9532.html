930810 FT 10 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Dexter quits as England cricket selector Ted Dexter (left) is to stand down as chairman of the England cricket selectors on August 31, seven months ahead of schedule. The announcement came as England lost the fifth Test to Australia at Edgbaston, Birmingham, by eight wickets. Australia lead the six-match series 4-0. England have lost nine of their last 10 Tests.
