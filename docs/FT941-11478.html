940204 FT 04 FEB 94 / Rafsanjani 'plotters' arrested Iran said yesterday a man who fired shots during a speech by President Akbar Hashemi Rafsanjani had been part of an assassination plot and that all his accomplices had been arrested, Reuter reports from Nicosia. Tehran Radio quoted a security chief saying: 'In the wake of detailed intelligence work and the confessions of the assailant, all the accomplices and planners of this disgraceful action have been arrested.'
