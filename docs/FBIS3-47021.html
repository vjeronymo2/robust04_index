Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 7 KYODO -- The Posts and Telecommunications Ministry announced Friday [7 January] that it will lower interest rates on some fixed-amount savings and time savings by 0.05 percentage point, effective Monday. The rate on fixed-amount savings of less than six months will be kept unchanged at 1.22 percent per year while those on savings accounts of three years or more will be cut from the present 2.05 percent to 2.00 percent, ministry officials said. The rate on time savings with a deposit period of three years will be reduced from 2.10 percent to 2.05 percent.
