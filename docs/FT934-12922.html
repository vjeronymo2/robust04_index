931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / Chad 'foils' rebel coup Chad said yesterday it had foiled a coup attempt by rebel leader Abbas Kotti, Reuter reports from N'Djamena. Residents reported shooting and said troops and tanks were on the streets. Several rebel groups in the impoverished, country are threatening the rule of President Idriss Deby, who took power in a coup in 1990. Kotti, who signed a peace accord with the government last week, died of wounds in an exchange of fire with security forces trying to arrest him, the government said.
