BFN [Text] Prime Minister Goh Chok Tong, who is in London on a three-day official visit, is holding talks with his counterpart John Major. Earlier, Mr. Goh laid a wreath at the tomb of the unknown soldiers in Westminister Abbey and was given a ceremonial welcome at the Foreign and Commonwealth Office. This is Mr. Goh's first trip to Briatin as prime minister. Mr. Major will host a lunch at Downing Street in Mr. Goh's honor. Mr. Goh, whose delegation includes senior cabinet ministers and a high-powered business group, will be promoting Singapore as a business hub to potential European investors. The highlight of his trip will be the Singapore Forum tomorrow, where he will be addressing the captains of British and European industries.
