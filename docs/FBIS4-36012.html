BFN [Statement issued by the Council of Ministers; place and date not given] [Text] The government of the Republic of Yemen has taken cognizance of UN Security Council Resolution 924 of 1 June 1994 passed on the situation in the Republic of Yemen. While expressing its appreciation of the wording of the resolution, which addressed the situation in our country from the perspective of the national unity and constitutional legitimacy of the Republic of Yemen, and in accordance with the firm principles enshrined in the UN Charter, chief among which were the principles pertaining to national sovereignty, territorial integrity, and noninterference in domestic affairs -- something that would lead to an end to the bloodshed and to the start of a (?dialogue) to resolve internal disputes through peaceful means and reestablish peace and stability in our country --, the government of the Republic of Yemen reiterates its willingness to deal with the resolution in a positive spirit, and in a manner that would result in its implementation in an integrated manner by all concerned parties.
