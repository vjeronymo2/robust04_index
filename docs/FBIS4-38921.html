BFN [Text] Yerevan, 2 June (SNARK) -- Today the Armenian Vice-President Gagik Arutunyan flew to France and USA, to discuss the issues related with All-Armenian Foundation "Hayastan" ("Armenia"). As SNARK reporter was told in the press bureau of Armenian President, Arutunyan will also attend the celebration of Hrayr Hovnanian, the chairman of "Armenian American Assembly" in New York. It has also become known to SNARK that Gagik Arutunyan has taken with him a video-address of Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan to Armenian Diaspora in USA.
