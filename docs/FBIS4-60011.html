BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 17 May (WAKH) -- His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the United Arab Emirates [UAE], received a telephone call from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih this evening. The Emirates News Agency said views were exchanged on the current developments in Yemen and the bloody fighting between brothers. It added that Shaykh Zayid stressed the urgent need to stop Yemeni fighting in order to pave the way for the continuation of his highness's efforts to reconcile the Yemeni brothers' views. The UAE president appealed to the Yemeni president to do his best to put an immediate end to fighting and to exercise maximum self-restraint in order to spare blood and safeguard the people's lives. He said there will be no victor or vanquished among the sons of the one country and that the real loser will be the Yemeni people, who will suffer from the destruction inflicted on them. Shaykh Zayid regretted what is happening as well as the Yemeni president's failure to respond to the repeated calls to stop the fighting, stressing that what is taking place is a disaster for Yemen and the Arab nation.
