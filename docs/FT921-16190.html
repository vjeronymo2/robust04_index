920106 FT 06 JAN 92 / UK acts over tourist murders The Foreign Office said yesterday it was seeking an explanation from Angola for the murder of four Britons in an ambush near a camp holding former rebels in the south of the country, Reuter reports from London. The victims were among a party of seven British tourists attacked near Quilengues, in the southern province of Huila, on Friday night. Two were injured and one escaped unharmed.
