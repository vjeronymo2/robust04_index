Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Two more domestic airports Hanoi-Dien Bien in Lai Chau Province, and Hanoi-Na San in Son La Province will be reopened by mid-April. More than 1 billion dong (roughly $100,000) have been spent in rebuilding the Dien Bien Airport, and about $25,000 will be needed for the ugrading of the Na San Airport.
