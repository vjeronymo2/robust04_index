Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, January 13 (AZERTAC]--The Armenian armed forces suffering defeat caused by the Azeri national army, decided to revenge itself on civilian population of Azerbaijan. The districts of Kazakhskiy, Kedabekskiy and Tauzskiy, which border on Armenia in the south-west, were subject to heavy shelling of the Armenian artillery yesterday. There are victims and heavy destruction. The Defence Ministry says yesterday's fight did not change the situation at the front. The Armenian armed forces attempts to attack the village of Hakop-Kamari from Terter, Agdam and Beylagan directions were stopped by the Azeri national army. The enemy suffered heavy casualties.
