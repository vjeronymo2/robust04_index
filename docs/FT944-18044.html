941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Bradford Property Bradford Property Trust is making an offer to acquire Harborne Tenants, which will value the property investment company at Pounds 11.4m. The offer values each Harborne ordinary share at 230p and each preference share at 60p. BPT currently owns 28.1 per cent of Harborne's ordinary and about 23.7 per cent of its preference shares. Harborne owns tenanted residential properties in the Midlands.
