November 11, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

TRI-VALLEY LEAGUE

Oak Park 34, St. Bonaventure 8 -- Junior tailback Kent Richter rushed for 221 yards in 17 carries at Oak Park to break the Eagles' single-game rushing record set by Erik Affholter.

Richter, who finished with 1,177 yards, scored two touchdowns to give him 15, another school record. His second touchdown was a 92-yard run in the third period to close out the Eagles' scoring. GREG MEADE
