940204 FT 04 FEB 94 / International Company News: UNP Holdings raises CDollars 12.5m International UNP Holdings, the Toronto stock exchange-listed company which specialises in buying control of and turning round medium-sized Polish companies, has raised CDollars 12.5m (USDollars 9.4m) by the private placement of 13.8m new shares, writes Anthony Robinson, Eastern Europe editor. The lead investor is Barings Investment Management, which has taken up shares worth CDollars 7.86m. UNP has raised Dollars 42m since 1989 for its ventures in Poland and has turned round three former state owned companies.
