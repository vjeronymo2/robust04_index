920311 FT 11 MAR 92 / Fiji poll date announced Fijians will go to the polls between May 16 and May 23 in the first general election under the islands' racially-biased constitution, introduced in 1990 in the wake of two military coups, writes Kevin Brown in Sydney. The dates were announced in Suva yesterday by Fiji's Electoral Commission, which will supervise the election. Mr Qoriniasi Bale, chairman, said the timetable was subject to approval by President Ratu Sir Peniai Ganilau, the head of state.
