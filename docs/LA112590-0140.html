November 25, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

O.C. PLAYERS ELIMINATED FROM GOLF TOURNAMENT

Tiger Woods of Cypress and Kellee Booth of Coto de Caza were beaten Saturday in the championship flight quarterfinals of the Rolex Junior Golf Classic at the La Paloma Country Club.

Woods, who had won his opening round match against Eric Dollenberg of Flourtown, Pa., 2 and 1, was eliminated by Grant Masson of Richardson, Tex., 3 and 2.

Booth, the qualifying medalist, defeated Jo Jo Robertson of Roswell, N.M., 2 and 1, but was beaten by Heather Bowie of Edmond, Okla., 4 and 2.
