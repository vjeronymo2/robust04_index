940308 FT 08 MAR 94 / Business switch for Lloyd's agencies Willis Faber & Dumas (Agencies) is to take responsibility for managing the affairs of Names participating at Lloyd's through the Janson Green agency, in a move underlining rationalisation among agency businesses at the insurance market. Willis Faber is part of Willis Corroon, the insurance broker, and will now be one of the largest members' agents at Lloyd's. Janson Green will focus on its managing agents business, administering the affairs of Lloyd's syndicates.
