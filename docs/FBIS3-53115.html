Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa today held talks with Yusuf al-'Alawi, Omani minister of state for foreign affairs, who stated that they exchanged views on issues of interest to both countries. He said they agreed to continue consultations on coordination between Egypt and Oman on issues of interest to them. He emphasized that there is an identity of views between Egypt and Oman on the various issues. The Omani minister added that they had also discussed the peace process, particularly the multilateral negotiations and the future of these negotiations. He said that Oman supports efforts to enhance Arab solidarity. Foreign Minister Musa stated that the conference of the Damascus Declaration foreign ministers expressed the positions of the participating countries on current problems. He said that his meeting with al-'Alawi today was a follow-up to what took place in Damascus. They also discussed the Arab situation and bilateral relations, and exchanged views on the peace process, particularly on the bilateral and multilateral tracks, he said.
