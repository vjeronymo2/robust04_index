BFN [Text] The situation has not altered in the city of Kuito. Clashes between government and Black Cockerel troops intensified since the afternoon of 12 June when about 800 men of the rebel organization coming from Ukuma, Huambo Province, arrived in this city. An Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola soldier, who has also come from Ukuma and asked to not to be named after turning himself over to government forces, due to worry concerning the security of his family, said the 800 soldiers of Jonas Savimbi have brought an assortment of war materiel carried by five container trucks.
