930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / Deal proposed to divide Bosnia Croatian president Franjo Tudjman waves to journalists on arrival at Geneva peace talks where he announced that Croatia and Serbia have agreed a joint Bosnian peace proposal which would divide the country into three ethnic states. Mr Tudjman's announcement followed a three-hour meeting with his Serbian, Bosnian and Montenegrin opposite numbers. Croatia and Serbia peace proposal, Page 2
