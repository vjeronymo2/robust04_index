BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin this afternoon met with Viktor Posuvalyuk, head of the Russian Foreign Ministry's Middle East Department. The Russian official, who arrived in the Middle East as the special envoy of President Yeltsin, briefed Rabin about his meetings in Tunis and Jordan. Our political correspondent Shlomo Raz reports that in the meeting, Posuvalyuk made it clear that Russia wants to preserve a neutral attitude in the Middle East political process and is interested in coordinating positions with the United States in the political negotiations.
