August 2, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

DRIVER DIES WHEN TRUCK OVERTURNS

A Van Nuys woman was killed Wednesday morning when her pickup truck overturned on a curve on Laurel Canyon Road in Studio City, Los Angeles police said.

Sandra Jean Ettinger, 20, who had recently moved to Van Nuys from Arizona, died instantly in the 5:20 a.m. accident on the winding canyon road about half a mile north of Mulholland Drive, Officer Leona Thomas said.

Police said Ettinger apparently lost control of the vehicle while driving at an unsafe speed on a curve.

The pickup overturned and ejected her. It overturned several more times and hit a concrete block in front of a house.

The accident closed two of the four lanes during the morning rush hour.

Southbound traffic was backed up two miles to Ventura Boulevard.
