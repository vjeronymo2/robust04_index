Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] In a letter sent today to the UN Security Council, Alija Izetbegovic, president of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Presidency informs this body that the Serbian aggressor has launched a fierce attack at the region of Bihac. The Bosnia-Herzegovina Army has successfully repelled the brunt of the enemy attack, but everything implies that the Serbs will not refrain from trying to capture this region, which is a safe haven under the control of UN forces. President Izetbegovic has asked the international community's Security Council and NATO to intervene swiftly to prevent new massacres and ethnic cleansing in this part of Bosnia-Herzegovina.
