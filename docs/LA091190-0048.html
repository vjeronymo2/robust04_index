FESTIVAL '90;

FESTIVAL SCREEN HIGHLIGHTS

Following are reviews of highlights of screenings in the Los Angeles Festival today and Wednesday:

Today

The Elephant Keeper

Thailand

Screens at 8 p.m., Los Feliz Theater, 1822 N. Vermont Ave.

Tedious, interminable, needlessly repetitive Thai film protesting the decimation of the country's magnificent teak forest and centering on a man, an elephant keeper, seeking to survive this destruction. Prince Chatri Chalerm Yukol brings considerable polish and technical finesse to his film, but the urgency of its theme is dissipated by its sheer ponderousness. KEVIN THOMAS
