940624 FT 24 JUN 94 / Exports ready to 'take off' Government policies had established a platform for British exports to 'take off' in the 1990s, Mr Richard Needham, the trade minister, told the Commons last night. 'There is an enormous chance to rebuild Britain's share of world trade,' he said. Emphasising the success already achieved, Mr Needham said the cover provided by the Export Credits Guarantee Department was in danger of running out 'because we have used so much of it'.
