Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] According to well-informed sources, President Boris Yeltsin has returned to the government for reworking the draft decree on a cabinet reform. The draft was submitted to the president at the end of last year. Yeltsin discussed its details with Prime Minister Chernomyrdin and several other leading figures. The sources say the president had questioned several key provisions of the draft. The sources believe the resignation of Vice-Premiers Aleksandr Shokhin and Sergey Shakhray is a decided issue. They also feel Vice-Premiers Yuriy Yarov and Aleksandr Zaveryukha are going to be dropped from the cabinet too. As for Anatoliy Chubays, it is not clear whether he is going to leave the government altogether or remain the head of the State Property Committee without being a vice-premier.
