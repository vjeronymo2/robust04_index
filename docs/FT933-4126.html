930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / Lib Dems urge consumer ministry A WHITEHALL department of consumer protection to bring together responsibility for an extended Citizen's Charter, regulation of utilities and consumer protection work, and compulsory self-regulation schemes for all trades and professions are among the proposals set out in a Liberal Democrat consultation paper on consumer rights. Mr Matthew Taylor, the Liberal Democrat Citizen's Charter spokesman, said the paper, to be discussed at the party's conference next month, would provide 'a strong voice for the consumer at the heart of government'.
