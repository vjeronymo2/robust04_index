921128 FT 28 NOV 92 / International Company News: US group buys Greek shipyard THE HELLENIC Industrial Development Bank (ETVA) has agreed to sell Neorion Shipyards, a state-owned yard on the Aegean island of Syros, to a US shipping company for Dr3.3bn (Dollars 16.5m), writes Kerin Hope in Athens. Ionian American Maritime, owned by Mr Spyros Mylonas, made the highest of three bids for the yard, offering Dr300m in cash with the remainder to be paid in seven annual instalments.
