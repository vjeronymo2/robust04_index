920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Loss of Pounds 98,000 at James Dickie James Dickie, the Scottish engineering group which saw a return to profits in the second half of 1990-91, showed a loss of Pounds 98,000 for the six months to February 29 1992, compared with a deficit of Pounds 57,000. Turnover fell from Pounds 16.2m to Pounds 6.9m. The continuing businesses showed profits in the second quarter and this improvement is expected to continue to the financial year end. The loss was struck after interest of Pounds 211,000 (Pounds 308,000). Closure costs were reflected in extraordinary charges of Pounds 194,000.
