Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 30 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met here this morning with Robert Kimmit, former U.S. deputy secretary of state. They exchanged views on Sino-U.S. relations and issues of common concern. Chinese Vice-Foreign Minister Liu Huaqiu attended the meeting. Kimmit arrived here Tuesday [29 March] as guest of the Chinese Foreign Ministry.
