March 10, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"It is the most repugnant proposal I have witnessed in government. I am not willing to sacrifice a generation of young people to be housed in Quonset huts or makeshift classrooms. . . ."

-- Beverly Hills Mayor Robert K. Tanenbaum, referring to a proposal to tear down the 60-year-old high school and replace it with a Century City-style high-rise in order to raise money for the school district.
