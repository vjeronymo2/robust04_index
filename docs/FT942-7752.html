940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Scottish Radio Scottish Radio Holdings, formerly known as Radio Clyde, achieved a 29 per cent increase in pre-tax profits to Pounds 1.97m for the half year ended March 31. Turnover advanced 15 per cent to Pounds 8.1m - through its broadcasting outlets the USM-traded company serves more than 85 per cent of the Scottish population. Earnings rose to 14.1p (12p) and the interim dividend is lifted 1p to 4.5p. The comparative profits figure included exceptional income of Pounds 273,000.
