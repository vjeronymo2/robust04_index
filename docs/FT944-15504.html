941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / UK Company News: BMSS ahead BMSS, the USM-quoted builders' merchant, saw pre-tax profits rise by 39 per cent from Pounds 207,000 to Pounds 287,000 in the six months to July 31. Although margins remained under pressure, the result reflected a modest improvement in activity levels in the construction industry. Turnover improved 25 per cent to Pounds 9.41m; the company said the second half had started encouragingly with sales showing a significant increase over last year. Earnings per share climbed to 2.4p (1.7p) and the interim dividend is raised to 2p (1.5p).
