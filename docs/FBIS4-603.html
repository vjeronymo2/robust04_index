BFN [Text] The chairman of the IEC [Independent Electoral Commission], Mr. Justice Kriegler, says the commission will be ready on the 26th this month to take special votes. It will also be ready to receive thousands of voters at the approximately 9,000 voting stations on the following two days. Mr. Justice Kriegler said the location of at least 99 percent of the voting stations had already been determined. Mr. Kriegler said two plane loads of ballot papers had arrived in South Africa from London yesterday and have been taken to places of safe-keeping. He called on people, particularly in isolated places, to make themselves available to act as monitors at voting stations.
