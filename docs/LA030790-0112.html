March 7, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

UNDAUNTED: Although Ron Kovic has announced that he will not run for Congress (B1), Democrats here still hope to reclaim two marginal local districts. . . . Party analysts say there may be as many as 36,000 "unregistered potential loyal Democrats" in Curt Pringle's (R-Garden Grove) 72nd Assembly District, which overlaps with the congressional seat held by Rep. Robert K. Dornan. . . . Now, Democrats say, they must find these voters and register them. Kristina Lindgren and Mark Pinsky
