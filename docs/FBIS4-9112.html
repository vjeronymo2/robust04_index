BFN [Text] Sarajevo, April 21 (AFP) -- A convoy of 100 UN troops and 41 medical personnel left Sarajevo Thursday for the battered Moslem enclave of Gorazde in eastern Bosnia, UNPROFOR spokesman Major Rob Annink said. The convoy, including French, British and Ukrainian troops, would attempt to enter the enclave, where 65,000 are under Serbian attack, if the fighting there was not too intense, Annink said.
