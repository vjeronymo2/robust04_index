BFN [Viktor Krokhmalyuk report: "False Alarm"] [Text] At 0220 hours on 29 May, the generator of unit No. 1 -- the only one at the Khmelnytskyy Atomic Plant -- was disconnected from the network as a result of a false triggering of the system of protection of the block transformer due to a short circuit in the strands of the control cable. The automatic system unloaded the reactor installation to the level of 10 percent of its nominal load. After the fault was repaired power unit No. 1 was reconnected to the network at 0230 hours on 30 May. There were no violations of the limits or conditions of safe operation or changes in radioactivity.
