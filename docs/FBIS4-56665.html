BFN [Excerpt] Radio Antilles International will soon be back on the air, station director Jacques Sampeur said on Signal FM on 14 May. The financial reserves of the station, however, are not sufficient to cover the repair fees, he added, so a lottery has been organized to help the administration with its undertakings. Sampeur notes that at one time, the UN Development Program [PNUD] had drawn up a list [not further identified] to help the station, but this was destroyed in the crush of the 30 September 1991 events. He nevertheless pointed out that this help will need to be extended. [passage omitted]
