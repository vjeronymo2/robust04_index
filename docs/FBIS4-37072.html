BFN [Text] Dr. 'Ali al-Hajj Muhammad, minister at the federal government bureau and official government spokesman on the peace negotiations, met Ambassador Melissa Wells, the U.S. president's envoy, yesterday at his office in the Friendship Hall in Khartoum. Dr. 'Ali al-Hajj confirmed to the ambassador that the rebel movement is not serious in the negotiations, pointing out the late arrival of the movement's delegation at the recent negotiations in Nairobi. He praised the Intergovernmental Authority on Drought and Development countries' initiative and their constructive role in establishing peace in the country.
