BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' today received Viktor Posuvalyuk, Russian President Boris Yeltsin's envoy. Attending the meeting were Dr. Ghassan Raslan, director of the East Europe Department at the Foreign Ministry, and Aleksandr Zotov, Russian ambassador in Damascus. The talks dealt with developments in the peace process, and Israel's arrogant policies, and its stands obstructing the achievement of a just and comprehensive peace in the region. In this regard, the foreign minister urged the Russian Federation and the United States, in their capacity as the peace process sponsors, to denounce these Israeli policies and stands, particularly the recent terroristic kidnapping operation carried out by the Israeli troops inside the Lebanese territory. The talks also dealt with relations between Syria and Russia and means of promoting them.
