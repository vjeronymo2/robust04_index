940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / Telford to get ice machine factory Hoshizaki Electric, a private Japanese company making ice machines, is to set up a plant to supply the European market at Telford, Shropshire. The company, which has annual turnover of more than Pounds 500m, holds 70 per cent of the Japanese market. It will spend about Pounds 1m setting up the operation, employing 15 people initially and 100 by 1996. Production schedules call for 3,000 units this year, climbing to 10,000 in 1996.
