August 9, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: TOURISM

Consulting Firm: Management Resources, a consulting firm that does extensive work for expositions and amusement parks, has several new projects in the works, including jobs for both Walt Disney Co. and MCA/Universal Studios.

The firm, which just moved to new offices in Tustin, is helping to market Disney's Magic Kingdom Club for employees. It also does personnel planning and operations for the new Universal Studios attraction in Orlando, Fla.

In addition, the company is doing consulting work for a 1995 World's Fair that would be split between Vienna and Budapest.
