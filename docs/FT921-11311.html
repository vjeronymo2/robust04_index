920201 FT 01 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Pounds 58,000 loss for Courtyard Leisure Courtyard Leisure, the USM-quoted operator of licensed eating places, incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 58,000 in the six months to September 30 1991, against a profit previously of Pounds 49,000. The loss took account of Pounds 38,000 exceptional charges representing staff and establishment costs. Turnover rose to Pounds 805,000 (Pounds 661,000). Losses per share were 0.65p.
