Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] Latvian Prime Minister Birkavs told journalists today that Latvia will actively--dependent on financial possibilities-- start cooperating within the framework of the NATO Partnership for Peace program. Birkavs comments came following his return from Brussels. On Monday in Brussels, Birkavs confirmed with his signature Latvia's agreement to the new NATO program. Latvia will initially participate in the formation of a Baltic peacekeeping battalion, arranging its military structures so they correspond with NATO standards. Moreover, Latvia will try to use all the information received from the NATO, as well as taking part in NATO exercises and various educational seminars.
