940913 FT 13 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Candover net asset value advances 7% Candover Investments, the investment trust specialising in large management buyouts, saw a 7 per cent increase in net assets per share in the six months from December 31, up from 310p to 330p, and a 17 per cent increase from 281p on June 30 1993. Pre-tax profits for the first half were ahead at Pounds 2.4m (Pounds 1.6m), including a Pounds 724,000 non-recurring contribution from an investee company. Profit after tax was also ahead, at Pounds 1.61m, against Pounds 1.09m. Following an additional Pounds 9m contribution, the Candover 1994 Fund to finance large buy-outs and buy-ins now stands at Pounds 222m and a final closing is expected before the year end. Earnings per share came out at 7.18p (4.87p). The interim dividend is increased to 4.15p (3.95p).
