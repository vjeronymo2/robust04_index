June 3, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

TRACK SHOTS INTERRUPTED THRILLING VIEW OF WIVES

What a race on ABC!

Teresa Fittipaldi took a big lead, but Shelley Unser battled back. Then it was Teresa, then Shelley. Teresa. Shelley. Then a split-screen of both!

Too bad they kept interrupting the race with pictures of silly cars.

PAUL SCHOWALTER

Ontario
