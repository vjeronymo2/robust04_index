940422 FT 22 APR 94 / International Company News: Benetton lifts dividend after 12% advance Benetton, the Italian clothing group, again defied the worldwide recession, increasing net consolidated profits last year by a further 12.6 per cent to L208bn (Dollars 86m), from L185bn in 1992. The company backed up an optimistic statement by proposing a 10 per cent increase in the dividend, to L385 a share from L350. Overall consolidated sales in the 120 countries where Benetton is now established rose to L2,751bn, against L2,512bn in 1992.
