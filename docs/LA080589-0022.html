August 5, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

LOSING FOOTBALL TEAMS IS THE COLISEUM'S FAULT

If the Raiders leave (which I hope they don't), blame it on the Coliseum. That is the worst place to watch a football game. With the "Olympic" track and one end zone being about 100 yards away from the playing field, the crowd is totally removed from the game.

Why pay to go in a stadium when you can't even see the game?

JOHN FLETCHER

El Cajon
