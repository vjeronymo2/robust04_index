920718 FT 18 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Greenfriar Invest On earnings per share down from 3.36p to 3.14p in the first half of 1992, Greenfriar Investment is holding its interim dividend at 2.1p. The final payment is expected to be at least maintained at 4p. At June 30 the net asset value had risen to 351.1p, compared with 345.8p six months' earlier and with 340.4p in June 1991. Total income in the period was Pounds 670,000 (Pounds 829,000).
