BFN [Text] The Haifa District Court has lifted the ban on reporting the detention of two Israeli Arabs, residents of West Barta'ah, suspected of aiding the terrorist who perpetrated the attack at Hadera's central bus station. The two -- 'Atif Kabaha, 46, and Majid Kabaha, 48 -- are suspected of driving the terrorist from East Barta'ah in the territories to Hadera's central bus station under orders from Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] in return for money. The court extended their remand for another 15 days. They are not cooperating with the police. Our correspondent Nati Silberman notes that the police take a grave view of cooperation between Israeli Arabs and terrorists.
