[FRL&hyph;5112&hyph;2] Iron and Steel Sector of the Common Sense Initiative; Notice of Open Meeting The Environmental Protection Agency is convening a meeting of persons interested in the Iron and Steel sector of the Agency's Common Sense Initiative. The purpose of the meeting will be to discuss potential issues or projects that the sector may choose to pursue. The meeting will be held at the Old Colony Inn, 625 First Street, Alexandria, Virginia from 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. eastern standard time on Wednesday, December 14, 1994. Time will be allotted during the meeting for public statements. For more information, please contact Ms. Mary Byrne at 312&hyph;353&hyph;2315 or Ms. Judith Hecht at 202&hyph;260&hyph;5682.
