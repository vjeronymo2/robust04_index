May 14, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ADDING INSULT TO INJURY

I found a few interesting points in the story on John Mack. During his sentencing, Mack was described as being under "intense psychological pressure from 72-hour workweeks and a failing 3-week-old marriage." I also noticed that currently Mack is "handling major responsibilities under considerable pressure."

KEVIN STEINFELD

Seal Beach
