940221 FT 21 FEB 94 / Business Travel (Travel Update): Southwest on time Southwest airlines was the best time keeper among US airlines last year, according to official figures published last week by the Transport Department. Southwest completed 89.7 per cent of its flights on time, defined as within 15 minutes of the scheduled arrival time. Northwest was second with 85.9 per cent, closely followed by America west at 85.5 per cent. Significantly perhaps some of the largest US carriers were well down the list: USAir stood at 82.9 per cent; TWA 82.6 per cent; American 80.8 per cent; Continental 79 per cent; United 78.5 per cent and Delta 76.7 per cent.
