920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / World Trade News: Turkey to seek Arab financing Turkey is to seek Arab finance to establish a regional development bank to service the Black Sea and Central Asian states after last week's framework economic agreement signed in Istanbul, writes John Murray Brown in Istanbul. Turkey's proposal envisages a bank providing insurance cover and a settlement mechanism for trade between Turkey, Greece and Albania and the eight former communist states around the Black Sea; Russia, Ukraine, Moldova, Georgia, Romania, Bulgaria, Armenia and Azerbaijan. Mrs Tansu Ciller, Turkey's chief economics minister, said Turkish officials would visit Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, today to sign an agreement with the Islamic Development Bank.
