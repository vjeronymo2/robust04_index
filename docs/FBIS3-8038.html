Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Tirana District Court spokesman reports that the trial of Romeo Lici, Martin Leka, Bashkim Feta, and Aleksander Frangaj, charged with conspiring to reveal state secrets, defined by Articles 122 and 134 of the Penal Code, ended today. At the end of the trial, the judicial body decided as follows: To declare the defendants Romeo Lici and Martin Leka guilty, charged with the criminal act of revealing state secrets, anticipated by Articles 122 and 13 of the Penal Code, and to sentence them to four years and 18 months imprisonment, respectively. The defendant Bashkim Feta was declared guilty for the criminal act of having a careless attitude while on duty, anticipated by Article 108 of the Penal Code, and was convicted with the preimprisonment term. Regarding Aleksander Frangaj, the court decided to suspend investigations for lack of elements of the crime motive.
