BFN [Text] We have received the following announcement by the Press Center of the Army of Bosnia-Herzegovina: On 7 April 1994, beginning at 1800, Army General Rasim Delic issued the order to units of the Army of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina on a general cease-fire with the Serbian aggressor's side to last 24 hours. This order ensued as a result of the insistence of the Security Council and Mr. Akashi in order to create necessary conditions for military-political talks with the representatives of the Serbian aggressor. The order issued to the units of the Army of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina fully implies meeting the demands presented by representatives of the Army of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina. This above all applies to stopping the offensive of the Serbian aggressor's forces on Gorazde and Bihac, which was presented to General Rose and the public during the day.
