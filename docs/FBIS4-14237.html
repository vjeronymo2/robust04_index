BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, this afternoon called on brother Dr. Hasan Muhammad Makki, the first deputy prime minister, who has been hospitalized following a sinful assassination bid today. The brother chairman inquired about brother Makki's health and expressed regret for this deplorable incident. He wished the brother first deputy prime minister speedy recovery. He also expressed condolences for the families of the three martyred escorts of the first deputy prime minister who fell in that regrettable incident. The brother chairman was accompanied by brother Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar, deputy prime minister and minister of industry; Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani, minister of planning and development; Brigadier General Yahya al-Mutwakkil, minister of interior; and Husayn al-Masiri, Sanaa mayor.
