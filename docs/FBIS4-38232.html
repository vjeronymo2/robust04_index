BFN [Text] Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has stated that during the contacts held among the foreign ministers of the Damascus Declaration countries an agreement was reached to convene a new round of meetings for the Declaration countries' foreign ministers on 5 July 1994 in Kuwait. The foreign ministers will discuss issues of interest to the Arab nation, led by developments in Yemen and how to end the fighting there. The ministers will also discuss how to bolster cooperation in the fields stipulated by the Declaration. Foreign Minister Musa also commented on the European Union's [EU] Corfu summit statement. In particular, he noted the welcome and support expressed in the statement for the contacts currently being held with Egypt to find a new agreement between Egypt and the EU. He said the EU's agreement was based on talks Egypt held with the EU foreign ministers during the meetings of the Egyptian-European Cooperation Council in Brussels last month. This decision is seen as support for cooperation between Egypt and the EU.
