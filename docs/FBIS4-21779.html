BFN [Text] The Central District police and the Shin Bet have arrested two Khan Yunus residents suspected of attacking Yitzhaq Rutenberg with axes at a construction site in Petah Tiqva yesterday. Our correspondent Shim'on Vilna'i reports that the two men were arrested following exhaustive intelligence efforts in a joint operation by the Shin Bet and the police. The two suspects, both 21 years of age, were found in possession of Rutenberg's identity card. They were staying in Israel without permits.
