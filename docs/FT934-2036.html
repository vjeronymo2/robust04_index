931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / Aramco bids for Philippine group Aramco, the Saudi Arabian oil company, yesterday submitted a bid of Dollars 502m (Pounds 336m) for 40 per cent of Petron Corporation, the Philippines' largest oil refining and marketing company owned by the state Philippine National Oil Company (PNOC), writes Jose Galang in Manila. The bid represents the largest bid for a government asset under the country's seven-year privatisation programme.
