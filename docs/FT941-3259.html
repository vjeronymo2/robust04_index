940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / Tough rules for Malawi election Malawi's Electoral Commission announced tough new regulations yesterday following reports of widespread intimidation, bribery and violence ahead of the first multi-party elections in May, Reuter reports from Blantyre. Ms Anastazia Msosa, commission head, told the press the commission had decided to bar chiefs and other traditional leaders from acting as monitors for any political parties.
