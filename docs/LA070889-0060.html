July 8, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NICARAGUA EXPELS ELECTION OBSERVER

The leftist Sandinista government has expelled a U.S. citizen who planned to observe next February's elections, the Interior Ministry said Friday.

A ministry spokeswoman said Antonio Ybarra was given 48 hours on Wednesday evening to leave the country for violating immigration procedures. She said Ybarra failed to notify immigration authorities of a change of address.
