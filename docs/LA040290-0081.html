April 2, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Boesky Due for Release: Ivan F. Boesky, Wall Street's most notorious felon, may be a free man this week. The former arbitrager is due for official release on Wednesday, two years after he entered prison for his role in the nation's biggest insider trading scandal, a source close to Boesky said. However, the source said Boesky was released into home custody about six weeks ago.
