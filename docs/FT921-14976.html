920113 FT 13 JAN 92 / India in clear Pressure on India to sign the nuclear non-proliferation treaty (NPT) is expected to ease because of a statement by a key US senator that he does not think the country has a nuclear weapons programme, writes KK Sharma in New Delhi. Senator Larry Pressler, who is the author of the amendment barring US aid to Pakistan because of evidence of a nuclear weapons programme, said in New Delhi at the weekend that he did not think the amendment should be extended to India.
