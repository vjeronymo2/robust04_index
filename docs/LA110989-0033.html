November 9, 1989, Thursday, Valley Edition

CSUN MEN'S BASKETBALL TEAM SIGNS 2 TO LETTERS OF INTENT

Mark Wilkirson of Santa Ana Foothill High and Dorian Manigo of San Francisco Riordan signed national letters of intent with the Cal State Northridge men's basketball team Wednesday.

Wilkirson, a 6-foot-5, 175-pound swingman, and Manigo, a 6-1, 160-pound point guard, signed on the first day of the National Collegiate Athletic Assn. early-signing period. They will enroll at Northridge next fall.

Wilkirson averaged 17 points, nine rebounds and four assists a game as a junior and was named to the All-Century League team. Manigo averaged 16 points, four rebounds and five assists a game and was named to the All-Bay Area team.

Manigo is the second Riordan player to sign with Northridge in the past year, joining Percy Fisher, a freshman forward. JOHN ORTEGA
