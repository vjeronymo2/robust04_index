940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / Arts (Openings): London theatre The Rattigan Festival continues. After recent revivals of such plays by Terence Rattigan as 'The Deep Blue Sea', 'Separate Tables' and 'The Browning Version', his famous drama 'The Winslow Boy' returns to the West End this week. Peter Barkworth takes the role played by Robert Donat in the movie version, Nyree Dawn Porter co-stars; Wyn Jones directs.
