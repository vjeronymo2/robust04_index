March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CINEMA SOUNDS

Sound effects editors do more than just "gather" effects. Today's sound-conscious directors demand the "sound that no one has heard before."

To service that demand, many sound editors have become sound designers, generating and pre-mixing special sound effects as well as supervising and organizing the thousands of "normal" effects.

Some of our craftspeople, the Foley artists, perform sound effects, "live" to filmed picture on a specialized recording stage, and their contributions go unrecognized.

DAVID STONE, Editor, VANESSA AMENT, Publisher, Moviesound Newsletter
