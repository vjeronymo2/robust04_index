921217 FT 17 DEC 92 / Tax switch for insurance offices THE GOVERNMENT will change the way it taxes UK branches of overseas life insurance offices, Mr Stephen Dorrell, financial secretary to the Treasury, said yesterday. Mr Dorrell said in a Commons written answer that the 1993 Finance Bill would include amendments updating rules that have hardly changed since the First World War. There are relatively few such overseas branches, but the Inland Revenue said their share of the UK life insurance market was significant.
