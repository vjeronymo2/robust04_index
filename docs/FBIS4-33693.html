BFN [Text] Claudette Werleigh, the Aristide government's foreign minister, considers the latest OAS General Assembly measure a very firm message to the de facto authorities in Haiti. The 34 OAS foreign ministers adopted a resolution on an embargo on commercial flights to and from Haiti and on freezing the financial assets of members of the Haitian provisional government: [Begin Werleigh recording, in Creole] This is why they mention in the resolution....[pauses] One of the last points involves working toward professionalizing the Army so that we can have an Army that protects the territory and helps us form the new police force provided for in our Constitution rather than one that tortures people, kills, and destroys. [end recording]
