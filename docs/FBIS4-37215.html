BFN [Text] Israel has so far prevented Dr. Samir Ghawshah, the official in charge of the labor portfolio in the Palestinian Authority; Fatah Central Committee member Abu-Mahir Ghunaym; and poet Mahmud Darwish from entering the autonomy area. Our correspondent Moti Amir has learned this from Palestinian sources. Fadil Tahbub, member of the Popular Struggle Front, told our correspondent that Dr. Ghawshah, leader of the front, seeks to enter the autonomy area in order to turn his organization into a political party which will be able to run in the autonomy elections. [Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 0800 GMT on 20 June adds: "A senior security source told our correspondent Moti Amir that nobody is preventing Samir Ghawshah from entering the autonomy area. The source denied the Palestinian sources' claims that Israel is preventing Dr. Ghawshah from entering the autonomy." ["Dr. Ghawshah told our correspondent that the PLO has submitted a list of names of approximately 500 PLO members who are slated to arrive in the autonomy, including approximately one-third of the Fatah Central Committee members in Tunis. So far, Israel has not agreed to their request to enter the autonomy."]
