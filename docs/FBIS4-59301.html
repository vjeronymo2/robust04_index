BFN [Text] Tehran, May 11, IRNA -- The defence minister of Zimbabwe Mowoon Mahaji in his meeting with Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati here Tuesday, after describing the situation of some African countries that are involved in war called for Iran's support for establishing peace and stability in Africa. The foreign minister emphasizing the importance of peace and stability in Africa declared the preparedness of Islamic Republic of Iran for bringing peaceful means to fruitation [as received] in this region.
