Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Mikhail Shevtsov] [Text] Moscow, 11 Feb -- An Abkhazian Defense Ministry report received here today says the liquidation of Georgian subversive groups operation in Galskiy Rayon has been completed. During the operation 10 bandit formations and two subversive camps were destroyed, a depot of ammunition and various types of small arms, grenade launchers, artillery systems, a tank, and an infantry fighting vehicle were seized. According to the same source, at the present time Abkhazian armed formations are carrying out measures to strengthen the border along the Inguri River and intensify the guarding of the Inguri hydroelectric power station installations. Combat engineer units of the Abkhazian Armed Forces are clearing the territory of mines. Also today, the Abkhazian Supreme Soviet Chairman Vladislav Ardzinba met the leader of the UN mission in Abkhazia and members of the planning group under the United Nations. The deployment of the peacemaking forces along the confrontation line on the Inguri River and refugees' problems were discussed.
