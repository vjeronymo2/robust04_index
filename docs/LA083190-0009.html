August 31, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

FUROR OVER NEW LITERATURE TEXTBOOKS

The debate in Yucaipa over the "Impressions" reading series brings to mind another book that has long been banned in our public schools.

It is a compilation of stories containing numerous descriptions of murder, torture, genocide, rape, bestiality, adultery, incest, homosexuality, group sex and devil worship. Ironically, it is still found in most church-run schools. It is, of course, the Bible.

STEVE MILLS

Glendale
