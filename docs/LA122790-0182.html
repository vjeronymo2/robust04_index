December 27, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: FULLERTON;

3 CITY EMPLOYEES WIN YEARLY HONORS

The city has named three people as Employees and Manager of the Year.

The 1990 honorees were personnel assistant Kyoko Nishitsuji, named General Government Employee of the Year; Fullerton Police Officer John Minn, named Safety Employee of the Year; and Verleen Sparks, police records supervisor, named Manager of the Year.

Each winner received a check for $350 and their names have been inscribed on a plaque in the lobby of Fullerton City Hall.
