920226 FT 26 FEB 92 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and cotton JUTE February/March: c and f Dundee; BTC Dollars 415, BWC n/o, BTD Dollars 390, BWD n/o; c and f Antwerp; BTC Dollars 390, BWC Dollars 390, BTD Dollars 360, BWD Dollars 360. COTTON Liverpool - Spot and shipment sales amounted to 149 tonnes for the week ending 21 February, compared with 323 in the previous week. Subdued off take did not bring many operations. Support was forthcoming in certain specialist styles notably Spanish and American.
