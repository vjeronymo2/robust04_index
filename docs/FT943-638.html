940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Paramount higher Paramount, the public house operator, raised pre-tax profits by 17 per cent from Pounds 450,000 to Pounds 527,000 in the year ended May 31. Turnover was Pounds 1m higher at Pounds 3.56m. Fully diluted earnings per share came to 0.6p (0.43p) and the final dividend has been stepped up from 0.15p to 0.2p, on capital increased by June's Pounds 5.4m rights issue.
