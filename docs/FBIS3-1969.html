Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shenzhen, March 3 (XINHUA) -- Shenzhen City, one of China's special economic zones, is to build an auto manufacturing center, according to local government sources. The center will aim to put new life into the city's auto industry, and thus promote related industries. In the government plan, by the year 2000 auto manufacturing will become one of the city's pillar industries, with an output of ten billion yuan, accounting for ten percent of Shenzhen's total industrial output that year. In its preliminary efforts, the city government will invest 150 million yuan (about 17.2 million U.S. dollars) in the construction of the center.
