BFN [Text] Emomali Rakhmonov, head of state and chairman of the Tajikistan Supreme Soviet, left today for St. Petersburg to attend a session of the CIS interparliamentary assembly. Emomali Rakhmonov was seen off at Dushanbe Airport by Abdujalil Samadov, chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan; Koimdodov, deputy chairman of the Republic's Supreme Soviet; the chairmen of the Supreme Soviet committees; the deputy chairmen of the Council of Ministers; and Senkevich, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the Russian Federation to Tajikistan.
