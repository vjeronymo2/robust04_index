BFN [Text] Cairo, May 4 KYODO -- Japanese Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa left Cairo Wednesday [4 May] for Israel after meeting with Egyptian President Husni Mubarak and PLO Leader Yasir 'Arafat and attending the signing ceremony of a historic agreement on limited Palestinian self-rule for Gaza and Jericho. After Egypt and Israel, Kakizawa will visit Jordan, Syria and the occupied territories.
