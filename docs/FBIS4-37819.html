BFN [Report from Nicosia] [Excerpt] Authorities of [Saudi King] Fahd launched a wide arrest campaign against elements opposed to the policy of al-Sa'ud over the past two weeks. Travelers from Jeddah said the arrests, carried out by intelligence elements of Fahd's regime, included a large number of citizens from the Kingdom's southern areas. Those arrested include religious men and tribal chieftains who refuse the regime's interference in Yemen's internal affairs and demand preserving the unity of the two parts of Yemen. [passage omitted]
