BFN [Excerpt] The spiral of terrorism continues. The security services announced today that two citizens have been assassinated by two terrorists in the provinces of Boumerdes and Tiaret. The imam of the Baghlia mosque in Boumerdes province was stabbed to death; his body was found near an elementary school in Bourj Menial municipality.
