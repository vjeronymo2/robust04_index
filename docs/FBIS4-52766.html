BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, May 12 (OANA-BERNAMA) -- A total of 104 Vietnamese illegal immigrants from the Sungai Besi temporary camp returned home yesterday under the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) voluntary repatriation (Volrep) programme. Federal Task Force principal assistant director (settlement) Kamarulzaman Murad said all those who returned home had not been accorded refugee status after screening. A total of 3,054 Vietnamese illegal mmigrants had voluntarily returned home since the programme was started three years ago. Kamarul said there were some 6,820 boat people at the Sungai Besi camp and those who were not repatriated after the expiry of the Volrep programme next year would be sent home in stages before 1996. Those who returned home under the programme could resettle in third countries like the United States and Canada under the orderly departure programme (ODP) which is only available in Vietnam.
