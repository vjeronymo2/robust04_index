BFN [Text] Sanaa, 17 Jun (INA)--The Yemeni security forces have arrested a saboteur whom the Saudi authorities sent to carry out acts of sabotage in Yemen as part of the Saudi conspiracy against Yemeni unity and the Saudi support for the secessionists in southern Yemen. An informed Yemeni security source told an Iraqi News Agency correspondent in Sanaa today that the security forces last night arrested one of the Saudi regime's agents after he blew up a house in the area of Bajil, approximately 50 km east of the eastern al-Hudaydah Governorate. The source explained that the explosion killed one citizen and wounded seven others. He also revealed that the security authorities arrested three other saboteurs who confessed they were recruited by the Saudi authorities to carry out bombings in Yemen. They were arrested after their recent arrival from Saudi Arabia, he added.
