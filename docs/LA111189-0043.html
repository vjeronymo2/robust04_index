November 11, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

ALPHA LEAGUE

Western Christian 14, L.A. Baptist 7 -- A 70-yard drive and L. A. Baptist's chances for an automatic playoff bid ended when tailback Colyn Van Buren's pass was intercepted by Terry Noice at Western Christian's two-yard line with a minute to play.

Playing for second place at Monroe High, Western Christian (5-5, 4-1) took an early lead as Jix Cortes scored on first-quarter runs of 39 and two yards. Cortes finished with 116 yards in 19 carries.

L. A. Baptist (7-3, 3-2) broke into Lancer territory only once in the first half, but a Chris Robinson pass was intercepted by Noice, ending its only threat. Midway through the third quarter, Dinari Williams' fumble was recovered by L. A. Baptist's Jeff Hampsten at the Lancers' 21-yard line. Six plays later, Van Buren ran one yard for a score.

L. A. Baptist had the ball on its two-yard line after a Lancer punt, then an 18-yard run by Dan Stepper and three consecutive completions by Robinson, including two for 51 yards to Kurt Rayners, put the ball on the Lancers' 28 before Van Buren's interception. ANDY MEYERS
