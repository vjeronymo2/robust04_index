930201 FT 01 FEB 93 / Nalgo increases its membership NALGO, the public services union, was one of the few large unions to grow last year, underlining the extent to which the public sector has become the stronghold of union membership. The latest membership figures show the union with 764,062 members, up 4,327 on 1991. In July Nalgo is to merge with the other public service unions Nupe and Cohse to form the 1.4m-strong Unison union.
