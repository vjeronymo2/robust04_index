SUMMARY: The Corporation for National and Community Service, gives notice under Public Law 92&hyph;463 (Federal Advisory Committee Act), that it will hold a meeting of the Civilian Community Corps (CCC) Advisory Board. The Board advises the Director of the CCC concerning the administration of the program and assists in the development and administration of the Corps. This meeting of the Board will discuss the progress to date and future direction of the program. The meeting will be open to the public up to the seating capacity of the room.
