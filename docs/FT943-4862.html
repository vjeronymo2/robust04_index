940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / Shop staff told to accept pay cuts Northern Ireland Electricity has called on staff in its Shop Electric high-street showrooms to accept new employment terms, including pay cuts averaging 6.1 per cent - up to 14 per cent for some - or face the loss of 400 jobs. To cushion the impact of the pay cuts, NIE Retail has offered full-time staff Pounds 8,750 in compensation and a phasing-in of the reduction for those worst hit.
