December 23, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

WHERE IS MR. SMITH?

As a registered, turned-off, almost election-ignoring voter, I got a breath of fresh air from "Where Is Mr. Smith?" It was wonderful to find that there are Americans who care about conditions in this country (including Brownstein and Easton), who have some power to change things.

MARIAN C. DUFFETT

Los Angeles
