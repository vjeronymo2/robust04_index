930212 FT 12 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Gen Consolidated net asset value rises Net asset value per capital share of General Consolidated Investment Trust amounted to 182p at December 31 compared with 155.6p a year earlier. Earnings per income share dipped to 9.56p (10.7p). The final dividend is reduced to 1.71p (2.75p), making a total of 9.37p (10.41p).
