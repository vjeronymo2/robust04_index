Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A comprehensive plan to tackle South Africa's spiraling political violence should be completed within days. This, according to ANC [African National Congress] leader Nelson Mandela. The plan, put together by South Africa's main political players, would not immediately ease the plight of people living in violence-ridden townships. [Begin Mandela recording] We should not have exaggerated expectations. We are putting that plan this week, but it's going to take some time before all the interested parties are able to put an end to the violence, and to ensure that people resume normal life. [end recording]
