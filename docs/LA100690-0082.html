October 6, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

IF JACK CLARK RETURNS, AT LEAST ONE FAN WON'T

I just read today's article on Tony Gwynn, and I have to say Jack Clark is a lost cause! He's a bad example of a team leader.

First of all, we pay him the big bucks to throw bases around. Secondly, it seems he's always sick or hurt. For one who complains a lot, if he doesn't like it, he can leave. At least Tony Gwynn earns his money.

JIM SKERTICH, San Diego
