BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, 28 June (OANA/BERNAMA) -- Malaysian Housing and Local Government Minister Dr. Ting Chiew Peh led an 11-member delegation on a one-week official visit to Iran. The objective of the visit which began Monday, was to promote closer cooperation between Malaysia and Iran in the field of housing, urban planning and construction. The agenda of the visit includes a meeting between Dr. Ting and the Iranian Minister of Housing and Urban Development Engineer Akhundi.
