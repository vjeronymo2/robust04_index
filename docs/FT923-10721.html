920729 FT 29 JUL 92 / International Company News: New Zealand Post plunges into red NEW Zealand Post, the national postal-service operator hit by heavy restructuring costs, yesterday reported attributable losses of NZDollars 2.2m for the year to March 31, compared with profits of NZDollars 30.03m (USDollars 16.4m) a year earlier. The company, whose directors expect it to be offered for sale early in 1993, reported net profits of NZDollars 5.37m down from NZDollars 27.46m, after restructuring costs of NZDollars 42.69m.
