940207 FT 07 FEB 94 / Warning on lack of young workers Older workers will still be needed as the 'demographic time bomb' of a shortage of young workers has not disappeared, says Mr Peter Warr, of Sheffield University's psychology department. He says in The Human Resource Management Journal that the recession has hidden the decline in the number of people aged under 35 who will be in the labour force over the next 12 years.
