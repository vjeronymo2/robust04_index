Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Minister Shim'on Shetrit says there is a solid majority in the Labor Party for the inclusion of [Tzomet Party leader] Rafa'el Etan in the government. According to him, Yitzhaq Rabin and Shim'on Peres are in favor of this. It became clear to him in talks with the party leaders that the majority of them support the inclusion of Tzomet on the basis of the government's basic guidelines and Tzomet's conditions, which are consistent with the government's basic guidelines. This is reported by our parties affairs correspondent Shmu'el Tal.
