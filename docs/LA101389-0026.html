October 13, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

ON THE RECORD

"They are depicting Arabs in a very derogatory manner -- as conniving, with hook noses, as ugly -- and this image has been haunting the Arab community."

-- Terri Ahwal, board member of the Detroit Arab-American Anti-Discrimination Committee, explaining the organization's boycott of Spencer Gifts Inc., a retail chain that is selling Halloween masks that look like PLO leader Yasser Arafat.
