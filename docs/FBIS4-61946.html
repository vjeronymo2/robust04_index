BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast: Report over video of operations at missile silos; figures in brackets denote broadcast time in GMT in hours, minutes, and seconds] [Text] The planned evacuation of nuclear munitions [yadernyye boyepripasy] from Ukrainian territory to Russia is continuing. To date, 180 units of nuclear munitions have been taken out of the country. They will be destroyed at Russian plants in accordance with the trilateral agreement signed by Russia, Ukraine, and the United States.
