November 17, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

TRAMMELL SPARKS RANCHO ALAMITOS

Ulysses Trammell rushed for 292 yards and three touchdowns in 45 carries to lead Rancho Alamitos to a 32-0 victory over Pomona in the first round of the Division VI playoffs at Bonita High School in La Verne.

Rancho Alamitos (9-2) advanced to the second round against Brea-Olinda, a 24-17 winner over Corona del Mar Friday night. Pomona ended the season 5-6.
