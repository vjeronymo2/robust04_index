Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] [Announcer] The first 102 German blue helmets left Somalia this morning without incident. In Mogadishu harbor, the Bundeswehr soldiers boarded the German frigate Cologne, heading for Mombasa in the neighboring country of Kenya. From there, the German UN soldiers are to start their flight home on 15 February. The Cologne arrived in Mogadishu under the strictest security provisions. The military leadership said that the reason for this was that terrorist attacks could not be ruled out.
