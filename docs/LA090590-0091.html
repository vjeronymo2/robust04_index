September 5, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Ex-Disney Exec Pleads Guilty: A former Walt Disney Co. executive pleaded guilty to tax evasion charges for taking tens of thousands of dollars and a gold Rolex watch as kickbacks from foreign souvenir suppliers. Geoffrey G. Gleed, 52, of Anaheim, ex-vice president of purchasing for Disney, pleaded guilty to two counts of tax evasion for not declaring money he received from Kanzo Itoh & Co. of Japan and Kutex International Ltd. of Hong Kong, prosecutors said.
