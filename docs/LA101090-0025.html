October 10, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

New Role for Gene Siskel: Will the audience give Gene Siskel, half of the movie-reviewing duo of Siskel & Ebert, a thumbs up for his first interview of a celebrity guest on the pair's syndicated show? Siskel will talk to composer-producer Quincy Jones about composing for films on the show that airs this weekend. Selected filmmakers will be interviewed on the show during the course of the season. CLAUDIA PUIG
