Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Managua, 19 Mar (DPA) -- The 3-80 Northern Front, the largest group of armed rebels in Nicaragua since the disarmament of the Nicaraguan Resistance (contras), announced today after a series of agreements were reached with President Violeta Chamorro's government it will continue the disarmament process. Jose Angel Talavera (El Chacal), the rebels' leader, said in Managua next week his men, estimated at more than 200, will continue to turn in their weapons in the Quilali region, approximately 320 km north of Managua. The armed group suspended the disarmament process last week, fearing the government would not meet its commitments. Talavera said he hopes this time the government will meet its commitment with the demobilized, adding on previous occasions it had not. The government offered a series of social benefits for the contras' disarmament; nevertheless, there are constant protests against the government's lack of fulfillment.
