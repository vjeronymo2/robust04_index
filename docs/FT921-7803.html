920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / Business quality award proposed A PROPOSED National Quality Award to further British companies' commitment to quality was announced yesterday by Mr Peter Lilley, trade and industry secretary. Sir Denys Henderson, Imperial Chemical Industries chairman, will head a steering group to study the idea and report on how the award, which is due to start next year, could help raise the performance of British business. 'We must aim to make the label 'Made in Britain' a symbol of quality again,' Mr Lilley told Confederation of British Industry members in Gateshead.
