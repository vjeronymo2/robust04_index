BFN [From "The Voz Andes Newscast"] [Excerpt] Union leaders reported that at least five IESS employees were injured in a clash with police when they were protesting government plans to privatize the IESS. A union leader said the injuries were inflicted by members of the security forces during an act of brutal repression in which tear gas and police sticks were used. I want to express my strong condemnation of this criminal repression, the leader said. Five of our fellow workers were injured solely for the crime of defending social security, he added.
