930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / International Company News: Changes at the top for SocGen SOCIETE Generale, one of France's largest banks, has shuffled its senior management by making Mr Daniel Bouton, 42, chief executive, succeeding Mr Jean-Paul Delacour, 62, who becomes deputy chairman. Mr Bouton joined Societe Generale two years ago from the finance ministry, where he held senior roles under socialist and conservative governments. His previous position at the bank was as director in charge of special projects.
