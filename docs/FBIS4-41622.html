BFN [Text] Kiev -- To the question from a UNIAN correspondent: "What will you do if you find out that you have been elected president of Ukraine or that you have gone through to the second round of the election," Leonid Kuchma, a candidate for president of Ukraine, noted: "If I am not elected, I would cross myself with happiness." In the event of his election he would take his first steps in the economy because "the point from which one should begin is to bring order to one's home." With regard to possible cooperation with Vitaliy Masol, prime minister of Ukraine, if he is elected president, Leonid Kuchma replied that this is a question to which he does not yet know the answer at the moment.
