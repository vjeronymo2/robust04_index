BFN [Announcer-read report; from the "Aty-Baty" program] [Text] Leaflets appeared in the city of Murmansk-150, which is the home for the first submarine flotilla of the Northern Fleet. They call on officers and warrant officers to carry out a joint protest act against the delays of pay and unsatisfactory supply of clothing and related gear. The leaflets propose to block the road leading from living quarters to the submarine base.
