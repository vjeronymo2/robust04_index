BFN [Text] Brother Rashid Bin-'Abdallah, foreign minister of the United Arab Emirates [UAE], and Safwat al-Sharif, Egyptian information minister, arrived in Sanaa today on a visit to our country. They will meet with brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', Egyptian Information Minister Safwat al-Sharif said he and his brother, Rashid Bin-'Abdallah, are carrying a joint message from President Mubarak and Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan to bring Yemeni views closer so Yemen will remain within the Arab fold. The letter also represents joint Arab efforts. The joint delegation was received by brother Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani, minister of planning and development; Hasan al-Lawzi, minister of information; and the Egyptian and UAE ambassador in Sanaa.
