BFN [From the NTV newscast] [Text] Wu Banggou, CPC Politburo member and CPC secretary general in Shanghai, appeared to be very happy with his visit to Cuba, which continued this afternoon in Matanzas Province. Wu Banggou and his delegation were accompanied by Politburo member Yadira Garcia, first secretary of the party in Matanzas Province, on a tour during which they learned of the developments in the tourist and oil industry programs. At the Melia Varadero Hotel, the delegation was informed of the profits made by this and other joint capital enterprises, as well as the basis and characteristics for this type of business. Cuba's largest producing oil field lies near Varadero Beach. The oil field's workers have an annual production goal of 930,000 tons, nearly 75 percent of Cuba's production goal. Work is aimed at surpassing this goal. Wu Banggou was informed of the joint operations with Canadian and French companies and the progress in the program designed to increase production and explore the possibilities of new drilling areas. In an interview for this newscast, Wu Banggou stressed the need to strengthen economic cooperation between Cuba and the PRC even more.
