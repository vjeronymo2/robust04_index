BFN [Text] The Democratic Republic of Yemen has informed the UN secretary general, Dr. Butrus-Ghali, of the latest developments in the situation in our country, and the dangers of the escalation resorted to by the Sanaa ruling regime represented in striking the cities and the residential quarters, especially Aden. The Democratic Republic of Yemen appealed to Dr. Butrus-Ghali to submit these developments to the security council. This was stated by 'Abdallah 'Abd-al-Majid al-Asnaj, deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs. He said that the Democratic Republic of Yemen also demanded the UN Security Council to treat 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and his regime as war criminals, confirming that our country was moving at the political and diplomatic levels to stop the war.
