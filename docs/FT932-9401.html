930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / World News in Brief: Gold price soars to 17-month high Frenzied buying re-ignited the fire started recently in the gold market by high-profile investors Sir James Goldsmith and George Soros. The price soared in London by nearly 3.5 per cent to Dollars 369.15 a troy ounce, reaching its highest level for 17 months. Gold started the year at a seven-year low of Dollars 327 an ounce.
