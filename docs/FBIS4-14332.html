BFN [Poll by the Dafah Institute headed by Dr. Mina Zemah] [Text] The prime minister said: "Peace is more important for Israel's future and security than this or that group of settlements." Do you agree or disagree with that statement? Agree: 51 percent; Disagree: 45 percent; No comment: 4 percent. The prime minister continued: "I supported and will continue to support the removal of Golan settlements for the sake of peace." Do you agree or disagree with that position? Agree: 39 percent; Disagree: 58 percent; No comment: 3 percent. In your opinion, should the prime minister have made those remarks in public? Should have: 50 percent; Should not have: 47 percent; No comment: 3 percent. The poll was conducted on Tuesday and Wednesday among 547 interviewees, which make up a representative example of the adult population. Maximum margin of error is 4 percent.
