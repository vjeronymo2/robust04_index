BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Romanov] [Text] Beijing, 13 Jun -- During a visit to Moscow at the end of June by Qian Qichen, foreign minister and vice premier of the State Council of the PRC, an agreement between Russia and the PRC relating to the western section of the border between Russia and China will be initialled. This was agreed upon during talks between a joint delegation representing the Governments of the Russian Federation, the Republics of Kazakhstan, Kirghizia [Kyrgyzstan], and Tajikistan and a government delegation from the PRC, at the fifth round of meetings of the working group preparing draft agreements on the border, which ended today. The working group also discussed issues relating to preparing border agreements between China, Kirghizia, and Tajikistan.
