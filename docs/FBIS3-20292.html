Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- The FRG Government has explicitly welcomed the fact that all the partners have agreed to the proposal on the question of a blocking minority in an enlarged European Union [EU]. "The adoption of this compromise proposal now clears the road for the planned enlargement of the EU by Austria, Sweden, Finland, and Norway on 1 January 1995 from the point of view of the 12 member states," it is stated in a statement released by Deputy Government Spokesman Norbert Schaefer in Bonn on Tuesday [29 March].
