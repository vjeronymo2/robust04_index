940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Exceptional gain cuts Prospect loss to Pounds 0.5m Prospect Industries, the specialist engineer, reported a lower seasonal loss of Pounds 489,000 pre-tax for the six months to March 31, against Pounds 1.24m. The result was helped by an exceptional gain of Pounds 736,000 arising from the sale of a loan note. Mr Philip Wilbraham, chairman, said the company continued to operate in an aggressive and competitive environment. However, it had won a record number of orders, including increased work overseas. He added: 'At this stage of the year the future looks encouraging.' Taking into account the outlook the interim dividend is raised from 0.275p to 0.29p. Losses per share were down at 0.08p (0.56p) or 0.05p (0.43p) fully diluted. Turnover slipped to Pounds 21.4m against Pounds 22.1m including Pounds 594,000 from discontinued activities. The operating loss from continuing activities was lower at Pounds 1.01m (Pounds 1.03m).
