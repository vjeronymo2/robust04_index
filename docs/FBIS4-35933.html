BFN [Text] Sanaa has announced that it will delay its decision to accept a cease-fire, as demanded by the United Nations, as a result of Saudi statements that the Arabian Gulf states may move if battles in Yemen continue. This was announced by a northern official, who said verbatim that Sanaa was about to officially inform the UN secretary general of its agreement on a cease-fire, but that Saudi Foreign Minister Sa'ud al-Faysal's statements delayed that announcement.
