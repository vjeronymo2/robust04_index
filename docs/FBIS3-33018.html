Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Morning Information Review" newscast] [Text] Canadian lawmakers from Ontario Province have emphasized their intention to help our country in sectors like public health, education, and transportation during a meeting with Reynaldo Endis, president of the Santiago de Cuba Provincial Assembly. The Canadians voiced their willingness to conduct in their country a broad campaign of solidarity with Cuba. They also expressed their admiration of Cuba's achievements in different areas and efforts by the people to maintain and defend the gains of the revolution. The Canadian lawmakers will conclude their tour of the eastern province of Santiago de Cuba after visiting places of economic and social interest.
