920729 FT 29 JUL 92 / International Company News: Strong June sales lift Eastman Kodak STRONG JUNE sales were behind a small improvement in second-quarter profits at Eastman Kodak, the US photographic products group, agencies report from Rochester. Net income amounted to Dollars 361m or Dollars 1.11 a share, against Dollars 357m or Dollars 1.10 in the same period last year. This brought net income for the first half to Dollars 506m or Dollars 1.56 a share, against Dollars 535m or Dollars 1.65 a year earlier. Fully diluted earnings per share were Dollars 1.51 against Dollars 1.64 for the half-year and Dollars 1.06 against Dollars 1.09 for the latest quarter. First-half sales totalled Dollars 975bn against Dollars 9.52bn last year with the second quarter generating Dollars 5.27bn, compared with Dollars 5.03bn the same period a year earlier. Mr Kay Whitmore, chairman, said: 'The results we delivered for the first half were consistent with our 1992 operating plan and this gives us confidence that the year as a whole will deliver what we expect: moderately higher sales and good gains in earnings,'.
