930712 FT 12 JUL 93 / Clerics join Saudi cabinet Saudi Arabia's King Fahd has made two senior Moslem clerics cabinet ministers and ordered the setting up of an Islamic guidance ministry, Reuter reports from Dubai. Sheikh Abdel Aziz Bin Abdullah Bin Baz, a member of the influential Higher Council of Ulema, was named president of the council, a position that will now carry ministerial rank. The decree also named Sheikh Bin Baz as the General Mufti of Saudi Arabia, the country's most senior religious post.
