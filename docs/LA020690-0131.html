February 6, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

LA QUINTA GETS FIRST VICTORY OF SEASON

La Quinta High School won its first boys' basketball game of the season Monday night, defeating Bolsa Grande, 73-68, in an overtime Garden Grove League game at La Quinta.

La Quinta's Lloyd Bennett tied the score with a jump shot with about 18 seconds remaining in regulation.

Justin Sogoian had 27 points and 12 rebounds for La Quinta (1-19 overall and 1-11 in the Garden Grove League), which was the only winless team in the county.

"We've played well enough to win quite a few games and finally had a couple of things fall our way," La Quinta Coach Jim Perry said.

Hinh Bui scored 22 points and Sy Sayarath scored 21 for Bolsa Grande (7-12, 5-7).
