Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 15 Feb (TANJUG) -- There will be no withdrawal of Serb artillery, Colonel General Ratko Mladic, chief of the general staff of the army of the Srpska republic, said today following his meeting with UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] commanders in Lukavica. "We are not going to leave our people without protection. They have always lived in this area. We have no right to do such a thing and nothing can force us to leave our people at the mercy of fanatical Muslim units." Mladic talked today to Generals Jean Cot and Michael Rose, UNPROFOR commanders for the former Yugoslavia and ex-Bosnia-Herzegovina. Vitaliy Churkin, Russia's deputy minister for foreign affairs, attended part of this meeting.
