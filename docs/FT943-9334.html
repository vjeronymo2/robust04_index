940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / Further 207 jobs shed at Swans A further 207 job losses were yesterday announced at Swan Hunter, the Tyneside shipbuilder, The redundancies affect workers laid off since May 31. The receivers injected a new urgency to the struggle to find a buyer for the company by insisting that unless Constructions Mecaniques de Normandie - the sole prospective bidder for the yard as a going concern - starts paying the Pounds 200,000-a-month wage bill of the technical and design team by the end of August, the team's 100 members will be made redundant. Mr Fred Henderson of CMN said that if the design team went there was no prospect of CMN buying Swans. 'There's no possibility of getting a workload then,' he added.
