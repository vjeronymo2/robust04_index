941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / International Company News: Daewoo plans stake in Indian firm Daewoo Corporation of South Korea is planning to take an equity stake in a finance company floated by DCM of India, with which it has a joint venture to manufacture cars, a senior official of the local company said on Wednesday, Reuter reports from New Delhi. The official said the South Korean company was keen to take a stake in DCM Financial Services, through its Daewoo Securities offshoot, to finance the sale of cars to be manufactured in India.
