BFN [Unattributed report] [Text] Ukraine's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary in the Polish Republic H. Udovenko handed in the letter from Ukraine's foreign minister A. Zlenko on Ukraine's position regarding the recent events in the Crimea to Polish deputy foreign minister I. Byczewski. I. Byczewski showed understanding of Ukraine's position and pointed out that the situation in the Crimea must only be resolved by peaceful means. He also stressed that Poland has been consistently pursuing the policy of support for the strengthening of Ukrainian independence and its integration into European and world structures.
