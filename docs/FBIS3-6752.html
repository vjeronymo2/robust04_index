Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 18 (KNS-KCNA) [dateline as received] -- Ho Chong-man, chief vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon), gave a lecture before officials of the Japan Social-Democratic Party on March 16 at a request of the party. Present on the occasion were Secretary General Wataru Kubo and Vice-Chairman and concurrently International Department Director Issei Inoue of the party, Chairman of the Japan-Korea committee of the party and member of the House of Representatives Yuzuru Shimazaki and other Social-Democratic members of the House of Representatives and of the House of Councillors. In his lecture the chief vice-chairman elaborated on the greatness of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il and on the successful socialist construction in Korea under the wise guidance of the dear leader. He said the DPRK would, in the future, too, adhere to the spirit of the three-party joint declaration on DPRK-Japan relations which was adopted between the Workers' Party of Korea, the Japan Liberal-Democratic Party and the Japan Social-Democratic Party in September 1990.
