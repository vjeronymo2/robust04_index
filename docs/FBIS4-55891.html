The majority party proposes the speaker of the chamber. Two deputy speakers are elected from the ranks of opposition parties and one from the majority party, at either the ruling HDZ's [Croatian Democratic Party] or opposition's proposal. The first deputy speaker is proposed by the opposition. [passage omitted] After Josip Manolic was relieved of duty by a majority vote, Professor Katica Ivanisevic of the HDZ was unanimously elected speaker of the chamber. [passage omitted]
