Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Scalfaro Takes Week's Sick Leave"] [Text] Rome, 2 Feb (ANSA) -- It was confirmed this morning that President Oscar Luigi Scalfaro has been forced to take a week off duty by an attack of "acute bronchitis with a high fever." Scalfaro was rumoured to be sick when he cancelled official engagements on Monday [31 January] and Tuesday; doctors have now extended his sick leave until the end of the week.
