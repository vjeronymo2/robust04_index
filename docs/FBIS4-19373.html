BFN [Text] Regarding yesterday's incident with the small Greek ship that is being held by the Albanian authorities at Sarande, Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias said that, as in the past, a solution will be found through diplomatic means. Vice Consul Vasilios Bournovas from the Greek consulate at Gjirokaster today visited the captain of the Greek ship. Prosecutor Khristos Milos, who is in charge of the case, has informed the vice-consul that those arrested will be detained until Monday [18 April], when he hopes that they will be freed.
