941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Johannesburg extended recent losses in slow, lacklustre trade as the market remained under pressure from a soft bullion price and reduced speculation about an imminent scrapping of the financial rand. The gold shares index declined 36 to 2,330, industrials finished the day unchanged at 6,271 and the overall index shed 21 to 5,595.
