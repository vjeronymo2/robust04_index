BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 27 Apr (NA) -- Another arrest was made at midnight by the Buenos Aires Province Police in the investigation San Isidro Federal Judge Roberto Marquevich is carrying out into the discovery of a powerful arms cache on an island in the Tigre District. According to court sources, the arrested person has been identified as Daniel Sinaglia and, as in the case of Alejandro Sucksdorf, he carried out intelligence activities for the Army. Sinaglia's arrest was made at midnight in the town of San Fernando, according to sources close to Judge Marquevich.
