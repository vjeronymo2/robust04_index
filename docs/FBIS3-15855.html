Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riga, Mar 08, BNS - The Latvian Navy plans to participate in NATO's Baltic Sea exercise, Baltops 94, in June, Cmdr. Gaidis Zeibots told BNS. The training will be attended by 40 ships from the Baltic Sea countries and warships from the Netherlands and Belgium. Latvia will send its M-02 deminer [mine sweeper] to the maneuvers. The Germany-presented vessel, however, needs to be duly equipped and armed by then.
