BFN [Text] Vienna, May 5, IRNA--Iran's minister of heavy industries, Hadi Nejad-Hoseynian, concluding his 4-day official visit to the Czech Republic, signed with Czech trade and industry minister, Vladimir Dlouhy, in Prague Thursday a memorandum of understanding on bilateral cooperation in trade, banking, technical, industrial, agriculture and transportation. During his stay in the Czech Republic, Nejad-Hoseynian held talks with Czech Prime Minister Vaclav Klaus, Deputy Prime Minister and Agriculture Minister Josef Lux, Transport Minister Jan Strasky, Privatization Minister Jan Skalicky, and Trade Minister Karel Dyba. Topics of Nejad-Hoseynian's discussions with Czech officials were matters related to expansion of trade, as well as bilateral relations.
