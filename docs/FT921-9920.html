920208 FT 08 FEB 92 / Benefits boost for bakery pensioners PENSIONERS of Jacobs Bakery will received enhanced benefits out of a Pounds 35m pension fund surplus, the TGWU general union said yesterday. The company was acquired in 1989 by BSN, the food multinational, from RJR Nabisco, the US group. The High Court has endorsed an agreement which will mean pension fund members will receive 72.5 per cent of the surplus, said the union.
