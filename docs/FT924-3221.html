921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / Pounds 35m grant for Manchester arena THE government yesterday announced the largest city grant for a single project - Pounds 35.5m towards an indoor arena as part of Manchester's bid to stage the 2000 Olympic games. The Pounds 50m arena will be part of a Pounds 200m redevelopment of Manchester's Victoria Station by Vector Investments, part of the Bovis construction group.
