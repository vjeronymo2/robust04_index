Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Peruvian economic developments through 22 February: Through a decree published in the official government gazette, the Energy and Mines Ministry has officially outlined its agreement to guarantee and promote investments. The agreement's eighth clause indicates that government guarantees will cover 15 years, starting from the date of crediting the investment. Such investments must be approved by the National Mining Directorate. (Lima Radio Programas del Peru Network in Spanish 1800 GMT 9 Feb 94) The Central Reserve Bank has reported that Peru's net international reserves fluctuate between $1.7 and $1.8 billion. Those figures represent the highest reserve level in recent years, bank officials added. (Lima Global de Television Network in Spanish 0100 GMT 16 Feb 94)
