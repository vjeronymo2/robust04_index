941128 FT 28 NOV 94 / Chairman died of shotgun wounds A company chairman whose death was announced last week died of shotgun wounds, it was disclosed last night. Mr Nigel Openshaw, chairman of engineering and property group McLeod Russel Holdings, had been arrested for drink driving on Wednesday and held overnight in cells. He returned to his home in Preston Candover, Hampshire. Police later found his body with a head wound. A shotgun was lying nearby.
