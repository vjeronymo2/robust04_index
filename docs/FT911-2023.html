910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Vaux Vaux, the Sunderland-based brewing and hotels group, is to buy 43 pubs from Courage. The Pounds 4m deal brings the total number of pubs Vaux has acquired over the past 18 months to 119. The Courage pubs, which are all tenanted, trade under the John Smiths name and are mainly in Yorkshire, Lancashire, Nottinghamshire and Derbyshire.
