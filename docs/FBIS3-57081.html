Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Ivan Rybkin Supports the Idea of Amnesty"] [Text] State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin supports the idea of amnestying the protagonists in the 19-21 August 1991 and September-October 1993 events in Moscow "with the exception of those who have committed an obvious criminal act." Mr. Rybkin is convinced that "this would be a necessary prerequisite for civil peace and accord."
