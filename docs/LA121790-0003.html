December 17, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

FRANCISCO GABILONDO SOLER; SONGWRITER KNOWN AS 'CRI-CRI'

Francisco Gabilondo Soler, 83, the children's songwriter known as "Cri-Cri" whose compositions have been sung throughout the Spanish-speaking world. Gabilondo Soler wrote and performed hundreds of songs that captivated Mexican radio audiences for years and made his nickname -- from a child's word for cricket -- a household word throughout his country. In Mexico City on Friday of a heart attack.
