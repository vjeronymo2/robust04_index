Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Mexico City, 18 Feb (NOTIMEX) -- Mexican Foreign Relations Secretary Manuel Tello and OAS Secretary General Joao Baena Soares today declared the adjournment of the 20th OAS special session. The meeting, which began here yesterday, was originally scheduled to close tomorrow following six plenary meetings, but the session's conclusion was unexpectedly moved forward. During the closure announcement, information concerning an agreement entitled "Framework of Policies and Priorities for Unified Cooperation for Development" was released, which was signed by the 34 countries attending the session. There was no official explanation on why the agenda was changed, which reliable sources attributed only to a "time adjustment."
