921023 FT 23 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Cradley improves 30% to Pounds 1.05m Despite the demise of some customers and reduced size of several magazines, Cradley Group Holdings, the lithographic printer, managed to increase pre-tax profits by 30 per cent in the year to June 30. The rise from Pounds 807,000 to Pounds 1.05m was achieved on sales up slightly at Pounds 28.7m (Pounds 28.4m). Net borrowings at the year end were down from Pounds 500,000 to Pounds 3.6m and the dividend is lifted to 1.05p (1p) on earnings per share of 2.1p (1.8p).
