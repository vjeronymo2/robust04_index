Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report; See SELECTED VIDEO REPORTS section for video footage of incident] [Text] According to initial reports, one person was killed and 10 were injured when a bomb placed in a garbage bin at the Tuzla train station in Istanbul exploded. According to a TRT correspondent, a bomb placed in a garbage bin at the Tuzla train station exploded around 0915. One person was killed and 10 were injured in the explosion. Most of the victims consist of the non-commissioned officers at the Tuzla Infantry School. Investigation into the incident is underway.
