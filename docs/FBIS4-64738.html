BFN [Excerpt] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- At the beginning of his short visit to Germany, Japanese Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata on Thursday [5 May] met with Federal Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel at the Petersberg [government guest house] outside Bonn. The German Foreign Ministry said the talks will focus on the state of German-Japanese relations, the relationship between the European Union and Japan, questions concerning the southeast Asian region, and the situation in Central and East Europe.
