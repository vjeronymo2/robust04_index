BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 29 Jun -- At about 2300 on Tuesday a border detail in the sector of the 12th border outpost of the Moscow border detachment was subjected to intense shelling. The Russian border guards returned fire and neutralized the firing points. There were no casualties among the Russian border guards. An ITAR-TASS correspondent found this out from informed military sources in Dushanbe. This is already the third case this week of firing on Russian border guard detachments and border posts from Afghan territory.
