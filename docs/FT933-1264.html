930924 FT 24 SEP 93 / International Capital Markets: Cedel service to use US Treasuries CEDEL, the Luxembourg-based securities clearing house, is to launch a service which will allow customers to use US Treasuries as collateral for its financing facilities and for international settlement. This is the first time Treasuries will be used as collateral outside the US, said Mr Michel Vermaerke, a senior manager at Cedel. The service will be launched on November 3. Cedel's service will also cover delivery of Treasuries to and from the domestic market, custody and safe-keeping. The International Swaps & Derivatives Association is to conduct a survey on management practices in the derivatives market, following the Group of Thirty report.
