920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / International Company News: Former Foster's chief appointed to Coles Myer MR PETER BARTELS, former Foster's Brewing Group chief executive, has been appointed chief executive officer of Coles Myer, Australia's biggest retailer. The appointment confirms speculation fuelled by Mr Bartels' resignation from Foster's in March, reportedly after strong disagreements with the company's then key shareholder, Mr John Elliott. Mr Bartels will take up his new appointment on July 27, replacing Mr Brian Quinn, the current Coles chief.
