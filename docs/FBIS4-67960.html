BFN [AFP, YONHAP from Vienna] [Text] The International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] on 4 May disclosed that North Korea has reaffirmed its stance of not allowing collection of samples when the fuel rods at North Korea's nuclear reactor in Yonbyon are replaced. IAEA Spokesman Hans Meyer said that the IAEA will not send for the time being its inspectors to North Korea since the latest response from the North Korean Government falls short of expectations.
