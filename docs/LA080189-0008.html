August 1, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

U.S. STRATEGY FOR NUCLEAR RESPONSE

Words cannot begin to express my feelings upon reading your account of the Bush Administration's latest nuclear strategy, a plan called SIOP-7.

Words like shock, anger, fear, outrage, sadness and laughter. They can't be serious -- there must be something funny about this. Yes -- the joke's on us mere mortal taxpayers.

When are they going to stop this waste? When are we?

HAROLD WITTNER

Los Angeles
