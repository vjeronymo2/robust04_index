BFN [Text] The Democratic Kampuchea [DK] representative in Phnom Penh announced on 18 June that Chan Youran, DK Party's representative to the meeting of the Phnom Penh Roundtable Commission, and his assistants left Phnom Penh by plane on the morning of 18 June. All other officials of the DK Representative's Office will be leaving Phnom Penh on 19 June at the demand of the Royal Government of Cambodia [RGC]'s Ministry of Interior. On the night of 17 June, DK Chairman Khieu Samphan sent an urgent telegram to His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk informing him of the Ministry of Interior's demand that all DK representatives leave Phnom Penh. On the morning of 18 June, His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk sent a reply calling on DK representatives to quickly leave Phnom Penh to avoid any incident that might lead to the deterioration of the situation. In his reply, the King also said that he would maintain good relations with Mr. Khieu Samphan and the DK Party as before. On the night of 17 June, the RGC Ministry of Interior issued an expulsion order released through radio and television, saying that due to the failure of the 15-16 June meeting of the Phnom Penh Roundtable Commission, the DK representatives and staff must leave Phnom Penh before 19 June because the Ministry of Interior might not be able to provide security for them.
