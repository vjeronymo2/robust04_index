941224 FT 24 DEC 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Johannesburg edged ahead as industrials managed to overcome negative early sentiment while golds struggled to curb earlier losses. The overall index gained 4.3 at 5,803, industrials picked up 20.0 to 6,926.2 and gold finished 9.5 lower at 1,955.1 The feature of the session was M-Net's R2 rise to R15 on news of an international pay television deal with Richemont and Multichoice.
