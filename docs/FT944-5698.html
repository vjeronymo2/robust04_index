941202 FT 02 DEC 94 / Channel 4 faces complaint request Channel 4 is to be asked to broadcast an adjudication of the Broadcasting Complaints Commission criticising aspects of one of its programmes - Close to Home: A Tale of Two Cities - about the Edinburgh 'Fettesgate' affair. Fettesgate involved the theft of documents from an Edinburgh police station detailing alleged offences by gay lawyers. The BCC ruled the programme's linking of Mr Derek Donaldson to the events concerned involved significant unfairness to him.
