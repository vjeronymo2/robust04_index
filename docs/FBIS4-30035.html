BFN [Text] Recently, the Dong Nai Province Agricultural Materials Corporation imported 40 tonnes of poor quality DK888 corn strains from Thailand for sale to peasants in Xuan Loc District, Dong Nai Province, causing heavy losses to production. After reviewing a report by the Government Office and the action taken by the Ministry of Agriculture and Food Industry and the Dong Nai Province people's committee, the prime minister of the Government instructed the people's committee to prepare a detailed report on the case to determine the responsibility of the Agricultural Materials Corporation [AMC] for the imports and sale of poor quality corn strains to peasants, take appropriate disciplinary action against those individuals involved, and adopt urgent practical measures to ensure the interests of the peasants. The prime minister also instructed the people's commitee to direct the Agricultural Materials Corporation in contacting the Thai exporter to resolve this problem. Following this incident, the prime minister instructed state management agencies and organs and units in charge of supplying agricultural materials countrywide to uphold a sense of responsibility in preventing a recurrence of such an incident.
