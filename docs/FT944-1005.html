941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / UK Company News: BFI extends Attwoods offers Browning-Ferris Industries of the US, which has acquired Attwoods, the UK waste company, via a Pounds 391m hostile bid, has extended its final offers until January 11, and reserves the right to extend the ordinary offer further. BFI has received acceptances in respect of 261.5m ordinary shares, including those represented by ADSs, being 92.4 per cent of the issued ordinary capital, and 54.5m preference shares, representing 82.98 per cent.
