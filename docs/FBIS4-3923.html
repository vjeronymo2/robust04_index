BFN ["Resolution of the Second Session of the Eighth Anhui Provincial People's Congress on the Implementation of the 1993 Provincial Economic and Social Development Plan and the Plan for 1994 Adopted by the Second Session of the Eighth Anhui Provincial People's Congress on 12 April 1994"] [Text] Through examination and after consulting the Examination Committees' report on the provincial economic and social development plan as well as fiscal budget, the Second Session of the Eighth Anhui Provincial People's Congress has decided to approve the 1994 Anhui provincial economic and social development plan and also the "Report on the Implementation of the 1993 Anhui Provincial Economic and Social Development Plan and the Draft Plan for 1994" made by Chairman Huang Yuezhong of the provincial Planning Commission. This is a crucial year for promoting reform in an all-round manner and for accelerating the establishment of a socialist market economic structure. Following the guidelines of the Second Session of the Eighth Anhui Provincial People's Congress, governments at various levels should have a good grasp of the relations between reform, development, and stability; should further deepen various reforms; should strengthen and improve macrocontrol; should strictly control the increase of commodity prices; should show actual concern for the living condition of the masses; and should promote a sustained, rapid, and sound development of the national economy. It is necessary to place the development of agriculture first; promote all-round development of the rural economy; increase the peasants' income; vigorously promote the establishment of a modern enterprise system; improve the economic structure; quicken technological progress; strengthen operations and management; increase economic efficiency; effectively improve the investment structure; attach importance to building key projects; open up wider to the outside world; and seriously attach importance to education, science, and technological work. Governments at various levels throughout the entire province must improve their style, struggle in unity, and mobilize and rely on the masses to fulfill the 1994 economic and social development plan in an all-round manner.
