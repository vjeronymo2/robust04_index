941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / International Company News: Southern takes control of Empresa Southern Co's Southern Electric International has acquired majority ownership of Empresa Electrica del Norte Grande A, a Chilean utility, for about Dollars 58m, reports AP-DJ. Southern Electric now holds a 59.5 per cent stake in Empresa after buying an additional 21 per cent of the company from Corporacion de Fomento da la Producion, a government agency responsible for fostering development in Chile. The government of Chile is selling the shares as part of its privatisation program. Southern Electric bought a 38.5 per cent stake in the utility in December 1993. Empresa Electrica operates 96MW of electric generating capacity.
