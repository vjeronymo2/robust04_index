December 19, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SAN CLEMENTE;

PUBLIC INVITED TO GROWTH DISCUSSION

Results of a yearlong growth-management study by a citizens committee that is recommending changes in city development policies is being discussed at a Planning Commission public hearing tonight.

Topics reviewed by the nine-person Growth Management Committee were emergency medical response, open space, schools, sewers, roads, water, libraries, parking, police and a new civic center.

Commissioners last month were introduced to the study and continued a public hearing until tonight. The 7 p.m. meeting at City Hall will be televised live on Dimension Cable Channel 3 and will be rebroadcast at 9 a.m. and noon Saturday.
