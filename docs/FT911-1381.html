910507 FT 07 MAY 91 / EC approves drugs venture THE European Commission has ruled that consumer benefits outweigh the anti-competitive effect of a new pharmaceuticals joint venture between Du Pont de Nemours and Merck, two of the world's largest chemicals companies. Under last July's deal Du Pont, the US's biggest chemicals group, will inject its pharmaceuticals and radiopharmaceutical imaging activities into a joint venture vehicle.
