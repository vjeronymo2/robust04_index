941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Porth losses Losses at Porth Group, the USM-quoted decorations, packaging and framing products concern, incurred increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 2.08m for the first half of 1994, against Pounds 1.89m last time. Turnover slipped to Pounds 2.11m (Pounds 2.2m) and interest charges were Pounds 306,000 (Pounds 189,000). Losses per share came to 10.8p (9.8p).
