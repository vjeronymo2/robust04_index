930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / International Company News: Cypress turns in fourth-quarter deficit of Dollars 23m CYPRESS Semiconductor recorded a net loss for its fourth quarter and year after restructuring charges. Fourth-quarter sales were down 5 per cent as prices for the Silicon Valley chipmaker's main products came under pressure, writes Louise Kehoe in San Francisco. The net loss for the fourth quarter was Dollars 23.7m, or 66 cents a share, compared with net income of Dollars 5.9m, or 15 cents, in the fourth quarter of 1991. For the full year, Cypress posted net losses of Dollars 21m, or 56 cents, compared with net income of Dollars 34.2m, or 85 cents, for 1991.
