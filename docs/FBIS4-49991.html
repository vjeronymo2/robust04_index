BFN [Text] Beijing, May 10 (XINHUA) -- China hopes that its friendship and cooperation with Hungary will be furthered, a Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman said here today. He made the remarks when asked to comment on the results of the Hungarian general election, which the Socialist Party won. He said the elections are "the internal affairs of Hungary. We respect the choice of the Hungarian people." The spokesman stressed that China hopes and believes that Sino- Hungarian friendly and cooperative ties will gain an allround, sustained and steady development on the basis of the Five Principles of Peaceful Co-existence.
