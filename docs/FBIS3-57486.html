Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Ivanov] [Text] Moscow January 27 TASS -- Russian Premier Viktor Chernomyrdin departed for Italy this morning for a two-day official visit. According to the visit programme, Chernomyrdin will meet President Oscar Luigi Scalfaro and Premier Carlo Azeglio Ciampi. It is expected that the Russian premier will be received by Pope John Paul II and will have meetings with members of the Italian business community. At the Russian-Italian talks, the sides plan to discuss the entire range of bilateral relations, implementation of earlier understandings between the two countries, Russia-Italian partnership in international relations, the formation of new mechanisms for a pan-European system of security and cooperation through fuller use of the potential of the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe. It is expected that the intergovernment talks will result in signing a statement by the two governments, an intergovernment agreement on the status of burial places of Russian servicemen and civilians in Italy and Italian servicemen in Russia, who died during the Second World War as well as a memorandum of intent concerning an agreement on encouragement and protection of capital investments.
