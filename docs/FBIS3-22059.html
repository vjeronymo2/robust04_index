Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Guangzhou, 14 Mar (ZHONGGUO TONGXUN SHE)--Last year, the Guangzhou City police seized 145.3 kg of heroin and over 3.9 kg of "ice" and investigated and punished over 6,500 people involving in drug trafficking and drug abuse. In reporting to the city people's congress, the Guangzhou City Public Security Bureau said that it also seized 1,425 guns of various kinds (of which 57 were designed for military use) and 3,237 bullets last year. [passage omitted] A Guangzhou City Public Security Bureau report says that the social order situation in Guangzhou remains quite grim. The number of serious criminal cases is increasing steadily, the damage resulting from organized crime is increasing day by day, and some criminal gangs are becoming underworld societies. [passage omitted] Deputy Bureau Director Wang Long said that, this year, the Guangzhou police will make great efforts to improve Guangzhou's social order and will curb the momentum in the rising number of major and serious cases, will send more policemen to patrol the streets, will arm traffic policemen so that they can take care of both traffic and social order, and will strengthen the management of people from other localities and mobilize the residents to set up "small security zones" to promote the comprehensive improvement of social order.
