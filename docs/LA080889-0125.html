August 8, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Joan Kroc -- Because incorrect information was supplied to The Times by a spokesman in the Padres' front office, Joan Kroc's affiliation with McDonald's Corp. was reported erroneously Saturday. She is a "major shareholder" in the corporation, San Diego Padres official Chuck Gelman said Monday. She is not on the firm's board of directors.
