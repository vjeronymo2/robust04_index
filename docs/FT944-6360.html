941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Technical fault halts Eurostar Eurostar, the high-speed train service which links London with Paris and Brussels through the Channel tunnel, yesterday suffered its first breakdown since it began carrying fare-paying passengers. The 8.23am train from London to Paris developed a technical fault in northern France and stopped at Haute Picardie station. The 633 passengers were transferred to another train and arrived in Paris two hours late. They would receive a refund, French rail authority SNCF said.
