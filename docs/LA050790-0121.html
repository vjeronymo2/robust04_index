May 7, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

HOGAN WEDS 'CROCODILE' CO-STAR

"Crocodile Dundee" star Paul Hogan and his co-star Linda Kozlowski were married Saturday at the $2-million mansion he built for her as a wedding gift.

Reporters were barred from the ceremony at the mansion in Byron Bay, in the far north of eastern Australia's state of New South Wales, but Hogan said about 100 friends, relatives and "a couple of mates" were invited.

Hogan, 51, was a painter on the Sydney bridge when he got into show business. In the two hit "Crocodile' Dundee" movies, he played an Australian rustic who fell in love with an American journalist played by Kozlowski, 33.
