930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / International Company News: Spain approves Tabacalera and RJR food deal THE SALE of a 50 per cent stake in Grupo Royal Brands, the food subsidiary of Tabacalera, the state-owned tobacco monopoly, to RJR Nabisco, the US food group, for Pta17.25bn (Dollars 133m) has been approved by the Spanish government, AP-DJ reports from Madrid. Tabacalera and RJR Nabisco are to set up a joint venture, called RJR Alimentacion, to hold Royal Brands operations. Under the terms of the transaction, RJR Nabisco has the option to buy Tabacalera's stake in the joint venture within a year of March 31 1994.
