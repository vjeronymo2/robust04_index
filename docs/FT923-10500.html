920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / International Capital Markets: Arab Banking Corp rises 14% ARAB Banking Corporation (ABC), the Bahrain-based bank, has announced a 14 per cent rise in pre-tax profits to Dollars 65m for the first six months. The group's assets rose 2.5 per cent to Dollars 20.1bn, while deposits rose to Dollars 16.9bn from Dollars 16.2bn, with customer deposits rising 7.7 per cent to Dollars 10.6bn as interbank deposits remained steady at Dollars 6.3bn. Loans and advances rose 10 per cent to Dollars 11.1m. ABC last year turned in pre-tax profits of Dollars 90m after reporting losses of Dollars 47m the preceding year - due to the effects of the Gulf war. Mr Abdulla Saudi, president and chief executive, said interest income rose to 64 per cent of earnings from 60 per cent.
