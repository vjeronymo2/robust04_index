Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Khartoum, 17 Jan (SUNA) -- The Iranian construction minister arrived in Khartoum on Sunday heading a high-level delegation on a several day visit to the country. [passage omitted] The Iranian construction minister asserted in a press statement upon arrival that the visit comes in the context of boosting bilateral relations in the various fields which emanate from the Islamic motive linking Sudan and Iran. He added that he was accompanied by representatives of the different Iranian ministers noting that talks would be held between the two sides notably on a number of trade issues. The Iranian minister hoped that the talks would be fruitful toward gaining more consolidated bonds in other issues and creating new mechanisms with the aim of boosting mutual cooperation between the two countries. He also said he would visit the projects of the Iranian Jihad Construction Organization in the Sudan. Many agreements which have been concluded between Sudan and Iran have been implemented in the last period, he explained, indicating that the visit aimed at finding appropriate methods towards implementing the agreements concluded between Iran and Sudan.
