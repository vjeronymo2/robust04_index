BFN [Text] Riots erupted tonight in the Qalandiyah refugee camp, south of Ramallah. Our correspondent in the territories Golan Yokhpaz reports that five Arab residents were wounded by IDF [Israel Defense Forces] troops gunfire in the course of clashes between the residents and the security forces. According to local residents, the five were evacuated to a hospital in Ramallah. Two of them are in serious condition. The riots erupted during a mourning procession the camp residents held in memory of a local young man killed by soldiers during disturbances last weekend.
