BFN [Text] Egypt has voiced concern over the repeated noncompliance with a cease-fire in Yemen, warning that a cease-fire should take priority over any other consideration. Foreign Ministry spokesman Counselor Nazi al-Khudayr made a statement to our radio correspondent commenting on the unsuccessful attempts to maintain a cease-fire in Yemen. He said: Egypt backs any efforts to resolve the disputes among the brothers in Yemen in a peaceful manner that will preserve the country's remaining resources and the mutual trust among its sons.
