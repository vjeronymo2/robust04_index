930520 FT 20 MAY 93 / Violence flares again in Denmark STREET violence in Copenhagen flared up again last night in the wake of the Danes' endorsement of the Maastricht treaty. On Tuesday, 11 demonstrators were wounded and 26 police injured when armed police (above) confronted rioters. Last night, police fired tear gas and sealed off streets in response to the protests. Meanwhile, the government announced a package of tax cuts and Denmark's central bank cut the official discount rate to 8.25 per cent from 9.25 per cent. Report, Page 20
