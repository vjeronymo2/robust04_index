BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 28 [date as received] (KNS-KCNA) -- Koreans in Japan strongly protested against the Japanese authorities' crackdown on the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon). Officials under the Osaka prefectural headquarters of Chongnyon, some 200 Koreans in Japan and young people under the Hiroshima prefectural headquarters of the League of Korean Youth in Japan (Chochong) May 25 lodged a protest with the Osaka prefectural police headquarters and the Higashi Police Station and strongly demanded an apology and return of the confiscated materials. All branch officials and compatriots under the Tokyo metropolitan headquarters of Chongnyon conducted a street propaganda at 19 railway stations in Tokyo over May 26-27 and distributed 100,000 copies of a handbill condemning the police brutalities. A meeting of activists of the Osaka prefectural headquarters of Chochong was held on May 21 to firmly defend Chongnyon organizations and more staunchly struggle against the Japanese authorities. Protests also were launched vigorously among Koreans under Chongnyon in different areas of Japan including Aichi and Hiroshima prefectures.
