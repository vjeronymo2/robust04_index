Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 22 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The leaders of "the coalition of the four unions" organized in the middle of the last year (the Mongolian Democratic Union, the New Progressive Union, the Union of Mongolian Youth Organizations, the Union of Mongolian Students) have met here to exchange views and discuss the situation in the country created in connection with the scandal about involvement of the leaders of the parliament and the government in corruption. According the results of the meeting it was decided to resume activity, coordinate their further actions with the coalition of the Mongolian National Democratic Party and the Mongolian Social Democratic Party.
