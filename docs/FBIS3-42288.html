Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Interior Minister Hasan al-Alfi has stated that 23 terrorists have been extradited to Egypt from a number of countries and that there are agreements on the extradition of criminals that the interior ministry has concluded with these countries. The minister added that negotiations are continuing with some other countries to extradite terrorists who have fled there. He said that the Interior Ministry stresses that it is providing the necessary protection to anyone who gives information or statements that help expose any terrorist operations or leads to the arrest of terrorists.
