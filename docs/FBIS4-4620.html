BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 4 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song on April 4 met and congratulated the delegates to the national conference of party cell secretaries. When President Kim Il-song arrived, the delegates enthusiastically welcomed him with stormy cheers of "hurrah!". He was presented with a basket of fragrant flowers representing boundless reverence and loyal heart of the party cell secretaries of the whole party. Present there were member of the Presidium of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party [WPK] of Korea O Chin-u, members of the Political Bureau of the C.C. [Central Committee], the WPK Yi Chong-ok, Pak Song-chol and Kim Yong-chu and other members and alternate members of the Political Bureau and secretaries of the C.C., the WPK. President Kim Il-song congratulated the delegates who had made great contributions to strengthening and developing the party into an invincible revolutionary party and further consolidating our socialist stronghold by raising the militant function and role of the party cells and posed for a photograph with them.
