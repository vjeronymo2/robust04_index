940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / Acquisitions fall to Pounds 2.7bn Expenditure on acquisitions in the UK fell to Pounds 2.7bn in the first quarter of the year from Pounds 2.8bn in the previous quarter, Central Statistical Office figures showed yesterday. The number of UK acquisitions by industrial and commercial companies rose from 164 to 176. In the first quarter of last year there were 103 acquisitions with a value of Pounds 1.6bn.
