921031 FT 31 OCT 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG ended mixed after a sideways drift for most of the day as the market looked for fresh factors. Trading picked up slightly towards the close on month-end position-squaring. Some shares, like Pick 'n Pay and Amgold were also bought ahead of the last day to register for dividends. The overall index was unchanged at 3,017, down slightly on the week. The industrial index was 3 off at 4,013 and the gold index was up 1 at 816.
