"The Adventures of Buckaroo Banzai" (1984), directed by W.D. Richter. 103 minutes.

Rated PG-13. Forget Dick Tracy, Batman, Darkman, The Flash, et al. Buckaroo Banzai is the greatest comic-book hero who never was. He's also the hippest super-hero around. When not fronting his band of New Jersey rock 'n' rollers, he's busy creating a device to penetrate the eighth dimension. It might take two or three viewings just to figure out what the heck is going on, but the ride is worth it. RANDY LEWIS
