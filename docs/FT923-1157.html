920924 FT 24 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Henderson Highland net assets lower Henderson Highland Trust had a net asset value of 85.1p at August 31, down from 96.7p end February. Attributable profits for the half-year to end-August amounted to Pounds 693,000 (Pounds 759,000) equivalent to earnings of 2.65p (2.91p) per share. A second interim dividend of 1.4p makes 2.8p so far this year.
