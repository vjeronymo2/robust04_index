August 10, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

DOYOUSEEWHATISEE WINS AT DEL MAR

Doyouseewhatisee led wire to wire, beating favored Green Baby by three lengths in the $82,050 De Anza Stakes for 2-year-olds Wednesday at Del Mar.

Doyouseewhatisee, ridden by Martin Pedroza, covered the six furlongs in 1:09 2/5 for his second straight victory. The fourth betting choice of the crowd of 14,382 returned $13.60, $5.40 and $4.20.

Green Baby, the 8-5 favorite, paid $3.20 and $3.20, and Drag Race returned $6.80.
