Language: English Article Type:BFN [From 6 January POSTIMEES, p 3] [Text] Thursday, January 6 -- The Defence Police should handle the section of the Russian Liberal Democratic Party in Estonia, Justice Minister Kaido Kama said yesterday. Kama referred to a claim by a Pyotr Rozhok that a section of the party, headed by the extremist Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, is active in Estonia. Since only registered political organisations are allowed in Estonia and only Estonian citizens can form parties, the activities of the Russian Liberal Democratic party in Estonia are illegal, Kama stressed.
