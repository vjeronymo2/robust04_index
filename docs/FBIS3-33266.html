Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Managua, 17 Feb (ACAN-EFE) -- Nicaraguan President Violeta Chamorro today appointed Luis Rivas Leiva, a physician and Social Democrat, to the post of director of Social Communications of the Presidency of the Republic. Chamorro also signed a decree creating the Social Communications Directorate, which dissolves the Public Relations and Official Information Directorate of the Presidency, as well as the Information and Press Directorate of the Presidency. The Public Relations and Official Information Directorate was previously headed by Danilo Lacayo Lanzas, a lawyer and Social Democrat, who resigned on Tuesday to practice his profession and return to the private sector, according to official sources. The Information and Press Directorate of the Presidency was in charge of reporting the activities of cabinet members and was headed by Ernesto Robleto, who resigned from the position in the latter part of 1993, also to return to the private sector. Chamorro appointed journalist Maria Alicia Talavera Sevilla, former editor of BARRICADA newspaper in Managua, as deputy director of the Social Communications Directorate. Both officials were sworn in by the Nicaraguan president in her office this afternoon.
