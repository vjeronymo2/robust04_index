Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] King Husayn has said the signing of the Yemeni accord, which was supposed to take place in Amman on Sunday [6 February], has been postponed due to differences between the northerners and the southerners. King Husayn reiterated that Jordan will continue to exert efforts to resolve the Yemeni crisis. However, he added, some time is needed before reaching results.
