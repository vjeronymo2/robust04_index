BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow June 22 TASS -- President Boris Yelisin received Mr Federico Mayor, director-general of the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organisation (UNESCO), in the Kremlin on Wednesday and presented the Peoples' Friendship Order to him "for a big contribution to the development of international intellectual cooperation in the name of peace and progress", the presidential press service announced. Yeltsin reaffirmed Russia's invariable support for this international organisation as a unique forum for international cooperation in culture, science, education and communications. Considering democratic processes in Russia, Yeltsin emphasised his interest in a success of UNESCO's world culture programme. The UNESCO chief told the Russian president in detail about UNESCO activities in the coming year 1995 which has been designated by UNESCO as the year of tolerance.
