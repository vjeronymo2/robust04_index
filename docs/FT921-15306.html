920110 FT 10 JAN 92 / International Capital Markets: CPFP to take CDollars 375m charge CANADIAN Pacific Forest Products, one of Canada's largest pulp and paper groups which is 80 per cent owned by Canadian Pacific Ltd, , is to close down several older paper machines and axe nearly 2,000 jobs, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Together with other restructuring moves, CPFP will take a special CDollars 375m (USDollars 328.9m) after-tax charge in its 1991 accounts. In the first nine months, it posted a loss of CDollars 107m or C2.44 a share on sales of CDollars 1.5bn.
