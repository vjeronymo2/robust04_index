BFN [Republican Decree No. 22 [as heard] issued at the presidency in Sanaa on on 10 May 1994--read by announcer] [Text] The chairman of the Presidential Council, cognizant of the Constitution of the Republic of Yemen and Law No. 67 of 1991 on service in the Armed Forces and Security, and upon the recommendation of the brother defense minister and the acting prime minister, and after the approval of the Council of Ministers and the endorsement of the Presidential Council, decrees the following: Article I. Staff Colonel Ahmad 'Abdallah al-Hasani shall be appointed commander of the Naval Forces. Article II. This decree will be valid as of date of issue and will be published in the Official Gazette. Issued at the Presidency in Sanaa on 30 Dhu al-Qi'dah 1414 Hegira, corresponding to 10 May 1994. [Signed] Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar, acting prime minister Staff Brigadier General 'Abd-Rabbuh Mansur Hadi, minister of defense Following is the biography of Staff Colonel Ahmad 'Abdallah Muhammad Nasir al-Hasani: Born in 1949. Place of birth: Abyan Governorate, Mudiyah District. He joined a number of specialized military courses in the naval field in Britain, Uganda, and the former USSR. He holds a master's degree from the Naval Academy. He assumed several leading posts and jobs, including member of the staff command of the Naval Forces and Coastal Defense before the unity, commander of the Naval Forces and Coastal Defense before the unity, and adviser to the defense minister after the unity. He was one of the strugglers of the liberation war. He was awarded several medals.
