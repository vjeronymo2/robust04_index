BFN [Text] Police believe the missing Russian diplomat, third secretary Igor Makeev, is still alive but his whereabouts is a mystery. A high-level police officer, who requested anonymity, said yesterday after he was told of Mr. Makeev's disappearance that several police units had launched a search for him. He was last seen leaving Ocean Apartment in Soi Saladaeng off Silom Road on March 22. The Institute of Forensic Medicine confirmed that Mr. Makeev was not on the list of dead sent to the Police Hospital. Since Mr. Makeev was also responsible for looking after Russian women in Thailand, the police went to bars and nightclubs in Soi Sawatdi off Sukhumwit Road but found no traces of the Russian. The police are awaiting confirmation from the Immigration Police Bureau as to whether or not Mr. Makeev has left Thailand.
