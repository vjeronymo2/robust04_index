941102 FT 02 NOV 94 / Angolan oil town captured Angolan government forces have recaptured an important oil town from Unita rebels in heavy fighting, state media reported yesterday less than 24 hours after the two sides initialled a peace accord. Government troops had also been advancing on the Unita stronghold of Huambo, a government official said. Under the accord reached in the Zambian capital, Lusaka, the government and Unita are to declare a ceasefire on November 17.
