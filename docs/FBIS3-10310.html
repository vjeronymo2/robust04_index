Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Colombian Ambassador to Cuba (Ricardo Santamaria Salamanca) said today that Cuba and Colombia will reach important agreements this quarter that will help create a dynamic scenario for business deals and the influx of capital and businessmen. The ambassador also stressed the growing pace of negotiations between the two countries in various sectors of the economy, science, culture, and health. The Colombian ambassador added that a Colombian Foreign Trade Ministry delegation will visit Cuba next week to discuss an agreement on patents, licensing, floor tiles, and plumbing supply products.
