Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "If a Criminal Escapes From Ukraine to Israel, He Will Not Get Away With It"] [Text] Ukrainian Minister of Internal Affairs Andriy Vasylyshyn held fruitful negotiations with Moshe Shahal, Israeli minister of police, power engineering, and infrastructure, who visited Ukraine at the invitation of his Ukrainian colleague.
