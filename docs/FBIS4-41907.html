BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents] [Text] Kiev 28 June TASS -- About 80 per cent of residents in the Crimean sea port of Sevastopol voted on Sunday for basing of the Russian Black Sea Fleet in their city, the press service of the Crimean Parliament told TASS on Tuesday. The plebiscite was held on a par with the first round of Ukrainian presidential elections.
