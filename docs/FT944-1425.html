941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Burndene lower Burndene Investments reported a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 5.8m to Pounds 4.54m in the year to October 1. Profits from the caravan and mobile home division fell to Pounds 3.76m (Pounds 4.16m) while property, finance and administration contributed a much lower Pounds 153,332 (Pounds 1.04m). Turnover improved to Pounds 62.2m (Pounds 59m). Earnings per share fell from 3.73p to 2.96p. The dividend is raised to 1.925p with a lower final of 1.175p (1.25p).
