Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 25 KYODO --- Air Canada will open routes to Japan after the new Kansai International Airport opens in September, the Transport Ministry said Friday [25 February]. Ministry officials said the two countries agreed at bilateral talks held in Tokyo that Air Canada will operate four Boeing 747 flights weekly between Canada and the new airport. Air Canada thus follows Canadian Airlines International, which already serves routes between Japan and Canada.
