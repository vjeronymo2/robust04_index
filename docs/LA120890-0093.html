December 8, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Mortgage Rates Lower: Fixed-rate mortgages averaged 9.81% this week, down from 9.9% last week, according to a national survey released by the Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp. The rate was the lowest since rates averaged 9.8% last January. On one-year adjustable rate mortgages, lenders were asking an average initial rate of 8.04%, down from 8.08% last week. The rates do not include add-on fees known as points.
