November 30, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

PROTEST: As the Persian Gulf buildup continues (A1), and more local Marines head out (B1), scattered protests against U.S. military involvement continue. . . . A countywide event is set Sunday afternoon at Mile Square Park in Fountain Valley, sponsored by a coalition of anti-war groups. . . . They hope this time they can attract mainstream Orange County into their camp. "People in all segments of the population are very fearful of war," says a spokeswoman. Among the speakers: Tony Russo of Pentagon Papers fame. Helene Elliott and Danny Sullivan
