Language: English Article Type:BFN [From 23 February HOMMIKULEHT, PAEVALEHT, pp 1,3; "News Bulletin" No. 37/94] [Text] Wednesday, February 23 -- Colonel Arvo Sirel, commander of the Estonian-Latvian-Lithuanian joint manoeuvre completed near Vdru yesterday, stressed that the exercise served its purpose. He admitted that the exercise was largely conditional and that the terrain was chosen in the interest of visibility. The exercise proved the capability of the Baltic soldiers to operate jointly. Further development of cooperation should concern the unification of communication, tactical control, coast guard and air defence, head of the Lithuanian GHQ training section Colonel Romas ibas said. Finnish, Swedish, Norwegian and British military attaches observed the exercise.
