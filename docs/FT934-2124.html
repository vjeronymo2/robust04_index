931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Bromsgrove Inds raises Dollars 18m in US Bromsgrove Industries, the West Midlands specialist engineer, is raising Dollars 18m (Pounds 12m) through the issue of senior unsecured loan notes to two US insurance companies. The notes, bearing a 7.8 per cent coupon, have a final maturity date of 2007. Repayment in tranches of Dollars 2m will be made from 2003 to 2007.
