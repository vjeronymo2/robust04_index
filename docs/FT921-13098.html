920123 FT 23 JAN 92 / International Capital Markets: Cresvale launches specialist fund CRESVALE, the equity-related securities firm, is launching an unusual fund which will invest in the Japanese stock market using equity warrants and futures, writes Tracy Corrigan. A basket of Japanese equity warrants on stocks in the Nikkei 225 index is purchased, and that position is then hedged through index futures, in order to neutralise market risk. The Hedge Fund is an open-ended fund listed in Luxembourg. The fund will be marketed to institutional investors.
