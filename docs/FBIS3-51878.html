Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Communique issued by the Presidency of the Republic in Panama City on 25 January; from the "Telemetro Reports" newscast] [Text] His Excellency President Guillermo Endara Galimany, by virtue of the power vested in him, has appointed the following persons to occupy the mayoral posts in the following districts: Alcibiades Bustavino, mayor of Pese; Mario Melendez, mayor of Baru, Puerto Armuelles; Dora Lesbia Palacios, mayor of Anton; Enrique Batista, mayor of Rio de Jesus; Hector Ovidio Herrera, mayor of Atalaya; Nelson Martinez, mayor of Canazas; Adriano Moscoso, mayor of Santamaria; and Gabriela Pino Cosio, mayor of Calobre.
