BFN [Text] Vietnamese Deputy Minister of Trade Nguyen Xuan Quang and Thai Deputy Minister of Trade Chaiyot Sasomsap have signed an agreement on exchanging information on production, marketing, and rice exports. The two sides agreed to exchange information on the volume and types of export rice, marketing, rice production, and rice export policies on a quarterly and yearly basis. The two sides also unanimously agreed to hold a ministerial-level meeting once a year and a specialist-level meeting twice a year.
