BFN [Text] This is what IMA-PRESS has to say: At 1530 local time [0530 GMT] today, the inhabitants of Vladivostok saw a huge explosive mushroom, similar to that from a nuclear explosion. Lieutenant-Colonel (Kuliyev), duty officer of the Maritime region's civil defense, reported the storage depot contained neither chemical nor biological nor nuclear munitions, but only unguided [neupravlayemiye] missiles, shells, and depth charges. At 2230 Vladivostok time [1230 GMT], the fire and explosions were continuing. The area was concealed by thick smoke, therefore it is difficult to assess the damage. There is information about a great number of injured, mostly people hurt by glass splinters from windows which were put out in all the surrounding settlements. The director of Vladivostok private radio station, (Dmitriy Bruzgin), informed IMA-PRESS that more than 10 eyewitnesses had phoned the radio with reports of people injured. Pioneer camps, dormitories and hotels are being prepared for the evacuation of settlements near to the scene.
