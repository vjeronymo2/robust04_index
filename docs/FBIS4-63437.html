BFN [Text] Yerevan, May 20 (SNARK) A protocol on trade-economic cooperation between Crimea and Armenia is signed in Simferopol. The press-center of the Armenian Government told SNARK an agreement on establishing an Association "Crimea-Armenia Trade House" was reached between Industry Minister Ashot Safaryan and President Yuri Meshkov. The Association will have offices in Yerevan and Simferopol. During the Armenian minister's to Crimea [as received], the sides discussed questions of mutual airports granting for transit flights and using Crimean ports for shipment to Armenia. The Armenian side expressed her readiness to take part in the state program of Armenians' return to Crimea, deported 50 years ago.
