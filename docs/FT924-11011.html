921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / Parliament and Politics: Financial sector jobs show fall EMPLOYMENT in Britain's financial services sector has fallen by 25,000 in the last year, Mr Patrick McLoughlin, junior employment secretary, said yesterday. There were 623,700 employees in banking, finance and auxiliary activities in June, compared with 648,700 a year before, the figures show. Ms Joyce Quin, a Labour employment spokesman, said: 'Now we see a huge jobs crisis facing the very sector on which we were told we would have to rely in the future.'
