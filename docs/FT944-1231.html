941221 FT 21 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Forminster edges up to Pounds 1.09m Mild weather restricted interim profits growth at Forminster, the clothing importer and manufacturer, as demand for winter clothes fell short of the trade's expectations. The pre-tax figure for the six months to October 31 edged ahead from Pounds 1.08m to Pounds 1.09m, on static turnover of Pounds 12.6m (Pounds 12.5m). Earnings per share were 5.64p (5.35p). The interim dividend rises to 1.04p (0.926p). The company has also bought 100,000 of its ordinary 2p shares for cancellation at 148p each.
