BFN [Text] Sanaa, May 11 (MENA) -- The number of people killed since war erupted in Yemen has reached 119, eight of them Somalis, while the wounded reached 489. Director of Military Hospital and Health Service here Muhammad Naji told MENA correspondent today that out of those casualties 79 military officers were killed and 299 were wounded. The rest are civilians and foreigners. The number of Somalis wounded in the Yemen civil war has reached 39, director of health services said. There are several thousand Somalis living here that have sought refuge in Yemen after the start of the civil war in their country, he said. Southern forces used attack a military hospital in one of the northern regions, he added [sentence as received].
