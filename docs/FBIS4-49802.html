BFN [From the "Morning News" program] [Text] On 17 April Jiang Chunyun, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the Shandong provincial CPC Committee, met with members of a Shanghai study group in Shandong led by Wang Liping, deputy secretary of the Shanghai municipal CPC Committee, and Vice Mayor Zhao Qizheng. The two sides discussed ways to further economic cooperation between their localities and held that Shanghai and Shandong will cooperate to launch a new round of south-north economic contacts. According to JIEFANG RIBAO, the Shanghai study group was organized based on opinions by Wu Bangguo, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the Shanghai municipal CPC Committee, and Mayor Huang Ju following their inspection of Shandong. Comrade Wang Liping, deputy secretary of the Shanghai municipal CPC Committee, said: We are determined to come to Shandong to learn from its experience, and will have handsome rewards. After returning to Shanghai, we will earnestly study and materialize the Shandong experience for quick results. Vice Mayor Zhao Qizheng stated emphatically: The opportunity to develop and open up the Pudong district to the outside world belongs to the entire nation. It is Shanghai's responsibility to share this opportunity with Shandong.
