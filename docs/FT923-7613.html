920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / Construction Contracts: Binfield Manor JOHN LELLIOTT CONSTR-UCTION GROUP has been awarded a total of Pounds 18m worth of contracts including the Pounds 4m restoration of Binfield Manor, Forest Road, Binfield, Berkshire. Work on this Grade ll listed building involves stripping out, installation of M & E services and fitting out, including marble floors and carved wood panelling.
