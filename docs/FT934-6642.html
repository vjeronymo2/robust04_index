931124 FT 24 NOV 93 / FT's Joe Rogaly wins award Joe Rogaly, the FT's political commentator, has won the Edgar Wallace Trophy for Outstanding Reporting. Mr Rogaly won the award from the London Press Club for the consistently high quality of his reporting and comment on the British political scene. Robert Peston, head of the FT's Special Reports Team, was highly commended in the Scoop of the Year award for his reporting on financial extravagance at the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development. The winner in this category was Clare Henderson of the Grimsby Evening Telegraph, whose story about Mr Norman Lamont's resignation as chancellor beat the Downing Street announcement by five hours.
