January 29, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

JAPANESE CAR MAKERS

I'm sick and tired of reading so much about the superiority of the Japanese automobiles and that American cars have not caught up on quality.

I for one feel the design and quality of the big three are as good as any foreign make. I've come back to buying American cars; I bought an '89 Mustang convertible last year and recently traded my Nissan for a Ford Ranger truck. I've come back -- I wish everyone would.

JACK D. GILLESPIE

Diamond Bar
