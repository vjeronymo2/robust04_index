BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 23 Apr (WAKH) -- Shaykh Hamdan Bin-Zayid Al Nuhayyan, United Arab Emirates [UAE] minister of state for foreign affairs, this evening received Mahmud 'Abbas, also known as Abu-Mazin, PLO Executive Committee member, who is currently visiting the UAE. Discussions dealt with developments in the Middle East peace process. Abu-Mazin explained in detail the Palestinian-Israeli peace negotiations, currently under way in Cairo, and the possibility of signing a declaration of principles, according to which the Palestinian police forces will enter Gaza Strip and Jericho.
