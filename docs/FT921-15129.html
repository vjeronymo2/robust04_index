920111 FT 11 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Lilleshall in Pounds 2.8m plastics acquisition At a cost of Pounds 2.79m Lilleshall has acquired Solarfront, which makes plastic garden and houseware products through its subsidiary Crystalware Products. This extends Lilleshall's engineering and plastics activities and provides it with a branded consumer product. Consideration for the acquisition is Pounds 1.43m cash and the issue of 1.4m shares, 250,000 of which have been retained by the vendor and the balance placed with institutions at 96p. Solarfront was incorporated in July 1990. For the period to July 31 1991 it made pre-tax profits of Pounds 600,000 on turnover of Pounds 3.8m, while in that year Crystalware earned Pounds 800,000 on sales of Pounds 4.9m.
