921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Intercare makes Pounds 400,000 purchase Intercare Group has acquired the business and assets of PJ Burns, which trades as Castle Pharmaceuticals, for Pounds 426,015 cash. Some Pounds 85,000 of the consideration has been placed on deposit in a joint retention account and will be released to the vendor in two instalments on November 1 1993 and October 31 1994 subject to his continuing to be employed with Impharm, a subsidiary of Intercare, at those dates. In the year to September 30 1992, Castle's pre-tax profits were Pounds 106,790 on turnover of Pounds 1.86m; net assets at that date amounted to Pounds 469,240.
