Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ARMENPRESS correspondent for TASS] [Text] Yerevan, 28 Jan -- Bloody fighting is continuing in Nagorno-Karabakh, the Nagorno-Karabakh defense army reports today. The Azerbajiani forces are on the offensive in the direction of the Kelbadzharskiy and Mardakertskiy rayons, in the process of which "the Azerbaijanis are incurring serious losses. Yesterday they lost over 70 killed and 19 armored vehicles captured in this sector of the front alone." In connection with a report in Azerbaijan's mass media claiming a "steep increase in the supplies of weapons to Armenia by Russia," the head of the Armenian Defense Ministry's press service told the ARMENPRESS-TASS correspondent that "military cooperation between Russia and Armenia is being strictly adhered to within the framework of the status of the Russian forces in Armenia. No deliveries of weapons not regulated by this agreement have been made or are being made."
