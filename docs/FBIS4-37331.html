BFN [Text] 'Abdallah al-Asnaj, appointed foreign minister of the self-declared Democratic Republic of Yemen, has stated that the South Yemen delegation will leave Cairo today to resume its tour of the Arab Maghreb states after it became apparent that Lakhdar Brahimi's meeting with the Sanaa delegation produced no positive results. Al-Asnaj praised President Husni Mubarak's efforts to end the tragic war in Yemen.
