October 23, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SCHOOLS: Anaheim City School District parents are expected to endorse year-round schools for their overcrowded district, where enrollments increased 5% this year. . . . Supt. Meliton Lopez hopes that results of a recent survey will show that parents support the change. . . . He noted a recent Times Orange County poll showing that 58% of county residents favor year-round schools. . . . Anaheim would be the county's fifth district with year-round scheduling, which would begin in July, 1990. Lanie Jones and Rick VanderKyff
