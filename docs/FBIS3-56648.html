Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] At a meeting with a group of Federation Council deputies, leaders of regional executive bodies, held on Friday [14 January] in Moscow, Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin declared that the presidential decree on personal appointments to the composition of the country's government under re-organization would be made public next Tuesday, one of the parliamentarians who participated in the meeting and who asked not to be named told Interfax. According to well informed sources, the draft document is almost ready. The source believes that Chernomyrdin has recommended the president to confirm Oleg Soskovets and Yegor Gaydar as first vice premiers. The decision concerning candidates for vice premierships is expected to be adopted on Monday.
