BFN [Excerpt] Lieutenant General 'Umar Hasan Ahmad al-Bashir, the president of the Republic and commander in chief of the people's Armed Forces, attended the big popular rally organized by the citizens yesterday evening in front of general headquarters to mark the recapture of the town of Kajo Kaji. The rally was addressed by Major Gen. Zuhayr Muhammad Salih, the first vice president of the Republic, who declared that the rebels still had the opportunity to enter into negotiations for the sake of construction and reconstruction. His excellency announced that peace was prevailing countrywide and that enormous numbers of rebels were surrendering to the Armed Forces at the headquarters of state governments. He said the time had now come to get caravans of support moving to realize and restore stability following the defeat of the rebellion. He added that the mobilization of the ultimate force currently being equipped would move in order to bring about construction and reconstruction. [passage omitted]
