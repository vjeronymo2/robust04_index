November 21, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

CRAYTON, SMITH ARE NAMED TOP PLAYERS IN DIVISION

Estrus Crayton, a running back from Rancho Santiago College, was selected as the Mission Conference Central Division offensive player of the year for the second consecutive year, it was announced Tuesday.

Golden West's Ray Smith, a lineman, was picked as the defensive player of the year. Smith led Golden West with 89 tackles and 6 1/2 sacks.

Crayton, who set 11 single-season or career records, led Orange County rushers with 1,112 yards this season.

Bill Workman, who led OCC to the divisional title, was named coach of the year.
