Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By 'Abd-al-Ra'uf Arna'ut] [Excerpt] Jerusalem -- Three Palestinian factions allied within the PLO's framework and supportive of the peace process and the recent Palestinian-Israeli agreement have declared their intention to formulate a joint action program that enhances their joint role in defense of the PLO, supports their political positions, and develops popular rallying around those positions. In a statement issued yesterday by their representatives, Fatah, the Palestinian People's Party, and Fida [Palestinian Democratic Union] announced that an expanded and urgent leadership meeting will be held to discuss and approve the joint action paper and create a suitable mechanism for implementing it to encompass all regions and principal organizations belonging to the three factions.
