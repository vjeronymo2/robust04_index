920410 FT 10 APR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Apples from the Southern Hemisphere are this week's best fruit buy. Choose from Cape Golden Delicious at 50-65p a lb (60-70p) or Chilean Granny Smith at 55-60p a lb (55-65p). English cauliflowers are abundant with prices ranging from 60-70p each (60-70p). Other good buys include Egyptian new potatoes at 20-25p a lb (22-25p) and parsnips at 30-40p a lb (30-40p). English, Dutch and Guernsey tomatoes are plentiful this week at 60-70p a lb (80-90p). Spania celery at 55-65p (55-65p) a head is a good buy along withEnglish round lettuce at 20-30p (20-30p) a head. NB Last week's prices shown in brackets.
