Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Editorial: "The Mouthpiece"] [Text] By choosing the role of world terrorist and troublemaker, NATO does not even maintain any pretenses. In the game of "acquiring vital countries" in the Balkans, it even cancels the now-pretentious international security mechanisms for stability and balance, such as the UN Security Council. And in order to make its decisions "legal," it does not even "tolerate" suspicion under any motive. Therefore, to Russia's proposal for an emergency convening of the UN Security Council to examine NATO's decision for bombings in Bosnia-Herzegovina, the United States and France reacted by claiming that it was useless because NATO's decision is already covered by the United Nations! No reservation "will prevent implementation of the decision," cynically said the French Defense Minister Francois Leotard. This is a statement that indicates the mercilessness of international imperialism and the sham role of international organizations, which tend to become mouthpieces of the "powerful."
