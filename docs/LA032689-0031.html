March 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

LIVING IN THE MADONNA MOMENT

Calendar's Feb. 6 article on Madonna's efforts to become the first pop artist to sing in China was way off base.

Troy Cory was not only the first American pop singer to be featured and perform at concerts in China, but was the first American singer to perform live on its television network.

ADAM BIGWOOD

Vice President
