April 17, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

BHUTTO IN MECCA FOR TALKS WITH KING

Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto arrived Monday on a three-day visit to Mecca.

Diplomatic sources said she will discuss a variety of issues with King Fahd, including unrest in the Indian state of Kashmir.

Bhutto, the only female head of state in the Muslim world, was accompanied by a 45-member entourage, including her husband, Asif Ali Zardari, senior Cabinet ministers and high-ranking military officials.
