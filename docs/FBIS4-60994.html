BFN [Text] The CSCE Minsk Group delegation lead by Ambassador Mathias Mosberg returned to Yerevan from Stepanakert in the evening of 1 May. The First Deputy Foreign Minister of Armenia Zhirair Liparityan received the delegation today. The Foreign Ministry's press and public relations service reports that matters pertaining to the possibility of a cease-fire in the Karabakh conflict zone was discussed during the exchange of views. Ambassador Mosberg met the heads of the diplomatic missions of the CSCE member countries in Yerevan at noon. The delegation left Yerevan the same day.
