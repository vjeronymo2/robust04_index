Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A military spokesman for the General Command of the al-'Asifah Forces -- the command of the Fatah forces inside the occupied territories -- has stated the following: Our cells operating inside the occupied homeland have planted advanced explosive charges in the Haifa chemical factory north of the industrial zone, which is adjacent to the Haifa oil refineries. The charges went off at 1300 on Monday, 7 February, 1994, destroying the factory and setting huge fires that spread to neighboring factories and installations. The military spokesman added that, since the operation, rescue and fire fighting teams, the police, and the mobile environment protection unit for treating dangerous materials have been making great attempts to place the situation under control, while clouds of black smoke covered the whole Gulf of Haifa.
