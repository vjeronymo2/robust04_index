BFN [Text] The state of Slovak-Portuguese relations and their future were evaluated by Slovak Foreign Minister Eduard Kukan and Portuguese Foreign Minister Jose Manuel Durao Barroso at their meeting at Lisbon today. They observed that there were no problems in mutual relations and noted that there were great possibilities in developing economic and trade cooperation. Later, Slovak Foreign Minister Eduard Kukan also met Forass d'Avella, the Portuguese parliament deputy speaker, who assured him that the passage of Slovakia's association agreement with the European Union in the Portuguese parliament would be smooth in spite of the heavy parliamentary workload.
