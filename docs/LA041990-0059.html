April 19, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Bonnie Brittain Hall will leave South Coast Repertory on June 1, after seven years as director of development.

During Hall's tenure, SCR has raised a total of $9 million in capital funds and more than $7 million in operating funds. Louise Cummings, the annual fund director at the La Jolla Playhouse, has been hired to replace Hall, 33, and will begin at SCR on May 14. Cummings, 46, was one of a half-dozen candidates interviewed for the SCR post, Hall said.
