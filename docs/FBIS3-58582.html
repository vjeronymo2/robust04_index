Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The House of Representatives approved the 1994 state budgets by a majority vote. DISI [Democratic Rally], AKEL [Restorative Party of the Working People], DIKO [Democratic Party], and Liberals Party Chairman Nikolaos Rolandhis voted for the budgets. Socialist Party EDEK [United Democratic Union of Cyprus] voted against them, except for the Refugee-Relief Fund allocations. The budgets project 1.043 billion pounds in expenditures, 843 million pounds in revenue, and a 200-million pound deficit. [passage omitted] Out of the 47 deputies present, 42 voted for the budgets and five voted against.
