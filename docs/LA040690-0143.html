April 6, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

FAST LANE: Steven Bren, son of Irvine Co. Chairman Donald L. Bren, makes his 1990 racing debut this weekend at the Phoenix International Raceway (C4). . . . The Newport Beach car dealer hopes to race in the Indianapolis 500 someday.

If so, he may get more attention in a week than his father gets in months. . . . The latest publication to seek an interview with the publicity-shy elder Bren is Orange Coast Magazine. A seven-page spread in the current issue explains how they didn't get an interview. Ann Conway and Steve Emmons
