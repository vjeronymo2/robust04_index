February 18, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

MORALITY AND LYLE WHEELER'S MISSING OSCARS

How pathetic it is that because of some quirk, a Long Beach couple, Sherry and Tom, came to possess the five Oscars of a talented art director ("The Diary of Lyle Wheeler's Oscars" by Daniel Cerone, Feb. 8). It is a measure of the caliber of these people that they refuse to be identified by their last names.

JESSICA DAVIS

Hollywood
