February 11, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BLACK CATEGORIES?

I was disappointed that George Michael won in the favorite R&B male soul and favorite R&B soul album categories at the American Music Awards telecast ("American 'Michael' Awards: More Advertising Than Art," by Robert Hilburn, Feb. 1). Categories have been developed to recognize a variety of musical styles and artists. The soul category traditionally has been reserved to honor black artists.

COLLEEN S. MORRIS

Pomona
