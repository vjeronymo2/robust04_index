930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / International Company News in Brief: Sandoz Sandoz, the Swiss pharmaceuticals and chemicals group, said Mac, its Italian subsidiary, has acquired Veneziani from MaxFin of Milan. MaxFin is a member of the Varasi group, Reuter reports from Zurich. Veneziani is a leading manufacturer of high-performance corrosion protection products for the construction industry. It was founded in 1863 and achieved sales of more than L40bn (Dollars 27m) in 1992. It employs 130 people in two plants. The terms for the sales were not disclosed.
