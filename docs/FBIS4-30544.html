BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 25 (KCNA) -- An inaugural meeting of the Mozambican committee for functions of solidarity with the Korean people was held in Maputo on June 16 on the occasion of the month of international solidarity with the Korean people (June 25-July 27). Manuel Jose Antonio, home minister and chairman of the Mozambican committee for friendship with the people of the DPRK, was elected chairman of the new-born committee. At the meeting, the chairman said various functions would be organised to denounce the crimes of U.S. imperialism and support the Korean people's cause of national reunification during the month of international solidarity with the Korean people. He vowed to take the lead in the efforts to support the Korean people's cause of socialism and actively contribute to invariably developing friendly relations with the Korean people.
