BFN [Text] Michel Rocard resigned his post of first secretary of the Socialist Party on today. The National Council of the Socialist Party issued a vote of no-confidence in Rocard, who had suggested broadening the leadership of the Socialist Party and "going beyond the ossification of factionalism." The resolution put forward by Michel Rocard received 88 votes in favor, 129 against, 48 abstentions, and two refusals to vote.
