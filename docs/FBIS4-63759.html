BFN [Unattributed item from roundup of ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA, ITAR-TASS reports under the "Yesterday, Today, and Tomorrow" rubric: "Are Chechens To Make Friends with Cossacks?"] [Text] President of the Chechen Republic Dzhokhar Dudayev has proposed to the Terek Cossacks that both cooperate for the sake of peace and order in the Caucasus. At his latest meeting with Terek Cossack Hetman Vasiliy Starodubtsev in Groznyy, he stated that there have not been and there still are no real conflicts between Chechens and Cossacks and they could definitely present a united front to resolve the majority of their problems. Representatives of the Terek Cossacks invited the Chechen president to their Great Assembly which will take place in the city of Kizlyar 28 May.
