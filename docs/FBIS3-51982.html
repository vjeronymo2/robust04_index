Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, in a solemn ceremony, Commander of the Revolution Juan Almeida Bosque, vice president of the Council of State, received the credentials of the ambassadors of Hungary and Mongolia. Mr. (Jozsef Nemeth) presented the documents that accredit him as ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the Republic of Hungary to Cuba. Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina participated in the ceremony. Afterwards, Mr. (Darigiyn Punzag) also presented his credentials which accredit him as the new ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of Mongolia to Cuba. The two diplomats earlier placed floral wreaths at the Jose Marti Monument.
