Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Taranov] [Text] Moscow March 27 TASS -- President Nursultan Nazarbayev of Kazakhstan has just arrived here. At Vnukovo Airport he was met by Russian Federation Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and Vice-Premier Aleksandr Shokhin. Nazarbayev's first official visit to the Russian Federation begins on Monday. A package of Russo-Kazakhstan documents are expected to be signed during the visit. The documents will be of great importance for the strengthening of bilateral cooperation. Experts of the two countries have on the whole completed their work on the drafting of Russo-Kazakhstan documents which are to be signed during the talks, according to a member of the Kazakhstan delegation.
