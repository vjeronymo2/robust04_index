930525 FT 25 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: Tory poll setback THE Tories have fallen to fourth place in Scotland, where they have the support of only 16 per cent of voters, an opinion poll for The Herald newspaper in Glasgow suggested yesterday. Voting intentions were: the Labour party 44 per cent, the Scottish National party 22 per cent, the Liberal Democrats 17 per cent and the Tories 16 per cent.
