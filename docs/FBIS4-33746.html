BFN [From Ronaldo Brasiliense's column: "JB Report"] [Text] President Itamar Franco already has surpassed Fernando Collor by far in terms of agrarian reform. In his 31 months in office, Collor expropriated only 20,000 hectares of land and assisted 494 families. During the Itamar Franco administration, 763,000 hectares have been expropriated and 19,000 families of landless rural workers assisted.
