930430 FT 30 APR 93 / UK Company News: Comac Comac Group, the USM-quoted computer consultant, lifted pre-tax profits by 9 per cent, from Pounds 362,424 to Pounds 394,723, in 1992 and is paying a final dividend of 1.5p. The result was struck after exceptional costs of Pounds 52,348 relating to an abortive acquisition. The group was continuing to strengthen its balance sheet and seeking a suitable acquisition. Turnover improved from Pounds 9.97m to Pounds 11.1m; operating income amounted to Pounds 481,710 (Pounds 439,641). Earnings per share slipped to 3.94p (4.05p).
