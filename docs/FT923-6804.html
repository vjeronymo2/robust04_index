920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Wagon Ind acquires Phoenix Supplies Wagon Industrial, the Telford-based materials handling, engineering and motor components group, has bought Phoenix Supplies, which provides presswork for the electrical and automotive industries. It is paying an initial Pounds 1.2m, comprising Pounds 435,000 cash, Pounds 365,000 in loan notes and 98,824 shares, and could pay a further Pounds 400,000 in loan notes next year depending on Phoenix's financial performance up to March 1993. Wagon plans to run the Phoenix business alongside its presswork operations, centred on its Edward Rose subsidiary.
