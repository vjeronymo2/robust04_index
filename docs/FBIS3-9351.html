Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES 22/3/1994 -- The Senate resumed Tuesday, March 22, the debates of the bill on war veterans and widows. The parliamentary group of the Hungarian Democratic Union of Romania, UDMR, asked for permission to make a political declaration on part of the articles of the said bill. Submitted to vote, the request was rejected. Consequently the members of the UDMR parliamentary group walked out of the Senate Hall. The Senate passed in its Monday, March 21 sitting an amendment to an article of the bill on war veterans and widows saying that the residents of Romanian territories occupied over 1940-1945, that were called to arms fight in the Armies of other states are considered to be war veterans only "if they did not fight against the Romanian Army." The members of the UDMR group opposed the amendment.
