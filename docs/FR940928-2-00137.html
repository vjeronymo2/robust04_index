SUMMARY: In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980 (title 44, U.S. Code, chapter 35), this notice announces a request for reclearance of an information collection. OPM Form 1530, Report of Medical Examination of Person Electing Survivor Benefit (CSRS), is used to collect sufficient information from the required medical examination regarding an annuitant's health. This information is then used to determine whether the insurable interest survivor benefits election can be allowed. Approximately 500 OPM 1530 forms are completed annually. It takes approximately 90 minutes to complete this form. The total annual burden is 750 hours. For copies of this proposal, contact C. Ronald Trueworthy on (703) 908&hyph;8550.
