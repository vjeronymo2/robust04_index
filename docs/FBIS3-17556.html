Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Ukraine -- China: the First Agreement Signed Between Regions"] [Text] An oblast state administration delegation headed by presidential representative in Kiev Oblast Ivan Kapshtyk returned home from the Chinese Hopeh Province. In view of the great significance of establishing and expanding mutually advantageous economic, scientific-technological, and cultural partnership between Kiev Oblast and the Hopeh Province, an agreement on friendship and cooperation between them was signed. "Together with deputy head of the government of the province Han Nanpen [name as transliterated], we have made a constructive step toward cooperation at the level of regions," said I. Kapshtyk. "Both we and they are very interested in investing in joint enterprises that are being created, in other forms of entrepreneurship, in the light and foodstuffs industry, in the sphere of powder metallurgy, and trade.
