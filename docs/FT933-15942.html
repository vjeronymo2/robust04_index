930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / World Stock Markets: Canada TORONTO eased in lacklustre trading as the US holiday subdued dealings. The TSE 300 index shed 7.2 to 3,990.5 and falls led rises by 388 to 303. The oil and gas index lost 1.7 per cent. Brokers said there was concern that Iraq would reach agreement with the UN to start selling oil on the world markets again after facing long-term sanctions. Wall Street was closed for Independence Day.
