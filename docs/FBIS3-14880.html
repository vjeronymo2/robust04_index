Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] At a meeting of members of the Cabinet of Ministers, North Ossetian President Akhsarbek Galazov said he was ready for dialogue with Ingushetia President Ruslan Aushev. He said: We need to implement the presidential decree and the decision by the Russian Government on returning refugees to their places of abode on the territory of North Ossetia and Ingushetia. Galazov categorically ruled out the possibility of granting additional powers to the interim administration on the territory of North Ossetia and Ingushetia and, for the first time, called for the lifting of the state of emergency regime on part of the territory of North Ossetia.
