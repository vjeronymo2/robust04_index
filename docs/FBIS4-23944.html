BFN [Text] Francisco Rupansana, co-chairman of the National Commission for Territorial Administration on the Mozambique National Resistance's [Renamo] side, says that there are no major problems in the process of reintegrating areas under Renamo control. Francisco Rupansana told Radio Mozambique in Chimoio that the government must carry out an efficient coordination work to once and for all resolve the problem of double administration. In Macossa District in Manica Province, Rupansana attended the signing of a contract by Renamo administrator Antonio Pedemo for the reintegration of the district in the country's single administration.
