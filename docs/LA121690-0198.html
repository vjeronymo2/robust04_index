December 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Jack Clark left the feuding San Diego Padres and agreed to a three-year, $8.7-million contract with the Boston Red Sox.

Clark, 35, could make as much as $10.2 million if he earns the $500,000 per year in performance bonuses. The four-time All-Star will be the designated hitter for the Red Sox.
