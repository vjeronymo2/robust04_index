November 16, 1990, Friday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURY COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: CAMARILLO;

COMMISSION OKS CHURCH ADDITION

The Planning Commission approved the construction of a 1,740-square-foot computer room at the historic St. Mary Magdalen Church.

The nearly five-acre parcel south of Ventura Boulevard and west of Lewis Road is the site of a parochial school and has been improved over the years with a chapel, convent, rectory and classroom buildings.

The newest addition will adjoin an existing building and will be finished with stucco and wood siding. RUSS CHARVONIA
