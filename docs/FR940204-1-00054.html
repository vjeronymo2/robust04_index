DEPARTMENT OF THE TREASURY Internal Revenue Service 26 CFR Part 1 [EE&hyph;61&hyph;93] RIN 1545&hyph;AS23 Disallowance of Deductions for Employee Remuneration in Excess of $1,000,000; Correction AGENCY: Internal Revenue Service (IRS), Treasury. ACTION: Correction to notice of proposed rulemaking.

SUMMARY: This document contains corrections to the notice of proposed rulemaking (EE&hyph;61&hyph;93), which was published in the Federal Register for Monday, December 20, 1993 (58 FR 66310). The proposed regulations relate to the disallowance of deductions for employee remuneration in excess of $1,000,000. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Robert Misner or Charles T. Deliee, (202) 622&hyph;6060 (not a toll-free call). SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: Background The notice of proposed rulemaking that is the subject of these corrections contains proposed amendments to the Income Tax Regulations (26 CFR part 1) under section 162(m) of the Internal Revenue Code. These regulations are proposed to conform the Income Tax Regulations to section 13211 of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1993 (Pub. L. 103&hyph;66), which added subsection (m) to section 162 of the Code. Need for Correction As published, the proposed rulemaking contains errors which may prove to be misleading and are in need of clarification. Correction of Publication Accordingly, the publication of proposed rulemaking (EE&hyph;61&hyph;93), which was the subject of FR Doc. 93&hyph;30993, is corrected as follows: §1.162&hyph;27 [Corrected] 1. On page 66314, column 2, §1.162&hyph;27(c)(3)(ii)(A), line 2, the language ``3121(a)(1) through section 3121(a)(5)(D)'' is corrected to read ``3121(a)(5)(A) through section 3121(a)(5)(D)''. 2. On page 66318, column 3, §1.162&hyph;27(e)(4)(viii), under Example 3 , lines 10 and 11 from the top of that column, the language ``disclose the maximum amount of compensation that any executive may receive'' is corrected to read ``disclose that the maximum amount of compensation any executive may receive''.
