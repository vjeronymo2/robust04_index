BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi stressed that the Israeli raid on al-Biqa' today was a major massacre that violated all human and moral values. Following his meeting today with President al-Hirawi, Milhim Karam, doyen of the newspaper editors, cited the president as saying that while Lebanon was following up on its complaint to the United Nations over the Israeli piracy of Lebanese water, it was hit by a new aggression that demolishes all the Israeli excuses and pretexts. The president stressed that Lebanon will proceed with the complaint on the theft of Lebanese water to ascertain the extent of this piracy. Karam added that President al-Hirawi mentioned that three brigades from the Lebanese Army will start a deployment in the al-Shuf and 'Alayh Districts on 15 June to ensure the return of the displaced persons.
