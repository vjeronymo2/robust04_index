920702 FT 02 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Exceptionals raise losses at CRP Losses at CRP Leisure, the USM-quoted company formerly known as PE Kemp, deepened from Pounds 155,181 to Pounds 195,668 over the six months to April 30. The figure, however, included an exceptional provision of Pounds 71,576 relating to the closure of Theatretheme. Turnover of continuing activities amounted to Pounds 625,718 but included only five weeks of the recent Pounds 3.2m purchase of CR Pursehouse. Losses per share were 1.2p (1.1p).
