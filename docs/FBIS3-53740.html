Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Israeli occupation forces have continued their attacks on villages and towns in the south. Israeli heavy artillery shelled Qabrikha, Wadi al-Saluqi, and the perimeters of the Tallusah position. This was accompanied by raking with machine guns. Earlier, the outskirts of al-Nabatiyah al-Fawqa and Kafr Tibnit came under artillery fire and raking with machine-gun fire. The area around Majdal Silm and Shaqra also came under artillery fire. Israeli helicopter gunships also strafed those areas. The Amal Movement has announced that one of its groups has attacked the Israeli position on Tallusah hill, using automatic weapons and mortars. A 15-minute clash ensued with the position's guards, during which the resistance fighters scored direct hits and destroyed the observation tower.
