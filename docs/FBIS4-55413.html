BFN [Excerpt] Zvornik, Bratunac, 15 May (TANJUG) -- In their attacks on some areas in the republic of Srpska [in Bosnia-Herzegovina] Muslim units have once again used chemical weapons, representatives of the VRS [army of the republic of Srpska] said today. Serb defensive positions on Mount Majevica and in Mala, Velika Jelaca, and Lisaca have been attacked by Muslims using shells filled with chlorine-based chemicals. In their attacks on Serbs from the Tuzla area Muslim units used chemicals on several occasions, although international conventions prohibit the use of such weapons. [passage omitted]
