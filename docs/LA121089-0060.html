December 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

DIFFERENT VIEWS ON 'MINORITY MOM'

Thanks for reminding your readers that though we're a minority, "stay-at-home moms" do still exist. But rather than call us "rare" and "vanishing," please substitute the words "endangered species." Vanishing means we're on our way out, but endangered means we could use a little protection. The media can start by resisting using the words "stay-at-home," "quaintly anachronistic," "an anomaly" or "a fantasy" to describe a full-time homemaker mom of the '90s.

MARILYN PASSWATER

Founder, Mom's Club of Redlands

Redlands
