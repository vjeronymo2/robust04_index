Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cotonou, 14 Feb (AFP) -- A Benin diplomat serving in the United States was questioned at New York Airport after being found with four kilos (eight pounds) of heroin, the Foreign Ministry said here Monday. The drug was hidden in the false bottom of a suitcase carried by Abdoul Karim as he arrived in the United States on February 3, the ministry added.
