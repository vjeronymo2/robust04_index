May 20, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

DARTS AND LAURELS TO THOSE INVOLVED WITH 'ROE VS. WADE'

How ironic that those who fancy themselves "pro-choice" howl the loudest when those who are against abortion choose to boycott certain products. Would they have us all be legally required to buy that which we do not choose to buy?

MARILOU D'ELIA

Woodland Hills
