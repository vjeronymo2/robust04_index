921212 FT 12 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Carr's Milling in profit WITH ALL trading sectors performing better, Carr's Milling Industries returned to pre-tax profits for the year ended August 29, making Pounds 277,000, against a loss of Pounds 228,000. The profit was struck after rationalisation costs of Pounds 237,000 in the baking division. Below the line there were extraordinary charges of Pounds 405,000 covering a decision to withdraw from the loss-making egg production and packing business, which will be completed within the next few months. Turnover rose to Pounds 70.8m (Pounds 68.9m) and included Pounds 2.6m from egg production and packing. Earnings per share were 2.6p (losses 1.2p) and the final dividend is 2.9p for a maintained total of 3.9p.
