920808 FT 08 AUG 92 / International Company News: Molex ahead in final quarter MOLEX, a leading US electronic components company, reported fourth-quarter net income of Dollars 19.6m, or 39 cents a share, up from Dollars 17.2m, or 35 cents. Revenues in the quarter rose 11.5 per cent to Dollars 208.2m from Dollars 186.8m. For its full fiscal 1992 year, Molex increased its net income to Dollars 67.5m, or Dollars 1.35 a share, from Dollars 64.6m, or Dollars 1.30. Revenues for the year were a record Dollars 776.2m, a gain of 9.6 per cent over last year's Dollars 708m. Molex, a Fortune 500 company with operations in 20 countries, said its results exceeded the general performance of the electronic connector industry. Research and development spending rose 17 per cent to Dollars 47.6m in the year.
