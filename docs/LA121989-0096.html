December 19, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ADAM KEEFE NAMED PAC-10 PLAYER OF WEEK

Stanford forward Adam Keefe, a graduate of Woodbridge High School, was named the Pacific-10 Conference player of the week Monday.

Keefe, a 6-foot-9 sophomore from Irvine, made 11 of 12 shots and had six rebounds in a 74-53 victory over San Francisco. Stanford is 5-0.
