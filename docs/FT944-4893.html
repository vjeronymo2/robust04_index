941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / World Stock Markets: Johannesburg golds weaken Shares in South Africa were mixed as a weak bullion price depressed golds, but industrials firmed in line with gains on global equity markets. The overall index shed 0.8 to 5,719.4, industrials were 32.9 better at 6,912.5 and golds lost 30.3 at 1,881.1. De Beers gained R1 at R90, but Anglos slipped R1.50 to R228. Reflecting the soft sentiment among golds, Kloof fell R1 to R55.75 and Vaal Reefs R5 to R345. Platinum losses were limited, with Rusplats off 50 cents at R106. Elsewhere, Remgro put on 50 cents at R28 and SAB improved 50 cents to R97.75.
