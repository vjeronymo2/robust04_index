December 30, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

EVOLUTION, CREATIONISM AND PUBLIC EDUCATION

In reference to the editorial, "Creationists Should Give It A Rest," Dec. 16, the author states "These recurrent and narrow efforts to foist a particular religious view . . . "

It seems to me that by only teaching one theory in the schools and never mentioning that there are others and what they are is by far the more narrow view.

JO LYNNE GLINES

Cerritos
