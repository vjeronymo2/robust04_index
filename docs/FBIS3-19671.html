Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Armenian Foreign Minister Vahan Papazyan today met NATO Secretary-General Manfred Woerner at NATO headquarters in Brussels. The sides discussed wide-ranging issues during the meeting, including the peaceful resolution of the Karabakh conflict and matters pertaining to the Partnership for Peace program. Vahan Papazyan and Manfred Woerner expressed readiness to continue cooperation and consultations. Armenian ambassador to the UK Armen Sargisyan and Armenian Permanent Representative to NATO Badalyan were present at the meeting.
