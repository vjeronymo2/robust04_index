BFN [Text] Azeri President Geydar Aliyev favors the signing of a comprehensive political agreement, which would solve all problems associated with the Armenian-Azeri conflict in a just manner. In line with the document, international forces or observers will be deployed in the region and the problem of security for Karabakh Armenians will be solved, said the president during the meeting in Baku on Wednesday with the chairman of the Minsk CSCE group, Jan Eliasson. The president repeated the position of Azerbaijan according to which it is necessary to observe the cease-fire and achieve the liberation of the captured territory of Azerbaijan. Aliyev accused Armenia of dual policies towards the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe, for example, of Yerevan's refusal to sign the CSCE plan for the conflict settlement, said the Azeri leader. Eliasson said that the CSCE should act as a mediator in the settlement of the Armenian-Azeri conflict.
