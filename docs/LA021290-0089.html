February 12, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

WATER SHORTAGE: Not everyone heard right away about the recall of Perrier, the chic French mineral water (A3). . . . At Farmer's Market in chic Newport Beach, the manager said "not one" customer had asked Sunday about the recall. . . . Nevertheless, owner Mike Walker said, "I pulled it off the shelf as soon as I learned it was on every TV channel." Dana Parsons and Kristina Lindgren
