Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organization, Yasir 'Arafat, is to visit Switzerland for the first time with Swiss government officials. The Swiss Foreign Ministry in Berne said no agenda had yet been set for the talks between Mr. 'Arafat and the Swiss president, Otto Stich, as well as the foreign minister, Flavio Cotti. However, one ministry spokesman said Switzerland's invitation to the PLO to open an office in Bern would be discussed. Correspondents say his visit is likely to coincide with the annual world economic forum which begins in Davos on 27 January.
