Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Gulf sources have disclosed that a meeting was held in London last Sunday [23 January] between Qatari Foreign Minister Shaykh Hamad Bin-Jasim and Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres and Minister of Energy Shahal [title as received]. They discussed ways to supply Israel with gas, an issue which AL-ANWAR has mentioned before. The sources said the talks took place without the knowledge or consent of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, whose territory the gas pipes must pass through to reach Israel. Asked by AL-ANWAR of his view on these talks, a Gulf Cooperation Council [GCC] official said he had not heard about these contacts and meetings before. Any developments between Qatar and Israel, he added, are absolutely not in accord with GCC policy.
