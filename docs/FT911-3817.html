910423 FT 23 APR 91 / International Company News: US insurance broker ahead MARSH & McLennan, the world largest insurance broker, yesterday reported firstquarter income of Dollars 96.6m after tax, against Dollars 94.2m in the same period a year earlier. The increase was scored on total revenues up to Dollars 744.1m from Dollars 695.7m. The rise in operating income was slightly larger - up to Dollars 171.2m from Dollars 161.8m after expenses increased by 7.3 per cent to Dollars 572.9m. But interest income was reduced to Dollars 6.7m from Dollars 7.5m.
