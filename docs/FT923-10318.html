920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / Observer: Dear John . . . Whoever the political limelight has been falling on lately, it is certainly not Liberal Democrat leader Paddy Ashdown. So perhaps he's relying on not getting any publicity for his latest letter to John Major. Addressing the plight of refugees from former Yugoslavia, Ashdown begins: 'You were magnificent when, as chairman of the G7, you led the world in taking action on the tragedy of the Kurds.' With opponents like that, who needs friends?
