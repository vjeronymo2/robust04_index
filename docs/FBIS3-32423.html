Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A delegation of Haitian legislators is visiting the United States for high-level consultations. The Haitian legislators, including Firmin Jean-Louis and Frantz Robert Monde, are scheduled to meet with officials of the U.S. State Department and, possibly, with President-in-Exile Jean-Bertrand Aristide. Minister of National Education Victor Benoit, U.S. Ambassador William Lacy Swing, and Dante Caputo's political adviser, Leandro Despouilles, are with the delegation. Haitian legislative delegates include Senator Julio Larosiliere; the president of the Chamber of Deputies, Deputy Frantz Robert Monde; and Deputies Jean Talendier [Desjardins] and Jean-Jacques Seignon.
