940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Dyson acquisition As part of its diversification into ceramics and refractories, J&J Dyson has acquired Norton of Stoke-on-Trent's Gimson secondary kiln furniture and chimney liner business. The business, which is expected to make annual profits of about Pounds 250,000, has been bought through its subsidiary, Dyson Industries, at a cost of Pounds 2.3m.
