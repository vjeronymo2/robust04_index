Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Budapest, February 4 (XINHUA) -- China and Hungary signed the 1994 sports cooperation agreement here Friday [4 February]. Under the agreement, officials of the Hungarian National Sports Bureau will visit China this year. Among the other sports exchanges, Hungary will send swimming coaches to China to help train Chinese swimmers, and field table tennis players to compete and train in China. In return, Chinese swimmers and canoeing team will take part in international tournaments held in Hungary during the year.
