September 8, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

Glendale 36, Alhambra 13 -- Junior running back Pathon Rucker rushed for 191 yards in 25 carries and scored three touchdowns to lead Glendale at Alhambra.

Rucker scored touchdowns on runs of four, 12 and 15 yards, and also ran for a two-point conversion that gave Glendale a 14-7 lead late in the second quarter. KIRBY LEE
