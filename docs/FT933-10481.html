930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / National Savings raises rates NATIONAL SAVINGS, the government financing arm, has increased the interest rates it pays to larger investors. Those who invest more than Pounds 25,000 in income bonds will receive 7.25 per cent (up from 7 per cent) from tomorrow. The rate paid on investment account balances of more than Pounds 25,000 will be increased from 6.25 per cent to 6.5 per cent. In each case the rates paid on investments of less than Pounds 25,000 will be unchanged.
