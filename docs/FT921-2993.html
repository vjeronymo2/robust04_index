920317 FT 17 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Thai-Euro Fund first dividend Thai-Euro Fund is proposing a maiden dividend after outperforming the Thai stock market index in the second half and boosting net asset value at the year end from Dollars 16.55 (Pounds 9.40p) to Dollars 18.40. Total revenue was up at Dollars 5.95m (Dollars 5.3m) and retained revenue stood at Dollars 1.18m (Dollars 2.28m). Earnings per share came out at 38.2 cents (30.4 cents) and the proposed dividend is 22.5 cents.
