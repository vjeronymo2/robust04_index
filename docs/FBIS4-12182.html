BFN [Text] Damascus April 5, IRNA -- Visiting Vice Speaker of Iran's Parliament (Majlis) Hojjat ol-Eslam Hasan Ruhani told a group of Iraqi dissidents here Tuesday that Iran felt itself obliged to support Iraqi Muslims. He further expressed Iran's concern for the fate of the Iraqi Muslims and said the Iraqi nation would surely come to master its own destiny in a near future. Ruhani, also the secretary of the supreme National Security Council, commented that the arrogant world employed all in its might to hinder the victory of the Islamic revolution and liberation of the Iraqi nation. He urged the Iraqi opposition to have solidarity to get the best from the present conditions and play a more active role in the international circles.
