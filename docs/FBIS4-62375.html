BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Roman Zadunaiskiy] [Text] Moscow May 13 TASS -- The Federal Counterintelligence Service (FCS) detained on Friday a group of people suspected of saboteur acts on transport and of other crimes committed in Russia and other countries of the Commonwealth of Independent States, ITAR- TASS learnt at the FCS public relations centre. The service instituted a criminal case. It is conducting search and other investigative measures together with the chief military prosecutor's office.
