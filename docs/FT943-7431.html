940823 FT 23 AUG 94 / International Company News: Bombardier up in second term Bombardier, the inter-national aerospace, transit equipment and snowmobile group, said second-quarter net profit rose 40 per cent on a revenue gain of 6 per cent. Mr Laurent Beaudoin, chairman, said the profit gain came from strength in all sectors. Profit for the three months ended July 31 was CDollars 54.5m (USDollars 39.4), or 33 cents a share, against CDollars 39.2m, or 25 cents, a year earlier. Revenues were CDollars 1.17bn against CDollars 1.11bn. First-half profit was CDollars 106.9m, or 64 cents a share, up 36 per cent from CDollars 78.4m, or 50 cents. Revenues were CDollars 2.34bn, up just over 1 per cent.
