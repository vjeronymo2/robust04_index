930424 FT 24 APR 93 / Finance & The Family: Nationwide postal account - At a Glance A new postal account is being launched on Monday by Nationwide building society. The minimum investment for opening InvestDirect is Pounds 2,000. The account pays a tiered rate of intrest from 5 per cent gross on amounts up to Pounds 10,000 through to 6.7 per cent gross on Pounds 100,000 and above. Interest rate details are available by calling 0800 400417. The account comes with a cashcard for cash withdrawals. See pIV
