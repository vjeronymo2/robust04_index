Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Asmara, January 13 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen wound up his two-day visit to Eritrea today and left for the Sudan. During his visit to Eritrea, Qian Qichen, who is also Chinese vice-premier, held talks respectively with Eritrean President Isayas Afewerki and Foreign Minister Muhammad Sharifo. They exchanged views on the bilateral relations and the world affairs. Qian Qichen signed an agreement with Sherifo on donating goods to Eritrea. This is the first visit of the Chinese foreign minister to Eritrea since the two countries established the diplomatic relations in May last year.
