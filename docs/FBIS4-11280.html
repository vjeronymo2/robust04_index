BFN [Text] This morning, Unit No. 4 of the Carlos Manuel de Cespedes power plant in Cienfuegos, with a generating capacity of 158 megawatts, had to be taken offline due to a rupture in one of the unit's boiler pipes. This situation will cause greater outage problems than those that took place over the past weekend. Jorge Carballo of the Electric Union pointed out that this unit should be repaired within the next 72 hours. He added that workers are now waiting for the boiler to cool down to begin repairs. When this unit goes back into service, units No. 1 of Santa Cruz del Norte and No. 6 of Diez de Octubre will be taken offline for maintenance.
