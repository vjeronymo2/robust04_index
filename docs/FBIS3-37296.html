Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "The Ukrainian Civic Congress of the Crimea Has Finally Been Registered"] [Text] It took the leadership of the Ukrainian Civic Congress of the Crimea [UHCC] more than two months to have its party registered. The documents were returned several times for amendment. Finally, the long-awaited registration certificate has been received at the Crimean Ministry of Justice. The party is in favor of strengthening the status of the Crimean Republic as an autonomous and integral part of Ukraine. It will defend the interests of the peninsula's Ukrainian population. It will give much attention to military issues, especially the political and social protection of those Ukrainian servicemen who are serving in the Crimean Republic. The party intends to participate in the Crimean Supreme Council elections.
