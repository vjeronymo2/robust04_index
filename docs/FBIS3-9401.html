Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [TA SR report: "Slovaks Trust the United Nations"] [Text] Bratislava--The Public Opinion Research Center at the Slovak Defense Ministry has conducted a survey. From the point of view of the security of Slovakia, 25.6 percent of the respondents trust the UN most, 12.4 percent the European security system, and 11.4 percent NATO membership. The citizens showed most trust in the UN--53.9 percent--while 23.1 percent trust NATO. Of the respondents, 44.7 percent were in favor of Slovakia's joining NATO, while 20.2 percent opposed it. The remaining 35.1 percent could not decide. NATO was described as more or less the only guarantor of European security by 55.4 percent, while 25.2 percent disagreed with this.
