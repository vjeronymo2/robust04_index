BFN [Text] The Worl Bank's Board of Directors approved a loan of $200 million to Russia. The statement from the major international credit and financial organization says that these funds will be directed to giving technical assistance to private banks and to helping Russia's Central Bank to train inspectors and to create a system of early discovery of problem banks. The European Bank of Reconstruction and Development, the European Community, and private investors will also take part in financing this program. The $200 million is being granted to Russia for a term of 17 years. No payments need to be made on it for the first five years.
