BFN [Text] According to information received tonight by the Cyprus Broadcasting Corporation correspondent in New York, intense negotiations are to be held at the United Nations after the collapse of efforts by Joe Clark, the UN secretary general's representative in Cyprus, to implement the confidence-building measures. Clark is expected to arrive in New York tonight and will have an emergency meeting with UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali. UN Security Council circles told our correspondent that Clark is disappointed with the deadlock and is seriously considering whether to renew his contract, which expires on 15 May.
