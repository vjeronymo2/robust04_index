931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Mexican stocks continued their record breaking ways, the IPC index closing 62.05, or 2.7 per cent, up at 2,386.14. Investors have been encouraged by reports that two major rating agencies could lift the country's investment rating, which currently stands at BB+. Telmex led buying activity, with US gains lifting the 'L' shares by 2.9 per cent.
