940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Fleming Chinese The Fleming Chinese Investment Trust had a net asset value per share of 92.2p at March 31, compared with an initial value of 97.2p when the shares commenced trading on October 19. For the period from September 10, the date of incorporation, to March 31, net revenue totalled Pounds 93,541, giving earnings of 0.16p per share. Total assets amounted to Pounds 55.6m.
