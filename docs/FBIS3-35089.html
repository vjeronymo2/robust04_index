Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres says Israel will take all the necessary measures to stop Lebanese terrorists from launching katyusha rockets towards northern Israel. Peres made the comment after a meeting in Jerusalem this morning with his Portuguese counterpart, Jose Durao Barroso. The foreign minister of Portugal said his country supports the new trade agreement between Israel and the European Union.
