Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow February 3 TASS -- The date of a visit of Japanese Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata to Russia will be agreed upon after consultations between deputy foreign ministers of the two countries and meetings of a working group on peace treaty, scheduled for 21-22 February, a high ranking official of the Russian Foreign Ministry told ITAR-TASS on Thursday. Hata was invited to Russia on an official visit in October 1993 when President Boris Yeltsin was in Japan.
