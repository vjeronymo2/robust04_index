Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] One more word on the NATO summit to say that Vladimir Zhirinovskiy has denounced the French proposal on air strikes in Bosnia. In his tactful style, he described the proposal as being fascist. He said so in the Duma corridor today during the first session of the two chambers of the new parliament. Before it started its work, the Duma observed two minutes of silence in memory of the victims of the revolt of last October.
