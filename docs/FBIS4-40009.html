BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko, Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev 10 June TASS -- An inconsiderable increase of technological leakage of contaminated water from the Chernobyl nuclear power plant's basin of worked-out fuel assemblies has been registered today, head of the Ukrainian State Committee for Nuclear Power Engineering's Emergency-Dispatcher Department Sergey Nazarenko told ITAR-TASS. In his words, the system of technological water collection excludes their leakage in the environment and provides for reliable collection of the contaminated water and its processing. The radiation situation at the Chernobyl nuclear power plant is now normal, he said.
