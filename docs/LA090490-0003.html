September 4, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

COST OF U.S. INTERVENTION IN THE MIDEAST

We were quick to do everything to mobilize the military to defend Saudi Arabia.

We were quick to do nothing to defend those the soldiers left at home from price gouging.

We were quick to do nothing to defend working mothers who need leave time to bear America's newborn babes -- our future soldiers. In fact the Administration mobilized its forces and vetoed the legislation granting such leave.

ALBERT NASSER

Irvine
