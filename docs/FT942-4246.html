940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Caffyns jumps to Pounds 707,000 Despite an increase in stock write-downs Caffyns, the motor dealer, reported a jump in pre-tax profits from Pounds 262,000 to Pounds 707,000 for the year to March 31. Turnover increased to Pounds 150.9m (Pounds 123.9m). Operating profits moved ahead to Pounds 1.7m (Pounds 1.42m) and the pre-tax result was helped by lower interest charges of Pounds 993,000 (Pounds 1.16m). Earnings per share advanced to 14.7p (0.8p) and the dividend is held at 11.5p with an unchanged final of 6.5p proposed.
