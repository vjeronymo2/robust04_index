920327 FT 27 MAR 92 / International Company News: Au Printemps surges to FFr1.86bn AU PRINTEMPS, the Paris stores group, unveiled a six-fold increase in profits for last year, boosted by a capital gain on the sale of a stake in the Euromarche and Viniprix supermarket chains. Group net profits rose to FFr1.86bn (Dollars 330m) from FFr317m in 1990, on sales up by 6 per cent at FFr31.28bn from FFr29.38bn. Earnings included a FFr1.84bn exceptional gain, against a FFr41m one-off gain in the previous year. Au Printemps received FFr2.26bn for the sale of its stakes last June in Euromarche and Viniprix to rival chain Carrefour.
