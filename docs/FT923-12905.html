920716 FT 16 JUL 92 / Algeria jails Islamic leaders AN Algerian court sentenced Islamic Salvation Front (FIS) leaders Abassi Madani and Ali Benhadj to 12 years in prison yesterday, Reuter reports from Algiers. They were charged with conspiracy against the state, which carries the death penalty. Their party, now outlawed, was blamed for plunging Algeria into 13 months of violence and political turmoil.
