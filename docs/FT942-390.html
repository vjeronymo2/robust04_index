940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Computer People to buy Span for Pounds 5.5m Computer People is to buy Span, which supplies information technology consultants, for Pounds 5.5m. The consideration will be satisfied by Pounds 2.4m in cash, Pounds 2.1m in unsecured loan notes and the issue of 561,798 shares. Computer People is planning to raise Pounds 5.8m through a placing and open offer of 3.96m shares at 158p, with a 2-for-7 clawback. The proceeds will provide the cash element for the acquisition and the balance used for refinancing Span's existing factoring arrangements. Computer People shares were unchanged at 170p yesterday.
