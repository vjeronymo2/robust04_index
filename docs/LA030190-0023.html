March 1, 1990, Thursday, South Bay Edition

GOLF TOURNAMENT FOR FOUNDATION ON APRIL 9

The El Segundo Educational Foundation, which raises supplemental funds for the El Segundo school system, will hold its second annual "Strokes of Genius" Golf Tournament April 9 at 9:30 a.m. at MountainGate Country Club, Interstate 405 at Mulholland Drive, West Los Angeles. For fee and sign-up information, call 772-2091, Ext. 225.
