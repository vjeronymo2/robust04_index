921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / International Company News: UK Company News in Brief PETROFINA, Belgium's largest industrial company, said it cut 1992 capital spending by 10-20 per cent against an initial budget of BFr52bn (Dollars 1.6bn), following an expected sharp fall in 1992 profit, Reuter reports. However, Petrofina said it would not institute any reductions affecting strategic projects of the company. The company said it had drawn up a capital spending budget of about BFr40bn in 1993, but gave no precise estimated final spending figure for 1992. ***** ABN Amro, the Netherlands' largest banking group, expects to make a public tender offer at between Fl 15 and Fl 16 a share for the stock it does not already own in Breevast, the property investment group, AP-DJ reports from Amsterdam, The Dutch bank said it holds about 21 per cent of Breevast's total shares outstanding. It did not give a time frame for the start of any offer.
