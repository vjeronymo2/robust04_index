BFN [Excerpt] Beijing, 24 Jun (XINHUA) -- The State Physical Culture and Sports Commission and the Chinese Federation for the Disabled held a news conference at the Great Hall of the People today and announced: A 602-member Chinese athletic delegation to the Sixth Far East and South Pacific Regional Handicapped People's Games was formally inaugurated. State Councillors Li Tieying and Peng Peiyun are honorary chairmen. Wu Qingtong, vice president of the Chinese Federation for the Disabled, is delegation head. [passage games]
