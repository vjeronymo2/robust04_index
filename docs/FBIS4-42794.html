BFN [Text] The National Council convened a meeting approximately one-half hour ago to continue discussion on the situation after the deadlock in the confidence-building measures [CBM's] and the UN report which established the lack of political will by the Turkish side. The debate opened last Friday [3 June] and President Glavkos Kliridhis verbally submitted suggestions. There was an initial exchange of views on the UN report and the future of the Cyprus issue. Attendees at today's meeting include Ioannis Matsis and Alekos Markidhis on behalf of the Democratic Rally [DISI]; Dhimitrios Khristofias and Andreas Khristou representing AKEL [Restorative Party of the Working People]; Spiros Kiprianou and Tasos Papadhopoulos representing DIKO [Democratic Party]; and Vasos Lissaridhis and Takis Khatzidhimitriou on behalf of EDEK [United Democratic Union of Cyprus]. Also present at the meeting are House of Representatives President Alexis Galanos, Government Spokesman Ioannis Kasoulidhis, Attorney General Mikhail Triandafillidhis, and Deputy Minister to President Pandelis Kouros.
