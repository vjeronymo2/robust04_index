Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chairman, Jammu Kashmir Liberation Front [JKLF], Mr. Amanullah Khan, arrived in Karachi early this morning after being released by the Belgian Government. He was received at the airport by the Kashmiri leaders and a large number of party workers. They chanted slogans in favor of independence of Kashmir and condemned the continuing Indian atrocities in occupied Kashmir. Mr. Amanullah was arrested in Brussels on 18 October when he had gone to attend a seminar on Kashmir. Talking to newsmen at the airport, the chairman, JKLF, welcomed the secretary-level talks on Kashmir between Pakistan and India, which are scheduled to be held in Islamabad on Sunday [2 January]. The Kashmiri leader, however, emphasized that the Kashmir problem must be resolved in accordance with the wishes of the Kashmiri people.
