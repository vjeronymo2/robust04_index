Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, January 9 (ATA) -- Fifty Albanian young fellows from Kosova were forcefully recruited by the Serbian Army during 1993. This is a special form of the repression of Serbian officials, a report of the Human Rights and Freedoms Defence Council in Prishtina said. The data provided by the report show that Albanian fellows who were forced to join the Serbian Army were born from 1966 to 1974. Most of them were arrested in streets or their homes and some of them were brought to military court. All Albanian recruits were sent to the barracks of the Serbian Army established outside Kosova.
