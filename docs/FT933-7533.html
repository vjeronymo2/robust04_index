930820 FT 20 AUG 93 / International Company News: US Justice Dept. says it will not oppose NY Times acquisition THE US Justice Department said it would not oppose the Dollars 1.1bn acquisition by the New York Times Co of the Boston Globe in what has been called the biggest deal in US newspaper history. The deal makes the Times the biggest newspaper chain in the north-east US. The Globe, the nation's 13th largest daily, has a daily circulation of 505,000 and 811,000 on Sunday. The Times has daily circulation of 1.2m and 1.8m onSunday, making it the nation's biggest metropolitan daily.
