Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] At the Stanford Hotel on the afternoon of 27 January, the Singapore Chinese General Chamber of Commerce sponsored a meeting on Henan Province's foreign economic cooperation and investment environment. Henan provincial party Secretary Li Changchun briefed the participants on the province's investment environment and expressed a warm welcome to Singapore industrialists and businessmen's cooperating with Henan. [passage omitted] Li Changchun's written speech was distributed to the participants. Li Changchun also explained to them Henan's five favorable conditions for the introduction of foreign investment. First, Henan is located in the central plains and has convenient railway and highway transportation facilities and three civilian airports: Zhengzhou Airport, Luoyang Airport, and Nanyang Airport. Zhengzhou International Airport is still under construction and will be completed in 1996. Second, it has a variety of mineral resources. Its outputs of farm products, industrial crops, and animal husbandry rank in the forefront of the country. It has adequate electric power supply. Third, it has a long history and beautiful mountains and rivers and is rich in cultural relic resources. Fourth, it is rich in labor resources and has low labor costs and hard-working and honest people. Fifth, the two high-tech development zones in Zhengzhou and Luoyang, which have been approved by the State Council, provide good environments for foreign investors in Henan. The State Council has also provided Zhengzhou City with the preferential policies offered to Chinese open coastal areas. In addition, we have the same language for communication with each other, similar cultures, and the same habits and customs. This is also a favorable condition. [passage omitted]
