930417 FT 17 APR 93 / Slovaks take to the streets More than 4,500 Slovaks took to the streets yesterday in Slovakia's biggest labour protest against government policies since independence on January 1, Reuter reports from Bratislava. At a mass rally outside the headquarters of the Slovak Confederation of Trade Unions, speakers accused the government of Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar of failing to cope with Slovakia's rapidly deteriorating economy. Trade union leaders have been alarmed by forecasts that unemployment will reach 20 per cent this year.
