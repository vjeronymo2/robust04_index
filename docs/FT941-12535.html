940129 FT 29 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Lookers Lookers, the Manchester-based motor group, is to sell its lossmaking Burtree Caravans business to Dave Barron Caravans for Pounds 2.8m. Burtree, based in Darlington, Co Durham, sells new and used static and touring caravans, trailer tents and accessories. Lookers said the sale would release capital employed of some Pounds 2m. Proceeds will be used to reduce borrowings.
