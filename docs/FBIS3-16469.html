Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Aleksey Agureyev and Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow March 17 TASS -- Relations between the United States and Russia are the factor determining the degree of security in the whole world. This is why so much importance is being attached to contacts between the leaders of their defence authorities. This was the key idea behind today's meeting in Moscow between Russian Defence Minister Pavel Grachev and U.S. Defence Secretary William Perry. A personal relationship between the two countries' defence ministers is no less important and need further deepening, the meeting was told. Following talks between the two delegations, William Perry and Pavel Grachev are due to hold a one-to-one conversation. The Russian minister told journalists he wanted to discuss with his opposite number from the United States the situation in Bosnia, Russia's participation in the Partnership for Peace programme, joint peacekeeping, the problem of nuclear arms, including their withdrawal from Ukraine and a wide range of problems relating to better relations between the military authorities of the two countries. Also today, William Perry is due to meet First Russian Deputy Defence Minister Andrey Kokoshin and Chief of Staff Mikhail Kolesnikov. According to American sources, Perry, 66, is a true professional with deep knowledge and extensive experience. He favors the development of the Stealth bombers, Cruise missiles and high- precision electronic-optical homing devices.
