941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Aberdeen Trust lifted to Pounds 5m The launch of new products and the general strength of global stock markets helped Aberdeen Trust, the fund management and accountancy services group, hoist pre-tax profits from Pounds 3.15m to Pounds 5.04m for the year to September 30. The improved result - which was struck after writing off goodwill of Pounds 1.5m - was further reflected in a Pounds 3.8m cut in net borrowings which left gearing at 23 per cent (45 per cent). Turnover rose to Pounds 17.7m (Pounds 14.8m). Earnings per share climbed to 4.01p (2.46p) while the final dividend is 1.4p for a total of 2p (1.5p).
