BFN [Text] Humberto Rodriguez Gonzalez, president of the People's Government in Santa Clara, has stated that the current process in which delegates present progress reports to their voters has begun in Villa Clara Province and is going well. Rodriguez said that the idea of backing the measures approved by the national parliament has prevailed at the meetings, and that the delegates are well-prepared, fully informed on every matter concerning the population, in an effort to find solutions, or at least study the causes, of the difficulties. The Santa Clara Administrative Council has agreed to begin having locally run enterprises reflect upon application of and compliance with the policy of (?cadres), very particularly those things having to do with workers' living standards. For this latter, an overall program will be formulated that will encourage an administrative leader to feel like one of the workers, but with greater responsibilities.
