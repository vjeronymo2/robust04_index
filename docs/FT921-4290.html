920310 FT 10 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Ramus Holdings back in the black Sales promotions and strict cost controls enabled Ramus Holdings, a manufacturer of ceramic tiles, kitchens and bedroom furniture, to return to the black for the half year ended December 31. Profits of Pounds 142,000 pre-tax compared with previous losses of Pounds 278,000. The company also achieved a 15 per cent increase in turnover to Pounds 27m. Earnings per share came out at 2.3p against losses of 4.3p last time. Force One Distributors, acquired for Pounds 108,000 at the end of December, was expected to contribute to profits in the next financial year.
