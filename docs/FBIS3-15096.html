Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Edict by Head of State Eduard Shevardnadze on setting up a republican committee to resolve issues connected with the agro-industrial complex] [Excerpts] In order to review the issues connected with the agro-industrial complex and resolve them in good time: a. A republican committee is to be established with the following composition: Georgian Prime Minister Otar Patsatsia to be chairman of the committee; Roin Liparteliani, chairman of the Georgian parliament's agrarian commission, to be deputy chairman of the committee. [passage omitted] b. The committee is regularly to discuss supplying agriculture with the raw material from the processing industry; discuss the issues connected with the implementation of interstate responsibilities for supplying the agricultural complex with lubricant materials, minerals, pesticides, and other materials, and supplying the state with foodstuffs; and discuss the formation of state resources, the improvement of the criminal situation in the republic, getting the country out of the crisis, and other problems connected with the work of the committee. The committee is to map out and resolve operationally the measures for overcoming the abovementioned matters. c. Ministries, departments, establishments, organizations, and the bodies of the authorities are to carry out the resolutions adopted by the committee. [signed] Eduard Shevardnadze
