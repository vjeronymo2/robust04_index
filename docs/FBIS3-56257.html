Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] According to Tajikistan's Foreign Ministry, the armed Tajik opposition is preventing Tajik refugees from returning home from Afghanistan. A Foreign Ministry source told Interfax on Thursday [13 January] that refugees were held up by opposition detachments based in Afghanistan's Konduz Province. The source said the last meeting between Tajik and Afghan officials to discuss the refugees' repatriation took place near the Afghan town of Shir Khan on Wednesday. Representatives of the Konduz governor once again assured the Tajik side that they would take urgent measures to unblock the road between the town of Konduz and the Shir Khan port to enable the refugees to return.
