BFN [Text] Yangon [Rangoon], June 23 (XINHUA) -- Myanmar [Burma] leader Senior General Than Shwe said here today that the visit of the Chinese military delegation to Myanmar will not only strengthen the understanding and friendship between the two armies but also contribute to the development of cooperation and relation between Myanmar and China. At the invitation of Myanmar Ministry of Defense, the Chinese goodwill military delegation of the People's Liberation Army [PLA], led by General Li Jiulong, commander of PLA Chengdu Military Region, arrived here on Wednesday to pay a one-week friendly visit to Myanmar. Also chairman of the Myanmar State Law and Order Restoration Council (SLORC) and commander-in-chief of defense services, than Shwe also said that he hopes that the exchange of the visits and the cooperation between the two countries and the two armies will further strengthen the friendly relationship, established by old leaders of the two countries, must be kept on generation by generation.
