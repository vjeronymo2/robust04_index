BFN [Report by Vitaliy Denisov under the "KRASNAYA ZVEZDA and News Agency Correspondents Report" rubric: "Will `Blue Helmets' Suit the National Guard?"] [Text] Tbilisi -- "All states belonging to the CIS must have peacemaking battalions, since we cannot rely on Russia all the time," Georgian parliamentary deputy Tengiz Kitovani believes. He has advocated the creation of the republic's own peacemaking units, so as to have the possibility of participating in the settlement of conflicts in neighboring regions. He proposes to form battalions of "blue helmets" on the basis of the National Guard and the recently disbanded military police. At the same time Kitovani ignored the question of where it is proposed to find the funds to maintain them.
