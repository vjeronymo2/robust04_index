920615 FT 15 JUN 92 / Construction Contracts: Army accommodation Contracts worth more than Pounds 14m have been awarded in the eastern region to RATTEE AND KETT, the Cambridge-based division of Mowlem South East. The largest contract, at Pounds 3.2m, has been awarded by Noreast Building Management and involves the construction of single living accommodation for junior army ranks at Waterbeach Barracks. Work has started and phased completion is scheduled for January 1994. Two contracts are in progress for Ciba-Geigy at Duxford. The first is for a Pounds 1.5m extension to the company's warehouse facilities at the agricultural division. The second involves the design, supply, construction and commissioning of a formulating plant for the plastics division. Rattee and Kett is handling the civil engineering and building work worth Pounds 742,000, whilst Mowlem Engineering is handling the process plant.
