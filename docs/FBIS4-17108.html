BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow, 21 Apr -- Boris Yeltsin hopes the Russian and Ukrainian military will be able to agree, Vyacheslav Kostikov, the Russian Federation president's press secretary told a Kremlin briefing today. He said "Defense Minister Grachev, now in Sevastopol, will be able to find a common language with the Ukrainian military and common sense will prevail." Vyacheslav Kostikov also noted that Boris Yeltsin's view concerning the Black Sea fleet could be described as cautious and moderate optimism. Commenting on a KOMSOMOLSKAYA PRAVDA article on the possibility of war between Ukraine and Russia, the president's press secretary said he has been in touch with the "relevant sources and received information that everything said in the article is "either outright lies, or deliberately distorted facts."
