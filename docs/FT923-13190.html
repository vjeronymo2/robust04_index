920715 FT 15 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Trust of Property net assets decline Net asset value per share of Trust of Property Shares stood at 69.02p at June 30. That compared with 77.31p a year earlier and 78.43p at the December 1991 year-end. Available revenue for the half year to end-June was Pounds 30,602 (Pounds 31,880), equal to earnings of 0.464p (0.483p).
