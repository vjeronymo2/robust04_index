BFN ["14 August Elections Temporarily Suspended" -- ACAN headline] [Text] Guatemala City, 23 Apr (ACAN-EFE) -- The legislative elections scheduled for 4 August [date as received] in Guatemala were temporarily suspended after the Supreme Court of Justice (CSJ) approved an amparo filed by Deputy Carlos Humberto Arroyave. The Solidarity Action Movement (MAS) legislator, who held a seat during deposed coupist President Jorge Serrano's (1991-1993) administration, claimed that the call for elections violated his political rights. In confirming the temporary suspension of the electoral process, a Supreme Electoral Tribunal (TSE) magistrate said today that his organization accepts but does not share the CSJ ruling.
