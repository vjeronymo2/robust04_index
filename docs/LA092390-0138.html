September 23, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Danny Sullivan wrested the pole position for today's Texaco-Havoline 200 from Penske teammate Rick Mears in a record-setting qualifying battle at Elkhart Lake, Wis.

Mears had a lap of 131.177 m.p.h. Then Sullivan turned his record-setting lap of 131.289. The former record was 130.474, set last year by Sullivan.
