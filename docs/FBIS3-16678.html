Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, Deputy Prime Minister Valeriy Shmarov held a meeting with William Miller, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the United States to Ukraine. During the meeting, which was held in an atmosphere of mutual understanding and good will, a range of pressing issues concerning the upcoming visit of U.S. Secretary of Defense William Perry to Ukraine were discussed. The parties exchanged opinions on the results of the recent Ukrainian-U.S. talks at the highest level.
