941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Doeflex 31% ahead to Pounds 0.95m Doeflex, the thermoplastics manufacturer, reported a 31 per cent rise in pre-tax profits for the six months to June 30, from Pounds 725,000 to Pounds 950,000. Turnover increased 16 per cent, from Pounds 15.9m to Pounds 18.5m. Earnings per share were 6.28p (5.22p) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 1.6p.
