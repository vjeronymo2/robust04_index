940408 FT 08 APR 94 / International Company News: RJR Nabisco to pay off debt RJR Nabisco, the US tobacco and foods group, is to pay off most of its Dollars 2bn worth of subordinated debentures on May 15 using a combination of fresh bank loans and cash, the company said yesterday, writes Richard Tomkins in New York. The debt carries an average interest rate of 15 per cent. RJR Nabisco said it was being paid off at the earliest possible redemption date as part of its continuing programme of replacing expensive borrowings with cheaper debt.
