BFN [Text] The party committee, people's council, people's committee, and Fatherland Front Committee of Ha Long town held a grand meeting at Hon Gai Stadium in Quang Ninh Province on the morning of 25 April to make public a government decree on establishing Ha Long town under Quang Ninh Province. Comrade Nguyen Binh Giang, member of the party Central Committee and secretary of the Quang Ninh Province party committee; Comrade Pham The Duyet, secretary of the Hanoi municipal party committee; and representatives of various central-level organs and of many provinces and cities nationwide attended the meeting. The meeting began with a street parade with the participation of tens of thousands of people, and was followed by a ceremonial reading of the government decree on the establishment of Ha Long town under the administration of Hon Gai city in Quang Ninh Province. The birth of Ha Long town will further spur the socioeconomic growth of Quang Ninh Province as well as of Bac Bo delta and is a significant event for the entire country. The grand meeting held to make public the government decree on the establishment of Ha Long town concluded with a variety show performed by thousands of youths and teenagers which marked the beginning of the first cultural and sports festival of Ha Long town.
