BFN [Text] One day before the hearing of the Federal Constitutional Court on armed forces foreign missions, Chancellor Kohl has stressed that Germany has to assume responsibility at the side of its partners. If peace and freedom in the world and Europe are at issue, the FRG must not stand aside, Kohl said in Bonn in the morning. During the reception for soldiers who had participated in humanitarian and peace missions, the chancellor warned that Germany's security and capacity to act in foreign politics are based on the allies' confidence. Therefore, the FRG must be a reliable partner.
