CSO ["Election Results in Ternopil Oblast"] [Text] In the second round to voting on 30 April [NB TO EDITOR: thus in original, but presumably should be 30 March?] in Ternopil Oblast, in Berezhany e.d. No. 358, Levko Horokhivskyy, deputy chairman of the URP, defeated Zynoviy Rayinchuk, the head of the rayon branch of the National Bank of Ukraine; in Zboriv e.d. No. 362, Andriy Zarudnyy, cochairman of the regional organization of Rukh, defeated Ivan Polevyy, head of the oblast organization of the URP; in Terebovlya e.d. No. 364, Bohdan Ternopilskyy (URP), deputy chief of the president's service on territorial issues, defeated Roman Kuper, deputy chairman of the Ternopil city council, the candidate of the Ukrainian Conservative Republican Party [UKRP]. In Halytskyy e.d. No. 356, where the race was between a Rukh candidate (Ihor Hereta) and an URP candidate (Yaroslav Dzhodzhyn), and in Kremenchuk e.d. [NB TO EDITOR: thus in the original, but should be Kremenets e.d.], No. 363 (the race was between Bohdan Kulchytskyy [Rukh], chairman of the city council, and Serhiy Shtohun, head of the Shumske Rayon Court), none of the candidates obtained the qualifying number of votes.
