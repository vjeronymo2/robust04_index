Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] According to available information, the security agencies in Sidon have apprehended a person who was wounded trying to throw an explosive charge at a shop in Darb al-Sim in eastern Sidon. A person helping him was killed in the explosion. In the north, a person was caught in Tripoli and another was caught in Funaydiq in 'Akkar. Both are suspected of blowing up a shop in al-Zahriyah, Tripoli. The security agencies are interrogating the apprehended persons, 30 in all, mostly Lebanese and Palestinians. Contacts are under way with officials in the 'Ayn al-Hulwah Palestinian camp to hand over some suspects who are inside the camp. Deputies who visited the president of the republic yesterday have cited him as saying that he has instructed the security agencies to step up efforts to uncover the circumstances surrounding recent explosions, particularly the blowing up of the Phalangist Party's central headquarters and the explosions that occurred in Tripoli.
