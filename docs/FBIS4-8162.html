BFN [Text] Strobe Talbott emphasized at a news conference that the atmosphere of the talks he had in Warsaw showed that Polish-U.S. relations are excellent. The deputy secretary of state assured Polish politicians about the continued support of the United States for our reforms. He also told them that Poland's security was of vital interest to the United States. [Begin Talbott recording, in English with superimposed Polish translation] We believe there will be no more spheres of influence in this part of Europe and that every country is going to cooperate with others on an equal footing, irrespective of their military strength. [end recording] Strobe Talbott did not reply to a question on whether the United States would meet some of the costs of Poland's participation in Partnership for Peace. He merely noted that certain costs were connected with the benefits that came from participating in the program.
