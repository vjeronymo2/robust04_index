BFN [Text] Sevastopol, Ukraine, May 20 (AFP) -- The Black Sea Fleet, shared between the Ukraine and Russia, threatened Friday [20 May] to intervene in Crimea after the region's parliament adopted a new constitution effectively declaring independence from Kiev. "It is clear that whatever the development of the situation, the Black Sea Fleet cannot remain outside events unfolding around it," the Fleet's Press Office said in a statement.
