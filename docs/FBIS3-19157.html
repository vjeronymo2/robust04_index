Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis left for Brussels today to address the session of the European Union [EU]-Cyprus joint parliamentary committee tomorrow. Mr. Mikhailidhis is expected to develop the positions of the Cyprus Government on the course of its relations with the EU under the light of the reexamination of the Cyprus application for EC accession in January 1995. The session will also be attended by a delegation from the House of Representatives headed by the cochairman of the committee House President Alexis Galanos.
