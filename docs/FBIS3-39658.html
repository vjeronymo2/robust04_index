Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Feb 17 (AA) -- Turkey on Thursday [17 February] proposed a draft resolution to Human Rights Commission which foresees struggle against racism and intolerance. Foreign Ministry officials told AA that the draft resolution will be presented during Human Rights Commission's meeting that will take place in Geneva on Friday. Officials said Turkey played a leading role against struggle with racism adding European Council accepted to initiate an action plan at a human rights summit on Oct 1993 in Vienna.
