941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / Commons security lapse set for inquiry The House of Commons authorities have launched an inquiry into how three people - including two Conservatives - entered the private Commons office of Mr Tony Blair, the Labour leader, Kevin Brown and David Owen write. The three, believed to be an MP's researcher and two friends, were found emerging from the doorway of Mr Blair's inner office on Friday night. Mr Blair's office said the incident was 'a matter for the House of Commons'.
