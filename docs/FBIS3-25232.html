Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Norberto dos Santos, minister of assistance and social reintegration, today announced the lifting of the ban on relief flights to areas controlled militarily by the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA]. This follows guarantees that UNITA would cease fire in Cuito and allow the World Food Program to operate in the region. [Begin Santos recording] The government believes conditions have been created for relief flights to resume to the city of Cuito as of 14 February. The ban imposed on UNITA-controlled areas has been lifted. [end recording]
