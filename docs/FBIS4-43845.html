BFN [Excerpt] The departure of President Glavkos Kliridhis for Corfu [planned for this afternoon] has been postponed until tomorrow due to the arrival of Joe Clark, the UN secretary general's special representative on Cyprus. Clark, who is expected to arrive in Cyprus late this afternoon, is carrying a letter from UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali to President Kliridhis and Rauf Denktas on the confidence-building measures.
