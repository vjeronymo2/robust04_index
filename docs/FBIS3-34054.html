Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Our correspondent Sulayman al-Shafa'i reports that in graffiti sprayed in the Jabalyah refugee camp in the Gaza Strip today, the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine assumed responsibility for the murder of taxi driver Elias Kohen in Beersheba earlier this week. In the Gaza Strip today, an 11-year-old girl was seriously wounded in the head by a bullet fired by Israeli soldiers. Eight Palestinians suspected of collaborating with Israel have been murdered in the Gaza Strip in the last two days.
