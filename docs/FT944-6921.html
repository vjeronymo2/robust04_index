941126 FT 26 NOV 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Spices Pepper prices were under pressure in western Europe because of the absence of buyers in the US, reports Man Producten. India reported a firmer local market, however. In the cloves market demand from India and Singapore encouraged exporters from Madagascar to increase prices. The Madgasacar crop, now in full swing, is expected to be smaller, largely as a result of last year's tornado. Nutmegs and mace prices remained generally stable.
