May 13, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

REVIEWING A LEGEND

Liska says O'Day's 1981 autobiography is "High Life, Hard Times." The correct title is "High Times, Hard Times" -- and to sensationalize O'Day's well-publicized battle with drugs is to do an incredible injustice to one of the true -- one of the only -- survivors of the swing era.

MICHAEL BRANTON

Hollywood
