BFN [Report by Juan Carlos Guillen] [Text] Deputy Julio Montoya has reported that the Directorate of Intelligence and Prevention Services dismantled Operation Rainbow in which drug traffickers were trying to export 300 tons of cocaine overseas. "I have come to discuss and announce the discovery of a major drug-trafficking network whose primary hubs were the ports of Maracaibo, Puerto Cabello, and Punto Fijo," Montoya said. Operation Rainbow used hollowed-out coconuts in which the drug, presumably cocaine, would be hidden. [Guillen] How many kilograms would be exported that way? [Montoya] Three-hundred tons of the drug.
