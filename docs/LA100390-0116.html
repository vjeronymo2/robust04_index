October 3, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP;

BURNS 2ND, CSUN 5TH IN GOLF INVITATIONAL

Led by Bob Burns' 54-hole total of 218, the Cal State Northridge men's golf team finished fifth Tuesday in the 20-team Wolfpack golf invitational at Lake Tahoe.

Burns, the defending NCAA Division II individual champion, fired rounds of 78, 69 and 71 in the two-day meet at the par-72 Edgewood Golf Course to tie for second individually. Players shot 27 holes a day.

Northridge finished at 986, 14 strokes behind first-place Nevada Reno, the host school. Fred Widicus of Virginia won medalist honors at 214.

Charlie Wi, a freshman from Westlake High, tied for fourth at 219 for Nevada Reno.

MEN'S SOCCER

Moorpark 3, Bakersfield 1 -- Moorpark (8-0, 3-0 in conference play) scored all of its goals in the first five minutes to defeat the Renegades (9-1, 2-1) in a Western State Conference match at Moorpark.

Billy Kranz, Eric Gunter and Robert Harris each scored a goal after getting an assist from Shaun Christensen.

WOMEN'S VOLLEYBALL

Pierce d. Bakersfield 15-9, 15-0, 16-14 -- Nathalie Zodan had seven blocks and six kills and teammate Lori Mertes added seven kills to help the Brahmas (7-0, 6-0) defeat Bakersfield (3-3) in a Western State Conference match at Pierce.

Moorpark d. West Los Angeles, 15-7, 15-8, 16-14 -- Nikki Ferreria had 12 kills and Kellee Roe added seven to lead the Raiders (3-3, 3-3) to a WSC victory at West L.A. (0-6, 0-6).

The Master's d. Mt. St. Mary's, 15-2, 15-1, 15-6 -- Carla Vande Steeg had 15 kills for host Master's in a NAIA District 3 match. Stacey Parch had seven aces for Master's (11-11, 3-4).

GIRLS' VOLLEYBALL

Thousand Oaks d. Simi Valley, 15-0, 15-7, 15-4 -- Erikka Gulbranson had eight kills and teammate Amee Stone added seven to lead the Lancers (8-1, 5-0) past Simi Valley (0-4 in league play) in a Marmonte League match at Thousand Oaks.

Newbury Park d. Agoura, 3-15, 7-15, 15-5, 15-5, 15-0 -- Jenny Cameron had 14 kills and teammates Lori Muzzonigro and Diane Haley added eight each to lead a Newbury Park rally in a Marmonte League match at Agoura.

Erin Mutch had 33 assists for Newbury Park (5-1, 4-1).

Notre Dame d. Chaminade, 15-6, 15-9, 15-9 -- Mary Strong had 11 kills and Maria Thorpe nine to lead Notre Dame (5-2, 1-0) to its fifth consecutive win in a Mission League opener at Chaminade.

Pam Powers had 10 kills for Chaminade (4-2, 0-1).

Bell-Jeff d. Louisville, 15-8, 15-9, 15-3 -- Andrea Serchaw had 10 kills to lead Bell-Jeff to a San Fernando Mission League win over Louisville at Bell-Jeff.

Saugus d. Palmdale, 17-15, 15-13, 15-3 -- Jeannie Lane had seven kills and Lindsay Estes had four for visiting Saugus (3-4) in a Golden League opener.

Valley Christian d. Cuyama, 15-8, 15-12, 8-15, 16-14 -- Rachel Gentry has six kills for host Valley Christian (6-1, 5-1) in a Coast Valley League match.

Chatsworth d. Taft, 15-7, 15-7, 15-0 -- Jody Paperno had nine kills and Heather Gorman contributed five aces for Chatsworth (5-0) in a Northwest Valley Conference match at Taft.
