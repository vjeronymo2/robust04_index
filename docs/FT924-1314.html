921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / International Company News: German TV stake for CNN CNN, the international television news network, has acquired a 27.5 per cent stake in N-TV, which operates Germany's only all-news television station, AP-DJ reports from Berlin. Mr Karl Ulrich Kuhlo, the founder of N-TV, said the deal resulted from 'short and intensive' talks, and expressed satisfaction that CNN 'will be a component of our future planning'.
