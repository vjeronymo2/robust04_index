930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Fleming Overseas net assets ahead The Fleming Overseas Invest-ment Trust reported net assets per share of 306.6p at June 30, compared with 214.7p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year to the end of June improved to Pounds 6.37m (Pounds 5.34m) for earnings per share of 4.78p against 4p. The proposed final dividend is 2.7p for an increased total of 4.2p (4p).
