930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Select Appointments seeks Pounds 9m Select Appointments is seeking to raise about Pounds 9m through an open offer of 9m cumulative redeemable preference shares. The offer is on the basis of 100 preference for every 2,390 ordinary. The proceeds will be used to repay about Pounds 7.24m of the debt owed to Waverley, which is underwriting the offer. Under the refinancing announced in September 1991 Waverley has made a number of advances and at June 30 1993 was owed a total of Pounds 11.6m.
