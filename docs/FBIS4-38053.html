BFN [Text] The Jerusalem City Council decided on a series of measures to prevent Yasir 'Arafat's visit in the city. In a special session held this morning, the mayor and all his eight deputies decided to set up three action committees: a public relations committee, a financial committee, and a committee which would organize a huge protest campaign. Our correspondent Aviv Bushinsky reports that, among other things, the Jerusalem City Council plans to fly in protesters from overseas aboard special planes.
