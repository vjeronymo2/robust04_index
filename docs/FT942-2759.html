940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Tinsley Robor shows advance to Pounds 450,000 Tinsley Robor, the printing and packaging company, reported pre-tax profits up from Pounds 15,000 to Pounds 450,000 in the year to March 31. Turnover rose 7.3 per cent to Pounds 29.5m, compared with Pounds 27.5m. Mr John Rose, chairman, said the company had made steady progress but the return on assets was still below the minimum acceptable. Earnings per share were 1.1p (losses 0.4p). The dividend is passed. He added that the present year had started in line with expectations of continued steady improvement. 'I expect that the coming year will provide the results which will enable our return to the dividend list.' The pre-tax figure was after charging termination costs of Pounds 218,000 and start-up losses for the new Netherlands plant of Pounds 206,000. Interest costs fell from Pounds 623,000 to Pounds 494,000.
