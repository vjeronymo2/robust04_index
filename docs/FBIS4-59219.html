BFN [Text] This morning in Pretoria, President of State 'Ezer Weizman held brief meetings with Cuban President Fidel Castro, Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto, and with the sultan of Oman. President Weizman told our correspondent Gid'on Kouts that Castro expressed an interest in ties with Israel. Castro added that the agreement Israel and the Palestinians had signed pleased him very much and that recently he met many Israelis. President Weizman invited the Cuban leader to visit Israel. Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto also expressed pleasure over the agreement between Israel and the Palestinians. In her conversation with Weizman, she did not rule out contacts between Pakistan and Israel.
