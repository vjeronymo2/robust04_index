940510 FT 10 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Logica buys US software provider Logica, the computing services concern, is acquiring the assets and some liabilities of Precision Software Corporation of the US for an initial Dollars 3.3m (Pounds 2.26m). Precision, founded in 1984, provides loan syndication and administration software to more than 100 banks and financial services organisations. The consideration will be followed by a further Dollars 2m upon certain targets being met. And there are provisions for Dollars 7m to be paid on a performance-related basis running until June 1998.
