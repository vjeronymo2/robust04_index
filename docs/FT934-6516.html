931124 FT 24 NOV 93 / International Company News: Foschini Foschini, the South African retail clothing and jewellery group, overcame difficult trading conditions to record a 19.5 per cent increase in pre-tax profits to R88.3m (Dollars 26.2m) in the six months to September, from R73.9m a year ago, writes Philip Gawith in Johannesburg.
