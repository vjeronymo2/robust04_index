Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpt] Turkey and Azerbaijan have reached an accord regarding the signing of a joint border protocol. Azerbaijani Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov, on an official visit to Turkey, held a meeting with General Dogan Gures, chief of the General Staff. Pointing out that there existed an agreement on military training between Azerbaijan and Turkey, Gasanov added that he will hold meetings on this issue. The guest minister later departed Turkey. Seeing the guest minister off, Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin said that Azerbaijan was entering 1994 hurt and with problems. Cetin said: [Begin Cetin recording] If the Armenian forces think they will hold on to the territories they occupied by force, they are wrong. We hope they will withdraw from the territories they have occupied. If they do not withdraw peacefully then one day they will have to leave those territories under other conditions. It is impossible that they will hold on to Azerbaijani territory they occupied by force in violation of all international regulations. [end recording] [passage omitted]
