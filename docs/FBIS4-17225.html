BFN [Text] We are going to build socialism following the European model with the equality of rights for various forms of ownership -- this according to entrepreneur Yayhen Shcherbanin, a winner in the recent Supreme Council elections, at the news conference in Donetsk. At the same time, the establishment of a centrist faction in the republican legislature was announced. This faction was established by the Donbass-based fund for the promotion of progressive reforms, which includes managers of large industrial factories and companies. All of them represent the authorities. [Words indistinct] will become deputies of the new Supreme Council who share goals and tasks of the fund's program. It is also taken into consideration that deputies from other oblasts of Ukraine will join this faction. According to members of this faction, the holding of early presidential elections is inadvisable. They believe that Leonid Kravchuk's powers should be prolonged until a new Ukrainian constitution is adopted. The immediate goal of the faction is to foster the establishment [as heard] of Soviets on all levels.
