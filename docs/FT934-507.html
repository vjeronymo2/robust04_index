931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Danae Trust net assets improve The net asset value of the capital shares of Danae Investment Trust improved to 71.11p at November 30, against 58.6p at the May year end and 45.48p at the interim stage last year. The value per income share stood at 51.68p at November 30, unchanged from its level six months earlier and down slightly from 51.79p a year ago. Net revenue for the six months to the end of November increased to Pounds 273,653 (Pounds 229,188). Earnings per income share rose to 3.76p (3.24p) and a same again dividend of 3.375p is declared.
