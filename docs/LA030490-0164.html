March 4, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

THE SHERIFF'S ACTIONS

The "silent majority" is sick and tired of Sheriff (Brad) Gates' errors in judgment. He continues to cost we taxpayers excessive amounts of monies for attorney fees, court costs and settlement damages.

Why should the County Board of Supervisors authorize these payments? Let him be responsible for his own actions and surely he'll be far more careful.

DAN BROWN

Newport Beach
