920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / International Company News: Commerzbank opens in Prague COMMERZBANK, Germany's third largest bank, has opened a representative office in Prague, the Czech capital, which it plans to upgrade to a fully-fledged branch by the beginning of next year. Commerzbank's commercial banking operations will focus on the financing of foreign trade and the provision of short-term credits. The bank also plans to advise on local and cross-border mergers and acquisitions.
