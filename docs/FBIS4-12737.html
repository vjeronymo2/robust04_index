BFN [Text] Vice President Taha Yasin Ramadan has received Ahmad Shakir, head of the Society for the Promotion of Arab-French Ties, and his accompanying delegation. Taha Yasin Ramadan said France should play a prominent and positive role in the current stage to help reestablish normal ties with Iraq and end the unjust embargo against it. He added that the creative achievements of the Iraqi people and the strugglers during the reconstruction drive -- including development and strategic projects in all fields accomplished with Iraqi capabilities and raw materials -- are the best evidence of the Iraq's cohesion and support for the leadership of President Saddam Husayn [words indistinct] continue to resist aggressive schemes [words indistinct]. Ahmad Shakir paid tribute to the Iraqi people's courageous stand under the leadership of struggler President Saddam Husayn in confronting the 30-state aggression and its episodes. The meeting was attended by the official in charge of the Foreign Relations Bureau of the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party.
