October 6, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

NONLEAGUE

Glendale 29, Burbank 0 -- Pathon Rucker and Hector Maya each scored two touchdowns to lead Glendale (5-0) at Burroughs.

Maya scored on runs of two and seven yards and Rucker on runs of seven and nine. Rucker, the area's leading rusher, gained 127 yards in 22 carries and May had 63 yards in 10 carries.

Although Burbank quarterback Jason Chandler completed 16 of 34 passes for 126 yards, Glendale's defense took the ball away four times, twice by fumble and twice by interception. GARY HYNES
