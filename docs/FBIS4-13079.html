BFN [Text] Presided over by esteemed Ahmad Shah Ahmadzai, minister of city construction and housing, the high session of ministers held a session today. The press office of the prime minister reported to BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY that the high session of ministers discussed the current situation in the country. After giving information on the result of the visits of the UN secretary general's special envoy and the ulema of the Islamic countries of Saudi Arabia and Pakistan, the basic points of the coordinated proposal of the Joint Conciliation Council of Afghanistan was discussed. After the proposal was discussed in the meeting, it was presented to the Cabinet peace delegation for requisite views and consultation for its further enrichment.
