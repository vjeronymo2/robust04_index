BFN [Text] The deadline set by the government for the surrender of firearms expired yesterday. No statement has so far been issued. Earlier, the government warned all groups in possession of firearms, whether lawfully or unlawfully, that the Army and the security committees will be forced to search for firearms if they are not surrendered. A government announcement called on armed groups to stop shooting and on the Army to maintain discipline and to use weapons for self defense. Speaking to reporters, Defense Minister Gedeon Fyiroko disclosed that the seizure of arms would take place in all parts of the country, that is the municipalities, divisions, and regions. Meanwhile, our reporter in Karuzi region has reported that two soldiers were shot and injured by unknown assailants.
