BFN [Report by BELINFORM correspondent Svetlana Kalinkina for TASS] [Text] Minsk 29 June TASS -- A Byelorussian [Belarusian] presidential election run-off will, most probably, be held on Sunday, 10 July. After numerous discussions, it is precisely this date, which has been suggested and is to be ratified by members of the Central Commission for the Election of a Byelorussian President, at a commission meeting to be held on Thursday. The official results of the counting of votes in the presidential elections are to be announced at the commission meeting. According to preliminary data, the results will not differ substantially from the already announced ones.
