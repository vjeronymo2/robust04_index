Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres said that a national referendum on the Golan Heights would not prevent the government from reaching a decision on the issue, nor would it tie the government's hands in the negotiations. When asked whether or not he was in favor of a national referendum, Peres replied: We will decide at the appropriate time. I am not rejecting the idea out of hand. The foreign minister was speaking at Bet Berl during a ceremony in which scholarships were awarded to Jewish and Arab students.
