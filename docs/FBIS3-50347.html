Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Consumer Basket For January"] [Text] According to the calculations of the Republic Bureau for the Advancement of the Household, the January consumer basket is some 12.4 percent more expensive than the December one, while the December figure is 13 percent higher than in November. In November last year 4,970 denars were needed for the minimum required food for a four member family, in December 5,799, while during the first months of this year this figure rose to 6,519 denars. The price of the consumer basket is constantly rising and during the period January-December its value increased 2.5 times, that is, 276 percent. The January basket on the other hand compared to the same last years has increased 300 percent. The indicators used to show this increase are the price increases of the milk, pastas, bread and the 11 percent increase in the price of milk.
