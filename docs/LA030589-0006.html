March 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

I want to thank the creators and writers of "The Wonder Years" for producing the most brilliant comedy series since "Taxi." I am 33 years old and grew up in western Pennsylvania, where I was Kevin Arnold's age in the '60s. It seems as though that series was made about my life. We said and did everything they do in the show. "Wonder Years" is a comedy filled with sad moments, dramatic moments and hilarious moments -- not just a stream of silly punch lines. Thanks for the careful thought, research and quality that goes into such a wonderful series.

James J. Germann, Northridge
