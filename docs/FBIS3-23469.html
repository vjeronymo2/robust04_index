Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Istanbul, Kadikoy, March 19 (A.A) -- The explosion of a sound bomb in the Haydarpasa train station wounded a student from Singapore, on Saturday. The bomb planted in a toilet exploded at the Haydarpasa railway station. Police identified the victim as 20-year-old Me Can Kian. A bomb also exploded at a service station next to Uskudar square the same day, hours before main opposition leader Mesut Yilmaz, head of the Motherland Party, was to stage a political rally. No one was hurt. Police said no one immediately claimed responsibility for the Istanbul bombings. Three people, suspected to have links with the raids, are being sought, the police said.
