Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpt] Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin has said the United Nations needs additional troops to protect peace and to help bring about an agreement in Bosnia-Herzegovina. Therefore, he said, the tendency is growing to ask for a military force from Turkey. The foreign minister was speaking at Esenboga Airport on his return from visits to New Zealand and Australia. He pointed out that the United Nations and other international organizations are now at the stage of officially asking Turkey to send a military force to Bosnia-Herzegovina. In line with this request, Cetin said, Turkey can send a battalion to Bosnia-Herzegovina. [passage omitted]
