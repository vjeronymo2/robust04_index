BFN [From 4 April HOMMIKULEHT, p 1; "News Bulletin" No. 63/94] [Text] The Estonian Interior Ministry is interested in bilateral cooperation agreements with Russia concerning operations against crime, Interior Minister Heiki Arike said. But he added that Estonia will not sign agreements concerning all CIS states. Arike added that he had discussed the need for continued negotiations with Russian Ambassador Aleksandr Trofimov recently. He hoped that the talks between ministries will lead to an agreement between the governments. Russia had so far favoured only agreements signed within the CIS framework and concerning all member states, Arike explained. He said that the Interior Ministry is cooperating efficiently with Finland, Sweden, Germany, Great Britain and Italy.
