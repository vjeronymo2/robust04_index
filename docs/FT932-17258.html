930401 FT 01 APR 93 / International Company News: Melamed quits as Globex chairman MR Leo Melamed, the Chicago Mercantile Exchange leader who pioneered the creation of Globex, the international electronic trading system for futures and options, has announced he would step down as chairman of the Globex Corporation at the end of May after serving one year in the post. Mr Melamed said he was leaving to devote more energy to his futures clearing firm, Dellsher Investment Company. He has been seeking regulatory approval to sell the firm to the Japanese bank, Sakura.
