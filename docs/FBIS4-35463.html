BFN [Excerpts] A delegation from the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan [PUK] and a delegation from the Kurdistan Democratic Party [KDP] held a meeting Monday [30 May] evening at the PUK Political Bureau headquarters in Irbil. Ahmad al-Jalabi, president of the Executive Council of the Unified Iraqi National Congress, attended the meeting. [passage omitted] At the meeting, which was characterized by a cordial and brotherly atmosphere and a high sense of responsibility, the current situation in Kurdistan in general and the security situation in Irbil in particular were discussed. At their one-hour meeting the conferees stressed the need to maintain calm and security.
