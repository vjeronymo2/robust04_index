941103 FT 03 NOV 94 / UK Company News: RPS Pounds 1.9m buy RPS, the USM-quoted environmental consultancy, is to acquire three subsidiaries of MTS International for Pounds 1.9m. The acquisition will be funded by a placing and open offer of 2.92m shares at 65p each. Qualifying shareholders will be invited to apply for shares on a 1-for-4.1535 basis. The three companies, Thomson MTS in Milton Keynes, NOHS in Manchester and Environmental Monitoring (Scotland), comprise the environmental section of MTS International, which is part of the metal technology division of Bodycote International. Their combined operating profits for 1993 were Pounds 463,000.
