January 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

FRENCH LOTTERY: RAGS TO RICHES TO RICHES TALE FOR HOMELESS WINNER

A tramp who spent his last few francs on a French lottery ticket -- and won -- found he already had a small fortune when he put the check in a bank account he had not used for 20 years.

The homeless man, who has not been identified, won 70,000 francs -- $11,500 -- in France's national lottery. Told he needed a bank account to cash the check, he remembered an account he held in eastern France.

Lottery officials who investigated the man's claim discovered the man had unknowingly accrued $50,000 in dividends from shares in a firm he once worked for.
