920321 FT 21 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Fired Earth Tiles drops to Pounds 302,000 In what it described as the worst two years in living memory, Fired Earth Tiles, the specialist tile retailer, saw pre-tax profits fall further from Pounds 753,000 in 1990 to Pounds 302,000 last year. Earnings per share fell from 8.56p to 3.58p, but the dividend is unchanged at 4.025p with a second interim of 2.4p. While overall turnover was little changed at Pounds 5.26m (Pounds 5.04m), sales from the Merchant Tiler shops, selling less expensive tiles, doubled to Pounds 1.8m (Pounds 900,000), with the average number of outlets rising from 6 to 10.
