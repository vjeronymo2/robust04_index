Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] According to a briefing by a related department, in 1993, the fulfillment of Henan's major economic indices increased markedly from the previous year, for which the gross national product was 153 billion yuan, up by 15.7 percent, ranking seventh in China. The increase in grain output was 36.39 million tons, ranking third in the country. Gross industrial output value increased by 29.1 percent from the previous year, for which the gross industrial output value of enterprises at and above the township level was 130.035 billion yuan, ranking 14th.
