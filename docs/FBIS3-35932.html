Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN ["Edict of the Republic of Georgia State Minister, Chairman of the Temporary Committee on the State of Emergency in Western Georgia, on the Extension of the Curfew"] [Text] To extend the curfew in the towns of Samtredia, Poti, Abasha, Senaki, Martvili, Khoni, Khobi, Lanchkhuti, Ozurgeti, Chokhatauri, Vani, Tsalenjikha, Zugdidi, and Chkhorotsqu until 20 February 1994. The curfew operates between 2000 and 0600. [Signed] Givi Lominadze, Republic of Georgia state minister, chairman of the Temporary Committee for the State of Emergency in Western Georgia.
