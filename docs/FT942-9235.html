940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / Observer: Foot down It's not just the policemen, but the joy-riders too, who get younger by the day. And Boots, the chemist, is doing its bit to curb their activities. Its catarrh syrup, for 1-12 year olds, carries a warning that a dose of the medicine causes drowsiness. 'If affected do not drive or operate machinery,' it says.
