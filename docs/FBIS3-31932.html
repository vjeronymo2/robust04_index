Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Guatemala City, 28 Jan (NOTIMEX) -- Police sources reported that the Guatemalan National Police had seized five RPG-7 grenades today in a local restaurant and that a dynamite charge placed at the base of an electricity transmission tower had been deactivated. The National Police Public Relations Department disclosed that the five grenades were hidden in different areas of "Los Antojitos" restaurant which specializes in Mexican food. Four days ago the police also seized 10 rifle grenades in several commercial establishments located throughout Guatemala City. The police report says that a powerful dynamite charge which had been placed at the base of an electricity transmission tower was deactivated this morning. The electricity tower is located in Villanueva colony, at kilometer 13. [passage omitted]
