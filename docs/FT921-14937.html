920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / Toyota components depot planned THE STORAGE and supply base for components for the new Toyota car plant at Burnaston, Derbyshire, is to be set up at Stretton Business Park, 3.5 miles away at Burton-upon-Trent in east Staffordshire. Land for the park was assembled by East Staffordshire District Council which sold it to Hyperion Properties, a subsidiary of NFC, the freight group.
