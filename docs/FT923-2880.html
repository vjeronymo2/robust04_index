920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Tighter margins cut Fii by 10% FII GROUP, the footwear manufacturer, blamed tighter margins caused by the difficult business environment for a further fall in pre-tax profits in the year to May 31. Profits were down 10 per cent at Pounds 6.46m (Pounds 7.15m) despite turnover being maintained at Pounds 80.2m, near the previous year's record of Pounds 80.3m. Within that exports increased 11 per cent to Pounds 11.5m. Profits included net interest received of Pounds 780,000 (Pounds 789,000) from record cash balances of Pounds 10m at the year end. Turnover in the footwear division fell slightly to Pounds 68.6m (Pounds 69.5m) for operating profits of Pounds 6.1m (Pounds 6.5m). The scientific and technical division, which distributes medical and laboratory equipment and conducts research, reported losses of Pounds 100,000 (Pounds 600,000 profits) on turnover 7 per cent higher at Pounds 11.6m. Earnings per share were 30.8p (33.5p) and an increased final dividend of 8.5p is proposed for a total of 14p (12.75p).
