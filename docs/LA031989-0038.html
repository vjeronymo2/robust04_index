March 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

IN DEFENSE OF A 'HOMEMAKER FROM THE MIDWEST'

As for Rakolta's complaints about gratuitous sex, women in garter belts, no bras, etc.: These are fast becoming acceptable "funnies" among the public ding-dongs. TV is not just going down the tubes . . . it is dropping into the outhouse hole!

DEAN M. NORTON

Los Angeles
