940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Gartmore launches Latin American growth fund Gartmore Investment Management is launching a Latin America New Growth fund in co-operation with Indosuez Capital. The fund will invest in smaller companies throughout Latin America, but primarily in Brazil, Argentina and Mexico. The closed-end fund,which will be dollar denominated and listed in Dublin from the end of February,hopes to raise about Dollars 100m from institutions. Indosuez Capital has a network of offices in Latin America, and will provide local research.
