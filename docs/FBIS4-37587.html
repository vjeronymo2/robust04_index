BFN [Text] Israeli warplanes attacked al-Luwayzah village in Iqlim al-Tuffah at 1805 today. The warplanes fired several rockets at the village, following which columns of smoke could be seen in the area. Israeli warplanes are currently carrying out low-altitude flights over al-Nabatiyah and Iqlim al-Tuffah. The Lebanese Army is firing on the planes. [Tel Aviv IDF Radio in Hebrew at 1930 GMT on 21 June in a similar report adds: "Israeli Air Force jets this evening attacked terrorist targets in the Jabal Safi region in south Lebanon. These targets serve as Hizballah training bases and staging points for attacks. The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] spokesman said that the pilots reported accurate hits and that all the planes returned safely to base."] [Beirut Radio Lebanon in Arabic at 1530 GMT on 21 June reports: "In response to the Israeli attacks, resistance fighters attacked a post of the occupation forces in al-Zaffatah, setting a truck on fire. Columns of smoke were seen rising from the attacked position."]
