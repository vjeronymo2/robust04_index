920828 FT 28 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Net assets down at Scottish Eastern Scottish Eastern Investment Trust had net assets, after deducting prior charges at par, of 62.8p per share at July 31, down from 70.7p 12 months earlier and 69.5p at the January year-end. The trust reported attributable revenue of Pounds 5.51m (Pounds 4.51m) for the six months to end-July, equivalent to earnings of 0.87p (0.71p) per share. The interim dividend goes up from 0.46p to 0.5p.
