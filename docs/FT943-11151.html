940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): National Westminster - Other Companies National Westminster: Tomorrow the bank is expected to report first-half pre-tax profits of Pounds 600m-Pounds 727m. This compares with Pounds 421m for the same period last year. The lowest estimate for the interim dividend puts it at 6.4p per share - the same as last year. The the highest is for 7.5p.
