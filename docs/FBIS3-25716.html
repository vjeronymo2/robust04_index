Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "African News" program] [Text] State radio in Cameroon has given an account of fighting which took place early this month in the Bakassi Peninsula area which is disputed with Nigeria. It quoted a Cameroonian military official as saying 10 Nigerian boats, each carrying about 10 soldiers, had attacked a Cameroonian position. The radio said two of the boats were sunk and one Cameroonian soldier was killed and one wounded in the exchange nine days ago. A delegation from Cameroon has since held talks in Nigeria aimed at reducing tension.
