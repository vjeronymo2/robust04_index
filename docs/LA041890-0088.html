April 18, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski, who got back over the $900 mark Sunday with a $131 profit, will try to keep his winning ways going today when his wager is a $20 win parlay from Valueval in the fourth to Popping Champagne in the seventh. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, starts the week at $938.40.
