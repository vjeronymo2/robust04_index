BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Mainayev] [Text] Bishkek 1 April, TASS -- General Andrey Nikolayev, commander of the Russian frontier troops, is expected to arrive in Bishkek on Friday for the signing of a package of agreements on the legal basis of the stay of Russian frontier guards in Kyrgyzstan territory. The documents to be signed include the treaty on the supply of Russian frontier guards in Kyrgyzstan, the treaty on cooperation with the Kyrgyzstan State National Security Committee, the treaty with the Kyrgyzstan Defence Ministry on regulations for Kyrgyz recruits service in the ranks of Russian frontier guards in Kyrgyzstan. A meeting between Kyrgyzstan President Askar Akayev and General Andrey Nikolayev is planned. The commander of the Russian frontier troops arrives in Bishkek upon the conclusion of his visit to Tajikistan. He had made a fact-finding trip to the Tajik-Afghan border. General Nikolayev intends to report on the situation in the region to a forthcoming meeting of CIS heads of state and to note the need for the observance of arrangements on joint protection on the border. At a news conference in Dushanbe, Nikolayev said that Russia has strategic interests in the region and noted that Russian frontier guards are not to be withdrawn from Tajikistan's territory in the years ahead.
