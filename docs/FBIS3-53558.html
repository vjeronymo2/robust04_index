Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday evening in Aden, brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, received the EC ambassadors to our country. They discussed relations between our country and the EC states, and exchanged views on current developments. Brother al-Bid expressed appreciation for the positions of the EC states which support unity, democracy, and modernization in our country. He stressed his eagerness to continue both the national dialogue and the search for peaceful solutions to the issues and difficulties facing the march of national construction away from violence or force in a way that will enhance unity and democracy and consolidate the construction of a modern Yemeni state. Al-Bid also indicated our country's eagerness to bolster and promote its relations with the EC states in a way that will serve the common interests based on the exchange of benefits between our people and the peoples of these states. For their part, the EC ambassadors expressed their countries' interest in developments in our country. They also emphasized their support for unity and democracy and eagerness to resolve all differences by peaceful means and through a genuine and responsible dialogue.
