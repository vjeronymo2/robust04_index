Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 12 (KCNA) -- A great upswing has been noticed in production in different domains of the national economy of Korea in the new year. The Musan mining complex, a major iron headings producer of the nation, set itself the goal of producing 1.5 million more tons of iron headings this year than last year and has overfulfilled daily quotas since the beginning of the new year. The Songjin steel complex topped the first-day's assignments of the new year 3.4 percent in steel and 5.1 percent in rolled steel. It has since kept a high and steady rate of production. Coal mines in North Hamgyong Province has lifted the daily amount of tunneling and coal output more than 20 percent by introducing a rational coal cutting method. The Sunchon and Anju district coal mining complexes also have overfulfilled their daily assignments on all indices since the beginning of the year. The east Pyongyang thermal power station generates 80 percent more electricity and produces 2.4 times more hot water for central heating on a daily average this year than at the end of last year. At the Kaesong textile mill, the daily output of fabrics is 40 percent up and that of yarn 30 percent up on the daily average of last year.
