930212 FT 12 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Lang says government is backing campaign for children's hospice Mr Ian Lang, Scottish secretary, said in the Commons last night that the government is backing a campaign to establish a children's hospice north of the border. In a written reply, he said ministers would support the Children's Hospice Association Scotland in its proposal to set up a hospice to serve the whole of Scotland. The association was also being awarded a state grant to help with administration costs, he added.
