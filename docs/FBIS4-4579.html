BFN ["Communique" issued in Phnom Penh on 31 March] [Text] The National Assembly Secretariat is honored to inform the public that the second session of the National Assembly's first legislature will be held on Tuesday, 5 April 1994, at 0800 at the National Assembly. At this plenary session, the Assembly will listen to a message from His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk of the Kingdom of Cambodia; a report on the activities of the National Assembly Standing Committee; and debate and decide on the invalidation of National Assembly members' status and the validity of new Assembly members. The public is hereby informed. [Dated] Phnom Penh, 31 March 1994
