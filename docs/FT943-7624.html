940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Waterglade property buy Waterglade International, the lossmaking property developer, is to buy six shops with offices above from Associated Shop Properties of London. Consideration for the properties at Westbourne Grove, W, was Pounds 480,000, together with the assumption of a mortgage of Pounds 600,000. The EGM called for Thursday by rebel shareholders in a move to oust the board was cancelled on a technicality.
