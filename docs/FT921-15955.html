920107 FT 07 JAN 92 / Liverpool wins Pounds 7.5m funding THE GOVERNMENT yesterday awarded Pounds 7.5m of urban programme funding to two Liverpool projects. 'The Phil' - the city's Art Deco concert hall - will get Pounds 3m if the Royal Liverpool Philharmonic Society can match it with private-sector support. A city grant of Pounds 4.5m is being made towards the Pounds 42m re-development by the Walton Group of Exchange Buildings.
