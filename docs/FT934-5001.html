931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / Budget 93: Science budget set at Pounds 1.2bn Spending next year on scientific, engineering and social research, and post-graduate training, will total Pounds 1,242m, an increase of Pounds 73m over the estimated out-turn this year. This represents a small increase in real terms. Mr William Waldegrave, public services minister, said: 'We have protected the science budget and more than maintained its value in the coming year.'
