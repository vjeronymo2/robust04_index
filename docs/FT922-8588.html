920512 FT 12 MAY 92 / UK Company News In Brief RADIOTRUST net asset value at January 31 50.2p (57.2p): pre-tax loss for year to end-January Pounds 46,927 (Pounds 64,067): losses per share 0.3p (0.89p). ***** STRATAGEM has agreed to sell Visitronic, Visirecord and related assets which are engaged in computer software systems, stationery and related products for the motor trade to Kalamazoo Computer. Initial consideration is Pounds 450,000. Further payments totalling Pounds 300,000 will be made in instalments beginning in May 1993. Further deferred consideration up to Pounds 300,000 are performance-dependent. ***** WORTH INVESTMENT Trust: After-tax revenue was Pounds 9,000, against Pounds 94,000, for the year to March 31, equal to earnings of 0.03p (0.58p). The dividend is cut to 0.1p (0.21p). At year-end net asset value per share stood at 25.16p (33.54p). The fall in NAV reflected a write-down in the valuation of unlisted investments.
