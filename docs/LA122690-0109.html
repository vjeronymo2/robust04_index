December 26, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

SUNS ACTIVATE LANG; BATTLE OUT

The Phoenix Suns placed forward Kenny Battle on the injured list today and activated backup center Andrew Lang.

Lang, a third-year pro, injured his left wrist and also suffered a four-stitch cut over his left eye when he fell heavily to the floor during a Dec. 4 game at Chicago.

He was put on the injured list the next day.

Battle, a second-year pro who has played sparingly this season, sprained his left ankle in practice this week, according to team officials.
