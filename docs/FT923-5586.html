920829 FT 29 AUG 92 / Markets: At a Glance - Latin American trust has a mixed first half Latin American Investment Trust, the best performing trust of 1991, had much more volatile markets to deal with in the first half of 1992. The Brazilian stock market, for example, jumped 90.1 per cent in the first four months of the year but then dropped so sharply that the total rise for the first half was just 1.3 per cent. The trust's net asset per share increased 4.5 per cent to Dollars 1.85 over the six months to June 30.
