March 27, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

SOUTHLAND;

5 ILLEGALS HURT IN CRASH AT BELL

A truck carrying at least 35 illegal aliens overturned in Bell while being chased by police today, injuring five of those aboard, authorities said.

Two people, believed to be so-called coyotes or smugglers, were arrested after the truck crashed about 8:45 a.m. while turning a corner at Bell Avenue and Bissell Street, police Cmdr. Jim Edwards said.
