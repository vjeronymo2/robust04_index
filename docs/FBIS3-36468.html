Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] During the past 48 hours, the Azerbaijani army continued to clear the heights along the road linking Kelbadzhar and Mardakert of Armenian formations, the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry told Interfax. It alleges that the bodies of hundreds of Armenian soldiers killed in action lie in the snow. To avoid epidemics after the spring thaw, the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry's sanitary and epidemiological unit has been sent to the region. According to Baku, fighting continued yesterday in the southern section of the front near the strategic railway station Goradiz in the vicinity of the Azerbaijani-Iranian border. An Armenian tank was put out of action.
