May 19, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

VIETNAM;

FEW FRIENDS PRESENT TO SALUTE 'UNCLE HO'

Vietnam's Communist leaders, fast losing friends and funds as their socialist allies defect to capitalism, gave founding father Ho Chi Minh a loving but austere salute. The mood in Hanoi was somber as only a few foreign dignitaries attended a ceremony for the centenary of "Uncle Ho."
