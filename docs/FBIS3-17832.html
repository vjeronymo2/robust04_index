Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riga, Mar 29, BNS -- Latvia is sending Russia a note of protest in view of a recent incident between Russian military persons and Latvian Homeguards, during which a series of illegalities were detected in the conduct of Russian servicemen. The incident occured March 16 in the Riga district Stopini firing ground situated outside the territory of a nearby Russian military base. The Russian servicemen had staged field excercise on the firing ground without a permit from the local authority, neither had they movement permits from the bureau monitoring the troop withdrawal. The incident was settled peacefully and the detained servicemen returned to their unit. According to the note, Latvia hopes that similar incidents, which may have serious consequences, will be averted in the future. Latvian Foreign Ministry's Aivars Vovers invited Russian diplomat Valeri Nesterushkin Tuesday [29 March] to receive the note.
