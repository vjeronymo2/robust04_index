BFN [Text] United Nations, April 26 (XINHUA) -- Ji Chaozhu, U.N. under-secretary-general in charge of the Department for Development, Support and Management Services, will represent the United Nations at former U.S. President Richard Nixon's funeral on Wednesday [27 April]. Ji, a national of China, was the senior interpreter for the top Chinese leaders when Nixon was in China for his historical visit to the People's Republic. Nixon, President of the United States from 1969 to 1974, died last Friday. His remains were flown to his birthplace Yorba Linda, California, on Tuesday for the funeral.
