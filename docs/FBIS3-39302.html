Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Albanian Foreign Minister Alfred Serreqi arrived in Athens a short while ago. Serreqi will meet Greek Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias tomorrow in the framework of contacts between the European Union [EU] and Albania, because Papoulias is presiding over the EU Council of Ministers. It is expected that the two ministers will also discuss bilateral issues, with emphasis on the rights of the Greek minority in North Ipiros and on the problem of illegal Albanian immigrants in Greece.
