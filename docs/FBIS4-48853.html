BFN [Excerpt] A contingent of 324 Kenyan soldiers left the country this morning on a UN peacekeeping mission to the war-torn former Yugoslavia. The battalion, code-named Kenbat-3, comprises 27 officers under the command of Lieutenant Colonel (Julius Ayub Githiri). It will take over from Kenbat-2 which returns home after a stint in the former Yugoslavia. [passage omitted]
