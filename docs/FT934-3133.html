931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / Tendering is low in Scotland The private sector has had far less success winning council contracts in Scotland than in England and Wales, the Local Government Management Board says. No private contractors have won school catering contracts or cleaning contracts, other than building cleaning, in Scotland. In its strongest sector, building cleaning, the private sector won 18.7 per cent of contracts. In England and Wales, 54.6 per cent of such contracts went to the private sector.
