Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 21 (XINHUA) -- The 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between China and the Congo was marked here today at a reception given by the Chinese People's Association for Friendship With Foreign Countries and the China-Africa Friendship Association (CAFA). In their toasts, CAFA President Gong Dafei and Joseph Mpassi, charge d'affaires ad interim of the Congolese Embassy in Beijing, expressed satisfaction at the sustained development of Sino-Congolese friendship and cooperation in various fields over the past three decades. They agreed that there should be more official and people-to-people exchanges between the two sides in the future.
