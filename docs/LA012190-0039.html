January 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

MORRISON'S LEGACY

If Oliver Stone is making a movie about Morrison (or anything else), he should pay more attention to historical detail in his sound track.

In Stone's current film, "Born on the Fourth of July," Don McLean's "American Pie" was played as background music while the film covered the 1968-69 era. "American Pie" wasn't even released until 1971!

BOB PALMER

Moreno Valley
