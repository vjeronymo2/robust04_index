940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Waterhouse losses deepen to Pounds 0.2m Further losses at its Midlands business helped push Waterhouse, the USM-quoted office fitting company, further in to the red in the six months to end-March giving a deficit for the period of Pounds 200,890 against Pounds 51,114 previously. The outcome was struck on turnover down from Pounds 1.13m to Pounds 1.2m, and at the operating level there was a loss of Pounds 147,357 (Pounds 25,123). The Midlands business was responsible for Pounds 126,759 (Pounds 91,228) of that deficit. Losses per share came out at 8p (2p). However, as announced on June 7, the Midlands business is to be disposed of by way of a management buy-out, the terms of which were revealed by Waterhouse yesterday. The value of the net assets being disposed of amounts to Pounds 64,948. The proceeds of the sale, which is subject to shareholders' approval, will be used to reduce indebtedness and to provide working capital. The shares closed down 3p to 33p.
