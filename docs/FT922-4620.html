920604 FT 04 JUN 92 / Blue Arrow case appeals date set APPEALS against the convictions of four City advisers found guilty of conspiracy to defraud over the 1987 Blue Arrow rights issue will begin on July 13. Mr Jonathan Cohen, Mr David Reed, Mr Nicholas Wells - all former County NatWest directors - and Mr Martin Gibbs, a former Phillips & Drew director, were convicted in February after a year-long trial. The appeals are expected to last about two weeks.
