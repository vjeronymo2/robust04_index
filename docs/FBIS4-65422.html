BFN [Text] Spanish Defense Minister Julian Garcia Vargas has arrived in Turkey on an official visit. He was received at Esenboga Airport by National Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan. In a speech at the airport, Golhan pointed out that Turkey and Spain conduct joint political, economic, military, and social activities within NATO, the European Union, and the Western European Union. He stressed that this visit will further develop bilateral cooperation. Vargas said, in turn, that Turkey and Spain must get to know each other better and develop their relations.
