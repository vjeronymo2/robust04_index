Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 6 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet received here yesterday Norwegian Ambassador to Vietnam Willy Johan Frederiksen who paid him a courtesy visit. Prime Minister Kiet and the newly accredited ambassador expressed their pleasure at the development of the Vietnam-Norway relations of friendship and cooperation. They also expressed their desire to further broaden these relations in accordance with the potentials and interest of each country and the trend of development and cooperation in the world. The Norwegian ambassador affirmed that the Norwegian Government has always been ready to cooperate with and assist Vietnam. He said that in the coming time some Norwegian delegations will come to Vietnam to inquire into possibilities of cooperation in trade, and oil industry. He also wished that the Vietnamese people will obtain more achievements in the renovation process and in building their prosperous country. Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet highlighted Norway's success and experiences in economic management and expressed his hope that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries will effectively develop in the future.
