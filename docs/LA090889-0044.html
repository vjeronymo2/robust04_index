September 8, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

"Trump Card," a new syndicated bingo-like TV game show backed by real estate tycoon Donald Trump, has been tripped up by some last-minute contractual problems. Trump was set to make the official announcement Thursday morning at Trump Tower in New York City, but that press conference was canceled late Wednesday. However, a Lorimar executive said "Trump Card" is almost sure to be on the market in January at the annual National Assn. of Television Program Executives convention for a debut next fall. Trump will own a third of the program, as will both Lorimar Television and Warner Bros. Domestic Television Distribution. ALEENE MacMINN
