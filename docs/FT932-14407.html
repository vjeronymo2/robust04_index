930419 FT 19 APR 93 / UK Company News: Clyde Blowers acquisition Clyde Blowers has taken over Sturtevant Engineering in a deal worth Pounds 336,000 in cash. Sturtevant is based in Brighton and owns two trading subsidiaries known as Sturtevant Engineering Systems and Sturtevant Engineering and Manufacturing. Clyde has bought 98.8 per cent of the ordinary and the whole of the preference capital.
