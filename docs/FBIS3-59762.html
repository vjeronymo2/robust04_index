Language: Japanese Article Type:CSO [Text] The Federation of Electric Power Companies (Kohei Abe, chairman) will establish a Washington, D.C., office as early as this fall. The decision was made at the 21 July meeting of the chairmen of the nine electric power companies. Along with gathering information on such issues as U.S. atomic power and environmental policies, the office will also actively generate news on Japan's energy situation. Tokyo Electric Power Company and other utilities have overseas offices, but this will be the federation's first overseas office.
