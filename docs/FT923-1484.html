920923 FT 23 SEP 92 / Occidental sues over Piper Alpha OCCIDENTAL, the oil company that owned the ill-fated Piper Alpha platform, is suing 26 contractor companies and at least one survivor after paying out millions of pounds in compensation after the deaths of 167 men in the disaster. Papers have been lodged at the Court of Session in Edinburgh for at least one case, against Stena Offshore and a survivor. Occidental is suing for Pounds 380,000. Legal sources said yesterday the company was seeking more than Pounds 100m in total.
