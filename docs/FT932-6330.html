930528 FT 28 MAY 93 / International Company News: Losses deepen at NZ group RJI, the New Zealand property group formerly known as Robt Jones Investments, yesterday revealed deepening net losses of NZDollars 297.7m (USDollars 162.9m) for the 12 months to March 31, compared with NZDollars 132.9m the year before. It also announced said it was changing its name to Tasman Properties. The company said it had written down its property by NZDollars 301.6m. Mr John McCarthy, the new chief executive, said the company had reduced its debt by NZDollars 34m to NZDollars 57m during i the year.
