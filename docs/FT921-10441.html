920206 FT 06 FEB 92 / UK Company News: British Gas in French joint venture British Gas has formed a 50/50 joint venture with Sade, a French project management and construction company. Sade is a subsidiary of Compagnie Generale des Eaux, which is largely involved in services to the water industry. The new company will trade under the name of Temare (Technologie de Maintenance de Reseaux). It will allow British Gas to market its high technology pipe maintenance services to the French water industry. Initially Temare will be involved only in swagelining, a system of rehabilitating pipelines by inserting a close - fit polythene liner.
