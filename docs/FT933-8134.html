930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / Liberia factions agree government Liberia's three former warring factions yesterday agreed a five-member transitional government to steer the country to elections next February, Reuter reports from Cotonou. The factions each chose one member of the council and the two remaining posts were chosen by a group of eminent Liberians selected by the factions. The agreement left Charles Taylor's National Patriotic Front of Liberia in a minority.
