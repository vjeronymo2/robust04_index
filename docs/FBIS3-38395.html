Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] The Bundeswehr will not be able to participate in any further missions abroad for the time being. This is intended to save some 100 million German marks [DM]. In addition, the Bundeswehr intends not to fill 1,500 positions that will fall vacant in the civilian sphere, to reduce the number of noncareer soldiers, and to extend the transfer of units to eastern Germany over a longer period of time. These are parts of the list of cuts intended to reduce the defense budget by DM1.25 billion.
