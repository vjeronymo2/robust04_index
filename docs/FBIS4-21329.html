Heroin Cartels NC2204103794 Athens Elliniki Radhiofonia Radio Network in Greek 0900 GMT 22 Apr 94 NC2204103794 Athens Elliniki Radhiofonia Radio Network Greek BFN [Text] Customs officers at the Kipoi checkpoint in the area of Evros [on the Turkish border] early this morning succeed in striking the biggest blow in recent years against the international heroin cartels. Customs officers discovered 121 kilos of heroin hidden in a camper. The camper belongs to German nationals (Juergen Maert), 31, and his wife (Dona Annette), 26, who had left Turkey heading for London. During the interrogation, the Germans told the customs officers that they knew of the heroin's existence in their camper, and that they were going to receive 100,000 German marks upon its delivery in London. The drugs and camper were confiscated and the two Germans will appear before the Alexandroupolis prosecutor within the day.
