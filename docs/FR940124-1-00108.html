(Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1099a&hyph;3) §667.22 Disapproval of State review standards. (a) (1) Each State shall submit to the Secretary for his evaluation the review standards it developed under §667.21. (2) The State shall also submit to the Secretary an explanation of how each standard was established, including_ (i) An explanation of the relationship between each of the State's review standards and the related or comparable Title IV, HEA standards and requirements; (ii) If applicable, an explanation of the reasons for establishing different review standards for different types of institutions or educational programs; and (iii) A description of the manner in which institutions were consulted in developing the review standards.
