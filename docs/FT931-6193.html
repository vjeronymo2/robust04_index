930303 FT 03 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: Harrods bomb suspects arrested in armed raid Two men were being questioned about the IRA bombing of Harrods in January after an armed raid last night on a house in Stoke Newington, north London, hours after a police video of two suspects was shown on television. Scotland Yard said shots were fired at anti-terrorist squad detectives and a tactical firearms unit during the raid. Police did not return fire. Firearms and explosives were recovered from the house.
