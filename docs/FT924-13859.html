921013 FT 13 OCT 92 / International Company News: Recovery in demand lifts earnings sharply at CPC CPC International, the US food company whose products range from Hellmann's mayonnaise to Knorr soups, saw after-tax profits rise from Dollars 75.5m to Dollars 117.4m in the three months to end-September. The strong improvement came on sales up by 7.7 per cent to Dollars 1.6bn, and translated into an increase in earnings per share from 48 cents to 76 cents. The figures take after-tax profits for the first nine months to Dollars 318.8m, compared with Dollars 262.6m at the same stage in 1991. Sales for Dollars 4.81bn, compared with Dollars 4.64bn. CPC said the advance reflected 'a solid pick-up' in the North American consumer foods business, and that it expected a good performance in the final quarter. But it admitted it was still feeling the 'effects of recession' in several European countries.
