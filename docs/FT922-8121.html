920514 FT 14 MAY 92 / Parliament and Politics: Waldegrave warns of threat to departments under Citizen's Charter MR William Waldegrave, the minister for the Citizen's Charter, warned last night that the careers of ministers and permanent secretaries could suffer if their departments fail to implement the charter. Mr Waldegrave told The Times that the prime minister was keen for the charter to receive a fresh impetus as part of his plans to reform the machinery of Whitehall.
