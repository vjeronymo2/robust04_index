BFN [Text] Australian Prime Minister Paul Keating, his wife, and delegation arrived on board a special Royal Thai Air Force plane at Don Muang Airport at 1530 today to begin an official visit to Thailand until 10 April as guests of the Thai Government. The Australian prime minister is scheduled to meet with Prime Minister Chuan Likphai tomorrow and be received by his majesty the king in the evening. On 8 April, he will proceed to Nong Khai Province to participate in the official opening of the Thai-Lao Friendship Bridge.
