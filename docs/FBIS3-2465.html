Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 5 Mar (XINHUA) -- The "National People's Congress [NPC] Standing Committee Supplementary Regulations on Harshly Punishing Crimes of Organizing the Transportation of People Across the Territory (Border Areas) Illegally" adopted by the NPC Standing Committee at its sixth session on 5 March 1994 are now promulgated. They become effective upon promulgation. [Signed] Jiang Zemin, PRC President [Dated] 5 March 1994
