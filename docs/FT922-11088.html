920428 FT 28 APR 92 / International Company News: Hershey Foods ahead to Dollars 58m HERSHEY Foods, the US confectionery group, reported higher first-quarter earnings, driven by improved results from US chocolate operations, Bloomberg reports. Net income rose to Dollars 58.92m, or 65 cents a share, from Dollars 48.64m, or 54 cents, in the 1991 first quarter. Sales advanced to Dollars 800.97m from Dollars 684.57m. Hershey Chocolate USA was the principal contributor to earnings. The consolidation of Nacional de Dulces and Gubor Schokoladen, acquired in 1991, also boosted results.
