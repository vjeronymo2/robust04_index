CSO [News department report: "News From the Ministry of Foreign Affairs"] [Text] An official opening ceremony of the Republic of Armenia Embassy in Romania was held on 24 April in Bucharest. Among those present were Armenian Minister of Foreign Affairs Vahan Papazyan; Armenian Ambassador to Romania Georgi Kazinyan; Romania's Secretary of State Ioan Mircea Pascu; and heads of foreign diplomatic representations accredited to Romania. The embassy was blessed by the head of the Armenian congregation in Romania and Bulgaria, Archbishop Tirayr Martikyan. * * * On 25 April, Zhirayr Liparutyan, Armenia's first deputy minister of foreign affairs, received the Netherlands ambassador to Russia and Armenia, Baron de Vos Van Steenwijk. In the course of the meeting, they exchanged opinions regarding development of bilateral relations, the situation in the region, and the efforts of Armenian leadership in the matter of resolving the Karabakh conflict.
