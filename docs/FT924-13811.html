921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / World News In Brief: Favourites share Booker prize The Booker Prize for fiction is shared between two novelists on the short list of six - both favourites with the bookmakers. They are Sri Lankan-born Michael Ondaatje (left) for The English Patient and Durham-born Barry Unsworth for Sacred Hunger. Each received a cheque for Pounds 10,000.
