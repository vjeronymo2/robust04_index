931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / International Company News: HK tunnel finance agreed THE Western Harbour Tunnel Company, which is to build Hong Kong's third harbour tunnel, yesterday signed an agreement with 22 international banks to raise HKDollars 5.2bn (USDollars 673m) over 15 years for construction costs, writes Louise Lucas in Hong Kong. The financing was clinched despite what arrangers Wardley Capital described as tight pricing.
