941110 FT 10 NOV 94 / International Company News: China Airlines hit by private competition China Airlines, Taiwan's flagship carrier, said sales in the first nine months fell 1 per cent compared with last year, to TDollars 32.22bn (USDollars 1.2bn), AP-DJ reports from Taipei. The airline faces strong competition from Taiwan's first privately-owned international carrier, Eva Airways. CA's Japan and Hong Kong routes account for a large portion of sales.
