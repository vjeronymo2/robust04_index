BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent Aleksey Trotsenko for TASS] [Text] Kiev 16 June TASS -- Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko and his Israeli counterpart Shim'on Peres have praised results of the latter's two days' official visit to Ukraine. "Successful development of friendship and cooperation between our countries" was once again reaffirmed during the visit, Zlenko told a press conference on Thursday [16 Jun]. According to him, Ukraine became native land for many Jews. They made a contribution to Ukrainian culture. In this connection, he mentioned large-scale festivities held in Ukraine to mark the 135th anniversary of Sholom Aleykhem, a classic of Jewish literature. According to Peres, his stay in Ukraine has made a very good impression. I am sure we will be able to open a new chapter in the development of friendship and cooperation between Israel and Ukraine, Peres said.
