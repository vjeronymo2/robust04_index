941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / International Company News: Federated completes Macy acquisition Federated Department Stores, owner of Bloomingdale's, yesterday became one of the biggest retailing groups in the US when it completed its Dollars 4bn acquisition of RH Macy, operator of the Macy's and Bullock's chains, writes Richard Tomkins. Creditors of RH Macy, which has been operating under Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection since January 1992, will receive 55.6m Federated shares, warrants to buy 18m more, and Dollars 2.8bn in cash and debt securities.
