BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] The program for the return of refugees to their homelands will be signed by both the North Ossetian and the Ingush sides. A RIA correspondent has learned this from Aleksandr Chelyshev, deputy minister of civil defense, emergencies, and natural disasters. He also revealed that around 40-45 billion rubles will be needed to restore housing in North Ossetia's Prigorodnyy Rayon. Part of this sum has already been received in the region and will be used toward the construction and restoration works.
