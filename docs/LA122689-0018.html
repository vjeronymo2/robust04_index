December 26, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

ARSON DETECTIVES PROBE POWAY FIRE

San Diego County Sheriff's Department arson detectives are investigating a Christmas Day fire at a vacant Poway house that caused $75,000 worth of damage.

The fire at 14754 Gail Park Lane was reported to the Poway Fire Department at 1:26 a.m. Three units from Poway and San Diego responded.

Arson investigators were called in because of "suspicious fire indicators," a sheriff's spokesman said.
