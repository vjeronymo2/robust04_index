BFN [Text] Today in the Friday prayers sermons in Tehran Ayatollah Ahmad Jannati stressed that fighting against slackness and strengthening working ethics during the period of reconstruction should be given top priority. Referring to the Workers' Week, the acting Friday imam of Tehran said: During the period of reconstruction the role of workers is very important and basic. He also stressed the constructive role of teachers. Jannati said that the issue of reconstruction has many different dimensions. He said that greater and more extensive use should be made of committed human resources in various fields. Ayatollah Jannati also stressed the importance of frugality in all fields. He pointed out that it is very important for Muslims to pay greater attention to the tragedy of Bosnia, and to find ways of solving that crisis.
