Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ukrainian Acting Prime Minister Yukhym Zvyahilskyy has instructed the ministries of communications and power engineering and Ukraine's TV and Radio Company to resume broadcasting TV programs in full throughout the territory of Ukraine. This decision was taken in response to requests to the Cabinet of Ministers by citizens. I would like to recall that since the end of last year, all TV channels have only worked in the evening due to the energy shortage. An exception was made for the duration of the winter Olympic games.
