BFN [Text] The Jordanian and Israeli delegations signed three documents in Washington yesterday. The documents comprise principles on borders, security, water, environmental issues, and other subjects. At the conclusion of two days of talks in Washington, the heads of both delegations announced that the talks would continue next month for the first time in Jordan and Israel. The talks will deal with transportation, aviation, trade, economic and tourism issues. Among other things, the delegations will discuss building a highway which will connect Jordan, Egypt, and Israel in the al-'Aqabah-Elat area. Jordan and Israel also decided to invite the Palestinians to participate in a new forum which will deal with economic, banking, and monetary matters.
