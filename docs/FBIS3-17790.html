Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] We have received from BELINFORM the text of a declaration signed by two deputy prime ministers and the ministers of defense, foreign affairs, and external economic relations. In this declaration, the officials reject the accusations made public by People's Deputy Alyaksandr Lukashenka in various mass media. In this regard, the declaration's authors insist that the Belarusian prosecutor general publicly make an official written assessment of the legitimacy of Lukashenka's actions, the declaration emphasized, and the accuracy of information concerned their alleged activities.
