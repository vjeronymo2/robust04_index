BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 14 (KCNA) -- Secretary of the Central Committee [C.C.] of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] Hwang Chang-yop Monday met and had a friendly talk at the Mansudae Assembly Hall with the delegation of the Japan Social-Democratic Party (JSDP) led by Director of the General Affairs Department of the Central Executive Committee of the Party and member of the House of Representatives Kazo Watanabe. Vice-Department Director of the C.C., the WPK Kim Yang-kon was on hand.
