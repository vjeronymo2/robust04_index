BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 21 (KCNA) -- A delegation of the Turkish Association for Friendship With the People of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea led by Ali Kalan, chairman of the association and deputy general secretary of the Workers' Party of turkey, arrived here today. It was met at the airport by Chun Yon-ok, vice-chairperson of the Korean Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and chairperson of the Korea-Turkey Friendship Association.
