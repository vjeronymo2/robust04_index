May 13, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

Let's get "Hunter" back to Saturday night! We always looked forward to NBC's Saturday schedule in our house.

Not anymore, however. We will be turning to Connie Chung on CBS at 10 p.m. so long as "Carol & Company" and "Down Home" air in "Hunter's" old time slot.

Ann P. Hall, San Antonio, Tex.
