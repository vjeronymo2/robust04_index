Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Cavaco Silva reacted to a documentary on East Timor denouncing a second massacre by Indonesian troops. The prime minister was speaking at a news conference under way at Sao Bento. Cavaco Silva is giving a joint news conference with his Israeli counterpart. Raquel Abecassis reports: [Abecassis] This was the first question Cavaco Silva had to answer. He spoke without any problem about the documentary shown on television on the second massacre in East Timor: [Begin Cavaco recording] Once again we receive shocking images of Indonesia's behavior in Timor. We hope that these images will help the international community to be even more aware of the need to pressure the Indonesian Government as much as possible to respect human rights in the territory. I can also say that we were already working with information very similar to that revealed yesterday, information we have received from the resistance [words indistinct]. [end recording]
