September 18, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

American Jim Spivey outran Sergei Afanasiev of the Soviet Union to win the 1,500-meter race at a track and field meet in Padua, Italy. Spivey was clocked in 3:36.85 minutes.

Renaldo Nehemiah beat fellow American Eric Cannon in the 110-meter hurdles. Nehemiah was clocked in 13.53 seconds, Cannon in 13.85.
