931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / Water cut-offs fall by 42% WATER COMPANIES are cutting off far fewer households than last year for failing to pay bills, figures published yesterday by the water industry show. The number of customers disconnected by the big 10 water and sewerage companies in the six months to September was 3,362, a 42 per cent fall on the corresponding period of 1992, the water industry said. Disconnections by Anglian Water leapt from 87 last year to 410, however. The company said it had tried last year to reach compromises with people who had difficulties in paying. 'Now we are left with a hard core who won't pay at all.'
