Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] An IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldier was stabbed at the French Hill junction in north Jerusalem a short time ago. The assailant was shot and killed by Border Police soldiers. Our correspondent 'Ofra Nehmad reports that the wounded soldier, who was taken to Hadassah-'En Kerem Hospital, is listed in moderate condition. Our correspondent Aviv Bushinsky reports that the terrorist jumped the soldier, stabbed him several times, grabbed his personal weapon, and fled the site. Border Police soldiers pursued him in a jeep and shot and killed him.
