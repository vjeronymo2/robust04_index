Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Dutch Prime Minister Ruud Lubbers has expressed his full support of the American plan to gradually expand NATO by eventually allowing East European countries to join. Mr. Lubbers was speaking after a meeting with U.S. President Bill Clinton. He and several cabinet members are currently in Washington on an official two-day visit. The prime minister's meeting with President Clinton, which was described as relaxed and friendly, mainly concentrated on NATO's expansion. [passage omitted]
