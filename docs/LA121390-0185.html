December 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

VICTIMS' FAMILIES THANKFUL FOR GIFTS

The families of Brian Miles, Glenn Wise and Larry Hokenson would like to convey their sincere gratitude to those who contributed to the Pasadena Water and Power Memorial Fund dedicated to the memory of those who lost their lives in the July 12 underground electrical explosion in Pasadena.

The thoughtfulness and generosity of the community was, and is, most appreciated.

ELLEN WISE

Temple City
