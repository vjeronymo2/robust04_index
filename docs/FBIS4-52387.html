BFN [Text] Seoul, May 9 (YONHAP) -- The cabinet on Monday approved a number of draft agreements to be signed with foreign countries. They included a draft double taxation prevention agreement set to be concluded with Vietnam within the month at the earliest. Others were a draft agreement on the establishment of a Korea-China Industrial Cooperation Council: a draft memorandum on Korea-China Civil Aeronautical Cooperation and Development: a draft Korea-Argentina investment guarantee agreement: and a draft memorandum on Korea-Philippine Defense Industry Cooperation. Meanwhile, South Korea and the Uzbekistan Republic initialed a bilateral aviation agreement in Seoul Monday. The agreement is expected to be signed during President Kim Yong-sam's visit to the Republic early next month.
