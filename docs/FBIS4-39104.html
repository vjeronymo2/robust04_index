BFN ["The Armistice Is Being Followed"--SNARK headline] [Text] Stepanakert, June 4 (SNARK) -- Relative quietness exists at the Karabakh fronts for about a fortnight. The Karabakh State Department of Press and Information told SNARK the from-time-to-time single firings of the positions of the Karabakh Defense Army brought to no serious consequences. The Karabakh side pays no back action to such provocations.
