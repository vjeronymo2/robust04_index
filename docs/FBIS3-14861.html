Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 28 Feb (EFE) -- Anatoliy Krasikov, chief of the Russian Presidency's information service, says that Russia "regrets" the incident which took place today in northwestern Bosnia where four Serbian airplanes were downed by two U.S. F-16's. "We regret the incident because Russia is working toward a peaceful solution of the conflict in former Yugoslavia," the ranking official said. Krasikov added that the Russian Government, through either the Foreign Ministry or Defense Ministry, will issue an official statement on the incident.
