930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / Firth Rixson to shed 93 forge jobs THE FORMER British Rail wagon works forge at Shildon, County Durham, is to close with the loss of 93 jobs, Firth Rixson, owner of the forge, said yesterday. Firth Rixson, a subsidiary of Johnson and Firth Brown, said it would consolidate its forging division operations into two sites at Sheffield and Derbyshire because of overcapacity resulting from a worldwide fall in demand.
