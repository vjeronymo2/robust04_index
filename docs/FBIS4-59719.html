BFN [Text] Damascus -- At the request of the U.S. side, a session of talks was held between Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' and U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher at the Foreign Ministry this morning. The talks were attended by Walid al-Mu'allim, Syrian ambassador in Washington, and Dennis Ross, U.S. coordinator of the Middle East peace process. During this session, the two sides continued the discussion of the ideas and proposals that were dealt with by His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad and Christopher yesterday. U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher and the delegation accompanying him left Damascus at 1600 today at the end of a two-day visit to Syria. He was seen off at Damascus International Airport by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', the Syrian ambassador in Washington, and the U.S. ambassador in Damascus.
