BFN [Text] Beijing, April 14 (XINHUA) -- A delegation from China's National People's Congress (NPC) is to visit New Zealand, Western Samoa, Papua New Guinea and Australia later this month, a Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman announced here this afternoon. According to the spokesman, the delegation, which will be headed by Vice-Chairman Tian Jiyun of the NPC Standing Committee, will pay official good-will visits to the above mentioned four countries from April 18 at the invitation of their parliaments.
