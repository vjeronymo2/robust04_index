BFN [Excerpt] Soldiers from the 1st Corps of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army carried out a successful military operation today by liberating the Nisici plateau, north of Sarajevo, and a part of the occupied territory between Sarajevo and Olovo. Velid Djindo reporting from Olovo. [Djindo] The aggressor's plan, named Zvijezda 94, was thwarted by a well-planned and coordinated operation by units from the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army's 1st Corps. More important positions have been liberated in a brilliant counterattack and a 10-kilometer-wide part of the front line was pushed between 1 and 5 kilometers forward. Our units have captured the villages of Milovac, Dovorine, Orahak, Karacici, Dalja, Brda, Zicki, and Citlucki Zalik as well as the Bukva, Kosijenci, Mali Hum, and Veliki Hum hills. New areas are being captured. Our soldiers have seized large quantities of weapons, ammunition, and military equipment and the enemy suffered considerable losses in manpower. [passage omitted]
