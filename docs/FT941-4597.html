940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Select Appts cuts deficit to Pounds 0.64m Select Appointments (Holdings), the USM-quoted international employment agency, reported a reduced loss of Pounds 642,000 for 1993, on sales up 61 per cent at Pounds 35m. The outcome was little changed from the first half when a Pounds 692,000 deficit was announced. For 1992 a deficit of Pounds 2.53m was incurred. Mr Anthony Martin, the chairman, said that activity during 1993 had included the opening of six new offices in the UK and acquisitions in the US, Hong Kong and London. Some Pounds 340,000 spent on expansion has been charged against operating profits. Two issues of preference shares during the period raised Pounds 15m. Out of this Pounds 7.24m debt was paid off and Pounds 2.54m was cancelled. In December, a capital reduction was completed and Select eliminated its deficit on reserves. At the year end the company had cash of Pounds 1.6m. Earnings per share came to 0.83p (1.06p losses) basic and 0.63p (0.95p losses) fully diluted.
