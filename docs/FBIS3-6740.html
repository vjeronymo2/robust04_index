Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 8 (KCNA) -- Feelings of reverence for the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il are deepening among the South Korean people, the Seoul-based radio Voice of National Salvation reported. A middle school teacher surnamed Kim in Ryongin-myon, San county, South Chungchong Province, talking with villagers about the situation some time ago said: "The dear leader Kim Chong-il is a peerless legendary hero and mythical great man. "There are many myth-like stories about his outstanding guidance and leadership ability. "He is indeed a [word indistinct] of the times possessed of almighty strength and wisdom and a distinguished great man produced by heaven." An old man Choe Ho was once a scholar, said: "When I was young I majored in history. History has not known of such leader as the dear leader Kim Chong-il who sends birthday tables to unknown old persons. "He shapes a man [words indistinct], placing the people in the centre of all thinking and making everything serve them. "My supreme wish is to live under his administration." One day in late February, Professor Kim in Tongrae District, Husan, told his colleagues: "During my trip to a European country toward the end of last year, a university professor there invited me to his home. A picture of dear General Kim Chong-il was placed in the centre of his study. He showed me the dear leader's works and told me that he was an adherent to the great chuche idea, the sole guiding idea of the present time. [Words indistinct] Comrade Kim Chong-il who indicates the path head of mankind and wisely guides them. "Hearing his words, I felt a great national [words indistinct], reunification, looking up to the wise General Kim Chong-il as the leader of the nation."
