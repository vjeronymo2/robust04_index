940331 FT 31 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Thorn EMI Thorn EMI has sold its Systron Donner Safety Systems division to Whittaker of Los Angeles for Dollars 12.75m (Pounds 8.7m) cash. In addition, it has received Dollars 750,000 in cash as prepayment of rent on a three-year lease of the safety division's facility in Concord, California. The safety systems division designs and makes aircraft engine fire and overheat detection systems. Thorn will invest the proceeds in its continuing businesses.
