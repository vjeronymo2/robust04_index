Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondents Viktor Demydenko and Oleksiy Petrunya] [Text] Kiev, 20 Jan--The Kazakh president's plane has just landed at Boryspil international airport, where the state flags of Ukraine and the Republic of Kazakhstan were hoisted. At the aircraft's ramp, Nursultan Nazarbayev was met by Acting Ukrainian Prime Minister Yukhym Zvyahilskyy, Foreign Affairs Minister Anatoliy Zlenko, Ukraine's Presidential Administration Secretary Mykola Homenko, and other officials. Girls in Ukrainian national costumes welcomed the Kazakh president with bread and salt. The motorcade has left Boryspil airport for Kiev, where a meeting between the Ukrainian and Kazakh presidents and Ukrainian-Kazakh negotiations are due to take place.
