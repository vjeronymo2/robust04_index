Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] Two commanders of Karmal-Dostam's infamous militia were killed recently in Faryab Province. Sources of the Armed Forces told BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY's military correspondent: As a result of the successful operations by the mojahedin and the forces of the Islamic Army, dozens of militia including two of their commanders -- Nasim Chapaw and Zar Alam -- were killed, and many areas have been cleared of Dostam's militia. According to another report, Gol Mohammad, known as Gol Pahlawan, brother of Rasul Pahlawan [Dostam's commander in Meymaneh, capital of Faryab], has been seriously wounded in a confrontation with mojahedin in Faryab Province.
