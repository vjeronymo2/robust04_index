920616 FT 16 JUN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was good general demand this week, reports the Tea Brokers' Asociation. Better liquoring East Africans were again a strong feature with prices often dearer. Mediums showed some irregularity but remained about steady. Plain teas tended easier with some withdrawals. Offshore fair demand in the cif section. Brightest Kenyas were firm to dearer but other descriptionss declined by 2 to 3p where sold. Fob teas from Mauritius attracted little interest. The highest price realised this week was 165p for three kenya pf. 1's. Quotations:quality 155p, good medium 138p, medium 100p, low medium 83p.
