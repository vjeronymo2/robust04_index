940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / UK Company News: River & Merc Small The net asset value per share of River & Mercantile Smaller Companies Investment Trust was 143.6p at July 31, a rise of 8.6 per cent on the 132.2p 12 months earlier. Net revenue for the year to the end of July was Pounds 1.03m (Pounds 1.02m) for earnings per share of 4.11p (4.07p). The proposed final dividend is 2.8875p (2.75p) for a total of 4.1375p (4p).
