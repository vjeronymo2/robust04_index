Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi and Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri returned to Beirut this afternoon after meeting with Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad in al-Qardahah. Al-Hirawi and Birri left for Syria early today to hold a private meeting with the Syrian president. They boarded a plane sent by President al-Asad to Beirut Airport. The NATIONAL NEWS AGENCY learned that President al-Asad left the delegations, which were still coming to his house to offer condolences, and met with President al-Hirawi for one and a half hours in the presence of Speaker Birri and Syrian Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam. They reviewed the situation in the region and the peace negotiations.
