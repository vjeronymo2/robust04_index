BFN [Excerpts] By 30 votes to 22, with 2 abstentions, the Constitution and Justice Committee [CCJ] of the Chamber of Deputies has just acquitted Deputy Ricardo Fiuza of the PFL [Liberal Front Party] Pernambuco, from charges of being involved in the budget scandal. [passage omitted] Fiuza's acquittal has yet to be confirmed by the Chamber of Deputies plenary. The Attorney General's Office press secretary has reported that the attorney general will file charges against Deputy Ricardo Fiuza before the Federal Supreme Court for forgery of documents when he added amendments to the 1992 Budget Bill after it was approved by Congress.
