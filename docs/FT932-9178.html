930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Bananas are a great fruit buy this week at 45-55p a lb (45-55p), along with Cape Golden Delicious apples at 40-45p a lb (40-50p). Spania Strawberries at 55-60p per 8oz punnet (70-80p), and Galia melons at Pounds 1.00-1.40 each are also worth looking out for. As supplies of Jersey Royal new potatoes increase the price has decreased to 25-45p a lb (55-80p). English cauliflower at 45-60p each (50-60p) and breakfast mushrooms at 50-60p a 1/2 lb (50-60p) round off the best veg buys. Chinese leaves at 75-80p a lb (75-90p), Canary Island tomatoes at 50-60p a lb (55-60p) and Round lettuce at 30-35p each (35-45p) are all good salad buys.
