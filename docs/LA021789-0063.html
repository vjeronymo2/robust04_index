February 17, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MAN BARRED FROM ONE JURY HE'D LIKE TO BE ON

Calvin Gene Eslick is considered a shoo-in for an exemption from jury duty later this month, whether he wants it or not. His name was selected at random for a jury pool in the Feb. 27 trial of a man charged with first-degree murder in the stabbing death of his estranged wife. Eslick is the defendant.
