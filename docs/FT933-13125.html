930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / Pounds 9.4m deficit for Docklands body THE London Docklands Development Corporation yesterday announced a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 9.4m for the year to March 31, down from a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 52.9m the previous year. After taking account of a Pounds 36.5m tax credit, which resulted from a reassessment of the LDDC's tax liabilities, it made a post-tax surplus of Pounds 25.7m. During the year, it received government grants of Pounds 182.5m (Pounds 240.4m).
