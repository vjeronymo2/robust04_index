BFN [Text] Roosevelt Johnson's Krahn faction of ULIMO [United Liberation Movement for Democracy in Liberia] is reported to have taken control of Kle in Bomi County. Johnson's faction seized the town after several hours of gun battle with Alhaji Koromah's Mandingo faction in which (?three guerrilas) of Alhaji Koromah were killed by Johnson's forces. The town of Kle was previously occupied by the Mandingo faction.
