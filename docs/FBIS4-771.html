Church of the Province of Southern Africa A maximum of three observers will be deployed at each voting station. Both domestic (local) and international observers will have two full days of IEC training. International observers will be able to attend sessions at national office on the 19-20 April or on the 20-21, or 22-23 at a regional office. Domestic observers will be able to attend either of the latter two training sessions. All training sessions will cover the same material. Additional training of international observers takes place in their home country by their organisation. The IEC has provided the (sample) training manual and handbook to registered overseas organisations. It should be noted that, unlike monitors, observers have no intervention powers. They are not employees of the IEC and make their reports to their accredited organisations. These organisations then report to the IEC. Liaison between international and domestic observers will be overseen by the monitoring directorate; a close relationship is expected.
