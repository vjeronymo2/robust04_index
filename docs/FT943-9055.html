940813 FT 13 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Fleming Enterprise Net asset value at Fleming Enterprise Investment Trust was 208.1p per share at June 30, up from 201.6p a year earlier. The trust, which seeks capital growth through investment in medium-sized UK companies, reported net revenue of Pounds 2.14m for the 12 months, against an adjusted Pounds 2.22m, equivalent to earnings of 5.35p (5.56p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 3.4p makes 4.8p (4.7p) for the year.
