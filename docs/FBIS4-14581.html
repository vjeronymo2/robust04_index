BFN [By ITAR-TASS string correspondent Sergey Luchnikov] [Text] Pskov (Pechora stretch of the Russo-Estonian border) April 1 TASS -- A Russian border patrol was fired upon in Pechora district of Pskov region, on the border with Estonia. According to preliminary information, received from competent sources, two violators, who crossed the border from the Estonian side, were stopped by a Russian border patrol at 3 a.m. on April 1st 50 kilometres from the Russia town of Pskov. They were approximately 25 and 40 years old. The older man said his name was Vladimir Ilyich and that he was an Afghan war veteran, while the younger man said he was a pilot of Pskov airport. One of them pulled out a "TT" pistol and started shooting at the patrol when the borderguards reported on the incident to their superiors and asked for a car to bring the violators to the border post. The patrol commander was killed by a bullet in the heart and two of his subordinates were wounded. Both violators fled to Estonia. They are now being sought by Russian and Estonian special services.
