BFN [Text] Cajnice, 1 Apr (TANJUG) -- Following a short overnight lull, fighting in the triangle between Cajnice, Foca and Gorazde continued unabated around 0500 today, even though an attempted Muslim breakthrough launched from Gorazde on Tuesday and Wednesday ended in complete failure, the command of the Serb forces announced. The command said that the fiercest fighting was in the region of Gradina hill. These are the fiercest Serb-Muslim clashes in this area since the beginning of the war, with both sides using all types of weapons. The surrounding mountains have been ringing with deafening explosions and incessant shooting. Two Serb fighters were killed in almost hand-to-hand fighting yesterday. According to Serb Command estimates, about 100 members of the Muslim units have been put out of action.
