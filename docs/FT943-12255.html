940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Sherwood Computer Sherwood Computer Services, has sold Sherwood Consort Data, its subsidiary providing software and services to the stockbroking market, to its management for a maximum of Pounds 450,000. The directors said the disposal is in line with declared strategy of concentrating on core activities and, in particular, to reduce conflict with City Deal Services, the company's own execution-only stockbroking arm. In 1993 Consort had a turnover of Pounds 835,000 and a net loss of Pounds 1,000. It had a net deficit on reserves of Pounds 33,000. Sherwood Computer received Pounds 250,000 on completion and will receive a further consideration up to a maximum Pounds 200,000 payable as a royalty on licence sales. Proceeds will reduce the company's overdraft.
