Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Article by "(jpk)": "Dyba Has Recalled His Deputy"] [Text] Prague--Economy Minister K. Dyba yesterday relieved his deputy, L. Chrudina, from his post and from his obligation to perform his duties until the end of March. The minister thanked his deputy, who, among other things, had been entrusted with drafting a report on telecommunications policy, which has been a major liability to Dyba's ministry since January and has slowed down the system for privatizing Czech telecommunications. Chrudina's recall was formulated "by mutual agreement."
