BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz, 13 Jun (ITAR-TASS) -- Issues relating to settling relations between North Ossetia and Ingushetia and the return of refugees to their previous residence were discussed at a meeting in Vladikavkaz today between Akhsarbek Galazov, president of North Ossetia, and Shmidt Dzoblayev, secretary general of the Assembly of Democratic Forces of the Northern Caucasus. The latter briefed Akhsarbek Galazov on his meeting in Nazran today with Ruslan Aushev, president of the Ingush republic, during which the issue of seeking a path to peace and accord in the region was discussed. The leader of the movement proposed that a conference of the elders and religious authorities of the region on reconciliation should be held soon within the framework of stabilizing the situation in the conflict zone with the mediation of the Assembly of Democratic Forces of the Northern Caucasus. In his opinion, this would make it possible to create real prerequisites for implementing the 30th May decree of the Russian president "On Introducing a State of Emergency on Part of the Territories of the Republic of North Ossetia and the Ingush Republic." The Assembly's secretary general also discussed with Akhsarbek Galazov the issue of relations with the Chechen republic, of involving its leader in the work of the commission to regulate Ossetia-Ingush relations and also of his plans connected with the forthcoming meeting with Dzhokhar Dudayev, leader of Chechnya. Shmidt Dzoblayev said that Groznyy intends to examine relations between Chechnya and Russia, relating to regulating talks with its leadership and to the role of Chechnya in establishing law and order, peace, and calm in the region.
