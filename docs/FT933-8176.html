930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / International Company News: Seibu Saison plans to list hotels chain JAPAN'S Seibu Saison group, led by supermarket operator Seiyu, will list its wholly-owned Intercontinental Hotels Chain (IHC) in either New York or London in two or three years, Reuter reports from Tokyo. Saison will spin off IHC's hotel operating and property management arms into two units and list the former.
