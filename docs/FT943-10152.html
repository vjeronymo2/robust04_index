940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / Finance and the Family: Accolade for FT journalist - At a glance Bethan Hutton, one of the main writers for the Weekend FT Finance and the Family section, was last week named runner-up in the consumer section in the Longford Young Journalist of the Year Awards 1994. The panel of judges included Vincent Duggleby, Presenter of the BBC's Money Box - Live and Janette Rutterford, Abbey National professor of Financial Management at the Open University Business School.
