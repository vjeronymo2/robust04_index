920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / Transport exhibition TOP: A Journey Through Time, an exhibition of transport photographs from 1880 to 1965, opens today at the London Transport Museum, Covent Garden, London. In the 1935 picture are trainee conductors receiving instruction at the Central Buses Training School at Chiswick bus works. ABOVE: The first of a new generation of London buses has been launched by Mr Steven Norris, transport minister. Twenty-three Spectra buses, manufactured by Optare, the Leeds-based subsidiary of United Bus, will be operated by London Central, a subsidiary of London Buses. The Spectra has a technologically advanced body structure, with a low-floor underframe and low entrance steps to make boarding easier.
