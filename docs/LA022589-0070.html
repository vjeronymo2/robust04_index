February 25, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Greg Robinson, 37, a member of the UCLA football coaching staff for the last seven years, was named the Bruins' offensive coordinator, replacing Steve Axman, who resigned Thursday. UCLA Coach Terry Donahue also announced that Dave Currey, 45, former head coach at Cal State Long Beach and Cincinnati, will join the Bruins' staff as an offensive coach.
