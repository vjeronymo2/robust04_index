Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi January 29 TASS -- Georgian leader Eduard Shevardnadze said that Russian President Boris Yeltsin's upcoming visit to Georgia will signify the recognition of Georgia's independence. Speaking at a meeting of heads of cities and districts in Tbilisi on Saturday, Shevardnadze said that "the upcoming visit by Russian President Boris Yeltsin will signify the recognition of Georgia's independence." "The establishment of normal relations with Russia will meet our most vital interests. We will receive the honourable guest as deserved by the president of a great power," Shevardnadze said.
