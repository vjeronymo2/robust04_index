BFN [Excerpt] Prague, April 9 (CTK) -- Jiri Ryvola, Chrudos Belohrad, Zdenek Malek, and Milan Stech were elected vice-chairmen of the Bohemian-Moravian Chamber of Trade Unions (CMKOS) on Friday [8 April] night during the chambers first congress, which is running through today at the Palace of Culture. The first day of the congress, which opened on Friday, finally adjourned at 2:30 a.m. Saturday morning. [passage omitted]
