BFN [Text] Our correspondent Nikolay Kandiashvili has just reported on the telephone that the current stage of the Georgian-Abkhazian talks on the issue of the return of refugees ended in Sochi late this evening. Nikolay Chelkov, head of the Russian delegation, said the talks were proceeding satisfactorily. Thanks to the efforts of the Russian side and the goodwill of all the participants, a quadrilateral agreement that will help resolve a number of problems connected with refugees returning to the places of their permanent abode was signed today.
