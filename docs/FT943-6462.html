940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / International Company News: Hudson's Bay advances 14% Hudson's Bay, Canada's biggest retailer, said second-quarter net profit rose 13.9 per cent to CDollars 18.8m (USDollars 13.6m), or 33 cents a share, from CDollars 16.5m, or 32 cents, a year earlier, with better results from the main Bay department store chain, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Sales were CDollars 520m, up 10.5 per cent, with the Bay division gaining 12.9 per cent and Zellers up 9.8 per cent. Including special items, final profit in the latest quarter was CDollars 40.9m or 72 cents a share. First-half profit before special items was CDollars 22.2m, or 39 cents a share, against CDollars 12.1m, or 23 cents, on fewer shares outstanding. Sales were CDollars 2.5bn against CDollars 2.3bn. The latest period included the former Woodward stores in western Canada.
