May 1, 1990, Tuesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: SIMI VALLEY;

YOUTH ADVISORY PANELISTS SOUGHT

Applicants are being sought to fill four vacancies on the Simi Valley Youth Employment Service Advisory Board.

The group serves as an advisory body to the Simi Valley City Council and as a liaison among local government organizations, schools, young people and businesses.

The board is seeking one student from Apollo High School and one from Royal High School, one representative from a local government agency and one from a local business to complete the four terms of office. Three were vacated about two weeks ago, and one has been vacant since December.

The four will serve until November, when the board makes its annual appointments.

The 15-member board consists of local government and business representatives, faculty or staff members of the Simi Valley Unified School District and students from local high schools. The group meets the third Wednesday of each month at 8 a.m. at City Hall.

Applicants must complete an application and an interview. Appointments are made by the mayor of Simi Valley. KIM KASH
