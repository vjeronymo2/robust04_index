940416 FT 16 APR 94 / Markets: Tesco profits tumble - At a Glance Profits of supermarket giant Tesco fell from Pounds 558m to Pounds 435m in the year to February 26. But the stock market had long realised that Tesco's margins would be crunched by the grocery war. The company's statement that trading was showing a 'pleasing improvement' helped the share price hold steady.
