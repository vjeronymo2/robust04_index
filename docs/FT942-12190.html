940430 FT 30 APR 94 / Finance and the Family: Two new 'Which?' editions - At a glance New editions of two of the staple Which? financial handbooks should save you from addling your memory with too many boring facts. Which? way to save tax 1994 makes it possible for the self-flagellatory to handle their own tax affairs. Which? way to save and invest does a splendid job on the mechanics of investment and savings, though it has understandably little feel for stockpicking. Both books cost Pounds 13.99 in paperback.
