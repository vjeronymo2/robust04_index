BFN ["Directive of the Russian Federation President" No. 174-rp, signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin and dated 5 April 1994] [Text] To agree with the proposal of the Russian Ministry of Defense, coordinated with the Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on the creation of military bases of the Russian Federation Armed Forces on the territories of the member states of the CIS and the Latvian Republic in order to ensure the security of the Russian Federation and the aforementioned states, and also for testing new arms and military equipment. The Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, jointly with the Russian Ministry of Defense and other interested Russian Federation ministries and departments, are to conduct talks on this question with member states of the CIS and the Latvian Republic, and formulate the agreements reached in appropriate documents. [Signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin [Dated] 5 April 1994 No. 174-rp
