940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Fleming Overseas Fleming Overseas Investment Trust increased net asset value per share by nine per cent from 306.6p to 334.6p in the year to June 30. Net available revenue came out at Pounds 6.25m (Pounds 6.35m) and earnings per share were 4.7p (4.78p). The recommended final dividend of 2.75p (2.7p) makes a total of 4.25p (4.2p).
