Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The president's press and information office has just issued a report on President Berisha's meeting with the Macedonian foreign minister. Albania is interested in stability in Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], President Berisha said at this meeting today. Albania is of the opinion that respect for all the rights of the Albanian population there is a condition for stability. Thus, the census and the opening of the Pedagogical School will be important steps in this direction. On the other hand, Berisha invites Albanian political forces to work for the stability of Macedonia, as this is in their interest, as well as that of the Albanian nation and the whole region. President Berisha said that by exchanging greetings with President Gligorov, I want to assure you that Albania is ready to cooperate with you to increase the spirit of understanding and cooperation and establish them on sound bases. To overcome the blockade difficulties, Albania offers it all port and transport facilities and hopes that the conflicts between Macedonia and Greece can be overcome through dialogue. Macedonian Foreign Minister Crvenkovski said that, by conveying President Gligorov's greetings to President Berisha, he would like to express the deep gratitude of our government for the support that Albania has given to Macedonia. We thank you for the facilities that you are creating for us at these difficult moments of the blockade. He again stressed his government's desire to strengthen cooperation with Albania. Macedonian Foreign Minister Crvenkovski has ended his trip to our country.
