BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 7 (KCNA) -- A congratulatory group of Koreans in China under the National Alliance for the Country's Reunification (Pomminnyon) led by Nam Su-nam, first vice-chairman of the Pomminnyon headquarters of Koreans in China, arrived here today to celebrate the 15th of April. Also arriving here were a Cuban art troupe, a Romanian art troupe, a group of Russian actors and actresses, Russian acrobats, Russian soloists, a Moldova art troupe, a Bulgarian acrobatic troupe, the Kubalinka folk music troupe of Belarus, a Syrian musician, a trio of the Congo, the Donbas dance group of Ukraine, Ukrainean soloists and Yugoslav soloists to participate in the 12th April spring friendship art festival.
