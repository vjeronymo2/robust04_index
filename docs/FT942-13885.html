940422 FT 22 APR 94 / UK Company News: Yorklyde rises 35% to Pounds 2.98m Shares in Yorklyde, the Huddersfield-based maker of fine cloths, scarves and rugs, rose by 31p to 270p yesterday on news of a 35 per cent rise in pre-tax profits to Pounds 2.98m for the 12 months to January 31. The advance, from Pounds 2.2m, was achieved on sales of Pounds 15.4m (Pounds 13.3m). Mr Charles Brook, chairman, said all divisions had experienced increased activity, which was being maintained in the current year. The dividend is raised to 6p (5.2p) via a proposed final of 3.8p. Earnings per share jumped 32 per cent to 20.6p (15.6p).
