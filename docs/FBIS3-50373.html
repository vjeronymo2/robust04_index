Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Serbs are preparing a major offensive against Bihac, and their aim is to take this town and link the republic of Srpska with the so-called Krajina, said General Vlado Santic, Chief of General Staff of the HVO [Croatian Defense Council] in the Bihac region. He has left this town and is on a temporary visit to Split. Gen. Santic said that Serbs were amassing tanks and howitzers around Bihac and bringing in fresh troops from parts of Bosnia-Herzegovina unaffected by war.
