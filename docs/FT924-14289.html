921010 FT 10 OCT 92 / Finance & The Family: Guaranteed rate from Woolwich Woolwich is promising to guarantee the interest rate - for just under three months - on its Premium Investment Plus. The one year term account guarantees to pay a minimum of 5 per cent above the ordinary share rate. The rates are 9.75 per cent gross on a minimum balance of Pounds 10,000; 10.2 per cent gross on Pounds 25,000 and 10.5 per cent on Pounds 50,000 or more. These will be held until January 1993. Woolwich this week increased the interest rate on deposits of Pounds 1 to Pounds 499 on its Prime Gold account from 2.35 per cent gross to 3.5 per cent gross.
