930406 FT 06 APR 93 / Chad forum agrees charter Chad's national pro-democracy conference has approved a charter setting rules for a 12-month transition to multi-party rule in the central African country. Reuter reports from N'djamena. The all-party forum voted at the weekend to adopt a 'transitional charter' governing the roles of the president, transitional government and interim parliament in the run-up to Chad's first free polls since independence from France in 1960.
