930616 FT 16 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Cardiff Property ahead CARDIFF Property yesterday announced pre-tax profits up 27 per cent to Pounds 35,861 for the half year to March 31; it also announced an offer to acquire the outstanding shares in Village Residential. Cardiff currently owns 32.8 per cent of Village and a cash offer with part share alternative is being made to acquire the outstanding equity. Village is an unquoted residential development company. The assets have been managed by Cardiff since that date. The offer values Village at Pounds 970,000. Earnings were 1.19p (1.04p) and the interim is maintained at 0.85p.
