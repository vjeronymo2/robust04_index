BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 9 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received congratulatory messages from foreign party leaders on his birthday. Messages came from Eric Trevett, general secretary of the C.C. [Central Committee], the new Communist Party of Great Britain; Armando Villa Nueva, general secretary of the National Executive Committee of the American Popular Revolutionary Alliance of Peru; Luis Mateo Munos, general secretary of the Socialist Party of Peru; Gustavo Tablada, chairman of the Nicaraguan Socialist Party; Terence Marryshow, political leader of the Maurice Bishop Patriotic Movement of Grenada; George Belle, secretary general of the Barbados Workers' Party; Rene Mauge Mosquera, general secretary of the C.C., the Communist Party of Ecuador and chairman of the Broad Left Democratic Front of Ecuador; and Rigoberto Padilla Ruz, general secretary of the C.C., the Communist Party of Honduras. They extend warmest congratulations to President Kim Il-song and wish him a long life in good health. They also express firm solidarity with the Workers' Party and the people of Korea in the efforts for peace and reunification of the country. President Kim Il-song received a congratulatory message also from the Executive Committee of the C.C., the Communist Party of Uruguay on his birthday.
