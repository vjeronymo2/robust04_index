940905 FT 05 SEP 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Hong Kong Sun Hung Kai plans to set up a joint venture fund with Shanghai Shenyin Securities to invest in China's A share markets, now confined to domestic investors. The size of the fund has yet to be finalised, but it is expected to range from Dollars 50m to hundreds of millions of dollars. The plan is subject to approval from Beijing.
