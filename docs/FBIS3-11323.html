Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Article by Liz Martinez] [Text] Health and Social Welfare Minister Vicente Perez Davila presented his irrevocable resignation yesterday. Health problems related to his gastrointestinal system have forced the minister to resign. Perez Davila coordinated the health area of President Rafael Caldera's government and later headed the liaison commission with the Health and Social Welfare Ministry. In spite of his youth and having a good professional record, today Perez Davila is leaving the ministry for health reasons. He expressed his regrets to President Rafael Caldera. It was learned yesterday morning that Perez Davila sent a letter to the president of the Republic in which he explained the reasons for his resignation. In that letter he told President Rafael Caldera that he is sure the work of the new authorities at the Health and Social Welfare Ministry will be successful, because the persons working in the ministry are highly qualified and motivated. President Caldera will now have to appoint a new health and social welfare minister, which must be a person highly qualified to direct a ministry confronting a serious hospital crisis.
