930819 FT 19 AUG 93 / One in five UK exporters suffer losses ONE IN five UK exporters has suffered losses during the past year because of non-payment by customers elsewhere in the European Community, NCM Credit Insurance, Britain's leading export credit insurer, said yesterday, David Dodwell writes. The cost of payment delays rose 52 per cent in the 12 months to June 30, NCM said in its quarterly study, which draws on the records of 2.5m companies worldwide. Losses due to non-payment were highest in France, where 20 per cent of exporters reported losses in the year to June 30.
