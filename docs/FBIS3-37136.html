Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow February 11 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin was prepared for telephone talks with President Bill Clinton on Bosnia on February 9 and 10, as proposed by the U.S. side, but the talks did not take place for reasons beyond the Russian side's control, according to a competent source in the Russian president's staff. Officials of the presidential communications service told ITAR-TASS the Russian side had ensured all the required technical prerequisites for the telephone talks between the two presidents.
