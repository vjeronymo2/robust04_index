930403 FT 03 APR 93 / UK Company News: 25% pay rise for Laporte executive Mr Ken Minton, chief executive and managing director of Laporte, the chemicals company, received a 25 per cent increase in his overall pay, from Pounds 277,596 to Pounds 348,003, for the 53 weeks ended January 3. Mr Minton said the figure, based on performance and achievement, has been set by the company's remuneration committee.
