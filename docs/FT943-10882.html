940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: The Sun's MacKenzie quits BSkyB Kelvin MacKenzie, who dominated The Sun for 13 years, resigned as managing director of BSkyB last night less than seven months after joining the satellite television station owned by News International. MacKenzie blamed a personality clash for his resignation. There was speculation that he might be on his way back to national newspapers. Page 16; Court backs radio licence loophole, Page 6
