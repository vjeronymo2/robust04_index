Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] President Joaquim Chissano today begins his trip home after attending the World Economic Forum in Davos, Switzerland. Chissano will stop over in Lisbon for talks with President Mario Soares and Portuguese Prime Minister Cavaco Silva. The head of state will also stop over in Luanda for a tripartite meeting with Angolan President Jose Eduardo dos Santos and African National Congress leader Nelson Mandela.
