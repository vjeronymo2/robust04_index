Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] An Egyptian parliamentary delegation led by People's Assembly Speaker Dr. Ahmad Fathi Surur returned to Cairo last night, having participated in the 91st session of the Interparliamentary Union, which was held in Paris. During the session, which lasted a week, the Egyptian delegation urged protection for the Palestinians living in the occupied territories, calling for a halt to Israel's illegitimate practices against the Palestinians. The delegation also denounced Serbian atrocities against Bosnian Muslims, calling on the international community to take action to end the atrocities.
