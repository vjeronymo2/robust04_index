January 8, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Thomas Muster of Austria, Emilio Sanchez of Spain and Soviet teen-ager Natalia Zvereva won warm-ups for the Australian Open tennis tournament.

Muster beat Jimmy Arias, 3-6, 6-2, 7-5 at Adelaide; Sanchez beat Richey Reneberg, 6-7 (3-7), 6-4, 4-6, 6-4, 6-1, at Wellington, N.Z., and Zvereva beat Australia's Rachel McQuillan, 6-4, 6-0 at Brisbane.
