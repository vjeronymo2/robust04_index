BFN [Text] In conformity with a decree issued by President Nazarbayev, Yuriy Ivanovich Lavrinenko is appointed head of the East Kazakhstan Oblast administration. Galymzhan Badylzhan-uly Zhakianov is appointed head of the Semipalatinsk Oblast administration. Albert Gergardovich Salomatin is appointed head of Zhezkazgan Oblast administration. The latter is relieved of his post as the Kazakh minister of industry. Ravil Tazhkora-uly Shardabayev is appointed minister of oil and gas of Kazakhstan. Vyacheslav Vasilievich Kostyuchenko is appointed minister of trade and industry of Kazakhstan. Baykarim William-uly Totenov is appointed minister of social insurance minister of Kazakhstan.
