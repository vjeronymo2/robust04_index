July 1, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

EDITOR SORRY HE PICKED A BONE WITH MILLIE

"The Washingtonian" magazine Friday said it was sorry it called the presidential pet the ugliest canine in the capital.

"Before you go away for the July 4th weekend, we would like to apologize for picking Millie the ugliest dog in Washington," Editor John A. Limpert said in a letter delivered Friday to the White House. He also sent a gift of dog biscuits to the offended beast.

"You'll be pleased to know that our mail and phone calls are running about 99 to 1 in favor of Millie," he said.
