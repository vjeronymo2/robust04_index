BFN [No video available] [Text] A new taxation system will begin to operate in Ukraine from 1 January 1995. It envisages a change from taxing enterprises' income to taxing their profits. Vitaliy Ilyin, the head of our state's state tax inspectorate, stated this in an interview for the press. He emphasized that the introduction of new legislative acts was planned for this year, 1994. But it was impossible to implement this in conditions of an economic crisis and inflation.
