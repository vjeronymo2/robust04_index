Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Choe Ki-chong, chairman of the DPRK Education Committee, and his delegation left Damascus this evening after a three-day visit to Syria during which he met with President Hafiz al-Asad. He was seen off at Damascus international airport by Education Minister Muhammad Ghassan al-Halabi and the DPRK ambassador in Damascus. The guest minister held educational talks on means of promoting educational relations through the implementation of the Syrian-DPRK cultural agreement.
