920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / International Company News: MGM-Pathe reveals Dollars 233m loss MGM-PATHE Commun-ications, the financially troubled Hollywood studio controlled by Credit Lyonnais, the French state bank, yesterday unveiled a Dollars 233.2m net loss for the 39-week period to September 28, writes Alan Friedman. The loss, struck on 47 per cent higher revenues of Dollars 703m, compares with a Dollars 65.2m loss in the nine-month period to August 28 1990. Mr Gary Lieberthal, chairman of the television division of Sony's Columbia Pictures, announced his departure yesterday. A successor has not yet been named.
