BFN [Text] Rafah, 18 May (MENA)--A delegation of Palestinian civilian policemen specializing in traffic, investigation, passport, and police station work crossed Rafah border point today into Gaza. Another group that is trained to protect individuals and vital installations, perform basic security operations, and guard civilian administrations, ports, borders, and service departments also crossed Rafah border point today. They were led by Brigadier Generals 'Abd-al-Hayy Muhammad 'Abd-al-Wahid and Musa 'Arafat. The two groups, totalling 530 people, are from the 'Ayn Jalut and maritime police forces and had received training in Egypt.
