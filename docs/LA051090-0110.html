May 10, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

5 CLU PLAYERS NAMED ALL-DISTRICT IN SOFTBALL

Five Cal Lutheran players have been selected to the All-District 3 softball team. Pitchers DeeAndra Pilkington and Leslie Stevens, catcher Cheryl Aschenbach, shortstop Dani Piotrowski and utility player Kim White were chosen in balloting by District 3 coaches.

Third baseman Tina Bumgarner and outfielders Michelle Campos and Brenda Frafjord represented Cal Lutheran, the District 3 regular-season and tournament champion, on the second team.
