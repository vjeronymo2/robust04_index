930824 FT 24 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Home Office says defecting Iraqi diplomats can stay Two senior Iraqi diplomats who defected to the UK are to be allowed to stay in the country the Home Office confirmed last night. The Iraqi National Congress, an umbrella group for opponents of President Saddam Hussein in the west, said the two - whom it described as long-serving ambassadors - would announce at a news conference in London today that they were joining the opposition movement.
