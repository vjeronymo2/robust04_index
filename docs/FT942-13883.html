940422 FT 22 APR 94 / UK Company News: Wyndeham Press makes Pounds 6.54m acquisitions Wyndeham Press Group, the printing and packaging company formerly known as SW Wood, is making three acquisitions for a total of up to Pounds 6.54m and raising Pounds 5.26m through a placing and open offer. It also forecast pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.7m (Pounds 1.02m) for the year to March 31 and expects to pay a final dividend of 1.5p for a total of 2.25p (1.25p). It is buying Westway, a lithographic printer, and B+P, its associated finishing house, for a total of Pounds 5.54m, comprising Pounds 5.33m cash and the balance in shares. Unity Paper Tubes, which makes cardboard tubes, is being acquired for up to Pounds 1m in shares and a profit and net asset-related cash element of about Pounds 850,000. Most of the cash payments will be met from the issue of 5.26m shares which have been placed at 100p. Shareholders can apply for shares on an 8-for-25 basis. The shares closed at 105p yesterday, down 1p. Westway reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 710,000 (Pounds 286,000) in the year to January 31 on turnover of Pounds 4.52m (Pounds 3.56m). Profits for B+P for 1993 were Pounds 289,000 (Pounds 115,000) on turnover of Pounds 2.08m (Pounds 1.76m). For the year to March 31 1993 Unity reported profits of Pounds 111,000.
