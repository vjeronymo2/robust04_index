October 13, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

ALPHA LEAGUE

Montclair Prep 49, Western Christian 0 -- Sophomore running back Eliel Swinton rushed for 234 yards in 27 carries and scored four touchdowns to lead Montclair Prep (3-3, 1-0) at Pierce College.

Three Western Christian turnovers in the first half led to Montclair Prep scores and the Mounties took a 35-0 halftime lead. Western Christian (1-5, 0-1) was held to 10 yards in the first half. DAVID DONNELLY
