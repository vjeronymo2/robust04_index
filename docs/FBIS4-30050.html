BFN [Text] Seoul, June 23 (YONHAP) -- North Korea has invited South Korean sports officials to a meeting this weekend to discuss the second East Asia games slated for Sept. 20 next year in Pyongyang. An official at the Korean Amateur Sports Association (KASA) said on Thursday that the Pyongyang East Asia Games Organizing Committee extended the invitation to KASA, informing that the Pyongyang games' eighth consultative meeting would be held in Taipei on June 26-28. The message dated May 25 was delivered through the Taiwan Olympic Committee. A copy of the message was later sent to the Korean Olympic Committee [KOC] via the Chinese Olympic Committee, the official said. Originally North Korea planned to hold the eighth meeting in Japan during the Hiroshima Asian games period. But, at the suggestion by games member countries that it would be too late, North Korea advanced the time of the meeting to this weekend, a sports source said. In response to the invitation, KASA would send three delegates including Pak Sang-ha, KOC International Committee chairman, to the Taipei meeting, the official added.
