940221 FT 21 FEB 94 / Company News (This Week): Unilever - Other Companies Full year figures from the Anglo-Dutch giant are due tomorrow with estimated profits ranging from Pounds 2.25bn pre-tax to Pounds 2.325bn, against Pounds 2.029bn previously. Trading has been tough in Europe, mixed in the US and strong in Asia-Pacific. There may be big rationalisation charges.
