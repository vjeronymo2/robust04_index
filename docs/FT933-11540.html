930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / Rees-Mogg ruling due on Friday THE RESULT of Lord Rees-Mogg's High Court move to block ratification of the Maastricht Treaty is due on Friday. Yesterday Lord Justice Lloyd, sitting with Lord Justice Mann and Mr Justice Auld, said the court needed 'a little time to consider our judgment'. Whatever the decision, the case is expected to go to the Court of Appeal.
