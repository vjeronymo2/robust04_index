BFN [Text] A delegation of the Russian Foreign Ministry headed by Deputy Minister Sergey Krylov arrived in Minsk for a working visit. The members of the delegation were received by Deputy Prime Minister Uladzimir Zalamay and Foreign Minister Pyotr Krawchanka. During the talks, the parties discussed interaction between the two foreign ministries and current bilateral relations between their states. A protocol regarding cooperation between the foreign ministries was agreed upon and initialed.
