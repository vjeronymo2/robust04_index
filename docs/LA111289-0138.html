November 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

LAGUNA CANYON;

BUILDING PLAN DRAWS 2,000 PROTESTERS

About 2,000 people marched through Laguna Canyon to protest a proposed project that would bring 3,000 new homes and a golf course to the rustic area. The Irvine Co.'s Laguna Laurel project -- a proposed 3,000-home residential community and golf course surrounding three lakes -- "would forever change this pristine site," endangering unique plant and animal habitats, said Henry Huggins, a march coordinator. The project has been approved by county supervisors but construction has not begun.
