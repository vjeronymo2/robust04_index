Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Fu'ad al-Batayinah, charge d'affaires of the Jordanian mission at the United Nations, has called on the Security Council to embark on measures to protect the Palestinians under occupation in accordance with the Security Council resolutions on the illegitimacy of Israeli settlements. Addressing the Security Council during its session last night to discuss the massacre at the holy Ibrahimi Mosque, al-Batayinah said: What is required is to provide effective protection for the Palestinian people to ensure their safety, security, and basic liberties, to save the holy places from desecration, and to remove the Israeli settlements. The objective, he added, is to continue the peace process to reach a just and honorable settlement based on Israeli withdrawal from all occupied Arab territory and to ensure the Palestinian people's legitimate rights.
