April 28, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MEESE ON CRIME FIGHTING

Ironic The Times would publish an article on fighting crime by Mr. Law and Order -- I don't remember, my goal was to protect the presidency -- Ed Meese. Strange, too, the number of characters who've flown the flag of law and order: Spiro Agnew, Richard Nixon, Ronald Reagan, Oliver North, et al.

RENE ELFER

Whittier
