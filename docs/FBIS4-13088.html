BFN [Text] In Jammu and Kashmir, security forces claim to have achieved notable success in their operations against the militants. One hundred and three militants were killed during the last month. Security forces have seized more than 13,000 weapons, including 11 lakh ammunition, and about 40,000 grenades. An official spokesman told our Jammu correspondent that this is mainly because of cooperation being extended by people who are fed up with militancy sponsored by Pakistan.
