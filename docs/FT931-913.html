930327 FT 27 MAR 93 / Canary Wharf plan outlined THE ADMINISTRATORS of Canary Wharf, the office development in London's Docklands, have outlined proposals to end the administration. Unsecured creditors would receive 15p in the pound, totalling about Pounds 70m. The funds for the proposed voluntary arrangement would be made available by the project's banks if a successful exit from administration was agreed.
