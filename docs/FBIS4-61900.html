BFN ["News Release"] [Text] On Tuesday [10 May], biggest Estonian opposition party Estonian Centrist Party and Estonian Entrepreneurs Party signed a cooperation protocol, which determines basis for further closer cooperation of the parties, spokesman of the Centrist Party told ETA. "Estonian political forces are split up and personality- centered," the protocol says, adding that "time demanded cooperation of parties with close programmic positions", forecasting association of Estonian Centrist oriented political powers to achieve better results in the interests of the Estonian state. The document prescribes cooperation in opposition, both to organize joint political actions and to prepare a programmed to "lead Estonia's economic and political government after the next parliamentary elections".
