930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Regal Hotel Regal Hotel Group, the USM-quoted company in the process of restructuring, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.09m for the year to December 27 1992, against Pounds 844,000. Turnover rose from Pounds 725,000 to Pounds 2.95m. The figures cover the three hotels owned by its Bramhope offshoot but not the three acquisitions announced since the year end. Losses per share were 0.59p (7.85p) on the capital increased by the Pounds 800,000 rights issue.
