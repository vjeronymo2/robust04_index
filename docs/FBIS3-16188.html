Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Akimov] [Text] Almaty March 15 TASS -- Local press in Kazakhstan has reported that a strong fire damaged one of the assembly and testing installations and headquarters of a military unit which serviced the installation in Baykonur, the former Soviet Union's main launching site, now contested by Russia and Kazakhstan. According to informed sources, the preliminary damages amount to three billion roubles. Fortunately only telemetric devices were damaged apart from the buildings. All other technological equipment is intact. The cosmodrome has a back-up set of equipment, and although the lift-off of the space ferry Progress, scheduled for March 18, will not be put off, specialists say that the fire will not delay the space programme.
