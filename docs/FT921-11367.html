920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Specialeyes sees pressure on margins Specialeyes, the USM-quoted retail optician, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 56,000 to Pounds 90,000 in the 24 weeks to November 16. Turnover improved 12 per cent to Pounds 7.5m and trading profit a similar amount to Pounds 136,000. The pre-tax line was struck after new store opening/closure costs of Pounds 108,000, offset by a release of reorganisation provisions of Pounds 106,000. Earnings per share were 0.4p (0.38p). Eight new high street stores were opened which together with one new Allders concession brought the total outlets to 68. The company's year-end is being changed to November 30 and the current accounting period will cover 18 months. Shareholders will receive a report for the 12 months to May 31.
