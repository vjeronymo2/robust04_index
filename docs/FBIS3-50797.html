Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] San Jose, 7 Jan (NOTIMEX) -- Today Costa Rican President Rafael Angel Calderon expressed his solidarity with the Mexican Government, and he called on the rebel groups operating in several towns in the Mexican state of Chiapas to stop the violence. The president said in a telephone call, "I expressed our broad and brotherly solidarity to Mexican President Carlos Salinas de Gortari, and today I want to take this opportunity to publicly reiterate it." Calderon said that the violent actions in Chiapas are unjustified now that Salinas de Gortari "has made Mexico a nation of great economic and social development, the fruits of which are already being experienced by the people." Violence, he stressed, has never been a foundation for democratization processes or a permanent base for social achievements.
