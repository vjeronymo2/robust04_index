930112 FT 12 JAN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Mayhew claims shift within IRA PRESSURE within the IRA for an end to violence is growing, Sir Patrick Mayhew, the Northern Ireland secretary, said yesterday. Leaders of the republican terrorist organisation knew no British government, either Labour or Conservative, would talk to those who resort to violence, Sir Patrick said. He said talks on the province's future would resume once the new Irish government was in place. The last initiative collapsed in November.
