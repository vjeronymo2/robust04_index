Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday, a Ukrainian military delegation headed by Defense Minister Vitaliy Radetskyy visited Constanta, where they met with Vice Admiral George Antonescu, chief of the Romanian Navy Joint Chiefs of Staff. They also visited the torpedo boat [word indistinct] and the Navy Academy. After returning to Bucharest, Vitaliy Radetskyy met with Romanian Foreign Minister Teodor Melescanu. In the course of the conversation, the sides emphasized the contribution that both countries can make in strengthening security and stability in the region that is currently experiencing difficulties. The Ukrainian defense minister was received by Romanian President Ion Iliescu. During their talks, a mutual desire to develop Romanian-Ukrainian relations in the interests of both countries and people was emphasized.
