Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vienna, Jan. 24 (YONHAP) -- North Korea and the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) resumed their working-level contact here on Monday morning local time on the issue of inspecting North Korean nuclear facilities. A source said that at the fifth contact taking placing at the IAEA headquarters, North Korea was giving its position with regard to the scope of inspection laid down by the IAEA last week.
