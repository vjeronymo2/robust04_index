BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Mikhail Shevtsov] [Text] Moscow, 25 Apr -- A visit to Ukraine by Russian President Boris Yeltsin, during which he is expected to sign a large-scale treaty between the two states, is being mooted, Yuriy Dubinin, special envoy of the Russian Foreign Ministry, said today at a news conference at the Russian Defense Ministry. Yuriy Dubinin explained to the ITAR-TASS correspondent that agreement on this had been reached during Boris Yeltsin's meeting with Leonid Kravchuk in Moscow on 15 April. The exact date of the visit has not yet been finalized, the diplomat noted.
