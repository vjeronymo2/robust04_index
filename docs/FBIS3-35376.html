Language: Dari Article Type:BFN [Text] Esteemed Sardar Asef Ahmad Ali, the Pakistani minister of foreign affairs, arrived this afternoon in Kabul. According to a report by BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY, the esteemed Minister of Foreign Affairs of Pakistan met Ustad Abdol Ali Mazari, General Secretary of Hezb-e Wahdat-e Eslami-ye [Islamic Unity Party of Afghanistan], at 1700, and held talks on issues of interest. The esteemed minister of foreign affairs of Pakistan, later, took part in an eftar [evening break fast] function launched in his honor by esteemed Abdorrab Rasul Sayyaf, Amir of Ettehad-e Eslami [Islamic Union of Afghanistan], and later formal talks were held between Foreign Minister of Pakistan and Sayyaf.
