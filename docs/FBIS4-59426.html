BFN [Text] A soldier in an IDF [Israel Defense Forces] outpost in Jabalyah in the Gaza Strip was wounded in the knee by terrorists' gunfire. The soldier was taken to Soroka Hospital in Beersheba. Our Army affairs correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that several shots were fired at the outpost. The soldiers returned fire, and then another salvo was fired at them. A man suffering gunshot wounds was later found in al-Shifa' Hospital in Gaza, and the possibility that he may have been one of the terrorists is currently being examined. A truck driver was wounded by gunfire near the settlement of Morag in the Qatif Bloc. He was moderately wounded and was evacuated to Soroka Hospital in Beersheba.
