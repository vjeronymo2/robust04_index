930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / Construction Contracts: Sports facility in Hong Kong WEMBLEY has won a 10-year contract to manage the new 40,000 capacity Hong Kong Stadium (pictured) in Happy Valley from the Urban Development Council on behalf of the Government and funded by the Royal Hong Kong Jockey Club. The Pounds 71m stadium is one of the most significant new building projects in the region and on completion in 1994 will become the territory's premier sport and entertainment venue. It replaces the existing 28,000 capacity venue, which has been demolished to make way for the new development.
