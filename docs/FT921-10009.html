920208 FT 08 FEB 92 / Finance & the Family: Sun Life looks to Pacific Sun Life is launching a Pacific Growth unit trust, which will invest in Far East economies, excluding Japan. The seven countries in which it invests (and the proportions of the portfolio each is expected to represent) are as follows: Hong Kong (36 per cent), Australia (24), Singapore (20), Malaysia (9), South Korea (1) Thailand (2) and Taiwan (2). Around 5 per cent of the portfolio will be held in cash. There will be a 1 per cent discount during the offer period, which runs until February 28. Minimum investment is Pounds 1,000, and there is a monthly savings plan option starting at Pounds 25. Front-end charge is 5.75 per cent, annual charge 1.5 per cent.
