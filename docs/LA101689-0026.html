October 16, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Comedian Roseanne Barr may have bared it all at the opening game of the World Series in Oakland, but her publicist doesn't know anything about it. With her team, the Giants, trailing 5-0 under a moonlit sky, Barr and her fiance Tom Arnold reportedly pulled down their pants to express their feelings -- Barr revealing a tattoo declaring "Tom" and Arnold baring a tattoo saying "Rosie." "I have no idea whether she did or not," Barr's personal manager Arlene Rothberg said when asked about the reported crowd "mooning" incident. Other more subdued notable fans at the game included Huey Lewis, Tom Brokaw, Peter Ueberroth, John Travolta, Michael Keaton, Reggie Jackson and John Fogarty. Shauna Snow
