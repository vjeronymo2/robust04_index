BFN [Text] Referring to the historic Israeli-PLO Agreement, the Foreign Ministry, representing the European Union [EU], stressed that the EU warmly welcomes the signing of the agreement and particularly respects the two sides' decisiveness not to allow extremist elements to raise obstacles in front of peace. The EU is calling on all sides to play full and positive roles on the bilateral and multilateral levels of the peace agreement. New Democracy hailed the agreement between Israel and the Palestinians, describing it as a historic and courageous step toward a permanent peace settlement in the region. Political Spring expressed hope that the courageous message of peace will find imitators in every distressed corner of the world, especially Bosnia-Herzegovina. A Coalition of the Left and Progress statement notes that the dream of past decades is now becoming a reality and the lengthy struggles of the heroic Palestinian people are justified.
