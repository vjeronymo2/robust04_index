BFN [Excerpt] President Lennart Meri yesterday released Finance Minister Heiki Kranich and Culture and Education Minister Paul-Eerik Rummo from their posts. The official notice from the presidential press office late last night says that until the new ministers are appointed, Economics Minister Toivo Jurgenson will be acting finance minister, and Population Minister Peeter Olesk will be acting culture and education minister. Yesterday Prime Minister Mart Laar offered the post of finance minister to Andres Lipstok, the district elder of Laanemaa district. Andres Lipstok graduated from the economics department of Tartu University where he specialized in finance and credit, and the prime minister has invited him to join the government as a financial specialist. According to the prime minister's secretariat, Lipstok agreed to take the post of finance minister if the [government] coalition agrees. [passage omitted]
