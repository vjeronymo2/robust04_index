920512 FT 12 MAY 92 / UK Company News: LAIT improves 21% to Pounds 1.03m Pre-tax profits of London & Associated Investment Trust, which was recently reclassified as a property company, improved by 21 per cent to Pounds 1.03m for the year to end-December. Earnings per share declined to 1.14p (1.34p). A proposed final dividend of 0.48p lifts the total to 0.53p (0.5p) on the capital increased via last summer's Pounds 5m rights issue. Year-end gearing amounted to 32 per cent and cash deposits currently stand at Pounds 3m. The group's property portfolio of more than 500 shops, which currently produces a rental income in excess of Pounds 4m per annum, will be externally valued at December 31. Profits of Bisichi Mining, which is 40 per cent-owned by LAIT, slipped from Pounds 137,000 to Pounds 106,000 pre-tax over the 12 months to end-December. Earnings amounted to 0.75p (1.01p) and the dividend is a same-again 0.6p.
