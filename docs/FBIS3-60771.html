Language: Tagalog Article Type:BFN [Text] Another Muslim mosque in Davao was bombed at dawn today, injuring five persons. According to the report, the incident happened at about 0200. The mosque was reportedly full of Maranao Muslims that were praying when the bombing occurred. The incident is now being investigated by government authorities who still has no strong leads. No group has also claimed responsibility for the bombing incident. The incident is the third bombing that happened in religious centers in Davao for the past three days.
