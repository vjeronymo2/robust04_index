December 23, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

RIDING HIGH

Thank you very much for showing that Winona Ryder "Acting as Fast as She Can," Dec. 9) is a meritorious young woman rather than a silly teen-ager.

Ryder is one of the best actresses out there today. It doesn't matter if she's 19 or 40, she knows what she wants and she's getting it.

VALENTINA SILVA

Norwalk
