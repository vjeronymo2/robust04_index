931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / International Company News: Body Shop lifts profits to Pounds 10m BODY Shop International, the natural cosmetics and toiletries manufacturer and retailer, increased interim pre-tax profits to Pounds 10m (Dollars 15.1m) from Pounds 8.3m, ahead of expectations, writes Maggie Urry in London. UK sales rose 6.9 per cent to Pounds 37.4m, although price increases were 'minimal' and operating profits were up 5 per cent to Pounds 3.9m. US sales rose 77.7 per cent to Pounds 19.9m and operating profits were up to Pounds 1.3m from Pounds 800,000. Sales from the rest of the world rose 21.4 per cent to Pounds 25.5m, with operating profits up 8 per cent to Pounds 5.5m. Lex, Page 18
