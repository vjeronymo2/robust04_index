930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / World Trade News: Satellite first for new entrant A US-Russian joint venture to sell satellite launches on Russian rockets has won its first contract, Daniel Green reports from London. Lockheed-Khrunichev-Energia International (LKEI), competing against Arianespace, McDonnell Douglas and General Dynamics, has won an agreement to launch up to five satellites built by Space Systems Loral, a US satellite builder. Launches on Russian satellites cost about two-thirds those on western launchers.
