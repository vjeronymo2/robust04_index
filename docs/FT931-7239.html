930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Farringford subscription terms revealed Farringford, the one-time soft drinks maker whose only current operating subsidiary is the Farringford Hotel (Freshwater) on the Isle of Wight, has revealed the terms of its subscription agreement announced on January 22. Under the terms, Mr Giuliano Lotto, Pontin Investments and Northern Trust Company will together subscribe for 500,000 5 per cent convertible redeemable preference shares of Pounds 1 each at par. In the six months to August 31, the company reported losses of Pounds 151,000 on turnover of Pounds 387,000. Sir Fred Pontin holds a 36.6 per cent stake in Farringford.
