940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / Condom maker gives pledges LRC Products, manufacturer of Durex condoms, has given undertakings not to enter into agreements under which wholesalers or retailers will stock only LRC condoms. The company, which has about 75 per cent of the UK condom market, was asked to give the undertakings by Sir Bryan Carsberg, director-general of fair trading, after a Monopolies and Mergers Commission investigation this year.
