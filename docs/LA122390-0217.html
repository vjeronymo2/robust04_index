December 23, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: STUDENT UNION FUND GETS $600,000 PROMISE

The Claremont Graduate School has received a "challenge grant" of $600,000 from the Weingart Foundation of Los Angeles toward the cost of constructing and furnishing a new Graduate Student Union building.

In order to receive the grant, the school must raise additional funds to finance the estimated $4.3 million project.
