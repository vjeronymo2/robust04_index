Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] There was a Turkish provocation on the day of our national holiday [25 March]. According to reports, yesterday morning when the military parade was taking place in Athens, about 20 Turkish F-16 and F-4 fighter aircraft entered the central Aegean without any previous notice, thus violating our national airspace and infringing on the rules of air traffic. Greek aircraft took off immediately and hastened to intercept the Turkish aircraft.
