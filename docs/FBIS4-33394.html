BFN [From the "Union Radio News" newscast] [Text] The Venezuelan church will take care of Haitian refugees who have been selected by the committees that comprise the UN High Commissioner for Refugees [UNHCR] and the U.S. Immigration and Naturalization Service. (Gay Prim), regional representative for the UNHCR, said that the selection program for these people will cost approximately $1.5 million. It was also learned that the Haitian refugees may also choose between Canada, the United States, France, and Argentina, which are countries comprising the Friends of Haiti Group.
