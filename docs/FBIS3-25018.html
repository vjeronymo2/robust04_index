Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Somalia: Gino Del Nero, a rescue worker working for the Italian NGO, CISP, the International Committee for the Development of Peoples, was kidnapped this morning at Dusa Mareb, 300 km east of Mogadishu, where the organization has a base for rescue operations. It has been reported that Del Nero was kidnapped as he was returning to the base. The kidnappers reportedly asked for a $10,000 ransom.
