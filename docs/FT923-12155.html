920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Goode Durrant - Correction Goode Durrant, the mini-conglomerate involved in vehicle hire, motor distribution and housebuilding, incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.13m in the year to April 30. The Pounds 2.98m referred to in Saturday's FT was the after-tax loss.
