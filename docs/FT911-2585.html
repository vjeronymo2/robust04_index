910429 FT 29 APR 91 / European bank plea by NatWest A EUROPEAN central bank should be based in London, already the leading financial centre of Europe, Lord Alexander, chairman of National Westminster Bank, said yesterday. Writing in NatWest's Quarterly Review, he urged the government to play a positive role in the development of the European monetary system. 'Our pre-eminence must be maintained on merit, or jobs, prosperity and international influence will evaporate overnight,' he said.
