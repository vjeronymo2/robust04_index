BFN [Text] A military spokesman in Aden today accused northern armored forces of encircling a southern brigade and of taking up fighting positions in an area adjacent to the southern 1st Brigade in Yarim. The southern Yemeni spokesman warned of the consequences of involving the country in a destructive civil war, particularly after last week's clashes between southern and northern military units in the 'Imran area which left more than 200 soldiers killed or wounded and caused heavy losses of equipment.
