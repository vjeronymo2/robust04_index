Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By BUSINESS-TASS correspondent Oleg Deyneko] [Text] Moscow, 24 Jan -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin did not accept the resignation of Finance Minster Boris Fedorov. This was announced at a news conference today by Russian Economic Minister Aleksandr Shokhin. Journalists were told that the right to make a final decision is left to Boris Fedorov.
