BFN [Text] Riga, 16 May, LETA -- On Sunday [15 May] Prime Minister Valdis Birkavs travelled to Finland for an official visit. The governmental information agency reports that the visit includes talks with Finnish Premier Esko Aho on economic cooperation of both states, cooperation between the Latvian and Finnish police, custom and frontier security services. The discussion also involves regional issues. The head of the Latvian Government will also meet with the president of Finland. On Wednesday Birkavs will return to Riga.
