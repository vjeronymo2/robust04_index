BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] There was movement today in high-ranking positions of the federal government. Approximately 45 minutes ago, I received the following bulletin from the Presidency: Today, the president of the republic appointed Humberto Benitez Trevino as attorney general of the republic, replacing Diego Valades Rios, who was appointed Supreme Court justice. This appointment will be submitted to the Senate for approval. Benitez Trevino served as attorney general of the Federal District until today. The president of the republic designated Ricardo Canavati Tafich as deputy secretary of urban development and infrastructure, replacing Architect Jorge Lepe Garcia, who was designated deputy secretary of housing and real estate of Secretariat of Social Development. Lepe Garcia will in turn replace Alfredo Phillips Olmedo, who was nominated by the president to preside over the General Assembly of the Institute of the National Housing Fund for Workers, Infonavit. This is the end of the bulletin. Following these changes, it is still not known who will be the attorney general of the Federal District.
