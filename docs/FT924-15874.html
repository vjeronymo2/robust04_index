921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / Ethiopia devalues Ethiopia devalued its birr currency by 58.6 per cent yesterday in a package of reforms to shake off the legacy of 17 years of Marxist rule, Reuter reports from Addis Ababa. The value of the birr has been 2.07 to the dollar for more than 25 years. Its new rate is 5.00 to the dollar.
