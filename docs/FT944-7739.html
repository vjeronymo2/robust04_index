941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / UK Company News: N American Gas North American Gas Investment Trust had net asset value per share of 94.4p at October 31, compared with 92.9p at the July 31 year end and 100.4p 15 months earlier. The trust, which invests in oil and gas production in Canada and the US, reduced its net deficit from Pounds 3.01m to Pounds 240,000 in the year to the end of July. Losses per share were 0.68p (8.54p). The disposal last week of its holding in Atlantis Resources resulted in proceeds of CDollars 23.4m (Pounds 10.4m), realising 32 per cent of the company's net assets. In the three months to October 31 the net deficit was Pounds 85,000, against revenue of Pounds 43,000 for losses per share of 0.24p (earnings 0.12p).
