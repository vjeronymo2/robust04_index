Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moldovan President Mircea Snegur has not issued and does not intend to issue a decree on suspending barter trade between Romania and the Republic of Moldova, a press statement received today from the Press Office of the president of Moldova emphasizes. Regrets are expressed that, without prior verification, this piece of news published by TARA newspaper on 5 March was repeated by the media in Bucharest.
