BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Dmitriy Zelenin] [Text] Beirut June 6 TASS -- Russian Chief of Staff, Army General Mikhail Kolesnikov, on Sunday held talks in Damascus with his Syrian counterpart, General Hikmat al-Shihabi. According to officials, the two chiefs of staff discussed "cooperation between the Syrian and Russian armies in the interests of the two friendly countries". The sources said that high-ranking Syrian and Russian officers were present, but no other details of the talks were disclosed. Mikhail Kolesnikov arrived in the Syrian capital on Sunday and is due to meet Syrian Defence Minister, Mustafa Talas on Monday. The visit was arranged in pursuance of the accords reached during the negotiations in Damascus in April with the Russian government delegation led by First Deputy Chairman of the Russian Government Oleg Soskovets.
