940117 FT 17 JAN 94 / Trusts attacked over equality National Health Service trusts have made 'uneven progress' on achieving equal opportunities for employees, according to Industrial Relations services, the independent body. In a report it says more than a third of respondents had not yet established an equal opportunity action plan. However. as many as 80 per cent of the trusts said they had carried out an audit to establish the profile of their staff. 'Many trusts are not giving the same priority to addressing race and disability issues as they are to gender issues,' it adds.
