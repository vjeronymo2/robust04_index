BFN [Text] Riga, 5 May (LETA) -- On Wednesday [4 May], Latvian President Guntis Ulmanis received the American ambassador to Latvia, Ints Silins, who handed over the letters from U.S. President Bill Clinton to Mr. Ulmanis. According to the President's Chancellery, one of the letters contains a positive evaluation of the actions of the Latvian president in normalization of the Latvian-Russian relations. According to Bill Clinton, these actions will promote security of Europe.
