Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Taking into account the proposal of chairman of the Tajik Council of Ministers submitted to execute the decree of the Supreme Council of the Republic of Tajikistan on the structure of the Council of Ministers, the presidium of the Tajik Supreme Council decreed, as an exception, the following structure of the Tajik Council of Ministers. Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan. First deputy chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan. Four deputy chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan. Administrator of Affairs of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan. Twenty two ministries, in particular: The Ministry of Economy and Forecasting of the Republic of Tajikistan. The Ministry of Finance of the Republic of Tajikistan. The Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations [words indistinct] of the Republic of Tajikistan. The Ministry of Agriculture of the Republic of Tajikistan. The Ministry of Industry of the Republic of Tajikistan. The Ministry of Construction of the Republic of Tajikistan and others. The State Committees [of the Republic of Tajikistan]. The State Committee of the Republic of Tajikistan on TV and Radio. The State Committee of the Republic of Tajikistan on Construction and Architecture. The State Committee of the Republic of Tajikistan on State Property. The above mentioned decree will be submitted for approval at the regular session of the Tajik Supreme Council. The full text of the ministries will be published in the press.
