Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Today the Council of Ministers held its regular meeting under the chairmanship of Lieutenant General 'Umar Hasan Ahmad al-Bashir, president of the Republic. At the beginning of the meeting the council was briefed by Major General al-Zubayr Muhammad Salih, the vice president of the Republic, on the outcome of the meeting of the joint Sudanese-Ethiopian ministerial committee held in Khartoum recently. His excellency said that the free political management of the two leaderships in the two countries had pushed the bilateral relations to a wider horizon so as to serve the issues of development and cooperation between the two sides. Maj. Gen. al-Zubayr Muhammad Salih added that a full spirit of understanding prevailed in the meetings which dealt with political, security, and economic issues. He stressed that Sudanese-Ethiopian relations had acquired strength and force due to the mutual confidence between the two countries as a result of the depth of relations linking the two fraternal countries.
