November 27, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

CRACKDOWN: Get into a car-pool lane without at least one passenger aboard and you are more likely to get caught nowadays. . . . The CHP in Orange County is assigning officers overtime to beef up enforcement, but that still falls far short of enforcement programs in Washington and Virginia, where motorists who see a car-pool violator can call a toll-free number to report the license number. Dana Parsons and Steve Emmons
