January 28, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

'ANATOMY OF BULLET WOUND'

Your article is the first factual and objective piece of journalism that had anything to do with firearms that I've ever seen in a major newspaper.

Paul Dean's writing style had little of the traditional slanted, emotional content designed to provoke a predetermined response, telling the reader what to think.

The use of deadly force is a course of last resort to protect life and property, not a method to settle a drunken dispute (or a sober dispute for that matter). You are to be applauded for bringing a little reality to this terribly misunderstood issue.

CRAIG O. SMITH, Santa Barbara
