941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Land Rover of the UK - Ventures and Contracts Land Rover of the UK has won a series of contracts totalling Dollars 16m to supply vehicles to Italy. The company is selling 840 Defender four-wheel-drive vehicles to Italy's Carabinieri police and another 290 Defenders to the Italian forestry commission, fire brigade and financial police.
