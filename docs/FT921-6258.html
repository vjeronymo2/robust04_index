920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / International Company News: Bahrain bank back in the black BAHRAIN Middle East Bank (BMB), a Bahrain offshore bank severely hit by the Gulf war, has returned to profit with net earnings of Dollars 1.5m for 1991, after losing Dollars 38.8m in 1990. The bank, one of Bahrain's smaller offshore units which has sought increasingly to diversify into US and European markets, set aside a further Dollars 1.7m of provisions against its sovereign lending in 1991.
