Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, Feb 7 (CTK) -- Defense Minister Antonin Baudys said Saturday's [5 February] shelling of innocent civilians at Sarajevo market was the height of cynicism and monstrosity, ministerial spokesman Zdenek Borkovec told CTK today. Borkovec conveyed Baudys's view on military intervention, lifting the embargo on arms exports to Bosnia and the possible breaking of Sarajevo blockade: These are seemingly simple questions but "in fact, these are the most complicated situations, one can meet in the world." According to Baudys, the fighting parties should first of all bear in mind that the solution depends on their good will. External intervention is the last and most extreme solution, Borkovec quoted him as saying.
