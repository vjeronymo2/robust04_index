941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada TORONTO prices followed in the wake of gains in New York ground with help also from the stronger Canadian dollar and higher bond prices after Monday's public holiday. The TSE 300 composite index was up 24.59 points, or 0.57 per cent, to 4,315.39, with 400 winners to 361 losers. Volume of 53.34m shares valued at CDollars 743.8m compared with Friday's 49.49m valued at CDollars 696.9m. Overall, 12 of the 14 sub-indexes were up led by industrial products. Precious metals was off 0.56 per cent.
