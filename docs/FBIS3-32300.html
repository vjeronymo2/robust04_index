Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] At 1800 this afternoon President Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada installed recently promoted General Luis Rocha Martinez as the new commander of the Bolivian police. Before installing the new police commander, the president praised the work done by the deposed General Einar Lopez: [Begin Sanchez recording] We must outline the important contribution made by Gen. Lopez, who is leaving this post, not only because of his absolutely honest performance, but also because of his dedication to the institution, his service to the community, and his important contribution to the beginning of a process, a plan, a project to reorganize the institution. [end recording] The president said the country will need the services of Gen. Lopez when analyzing the measures necessary to bring the police closer to the people. Gen. Einar Lopez was not present during the ceremony at government palace. The president also said that Gen. Rocha Martinez, the new police commander, was a man of unblemished honesty. [passage omitted]
