BFN [Report by Ahmad 'Izz-al-Din at the Presidential Palace--recorded] [Excerpt] Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri has denounced Israel's piracy, which contradicts all norms and proves that Israel is not serious about peace. Prime Minister al-Hariri denied that there is any hesitation about adopting a stance on the Israeli piracy, noting that Lebanon is studying the possibility of submitting a complaint against this crime. President Ilyas al-Hirawi and Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri held a meeting this morning during which they discussed the Israeli piracy operation and the measures the government will adopt.
