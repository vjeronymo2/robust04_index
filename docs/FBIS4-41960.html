BFN [Text] On Tuesday miners of five mines of Ordzhonikidzeugol corporation in Yenakiyevo, Donetsk coal basin, went on strike demanding payment of delayed wages, disability benefits and holiday bonuses. They also demand the dismissal of the general director of the corporation Aleksandr Poshtuk. The labor action is led by the Independent Miners' Union, one of the biggest in Ukraine. According to the local strike committee, miners have not been paid since April at many facilities. Donetskugol corporation has not been paying its employees since May. On Tuesday the Ukrainian parliament was told that the reason for the labor action at Pervomaiskugol mining corporation in the Lugansk Region was the same. There, miners refused to return to the surface after the shift. According to Interfax-Ukraine, the miners' wages are between three and five million karbovantsy ($70-$120) a month.
