Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] Despite difficulties and harsh competition, Hanoi's state-run trade sector took positive steps in 1993 to expand its services. As a result, it posted a turnover of more than 457 billion dong. The food catering service alone generated nearly 83 billion dong and contributed more than 10 billion dong to the state budget. The total volume of this sector's trading activities accounts for more than 50 percent of all trading activities in the market. Of this figure, retail services account for 23 percent and wholesale services account for the remainder. This sector's corporations, enterprises, and stores have invested more than 50 billion dong to carry out capital construction projects, build mini-hotels, and modernize equipment in order to improve their performance.
