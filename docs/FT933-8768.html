930813 FT 13 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Management buys Lowe Alpine Lowe Alpine, the outdoor pursuits equipment group, has been bought by its management in a deal worth Pounds 14.4m from Famco Holdings. The Cumbria-based company reported sales of Pounds 22.3m in the year to March 31 1993. The funding includes Pounds 5.3m equity from Phildrew Ventures and bank loans of Pounds 8.6m. Famco is a private company owned by the Clarke family and has a controlling stake in Silentnight.
