December 15, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

ETHICS HEARINGS AND CRANSTON

During the 1980s I can remember reading about the idolization of Donald Trump, Michael Milken, Charles Keating, Ronald Reagan, Neil Bush and countless other individuals who robbed and spent our country into a trillion-dollar debt.

Now that we must pay back this huge debt, all of them are pulling out empty pockets and pointing their finger at someone else.

I assert that the 1980s can be characterized as the "me" generation and the 1990s can be characterized as the "not me" generation.

JOHN PECORA, Lake Elsinore
