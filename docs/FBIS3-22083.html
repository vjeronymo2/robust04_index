Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Drug Seizure at Nadlac Customs"] [Text] At the Nadlac customs checkpoint on the Romanian-Hungarian border, Hungarian customs officers seized 10 kilograms 200 grams of heroin. The drugs were hidden inside the doors of a car with Rome license plates, occupied by two Macedonians. According to them, they received the heroin in Bulgaria and were expected to transport it to Italy.
