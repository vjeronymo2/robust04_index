November 8, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

LEBANON;

ISRAEL HITS HEZBOLLAH BASES

Israeli helicopter gunships attacked bases of the Iranian-backed Hezbollah (Party of God), damaging equipment and wounding three people, Muslim security sources said. Firing rockets and machine guns, the aircraft scored direct hits on Hezbollah mountain hide-outs about 9 miles east of the port city of Sidon. In Tel Aviv, an army spokesman said Israeli aircraft attacked "terrorist targets."
