Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent John Polishchuk] [Text] St. Petersburg February 18 TASS -- About fifty St. Petersburg residents gathered on Thursday night [17 February] outside the U.S. Consulate General here to express their support of Bosnian Serbs. After demonstrating for half an hour with a megaphone, placards and icons near the consulate, the protesters made an attempt to present self-made transparancies to consulate officials, but were not admitted into the building. After that, the gathering peacefully dispersed.
