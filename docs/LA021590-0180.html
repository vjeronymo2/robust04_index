February 15, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

BUSINESS SHOWCASE TO BE HELD FEB. 28

The Business Development Committee of the Orange Chamber of Commerce will sponsor a Business Showcase from 5 to 8 p.m. Feb. 28 at the Doubletree Hotel in Orange.

The event, which will be held in conjunction with the February Business After Hours mixer, is designed to let people acquaint themselves with products and services offered in Orange County.

Information on becoming a chamber member will be available at the event, which will be open to the public. Cost is $5. For more information, call the Chamber office at (714) 538-3581.
