BFN [Text] Liamine Zeroual, head of state and current president of the Arab Maghreb Union, emphasized his determination to build a harmonious and prosperous Arab Maghreb in accordance with the aspirations of the peoples of the region. In a message he addressed today on the occasion of the sixth anniversary of the Zeralda summit, Zeroual said that in the world of today, which is characterized by fast transformations and in which the weight of political and economic groupings is increasing, the Arab Maghreb should face the challenge represented by its union in order to occupy its rightful place in the new order of international relations.
