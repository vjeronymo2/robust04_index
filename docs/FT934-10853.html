931103 FT 03 NOV 93 / International Company News: Proton sales drive PERUSAHAAN Otomobil Nasional Bhd (Proton), Malaysia's national car company, plans to start exporting left-hand drive cars to continental Europe by the middle of 1994, the company said, Reuter reports from Kuala Lumpur. Proton, in which Japan's Mitsubishi Corp and Mitsubishi Motors have a total 17 per cent stake, has formed a joint venture to sell its cars in Europe.
