Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Army General Rasim Delic, chief of general staff of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army, and Colonel General Ante Roso, chief of general staff of the Croat Defense Council [HVO], signed an agreement in Sarajevo today on the organization of the joint command of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army and the HVO. The agreement defines the organization and tasks of the joint commands in the interim period, the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army press center reports. It was agreed that joint commands in sectors and military districts should become operational as soon as possible. According to the agreement the organization of the federal army, i.e., the army of the federation, will follow immediately after the proclamation of the federal constitution.
