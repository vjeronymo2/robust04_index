BFN [Text] Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa, who is currently in Brussels, met with Willem van Eekelen, secretary general of the Western European Union [WEU] this afternoon. They discussed cooperation between Egypt and the WEU, especially with regard to the economy and development. They also discussed the role of the WEU in supporting the peace process, especially in providing necessary aid to the Palestinian authority in Gaza and Jericho. The foreign minister will continue his meetings this afternoon. He will meet with the deputy chairman of the European committee. He will also meet with the foreign ministers of France, Germany, Denmark, and Greece to discuss the situation in the Middle East and the WEU's role in supporting the peace process. The meetings of the Egyptian-European Cooperation Council will begin this evening in Brussels under the chairmanship of Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa to discuss bolstering cooperation between the two sides as well as the new agreement on increasing Egyptian exports to the WEU.
