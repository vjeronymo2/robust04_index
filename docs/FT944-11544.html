941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / UK Company News: En-tout-cas expands En-tout-cas has agreed a deal with Balsam of Germany which will, the company says, create a major player in the world market for synthetic sports surfaces and allied sports equipment. The new company, ETC (Holdings), will have an initial annual turnover exceeding Pounds 50m and gross assets of more than Pounds 22m.
