Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] After the call of Algerian scholars and preachers for a meeting this Thursday for a conference entitled Reconciliation is Good, a large crowd complied with the call and came to Harcha Hall in Algiers. In this meeting, the Islamic Republican Rally [Tajammu' Islami Jumhuri] was set up. [passage omitted]. [Begin Cheikh Mahfouz Nahnah, chairman of the Islamic Society Movement -- HAMAS -- recording] It is the Islamic Republican Rally and its acronym is TAJ [crown]. We want to have under this TAJ scholars, preachers, the FIS [Islamic Salvation Front], regardless of whether it was dissolved or not, the al-Nahdah, the Umma [Party], and all the patriotic people who have integrity and all the men of Algeria who want to form a bulwark against the factors of fragmentation. [end recording]
