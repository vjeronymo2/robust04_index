Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, has asserted that the accord document reached by the Political Forces Dialogue Committee is a document of national consensus. It also represents a new social contract to rectify the course of Yemeni unity and formulate new bases to build a modern Yemeni state in which security, justice, stability, and development prevail. In a speech he delivered this morning to the commanders of military units, security agencies, and [words indistinct] in the commercial and economic capital, Aden, he said the accord document reflects the Yemeni people's strength and their possession of resources and capabilities that render serious, responsible dialogue a means of finding solutions to problems, away from violence and threats to use force. The accord document also confirms that the Yemeni people have chosen the path of democracy, peaceful, democratic action, and heeding the voice of reason to address the challenges and overcome the obstacles facing the process of unity and democracy. The vice chairman of the Presidential Council stressed the importance of implementing the items contained in the accord document. He pointed out that the historic value of this document lies in the ability of the masses to translate it into concrete reality in all walks of life. He added that this major national mission requires the efforts of all sincere national forces that seek to build a state with institutions where law and order prevail and democracy and development are achieved. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, urged the commanders of military and security units to be the first to implement the accord document and to set a good example of how to run units, enhance the principle of discipline, and implement their assigned programs and plans tirelessly. He also urged them to improve the living conditions and boost the morale of the personnel of their institutions and [words indistinct] to enable them to perform their duties by defending the homeland and safeguarding its achievements. He further urged them to demonstrate a serious, sincere position toward the issues of the people and the homeland. The meeting was attended by Defense Minister brother Brigadier General Haytham Qasim Tahir and several military commanders.
