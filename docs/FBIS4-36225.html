BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn chaired part of the cabinet's session at the Prime Ministry at noon today in the presence of His Highness Crown Prince Hasan, Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al- Majali, and Royal Hashemite Court Chief, His Excellency al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir. In a news conference following the session, His Majesty King Husayn said a review was made of both the regional situation and the expected developments in the coming phase. His majesty added that during the session there was emphasis on the need for this country to remain cohesive as one family from various origins and descents. He said we should enjoy awareness and affiliation, and that we should also study all that took place and has thus far taken place, so we will draw the conclusions required for effective action that guarantees the country's future in terms of its steadfastness, in order to serve the kinsfolk and brothers in Palestine with regard to their rights on their national soil.
