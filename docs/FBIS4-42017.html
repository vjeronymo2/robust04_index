BFN [Text] The leader of a Cuban governmental delegation, Vice President of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Cuba Lionel Soto, referred to his delegation's visit to Ukraine as very important. At a news conference in Kiev, Lionel Soto pointed out that the main objective of the visit was to restore all the economic relations which Ukraine and Cuba maintained during the Soviet Union's existence. After the disintegration of the USSR, he pointed out, these relations have been almost abolished. The head of the Cuban governmental delegation also declared that there are no political differences between our two states and that our peoples are friendly peoples. At present, Lionel Soto said, we must get down to specific work on developing our relations.
