June 24, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

MOCA NO FUN?

Why can't the good sense of some of your book reviewers and entertainment critics ever show up on your editorial pages?

The latest piece to raise that question is Terry Atkinson's June 19 review of KCET-TV's "Take Five" programs. I realize that this kind of "the-emperor-is-bare-rumped" commentary will make it difficult for some of The Times' senior folk to move in their accustomed high social circles.

Nonetheless, I hope that Terry Atkinson will not be a casualty of his on-point reportage. The description of the Museum of Contemporary Art as "an aggravating, depressing home for no-point, no-fun modern art" is long overdue.

CRUGER L. BRIGHT

La Habra Heights
