930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / Yemeni ruling party in poll lead President Ali Abdullah Saleh's ruling party enjoyed a healthy lead yesterday in united Yemen's first multi-party elections despite tough competition from an opposition Islamic group, Reuter reports from Sanaa. Results from 284 of the 301 constituencies gave Saleh's General People's Congress 120 seats - short of an overall majority but more than double its nearest challenger.
