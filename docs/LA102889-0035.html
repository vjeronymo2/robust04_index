October 28, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BAY AREA QUAKE AFTERMATH AND FREEWAY SAFETY

Let us exalt the scores of civil authorities, rescue workers and others who have helped in the aftermath of the devastating earthquake. Responding to their highly diversified and demanding calls with alacrity, they have helped ease the pain of this terrible tragedy. Many civilians, of their own volition, have given their time, money and blood to assist in the recovery efforts. The way the people of this state and country have pulled together to help the victims of this terrible natural disaster shows our pre-eminence over others.

HELEN KNIPE

Torrance
