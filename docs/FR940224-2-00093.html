Health Resources and Services Administration Advisory Council; Meeting In accordance with section 10(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92&hyph;463), announcement is made of the following National Advisory body scheduled to meet during the month of April 1994. Name: Departments of Family Medicine Review Committee. Date and Time: April 4&hyph;6, 1994, 8:30 a.m. Place: Holiday Inn Crowne Plaza, Regency Room, 1750 Rockville Pike, Rockville, Maryland 20852. Open on April 4, 8:30 a.m.&hyph;9:30 a.m. Closed for Remainder of Meeting. Purpose: The Departments of Family Medicine Review Committee shall review applications that assist in meeting the costs of establishing, maintaining, or improving academic administrative units (which may be departments, divisions, or other units) to provide clinical instruction in family medicine. Agenda: The open portion of the meeting will cover welcome and opening remarks, financial management and legislative implementation updates, and overview of the review process. The meeting will be closed to the public on April 4, at 11 a.m. for the remainder of the meeting for the review of grant applications. The closing is in accordance with the provisions set forth in section 552b(c)(6), title 5 U.S.C., and the Determination by the Associate Administrator for Policy Coordination, Health Resources and Services Administration, pursuant to Public Law 92&hyph;463. Anyone wishing to obtain a roster of members, minutes of meeting, or other relevant information regarding the subject Committee should contact Mrs. Sherry Whipple, Executive Secretary, Departments of Family Medicine Review Committee, room 4C&hyph;18, Parklawn Building, 5600 Fishers Lane, Rockville, Maryland 20857, Telephone (301) 443&hyph;6874. Agenda Items are subject to change as priorities dictate. Dated: February 17, 1994. Jackie E. Baum, Advisory Committee Management Officer, HRSA.
