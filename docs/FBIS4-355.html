BFN [Text] Paris, April 8 (AFP) -- At least 22 priests and nuns have been killed in Rwanda since clashes began there Thursday [7 April], church officials said Friday in Kigali, reached by telephone from Paris. Six priests and nine nuns were killed in Kigali itself and six priests and a nun in the town of Nyundo, in the northwest province of Gisenye, the official at the papal nuncio's office said.
