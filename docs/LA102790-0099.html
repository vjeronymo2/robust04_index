October 27, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP;

PACIFIC LEAGUE

Arcadia 17, Hoover 14 -- Neil Voskeritchian kicked a 32-yard field goal with 1:36 to play to lift Arcadia (3-5, 1-2) past visiting Hoover (2-6, 0-3). Arcadia tailback Joe Serrano rushed for 148 yards in 32 carries and scored two touchdowns. SCOTT KATCHER
