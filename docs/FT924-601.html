921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / UK Company News: HTV gets go-ahead for land sale Having been granted planning permission, HTV Group announced it is now able to complete the sale of 9.3 acres of undeveloped land at Culverhouse Cross, Cardiff. In response to an appeal by the developer, Atlantic Property Developments, the planning inspector gave his formal consent to the planning application made on December 17. Subject to the statutory period to challenge the appeal decision, the sale becomes unconditional on March 17 1993. On the agreement becoming unconditional HTV will receive Pounds 2.5m - the group has already received Pounds 1.5m cash - representing a surplus of Pounds 1.8m above book value.
