920828 FT 28 AUG 92 / Chemical arms step A treaty banning the use, possession and manufacture of chemical weapons has passed one of its final hurdles on the way to adoption by the United Nations, Reuter reports from Geneva. After 24 years of talks, it has been agreed by a committee of the 39-nation Conference on Disarmament. The draft treaty is contained in a report that will be examined by a plenary session of the UN-sponsored conference next week.
