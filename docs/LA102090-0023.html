October 20, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

EVERYONE IS COMING TO THE RAMS' DEFENSE, OR, RATHER, LACK OF IT

Regarding John Robinson's quote after Sunday's 38-9 loss to the Bears: "You have to keep the ship from sinking before you can think about moving it."

Something tells me the ship is the Titanic and it's already hit the iceberg.

JIM BARTON

Van Nuys
