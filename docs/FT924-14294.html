921010 FT 10 OCT 92 / Finance & The Family: House prices still slipping Homeowners will not be cheered by the news that house prices fell by 3.1 per cent last month, according to the Halifax price index. This is more than twice as large as the previous fall of 1.3 per cent in December 1991. The graph shows a steady 7.4 per cent fall in the index over the year to September. Details: Page 7, Section One
