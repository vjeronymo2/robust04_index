921015 FT 15 OCT 92 / Maternity seen as career handicap MANY WOMEN with prof-essional or managerial jobs feel their career prospects are damaged after taking a break to have a baby, Diane Summers writes. The finding comes in a survey of nearly 800 women published today by Sussex University on behalf of employers including Marks and Spencer and Barclays Bank. Before taking a career break, 84 per cent of women were very satisfied or satisfied with the way they were promoted at work. After returning to work, that dropped to 64 per cent.
