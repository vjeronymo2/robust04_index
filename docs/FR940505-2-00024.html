[Docket No. CP94&hyph;161&hyph;000] Avoca Natural Gas Storage; Site Visit April 29, 1994. On May 4 and 5, 1994, the Office of Pipeline Regulation staff will conduct a site visit with representatives of Avoca Natural Gas Storage for the facilities proposed in the Avoca Gas Storage Field Project. The proposed facilities are near the towns of Avoca and Wallace in Steuben County, New York. Parties to the proceeding may attend. Those planning to attend must provide their own transportation.
