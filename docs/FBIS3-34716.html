Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Responding to reports from Syria and Lebanon alleging that the Israel Defense Forces [IDF]] is reinforcing its troops, a senior military source says that the IDF has not beefed up its forces in the security zone in Lebanon. Speaking to our reporter Yo'av Aviv, the source said that the reports came in the wake of fears of a harsh IDF retaliation following the incident in which four soldiers were killed and five injured. Official military sources also denied an IDF beef up in the security zone.
