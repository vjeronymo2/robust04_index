Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Summary] The Georgian Supreme Court today sentenced the killer of Fred Woodruff, a U.S. diplomat attached to the U.S. embassy in Tbilisi, to 15 years' imprisonment . In an incident that took place on a highway near Tbilisi on 8 August 1993, a private in the Georgian Defense Ministry's 3d battalion, Anzor Sharmaidze, fired at a passing car carrying Woodruff, killing the American on the spot.
