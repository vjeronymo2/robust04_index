BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, today received UK Ambassador Robert Douglas Gordon. They reviewed bilateral relations and the developments of the situation in our country, as well as issues of interest to the two countries. During the meeting, the British ambassador reiterated the United Kingdom's support for Yemeni unity and democracy, and its eagerness to promote its relations with the Republic of Yemen in all fields in the service of the two friendly countries' common interests. The brother chairman of the Presidential Council expressed appreciation for the British stand supporting unity and democracy. He stressed that this stand genuinely reflects British interest in consolidating security and stability in Yemen and the region. He stressed our country's eagerness to promote its relations with Britain and to expand the spheres of cooperation. He indicated that Yemeni-British relations will witness further development and prosperity. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah.
