930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / World Trade News: AEG in rail deal with Czechs Germany's AEG and CKD of Prague have formed a joint venture to make rail systems which could employ up to 2,000 people in the Czech Republic by next year, the Daimler-Benz subsidiary has announced Christopher Parkes reports from Frankfurt. The link with the Czech group, internationally known for its Tatra brand trams, is expected to generate sales this year of DM170m (Pounds 68m) rising to DM300m in 1994. The German group, which will have a 55 per cent stake in the venture, said investments could reach DM100m in the medium term.
