921006 FT 06 OCT 92 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Brazilian equities dropped by 7.2 per cent in midday trading as uncertainty over acting President Itamar Franco's new economic team gripped the market, Reuter reports from Sao Paulo. Telebras, the state telecommunications company which accounts for the bulk of market turnover, dropped sharply, trading just after noon at Cr121, a fall of 11 per cent.
