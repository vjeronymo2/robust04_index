BFN [From the "Provincial News Hookup" program] [Excerpts] A national conference on the comprehensive management work on public security in rural areas opened in Wujiang City on 8 June. Ren Jianxin, CPC Central Committee Secretariat member and Central Committee for Comprehensive Management of Public Security [CCCMPS] chairman, chaired the conference and relayed important instructions from central leading comrades Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, and Qiao Shi. Luo Gan, state councillor and CCCMPS vice chairman, addressed the meeting. [passage omitted on Luo Gan's speech, covered by third referent item] Chen Huanyou, secretary of the Jiangsu Provincial CPC Committee and governor of Jiangsu Province, also spoke at the meeting. He said: The convocation of this national conference in Jiangsu is a great encouragement to us. It will spur us on; provide a very good opportunity for us to learn from fraternal provinces, municipalities, and autonomous regions; and forcefully promote Jiangsu's comprehensive management of public security and other work. Chen Huanyou briefed the conferees on the situation of Jiangsu's economic development and its comprehensive efforts to improve public order in rural areas. Cao Hongming, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee, delivered a speech: "Strive To Achieve an Economic Prosperity and Social Stability and Progress in Rural Areas Under the New Situation of Reform and Opening Up."
