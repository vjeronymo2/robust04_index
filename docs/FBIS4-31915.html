BFN [Text] The negotiations that were interrupted at 0130 between the government team, which includes State Ministers Dan Mircea Popescu and Florin Georgescu, Viorel Hrebenciuc, general secretary of the government, and Octavian Partenie, head of the office in charge of the government's relations with trade unions and employers organization, and counselors, experts, and representatives of the Alfa Cartel and the National Trade Union Block and delegations led by Alfa Cartel Bogdan Hossu, resumed shortly before 1500. In the first 15 minutes, the representatives of trade unions explained that the minutes of the meeting, which were completed last night but not signed, include the government's viewpoint but, as far as the viewpoint of trade unions are concerned, there are several controversial points. Romulus Nita, vice president of the Alfa Cartel, immediately insisted that the talks should focus on Government Ordinance Number 13 to find ways to invalidate it. At this hour, the talks are in progress, our correspondents informed us. Meantime, our correspondent informed us on the phone that Victoria Square continues to be occupied by Alfa Cartel trade union members. Trade union representatives from Focsani and Tulcea have joined the groups of demonstrators. The size of the law enforcement units, as well as their technical equipment, have also increased considerably.
