Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The following statement was released by the esteemed leader's office on observation of the crescent of the month of Shawwal: In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful. May God accept the religious duty of fasting and prayers by all Muslim brothers and sisters. By congratulating the auspicious feast of Id al-Fitr to world Muslims, particularly to the dear nation of Iran, it is hereby announced that, according to reliable reports, the crescent of the month of Shawwal has been seen in Khuzestan and Esfahan Provinces. The esteemed leader, His Eminence Ayatollah Khamene'i, is certain that the month of Shawwal has begun. Tomorrow, 13 March, is herefore the first of Shawwal and the auspicious feast of Id al-Fitr; and the glorious Id al-Fitr prayers will be held all over the country tomorrow morning. [Signed] The esteemed leader's office.
