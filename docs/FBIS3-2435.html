Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 9 Mar (XINHUA) -- The name list of executive chairmen of the Second Session Presidium of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC] The First Meeting of the Second Session Presidium of the Eighth NPC held on 9 March 1994 elected Qiao Shi, Tian Jiyun, Wang Hanbin, Ni Zhifu, Chen Muhua (female), Fei Xiaotong, Sun Qimeng, Lei Jieqiong (female), Qin Jiwei, Li Ximing, Wang Bingqian, Bagbalha Geleg Namgyai, Wang Guangying, Cheng Siyuan, Lu Jiaxi, Buhe, Tomur Dawamat, Li Peiyao, Wu Jieping, and Cao Zhi as executive chairmen of the presidium.
