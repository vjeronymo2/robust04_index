Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Asyut, 27 Jan (MENA)--Two police officers escorting Colonel Jamal Zaki Matta, commander of the Asyut Security Service Vehicles Department, were killed tonight when unknown assailants fired at the colonel's vehicle as he was heading to work in Asyut. Col. Matta was not hurt in the attack. His driver was slightly injured. Police have cordoned off the scene of the attack and are conducting a search for the perpetrators. The Prosecution Office has started investigating the case.
