930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / Observer: Slip-up Last night's 'Dance to the Beat of Labour in Power' permitted a nostalgic Labour party fringe to hang loose in Brighton to the beat of 1970s disco hits. Harking back to the era when the left last called the tunes at Westminster, the revellers unaccountably omitted to supplement old favourites such as 'When will I see you again' with, suitably remixed from the film Grease, 'You're the one-member-one-vote that I want'.
