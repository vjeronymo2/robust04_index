BFN [Text] Seoul, June 11 KYODO -- Japanese Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa arrived in Seoul shortly before noon Saturday [11 June] for talks with his South Korean counterpart Han Sung-chu and President Kim Yong-sam on steps to deal with the North Korean nuclear issue. After Seoul, Kakizawa will visit Beijing for talks with Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen before returning to Tokyo on Sunday night.
