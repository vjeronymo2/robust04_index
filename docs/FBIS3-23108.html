Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A group calling itself the Islamic Battalions [al-kata'ib al-islamiyah] has threatened revenge for the martyrs of Palestine. The group said that hardline Islamists in Egypt will begin to attack the Americans and Israelis. Carrying the report, REUTER said that the group made the threat in a statement seemingly faxed from Beirut. The statement gave the Americans in Egypt 48 hours to leave the country. The statement said: We warn every American in Egypt that he will be a target for the Islamist mujahidin in the country. The group claimed responsibility for a device exploding in front of the Egyptian American Bank in Cairo last night. The Islamic Group in Egypt earlier claimed responsibility for the same explosion as well as for recent attacks against banks and policemen in Egypt.
