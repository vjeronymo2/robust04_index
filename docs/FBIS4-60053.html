BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, chairman of the Presidential Council and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, yesterday evening held telephone conversations with his brother, His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal, king of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan; His Excellency Muhammad Husni Mubarak, president of Egypt; His Excellency Hafiz al-Asad, president of the Syrian Arab Republic; the custodian of the two holy places, King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, king of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia; and His Majesty Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al-Nahayyan, president of the UAE. During the telephone conversations, Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid briefed his brothers, the kings and Arab heads of state on the latest developments affecting the Yemeni arena, as well as developments in the fighting and the war, which was imposed on our people in the southern and eastern governorates by 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and the al-Ahmar family. He also exchanged greetings with them on the occasion of the blessed 'Id al-Adha, saying he hoped they and their fraternal peoples would achieve further progress and prosperity.
