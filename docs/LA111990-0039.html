November 19, 1990, Monday, San Diego County Edition

PEDESTRIAN INJURED BY UTILITIES TRUCK

A pedestrian was injured in Mission Beach when he ran in front of a water utilities truck, San Diego police said.

The pedestrian, whose name was not released, was hit about 8:30 p.m. in 900 block of Mission Bay Drive, police said. The truck driver, who was being followed by his supervisor in another vehicle, was not cited.
