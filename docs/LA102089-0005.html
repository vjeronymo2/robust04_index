October 20, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

KEEPING RTD BUSES ON TIME

I am a bus operator working out of Division 5 in South-Central Los Angeles. This slam at my fellow operators angers me. I love my job and what I do. But I'm sure that the author wants answers not explanations. We stress teamwork on the job and most of the time it works as it was intended.

Sir, this is a large city, a first-rate city with a first-rate transit system. With so many people unable to afford a car, let alone the necessary insurance costs that go with owning that car, we provide a lifeline for seniors, students, shoppers and working people. We're their wheels and we really do the best we can at all times. And it isn't always easy.

GEORGE STAFFORD

Manhattan Beach
