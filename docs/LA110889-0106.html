November 8, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

FUTURISTIC: ATM cards are about to conquer Carl's Jr. (D5), but what's next? . . . Twenty years from now, one bank official says, ATM cards may contain microchips to store such information as your medical history and financial and insurance status, to ease the paper-work problem at the doctor's office. . . . Already, California consumers can use their ATM cards to shop at 2,800 locations, including groceries, shoe stores and service stations. Steve Emmons and Dana Parsons
