920225 FT 25 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Principal Hotels Principal Hotels Group, the shares of which were suspended last Friday while the company's financial position was clarified, went into administrative receivership last night with borrowings of more than Pounds 100m. Mr Edward Klempka, a partner in Coopers & Lybrand Deloitte's Leeds office, said: 'The hotels are viable. We will continue to expand until they can be sold.' A management buy-out, trade sale or even reflotation are possible options. The Harrogate-based group has 19 hotels in Britain, two each Denmark and the Netherlands and one in France.
