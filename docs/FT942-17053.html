940407 FT 07 APR 94 / Tory support stays at 13% in Scotland The Conservatives still only have 13 per cent support in Scotland - the 20-year low they sank to in February - a System Three poll for The Herald newspaper in Glasgow found. Labour is put on 46 per cent, the Scottish National party on 26 per cent and the Liberal Democrats on 14 per cent. The figures exclude 13 per cent 'don't knows'.
