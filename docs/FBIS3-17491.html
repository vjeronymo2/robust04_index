Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow March 27 TASS -- Vladimir -- Elections in the Vladimir region were completed in 17 out of 21 constituencies with the overall turnout of 30 per cent. Kurgan -- Elections were recognised valid in all constituencies of the Kurgan region with the overall turnout of 43.8 per cent. Bryansk -- The elections in the Bryansk region were completed with the overall turnout of 35.6 per cent. The highest turnout was reported in areas affected by the Chernobyl nuclear disaster. Rostov-on-Don -- Over 20 per cent of electors had cast their votes by 20:00 p.m. Moscow time in 39 out of 45 constituencies of the Rostov region. Positive results have been reported from the Samara and Chuvashia regions with the 30.5 and 36 per cent turnout, respectively. In the Ivanovo region, the turnout was low, except for rural regions. The turnout in the regional centre was 19.4 per cent, in some other towns - 21.4 per cent. A higher turnout was reported in rural areas, reaching 38.3 per cent.
