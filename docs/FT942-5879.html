940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Sturge advances to Pounds 493,000 at midway Sturge Holdings, the Lloyd's underwriting agency, reported pre-tax up from Pounds 325,000 to Pounds 493,000 in the six months ended March 31 1994. The directors said an increase of Pounds 1.6m in stockbroking income to Pounds 7.95m was more than offset by a reduction of Pounds 2.9m in insurance agency income to Pounds 4.85m, which resulted from lower capacity under management and reduced levels of run-off management fees. The operating loss of insurance agencies was Pounds 1.84m (Pounds 1.99m) while stockbroking profit was Pounds 1.37m (Pounds 951,000). Net profit emerged at Pounds 243,000 (Pounds 196,000) and earnings per share were 0.5p (0.4p). The interim dividend is held at 1p.
