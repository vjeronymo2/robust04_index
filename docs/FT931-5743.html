930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / UK Company News: SR Gent ahead 47% at Pounds 986,000 SR Gent, a fashion garment supplier to Marks and Spencer, achieved a 47 per cent profits improvement to Pounds 986,000 pre-tax for the half year ended December 31. A fall in turnover to Pounds 59.8m (Pounds 67.8m) reflected a decision to focus more closely on products with higher margin contribution. However, sales were expected to recover in the second half. Earnings rose to 1.5p (1p) and the interim dividend is held at 0.75p.
