Language: French Article Type:BFN [Article signed MJN: "Bomb Alert at Central Station; a Certain Iranian Socialist Center"; as released by Brussels BELGA database] [Excerpt] Brussels, 24 Mar (BELGA) -- The police received a telephone call at 1315 GMT today advising that a bomb was set to explode at the Brussels Central Train Station at 1440 GMT. The threat was issued by a certain "Iranian Socialist Center." The Brussels police deputy inspector announced that such a center does not exist. [passage omitted]
