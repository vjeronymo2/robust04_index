BFN [Text of "Statement of the President of the Russian Federation": "Boris Yeltsin: `I Confirm the Proposal To Hold a Summit Meeting Between Russia, the United States, and the European Union and with the Participation of the United Nations'"] [Text] "The conflict in Bosnia-Herzegovina is on the brink of a dangerous escalation despite Russia's energetic diplomatic efforts undertaken jointly with the United Nations and other members of the international community. The Bosnian Serb leadership must honor the commitments that it made to Russia, end the attacks and withdraw from the city of Gorazde, which has been declared a "safe area" by the United Nations, ensure the conditions for the entry of UN forces there, and end the blockade of UN personnel in Bosnia-Herzegovina. "The world community is called upon to adopt resolute measures to achieve a political solution to the Bosnian crisis. "In this connection I confirm the proposal to hold a summit meeting between Russia, the United States, and the European Union with the participation of the United Nations. "Of course, such a meeting must be thoroughly prepared. The corresponding instructions have been given to the Russian Federation Foreign Ministry." [Dated] The Kremlin, Moscow, 19 April 1994.
