930312 FT 12 MAR 93 / UK Company News: David Lloyd placing and offer 'well received' The placing and public offering of shares in David Lloyd Leisure, the tennis and fitness chain run by the former Davis Cup player, were 'very well received' by investors, Mr David Cohen of Robert Fleming Securities said yesterday. Dealings in the 39.36m shares, priced at 150p to raise Pounds 59m, are due to begin on March 19.
