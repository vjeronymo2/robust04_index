March 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $30 when Final Forecast, the front horse in his win parlay, finished out of the money in the fourth race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,148.40.

Bob's bet today is a $10 triple from Pewter and Benedict's Printz in the sixth to Bright And Right in the seventh to Prized and Frankly Perfect in the eighth.

Saturday's Bankroll $1,178.40

Saturday's Loss $30
