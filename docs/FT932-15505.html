930413 FT 13 APR 93 / Construction Contracts: Major social housing developments Two social housing contracts with a combined value of Pounds 17m, to design and build a total of 364 homes and undertake a major hospital refurbishment programme, have been awarded to LAING HOMES SPECIAL PROJECTS. The first contract awarded by the London and Quadrant Housing Association, is for a Pounds 7m scheme to design and build 118 homes for rent, and to refurbish hospital buildings at Chingford Hospital in the London Borough of Waltham Forest. Work is due to start in April with completion scheduled for 1995. A second contract from the London and Quadrant Housing Association and Tower Housing Association, is for the design and build of 247 dwellings on the Buttsbury Estate in the London Borough of Redbridge. The Pounds 10m development programme will see the medium rise, high density council homes replaced by a range of two/three-storey houses and flats.
