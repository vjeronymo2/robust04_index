BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 12 (KCNA) -- Vice-Premier Chang-chol met and had a friendly conversation at the Mansudae Assembly Hall Monday with Abdul Rahman Abdul Farah, vice-minister of culture of Egypt who was participating in the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival. Present there were Vice-Minister of Culture and Art Choe Yong-ku and Egyptian Ambassador to Korea Ali Hegazi.
