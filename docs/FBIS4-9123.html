BFN [Text] Bosnian Serbs launched three artillery attacks on Zupanja and Bosnjaci at around 1445 today. The early warning center in Zupanja sounded a general alert at 1445 and again at 1600, when a second attack began. A third attack began at 1650. Most of the shells landed in the Zupanja area. According to the reports we have received so far, dozens of mortar shells were fired on Zupanja and Bosnjaci, causing considerable material damage. Nobody was injured in the attacks.
