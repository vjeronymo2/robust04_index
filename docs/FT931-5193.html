930306 FT 06 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: Italian cabinet moves to limit corruption fall-out The cabinet of Italian prime minister Giuliano Amato last night approved measures to limit the damage caused by a political corruption and bribery scandal which is tearing the country apart. Ignoring opposition protests that it was granting an amnesty, the cabinet backed a package changing the rules on party funding and offering leniency to corrupt politicians who confess, return bribes and leave office.
