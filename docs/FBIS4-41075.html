BFN [Text] Baku, June 20 (TURAN)--The head of Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic [NAR] Supreme Mejlis Namik Gasanov was in Iran from June 15 until June 18 with an official visit, told TURAN the chief of parliament press-service Ali Gasanov. During the visit Gasanov met with Ali Abdol-Alizade, ostandar (head) of province Eastern Azerbaijan. An agreement on cargo transportation from Baku to Nakhichevan through Iran and from Turkey to Azerbaijan through Iran was reached. Gasanov and Abdol-Alizade signed an executive agreement between province Eastern Azerbaijan (Iran) and NAR. According to the agreement goods totalling 8.8 million US dollars, 20,000 tons of grain, 15,000 tons of kerosene, 20,000 tons of mazut, 10,000 tons of diesel fuel, 5,000 tons of coal, 3,000 tons of sugar and 1200 tons of liquefied gas will be delivered to Nakhichevan from East Azerbaijan. Gurbanali Saadat, ostandar of Western Azerbaijan province met with Namik Gasanov in Urmie on June 17. The sides discussed the issues of board trade, of cultural and humanitarian cooperation. An agreement on trade and economic cooperation was signed on June 18. According to agreement, fuel and products totalling 3.5 million dollars will be delivered to Nakhichevan from West Azerbaijan within 6 months. During the visit Namik Gasanov met also with business circles of Iran, visited tractor plant and enterprise dealing with fruit juice production.
