930422 FT 22 APR 93 / UK Company News: Fleming Income net asset value at 77.53p At the end of its first year of trading, Fleming Income & Capital Investment Trust had a net asset value of 77.53p per share. Net income for the period to March 31 1993 amounted to Pounds 5.33m for earnings per share of 5.77p. Forecast earnings were 4.725p. A fourth quarter interim dividend of 1.725p, already announced, makes a total of 4.725p, as forecast.
