940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / UK Company News: ECIC raises Pounds 17.2m in placing Energy Capital Investment Company, an investment vehicle specialising in project finance for the oil and gas industry in the US, yesterday announced it had raised Pounds 17.2m in an institutional placing. The company said institutional investors had subscribed for five shares and one warrant at a unit price of Pounds 5. A total of 18m shares and 3.6m warrants had been placed, about half of which was taken by UK institutions. The shares are expected to begin trading on February 10.
