CSO ["Decree of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tatarstan: On Priority Measures To Stabilize the Economy of the Republic of Tatarstan"] [Text] The Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tatarstan resolves: 1. To assume as a basis the "Priority Measures To Stabilize the Economy of the Republic of Tatarstan" drawn up and presented by the Government of the Republic of Tatarstan. 2. To instruct the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Tatarstan to study the proposals and observations submitted by the people's deputies and also the findings of the standing commissions of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tatarstan, to adopt the necessary measures, and to report at the next session of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tatarstan. 3. That the Government of the Republic of Tatarstan shall, in accordance with the Constitution of the Republic of Tatarstan, the treaty of the Russian Federation and the Republic of Tatarstan "Delineation of Terms of Reference and the Mutual Delegation of Authority Between Organs of State Power of the Russian Federation and Organs of State Power of the Republic of Tatarstan," and the "Priority Measures To Stabilize the Economy of the Republic of Tatarstan," that have been assumed as a basis, draw up a State Program of Economic and Social Progress of the Republic of Tatarstan and present it to the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tatarstan in October 1994. 4. To exclude in connection with the adoption of this decree clause 2 of the decree of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tatarstan of 21 April 1994 "Procedure of the Implementation of the Law of the Republic of Tatarstan 'Budget System of the Republic of Tatarstan for 1994.'" To consider clauses 3, 4, and 5 of the said decree clauses 2, 3, and 4 respectively. [Signed] F. Mukhametshin, chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tatarstan Kazan, 8 June 1994
