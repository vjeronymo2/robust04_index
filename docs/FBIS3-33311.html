Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Commander in Chief Fidel Castro has met with Manuel Hermoso Rojas, president of the government of the autonomous region of the Canary Islands, and his delegation to discuss issues of interest to both regions. Carlos Lage, Osmany Cienfuegos, Jose Ramon Fernandez, and other officials were also in attendance. Since 18 February, Hermoso Rojas has been following a busy agenda. Cuba has a large community of Canarians and their descendants.
