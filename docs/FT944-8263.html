941121 FT 21 NOV 94 / Company News (This Week): Glassmaker likely to cut a fine figure - Other companies Pilkington: The strong profit recovery at the glassmaker has continued in the six months to September, with analysts forecasting a figure tomorrow of Pounds 45m-Pounds 55m before tax and exceptional items, up from Pounds 30.5m in the first half of last year. The improvement stems largely from increased capacity utilisation and some price rises.
