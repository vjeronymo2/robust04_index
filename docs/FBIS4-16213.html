BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Amangeldy Akhmetalimov, Gennadiy Kulagin] [Text] Alma-Ata [Almaty], 13 Apr -- The Kazakhstan president's press service has indirectly confirmed the fact that Nursultan Nazarbayev is unlikely to attend the Moscow summit of CIS states. Having remarked that Kazakhstan's official delegation is leaving for Moscow tomorrow, the press service specified: President Nazarbayev is taking part in preparing documents for the summit. But in the last few days he cancelled a number of earlier planned events in his difficult working schedule, on doctors' advice.
