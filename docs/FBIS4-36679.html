BFN [Text] Brother Salim Salih Muhammad, Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] Presidential Council member, talked by phone from his place of residence in London with brother Lakhdar Brahimi, envoy of Butrus Butrus-Ghali, UN secretary general, who is currently in Jeddah continuing his efforts to stop the fighting, prevent Yemeni bloodshed, and implement the Security Council's resolution. During the contact, brother Salim Salih Muhammad explained the recent developments, Sanaa's refusal to abide by the cease-fire, and the imposition of a state of siege on Aden by threatening to cut the water supplies, hit the economic and civilian utilities and shell the popular quarters without discrimination, a matter which led to the killing of hundreds of women, children, and old men. He affirmed to him the importance of speeding up the implementation of the Security Council's resolution and sending international observers to supervise the ceasefire and start the talks, or take deterrent measures against the side which rejected these international resolutions. Lakhdar Brahimi affirmed the importance of stopping the fighting and implementing the Security Council's resolutions immediately, and that he was about to meet 'Ali Salim al-Bid and the other leaders in order to enable him to submit his report to Butrus- Ghali, UN secretary general, who would in turn present it to the UN Security Council to take additional resolutions which would lead to the imposition of the state of stability and peace in Yemen and the rest of the region.
