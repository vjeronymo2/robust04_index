Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Feb (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak and the delegation accompanying him returned safely to Cairo this evening, following a one-day visit to Turkey at the invitation of Turkish President Suleyman Demirel. During the visit, President Mubarak held a series of intensive talks and important political meetings with the Turkish president, Prime Minister Tansu Ciller, Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin, and a number of political and party figures. The purpose of the talks was to bolster ties of cooperation in the political, economic, trade, cultural, and media fields.
