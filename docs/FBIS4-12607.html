BFN [Italicized passages recorded] [Text] [Announcer] Salim Salih Muhammad, member of the Yemeni Presidential Council and assistant secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], accompanied by Dr. 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Dali, member of the YSP Political Bureau and secretary of foreign relations, arrived in Amman today on a two-day visit to Jordan during which they will meet with His Majesty King Husayn and senior officials. The visit comes within the framework of their tour of the region. Upon arrival at Amman Civil Airport, Salim Salih Muhammad, member of the Yemeni Presidential Council, spoke to reporters about his visit Jordan. He said: [Muhammad] First, we carry the greetings of the Yemeni people and of their political leadership and parties to His Majesty King Husayn and the fraternal Jordanian people. We also carry our great appreciation for the efforts exerted by our Jordanian brothers and by His Majesty King Husayn, including both the efforts that preceded the signing of the accord document and the efforts that followed, represented by the great role played by fraternal Jordan within the military committee, as well as its political efforts aimed at solving the existing political crisis. Our current tour comes within the framework of efforts to inform our brothers in Jordan and the Arab brothers of the nature of the present situation. It also comes within the framework of a request for help, especially from fraternal Jordan, to overcome problems and certain difficulties we are facing. We specifically ask fraternal Jordan to continue this role because, in our view, it is a role that does not concern the Yemenis alone, but concerns all Arabs. It is an honorable pan-Arab role. We reiterate our thanks to fraternal Jordan, to His Majesty King Husayn, and to the Jordanian Government. [Announcer] On Yemeni efforts to implement the Yemeni accord document, Salim Salih Muhammad said: [Muhammad] The dialogue is still going on. What is important to us now is to obstruct the military course -- a course that is frightening to us -- and to reinforce the other course -- the course of dialogue -- in order to begin implementing this document that sets the practical and sound solutions to correct the Yemeni situation. Moreover, it is now important to us to build the new Yemen, which secures rights and duties for all its people. [Announcer] On the presence of other Arab mediation efforts. He said: [Muhammad] We do not view mediation efforts as separate efforts but as concentrated Arab efforts. Therefore, we welcome all our Arab brothers' support for the ongoing efforts inside Yemen. At the same time, we particularly welcome the Arab efforts represented by the roles played by the two fraternal countries, Jordan and Oman. [Announcer] On the practical steps that should be taken in order to overcome the crisis in Yemen, Salim Salih Muhammad said: [Muhammad] At the Amman meeting held in this good land during the holy month of Ramadan, certain issues were defined in terms of beginning to implement the provisions of the accord document, starting with the security issue, which worries the Yemenis and really needs immediate implementation in order to bring things back to normal in Yemen. Afterward, we will get into the priorities according to a fixed timetable. This is the normal course in order to get the crisis and Yemen out of the dark tunnel.
