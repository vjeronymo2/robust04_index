Housing Guaranty Program; Investment Opportunity The U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID) has authorized the guaranty of loans to the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka (``Borrower'') as part of USAID's development assistance program. At this time, the Government of Sri Lanka has authorized USAID to request proposals from eligible lenders for a loan under this program of $10 Million U.S. Dollars (US$10,000,000). The Government of Sri Lanka (``GOSL'') has retained the services of a Financial Advisor for this borrowing. Interested U.S. lenders or investment bankers may submit bids either directly to the Borrower's representative and USAID or through the Financial Advisor retained by GOSL for the borrowing.
