[Docket No. RP94&hyph;343&hyph;002] NorAm Gas Transmission Company; Notice Of Filing December 16, 1994. Take notice that on December 14, 1994, NorAm Gas Transmission Company (NGT) tendered for filing as part of its FERC Gas Tariff, Fourth Revised Volume No. 1, revised Original Sheet No. 126, to become effective February 1, 1995. NGT states that the revised tariff sheet describes, in greater detail than as originally filed, the area around the Chandler Compressor Station in which pool receipts may be designated to any one of NGT's three zones, by stating that receipt points in Pittsburg, Latimer, Haskell, and Pushmataha Counties in Oklahoma, and receipt points on Lines O West, OT&hyph;12, and OT&hyph;16, will be eligible receipt points for all of NGT's zones. Any person desiring to protest said filing should file a protest with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, 825 North Capitol Street, NE., Washington, D.C. 20426, in accordance with Rule 211 of the Commission's Rules of Practice and Procedure (18 CFR 385.214). All such protests should be filed on or before December 23, 1994. Protests will be considered by the Commission in determining the appropriate action to be taken, but will not serve to make protestants parties to the proceeding. Any person wishing to become a party must file a motion to intervene. Copies of this filing are on file with the Commission and are available for public inspection in the Public Reference Room.
