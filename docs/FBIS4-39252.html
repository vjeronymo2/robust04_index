BFN [Andrey Illarionov report: "Yet Another Plan"] [Text] Novosibirsk -- The Novosibirsk Oblast Administration has drawn up a plan to stabilize the territory's economic position. An extensive list of measures has been conceived aimed at seeking funding to resolve priority tasks in the oblast and to elaborate mechanisms for state regulation of the economy. The plan's priorities include the development of agricultural production and support for the fuel and energy complex. The social section of the plan is aimed at preventing mass unemployment and a decline in living standards. Scientists and specialists in Novosibirsk and the oblast's rayons will have to resolve such difficult tasks as organizing transactions between enterprises and ensuring economic regulation of food prices. A science support fund is to be set up.
