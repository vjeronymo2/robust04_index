BFN [From the "Exclusive" program] [Text] Marcelino dos Santos, Mozambique's Assembly president, said during a news conference that there is an historic demand for Frelimo [Mozambique Liberation Front] to win the elections and there is no possibility that its main opposition, Renamo [Mazambique National Resistance], could wage a war as UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] did in Angola after the elections. Dos Santos also said the conditions under which his country will celebrate its national holiday on 25 June. Dos Santos stressed that he was in Cuba to learn firsthand the situation there and the manner in which the people are ready to resist and triumph. [Begin Dos Santos recording, in Portuguese] I would like to express our complete solidarity with the Republic of Cuba. We must fight this international illegality, the economic blockade against Cuba, which is an international bastion. Let us not forget that the peoples of the world need Cuba to remain firm, and that it is important for the progress of all peoples. Furthermore, I would say the victory of the Cuban people is necessary for all the peoples of the world. [end recording]
