BFN [Excerpts] Brother Yasir 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, yesterday evening made a telephone call to His Excellency President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, president of the Yemeni Republic. President 'Arafat asked the Yemeni president to overcome the current Yemeni crisis by brotherly means and to stop the spilling of Yemeni blood, especially on the occasion of the pilgrimage and the blessed feast of 'Id al-Adha. [passage omitted] The president also made a similar contact with Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid, hoping that the current crisis in Yemen will be overcome by brotherly means and stopping the spilling of Arab blood in Yemen during these blessed days.
