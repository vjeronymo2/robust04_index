941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / ADB approves Kazakh loans The Asian Development Bank has approved its first two loans, totalling Dollars 60m, to the central Asian republic of Kazakhstan, the bank said yesterday. The bank said the loans to Kazakhstan are designed to help it in the transition to a market-based economy. A Dollars 554,000 technical assistance grant comes with the loans. The loans will support the government's stabilisation and structural reform programme by helping bridge an external financing gap to prevent a further decline in output and deterioration of living standards, the bank said.
