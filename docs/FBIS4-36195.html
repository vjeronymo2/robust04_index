BFN [Text] The deputy prime minister of Russia, Mr. Yuriy Yarov, says Indo-Russian cooperation in nuclear science will continue, particularly in the exchange of technology and scientists. He was speaking in Bombay yesterday after visiting the Bhaba Atomic Research Center. Mr. Yarov lauded India's nuclear science development. The Russian deputy prime minister also visited the Mazagaon dock. Mr. Yarov called for joint ventures between India and Russia in the ship-building area.
