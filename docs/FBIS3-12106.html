Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] San Salvador de Jujuy, 29 Mar (NA) -- More than 1,000 Jujuy Province government workers at noon today destroyed public property outside the provincial Government House in response to Jujuy Government Minister Antonio Paliari's refusal to approve a salary increase for the workers. After the incidents, police personnel used tear gas to disperse the demonstrators. No major damage or injuries have been reported.
