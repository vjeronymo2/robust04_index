Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Occhetto Hit by Campaign Fatigue"] [Text] Bologna, 21 Mar (ANSA) -- Democratic Party of the Left leader Achille Occhetto cancelled three election meetings in and around Bologna today, according to aides, because he had been hit by campaign trail fatigue. But they said he would be speaking at two major rallies, one at Parma this evening and in Bologna's main square, Piazza Maggiore, tomorrow evening. Occhetto, who was 58 on March 3, is touring the constituency he has chosen to stand in for the March 27/28 general election.
