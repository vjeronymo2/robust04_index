BFN [Text] Hizballah representatives in the Lebanese Chamber of Deputies today accused U.S. Ambassador in Beirut Mark Hambley of interfering in Lebanon's internal affairs and insulting the sentiments of the Lebanese people. The deputies issued a statement pointing to the American ambassador's illogical movements and his threats to political trends in Lebanon. The statement says the American ambassador's moves violate diplomatic norms and aim to stand against the Lebanese Islamic and popular resistance against the Zionist occupier. The statement also stresses that recent military movements by the Zionist entity in south Lebanon are aimed at clearing that region of its inhabitants.
