EFFECTIVE DATE: November 18, 1994. Food and Drug Administration [Docket No. 94N&hyph;0368] The Upjohn Co., et al.; Withdrawal of Approval of Seven Abbreviated Antibiotic Applications AGENCY: Food and Drug Administration, HHS. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is withdrawing approval of seven abbreviated antibiotic applications (AADA's). The holders of the AADA's notified the agency in writing that the drug products were no longer marketed and requested that the approval of the applications be withdrawn.
