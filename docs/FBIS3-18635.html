Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Italian soldiers could be sent to Bosnia to reinforce the UN peacekeeping contingent. France and Great Britain have requested this from UN Secretary General Butrus-Ghali. Foreign Minister Beniamino Andreatta said that he has not yet received any official request but that he would not set any conditions. Although Defense Minister Fabio Fabbri has not denied the possibility, he pointed out that the government would have to make a collective decision.
