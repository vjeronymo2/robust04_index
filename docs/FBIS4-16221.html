BFN [Report attributed to the BBC] [Text] President of the Crimean Autonomous Republic Yuriy Meshkov subjected the decision by the Ukrainian president to create the institution of Ukrainian presidential representative in the Crimea to sharp criticism. In his televised speech, Meshkov called the Ukrainian Government "the most dangerous in Europe." He pointed out that the appointment of such a representative is a political provocation, which may do harm to the development of the democratic process on the peninsula. This was reported by the BBC.
