940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Airflow drops 32% to Pounds 1m Pre-tax profits of Airflow Streamlines, a maker of cabins for industrial tractors and trucks, fell by 32 per cent from Pounds 1.47m to Pounds 1m. Turnover was 8 per cent higher at Pounds 71.4m. The company said margins remained under pressure and better results would have to be achieved by continuing programmes of cost reduction and improved operating efficiency. Earnings per share dropped from 10.38p to 6.65p, while a final dividend of 2p makes an unchanged total of 3p.
