Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report] [Text] Ayatollah Khamene'i received President Saparmurad Niyazov on Thursday evening, 6 January. Niyazov's delegation, President Hashemi-Rafsanjani, and (?Neshati), who is a member of the Islamic Republic of Iran's delegation, which will hold talks with the visiting guests, attended the meeting. Ayatollah Khamene'i described Turkmenistan's declaration of independence as a divine blessing for the people of that republic and said he is convinced that the foundations of Turkmenistan will be gradually strengthened. Khamene'i described Turkmenistan's rich natural resources and 10 million hectares of productive land as an opportunity for that republic to become developed. In addition, Khamene'i drew President Niyazov's attention to the opposition of exploitive forces to the improvement of cooperation among the regional countries and pointed to the importance of establishing friendly relations between Iran and Turkmenistan. He said: The Islamic peoples of Iran and Turkmenistan are linked by cultural relations. In view of their faith, religion, and traditions, close relations exist between them. For his part, President Niyazov said that improved relations between the two countries are essential. He asserted: Turkmenistan's people are making an effort to restore their history and reinstate their freedom and cultural traditions.
