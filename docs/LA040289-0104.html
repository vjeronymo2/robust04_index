April 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

6TH BODY IN 15 DAYS FOUND AT GRIFFITH PARK

Hikers in Griffith Park discovered a decomposed body Saturday, bringing to six the number of corpses found in the Los Angeles park in the last 15 days, police said.

The hikers, walking along one of the many winding paths in the 4,000-acre park, made the discovery at 4:30 p.m., Sgt. Joe Ramm said.

"The body is so decomposed that we're not even sure if it's a man or a woman at this point," Ramm said.
