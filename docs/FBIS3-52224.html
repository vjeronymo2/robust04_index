Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] There has been reports of a series of bomb blasts in Doda, Bhadarwah, and Kishtwar areas of Jammu region in occupied Kashmir. Commenting on the explosions, a Foreign Office spokesman said this is a manifestation of the alienation of the people of Kashmir with Indian rule and their response to brutal repression by occupation troops. The situation in Jammu region is reported tense following the bomb blasts at various places. Indian troops have shot dead another five people during siege and search operations in Ganderbal, Khaniyar, Badgam, and (Laulab) areas. A number of people were also rounded up during the operations. Mojahedin in retaliation have attacked bunkers and other military targets in nine localities of Srinagar. Several troops were injured when Mojahedin launched a surprise attack on an Indian army contingent near Kulgam area. Two bridges of military importance were also destroyed by Mojahedin in Jammu region.
