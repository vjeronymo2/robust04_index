May 31, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Trump Shuttle Picks Ad Agency: It didn't take long for the Trump Shuttle to shuttle in a new ad agency. The former Eastern Airlines Northeast Shuttle, which real estate developer Donald J. Trump gained control of last week, named the New York ad firm Scali, McCabe, Sloves Inc. as its agency of record. The agency, which creates ads for Perdue Chicken and Volvo, plans to begin an ad campaign within the next few weeks. Although the agency won't say, many expect the new campaign to focus on the reduced rates the shuttle plans to charge -- and Trump himself is expected to play a prominent role in the ads.
