BFN [Report by Eytan Rabin] [Text] PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat decided not to appoint Muhammad Dahlan and Jibril al-Rujub as heads of security in the Gaza Strip and the West Bank. The HA'ARETZ correspondent has learned from sources in Tunis that 'Arafat decided to appoint General Misbah Saqr, an inhabitant of al-Shaja'iyah in the Gaza Strip, as head of the security services in the West Bank and the Gaza Strip. According to Palestinian sources, 'Arafat chose Saqr, a close confidant, as Dahlan's and al-Rujub's superior because of their close ties with Israeli security elements. Dahlan and al-Rujub are due in the territories today.
