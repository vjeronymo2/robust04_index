BFN [Text] Iraq has informed the Arab League that the rulers of Saudi Arabia and Kuwait have continued to provide facilities to U.S., British, and French aircraft in their violations of Iraqi airspace. These violations constitute a serious threat to Iraq's security, sovereignty, and national unity. In a letter addressed the day before yesterday to the Arab League's secretary general, Iraq's permanent representative said that between 9 and 16 June, these aircraft conducted 301 hostile sorties and violated Iraqi airspace after taking off from bases in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait. The Iraqi envoy attached to his letter lists detailing these violations.
