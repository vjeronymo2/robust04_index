BFN [Text] Iguacu, Brazil, April 24 (XINHUA) -- A senior Chinese party delegation headed by Hu Jintao, a member of the Politburo of the Standing Committee of the Chinese Communist Party (CCP) Central Committee, arrived here today for an official visit to Brazil. During the visit, Hu Jintao will meet with the Brazilian Government and parliamentary leaders. Hu came here after winding up a four-day official tour of Argentina, during which, he was received by Argentine President Carlos Menem and met with some government, parliamentary and party officials. Brazil is his last leg of the three-nation Latin American tour which has also taken him to Uruguay.
