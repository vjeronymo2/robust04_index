920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / Commodities and Agriculture: CIS reduces nickel exports EXPORTS of unwrought, unalloyed nickel from the Commonwealth of Independent States to Germany and Japan slumped in the first quarter of 1992, says the International Nickel Study Group, Reuter reports from the Hague. Germany took 2,751 tonnes after 5,035 in the same period last year and 4,426 in 1990. Japan took 1,180 tonnes, down from 6,476 and 3,034.
