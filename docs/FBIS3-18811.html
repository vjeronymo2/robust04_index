Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Bonn (DPA) -- On Tuesday [8 March] in Bonn delegates to the Federal Convention of the Christian Democratic Union/Christian Social Union [CDU/CSU] officially nominated Roman Herzog as candidate for the presidential election. In a secret ballot Herzog won 448 of the 466 votes. Six delegates voted against him, nine abstained, and three votes were invalid. [passage omitted]
