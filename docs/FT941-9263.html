940216 FT 16 FEB 94 / Russia rescinds spirit tax rise The Russian government, responding to protests from vodka producers, said it would rescind a rise in excise duties, Reuter reports from Moscow. News agencies said Prime Minister Victor Chernomyrdin signed an order rolling back the excise duty from 90 per cent to the pre-New Year level of 85 per cent. The government was imposing a rise in import duties on foreign vodka from 100 per cent to 300 per cent.
