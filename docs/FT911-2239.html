910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / International Company News: Lego sales up against trend LEGO, the Danish toys group, increased sales by 15 per cent in a world market which showed little growth, writes Hilary Barnes in Copenhagen. Sales in Germany, the group's biggest single market, went ahead by 20 per cent following the integration of East and West Germany. In the US, the next largest market, sales rose 15 per cent. Sales measured in kroneadvanced 7.5 per cent to DKr3.92bn (Dollars 580m). Pre-tax profits fell from DKr505m to DKr447m.
