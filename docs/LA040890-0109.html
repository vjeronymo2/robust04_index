April 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $20 Saturday when Forest Fealty, the front horse in his win parlay, finished second to Patches in the third race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $899.40.

Bob's bet today is $50 to win on Profit Key in the seventh.
