941015 FT 15 OCT 94 / Finance and the Family: FT personal finance quarterly - At a glance The autumn issue of the FT Quarterly Review of Personal Finance, to be published with next Friday's paper and repeated with Saturday's, puts the Budget into context. It will examine the constraints on the chancellor, investors' worries, and possible steps they might take in advance. In the review's survey section, investors and the industry focus on unit trusts.
