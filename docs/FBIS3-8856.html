Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, March 16 (CTK) -- Lubomir Harach, proposed by the Democratic Left Party (SDL), was today appointed education and science minister in the new Slovak Government. Lubormir Harach was born on March 3, 1953, in Horna Zdan, central Slovakia. In 1976 he graduated from the Faculty of Nuclear Physics of Prague's Technical University. Between 1976 and 1987 he taught at Slovakia's Technical University in Bratislava, from 1987 to 1990 he worked at the Slovak Education Ministry's Universities Department. In 1990 he was appointed a director of the Information and Education Prognostics Institute.
