930805 FT 05 AUG 93 / International Company News: Commerzbank buys CCB stake COMMERZBANK has acquired a 35 per cent stake in Commerz-Credit-Bank Europartner from Credit Lyonnais, giving it full control of CCB, Reuter reports from Frankfurt. CCB is a joint venture founded by Commerzbank and the French bank in 1974. Saarbruecken-based CCB was the result of a merger between the two banks.
