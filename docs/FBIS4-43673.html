BFN [Text] Kiel (DPA) -- Next year German soldiers will go to Russia for joint military exercises. In return, in 1996 Russian units are to train together with the Bundeswehr in western Germany, Defense Minister Volker Ruehe, Christian Democratic Union, said at the 100th Kiel Week on Saturday [18 June]. "Europe is growing together best when people meet," Ruehe said. This also applies to the armed forces. Today Central Europe has the opportunity to become the anchor of stability for the entire continent. For this purpose, cooperation with Russia must be intensified. "Security and peace in Europe can be achieved only together with and not against Russia." At the same time, the minister announced that he will raise the number of reservists to such an extent that 100,000 of them can participate in exercises every year.
