September 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

'WAR IN EUROPE: 50-YEAR LEGACY'

I must write to tell you how well written Tyler Marshall's war story was ("War Haunts, Still Shapes the World," Aug. 27). I was 20 when the war started and I think I have read many, many reports of the war but none better than his.

BELLE RUSSO

Lawndale
