Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 2 Mar (XINHUA) -- The Henry Fok Ying-tung Sports Foundation held an award ceremony at the VIP Room of Beijing's Shoudu Stadium this afternoon. Li Tieying, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and state councillor, attended the ceremony and presented awards to China's outstanding winter athletes. At the recent 17th Winter Olympics in Norway's Lillihammer, Chinese athlete Zhang Yanmei won the silver medal in the women's short-track 500 meter speed skating race while Ye Qiaobo and Chen Lu respectively won the bronze medal in the women's 1,000 meter speed skating and the women's figure skating. He Zhenliang, vice chairman of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission, spoke at the award ceremony, saying Mr. Fok and the foundation had entrusted him and the others to present the medals and cash awards to the Chinese medalists. According to the foundation's charter, a Chinese silver medalist in the 17th Winter Olympics will receive a 250-gram gold medal and a cash award of $10,000 while a bronze medalist will receive a 150- gram gold medal and a cash award of $5,000. Li Tieying; Wu Shaozu, minister in charge of the State Physical Culture and Sports Commission; and He Zhenliang respectively presented the medals and cash awards to Zhang Yanmei, Chen Lu, and Ye Qiaobo. Ye Qiaobo, who has not returned yet, asked her representative to receive the medal and awards on her behalf.
