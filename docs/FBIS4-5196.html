BFN [Text] Vientiane, April 7 (KPL) -- Prime Minister of the Lao PDR [Lao People's Democratic Republic] Khamtai Siphandon, on April 5, received at Prime Minister's Office Mr. Phan Van Khai, president of the Vietnamese-Lao Cooperation Committee on official visit here. On this occasion Mr. Phan Van Khai informed Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon about the result of the 16th session of the Lao and Vietnamese cooperation committees and expressed thanks for the warm reception given by Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon. Vietnamese vice prime minister highly valued the many sided successes of the Lao people in the implementation of the renovation policy under the able leadership of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party currently headed by President Khamtai Siphandon. He also wished the fraternal Lao people still greater success. Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon, for his part, highly valued the visit of the high level delegation of Vietnam, considering it as a contribution to the development and strengthening of the friendly relations, special solidarity and all-round cooperation between the two nations. The Lao prime minister reaffirmed that the Lao PDR and the SR [Socialist Republic] of Vietnam enjoyed a long standing friendly relation and that the Vietnamese party, government and people would still provide assistance and cooperation to the Lao PDR.
