BFN [Text] Xian, June 7 (XINHUA) -- China Northwest Airlines has contacted the families of the 13 passengers from outside the mainland who died in a plane crash near Xian yesterday. The 146 passengers and 14 crew died when China Northwest Airlines passenger plane crashed 30 kilometers southeast of Xian. There were no survivors. Of the passengers, four were from Italy, two from the United States, two from Britain, one from Switzerland, three from Hong Kong and one from Taiwan, with the rest all from the Chinese Mainland, according to airlines officials. The officials said that the bodies of these passengers are being properly taken care of. Police are now looking for the possessions of these passengers around the site of the crash in Xian's suburban Changan County. The cause of the crash is still being investigated, but rescue teams have already recovered the "black box" containing the flight recordings, which is being analyzed, officials said.
