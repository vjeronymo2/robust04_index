BFN [Text] According to foreign reports, Russian Vice Foreign Minister Aleksandr Panov on 29 March says that Russia will render military assistance to the DPRK in accordance with the Friendship, Cooperation and Mutual Assistance Treaty signed in 1961 in the event it is invaded [chimnyak]. He stressed that the Russia Government will fulfill its treaty obligations and take steps coninciding with this purpose [mokchoke puhaptoenun chochi].
