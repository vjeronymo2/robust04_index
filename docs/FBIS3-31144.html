Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, February 10 (ATA) -- President of Malta Dr. Censu Tabone completed his official visit in Albania on February 9. Dr. Tabone came to Albania at the invitation of President of Albania Sali Berisha. During his four day visit in Albania, President Tabone met with senior Albanian officials including President Berisha, Premier Aleksander Meksi and the Speaker of the parliament Pjeter Arbnori. The talks focused on the concretization of the relations between the two countries.
