930118 FT 18 JAN 93 / Haiti army backs talks THE Haitian army announced yesterday it had agreed to UN-brokered negotiations to advance democracy in the country, AP reports from Port-au-Prince. It had also agreed to the appointment of an interim prime minister, a UN official said. The announcement followed delivery of a letter from exiled President Jean-Bertrand Aristide in which he agreed to talk to coup supporters for the first time since his overthrow in September 1991. It also followed calls by US President-elect Bill Clinton for Haitians not to begin an exodus.
