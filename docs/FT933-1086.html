930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Secure Trust For the six months to June 30, pre-tax profits of Secure Trust, which provides home banking, money management and other financial services, expanded from Pounds 3.44m to Pounds 3.71m. Earnings per share were higher at 17.3p (16.1p) while the interim dividend is increased to 4.5p (4p).
