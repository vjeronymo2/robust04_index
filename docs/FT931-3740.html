930313 FT 13 MAR 93 / UN condemnation for Unita The UN Security Council yesterday threatened unspecified action against Unita rebels in Angola, Reuter reports from the United Nations. A council resolution, passed unanimously, 'strongly condemns' violations by Unita of peace accords, its failure to take part in political institutions, its withdrawal from the new Angolan armed forces and 'its seizure by force of provincial capital and municipalities'.
