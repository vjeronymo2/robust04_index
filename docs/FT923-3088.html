920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Arthur Wood in red and no sign of upturn Arthur Wood & Son (Longport), a maker of earthenware, reported a first half pre-tax loss of Pounds 42,724, compared with a profit of Pounds 40,562, reflecting an 8 per cent drop in turnover, from Pounds 1.71m to Pounds 1.57m. Losses per share came out at 1.69p (1.36p). Mr Anthony Wood, chairman, said trade conditions had worsened in the period and there was no sign of any improvement.
