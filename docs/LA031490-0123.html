March 14, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: FULLERTON;

GREEN FEES TO RISE AT CITY GOLF COURSE

Green fees at Fullerton Golf Course will go up slightly April 1 to pay for improvements and higher maintenance costs.

An 18-hole round of golf will increase from $10 to $11 on weekdays, and from $14.50 to $15 on weekends. The cost of a bucket of balls for the driving range will increase 25 cents for all three bucket sizes. Electric cart rentals will increase by $1.

The increased fees will help pay for major improvements at the course, including installation of concrete paths for golf carts, Community Services director Ronald E. Hagan said.

The last fee increase at the city-owned golf course occurred in June, 1989.
