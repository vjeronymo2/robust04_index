930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Fyffes in Spanish joint venture Fyffes Group, the Dublin-based fruit wholesaler, has entered into a joint venture with Coplaca (Grupo Regional de Cooperativas del Archipielago Canario), the largest banana producing co-operative in the Canary Islands. The two companies are establishing a Spanish company on a 50:50 basis to market, distribute and ripen bananas from the Canaries in continental Europe. The new company is expected to have annual sales of Dollars 100m (Pounds 65m).
