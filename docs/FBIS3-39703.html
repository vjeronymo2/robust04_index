Language: German Article Type:BFN ["U.M."-initialed report: "New Increase in Number of Asylum Applications by 38 Percent"] [Excerpt] There were 24,739 asylum applications in 1993, a 38 percent increase over the previous year and similar to the 1989 level. More than 25 percent of the applicants came from the Bosnia-Herzegovina conflict area. At the end of 1993, a total of 118,321 asylum-seekers or refugees were staying in Switzerland. [passage omitted]
