930731 FT 31 JUL 93 / Finance & the Family: Rally lifts smaller companies - At a Glance Small company shares benefited from the market rally which saw the FT-SE 100 index surge above 2,900 this week. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) rose 1 per cent from 1,464.03 to 1,478.91 over the week to July 29.
