Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Bulletin No. 20 issued by the Mexican National Defense Secretariat on the situation in Chiapas; place and date not given; from the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] Military personnel continue to provide security to the towns of San Cristobal de las Casas, Rancho Nuevo, Oxchuc, Altamirano, Ocosingo, Las Margaritas, Comitan, and Escopetazo in case of possible new attacks and in response to the civilian population's demand for the presence of troops. Yesterday, residents in Oxchuc turned in to the authorities 16 lawbreakers who participated in various vandalistic actions since 1 January. Social work continues through the distribution of food, blankets, medicines, and potable water.
