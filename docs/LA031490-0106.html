March 14, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Avon Proxy Fight Launched: A group linked to Mary Kay Cosmetics will seek four seats on Avon Products Inc.'s board to advocate selling the direct sales company in an effort to maximize shareholder value. In a full-page advertisement in the New York Times, Chartwell Associates L.P., which currently hold 9.9% of Avon's outstanding stock, said it might propose merging with Avon itself. Among the partners in Chartwell is John P. Rochon, the vice chairman of Mary Kay Corp.
