BFN [Text] The border villages of the Kazakh region in the northwest of Azerbaijan came under "intensive fire" from guns and tanks from the side of the Armenian village of Voskepar on Friday night, the press service of the Azeri Defense Ministry reported. The shelling inflicted damage to the villages of Gushchu-Airym and Mazam. Several civilians were wounded. Also on Saturday the village of Sadarak and the border strip of the Shakhbuzsky region of the Nakhichevan autonomous republic were shelled from the Armenian territory. According to the Azeri Defense Ministry, "Armenian troops carry out planned destruction of houses, communications, historical monuments in the city of Agdam and a number of seized villages of the Agdam region."
