BFN [Russian Federation Government Decree No. 479 "On Giving Enterprises the Right To Participate in the Russian Federation's Military-Technical Cooperation With Foreign Countries," dated Moscow, 6 May 1994, and signed by Russian Federation Government Chairman V. Chernomyrdin] [Text] In order to raise the efficiency of the Russian Federation's military-technical cooperation with foreign countries, the Russian Federation Government decrees: 1. To ratify the Statute on the Attestation and Registration of Enterprises, giving them the right to carry out exports of weapons and military hardware and of military work and services, and put it into effect as of 1 May 1994. 2. To give enterprises which develop and manufacture weapons and military hardware and are attested and registered as participants in foreign economic activity in the sphere of military-technical cooperation, the following rights: to seek out foreign clients in countries with which military-technical cooperation is not prohibited, to carry out demonstrations and, during talks, to give the tactical and technical specifications of weapons and military hardware whose export is authorized, to announce the guideline price agreed according to the established procedure, and to conduct advertising work and other marketing work; to sign contracts and, on the basis of licenses obtained according to the established procedure, to independently export weapons and military hardware they have manufactured over and above the state defense order, as well as military work and services; to select agents from among the organizations which have obtained, according to the established procedure, permits to conduct foreign economic activity in the sphere of military-technical cooperation. 3. As a partial amendment to Point 3 of the Statute on the Procedure for Licensing the Export and Import of Military Output (Work, Services) on the Territory of the Russian Federation, ratified by decree No. 80 of the Russian Federation Council of Ministers-Government dated 28 January 1993 (SOBRANIYE AKTOV PREZIDENTA I PRAVITELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII, 1993, No. 6, p 484), to prescribe that the export of spare parts, training equipment, and accessories for weapons and military hardware earlier supplied to foreign countries, and also the carrying out of work as part of their technical servicing and repair (including the use of series-produced subassemblies to replace those that have been taken out of production) is carried out without the formulation of a Russian Federation Government decision. [signed] Russian Federation Government Chairman V. Chernomyrdin
