941026 FT 26 OCT 94 / Call for trade to transcend politics The main trading nations should rise above politics in choosing a leader for the World Trade Organisation, Professor Jagdish Bhagwati of New York's Columbia University said in London last night at a memorial lecture for Harold Wincott, a former Financial Times journalist. Prof Bhagwati, a former economic policy adviser to the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade, said the new WTO faced a historic challenge in finding solutions to new sources of world trading conflict.
