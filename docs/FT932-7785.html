930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Minstergate MINSTERGATE: Pre-tax profit for six months to February 28 was Pounds 723,000 (Pounds 951,000). Sales Pounds 1.49m (Pounds 1.93m); interest received Pounds 793,000 (Pounds 958,000) and loss on trading activities Pounds 83,000 (Pounds 24,000). Earnings per share 18.1p (25.43p).
