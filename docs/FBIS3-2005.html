Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 2 (XINHUA) -- Today's economic news briefs: Tianjin Company To Be Listed in HK [Hong Kong] [subhead] A Tianjin-based chemical firm, the Tianjin Bohai Chemical (Group) Co.,Ltd., which is the biggest salt chemical production center in China, will soon be listed in Hong Kong.

Sheet Glass Furnace in Operation
China's first electronic sheet glass production furnace has gone into operation in Qinhuangdao, a port city in north China's Hebei Province. It can produce electronic glass sheet from 0.5 mm to 3 mm thick for the electronics, energy, and medical and health industries. The total investment in the project is 110 million yuan, and it will have an annual glass production of 2.41 million sq m.
Steel Output
The total steel production output of the Shougang (Capital Iron and Steel) Corporation in 1993 hit over seven million tons, with 3.98 million tons, about 60 percent of the total, produced by continuous casting. The company's steel output in 1992 was 5.7 million tons, and continuous-cast steel totalled 2.76 million tons in the same year.
