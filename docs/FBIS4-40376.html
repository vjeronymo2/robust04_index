BFN [Russian Federation president's Edict No. 1182 "On the Issue and Circulation of Housing Certificates"] [Text] With a view to expanding the use of funds owned by the population and from other nonbudget sources of financing in the housing sphere and protecting them from inflation I decree: 1. That it be established that the corporate bodies with the rights of clients when constructing apartment blocks, a land plot allocated within the established procedure for housing construction, and planning documents for housing construction have the right to attract funds from citizens of the Russian Federation using housing certificates. 2. That the attached statute on the issue and circulation of housing certificates be approved. 3. That the Russian Federation Government submit before 1 August 1994 to the Federal Assembly State Duma a draft law on the procedure for taxing operations involving housing certificates. 4. The Russian Federation Ministry of Finances and the Russian Federation State Committee for Questions of Architecture and Construction must elaborate and approve within two months an Instruction on the procedure for the issue and circulation of housing certificates and also for their repayment, including through the provision of housing for their owners. 5. This edict enters into force from the moment it is published. [signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin, Moscow, Kremlin, 10 June 1994.
