940719 FT 19 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Elswick completes Pounds 400,000 acquisition Elswick, the packaging, paper and printing concern, yesterday announced it had completed the acquisition of Joseph Steinfeld for Pounds 400,000 cash. Steinfeld, a maker of woven labels and ancillary products for the clothing industry, incurred a deficit of Pounds 59,000 on sales of Pounds 1.17m in the year to end-January. Shareholders' funds were Pounds 772,000.
