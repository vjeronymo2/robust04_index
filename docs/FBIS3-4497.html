Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Changchun, March 19 (XINHUA) -- Northeast China's Jilin Province is speeding up the construction of railways, expressways and ports to promote its economic cooperation with foreign countries and regions. The province, with easy access to the sea through its borders with Russia and the People's Democratic Republic of Korea, is now quickening its building of four routes leading to the outside world. The four routes, mainly railways and expressways, will link up with China's two railways leading to Russia and Korea and extended to port cities of Russia and Korea. The province is also renovating its two airports and increasing domestic and international flights and routes. So far four air routes have been opened with irregular flights from Changchun to Hong Kong and to cities of Russia, Japan, and the Republic of Korea.
