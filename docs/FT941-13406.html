940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / World Trade News: Gas project for Mozambique Mozambique stands to increase its export earnings through its Pande gas field project, writes Leslie Crawford in Maputo. Located in the southern province of Inhambane, the field has reserves of 55bn cubic metres. ENH, the Mozambique state oil company, has entered into an agreement with Sasol of South Africa, which would buy and market the natural gas in South Africa.
