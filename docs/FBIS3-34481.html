Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Referring to Israeli threats, Shaykh Nabil Qawuq, a Hizballah deputy in the south, has stated that the resistance will not stand idly by in the face of Israeli escalation. He noted that it is no longer acceptable for the state to remain as a spectator. In a statement today, Qawuq called on the state to adopt a responsible stance and to prepare to face these threats.
