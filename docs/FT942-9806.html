940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Critchley pays Pounds 2.5m for Woodchester site Critchley, the electrical cable accessories manufacturer, is paying Pounds 2.5m for a 15.5 acre site, comprising a factory and offices at Woodchester, Gloucestershire. In its last accounts, Critchley announced a plan to spend Pounds 2.4m to develop the Brimscombe site. However, it has now been decided that the acquisition of the Woodchester property represents a more attractive alternative.
