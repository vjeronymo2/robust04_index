May 17, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

SUPER FAN TRIUMPHS

Super Fan caught the leaders coming out of the backstretch, then ran through heavy rain over the soft turf course to win the $35,200 Regret Prep by 1 1/2 lengths Wednesday at Churchill Downs. Super Fan, under Keith Allen, covered the mile in 1:40 4-5 and paid $3.20, $2.20 and $2.10. The 3-year-old filly, owned by Warner L. Jones Jr., earned $24,310, increasing her winnings to $126,163 for her sixth win in 13 starts.
