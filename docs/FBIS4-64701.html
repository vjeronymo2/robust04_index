BFN [Text] National Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan has left on an official visit to France. In a statement at Esenboga Airport prior to his departure, Golhan said that after the three-day visit to France as the official guest of French Defense Minister Francois Leotard, he will attend the Western European Union ministerial meeting to be held in Luxembourg on 9 May. He added that he will be discussing issues concerning NATO, Cyprus, Bosnia-Herzegovina, Azerbaijan, and international problems with French officials. He noted that defense industry cooperation activities between the two countries, which have significantly developed in recent years, will also be reviewed.
