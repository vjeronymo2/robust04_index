BFN [Report: "Jiang Zemin Inspects Zhuhai"] [Text] Zhuhai (TA KUNG PAO) -- Central Committee General Secretary Jiang Zemin is now on an inspection tour of Zhuhai. Accompanied by Guangdong CPC Committee Secretary Xie Fei and Zhuhai Mayor Liang Guangda on the afternoon of 17 June, Jiang Zemin inspected a series of large enterprises in Zhuhai, including Asia Simulation, Geli Air Conditioning, and the Giant Group. Jiang Zemin continued his inspection of Zhuhai on 18 June, and made arrangements to meet with some of Zhuhai's entrepreneurs in the evening. President Jiang Zemin inspected Zhuhai four years ago. He is staying at Zhuhai's Shijing Mountain Villa during his current trip, just as he did during his previous trip.
