Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Foreign Minister Willy Claes intends to sue the newspaper HET LAATSTE NIEUWS for libel. The minister announced this in BRTN's Sunday morning [30 January] television program The Seventh Day. HET LAATSTE NIEUWS yesterday wrote that Claes had been in favor of accepting a 15 million franc bribe from the Italian helicopter company Agusta. [passage omitted] Minister Claes has fervently denied his involvement and says he never even discussed bribes with the Agusta company.
