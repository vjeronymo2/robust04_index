November 7, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Altman Tribute: The American Cinematheque will pay tribute to film director Robert Altman this weekend.

Celebrities expected to attend the event at the Directors Guild of America headquarters in Hollywood include John Travolta, Lily Tomlin and Sally Kellerman, whose careers the filmmaker helped launch. Altman's "MASH" will be screened Friday and his "Nashville" will be screened Saturday. Kellerman will participate in a question-and-answer session following "MASH" Friday. Tomlin will help Altman introduce "Nashville" on Saturday. CLAUDIA PUIG
