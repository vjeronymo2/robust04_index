BFN [Excerpts] The parents of the three MIA's from the al-Sultan Ya'qub battle in Lebanon in 1982 claim that their sons are alive and held by Lebanese elements in the Lebanese al-Biqa'. They say they are basing their claim on new information they received from eyewitnesses. [passage omitted] Following the al-Sultan Ya'qub battle, a U.S. television team photographed a Syrian tank carrying what seemed to be three Israeli soldiers. Today the parents disclose that both they and the Israeli Government possess information that their sons are safe and sound. [passage omitted] Security elements reemphasized today that the Israeli Government does not spare any effort to obtain new information about IDF [Israel Defense Forces] MIA.
