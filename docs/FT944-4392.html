941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / UK Company News: John Tams growth Increased demand boosted interim results of John Tams Group, but an exceptional reorganisation charge of Pounds 190,000 this time meant pre-tax profits of the USM-quoted china and earthenware maker showed a 12 per cent advance from Pounds 501,000 to Pounds 563,000. Turnover for the six months to September 30 grew 5.3 per cent to Pounds 12.2m (Pounds 11.6m), of which exports account for 52 per cent. Earnings per share rose to 1.5p (1.42p) and an unchanged interim dividend of 1.6p has been declared.
