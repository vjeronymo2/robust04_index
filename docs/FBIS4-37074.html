BFN [Text] Pakistan has established contact with the United Nations on increasing aggressive Indian activities along the Line of Control [LOC] in Kashmir. A Foreign Office spokesman said in Islamabad today that Pakistan condemns India's aggressive action against UN observers deployed along the LOC. The spokesman also expressed concern over the statement by Rajesh Pilot, Indian minister of state for external affairs [position as heard], about the use of helicopter gunships against the Kashmiri people in the occupied territories. The spokesman said that it might escalate to a dangerous level the Indian campaign of atrocities in occupied Kashmir. He said that India should not forget what happened in Afghanistan.
