910419 FT 19 APR 91 / World Trade News: China retail venture Yaohan International, a Hong Kong-based Japanese retailing and catering group, is to build China's first foreign-owned department store at Pudong, a special economic zone to be developed in Shanghai over the next 20 to 30 years, writes John Elliott in Hong Kong. It is taking a 51 per cent stake in a HKDollars 780m joint venture with Shanghai's No 1 Department Store to build China's largest shopping centre. Construction starts next year.
