940324 FT 24 MAR 94 / Iran to develop port city President Ali Akbar Hashemi Rafsanjani yesterday inaugurated Iran's biggest pier, near Bandar Abbas, south Iran, and announced plans to develop the port city into an economic and industrial area, Reuter reports from Tehran. The new pier, 12.5 miles west of Bandar Abbas, was built with a 4.4 mile channel alongside, allowing bulk carriers with a capacity of 100,000 tonnes to berth.
