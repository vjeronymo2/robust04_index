BFN [Text] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos today described Albanian President Sali Berisha's letter to the EU [European Union] commission as positive. At the same time, Venizelos stressed that even with a few days' delay, the Albanian side's reaction to Greece's proposals to defuse the situation make things easier. Venizelos said that in the past few days, the Albanian side attempted a dangerous spread of the tension, but Greece's stance was calm and firm from the beginning.
