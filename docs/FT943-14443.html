940714 FT 14 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Irish Life sales rise 15% to IPounds 185.6m Total sales for the first six months of 1994 at Irish Life, the insurance group, rose 15 per cent to IPounds 185.6m (Pounds 183.4m), while in Ireland recurring premium sales totalled IPounds 28.8m, up 11 per cent on 1993. Single premium sales increased by 6 per cent over the year to IPounds 64.5m. Sales in the UK fell by 37 per cent to IPounds 13.2m.
