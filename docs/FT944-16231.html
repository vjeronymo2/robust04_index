941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / People: British Thornton Holdings British Thornton Holdings, the packaging and furniture group which issued a profits warning last week, has appointed a new group finance director. Clive Campion, 53, has slotted into the position made vacant in June when John Edwards left to join Ionica. He has been working as a consultant since he resigned from Automated Security Holdings, where he was finance director from August 1988 until January 1993. He was paid Pounds 222,000 on termination of contract. The grammar-school educated Campion took his accountancy articles with Grant Thornton. Before joining ASH he was finance director of Electronic Rentals Group.
