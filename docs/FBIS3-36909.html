Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Immediately after the morning news bulletin, transmission of Russian Television ceased in Ivanovo. The transmission of Ostankino First Channel Television, St. Petersburg TV, and Mayak, Yunost, and Radio Odin radio stations were also stopped. On all three TV channels Ivanovo people will only be able to view evening news bulletins. Local TV programs will be transmitted without change since the Ivanovo TV and radio company has no debt to the communications workers, our correspondent in that town, Oleg Alalykin, reports.
