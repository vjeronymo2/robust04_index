941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / Business Travel: London City London City Airport has launched new routes to Geneva and Hamburg, and says it is celebrating another 'tremendous increase' in passenger numbers. Crossair, the Swiss airline, which has services to Zurich and Lugano, has added a twice-daily flight to Geneva. And Lufthansa has added five flights a week to Hamburg, as well as improving its flight schedule to Frankfurt. London City Airport says 49,680 passengers used its flights last month, a 76 per cent gain on October last year. It now serves 11 destinations, but plans to add to them at year's turn.
