August 11, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SIGNS OF THE TIMES LOOK BAD FOR FANS

Today's paper advertises the "Jose Canseco Baseball Card Show" at only $22 a crack for his autograph.

The same athlete who just signed a $23.5-million contract for the next five years.

What a sad message we're sending the children: the greed of today's idols. Where have all the real heroes gone? Certainly, they are not to be found in the ranks of today's athletes.

JOSEPHINE QUIRARTE

Redondo Beach
