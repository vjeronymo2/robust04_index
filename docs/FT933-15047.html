930710 FT 10 JUL 93 / Finance and the Family: Age Concern's capital book - At a Glance Age Concern England has published a book Using Your Home as Capital for those seeking to raise money from their house. The book covers the main options and is suitably harsh on the ones which have caused investors problems in the past. For example, under the heading 'Investment Bond Income Schemes', the authors write 'These schemes are not recommended under any circumstances' and their advantages are described as 'nil'. Written by Cecil Hinton and David Bookbinder, the book is available for Pounds 4.50 from Dept UHC, Age Concern England, 1268 London Road, London SW16 4ER.
