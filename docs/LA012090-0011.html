January 20, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

STUART MURDER IN BOSTON AND RACISM IN U.S.

Is it any wonder that so many people hold the news media in such low regard? The "black suspect in Boston" episode is another example of how the media might have helped to alleviate tensions, but instead aggravated them.

Responsibility -- 0.

Sensationalism -- 100.

What a sorry display.

JOHN DEGATINA

Los Angeles
