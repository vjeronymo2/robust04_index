February 3, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

The Walt Disney Concert Hall Committee has selected Dworsky Associates of Los Angeles as executive architect to work with designated architect Frank O. Gehry and his Venice, Calif., firm in designing the future home of the Los Angeles Philharmonic on Bunker Hill. Dworsky Associates will prepare working drawings for construction to meet county and building code specifications. Disney Hall is expected to open in the fall of 1992. ALEENE MacMINN
