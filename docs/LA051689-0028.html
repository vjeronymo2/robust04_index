May 16, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

The Juilliard School will award honorary doctorates on Friday at its commencement exercises to conductor Zubin Mehta, director Mike Nichols, cellist Yo-Yo Ma, choreographer Agnes de Mille and philanthropist Avery Fisher. Juilliard alumna Leontyne Price, who received one of the school's first honorary Doctor of Musical Arts degrees in 1987, will deliver the commencement speech. ALEENE MacMINN
