920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Barr & Wallace Arnold down 32% Recession and certain non-recurring charges left pre-tax profits of Barr & Wallace Arnold Trust 32 per cent lower at Pounds 567,000 in the first half of 1992, against Pounds 838,000. The comparable figure was struck after losses of Pounds 395,000 relating to businesses discontinued in 1991. Turnover for this Leeds-based leisure and motor distribution company improved slightly to Pounds 110.5m (Pounds 109.4m). Earnings per share fell to 3p (4.3p) but the interim dividend is held at 3p.
