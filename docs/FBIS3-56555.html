Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The car of Colonel Nurali Sheraliyev, deputy defense minister of the republic, was fire upon by unknown gunmen yesterday evening. Sheraliyev was returning to Dushanbe from the town of Nurek [80km south-east of Dushanbe]. The driver was killed and Colonel Sheraliyev and another person in the car were wounded as a result of the armed attack which took place between the towns of Nurek and Kofarnihon [25km south-east of Dushanbe]. The competent bodies have begun an investigation of the case. This was told to a correspondent of Khovar news agency by the reliable sources.
