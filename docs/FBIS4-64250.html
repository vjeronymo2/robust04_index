BFN [Report signed "dan": "VGOe's Buchner Has Resigned"] [Excerpt] Linz/Vienna -- "I will remain a member as long as the VGOe [United Greens of Austria] exists," [VGOe Chairman] Josef Buchner said, who announced his resignation from all VGOe functions at a press conference in Linz on Friday [29 April]. The reason for his resignation is his bitterness about statements by former party colleagues, who have claimed that the VGOe has shifted to the right under Buchner's leadership. "This is not true," Buchner told DER STANDARD, "I will not let anyone slander me." [passage omitted]
