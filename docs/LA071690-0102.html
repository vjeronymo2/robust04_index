July 16, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BATTLING THE MEDFLY

The California Department of Food and Agriculture will conduct aerial spraying of malathion tonight beginning at 9, weather permitting.

Area: 15 square miles encompassing parts of Arcadia, El Monte, Rosemead, San Gabriel, South El Monte and Temple City; 16 square miles in the Los Angeles area encompassing Atwater, Glassell Park, Mt. Washington and parts of Eagle Rock, Glendale and Highland Park.

Precautions: Stay indoors, keep animals indoors, wash animal dishes and toys left outside, cover cars, keep doors and windows closed.

Information: Toll-free numbers for the agricultural commissioner: (800) 356-2894; (800) 225-1346.
