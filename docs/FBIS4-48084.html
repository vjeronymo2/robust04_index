BFN [Text] Pretoria, 10 May (MENA)-- Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa met today with new South African Foreign Minister Alfred Nzo. During the meeting, the two ministers discussed bilateral relations and ways to develop them. They also discussed South Africa's role and its relations with the OAU and the Nonaligned Movement, which will hold a meeting at the foreign ministers' level in Cairo late this month.
