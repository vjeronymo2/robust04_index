930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / US supported over Bosnian policy Congressional leaders yesterday voiced support for President Bill Clinton's Bosnia policy, Reuter reports from Washington. 'There's no disagreement here among the leadership on either side ..there will be bipartisan support,' Senate Republican leader Bob Dole said after meeting Mr Clinton. House of Representatives' speaker Thomas Foley, the top Democrat in Congress, said there was 'very great determination expressed that we have to see to it that ..we stop the actions that are taking place in Bosnia.'
