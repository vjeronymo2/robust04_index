940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / People: CRH The death of Des Traynor, the chairman of CRH, has prompted a reshuffle at the top of the building materials group, one of Ireland's biggest and most successful companies. Tony Barry, currently chief executive, is to become chairman, while Don Godson becomes chief executive designate. He is currently chief executive of the group's US operations. Barry intends to relinquish his executive role immediately Godson takes up his appointment, no later than the end of this year. Godson, 55, joined CRH in 1968 as general manager development. He was appointed US chief executive in 1978 and joined the CRH board in 1980.
