930417 FT 17 APR 93 / Finance and the Family: Record high for Hang Seng - At a glance The Hong Kong stock market reached a record high this week as traders celebrated the resumption of talks between Britain and China on the colony's future. The Hang Seng index rose nearly 6 per cent on Wednesday to close at 6,789, before falling back slightly on Thursday and Friday. The news also lifted the shares of Hongkong & Shanghai Banking Corporation, owners of the Midland Bank, and a constituent of the FT-SE 100 index.
