BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Lyudmila Aleksandrova, Nikita Yermakov, Yuriy Sizov and Vladimir Shekhovtsov] [Summary] Madrid, 13 Apr -- The official farewell ceremony for President Boris Yeltsin has been held in Madrid's El Pardo palace. The president was seen off by King Juan Carlos. Boris Yeltsin has now left Madrid by plane for Barcelona, where he will meet leaders of the autonomous government of Catalonia and the city authorities and tour Olympic facilities.
