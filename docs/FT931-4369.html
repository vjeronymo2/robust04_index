930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / Observer: Second match When lighting up a gasper after reporting record cigarette profits on National No-Smoking Day, BAT Industries' Sir Patrick Sheehy wasn't raising two fingers at the anti-tobacco lobby. He customarily has a post-meeting fag. But he did point out that the coincidence was not the first of its kind. The company's recent agm had been held on International No-Smoking Day, which the lobbyists had described as 'a PR cock-up'. 'This makes a notable double,' the 62-year-old chairman said unabashed.
