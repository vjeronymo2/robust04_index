BFN [Text] After the latest control assessment in the country's western region, when Havana was declared the winner, Sugar Industry Minister Nelson Torres said the climate is favorable for making a maximum effort in cutting and grinding sugarcane with greater efficiency. He said that Havana, Sancti Spiritus, and Cienfuegos are the provinces with the greatest possibilities for meeting the established plan before 1 May. He also urged all other provinces to set concrete goals for the international labor day. Nelson Torres explained the country's need to complete the planting of 5,000 caballerias of sugarcane this month during the Spring campaign. In the assessment by region, the western region has overpowered the eastern region, while Havana Province reached the best production level and was also one of the best in sugar production, sugarcane planting, and care of plantations.
