930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Fleming Far Eastern Fleming Far Eastern, the investment trust, has negotiated a loan of Dollars 90m (Pounds 58m) from a syndicate of banks. It was arranged by Robert Fleming. The loan, which will replace an existing Dollars 53m loan repayable on September 30, is at a fixed interest rate of 5.69 per cent a year and is repayable on September 30 1998.
