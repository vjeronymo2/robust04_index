May 8, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

COFFEE COMPLAINT

We are certainly a most accommodating people: Not one word of complaint has been heard concerning the obvious collusion of all major coffee manufacturers who now sell us 13 ounces of coffee in the same size can that once held a full pound. The former 48-ounce can now gives us only 39 ounces. Same cost. Sometimes a little higher.

You have to wonder why this sneaky ploy is OK with everyone, right?

Yet, we're being robbed!

HANNAH SAMPSON

Torrance
