940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / Engineering pay rises held at 2% Pay rises in the engineering industry have remained at about 2 per cent, the Engineering Employers Federation says. The average settlement in the three months to February was 2.17 per cent, compared with 2.16 per cent in the three months to January. There were pay freezes at 71 companies and deals of less than 2 per cent at 100 firms. More than 50 firms agreed deals of more than 4 per cent and five gave workers rises of more than 5 per cent.
