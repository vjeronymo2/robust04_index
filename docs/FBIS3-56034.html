Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow January 13 tass -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has decreed that Anatoliy Chubays, chairman of the Russian State Property Managing Committee, will hold his post of a deputy prime minister in the new cabinet, the press service of the Russian president reported on Thursday. Chubays was appointed deputy chairman of the government above the number of four vice-premiers named in Yeltsin's decree of January 10, the press service said.
