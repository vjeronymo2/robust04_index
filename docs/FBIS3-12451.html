Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] At today's session of the judicial authorities, which was chaired by Ayatollah Yazdi, the head of the judiciary, Mr. Yazdi condemned the Zionists' savage attack at the Friday prayers services in al-Khalil. He said: The negligence of the advocates of human rights and international organizations, including the United Nations, in such cases, and the lack of prompt practical deterrent measures cause public opinion to doubt the sincerity of those organizations. He added: This situation is such that there is a need to form a powerful international criminal court, higher than The Hague Court of Justice -- to realize rights, carry out justice, and punish offenders.
