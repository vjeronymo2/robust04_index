Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 25 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to the creating staff of serial TV dramas of the Radio and TV Broadcasting Committee of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The staff has created many serial TV dramas conforming to the intention of the party and the demand of the people. They produced 9 dramas of 80 parts including "Joy" and "The Morning on the Plateau" over the past one year.
