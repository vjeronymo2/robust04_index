Language: Afrikaans Article Type:BFN [Text] Six hundred people armed with automatic weapons, assegais, and knobsticks last night attacked and robbed passengers on a train en route from Pretoria to Johannesburg. The attack took place at Oakmore Station northeast of Johannesburg. Police confiscated at least three AK-47 rifles and four pistols when the train was searched at Birchley Station. No arrests have yet been made.
