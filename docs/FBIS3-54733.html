Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow December 31 -- On Friday [31 December], Russian Federation Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin appointed Yuriy Sizov and Yuriy Subbotin as deputy directors-general of ITAR-TASS, the official information and telegraph agency of Russia. The appointments were made in accordance with President Yeltsin's recent decree on ITAR-TASS.
