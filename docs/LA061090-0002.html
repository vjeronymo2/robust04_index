June 10, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

EARTH FIRST!

I read "The FBI vs. the Monkeywrenchers" and was impressed, as usual, with your excellent journalism. We can't help but admire people who will lay their life on the line, trying, usually in vain, to save what the government allows to be systematically destroyed.

It left me wondering, what did this investigation cost me as a taxpayer? Instead of wasting our tax dollars defending what we all know is wrong, they should consider hiring Dave Foreman as a consultant. He has, as all of us on this overpopulated planet, legitimate fear about its fate.

I think it is about time our government finally listens, and becomes our leader, to make the changes to save our air, water and resources. If they do not listen, eventually there will only be the silence of the "End of Nature."

KAREN BENZEL

Marina del Rey
