940516 FT 16 MAY 94 / Magistrates grant Stoy summons Bow Street magistrates have granted a summons against Mr George Auger, senior insolvency partner at Stoy Hayward, the London accountants, and two others, alleging that they stole 'the whole of the issued share capital in Tradam Trading Company (Bahamas) Ltd and one half of the issued share capital of Whittakers Investments Ltd'. The summons follows a private prosecution by Mr Jeffrey Port on behalf of his three children.
