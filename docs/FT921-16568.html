920102 FT 02 JAN 92 / Greece recognises eight more former Soviet republics GREECE has recognised eight more former Soviet republics, in line with its European Community partners, Reuter reports from Athens. A Foreign Ministry statement said that after the recognition of Russia, Greece would open diplomatic ties with Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belorussia, Kazakhstan, Moldova, Turkmenistan, Ukraine and Uzbekistan. The Vatican announced yesterday it had recognised the independence of Russia and said it would be willing to consider requests for recognition from the other states of the former Soviet Union.
