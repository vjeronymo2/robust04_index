BFN [Text] The Sejm Social Policy and Health Commissions have come out in favor of including contraceptives on the list of medicines for which only 30 percent of the price has to be paid. They have also come out in favor of introducting sex education in schools and of withdrawing handbooks containing inadequate sex information. Today the commissions are discussing implementation of the laws on family planning and conditions permitting abortion. The deputies have adopted the text of recommendations for the prime minister, for which they demand government implementation.
