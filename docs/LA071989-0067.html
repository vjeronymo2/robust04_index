July 19, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

MAN HIT ON HEAD DURING POSSIBLE ROBBERY DIES

An elderly San Diego man who was struck in the head Sunday, during what police say may have been a robbery attempt, died Tuesday, authorities said.

Louis Ladd, 77, of the 2500 block Commercial Street was pronounced dead at 2:10 p.m. at UC San Diego Medical Center, said Deputy Coroner George Dickason.

An autopsy was scheduled for today.

Ladd was struck during a struggle at his home Sunday, said police spokesman Bill Robinson.

Information on the incident was sketchy and police asked witnesses to call 235-TIPS.
