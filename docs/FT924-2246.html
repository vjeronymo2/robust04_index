921214 FT 14 DEC 92 / Construction Contracts: Mixed batch Walsall-based KENDRICK CONSTRUCTION has boosted its activity in the West Midlands by winning nearly Pounds 10m of new work. Heading the list are two contracts for Birmingham City Council, totalling Pounds 6m, to be undertaken by the company's repairs and maintenance division. It is also carrying out a Pounds 1.5m extension and new build project at Colton Hills School for Wolverhampton Metropolitan Borough Council. The design and build division has secured a Pounds 1.5m office block development in Droitwich for the Crown Prosecution Service.
