920603 FT 03 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Perpetual shows recovery to Pounds 2.18m Profits of Perpetual, the investment management and computer services company, rose to Pounds 2.18m pre-tax for the six months to March 31. That compared with Pounds 903,000 for the same period last year when trading was adversely affected by the Gulf war. The figure was struck on turnover of Pounds 121m (Pounds 60.3m). Funds under management increased by 9 per cent to Pounds 753m. Earnings per share rose to 6.14p (2.37p) and the interim dividend is lifted to 1.2p (0.8p). Directors said that with investor confidence 'now improved in the UK' current sales were running at a high level with funds invested in the UK outperforming those in other markets.
