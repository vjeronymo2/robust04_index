921214 FT 14 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Drayton Recovery revenue at Pounds 796,000 Drayton Recovery Trust, which was launched last year, reported net asset value of 77p as at October 31. Net revenue for the period from August 23 to the end of October was Pounds 796,000 for earnings per share of 9.63p. A final dividend of 3.95p is proposed making a total for the period of 7.95p.
