Language: Kinyarwanda Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Kigali Town Security Committee today met under the chairmanship of the prefect of Kigali, Colonel Tharcisse Renzaho. The committee decided to curtail the curfew in the town. Beginning Wednesday [2 March] the curfew will start at 2200 and end at 0500 in the Kigali Town Prefecture. [passage omitted]
