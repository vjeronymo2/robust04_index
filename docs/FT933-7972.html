930818 FT 18 AUG 93 / International Company News: ISS plans issue to fund purchase ISS, Denmark's international cleaning services group, plans to make a share issue in the first half of next year, according to the group's first-half interim report. The issue will help finance the acquisition earlier this year of the US cleaning company, National, from Britain's NuSwift for Dollars 93m. ISS's first-half sales were DKr5.90bn, up from DKr5.58bn last time. Operating profits fell to DKr233m from DKr253m, profits after net financial items to DKr145m from DKr168m and consolidated net profits to DKr88m from DKr121m. Earnings per share fell to DKr3.67 from DKr4.64.
