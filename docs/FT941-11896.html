940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / Nestle fined after Aero worker hurt Nestle, the international food consortium, was fined Pounds 12,000 yesterday after it admitted failing to ensure the safety of a worker who fell into a chocolate mixer. Mr Alex Tuvey-Smith was seriously injured when he fell into a hot peppermint Aero machine he was cleaning at Nestle UK's factory in York, Mr Bill Cook, prosecuting for the Health and Safety Executive, told York magistrates. Nestle UK was also ordered to pay Pounds 1,095 costs.
