920428 FT 28 APR 92 / Levitt case sent to crown court chairman of the collapsed Levitt group - and three other men was yesterday transferred from Bow Street Magistrates Court for trial at Southwark Crown Court. Mr Levitt is charged with 62 offences involving Pounds 20m. The charges include conspiracy to defraud, theft, false accounting, obtaining property and services by deception, making misleading forecasts or statements to induce investment, forgery, furnishing false information and falsifying company documents. Jointly charged with Mr Levitt on certain matters are three other former Levitt group executives: Mr Robert Price, financial director, who faces seven charges, Mr Mark Reed, 28 charges, and Mr Alan McNamara, 19 charges. All four men are on bail pending trial.
