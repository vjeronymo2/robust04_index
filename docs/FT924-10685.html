921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Shiloh behind in worsening conditions Pre-tax profit at Shiloh slumped 47 per cent in the half year to October 3 as trading conditions worsened. The spinning group, which also makes health care products and protective clothing, lifted sales to Pounds 12.3m (Pounds 11.3m) but saw profit fall to Pounds 201,000 (Pounds 380,000). A breakdown of operating profit showed textile spinning contributed Pounds 58,000 (Pounds 106,000), health care and clothing Pounds 169,000 (Pounds 276,000) and packaging Pounds 4,500 (loss under Pounds 1,000). Earnings per share fell to 2.36p (4.48p) and the interim dividend is again 0.875p.
