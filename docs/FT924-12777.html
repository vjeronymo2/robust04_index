921019 FT 19 OCT 92 / Observer: In the dark Ever wondered why some London cabbies seem to drive around without their 'for hire' signs illuminated even when they are looking for fares? A colleague who successfully hailed one such cab, got in, gave his destination, and asked the driver why he hadn't bothered to switch his sign on. 'It is on,' the cabbie replied. 'Trouble is, I spend so much time driving around empty these days that the bleedin' bulb's gone.'
