930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / Maids repatriated from Kuwait About 20 Indian domestic workers alleging misconduct by employers flew home two weeks ago under a Kuwaiti-funded repatriation programme, the embassy said yesterday, Reuter reports from Kuwait. The women had been living at an embassy refuge for several months. Their return home coincided with the repatriation of about 400 Filipino maids who had also accused employers of abuse. The embassy said the main allegations by the Indian maids were non-payment of wages and excessive work.
