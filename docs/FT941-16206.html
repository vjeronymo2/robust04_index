940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Mexican stock prices opened sharply weaker and extended their fall after the weekend saw the Chiapas uprising spread into the Mexico City valley. By the close the IPC index was down 165.92, or 6.32 per cent, to 2,459.11, the biggest one-day percentage fall since October 16, 1989, when the market dropped 8.05 per cent. Brazil, in contrast, saw a further rise of 3.9 per cent after a climb of 7.2 per cent on Friday, the latest gains coming in an extended rally based on politico-economic hopes. The Bovespa index finished 1,899 higher at 49,539.
