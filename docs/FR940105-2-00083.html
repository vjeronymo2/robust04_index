Withdrawal of Notice of Proposed Exemption In the matter of Kalon L. Kelley IRA and Karen R. Kelley IRA (the IRAs), located in Santa Barbara, California [Exemption Application Nos. D&hyph;9167 and D&hyph;9168] In the Federal Register dated December 17, 1993 (58 FR 66031/66032), the Department of Labor (the Department) published a notice of proposed exemption from certain taxes imposed by the Internal Revenue Code of 1986. The notice of proposed exemption concerned the prospective cash sale of certain commercial real property (the Property) by the IRAs to Kalon L. Kelley and Karen R. Kelley (the Applicants), disqualified persons with respect to the IRAs. On December 17, 1993, the applicant informed the Department that it wished to withdraw the notice of proposed exemption. Accordingly, the notice of proposed exemption is hereby withdrawn. Signed at Washington, DC, this 30th day of December, 1993. Ivan Strasfeld, Director, Office of Exemption Determinations, Pension and Welfare Benefits Administration, Department of Labor.
