Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Nairobi, 22 Mar (SUNA) -- The first mutual talks session between the Sudanese Government delegation and the two delegations of rebel movement began here Tuesday [22 March] at the Kenyan Foreign Ministry under the sponsorship of Intergovernmental Authority on Drought and Development (IGADD) committee. Representative of Kenyan Foreign Ministry said that the meetings would be closed. SUNA learned that every delegation will participate with six members in addition to the mediation team which including the four states' foreign ministers of IGADD. [sentence as received] It is to be noted that there is a direction to discuss the relief transportation to the war affected areas as urgent issue before start of discussing of the agenda.
