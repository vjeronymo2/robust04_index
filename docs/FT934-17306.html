931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / UK Company News: FW Thorpe drops 9% to Pounds 1.66m AFTER CONTINUED capital investment in Compact Lighting, pre-tax profits of FW Thorpe, the manufacturer of lighting equipment, fell by 9 per cent, from Pounds 1.83m to Pounds 1.66m, in the year to end-June. Turnover, however, increased from Pounds 14.7m to Pounds 16.1m. At the operating level profits fell to Pounds 1.5m (Pounds 1.62m). The pre-tax outcome was struck after interest receivable of Pounds 251,000 (Pounds 216,000) and an increased share of losses of an associated undertaking amounting to Pounds 57,000 (Pounds 49,000). Earnings per share came out at 8.6p (9.7p) and an improved final of 1.87p (1.7p) raises the total to 2.67p (2.5p).
