BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 23 -- An agreement on cooperation between the Chambers of Commerce and Industry of Vietnam and Laos was signed in Hanoi today. Under the three-year agreement, the two chambers will push up the economic cooperation between the two countries by exchanging visits of commercial and economic delegations, and information in these fields. Signatories were First Vice President of the Vietnam Chamber of Commerce and Industry Doan Ngoc Bon and President of the Laos National Chamber of Commerce and Industry (Xixuc Xisombat).
