January 11, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Corday Exits CBS: CBS Entertainment executive Barbara Corday, former head of Columbia Pictures Television and co-creator of "Cagney & Lacey," will leave CBS. The announcement from the network, which is struggling to get out of the ratings cellar, said simply: "After cordial discussions, CBS and Barbara Corday have decided to end their present contractual relationship." Corday has been in her present job as executive vice president, prime-time programs, since August, 1988. The announcement marked the second major executive change at CBS Entertainment this month. On Jan. 1, Jeff Sagansky replaced Kim LeMasters as president of CBS DANIEL CERONE
