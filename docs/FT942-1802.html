940622 FT 22 JUN 94 / International Company News: Gantry increases stake in target Gantry, the corporate vehicle through which an investment partnership owned by Enron of the US and the California Public Employees Retirement System is making an ADollars 356.7m (USDollars 263.2m) bid for Bridge Oil, is thought to have increased its stake in its target to around 4 per cent. The Gantry bid, of 85 cents a share, now tops a rival offer of 80 cents a share, from Texas-based Parker & Parsley.
