BFN [Text] Moscow May 24 ITAR-TASS -- State Duma Speaker Ivan Rybkin on Tuesday received a Slovak National Council delegation led by Ivan Laluga, chairman of the Foreign Policy Committee, the Duma press service announced. The two sides discussed prospects for an improvement and development of mutually beneficial contacts between Russia and Slovakia in the economic, scientific and cultural fields, pointing out the importance of developing interparliamentary contacts. Laluga conveyed an official invitation from the chairman of the National Council of the Slovak Republic to the State Duma speaker to visit Slovakia at the head of a Russian parliamentary delegation.
