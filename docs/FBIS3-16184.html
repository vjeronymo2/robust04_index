Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Irina Litvinova (Riga) report: "Latvia Can 'Quickly Refute Russia's Unfair Accusations'"] [Text] The founders of the open public "Baltic Information Agency" foundation have decided to open a branch in Russia. According to Gunars Meierovics, Latvian Foreign Ministry minister of state for the affairs of the Baltic and Nordic countries and a Saeima deputy, the creation of this foundation will provide an opportunity to popularize Latvia's opinion on political questions and "refute pretty quickly various unfair accusations on Russia's part."
