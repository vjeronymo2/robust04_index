930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / People: BASF Barry Stickings (above) has been appointed managing director of BASF plc, the UK subsidiary of the German chemical group, and head of BASF north Europe regional division. He replaces Bryan Rigby who retires in June. Stickings was group vice-president industrial organics at BASF Corporation in the US. UK born, he has a chemistry degree from Oxford and joined BASF in Canada in 1971.
