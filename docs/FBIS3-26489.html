Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Report: "Deng Xiaoping Toured Shanghai, and Visited Suzhou and Wuxi"] [Text] At present, CPC senior leader Deng Xiaoping is resting in Shanghai together with his family, and has no intention of leaving Shanghai in the near future. Reportedly, after he arrived in Shanghai late last year, he occasionally went out. In addition to visiting some places in Shanghai, he also made a tour to Suzhou and Wuxi. According to reports by some Shanghai newspapers, on 23 December 1993, Deng Xiaoping inspected Yangpu Bridge in the company of Deng Xiaoping [as published]. [Hong Kong TA KUNG PAO in Chinese 8 February 94 page two carries a similar report which asserts that "on 13 December 1993, accompanied by his beloved daughter Mao Mao, Deng Xiaoping inspected the Yangpu Bridge in Shanghai."] There, he got out of the car and walked on the bridge. He also visited the Bund. On New Year's Day, he came to the New Jinjiang Hotel. Reportedly, Shanghai municipal leaders reported Shanghai's recent development situation to Deng Xiaoping, who expressed satisfaction at Shanghai's developments and changes. Most members of Deng Xiaoping's family are now also in Shanghai, accompanying old Deng. His eldest son Deng Pufang appeared on a public occasion on 4 February. For the last few years, Deng Xiaoping was used to spending the Chinese New Year holiday in Shanghai. It is expected that he will also stay in Shanghai in the coming Chinese New Year.
