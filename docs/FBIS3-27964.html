Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 2 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received Gabonese Ambassador Benjamin Legnongo-Ndumba to Korea today. Present on the occasion was Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam. The ambassador conveyed a personal letter and a gift of Gabonese President El Hadj Omar Bongo to President Kim Il-song. President Kim Il-song expressed thanks for this and conversed with him.
