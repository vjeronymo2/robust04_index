921113 FT 13 NOV 92 / UK Company News: P&S warns of fall in second half PORTSMOUTH & Sunderland Newspapers yesterday reported a 30 per cent rise in first-half profits. However, a warning from the publishing, printing and retailing group about prospects for the second half left the shares 22p lower at 483p. Sales in the 26 weeks to September 26 rose from Pounds 48m to Pounds 52.3m. After reduced interest payable of Pounds 43,000 (Pounds 296,000) pre-tax profits came out at Pounds 3.38m (Pounds 2.6m). In the year to March 28 1992 profits fell to Pounds 4.39m (Pounds 5.28m) after redundancy costs. The interim dividend is raised to 2.84p (2.73p) on earnings per share of 20.2p (14.4p).
