941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Molyneux expands Molyneux Estates is paying Pounds 3.05m for Hayes Gate House, a 76,000 sq ft office building in west London. The acquisition will be funded from existing resources and the proceeds of a cash placing of 2.14m new ordinary shares at 70p apiece. The directors believe the likely sustainable annual rental income of the property will be about Pounds 500,000, increasing Molyneux's net rent receivable to some Pounds 7.6m a year.
