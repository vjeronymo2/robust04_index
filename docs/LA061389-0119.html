June 13, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

S. KOREAN AIRLINE BUYS UP TO 4 BOEING 747-400S

Asiana Airlines of Seoul, South Korea, has ordered two Boeing 747-400s and placed options on two more, the Boeing Co. announced.

Neither Boeing nor Asiana issued the exact cost of the deal, although Boeing said the two firm orders are worth more than $285 million.

The price range for a 747-400 runs $116 million to $136 million, but Boeing spokesman Randy Harrison said the order also includes spare parts and other equipment.
