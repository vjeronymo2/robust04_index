April 21, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO EARNINGS

Cipher Data Products: The manufacturer of computer tape drives, a data storage device, reported a third-quarter profit of $2.9 million on revenue of $51.2 million, up from a profit of $2.5 million on sales of $44.7 million for the same quarter last year. For the first three quarters of the current fiscal year, net income totaled $9.9 million on sales of $157.1 million, up from a profit of $5.4 million on sales last year of $129.5 million.
