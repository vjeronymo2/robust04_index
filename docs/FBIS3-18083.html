Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of Prime Minister Ciller] [Text] Prime Minister Tansu Ciller has sent a message to Palestinian Head of State Yasir 'Arafat expressing her regrets over the Hebron incident. She said she hopes that the incident will not prevent the peace process on which the Palestinian people have pinned their hopes. In her message, Ciller expressed the belief that at this critical stage of the peace talks, the regrettable incident will not undermine the progress achieved so far. Ciller extended her condolences to the families of those killed in the incident, and a speedy recovery to the wounded. Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin also sent a message to 'Arafat strongly condemning the incident. I hope, he said, this was an isolated incident. Cetin expressed the hope that the reactions to the incident will not increase the tension in the area.
