BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 31 (KCNA) -- A national conference of party cell secretaries has opened at the Pyongyang indoor stadium today. It is the first conference of this kind in the history of the Workers' Party of Korea (WPK). It is a significant conference proving the validity and vitality of the WPK's chuche-oriented line of party building, a conference of loyalty and single-hearted unity demonstrating the revolutionary faith and unshakable will of the members of the WPK to accomplish the cause of socialism, closely united around the party and the leader. A portrait of the great leader President Kim Il-song is placed on the platform of the meeting hall against the background of the flag of the WPK. The conference is attended by party cell secretaries and party officials in the capital and local areas, more than 10,000 in all. Present there are O Chin-u, member of the Presidium of the Politburo of the C.C. [Central Committee], the WPK; Kang Song-san, Yi Chong-ok, Pak Song-chol, Kim Yong-chu and other Politburo members and alternate Politburo members, secretaries of the C.C., the WPK and leading party officials in the capital and provinces. Kye Ung-tae, Politburo member and secretary of the C.C., the WPK, conveyed a letter of congratulations sent by President Kim Il-song to the delegates to the conference. Secretary of the C.C., the WPK Kim Ki-nam delivered a report at the conference. Then, a congratulatory group of the Korean children's union entered the meeting hall and read its congratulatory letter to the conference. Speeches were made. The conference continues.
