Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Romero Piriz Ballon, special representative of the UN secretary general in Tajikistan affairs, met Foreign Minister Velayati today and handed him a written message from the UN secretary general for Hashemi-Rafsanjani. Ballon said: The UN secretary general has asked me to covey his appreciation and thanks to your government for the Islamic Republic of Iran's constructive efforts and the positive and worthy role in resolving the Tajikistan crisis. In response, Velayati said: The Islamic Republic of Iran has always followed Tajikistan issues with concern, and we have always believed that a resolution of the Tajik crisis is only possible through negotiation and peaceful means. The Islamic Republic of Iran will support any move which guarantees honorable peace and tranquillity and the rights of all interested parties.
