930115 FT 15 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Administrator considers sale of Macmillan Price Waterhouse, administrators to the Maxwell Communication Corporation plans to push ahead with investigating the possibility of a private sale of Macmillan, the US publisher, writes Raymond Snoddy. Last year Price Waterhouse said it planned to sell the non-publishing interests of MCC including the Official Airline Guides. The process of selling OAG is now into the second round. Those interested include Reed Elsevier, the international publishing group. Although a sale of Macmillan was never ruled out Price Waterhouse had previously suggested that a flotation towards the end of next year was more likely. Mr Mark Homan, senior joint administrator said yesterday it had always been intended to consider a sale of Macmillan 'within the framework of a potential public offering'. 'In recent weeks both we and our US advisers JP Morgan have been inundated with expressions of interest in Macmillan and in Official Airline Guides,' Mr Homan said.
