920512 FT 12 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Low & Bonar makes Dutch purchase Low & Bonar, the Dundee-based packaging company, is paying Fl 36.6m (Pounds 11.1m) cash for Koninklijke Tufton (Royal Tufton), a specialist floorcovering company based in the Netherlands. Royal Tufton reported 1991 pre-tax profits of Fl 5.5m on turnover of Fl 55.1m. Net assets at the end of the year were valued at Fl 15.4m.
