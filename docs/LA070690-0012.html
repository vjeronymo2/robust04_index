July 6, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

THE INJUSTICE OF TRUMP'S NEW BUDGET

Re "Los Angeles on $450,000? No Way, (July 3)": There can never be peace in this favored land until there is justice. Tell me if you can where is the justice in Donald Trump having $450,000 to spend every single month when an army of children cry themselves to sleep every night because it hurts to be hungry.

GLADYS FOREMAN

Los Angeles
