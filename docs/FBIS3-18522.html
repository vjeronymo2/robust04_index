Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Cologne (DDP/ADN) -- IG Metall and the metalworkers employers have agreed to top level talks on 4 March concerning the pay dispute in the west German metal industry, according to Gesamtmetall [employers' organization]. DDP/ADN learned this from Dieter Kirchner, the main business manager of Gesamtmetall, in Cologne on 3 March. The meeting will take place in Hanover, late in the afternoon of 4 March.
