BFN [Text] Urumqi, April 25 (XINHUA) -- Australian Governor-General Bill Hayden concluded his five-day visit to the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region here today and left here by special plane this morning for Xiamen. Hayden told reporters at the airport that his visit to Xinjiang enhanced his understanding of the great achievement of China's economic development. Accompanied by chairman of the Chinese Government reception commission and Vice-Minister in Charge of the State Economic and Trade Commission Yu Xiaosong, Hayden and his party arrived here on April 21 from Beijing. Hayden and his party visited Urumqi, Kashgar and Turpan and gained more knowledge about Xinjiang's economic development, folklore, and the development of cooperative projects between Australia and Xinjiang. The Australian guests said that their visit to Xinjiang has left a deep impression on their minds.
