920619 FT 19 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Davenport Vernon moves ahead 47% Davenport Vernon, the Buckinghamshire-based multi-franchised motor group, lifted pre-tax profits by 47 per cent from Pounds 547,000 to Pounds 805,000 in the six months to March 31. Turnover rose from Pounds 48.9m to Pounds 50.6m and there was a 15 per cent improvement in operating profits to Pounds 1.29m (Pounds 1.12m). In addition, interest charges fell to Pounds 480,000 (Pounds 577,000). The interim dividend is held at 1.5p, payable from earnings ahead to 4.1p (2.8p) per share.
