Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi, Chamber of Deputies speaker Nabih Birri, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, and Foreign Minister Faris Buwyaz arrived at the B'abda Palace 10 minutes ago, following the conclusion of the Syrian-Lebanese summit meeting in Damascus. The men returned in al-Hariri's car, which he drove himself. A meeting began in the B'abda Palace at 1705 between President al-Hirawi, al-Hariri, and Buwayz to assess the summit. Speaker Birri has returned to his home.
