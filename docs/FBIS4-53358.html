BFN [YONHAP report from Beijing] [Text] Choe Han-chun, councilor of the North Korean Embassy in Beijing, stated on 16 May that there is no reason North Korea should receive U.S. sanctions, even if North Korea has begun changing its nuclear fuel rods without the presence of the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA]. The North Korean councilor said in a news conference held in Beijing that IAEA inspectors could have arrived in time to witness the replacement work as they had enough advance notice.
