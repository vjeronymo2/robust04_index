921208 FT 08 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Acquisition gives boost to Cranswick Cranswick, the USM-quoted pork products and animal feeds company, lifted interim pre-tax profits by 47 per cent, from Pounds 659,000 to Pounds 968,000, in the six months to September 26. Turnover was 31 per cent higher at Pounds 49.7m and the company said the second half of the year had started well. The improved performance was mainly due to the acquisition last November of Yorkshire Country Pork. Sales of pork products jumped by Pounds 12.5m to Pounds 17m during the period. Earnings increased to 6.4p (6.3p) per share. The interim dividend is 2.4p (2.3p).
