Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "24 Hours" Newscast] [Text] Cuauhtemoc Cardenas, presidential candidate for the Party of the Democratic Revolution [PRD], visited Nuevo Laredo, Tamaulipas, where he met with supporters of the political groups that back his candidacy. Cardenas Solorzano said emphatically that he would not object to having Manuel Camacho Solis run on the PRD ticket for the assembly seat for the district where he lives in the Federal District. [Begin Cardenas recording] I would not object to his being a PRD candidate for a seat in the Assembly. [end recording]
