(1) Liquified petroleum gas-fuel used for exhaust and evaporative emission testing and in service accumulation shall be commercially available liquefied petroleum gas-fuel. (ii) The Administrator shall determine the liquefied petroleum gas-fuel to be used for testing and service accumulation.
