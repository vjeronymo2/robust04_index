940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Schroder Split Fund Schroder Split Fund had a net asset value per capital share of 74.97p at July 31 compared with 80.93p a year earlier. Net available revenue for the six months to end July slipped to Pounds 2.21m (Pounds 2.24m) for earnings of 3.91p (3.95p) per income share. The interim dividend is lifted to 3.6p (3.375p).
