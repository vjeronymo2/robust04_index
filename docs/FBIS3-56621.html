Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] At about 7:30, January 16, following an intensive artillery preparation, the Azerbaijani troops assumed an offensive in two directions -- the Matagis-Akop and Kamari section in the Mardakert Region, and the Askeran-Agdam direction, informed sources in Stepanakert told Interfax. By 10:00, the Azerbaijani attacks were rebuffed by Karabakh forces in the Northern direction. One Azerbaijani tank was destroyed and 30 Azerbaijani soldiers were killed. The Azerbaijani army had to retreat to the earlier assumed positions. By midday, heavy fighting continued at the eastern limits of Nagorno-Karabakh. Both sides are sustaining losses. Local fighting continues in the other sections of the front. The Azerbaijani Defense Ministry announced, meanwhile, that on Sunday morning, well-equipped Armenian units assumed an offensive in the Fizuli Region, moving towards the Iranian border. Attacks are being made on the villages of Goradiz and Derdchinar. During fierce combat operations the Azerbaijani army rebuffed the Armenian attack, throwing Armenian units back to the initial positions. A source in the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry announced that two Armenian tanks, one armored vehicle and 1 artillery piece had been destroyed. Armenians suffered great losses. The same source reports, that in December and January, more than a thousand Armenian soldiers were killed or wounded.
