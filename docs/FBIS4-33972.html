BFN [From the "Early Morning" newscast] [Text] After learning of the Zapatist National Liberation Army's rejection of the peace proposals presented by the Mexican Government, the Presidency of the republic issued the following communique: In line with the will of the Mexican Government to safeguard peace and to obtain the economic and political development of the region that is in crisis, President Carlos Salinas de Gortari has ordered the secretaries of National Defense, Government, Social Development, Communication and Transport, and Health to continue rigorously complying with programs previously decided upon. The communique adds: The president has ordered the National Defense secretary to maintain the cease-fire unilaterally declared on 12 January to continue patrol and relief operations along the country's southern border, and to conduct the necessary operations to prevent the smuggling of weapons into the region. The president also ordered the commissioner for peace to report to the people about these specific decisions and about the government's decision to continue promoting peace through a political resolution of the conflict.
