Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] A forced mobilization of Croats is being carried out in Zenica, the Croatian Defense Council's Zenica information service has reported. The Croats forcibly mobilized by the Muslim army are being sent to the front lines and forced to shoot at their dearest ones -- fathers, brothers, and sons. Recalcitrant Croats and those refusing to be mobilized are being arrested in large numbers. The building housing the Croatian cultural center, which is a symbol of Croatian struggle, has been turned into a Muslim forces' military building. Before that it was a frequent target of Muslim fire. The Croatian population of Zenica is quietly resisting all forms of imposed Muslim authority.
