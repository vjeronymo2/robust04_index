Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] This evening, at his house in Barbus, Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri received a Hizballah delegation of Hizballah Secretary General Hasan Nasrallah; the head of the Hizballah Political Bureau, Shaykh Husayn Khalil; and Wafiq Safa'. The meeting was attended by Housing and Cooperative Minister Mahmud Abu-Hamdan and Amal Movement Political Bureau member Ahmad al- Ba'labakki. The discussion dealt with developments in general and the Israeli escalation in the south.
