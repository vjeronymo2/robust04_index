BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anatoliy Yurkin] [Text] Moscow June 27 TASS -- An officer was killed and two soldiers wounded in an overnight attack on the guarding unit of a tank regiment deployed in the Russian Baltic enclave of Kaliningrad. TASS learned from the press service of the Russian Ground forces on Monday that the officer was in command of the guards and that the two soldiers were only slightly wounded. However, yet another guard disappeared from his post. Investigation is underway and representatives of the main headquarters of the ground forces have left for the regiment.
