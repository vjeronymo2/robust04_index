920905 FT 05 SEP 92 / Zhivkov jailed for 7 years Former Bulgarian communist dictator Todor Zhivkov, pictured outside Sofia's courthouse yesterday, where he was sentenced to seven years in jail after a 18 month trial, Reuter reports from Sofia. Mr Zhivkov, 80, was convicted for embezzling Lv21.5m (then Dollars 24m) from public funds to buy 67 western cars and 72 palatial apartments for relatives and friends. He said he had been convicted on trumped-up charges and that history would be his true judge. Mr Zhivkov still faces charges of sponsoring terrorism, discriminating against Bulgarian ethnic Turk community, setting up Soviet-style labour camps and ruining the country's economy.
