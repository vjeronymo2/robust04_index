940702 FT 02 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Inveresk Inveresk, the Scottish speciality paper manufacturer which floated on the Stock Exchange last June, reported a pre-tax profit of Pounds 4.5m on turnover of Pounds 47.9m for the six months to June 4. The outcome compared with a profit of Pounds 4.53m on turnover of Pounds 46.9m last time. Earnings per share were reduced from 8.6p to 6p, reflecting the increased number of shares following the flotation and a tax charge of 30 per cent against 9.5 per cent previously. An interim dividend of 1.84p is declared.
