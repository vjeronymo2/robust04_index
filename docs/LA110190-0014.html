November 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MEGAMOUTH SHARK

Anyone who has ever desired the existence of sea monsters (and a keen desire that is) must have rejoiced over the picture of the megamouth shark that ran on Oct. 23. The appearance of this fantastic creature conveys the hopeful news that our weary old world still has a few mysteries up its sleeve. I am considerably bucked up.

P.B. MILLER

Los Angeles
