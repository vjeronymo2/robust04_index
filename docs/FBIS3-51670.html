Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Briefs" column] [Text] Guerrillas presumed to be members of the FARC [Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia] on 17 January on the outskirts of Villavicencio kidnapped two U.S. citizens who were with an evangelical mission, apparently in reprisal for the presence of U.S. troops in Colombia. According to the authorities, the subversives entered La Esperanza estate in the capital of Meta, subdued its residents, and after identifying each of them kidnapped Americans Timothy Bay and Stevel Welth.
