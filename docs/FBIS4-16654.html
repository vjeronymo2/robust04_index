BFN [Fedor Yemchenko report under the "Such News" rubric: "FITUR: Criticism from Left and Right"] [Text] An all-Russia conference of trade union committee chairmen has been held at the FITUR [Federation of Independent Trade Unions of Russia]. Those who are directly responsible for resolving the socioeconomic problems of labor collectives gathered for the representative forum. Analyzing the working people's present situation, the trade union leaders attempted to formulate a unified strategy to protect the working people's interests. All the speakers emphasized that this can be achieved only by pooling the efforts of all trade union organizations. At the conference there was criticism of the FITUR leadership and accusations of its excessive politeness [loyalnost] in relations with power structures. Representatives of Working Moscow set up their picket outside the FITUR building yesterday. The pickets accuse the FITUR leadership of infringing the working people's interests with the General Agreement between the government, employers, and trade unions, which they signed recently.
