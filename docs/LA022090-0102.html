February 20, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

CYPRESS ROUTS MIRACOSTA, 26-8

Cypress College scored 10 runs in the fourth inning and seven in the fifth, routing MiraCosta, 26-8, in a nonconference baseball game Monday at Cypress.

Doug McConathy went four-for-five with three doubles and five runs batted in, and Mike Scott and Jason Bates each had four RBIs for Cypress (10-3).
