BFN [TA SR report: "Romanies Jointly"] [Text] Kosice--An agreement on joint entry into the elections was signed in Kosice yesterday by representatives of the five largest Romany parties and movements from east and central Slovakia. In concluding the agreement, the chairmen of the Romany Congress, the Romany Civic Initiative, the Union of the Romany Civic Initiative, the Romany Party of Social Democracy, and the Party of Integration of Romanies proceeded from their parties' failure in the last elections, which was caused by their own fragmentation. There are at present eight political parties and 12 cultural associations of Romanies in Slovakia associating almost 90 percent of the 520,000 Romanies, most of whom live in eastern Slovakia. According to the parties to the agreement, they also contemplate joining an electoral coalition but have not yet decided on a partner.
