BFN [Text] INTERFAX reports, citing Baku television, that Azerbaijan President Heydar Aliyev has visited areas adjacent to the Karabakh conflict zone. In the Beylaganskiy [Beylagan] area, he held a meeting with commanders of Azerbaijan army units. Aliyev pointed out that it is the sacred duty of every serviceman to liberate territories which have been seized. The report does not say when Heydar Aliyev visited the frontline rayons. However, reports on an offensive by Azerbaijani troops came in from the conflict zone on Sunday.
