July 21, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Eldorado Bancorp: The parent company of Tustin-based Eldorado Bank reported second-quarter income of $912,000, 10% higher than the $826,000 posted for the year-earlier quarter. For the first six months of 1990, net income increased 6% to $1.9 million from $1.8 million. Total assets rose 15% to $291.3 million from $253.5 million.
