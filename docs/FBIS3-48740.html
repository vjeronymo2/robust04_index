Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] Concerted efforts are being made to produce 800 million baskets of paddy annually to realize the major objective of the state government in developing the national economy. Double cropping and cultivation of summer paddy are being carried out to achieve this objective. Ten members of the Karen armed organization in the jungle, who do not wish to see the economic progress of the nation and the farmers, mined and destroyed three Chinese-made Dinzin Brand 400 horsepower water pumps being used for cultivation of summer paddy in Mogaung village tract, Thaton Township, after midnight on 24 January. Our military columns are pursuing the members of the armed Karen organization who mined and destroyed the water pumps.
