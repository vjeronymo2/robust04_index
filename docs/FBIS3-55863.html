Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the military and radio intelligence of the Nagorno-Karabakh army, Azerbaijanis send units composed mostly of citizens of various CIS states as well as Turkey and Afghanistan to the areas of the fiercest fighting. A resident of the Russian city of Vladivostok, now prisoner-of-war Oleg Pilipenko, said that the Azerbaijani authorities arrest Russians who temporarily reside on the territory of Azerbaijan and force them into enrolling to the Azerbaijani army by beating. According to sources in Stepanakert, Pilipenko recently appealed to the Russian authorities to help him and other Russians who were made to serve in the Azerbaijani army. Another POW, resident of the Turkish town of Sivac Abil Umah, stated that a group of officers of the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry was touring Turkey at the moment drafting Turkish jobless into the national army. Meanwhile, Azerbaijani President Geydar Aliyev recently said Baku had irrefutable evidence of mercenaries' participation in the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict on the Armenian side.
