BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Albert Kochetkov] [Text] Tbilisi, 9 Jun -- The so-called Abkhazian Liberation Union [ALU] is protesting against the introduction of Russian peacekeeping forces in Abkhazia, and is demanding that the Moscow agreements on this issue be cancelled. The ALU is threatening mass protest actions. This is according to a petition issued by the Union, which was handed over to the Russian Embassy in Tbilisi 9 June. The document also says that "members of the ALU will lie down on railway tracks in order to stop trains carrying occupation troops entering the conflict zone."
