Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow March 30 TASS -- President of Kazakhstan Nursultan Nazarbayev completed his first official visit to Russia on Wednesday. An official farewell ceremony was held in St. George Hall of the Kremlin. Nazarbayev and Russian President Boris Yeltsin thanked their delegations for the work.
