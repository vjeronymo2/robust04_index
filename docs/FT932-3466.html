930614 FT 14 JUN 93 / International Company News: Sharp decline to Pounds 0.22m for A Cohen A reduction in pre-tax profits from Pounds 783,000 to Pounds 215,000 was announced by A Cohen for 1992. Turnover for the company, which makes non-ferrous metal ingots and is involved in the reclamation and trading of recyclable material, declined to Pounds 78.4m (Pounds 83.7m). The company blamed recession in all its markets for the result and the large imbalance between demand for and supply of aluminium. The deficit after tax was Pounds 512,000 (Pounds 343,000), equivalent to losses per share of 31.68p (21.2p). The proposed final dividend is maintained, however, at 3.4p, giving a reduced total of 6.8p (10p).
