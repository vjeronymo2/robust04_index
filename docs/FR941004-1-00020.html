Proposed Regulations For the reasons set out in the preamble, the Coast Guard proposes to amend Part 117 of Title 33, Code of Federal Regulations, as follows: PART 117_DRAWBRIDGE OPERATION REGULATIONS 1. The authority citation for Part 117 continues to read as follows: Authority: 33 U.S.C. 499; 49 CFR 1.46; 33 CFR 1.05&hyph;1(g). 2. Section 117.459 is revised to read as follows: 117.459 Kelso Bayou. The draw of the S27 bridge mile 0.7 at Hackerry, shall open on signal from 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. and open on 4 hours notice from 7 p.m. to 7 a.m. from about May 25 (the beginning of shrimp season as set by the state yearly), until October 31. Then from November 1 through December 22, the draw shall open on signal from 7 a.m. to 3 p.m. and from 3 p.m. to 7 a.m. the draw shall open on 4 hours notice. From December 22 until about May 25 the draw shall open on 24 hours notice. Dated: September 20, 1994. R.C. North, Rear Admiral, U.S. Coast Guard, Commander, Eight Coast Guard District.
