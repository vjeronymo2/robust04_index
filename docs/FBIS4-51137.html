BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 23 KYODO -- A passenger car is the most desirable product among young Beijing consumers, according to a recent private survey. The survey, conducted in January by Hori and Co., a Tokyo-based business consulting firm, also showed that more than 30 percent of the respondents preferred white in color. The survey covered 1,000 residents of Beijing aged 16-30, nearly 80 percent of them white-collar workers. About half of the pollees had a monthly income equivalent to about 10,400 yen to 14,300 yen. But a car costs the equivalent of between 650,000 yen and 2.6 million yen. Next to a car, pollees said they wanted a condominium and a camcorder, according to the survey.
