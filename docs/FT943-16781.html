940702 FT 02 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Partridge Fine Arts Partridge Fine Arts, the antique dealer, reported a 50 per cent increase in the pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.05m to Pounds 1.56m in the six months to April 30. The outcome reflected improved trading and a Pounds 225,000 insurance policy surplus. Turnover was Pounds 6.67m (Pounds 5.85m). Earnings per share came out at 5.09p (3.22p). An interim dividend of 1.4p (1.2p) is declared.
