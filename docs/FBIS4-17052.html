BFN [Text] Riga, Apr 19, BNS -- The Latvian National Independence Movement faction claims that its members Alexander Kirsteins and Juris Sinka are not responsible for the initialed agreements on Russian troop withdrawal. Sinka and Kirsteins were absent from a government sitting in which the text of the package was approved, Kirsteins said. The two MPs are members of the Latvian delegation for talks with Russia.
