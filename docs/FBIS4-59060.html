BFN [Text] Riyadh, 10 May -- In implementation of the directives of the servant of the two holy places, King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, supreme commander of the Armed Forces, within the context of his support for the Palestinian cause, issued his noble order to a group of Royal Saudi Air Force planes to help in the operation of transporting the Palestinian police force to al-'Arish in Egypt. A group of C-130 airplanes belonging to the Saudi Royal Air Force have this morning transported the second batch of the Palestinian police force to al-'Arish in preparation for assuming their responsibilities in the Gaza Strip and the Jericho area in the West Bank. This participation falls within the interest of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia's Government in supporting the Palestinian cause and Palestinian people.
