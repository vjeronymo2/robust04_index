BFN [Text] Daugavpils, Latvia, Apr 08, BNS -- The leaders of the east Latvian city Daugavpils took over the authority of the Daugavpils fortress from the Russian servicemen Thursday [7 April]. The fortress housed an aviation institute that was sent Russia last year. [sentence as received] Until recently, a 150-men unit was stationed in the historic buildings. The municipality plans to locate there a Home Guard unit, a faculty of the Daugavpils Pedagogical University and a tuberculosis hospital. However, the final resolution on the fate of the fortress rests with the Latvian Government.
