910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Static second half for Laser-Scan Laser-Scan Holdings, an electronic data processing equipment and computer services company, reported profits of Pounds 769,000 for 1990, against losses of Pounds 1.3m. Most of the profit was earned in the first half. The second half was affected by a moratorium on orders by the Ministry of Defence and the economic downturn. The comparative figure included significant losses associated with discontinued businesses. Operating profit for 1990 was Pounds 828,000, compared Pounds 854,000 from continuing businesses last time. Turnover was Pounds 7.34m (Pounds 11.81m). Earnings per share amounted to 4.7p (losses 11.4p).
