940805 FT 05 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Emerging Markets Trust seeks Pounds 24m The Emerging Markets Country Investment Trust aims to raise Pounds 24m in a conversion share issue. The placing, made on August 1, closes on or before September 6. City of London, the investment manager, said the placing was aimed at clients of professional investors and institutional investors. Expenses of the issue will be capped at 3.75 per cent. Trading in shares of the investment trust, which was created through the conversion of units in the emerging markets unit trust run by City of London Investment management, started last week.
