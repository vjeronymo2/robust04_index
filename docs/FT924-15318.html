921005 FT 05 OCT 92 / Construction Contracts: Power plant at Tooting hospital Wandsworth Health Authority has awarded a Pounds 3.2m contract for a combined heat and power plant at St George's Hospital, Tooting to MOWLEM ENGINEERING of Bromborough, Wirral and Peterlee, County Durham, a division of John Mowlem Construction. The turnkey contract is for the design, procurement, installation and commissioning of a 4.4 MWe European Gas Turbine Typhoon, a 12 tonnes per hour unfired heat recovery steam boiler and gas compressor, together with all building and civil engineering work. The existing boiler house will be modified to accommodate the boiler and electrical switchgear and a new gas turbine hall will also accommodate, at first floor level, a new control room as an extension to the boiler plant control room.
