Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 21/1/1994 -- 9 out of 32 members of Moldovan Government were temporarily dismissed for the period of electoral campaign according to the presidential decree, stated to BASA Ion Sofronie, government press secretary. Here is the list of people on temporary dismissal: Andrei Sangheli, Moldovan premier (candidate of Moldovan Agrarian Democratic Party-PDAM); Nicolae Oleinic, vice premier (PDAM); Valentin Cunev, vice premier (PDAM); Vitalie Gorincioi, minister of agriculture and food (PDAM); Gheorghe Ghidirim, minister of health (Democratic Party of Labour); Petru Sandulache, minister of youth, sport and tourism (Reform Party); Visarion Cesuev, head of State Department for Privatization (Social-Democratic Party); Ion Ciocanu, head of Department of Languages (National Christian Party). Ion Ungureanu, minister of culture, also requested temporary dismissal in case of his registration as an independent candidate. Sofronie added that Premier Sangheli could execute his power in exceptional situations. His last execution of power was due to the emergency state decreed in the Dniester region.
