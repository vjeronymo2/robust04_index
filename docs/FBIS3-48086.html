Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 17 (KCNA) -- Foreign media gave publicity to the New Year address of the great leader President Kim Il-song. It was carried by papers NHAN DAN, QUAN DOI NHAN DAN and HANOI MOI of Vietnam, YANBIAN DAILY of China, CAMBODGE of Cambodia, BEOPAR and NIDA-E WAQT of Pakistan, BASY VAVA of Madagascar, MONITOR of Ethiopia, HOROYA of Guinea, AURORE of Mali, THE REPUBLIC, SUNDAY CHAMPION and CONCORD of Nigeria, THE DAILY TOPIC, the NEW VISION, THE MONITOR, and UGANDA EYOGERA of Uganda, AL-SHARQ and AL-LIWAU of Lebanon, LA NATION and ETINCELLE of Zaire, PEOPLE'S VOICE OF ZIMBABWE, MFANYAKAZI of Tanzania, MAY 22 of Yemen, AL MAOQIF of Tunisia, NOTICIAS DA AMADORA of Portugal and EL MATUTINO of Peru. AKP News Agency and Radio Voice of FUNCINPEC [National United Front for an Independent, Neutral, Peaceful and Cooperative Cambodia Party] of Cambodia, TV of Laos, Koryo-language radio of Kazakhstan, AMP News Agency of Madagascar, The News Agency of Guinea, Radio People of Denmark and radios of Nicaragua, too, reported the New Year address. They said that President Kim Il-song in his New Year address referred to the achievements made in the socialist construction last year and to the tasks for this year and clarified the principle and way for reunifying Korea, the way for a solution of the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula and DPRK's foreign policy.
