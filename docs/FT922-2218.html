920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Dundee & London assets decline Net asset value at Dundee and London Investment Trust declined to 262p at April 30 1992. That compared with 277p six months' earlier and with 282p at April 30 1991. For the half year ended April 30 1992 pre-tax revenue came to Pounds 1.08m (Pounds 1.3m). The interim dividend is again 3.8p.
