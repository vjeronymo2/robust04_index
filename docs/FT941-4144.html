940312 FT 12 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Birkby wins 92% of In Shops The recommended offer by Birkby for In Shops, a listed retail space management company, has been accepted in respect of about 92 per cent of the ordinary equity. Valid acceptances received totalled 47.13m shares. The figure included 9.98m shares which were the subject of irrevocable undertakings to accept the offer. The offer has been declared unconditional in all respects and remains open for acceptance until further notice.
