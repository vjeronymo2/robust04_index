February 28, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

The Laguna Art Museum will host a coffee reception for prospective docent volunteers Tuesday at 9:30 a.m. in the museum's California Gallery to provide details of docent training and responsibilities. Docents attend monthly general meetings and guide adult and/or children's tours two or three times a month through the museum's main site at 307 Cliff Drive in Laguna Beach and through its satellite at South Coast Plaza. Information: call the curator of education at (714) 494-8971.
