Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The project of laying 318 km of gas pipelines from Esfahan to Yazd has been completed. According to a report from our correspondent, this pipeline comprises two separate sections, which start from Kuhpayeh, and after crossing the regions of Na'in, Haqda, Ardakan, Meybod, Ashk-e Zar, and Zarch reaches the city of Yazd. The Housing Development of the Oppressed and the War Disabled Foundation of the Islamic revolution completed this project under the supervision of the Iranian National Gas Company at the cost of 8 billion rials and 20 million dollars in hard currency.
