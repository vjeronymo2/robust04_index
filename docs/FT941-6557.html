940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Wm Ransom & Son William Ransom & Son, the pharmaceutical products maker, said the dispute concerning costs relating to the proposed acquisition of a property in Biggleswade, Bedfordshire, had been settled and it had received Pounds 325,000 including all costs and expenses. Trading in the fourth quarter continued to be patchy and the company expected pre-tax profits for the year to be between Pounds 900,000 and Pounds 1m, including the above settlement. The shares fell 1p to 63p.
