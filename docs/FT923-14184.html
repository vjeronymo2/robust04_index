920709 FT 09 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Elswick expects to pay interim dividend Elswick, the print and packaging concern, said that following the disposal of its lossmaking cycles division, it had been successful in its court application for consent to utilise the share premium account in order to eliminate the deficit on its profit and loss account. Mr Harold Cross, chairman, therefore reaffirmed his statement with the last annual report that an interim dividend for the current year would be paid in January 1993. The last final dividend was omitted leaving a total of 0.22p (0.6p) for the 12 months to January 31 1992. Pre-tax profits for that year were static at Pounds 2.25m (Pounds 2.22m).
