930402 FT 02 APR 93 / International Company News: Electrolux in Hungary deal ELECTROLUX, the Swedish white goods manufacturer, is to invest a further Ft1bn (Dollars 11m) over the next two years in Lehel, the company's Hungarian refrigerator manufacturer. Mr Leif Johansson, Electrolux group president, said the decision followed a rise in productivity at Lehel and an increase in its share of the Hungarian market.
