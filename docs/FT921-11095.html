920203 FT 03 FEB 92 / Construction Contracts: Kettering village WHITE YOUNG has been appointed by Balfour Beatty Building as civil and structural consultants on the Pounds 11.1m Kettering leisure village project. The 12,350 sq metre complex will provide a leisure pool, bowling facilities, gymnasium, fitness centre, disco, wine bar and multi-purpose hall. The structure will consist of about 350 tonnes of structural steel including 40 metre span lattice girders.
