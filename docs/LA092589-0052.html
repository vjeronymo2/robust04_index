September 25, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

HOUSE VOTES TO PROTECT FLAG

My heart sank when I heard it is now illegal to "desecrate" the flag. Who is to say what is desecration? How can it possibly be distinguished from freedom of expression?

Our elected representatives championed this simplistic issue to improve their popularity ratings. Unfortunately, doing what's popular is more important to them than doing what's right. And if outlawing flag-burning will improve their ratings, that's what matters. Not whether our basic rights are at stake.

Expressing dissatisfaction with government is what our country was founded upon. Our lawmakers have now removed that right from us, and we have supported it.

SUSAN HOHENHAUSEN

Long Beach
