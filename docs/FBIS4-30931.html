CSO [Unattributed report: "Constitutional Proposal Changes"] [Text] The Macedonian Council on Ethnic Relations held a session, discussing amendments to the Constitution, proposed by its member Bosko Despotic, representative of the Serbs and Montenegrins in Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia]. The Coordinating Committee of the Association of Serbs and Montenegrins in Macedonia submitted a request for changes in the constitution, to state Serbs, not mentioning Montenegrins, along with other minorities living in Macedonia. The second amendment proposes equal participation of Serbs in the work of the Ethnic Council. The Council is to decide on the proposed amendments during one of its next sessions.
