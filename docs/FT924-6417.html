921121 FT 21 NOV 92 / German attacks on foreigners rise Violent attacks on foreigners living in Germany are rising significantly, with most incidents committed in western Germany and by young people, according to a report by the Interior Ministry of Lower Saxony. Between January 1 and November 8, right-wing extremists used violence against 1,760 people, compared to 1,483 over the same period last year. Eleven people have been killed this year.
