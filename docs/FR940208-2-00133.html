The Department of Treasury has submitted the following public information collection requirement(s) to OMB for review and clearance under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980, Public Law 96&hyph;511. Copies of the submission(s) may be obtained by calling the Treasury Bureau Clearance Officer listed.
