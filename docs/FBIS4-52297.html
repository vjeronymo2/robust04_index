BFN [Text] Vientiane, May 5 (KPL) -- On May 10, 1994 at airport, a U.S. airlift of humanitarian supplies will be handed over to Lao officials and representatives of the World Vision for distribution in the Lao PDR [People's Democratic Republic], according to a USIS press release issued here yesterday. The equipment will be presented by U.S. Ambassador to the Lao PDR Victor L. Tomseth The medical supplies are donated by the World Vision Relief and Development for its operations in Laos. The school equipment is a donation from the U.S. Military. The International Lao-American Organization donated the books. This shipment is another example of assistance supplied from the United States to meet the humanitarian needs of the Lao people.
