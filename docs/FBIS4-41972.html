BFN [Text] The prime minister, Mr. P.V. Narasimha Rao, arrived in Moscow a short while ago. He was received by at the airport by the Russian prime minister, Mr. Chernomyrdin, and a host of other prominent Russian leaders, including the minister of state for foreign affairs and the Russia ambassador to India. The Indian ambassador, Mr. R. Sen, and other members of the mission were also present. Our special correspondent, I.J. Behal, reports from Moscow that the prime minister was presented a guard of honor by the contingents of the three services. The prime minister will be accorded a ceremonial welcome tomorrow morning at St. George's Hall at the Kremlin, which will be followed by talks with President Yeltsin.
