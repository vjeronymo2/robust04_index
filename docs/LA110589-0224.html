November 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NICARAGUA ENDS CONTRA TRUCE

Come now, President Bush! You can't have it both ways.

Either you are a law-and-order President, or you are not.

You cannot condemn crime and criminals in the United States and condone crime by the Contras in Nicaragua.

And if you pose as a democrat, you cannot bully, threaten, and breach U.S. obligations as a member of the United Nations by interfering in the internal affairs of another nation.

LEO NEALON

Huntington Beach
