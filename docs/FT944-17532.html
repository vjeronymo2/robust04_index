941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Ellis & Everard Ellis & Everard, the Bradford-based chemicals distributor, has achieved sales of Pounds 200m in the first five months of the current year. This compared with Pounds 176m at the same time last year and Pounds 183.5m in the year to April 30 1994. Mr Jonathan Wood, chairman, told the annual meeting that sales made in the UK and Ireland rose 17 per cent to Pounds 87.8m and in the US a 12 per cent rise made Pounds 112.7m. Large price increases were beginning to come through in the chemicals which Ellis distributes, although many were still below the levels of three years ago. There was particularly good progress in the US.
