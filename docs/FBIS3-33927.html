Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By Da'ud Kuttab] [Tex] Jerusalem -- AL-QUDS has learned from Palestinian sources in Tunis that Faruq Qaddumi, head of the PLO Political Department, has signed a letter of appointment designating Hasan 'Abd-al-Rahman as the PLO's envoy in Washington. According to the appointment, 'Abd-al-Rahman will attend to diplomatic affairs with the U.S. State Department and foreign embassies, while Anis al-Barghuthi will continue working with the Palestinian community and organizations in the United States.
