BFN [Text] Tianjin, May 28 (XINHUA) -- Companies from the Republic of Korea and Hong Kong and 325 enterprises from 26 provinces and municipalities within China are taking part in the 1994 Tianjin export commodities fair, which opened here today and will last for a week. Commodities on show at the fair, the largest annual local export fair in northern China, cover more than a dozen ranges, including grain and oil, local produce and livestock products, light industry products, cultural and sports products, textile products, and chemical and pharmaceutical products. Officials from the organizing committee said that some 3,000 overseas businessmen are expected to come to the fair in the coming few days. The officials said that the trade volume at the fair is expected to top 400 million U.S. dollars. Talks on Sino-foreign cooperation or joint venture projects will also be held. The fair, which was started five years ago, has been jointly organized by a dozen provinces in north-west, south-west and northern China.
