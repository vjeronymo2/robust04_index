BFN [From the "This Morning" newscast] [Text] After signing an important agreement in Brussels the week of 28 March, Nicaragua withdrew its banana suits against the EU regarding the purchasing quotas and tariffs imposed by the EU on banana imports from Latin America. The agreement will benefit Nicaraguan banana exports. According to reports from Belgium, Nicaragua, Costa Rica, Honduras, and Panama agreed not to begin any disputes within the framework of GATT against the 12 European banana buyers until 31 December 2002. The agreement contemplates an increase in the banana purchasing quotas from 2 million tons a year to 2.2 million tons for 1995.
