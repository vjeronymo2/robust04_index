March 9, 1989, Thursday, Valley Edition

MASTERS FALLS, 72-47, IN NATIONAL TOURNAMENT

The Master's College basketball team trailed Christian Heritage by four points at intermission but was outscored by 21 in the second half and lost, 72-47, in the first round of the National Christian College Athletic Assn. tournament at Grossmont College in El Cajon on Wednesday.

Tom Bruner scored 13 points but was the only player to reach double figures for The Master's (15-15).

Brad Soucie paced Christian Heritage (24-8), also of El Cajon, with 23 points.
