November 25, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

CONGRESS VOTES ITSELF A HEFTY PAY RAISE

Once again the Congress has taken up the issue of a pay raise for its members. This time they have the pay raise linked to ethics reform. Wouldn't it be nice to have elected representatives who do not feel that doing the ethical thing requires a reward.

I will not vote for any member who votes himself a raise or sets up a way for members to get a raise without them having to vote on the raise. Many people in government deserve and should get a raise, but Congress is not among the group. Let's put people in office who deserve our trust.

WILLIAM V. FERRARO

Seal Beach
