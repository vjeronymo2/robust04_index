Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 18 KYODO -- Japanese food manufacturer Ajinomoto Co. on Friday [18 February] announced a decision to use imported rice in its frozen foods such as pilaff. Sushi chain operator Kyotaru Co. also said it is experimenting with imported rice for use in its takeout and restaurant food. Nichiro Corp. said it will start selling frozen pilaff based on Thai rice next month. Nippon Suisan Kaisha Ltd. also plans to use imported rice in its frozen food.
