930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Sedgwick acquires Medisure Sedgwick Group is to expand its operations via the acquisition of 94.9 per cent of Medisure Marketing and Management, a supplier of private health insurance. Following completion of the deal an offer will be made to minority shareholders. The acquisition will be financed by the issue of 6.87m shares, of which 6.23m are being placed. A further 4.43m shares will be issued to buy out minorities and raise about Pounds 6m for general resources.
