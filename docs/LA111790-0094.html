November 17, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP;

8-MAN DIVISION

Faith Baptist 44, Brentwood 20 -- Sophomore running back Alex Estrada scored five touchdowns to lead Faith Baptist (9-1) to a semifinals victory at Brentwood (6-3). Estrada scored on runs of 22, seven and four yards, a 25-yard pass and a 76-yard kickoff return. He rushed for 97 yards in 14 carries.
