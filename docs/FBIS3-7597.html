Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Naha, March 30 KYODO -- The Okinawa Prefectural Assembly on Wednesday [30 March] approved the appointment of Mitsuko Tomon to be vice governor of Japan's southernmost prefecture. Tomon, 51, succeeds Hiroko Sho, 61, also a woman. She is the fourth female vice governor in Japan, officials said.
