Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Ankara, Feb 21 (AA) -- Premier Tansu Ciller said on Monday [21 February] that she was pleased with the result reached in Sarajevo. Ciller said the result was reached by diplomatic ways urging that the same initiations should be taken on the other six regions like Sarajevo. `Turkey will always support such initiations,' she added. Premier called the improvements `positive,' saying Serbia has removed 86 percent of their heavy weapons. Ciller recalled that the U.S. President Bill Clinton called her on the phone three days ago and told her of the U.S. determination on the issue. [passage omitted]
