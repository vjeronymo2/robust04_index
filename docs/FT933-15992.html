930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Natl Express buys in Europe National Express Group, the scheduled coach operator, is acquiring Eurolines Nederland for Fl 4m (Pounds 1.43m) cash. Eurolines Nederland is one of the principal companies in the pan-European grouping of international coach services and a partner of Eurolines (UK), National Express's second largest subsidiary. The Dutch company reported profits before tax of Fl 294,000 for the 1992 year. Net assets at the end of the year amounted to Fl 545,000.
