Language: Estonian Article Type:BFN [Text] Juri Kahn, ambassador of the Republic of Estonia to Moscow, has been charged with the task of protesting in connection with the continued Russian campaign of statements which fabricate and distort reality in the Republic of Estonia. This was reported to BNS [Baltic News Service] today by the Estonian Foreign Ministry's press service. The ministry's press representative, Mari-Ann Rikken, said that Juri Kahn is due to present Estonia's protest to Russian officials in the course of the day. Repeated attempts by top Russian officials to stick slanderous labels to Estonia have been directed at influencing the world public and international organizations. These are attempts to isolate Estonia [words indistinct] The news item states that Estonian legislation allows every individual to acquire citizenship regardless of their ethnic origin. Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev stated yesterday that about one third of the population in Estonia and in other Baltic countries is being segregated along the ethnic principle.
