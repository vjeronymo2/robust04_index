BFN [Text] The minister of education, Professor Sibusiso Bengu, says black people will remain an underclass in the economy unless his department intervenes decisively to improve their educational qualifications. With this in mind, he said he had initiated a series of processes to bring about the restructuring of the education system. Prof. Bhengu said the directors general and other managers in the old system would remain in charge of their departments until the new system was in place.
