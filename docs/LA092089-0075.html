September 20, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT

David Nuell, the executive producer of "Entertainment Tonight," will become senior executive producer of NBC's "Today" show in January. Marty Ryan, the current executive producer of "Today," will report to Nuell. This summer, Dick Ebersol, president of NBC Sports, was given the additional responsibility of being the NBC News executive in charge of "Today." Nuell, who will report to Ebersol, also will develop a program for NBC Sports as part of the network's coverage of the 1992 Olympics in Barcelona. In addition, he will also develop a daytime series for NBC entertainment. Nuell joined "ET" in 1986 and is credited with helping return the show to the top of the syndicated ratings. Before "ET," he worked for 20 years at local NBC stations. ALEENE MacMINN
