920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / Ceasefire in Abkhazia The leaders of Russia and Georgia yesterday agreed a ceasefire in a conflict sweeping the secessionist Georgian region of Abkhazia and persuaded the reluctant Abkhazian leader to sign the document. Russian president Boris Yeltsin and Mr Eduard Shevardnadze, his Georgian counterpart, also agreed to set up a peace-keeping force in which Russian troops would take part.
