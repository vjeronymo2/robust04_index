BFN [Excerpts] Cajnice, 18 Apr (TANJUG) -- The command of the Herzegovina Corps [of the Srpska army] announced this morning that, following yesterday's cease-fire on the Gorazde battlefield, Serb forces stopped advancing toward Gorazde. However, following a brief lull overnight, the Muslims in Gorazde started opening sniper and antiaircraft fire on the Serb lines, and the fire is being stepped up. The command of the Serb forces issued sharp warnings that if the fire does not stop immediately it will respond with all available weapons. [passage omitted] After they captured Biserna hill above Gorazde yesterday, the Herzegovina Corps' fighters liberated the whole of the right bank of the River Drina. The last Muslim soldiers fled yesterday afternoon across bridges in Gorazde to the left bank. The Serb forces' frontline is currently in the town itself, on the right bank of the River Drina. Serb units yesterday advanced considerably from the direction of Kopaci, north of Gorazde, and reached the town's suburbs. A considerable chunk of territory on the left bank of the Drina was also liberated yesterday. The areas of Jelah, Konjbaba, and Sjedokosa are now under Serb control. In addition to the central town area, the Muslims control the area on the left bank of the River Drina, 7 or 8 kms toward Foca.
