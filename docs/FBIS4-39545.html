BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan June 9 TASS -- Russian Defence Minister Pavel Grachev, who arrived in Armenia on Wednesday, is visiting today the Russian troops deployed in the republic. Grachev is familiarising himself with the course of military training, supplies to the troops and their daily life. The Russian minister told ITAR-TASS that he intended to meet officers, sergeants and soldiers to discuss priorities. On Wednesday, Grachev and Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan discussed the deployment of Russian military bases in Armenia. According to an agreement signed by the two countries' leaders, a Russian motor-rifle division has been deployed in Giumri and a regiment has been based near Yerevan. Grachev was satisfied with the level of cooperation with the Armenian Defence Ministry. He called the interaction between the Armenian national army and the Russian troops deployed in Armenia "the best possible". The Russian minister is scheduled to meet the leadership of the Armenian Defence Ministry later today.
