930703 FT 03 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Tamaris returns to black in second half Tamaris, the nursing home operator, returned to profits in the second half of the year to the end of March. But it was insufficient to overcome the first half loss leaving full year pre-tax losses at Pounds 50,000, against Pounds 955,000. Turnover advanced from Pounds 2.8m to Pounds 3.38m. Losses per share came out at 1.45p (6.55p) or 4.57p (0.21p) fully diluted.
