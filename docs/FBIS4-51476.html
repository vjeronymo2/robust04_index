BFN [Text] Beijing, May 27 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin said here today that the development of China's petro-chemical industry, one of the country's pillar industries, had bright prospects. He said this while meeting with Lucio Noto, chairman and chief executive officer of the Mobil Corporation of the United States. Jiang spoke highly of the economic and technological cooperation Mobil had had with China. Noto said that Mobil was preparing to expand its business in China. It is the first visit to China by Noto since he became head of the U.S. oil giant last March.
