April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NATION IN BRIEF;

TEXAS;

GREYHOUND REPORTS MORE BUS SHOOTINGS

Two more Greyhound buses were shot at, the company said, bringing to 32 the shooting attacks since March 2, when 6,300 drivers and about 3,000 office and maintenance workers struck the firm.

There were no injuries in the latest incidents. A bullet ricocheted off a San Antonio-bound bus at a Dallas area freeway interchange. A few hours later a gunshot struck a bus en route from Birmingham, Ala., to Nashville, said Greyhound spokesman Kevin Fry.
