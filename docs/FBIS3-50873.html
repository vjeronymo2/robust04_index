Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Caracas, Jan 7 (AFP) -- A Caracas radio station has reported that a helicopter of the militarized Venezuelan National Guard was downed in the Cutufi area near the Arauca River, close to the border with Colombia, after an attack by Colombian guerrillas. Reports by Radio Caracas indicate that according to police sources in the area, a helicopter that was patrolling the border area was downed by a Colombian rebel commando. The crash had initially been attributed to technical problems. Two military policemen were injured. Colombian rebels attacks, which have resulted in five deaths and the destruction of several military vehicles, have created friction between Bogota and Caracas over the past few months.
