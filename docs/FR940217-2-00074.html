Notice of Submission of Proposed Information Collection to OMB Proposal: Application for Fiscal Year 1993 Family Investment Centers (FR&hyph;3398). Office: Public and Indian Housing. Description of the Need for the Information and Its Proposed Use: The information collection is required in connection with the issuance of a Notice of Funding Availability which announces funding for Family Investment Centers. Under the program, grants will be provided to public housing agencies and Indian housing authorities to assist families living in public housing with better access to education and job opportunities to achieve self-sufficiency and independence. Form Number: None. Respondents: State or Local Governments. Frequency of Submission: Recordkeeping and one-time. Reporting Burden:
