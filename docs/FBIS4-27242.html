BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 30 -- A Vietnamese delegation led by Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam is attending the 11th conference of the foreign ministers of the non-aligned countries to be held in Cairo (Egypt) from May 31 to June 3. The conference will review the implementation of the decision of the 10th non-aligned summit conference held in Jakarta (Indonesia) on September 1992, and discuss orientation of the Non-aligned Movement's activities in the coming period, in preparation for the 11th non-aligned summit to be held in 1995.
