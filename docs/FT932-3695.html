930612 FT 12 JUN 93 / Finance and the Family: Where to find private client brokers -At a glance Those seeking a private client stockbroker can obtain a list from the Association of Private Client Investment Managers and Stockbrokers by writing to: APCIMS, 5th floor, 20 Dysart Street, London EC2A 2BX. The list is also available to those calling 0891-335521, a line which is charged at 36p per minute, or 48p at other times.
