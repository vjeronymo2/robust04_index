940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Bowthorpe expands with Pounds 4.4m buy Bowthorpe, the electrical and electronic components group, has purchased the business and certain net assets of Bohlin Instruments from Metric (Netherlands) for Pounds 4.4m cash. Bohlin's products are used to measure flow and deformation of materials such as paint and plastic.
