July 11, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

More Love Letters: More starry names have been announced for the changing casts of "Love Letters" at the Canon Theatre in Beverly Hills. Following this week's duo of Michael Tucker and Jill Eikenberry will be Patrick Cassidy and Dinah Manoff, July 17-22; Andrew McCarthy and Molly Ringwald, July 23-29, and Kate Jackson and Charles Kimbrough, July 31-Aug. 5. GREG BRAXTON and BETH KLEID
