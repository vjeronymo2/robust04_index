BFN [Text] London, May 10 (XINHUA) -- Britain has appointed veteran diplomat Len Appleyard as its next ambassador to China, the Foreign Office announced today. Appleyard, who replaces the retiring Sir Robin McLaren, will take over his post in September, the Foreign Office said in a statement. The 55-year-old Appleyard, who is a Mandarin speaker, served as a second secretary in the British Embassy in Beijing in the early 1970's. He also worked as a counselor in Paris and as ambassador to Hungary from 1986 to 1989 before working in London as political director in the Foreign Office.
