Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Israel Sarmiento, chairman of the Venezuelan National Sports Institute, has reported that a group of Cuban experts will help prepare the Venezuelan delegation to the 12th Panamerican Games that will be held in Mar del Plata. Sarmiento and Conrado Martinez Corona, head of INDER [National Institute for Sports, Physical Education, and Recreation], have renewed an exchange and cooperation protocol that will be in effect during 1994. Venezuela will receive a total of 95 Cuban sports advisers who will work at the Venezuelan sports institute and in the states of Falcon, Carabobo, Lara, Cojedes, Anzuategui, Merida, Guarico, Apure, Trujillo, and Zulia. Sarmiento told the press that the services of these coaches will also be extended to Aragua, Monagas, Bolivar, Barinas, Tachira, and (Yara). [passage omitted]
