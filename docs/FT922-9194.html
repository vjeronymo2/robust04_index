920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / Breakaway union Mr STEVE TURNER, former general secretary of the National Union of Journalists, plans to form a breakaway organisation after being dismissed by his own executive, it was disclosed last night. Mr Turner will call the new union the British Association of Journalists. He was elected general secretary for a five-year term by the union in 1990, but was dismissed last July after disagreements with executive members.
