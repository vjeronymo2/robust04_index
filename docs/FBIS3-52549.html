Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The ministerial economic group held a meeting today under Prime Minister Dr. 'Atif Sidqi during which it continued to assess the second stage of the economic reform program and the steps for reforming the financial structure of the business sector companies by expanding the ownership base by selling some company shares to the people and employees. Dr. Yusuf Butrus Ghali, minister of state for international cooperation, stated that the review showed that the rate of inflation has decreased to less than 10 percent, that the exchange rate of foreign currencies has remained stable, and that the budget deficit has not increased beyond the target limit. He added that the review of the balance of payments showed that the foreign currency reserves held by the Egyptian Central Bank have increased to $16 billion. The ministerial economic group also discussed a number of measures to increase the rates of development and investments.
