920908 FT 08 SEP 92 / Sudan relief airlift halted Rebels gunner have forced the United Nations to halt a relief airlift to the besieged south Sudan city of Juba, where some 300,000 people are short of food, Reuter reports from Kampala. It is the third time in two weeks the flights have been halted by rebel fire. A World Food Programme official, Mr Ben Martinson, said they would not resume until both sides in the eight-year civil war guaranteed their safety.
