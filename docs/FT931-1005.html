930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / Pounds 3.1m grant for rail freighting THE DEPARTMENT of Transport yesterday gave one of its biggest grants yet to a freight carrier to encourage it to use rail instead of road for carrying goods through environmentally sensitive areas. Tilcon, a construction company owned by the BTR industrial conglomerate, will get Pounds 3.1m to defray the cost of using rail to carry 500,000 tonnes of limestone a year from a quarry in the Yorkshire Dales to Hull and Leeds.
