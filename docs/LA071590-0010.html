July 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

MIXED MEDIA;

*** "LIVE ON BROADWAY" BARRY MANILOW 6 WEST VIDEOCASSETTE ($19.98): ITEMS IN THIS SURVEY OF POP-RELATED VIDEOS, BOOKS AND LASER DISCS ARE RATED ON A SCALE OF ONE STAR (POOR) TO FIVE (A CLASSIC).

Manilow appeals mainly to the straight-laced, over-35 crowd that prefers ballads brimming with melodrama -- the chief ingredient in Manilow staples like "Mandy" and "I Write the Songs."

That audience should love this swiftly-paced, well-produced, 90-minute tape of Manilow's Broadway show -- with the songs structured around key points in his life and career. Though mainly a solo show, Manilow uses supporting performers for several numbers. Never an exceptional singer, he makes maximum use of his strengths -- earnestness, an amiable personality and a knack for making his audience feel warm and comfortable. DENNIS HUNT
