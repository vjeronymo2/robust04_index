BFN [Summary] [no dateline as received] The Central Electoral Commission has reported on the results of the voting in another three oblasts -- Volyn, Sumy, and Khmelnitskiy. According to the results, Leonid Kravchuk received 40.87 percent and Leonid Kuchma 23.83 percent of the vote. Results from Volyn Oblast: Valeriy Babych, 1.83 percent; Leonid Kravchuk, 70.65 percent; Leonid Kuchma, 5.63 percent; Volodymyr Lanovyy, 10.94 percent; Oleksandr Moroz, 7.72 percent; Ivan Plyushch, 1.33 percent; and Petro Talanchuk, 0.6 percent. Results from Sumy oblast: Valeriy Babych, 2.51 percent; Leonid Kravchuk, 23.04 percent; Leonid Kuchma, 30.29 percent; Volodymyr Lanovyy, 11.5 percent; Oleksandr Moroz, 24.95 percent; Ivan Plyusch, 1.73 percent; and Petro Talanchuk, 0.67 percent. Results from Khmelnitskiy Oblast: Valeriy Babych, 1.8 percent; Leonid Kravchuk, 40 percent; Leonid Kuchma, 15.6 percent; Volodymyr Lanovyy, 11.5 percent; Oleksandr Moroz, 23.5 percent; Ivan Plyushch, 1.9 percent; and Petro Talanchuk, 1.7 percent.
