Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Everything continues to be simple in the plans of Vladimir Zhirinovskiy. At a news conference today he stated that no one can or should interfere in the affairs of the Transcaucasus or Central Asia to prevent a new Lebanon. The leader of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia said that he planned to occupy the position of Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, and as his first step in the ministerial post intended to invite Tokyo to sign a peace treaty. If it refuses Zhirinovskiy promises to use the Russian Navy to blockade all the Japanese islands.
