October 29, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

It's Only Rock 'n' Roll: Saying there's no evidence rock music leads youngsters to sex and drugs, New York Education Commissioner Thomas Sobol on Friday rejected a parent's plea to remove rock posters from a junior high school classroom. Sobol said the parent who brought the complaint failed to show that any of the students who use Room 109 have been adversely affected. CLAUDIA PUIG
