BFN [Excerpts] Foreign Minister Husayn Sulayman Abu-Salih reviewed the problems facing the African continent at the ministry's regular forum yesterday, stressing the necessity of establishing a peace fund to strengthen the mechanism for settling African problems. [passage omitted] On the situation in the south of the country, the foreign minister confirmed that the government adheres to the principle of negotiation to achieve the peace that it seeks, despite the advances made by the Armed Forces in the fighting arenas. Dr. Abu-Salih confirmed that the sabotage conspiracy case of the dissolved Ummah Party, which included criminal operations directed at the security and stability of the country, is now before the judiciary, where an investigation is being carried out on the role of the intelligence elements of the foreign state concerned.
