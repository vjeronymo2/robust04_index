Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg Jan 20 SAPA -- African National Congress [ANC] leader Nelson Mandela on Thursday [20 January] called for the end of township rent and service payment boycotts. Speaking at the local government summit at the World Trade Centre outside Johannesburg, he said this step should be coupled with an immediate start in the improvement and development of local facilities, especially for poor black communities. "We support the principle that people should pay for the services they receive," he said. His words were received with applause from the 2,000 delegates at the summit.
