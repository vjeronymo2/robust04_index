August 10, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

CUTTING WATER USE AND DWP LEAKS

We in Los Angeles have just survived July, the hottest month in recorded history.

Our lawns and plants are dying of thirst. Yet, despite these obstacles, we nearly achieved our 10% water conservation goal, missing by only two-tenths of one percent (9.8%).

Still the Department of Water and Power and Mayor Tom Bradley feel the need of brandishing the club of mandatory rationing (Part B, Aug. 3). Come on, give us a break!

JERRY COWLE

Pacific Palisades
