This rule also updates the list of military and police entities. This rule adds the company Denel (Pty) Ltd. and certain of its subsidiaries to the list of South African military and police entities, and removes Musgrave, a former subsidiary of Armscor, from the list. Denel was formed from several former Armscor subsidiaries, and produces a variety of products for the South African military as well as the civilian population. Finally, this rule removes remaining restrictions on exports to Walvis Bay. On February 28, 1994, South Africa returned Walvis Bay to Namibia. Rulemaking Requirements 1.
