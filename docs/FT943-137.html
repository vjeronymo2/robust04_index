940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / UK Company News: MTL Instruments MTL Instruments Group, USM-quoted maker of intrinsic safety equipment, saw pre-tax profits advance 15 per cent from Pounds 2.1m to Pounds 2.42m in the first half of 1994. Turnover climbed 31 per cent from Pounds 10.7m to Pounds 14m. Excluding Transition Technology which was acquired in the second half of last year, sales were up 23 per cent. Earnings per share increased to 8.6p (7.4p) and the interim dividend has been raised to 1.9p (1.7p).
