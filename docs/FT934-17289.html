931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / UK Company News: F&C US Smaller Foreign & Colonial US Smaller Companies lifted net asset value by 2.2 per cent over the first six months of its life. The figure at June 30 was 98.6p per share, or with warrants exercised, 98.9p, against an initial value of 96.5p. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 244,000, equivalent to earnings of 0.49p per share.
