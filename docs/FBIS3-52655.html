Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman -- AL-'AHD -- A high-ranking Palestinian source in Amman has said that Iraqi-Palestinian relations are not in good shape because of Iraq's position on the peace process, which opposes the stance of the PLO. It has been learned that these relations became strained recently after Saddam Husayn offered to let Palestinian troops in Iraq join Iraqi troops, something which angered Yasir 'Arafat and prompted him to delay the move of Palestinian troops from Iraq to Jericho.
