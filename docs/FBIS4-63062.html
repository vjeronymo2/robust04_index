BFN [Text] A conference of experts ended in the CIS headquarters in Minsk Wednesday. The meeting completed the development of the constituent documents of a new standing CIS body, Economic Union Commission, under the CIS Coordinating and Consultative Committee. According to the documents, the functions of the new body include developing documents for setting up a mechanism for the creation of a single economic space and analyzing the progress of the economic development of the CIS states with the purpose of shaping up coordinated economic and social policies. The Economic Union Commission is also expected to develop measures to support CIS interstate structures. It is planned for the commission's headquarters to be located in Minsk. If the CIS member-states decide to set up the commission, this will require an amendment in the CIS Charter which does not envision such a structure.
