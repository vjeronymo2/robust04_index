BFN [Text] The Iraqi Broadcasting Corporation, a body affiliated with the Iraqi National Congress, which is composed of organizations opposing the Iraqi regime, has said that Al-Hajj 'Abd-al-Latif al-Hajj Mahmud Bunniyah was killed in an accident planned against his car while he was traveling to Jordan. The corporation said that Bunniyah, who is from one of the most important merchant families in Iraq, had recently criticized Baghdad's financial policy which led to the deterioration of the Iraqi dinar.
