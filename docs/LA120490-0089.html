December 4, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: DANCE

ABT Dancers' New Contract: American Ballet Theatre dancers are expected in the rehearsal studio today following an agreement reached midnight Friday after an intense 10-hour negotiating session. Dancers won a $2 increase per day, but will double up in hotel rooms for two foreign tours. For domestic tours -- such as the Orange County Performing Arts Center in March -- dancers will get individual rooms. CLAUDIA PUIG
