930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Johnson Matthey sees improvement There were 'signs of improvement' is some of the most important markets served by Johnson Matthey, the world's biggest platinum group metals marketing group, reported Mr David Davies, chairman, at the annual meeting. In particular, recovery in the US had benefited North American sales by the catalytic systems and materials technology divisions. The recent improvement in platinum, palladium and gold prices had largely offset a further fall in the price of rhodium, Mr Davies pointed out. He reported that more than 90 per cent of shareholders had accepted an enhanced scrip alternative to the final dividend which would save the group Pounds 3.8m of advance corporation tax and also benefit shareholders.
