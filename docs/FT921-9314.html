920212 FT 12 FEB 92 / International Capital Markets: UOB sets up venture arm SINGAPORE's United Overseas Bank (UOB) has set up a venture capital company to invest in expanding companies in Singapore and the Asia Pacific region, Reuter reports. The company, UOB Venture Investments, with an authorised capital of SDollars 1m (USDollars 617m), said it would seek to achieve medium to long-term capital appreciation through investments in emerging growth companies in the region. Singapore's venture capital funds totalled SDollars 2.07bn at the end of November, up from SDollars 1.25bn at the end of 1990, the state Economic Development Board said in December.
