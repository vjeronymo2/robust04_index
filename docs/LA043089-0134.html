April 30, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

INTEGRATED COMPUTER TAKES LEASE IN PLAZA LA REINA

Integrated Computer Systems has leased about 34,000 square feet of office space at Plaza La Reina, 6053 W. Century Blvd., for a consideration in excess of $3.4 million. The West Los Angeles office of Julien J. Studley Inc. represented Borstein Enterprises and Metropolitan Life Insurance, the owners, in the transaction, while Coldwell Banker represented Integrated Computer Systems.
