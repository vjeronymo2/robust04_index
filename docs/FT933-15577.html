930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / Estate agents warn on recovery THE National Association of Estate Agents yesterday called for an immediate interest rate cut of at least 1 percentage point. Its monthly market trends survey of 200 members showed a drop in the level of inquiries, viewings and offers last month. Mr David Goldsworthy, president, said: 'There are increasing warning signs that cracks could be starting to appear in the UK's economic recovery.'
