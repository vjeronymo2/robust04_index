SMALL BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION Little Rock District Advisory Council; Public Meeting The U.S. Small Business Administration Little Rock District Advisory Council will hold a public meeting at 9 a.m. on Monday, April 25, 1994, at the U.S. Small Business Administration, 2120 Riverfront Drive, suite 100, Little Rock, Arkansas, to discuss such matters as may be presented by members, staff of the U.S. Small Business Administration, or others present. For further information, write or call Joseph T. Foglia, District Director, U.S. Small Business Administration, 2120 Riverfront Drive, suite 100, Little Rock, Arkansas 72202, (501) 324&hyph;5871.
