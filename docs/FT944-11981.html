941103 FT 03 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Broadgate Inv Trust Net asset value per share at Broadgate Investment Trust fell from 123.62p to 119.73p over the 12 months to September 30. The fully diluted figures showed a fall from 119.68p to 116.44p. Net revenue for the year was Pounds 89,574, compared with Pounds 108,040 for the previous 14 months from its listing. Earnings per share were 1.79p (2.16p) and a single final dividend of 1.6p (1.8p) is proposed.
