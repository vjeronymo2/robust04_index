940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Acquisitions boost London Industrial Acquisitions helped London Industrial, the property investment company, report pre-tax profits for the three months to June 30 more than doubled at Pounds 485,200, against Pounds 197,100. Turnover increased from Pounds 1.55m to Pounds 2.18m with rental income contributing Pounds 1.74m, against Pounds 1.29m. Earnings per share came out at 4.7p (3.4p).
