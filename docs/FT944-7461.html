941124 FT 24 NOV 94 / UK Company News: L&G trust launch will raise minimum Pounds 29.8m Legal & General Group is inviting offers for up to 150m shares at 100p each, with warrants attached on a 1-for-5 basis, in its new investment trust, the Legal & General Recovery Investment Trust. The minimum investment is Pounds 1,000 with management charges of 3/4 per cent. Panmure Gordon, sponsor to the offer, has received irrevocable undertakings for up to 41.3m shares. With 31.2m shares having been underwritten the offer will raise, net of expenses, a minimum Pounds 29.8m and a maximum Pounds 143.4m. The warrants can be exercised for 100p within 10 years. The trust has a life of not more than 10 years. The aim of the trust is to achieve long-term capital growth and outperform the FT-SE-A All-Share Index by investing in UK-quoted equities with recovery potential. It is being managed by the same team as the Legal & General UK Recovery Unit Trust.
