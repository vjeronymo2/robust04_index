930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / UK Company News: JIB Group advances to Pounds 11.7m JIB GROUP, the insurance broker, yesterday announced a 9 per cent increase in pre-tax profits to Pounds 11.7m for the first six months of 1993. Earnings per share were up 12 per cent to 6.5p (5.8p). The interim dividend is unchanged at 2.5p. Turnover from insurance broking increased by 19 per cent to Pounds 100.6m ,with about half the increase attributable to the strength of the US dollar in the period compared to last year. Income from the Lloyd's members' agency fell to Pounds 800,000 (Pounds 1.3m). Investment income was Pounds 8.4m (Pounds 9.5m). Mr John Barton, chief executive, said that JIB had set aside Pounds 250,000 to meet costs arising from litigation involving its Lloyd's members' agency.
