BFN [Text] The censure motion against the Vacaroiu government, which was officially handed in last night to the Chamber of Deputies and the Senate will be presented on Monday [27 June] during the joint session of the two chambers of parliament scheduled to begin at 1930. The debates on the censure motion are to take place on Thursday, 30 June, also during the joint session of the Chamber of Deputies and the senate, report our correspondents.
