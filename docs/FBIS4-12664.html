BFN [Text] In Kashmir valley, one Pak-trained militant belonging to Hezbul Mojahedin was killed and seven others arrested in Uri sector by the Army yesterday evening. A defense spokesman said an Army patrol party, seeing a trip wire being cut, [as heard] immediately cordoned off the area and found that some militants were hiding there. The troops asked them to surrender, but the militants fired at them. The fire was returned, killing one militant. A huge cache of arms and ammunition was also recovered. These include three AK rifles, three pistols, and 3,800 rounds of ammunition. In Jammu and Kashmir, the police have arrested a militant responsible for four incidents of firing and bomb blast in Jammu city. The militant identified as Harvinder Singh was employed as a constable in the special service bureau. The police have also arrested a top criminal, who was responsible for 18 highway robberies in Jammu and Udhampur Districts.
