May 17, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Orion Pictures Corp.: The New York-based movie company announced net income of $15.1 million for the year ended Feb. 28, an increase of 8% from net income of $13.9 million the year before. Revenue was $485.2 million, up 3.5% from $468.9 million in the preceding year. The company didn't elaborate on the results.
