BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 7 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet gave a banquet here this evening in honour of Swedish Prime Minister Carl Bildt and his party. The two prime ministers delivered speeches and raised toasts to the further development and consolidation of the friendship and mutually beneficial cooperation between Vietnam and Sweden. This afternoon, a ceremony was held to exchange documents on the agreement on avoidance of double taxation recently signed between the two countries in Stockholm. The exchange was witnessed by P.M. Vo Van Kiet and P.M. Carl Bildt. Vice Foreign Minister Nguyen Di Nien and Swedish Ambassador to Vietnam Mats Aberg were also present at the event.
