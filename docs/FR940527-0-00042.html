DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION Coast Guard 33 CFR Part 100 [CGD 09&hyph;94&hyph;007] RIN 2115&hyph;AA97 Special Local Regulation; Tallship Erie 94, Lake Erie, Presque Isle Bay, Erie, PA AGENCY: Coast Guard, DOT. ACTION: Temporary final rule.

SUMMARY: A special local regulation is being adopted for the marine event, Tallship Erie 94. This event will be held on Presque Isle Bay and Lake Erie, Erie, PA on July 8, 1994. The Tallship Erie 94 will have an estimated 15&hyph;20, 70&hyph;200 foot sailing ships, parading in a closed course on Lake Erie and will have an unusually large concentration of spectator vessels which could pose hazards to navigation in the area. This regulation is needed to provide for the safety of life, limb, and property on navigable waters during the event. Discussion of Regulation The Tallship Erie 94 will be held on Presque Isle Bay and Lake Erie on July 8, 1994. This regulation restricts general navigation on Presque Isle Bay and Erie Harbor from the Perry's Landing to Erie Harbor Entrance Lighted Buoy 2 (LLNR 3520). This event will have an estimated 15&hyph;20, 70&hyph;200 foot sailing ships, parading in a closed course on Lake Erie and will have an usually large concentration of spectator vessels which could pose hazards to navigation in the area. This regulation is necessary to ensure the protection of life, limb, and property during this event. Any vessel desiring to transit the regulated area may do so only with prior approval of the Patrol Commander (Officer in Charger, U.S. Cost Guard Station Erie, PA). This regulation is issued pursuant to 33 U.S.C. 1231 as set out in the authority citation for all of part 165.
