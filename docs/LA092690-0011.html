September 26, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Rourke, Otis Settle: Mickey Rourke and Carre Otis, co-stars of the steamy movie "Wild Orchid," have reached an agreement with the movie's producers over the selling of nude photos of the pair to Playboy magazine. The actors each filed suit in May seeking general and punitive damages against Vision Entertainment for the alleged unauthorized release of movie photographs that were printed in Playboy in June and in the April issue of the German magazine Kino. No details of the settlement were disclosed. SHAUNA SNOW
