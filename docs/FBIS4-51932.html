BFN [By correspondent Hiroki Fukuta] [Text] Jerusalem, 4 May -- Commenting on Justice Minister Shigeto Nagano's remark that the 1937 Nanjing atrocity [Nankin gyakusatsu] is a fabrication, Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa, who is visiting Israel, told a group of Japanese correspondents on 4 May: "Japan should be engaged in soul-searching over its past act of aggression. If the justice minister's remark has caused a misunderstanding among Japan's neighboring nations, as foreign minister I will work to dispel that misunderstanding."
