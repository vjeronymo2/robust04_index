BFN [Telephone report by Shpetim Roqi from Bucharest] [Text] President Berisha was received this afternoon at the Chamber of Deputies by Chairman Adrian Nastase and Senate Vice President Ioan Lup. President Berisha acquainted the Romanian parliament representatives with Albania's achievements in the field of legislation. There exists greater cooperation possibilities with Romania, he said, not only in the field of economy and politics but also between our parliaments. President Berisha noted that we are for dialogue between Prishtina and Belgrade in the presence of a third party. For this, we demand that you insist that Belgrade enter a dialogue. President Berisha also requested Romania's support for Albania's full membership to the Council of Europe [CE], since Albania long ago fulfilled conditions for this membership. For their part, the Romanian representatives said that they feel close to Albania and regard cooperation with the Albanian parliament highly. They assured President Berisha that they will strongly support Albania's full membership in the CE. The meetings are to continue this evening and tomorrow morning.
