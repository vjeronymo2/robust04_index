Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Cecilia Pachano from "The News" newscast] [Text] President of the Republic Rafael Caldera has signed orders to release the 23 military officers and soldiers who participated in the 4 October and 27 November 1992 coups. Of the total number, 18 soldiers were involved in the 27 November coup, while the other five participated in the 4 October attempt. Both coups took place during the administration of former President Carlos Andres Perez. The presidential pardon ensures the release of Army Lieutenant Colonel Raul Henriquez Salmeron, retired Army Lt. Col. Miguel Armando Madrid Bustamante, retired Frigate Lt. (Arne Stevenson Chacones Escamillo), National Police Major Carlos Jesus Salima Colina, Air Force Maj. Jose Rafael Azuna Olivieri, Air Force Sergeant Tony Arenas Cordero, Air Technician Rigoberto Serrano Sanchez, Army Captain Carlos Guillen Celis, Capt. Joaquin Suarez, Sgt. (Ivan Alexander Fleiter Rosales), Raul Antonio Valera Marin, Jesus Maria Lopez, Juan Ramon Mena Soriano, Angel Didio Lobo, Wilfredo Aguilera Orellana, Hermes Rafael Cabello Rodriguez, Antonio Julian Lestre, Juan Ubaldo Balladares Padilla, Moises Daniel Flores, Antonio Cubides Subero, (Juan Carlos Herrerabalbuena), Jorge Alonso Flores, and Eduardo Jose Garrido Martinez.
