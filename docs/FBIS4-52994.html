BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 14 (KCNA) -- The committee for the promotion of the repatriation of Kim In-so and Ham Se-hwan, who are kept in South Korea after being subjected to a long prison life for refusing to be converted, was formed in Moscow on May 5. Golyakov, first vice-chairman of the coordinating committee of Veterans of the Commonwealth of Independent States, was elected its chairman.
