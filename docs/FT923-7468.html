920818 FT 18 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Emap lifts stake in Kiss FM Radio Emap, the printing and publishing group, has increased its holding in its Kiss FM Radio subsidiary. The increased stake - Emap now controls 42.4 per cent of the A shares and 99.7 per cent of the B shares - was achieved by taking a further 37.3 per cent of the A and 0.4 per cent of the B for a total of Pounds 149,000. The consideration was settled by the issue of 46,010 shares and Pounds 26,358 cash.
