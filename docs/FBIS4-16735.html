BFN [Report by UKRINFORM for TASS] [Text] Kiev 18 April TASS -- A group of Ukrainian soldiers was flown from Kiev to Sarajevo on Monday as part of Ukraine's peace-keeping mission in the former Yugoslavia. They were the first contingent of a battalion to be airlifted to Sarajevo within the next couple of days. The battalion will be deployed near the besieged Bosnian town of Gorazde. A team of Ukrainian foreign ministry officials flew on board the same plane for talks with the warring sides to negotiate the safety of the Ukrainian soldiers.
