Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 31 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-President Rong Yiren said here today that the maintenance of good Sino-U.S. ties is in line with the interests of the two peoples and is essential to world peace and stability. He said this while meeting with visiting former U.S. Under Secretary of State and managing director of Lehman Brothers, Robert M. Kimmitt, this morning. Rong expressed his hope that people from the two nations would broaden contacts and understanding through various channels and improve bilateral cooperation of friendship in all fields. The vice-president also briefed the guest on China's reform and opening drive, saying that business people, including those from the [United] States, are welcome to conduct economic and trade cooperation in China. Kimmitt said that he was glad that Lehman Brothers has the opportunity to cooperate with China and he hoped this cooperation would be promoted. Chinese Vice-Foreign Minister Liu Huaqiu attended the meeting.
