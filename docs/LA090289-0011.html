September 2, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

'HEROIC' VOYAGER, ITS HUMAN GUIDES

Tony Taylor's lovingly sculpted essay on Voyager's odyssey to Neptune and beyond was, by itself, worth the price of admission to the Aug. 6 Times. Scientists might take note; information poetically expressed -- even technical information -- is a potent, effective force.

TROY J. HOLTER

Helena, Mont.
