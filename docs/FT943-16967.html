940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / Observer: Fare play? The break-up of British Rail has given the various operators a field day in passing the buck. Wednesday's rail strike afforded a signwriter at Guildford station this opportunity to confuse: 'Whilst South West Trains are not directly involved in the dispute we regret the problems that this will cause.' It's not our fault guv - but we'd like it to be.
