BFN [Unattributed article: "Member States Endorse `Pangalos Compromise' on Institutional Change"; as released by EUROPE] [Excerpt] On Tuesday evening [29 March], the Member States officially gave the Council Secretariat their acceptance of the compromise by the Greek Presidency concerning the modalities for qualified majority vote within the enlarged Council. The Committee of Permanent Representatives, at deputy level, will formally note this on Wednesday morning and, immediately after, the conference for negotiation with the four applicant countries will be convened to note the end of negotiations with agreement on all aspects, including the institutional issue.
