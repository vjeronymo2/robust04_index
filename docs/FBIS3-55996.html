Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow January 13 TASS -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin welcomed U.S. President Bill Clinton who arrived in Moscow early on Thursday [13 January]. The official welcoming ceremony with a guard of honour and national anthems was not held because of the late time of arrival. From the airport Clinton's motorcade left for the "Radisson- Slavyanskaya" hotel where the U.S. President will be staying. It is a 30-minute drive from the airport to the hotel.
