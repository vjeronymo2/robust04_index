BFN [Text] Bosnia-Herzegovina Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic had a telephone conversation with U.S. President Bill Clinton's special envoy Charles Redman. Mr Redman informed Prime Minister Silajdzic on the arrival of a contact group in Sarajevo on Thursday, 5 May. The Bosnia-Herzegovina prime minister informed Redman that the negotiations have been brought into question because of the disrespect for the NATO and UN ultimatum on the withdrawal of the aggressor forces from around Gorazde. Silajdzic emphasized the aggressor forces did not withdraw to a distance of three km from Gorazde and did not withdraw their heavy artillery from the 20 km exclusion zone from the town center as demanded by the ultimatum.
