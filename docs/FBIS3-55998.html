Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Ivan Ivanov and Gennadiy Talalayev] [Text] Moscow January 13 TASS--The Russian-American summit talks began in the Kremlin today in the morning immediately after the official greeting ceremony. After the beginning of the talks both presidents expressed confidence that the dialogue between Russia and the U.S. would promote the consolidation of world peace and tranquility, as well as the deepening of cooperation between the two countries. On the first stage the talks are held in a narrow format at the Green Hall of the Grand Kremlin Palace. Aside from Yeltsin and Clinton, they are attended by Dmitriy Ryurikov, assistant to the Russian president, and Strobe Talbott, special adviser to the U.S. secretary of state on the new independent states. According to the information from reliable sources, during their first one-hour meeting the Russian and U.S. presidents are expected to discuss issues dealing with economic and political cooperation between the two countries, the implementation of the Russian reform, the consolidation of international security, specifically, the settlement of regional conflicts and the non-proliferation of nuclear weapons. Probably, they will touch upon the mechanism of putting into effect Ukraine's decision on the withdrawal of nuclear warheads from its territory.
