January 3, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

DON'T IGNORE SINUSITIS

Question: I went to my doctor because I wasn't feeling well, and he told me that I had a case of sinusitis.

He also seemed concerned that I had waited as long as I had before making an appointment to speak with him. I'm not a hero, and if I had a pain in my chest or anything like that I'd get myself to an emergency room as soon as possible. But I don't think that a case of sinusitis is in the same ballpark. Why is it such a big deal?

Answer: Sinusitis can be a serious infection. Under some circumstances, for example, it can lead to brain abscesses.
