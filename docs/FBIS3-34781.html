Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Israeli warplanes carried out a raid on Mlita hill in Iqlim al-Tuffah at 1000 today. One of the planes fired four missiles at the target. The planes continued to fly low over the area until 1010, drawing antiaircraft fire from Lebanese Army positions. At 1030, Israeli aircraft again raided Iqlim al-Tuffah. Israeli military aircraft flew over al-Nabatiyah and Iqlim al-Tuffah at 0930 today, staging mock raids. The military aircraft were accompanied by an Israeli MK reconnaissance plane.
