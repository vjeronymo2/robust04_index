January 19, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Union Pacific Corp.: The Bethlehem, Pa., railroad company's 1989 profit dropped 8% from the previous year to $594.5 million. Revenue totaled $6.5 billion, up 7%. The company said the 1988 figures included income from the sale of one refinery and the sale of a 50% interest in another.
