Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 13 (KCNA) -- Civilian organisations numbering 195 in South Korea met in Seoul on March 10 and inaugurated the "headquarters of all-people movement for defending our agriculture", according to a report. The headquarters resolved to curb the "parliamentary endorsement" of agricultural market opening by the Kim Yong-sam group and launch a boycott against imported agricultural products.
