September 30, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

BIG-GAME HUNTER ON CHOPPING BLOCK FOR HER PURSUITS

"Changing Stripes," by Rich Tosches, glamorizes a 42-year-old woman's gleeful transformation into a big-game hunter.

Why don't you look now for a middle-aged lady who just discovered pornography and glamorize that, too? It's legal, also!

TIFFANY BURTON, Studio City
