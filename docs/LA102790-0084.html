October 27, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

WRONG ABOUT KIDS

I'm sorry to say so but the article you wrote on the New Kids concert was very bad.

Maybe the fans enjoyed the concert. Also maybe we enjoy wasting our money on them.

Another thing I'd like to see is you get up on stage and try to sing after dancing and trying to catch your breath. Just because you don't like their music doesn't mean you can put them down.

A lot of people think the New Kids are great, so if you don't have anything nice to say about them, why don't you just shut up?

RACHEL MOEDER

Diamond Bar
