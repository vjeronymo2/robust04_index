EFFECTIVE DATE: May 6, 1994. DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE 28 CFR Part 0 Redelegation of Functions; Delegation of Authority to Drug Enforcement Administration Official AGENCY: Department of Justice. ACTION: Notice of clarification of final rule.

SUMMARY: This document clarifies the Final Rule published May 6, 1994 (59 FR 23637), regarding the redelegation of certain functions and authority which were vested in the Attorney General by the Controlled Substances Act and subsequently delegated to the Administrator of the Drug Enforcement Administration. In that Final Rule, the Administrator redelegated to the Deputy Administrator the authority to exercise all necessary functions under 21 CFR Part 1300 which have not already been redelegated in subpart R of 28 CFR 0.104. This notice will serve to clarify that all functions under 21 CFR Parts 1300&hyph;1316 were delegated to the Deputy Administrator by the Final Rule published May 6, 1994. EFFECTIVE DATE: May 6, 1994.
