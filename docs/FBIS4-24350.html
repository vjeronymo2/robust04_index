BFN [Text] Fresh clashes occurred between opposition supporters and security forces in the Congolese capital Brazzaville. At least two people were killed in Pila in the eastern part of Brazzaville. Tension seems to have ebbed but sporadic shots from automatic weapons were still heard last night at Pila considered as the stronghold of the supporters of former President Sassou Nguesso. This new tension followed the kidnapping last Saturday of two sympathizers of the Presidential Group in Bacongo in the sourthen part of Brazzaville and stronghold of the members of the Congolese Movement for Democracy and Integral Development led by Bernard Kolelas.
