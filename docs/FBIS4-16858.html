BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Glazovskaya] [Text] Ashkhabad [Ashgabat] 19 April TASS -- "Any attempts to intervene in state affairs will be decisively stopped," said President Saparmurad Niyazov during a meeting with religious workers of Turkemnistan today. He also emphasised that Turkmenistan is a "secular democratic state laws of which give everbody right to freedom of religion." The head of the Turkmen state called on religious workers to help maintain in the future civic peace and social concord in the country which is the most important condition for fulfilling the nationwide program of "10 years of prosperity." Niyazov also appealed to use the church's authority for waging war against drug addiction, an evil which is beginning to corrupt the youth. Saparmurad Niyazov announced the creation of a council of religious affairs under the president of Turkmenistan which will consist of Moslem Qazis together with workers of the Russian Orthodox Church. According to an announcement, the national festival Kurban Bairam (Id) will be celebrated in Turkmenistan for three days from 20 to 22 May.
