931202 FT 02 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Derwent pays Pounds 9m for offices Derwent Valley Holdings, through its Colebrook Estates subsidiary, is buying a 75,000 sq ft 10-storey leasehold office building for Pounds 9.23m. The property, at London's Hyde Park Corner, is held on a 70-year unexpired ground rent of Pounds 2,750 a year. Its rental income amounts to Pounds 1.67m a year with most leases due for renewal at the end of 1994. The consideration will be satisfied from the company's existing facilities.
