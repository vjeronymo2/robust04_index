BFN [Text] After attending the ceremony to mark the handover of the border immigration station at the Lao-Thai friendship bridge, His Excellency [H.E.] Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon bade farewell to H.E. Australian Prime Minister Paul Keating and his delegation at the border station. H.E. Khamtai Siphandon expressed thanks to H.E. Paul Keating for the aid in the border station construction. He also hailed the success of the high-level delegation of the Government of the Commonwealth of Australia in its visit to the Lao People's Democratic Republic. He wished H.E. Paul Keating, his wife, and delegation a good trip. H.E. Prime Minister and his delegation left Vientiane for Bangkok by a special plane this afternoon for a visit to the Kingdom of Thailand.
