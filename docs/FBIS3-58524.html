Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] The two main participants in the skinhead attack against U.S. tobogganists in the Thuringian city of Oberhof have been sentenced to youth custody of two years and eight months and one year, respectively. The municipal court in Suhl found them guilty of grievous bodily harm. In October, along with other skinheads, the two men beat up U.S. tobogganist Duncan Kennedy, who tried to protect a black teammate. The defendants apologized to their victim today. Former offenses of the two men were included in the verdict.
