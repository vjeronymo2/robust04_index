January 10, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

LEARNING TO READ

It is very annoying to know that illiteracy has become an accumulated problem in the United States.

Possibly, literacy centers could be made to help assist people in learning. Or, with all of the different teaching aids for literate people, maybe these same companies could make teaching aids for the illiterate. The libraries could offer free classes to develop reading and writing skills. Correctional facilities should automatically make prisoners attend classes.

RANDY RETTIG

Dana Point
