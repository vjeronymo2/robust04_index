BFN [By Benjamin Yeh] [Text] Taipei, May 11 (CNA) -- The International Commercial Bank of China on Wednesday [11 May] extended a U.S.$8.5 million loan to Nauru to help the Pacific island country develop infrastructure. Pai Pei-ying, the chairman of the Taipei-based bank, and Nauru President Bernard Dowiyoko sealed the contract in a ceremony held Wednesday afternoon. The Republic of China [ROC] maintains official diplomatic links with Nauru.
