September 2, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

THIS WEEK

BULKING BEAUTIES: Over 100 bodybuilders will compete in the annual NPC Women's and Mixed Pairs National Bodybuilding Championships at the Long Beach Convention and Entertainment Center on Saturday.

Semifinal competition begins at 9 a.m. and finals at 6:30 p.m. J2

INDOOR HACKERS: The Leukemia Society's 2nd Annual Indoor Golf Tournament will feature 36 foursomes putting their way through the Radisson Hotel in the City of Commerce. Registration begins at 4:30 p.m. Wednesday. J2

INTERNATIONAL FESTIVAL: Carnival rides, music, dance and food from many cultures will be featured in Lynwood's Annual International Unity Festival at Congressional Park, 3798 Martin Luther King Jr. Blvd., Lynwood, through Monday. J2
