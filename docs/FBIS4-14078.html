BFN [Interview with Lieutenant General Mohammad Gol Aryubwal, deputy defense minister, by unidentified correspondent in Kabul; date not given -- recorded] [Excerpt] [Correspondent] Esteemed Lieutenant General Mohammad Gol Aryubwal, deputy defense minister of the Islamic State of Afghanistan, I thank you for receiving us for the interview. I would like to ask you -- since we are now completing the second anniversary of the Islamic revolution -- since the fall of the previous regime and the disintegration of its arme, the country has had a new national Islamic Army. Could you please give us information about the organization and ideological work in the Army? [Aryubwal] In the name of God the compassionate, the merciful. As you said, the Army of Afghanistan disintegrated. It was an organized plan of the longstanding enemies of Afghanistan. For ages they planned to destroy the national treasures of Afghanistan -- the national archive of Afghanistan, the museums, and to eliminate the Afghan institutions -- to destroy the national Army of Afghanistan -- and with the implementation of that plan Afghanistan was faced with a strange situation which is the cause of the sorrow of every Afghan, every Muslim, every patriot. Through their servants they took all Afghan treasures to their countries. In practice now there is nothing in Afghanistan. But although Afghanistan was confronted with a civil war by those enemies of its freedom, the Islamic State of Afghanistan was able to establish such an army to at least defend the national honor of Afghanistan, that is to safeguard its national unity and national sovereignty. And despite the problems created for it by the enemies, the Afghan Army has been able to protect national sovereignty and national unity and to abort many of the enemies' conspiracies. As I said before despite the fact that the Army is faced with big problems -- for a single moment the Afghan Army has not been allowed to be self-confident, to think about itself -- yet, thanks God, a large number of patriotic and nationalist trained officers, from every class, every stratum, every profession came together and organized themselves. As a result of this organization, thus far several conspiracies, created especially by [word indistinct] enemies of Afghanistan about which the world knows, have been aborted by this Army, and God willing so far as possible it will defend the national honor of the country at any moment, day and night. From the ideological point of view too the Islamic Army is progressing and attention has been paid to the ideological life of the Army. Courses have been set up and work continues. It is hoped that when the Afghan Army can rest for a while it is expected of it to [words indistinct]. [Correspondent] Unfortunately since the victory of the Islamic revolution there had been fighting in various directions in Afghanistan. Recent incidents would have caused very grave consequences, but the mojahedin and officials of our Islamic Army were able to prevent those grave incidents. Please shed some light on the activities and the share of the officials and units of the Islamic Army and mojahedin in the dislodging of the recent incidents. [Aryubwal] As I said before, the longstanding enemies of Afghanistan have not for a moment allowed the Afghan nation, Army, and state to dress Afghanistan's wounds so as to take care of its affairs and pay attention to its national unity and reconstruction. [Words indistinct] especially after the recent conspiracy this heroic, Muslim and patriotic Army, which cannot even be called an army compared with other armies, was able to abort a dangerous conspiracy, a coup d'etat. Not only did it abort the coup d'etat, but it also gained many victories, which are a cause of pride for this Army. Military experts can understand the fact that an army, which even cannot be called an army, was able to abort such a dangerous coup d'etat that was supported by many foreign powers, and to capture many areas from the enemies. For example since the failed coup d'etat of 11 Jadi [1 January] this Army has cleared up bravely the area of the airport, where previously militia forces resided, and the areas of Qabelbai, Hudkhel, Banayi, Alokhel, Milli Bus, Microrayon Bridge, the area of Idgah mosque, the surroundings of Balahesar, Maiwand Wat, [east and south of Kabul] the surroundings of Bagh-e Babor, the surrounding of Dehmazang, the cultural area of Kabul, and many other areas in Kabul. Outside Kabul many victories have been achieved. For instance, Parwan Province has been completely cleared of the enemies and many positions have been conquered. The enemy is in very small area of Kapisa Province, God willing, and in a short time that area will also be cleared [words indistinct]. In northern areas such as in Faryab Province many areas have been cleared of the militia. The area of Pol-e Khomri [in Baghlan] is completely cleared. Northern Salang, Khinjan, Doshi, Samangan Province, and the districts of Balkh Province have been conquered. In the near future we will hear about other conquests too. [passage omitted]
