931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Rolfe & Nolan falls but lifts dividend Rolfe & Nolan, the futures and options computer bureau and software specialist, announced lower pre-tax profits for the six months ended August 31 of Pounds 609,000, against Pounds 707,000. After higher tax of Pounds 441,000 (Pounds 295,000), earnings per share fell from 8.9p to 6.1p. The interim dividend, however, is lifted to 2.805p (2.55p). In Europe the pre-tax surplus rose from Pounds 813,000 to Pounds 1.21m but North American losses grew to Pounds 600,000 (Pounds 106,000). Turnover expanded from Pounds 5.31m to Pounds 6.06m. Directors said group liquidity remained strong, with net short-term bank deposits of Pounds 2.5m.
