BFN [Text] Dutch Foreign Minister Peter Kooijmans has expressed concern at the capture in Bosnia of around 200 UN personnel. Among them are three Dutch nationals. They have been taken hostage by Serbs in actions following NATO air strikes against Serb positions near Gorazde. Mr. Kooijmans emphasized that the air strikes were in accordance with a UN resolution, and he called the Serb reaction completely unjustified.
