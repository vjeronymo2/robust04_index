BFN [Report by BELINFORM correspondent Svetlana Kalinkina -- TASS] [Text] Minsk, 16 May -- Fulfilling the decision of the Holy Synod of the Russian Orthodox Church, the Belarusian clergy will not take part in the Republic's first presidential election. A BELINFORM correspondent was informed at the office of the diocese of the Russian Orthodox Church that there is no substance in reports that the clergy are canvassing for one candidate or another or participating in groups. At the same time some church ministers have until now been actively engaging in politics; deputies at various levels are permitted to complete their term in office as set out by the law.
