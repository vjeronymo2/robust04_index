BFN [Text] The Armenian parliamentarian, writer, and public speaker Vardges Petrosyan was killed by (?a single) shot fired by unidentified persons at around 1700 today at the entrance of his residence on Khorenatsi Street on his return from work. The Armenian Interior Ministry press service reported that Interior Minister Vano Siradegyan has gone to the site of the incident with an operations team. The public prosecutor and the inspector at the public prosecutor's office in Spandaryan district have also gone there. A criminal investigation has been launched and it is being conducted by the public prosecutor's office in Spandaryan district.
