920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Towles Towles, the hosiery maker and wholesaler of textiles, made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 1,000, compared with a loss of Pounds 56,000, in the year ended February 28 1992. Turnover edged ahead from Pounds 15m to Pounds 15.4m. Earnings were 0.33p (losses 1.53p) and the dividend is again 0.25p.
