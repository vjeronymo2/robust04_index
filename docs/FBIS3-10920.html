Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From "The Voz Andes Newscast"] [Text] The international economic community is watching Ecuador closely and has confidence in the country and the macroeconomic policies instituted by the national government, a Foreign Ministry press bulletin has announced. The bulletin was issued on the occasion of the conclusion of negotiations for an agreement between Ecuador and Switzerland to prevent double taxation. Approximately 70 companies and capital enterprises from the Swiss Confederation have invested or are investing $30 million in our country. This represents 7 percent of the total of direct foreign investments in Ecuador. This amount is surpassed only by Swiss investments in Argentina.
