Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Vladimir Taranov] [Text] Moscow March 1 TASS [dateline as received] -- "There are good grounds for the ratification of treaty of the friendly relations between Russia and Czechia," Jiri Pein, the chairman of the foreign relations committee of the chamber of deputies of the Czech Republic, told ITAR-TASS upon the conclusion of a visit of a parliamentary delegation of the Czech Republic to Moscow. He said the Russian side promised to speed up the ratification of the treaty which is of much importance for creating the legal grounds for cooperation between Russia and Czechia. The Czech parliamentarian said that meetings with leaders of the Russian State Duma and Federation Council, with representatives of the Russian Foreign Ministry and of Russian political parties indicated that changes had taken place in the Russian foreign policy. During meetings and conversations that the Czech parliamentary delegation had in Moscow the sides were not striving to convince each other of anything. They only exchanged opinions on matters of mutual interest, specifically, the attitude to the "Partnership for Peace", European security and prospects for economic cooperation. "We got convinced that Russia respects Czechia's sovereignty", Jiri Pein said.
