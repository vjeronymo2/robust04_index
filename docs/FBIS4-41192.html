BFN [Text] Resignation of the officials of the Gorno-Badakhshan Autonomous Oblast of Tajikistan was demanded by the participants of a small illegal rally, which took place in Khorog, the administrative center of the oblast. A correspondent of the ITAR-TASS news agency was told this by an independent and well-informed source of Gorno-Badakhshan. The demonstrators, among whom were many women and old people, accused the local authorities of being unable to end legal tyranny and the growth of organized crime. The demonstrators pointed out that the processes of surrendering weapons, which are illegally kept by the population, and the disbanding of illegal armed formations are extremely slow in Khorog as well as in other rayons of the autonomous oblast. [words indistinct] Saydamir Zukhurov, minister of security, confirmed the report on illegal rally in Khorog. However, according to his words there were no demands of resignation of the oblast's leadership. The people wanted to learn from the officials of Gorno-Badakhshan Autonomous Oblast when law and order finally would be established on the territory of the autonomous oblast and after receiving full and specific answers the demonstrators peacefully went away, stated Zukhurov, minister of security of Tajikistan.
