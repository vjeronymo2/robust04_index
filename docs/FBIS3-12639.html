Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Security sources that are well-informed about the investigations into the explosion in the Sayyidat al-Najat Church in Zuq Mikha'il have denied reports by REUTER that four women have been detained and will be interrogated. The sources told our radio station that this report is totally untrue. REUTER had reported that the Lebanese security forces today arrested four women suspected of having taken part in the explosion in the church. The sources said the women were detained at dawn today and that initial investigations showed that they had planted a bomb next to the altar during the Sunday mass.
