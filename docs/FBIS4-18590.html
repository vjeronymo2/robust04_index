BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] The Central Bank has fixed the official effective selling rate of the U.S. dollar for tomorrow at 40,052 Turkish lira [TL] and the German mark at TL23,361. According to these rates, the U.S. dollar rose 24.5 percent and the German mark 23 percent against today's prices. The official effective buying rate for the U.S. dollar for tomorrow is fixed at TL39,813 and the German mark at TL23,221. Meanwhile, yesterday's activity in the free foreign exchange markets is continuing today. The U.S. dollar, which was TL30,400 when the free market closed yesterday, opened up to transactions at TL33,000. The U.S. dollar which continued to rise during the day reached TL39,500 in certain exchange bureaus. The German mark started the day at TL21,000 and closed at TL22,500.
