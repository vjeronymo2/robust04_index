Language: Estonian Article Type:BFN [Text] By order of Defense Minister Juri Luik, an interdepartmental commission has been set up to prepare the Partnership for Peace cooperation program on the Estonian side. The commission is led by Toivo Klaar, head of the Foreign Ministry's policy department. Members of the commission are: Aivo Oras of the Foreign Ministry, Tarmo Molder, the Defense Ministry's deputy chancellor, and Mart Tiru, head of the defense forces headquarters administration.
