930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Hanson scrip gets go-ahead Shareholders in Hanson, the Anglo-American conglomerate, yesterday agreed changes to the company's articles of association providing for and approving a scrip dividend plan, writes Andrew Bolger. Those attending the EGM then heard an appeal from the United Mine Workers of America for Hanson to move to settle the strike by coal workers which involves the group's Peabody mines in the US. Mr Martin Taylor, Hanson's vice-chairman, said 'We are waiting for the UMWA to return to the negotiating table.'
