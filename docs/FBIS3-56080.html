Language: Lithuanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Lithuanian Government declared at its closed meeting on Wednesday [12 January] as restricted all secret documents of the government institutions, the procuracy, courts, all structures of the Lithuanian Communist Party, offices, enterprises, and organizations during the period of Soviet occupation of Lithuania. The government press representative also announced that it was decided at the same meeting to abolish unilaterally visas to the citizens of Japan, Switzerland, Liechtenstein, and Italy.
