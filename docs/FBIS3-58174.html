Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Dutch Minister of Home Affairs, Ien Dales, has died suddenly at the age of 62. Ms. Dales joined the left of center coalition government headed by Prime Minister Ruud Lubbers in 1989. In a word of praise for her work, Deputy Prime Minister Wim Kok spoke this morning of her courage in introducing controversial proposals to cut the costs of public health care. The Dutch Minister of Justice, Ernst Hirsch Ballin, will be temporarily in charge of the Ministry of Home Affairs.
