940802 FT 02 AUG 94 / UK Company News: TR Smaller raises net asset value 14% After deducting prior changes at par, the net asset value of TR Smaller Companies Investment Trust improved by some 14 per cent - from 182.6p to 207.7p per share - during the year to May 31. The trust, part of the Henderson Touche Remnant stable, reported available revenue down at Pounds 7.1m (Pounds 7.68m) for earnings of 3.99p (4.31p) per share. Nevertheless, the final dividend is held at 2.3p, maintaining the total for the year at 3.8p.
