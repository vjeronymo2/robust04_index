Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The regime has continued its brutal oppressive acts against the Iraqi people, seeking to destroy the marshlands in southern Iraq. The regime's artillery shelled the regions of (Bahilah) Nahr Salih, Abu-Saddah, (Muray'an) (al-Mifrakh), and al-Waki in al-Madinah District in al-Basrah Governorate. Our sources in southern Iraq said the shelling was so intense that scores of houses were destroyed and heavy losses inflicted on citizens' property. According to the latest information available to our sources, a 15-year old girl was martyred in the shelling, which is still continuing.
