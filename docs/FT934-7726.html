931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / World Stock Markets: Mexico MEXICAN stocks ended a six-session winning streak and closed lower as investors grew cautious in late trade ahead of the US House of Representatives' Nafta vote. The IPC index was off 10.85 at 2,148.50 after an intraday record high of 2,177.30. Volume was a brisk 170.8m shares.
