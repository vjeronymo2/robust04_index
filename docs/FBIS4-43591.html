BFN [Excerpt] Gorel Thurdin is the new environment minister in Sweden, but in the future, all matters concerning the Oresund Bridge will be submitted to cabinet minister Reidunn Lauren. After giving its consent to the Oresund Bridge, the government is now leaving the matter to the Standing Committee on the Constitution and the water rights court, which will decide on the environmental conditions for building the bridge. The government will not discuss the bridge matter any more, cabinet minister Reidunn Lauren says. [passage omitted]
