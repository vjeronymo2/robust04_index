921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / Reductions in Britain's diplomatic posts Sweeping reductions in Britain's diplomatic posts are expected as part of next week's public expenditure cuts following the Autumn Statement, writes Robert Mauthner. Some 27 diplomatic posts, including embassies, consulates-general and consulates, many of them in Africa and Latin America, may be axed. Cash cuts will hit foreign postings Page 10
