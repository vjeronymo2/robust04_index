Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] At the emergency telephone conference held by the provincial government on 24 February, Vice Governor Gu Erxiong pointed out: Major problems existing in the province's current cotton production include: Peasants' enthusiasm in growing cotton needs to be mobilized further; some localities have not yet assigned cotton growing quotas to peasant households and plots; some localities have seen the trend of reducing the acreage sown to summer cotton; cotton seeds are of poor quality and in short supply; and peasants have not prepared sufficient means of production for growing cotton. In light of these problems, Gu Erxiong stressed: The month before the spring sowing of cotton is the key to decide the success or failure in this year's cotton production. Governments at all levels should adopt feasible measures to successfully assign cotton growing quotas to peasants and ensure the scale of cotton growing acreage.
