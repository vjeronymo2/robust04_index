930202 FT 02 FEB 93 / Management (The Growing Business): Fifth less new starts in 1992 - In A Nutshell A total of 380,000 new businesses were started in 1992, a fall of 22 per cent on the year before and well below the peak of 520,000 in 1990, according to National Westminster Bank. Since 1988 an estimated 2.4m new businesses have been formed of which two thirds are still thought to be trading. More businesses are making use of the founder's personal resources to start. In 1992, 65 per cent used their own money compared with 37 per cent in 1991 while just 20 per cent used bank finance compared with 41 per cent.
