BFN [Text] Vienna, May 21 (AFP) -- North Korea has agreed to the visit of a new high-level mission of the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) to discuss the removal of fuel rods from its Yongbyon nuclear complex, the IAEA announced Saturday [21 May]. The mission will leave Vienna Sunday and arrive in North Korea Tuesday. "It will clarify inspection measures necessary in relation with the discharging of the reactor, an IAEA statement said.
