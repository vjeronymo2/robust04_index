930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / International Company News: Sega upgrades profit forecast for current fiscal year SEGA Enterprises, a leading Japanese video-game maker, has revised upwards its earnings forecasts for the fiscal year ending March 31, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. The company says it is seeing strong demand for its arcade-style video games worldwide and home-use units overseas. Sega predicts full-year sales of Y341bn (Dollars 2.75bn). up 60 per cent, and pre-tax profits of Y55bn, up 64 per cent.
