920219 FT 19 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Scottish Eastern net assets ahead 23% Net asset value of the Martin Currie-managed Scottish Eastern Investment Trust jumped 23 per cent over the 12 months to January 31. Taking prior charges at par, the improvement - from 56.5p to 69.5p - compared with a rise over the same period of 18.5 per cent in the FT-A All-Share Index. Earnings per share were unchanged at 1.41p from attributable revenue of Pounds 8.94m (Pounds 8.93m). A proposed final dividend of 0.96p brings the total for the year to 1.42p (1.35p).
