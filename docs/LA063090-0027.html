June 30, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Jeff Grayer, a guard with the Milwaukee Bucks, was arraigned on two counts of assaulting a police officer, authorities in Flint, Mich., said.

Grayer and Terence Greene, another former high school basketball star from Flint, were arrested Thursday night during a scuffle with two officers at the scene of a minor traffic accident, police said.
