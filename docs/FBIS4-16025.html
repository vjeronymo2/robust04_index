BFN [From the "Diplomatic Panorama" feature: Reports by Diplomatic Correspondents Igor Porshnev, Vitaliy Trubetskoy, and others] [Text] Russian President Boris Yeltsin expressed the hope that the NATO air strikes against Serbian positions near Gorazde would not contribute to confrontations or increase the scale of the bloodshed. He told an INTERFAX correspondent in Madrid Tuesday that Russia will keep in touch with Butrus Butrus-Ghali and Bill Clinton to avoid a repetition of the incident.
