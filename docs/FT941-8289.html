940221 FT 21 FEB 94 / One in five are teleworkers One in five British workers now spends at least some of the week working from home, but most employees are not encouraged to do so, British Telecommunications said yesterday . More than half of 500 full-time workers interviewed by Gallup said flexibility would be the biggest benefit of 'teleworking' - using computers, faxes and phones to stay in touch with work.
