BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 20 June -- Three members of the militia staff were killed and 12 wounded during the day on Sunday [19 June] as a result of a clash that occurred in the mountainous Fayzabad rayon of Tajikistan. Besides this, the fate is still not known of two militia men whose bodies were not discovered at the scene of the exchange of fire. The staff of the internal affairs directorate of the neighboring (Kofarnihan) rayon were the victims of an attack by one of the bandit formations. According to information from Tajikistan's Ministry of Internal Affairs the group of militia staff were ambushed while on an official assignment. A criminal case has been instituted with regard to the crime.
