July 19, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

THEIR BEST SHOT;

S&L JACKPOT FOR WHOM?

Re the savings and loan disaster: When Reagan signed the 1982 deregulation act that made it possible, for whom was he speaking when he said, "All in all, I think we have hit the jackpot"? Certainly not the taxpayer.

E.P. BENSON

Newport Beach
