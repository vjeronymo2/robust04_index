BFN [Text] Lieutenant General Tun Kyi, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council and minister of trade, welcomed Datuk Sri Dr. Lim Keng, primary industries minister of Malaysia, and a seven-member entrepreneurial delegation at Yangon [Rangoon] airport at 1550. Trade Minister Lt. Gen. Tun Kyi hosted a dinner in honor of the visiting Malaysian delegation at the People's Park Restaurant in the evening.
