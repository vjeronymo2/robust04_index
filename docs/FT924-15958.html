921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Aran Energy improves Aran Energy improved its pre-tax result to IPounds 38,000 (Pounds 38,400) in the first half of 1992. In the comparable period the Dublin-based oil and gas exploration company lost IPounds 203,000. Aran said the results reflected the low level of production during the period. Since then significant oil production revenues have been acquired via the acquisition of a 26 per cent share of production of the Dunlin oilfield for a minimum of Dollars 19m (Pounds 11.1m). Sales in the six months came to IPounds 14.2m (IPounds 14.5m). Loss per share was reduced to 0.05p (0.14p).
