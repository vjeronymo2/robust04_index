Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 29 Mar (TANJUG) -- The radio of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna has reported that this morning Croat deputies refused to enter the hall in Sarajevo, where the Muslim-Croat assembly is meeting, until Croat national symbols are displayed along with Muslim symbols. Ivan Bender, chairman of the chamber of deputies of the Assembly of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna, told Croatian TV upon his arrival in Sarajevo that he would lodge a protest against the decision to start the Assembly proceedings without delegates from the Croatian Democratic Union of former Bosnia-Herzegovina. According to Muslim Sarajevo radio, today's session has been postponed and will start at 1400.
