910418 FT 18 APR 91 / Fraud case remands A VENEZUELAN investment banker and an American financial consultant were remanded in custody for a week at Marlborough Street Court in London yesterday, charged in connection with an alleged plot to defraud a merchant bank out of a Pounds 425,000 loan. Mr Adrian Simionescu-Marin and Mr Ronald Conquest are accused of conspiring with others between April 10 and 15 to defraud Merrill Lynch, Pierce, Fenner and Smith.
