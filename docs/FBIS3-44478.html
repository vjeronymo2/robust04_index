Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, December 31 (XINHUA) -- China's state statistics bureau will make use of its abilities in collecting information and better serve the farmers and township enterprises next year. Shao Zongming, deputy director of the bureau, said recently that all the statistics organizations will make grass-root investigations and provide information for leaders of the party and government departments to make decisions and formulate policies. The bureau will research how to increase farmers' incomes, develop rural markets, further improve the social services system for agricultural production and strengthen the agricultural foundations. It will also conduct researches on the price scissors of industrial and agricultural products and monitor the conditions in the socio-economic performance in the rural areas. Facing the market economy for only a short time, farmers often encounter puzzling difficulties. Shao said that the statistical organizations will help them by supplying information and guiding their production, so as to protect their interests.
