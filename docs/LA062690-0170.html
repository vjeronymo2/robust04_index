June 26, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

GALA TO OPEN GARSON CENTER

A gala tribute to Greer Garson will be held at New Mexico's College of Santa Fe Oct. 20 for the opening of the Greer Garson Communications Center and Studios.

The English-born Academy Award winner, who makes her home in Santa Fe, made the project possible by her financial gift to the college more than a year ago.

The communications center and studios are the latest in a list of contributions to the college by Garson and her late husband, E. E. (Buddy) Fogelson. The Garson Studios provides 22,000 square feet of filming space, the largest such film-TV studio facility in New Mexico.
