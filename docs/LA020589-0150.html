February 5, 1989, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

DEAD MAN BELIEVED VICTIM OF HOMICIDE

The body of an unidentified man was found in a motel room in National City on Saturday, police said.

Officers responded to a call shortly before 11 a.m. from the clerk at the motel in the 600 block of Roosevelt Avenue, Lt. Merrell Davis said. Upon arrival the officers discovered the body, apparently a homicide victim.

An investigation is continuing, Davis said.
