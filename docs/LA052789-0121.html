May 27, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

DAVIS NAMED GIRLS' BASKETBALL COACH AT EDISON

Joey Davis has been hired as the girls' basketball coach at Edison High School, Athletic Director Bruce Belcher said Friday.

Davis, 24, coached freshman boys' basketball at Servite for the past five seasons. He replaces Randy Williams, who resigned in April.

Davis also was an assistant coach for the girls' volleyball team at Brea-Olinda High and was the junior varsity softball coach for the Wildcats.
