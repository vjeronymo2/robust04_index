940516 FT 16 MAY 94 / International Company News: Hollinger ahead in first quarter Hollinger, the Canadian holding company of Mr Conrad Black, says its publishing properties are improving with a significant recovery in advertising, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. First-quarter profit was CDollars 12.6m (USDollars 9.1m) or 22 cents a share, against CDollars 13.7m or 20 cents a year earlier. Hollinger owns newspaper holdings in North America, Australia and Israel, and 66.2 per cent of the Telegraph Group in the UK.
