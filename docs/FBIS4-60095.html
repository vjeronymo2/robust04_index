BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received Jack Dyuf, the director general of the UN's Food and Agriculture Organization [FAO] at 1215 today. The meeting was attended by Agriculture Minister As'ad Mustafa and the FAO Damascus office director, Mustafa Nasir. Talks dealt with the organization's role in agricultural development in the world and in realizing food security in the future. The president expressed Syria's appreciation for the FAO efforts and Syria's eagerness to cooperate with it to realize its aims. The FAO director expressed admiration for the Syrian agricultural policy and promised that the FAO will contribute toward raising Syria's agricultural production capacity. The FAO director left Damascus today after a two-day visit during which he met with President Hafiz al-Asad. He was seen off by As'ad Mustafa, the Agriculture Minister, Yahya Baqqur, director general of the Arab Organization for Agricultural Development; the assistant agriculture minister; the regional FAO representative in Damascus; and senior agriculture Ministry and UN officials in Damascus.
