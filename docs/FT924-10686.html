921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Lendu swings round to profit of Pounds 46,000 Lendu Holdings, whose activities cover irrigated cotton and sheep and cereal farming in Australia, turned round from a loss of Pounds 174,000 to a pre-tax profit of Pounds 46,000 in the six months ended June 30. Trading losses were cut from Pounds 199,000 to Pounds 2,000, and there was a Pounds 140,000 gain on sale of fixed asset investments. Earnings per share came to 0.31p (losses 1.26p). The sale of Lendu Rubber Estates to Bertam Holdings will be completed early next month, leaving the focus on the Australian agriculture sector. The current accounting period runs for 18 months to June 30. A second interim report will be issued early next year.
