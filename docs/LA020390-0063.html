February 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Quarterback Randall Cunningham of the Philadelphia Eagles is the highest paid player in the NFL, averaging $2,562,857 a year, according to a survey compiled by the NFL Player's Assn. and published in the Baltimore Sun.

Cleveland quarterback Bernie Kosar was second at an average $2,328,571 a year, followed by Denver quarterback John Elway, listed at $2,116,667 a year.
