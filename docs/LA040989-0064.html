April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

YOU'LL TAKE GOOD RIBBING

Got a hankerin' for a slab o' ribs?

Angelena's Kitchen Table, 4515 Sepulveda Blvd., Sherman Oaks. (818) 788-0465. Style: Not ordering the ribs would be a sin. Setting: Solid soul -- soul music, soul celeb photos and soul food. Recommended: Granddaddy's BBQ pork ribs. Cost per person: $9.75.

Carl's BBQ, 5953 W. Pico Blvd., Los Angeles. (213) 934-0637. Style: Take-out ribs for that gnawing hunger. Setting: Mini-mall melange. Recommended: Pork ribs. Cost per person: $7.75.

Dr. Hogly Woglys Tyler, Texas Bar-B-Que, 8136 Sepulveda Blvd., Van Nuys. (818) 780-6701. Style: Texas-style barbecue for the extremely hungry. Setting: Bare bones. Recommended: Beef ribs. Cost per person: $8.95.

Miller's Acorn, 23360 Lake Manor Drive, Chatsworth. (818) 888-2099. Style: Italian-American macho for garlic lovers. Setting: Rural roadhouse. Recommended: Baby back ribs. Cost per person: $12.

Rosies BBQ Grillery, 11845 W. Olympic Blvd., Los Angeles. (213) 473-8533. (Also located in Northridge and Sherman Oaks.) Style: Mesquite-grilled ribs for non-foodies. Setting: New Southwestern pretty. Recommended: Spicy Southern spare ribs. Cost per person: $10.25. Kathie Jenkins
