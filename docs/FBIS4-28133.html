BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 6 (KCNA) -- A Korean Trade Union delegation led by Chu Song-il, chairman of the Central Committee [C.C.] of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea [GFTUK], left here today for a visit to China. It was seen off at Pyongyang railway station by vice-chairman of the C.C., the GFTUK Kim Kuk-sam and Chinese ambassador to Korea Qiao Zonghuai.
