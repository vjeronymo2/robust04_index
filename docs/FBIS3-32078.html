Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Several international news agencies report that Caracas has revealed the contents of the technical cooperation agreement signed between the Health Ministries of Venezuela and Cuba, together with the World Health Organization [WHO], for the development of joint research and training. The document establishes an exchange of professional personnel, technical assistance, and advice for the preparation of sanitary projects between the Cuban and Venezuelan entities. Furthermore, the WHO will oversee the work of the personnel assigned to the cooperation projects of the two nations.
