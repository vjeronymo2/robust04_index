November 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

'TEACHING AMERICA' LESSONS

Teach for America is a valuable program that should be pursued.

Despite the many arguments against the program, the advantages outweigh the defaults.

Some critics argue that the cost is too much. Like any business, one must invest in order to succeed. Others have disapproved because of the inexperience of the teachers. This may be true, but a with all new teachers, time will ease the tensions and facilitate the teaching process.

As a student, I believe that the earlier people finish their educations and enter the professional fields, the more zeal and love they (will) have for their jobs.

KATHIE H. KIM

San Diego
