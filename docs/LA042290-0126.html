April 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

FREEWAY REPAIRS ANNOUNCED

Caltrans said it plans the following freeway work in the Southeast / Long Beach area this week:

ARTESIA FREEWAY (91)

1. Cerritos: Westbound Bloomfield Avenue on-ramp, Monday and Tuesday, 8:30 a.m. to 2 p.m., ditch cleaning.

2. Cerritos: Eastbound / southbound Norwalk Boulevard on-ramp, Tuesday, 1:15 to 2:45 p.m., stencil repair.

3. Cerritos: Eastbound Bloomfield Avenue off-ramp, Wednesday, 9 to 11 a.m., stencil repair.

4. Cerritos: Eastbound Carmenita Avenue off-ramp, Wednesday, noon to 2 p.m., stencil repair.

LONG BEACH FREEWAY (710)

5. Commerce: Northbound 710 Freeway connector to northbound Santa Ana Freeway (I-5), today, 6:30 to 11:30 a.m., guardrail repair.

6. Compton: Southbound / eastbound Alondra Boulevard on-ramp, Friday, noon to 1:30 p.m., stencil repair.

7. Long Beach: Southbound / westbound Pacific Coast Highway on-ramp, Thursday, 9 to 10:15 a.m. and 11 a.m. to 1:30 p.m., stencil repair.

8. Long Beach: Southbound / westbound Willow Street off-ramp, Friday, 9 to 11 a.m., stencil repair.

SAN DIEGO FREEWAY (405)

9. Long Beach: Northbound and southbound No. 1 lanes, Pacific Avenue to Dominguez Channel, Monday through Friday, 9 a.m. to 2 p.m., glare screen repair.

SAN GABRIEL RIVER FREEWAY (605)

10. Pico Rivera: Southbound Slauson Avenue on-ramp, Thursday, 9:30 a.m. to 3 p.m, guardrail repair.

11. Santa Fe Springs: Northbound Telegraph Road off-ramp, Wednesday, 9:30 a.m. to 3 p.m., guardrail repair.

SANTA ANA FREEWAY (5)

12. Norwalk: Southbound Rosecrans Avenue off-ramp, Tuesday, 8:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m., landscaping.

13. Santa Fe Springs: Northbound Valley View Avenue off-ramp and on-ramp, Wednesday and Thursday, 8:30 a.m. to 2 p.m., ditch cleaning.
