BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lev Rumyantsev] [Text] St. Petersburg June 10 TASS -- Vietnamese Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet arrived in St. Petersburg on an unofficial visit on Friday. On Saturday he will meet with governor of the Leningrad region Aleksander belyakov and St. Petersburg Mayor Anatoliy Sobchak. During his visit the Vietnamese prime minister will visit the Hermitage, the St. Peter Fortress and other places. On June 12 Vo Van Kiet will leave for Kazakhstan and on June 15- 17 arrive in Russia on an official visit to meet with Russian President Boris Yeltsin and hold talks with Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin to discuss bilateral relations and international issues.
