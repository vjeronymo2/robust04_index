Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 24) TURAN--Today Azerbaijan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs sent the note to Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Azerbaijan makes a protest due to illegal arrival of Deputy of the Russian Duma, Nikolay Lysenko, in Khankendi [Stepanakert] and his statements which are aimed against sovereignty and territorial integrity of Azerbaijan. "Such statements of Russian Deputy can spoil Azerbaijani-Russian relations", says the document. The leader of the Russian National-Republican Party, Nikolai Lysenko, said on March 18 in Yerevan that "Russia must recognize Nagorno-Karabakh republic". He added that "Nagorno-Karabakh has the right to exist as a sovereign republic. Azerbaijan's aggression against Karabakh would have been impossible if Turkey did not back it. Russia, Armenia and Nagorno-Karabakh have common geostrategic interests".
