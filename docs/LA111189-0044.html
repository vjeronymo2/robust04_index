November 11, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

DESERT-INYO LARGE LEAGUE

Tehachapi 42, Paraclete 12 -- The Warriors (9-1, 5-0) scored on their first seven possessions at Antelope Valley College to finish the league season undefeated.

Paraclete (2-8, 0-5) scored on a 52-yard pass from Chuck Lang to Jorge Ordaz and on Lang's 27-yard fumble return on the game's final play.
