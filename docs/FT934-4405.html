931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / International Capital Markets: Record Chicago futures volume The Chicago Board of Trade, the world's busiest futures exchange, posted an all-time single-month volume record in November, with a turnover of 19.7m contracts, up 64.7 per cent on a year ago. For the first 11 months of this year the board reported volume up 18 per cent at 165.8m contracts, above its previous world record of 154mfor all of 1990. The Chicago Mercantile Exchange is also reported record year-to-date figures, with 13.6m contracts in November, up 22 per cent.
