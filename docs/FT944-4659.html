941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Northamber rises The recent tentative improvements seen by Northamber, the computer peripherals distributer, accelerated during the six months to October 31 helping it to pre-tax profits of Pounds 536,000, against losses of Pounds 54,000. Turnover rose 62 per cent to Pounds 74m (Pounds 45.7m) and the company has decided to introduce an interim dividend with a 0.5p payment from earnings per share of 2.07p (losses 0.3p).
