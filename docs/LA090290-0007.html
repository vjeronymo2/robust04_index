September 2, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: GRILL CRAZY

Pathe Entertainment is stil trying o get clearances to use "Harley Davidson and the Marlboro Man" as a movie title. But the company has nailed down its stars: They'll play the title characters, two buddies who reunite to save an old pal's restaurant- "The Rock 'n' Roll Grill-when it's threatened by Aryan drug dealers.

Filming begins this fall, with Simon Wincer directing for producer Jere Henshaw. John M. Wilson
