930224 FT 24 FEB 93 / UK Company News: TR European Net asset value of TR European Growth Trust amounted to 99.2p at December 31, compared with 97.9p six months earlier. Total revenue rose from Pounds 530,000 to Pounds 661,000 in the interim period and revenue after tax emerged at Pounds 332,000 (Pounds 219,000). Earnings per share were 0.91p (0.6p).
