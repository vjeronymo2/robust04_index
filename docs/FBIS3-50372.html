Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Bosnia-Herzegovina Government held a session today that addressed the privatization of the social property. The government believes that the identification of all social property should be carried out with the aim of its privatization. At the end of the open part of the session, Bosnia-Herzegovina Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic informed all present that there are indications that major Croatian Army forces are concentrated on the Bosnia-Herzegovina territory, around Prozor, to be precise. He proposed that the government requests additional information about the presence of the Croatian Armed Forces on Bosnia-Herzegovina soil to facilitate a decision about further measures, that is to say, that the government could decide in the course of tomorrow whether to demand an urgent session of the UN Security Council.
