Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 17/3/1994 -- The special envoy of Russian President, Vladlen Vasev arrived last night in Chisinau to mediate the Dniester conflict. Tomorrow, 17 March, he will meet with President Snegur, who returns today from Brussels. A visit to Tiraspol is also scheduled for tomorrow, where Vasev will meet decisionmakers of the self-proclaimed Dniester republic. Sources from the Russian Embassy told BASA that Vasev will stay in Moldova for about a week. During the summer of 1992 military conflict Vasev was in the group of Russian representatives as an observer in the four-party mechanism for solving the conflict. On 10 March, communicating about the meeting in Moscow of Dniester President Igor Smirnov with officials from the State Duma of Russia, Tiraspol radio asserted that Vasev was nominated as mediator at the insistence of the Dniester leader.
