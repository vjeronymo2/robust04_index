Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "National News Hookup" program] [Text] At the invitation of State President Jiang Zemin, ROK President Kim Yong-sam began an official visit to China on 26 March. This afternoon, President Jiang Zemin presided over a ceremony at the Great Hall of the People to warmly welcome President Kim Yong-sam and his wife. Present at the welcoming ceremony were Tian Jiyun, vice chairman of the National People's Congress Standing Committee; Qian Qichen, vice premier of the State Council and minister of foreign affairs; Wu Jichuan, chairman of the Chinese Government Reception Committee and minister of posts and communications; Li Ganliu, vice chairman of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Li Zhongli, minister of finance; Wang Zhongyu, minister in charge of the State Economic and Trade Commission; Liu Zhongde, minister of culture; and other officials. Principal members of President Kim Yong-sam's entourage included Minister of Foreign Affairs Han Sung-chu. Born in 1927 on Geoje Island in the ROK's South Kyongsang Province, Kim Yong-sam was elected a member of the 14th parliament, president of the Democratic Liberal Party [DLP], and DLP presidential candidate in 1992. He later became the 14th president of the ROK in December 1992.
