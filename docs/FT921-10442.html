920206 FT 06 FEB 92 / UK Company News: British Thornton in the black British Thornton Holdings, a manufacturer of educational and scientific furniture, returned profits of Pounds 20,000 pre-tax for the half year to October 31. The result, which compared with previous losses of Pounds 413,000, was achieved from turnover of Pounds 1.1m (Pounds 354,000) and excluded benefits from acquisitions made in October. Earnings per share emerged at 0.1p (losses 1.2p). Directors expect to pay a maiden dividend following the full year results. The group moved from the USM to a full listing in October. With the proceeds of last year's rights issue, positive cash flow and a strong balance sheet it is actively seeking acquisitions to expand its core businesses.
