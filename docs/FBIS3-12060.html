Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] After talking with Finance Minister Fernando Henrique Cardoso, Sao Paulo Governor Luiz Antonio Fleury Filho said he will not support Cardoso's presidential candidacy. He will instead support the Brazilian Democratic Movement Party [PMDB] candidate. He favors holding primaries at the PMDB to choose a consensus candidate for the party. He added, however, that he will campaign for Orestes Quercia. There are three candidates for the PMDB primaries: Orestes Quercia, Governor Roberto Requiao, and former President Jose Sarney, who is a senator.
