September 8, 1990, Saturday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: EL RIO;

BOY, 15, ACCIDENTALLY SHOT BY A FRIEND

A 15-year-old El Rio boy underwent emergency surgery Friday after being accidentally shot by a friend who was examining a handgun, a Ventura County sheriff's spokesman said.

Joe Adane suffered arm and chest wounds in the 11:15 a.m. shooting on Balboa Street, Senior Sheriff's Deputy Steve Joneson said.

Adane was listed in fair condition after surgery at St. John's Regional Medical Center, a hospital spokeswoman said.

According to the Sheriff's Department, Raymond Coronel, 15, was holding a .22-caliber pistol as he and Adane examined the weapon. The gun discharged, and a bullet struck Adane in the arm and entered his chest.

Joneson said that investigators have deemed the shooting an accident and that no charges have been filed. CHRISTOPHER PUMMER
