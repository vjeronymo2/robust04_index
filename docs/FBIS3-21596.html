Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "No to Rally Passions Within Troops!"] [Text] Ukraine's minister of defense Army General V. Radetskyy visited a garrison stationed in Vinnytsya Oblast. During the meeting with officers and generals at the headquarters of Ukraine's Air Force, Vitaliy Radetskyy stressed that the merger of the Air Force and Air Defense had been suspended. It is necessary to work on this problem as a promising one. The defense minister presented the new Air Force commander -- Lieutenant General Volodymyr Antonets, appointed to the post by a Ukrainian presidential edict. "We are military people and must fulfill our supreme commander's order," commented the minister upon the changes in the personnel. "Rally passions within troops are inadmissible...." Radetskyy also met with officers of the command of the 43d Strategic Rocket Troops and stressed once again that Ukraine is a missile and space power and that even after the program for nuclear disarmament is completed, missile specialists will have to fulfill specific work for the defense of our country.
