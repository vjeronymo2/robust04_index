930525 FT 25 MAY 93 / Seeboard staff accept pay deal SEEBOARD, the electricity supplier, expects to save about Pounds 1.5m a year after the decision by its 900 retail staff to accept new pay and conditions from June 1. The agreement includes market-related salaries, performance related pay and more flexible working patterns. It follows the introduction of revised terms of employment for staff engaged on electrical contracting work.
