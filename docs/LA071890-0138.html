July 18, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Provena Foods Inc.: The Santa Ana specialty food-processing and distributing company reported net income of $196,223 for the second quarter, contrasted with a loss of $68,439 for the period last year. Sales jumped 30% to $10.5 million from $8.1 million. Net income for the first half of the year more than tripled to $395,028, and sales rose 26% to $19.9 million from $15.8 million.
