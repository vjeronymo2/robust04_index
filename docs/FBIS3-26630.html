Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Jiang Zeming's speech at a get-together for Spring Festival group greetings in Beijing on 10 February 1994] [Text] [Video shows medium close-up shot of Jiang wearing a Mao-type jacket, standing in front of a bank on microphones] Comrades and friends: Now we begin the get-together for 1994 Spring Festival group greetings. [applause] Today is a traditional festival of the people of our country. On this joyous day, I would like to wish you a happy new year on behalf of the party Central Committee and the State Council. [applause] Present at today's get-together for group greetings are more than 4,000 people, including leading party and state comrades; responsible comrades of central party, government, army, and mass organizations; retired comrades who have made contributions to the socialist revolution and construction; responsible persons of various democratic parties and patriotic personages without party affiliation; and representatives of minority nationalities; as well as foreign experts assisting our country in construction and representatives from Beijing's various circles. We have happily got together here to usher in the Spring Festival and to spend the joyous holiday. [applause] Now I would like to invite Li Peng, Standing Committee member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and Premier of the State Council, to speak. [video cuts to show the audience applauding]
