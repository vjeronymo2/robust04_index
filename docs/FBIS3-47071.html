Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 10 KYODO -- Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana Madariaga will visit Japan from Friday to Sunday on the first leg of an Asian tour that will also take him to South Korea, Thailand and Malaysia, Foreign Ministry officials said Monday [8 January]. Solana, 51, will hold talks with his Japanese counterpart Tsutomu Hata on developments in Europe and the Asia-Pacific region, the officials said. He will leave for South Korea on Sunday.
