BFN [Text] Yasin Hamid, 22, of al-Birah who was wounded during the dispersal of stone throwers yesterday has died of his wounds. His funeral took place in al-Birah today. Riots broke out in al-Birah. The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] imposed a curfew on the Qalandiyah refugee camp. Our correspondent Amir Hay notes that 12 residents were wounded in the incidents which took place between the camp residents and the security forces. [Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 1900 GMT on 20 April adds that "two Israelis, residents of the Qatif Bloc, were slightly wounded in a shooting by terrorists who ambushed them on the Kisufim-Khan Yunus road this evening. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that the wounded drove their car by themselves to the Kisufim roadblock and from there they were evacuated to the Beersheba Soroka Hospital. IDF units are conducting searches."]
