940128 FT 28 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: Gales hit Britain Houses were damaged and lorries blown over as gales of up to 70mph swept across Britain. In Scotland, two lorries were blown on to their sides on the M8 motorway and the roof was blown off a building in Glasgow. In Kent, a lorry carrying 26 tons of mackerel was blown on to its roof.
