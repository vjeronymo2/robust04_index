931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Abtrust New Thai net assets at 126.35p Abtrust New Thai Investment Trust had a net asset value, unadjusted for warrants, of 126.35p per share at August 31. The figure showed a marginal fall on the value of 133.02p at the trust's February year-end, but a year-on-year increase of almost 85 per cent on the corresponding 68.48p. Net revenue for the six months to end-August dipped to Pounds 98,857 (Pounds 129,241) but Mr Alan Henderson, chairman, said the full year figures should be satisfactory.
