Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Phnom Penh AKP January 17 -- A high-level delegation of the Royal Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia left here this morning for Beijing for a five-day official visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese Premier Li Peng. The 25-strong delegation, led by the First Prime Minister Prince Norodom Ranariddh and Second Prime Minister Hun Sen consists of senior government figures including the ministers of foreign affairs, industry, commerce, agriculture, planning, public work and transport. The visit is aimed at strengthening bilateral relations between the two countries. [passage omitted]
