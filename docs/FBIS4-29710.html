BFN [Text] A friendship visiting group of the People's Liberation Army [PLA] of China led by Colonel General Wang Ke, commander of the Shenyang Military Area Command of the PLA, arrived in Pyongyang by plane on 21 June. The delegation was greeted at the airport by Li Chong-san, general of the Korean People's Army; other generals and officers; and Qiao Zonghuai, Chinese ambassador to our country. A banquet was given to the delegation at the Ministry of People's Armed Forces on the evening of 21 June.
