930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / International Company News In Brief: Moody's Investors Service Moody's Investors Service, the credit rating agency, yesterday raised its debt ratings on heavily indebted Time Warner. Some Dollars 12bn of borrowings are affected. Time Warner's senior debt rating goes from Ba2 to Ba1, writes Martin Dickson. Moody's said a proposed Dollars 2.5bn investment by telephone group US West in Time Warner's entertainment business, together with anticipated cash flow and revenue growth in the consolidated Time Warner business, should result in a gradual improvement in the group's highly leveraged financial structure.
