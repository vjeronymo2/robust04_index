December 16, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

FIFTH HOLOCAUST FILM

As Patrick Goldstein reported in his Dec. 7 article, "The Holocaust -- A Tough Sell for the Holidays?," distributing a Holocaust-related film poses special challenges during the holiday season.

Unfortunately, Goldstein has added to the challenge by an oversight. There are not just "four films simultaneously in the market with Holocaust themes." There is also "Weapons of the Spirit," an award-winning, feature documentary that tells the story of a Christian village in France that saved 5,000 Jews from the Nazis.

After runs in New York City and Los Angeles, the film is doing well in Boston, San Francisco and Philadelphia. It will be gradually opened in all major markets throughout the holidays and into the new year. (Incidentally, "Weapons of the Spirit" was circulating under that title long before the title of "Triumph of the Spirit" was announced.)
