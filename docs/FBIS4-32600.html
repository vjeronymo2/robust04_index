BFN [Text] Italian Foreign Minister Antonio Martino has held talks in Sarajevo with Bosnia-Herzegovina Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic. After the talks Prime Minister Silajdzic said that the external borders of Bosnia-Herzegovina cannot be haggled over and cannot be the subject of any political negotiations. According to him, this message was clearly conveyed to the Italian foreign minister. It was stressed that only an internal transformation of Bosnia-Herzegovina can be discussed. The Italian minister said that he has come to Sarajevo to conduct preparations for the September meeting of G-7 and Russia [in Naples]. He did not comment on the current proposals on the resolution of the crisis in Bosnia-Herzegovina, but said that the situation will be discussed in detail at the Naples meeting.
