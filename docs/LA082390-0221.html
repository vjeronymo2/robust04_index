August 23, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

HOT TOPICS: SOME TIPS ON SCHOOL FASHIONS

Do you already have the perfect outfit for the first day of school laid out on your bed? Or are you still stalking the malls, hunting for the jeans and T-shirt that will show everyone you are fashion-hip and socially conscious?

Don't worry, high school students from around the county have offered some fashion tips for this fall. Hot Topics asked, "What will be cool to wear back to school?"

"Neon shorts and shirts -- lots of sporting stuff."

Adam Smith, 14, freshman, La Habra

"Any clothes from Wet Seal."

Trisha Michaelson, 13, freshman, El Toro

"Skateboarding T-shirts like H-street."

Rick Bohlen, 16, junior, Savanna

"Guess and Esprit jean skirts."

Tracy Hoon, 14, sophomore, Esperanza

"Volleyball shorts and Oakleys."

Scott Bloom, 15, sophomore, Santa Margarita

"Quiksilver T-shirts."

Mike Medor, 13, freshman, Dana Hills

"Loud colors and more sophisticated, chic-ish things."

Hillary Zirkle, 15 sophomore, Huntington Beach

"A black-leather jacket."

Mari Tsurimoto, 16, senior, La Quinta

"Dress clothes like Stussy, stuff that you can look nice in."

Shane Bohlen, 14, freshman, Savanna
