930408 FT 08 APR 93 / BR sells non-core business for Pounds 5m TRANSMARK, British Rail's international consultancy business, has been sold for about Pounds 5m to Halcrow, the engineering consultants, as part of the privatisation of BR's non-core businesses. Transmark, which has worked on more than 200 rail projects in 30 countries, made an operating surplus of Pounds 1.1m ona turnover of Pounds 10m in its last financial year. Halcrow said that Transmark's background in rail operation and design would compliment its own engineering and transportation skills.
