931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / International Company News: Sprint improves to Dollars 122m for quarter SPRINT, the US long-distance and local telephone group, lifted net income to Dollars 122.2m, or 35 cents, from Dollars 119.7m, or 35 cents, last year. However, it said that income from continuing operations totalled Dollars 178m, or 51 cents, excluding special items. This was a 42 per cent increase from last year's Dollars 125m, or 37 cents. Revenues rose 9 per cent to Dollars 2.87bn.
