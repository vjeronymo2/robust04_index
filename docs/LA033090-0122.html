March 30, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Terry L. Talley has been named the new chief financial officer of Robinson's department store chain.

Talley, 46, currently is the senior vice president-finance for a sister company, the 16-store May Co., Ohio, chain. His appointment takes effect April 15. Talley replaces Stephen Douglass, 40, who in February became chairman of May Co., Ohio. Both Robinson's, a Los Angeles-based department store chain with 29 stores, and May Co., Ohio, are owned by St. Louis-based May Department Stores.
