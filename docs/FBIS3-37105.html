Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow February 12 TASS -- The 3rd border post of the Pyanj border unit was attacked by a group of militants on February 11. The border detail and reinforcement defeated the enemy. A border guard was wounded, sources at the Russian border troops' press service told ITAR-TASS here today. Another fight was reported upon the reinforcement's return to the border post. The attackers were put under fire and defeated. The border section is now under full control. The 12th border post of the Moscow border unit was repeatedly shelled from the rear on the same day. The militants used fire arms, rockets and grenade launchers. All fire nests of the attackers were suppressed. The losses of militants are being specified. There are no casualties amongst border guards.
