October 18, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

UCLA TO PLAY USC IN PAC-10 VOLLEYBALL

No. 2 UCLA will play host to No. 6 USC in a Pacific 10 Conference women's volleyball match at 7 tonight at Pauley Pavilion.

The Bruins (14-1 overall, 8-0 in the Pac-10) lead the series against the Trojans, 32-28. The Trojans (13-4, 7-1) began the season ranked 16th.
