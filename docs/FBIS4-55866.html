BFN [Excerpt] A military parade was held and the reconstructed part of Bosut barracks [in Vinkovci] was consecrated as the Croatian Army's 5th A Brigade marked the forthcoming Statehood Day of the Republic of Croatia today. Despite the fact that it was formed on 25 October 1992, the 5th Guards' Brigade Hawks has an impressive list of military achievements and it is made up of experienced soldiers from Slavonia. As General Janko Bobetko [chief of the Croatian of General Staff] stressed, this brigade earned its historic diploma in Vukovar and all the battlefields where Croatia was defended.
