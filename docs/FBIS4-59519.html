BFN [Excerpt] A responsible source at the Ministry of Defense has denied what was transmitted by Sanaa radio in its news bulletin of 3:00 PM this afternoon, that is, the forces of al-Ahmar gang had advanced towards al-Dali' and al-Anad and restored the al-Musaymir Center to legitimacy according to its claim. The source added that the report transmitted by the said radio and attributed to a so-called responsible source in the Ministry of Defense about the advance of the forces of the al-Ahmar gang in many directions, was not based on any truth at all. It is just part of the psychological warfare and misleading media reporting. It is cheap lies to which the news media of the al-Ahmar gang had become accustomed. It is a gang headed by a man who is not restrained by anyone. [passage omitted]
