Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] Several people, helped by about 1,000 sympathizers of the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia and Vladimir Meciar, who gathered in front of the parliament building, have physically assaulted Hungarian coalition deputies Vojtech Bugar, chairman of the Hungarian Christian Democratic Movement [MKHD], and Pal Csaky, chairman of the MKHD-Coexistance parliamentary group of deputies. Asked by a reporter from the Press Agency of the Slovak Republic why they did not ask for the protection of the policemen present, Vojtech Bugar said that the policeman whom he addressed said to him to gather 10,000 policemen and then he would oblige him. Apart from slogans in support of Vladimir Meciar and the government, citizens who gathered in front of the parliament building chanted various appeals such as: Tomorrow we will take to the streets. Approximately 300 people gathered in Primacialni Square in front of President Kovac's residence tonight, calling on him to resign.
