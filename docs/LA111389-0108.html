November 13, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

HARCOURT LOSES $102 MILLION

Harcourt Brace Jovanovich Inc. today reported a loss for the third quarter as it took charges related to its schoolbook division and for refinancing its huge debt.

The net loss totaled $102.7 million, compared to a profit of $60.2 million in last year's quarter. Revenues fell to $463.2 million from $470.1 million.

Harcourt said it expects to report a large fourth-quarter gain on the previously announced sale of its theme parks and related holdings to Anheuser-Busch Cos. Inc. for $1.1 billion. The sale is scheduled to close within a month.
