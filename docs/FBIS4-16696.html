BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 19 Apr -- Talks began here today between Yasir 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the Executive Committee of the PLO, and Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev. Before the meeting began, Kozyrev expressed gratitude to the Palestinian leader for his "courageous act," which made progress possible in the process toward a peaceful settlement in the Middle East.
