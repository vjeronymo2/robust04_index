BFN [Report by Raul Sanchez Carrillo from the "Early Morning" newscast] [Text] Now, we are at the Political Council of the National Executive Committee of the Institutional Revolutionary Party [PRI]. Ignacio Pichardo Pagaza was sworn in as secretary general of the PRI National Executive Committee a few minutes ago. Ignacio Pichardo Pagaza, who was former governor of Mexico State, will replace Jose Luis La Madrid Sauza, who resigned yesterday to accept the post of campaign coordinator for PRI presidential candidate Ernesto Zedillo Ponce de Leon. Here at the Political Council, headed by Fernando Ortiz Arana as president of the National Executive Committee of the PRI, the election of Ignacio Pichardo Pagaza was unanimous. Representatives of the three groups that make up the party brought him to this room where the event is being held. Currently, an agreement is being read which states that on orders from the candidate, Zedillo Ponce de Leon, himself, the mourning for the death of Luis Donaldo Colosio, who was cowardly assassinated, will be perpetuated within the party. His political ideology will be respected and fulfilled so as to strengthen the new presidential candidate.
