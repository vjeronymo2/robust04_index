940422 FT 22 APR 94 / UK Company News: Ethical Holdings Ethical Holdings, the pharmaceuticals group which is quoted on the Nasdaq market in the US, reported increased net losses of Pounds 2.36m for the six months to February 28, against Pounds 933,000. Revenues advanced by 17 per cent from Pounds 2.96m to Pounds 3.47m. Losses per share were 17p, against 10p.
