BFN [Text] This morning, Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' received Mr. Kazem (Khawansari), director of the Middle East and North Africa Department at the Iranian Foreign Ministry. The meeting was attended by Walid Rajab, director of Asia Department; and Hasan Akhtari, the Iranian ambassador in Damascus. They talked about regional developments and relations between the two friends -- the Syrian Arab Republic and the Islamic Republic of Iran.
