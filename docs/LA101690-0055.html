October 16, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

PATERSON ON REFUSING TO GO TO SAUDI ARABIA

Three cheers for Paterson, who refused to join Bush's Raiders for a free tankful of gas. Unless that person who directs people to watch his babbling lips is impeached now, God help us all.

I love the Marine Corps and my country which I served for 30 years, 1946-1976.

LOUIS DE LUCREZIA

Los Angeles
