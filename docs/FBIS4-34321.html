BFN [Text] Havana, 18 Jun (AFP) -- Cuban police sources reported that at least eight of the 118 Cuban refugees who have been at the Belgian ambassador's residence in Havana since 18 May left this afternoon. The same sources said that most of those who left were women and children. Reporters near the residence verified that there was more movement than usual of diplomatic vehicles, but they were unable to determine the exact number of people leaving the residence. Another 21 persons have taken refuge at the German Embassy ,and eight are at the Chilean Consulate, both located in the same neighborhood of Miramar. These people hope to obtain political asylum abroad.
