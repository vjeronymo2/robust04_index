940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / Swastika girl injured herself The 17-year-old disabled German girl who claimed three neo-Nazis carved a swastika into her cheek this week, apparently did it herself, the Saxony-Anhalt prosecutor's office said yesterday, AP reports from Halle. News of the attack stunned Germans. Politicians demanded quick police action, President Richard von Weizsaecker condemned the attack and 10,000 demonstrated in Halle.
