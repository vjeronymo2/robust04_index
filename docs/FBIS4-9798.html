BFN [From the "Early Morning" newscast] [Text] During his visit to Agualeguas, Nuevo Leon, President Carlos Salinas de Gortari sent a message of unity and harmony to all Mexicans. In reference to Jose Cordoba Montoya, the president indicated that he is a Mexican citizen who works with devotion and his experience will help spur on the IDB [Inter-American Development Bank], an institution that is of interest to Latin America. Later, President Salinas was asked his opinion on the appointment of the new PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party] presidential candidate. [Begin recording] [Salinas] Most of all it is a message of unity, encouragement and harmony. [Unidentified speaker] Welcome, Carlos. [Salinas] Thank you. [Speaker] God bless you. [Unidentified reporter] Mr. President, with respect to [words indistinct] of your adviser [Jose] Cordoba Montoya, what were the reasons? [Salinas] Mr. Jose Cordoba is a Mexican citizen who has worked loyally. I am very fond of him and value his services, experience, and capability as related to economic issues. I am quite sure these attributes will allow him to contribute positively to an institution such as the IDB which is very important to Latin America's economic and financial interests. [Reporter] Is there any advice you could give him? [Salinas] [words indistinct] [Reporter] Are there any recommendations you could make to Mr. Montoya now that he is about to embark on another duty? [Salinas] Only one. I can only recommend that he use his great capabilities and experience in [words indistinct]. [Reporter] Mr. President, does the mourning for Mr. Colosio's death continue? [Salinas] Friday was declared a day of national mourning as a result of his death.... [Reporter, interrupting] Is this why you have to return? [Salinas] Most of all because of fondness for a lost friend. [Reporter] Mr. President, what can you say about the country's current situation? [Salinas] I repeat, my message is one of unity, encouragement, and harmony. [end recording] Later on, President Salinas was asked about the nomination of the new PRI presidential candidate to which he pointed out that during 1993 Mr. Zedillo had been one of the leading contenders and that he is an intelligent person committed to democracy and the legal order. The president added that Mr. Zedillo will be an element of unity and harmony.
