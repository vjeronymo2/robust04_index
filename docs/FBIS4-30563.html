BFN [Text] Panmunjom, June 28 (KCNA) -- A preliminary contact for North-South top-level talks began at the Southern portion of Panmunjom at 10 a.m. today. Present at the contact on the North side were chief delegate Kim Yong-sun, chairman of the Reunification Policy Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, and delegates An Pyong-su, vice-chairman of the Committee for the Peaceful Reunification of the Fatherland, and Paek Nam-chun, chief councillor of the Administration Council. The contact is going on.
