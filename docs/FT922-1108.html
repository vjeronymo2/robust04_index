920624 FT 24 JUN 92 / Derbyshire police given deadline DERBYSHIRE police force was yesterday given until the end of this year to achieve standards high enough to regain the certificate of efficiency it was refused by the Inspectorate of Constabulary. The force is take immediate action to deal with problems identified in the inspectorate's report by Mr Geoffrey Dear, inspector of constabulary. In return Mr Kenneth Clarke, home secretary, will not withhold Pounds 37m in government funds nor amalgamate Derbyshire with another force. The report said that policing in Derbyshire was 'inadequate' as a result of a decade of underfunding.
