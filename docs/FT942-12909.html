940427 FT 27 APR 94 / International Company News: Deutsche Bank wins banking licence Deutsche Bank has been granted a branch banking licence in Australia. The German company said this would give it more flexibility in expanding its wholesale and investment banking business in the country, although it stressed that it had no intention of moving into the retail banking market. It said that Bain & Co, in which it has a 100 per cent interest, would continue to operate Deutsche Bank's investment banking operations in Australia.
