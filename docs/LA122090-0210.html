December 20, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

MONKEY BUSINESS: The Santa Ana Zoo isn't happy to have this monkey off its back. The zoo has decided to say goodby to two very rare primates -- red-backed squirrel monkeys -- and send them to Phoenix. . . . There, the two monkeys will join a third friend recently captured, so the Phoenix Zoo can start the nation's first captive-breeding program for the monkeys. Santa Ana Zoo officials say it's their small way of helping out a species that has almost vanished from the world. Jon Nalick and Mark Landsbaum
