BFN [Text] Beijing, May 19 (XINHUA) -- The Sino-British airport committee will hold a meeting in Hong Kong on May 20, a Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman said here today. The spokesman said that China will, as always, in accordance with the memorandum of understanding on the construction of the new airport in Hong Kong, make continuous efforts so that the two sides can reach an agreement on the financial arrangement of the new airport at an early date.
