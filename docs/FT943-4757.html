940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / UK Company News: TR Euro Growth TR European Growth Trust achieved a 45 per cent increase in net asset value per share from 117.64p to 170.99p over the 12 months to June 30. The fully diluted figure rose 42 per cent from 116.13p to 164.62p. For the year to the end of June net revenue fell from Pounds 1.01m to Pounds 985,000 for earnings per share of 2.48p (2.61p). Directors propose maintaining the single final dividend at 1.7p.
