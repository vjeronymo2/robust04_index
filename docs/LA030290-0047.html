March 2, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP ALBUM CHART;

ABDUL-JACKSON DUEL CONTINUES

In the long-running battle among dance-music divas Paula Abdul and Janet Jackson for pop-chart supremacy, Abdul continues to reign on the Billboard magazine pop album chart. But in the next week or two, Jackson could unseat Abdul.

Jackson's "Escapade" single, currently No. 1 on the pop singles' chart, is hotter than Abdul's "Opposites Attract" single, which is No. 6 and sliding down the chart. Also, Jackson is getting a lot of publicity because of her first tour. Buoyed by her Grammy haul, Bonnie Raitt's "Nick of Time" album vaulted from No. 40 to No. 22.

* The Polish female vocalist Basia, who sings both soul and Latin-flavored songs, is no flash-in-the-pan.
