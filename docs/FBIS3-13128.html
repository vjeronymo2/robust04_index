Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 Mar (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak and Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih held talks at the Presidential Office in Heliopolis this evening immediately after the Yemeni president's arrival in Cairo. The conferees reviewed the latest developments in the region in light of the current events in Yemen and the possibility of resolving the differences between the rival parties in that country. They also exchanged views on world and regional issues of mutual interest.
