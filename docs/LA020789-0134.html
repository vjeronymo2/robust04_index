February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

SUNSET BEACH;

DRIVER HELD AFTER CACHE OF MARIJUANA IS FOUND

A driver in Sunset Beach was arrested after 100 pounds of marijuana was found in the trunk of his car, California Highway Patrol officers said Monday.

John W. Robinson II, 33, a regular patron of the Best Western Sunset Beach Inn in Sunset Beach, had a minor accident Friday night in which his rented 1988 Bonneville struck another vehicle on Pacific Coast Highway, CHP Traffic Officer Richard Hoffpauer said.

Robinson returned to the motel, where an acquaintance noticed that he appeared drunk and took Robinson's car keys from him. When Robinson asked to get something from his trunk, the acquaintance saw the marijuana and notified police.
