3. Section 201.52(b) is revised to read as follows: §201.52 Extended credit for depository institutions. * * * * * (b) Extended credit. For extended credit to depository institutions under §201.3(c), for credit outstanding for more than 30 days, a flexible rate will be charged that takes into account rates on market sources of funds, but in no case will the rate charged be less than the rate for adjustment credit, as set out in §201.51, plus one-half percentage point. At the discretion of the Federal Reserve Bank, the 30-day time period may be shortened. By order of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, November 18, 1994. William W.
