BFN [Statement by Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa at the House of Councillors' Foreign Affairs Committee on 20 June -- recorded] [Text] I believe it is quite impossible for the United States to take the position of ignoring altogether what has occurred in the past. On Japan's part, as members of this committee just pointed out, since the production of nuclear weapons on the Korean peninsula represents a direct threat, we maintain that there is a need to continue efforts to verify even the past operational records of nuclear reactors.
