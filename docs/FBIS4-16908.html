BFN [Text] A delegation of Ukrainian naval servicemen, headed by Rear Admiral Mykola Kostrov, deputy commander in chief of Ukraine's Navy, is currently visiting the United States. The press center of the Ukrainian Navy reports in connection with this that during their trip, members of the delegation will visit military education facilities, educational centers, and U.S. Marine training grounds. Ukrainian officers have been offered the possibility to observe a joint training exercise of U.S. Navy personnel and Marines on the Atlantic coast. All expenses connected with the delegation's trip are being covered by the U.S. side.
