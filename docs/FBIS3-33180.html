Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Representatives of the State Committee for Economic Cooperation and of the Sao Paulo Foundation for Support of Research, have signed in this capital a technical-scientific cooperation agreement. The document was signed by Ernesto Melendez, minister president of the State Committee for Economic Cooperation; Roberto Rivas, director of the America Department of the State Committee for Economic Cooperation; Roberto Muller, secretary of the Sao Paulo Science, Technology, and Economic Development Department; and Jose Roberto Perez [name as heard], science director of the Brazilian institute. For three years the two parties will contribute to promoting cooperation in all areas of science and technology. They also agreed to extend invitations to researchers and technology experts to participate in the research processes and as lecturers in scientific conferences.
