Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Feb. 2, IRNA -- In a meeting here Wednesday with Deputy Prime Minister of Western Australia Hendy Kuan, Iran's deputy foreign minister for Asia-Pacific affairs, Allaeddin Borujerdi, said Iran was ready to export nonoil products to Australia. He said Iran looks forward to balanced trade reltions with Australia. The visiting Australian official said his country would exert efforts for ensuring balanced trade ties with Iran. He also expressed hope that relations between Iran and Australia would continuously develop.
