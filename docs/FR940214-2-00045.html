Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration Suspension Lifted; Laboratory Again Meets Minimum Standards to Engage in Urine Drug Testing for Federal Agencies AGENCY: Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration, HHS (Formerly: National Institute on Drug Abuse, ADAMHA, HHS). ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: The Department of Health and Human Services notifies Federal Agencies of the laboratories currently certified to meet standards of Subpart C of Mandatory Guidelines for Federal Workplace Drug Testing Programs (53 FR 11986) dated April 11, 1988. The following laboratory's certification to engage in urine drug testing for Federal agencies was suspended on April 8, 1993 (58 FR 19835, April 16, 1993) and was reinstated effective February 2, 1994: Eagle Forensic Laboratory, Inc., 950 North Federal Highway, suite 308, Pompano Beach, FL 33062, 305&hyph;946&hyph;4324.
