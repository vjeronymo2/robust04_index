Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Russia intends to do everything possible to facilitate the negotiations between the leadership of Tajikistan and the opposition, Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev declared on arrival in Dushanbe from Vladivostok today. Another important task for Russia is to support Tajikistan as a CIS state, the minister said. Kozyrev is meeting in Dushanbe today with the Tajik foreign minister and the foreign ministers of a number of other Central Asian states. He pointed out in particular that there must be no wrecking of the negotiations with the opposition as happened last week following the killing of Deputy Prime Minister Nazarshoyev. Kozyrev is convinced that, together with the Tajik leadership, he will succeed in settling this issue, INTERFAX reports.
