BFN [Text] Sanaa, 27 Mar (INA)--A member of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], a party to the ruling coalition, was assassinated the day before yesterday [25 March]. An INA correspondent in Sanaa today cited a YSP official source as saying that the body of Haydar 'Abdallah Ghalib was found near the University of Sanaa after he was stabbed to death by unknown persons.
