940203 FT 03 FEB 94 / Four fight for OECD post Germany put forward Mr Lorenz Schomerus, a senior Bonn Economics Ministry official, as a candidate for the post of secretary-general of the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development, writes Peter Norman, Economics Editor. Other candidates for the OECD post are Lord Lawson, the former UK chancellor, Mr Donald Johnston, a former Canadian economics minister, and Mr Jean-Claude Paye, the incumbent.
