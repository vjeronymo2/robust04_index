930320 FT 20 MAR 93 / Tory by-election candidate chosen MR JULIAN DAVIDSON, a consultant in design, printing and advertising, was yesterday selected to fight the forthcoming Newbury by-election for the Conservatives. He was chosen from a shortlist of three which included Mr John Maples, a former Treasury minister who lost his Lewisham West seat at the last general election. Mr Davidson, 30, unsuccessfully opposed Mr Paddy Ashdown, the Liberal Democrat leader, at the last general election. He will defend a 12,357 majority at Newbury.
