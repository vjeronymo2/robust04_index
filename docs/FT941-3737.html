940315 FT 15 MAR 94 / People: Constructive careers Clive Groom, formerly md of Matthew Hall, has been appointed chief executive of Building and Property Management Services, recently bought by AMEC from the government. He is replaced by joint mds: John Maclean and John Pite. Alister McKerrell has been appointed md of Denco, an AMEC company, on the retirement of Barrie Carter, who becomes a non-executive director.
