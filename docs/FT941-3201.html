940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / Labour MPs in pivotal position Many council leaders who oppose the government's review of local-authority responsibilities have started lobbying Labour MPs, who have been placed in a pivotal position by dissension among Tories. In the past week the Labour leaders of 38 English and Welsh county councils have called on the party to abandon its support for the review, claiming it will be a 'political disaster'. But the leaders of more than 100 Labour-run district councils have urged the party's MPs not to drop their strong support for unitary councils, which combine the present functions of districts and councils.
