941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Windsor makes Pounds 1m Windsor, the specialist LLoyd's broker, reported a pre-tax profit of Pounds 1.05m for the year to September 30, helped by good profits from acquisitions. It is restoring dividend payments with a final of 0.5p per share. Operating income in the 12 months increased to Pounds 9.3m (Pounds 7.8m) including Pounds 1.31m from acquisitions. Provisions were much reduced at Pounds 49,000. The previous year carried costs of Pounds 1.82m, mainly relating to a property write-down, which resulted in a pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.11m. Earnings per share, adjusted to exclude exceptional items, were 2.65p (1.33p).
