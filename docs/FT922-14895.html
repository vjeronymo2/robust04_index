920403 FT 03 APR 92 / CD price ruling THE high price of compact discs in the UK reflects what consumers are prepared to pay rather than being the result of collusion between record companies and retailers, the Office of Fair Trading said yesterday. In a letter to companies involved in the music industry the OFT said no producer or retailer had a market share of more than 25 per cent, which would have constituted a scale monopoly.
