Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, today received brother Yusuf Bin-'Alawi, Omani minister of state for foreign affairs. The Omani minister conveyed to the president a message from his brother His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of Oman on the cordial fraternal relations exerted [as heard] by the brothers in Oman, particularly His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id, to bring viewpoints closer together, to contain the political crisis, and to safeguard Yemeni unity and stability. The president and the Omani minister reviewed bilateral relations and aspects of fraternal cooperation. They also reviewed regional and Arab developments and issues of common interest. The brother president praised the efforts exerted by the brothers in Oman and their support for our people to overcome the challenges imposed by the political crisis and its regrettable repercussions. He also praised the efforts exerted by the Omani military committee within the joint military committee and the success it rendered in halting and containing the military deterioration. The meeting was attended by brother Muhammad Salim Basindwah, foreign minister; brother 'Awad Bin-Mahfuz Bakathir, ambassador of Oman to our country; and Staff Major General 'Abd-al-'Alim Bin-Mustahil, head of the Omani team to the joint military committee.
