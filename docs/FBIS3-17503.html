Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] On Saturday populated centers in Azerbaijan's Tauz and Kazakh regions bordering Armenia (the republic's north-west) were subjected to artillery shelling from the contiguous territory, Azerbaijani Defense Ministry reported to Interfax. Populated centers situated in Azerbaijan's Agdam region came under fire on Friday evening and Saturday morning. At the night of March 26 the Armenian party undertook several attempts to launch an offensive in the southern part of the front in the direction of the rail way junction of Goradiz (situated on the border with Iran). One Armenian tank was destroyed in the course of hostilities. The attackers were moved back to the starting positions.
