BFN [Text] Cartagena de Indias, 15 Jun (NOTIMEX) -- Colombian President Cesar Gaviria Trujillo at 1820 (0120 GMT) [GMT time as received] closed the fourth summit meeting of the 22 leaders who signed the document entitled "Commerce and Integration as Element for Ibero-American Development." The closing was delayed approximately one hour because the discussion of the last point of the final document was expanded, an official source reported.
