Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Reports from Kabul suggest that an unofficial cease-fire has come into effect there. This is at a time when the residents of Kabul face growing problems in connection with obtaining food. In the meantime, the warring sides in Afghanistan have rejected rumors that an official cease-fire has been announced in Kabul. One official from Rabbani's faction said: The current cease-fire is a result of bad weather, snow, and the month of Ramadan and it is not, in any way, an official cease-fire.
