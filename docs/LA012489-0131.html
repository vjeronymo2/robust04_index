January 24, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

Terry J. Hartman has joined Van Dell & Associates Inc., a civil engineering, planning and surveying firm in Irvine, as senior vice president for project management. He will be responsible for overall direction of the project management group, providing consulting services in civil engineering, planning, project entitlement, landscape architecture and construction management. Previously, Hartman was director of project engineering for the Irvine Co.
