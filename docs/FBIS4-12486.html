BFN [Text] A round of Yemeni-Iranian talks was held in Tehran this evening. The talks were headed on the Yemeni side by Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah and on the Irani side by Iranian Foreign Minister Dr. 'Ali Akbar Velayati. Delegation members of both sides attended. During the meeting, issues of common interest were reviewed. The two sides also discussed bilateral ties and the means of promoting them. In a related development, the foreign minister and his delegation met with Iranian Commerce Minister Yahya ol-Eshaq at the Ministry of Commerce in Tehran at noon today. The talks addressed the fraternal bilateral ties, and the means of promoting them. Afterward, the foreign minister met with Iranian Culture and Higher Education Minister Mohammad Reza Hashemi-Golpayegani. The talks addressed the prospects for promoting bilateral cooperation. The two meetings were attended by the Yemeni ambassador in Tehran and the Iranian ambassador in Sanaa. Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah also met with Construction Jihad Minister Gholam Reza Foruzesh in Tehran this evening. They discussed bilateral ties as well as issues of common interest to the two fraternal states. The meeting was attended by the delegation accompanying the foreign minister, several officials of the Ministry of Construction Jihad, our ambassador in Tehran, and the Iranian ambassador to our country.
