March 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

REMEMBER TIM RICHMOND AS A GREAT RACE DRIVER

Elliott Almond's article, "Family Says Drug Test Falsified," has shed new light on the demise of one of NASCAR's bright stars.

Those of us who saw Tim Richmond race know that he had attained the driver's goal of being a part of the car.

Unfortunately, we will read of many more sports figures who will be maligned by bogus drug-testing procedures and those that will perish from AIDS.

T.L. COLEMAN SR., San Bernardino
