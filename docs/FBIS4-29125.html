BFN [Text] The meeting of the commission on the roundtable for national reconciliation and peace in Cambodia was held energetically at Damnak Chan Pavilion in the Royal Palace. His Excellency [H.E.] Ung Teaseam, under secretary of state for information, told national and international correspondents at the end of this first meeting that the participants discussed the first issue, that is, a cease-fire. Regarding this issue, the representatives of the Royal Government of Cambodia [RGC] and National Assembly followed His Majesty's idea. To speed up a concrete schedule for the cease-fire, the RGC representatives proposed a starting date of 30 June or earlier. Meanwhile, the Khmer Rouge [KR] side proposed that a control committee be set up and still refused to accept the proposed schedule. The roundtable meeting adjourned at 1200 and will resume work tomorrow morning at 0900.
