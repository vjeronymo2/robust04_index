BFN [Text] Manama, 10 May (WAKH) -- Shaykh Muhammad Bin-Mubarak Al Khalifah, Bahraini minister of foreign affairs, today received Robert Pelletreau, the U.S. assistant secretary of state for Near East and North African affairs, who is currently visiting Bahrain. During the meeting, the Bahraini foreign minister and Pelletreau exchanged views on friendly relations and cooperation between the State of Bahrain and the United States, ways of enhancing them for the benefit of the two friendly countries and peoples, and the issue of stability in the region. They also reviewed several issues of common interest, especially those pertaining to the situation in the Gulf region; current developments in Yemen; the peace process in the Middle East in light of the signing of the recent Palestinian-Israeli accord in Cairo; and the importance of activating negotiations on other tracks in order to achieve a just, comprehensive, and lasting peace in the Middle East.
