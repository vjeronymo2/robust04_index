BFN [Four decrees issued by the Georgian Parliament approving the appointment of three deputy prime ministers and the minister of finances in Tbilisi on 29 March] [Text] Decree of the Republic of Georgia on the Approval of Avtandil Margiani as Deputy Prime Minister of the Republic of Georgia The Georgian Parliament decrees: To approve Avtandil Margiani as deputy prime minister of the Republic of Georgia. [Signed] Vakhtang Goguadze, speaker of the Georgian Parliament, Tbilisi, 29 March 1994. Decree of the Georgian Parliament on the Approval of Tamaz Nadareishvili as Deputy Prime Minister of the Republic of Georgia The Georgian Parliament decrees: 1. To approve Tamaz Nadareishvili as deputy prime minister of the Republic of Georgia. 2. To terminate the powers of Tamaz Nadareishvili as a member of the Georgian Parliament in connection with his approval as deputy prime minister of the Republic of Georgia. [Signed] Vakhtang Goguadze, speaker of the Georgian Parliament, Tbilisi, 29 March 1994. Decree of the Georgian Parliament on the Approval of Teimuraz Basilia as Deputy Prime Minister of the Republic of Georgia The Georgian Parliament decrees: 1. To approve Teimuraz Basilia as deputy prime minister of the Republic of Georgia. 2. To terminate the powers of Teimuraz Basilia as a member of the Georgian Parliament in connection with his approval as deputy prime minister of the Republic of Georgia. [Signed] Vakhtang Goguadze, speaker of the Georgian Parliament, Tbilisi, 29 March 1994. Decree of the Georgian Parliament on the Approval of Davit Iakobidze as Minister of Finances of the Republic of Georgia The Georgian Parliament decrees: 1. To approve Davit Iakobidze as minister of finances of the Republic of Georgia. [Signed] Vakhtang Goguadze, speaker of the Georgian Parliament, Tbilisi, 29 March 1994.
