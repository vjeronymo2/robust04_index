July 1, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

EVERYBODY'S A CRITIC

After I tried a burger and fries at Meaty Meat Burgers, I became convinced that Reichl must have been hallucinating when she decided to recommend it.

A lousy $4 meal is not the end of the world, but it does cause me to look with suspicion on her other suggestions.

JOHN V. FANTE

Los Angeles
