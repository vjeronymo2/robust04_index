Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 4 (XINHUA) -- About 200 Chinese officials and foreign envoys as well as representatives of foreign enterprises gathered here this afternoon to celebrate the forthcoming traditional Chinese Spring Festival. Buhe, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China, and Ma Junru, director of the State Bureau of Foreign Experts Affairs (SBFEA), made speeches at the gathering. In his speech, Ma said that his bureau and the China Association for International Exchange of Personnel will, as always, continue to strengthen cooperation with all foreign governmental organizations, embassies and consulates in China, and other non-governmental organizations, enterprises and friends. "We will enthusiastically and effectively promote international talent exchanges, and absorb and draw lessons from the broad spectrum of human achievements so as to further promote China's reform and opening-up and modernization," Ma said. At the party, students from the Central University for Nationalities staged a show of costumes from China's 56 nationalities. The performance brought repeated applause from the crowds. The activity was sponsored by SBFEA, the Beijing Municipality Foreign Economic and Trade Committee and the Beijing Municipality Administration for Industry and Commerce.
