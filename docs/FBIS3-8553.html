Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, March 9 (ATA) -- Protocol between the Republic of Albania and Republic of Turkey for qualification of the military medical staff was signed in Ankara, on March 8, the Albanian Embassy in Ankara reports. The protocol was signed in the context of the "Agreement for Military Cooperation and Qualification" signed on November 19, 1992. The agreement will establish a mutual cooperation in the field of military medicine. The protocol was signed for the Turkish side by General Sherif Sabunju for Turkish side [as received] and for the Albanian side by the ambassador in Ankara, Skender Shkupi.
