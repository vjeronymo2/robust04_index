BFN [Report by Aleksey Venediktov from the State Duma--recorded] [Text] [Aleksey Venediktov] At the suggestion of the Russia's Choice faction, the evening session of the State Duma returned to the incident between Vladimir Zhirinovskiy and Deputies Vladimir Borzyuk and Valeriy Borshchev. The State Duma received a statement signed by STOLITSA magazine journalist Aleksey Pyatkovskiy, whose dictophone and glasses were broken and trampled upon by Zhirinovskiy. State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin instructed the committee on the internal organization of the Duma to investigate the incident. Rybkin stressed that he would personally hand over the journalist's statement to the bodies of the democratic state which is being formed together with the State Duma. [Announcer] When asked by our correspondent where the Chairman of the State Duma would send the assaulted journalist's statement, the State Duma chairman said: [Rybkin] I will definitely -- it is my duty -- pass the paper from your colleague Pyatkovskiy to the procurator general.
