920331 FT 31 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Hay & Robertson back in the black Hay & Robertson, the textile merchant, returned to profit in the half year to November 30 with a pre-tax figure of Pounds 21,500 against a loss of Pounds 136. Turnover totalled Pounds 241,000 and the company now had a 'promising' order book. Turnover compared with Pounds 3,800 last time, when the company was unable to find profitable trading opportunities and decided to keep its cash on bank deposit. Earnings per share came out at 0.029p (nil).
