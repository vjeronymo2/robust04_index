930224 FT 24 FEB 93 / UK Company News: London Finance Net asset value per share at London Finance & Investment Group stood at 20p at December 31 1992, against 20.84p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year amounted to Pounds 168,000 compared with losses of Pounds 3.69m. Earnings per share came to 0.67p (14.77p losses) and the proposed dividend is 0.4p (0.75p).
