920819 FT 19 AUG 92 / International Company News: Foster's confirms asset write-downs FOSTER'S Brewing Group, the diversified Australian beer producer, has confirmed market speculation that it will announce asset write-downs in its next annual earnings statement, due next month, writes Bruce Jacques in Sydney. The company was replying to the Australian Stock Exchange query about heavy falls in its share price. Foster's directors referred to a statement in June which indicated that the carrying value of assets was being continually evaluated and further adjustments may be necessary.
