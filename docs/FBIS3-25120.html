Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Home Affairs Minister Danie Schutte says nearly a million rands in capital is now leaving the country each month because of conflicting statements made by the ANC [African National Congress] on nationalization. Mr. Schutte told a group of Durban businessmen the role of the ANC, as the dominating partner in a future government, could also lead to foreign investor skepticism. The minister added that, last year alone, at least 9 billion rand had left the country.
