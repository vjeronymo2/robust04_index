November 24, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: FULLERTON;

DIRECTORS SOUGHT FOR CULTURAL CENTER

The board of trustees of the Muckenthaler Cultural Center is accepting applications from people interested in serving on the center's board of directors.

The application deadline is Dec. 14. Applicants are not required to be residents of Fullerton.

The board is the chief support group for the cultural center and provides input and direction on programming. The board also sponsors various fund-raising programs to help support center activities.

Applications can be obtained at the center, 1201 W. Malvern Ave., Fullerton. For more information, call (714) 738-6595.
