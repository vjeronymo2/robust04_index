BFN [Text] Vilnius, Apr 11, BNS -- The rate of inflation in Lithuania ran at 3.3 percent in March, the Lithuanian Statistics Department said. According to the report, prices for goods and services increased more rapidly in March than in February when inflation reached 2.9 percent. In March, expenditures on housing, fuel and energy prices jumped by 4.1 percent, but transportation and communication prices dropped by 1.1 percent. Food prices increased by 3.9 percent, tobacco and liquor edged up by 1.4 percent. The rate of inflation over the past three months was considerably lower that during the same period last year. Inflation last March was 21.4 percent. According to the official statistics, the overall inflation rate in Lithuania was 188.7 percent in 1993 and 1,263 percent in 1992.
