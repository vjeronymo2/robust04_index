July 26, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: CRIME FILE

The uncle of comedian Eddie Murphy was sentenced to four months in jail Monday in Toronto for sexually assaulting a teen-age fan after one of his nephew's concerts. Ray Murphy Sr., 50, head of security for his nephew, pleaded guilty to sexual assault. The prosecuting attorney said Murphy assaulted the girl in July, 1986, at a hotel after promising her that she could meet Eddie Murphy. The girl was 18 at the time. ALEENE MacMINN
