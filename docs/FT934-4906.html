931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / Nappy makers in joint venture Uni-Charm, Japan's biggest producer of children's nappies, has set up a joint venture with Molnlycke, part of Sweden's SCA forest products group, to make and sell 'panty-nappies' for the European market, writes Ronald van de Krol in the Hague. The venture, located at Molnlycke's existing facilities in the Netherlands, is 60 per cent-owned by Uni-Charm which is providing manufacturing technology while Molnlycke will handle marketing.
