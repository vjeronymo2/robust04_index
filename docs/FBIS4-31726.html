BFN [Excerpt] Petrovac, 14 Jun (TANJUG) -- Inter-Muslim clashes in Cazin Krajina [western Bosnia] have resumed for the fourth day running, local Muslim media have reported. Radio Velkaton in Velika Kladusa reports that fierce clashes continued throughout the night on almost all parts of the front. The fiercest attacks by Izetbegovic's 5th Corps were registered in the Trzac, Pecigrad, Liskovac, and Todorovo areas. According to the same source, both sides suffered a large number of wounded, killed, captured, and missing soldiers. Muslim Radio Bihac reports, and Radio Cazin and Muslim radio Sarajevo have confirmed, that Fikret Abdic's units launched fierce attacks over the past 24 hours, targeting Pecigrad and Todorovo with artillery weapons. Muslim Radio Bihac reports that Abdic's forces attacked a bus in the Cazin municipality and injured a number of passengers. Radio Bihac also reports that a large number of people have been injured, killed, or captured. [passage omitted]
