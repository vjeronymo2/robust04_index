930911 FT 11 SEP 93 / Markets: No-claims bonuses improved - At a glance General Accident and Lloyds Bank have both improved their no-claims bonuses for home insurance. GA has increased the discount for three claim-free years to 20 per cent from 15 per cent. The one-year discount is 5 per cent, and the maximum discount of 25 per cent is available after four claim-free years. Lloyds is introducing a 10 per cent no-claims discount after one year to replace its 5 per cent 'loyalty bonus' for customers renewing Value Cover home contents policies.
