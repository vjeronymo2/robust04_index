BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Rafael Bikbayev] [Text] Cairo April 7 TASS -- Egyptian President Husni Mubarak received Azeri Foreign Minister Hasan Hasanov here on Wednesday. After the end of the meeting Hasanov described the atmosphere at the talks as "excellent". "We discussed a whole range of issues dealing with bilateral cooperation, including in the political, economic and technological spheres." Azerbaijan seeks in every way to develop relations with "fraternal Egypt", he added. Hasanov conveyed to Husni Mubarak an invitation of Azeri President Heydar Aliyev to visit Baku. In his turn, the Egyptian president invited Aliyev to pay a visit to Egypt. The sides agreed to continue consultations and the coordination of actions within the U.N. framework and the Islamic Conference. They discussed as well the problem of Azerbaijan's joining of the Non-Aligned Movement. The Azeri foreign minister told Husni Mubarak about the latest developments connected with the Armenian-Azeri conflict and about the efforts, exerted for settling it. He urged all Arab countries to support his country in the sphere of the coordination of actions at international organisations.
