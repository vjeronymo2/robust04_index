May 3, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: WEST HOLLYWOOD;

SANTA MONICA BLVD. CLOSURES

Nighttime street repairs on Santa Monica Boulevard between La Brea Avenue and Doheny Drive will cause lane closures for about a week starting Monday. The closures will last from 10:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. At least one lane will be open in each direction at all times. For more information, call 972-7098.
