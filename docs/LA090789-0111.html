September 7, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Chevy Announces Rebates: Chevrolet announced rebates on two of its 1990 cars, making the GM division the first of the Big Three auto makers to do so. Chevrolet put a $750 cash rebate on all models of its 1990 Lumina sedan, a car introduced earlier in the year to replace the Chevrolet Celebrity, and an $800 rebate to buyers of the Geo Prizm or Chevrolet Nova.
