Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] A provincial judicial work meeting ended today. During discussions with leaders of prefectural and city party committees on the evening of 28 January, Liu Rongli, secretary of the provincial commission for political science and law, stressed: We should firmly carry out the instructions of the party Central Committee and the provincial party committee on legal work, make comprehensive arrangements in light of local conditions, bring focal points into prominence, and be strict in work to ensure political stability and social tranquility throughout the province and to promote the smooth carrying out of reform, opening up, and economic construction. Liu Rongli continued: A task of primary importance at present is to preserve social stability in a down-to-earth manner, to put into effect all policies and measures, and to strike severe blows at serious criminal offenses. [passage omitted] Leaders should pay close attention to wherever public security is chaotic, including highways and railways, should concentrate their efforts on rectifying social order in these places, and should not stop this rectification until social order changes for the better. Liu Rongli stressed the need to strengthen party leadership over judicial work and to speed up improvement of the judicial contingent. The key to fulfilling the heavy tasks in judicial work is to build a good judicial contingent, to improve the political and professional quality of judicial cadres, and to raise their law enforcement ability. [passage omitted]
