BFN [Text] A new checkpoint was established in the Maksim Gorkiy settlement of collective farms named after 1 May -- Pyandzhskiy Rayon. Our compatriots can return to their permanent places of residence from the Konduz Province of the Islamic State of Afghanistan via this checkpoint. This is the fourth checkpoint though which our compatriots can return to Tajikistan. Ismat Davlatov, deputy Minister of Labor and Employment, told us that it was expected that our compatriots will return tomorrow via the Hayraton-Termez- Shaartuz highway. It is expected that more than 1,200 inhabitants of our republic, who are living temporarily in the Islamic State of Afghanistan, will return home by train.
