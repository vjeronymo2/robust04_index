August 19, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

MOULTON PLAZA ACQUIRED BY HAWAIIAN FIRM

Capital Solutions, a Palo Alto real estate investor, has sold the Moulton Plaza shopping center in Laguna Hills for $19 million to Alexander & Baldwin Inc., a Hawaii farming and transportation concern.

The 133,000-square-foot center -- on Moulton Parkway between El Toro Road and Lake Forest Drive -- is almost entirely leased. The major tenants include a Hughes Market, the Department of Motor Vehicles and Security Pacific Bank.

Alexander & Baldwin, a publicly traded Honolulu company, has interests in ocean transport, agriculture and real estate development and management.

The purchase is part of a $100-million acquisition program run by San Francisco's AMB Investments Inc. for Alexander & Baldwin.
