Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, yesterday evening met King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, the custodian of the two holy mosques. Recent developments on the international and Arab arenas, the developments of the Palestinian issues, and the developments of the peace process in the Middle East were discussed during the meeting. Brother President Abu-'Ammar thanked King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, the custodian of the two holy mosques, for the support offered by the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to the Palestinian people and their just cause at all levels, as well as for his majesty's principled stances toward the issue of Palestine and holy Jerusalem, capital of the state of Palestine, until our people succeed in restoring all their constant national rights. Brother Abu-'Ammar stressed the importance of the efforts that the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia has been exerting, under the leadership of the custodian of the two holy mosques, in supporting the Palestinian cause and the steps toward a just peace at all Arab and international levels. The custodian of the two holy mosques expressed his support for the courageous stances along which the Palestinian leadership has proceeded for securing the rights of the Palestinian people, and stressed that the Kingdom will continue to support the Palestinian cause until the Palestinian people succeed in obtaining all their rights.
