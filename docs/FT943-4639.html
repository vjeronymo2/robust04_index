940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / World Trade News: Lucas Aerospace in joint venture with Chinese - Aviation industry contracts Lucas Aerospace, the UK engineering company, is to form a joint venture with the China National Aero-technology Import and Export Company, writes Bernard Gray. The joint venture will be shared equally and will have alternating chairmen, but Lucas will have management control of the manufacturing plant near Shanghai. Initially, the joint venture will provide a digital engine control system for the Chinese K-8 basic jet trainer. It will also be the preferred supplier in future engine control, flight control and power generation equipment. The venture could have a turnover in excess of Dollars 100m within 10 years.
