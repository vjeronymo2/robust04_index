930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Howard reduces loss to Pounds 205,000 Howard Holdings, the property development, plant hire and joinery company, reported a reduced loss of Pounds 205,000 pre-tax, against Pounds 814,000, in the year to April 30. The result was helped by exceptional profits of Pounds 214,000 compared with a charge of Pounds 113,000 last time. Turnover was lower at Pounds 3.07m (Pounds 5.83m). Losses per share came out at 0.57p (2.08p). The single final dividend is maintained at 0.5p.
