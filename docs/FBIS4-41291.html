BFN [Text] The Latvian Foreign Ministry reports that the authorized representatives of Latvia and Nicaragua in New York have signed a protocol on establishing diplomatic relations at the ambassadorial level. The signatories expressed the conviction that it will promote and strengthen cooperation between the two countries in diplomatic, interparliamentary, economic relations, and in the cultural sphere. The protocol was signed by Aivars Baumanis, Latvia's permanent representative to the United Nations, and Erich Asher, the permanent representative of Nicaragua to the United Nations.
