BFN [Text] Niamey, 15 May (AFP) -- Union sources have disclosed that the four officials of the Customs Workers Union [SNAD], including Secretary General Ibro Ayouba, arrested on 11 May were released today. The four officials were arrested by the gendarmes in Niamey for "interfering with the freedom to work." No reason was given for the officials' release. However, SNAD had decided to withdraw its notice for a five-day strike as of 16 May in order to have the officials released.
