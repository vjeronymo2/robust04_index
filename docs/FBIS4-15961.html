BFN [Text] The Belarus Foreign Ministry has received an official note from the Latvian Foreign Ministry asking for an expalanation about Belarus Foreign Minister Petr Kravchanka's statements about the discrimination against Belarusians in Latvia. On 5 April, after the talks with UN Secretary General Butrus-Butrus Ghali, Kravchanka stated that one of the main subjects of the talks was how Latvia and Estonia carry out their duties as regards Belarusians living there and the predicament of Belarusians in those countries. Kravchanka accused the two Baltic states and said that Belarusians living in Latvia and Estonia suffer from certain discrimination. He did not exclude the possibility of Belarus proposing that the United Nations pass a resolution on the legal status of Belarusians in the event it is not improved.
