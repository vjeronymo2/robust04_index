Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Umtata Jan 18 SAPA -- The Pan Africanist Congress [PAC] central Transkei region on Tuesday [18 January] expressed its shock and disgust at what it termed the PAC's unilateral decision, announced on Sunday, to suspend its armed struggle. PAC Regional Secretary Mfanelo Skwatsha said the move was not in line with national conference resolutions taken in Umtata last year. "The conference mandated the PAC leadership to negotiate a mutual cessation of hostilities with the regime. This move is nothing but capitulation and renegation on the part of the PAC leadership," Mr Skwatsha said. He called for a special conference for the PAC leadership to explain its decision.
