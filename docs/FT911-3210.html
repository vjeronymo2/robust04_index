910425 FT 25 APR 91 / UK Company News: Shani improves in spite of recession In spite of the recent gloom in the high street, Shani Group, the USM-quoted company which manufactures and supplies womens' and children's wear, yesterday reported a marginal increase in interim profits. The outcome for the six months to end-January - Pounds 764,000 against Pounds 755,000 - came on turnover down some 9 per cent to Pounds 5.92m (Pounds 6.52m). Mr Martin Hollis, chairman, said the performance reflected improved manufacturing and operational efficiency. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.6p, payable from earnings of 3.5p (3.3p) per share.
