September 21, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

RESTRICTIONS IMPOSED ON MOSQUE DESIGN

I could not believe that the political powers have nothing better to occupy their taxpayer-financed energy output than to limit the roof lines of a Muslim mosque to straight lines instead of the traditional dome shape to maintain the "look of the neighborhood."

Shall we, who have bumps on our noses, have them straightened to maintain an "American" look or some such thing? Why do we continue to insult our own intelligence by electing and appointing such misguided idiots as the ones who issued this biased edict?

MIKHAEL KOLLANDER

Canoga Park
