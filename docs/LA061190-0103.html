June 11, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

STATE;

SHARP QUAKE JOLTS WINE COUNTRY

A sharp earthquake that went "kerchunk" in the night caused little damage but woke up hundreds of people in Northern California's wine country early today, officials reported.

The 2:07 a.m. quake had a magnitude of 3.4 and was centered 7 miles northwest of Napa, according to the United States Geological Survey in Menlo Park.
