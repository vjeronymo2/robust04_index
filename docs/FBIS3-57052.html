Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Viktor Makarov] [Text] Syktyvkar, Komi Republic, January 23 TASS -- A new draft constitution of the Komi Republic, northern Russia, prepared by a special commission at the republic's Supreme Soviet has been published in the local press for public consideration. All state power bodies, public associations, labor collectives and inividuals are invited to submit their proposals and amendments to the commission, the Supreme Soviet presidium said in an address to the republic's residents, also released by the media. All proposals must be submitted before February 2, 1994. Under the presidium's decision, the draft constitution will be considered at the parliament's extraordinary session scheduled for February 15. The draft reads that the Komi Republic is a democratic legal state within the composition of the Russian Federation. The draft nominates the multinational population of the Komi Republic as "the bearer of the republic's sovereighnty and the only source of the state power" which may execute its powers both directly, through participation in referendums and elections, and indirectly, through a system of state and local self-rule agencies. In compliance with Russia' constitution, the state system of power bodies in the republic includes the state council, the head of the republic, the constitutional court, and a court system. Under the draft constitution, the state council is the highest representative and legislative body in the republic. The head of the republic is a "top official who directly leads the cabinet of ministers, which is the republic's highest executive power body".
