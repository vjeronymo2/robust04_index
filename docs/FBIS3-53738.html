Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Amal Movement has announced that on the anniversary of the martyrdom of one of its members, Abu-Jamal, one of its groups attacked an Israeli occupation forces' position on Hula hill at 0630 today, using automatic weapons and rockets. Subsequently, the enemy forces shelled Shaqra and Bra'shit, using 120-mm artillery. The Amal Movement also announced that one of its groups last night attacked an agent militia position on al-Shumariyah hill, using automatic weapons and rockets. The resistance fighters hit an observation tower and destroyed the position's fortifications.
