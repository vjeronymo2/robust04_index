931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / UK Company News: BIL Securities sells stake in Caffyns Caffyns, the Sussex-based motor dealer, yesterday announced that BIL Securities (Far East), the New Zealand investment company owned by Sir Ron Brierley, had disposed of its 23.6 per cent stake in the company. The disposal by the company, a wholly owned subsidiary of BIL (Far East Holdings), follows the sale of other stakes earlier this year in Guinness Peat Group and Budgens, the food retailing chain. The group's holding in Caffyns comprised 1.18m ordinary 50p shares and two tranches of preference shares. Caffyns shares rose 10p to close at 388p on the news.
