930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The pepper market was very firm for both black and white, while the price difference between the two types was very large, reports Man Producten. Whites prices remained steady and were slowly moving still higher because of low production figures in the Far East. Blacks prices moived sharply higher from the middle of the week. Apparently the relatively cheap origins, India and Brazil became aware of very low output in Malaysia and Lampong. White muntok/sarawak faq, spot, was quoted at Dollars 2,265 a tonne; with Jul/Aug delivery at Dollars 2,225; Aug/Sep Dollars 2,250; Novbember/December Dollars 2,275; and Jan/Feb Dollars 2,290.
