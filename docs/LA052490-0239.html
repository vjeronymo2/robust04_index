May 24, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: CHRISTY IHN WINS 5TH GRADE SPELLING BEE

Christy Ihn of Cameron School was the winner of the West Covina Unified School District's Fifth Grade Spelling Bee.

Lester Manalo of Wescove School was second and Jelani Kimble of Merlinda School third.

Fourteen students participated.
