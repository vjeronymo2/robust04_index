BFN [Text] A statement by the security services today said that on the basis of information, a terrorist network involved in an attempt to sabotage El Hadjar [steel] complex has been disbanded. The information led to the siege of a bakery in Guemar, the province of El Oued, the killing of four terrorists, the arrest of another six, and the seizure of a quantity of arms. Investigations led to the arrest of 29 people in (Oued Souf) and 14 in Annaba, and the killing of 14 terrorists in a hideout in (Oued El Annab) and two terrorists in (Djebel Gamgam) in the province of Tebessa, where five gendarmes were killed in an exchange of fire. This terrorist network was composed of people from Guemar and Oued Souf. Some of them were lying in wait at El Hadjar complex and had started to execute a plan to assassinate members of the security and sabotagie El Hadjar complex. This group was active under the leadership of the so-called (Basi Taher) who is a baker in the Guemar municipality.
