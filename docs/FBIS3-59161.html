Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Austrian Chancellor Franz Vranitzky met Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias. They both expressed their concern about the situation in Bosnia and stated their countries' willingness to contribute to the handling of the problem -- a problem that Papoulias described as almost insurmountable. Answering a question, Papoulias said Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou briefed the Austrian chancellor on the Greek position on Skopje. Vranitzky also met Chamber of Deputies Chairman Apostolos Kaklamanis.
