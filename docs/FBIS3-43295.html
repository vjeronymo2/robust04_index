Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Police have detained two senior PAC [Pan-Africanist Congress] members in connection with the attack last week on the Heidelberg Tavern in Cape Town. The two leading Western Cape PAC men are Michael Siyolo and Theo Mabusela. They have been held under Section 29 of the Internal Security Act. Four people were killed and five others injured in the attack. Meanwhile PAC spokesman Patricia de Lille says her organization is very disturbed by the police action: [Begin De Lille recording] It has become clear to us that it is now the norm that whenever something happens, like the massacre at Heidelberg Tavern, that the first to be arrested is PAC members, and we know it is the weakness of the South Africa. Police to bring the matters to book and every time make a scapegoat of the PAC members, and the PAC will not tolerate this. [end recording]
