Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 27 (XINHUA) -- Zhu Liang, chairman of Foreign Affairs Committee of the Chinese National People's Congress (NPC), met with and feted a delegation from Foreign Affairs Committee of the Danish Parliament. The 9-member delegation, headed by Peter Duetoft, chairman of Foreign Affairs Committee of Danish Parliament, is here on a visit to China as guest of NPC Foreign Affairs Committee.
