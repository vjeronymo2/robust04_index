931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / Technology (Worth Watching): Herbal remedies growing wildly An increasing number of European consumers are using herbal and homeopathic remedies rather than conventional drugs, according to the latest report from management consultants Datamonitor. Belgians have the highest consumption rate, spending an average of Dollars 8 (Pounds 6) annually per head on homeopathic remedies. But consumption is growing most quickly in the UK - by more than 15 per cent between 1987 and 1992 - with Nelson and Weleda the market leaders. The biggest single market in Europe is in Germany, where consumers bought Dollars 1.3bn worth of homeopathic and herbal remedies last year. Datamonitor: UK, 071 625 8548.
