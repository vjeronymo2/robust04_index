August 8, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

DEBATE OVER CAPITAL GAINS

In response to "Six Democrats Backing Capital Gains Tax" (Part I, July 25):

I do not see this as a partisan issue, nor should it be played as a Bush vs. Foley issue. Certainly it is not an "unnecessary giveaway to the rich."

As a retired middle-class professional in my 60s, I would like to sell my house (purchased in 1972) and move to a less expensive area. I have not done so because of unjust, high capital gains tax. I'm sure there are many, many people in my position.

It has been demonstrated that it would generate revenue by encouraging many people like myself to sell.

N.M. MYERS

Malibu
