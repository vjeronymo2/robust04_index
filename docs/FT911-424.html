910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / Trial for Channel tunnel companies FIVE member companies of TML, the Channel tunnel consortium, were committed for trial yesterday charged with failing to ensure the safety of employees. Balfour Beatty Construction, Costain Civil Engineering, Tarmac Construction, Taylor Woodrow Construction and Wimpey Major Projects are accused by the Health and Safety Executive that between December 12 1989 and January 10 1990 they failed to ensure the safety of employees concerning the movement of trains. The charge follows the death in January last year of Mr Keith Lynch, who was hit underground by wagons pushed by a locomotive at the Shakespeare Cliff site near Dover.
