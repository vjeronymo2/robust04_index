941224 FT 24 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Slight decline at CA Sperati CA Sperati (The Special Agency), the Greenwich-based group which imports buttons and trimmings from continental Europe and east Asia, reported profits before tax of Pounds 52,337 for the year to October 31. The modest decline from the comparable Pounds 54,336 came on turnover of Pounds 862,947 (Pounds 856,791). Earnings per share emerged at 37.18p (38.65p). There is again no dividend; the last distribution was in 1981.
