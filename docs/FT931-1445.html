930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Richardsons West Richardsons Westgarth, the steel stockholder and processor, achieved a rise in 1992 pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.76m to Pounds 1.92m in an environment described as 'generally hostile.' A final dividend of 1.75p makes a 3p (2.9p) total. The shares responded with an 8p rise to 77p. Sales improved from Pounds 45.4m to Pounds 53.5m and included a seven months' contribution from Tipton Steel Stock Holders (Stoke). Higher tax of Pounds 647,000 (Pounds 234,000) left earnings at 4.6p (5.9p). Assuming a 33 per cent tax charge earnings per share amounted to 4.7p (4.5p).
