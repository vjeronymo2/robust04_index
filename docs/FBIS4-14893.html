BFN [Text] Moscow, 4 April, IRNA -- Iran's Oil Minister Gholam Reza Aqazadeh arrived in Ashgabat Monday to hold talks with Turkmen officials on ways to expand Tehran-Ashgabat cooperation in areas of oil and gas. Aqazadeh who is headimg a delegation composed of oil and gas experts was welcomed by Turkmem Vice-President Batar Saragaev and his Turkmen counterpart Nazar Suyunov at the airport. Aqazadeh is also to take part at a session on the construction of a Turkmen pipeline to Turkey and Europe via Iran. The session will be chaired by the Turkmen president and attended by ministers of oil and energy from Russia, Turkmenistan, Turkey, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan and Kazakhstan as well as ambassadors of some European countries to Turkmenistan.
