BFN [Text] Finance Minister Rubens Ricupero has announced new decisions regarding the real currency. Ricupero, who met with the Chamber of Deputies Economy Committee, came to seek guarantees that farmers would continue to produce food. Today it was agreed that the government will renegotiate the farmers' debts, and give them up to 10 years to repay. The government will likewise pay the overdue farming insurance debts and suspend for three months the Bank of Brazil lawsuits against farmers in arrears. During a luncheon with commerce representatives, Ricupero explained the implementation of the new currency. During this luncheon he gave advance notice of some of the points of the economic plan: Credits: The government will not adopt any measure to reduce consumption of goods like cars and household appliances. Interest rates: They will continue to be high until the economy reaches stability. Contracts: Those in real cruzeiros will be converted into URV's [real value units] based on the URV rate of 1 July. Ufir [Reference Fiscal Unit]: It will not be abolished. It will continue to index income tax paid in advance and overdue taxes. Postdated checks: Banks will accept checks written in real cruzeiros until 1 August.
