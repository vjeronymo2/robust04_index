920629 FT 29 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Bett Bros Pounds 7.52m in the red midway BETT Brothers, the builder and property investment group, slid into the red for the six months to February 29. Pre-tax losses for the period amounted to Pounds 7.52m against profits of Pounds 513,000. For the year ended August 31 1991 there was an Pounds 8.85m deficit. Turnover fell to Pounds 9.49m (Pounds 17.8m). The pre-tax result was after an exceptional Pounds 8.4m (Pounds 4.8m) from the continuing deterioration in the commercial property market in the south of England, Mr Iain Bett, the chairman, said. There were losses per share of 50.11p (2.26p earnings) and there is no interim dividend (2.1p).
