April 26, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski got off on the wrong foot at Hollywood Park Wednesday when he lost his $50 win bet on Dr. Brent, who ran second in the seventh race. Bob, who started with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $950.

Bob's bets today are a $15 box from Picture Princess to Y'All Sing and a $10 box from Picture Perfect to Halomatic in the fourth race.

Wednesday's Bankroll $1,000

Wednesday's Loss $50

Current Bankroll $950
