BFN [Text] The Vatican, 27 Jun (INA) -- Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz met here today with Cardinal Sodano, the Vatican secretary of state. During the meeting, they discussed the good relations between Iraq and the Vatican. The deputy prime minister explained Iraq's position on the implementation of the Security Council resolutions and the effects of the continued unjust blockade against the Iraqi people. 'Aziz asserted that the legal and sound implementation of Security Council resolutions requires the immediate, unconditional lifting of this blockade. Cardinal Sodano noted his understanding of Iraq's position. He also reiterated the stand of the Vatican and His Holiness the Pope in rejecting the blockade. He also expressed his sympathy with the suffering of the Iraqi people as a result of this blockade. The deputy prime minister will meet with Pope John Paul II tomorrow.
