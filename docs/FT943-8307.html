940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / World Trade News: Statoil awards Norwegian oilfield contract Asea Brown Boveri, the Swiss-Swedish engineering group, said it had won a NKr350m (Dollars 51m) order from Statoil, the Norwegian state-owned oil company, to deliver valves and well-head systems for the Troll, Sleipner west and east and Veslefrikk fields, Reuter reports from Stockholm. ABB Offshore Technolgy would also be involved in the installation, servicing and testing of the equipment, the company said.
