Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Lithuanian prime minister Adolfas Slezevicius and Belarusian Ambassador to Lithuania Yevgeniy Voytovich have met to discuss relations between the two countries. Mr. Voytovich described the political situation in Belarus on the eve of presidential and parliamentary elections. Mr. Voytovich also asked for the prime minister's assistance in starting a Belarusian school in Vilnius. Earlier some Lithuanian politicians voiced their discontent over the appointment of Mr. Voytovich to the post of the ambassador to Lithuania, since he had allegedly made territorial claims to the Lithuanian capital Vilnius. Official sources in Minsk have denied these allegations.
