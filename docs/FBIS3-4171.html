Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 28 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin and President of the Republic of Korea (ROK) Kim Yong-sam began their talks here this morning. Sino-ROK ties and regional as well as international issues will be the main topics, according to sources here. Prior to the talks, Jiang presided over a welcoming ceremony in honor of Kim who came to Beijing yesterday on an official visit to China.
