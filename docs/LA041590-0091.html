April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DEMOCRATIC PRIMARY RACE

Death in the mornin', death in the evenin', death at supper time. . . .

Since viewing her recent TV ads I've sure got to agree with your columnists that Dianne Feinstein is the "zippiest" candidate running for governor of the great state of California.

Her commercials began by making hay from the tragic murders in San Francisco, and then moved to those two issues no upwardly-clawing politician can resist -- abortion on demand and capital punishment. What the great state of California needs to cope with a lot of complex problems is a no-nonsense solidly pro-death candidate.

Who says a woman can't be more like a man?

WILLIAM J. KEACH

Redondo Beach
