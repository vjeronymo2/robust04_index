Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 24 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received the high-level delegation of the Defence Ministry of the Royal Government of Cambodia headed by Co-Ministers of Defence General Tie Banh and General Tie Chamrat on a visit to Korea. On hand were Minister of People's Armed Forces O Chin-u and Cambodian Ambassador E.P. [extraordinary and plenipotentiary] to Korea Oum Mannorine. President Kim Il-song had a talk with the guests in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.
