920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Dunedin Income net asset value up 10% Dunedin Income Growth Investment Trust reported net asset value of 542.2p as at January 31 - a rise of 10 per cent over the year. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 7.96m, down from Pounds 8.17m, for earnings of 25.04p (25.72p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 17p brings the total for the year to 25.4p (24p).
