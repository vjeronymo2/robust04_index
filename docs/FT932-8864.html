930517 FT 17 MAY 93 / Construction Contracts: Social housing JOHN MOWLEM HOMES, part of the John Mowlem Group, has won a Pounds 26m project for a mix of social and low cost private housing at sites B and C of Blackbird Leys, on the south side of Oxford. The Pounds 16m social housing development, one of the largest of its kind in the south, is for a consortium of housing associations led by Ealing Family Housing Association and the City of Oxford, which is the enabling authority. The project covers an area of 22 acres and comprise 446 dwellings, of which 38 are for special needs and 60 for shared ownership, together with a community centre, shop, play areas, a linear park and infrastructure. A further 11 acres adjacent to the development have been acquired by Mowlem from the city to build for private sale more than 200 one, two and three-bedroom houses.
