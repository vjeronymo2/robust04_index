BFN ["Decree No. 23 of the President of the People's Republic of China"] [Text] Beijing, 12 May (XINHUA) -- "The State Compensation Law of the People's Republic of China," adopted at the seventh meeting of the Standing Committee of the PRC's Eighth National People's Congress on 12 May 1994, is hereby promulgated and will become effective beginning 1 January 1995. [Signed] Jiang Zemin, president of the People's Republic of China [Dated] 12 May 1994
