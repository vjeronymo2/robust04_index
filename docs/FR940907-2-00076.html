Release of Waybill Data The Commission has received a request from Harkins Cunningham and Consolidated Rail Corporation for permission to use certain data from the Commission's 1992 and 1993 I.C.C. Waybill Samples. A copy of the request (WB454&hyph;8/26/94) may be obtained from the I.C.C. Office of Economic and Environmental Analysis. The waybill sample contains confidential railroad and shipper data; therefore, if any parties object to this request, they should file their objections with the Director of the Commission's Office of Economic and Environmental Analysis within 14 calendar days of the date of this notice. The rules for release of waybill data are codified at 49 CFR 1244.8. Contact: James A. Nash, (202) 927&hyph;6196. Vernon A. Williams, Acting Secretary.
