November 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

ANGEL FANS TIRED OF BEING SUCKERED

I see the Angels have once again raised ticket and parking prices for season ticket-holders, their best customers. Given the continuing mediocrity of the team, don't you think the Angels should be giving refunds to last year's season ticket-holders? Or does Gene Autry agree with P.T. Barnum?

TOM CHAMBERS, Huntington Beach
