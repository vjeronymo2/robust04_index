September 29, 1989, Friday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

WALESA TO VISIT U.S. AND CANADA

Solidarity leader Lech Walesa will visit Canada, the United States and Venezuela in November in his first trip to the Western Hemisphere, Solidarity sources said today.

Walesa will start his two-week visit around Nov. 10, but the exact schedule is not yet final, the sources said. He is expected to meet leaders of the three countries, including President Bush, who had lunch with Walesa at his home in Gdansk during a visit in July.
