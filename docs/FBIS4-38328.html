BFN [Text] Moscow May 30 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed a decree on Monday imposing a state of emergency in parts of North Ossetia and Ingushetia. The state of emergency will be imposed from 14:00 on May 31, 1994 till 14:00 July 31, 1994. The decree also lifts as of 14:00 May 31 the state of emergency imposed by the presidential decrees of April 4 and April 27, 1994. The decree comes into effect from the day it is signed, the presidential press service said.
