Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Delegations from the PDS [Democratic Party of the Left], the AD [Democratic Alliance] and the Christian Socials have met and decided to unite on a common program for government to be presented to the other progressive forces. The points of convergence indicated in a communique issued at the end of the meeting were: the continuation of economic and financial reconstruction started by the [Prime Minister Carlo Azeglio] Ciampi government and radical innovations in social policy. The progressive round table will meet again next week immediately after the PSI's [Italian Socialist Party] general assembly.
