BFN [Text] Today, Cuban Foreign Minister concluded his tour of South America by responding to an invitation from the governor of the Argentine province of Misiones, Dr. Federico Ramon Puerta. During his 24-hour visit to Misiones, Robaina met with the leaders of several political parties of this northern province of Argentina and held conversations with Governor Federico Ramon Puerta. In their conversations, Robaina and Puerta ratified the agreements signed in Havana by Governor Puerta during his visit to our country. These agreements establish the sending of health care specialists, medical equipment, and medicine to Misiones in exchange for wood, paper, cardboard, flour, and soybean oil.
