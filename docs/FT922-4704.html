920604 FT 04 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Feltrim Mining shares suspended Feltrim Mining, traded on the Dublin Stock Exchange's Exploration Securities Market, had its shares suspended yesterday 'to allow clarification of its financial position'. The group said a trade investor had expressed interest in its gold leaching process. It is also finalising a placing announced in March. Further announcements would be made as soon as possible, Feltrim said.
