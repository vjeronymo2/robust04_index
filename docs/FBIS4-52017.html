BFN [Text] The National Assembly Judiciary Committee held a conference in Hue from 25 to 30 April to review a number of proposed laws and decrees to be presented for the National Assembly Standing Committee's consideration and approval. Attending the conference were representatives of the government Cadre Organization Committee, the party Central Committee Organization and Internal Affairs Departments, the National Assembly Economic and Budget Committee, the National Assembly Secretariat, and the People's Council and other agencies of Thua Thien-Hue Province. The conference heard reports on the bills amending and supplementing the Law on the People's Council and People's Committee; the Law on the Election of People's Councillors; the law regarding the rights and obligations of organizations at home in using land allocated or leased to them by the state; and the law regarding the rights and obligations of foreign organizations and individuals who are granted land leases in Vietnam. The conference also reviewed the Civil Servant Code and heard a report from the chief judge of the Supreme People's Court on the court's working mechanism. During their discussion, members of the National Assembly Judiciary Committee contributed many ideas to help the concerned agencies revise and perfect these drafts of laws and decrees, which will eventually contribute to the reconstruction of our nation..
