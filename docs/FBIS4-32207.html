BFN [Text] In Paris today, Slovak Defense Minister Pavol Kanis, who is visiting France for a day, signed an agreement on military cooperation between the two countries with his French counterpart, Francois Leotard. The agreement does not mention joint military exercises. According to Kanis, the new agreement is a continuation of cooperation between the two nations during the liberation of France and Slovakia in World War II. Leotard promised to take part in the celebrations of the Slovak National Uprising in August.
