August 26, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

WRONG TIME

Concerning the Aug. 19 interview with rock musician Bob Geldof: Samuel Pepys lived during the Restoration period in England, not the Reformation (as Geldof was quoted as saying).

The two periods were about 130 years apart: 1529 marked the beginning of the Reformation; 1660, the Restoration.

KIM THORNTON

Lake Forest
