Friday March 18, 1994 Part III Department of Defense General Services Administration National Aeronautics and Space Administration 48 CFR Parts 15, 35, and 52 Federal Acquisition Regulation; Withdrawal of Proposals; Proposed Rule Federal Register ␣/␣Vol. 59, No. 53␣/␣Friday, March 18, 1994␣/␣Proposed Rules DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION NATIONAL AERONAUTICS AND SPACE ADMINISTRATION 48 CFR Parts 15, 35, and 52 [FAR Cases 91&hyph;34 and 91&hyph;56] Federal Acquisition Regulation; Withdrawal of Proposals AGENCIES: Department of Defense (DOD), General Services Administration (GSA), and National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA). ACTION: Proposed rules; withdrawals.

SUMMARY: The Department of Defense, General Services Administration, and National Aeronautics and Space Administration have decided to withdraw two proposed rules prescribed in the Federal Register at 56 FR 64922, December 12, 1991, FAR Case 91&hyph;56, Research and Development Contracting, and at 56 FR 33826, July 23, 1991, FAR Case 91&hyph;34, Make or Buy Decisions. The decision was based on the Department of Defense's request to retain guidance in the Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement.
