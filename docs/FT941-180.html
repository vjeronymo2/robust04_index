940331 FT 31 MAR 94 / Rover plans Pounds 92m paint plant update Rover, the carmaker, plans to invest Pounds 92m over the next four years to modernise the paint shops at its three UK vehicle assembly plants. The group, acquired earlier this month by BMW of Germany, said yesterday it was initially investing Pounds 42m to refurbish one of the two paintshops at its Longbridge assembly plant in Birmingham, increasing capacity by 25 per cent to 250,000 cars a year.
