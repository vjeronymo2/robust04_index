November 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: ALTADENA;

RESERVOIR COMPLETION DATE

A reservoir under construction at 253 W. Harriet St. is expected to be completed by April, said Camille Dudley, chairwoman of the Town Council's Land Use Committee.

The reservoir, which will cost nearly $900,000, is being built by the Foothill Municipal Water District and will serve all of Altadena.
