940128 FT 28 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Witan Inv lifts net asset value to 271.7p Witan Investment reported a 28 per cent rise in net asset value per share over the 1993 year, from 212.8p to 271.7p. Retained revenue amounted to Pounds 680,000 (Pounds 373,000). The dividend for the year is raised to 5.8p (5.6p) via a final dividend of 3.1p. Earnings per share came through at 6.11p (5.71p).
