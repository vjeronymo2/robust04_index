BFN [Text] The president of the normal Senate has traveled to the United States. Firmin Jean-Louis departed Port-au-Prince on 15 April for Washington. The president of the normal Senate is scheduled to confer with President Aristide on the crisis. Senator Jean-Louis is also to go to Chicago to meet with American personalities. Before leaving Port-au-Prince, Sen. Jean-Louis proposed that the deputies pass the amnesty law before the nomination of a prime minister by the president in exile.
