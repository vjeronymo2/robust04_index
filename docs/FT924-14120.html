921012 FT 12 OCT 92 / Broackes steps down SIR Nigel Broackes is to give up control of Trafalgar House, the property, construction and shipping group he founded more than 30 years ago. He is to step down as chairman and become life president in a move that is part of a series of managerial changes at the group, which has thwarted an attempt by Hongkong Land to increase its stake substantially. Mr Allan Gormly, head of the engineering division, is replacing Sir Eric Parker as chief executive. Report, Page 16
