BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Fedor Angeliy] [Text] Kishinev [Chisinau] April 30 TASS -- Moldavian [Moldovan] President Mircea Snegur and Dniester Republic leader Igor Smirnov made a joint statement on Saturday outlining ways to resolve the Dniester conflict. The statement signed in presence of representatives of the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe (CSCE) and Russian President Boris Yeltsin's envoys said that a new social and political situation has emerged in the republic, creating prerequisites for successful resolution of the crisis. Both leaders welcomed the mediatory role of CSCE and the Russian Federation in settling the Dniester conflict. Snegur and Smirnov stressed the need to determine the status of the Dniester Republic, establish official relations, delegate and delineate powers and fix up a transitional period for gradual implementation of reached agreements in all spheres. The document also calls for establishing firm guarantees, including with international participation, ensuring full and unconditional implementation of these agreements.
