920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / International Capital Markets: Danish overseas debt set to fall DENMARK's overseas state debt is expected to fall to DKr77bn at the end of the year from DKr92.3bn at end-1991, provided the current account remains in surplus and private capital movements are at worst in balance, Reuter reports from Copenhagen. The central bank said the share of dollar-denominated debt rose to 19 per cent from 13 per cent in 1990 and that of Ecu debts rose to 20 per cent from 15 per cent.
