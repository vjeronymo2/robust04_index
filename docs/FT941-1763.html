940324 FT 24 MAR 94 / Japan motor output falls Japan's motor industry output fell 14.1 per cent in February against the same period last year, the 17th consecutive monthly fall, writes Paul Abrahams in Tokyo. The industry's performance underlines the depth of the recession. Output of cars, trucks and buses fell to 896,000 units, the sixth month in which the decline has been in double digits, a post-war record.
