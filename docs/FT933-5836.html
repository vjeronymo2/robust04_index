930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Greenacre Group GREENACRE GROUP has bought the assets and business of Clare Hall Nursing Home in Ston Easton, Bath, for Pounds 2.12m cash. Clare Hall is registered for 65 elderly, general medical patients, and for the year ended March 31 1993 turned in profits of Pounds 402,000 before both interest and tax. Greenacre now has 12 homes and 575 beds under operation or development.
