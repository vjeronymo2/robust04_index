Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Vector 21" newscast] [Text] Here in Mexico City, the Senate approved the presidential amnesty initiative for Chiapas State, with the opposition of PRD [Party of the Democratic Revolution] legislators. In a joint session held in the San Lazarus legislative chamber, the senators and deputies declared the end of an extraordinary session of Congress. At this moment, the legislators are on their way to Los Pinos official residence to inform President Carlos Salinas about the end of this extraordinary session. We expect this report to be rendered right now, at 1830 or possibly at 1900. We will keep you informed.
