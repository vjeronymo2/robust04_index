Tuesday September 6, 1994 Part VI Department of Transportation Federal Aviation Administration 14 CFR Parts 71 and 93 Offshore Airspace Reconfiguration; Valparaiso, Florida Terminal Area; Final Rule Federal Register ␣/␣Vol. 59, No. 171␣/␣Tuesday, September 6, 1994␣/␣Rules and Regulations DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION Federal Aviation Administration 14 CFR Parts 71 and 93 [Docket No. 26968; Amendment No. 71&hyph;24, 93&hyph;70] RIN 2120&hyph;AF45 Offshore Airspace Reconfiguration; Valparaiso, FL Terminal Area AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), DOT. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: This action retains the Valparaiso, Florida Terminal Area and Special Air Traffic Rules in part 93 of the Federal Aviation Regulations (FAR); revises the Class D airspace areas for Eglin Air Force Base (AFB), the Eglin Air Force (AF) Auxiliary No. 3 Duke Field, and Hurlburt Field; revises the Crestview Class E airspace area; and deletes the Eglin Class D North-South corridor. Additionally, this action modifies the established North-South and East-West corridors associated with the Valparaiso, Florida Terminal Area and Eglin AFB in part 93 of the FAR. This action is necessary to simplify operating procedures, airspace assignment and airspace use within the Valparaiso, Florida Terminal Area. EFFECTIVE DATE: This amendment is effective on December 8, 1994.
