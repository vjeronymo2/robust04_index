930601 FT 01 JUN 93 / International Company News: ICI demerger leads to changes in FT-SE Actuaries Share Indices As a result of the prospective demerger of ICI and Zeneca, which will come into effect today, all constituents of the FT-SE 100 have been ranked by size. Both ICI and Zeneca will be included in the FT-SE 100 Index, while English China Clays, the smallest capitalised constituent at close of trading Friday, has been removed from the FT-SE 100 Index. English China Clays will become a constituent of the FT-SE Mid 250 Index. Owners Abroad, the smallest capitalised constituent at close of trading, has been removed from the FT-SE Mid 250 Index; it will become a constituent of the FT-SE SmallCap Index. No companies will be removed from the FT-SE SmallCap. When the demerger of ICI and Zeneca comes into effect, ICI will remain a constituent of the Financial Times Ordinary Share Index (the '30 Share').
