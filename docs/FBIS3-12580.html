Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] At end of the polling for the 37 seats of the Senate today, unofficial results of all the seats have been received. The Pakistan People's Party has won 16 seats, the Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz Group, six; and the Awami National Party, three. The Pakistan Muslim League-Junejo Group, the Jamhoori Watan Party, and the Muhajir Qaumi Movement-Altaf Group have secured two seats each, while the Jamaat-i-Islami, the Balochistan National Movement-Hayee Group, the Balochistan National Movement-Mengal Group, the Jamiat-i-Ulema-i-Islam, and the Pakhtoonkhwa Milli Awami Party have one seat each. One Senate seat [words indistinct] has been won by Shafqat Mahmud. [passage omitted]
