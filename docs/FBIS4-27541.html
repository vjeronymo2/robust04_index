BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 30 (KCNA) -- Iron and steel production is increasing in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. According to data available at the Ministry of Metal Industry, the production of pig iron increased 1.8 times, and that of steel and rolled steel respectively 1.5 times this month till the 25th compared with the same period of last month. In the same period, the Kim Chaek iron and steel complex produced over 53,000 more tons of pig iron, 30,000 more tons of steel and 23,000 more tons of rolled steel. The production of pig iron has increased by 7 percent, and steel by 10 percent at the Hwanghae iron and steel complex. The Chollima steel complex and other steel works also have topped their daily production of rolled steel 20 percent as against last month.
