BFN [Report by correspondent Orestes Eujelles from the "Exclusive" program] [Text] Xiao Zeban, a member of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions Secretariat, has underscored the importance of coordinating Chinese and Cuban labor union efforts to obtain greater success. The Chinese labor union leader made this statement today, during a meeting in Havana between the high ranking delegation which he heads and members of the national secretariat of the Cuban Workers Federation [CTC]. Xiao expressed opposition to the U.S. blockade against Cuba on behalf of the 104 million members of the Chinese labor union movement and described the Cuban people as brave and audacious because of their firmness in light of the difficulties. National CTC Second Secretary Salvador Valdes highlighted Cuban and Chinese efforts to develop their economies without abandoning the important role of the workers and socialism.
