930112 FT 12 JAN 93 / Whites plan South African exodus A South African research group said yesterday that more than 250,000 whites were thinking of emigrating because of economic problems, crime and political uncertainty, Reuter reports from Johannesburg. Such fears were also discouraging foreign professionals from coming to South Africa. The Human Sciences Research Council said a 'frightening number' of white school-leavers, particularly Afrikaans-speakers, were thinking of leaving.
