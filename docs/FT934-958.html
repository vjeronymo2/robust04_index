931222 FT 22 DEC 93 / Watchdog attacks DVLA's accuracy More than one in three of drivers' records and one in four of vehicle records kept by the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency are inaccurate, says the National Audit Office. However, a number of the errors were 'relatively minor'. The Society of Motor Manufacturers and Traders said 3.6m of the 34m vehicle records had wrong or missing information at the time of the last vehicle census.
