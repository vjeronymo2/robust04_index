BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 31 (KCNA) -- The inaugural ceremony of the South Chungchong Provincial Federation of General Student Councils (Chungnam Chongnyon) was held in Chonan on May 6 and 7 with the attendance of some 2,000 students of 13 universities and colleges in South Chungchong Province, South Korea, according to a report. At the ceremony the students declared that 40,000 students would unite under the banner of the National Alliance of Youth and Students for the Country's Reunification (Pomchonghangnyon) and fight vigorously for independence, democracy and reunification and for the abolition of the "National Security Law". Students in an inaugural declaration said they would check the opening of agricultural market by the South Korean authorities.
