December 14, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Craig T. Gant has been named president and chief executive of United Business Interiors, a commercial furnishings company in Los Angeles. Gant, 40, had been senior vice president-operations, administration and chief financial officer of Wilson Business Products in Houston, a company affiliated with United Business Interiors. He succeeds John Jones, who left the company to pursue other business interests.
