BFN [Text] An action plan is to be launched in Jammu and Kashmir for improving the law and order situation. The BSF [Border Security Force], CRPF [Central Reserve Police Force], and the Jammu and Kashmir Police will coordinate in its implementation. The plan will have both short-term and long-term perspectives. The minister of state for home, Rajesh Pilot, discussed the details at a meeting he had with the minister of state for defense, Mr. Mallikarjun, in New Delhi yesterday. They reviewed the steps being taken to deal with the situation in Jammu and Kashmir, particularly in Doda District. The secretaries of home and defense, the director generals of BSF and CRPF, and senior Army and Home Ministry officials attended the discussions.
