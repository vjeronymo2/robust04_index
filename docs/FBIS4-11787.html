BFN [Text] The prime minister is conferring with opposition leaders on the situation in the territories. Before the meeting, the opposition leaders met and decided to urge Mr. Rabin to make a commitment not to evacuate any settlements. There was a large meeting at the prime minister's bureau this morning to sum up the agreement reached on Hebron security arrangements and the resumption of the Gaza-Jericho first talks. Our political correspondent reports that Israel has expressed displeasure with the suspension of the Israeli-PLO economic talks, which the PLO initiated. The delegation for talks on Gaza-Jericho security will leave for Cairo on Sunday and on Monday the delegation for talks on civilian matters will leave.
