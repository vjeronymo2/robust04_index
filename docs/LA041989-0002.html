April 19, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

LACC SCHEDULES MEMORIAL FOR DR. JOHN LOMBARDI

A memorial service for Dr. John Lombardi, president of Los Angeles City College from 1955 to 1966, will be held Tuesday at 1:30 p.m. in the campus Student Center.

Lombardi was 85 when he died Feb. 28 in Ventura.

After retiring from City College in 1966 he became assistant superintendent of all the community colleges for the Los Angeles district.
