Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 13 Jan (MENA)--A police warrant officer and two sentries were killed today when unknown assailants fired at them in front of a traffic police station in Asyut. According to an Interior Ministry source, unidentified gunmen fired at the Bani Murr traffic police station, near al-Qusiyah, in Asyut Governorate today, killing Warrant Officer Kamil Ahmad Salim of the traffic police and police sentries 'Ali Husayn Shaybub and 'Abd-al-Majid 'Abd-al-'Al Shihatah. The assailants managed to escape. Police investigators and forensic experts are examining the shooting scene. Police have begun an intensive search for the attackers.
