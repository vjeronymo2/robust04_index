BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 6 (XINHUA) -- The Agricultural Bank of China (ABC) opened a representative office in Tokyo today in an effort to exploit business activities in Japan. Inaugurating the office, ABC President Ma Yongwei said it will concentrate on collecting international financial information and raising commercial funds in Japan. The move is a step toward the ABC's internationalization and will serve to promote its exchange and cooperation with Japanese commercial banks, Ma said. The ABC has already set up representative offices in Hong Kong, London, New York and Singapore. The move followed recent opening of offices in Tokyo by the People's Bank of China, the Construction Bank and the Communication Bank. The Bank of China also has a Tokyo branch.
