920930 FT 30 SEP 92 / The ERM and Maastricht: Amato tries to calm nerves on bank deposits ITALY'S prime minister, Mr Giuliano Amato, yesterday sought to stop what he said was an irrational rush to withdraw cash from banks, by assuring the public that the government was not planning to touch their money. Bankers said there had been a trend among clients to sell up investment portfolios and withdraw cash, apparently fuelled by rumours the government was planning to freeze 30 per cent of bank deposits. 'It is not a rush, but it is noticeable,' said the director of one large bank.
