BFN [Text] Hojjat ol-Eslam val-Moslemin Mohammadi-Reyshahri, the representative of the vali-e faqih [vice-regent of the Supreme Jusrisconsult] and the supervisor of the Iranian pilgrimage, set off for Saudi Arabia this evening. He was seen off by Hojjat ol-Eslam val-Moslemin Mohammadi-Golpayegani, a representative of the exalted leader; Mr. Mo'ayeri, a representative of the president; and several of our country's political and religious personalities. In an interview with our correspondent before his departure, he referred to the exalted leadership's stress on the need for the realization of the principles delineated for Iranian pilgrims to the House of God by the great leader of the Islamic revolution [Khomeyni], and called upon pilgrims to concentrate all their efforts on the exaltation of the principles of pure Mohammadan Islam and the bond among Muslims. He expressed the hope that cooperation by the Saudi Arabian Government results in the better implementation of the envisaged programs for the Iranian pilgrims during the holy period of hajj. Hojjat ol-Eslam val-Moslemin Mohammadi-Reyshahri called on Iranian pilgrims to observe discipline and regulations and actively participate in the ceremonies and rites, and warned them to avoid any act that might mar the unity of Muslims. He stressed that during this year's hajj rites, Iranian pilgrims will perform their religious and divine duties, headed by the ceremony of disavowal of infidels.
