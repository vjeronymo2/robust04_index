920115 FT 15 JAN 92 / Air traffic record for Manchester A RECORD 10.8m passengers used Manchester airport last year in spite of the recession and the effects of the Gulf war. International traffic fell 2 per cent to 3m passengers and the recession caused a fall of 10 per cent in domestic passengers to 2m. Chartered business offset those falls with a 6 per cent rise to 5.8m.
