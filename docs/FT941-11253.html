940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / EU unemployment steady The unemployment rate in the European Union remained unchanged in December 1993 from November, at a seasonally adjusted 10.9per cent, writes Our Foreign Staff. Eurostat, the EU statistical office,yesterday reported that average annual unemployment in the EU was about 15.8m or 10.5 per cent in 1993, compared with 13.9m (9.4 per cent) in 1992. Unemployment rose in all EU countries between the two years - most notably in Spain, Germany, Italy and France.
