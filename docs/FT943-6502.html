940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / Observer: Greed kills Those Lloyd's victims who can still afford to run a shotgun are sporting a new fashion on the grouse moors this summer. Underneath their plus-fours, they are wearing shooting stockings emblazoned with the words 'Damn' and 'Lloyd's', c/o an enterprising firm called Nicholl Knitwear, based in Corbridge. Meanwhile, the grouse are wearing T-shirts saying: 'The one behind is fatter.'
