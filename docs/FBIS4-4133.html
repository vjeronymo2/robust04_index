BFN [From the "News" program] [Text] During a news briefing given by the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs today, a reporter asked the ministry's spokesman: A Taiwanese passenger plane crashed in Nagoya, Japan, on 26 April, and more than 200 people died. What is your comment? The spokesman replied: We want to express our condolences for our Taiwan countrymen and other passengers who died in the crash of the Taiwan passenger plane in Nagoya. We also want to express our sympathy to the families of those who died in the plane crash.
