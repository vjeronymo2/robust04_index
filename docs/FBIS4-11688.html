BFN [Text] The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] is preparing to evacuate all forces from most parts of the Gaza Strip and their deployment to the Qatif Bloc settlements and the northern edge of the Strip. A Qol Yisra'el reporter says that IDF commanders in the Gaza Strip and Jericho say that once the order is given, they will be able to complete the redeployment within 72 hours. Yesterday bulldozers began clearing land near the settlement of Vered Yeriho in order to set up a crossing point to and from the autonomous region of Jericho. An Army base and a police and border police post are also to be built near the crossing point. The Defense Ministry is considering setting up a special body which will deal with coordination between the IDF and security forces and the Palestinians in the autonomous zones of the Gaza Strip and Jericho. Qol Yisra'el's military affairs correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that a senior Army officer will head the committee.
