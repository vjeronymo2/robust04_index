930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Revised dividend information Due to the tax changes in the Budget the following companies have issued revised dividend information: Baird (William) is paying a second interim of 5.35p in lieu of the reported final, for a total of 8.9p. Midlands Electricity is to pay a second interim of 9p, making 15.35p to date. Perpetual has announced an interim of 1.8p (1.2p) with the payment date brought forward from the usual May to April 5. Prowting is paying a second interim of 1.7p in lieu of a final, making a total of 3.4p (5p) for the year.
