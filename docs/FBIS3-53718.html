Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Excellency the Ethiopian foreign minister, Mesfin Seyoum, has said he conveyed a message to the Servant of the Two Holy Places, King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud. The message was from the prime minister of the Ethiopian transitional government, Zenawi Meles. Seyoum presented the message when he was received by Prince Sultan Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, second deputy prime minister, minister of defense and aviation, and inspector general on 15 January. In a statement to SPA before leaving Jeddah today, Seyoum indicated that he discussed with Saudi officials a number of issues relating to the Horn of Africa, the Red Sea, and the Middle East, adding that the two countries had similar views on everything concerning this region. He added that he held a series of meetings with officials from the chambers of commerce in Riyadh and Jeddah as well as businessmen with a view to bolstering economic relations and commercial exchanges between the two countries.
