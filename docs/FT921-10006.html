920208 FT 08 FEB 92 / Finance & the Family: M&G outlines new trust M&G has issued preliminary details of its Recovery Investment Trust. Listing particulars will be published on February 14 and the offer will close on March 27. The split capital trust will have three classes of shares: income, capital and zero dividend preference. The yield on the trust is expected to be around that payable on M&G's Recovery unit trust, which currently pays 5.1 per cent.
