941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / World News in Brief: Orange County files for bankruptcy Orange County, California, hit by a loss of Dollars 1.5bn (Pounds 910m) from dealings in the bond markets, filed for bankruptcy early today. The county's board of supervisors said bankers had declined to roll over Dollars 1.2bn in repurchase agreements, resulting in default. The move is seen as a means of stopping investors from withdrawing funds.
