June 6, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

TRUMP AND L.A. SCHOOL BOARD BATTLE OVER AMBASSADOR SITE

Why haven't city officials offered a portion of the Central City West Development Project (Part A, May 22) as a possible school site? With Los Angeles' need for new schools coupled with the controversy surrounding the 23.5-acre Ambassador Hotel site, our city would surely make room in this 355-acre area for at least one new school.

GARY GARCIA

Los Angeles
