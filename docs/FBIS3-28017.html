Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 2 (KCNA) -- Gary Ackerman, chairman of the Subcommittee on Asian and Pacific Affairs of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the House of Representatives of the United States, was interviewed by a reporter of the newspaper USA TODAY on January 26 on the decision of the U.S. administration to deploy Patriot missiles in South Korea. He expressed apprehensions, saying the deployment of Patriot missiles will no doubt cause complex problems. We must foresee a rather strong protest and denunciation from North Korea, he added.
