BFN [Text] Yesterday in Vientiane capital, Khamphoui Keoboualapha, deputy prime minister of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR] and chairman of the Committee for Planning and Cooperation, received a courtesy call from the economic cooperation and development delegation of the Federal Republic of Germany [FRG] headed by Wighard Hardte, state secretary and deputy minister for economic cooperation and development, which is paying a visit to the LPDR. During the call, the host and guests conversed in an atmosphere of intimacy. Deputy Prime Minister Khamphoui Keoboualapha hailed and highly assessed the visit to Laos by the delegation, pointing out that it served to further strengthen the relations of friendship between the LPDR and the FRG. He wished the delegation success during its visit. The FRG economic cooperation and development delegation arrived in Vientiane at noon on the same day for a three-day visit.
