Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] In a statement, the Democratic Youth Forum, the Albanian Students Independent Union, the student wing of the Albanian Democratic Party, and the Political Persecutees Youth League express their deep indignation and strong protest against the Serbian occupying authorities in Prishtina who, provocatively and intentionally, have arrested the chairman of the Independent Students Union of Prishtina University, Fisnik Cukaj. They appeal to all European youth bodies to raise their voices to defend their Kosovar counterparts and exert pressure so that Fisnik Cukaj and others are not murdered by the Serbian dictatorship but can enjoy the legitimate right of European citizens.
