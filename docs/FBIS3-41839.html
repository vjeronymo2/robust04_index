Language: English Article Type:BFN [Following item transmitted via KYODO] [Text] As many as 339 people with symptoms of typhoid, among them 116 children, have been hospitalized in Volgodonsk (Rostov Region). The diagnosis of 213 patients, among them 73 children, has been confirmed, the press service of Russia's State Committee for Emergencies told Interfax. Sanitary measures are being taken in the city.
