940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / UK Company News: 2.4p maiden from Gartmore Shared Equity In the first published figures since its formation in October, Gartmore Shared Equity Trust reported a net asset value of 126p per geared ordinary income share at January 31. The net asset value of the zero dividend preference shares stood at 102.3p at the same date. After tax revenue for the period from October 21 to January 31 amounted to Pounds 496,000. Earnings per geared ordinary income share emerged at 1.37p. A maiden dividend of 2.4p is being declared. The trust was set up to offer shareholders in Gartmore Value Investments, a split-capital trust due to wind up in 1995, the opportunity to switch into an investment with a longer life. It would also have a wider spread of equities.
