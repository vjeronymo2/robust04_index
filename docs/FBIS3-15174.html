Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Russia will not recognize the creation of an independent state of Bosnian Serbs. This was stated by Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev in an interview to RIA. At the same time he underlined that Russia recognizes the Bosnian Serbs as one of the conflicting sides. The head of the Foreign Ministry paid special attention to the conclusion of an agreement on the airport in Tuzla. Kozyrev believes that this agreement could be assessed as the second breakthrough in the settlement of the Bosnian crisis, after Sarajevo. The Russian minister also added that Moscow does not insist on the sending of a U.S. peacemaking contingent to Bosnia.
