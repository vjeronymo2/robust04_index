Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] On Thursday the Ukrainian Supreme Council passed a law on the formation of local power and self-government bodies. According to the law, after the elections, which will be held on June 26 simultaneously with presidential elections, the Ukrainian law on presidential representatives will become ineffective. Under the law, chairmen of local councils of people's deputies are elected by the population of a region and become heads of executive committees of the corresponding councils. They are subordinate to the corresponding local councils. In issues related to authorities of the state and executive powers, local councils are subordinate to the Cabinet and executive bodies of superior councils.
