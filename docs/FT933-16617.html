930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Glenchewton Glenchewton achieved significant profits in the second half of 1992 and reduced the year's loss to Pounds 54,000. That compared with a deficit of Pounds 275,000 in the 8 months to December 31 1991. Sales totalled Pounds 28.2m (Pounds 20.6m) and losses per share worked through at 0.73p (1.54p).
