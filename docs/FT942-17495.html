940402 FT 02 APR 94 / International Company News: Growth at Sun Micro falls short of expectations Sun Microsystems, the US maker of computer work-stations and operating systems, said revenues in its third quarter were up only slightly from a year before - well below the 12-15 per cent growth rate generally expected by analysts. In a brief statement after the stock market closed on Thursday, Sun said it estimated that revenues in the period to March 27 were Dollars 1.17-Dollars 1.20bn, compared with Dollars 1.14bn a year earlier. Sales in the US were held back 'to some extent' by the introduction last week of new products, which had led some customers to delay purchases, said Michael Lehman, chief financial officer.
