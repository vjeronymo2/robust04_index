BFN [Text] Beijing, May 16 (XINHUA) -- The four-day China conference on market economy and trade opened here this morning. Chinese Vice-President Rong Yiren attended the opening ceremony and delivered a congratulatory speech. In his speech, Rong said that the United States has the most developed market economy in the Western world, and China is building its socialist market economy. He continued that it is of great significance for experts, scholars and entrepreneurs of the two countries to get together to discuss and study issues concerning the market economy so as to enhance mutual understanding between the two countries. President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship With Foreign Countries Qi Huaiyuan, president of the China Market Economy Society Wang Jue, and president of the Foundation for International Cooperation and Development Norman Swanson also spoke at the opening ceremony. Representatives from the Chinese Foreign Ministry, the Ministry of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade Attended the opening ceremony. About 300 representatives, including nearly 90 U.S. representatives from the fields of energy, communications, chemicals, aviation, textiles, computers and food processing, will hold discussions on issues of common interest such as securities and financial markets, banking and accounting principles, investment protection, joint ventures, real estate, traditional and non- traditional trade and taxation. Business negotiations will also be held during the conference.
