920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / International Company News: McDonald's posts 10% gain for quarter McDONALD'S, the US fast food company, posted 10 per cent gains in net income and per-share earnings for the second quarter and the first half, writes Barbara Durr in Chicago. Net income for the quarter was Dollars 254.1m, or 69 cents a share, against Dollars 232m, or 63 cents, a year ago. Net income for the first half was Dollars 441.5m, or Dollars 1.20, against Dollars 400.5m, or Dollars 1.09. Sales rose 9 per cent to Dollars 5.4bn in the quarter from Dollars 4.9bn last year. First-half sales also climbed 9 per cent, to Dollars 10.3bn from Dollars 9.5bn.
