940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / International Company News: Paper group strong Yuen Foong Yu Paper Manufacturing, Taiwan's leading paper maker, posted net profits of TDollars 860m (USDollars 32.7m) in the year to June 30 against TDollars 449m a year earlier. Tatung, the Taiwanese electrical appliance maker, saw after-tax profits in the year to June 30 rise to TDollars 2.88bn from TDollars 1.34bn last time.
