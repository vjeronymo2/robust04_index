931223 FT 23 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Courtaulds sells coatings business Courtaulds, the international chemical company, has disposed of its east London-based general industrial coatings business to Croda Polymers International. The sale comprises the goodwill and technology of the business for Pounds 2.7m, plus the value of the finished goods stock - no fixed assets are involved. Turnover in the year to March 31 1993 was Pounds 6m.
