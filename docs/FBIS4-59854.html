BFN [Text] Tehran, May 15, IRNA -- Kazakh Minister of Energy and Fuel resources Kadyr Baykenov and Oil Minister Gholam Reza Aqazadeh-Kho'i here Sunday discussed in detail construction of an lubricant factory in Kazakhstan and laying of pipelines for transfer of Kazakh crude oil by Iranian experts. The two sides also favoured long-term cooperation between Tehran and Almaty in the economic and oil fields, which, they believed, could serve as a "strong" base for flourishment of their industry and economy. They also expressed keenness on expansion and deepening of bilateral relations and reviewed formerly reached agreements between the two countries' officials on bilateral cooperation in the areas of oil and natural gas. Baykenov accompanies Kazakh Premier Sergey Tereshchenko in his Iran visit.
