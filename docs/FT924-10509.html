921030 FT 30 OCT 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Apples remain the best fruit buy, reports the FFVIB. Good English apples include: Spartan at 30-40p a lb (30-45p) and Cox at 35-45p a lb (35-45p). Kiwifruit at 16-20p each (16-20p), oranges at 12-25p each (12-25p) and pineapples at 80p-Pounds 1.00 each (80p-Pounds 1.00) round off the week's best buys. It's Halloween on Saturday so pumpkins are essential and reasonably priced at 15-20p a lb (15-20p). Root vegetables continue to be good value with carrots at 15-20p a lb (15-20p), and onions at 15-25p a lb (15-25p). Homegrown Chinese leaves at 40-50p a lb (50-65p) are excellent value.
