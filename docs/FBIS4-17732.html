BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Tamara Zamyatina and Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow, 28 Apr -- Vyacheslav Kostikov, press secretary of the Russian president, has described the signing of the Treaty on Social Accord as "a very major event in the history of Russia." The treaty "has become a reality," "Russia has entered a new political and historical dimension, and the foundations for the civilized existence of a civil society have been laid," he told journalists after the treaty signing ceremony. The press secretary said: "Only three people did not sign the treaty. They were Mr. Zyuganov, the leader of the Communist faction; Mr. Vinogradov, chairman of the Vladimir Oblast Soviet; and Mr. Tuleyev, chairman of the Kemerovo Oblast Soviet."
