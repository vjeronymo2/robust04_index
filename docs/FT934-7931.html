931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / Demjanjuk order revoked A US federal appeals court yesterday overturned the 1987 order under which accused Nazi death camp guard John Demjanjuk was extradited to Israel and said government prosecutors committed a fraud on the court in obtaining it, Reuter reports from Cincinnati. Mr Demjanjuk, 73, was freed by Israel after courts ruled that he was a victim of mistaken identify. He returned to the US in September.
