BFN [Text] In interaction with the Moscow Main Interior Department employees of the Federal Counterintelligence Service's [FCIS] Moscow and Moscow Region Department have detained a 10-strong group of people who were shipping contraband cargos of small guns from Estonia to Russia. The FCIS' Public Relations Center reported to Interfax that as many as 24 units of combat arms, TT pistols, Taurus-22, Luger-22, 390 small shot guns, 400 gas pistols, ammunitions, and 200,000 dollars were confiscated from the smugglers. An investigation is underway.
