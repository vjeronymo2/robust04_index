BFN ["All Matters Must Be Solved on Principle of National Independence"--KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, June 28 (KCNA) -- NODONG SINMUN today comes out with a by-lined article calling for solving all matters for national reunification on the principle of national independence. Noting that to adhere to the principle of national independence is an urgent demand for removing the danger of war created on the Korean peninsula and defending the exisoence of the nation, the article says: Keeping to the principle of national independence constitutes the starting point in achieving national reunification, the long cherished desire of the fellow countrymen, and establishing sovereignty of the nation throughout the country. Outside forces which divided our country will never make a present of reunification to our people. If some forces intend to attain a sinister aim in reliance on the strength of outside forces, negating the principle of national independence, it may cause grave consequences of victimizing the whole nation at the hands of outside forces. The reunification our nation wants is one to realize the independence of the nation, not one to live, shackled to others. The principle of national independence should be thoroughly observed to realize the cause of national reunification in conformity with the interests and demand of our nation. It is a lesson of history that if one depends on foreign forces and their interference is allowed, he will find himself in a miserable position in which he is compelled to study the pleasure of foreign forces even at the place of North-South dialogue and can not do anything for the nation, restricted by foreign forces. Anyone who wants to establish the sovereignty of the nation throughout the country by reunifying the country must naturally take the patriotic stand of demolishing the wall of division, joining hands with the fellow countrymen on the principle of national independence. Of importance at present in applying the principle of national independence is to thoroughly reject the idea of dependence on foreign forces, take the position of national independence and rely on the forces of national independence, not depending on outside forces. Dependence on foreign forces is the road to division, war and national ruin, and only national independence is the road to reunification, peace and prosperity. If the North and the South achieve peace and reunification by joining hands with each other, firmly adhering to the principle of national independence, the dignity and might of a reunified Korea will shine brilliantly all over the world.
