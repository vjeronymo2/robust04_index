940526 FT 26 MAY 94 / UK Company News: North American Gas net assets down Net asset value per share of North American Gas Investment Trust stood at 90.83p at April 30, against 101.68p a year earlier. For the nine months to end-April there were after-tax losses of Pounds 88,000 (Pounds 316,000 revenue), equivalent to 0.25p (0.9p earnings) per share.
