October 19, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: COMING ATTRACTIONS

Frank Sinatra and Michael Jackson will be among the superstars saluting Sammy Davis Jr. on his 60th anniversary in show business Nov. 13 at the Shrine Auditorium. Eddie Murphy will host the tribute, which will be taped for later telecasting on ABC. Proceeds from the event will go to the United Negro College Fund. The all-star cast includes Bill Cosby, Goldie Hawn, Gregory Hines and Liza Minnelli. ALEENE MacMINN
