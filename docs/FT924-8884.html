921109 FT 09 NOV 92 / Construction Contracts: Upgrading M25 orbital motorway BIRSE CONSTRUCTION has been awarded a motorway maintenance contract worth Pounds 11.9m. The contract relates to the partial reconstruction and overlay of a 7.5 kilometre section of the M25 between Junction 5 (Sevenoaks) and Junction 7 (M23). Birse Construction has also been given other road contracts, including the A563 for Leicestershire County Council (Pounds 2.5m) and at Trafford Park, Manchester (Pounds 1.4m).
