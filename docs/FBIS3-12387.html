Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, 24 Feb (INA) -- Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani, Yemeni minister of planning and development and member of the General Committee of the General People's Congress [GPC], has denied reports that a new Yemeni coalition government will be formed soon without the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP]. In a statement published today by the Bahraini newspaper AL-AYYAM, Dr. al-Iryani said these reports are baseless, pointing out that the formation of a new Yemeni government is presently not a subject of discussion. The paper disclosed that Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih has invited his "deputy" 'Ali Nasir Muhammad [former president of South Yemen] to form a new Yemeni government to replace the government of Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the current Yemeni prime minister. 'Ali Nasir Muhammad, however, rejected the offer.
