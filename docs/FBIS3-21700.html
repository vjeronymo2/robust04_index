Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 25 Feb (NA) -- In an operation resulting from the discovery on 24 February of 80 kg of cocaine on board the ship Eugenio C, personnel from the National Border Police and the Coast Guard seized drugs, cars, money, and documents, and arrested six people. In the operation, police raided several homes in the federal capital and Greater Buenos Aires and early this morning seized a large amount of marijuana; four cars--one Fiat Duna, one Fiat Uno, a Volkswagen, and a Ford Falcon; $697; 1.089 million lire; various documents; two high-precision scales; and 2 kg of different pieces of jewelry and gold bars. During the operation, six people were arrested. The overall result of the operation, which began on 24 February, was the seizure of 120 kg of cocaine and the arrest of 10 people. The most important part of the operation took place on board the Eugenio C, which was about to leave for the Brazilian port of Santos, where 80 kg of cocaine were found.
