Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, January 21 (SNARK) -- January 21 morning was characterized by relative peace at the Karabakh frontier. As SNARK was informed by the press-service of Headquarters of the Karabakh Defence Army, there was only positional exchange of fire in Mardakertskiy, Martuninskiy, Agdamskiy and Fizulinskiy regions. The most of the tension over the last 24 hours was in the front line part located in the north-west of Karabakh -- by the Omar pass, where military clashes took place. Today, about 11.30 a.m. several attack planes of the Azerbaijani Air Forces tried to violate Karabakh's air space. The details are being verified.
