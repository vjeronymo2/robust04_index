920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / Danes urge new role for WEU Canada's decision to withdraw its Nato troops from Europe underlines the importance of building up the Western European Union as the pact's European pillar, Danish Foreign Minister Uffe Ellemann-Jensen said yesterday, Reuter reports from Copenhagen. 'The European role in Nato must be carried out increasingly by Europeans,' he told Danish radio. The Canadian decision should come as no surprise, following US plans for a major cut in the number of its troops in Europe, he added. 'This development underlines the need for a new role for the WEU and the need for Denmark to join it,' he said.
