930208 FT 08 FEB 93 / Construction Contracts: Water projects BIRSE CONSTRUCTION has won contracts worth about Pounds 23m from four of the major water companies. The largest contract valued at Pounds 4.5m is for Anglian Water and comprises the civil engineering and building works for a new wastewater treatment plant at Cleethorpes. This is an extension to an existing Pounds 3.5m contract for the mechanical and electrical plant and equipment at the same site. A contract worth Pounds 3.8m to design and build a water treatment plant at Winterton has also been secured from the same client.
