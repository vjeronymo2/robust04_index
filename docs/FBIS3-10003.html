Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The widespread implementation of solutions to problems is an Interior Ministry [Minint] priority in 1994 in preparation for the Ninth Science and Technology Forum. The guidelines issued by Army Corps General Abelardo Colome Ibarra, a member of the Politburo and head of the Minint, calls for increasing the number of proposals to promote efficiency in defense tasks and help improve operational response time, as well as promote favorable welfare conditions for the troops.
