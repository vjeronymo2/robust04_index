940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: C&W offshoot lifts PLD stake to 33% Navona Communications, a wholly owned subsidiary of Cable and Wireless, has increased its interest in Petersburg Long Distance to 33.2 per cent. Navona, a private company incorporated in Bermuda, has acquired 1.65m common shares in PLD issued from treasury at Dollars 9.09 (Pounds 5.86) and a convertible debenture of Dollars 994,000 convertible into 109,000 common PLD shares. PLD is listed in New York and Toronto. Navona was acquired by C&W on March 29, when it held 25.1 per cent of PLD. Navona now has the right to acquire a further 1.54m common shares in PLD.
