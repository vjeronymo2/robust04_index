BFN [Text] Cairo, 17 Apr (MENA)--Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid today received Nayif Sa'ud al-Qadi, Jordan's ambassador in Cairo and permanent delegate to the Arab League. Ambassador al-Qadi stated afterward that he briefed Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid on Jordan's true stance on the peace process. He described the Israeli stance as false, noting that the intention is to pressure Jordan. He added that he conveyed to the Arab League secretary general the text of the Jordanian cabinet's statement on the (?latest) Israeli (?statements).
