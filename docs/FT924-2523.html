921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / Parliament and Politics: Package holiday measures passed NEW REGULATIONS designed to improve protection for package holidaymakers were approved by MPs last night despite coming under fire from both sides of the House. Mr Edward Leigh, a junior trade and industry minister, said the Package Travel, Package Holidays and Package Tours Regulations 1992 would provide 'a thorough and comprehensive framework for protection of customers.' But Mr Nigel Griffiths, shadow trade and industry spokesman, said the regulations - which come into force on January 1 in line with an EC directive - had been criticised by the tour industry, enforcement agencies and consumer bodies.
