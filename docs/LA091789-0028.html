September 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

.. . CLOSING QUOTE

"A few people are having major cows around the city at this point. If I had $100,000 to $200,000 of merchandise sitting at my back door and I couldn't ship it, I'd have a cow. But I don't."

-- Bernard Lax, president of a Los Angeles clothing manufacturing firm, on the nervousness of apparel makers who have orders slated for stores owned by the financially struggling Campeau Corp.
