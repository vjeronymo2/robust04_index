931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / N Sea platform contracts placed CONTRACTS for construction of the platform for the North Sea's Andrew field were placed yesterday by BP Exploration, the operator. The company placed the contract for the steel jacket with Highland Fabricators of Nigg, Ross-shire, and for the deck with Trafalgar House of Teesside. The 25-month construction begins next summer. The value of the UK's North Sea oil reserves fell by 7 per cent last month because of declining oil prices and a stronger pound, the Royal Bank of Scotland said yesterday.
