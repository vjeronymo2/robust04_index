BFN [Text] Sweden's negotiations on membership of the European Union [EU] have now definitely been concluded. In the Council of Ministers, the highest decision-making body in the EU, Sweden will get four votes; Sweden had asked for five votes in the negotiations. In the European Parliament Sweden will have 22 votes. If the result of the national referendum is "yes" in November, Sweden can become a member of the EU on 1 January 1995.
