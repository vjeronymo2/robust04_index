BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 23 (KCNA) -- The People's Vanguard of Costa Rica adopted a special resolution titled "Solidarity With the DPRK" on June 12, supporting the DPRK's proposal for denuclearising the Korean peninsula and expressing solidarity with the Workers' Party of Korea, the DPRK Government and people in the struggle against the new war provocation moves of the United States and the South Korean puppets. Pressure from the imperialist powers such as "sanctions" against the DPRK is a blasting fuse which may cause a war on the Korean peninsula and spread its flames worldwide, said the resolution, denouncing the United States and other imperialist powers for monopolizing nuclear weapons and using them as a means of threat and blackmail against other countries.
