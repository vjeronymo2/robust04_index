Language: German Article Type:BFN ["epd" report: "Bonn Development Aid for Tunisia"] [Text] Bonn, 25 Mar -- The Federal Government has promised 56.3 million marks [DM] in development aid to Tunisia. Of that, DM31.3 million need not be repaid. The country gets support for, among other things, steps to save water, the FRG Ministry of Economic Cooperation and Development announced in Bonn on Friday [25 March]. Tunisia will soon have developed all water reservoirs, while consumption will continue to increase.
