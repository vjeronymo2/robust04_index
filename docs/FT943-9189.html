940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Terms agreed for Leeds shopping mall Land Securities is to build a shopping centre with an estimated development cost of Pounds 100m at Churwell, Leeds. Land has reached agreement with White Rose (Leeds), a joint venture company between a subsidiary of Yorkshire Water and Evans of Leeds, the property company. Land will take a 999 year lease of the land in return for a share in the net rental income of the centre once it is opened.
