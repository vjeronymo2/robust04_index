930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Bennett & Fountain loses Pounds 1.58m Bennett & Fountain, the electrical group, continued to suffer from recession in the construction industry reporting losses of Pounds 1.58m pre-tax for the six months to December 31, against profits of Pounds 123,000. The operating loss was Pounds 564,000 (Pounds 819,000 profit), the result of worsening bad debts and the need for further branch closures.
