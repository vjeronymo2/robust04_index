BFN [Text] Windhoek May 2 SAPA -- Namibian Prime Minister Hage Geingob congratulated African National Congress President Nelson Mandela on Monday [2 May] on his party's victory in South Africa's first all race election. Mr Geingob said a new chapter in the history of Africa had begun. "From Cape to Cairo, Africa is free at last. I am elated and today my joy knows no bounds."
