Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Tahir Muhammad Hassun has been sworn in before leader President Saddam Husayn upon assuming his duties as mayor of Baghdad. Ghazi Ahmad Khattab, Staff Lieutenant General 'Abd-al-Wahid Shannan Al Ribat, and Mani' 'Abd-Rashid were also sworn in before leader President Saddam Husayn upon assuming their duties as governors of Salah al-Din, Ninawa, and al-Anbar Governorates respectively. The swearing-in ceremony was attended by Hatim Hamdan al-'Azzawi, head of the Presidential Office, and Watban Ibrahim al-Hasan, the minister of interior.
