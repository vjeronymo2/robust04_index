August 11, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: REAL ESTATE

Forbco Management Corp., the largest franchisee of Sizzler steak restaurants in Southern California, is moving over the Orange County line from Long Beach to Seal Beach, signing a $1.7-million, 10-year lease.

Forbco will lease 6,800 square feet from Bixby Ranch Co. of Long Beach, said Grubb & Ellis Co., which represented Forbco.
