Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Jan 27 -- Minister of Defence Piotr Kolodziejdzyk has declared he wants to put an end to the "war over frequencies" waged by his ministry with the National Radio and Television Council (KRRIT), according to parliamentary Deputy Marek Siwiec, a member of the council. Siwiec was speaking to reporters on Thursday following a meeting with Kolodziejdzyk. The meeting had been arranged after Siwiec had submitted a formal question in parliament. However, Siwiec added that he was still waiting for a formal reply from the minister. The minister informed Siwiec about the measures to be taken by the Armed Forces in order to hand over to civilian users a number of frequencies used by the Air Force telecommunications services. However, according to Siwiec, Kolodziejdzyk had argued that the problem could be successfuly overcome only on condition that the Army received an extra 2 trillion zlotys (ca. 90 m dollars) to finance necessary technical restructuring.
