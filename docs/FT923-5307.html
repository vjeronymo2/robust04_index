920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / China detains student activist Chinese police yesterday detained a former student activist who fled the country after the suppression of the pro-democracy movement in 1989, writes Yvonne Preston in Beijing. Shen Tong, aged 24, returned to China four weeks ago, the first protester to do so. He planned a news conference today to announce his intention to establish a Beijing branch of the Democracy for China Fund, a group he co-founded after fleeing to the US.
