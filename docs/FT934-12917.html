931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil POWER, telecom and oil sectors led a rally in Sao Paulo after Mr Fernando Henrique Cardoso, Brazil's economy minister, said that President Itamar Franco had agreed to privatise state utilities. The Bovespa index of 48 most active shares rebounded from its sharp losses of earlier this week to close 1,653 higher at 16,685.
