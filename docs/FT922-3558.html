920610 FT 10 JUN 92 / International Company News: Club Med sees FFr240m profit CLUB Mediterranee, the French leisure group, estimates it will have net attributable profit of FFr240m (Dollars 45m) and turnover of FFr8.4bn in the 12 months to October 31 this year, Reuter reports from Paris. The company also estimates it will have a net attributable profit of FFr20m on turnover of FFr3.8bn in the first half of fiscal 1992. Last year, Club Med posted a net attributable loss of FFr17m on sales of FFr7.6bn. REPSOL, Spain's state-owned petrochemicals company, will concentrate in coming future years on expanding international operations, but has no intention of diversifying from existing activities, Mr Oscar Fanjul, the chairman, said yesterday, Reuter reports from Madrid. 'To date we have consolidated in Spain but now international expansion will be a basic goal," he told the annual meeting.
