BFN [Text] Sinn Fein, the political wing of the IRA, has denied any links with the Islamic Republic of Iran. A Sinn Fein spokesman said that the British allegations are some vague propaganda without any basis and foundations. Moreover, Irish Prime Minister Albert Reynolds said that he was unaware of any links between the IRA and Iran. He said that so far he has not seen any firm evidences and reasons to prove the British claim. The London daily TIMES wrote that the British accusation against Iran is a biased political move, which have been carried out with America's coordination.
