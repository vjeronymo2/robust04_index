Language: Swedish Article Type:CSO [Finnish News Service/RITZAUS Bureau report: "Icelanders Want in EU"] [Text] Reykjavik -- A majority of Icelanders want talks to begin with the EU [European Union] on possible Icelandic membership, according to a recent opinion poll. In the survey, in which 600 qualified Icelandic voters were interviewed, 56 percent supported membership with the EU, while 43 percent were opposed.
