March 14, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski, who had his biggest winning day -- $359.10 on Sunday -- will try to keep his streak going today when he wagers a $25 win parlay from Maid O Gold in the third to Kimo's Kid in the seventh. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now back to $925.80.
