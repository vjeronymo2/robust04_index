930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / UN plea over Cyprus clash The UN yesterday urged restraint in Cyprus and said 13 of its troops had been injured at the weekend when hundreds of Greek-Cypriot motorcyclists tried to cross into the Turkish zone, Agencies report. On Sunday, up to 1,000 motorcyclists protesting against the 19-year division of Cyprus barged past Greek-Cypriot police and UN troops into the buffer zone, provoking fights and stone-throwing with Turkish troops.
