BFN [Text] Havana, June 6 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese Communist Party delegation, headed by Wu Bangguo, a member of the Politburo of the party Central Committee, arrived here today for a six-day official visit to Cuba. At Havana Airport, Wu spoke highly of the traditional friendship between the ruling parties of the two countries and the two peoples. He expressed the hope that his visit would enhance the mutual understanding and strengthen the friendly relations between China and Cuba. Wu said that the Chinese delegation was keen to exchange views with leaders of the Cuban Communist Party on the situation in their respective countries and bilateral relations.
