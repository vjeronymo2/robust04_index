BFN [Text] Our security correspondent told us a short while ago that in the context of investigations held by Justice Joseph Furayhah, investigating magistrate in the case involving the bombing in Sayyidat al-Najat church bombing, and as a result of the confessions made by a number of the Lebanese Forces' shock troops, an Army unit last night began a search for 17 bags said to contain bombs, powerful explosives, and detonators. The bags were believed to be in the possession of certain individuals living in 15 different villages. The unit succeeded in finding 12 bags and are looking for the rest. Several individuals were remanded in custody in connection with the recent findings.
