Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers Press Center has announced that according to information supplied by the medical team treating Prime Minister Lyuben Berov, the team made an extended visit to their patient at about 1800 hours this afternoon, with the surgeons Prof. Levi and Docent Mishev also in attendance. After a five-hour operation, the prime minister is in a stable condition. It was established that all internal organs were operating normally and that the patient's blood pressure was also normal.
