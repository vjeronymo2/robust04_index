930427 FT 27 APR 93 / 55 die in Indian aircraft crash At least 55 people were killed when an Indian Airlines Boeing 737 with 118 on board crashed near the western Indian town of Aurangabad yesterday, writes Shiraz Sidhva in New Delhi. The flight crashed soon after take-off from Aurangabad, a stop en route from Delhi to Bombay. Sixty-one people, including four crew, were rescued and two are still missing.
