Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The defense force has said that members of Self-Defense Units [SDU] are involved in incidents of violence, making it difficult to establish peace and stability on the East Rand. The commander of the defense force's Group 41, Colonel C.P du Toit, said members of the SDU were intimidating residents and were in possession of illegal weapons. Earlier the ANC [African National Congress] appointed a commission of inquiry into the activities of the units. However, the Ministry of Law and Order believes that the Goldstone Commission should carry out the investigation.
