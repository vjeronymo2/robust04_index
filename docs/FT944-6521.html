941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Cantab Pharmac Cantab Pharmaceuticals, the biopharmaceutical company, said it had met a significant milestone in the three months to September 30 with the opening of a pilot manufacturing facility on budget at Pounds 1.6m. In the nine months to end-September pre-tax losses were Pounds 2.71m (Pounds 2m) on turnover of Pounds 1.11m (Pounds 837,000). Losses per share were 27p (33p).
