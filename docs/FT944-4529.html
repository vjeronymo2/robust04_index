941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Lucky-Goldstar International agrees Singapore venture Lucky-Goldstar International, the trading arm of South Korea's Lucky-Goldstar Group, has agreed with Russia's Kumertau Aircraft to set up a joint venture based in Singapore to sell Russian civilian helicopters in Asia. Each holds a 45 per cent stake in the venture with paid-in capital of Dollars 600,000, and an Australian trading company has the remaining 10 per cent.
