BFN [Excerpts] [Announcer] [passage omitted] Our colleague Judit Laszlo now reports on the signing of the coalition agreement. [Laszlo] [passage omitted] All the personnel proposals will only become definitive when, together with the other chapters of the coalition agreement, they are adopted on 26 June by the Hungarian Socialist Party congress and the Alliance of Free Democrats delegate assembly. Prime Minister-nominee Gyula Horn also outlined the tasks of the immediate future. It is proposed that the parliamentary debate of the government program and the election of the head of government should take place 14-15 July. Parliamentary deputies should receive the program on 8 July. Another proposal is that the minister nominees should be heard by the relevant committees of the National Assembly 11-12 July.
