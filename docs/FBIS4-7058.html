BFN [Text] President Ramos has approved the adoption of several measures aimed at accelerating the implementation of the Brunei Darussalam, Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines-East ASEAN Growth Area (BIMP-EAGA), reporters learned yesterday. During the Cabinet meeting last Tuesday, the president directed the immediate establishment of a Philippine council to pursue the implementation of the BIMP-EAGA. He also ordered various government agencies to undertake activities for the operationalization of the EAGA and to identify additional flagship development projects in Mindanao to support the same. To boost awareness and speed up implementation, Mr. Ramos likewise ordered the creation of additional investment and tourism promotion councils (ITPCs) in major cities of Mindanao to conduct awareness campaign on the BIMP-EAGA. Currently, there are ITPCs in Davao, Zamboanga, and Cagayan de Oro. The additional councils will be located in Cotabato, Gen. Santos, Butuan, and Iligan. The EAGA was formally launched last March in Davao City.
