Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Alain Juppe defended a strong French position in Brussels yesterday. He was supported, in particular, by Belgium, the Netherlands, and Italy. According to today's edition of LE FIGARO, three French Navy warships, including the aircraft carrier Foch, are to leave Toulon in the next few hours to join three other French warships already in the Adriatic. The aircraft aboard the Foch -- some 20 Super Etendard -- would complement the Air Force elements made up of Mirage and Jaguar aircraft already based in Italy.
