940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / London Stock Exchange: Equity futures and options trading Increased fears on interest rates following the release of US employment data brought a retreat to Footsie futures, though the contract closed off the day's lows. Trading in a 40 point range, the September contract on the FT-SE 100 hit a high of 2,976 and a low of 2,937, before recovering to close at 2,959. Volume at 12,511 showed a slight increase. There was also an improvement in turnover in the traded options where the closing total was 33,376 contracts. Tomkins, with 1,028 lots dealt, was the busiest stock option.
