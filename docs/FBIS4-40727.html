BFN [Text] The information department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic told a KHOVAR correspondent that armed groups of the Islamic Rebirth Party of Tajikistan have assembled in the Badakhshan Province of Afghanistan close to the residential areas of Darvoz and Shugnan rayons and the Khorog city of the Gorno-Badakhshan Autonomous Oblast of Tajikistan. Their aim is to cross over to the territory of Tajikistan and wage armed struggles in the future against the government forces and to reach the Khoburabot Pass. Mercenaries from other countries are also included in the armed groups. Leadership of the Afghan province of Badakhshan, including its military commander Abdol Khalid (Basir), are not doing anything to stop the movement and operation of the armed groups on the Tajikistan-Afghanistan border. Explaining the situation, the headquarters of the group of Russian border guards in Tajikistan said that decisive measures will be taken against the armed groups attempting to violate and cross the border.
