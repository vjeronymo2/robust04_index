920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / UK Company News: GT Japan net asset value down 37% GT Japan Investment Trust saw its net asset value per share decline almost 37 per cent - from 235.4p to 149p - over the 12 months to June 30. The figure was estimated to have recovered to 165.4p by September 24. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 851,000 (Pounds 820,000) for earnings of 1.36p (1.31p) per share. The proposed final dividend is again 0.75p for a total of 1.4p (1.15p) including a special distribution of 0.25p.
