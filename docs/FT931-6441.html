930302 FT 02 MAR 93 / New York Police Commissioner Kelly outside the World Trade Centre towers New York Police Commissioner Raymond Kelly in front of the World Trade Centre towers, hit by a bomb last week that left five dead. Police officers have found traces of nitrate, an ingredient in dynamite, at the blast site but investigation is being hampered by the instability of the twisted rubble beneath the towers. However, authorities say they have a number of leads.
