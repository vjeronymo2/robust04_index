930713 FT 13 JUL 93 / Liberians seek ceasefire pact Liberia's three warring factions yesterday began drafting a new ceasefire accord to try to end the country's civil war, Reuter reports from Geneva. United Nations and African officials said the factions and the interim government of national unity were reworking a failed 1991 pact. The text was being finalised before going to a full session of the week-long negotiations, which began on Saturday at the UN's European headquarters.
