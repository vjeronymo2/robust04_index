BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- World public is strongly denouncing the U.S. belligerent moves on the Korean peninsula. The chairman of the Denmark-the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Friendship Association in his report at a meeting of the executive committee of the association held in Copenhagen on April 7 said that the United States in the past deployed nuclear and other weapons of mass destruction in South Korea on the plea of thwarting "threat from the North" and today it is bringing forward the "nuclear issue" of Korea as a pretext to keep hold on South Korea. If the United Nations wanted to call something in question, it should have accused the United States of shipping nuclear and other mass destruction weapons into South Korea in violation of the Korean Armistice Agreement and called attention to the non-compliance with the UN resolution on the withdrawal of foreign troops. The African regional committee for the study of the chuche idea in a statement April 13 said that the nuclear issue of the Korean peninsula can be resolved only through talks between the DPRK and the U.S. as it originated from the U.S. deployment of nuclear weapons in South Korea and its nuclear threat to the DPRK, and the [word indistinct] in an appeal on April 14 urged the U.S. administration to come out to a direct dialogue with the DPRK before it is too late.
