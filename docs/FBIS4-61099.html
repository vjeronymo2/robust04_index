BFN [Text] Leonid Kravchuk has issued an instruction introducing changes and amendments to the directive on measures to reduce cash circulation. Under the directive, with the aim of normalizing monetary circulation and reducing the rate of monetary emission growth on the territory of Ukraine, all mutual one-off payments for goods, work, services and securities to the value of more than six minimum wages that are effected by corporate bodies, regardless of their forms of ownership and kinds of activity, and by Ukrainian citizens, foreign citizens, and those without citizenship who carry out entrepreneurial activity, are to be solely non-cash. Commercial banks are to provide cash only on condition that there are appropriate grounds, and are to control its use in order to ensure that it is used for the declared purpose.
