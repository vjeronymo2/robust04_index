910418 FT 18 APR 91 / UK Company News: Densitron Intl halved to Pounds 0.59m Pre-tax profits of Densitron International, the electronics group, halved from Pounds 1.11m to Pounds 587,000 in 1990. The company said that profits had been adversely affected by losses, now checked, in the microwave operation and by a fall-off in business with east European countries. Earnings per share fell from 3.6p to 1.83p and the recommended final dividend is cut to 1p (1.65p) making 1.7p (2.35p) for the year. Turnover was slightly lower at Pounds 38.96m (Pounds 39.22m) and the taxable result came after an exceptional Pounds 114,000 credit, being the gain realised on repayment of an overseas loan.
