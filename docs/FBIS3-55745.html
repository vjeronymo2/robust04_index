Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Imamali Rakhmanov, the head of state and chairman of the Supreme Council of Tajikistan who has arrived in Nukus, the Uzbek town, to take part in the work of the conference of the heads of Central Asian states and Russia on the problems of the Aral Sea, met with President Nursultan Nazarbayev of Kazakhstan. Issues related to bilateral relations between Tajikistan and Kazakhstan have been discussed during the course of the meeting, which was held in an atmosphere of friendship. A meeting between Tajik head of state Rakhmanov and President Islam Karimov of Uzbekistan took place in Nukus the same day [11 January]. Bilateral relations between Tajikistan and Uzbekistan were discussed during the course of the meeting.
