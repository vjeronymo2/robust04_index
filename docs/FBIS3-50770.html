Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] One hundred forty children from Chernobyl arrived at Jose Marti International Airport today as part of the program of integrated care offered by our country. Over 13,000 Ukrainians, Russians, and Belarusians have been cared for over the past three years by the medical-recreational services in Tarara, east of the capital, where there is a joint facility, including a 200-student primary school, for diabetic and asthmatic children.
