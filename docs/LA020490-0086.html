February 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

Thanks a lot, NBC, for moving "Dear John" from Thursday to Wednesday nights opposite "Anything But Love."

It was perfectly placed on Thursday nights just before "L.A. Law," and now you've put it on at a time where it will get lost. I just don't understand the networks or why they do what they do. Can't they just leave things alone and let us enjoy what we have come home to watch at the end of a busy day?

Toni Jacobson, Los Angeles
