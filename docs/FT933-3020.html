930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / World Trade News: UK shipbuilder's victory in Israel Ferguson Shipbuilders, the Scottish shipyard, has won a court battle in Israel which should mean that it gains a Pounds 9m order to build two tugs for Israel Electric for use in the Israeli port of Hadera, writes James Buxton. Ferguson had submitted a tender 23 per cent lower than Israel Shipyards, but the state yard was awarded the deal.
