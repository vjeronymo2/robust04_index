Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by L. Elias] [Text] Mmabatho March 10 SAPA -- About 200 Bophuthatswana policemen on Thursday [10 March] afternoon assembled outside the South African Embassy in Mmabatho and handed over a memorandum demanding the homeland's re-incorporation into South Africa, full pay-out of their pension fund contributions and the opportunity to participate in free and fair elections. "We no longer want to kill our brothers," they said while waiting to hand over the memorandum to Ambassador Dr Tjaart van der Walt.
