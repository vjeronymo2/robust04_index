930203 FT 03 FEB 93 / World News in Brief: Queen threatens to sue Sun over leak of speech Lawyers acting for the Queen have threatened legal action against the Sun newspaper for alleged breach of copyright after it printed the text of her 1992 Christmas Day speech two days early. A letter from solicitors Farrer & Co to editor Kelvin MacKenzie said court proceedings would be started unless cash damages and costs were offered. The Sun said it would vigorously defend the action.
