Commission Session SK1404065094 Seoul YONHAP in English 0449 GMT 14 Apr 94 SK1404065094 Seoul YONHAP English BFN [Text] Berlin, April 14 (YONHAP) -- South Korean Ambassador to Austria Yi Si-yong was elected chairman of the 37th session of the Commission on Narcotic Drugs that opened Wednesday in Vienna. Ambassador Yi will preside over the regular session dealing with prevention of drug addiction and control of illicit traffic in narcotic drugs. The session will run until April 23. The commisson, consisting of 53 members, is a policy-making and coordinating body under the United Nations Economic and Social Council.
