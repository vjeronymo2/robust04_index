920111 FT 11 JAN 92 / IRA bombs in London Jan 10 1992: briefcase bomb explodes in Whitehall. Dec 23 1991: Underground shut after incendiary devices found. Dec 16 1991: small explosion on track near Clapham Junction closes mainline stations in London. Dec 14,15 1991: four firebombs discovered at Brent Cross and firebomb explodes in Sainsbury wing of National Gallery. Dec 1 1991: firebombs damage three shops in Tottenham Court Rd. and warehouse in Islington. Aug 8 1991: firebomb discovered in bookshop in Charing Cross Rd. Aug 5 1991: firebomb destroys lounge of pub in Charing Cross Rd. Feb 18 1991: two bombs explode at Paddington and Victoria stations, killing one and injuring 38. Feb 7 1991: mortar bombs fired at Downing St. July 20 1990: bomb explodes at Stock Exchange. June 25 1991: bomb at Carlton Club, St James's, injures seven. June 9 1990: bomb at Honourable Artillery Company Barracks, Islington, injures 17. May 14,16 1990: car bomb at Eltham injures five; car bomb at Wembley Army recruiting centre kills one and injures two. Aug 1 1988: bomb at Inglis Barracks, Mill Hill, kills one and injures eight. Dec 17 1983: car bomb at Harrods kills six and injures 91. Dec 12 1983: bomb at Army barracks, Woolwich, injures three. July 20 1982: car bomb in Hyde Park during Blues and Royals parade kills four and injures 20. July 20 1982: bomb in Regents Park bandstand kills seven and injures 30. Nov 23 1981: bomb at Government House, Woolwich, injures two. Oct 10 1981: nail bomb near Chelsea Barracks kills two and injures 40.
