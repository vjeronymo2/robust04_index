ACTION: Final rule; technical correction.

SUMMARY: On September 22,1 994 (59 FR 48702), the Department published a rule that made final amendments to regulations in 24 CFR parts 203 and 291. The regulations govern the disposition of HUD-acquired single family properties (part 291) and the circumstances under which HUD will accept such properties when they are occupied (part 203). The purpose of this document is to correct an omission in the Department's amendment to §291.110. EFFECTIVE DATE: The final rule, including this correction, is effective October 24, 1994.
