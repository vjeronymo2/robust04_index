920423 FT 23 APR 92 / UK Company News: Inishtech acquires florists supplier Inishtech, which has built up a portfolio of six companies in the paper, print and packaging sectors, is diversifying with the acquisition of Douthwaite Florists Sundries of Leeds. The initial consideration is Pounds 7.13m. There are two tranches of up to Pounds 2.36m and Pounds 6.4m in deferred consideration should profits reach certain targets. If the entire deferred consideration was to become payable then Douthwaite's pre-tax profits in 1996 would be some Pounds 6m. In 1991 Douthwaite made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 1.61m from sales of Pounds 14.4m.
