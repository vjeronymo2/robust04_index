Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Macao, February 19 (XINHUA) -- Li Shuilin, newly-appointed deputy director of the XINHUA NEWS AGENCY Macao branch, said that he was determined to do a good job at his new post through hard work. While meeting with reporters here today, Li said, friendly cooperation between the two Governments of China and Portugal and the rapid economic development on China's Mainland will create sound conditions for the economic development in Macao. Li, who arrived here on February 16, was formerly director of the Asia and Africa Affairs Department of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations and Trade.
