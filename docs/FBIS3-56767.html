Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Text] As part of his tour through the Central Asian states, Foreign Minister Lojze Peterle is visiting the Azerbaijani capital city of Baku. The hosts told him that the Azerbaijani parliament will discuss the recognition of Slovenia that very day and the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two states is to follow soon thereafter. Azerbaijani leaders expressed interest in economic cooperation with our country, particularly in the sphere of industry. They spoke in favor of reaching some basic interstate agreements.
