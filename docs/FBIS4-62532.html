BFN [Text] Today, the parliamentary assembly for economic cooperation among Black Sea countries held its third session devoted to legal and political issues. The session, which took place at the Metechi Hotel in Tbilisi, was attended by all the countries of the Black Sea basin. The speaker of the Georgian parliament, Vakhtang Goguadze, opened the session. Head of State Eduard Shevardnadze made a speech. The session will continue tomorrow.
