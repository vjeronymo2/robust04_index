BFN [Text] Cairo, 2 Jun (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak received a telephone call this evening from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and another call from 'Ali Salim al-Bid. During the calls, President Mubarak called for a cease-fire by the two parties of the Yemeni crisis and urged the two sides to keep their troops away from the combat areas, thus giving understanding a chance so that a solution to the crisis serving the interests of the Yemeni people can be found.
