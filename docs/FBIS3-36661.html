Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vilor Neyzmatov and Georgiy Shmelev] [Text] Tashkent February 8 TASS -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, currently on a visit to Uzbekistan, visited the Embassy of the Russian Federation in Tashkent on Tuesday [8 February]. Kozyrev arrived in Tashkent on Monday night. After Kozyrev's visit to the Russian Embassy Russo-Uzbek talks began. In an interview with ITAR-TASS Kozyrev said that the main aim of his visit is the preparation for the Russo-Uzbek summit scheduled in Moscow on February 25. During the visit Kozyrev intends to discuss with the leadership of the republic a wide range of problems pertaining to cooperation in economic, military-technical, and humanitarian spheres. Later in the day Kozyrev will meet with Uzbek President Islam Karimov and visit the University of World Economy and Diplomacy. On Tuesday evening Kozyrev will leave Tashkent for Almaty.
