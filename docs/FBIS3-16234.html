Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Dmitriy Chubarov] [Text] Moscow, 15 Mar -- Tsutomu Hata, the Japanese foreign minister, is to pay an official visit to Russia in the next few days, Grigoriy Karasin, the head of the department of press and information of the Russian Foreign Ministry, told Itar-Tass today. In the words of the Russian diplomat, "the holiday of Russian President Boris Yeltsin, who is in Sochi, will in no way affect the political content and the level of the official visit by the Japanese foreign minister." The program of the visit, Grigoriy Karasin added, shows that Russia attaches great significance to its relations with Japan. During his stay in Russia, the Japanese minister is expected to meet Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev.
