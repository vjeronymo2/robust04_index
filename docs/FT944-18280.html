941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets (South Africa): Golds face testing week Selected industrials attracted interest in otherwise lacklustre Johannesburg trading, with gold shares expected to face a testing week if bullion fails to rally. The overall index was 4 softer at 5,672, industrials added 11 at 6,290 and golds receded 17 to 2,425. De Beers moved ahead 75 cents to R102.50 as its climb continued, but Anglos finished unchanged at R240 and Gencor dipped 10 cents to R14.65. Barlows was R1.60 better at R30.35 after announcing plans for a Dollars 75m bond issue to international investors and saying that it expected strong full-year earnings growth. Group Five and Group Five Holdings reversed Friday's steady gains, relinquishing 20 cents and 15 cents at R4.80 and R4.60 respectively.
