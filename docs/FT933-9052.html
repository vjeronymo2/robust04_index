930812 FT 12 AUG 93 / Record month at BAA airports BAA, the airports operator, had its busiest month ever last month, handling 8.3m passengers. The biggest growth was in long-haul traffic, up 7.6 per cent on July last year. Stansted saw the biggest growth at BAA's three London airports, handling 302,400 passengers, a rise of 12 per cent. Heathrow's 4.7m passengers was an increase of 5.4 per cent, while the volume of cargo grew 10.2 per cent to more than 98,000 tonnes.
