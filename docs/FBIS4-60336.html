BFN [Text] The president, Mr. Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari, has expressed the hope that relations between the United States and Pakistan will further strengthen in the coming days. He was addressing an investiture ceremony in Rochester where an honorary degree of Doctor of Law was conferred on him by the University of Rochester. The president said shared ideals and values and unshakable commitment to democracy will bind the people of Pakistan and the United States into a special union. The president said we represent a Muslim democracy and we are the world's first product of cultural and intellectual pluralism. We are proud and respectful of all the elements that shaped our being. Mr. Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari said nothing is more important than Islam which has sustained and nourished us. He said Pakistan and the United States have many commonalities and are pluralistic societies. He said that the degree awarded to him will be a symbol of friendship and democratic brotherhood between the two countries.
