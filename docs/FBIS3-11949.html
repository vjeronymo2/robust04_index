Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] More than 509 km of telephone lines have already been installed in the mountainous areas of Granma Province, where 17 percent of Granma's population is congregated. An important element of the province's mountainous areas is that in 1993, approximately 1,700 people moved to areas of the Sierra Maestra; so far this year, 300 more people have moved into the area. As part of the development of the Turquino plan, the whole province is covered by the family doctor plan and education and health centers for the population have already been guaranteed. The mountainous areas make up 25 percent of Granma's provincial territory, of which an area of approximately 900 km has been electrified.
