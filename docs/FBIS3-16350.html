Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report from roundup of ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA and ITAR-TASS reports: "He Will Not Run for President"] [Text] "I do not intend to run for the post of the country's president even if I am asked." That was the position voiced by Moscow Mayor Yuriy Luzhkov. Yuriy Luzhkov denied the press' numerous claims that he created the Party of the Majority for his future election campaign. "I have nothing to do with it except for the fact that at the request of one of my friends I signed greetings to the constitutent congress of the Party of the Majority sinces its aim -- supporting the establishment of a class of entrepreneurs -- does not contradict my views," our interlocutor explained.
