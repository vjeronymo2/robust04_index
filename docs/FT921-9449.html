920212 FT 12 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Pounds 57,000 first half deficit at Elbief Despite an upturn in the second quarter Elbief ran into a loss of Pounds 57,000 for the half year ended October 31 1991, and is passing the interim dividend. Sales of the Elite range of clocks, mirrors and photoframes had varying fortunes; the US was badly hit by recession but Europe and other export markets increased. Sales of handbag frames continued to decrease. Overall, turnover dropped 8 per cent to Pounds 2.27m after being 27 per cent down in the opening three months. Despite cutting overheads and other cost savings, the group was unable to avoid the loss, which compared with a profit of Pounds 55,000 previously. Losses per share worked through at 0.04p. Last year there were earnings of 0.043p from which an interim dividend of 0.181p was paid.
