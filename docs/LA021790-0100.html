February 17, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

HIGH SCHOOL BASKETBALL ROUNDUP

Avocado League

El Camino 91, Carlsbad 61 -- Travis Gilley scored 18 points and Dee Boyer added 16 for El Camino (18-6, 11-1). El Camino tied with San Pasqual for the league title. Mark Tague scored 17 points for Carlsbad (8-14, 5-7).

San Pasqual 80, Ramona 62 -- Erik Meek led San Pasqual (17-5, 11-1) with 26 points and 15 rebounds. Bryan Benner added 18 points and eight rebounds. San Pasqual led 42-23 at halftime. Alfonso De La Nuez scored 16 points for Ramona (15-7, 6-6).

Oceanside 64, San Marcos 47 -- Owen Dennis scored 14 points and had five rebounds for Oceanside (13-12, 6-6). D.J. Lewis added 12 points and 10 rebounds. Oceanside led in every quarter. San Marcos finished 1-21, 0-12.

Palomar League

Torrey Pines 69, Fallbrook 60 -- The victory gave No. 4 Torrey Pines a share of the league title with Mt. Carmel. The Falcons have now won or shared three consecutive league titles. Bill Scheibe had 18 points and Chris Stevens added 16 for Torrey Pines (24-3, 12-2). Chris Miller had 32 for Fallbrook (16-10, 7-7).

Mt. Carmel 124, Vista 65 -- Mt. Carmel rebounded from its loss to Rancho Buena Vista Tuesday with the rout. The victory gave Mt. Carmel a share of the league title with Torrey Pines. Mt. Carmel (20-4, 12-2) out-rebounded Vista, 66-28. Vista was just 2 of 32 from three-point range. Shane Knight had 29 points and 16 rebounds for Mt. Carmel.

Rancho Buena Vista 76, Poway 65 -- The teams were tied after the third quarter but RBV outscored Poway, 22-11, in the fourth. Darryl Parker scored 36 points and had 19 rebounds for RBV (15-9, 10-4). Poway is 17-6, 8-6.

City Eastern League

Serra 66, Morse 48 -- Roderick Alexander scored 19 points and Desi Dixon added 17 as Serra (7-13, 1-7) got its first league victory.

City Harbor League

Coronado 59, Marian 40 -- James Stark scored 15 and Derek Wastila and Adam Smith added 13 apiece to give Coronado the league title. Coronado (16-9, 6-2) outscored Marian (8-14, 1-7), 18-8, in the third quarter.

Clairemont 80, Christian 67 -- Clairemont trailed by three at the end of the third quarter but outscored Christian 30-12 in the fourth quarter. Alex Love scored 34 points and had 13 rebounds for Clairemont (7-14, 3-5). Tony Clark scored 46 points for Christian (12-11, 5-3).

City Western League

Kearny 82, La Jolla 61 -- Darnay Scott scored 28 points and Mike Oviedo added 17 for Kearny (16-10, 4-4). Lupe Estrada scored 12 for La Jolla (4-18, 1-7).

USDHS 55, Mission Bay 41 -- USDHS outscored Mission Bay, 16-5, in the third quarter on its way to the victory at Mission Bay (4-16, 2-6). Peter Ellis scored 12 to lead USDHS (20-3, 7-1).

City Central League

Madison 76, Hoover 64 -- John McKenna scored 21 points and Kirk Cumming added 16 for Madison (3-22, 1-8). John Akridge led Hoover (5-18, 1-7) with 18 points.

Coastal League

Bishop's 58, Parker 54 -- Brian Allbers scored 28 points and had 11 rebounds to lead Bishop's (21-4, 12-0) to a league title.
