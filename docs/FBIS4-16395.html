BFN [Text] Moscow April 14 TASS -- The press service of Russian President Boris Yeltsin released on Thursday the following text of his statement: "1. With a view to preparing an agreement on reaching civic accord in Russia and taking into account the public opinion, the working group preparing the agreement on reaching civic accord in Russia shall consider the proposals made by the Federation Council and the State Duma of the Federal Assembly and the government of the Russian Federation and submit by April 20, 1994 proposals on its finalisation and the procedure for signing and implementing it. "2. Head of the Administration of the President of the Russian Federation Sergey A. Filatov shall organise a working meeting of the participants in the agreement on reaching civic accord in Russia to initial the text of the agreement and consider the procedure for its signing and implementation. "3. To convene an assembly of civic accord to sign the agreement on reaching civic accord in Russia an assembly in the Kremlin on April 28, 1994."
