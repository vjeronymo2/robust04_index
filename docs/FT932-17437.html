930401 FT 01 APR 93 / World Trade News: US fury over piracy by Taiwan companies A COALITION of leading trade associations in Washington is demanding sanctions against Taiwan unless it shows 'with specific actions' its intention to control 'staggering levels of piracy' of copyrighted works, videos, compact discs and computer software. The International Intellectual Property Alliance, which represents more than 1,500 companies, claims US copyright industries lost a total of Dollars 669m in Taiwan last year through piracy. Taiwan was accused of lacking 'the political will' to enforce an anti-piracy pact, signed with the US last June.
