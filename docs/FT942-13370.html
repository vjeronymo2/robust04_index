940426 FT 26 APR 94 / World News in Brief: Pounds 1.4bn Santander bid wins takeover battle for Banesto Banco Santander last night outbid its rivals to win control of Banco Espanol de Credito and create Spain's largest banking group. It will pay Pta281bn (Pounds 1.38bn) for a 60 per cent stake in the bank following a rescue operation conducted under the auspices of the Bank of Spain. Page 23; Editorial Comment, Page 21
