BFN [Text] Bitlis/Mutki, May 21 (A.A.) -- Terrorists from the separatist terror organization killed three village guards in Mutki township in eastern Bitlis Province, it was reported on Saturday. AA was told that the incident happened when separatist terrorists stopped a minibus going from Mutki to Meydan township. The terrorists forced the village guards to get off the minibus and shot them to death. An operation has been launched into the incident to capture the terrorists who fled after the incident.
