BFN [RIA report: "Russians Love To Buy Hard Currency"] [Text] The Russian population is increasing its spending on hard currency: 1.8253 trillion rubles [R] were spent last December, R1.8572 trillion in January, and... R1.8803 trillion in February. Compared with January 1993, when Russians spent R17 billion on hard currency, spending has increased virtually 111-fold. This is indicated in an analytical article prepared by the Russian Ministry of the Economy.
