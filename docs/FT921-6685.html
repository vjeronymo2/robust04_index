920226 FT 26 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Cookson sells two offshoots for Pounds 7.5m Cookson Group, the industrial materials company, is selling two wholly-owned subsidiaries to the US-based Titanium Industries for about Pounds 7.5m. Combined net assets of the two offshoots - Titanium International and Reactive Metal Fabricators (Anston) - at December 31 amounted to approximately Pounds 2.6m. They incurred pre-tax losses of Pounds 164,000 on sales of Pounds 10.8m over the year to that date.
