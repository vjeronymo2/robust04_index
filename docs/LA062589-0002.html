June 25, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

DEEP SPRINGS COLLEGE

As an alumnus of Deep Springs College, I want to commend Thomas J. Meyer and Max Aguilera-Hellweg for "School on the Range" (May 14). In both words and photographs, they allowed the place and people to speak for themselves, and kept to a minimum the stereotypic interpretations inevitable of "outsiders."

JAMES D. MOREFIELD

Claremont
