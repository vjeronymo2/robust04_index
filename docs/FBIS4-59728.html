BFN [Text] Mecca, May 14, IRNA -- Hojjat ol-Eslam Mohammad Mohammadi Reyshahri, the representative of the leader of the Islamic revolution for the annual hajj ceremony said here today that the rally for deliverance from disbelievers, will be held on Tuesday [17 May] in front of the leader's bureau in holy Mecca. The hojjat ol-eslam who was talking to the leaders of caravans and Iranian pilgrims in Mecca asked them to participate in the rally like previous years. He quoted the father of the Islamic revolution, the imam Khomeyni, as having said that participating in this rally was necessary. Reyshahri added that participants must observe peace and discipline in this rally, which is aimed at fostering Islamic unity and enlightening the ummah on the dangers besetting them from the disbelievers. He observed that God willing the rally will go ahead as planned without any untoward incident.
