BFN [Text] Brother Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, leader of the 1 September Libyan Revolution, has received a message from brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, on the latest developments in Yemen, fraternal bilateral ties, and the efforts currently under way to contain Yemeni developments in a manner that safeguards unity, as well as the national gains achieved thus far; namely, democracy and constitutional institutions. Brother Yahya Husayn al-'Arashi, minister of civil service and administrative reform and envoy of the Presidential Council chairman, relayed the message when Col. al-Qadhdhafi received him today. During the meeting, the brother minister expressed the appreciation of both the brother president and our Yemeni people for the support of the Libyan Jamahiriyah--leadership and people--for Yemeni unity.
