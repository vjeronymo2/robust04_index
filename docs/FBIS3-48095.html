Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 19 (KCNA) -- A deputies' group for bilateral cooperation between the House of Representatives of Indonesia and the Supreme People's Assembly of the DPRK was formed with a due ceremony in Jakarta on Jan. 12. The Indonesian House Speaker made a congratulatory speech at the ceremony before the formation of the group was declared. Next day a friendship gathering with the group members was arranged at the DPRK Embassy in Jakarta.
