Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 9 (XINHUA) -- China will reduce the import duties on three more products in a bid to further ease the tax burdens of enterprises and promote production, according to the State Council Tax Rules Committee. The three products are compressors for air conditioners, photocopying machine parts, and internal threaded copper pipes for air conditioner condensers. The range of import duty reduction is between 10 percent and 33 percent. The new interim tariff rates will be implemented soon by China's customs.
