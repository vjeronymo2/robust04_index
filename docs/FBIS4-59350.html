BFN [Text] The prime minister, Ms. Benazir Bhutto, has said that Pakistan would continue to extend its full support to the Kashmiri people in their just struggle against the Indian occupation forces so that they can achieve their right of self-determination according to the UN resolution. Speaking at the installation ceremony of the new colonel commandant of Baloch Regiment, she reiterated that Pakistan will not budge an inch from its principled stand on Kashmir. The prime minister said no nation can be subjugated for long with bullets and swords. The day will come when the Kashmiris will be able to achieve their rights, she said. The prime minister said Pakistan has no aggressive designs against any country. It believes in peaceful coexistence. It desires to have good neighborly relations with all on the basis of equality, but it cannot compromise on its principle just for the sake of friendship. Pakistan cannot allow anyone to cast an evil eye on its independence and solidarity. The prime minister said that the government is fully conscious of the needs of its armed forces and is taking all steps to meet their requirements. It is doing its best to keep the armed forces fully equipped to effectively defend the country to frustrate any aggression.
