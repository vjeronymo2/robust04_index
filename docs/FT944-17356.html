941008 FT 08 OCT 94 / London Stock Exchange: Equity futures and options trading Stock index futures moved ahead strongly for the second day running with turnover once again at a good level. The FT-SE 100 December contract closed 21 points higher at 3,017.0 for a two-day advance of 43 points. Trading volume was 13,892, against 13,092 on Thursday. At the official close the premium to the cash market was 18.3 points and the fair value premium around 15.5 points. Traded option volume was 27,669 lots, down from 39,117 Buyers moved in quickly once Wall Street came into the reckoning.
