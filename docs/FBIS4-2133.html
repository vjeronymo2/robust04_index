BFN [Text] Xian, April 5 (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, attended a ceremony in honor of Huangdi (Yellow Emperor) here today. Huangdi is a legendary ancestor of the Chinese nation. Chinese officials, topped by Li Ruihuan, who is on an inspection tour in Shaanxi, and representatives of Taiwan, Hong Kong and Macao compatriots and overseas Chinese presented flower baskets to and saluted the Huangdi mausoleum. After the rites concluded, Li Ruihuan planted a commemorative pine beside the mausoleum. Yesterday, a donation ceremony was also held here for the repair and renovation of the mausoleum. Li Ruihuan, honorary chairman of the China Huangdi Mausoleum Foundation, made a donation of 2,000 yuan. The fund donation started in 1992. The foundation had received 6.34 million yuan by the end of last year. More than 13 million yuan were donated to the foundation after the donation ceremony ended yesterday.
