Language: Afrikaans Article Type:BFN [Text] A spokesman for the Inkatha Freedom Party [IFP] in the Transvaal, Mr. Hennie Bekker, says the timing of the publication of the Goldstone Commission's report has to be seriously questioned. Mr. Bekker said in a radio program this morning that one could also ask whether the report would have seen the light if the IFP was going to participate in the election. On the alleged involvement of a prominent Transvaal IFP leader, Mr. Thembo Khoza, Mr. Bekker said the IFP believes in the legal principle that one is innocent until proven otherwise.
