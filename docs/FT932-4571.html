930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / Pickets gather at Times factory to vent their fury Pickets gathered outside the Timex factory in Dundee yesterday to vent their fury at workers entering the plant to do the work of those dismissed earlier in the year. The company 'clarified' the terms on which it was willing to re-hire sacked workers. It said they would face an average cut of less than 10 per cent, not 27 per cent as the pickets have claimed. The Scottish TUC called for a boycott of the company's products
