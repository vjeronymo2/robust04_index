Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday evening President Yasir 'Arafat received an urgent message from the Russian administration conveyed to him by an envoy of Russian President Yeltsin. The Russian leadership's message to 'Arafat came a few hours after the issuing of the Russian proposal in Moscow calling for the convening of a second international conference, similar to the Madrid conference, to activate the peace process after the Hebron massacre. But Israel was swift to express its opposition to this proposal and its preference for continuing the bilateral talks. The United States affirmed the same stance.
