BFN [Text] Tallinn, Apr 12, BNS -- During spring conscription from March 29 till April 10, only 780 men out of a planned 1040 turned up for service in the Estonian defense forces, the Defense Ministry told BNS. The majority of the conscripts, 561 men, are going to serve in the army; 165 were sent to the border guard forces, 36 to the internal defense regiment, and 18 to the rescue regiment. Conscription proceeded successfully in East Virumaa, Jarvamaa and the town of Valga, while Tallinn, Tartu and the Laane region had problems with meeting their quotas of conscripts. The next round of conscription is scheduled for June 25 through July 5, the Defense Ministry press service informs.
