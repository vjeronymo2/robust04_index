920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Spurs confirms sale of Paul Gascoigne The Pounds 5.5m sale of Paul Gascoigne, the England international footballer, to the Italian club Lazio was confirmed yesterday by Tottenham Hotspur, the quoted football club. Tottenham expects to get about Pounds 5m of the proceeds, which would wipe out its debt. Before Mr Alan Sugar, who also chairs Amstrad, and Mr Terry Venables, former team manager, bought the company nearly a year ago, borrowings peaked at about Pounds 15m.
