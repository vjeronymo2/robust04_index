Approximately $168 million will be awarded nationwide during FY 1994 for Title X services. The entire $168 million will be allocated among the 10 DHHS regions, and will be in turn awarded to public and private non-profit agencies located within the regions. Each regional office is responsible for evaluating applications, establishing priorities, and setting funding levels according to criteria in 42 CFR 59.11. This notice announces the availability of funds to provide family planning services in 11 States, Washington, DC and Guam. Competing grant applications are invited for the following areas:
