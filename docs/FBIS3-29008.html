Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Cuban Vice President Carlos Lage Davila described the conversations held with Brazilian President Itamar Franco and three ministers of his cabinet as very positive. On the third day of his official visit to Brazil, Lage held a press conference with more than 20 local and foreign reporters at the residence of the Cuban ambassador to Brazil. [passage omitted] When questioned on the lifting of the U.S. embargo on Vietnam, the Cuban leader answered that it is the result of the Asian country's perseverance in keeping alive their ideals and social system.
