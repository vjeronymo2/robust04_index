July 27, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

ARK., FLA. STATE JOINING SEC?

An Alabama newspaper reported today that Arkansas and Florida State have accepted invitations to join the Southeastern Conference.

The Alabama Journal quoted an unnamed source, identified as a "highly ranked SEC official," who confirmed that the two universities would join the SEC, bringing the number of SEC teams to 12.

Arkansas Athletic Director Frank Broyles said today a decision for his school would be announced by Aug. 15.
