920305 FT 05 MAR 92 / Call to improve careers guidance SMALL improvements in careers guidance could yield significant economic benefits, according to a report published by the Policy Studies Institute today. Large savings in unemployment and social security benefits could be gained from better guidance to avoid occupational mismatch and to raise academic attainment at schools. Both together could yield savings of more than Pounds 1.3bn a year, says the report. The Economic Value of Careers Guidance, PSI, 100 Park Village East, London NW1 3SR, Pounds 7.95.
