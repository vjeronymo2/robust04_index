910426 FT 26 APR 91 / UK Company News: Net asset values fall at Exmoor Dual IT Exmoor Dual Investment Trust reported net asset value per income share of 60.7p at February 28, against 61.1p a year earlier. Nav of the ordinary shares was 74.2p, compared with 78.4p. Net profits rose to Pounds 500,000 (Pounds 435,000) in the half-year to February 28, leaving earnings per income share at 5.67p (4.93p) and per ordinary share at 1p (0.87p). The second interim dividend per income share is 2.6p (2.35p).
