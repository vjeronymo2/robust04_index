921012 FT 12 OCT 92 / Construction Contracts: London projects ROOFF has secured a further five contracts, ranging from six to 12 months, with a combined value of over Pounds 6.5m. The company is constructing nine flats at Pellatt Grove, N22, and a day centre at Stanmore for Metropolitan Housing Trust; converting Furnival House, a hospital building at Colmeley Park, N6, into 120 student flats for Duoview Properties; constructing 27 housing units for Community Housing Association at Camden Gardens, NW1; and undertaking external refurbishment work for the Peabody Trust.
