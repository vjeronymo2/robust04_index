BFN [Text] The Black Sea Fleet ships stationed in Sevastopol, for instance the Admiral Golovko rocket cruiser, are being urgently supplied with armaments. This was announced to INTERFAX-Ukraine by Chief of the Ukrainian Navy press center, Nikolay Savchenko. He said the ships were prepared for military action. Savchenko also announced that several Ukrainian fleet ships, for instance the Getman Sagaidachny patrol ship and the Lutsk antisubmarine cruiser had not been supplied with electric power from the land. The Black See Fleet's command explained this by nonpayment for electric power already delivered. However, the Ukrainian Navy financial service said Kiev had no debts.
