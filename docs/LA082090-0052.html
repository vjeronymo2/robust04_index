August 20, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Trivial Pursuit: Eddie James of Nashville won Saturday's Elvis Trivia Contest as Memphis ended a week's commemoration of the anniversary of the death of Elvis Presley. James correctly recalled the title Presley said he would have used for his autobiography, "Through My Eyes." Presley died 13 years ago Thursday. BETH KLEID
