940422 FT 22 APR 94 / International Company News: Cummins Engine ahead at Dollars 54.6m Cummins Engine Company, the US manufacturer of heavy-duty diesel engines for trucks, school buses and power generation plants, recorded a strong improvement in its first quarter, writes Laurie Morse. Net income rose to Dollars 54.6m, or Dollars 1.35 a share, on sales of Dollars 1.1bn. This compares with Dollars 41.1m, or Dollars 1.12 a share, on sales of Dollars 1bn in last year's first quarter.
