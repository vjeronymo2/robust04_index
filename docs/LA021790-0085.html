February 17, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

FAN PUT TYSON, KING DOWN FOR THE COUNT

Once again the big-money people of boxing, the rich, the selfish money-hungry crooks are going into the gutter for the almighty dollar. They don't care about right or wrong or about Mike Tyson or Buster Douglas.

All they care about is the money they are not going to make because of the outcome of this event.

I love Mike Tyson. He is a great boxer and a fine man and he will probably win a rematch, but right is right and nobody has the right to take away what Douglas won. He beat Tyson badly and the people who control boxing don't like it because it costs them loads of money.

The credibility of boxing is on the line once again because big money has been hit in the pocket book.

Tony Altieri

Vista
