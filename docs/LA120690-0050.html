December 6, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

More Maxi-Musical Casting: Gary Morris, a sometime country singer who played Jean Valjean in "Les Miserables" on Broadway in 1987-88 and sang the role on "The Complete Symphonic Recording" of the show, will play the famous fugitive at the Pantages Theatre in Hollywood, opening Jan. 13. CLAUDIA PUIG
