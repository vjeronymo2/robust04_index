BFN [Text] Tunis, al-Tayr [April] 2, JAMAHIRYAH NEWS AGENCY -- Major al-Khuwaylidi al-Humaydi last night arrived by land [words indistinct] to take part in things of the Tunisian [words indistinct] 6th session of the Arab Maghreb Union's Presidential Council due to start its proceedings today in Tunis. A welcoming ceremony was held for Major al-Khuwaylidi al-Humaydi on his arrival to his residence where he was welcomed by the Tunisian Prime Minister Hamed Karoui, the Minister of the Interior Abdallah Kallel and the general delegate of the Great Jamahiryah to Tunisia. Major al-Khuwaylidi al-Humaydi is accompanied by delegation including the secretary of the General People's Committee for Unity and a number of experts.
