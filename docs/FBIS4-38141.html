BFN [Text] The Likud Knesset faction denounces Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin's announcement that 'Arafat has the right to pray and visit Jerusalem's holy places. Likud Chairman Binyamin Netanyahu told our correspondent that if the prime minister is serious in his intent not to permit the division of Jerusalem, then he must prevent 'Arafat from coming to the city and he must also close Orient House. Netanyahu believes that 'Arafat will not come to Jerusalem to pray but to divide the town and set up there a capital for the Palestinian state he plans and that Yitzhaq Rabin is assisting him in his endeavor.
