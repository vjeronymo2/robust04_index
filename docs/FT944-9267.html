941116 FT 16 NOV 94 / UK Company News: TT loses interest in Scantronic TT Group, the conglomerate, has ruled itself out of the bidding for Scantronic Holdings, the security components company. The group, which holds 3 per cent of Scantronic shares, said it had 'no intention in current circumstances of making an offer for Scantronic and intends, when appropriate, to dispose of its entire shareholdings'. Last month Menvier-Swain announced its interest in making a bid for Scantronic.
