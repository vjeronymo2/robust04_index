941008 FT 08 OCT 94 / Finance and the Family: Bears scared by US bogeyman - At a glance The UK stockmarket suffered another queasy week, as disappointing results showed up in its own back yard: the upset at Warburgs prompted a flurry of rumours about other securities firms' probable results. Bears then started looking across the Atlantic for new sources of worry - and US interest rates became the new bogeyman.
