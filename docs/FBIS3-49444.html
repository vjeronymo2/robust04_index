Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] After a relatively quiet night, the Muslim army began fresh artillery and infantry actions this morning. Ivica Saric reports for Croatian radio. [Saric] In addition to the shelling of Vitez, the positions of the Croat Defense Council [HVO] in the Buhine Kuce region came under fire at 0900. Heavy machine guns are being used in the main. The enemy began infantry attacks in the direction of the Vitez operational zone at 0930 with the intention of restoring control over the buildings it lost yesterday in Buhine Kuce. Fierce street fighting and a battle for every house has resumed. The defense formations are successfully repelling the attacks by the Muslim army. In the Vitez operational zone, they have evidence that in yesterday's shelling of Vitez the Muslim army used a type of cluster bomb. This refers to mortars which prior to exploding release dozens of the notorious bomblets which explode on impact. In addition to this, a number of shells which fell on Mali Mosunj in the municipality of Vitez were filled with chemicals. People felt sick and dizzy and there were visible traces of an inky color in those houses which were hit. [passage omitted]
