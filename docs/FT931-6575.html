930301 FT 01 MAR 93 / Construction Contracts: Research facility in Hampshire MATTHEW HALL has won a Pounds 12.7m building engineering services contract for the new centre for deep sea oceanography in Southampton. Working for main contractor, Wimpey Construction UK, Matthew Hall has been called in to supply and install the mechanical, electrical, public health and fire engineering services. The 25,000 sq metre building is being jointly funded by the Natural Environment Research Council and the University of Southampton and will house laboratories, offices, workshops, lecture theatres and other service facilities.
