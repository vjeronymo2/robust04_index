920428 FT 28 APR 92 / UK Company News: Pounds 20.2m sale at Union Discount Union Discount has agreed to sell part of its 'big ticket' leasing business to Humberclyde Investments for Pounds 20.2m. Union, which recently reported heavy losses, is one of London's nine discount houses through which the Bank of England channels liquidity to the commercial banking system and influences interest rates. It said the sale represented 44.6 per cent of its total 'big ticket' leasing book.
