BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 21 (KCNA) -- A friendly gathering with officials of the Chinese Embassy here took place at the Changdok School in Mangyongdae District, Pyongyang, on April 20. Invited there were officials of the Chinese Embassy. On hand were Chon Yon-ok, vice-chairperson of the Committee for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries, and teachers and pupils of the school. The participants went round the Changdok School associated with the revolutionary history of the great leader President Kim Il-song and saw an art performance by school children. They had a friendly talk.
