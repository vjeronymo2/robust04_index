940729 FT 29 JUL 94 / International Company News: Goodrich buys textile operation BF Goodrich, the US chemicals group, will acquire the textile coatings business of France's Rhone-Poulenc for an undisclosed price, AP-DJ reports. The coatings business employs 110 people with sales of Dollars 48m in 1993. Goodrich's specialty chemicals business recorded sales of Dollars 830m in 1993.
