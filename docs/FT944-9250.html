941116 FT 16 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Capitol improves Capitol Group, the specialist security company which came to the market in May, announced a 15 per cent rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 472,000 to Pounds 542,000 for the half year to September 30. All three divisions - investigatory, audit and stocktaking, and port and ferry security - had increased turnover and operating profits, Mr Michael Griffiths, chairman, said. He added that the second half had started well and the directors were looking for acquisition opportunities. Turnover amounted to Pounds 3.5m (Pounds 3.3m). Earnings per share came through at 3.92p (3.81p) and the dividend is 1.2p.
