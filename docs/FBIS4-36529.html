BFN [Report by Muhammad 'Amirah in Jericho] [Text] Jericho Mayor Jamil Sabri Khalaf has tendered his resignation to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat without stating the reasons. Although President 'Arafat rejected the resignation and asked Khalaf to remain in his post until general elections are held next month, Khalaf has decided to stay at home. Lieutenant Colonel Munthir Irshayd, the Palestinian Administration official in Jericho, who was appointed to this position by 'Arafat after the implementation of the first phase of self-rule, has denied Israeli media reports about the arrest of the mayor. In an exclusive statement to AL-DUSTUR, Irshayd said such Israeli rumors are part of Israel's psychological warfare and that the man (Khalaf) chose to stay at home without any coercion or pressure.
