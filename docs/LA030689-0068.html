March 6, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

TITANS LOSE TO CAL ON HOME RUN IN NINTH

Jeff Kent hit a solo home run in the ninth inning to give California a 5-4 victory over Cal State Fullerton Sunday in a nonconference baseball game at Fullerton.

Kent, who attended Edison High School, hit the first pitch off reliever Sam Colarusso (2-1) over the left-field fence. It was his first home run of the season.

Cal (18-3) took a 2-0 lead in the third, but Fullerton (10-6) scored three runs in the bottom of the inning to take a 3-2 lead.

In other nonconference action:

Portland 11, UC Irvine 9 -- The visiting Pilots scored nine runs in the seventh to erase a 7-1 deficit against the Anteaters (6-12-1).

In California Collegiate Athletic Assn men's tennis:

Chapman 6, Cal State Bakersfield 3 -- Olivier Amerlinck defeated Will Marino to lead the Panthers (5-5, 4-1) at Bakersfield.

In nonconference women's tennis:

UC Irvine 6, Wichita State 1 -- Courtney Weichsel defeated Gianarla DiLaura, 6-1, 6-1, to lead the Anteaters (5-9) at Nevada Las Vegas.
