941111 FT 11 NOV 94 / International Company News: First-quarter surge for Wesfarmers Wesfarmers, the Australian fertiliser, chemicals and coal group, yesterday said it was budgeting for an improved profit after tax and before abnormal items for the year to June 30 1995, after posting a 70.9 per cent rise in first-quarter net profit, Reuter reports from Perth. The company reported a net profit of ADollars 31.40m (USDollars 23.72m) for the three months to September 30, compared with ADollars 18.38m in the 1993-94 first quarter. Results were boosted by abnormal profits of ADollars 12.92m on the sale of investments. Sales rose to ADollars 530.15m in the quarter, from ADollars 443.37m a year earlier. Wesfarmers reported a net profit after tax and before abnormal items of ADollars 127.02m for the 1993-94 year.
