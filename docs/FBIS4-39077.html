BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 1/6/1994 -- The ninth round of the Moldovan-Russian negotiations concerning the status and terms of withdrawal of the 14th Russian Army from Moldovan territory will be held on 7 and 8 June in Moscow, stated to BASA Ion Nastac, head of the press secretariat of the Ministry of Defence. The official Moldovan delegation will be headed by Nicolae Osmochescu, vice-minister of foreign affairs, specified the cited source. Colonel Nicolae Tudureanu will lead the group of military experts. With the approval of both Moldovan and Russian delegations, representatives of Tiraspol will be allowed to attend the negotiations. The previous round of talks was held in March 1994 in Chisinau. The negotiators did not agree on the terms of withdrawal of the Russian armed forces from Moldova, for the Russian delegation wanted Moldova to grant Dniester a spectral juridical status in conjunction with its withdrawal. At this meeting the Moldovan delegation was headed by Anatol Taran, former ambassador of Moldova to Moscow.
