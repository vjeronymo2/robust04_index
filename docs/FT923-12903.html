920716 FT 16 JUL 92 / New Zealand's growth boost The stream of positive economic indicators in New Zealand continued yesterday, with data showing the economy grew by 1.1 per cent in the March quarter, the third successive quarterly rise, Reuter reports from Wellington. This followed news that inflation rose by only 1 per cent in the year to June, the lowest rate of all 24 countries in the OECD.
