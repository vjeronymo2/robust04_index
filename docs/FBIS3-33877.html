[Name indistinct] reports: [Reporter] According to the security forces, one terrorist was shot dead on Thursday in Amrossa, in the province of Blida, after he opened fire on the police from a mosque where he had taken refuge. Another terrorist was also shot dead in the district of Gourouba in Algiers. He was wanted for various crimes. An automatic pistol with a magazine and ammunition were found on him. Two other terrorists, who were also wanted, were shot dead on Saturday in the Casbah. An automatic pistol with a magazine, ammunition, and a professional identity card which had belonged to a policeman were found on him. On Saturday, in Lakhdaria, one terrorist was shot dead during a search operation. In Kouba, one 23-year-old man was arrested. He was the imam at the Benhaddad Mosque in Kouba. After appearing before the court for sending death threats, he was remanded in custody. And finally, in Laghouat, one terrorist was wounded and arrested at around 0130 on Sunday as he was attempting to set fire to the post office.
