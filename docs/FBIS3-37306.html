Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Armenian Defense Minister Serzh Sargisyan today received a British military delegation headed by General [name indistinct], the British military attache at the British Embassy in Moscow. The Defense Ministry Press Service reported that the general situation on the Armenian-Azerbaijani front was discussed during the meeting. The guests wanted to visit army units in Vardenisskiy and Gorisskiy rayons to get acquainted with the daily lives of the fighters of the Armenian Army.
