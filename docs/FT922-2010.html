920619 FT 19 JUN 92 / People: Moves in finance Tony Herbert, Denison Moxon and Jim Morris have been appointed directors of GREIG MIDDLETON. ***** Richard Kilsby, formerly vice-chairman of Charterhouse Bank, has been appointed an md of BANKERS TRUST COMPANY. Tony Moore, formerly with County Natwest, has joined Bankers Trust Company as an md and head of corporate finance origination for the UK and parts of Europe. ***** Richard Payne and Christopher Collins have been appointed directors of PANMURE GORDON. ***** James Jamieson has been appointed a director of the PRINCIPALITY BUILDING SOCIETY. ***** Stuart Bridges has been appointed to the board of TOUCHE REMNANT INVESTMENT MANAGEMENT. ***** Patrick Sellers has been appointed director of LLOYDS DEVELOPMENT CAPITAL. ***** Dermot Keegan is appointed director of retail sales for COUNTY NATWEST INVESTMENT MANAGEMENT. ***** Stephen Hargrave has been appointed chairman of the CHILDREN'S MEDICAL CHARITY INVESTMENT TRUST in place of Andrew Perloff who remains a director. Luke Johnson becomes deputy chairman. ***** Neil Williams is joining COUNTY NATWEST INVESTMENT MANAGEMENT as a UK economist from Crown Agents Asset Management.
