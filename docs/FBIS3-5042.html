Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, February 28 (KNS-KCNA) -- A central meeting of Korean Residents in Japan was held in Tokyo on February 28 to commemorate the 75th anniversary of the March 1st popular uprising. In his report, So Man-sul, vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon), said the March 1st popular uprising was a nationwide anti-Japanese people's resistance in which the Korean people rose in struggle to retake the territory of their country lost to the Japanese imperialists and restore the national sovereignty. The desire of the Korean people to achieve the sovereignty and independence of the country could be realised successfully as the respected leader Generalissimo Kim Il-song roused the entire fellow countrymen to accomplish the historical cause of national liberation, he added. He recalled that the great leader formulated the "10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country" and led the 70 million fellow countrymen in the struggle to achieve the national reunification under the banner of the great national unity. He condemned the anti-DPRK campaign of the reactionaries at home and abroad and called upon all the people to firmly unite as one and fight under the banner of the great national unity to open a new phase of national reunification.
