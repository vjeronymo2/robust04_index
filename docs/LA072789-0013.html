July 27, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

SPOTTED OWLS AND LOGGERS

It is difficult to have much sympathy for a person who would display a bumper sticker reading "Save a Logger, Eat an Owl." The loggers assume that humans are unquestionably more important than a bird. There are many of us who do not necessarily agree that the concerns of humans give them the right to drive other species to extinction. The loggers will not starve to death.

Let them find a more respectable line of work. Our country has been thoroughly butchered in the name of economic growth and "development" (a despicable euphemism). Thank God the ranks of environmentalists are growing and that now other species have a fighting chance. It's too late for the California grizzly and condor.

LINDA BAINBRIDGE

Altadena
