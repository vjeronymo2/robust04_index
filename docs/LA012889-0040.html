January 28, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

PRESIDENT BUSH'S INAUGURAL ADDRESS

Looking at the millions of dollars spent on himself -- via the inaugural festivities -- George "read my lips" Bush appears to be saying "let them eat cake" rather than something about a "kinder, gentler" nation. I wonder what some of those millions might have done for the homeless or programs for abused and neglected children?

JENNA LINDQUIST

Los Alamitos
