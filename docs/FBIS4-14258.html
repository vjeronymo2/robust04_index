BFN [Text] The president, Mr. Sardar Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari, has called upon the opposition to end its boycott and join the proceedings of the National Assembly. He was talking to newsmen in Islamabad this morning after laying the foundation stone of the National Police Academy. The president said boycott is a constitutional right of the opposition, but it is high time that politicians must play their due role to defuse tension and end polarization in the national body politic. He said even otherwise there is no justification for the opposition to resort to boycott at this moment. Replying to various questions, the president said he has done nothing unconstitutional as far as the NWFP [Northwest Frontier Province] is concerned. He said that Syed Sabir Shah, after losing majority in the Provincial Assembly, adopted improper tactics to sustain his government. He said it is a democratic norm that when one loses majority, he should step aside.
