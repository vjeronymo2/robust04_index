930122 FT 22 JAN 93 / Southend rail line to be re-signalled THE London Tilbury and Southend line, a probable early candidate for franchising under government privatisation plans, is to be re-signalled at a cost of Pounds 40m, British Rail announced yesterday. BR's Network SouthEast said yesterday it had secured funding for the project and the contract had been awarded to GEC Alsthom Signalling.
