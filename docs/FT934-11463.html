931101 FT 01 NOV 93 / Scuffles mark gathering of far-right party Scuffles between police and young militants marked a gathering of the Republicans, a prominent far-right party, in Rastatt, south-west Germany yesterday. The party, led by Mr Franz Schonhuber, a former Waffen SS sergeant, presents a disconcerting threat to the political establishment in next year's 'election marathon', when there will be 19 elections in Germany
