May 14, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Mexico Takes Step to Privatize Banks: Eight years after banks were nationalized, the lower house of Mexico's congress -- the Chamber of Deputies -- approved a constitutional amendment to return them to private ownership. The measure, proposed on May 2 by President Carlos Salinas de Gortari, now goes to the Senate, where its passage is virtually assured. A total of 31 state legislatures also must approve it.
