Language: Persian Article Type:CSO [First paragraph is KEYHAN-E HAVA'I introduction] [Text] Tabriz, 21 Dey [11 Jan] (IRNA) -- The 40,000-telephone center of Maragheh has become operational. On the occasion of the anniversary of the prophethood of the revered prophet of Islam, the "Sahand" Airport of the city of Maragheh became operational. During these ceremonies, in which the ministers of roads and transport, energy, and post, telegraph and telephone also participated, it was announced that the Maragheh Sahand Airport was built with 1.6 billion rials provided by the people. This airport, with a 3-km airstrip, is capable of handling small passenger airplanes. With the operation of this airport, the Maragheh-Tehran air route, with two flights per week, will soon be established. At the same time, the 40,000-telephone center of the city of Maragheh was also inaugurated this week. With the operation of this center, the number of telephones in Maragheh increased from 8,000 to 40,000. This center is the first completely digital telephone center in Eastern Azarbaijan.
