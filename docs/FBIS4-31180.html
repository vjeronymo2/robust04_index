BFN [Text] On 6 June Prime Minister Lyuben Berov met Ahmed Dogan, leader of the Movement for Rights and Freedoms [DPS], and expressed his intention to submit a proposal on personnel changes in the cabinet at the 8 and 9 June National Assembly sessions. Mr. Berov intends to propose Nedko Dobrilov, deputy minister of justice, for the post of minister of justice. Kiril Tsochev will be proposed for the post of minister of trade. Prime Minister Berov does not plan any structural changes in the Council of Ministers or new candidacies for the post of deputy prime minister. Dogan said after the meeting that the DPS will decide tomorrow whether it will support those proposals, after meetings with Dobrilov and Tsochev.
