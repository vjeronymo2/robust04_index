Dated: February 24, 1994. National Institute of Standards and Technology Meeting of Reference Materials Producers agency: National Institute of Standards and Technology, Commerce. action: Notice.

summary: The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) announces a meeting of reference materials producers and users for the purpose of discussing means of achieving traceability of environmental reference materials to national measurement standards. This meeting is being sponsored by the NIST and the American Chemical Society on Environmental Improvement in conjunction with the Pittsburgh Conference.
