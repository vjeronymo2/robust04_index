920414 FT 14 APR 92 / International Company News: Wachovia improves 26% to Dollars 106.1m WACHOVIA, the south-eastern regional US banking group, reported first-quarter net income yesterday of Dollars 106.1m, up 26 per cent from the Dollars 84.1m earned a year ago, writes Patrick Harverson in New York. The improvement resulted largely from higher net interest income and other service revenues, coupled with tighter controls on operating expenses. The company said total non-performing assets at the end of the first quarter amounted to Dollars 306m, up from Dollars 271m a year ago.
