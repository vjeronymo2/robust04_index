November 25, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

WOMEN OF 'MAGNOLIAS'

I get the uneasy feeling that the reviewer of "Steel Magnolias" is a man with uncontrollable sexist convictions. I am told that Rainer came from the recently defunct Los Angeles Herald Examiner. With close-minded people like him there, it's no wonder the paper folded.

A. DUDLEY JOHNSON JR.

Los Angeles
