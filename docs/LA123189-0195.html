December 31, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

AA MEMBERS TO OFFER SOBER SOCIALIZING

Members of Alcoholics Anonymous are sponsoring their annual "marathon meeting" for people who are planning to stay sober this New Year's Eve and may need a little support in that quest.

The round-the-clock meeting, at 705 N. Harbor Blvd., began Saturday morning and will continue until noon Monday. Members offer fellowship and refreshments for those who want to socialize during New Year's but who do not want to celebrate with alcohol.

The group usually holds the marathons several times a year during various holidays.
