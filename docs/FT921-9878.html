920210 FT 10 FEB 92 / Surge in Danish trade surplus DENMARK saw a record trade surplus last year, assisted by a 19.2 per cent increase in exports to Germany, writes Hilary Barnes in Copenhagen. The trade surplus increased from DKr20.6bn (Pounds 1.85bn) in 1990 to DKr22.7bn last year. Exports rose 6 per cent to DKr228.5bn, while imports climbed 5 per cent to DKr205.8bn. Exports to Germany increased to DKr51.2bn, against imports of DKr45.5bn.
