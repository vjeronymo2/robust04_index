BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn has ordered the allocation of 2 million dinars for poor families in the al-Sulaymaniyah Governorate. The comrade secretary of the al-Sulaymaniyah Branch of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party told an INA correspondent that leader President Saddam Husayn's generous contribution will be distributed to poor families to ease their suffering as a result of the continuation of the unjust blockade imposed on the unyielding Iraqi people.
