930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Revised dividends In the light of recent proposed Budget changes a number of companies yesterday made dividend statements. To comply with its Articles of Association, SmithKline Beecham has increased its fourth quarter 1992 dividend on A shares from 2.375p to 2.533p. London Forfaiting declared a second interim of 5.5p for 1992 in lieu of the proposed 5.5p final already announced. Sumit will pay a 1.4p interim in lieu of the proposed 1.4p final for 1992. TT Group declared a second interim of 3.6p for the year to December 26 in lieu of the 3.6p final forecast when it announced the offer for AB Electronic Products last December.
