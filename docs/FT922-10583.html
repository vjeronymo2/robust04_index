920430 FT 30 APR 92 / Survey of Relocation in The UK (13): British Gas plans to relocate 1,300 London staff BRITISH GAS is preparing to relocate 1,300 employees from London to a new 300,000 sq. ft headquarters to be built at Birmingham Business Park. The site was chosen after a detailed examination of potential venues throughout the east and west Midlands. The British Gas building, which should be ready by the end of 1994, will be developed on a 20-acre plot within Arlington's 39-acre business park, (pictured above and below). Companies moving to the business park in the last year included Wang, Sema, Severn Trent Water, Bull, Apricot, Charles Haswell, the Co-op Bank and Hutchison Telecommun- ications. They joined other companies such as Rank Xerox, Lucas, Digital, Equitable Life and the West Midlands Development Agency, already located there.
