April 16, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: WESTMINSTER;

PLANTING OF TREES IN PARKS PLANNED

Residents, students and community organizations will plant trees in 21 neighborhood parks April 28 as they participate in Global ReLeaf, a nationwide program with a goal of planting 100 million trees by 1991.

Residents who donate $30 will have a tree planted in a city park.
