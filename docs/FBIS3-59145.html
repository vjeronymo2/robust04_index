Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Berlin (ADN-BLN) -- The Second Criminal Bench of Berlin Higher Regional Court today sentenced a 61-year-old former IBM security officer from Wilhelmshaven to 12 years imprisonment for treason. Between 1975 and 1980, the defendant passed on some 35,000 sheets of confidential military documents from an archive of the IBM Special Systems company [IBM Sondersysteme] to the main Department II of the MfS [former GDR Ministry for State Security], which handled counterintelligence. In the event of a military conflict, the betrayal could have led to the complete destruction of NATO's navy.
