940926 FT 26 SEP 94 / Arts (Openings): New York The Metropolitan Opera opens its 1994-5 season tonight with a gala featuring the world's two leading tenors - Placido Domingo in Puccini's 'Il tabarro' and Luciano Pavarotti in Leoncavallo's 'I Pagliacci'. Domingo also stars in Mozart's 'Idomeneo', starting on Saturday, while Pavarotti sings in next week's performances of Puccini's 'Tosca''. The conductor on each occasion is James Levine.
