November 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

RONALD REAGAN'S $2-MILLION SPEAKING FEE

Your editorial on the travesty of Ronald Reagan hiring himself out for PR work in Japan will, hopefully, call a halt to this tacky, tasteless behavior by our former President. The Japanese, and the rest of the world too, must be laughing, sniggering and chortling over this gambit, buying the President of the U.S., and for only $2 million!

It highlights the avarice and the money-grubbing aspect of U.S. politics. We have no honor, no decency and everything is for sale -- the land, companies, the Congress, and now the former President. What must our uniformed servicemen and women think, seeing the spectacle of their former commander-in-chief doing day work for the Japanese empire.

Reagan disgraces himself and our country with his hustling.

PATRICK F. FLYNN

Yorba Linda
