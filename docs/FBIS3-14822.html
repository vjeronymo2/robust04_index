Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow February 27 TASS -- Vladimir Shumeyko, chairman of the Federation Council, has consented to the president's proposal to appoint Aleksey Ilyushenko as acting prosecutor-general of the Russian Federation. It has become known from reliable sources that the decision was taken "on the basis of the president's recommendation pending the consideration of an item to this effect at a Federation Council meeting in accordance with Article 102 of the Russian Constitution". The need to appoint an acting prosecutor-general arose in view of the resignation of Aleksey Kazannik from the post on Saturday. Kazannik disagrees with the release, by a State Duma decision, of those guilty of the October 3-4, 1993, bloodshed in Moscow, and regards it as unlawful and uncoordinated with law enforcement agencies.
