930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Compco Compco Holdings, the property investment and development group, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 171,257 to Pounds 200,140 in the year to March 25. Rental income from property improved to Pounds 2.11m (Pounds 2m). The proposed single final dividend is held at 12.96p, uncovered by earnings per share 2p higher at 7.33p.
