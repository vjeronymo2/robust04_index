BFN [Excerpt] According to a report dated 30 May from the front line, the combined forces of the Royal Government have already reached the Chi Neang point on Route 10, 18 km east of Pailin. No clashes have been reported, and the government troops, which seem to be very vigilant at this point in time, have not yet overrun the area. The report further says that the plateau Phnum Veng on Route 10 near Chineang is also being surrounded by government forces. A senior Army officer said the government forces' advance has not encountered any heavy attacks and that the smooth headway indicated that the Khmer Royal Armed Forces are on the alert and have never forgotten their recent shameful defeats.
