BFN [Text] Havana, 23 Jun (PL) -- A group of eight people, who had illegally occupied the Chilean consulate in this capital to force their departure from the country, voluntarily left the diplomatic mission this afternoon. Sources close to the consulate confirmed to PRENSA LATINA that the occupants left the mission at 1730 (2130 GMT) aboard two vehicles with diplomatic license plates and that they were taken to their respective homes by official Chilean personnel.
