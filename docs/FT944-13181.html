941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / UK Company News: I&S UK Smaller Net asset value at I&S UK Smaller Companies Trust edged ahead from 116.26p to 120.11p over the six months to September 30. The modest rise of 3.3 per cent, however, compared with a decline of 5.4 per cent in the FT-SE SmallCap (excluding investment trusts). The trust had gearing of just under 24 per cent at the period end, having drawn down borrowings of Pounds 6m in August at a fixed rate of 9.22 per cent for five years. Earnings per share dipped to 1.58p (1.76p) but the interim dividend is maintained at 1p.
