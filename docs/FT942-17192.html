940406 FT 06 APR 94 / UK Company News: Bletchley Motor An all-round improvement was reported by Bletchley Motor Group, with pre-tax profits rising by 45 per cent from Pounds 1.23m to Pounds 1.77m in the 1993 year. The advance was achieved on turnover almost 20 per cent ahead to Pounds 84.4m. Earnings emerged at 24.7p (19.6p) and a proposed final dividend of 5.1p makes a 9.85p (8.8p) total.
