June 18, 1989, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

Two Encinitas men were killed Saturday morning on Interstate 805 when the driver lost control of a pickup truck, the California Highway Patrol said.

The men, whose names were not released, were driving north on I-805 at Miramar Road around 4:15 a.m. The truck drifted into the center divider and the driver overcorrected, causing the truck to roll over several times. Both men were flung from the vehicle.
