Language: Amharic Article Type:BFN [Summary] The Ethiopian People's Democratic Movement [EPDM] third congress has decided that EPDM, which was struggling under the objective of national unity, will continue to struggle under the name of ANDM, or Amhara National's Democratic Movement [bihere amara demokrasiyawi nikinake] for the interest of the Amhara people while upholding its basic democratic principles. The congress decided this after assessing the organization's yearly development and realizing that it should now shoulder its national responsibility, after approving the code of conduct as stipulated in the organization's program.
