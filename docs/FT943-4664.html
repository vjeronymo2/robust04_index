940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: Benetton team made 'honest mistake' Flavio Briatore, managing director of Benetton Formula, arrived at the International Automobile Federation in Paris to hear that his Formula One motor racing team had escaped punishment for tampering with refuelling equipment. The federation ruled that Benetton had made an 'honest mistake' in removing a filter which led to a flash fire at July's German grand prix in Hockenheim.
