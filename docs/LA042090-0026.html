April 20, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PCC'S KEVIN DALLAS TURNS IN TOP TIME

Kevin Dallas of Pasadena City College won the 400-meter hurdles in 51.79 to highlight the Community College division of the Mt.

San Antonio Relays Thursday at Mt. San Antonio College. Long Beach City College won the 400-meter relay in 40.76 and El Camino College took the 800 relay in 1:26.57.

Today at 2 p.m., high school action for both boys and girls will feature the 3,000-meter runs and the 6,400-meter relays. Junior Julia Yendork of Walnut, the national leader in the triple jump is also scheduled to compete.
