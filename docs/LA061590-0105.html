June 15, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

AGOURA PAIR NAMED TO 2-A DIVISION TEAM

Agoura High's Brian Smith and Bob Kazmirski have been named to the Southern Section 2-A Division baseball team by sportswriters.

Kazmirski, a shortstop, batted .451 with 11 doubles, three home runs and 23 runs batted in. He has signed a letter of intent to play next season at Fresno State.

Smith, an outfielder who batted .423 and drove in 17 runs, has signed to play at Arizona State.

Santa Clara's Kwinn Knight, who batted .467, was a second-team selection.
