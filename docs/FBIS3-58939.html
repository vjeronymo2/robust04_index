Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- The Federal Government today confirmed reports that chemicals, the export of which to Iraq would have violated the UN embargo against that country, have been discovered on board the German freighter "Asian Senator." Government spokesman Dieter Vogel announced in Bonn that the Federal Government received information in mid-December that two containers with ammonium perchlorate were being transported from China to Beirut. The freight was described as harmless material in the manifest, whereas in fact it was fuel ingredients for the Iraqi missile program.
