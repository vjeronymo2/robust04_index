Section 4160.4 is revised to read as follows: §4160.4 Appeals. (a) Any person whose interest is adversely affected by a final decision of the authorized officer may appeal the decision for the purpose of a hearing before an administrative law judge under §4.470 of this title by filing a notice of appeal in the office of the authorized officer within 30 days after receipt of the final decision or within 30 days after the date the proposed decision becomes final as provided in §4160.3(a). Appeals and petitions for a stay of the decision shall be filed at the office of the authorized officer. The authorized officer shall promptly transmit the appeal and petition for stay to ensure their timely arrival at the appropriate Office of Hearings and Appeals. (b) A petition for a stay of the decision, if any, shall be filed with the authorized officer together with a notice of appeal.
