920218 FT 18 FEB 92 / International Company News: Storage Technology to acquire distributor STORAGE Technology, a US manufacturer of data storage sub-systems for high-performance computers, announced yesterday that it planned to buy Edata Scandinavia, its Scandinavian distributor, for Dollars 75m in cash. Edata, which employs 194 people, had 1990 revenues of around Dollars 68.3m, about three-quarters of it derived from Storage Technology products. It operates in Belgium, Denmark, Finland, Luxembourg, the Netherlands, Norway and Sweden. Storage Technology has European marketing operations in Austria, France, Germany, Italy, Switzerland and the UK. Mr Harris Ravine, Storage's executive vice-president for Europe, said the acquisition would increase its direct presence on the continent. He added that Edata in the past had concentrated on mainframe products, but in the last two years it diversified into mid-range markets, which was in line with Storage Technology's strategy. The US company has reached an agreement with key Edata shareholders which commits to its offer series A shares representing 70.4 per cent of the votes, and it is making a public offer for the remainder of the equity.
