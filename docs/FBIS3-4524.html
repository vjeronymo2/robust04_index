Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Almaty, March 30 (XINHUA) -- The Bank of China is doing well in Almaty, with business of more than 30 million U.S. dollars in 1993, its first year of operation in the capital of Kazakhstan. The Bank of China, the first foreign bank allowed to operate here, handles savings accounts and time deposits, and offers other security and financial services. The bank now has over 200 customers, and total deposits of more than one million dollars. The Bank of China has remitted 20 million dollars for its customers since it began operating here in April 1993. They also offer financial advice, exchange currencies, and assist customers in finding business parters. Director-Manager Xiang Chusheng said the bank will gradually expand its business and improve efficiency.
