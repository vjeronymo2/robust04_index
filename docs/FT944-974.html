941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / International Company News: Provident Life sells parts of health business Provident Life, one of the biggest providers of disability insurance in the US, is selling parts of its health insurance business for Dollars 310m, writes Richard Waters. Healthsource, a company based in New Hampshire which runs health maintenance organisations (HMOs), said it would buy Provident's group health and HMO operations.
