940203 FT 03 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Plasmon buys Intl Data Eng Plasmon, a privately-owned manufacturer of optical memory disks and disk systems, has paid an undisclosed amount to acquire International Data Engineering, a US company which makes the robotics at the heart of optical disk storage systems. Plasmon makes about 10,000 optical disks a month; its optical storage systems, or jukeboxes, cost between Pounds 9,000 and Pounds 200,000. Turnover was Pounds 13.5m last year, with profits of Pounds 292,000. IDE is based in Minneapolis, Minnesota, where it makes jukebox mechanisms and tape autoloaders.
