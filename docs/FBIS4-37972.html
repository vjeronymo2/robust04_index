BFN [Text] Brother 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani, minister of planning and development, currently in Washington, said that during his visit to the United States, he met with [name indistinct], the assistant U.S. secretary of state, and the National Security Council adviser on the Middle East. In a statement to the SABA' news agency, al-Iryani said that he discussed with U.S. officials several topics related to the cease-fire within the framework of UN Security Council Resolution 924 which is based on the fact that legitimacy belongs to the Republic of Yemen and that nothing else is legitimate. Al-Iryani said that Resolution 924 was the key to all discussions. He said everybody was eager to realize a cease-fire, noting that the National Defense Council's cease-fire declaration represents a very important step which affirms the credibility of the Republic of Yemen Government's commitment to implementing the Security Council resolution.
