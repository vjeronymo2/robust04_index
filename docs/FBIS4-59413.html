BFN [Text] Cairo, 12 May (MENA) -- The Egyptian Human Rights Organization [EHRO] issued a statement today dismissing a foreign news agency report attributed to an EHRO member that said the number of political detainees in Egypt is between 20,000 and 30,000. In its statement, EHRO said none of its officials had made such a statement during its news conference yesterday. EHRO stressed that the assertion that there are between 20,000 and 30,000 detainees is not true.
