920626 FT 26 JUN 92 / International Company News: General Mills lifts earnings GENERAL Mills, the US cereals and restaurant group, reported after-tax profits of Dollars 495.6m in the year to end-May, compared with Dollars 472.7m in the previous 12 months. In the final three months, net profit was Dollars 93.1m, compared with Dollars 90.9m previously. Both final-quarter and full-year figures for 1992 are scored after a Dollars 10m charge, relating to a potential lease adjustment being negotiated with RH Macy, the bankrupt US department store group.
