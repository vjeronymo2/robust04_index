BFN [Excerpt] Compared with the same period last year, our province's foreign trade and exports increased by a bigger margin in the first quarter this year. By the end of March, the province's overall export volume reached more than $310 million, an increase of 25.6 percent over the same period last year. In the first quarter of this year, our province's foreign trade and exports had the following features: The export growth rates of various export enterprises were uneven. The export volumes of specialized companies in our province felld, whereas industrial and commercial exports at the prefectural and city levels rose by a bigger margin. The exports of foreign-funded enterprises maintained their strong growth. In the first quarter, foreign-funded enterprises' export volumes reached $37.44 million, registering an increase of 450 percent over the corresponding period last year. This margin of increase was much higher than the average growth rate of foreign-funded enterprises throughout the country. With regard to the main countries and areas to which our province's products were exported, our exports to the traditional Hong Kong and Macao markets fell, while our exports to the EC countries increased fairly rapidly.
