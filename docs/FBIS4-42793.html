BFN [Text] Cyprus and Egypt signed a security agreement this morning aimed at combating organized crime, drug trafficking, and international terrorism. The agreement was signed by Justice and Public Order Minister Alekos Egangelou and by Nassar Zahir, Egypt's assistant interior minister, who began a five-day visit to Cyprus yesterday. In a statement after the signing of the agreement, Mr. Evangelou praised the importance of the agreement and also referred to the close relations between the two countries.
