Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By CRIME-PRESS for TASS] [Text] Moscow, 10 Mar -- A CRIME-PRESS correspondent was just informed at the press-service of the Moscow Directorate of the Federal Counterintelligence Service that special units have thoroughly checked the premises of the Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs with the help of special equipment to find the explosive device. Fortunately, the alarm was false: there is no bomb. Nevertheless, the quick and efficient evacuation of personnel has proved that Russian diplomats keep their cool under any circumstances.
