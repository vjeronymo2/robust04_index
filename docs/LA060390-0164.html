June 3, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

TAKIN ON THE CASH WINS KINDERGARTEN BY A LENGTH

Favored Takin On The Cash broke on the lead and was never really challenged in winning the $233,000 Kindergarten Stakes for 2-year-olds at Los Alamitos Saturday night.

With Danny Cardoza in the saddle for his third career Kindergarten victory, the Dash For Cash colt won by one length and was timed in 17.65 seconds for 350 yards.

Takin On The Cash has won three of his five career starts and earned $185,000.

Takin On The Cash had been beaten in his Kindergarten trial on May 23, but trainer Caesar Dominguez was confident.

Takin On The Cash paid $7.00, $4.00 and $2.80.

Raised Toperfection, with thoroughbred jockey Gary Stevens in the saddle, finished eighth.
