March 14, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

JONES, WILBORN SHARE TOP HONOR IN MID-VALLEY LEAGUE

Senior centers Dana Jones of North Hollywood High and Marquis Wilborn of Reseda have been named co-player of the year in the Mid-Valley League.

Other first-team selections are junior James Chen and senior Mike Basowski of Reseda, sophomore Robert Hill and senior Harry Marks of North Hollywood and senior Kenyon Wright of Monroe.

Reseda and North Hollywood shared the league title with 8-2 records, and North Hollywood won the City Section 3-A Division title.
