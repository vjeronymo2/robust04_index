Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Nikolay Styazhkin] [Text] Cherkessk February 14 TASS -- A session of the Karachayevo-Cherkess Supreme Soviet (Parliament) has adopted a resolution under which the chairman of the Karachayevo-Cherkess Council of Ministers will be vested with powers of the head of the republic until the adoption of a constitution. Under the resolution, the head of the republic will hold the top post in the republican executive authority, will form the republican Council of Ministers after discussing it with his assistants and will coordinate with its presidium the appointment and dismissal of ministers, heads of republican committees and senior officials from all other state management bodies. The fundamentals of the constitutional system of the Karachayevo-Cherkess republic will be outlined in acordance with the results of a republican referendum, scheduled for April 24, a spokesman for the republican parliament told ITAR-TASS. There will be only one question on the ballot paper: Do you think the introduction of the post of president is necessary in the Karachayevo-Cherkess republic?
