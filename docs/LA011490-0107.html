January 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski made a $21 profit when Vigorous Green won the sixth race and returned $3.20 to win.

His $35 parlay turned into a straight win bet when his first horse, Burn Annie, was scratched in the fifth race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,136.

Bob's bet today is $50 to win on Kamikaze Sam in the ninth.
