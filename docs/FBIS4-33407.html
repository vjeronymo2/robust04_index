BFN [Text] In order to face the sanctions and the total embargo, the government has formed a National Crisis Commission. By decree, the government of Judge Emile Jonassaint announced on Saturday 4 June the formation of a crisis commission in charge of managing the embargo and guaranteeing the rights of the population to food and work. The commission is also in charge of assessing the consequences of the embargo and is presided over by the minister of finances and economy.
