940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / World Commodities Prices: Copper retreats - Market Report A technical sell-off in late afternoon trading saw the London Metal Exchange three months delivery COPPER price tumble below Dollars 1,950 a tonne to find underlying support towards the Dollars 1,940 level. Copper's weakness spilled over into the ALUMINIUM market, where the three months price fell back to test support under Dollars 1,330-a-tonne following waves of speculative and trade selling. Commission house buying helped to underpin ZINC. Compiled from Reuter
