BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 10 Jun (NA) -- President Carlos Menem today appointed former Supreme Court Justice Rodolfo Barra as the new justice minister. The announcement was made by Barra shortly after 1300 at the press room of the Olivos presidential residence following a meeting with the chief of state. Barra will be installed at 1800 on 16 June. He replaces Jorge Maiorano, who will be the new ombudsman.
