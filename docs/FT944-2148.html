941217 FT 17 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Ensor back in the black with Pounds 176,000 Ensor Holdings, the USM-quoted supplier of building materials, swung back into the black with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 176,000 for the six months to September 30. The outcome compared with a deficit of Pounds 32,000 last time and with a loss of Pounds 180,000 at the March 31 year-end. Turnover for the six months grew to Pounds 7.36m (Pounds 6.93m). At the operating level profits were Pounds 204,000 (Pounds 23,000) and the interest charge was cut from Pounds 55,000 to Pounds 28,000. Earnings came out at 0.6p (0.1p losses).
