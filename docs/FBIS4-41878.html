BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow June 29 TASS -- General of the Army Pavel Grachev, Russian minister of defence, on Wednesday begins a three- day working tour of the north caucasian military district. Grachev is to meet with the command of the district, and will familiarise himself with the accommodation of newly-formed units and those withdrawn from abroad to the region. The defence minister plans to visit Russian peacekeeping units operating in the area of the Georgian-Abkhazian conflict.
