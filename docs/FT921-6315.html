920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / Technology (Worth Watching): Perfecting one's business English THE business executive who wants to perfect his or her understanding of English could take advantage of a training package developed in Sheffield by System Applied Technology. The Desktop English package is based around a word processor, but addresses each of the four skills needed - reading, writing, listening and speaking. The software is divided into three sections: business English, which can be tailored to specific industries; social English; and general English. The interactive package caters for students of all levels, using an in-built library of authentic resource material, such as newspaper articles, memos, minutes of meetings, reports, train timetables and more. System Applied Technology: UK, 0742 768682.
