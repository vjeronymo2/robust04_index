930113 FT 13 JAN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and Cotton JUTE January/February c and f Dundee BTC Dollars 360, BWC Dollars 380, BTD Dollars 325, BWD Dollars 340; c and f Antwerp BTC Dollars 340, BWC Dollars 340, BTD Dollars 315, BWD Dollars 315. COTTON Liverpool- Spot and shipment sales for the week ended January 8 amounted to 204 tonnes, compared with none in the previous week. Improved demand brought moderate purchases mainly in west African, central Asian and Greek descriptions.
