940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / UK Company News: All-round growth lifts Relyon 34% Relyon Group, the beds and polyurethane foam manufacturer which also supplies surveillance equipment, improved pre-tax profits by 34 per cent, from Pounds 1.94m to Pounds 2.6m, in the half year to June 30. All three of the subsidiaries had contributed to the increase, Mr Ralph Platt, chairman, said. Turnover amounted to Pounds 22m, against Pounds 20.3m which included Pounds 390,000 from discontinued operations. Earnings per share rose from 6.15p to 8.23p and the interim dividend is lifted to 2.1p (1.9p).
