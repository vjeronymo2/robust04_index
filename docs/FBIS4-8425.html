BFN [Text] The Romanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs has faxed us the following communique: We have taken note, from reports circulated by the media, of the fact that the Parliament of the Republic of Moldova has ratified certain documents on adherence to the CIS. In connection with this, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs will announce its position after examining the respective official documents with the purpose of assessing their possible implications for the bilateral agreements concluded by the two independent Romanian states, the communique received from Romania's Ministry of Foreign Affairs points out.
