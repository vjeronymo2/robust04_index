January 11, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

THE NATION

The former law partner of ex-Rep. Mario Biaggi was sentenced to six years in prison and fined $222,000 for his role in the Wedtech racketeering case. Bernard Ehrlich, 59, who was found guilty of 11 counts of racketeering, conspiracy, extortion and bribery, remains free on bail pending appeal. He was convicted last summer with Biaggi and three other people of turning the Wedtech Corp., the now-defunct South Bronx military contractor, into a racketeering enterprise for their personal gain.
