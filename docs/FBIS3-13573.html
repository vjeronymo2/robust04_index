Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] received U.S. Special Envoy Dennis Ross for the second time last night. This meeting came in continuation of the meeting that was held yesterday morning and lasted for three hours in the presence of the members of the two delegations. A totality of issues in connection with protecting the Palestinian people in the occupied territories and the developments of the peace process, in addition to the talks currently under way at the UN Security Council on the resolution condemning the massacre at the Ibrahimi mosque, despite the fact that this delay until Monday, 14 March 1994, was submitted by the U.S. representative last Saturday when the Russian representatives insisted on casting the votes at that time, were discussed in the meeting.
