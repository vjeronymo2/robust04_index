Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [TA SR report: "Appeal in Support of Referendum"] [Text] "Esteemed citizens, with your help we established the Slovak Republic as a sovereign state. We regret that we were unable to complete, with your support, the work that we strove to accomplish. We are confident that you will not forget this. Together with you, we are determined to realize our plans and aims. We are still keen on your support and favor. We will do everything in our power to achieve the aims outlined by Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar in his address to the National Council of the Slovak Republic on 11 March 1994. In the interest of a democratic Slovak Republic, give your support to the referendum!" This is stated in a Slovak Government statement that was made available to the TA SR yesterday evening.
