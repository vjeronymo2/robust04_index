December 2, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski was a $40 loser Friday when Ono Gummo ran third in the ninth race. The original wager was to be a win parlay from Bequest in the eighth to Ono Gummo but Bequest was scratched. The loss left him with a bankroll of $1,066.30. He started the meeting with a mythical bankroll of $1,000.

For today, Bob's bet is a $30 win parlay from Phantom X. in the fourth to Firth of Tay in the sixth.

Beginning Bankroll......$1,000

Friday's Loss...........$40.00

Current Bankroll........$1,066.30
