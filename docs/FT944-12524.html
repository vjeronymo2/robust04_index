941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Newport at Pounds 25,000 Newport Holdings, the property investment company which came to the market in March, had pre-tax profits of Pounds 25,000 in the three months to June 30. Net rents receivable amounted to Pounds 377,000 and earnings per share were 0.3p. The company had previously stated that such a short trading period would not justify payment of an interim dividend. Net asset value at the period-end was Pounds 6.26m. Two acquisitions made since then have increased net asset value by Pounds 630,000.
