920402 FT 02 APR 92 / Election 1992: FT Election Share Index SHARES that might benefit from a Conservative victory dropped sharply yesterday, falling twice as far as the FT-SE index of big-company stocks. 'Conservative gainers' are now over 9 per cent lower than they were at the start of the campaign, while 'Labour gainers' have dropped only a third as far. Courtaulds Textiles, which might face higher costs from a minimum wage, dropped 16p or 3 1/2 per cent to 431p.
