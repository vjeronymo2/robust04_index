October 17, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

A leaky sewage pipe has led to the posting of warning signs on a stretch of the Ocean Beach shore, the San Diego County Department of Health Services reported.

The pipe carries sewage from restrooms and a restaurant on the Ocean Beach fishing pier to the city's sewage system. Repairs are under way. About 250 yards of shore have been posted from the pier northward and will remain posted until this afternoon, the health department said.
