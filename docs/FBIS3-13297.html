Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Before we get into details of the news, we would like to note a new Israeli move that falls in the context of the continuous defiance and provocations the Hebrew state indulges in against Lebanese sovereignty. The enemy's prime minister, Yitzhaq Rabin, today toured the occupied border region. He met in Marj 'Uyun barracks with Antoine Lahd, commander of the agent militia, and a number of his military commanders. Radio Voice of the South, the mouthpiece of Lahd's South Lebanese Army, carried a statement by Rabin in which he insinuated that peace with Lebanon will come after the resistance is eliminated, particularly Hizballah; after the Lebanese Government proves its ability to maintain security north of the border area for six months; and after the soldiers and officers of Lahd's militia are absorbed in the Lebanese Army, as Rabin put it.
