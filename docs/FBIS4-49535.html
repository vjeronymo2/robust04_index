BFN [Text] Warsaw, may 7 (XINHUA) -- Zhang Wannian, chief of the general staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, arrived here today for an official goodwill visit. General Zhang is expected to meet with Polish President Lech Walesa, Prime Minister Waldeman Pawlak and his Polish counterpart General Tadeusz Wilecki for talks on military relations between the two countries and issues of common concern.
