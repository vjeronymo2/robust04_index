July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: BLOODY PG-13

Meanwhile, "Licence to Kill" is out as a graphic novel. But don't look for Timothy Dalton as a new comic book character.

The English star -- known before Bond for more dignified roles -- has refused to allow his likeness to be licensed in the just-out book from Eclipse Comics.

The rest of the film's cast, however, made it to the funny pages. Pat H. Broeske
