930208 FT 08 FEB 93 / World News in Brief: Tennis star Arthur Ashe dies of Aids Arthur Ashe, who won the Wimbledon men's title in 1975, died in New York of pneumonia resulting from Aids. He was 49. Ashe used the celebrity status he won on the tennis court to fight racism in sport. A heart attack forced him to retire in 1979 and he was infected with the Aids virus from blood he received as he recovered from heart surgery.
