930812 FT 12 AUG 93 / International Company News: US healthcare jobs to be cut JOHNSON & Johnson, the US healthcare group, has launched a voluntary early retirement programme and estimates at least 1,000 workers would accept the offer, Reuter reports. The company said another 2,000 jobs would be cut through restructuring in both the US and overseas. It added that the cost of the cuts would be Dollars 200m pre-tax and was covered in a previous adoption of an accounting change.
