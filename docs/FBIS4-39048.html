BFN [Text] The situation in the state of emergency zone will never stabilize if the Interim Administration on Ingush and South Ossetian territory and Vladikavkaz ignore the decree on the return of deported Ingush to their places of permanent residence issued by the Russian president. This was stated by Ingush President Ruslan Aushev at a press conference in Nazran today. The press conference was held on the decision of the Russian Federation Council which had confirmed the presidential decree on extending the state of emergency in the zone of the inter-ethnic conflict until July 31. Aushev said that the return of Ingush refugees would depend on how efficiently the Interim Administration headed by Vladimir Lozovoy would perform.
