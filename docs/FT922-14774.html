920404 FT 04 APR 92 / Finance & The Family Insurance and divorce - At a glance There could, alas, be strong demand for a new product from DBS Assured Services. Its Maintenance Protection Plan allows a woman to insure the life of her ex-husband, even without his knowledge or consent. As life insurance is rarely high on people's minds amid the acrimony of a divorce, any joint protection plans a couple may have taken out often lapse. The policy is underwritten at Lloyd's, and the protection costs Pounds 4.50 for every Pounds 1,000 sum assured.
