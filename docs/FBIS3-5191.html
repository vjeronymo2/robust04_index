Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 4 -- The Centre for Japan Study made its first public appearance here yesterday. Present at the ceremony were Prof. Dr. Nguyen Duy Quy, member of the Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee, and director of the National Centre for Social Science and Humanities, Hiroyuki Yushita, Japanese ambassador, and others. The Centre for Japan Study, under the National Centre for Social Science and Humanities, will conduct studies on Japan's history, economy, socio-polititics, culture and language with a view to providing scientific basis for the state to make domestic and foreign policies. The centre's establishment marked a new step of development in the relations between Vietnam and Japan.
