September 21, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

L.A. RAIDERS

Oakland, Sacramento, Irwindale and Fontana, don't blame Al Davis for your own shortcomings.

If Oakland had it together, the Raiders would still be there. Sacramento part-ownership, I think not; Irwindale couldn't get the money or the site in order. Fontana, well, who cares?

The Coliseum Commission should be forced into hard manual labor for its treatment of Davis. Thankfully, Spectacor stepped up and at least for now kept the Raiders in Los Angeles.

M.A. SCHELSKE

San Dimas
