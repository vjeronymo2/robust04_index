Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The security services today announced that five people were killed in the past 72 hours in a number of provinces. Among the victims was a 17-year-old secondary school student who was assassinated outside her home in the new council estate in the municipality of Meftah in Blida Province. Four gunmen forced their way into the home of a 62-year-old woman named Derradj Adjouba and killed her with a knife. A teacher of the Koran was shot dead in the municipality of Meddah in Chlef. In Algiers, the bodies of a customs officer and his wife were discovered in a council estate consisting of 310 apartments situated at the Gue de Constantine. In addition, the security services also announced the arrest of 12 members of armed groups in the provinces of Mila, Algiers, Djelfa, and Boumerdes. In western Algeria, three municipal buildings, one farmer's market, and two parking lots were set on fire in Relizane and Sidi Bel Abbes.
