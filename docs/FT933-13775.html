930717 FT 17 JUL 93 / British Coal pension hearing ends THE High Court hearing over whether British Coal can use its half share of a Pounds 1bn surplus in a staff pension fund to provide enhanced pensions for employees being made redundant ended yesterday. Mr Justice Vinelott is expected to give his judgment within the next fortnight. The pension fund trustees have argued that using the surplus in such a way breaches the fund's rules.
