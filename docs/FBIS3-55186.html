Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 5 Jan (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin met Sergey Shakhray, chairman of the Party of Russian Unity and Accord [PRUA] today, along with members of the PRUA faction in the State Duma -- Aleksandr Shokhin, Konstantin Zatulin, Yuriy Kalmykov, and others. The political situation and the consultations prior to parliamentary sessions were discussed along with the prospects for cooperation between the government and the State Duma, the composition of the Russian Government, and ministerial powers.
