September 17, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

SHELDON, DANNEMEYER AND GAY PRIDE FESTIVAL

Re the gay festival brawl: A total of six people were arrested for pushing, shoving and some for slugging. Out of the thousands who attended this event, what's the big deal?

There were no arrests for public intoxication, drugs or assaults with lethal weapons. I imagine that that is the best day the park has had.

JIM PLEMAN

Santa Ana
