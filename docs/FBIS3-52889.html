Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The chairman of the Indian Space Research Organization, Dr. U.R. Rao, has expressed confidence that India will be able to develop its cryogenic engine latest by 1998. He was addressing a press conference at Jaipur. Mr. Rao said the indigenous technology will be superior to the present ones available in the world. According to him, the next launch of the Indian Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle will take place within four to five months.
