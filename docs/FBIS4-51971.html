BFN [Text] The Vietnamese Communist Party delegation led by Hong Ha, secretary of the Vietnamese Communist Party Central Committee, left for home on a train on 28 April. Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Workers Party of Korea Central Committee; Kim Yang-kon, vice director of a department of the party Central Committee; and Duong Chinh Thuc, Vietnamese ambassador to the DPRK, bid farewell to the delegation at Pyongyang Station.
