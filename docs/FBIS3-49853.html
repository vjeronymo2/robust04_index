Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Report by Tihomir Vinkovic from Geneva] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] The Croatian standpoint with regard to Bosnia-Herzegovina is clear. Negotiations can take place on the basis of the European Council proposal dated 22 December and the Brussels conclusions. According to these, Croatia should get at least 17.5 percent of the territory of Bosnia-Herzegovina. The Muslim proposal which was given to the Croats yesterday goes below this -- only 13 percent. This is unacceptable to the Croats, it has been learned from the Croatian delegation. [passage omitted]
