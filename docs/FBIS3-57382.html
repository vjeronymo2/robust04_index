Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Roman Zadunaiskiy] [Text] Moscow January 26 TASS -- The Russian border guards in Tajikistan delivered two preventive strikes overnight on the strongholds of Tajik opposition and Mojahedin in Afghanistan and on the routes which the militants were expected to use to penetrate in Tajikistan, a spokesman of the Russian frontier troops in Moscow told TASS on Wednesday. The spokesman gave no further details.
