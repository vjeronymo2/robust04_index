930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Micro Focus buys Proximity for Pounds 2.6m Micro Focus has acquired Proximity Software of the US for Dollars 4.1m (Pounds 2.6m), to be satisfied by the issue of 120,000 new shares. The acquisition was described as a pooling of interest as it brings Proximity technology which it already uses in its products under Micro Focus's control.
