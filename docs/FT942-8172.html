940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / Finance and the Family: Tax increases push inflation rate up -At a glance Inflation rose by 2.6 per cent in the year to April compared with 2.3 per cent in the year to March, largely as a result of higher taxes. Underlying inflation - which excludes mortgage interest payments - was at its lowest for a generation at 2.3 per cent in April, down from 2.4 per cent in March.
