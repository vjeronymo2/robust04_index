930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / International Company News: SmithKline sale SMITHKLINE Beecham, the Anglo-American healthcare group, is to sell a portion of the assets and business of Smith Kline & French, its wholly owned Italian subsidiary, to Proctor & Gamble for an undisclosed amount. P&G is buying five pharmaceutical products with combined sales of more than Dollars 40m (Pounds 25.9m).
