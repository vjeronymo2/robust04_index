October 6, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

PREP ROUNDUP;

NO. 1 CORONA DEL MAR WINS IN WATER POLO

Top-ranked Corona del Mar High School remained undefeated by beating third-ranked El Dorado, 10-4, in a nonleague water polo game Friday at Corona del Mar.

Ryan Weir had five goals, Ted Simpkins had three and Robbie Pike had nine saves for Corona del Mar (10-0).

In other nonleague games:

Laguna Hills 15, El Modena 4 -- Stuart Gilbert and brothers Chad and J.J. Carvin each had four goals to lead Laguna Hills (7-1).

Orange 10, Trabuco Hills 9 -- Jeremy Adams scored the winning goal from about seven meters out with 3:16 left to play to lead host Orange (8-10).

In Orange League water polo:

Brea-Olinda 16, Savanna 6 -- Scott Estrada, Kyle Kilpatrick and Chad Carlberg each had three goals at Brea-Olinda (13-4, 2-0).

Cerritos water polo tournament:

Cypress 10, Whittier 9 (OT) -- John Lin scored with 2:13 left in sudden-death overtime to move Cypress (13-2) into the semifinals.
