BFN [Report under the "NEGA Reports" rubric: "Crimea"] [Text] A sitting of the Crimean Supreme Soviet Presidium has taken place [date not given]. The Presidium examined a draft law entitling parliament to overrule edicts of Crimea's president and disband local soviets in the event of violation of the Constitution of the Republic of Crimea. The 22 June session of the Supreme Soviet of Crimea will consider five recent edicts of the president of Crimea to see if they are in line with Republic of Crimea legislation. The next plenary sitting will concern itself with the question of immediate measures to stabilize the agro-industrial complex of the Republic of Crimea.
