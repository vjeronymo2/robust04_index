Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A delegation of the Alliance of Palestinian Forces [opposed to the Arab-Israeli peace talks] extended condolences to President Hafiz al-Asad in the city of al-Qardahah, in northern Syria, yesterday, Monday, 24 January. The delegation included Ahmad Jibril, secretary general of the Popular Forces for the Liberation of Palestine-General Command [PFLP-GC]; Dr. George Habash, secretary general of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine; 'Isam al-Qadi, secretary general of the Popular Liberation War Vanguards; Colonel Abu-Musa [Sa'id Musa Muragha], secretary of the Palestine National Liberation Movement [Fatah-the Uprising]; Dr. Fathi al-Shaqaqi, secretary general of the Islamic Jihad Movement in Palestine; Dr. Talal Naji, assistant secretary general of the PFLP-GC; Fadl Shururu, secretary of the PFLP-GC; 'Umar al-Shihabi, secretary of the PFLP-GC Central Committee; Zaki al-Zayn, member of the PFLP-GC Political Bureau; Sami Qandil, command member and Lebanon secretary of the Popular Liberation War Vanguards-al-Sa'iqah Forces; Abu-'Isa and Abu-Hazim [not further identified], members of the Central Committee of the Palestine National Liberation Movement [Fatah-the Uprising]; and Brigadier General Bashir Nasir, chief of staff of the al-Sa'iqah Forces. In the name of the Alliance of Palestinian Forces, the delegation extended condolences to President Hafiz al-Asad, his esteemed family, the political and party leadership in Syria, and the fraternal Syrian people, on the death of paratrooper golden knight Staff Major Basil al-Asad.
