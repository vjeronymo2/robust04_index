BFN [Text] Presidential Council Chairman Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih last night telephoned his brother His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal, king of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, and briefed him on the current situation in our country in light of the victories that our valiant Armed Forces, the forces of unity and legitimacy are scoring in repulsing the forces of rebellion and secession that have ignited the war in the homeland to implement their secessionist scheme of fragmenting the homeland. The two sides also discussed issues of mutual interest. The brother chairman valued the pan-Arab and noble stand of the brothers in Jordan, with his Majesty King Husayn at their head in support for our people and for unity and democracy in our country.
