BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 15 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The Mongolian president is going on the top consultations starting a day ago in order to overcome the opposition arising in connection with the hunger strike and other actions for resignation of the goverment carried out by the Mongolian Democratic Association (MDA) at the central square of the Mongolian capital. Particiated in the consultations are leaders of the three parliamentary parties and the Prime Minister P. Jasray. During the meeting the sides have agreed to consider the political actions for resignation of the government on the part of the MDA as a subject under discussion at the Great State Hural and make public the social position of the parliamentary parties referring to the situation shaped down through their leaders, informs the press service of the Mongolian president.
