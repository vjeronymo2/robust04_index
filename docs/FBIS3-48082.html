Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 15 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea received messages of greetings on the new year from president and head of the state of the Republic of Mali Alpha Oumar Konare, president of the Republic of Congo Pascal Lissouba, president of the Requblic of Cote d'Ivoire Henri Konan Bedie and prime minister of the Republic of St. Lucia John George Melvin Compton. They in the messages express the hope that the friendly and cooperative relations between their countries and the DPRK will grow stronger and develop in the interests of the peoples. Messages also came to the great leader President Kim Il-song on the new year from chairman of the People's Front of Workers, Peasants and Students of Peru Genaro Ledesma Izquieta, national secretary general of the Socialist Political Action Party of Peru Gustavo Mohme Ryona, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Socialist Party of Peru Guzman Rivera Castaneda, general secretary of the New Yugoslav Communist Movement Branko Kitanovic, chairman of the National Committee of the League of Communist-Movement for Yugoslavia Dragomir Draskovic, secretary general of the Central Committee of the People's Vanguard of Costa Rica Humberto Vargas Carbonel, general secretary of the Movement for Peace and Socialism of Italy Roberto Gabriele, secretary general of the United Left Movement of Dominica Miguel Mejia and general secretary of "New Alternative" of Venezuela Guillermo Garcia Ponce.
