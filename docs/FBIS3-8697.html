Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The entire authority of Bulgarian political forces must be committed to the problems of the Bulgarians living in the Western outlands, Zhan Videnov said after today's meeting of the leadership of the Bulgarian Socialist Party [BSP] with a delegation of the Democratic Union of the Bulgarians in Yugoslavia. The information regarding the banning of the Democratic Union of Bulgarians in Yugoslavia is unofficial, but if it is confirmed the BSP will react sharply, Zhan Videnov said.
