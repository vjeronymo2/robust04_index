930831 FT 31 AUG 93 / Malaysian fine Mr Ibrahim Mohamad, a prominent Malaysian businessmen, has been fined MDollars 500,000 (Pounds 131,000) for his share-dealing on the Kuala Lumpur market this year, writes Kieran Cooke in Singapore. He pleaded guilty to illegal dealing in Union Paper Holdings, a small paper mill operator listed on the second board of the Kuala Lumpur exchange.
