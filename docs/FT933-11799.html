930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / World Trade News: Japanese cosmetics dispute Kawachiya, a Tokyo-based discount retailer, has taken Shiseido, Japan's leading cosmetics maker, to the Fair Trade Commission for denying the retailer shipments of the company's cosmetics, Emiko Terazono writes from Tokyo. In a complaint filed yesterday with the FTC, the country's anti-monopoly watchdog, the retailer claimed Shiseido's actions were intended to stop the discounting of its high-grade cosmetics. Shiseido said Kawachiya was wholesaling cosmetics, rather than selling to retail customers.
