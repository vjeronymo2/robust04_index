940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Parkdean Leisure In its first interim statement as a public company, Parkdean Leisure, which operates holiday parks in Scotland and East Anglia, announced a reduced pre-tax loss and is paying a 1.9p interim dividend. The loss for the six months to June 1 - normally a lossmaking period as it reflects winter trading - was Pounds 537,000 (Pounds 812,000), helped by a reduction in interest payments to Pounds 86,000 (Pounds 373,000) as a result of the placing in October 1993. Turnover was ahead at Pounds 2.73m (Pounds 2.52m). Losses per share were 3.6p (18.7p).
