Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Who deployed the Interior Ministry Troops on the streets of Minsk yesterday? Ivan Gerasyuk addressed this question to the Supreme Soviet chairman as he took the floor during the so-called miscellaneous portion of today's Supreme Soviet session. Stanislav Stanislavovich [Shushkevich] confirmed that the deployment had indeed taken place. In the vicinity of the Music Comedy Theater, a battalion subordinated to the commander of the Interior Ministry Troops was deployed with full combat readiness. The commander has been summoned to the legislature and will provide his explanations. Meanwhile, People's Deputy (Verkhun) focused attention on changes in the tax policy and emphasized the fact that the tax on fuel has already increased 50 times compared to last year. Deputy Trusaw again addressed the issue of the eighth television channel. Deputy [name indistinct] expressed his concerns over the fate of unemployed, elderly, and invalid citizens. Deputy Prakapovich one more time called for the adoption of temporary measures to stabilize the situation in the agricultural sector. Deputies also addressed other important issues. Following the miscellaneous period, it was announced that the session will be held behind closed doors.
