November 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TIRAMI SU ;

IT'S THE MARSCARPONE AND LADYFINGER DESSERT YOU SEE EVERYWHERE.

Buon Gusto, 3110 Main St., Santa Monica. (213) 452-4288. Style: Mainstream modern, known for remarkable desserts. Setting: Pilasters, posters, paintings and precious patio. Tirami Su: $4.

Fresco, 514 S. Brand Blvd., Glendale. (818) 247-5541. Style: Serious contender for best Valley restaurant. Setting: Courtyard with skylight and fake ivy. Tirami Su: $5.

Il Tigullio, 8771 W. Pico Blvd., Los Angeles. (213) 273-1166. Style: Haute in a hut. Setting: Shoebox with green awning out on the sidewalk. Tirami Su: $4.50.

La Pergola, 15005 Ventura Blvd., Sherman Oaks. (818) 905-8402. Style: Neighborhood Italian in the spirit of the new Northern tradition. Setting: Off-white stucco walls with plenty of plants in plastic pots. Tirami Su: $3.50.

Tuttopasta, 417 S. Hill St., Los Angeles. (213) 621-2625. Style: Stepchild of trendy Tuttobene. Setting: Pure plastic set in old Subway Terminal building. Tirami Su: $2.50. Kathie Jenkins
