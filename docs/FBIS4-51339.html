BFN [By station correspondents Ni Zedong and Wang Shuping; from the "990 Morning News" program] [Text] Yesterday [25 May] morning, Minister of Communications Huang Zhendong and other leaders concerned rushed to the site where the Shanghai Ocean Shipping Corporation (?Group's passenger liner) capsized, to inspect and give work guidance. Huang demanded that the oil spill be prevented from polluting water and the [words indistinct] plan be implemented as soon as possible. On the very night of 23 May, the (?Maritime Supervision Bureau) dispatched four patrol boats after the liner Changcheng capsized to help with [words indistinct] and evacuation of passengers, maintain smooth water traffic, and lay oil encirclement and oil absorbing felt to try to recover the spilled oil and prevent environmental pollution. It has been learned that two firefighters have heroically laid down their lives when extinguishing fire on the Changcheng liner.
