March 14, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Opening-round games featuring Loyola Marymount against Arkansas and UCLA against Iowa State in the NCAA basketball tournament will be shown live by Channel 2.

The Loyola-Arkansas game, which will also be shown live on ESPN, is scheduled for Thursday at 11:30 a.m. The UCLA-Iowa State game Friday begins at 6:30 p.m.
