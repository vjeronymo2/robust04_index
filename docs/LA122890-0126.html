December 28, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

LBJ Family to Sell Radio Stations: The family of former President Lyndon B. Johnson has put the KLBJ radio stations on the market, a company executive said.

John Barr, executive vice president of Austin, Tex.-based LBJ Co., said the family has retained Ted Hepburn Co. to entertain offers for the AM and FM stations. Barr said their value has been placed at $13.5 million.
