May 23, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

HUNTINGTON BEACH MAN KILLED IN IDAHO CRASH

A California man who died in a car accident near St. Anthony has been identified as Thomas D. Fulkerson, 33, of Huntington Beach.

Fulkerson died Saturday when his car rolled over while passing a string of vehicles on a two-lane highway a mile north of Chester.

No one else was injured.
