921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / Tougher Citizen's Charter pledge STRENGTHENING existing charters for public services rather than publishing a stream of new ones will soon become the theme of the Citi zen's Charter programme, Mr William Waldegrave, minister for public service, said last night. He said that the emphasis would shift to reviewing the 26 charters already prepared, especially once charters had been published for the courts and for further and higher edu cation.
