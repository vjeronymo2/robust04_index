Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Brasilia, 24 Mar (EFE) -- Official sources reported that Brazilian President Itamar Franco today expressed his condolences to his Mexican counterpart, Carlos Salinas de Gortari, for the death of Mexican presidential candidate Luis Donaldo Colosio. In a fax addressed to Salinas de Gortari, Franco repudiated "the absurd and shocking violence" that cost Colosio's life while he was presiding over a meeting of his campaign for Mexico's presidency for the ruling Institutional Revolutionary Party. The Brazilian president hoped Mexico can continue its effort to consolidate its economic and social development with the support of the democratic institutions.
