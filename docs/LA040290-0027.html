April 2, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: ART

Museum Director Named: Fritz A. Frauchiger, chief executive officer of Honolulu's Contemporary Museum and former curator of the ARCO Center for Visual Art in Los Angeles, has been named director of the Palm Springs Desert Museum, effective July 1. "We are extremely pleased with his California background, his art background, education and experience," said Marshall M. Gelfand, museum board president. Frauchiger, who will succeed Morton Golden, was selected through "an exhaustive search" conducted by Korn/Ferry International, the firm that has placed many prominent museum directors. CLAUDIA PUIG and GREG BRAXTON
