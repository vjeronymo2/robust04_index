BFN [Text] Vo Van Kiet, prime minister of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, today completed his official visit to Ukraine. During the visit he met Ukraine's President Leonid Kravchuk. The head of the Vietnamese Government also met Acting Prime Minister Yukhym Zvyahilskyy and Oleksandr Tkachenko, first deputy chairman of the Supreme Council of Ukraine. A package of Ukrainian-Vietnamese agreements have been signed, in particular, a consular convention and intergovernmental agreements on attracting and protecting investments. A treaty on the principles of relations and cooperation between Ukraine and the Socialist Republic of Vietnam has been initialled. The Vietnamese prime minister expressed the conviction that the arrival of the Vietnamese delegation in Kiev and the negotiations will initiate a new, important stage of cooperation on a direct interstate basis.
