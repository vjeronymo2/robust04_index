BFN [Excerpt] Kuwait, April 5 (AFP) -- Egyptian police have arrested a Kuwaiti on suspicion of helping the Islamic militants waging war on the authorities, a source close to the government here said Tuesday [5 April]. He told AFP the man had been arrested last week at Cairo airport in "possession of a large sum of money" and was "suspected of collaborating with terrorist movements in Egypt." The man was an activist of the moderate Islamic Salafiyah group, the source said, without naming him. [passage omitted]
