BFN [Text] Reports reaching us say that heavy fighting took place yesterday in the areas of Mutara, Byumba, and Ruhengeri. In the Mutara Zone, our forces inflicted heavy casualties on government forces. Some of the government soldiers surrended to our forces voluntarily. Many were taken as prisoners of war, while others went into disarray. And in Kigali, Presidential Guards fired heavy rockets at King Faysal Hospital killing 29 people and wounding 70 others. This took place on 10 April 1994, just two days ago.
