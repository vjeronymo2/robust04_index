October 14, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

EX-CABINET MINISTER IN TURKEY SEEKS PRESIDENCY

Veteran politican Fethi Celikbas plans to run for president next week in a parliamentary vote expected to pit him against Prime Minister Turgut Ozal.

"A presidential election cannot be held with just one candidate," the Hurriyet newspaper Friday quoted Celikbas, a former Cabinet minister, as saying in a reference to Ozal, who is expected to declare his candidacy soon.
