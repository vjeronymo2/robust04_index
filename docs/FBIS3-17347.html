Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday Georgia officially joined the Partnership for Peace program, thus becoming the 12th eastern European region country to express their support for the NATO initiative. The document on the partnership was signed by Georgian Foreign Minister Aleksandre Chikvaidze. Speaking at the signing ceremony, he voiced his hope for the deployment of UN troops in Abkhazia, which has declared its independence from Georgia, in order to secure the return of displaced people to their homes from the territory of Abkhazia.
