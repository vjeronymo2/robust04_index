November 7, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

Cellist Injured: Veteran cellist Zara Nelsova has postponed her Nov. 16 appearance at UCLA, and will now play in Royce Hall, April 20. According to her management, the 71-year-old cellist fell and injured her hand. She won't be able to perform for three weeks. CLAUDIA PUIG
