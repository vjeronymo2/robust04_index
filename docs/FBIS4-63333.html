BFN [Text] An Estonian Foreign Ministry delegation is on a tour to the countries in the Caucasus this week. On 17 May, Foreign Ministry Deputy Chancellor Raul Malk and Azerbaijani Deputy Foreign Minister Albert Salamnov signed an agreement on consultations between the two countries' foreign ministries. The Estonian delegation also met Azerbaijani Trade Minister Ridvan Huseynov and . discussed with him prospects for trade between the two countries. Since 18 May, the delegation has been holding talks in Georgia. During the [word indistinct] discussions at the Georgian Foreign Ministry, both countries' topical foreign policy issues were discussed including the international aspects of the Abkhaz conflict and the Russian troop withdrawal from Estonia. Georgian Foreign Minister Aleksandre Chikvaidze and Estonian Foreign Ministry Deputy Chancellor Raul Malk signed an agreement on cooperation between the two countries' foreign policy departments. Today, the Estonian delegation will be meeting Georgian leader Eduard Shevardnadze. In the afternoon, the delegation will leave Tbilisi for Yerevan where an agreement on cooperation between the foreign ministries is due to be signed.
