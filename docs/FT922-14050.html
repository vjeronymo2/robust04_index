920408 FT 08 APR 92 / UK Company News: Second half limits City Centre fall AN IMPROVEMENT in the second half helped City Centre Restaurants limit the fall in pre-tax profits to 14 per cent in 1991. The first half was affected by the Gulf war, severe weather and transport disruption. Turnover was Pounds 79.8m (Pounds 75.7m) and pre-tax profits were Pounds 9.1m (Pounds 10.6m). Interest received fell to Pounds 62,000 (Pounds 544,000), reflecting investment in new branches. However, at the end of the period cash balances exceeded Pounds 4m and were expected to grow. Earnings per share were 3.79p (4.04p). A final of 1.01p makes a total of 1.46p (1.39p).
