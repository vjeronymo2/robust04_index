BFN [From the "Latvian Press Review"] [Text] Riga, April 6 (LETA) -- The Russian-language daily "SM- SEGODNJA" published the survey of a poll. Among 918 responders asked whether the Saeima [parliament] should ratify the troop withdrawal agreement with Russia or not, 38.8 percent found it a necessity, 22.3 percent said it is possible, 13.5 percent are against it and 25.4 percent of responders failed to answer. The comments upon the poll said the results promise vague hopes for the opponents of the agreement. Besides the support of ratification of the agreements among the native population is higher than that of the Russian-speakers.
