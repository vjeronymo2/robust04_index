930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / International Company News: HK and China Gas 19% ahead HONG KONG and China Gas, Hong Kong's monopoly gas supplier controlled by the Lee family's Henderson Investment, has increased first-half net profit 19 per cent to HKDollars 560.2m (USDollars 72.7m) up from Dollars HK472.2m in 1992. Turnover was HKDollars 1.68bn, up from HKDollars 1.53bn, due to a steady increase in Towngas sales. The company is paying an interim dividend of 12 cents a share, up from 10 cents in 1992. Hong Kong Gas recently completed Phase II of its new Tai Po plant, which, according to Mr Lee Shau-kee, chairman, will provide sufficient capacity 'to meet demand until the early 2000s'.
