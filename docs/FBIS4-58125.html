BFN [Text] An official round of talks was held at the Foreign Ministry at 1500 today between the Syrian side, led by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', and the U.S. side, led by Secretary of State Warren Christopher. The talks were attended by Syria's Ambassador in Washington Walid al-Mu'allim, and U.S. Ambassador in Damascus Christopher Ross. The talks focused on bilateral relations which were assessed in light of what had been agreed on at Geneva summit between Presidents Hafiz al-Asad and Bill Clinton. Talks also dealt with some regional and international issues. Christopher arrived in Damascus at 1430 this afternoon and was received at Damascus International Airport by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' and ranking Foreign Ministry officials, the Syrian ambassador in Washington, and the U.S. ambassador in Damascus.
