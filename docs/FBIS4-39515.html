BFN [Text] Plenary sessions of Crimea's Supreme Council resumed in Simferopol today. The Supreme Council's press center reports that the question of the summary document prepared by the joint working group of the Ukrainian and Crimean Supreme Councils was raised this morning. Information was also heard on the situation on the peninsula. The session then began to examine issues on the agenda. A draft law on deputies of the Crimean autonomy was submitted for discussion.
