930126 FT 26 JAN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Labour warns on R&D spending THE UK will suffer because of the sharp fall in spending on research and development in recent years, Mr Robin Cook, the shadow trade and industry secretary, warned yesterday. Government figures showed that in 1991 the UK had lost 10 per cent of its spending and workforce in R&D. The government's contribution had fallen by 22 per cent, Mr Cook said.
