940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Hungary A committee studying Hungary's new privatisation strategy, in an effort to streamline and speed up the process, will present its proposal to the government early next month. The proposal will include the decentralisation of decision making, allowing managers of smaller companies to arrange their sale. The committee, representing members of political parties and independent experts, will also recommend combining several companies in equity funds, which would be offered for sale.
