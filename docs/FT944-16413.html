941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Scottish Asian Investment SCOTTISH ASIAN Investment: fully diluted net asset value rose 39 per cent, from 213p to 295.6p per participating share over year to July 31. Value had further risen to 316.2p by October 7. Attributable revenue for year amounted to Pounds 59,930 (Pounds 92,485 loss).
