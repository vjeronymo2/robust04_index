Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Nanjing, February 27 (XINHUA) -- A working conference on organizational work of the Communist Party of China (CPC) held last week called on party committees at all levels to make investigations and strengthen macro-guidance to help a smooth shift to the market economy. Lu Feng and Zhao Zongnai, the head and deputy head of the Organizational Department of the Central Committee of CPC addressed the conference, which was held in Wuxi in East China's Jiangsu Province. The conference, sponsored by the CPC Organizational Department, was convened to explore effective solutions in organizational work for problems appearing in the current reform drive, so as to guarantee the country's economic development and social ability. Lu said that leading bodies should have a clear understanding of their roles under present conditions and should further emancipate the mind to deepen the reform of the personnel system. He stressed that making investigations and studying and strengthening macro-guidance are fundamental to enhancing efficiency and improving work-style in the field. Lu urged leading officials to be bold in carrying out reform and to be realistic in conducting investigations and study in order to find practical solutions. He also urged them to put stress on problems waiting for immediate resolution. In his speech, Zhao hoped that leading bodies will transform the findings from investigations and study into regulations, rules and concrete measures. He reaffirmed that the main task in the field is to offer an organizational guarantee for the establishment of the market-oriented economy. At the conference, delegates from ten provinces and cities, such as Jiangsu, Shanghai and Beijing, introduced their work experience. And the conference participants also set new tasks for themselves there.
