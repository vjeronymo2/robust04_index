BFN [TA SR report: "Zuzana's Premiere"] [Text] Bratislava -- Demonstrating the forms and potential of international military cooperation and of public control of the weapons industry is the aim of the second international exhibition of military technology, IDET '94, which will be held at the Brno fair grounds from 1 to 4 June 1994. The Association of the Slovak Republic Defense Industry will display there for the first time, under the auspices of the Ministry of Defense of the Slovak Republic, a new product from the ZTS [Heavy Engineering Works] Dubnica company -- the self-propelled 155 millimeter howitzer, mounted on a Tatra 815 chassis, code-named Zuzana, which conforms to NATO equipment standards.
