920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Burnfield Pounds 1.17m in the black Burnfield lifted its profit by Pounds 550,000 in 1991, and following a big reduction in exceptional costs, turned the previous pre-tax loss of Pounds 3.2m into a profit of Pounds 1.17m. The company comprises two businesses: Isopad makes electric surface heating systems, and Budenberg produces pressure gauges, calibration equipment and thermometers. Turnover was Pounds 20.6m, against Pounds 15.3m for the previous 11 months. Exceptional costs fell to Pounds 795,000 (Pounds 4.61m) and covered the reorganisation and restructuring of Isopad in Germany and France. Earnings were 4.8p (losses 24.8p) net of the exceptional item equal to 3.2p (losses 32.3p). The final dividend is 4.1p making 5.75p (5.5p).
