Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Jan (MENA)--Major Muhammad 'Abd-al-Hamid Hasan, chief of the Baqur police station, within Abu Tij center in Asyut Governorate, Upper Egypt, and Sergeant Major 'Antar Madani Muhammad were killed this morning and conscript Muhammad Mahrus Fath-al-Bab was wounded when unknown assailants fired on them. Making the statement, a responsible Interior Ministry security source said the major and the men accompanying him were passing by when two unidentified assailants fired at them killing the major and the sergeant major and wounding the conscript, who was taken to Abu Tij Hospital for treatment. The security source added that the Asyut security forces immediately went to the scene and the area is being combed to arrest the criminals. The prosecution has been informed to start investigations.
