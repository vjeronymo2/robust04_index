920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / World Trade News: Airbus 'did not get aid for US deal' AIRBUS Industrie, the EC aircraft manufacturing consortium, did not receive government support to underpin its Dollars 5bn (Pounds 2.6bn) deal with United Airlines, said Mr Mogens Peter Carl, a European Commission trade official, said yesterday, writes Andrew Hill in Brussels. Airbus announced earlier this month that United, the second largest US carrier, had placed an order for up to 100 Airbus A320 jets, instead of turning to its usual supplier Boeing of the US. Mr Mogens Peter Carl, a European Commission trade official, said US concerns about financing the deal had held up signing of the EC-US accord on subsidies for developing large civil aircraft.
