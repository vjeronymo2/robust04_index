May 19, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO EARNINGS

TCS Enterprises: Net income rose by 123% to $533,000 for the second quarter ended March 31, up from $239,000 during the like quarter a year ago, the San Diego firm reported. Net income rose by 55%, to $538,000, for the six-month period ended March 31, up from $347,000 a year earlier. Revenue figures for the quarter and six-month periods were not available Thursday, a company spokeswoman said.
