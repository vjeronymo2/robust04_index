BFN [Text] The Department of Environment and Natural Resources [DENR] Ecosystem Research and Development Bureau has launched today a project for the trial plantation of Nilad -- a mangrove species -- that will be planted for the greening and luxuriant growth of the environment's ecosystem. This was witnessed by DENR Secretary Angel Alcala, Manila Mayor Alfredo Lim, and other government officials. The launching of the project was held in Puerta, Parian.
