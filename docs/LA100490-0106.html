"The Pajama Game" (1957), directed by Stanley Donen and George Abbott. 101 minutes.

No rating. The plot is frivolous and obvious, the acting hammy, and much of the dialogue outright dumb, but who cares? This musical, starring Doris Day and John Raitt, is buoyed by Richard Adler/Jerry Ross songs and Bob Fosse choreography. And it's good clean American fun. DOUG LIST
