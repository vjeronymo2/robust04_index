940516 FT 16 MAY 94 / Observer: Green light The new South Africa can be a dangerous place. But first-time visitor Warren Edwardes, boss of Delphi Risk Management, was quite unnerved by the response to his query about the best way from the centre of Johannesburg to suburban Rosebank. 'Turn right; straight through the first roadblock; right at the next roadblock; follow the highway - third exit - go straight through the next three roadblocks and you're there,' said the helpful receptionist. Edwardes was seriously thinking of aborting his mission until it was pointed out that the receptionist was talking about robots - South African for traffic lights.
