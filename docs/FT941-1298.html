940325 FT 25 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Cray Pounds 10.3m disposal Cray Electronics announced yesterday the sale of its defence businesses for Pounds 10.3m to QVS Industries which will trade as Basys Holdings. The cash consideration includes the repayment of intra-group borrowings of Pounds 7.35m. The sale of Cray Technology (Gosport), Cray Marine and Cray Computer Graphics previously contributed pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.5m. Cray shares closed 3p down at 171p.
