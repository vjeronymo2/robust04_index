BFN [Text] AL-MAJD has learned that a two-hour meeting was held yesterday, Sunday, between Syrian Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam and a delegation from Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement]. The Hamas delegation was led by Dr. Musa Abu-Marzuq and included Engineer Ibrahim Ghawshah and Muhammad Nazzal, whose passports were recently released, and Abu-Muhammad Mustafa, the Hamas representative in Damascus. There were no details of what took place during the meeting. The Syrian-Hamas meeting comes before an expanded meeting of the alliance of the 10 Palestinian factions that oppose the settlement, which will be held today, Monday, in the evening. The meeting will address several issues, the most important of which are the settlement process and the situation of Palestinian refugees in Lebanon.
