930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / UK Company News: BET raises Pounds 18m from disposal BET is selling Reditune of Germany and Rediffusion Music, suppliers of commercial music services, to AEI Music Network of Seattle for Pounds 18m cash. The companies reported trading profits of Pounds 1.6m in the year to March 27, before exceptional charges of Pounds 2.1m, on turnover of Pounds 16.5m. Net assets at the end of the period were Pounds 3.5m.
