940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / International Company News: IBM to test HK interactive TV IBM has agreed to participate in an interactive television trial with Hongkong Telecommunications, AP-DJ reports from New York. Mr Paul Loftus, general manager of IBM's multimedia systems division, told a Bear Stearns technology conference that IBM would provide equipment and software for the trial, including set-top boxes, servers and storage. He said the trial would begin in August or September.
