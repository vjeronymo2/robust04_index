921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / Survey of Software at Work - Autumn 1992 (48): Lotus One Source - Product Profile Supplier: Lotus Development was founded in the US in 1982 at Cambridge, Massachusetts. In 1983, Lotus Development set up a subsidiary and international headquarters in the UK. Turnover: Dollars 829m worldwide for the year ending 1991. Employees: 300 in the UK, 4,200 worldwide. Products: Lotus One Source is a range of CD-ROM based company information services, launched in 1986. Powerful, easy-to-use, Lotus software provides the facility for comprehensive analysis and research of companies, industries, markets and trends. UK Private+: provides detailed information on over 145,000 UK public and private companies. CD/Europa: provides detailed information on over 50,000 companies across 18 countries in Europe. Data sources: UK Private+: ICC Online Ltd. CD/ Europa: Dun & Bradstreet. Number of users: One Source has over 12,000 customers worldwide. Price: prices start from around Pounds 6,000 for an annual subscription and include regularly updated compact discs, a CD-ROM reader, installation, training and telephone help-desk. Hardware and operating systems: One Source runs under DOS on any IBM PC AT or compatible. User views: 'Flexibility of search, level of detail.' Another user suggested that 'accuracy of ICC as data source is sometimes questionable, also not always up to date.' Contact: Lotus Development at Staines, Middx; tel 0784 455 445.
