930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Oceana Consld Shares in the Oceana Consolidated Company rose by 34 per cent yesterday after the financial services company reported sharply higher pre-tax profits of Pounds 837,000 for the year to March 31. The advance from a previous Pounds 72,000 was achieved on sales up 26 per cent at Pounds 11.3m (Pounds 9m). Operating profit of Pounds 577,000 compared with a Pounds 96,000 deficit last time. The shares added 17p to 67p and the company has increased the dividend for the year by 50 per cent to 1.5p on earnings of 10.10p (2.31p). On sales of Pounds 11.2m (Pounds 8.98m) stockbroking contributed profits of Pounds 1.42m (Pounds 1.02m).
