941111 FT 11 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Crest Nicholson Crest Nicholson, the builder and property developer, is paying Pounds 3m cash for CE Cowen (Builders), a family owned housebuilder based in Harrogate. Crest will also take on bank borrowings of about Pounds 2.9m. The purchase price is similar to Cowen's net tangible assets at March 31. The purchase is part of Crest's plans to expand geographical coverage. Cowen's Harrogate office will become the base for the new northern region.
