December 1, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

HOLIDAY ENTERTAINMENT AT THE MALLS

Area malls gear their marketing plans around the holiday season, and for good reason -- an estimated one out of five retail dollars is spent at malls in Orange County. Most of the money is spent between Thanksgiving and New Year's Day. Following are holiday plans for the 13 major malls.
