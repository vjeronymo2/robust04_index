930519 FT 19 MAY 93 / International Company News: Casio plunges 40% CASIO Computer, the leading Japanese digital watchmaker, announced a 39.6 per cent fall, to Y11.5bn (Dollars 103.60m), in pre-tax profit for the year ended in March. It blamed the decline on the rapid appreciation of the yen in recent months. Sales rose a mere 0.3 per cent to Y326bn during the year. Sales of electronic musical instruments declined by about 10 per cent, to 37.6bn, from the previous year, while sales of electronic calculators rose about 1 per cent to Y127.3bn. Casio shares, which trade on the first section of the Tokyo Stock Exchange, lost Y10 to Y1,070 yesterday.
