EFFECTIVE DATE: February 3, 1994. [T.D. 94&hyph;12] Recordation of Trade Name: ``Superior Seedless Grape Co.'' AGENCY: U.S. Customs Service, Department of the Treasury. ACTION: Notice of recordation.

SUMMARY: On October 27, 1993, a notice of application for the recordation under section 42 of the Act of July 5, 1946, as amended (15 U.S.C. 1124), of the trade name ``Superior Seedless Grape Co.,'' was published in the Federal Register (58 FR 57894). The notice advised that before final action was taken on the application, consideration would be given to any relevant data, views, or arguments submitted in writing by any person in opposition to the recordation and received not later than December 27, 1993. No responses were received in opposition to the notice. Accordingly, as provided in §133.14, Customs Regulations (19 CFR 133.14), the name ``Superior Seedless Grape Co.,'' is recorded as the trade name used by Sun World, Inc., a corporation organized under the laws of the State of Delaware, located at P. O. Box 1028, 53&hyph;990 Enterprise Way, Coachella, California 92223. The trade name is used in connection with table grapes. EFFECTIVE DATE: February 3, 1994.
