Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 29 (XINHUA) -- A national meeting was held here today to further crack down this year on fake goods and imitations. Xu Penghang, vice minister in charge of the State Economic and Trade Commission, presided over the meeting and urged the administrative bodies to pay major attention to cracking down on phony medicines, foodstuff, export goods, agricultural production materials and building materials and trade marks, and to deal with ten major cases already under investigation. He pointed out that a safeguarding system should be established, adding that more cases of fake goods had been exposed with the joint efforts of procuratorates and local governments. Last year the procuratorates of China dealt with 3,436 cases, recovering a sum of 72.34 million yuan.
