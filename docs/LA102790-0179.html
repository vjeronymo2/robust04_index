October 27, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

WRONG ABOUT KIDS

I felt compelled to give you some feedback to your article about the New Kids on the Block.

I'm the parent of a teen-ager who dearly loves the New Kids.

As a parent, I'm glad to have a group that sings, dances and entertains in a manner that I don't have to worry about the words of their songs. It is nice to have such a refreshing young group come to the forefront of the music world.

LEONARD MOEDER

Diamond Bar
