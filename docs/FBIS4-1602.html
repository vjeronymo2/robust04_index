BFN [Text] Guangzhou, March 14 (CNS) -- Romanian President Mr. Ion Iliescu said today during his visit to Guangdong that Romania would establish a permanent presence in the province to strengthen cooperation between China and Romania. The president and his party left for Romania this afternoon after a four-day visit to Shenzhen, Zhuhai and Guangzhou. Vice Governor of Guangdong Mr. Lu Ruihua met the Romanian party last night. He gave an account of the province's recent economic development and said he welcomed Romanian investment and cooperation in the province. Mr. Iliescu said he was very glad to see Guangdong's economic achievements and that Romania planned to set up a permanent presence there to strengthen exchange and cooperation with China.
