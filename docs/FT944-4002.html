941210 FT 10 DEC 94 / CBI chief urges independent Bank The chancellor's decision this week to raise base rates reinforced the need for a fully-independent Bank of England, Mr Howard Davies, director-general of the Confederation of British Industry, said yesterday. Mr Davies noted the statement on Thursday by Mr Eddie George, the Bank governor, that politics had been irrelevant in his recommendation of the rate increase but added: 'It looks as though companies are being made to pay for a lack of political management.'
