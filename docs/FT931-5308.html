930306 FT 06 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Darby in the red and interim omitted Darby Group, a USM-quoted maker of specialist glass products, dived into the red with a pre-tax loss of Pounds 471,000 in the six months to end August 1992. This compared with a profit of Pounds 262,000 last time. Losses per share came to 1.99p (2.2p earnings) and there is no interim dividend (1.2p) - last year's final was 0.8p.
