940625 FT 25 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Arthur Shaw Pounds 0.6m in the black Arthur Shaw, the USM-quoted maker and supplier of security fittings for windows and of engineered products and services, achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 607,000 in the year to April 3, compared with losses of Pounds 99,000. The group returned to profitability at the interim stage and Mr Ian Tickler, chairman, said he looked forward to further profit growth in the current year. The group was continuing to expand its research and development programme and widen its range of products and markets. Turnover improved by 20 per cent to Pounds 20.2m (Pounds 16.8m). Earnings per share came through at 4.67p (1.58p losses) and a final dividend of 1.5p is proposed, making 2p (nil) for the year.
