931125 FT 25 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Dunedin Worldwide Dunedin Worldwide Investment Trust lifted net asset value by 40 per cent, from 603.3p to 843.5p per share, over the 12 months to October 31. Net revenue improved to Pounds 3.29m, against Pounds 3.12m restated to allow for a change of policy on income from fixed interest securities, for earnings of 9.68p (9.15p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 7.1p maintains the total at 9.5p.
