Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] SRV President Le Duc Anh today received Mongolian President Punsalmaagiyn Ochirbat and his entourage on their arrival in Hanoi. On behalf of the government and people of Vietnam, President Le Duc Anh warmly welcomed President Ochirbat, his wife, and other Mongolian guests now on a visit to Vietnam, bringing with them the age-old relations of friendship and solidarity between the two countries. President Le Duc Anh held that this visit by the delegation would contribute to strengthening understanding and further consolidating the existing relationship between the two countries in conformity with the new situation at present. On behalf of his delegation, President Ochirbat sincerely thanked President Le Duc Anh and the Vietnamese Government for having reserved a cordial and grand welcome for his delegation. He emphasized that this visit to Vietnam is a good opportunity for Mongolia to exchange experiences with Vietnam and learn from Vietnam's experience acquired during its renovation process and national construction. The president expressed his firm belief that the long-standing relations of friendship between Mongolia and Vietnam will continue to be consolidated and developed for the benefit of each respective country. President Le Duc Anh and his wife hosted a banquet this evening for President Ochirbat, his wife, and their entourage. At the banquet, together with all those in attendance, Presidents Le Duc Anh and Ochirbat proposed toasts to the increasing consolidation and growth of the long-standing relations of friendship and cooperation between the two countries and to the fine success of President Ochirbat's visit to Vietnam. During the banquet, held at the presidential palace, President Ochirbat, his wife, and other Mongolian guests were treated to a unique art performance given in honor of their delegation.
