Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Tomorrow, His Majesty King Husayn will begin a visit to the fraternal State of Qatar, during which he will hold talks with his brother His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of the State of Qatar, on the regional state of affairs, means of promoting Arab solidarity, and the latest developments in the peace process in the aftermath of the recent passage of UN Security Council Resolution 904. His Majesty King Husayn today continued his talks with his brother His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of Oman. The talks addressed bilateral ties and the Arab state of affairs. Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs, has stated that His Majesty King Husayn's visit to Oman marks a significant historical juncture and a qualitative leap in inter-Arab ties. On his talks today with the Omani minister of state for foreign affairs, al-Hasan said in a telephone interview: [Begin recording] [Correspondent Samawi] Sir, what are the most important results of the talks held between His Majesty King Husayn and His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id, if you please? [Al-Hasan] On this good occasion, I would like to reiterate the deep and distinguished Jordanian-Omani relations, which have been reinforced by His Majesty King Husayn and his brother His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id. As you know, these relations are becoming stronger with time. These relations, which are fraternal in terms of dealing and frank in terms of coordination and consultation, have become an example for other countries. King Husayn's visit to his brother Sultan Qabus falls within this framework. These relations are based on the existing fraternal relations between the two leaders and two peoples. I can say that these relations are strong, firm, and continuous. [Samawi] Sir, his majesty the king will tomorrow begin an official visit to the fraternal State of Qatar. In your view, how important is this visit? What are the implications of this visit at this point in time? [Al-Hasan] His Majesty King Husayn's visit to the State of Qatar will add a new dimension to the good and special bilateral ties. God be praised, Jordanian-Qatari ties have continued to be promoted. The good ties binding his majesty the king to his brother Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, the amir of the State of Qatar, constitute the basis and the foundation nurturing these fraternal ties. The visit by his majesty the king to the State of Qatar is part of the continuous consultations and coordination under way between the two leaders and officials of the two countries on various bilateral, Arab, regional, and international issues. The visit is also aimed at examining the prospects of enhancing and promoting these ties in all spheres. His majesty the king's visit will undoubtedly mark a true and qualitative leap in bilateral ties. Moreover, it will add an additional brick to the edifice of the bilateral ties. It will also contribute to promoting and enhancing Arab solidarity. His majesty the king has always shown an eagerness to reinforce Arab solidarity. His majesty the king has urged continuous efforts to safeguard and protect Arab solidarity, and to restore inter-Arab cohesion so that Arabs may be able to meet challenges in these difficult and critical times. [end recording]
