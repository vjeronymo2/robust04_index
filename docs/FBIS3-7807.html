Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Reports from Prishtina confirm that the Serbian Army continues its movements throughout Kosova. Some 200 Serbian soldiers, tanks, and other heavy artillery have been deployed in Kacanik city. Near Kacanik, at Hani i Elezit, a border point between Kosova and FYROM [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia], the Serbian army killed another Albanian today -- 60-year-old Selman Pajazit Daci. The Kosova Information Center reports that Selman Daci was killed by the Serbian soldiers on the Prishtina-Skopje road. Other details have not been announced so far. There are reports of concentrations of the Serbian forces at a place called Cuka e Komoranit, near Gllogovc. A total of 13 tanks, dozens of military trucks, and other heavy artillery have been stationed there.
