BFN [Text] The integration of Umkhonto we Sizwe [Spear of the Nation; MK -- ANC armed wing] into the National Defense Force is reported to be going smoothly now, and more than 3,000 members of the former armed wing of the ANC will begin their training at Wallmansthall near Pretoria on Monday. The integration of MK into the defense force will cost an estimated 132 million rands. Negotiations on the integration of APLA [Azanian People's Liberation Army] are under way at the moment.
