For a period of 90 days from the date of publication of this notice, all persons who wish to submit comments in connection with the proposed continuation of withdrawal may present their views in writing to the California State Director of the Bureau of Land Management. The authorized officer of the Bureau of Land Management will undertake such investigations as are necessary to determine the existing and potential demand for the land and its resources. A report will also be prepared for consideration by the Secretary of the Interior, the President, and the Congress, who will determine whether or not the withdrawal will be continued and, if so, for how long. The final determination on the continuation of the withdrawal will be published in the Federal Register . The existing withdrawals will continue until such final determination is made. Nancy J.
