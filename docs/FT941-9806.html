940212 FT 12 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Fall at Intereurope Technology Services Profits before tax of Intereurope Technology Services fell from Pounds 542,000 to Pounds 348,000 for the half year ended December 31. Turnover of Pounds 4.69m compared with Pounds 4.83m. Earnings declined to 4.51p (7.05p) from which a maintained interim dividend of 2p is declared. The Hampshire-based group is engaged in technical publishing and support services. Reorganisation of the documentation division will result in a Pounds 500,000 charge in the second half, of which the cash cost is expected to be Pounds 250,000.
