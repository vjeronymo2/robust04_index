Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] North Korea has not submitted its list of champions to participate in the Winter Olympics in Lillehammer, Norway before the 31 January deadline. Thus, there is a great possibility that North Korea will not participate in the Winter Olympics to be held from 12 to 27 February. Some people view that the failure to present the list of champions is related to the political situation in North Korea, while others think that North Korea simply did not meet the deadline.
