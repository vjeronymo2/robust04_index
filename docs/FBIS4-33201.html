BFN [From the "RPC Television News" newscast] [Text] President Guillermo Endara has asked the members of the Rio Group to accept Panama's full reincorporation into the forum, which expelled Panama during the former regime of Manuel Antonio Noriega. Endara explained that the conditions that led to Panama's expulsion are a thing of the past and that the country now is involved in a democratic process that became stronger with the 8 May general elections -- elections in which opposition candidate Ernesto Perez Balladares was elected. Endara's petition was expressed in a letter he sent to the Rio Group. In the letter, Endara stressed: I feel an era is ending with this last diplomatic effort I am making today. With your support, my country and people will again occupy a place of honor which, because of a lack of democracy, was suddenly snatched away from them, and which now, thank God, is being returned.
