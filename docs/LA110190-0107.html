November 1, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

FIRE BURNS SCHOOL BUILDING IN LANCASTER

Fire caused an undetermined amount of damage to a maintenance building near Antelope Valley High School in Lancaster Wednesday night, authorities said.

The building at 3rd Street East and Avenue I began burning shortly before 8 p.m and was extinguished by 8:30 p.m., said Los Angeles County Fire Department Capt. Joseph Graham.

There were no injuries and the cause of the fire was not immediately known.
