941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / World Trade News: UK export credit to Russia - Contracts & Ventures Lloyds Bank, supported by the British Export Credit Guarantee scheme, has completed its first export credit to Russia with the signing of a DM114m (Dollars 74m) loan to Vnesheconombank (The Bank for Foreign Economic Affairs) to support the export of medical equipment to Russia. The project is for the supply of radiotherapy cancer treatment equipment, with parts manufactured in the UK and assembled in Russia in former military factories. The amount of the Lloyds Bank-led syndicated loan represents 85 per cent of the value of contracts signed between Russian buyers and Philips Medical Sytems-Radiotherapy, a division of Philips Electronics UK.
