940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Assets swap for Cairn and Kelt Cairn Energy, the oil and gas exploration and production company, is expanding its onshore activities in the UK with the acquisition of Kelt UK's southern England licence interests. Consideration for the deal, which takes in a 28 per cent interest in the Humbly Grove and Herriard fields and a 18.75 per cent holding in the Horndean and Singleton fields as well as seven other licences in the area, is Pounds 4.2m cash. Kelt will also acquire most of Cairn's Yorkshire interests, including a 14.25 per cent stake inthe Ryedale gas fields. Mr Bill Gammell, Cairn chief executive, said thedeal would 'give rise to considerable economies of scale in the combined operations'.
