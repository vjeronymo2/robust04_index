Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report from PRAVDA and ITAR-TASS column: "Russian Duma at U.S. Congress] [Text] A Russian parliamentary delegation headed by State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin flew to the United States overnight on an an official visit at the invitation of Thomas Foley, speaker of the House of Representatives. It will be received by leaders of the U.S. Congress and the presidential staff. Meetings are planned with representatives of the State Department, the Defense Department, and a number of other departments.
