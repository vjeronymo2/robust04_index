BFN [From the "Vesti" program] [Text] The Ingushetian president decreed a curfew today on part of the republic. Ruslan Aushev's decree states that this has been done because of a sharp exacerbation of the situation. Eight Georgians working on Ingushetian territory were killed on 27 May. The president of Ingushetia has released Bashir Aushev, republican minister of internal affairs, of his post.
