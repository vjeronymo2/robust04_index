930528 FT 28 MAY 93 / The Cabinet Reshuffle: Mr Lamont's horoscope prediction Patric Walker predicted in the Evening Standard that yesterday's horoscope for Taurus (Mr Lamont's sign) was: Although you would like nothing better than to pack your bags and leave, you have no option now but to stand your ground and face what has to be endured with courage and conviction. In time you will see that an apparently insoluble problem has a simple and quite obvious solution.
