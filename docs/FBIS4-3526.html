BFN [Text] Hong Kong, April 20 (XINHUA) -- Portugual's Prime Minister Anibal Cavaco da Silva met with Hong Kong Governor Chris Patten at noon today in the territory. The prime minister arrived here from Macao Tuesday [19 April] afternoon. Tuesday night, he attended a reception given in his honor by 200 members of the Portuguese community of Hong Kong. He is scheduled to leave Hong Kong tonight.
