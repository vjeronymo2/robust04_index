BFN [Text] Kiev, 1 Apr -- President of Crimea Yuriy Meshkov dismissed Valeriy Astakhov, the president of the Krym Television and Radio Company, on 31 March, although, according to the Crimean Council of Ministers regulation on the Krym State Television and Radio Company, approved at the instruction of the autonomous republic's Supreme Council, "a candidate for the post of president is nominated by the Council of Ministers and confirmed by the Supreme Council of Crimea, with the consent of the Ukrainian Television and Radio Company [Ukrteleradio], for a period of five years." In this connection, Mykola Okhmakevych, the president of the Ukrainian Television and Radio Company, filed a resolute protest to Yuriy Meshkov. The Krym Television and Radio Company, stresses Mr. Okhmakevych in his statement, is a constituent part of the Ukrainian Television and Radio Company. According to the currently valid resolutions of Ukraine's Council of Ministers, Ukraine's Supreme Council and the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Crimea, decision-making on personnel issues without the involvement of the Ukrteleradio president is a gross violation. Mr. Okhmakevych suggested Yuriy Meshkov revise his decision.
