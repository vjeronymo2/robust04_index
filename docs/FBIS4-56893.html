BFN [From the "Morning Information Review" newscast] [Text] A delegation from the Inter-American Human Rights Institute [IIDH], with headquarters in Costa Rica, ended its visit to Havana having met with the National Union of Cuban Lawyers [UNJC]. The delegation was led by Roberto Cuellar, assistant director of the institution, and was received by Cuban Justice Minister Carlos Amat and Deputies Ramon de la Cruz Ochoa and Lazaro Barredo. During the meeting, an agreement was reached for cooperation between the IIDH and the UNJC on a project involving training to promote that goal.
