Language: Dutch Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Foreign Ministry Advises Against Traveling To Algeria"] [Text] The Belgian Foreign Ministry issued an advisory on Friday [25 March] that anyone intending to travel to Algeria should postpone the trip until later, unless it is of the utmost urgency. The Ministry confirmed its concern for "individual security problems in Algeria," and asked Belgians who had not yet been able to leave Algeria to "make every possible effort to guarantee their safety as much as possible and to keep in regular contact with the Belgian Embassy in Algiers."
