941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / Lottery jackpot estimate raised Camelot, the National Lottery operator, last night raised its forecast for Saturday's jackpot to Pounds 5m, Raymond Snoddy writes. Ticket sales for this week's draw now exceed Pounds 18m, compared with sales of about Pounds 20m by Wednesday last week. The Pounds 5m estimated jackpot is an increase of Pounds 1m on the figure expected at the beginning of the week. Mr David Rigg, Camelot communications director, said yesterday: 'Overall, we are seeing sales continue at a very strong level. The final level of jackpot will obviously depend on the sales between now and 7.30 on Saturday evening.'
