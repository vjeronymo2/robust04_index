BFN [Text] Romanian President Ion Iliescu and Yasir 'Arafat, chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, held talks at Cotroceni Palace this afternoon in the presence of their two delegations. In a statement to journalists after the talks, Iliescu welcomed the Palestinian leader's presence in Bucharest and the fact that the Crans Montana Forum offers the opportunity for a meeting between 'Arafat and Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres, who is due to arrive in Bucharest this evening. On this occasion the various political figures present in Bucharest will be briefed on the current stage and future prospects of the peace process, President Iliescu said. 'Arafat expressed the conviction that President Ion Iliescu will make all efforts to ensure that positive results are achieved during his talks with the head of the Israeli diplomacy. In this context, 'Arafat emphasized his determination to search for political solutions to the Middle East problems.
