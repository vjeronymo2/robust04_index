Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] President Leonid Kravchuk has had a telephone conversation with French President Francois Mitterrand, during which the two leaders discussed prospects for the development of bilateral relations and issues related to Ukraine's practical steps toward implementing the START I Treaty. Both presidents spoke in favor of dynamically developing and deepening Ukrainian-French cooperation. In particular, the two presidents agreed that an aide of the French president will come to Kiev to take part in drafting a concrete program of mutual assistance in the most important economic areas. This was reported by the press service of the Ukrainian Government and president.
