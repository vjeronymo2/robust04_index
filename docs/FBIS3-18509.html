Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Major Decrease in Exports to Italy"] [Text] The recession in Italy and the devaluation of the lira have resulted in a 14-percent decrease in Austria's exports to the neighboring country to the south. Imports only decreased by 0.6 percent. In 1993 exports were valued at 36.9 billion schillings, compared with imports of 51.2 billion schillings. Above all mass products like wood and paper were affected by the decrease in exports.
