Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beirut, March 1, IRNA -- Iran's embassy in a statement issued here Monday strongly condemned the Sunday explosion of a bomb in a Maronite church in Juniyah port in Lebanon. Explosion of the church was a criminal act with the objective to cover the massacre of Muslim Palestinians in Hebron, the statement read. The embassy's statement which was quoted in Tuesday's papers here, stressed that "the hand which shed the Muslims blood in Hebron, was the same which exploded the Maronite church." At the end of the statement, the embassy expressed sympathy with the victims families as well as the Christian Lebanese community. Ten people were killed and 60 injured on Sunday due to the explosion of a bomb at the altar of the Maronite church in Juniyah port.
