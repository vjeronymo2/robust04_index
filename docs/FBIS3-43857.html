Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] A day of high drama in the mountain kingdom of Lesotho has come to a peaceful end. Clashing factions in the Royal Lesotho Defense Force resolved their difference late this afternoon. Tension between rival factions in the armed forces erupted into an armed confrontation yesterday, and Air Force Wing Commander Patrick Majare, was seriously wounded in a skirmish.
