Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Law enforcement bodies will use the entire arsenal of special means to prevent possible mass unrest in Moscow. This was reported by Yuriy Baturin, an aide to the Russian president for national security, in an interview to ITAR-TASS. According to him, the opposition groupings rejecting the Memorandum proposed by President Boris Yeltsin are preparing to hold major rallies and demonstrations in Moscow in May. To prevent a repeat of the events of May 1993, it is envisaged to use a number of technical contact-free means, including water-cannons. Yuriy Baturin stressed that in case of public order violations, legal mechanisms will be put into effect without delay, especially against those to whom the political amnesty was extended.
