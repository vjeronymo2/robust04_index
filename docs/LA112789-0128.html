November 27, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

6 RACES CANCELED DUE TO ICY TRACK

Dangerous track conditions forced officials to cancel the last six of eight races Sunday at Finger Lakes Race Track.

Track officials said jockeys found the track conditions too dangerous to race after the first two races had been run.

An overnight rain storm left water standing on the track surface, under which the ground had frozen during a recent cold snap, officials said.

Tuesday is the last day of racing for Finger Lakes' 54-day fall meet.
