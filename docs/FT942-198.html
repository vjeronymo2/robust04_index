940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / Pounds 11m expansion at television plant Korean-owned Samsung Electronics yesterday announced a Pounds 11m expansion for its colour television manufacturing plant at Billingham, Teesside. The Department of Trade and Industry is providing Pounds 2m in Regional Selective Assistance grant for the investment, which is expected to create 240 jobs over the next three years. The plant, which employs 300 people, is Samsung's only west European television factory.
