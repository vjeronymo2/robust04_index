930628 FT 28 JUN 93 / International Company News: Fletcher sells Chilean stake FLETCHER Challenge, the New Zealand conglomerate, is selling a 49 per cent stake in its Chilean forestry and newsprint operations for USDollars 122.5m to a group of US investors as part of its debt reduction programme. It has sold NZDollars 2.2bn (USDollars 1.2bn) worth of assets since last June. The investors included several that helped form a similar joint venture last year to buy a major shareholding in Fletcher's forests in in New Zealand.
