921023 FT 23 OCT 92 / Stand-off over Palestinian talks Palestinian negotiator Elias Freij called off a planned meeting with a British minister yesterday because Britain would not allow a Palestine Liberation Organisation representative to be present, Reuter reports from London. Mr Freij, mayor of Bethlehem, said a PLO rule that the local PLO representative must attend any meeting between a Palestinian official and a government minister in Europe had prevented him from seeing Mr Douglas Hogg, junior foreign office minister.
