BFN [Text] The Press Bureau of the Romanian Army has faxed us a communique in which the Army comments on certain information that appeared in the press regarding allegations that trains with military signs carrying Russian tanks and troops have transited Romania. The Press Office of the Armed Forces mentions that the information issued by the A.M. Press, quoting National Railway Company information, does not correspond to reality. Romania was not asked and consequently did not agree to the transit of military trains belonging to the Russian Federation across its territory, the Press Bureau of the Army states.
