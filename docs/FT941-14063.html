940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Equities on the Sao Paulo exchange were some 7.4 per cent higher following heavy trading, helped by expectations that congress will pass the government's economic programme next week. The Bovespa index ended 4,766 stronger at 68,968, while turnover reached Cr145.8bn (Dollars 348.3m), the highest mark so far this year. Telebras advanced 7.1 per cent and Eletrobras was up 11 per cent.
