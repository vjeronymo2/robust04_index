BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Ministers of State Necmettin Cevheri and Bekir Sami Dace, National Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan, and Interior Minister Nahit Mentese have resigned. According to a statement issued by the Prime Ministry Press Center, the ministers submitted their resignations to Prime Minister Tansu Ciller. Assessing the election results in their letters of resignation, the ministers noted that the True Path Party has emerged victorious despite difficult conditions. The ministers added that they chose to resign to enable the prime minister to act freely in the event a change might be deemed necessary in the government. In a statement she issued, Ciller said that a change in the government is not currently considered, adding that she views the resignation of the ministers as a postelection gesture.
