February 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: PASADENA STUDENT SERVES AS CURATOR

Lisa M. Morrow of Pasadena is among the student curators of "Roman Portraiture: Images of Character and Virtue," which will be at USC Fisher Gallery March 28 to April 20. Gallery hours are noon to 5 p.m. Tuesdays through Fridays and noon to 4 p.m. Saturdays.

The student curators have researched 14 Roman portraits in marble, which are on loan from the J. Paul Getty Museum in Malibu.
