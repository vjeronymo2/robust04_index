BFN [Text] Baghdad, 24 Apr (INA) -- President Saddam Husayn has received Mustafa al-Kharrubi, personal envoy to his excellency from his brother Libyan President Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi. Col. Mustafa al-Kharrubi conveyed to his excellency the president a letter from his brother Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi dealing with relations between the two brotherly countries, as well as issues of common interest. President Saddam Husayn asserted to the Libyan envoy Iraq's firm support for fraternal Libya against hostile plans targeting it. Col. Mustafa al-Kharrubi expressed to his excellency the support of the Libyan Government and people for fraternal Iraq in its just and legitimate struggle to confront the unjust blockade with all means. He also praised the steadfastness and firmness of our Iraqi mujahid people. Col. Mustafa al-Kharrubi was accompanied by Ahmad 'Abd-al-Salam Bin-Khayyal, under secretary at the Secretariat of the General People's Congress for Foreign Affairs. The meeting was attended by Tariq 'Aziz, member of the Revolution Command Council and deputy prime minister, and 'Abd-al-Jabbar 'Umar, Foreign Ministry under secretary.
