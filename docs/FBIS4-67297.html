CSO [Text] Jaipur, March 22--The Jaisalmer district police yesterday recovered 15.5 kg of heroin worth Rs 15 crores from the sand dunes at Lodharva village, on the Indo-Pakistan border. According to the police, the heroin was packed in a Pakistani marked bag and hidden in the sand dunes. It is believed the bag was brought in a `jonga' jeep to the village. The consignment was packed in 15 small bags, bearing the Pakistan sugar factory's emblem.
