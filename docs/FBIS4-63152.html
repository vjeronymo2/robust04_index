BFN [Unattributed report under the "NEGA Reports" rubric: "Azerbaijan"] [Text] The council of Democratic Congress chairmen held a regular meeting at the head office of the Azerbaijani Democratic Party. Participants in the meeting discussed the sociopolitical situation in the Republic. The meeting adopted a plan for the peaceful settlement of the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict drawn up by Democratic Congress members, which is seen as an alternative to the Russian peace plan. In the near future the plan will be passed to the Azerbaijani National Assembly for consideration. In addition, the member parties of the Democratic Congress issued a joint statement on the signing of the Bishkek protocol by Azerbaijani parliament speaker Rasul Guliyev on 8 May.
