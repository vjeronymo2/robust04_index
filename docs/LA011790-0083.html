January 17, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

ARGENTINA TO PLAY MEXICO AT COLISEUM

Argentina, the defending World Cup soccer champion which has yet to win after two exhibition games this year, will play Mexico tonight at 8 in the Coliseum.

Most of the players who will represent Argentina in this summer's World Cup in Italy are expected to be with the team tonight, a notable exception being Diego Maradona, star of the championship team. He is under contract to Napoli in the Italian League.

Argentina earlier lost to Monaco, 2-0, and played to a scoreless tie with Guatemala.

Mexico is expected to field a young team as it builds for the future. It was disqualified from this year's World Cup for using over-age players in a junior tournament.
