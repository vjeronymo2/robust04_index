BFN [Text] Baku, June 14 (TURAN) -- The press service of Azerbaijan's Ministry of Internal Affairs issued a statement regarding the explosion in the headquarters of the Yeni Azerbaycan [New Azerbaijan] party and called it "open political terror". The Ministry of Internal Affairs said that it will take drastic measures against those who are trying to destabilize the situation in the country. The assault on the headquarters of the Yeni Azerbaycan party, whose Chairman is President Aliyev, took place on June 12 at night when the building was empty. The building was fired at from machine guns and then there was explosion inside. Several parties, including the opposition parties of the Musavat, Azerbaijan Democratic Party and the Popular [People's] Front issued a statement in which they condemned this act of terrorism at the headquarters of Yeni Azerbaycan. The parties expressed concern about the fact that such acts against political parties are taking place with alarming frequency. The opposition parties demanded the creation of normal conditions for the activities of all parties in keeping with the Constitution of the Republic of Azerbaijan.
