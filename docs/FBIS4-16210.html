BFN [Decree No. 96-1 SF of the Federation Council of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly "On the Insolvency Crisis in the Russian Federation Economy," dated Moscow, 7 April 1994 and signed by V.F. Shumeyko, chairman of the Federation Council of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly] [Text] Having heard and discussed information provided by Russian Federation Government Chairman V.S. Chernomyrdin, the Federation Council notes that the Russian Federation Government lacks an action program to resolve the insolvency crisis in the Russian Federation economy. The Federation Council decrees: 1. That the Russian Federation Government and the Central Bank of the Russian Federation be instructed to submit a program for resolving the insolvency crisis in the Russian Federation economy to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly by 15 April 1994, and that in order to overcome the crisis they should recognize the following as priority measures: Unconditional implementation of the Russian Federation President's edict No. 1662 "On Improving Payments in the Economy and Increasing Liability for Timely Settlements" dated 19 October 1993; Repayment of money owed under the Russian Federation republic budget for 1993 taking price indexation into account; Elaboration and implementation of a system for supplementing enterprises' working capital; Elaboration and implementation of a system for mutual offsetting of debts; Repayment by the Russian Federation Government of debts owed to Russian enterprises by member states of the CIS; Introduction of amendments to the existing tax system designed to stimulate material production; Introduction of state regulation of transportation tariffs and the prices of energy resources; Introduction of state wage regulation; Reduction of expenditure on the upkeep of federal organs of executive power together with a transfer of some of their powers to organs of executive power of the components of the Russian Federation. 2. That the Central Bank of the Russian Federation and the organs of state power of the components of the Russian Federation be instructed to introduce a system for holding back money earmarked for wage payments in enterprises' accounts with a view to temporarily easing social tension pending the elimination of the insolvency crisis in the Russian Federation economy. 3. That the Central Bank of the Russian Federation be instructed to reduce its interest rates for loans by the Central Bank of the Russian Federation. 4. That the Federation Council Committee for the Budget, Finance, Foreign Currency, and Credit Regulation, Money Emission, Tax Policy, and Customs Regulation and the Federation Council Committee for Questions of Economic Reform, Ownership, and Property Relations, with the involvement of interested Federation Council committees and the participation of the Russian Federation Government and the Central Bank of the Russian Federation be tasked with preparing a package of draft laws on urgent measures to tackle the insolvency crisis in the Russian Federation economy. [Signed] V.F. Shumeyko, Chairman of the Federation Council of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly [dated] Moscow, 7 April 1994 No. 96-1 SF
