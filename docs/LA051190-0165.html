May 11, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT / COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE: FOCUS: BACK BAY COURT

Champion Development Co. has broken ground on Back Bay Court, a 24-unit specialty shopping center in Newport Beach.

Scheduled to open in the fall, the $13-million center will include retail stores, service businesses and restaurants. About 15% of the center's space has been leased.
