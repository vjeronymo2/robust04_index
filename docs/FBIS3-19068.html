Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] The Greek Cypriot side and the United Nations believe that the talks have entered their most serious and decisive phase. According to Cyprus Broadcasting Corporation information, the 10-page draft ideas document satisfies the Greek Cypriot side regarding access to Famagusta and the timetable for implementation. According to the same information, after studying the proposals and suggestions included in the draft ideas document, the Greek Cypriot side is ready to continue with the same goodwill the discussion within the framework of the proximity talks to reach an agreement.
