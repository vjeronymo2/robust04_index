October 22, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Irish-bred Go And Go defeated favored Robyn Dancer by a head to win the $300,000 Grade II Laurel Futurity at Laurel (Md.) Race Course.

Go And Go, running on dirt for the first time in five races, covered the 1 1/16 miles in 1:44 to gain his second stakes race and his third victory.
