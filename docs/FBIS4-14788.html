BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 470] [Text] Vilnius, April 1, ELTA -- Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas received a letter from Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk on Friday [1 April], presidential spokesman told ELTA. In the letter Kravchuk expressed his thanks for the approval of the Ukrainian foreign policy and appreciation of Ukraine's role in achieving stability and security in Europe. This appreciation was reflected in the statement signed by the Baltic presidents in Palanga on March 25. Kravchuk believes that the Ukrainian-Lithuanian relations has great prospects and that they will be based on partnership and cooperation in order to achieve stability and security in Europe as well as in the whole world.
