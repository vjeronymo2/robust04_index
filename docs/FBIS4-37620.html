BFN [Interview with Major General Yitzhaq Mordekhay, Northern Command commander, by Salman Tawil in Haifa on 22 June -- recorded in Hebrew with Arabic subtitles; processed from the Hebrew] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] [Tawil] Would you say the Hizballah use of Sagger missiles against IDF [Israel Defense Forces] forces constitutes a new element in their combat tactics?
