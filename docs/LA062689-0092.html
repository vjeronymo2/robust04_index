June 26, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

BRITAIN FINES PERKINS OVER POT

Actor Anthony Perkins was fined $310 today for smuggling a small amount of marijuana into Britain.

Perkins, 57, mailed 1.32 grams of marijuana with a street value of about $7 from his Los Angeles home to coincide with his arrival at a hotel in Wales where he was filming, the court was told.

Hotel staff, however, delivered the package to someone else named Perkins who opened it to find a small, aluminum-foil packet containing enough drugs for three or four cannabis cigarettes.

The American actor was arrested by two detectives when he checked in Sunday.
