930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / Arrests for Thai factory fire Thai police issued arrest warrants for four executives of the toy factory that burned to the ground last week killing about 200 workers, Reuter reports from Bangkok. Police said Mr Pichet Laokasem, Mr Churin Unhaphoum, Mr Chang Ming Kuang and Mr Chung Yuk Ming - charged with negligence and illegal modification of a building - faced up to 10 years in jail and a fine if found guilty.
