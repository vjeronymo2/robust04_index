930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Watts inquest opens SIR ROY Watts, the former chairman of Thames Water whose body was found in the Thames yesterday, had probably been dead for up to a week, an inquest in east London was told yesterday. Sir Roy, who had been chairman of Thames Water since 1983, disappeared a few days after being diagnosed as suffering from Parkinson's disease. The inquest was adjourned.
