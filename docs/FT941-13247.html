940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / International Company News: Demand lifts Posco profits to record Pohang Iron and Steel, the world's third largest steelmaker, reported unconsolidated net profits grew by 59 per cent to Won294.6bn (Dollars 364m) as sales rose 13 per cent to Won6,920bn, John Burton writes from Seoul. The South Korean state-controlled group said the record results were due to growing demand for steel products from the motor vehicle and construction industries. The completion of an extensive production expansion programme in 1992 had also contributed to profit growth as investment costs fell.
