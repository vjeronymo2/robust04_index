BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 18 (KCNA) -- Marshal of the Korean People's Army O Chin-u, Minister of People's Armed Forces, sent a message of greetings to Mohammad Foruzandeh, Minister of Defence and Armed Forces Logistics of the Islamic Republic of Iran, on the occasion of the 15th anniversary of Iranian Army Day. The message expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the peoples and Armies of the two countries would steadily be consolidated and developed and wished the Iranian minister bigger success in his work to increase the defence capabilities.
