BFN [Unattributed report under the "NEGA Reports" rubric: "Republic of Ingushetia"] [Text] According to a report from the press service of the permanent mission of the Republic of Ingushetia under the Russian Federation president, the meeting of working groups of the conciliation commission for Ingushetia and North Ossetia scheduled in the town of Terek (Kabardino-Balkaria) for 29 March did not take place owing to the Vladikavkaz delegation's failure to appear. The Ingush delegation returned to Nazran. Earlier, Republic of Ingushetia President Ruslan Aushev forwarded to Akhsarbek Galazov, head of the Republic of North Ossetia, a package of proposals on a settlement of relations between the two republics. Since terms for the return of Ingush refugees to North Ossetia and preparations for a meeting between the Republic's leaders early in April were supposed to form the main topic of the talks in Terek, in the Ingush side's assessment the failure of the North Ossetia delegation to come to the talks can be assessed as "boycotting the process of normalizing the situation in the region."
