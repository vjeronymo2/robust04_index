920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / Management (In A Nutshell): The grand plan A course aimed at helping managing directors and chief executives of companies in the Pounds 5m-Pounds 25m turnover range to develop and implement a strategic plan is to be held at Manchester Business School starting next March. The course, Fostering Advanced Management Excellence, consists of 124 hours spread over nine months and will involve business school staff helping chief executives and their boards of directors implement the plans they have produced. Contact Geoff Burton, MBS, Tel 061 275 6537. Fee Pounds 8,000. Up to nine places are available.
