BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 6 -- Mr Khamtai Siphandon, chairman of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party (LPRP) and prime minister of Laos, and Mr Nouhak Phoumsavan, president of the Laos, separately received in Vientiane on April 5 Mr Phan Van Khai, deputy prime minister and head of a delegation of the Vietnamese Government to the 16th session of the Vietnam-Laos Inter-Governmental Commission for Economic, Cultural, Scientific and Technological Cooperation held there. While receiving Deputy Prime Minister Phan Van Khai, Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon wished the special friendship and comprehensive cooperation between the two countries further development. For his part, Deputy Prime Minister Phan Van Khai highlighted the results of this session. During his reception, President Nouhak Phoumsavan reaffirmed that the Lao Government and people would do all they could to constantly consolidate and develop the relations of friendship and special fraternal solidarity between the two parties and states.
