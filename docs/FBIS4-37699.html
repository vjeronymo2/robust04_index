BFN [Text] His Royal Highness Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal, foreign affairs minister, met Alain Juppe, French foreign affairs minister, in Paris yesterday. During the meeting, the developments of the situation in Yemen were discussed, whereby the two sides affirmed the need to speed the implementation of the cease-fire in Yemen. Following the meeting, His Highness Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal stressed that Paris and Riyadh were in agreement on the priority of implementing the Security Council's resolution. His Highness added that the meeting with Juppe also dealt with Saudi-French bilateral relations. On his part, the French foreign affairs minister stressed that Saudi Arabia and France hoped that the two Yemeni sides would, at the earliest moment, adhere to the cease-fire stipulated by the UN Security Council's resolution No. 924.
