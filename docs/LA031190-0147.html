March 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BIG WEST CONFERENCE WOMEN'S TOURNAMENT

Nevada Las Vegas 91, Fresno State 71 -- Pauline Jordan scored 27 points and had 17 rebounds to lead the Rebels into the championship game.

Vicki Lander added 15, and Geannine Jordan and Janice Holliday each had 14 for the Rebels (27-2). ELLIOTT TEAFORD
