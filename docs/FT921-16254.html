920104 FT 04 JAN 92 / China recognises Uzbekistan China and newly independent Uzbekistan have established full diplomatic relations, the official New China News Agency said yesterday, Reuter reports from Beijing. It was the second member of the new Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS) to establish full ties with China. China and Russia set up ties at the ambassadorial level on December 27. At the same time China recognised the 10 other CIS republics and Georgia.
