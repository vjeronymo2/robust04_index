931110 FT 10 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Elswick sells Bomford for Pounds 7.7m Elswick is selling Bomford Turner, its grass-cutting machinery subsidiary, to the Texas-based Alamo Group for about Pounds 7.7m, to allow it to concentrate on its core print and packaging business. The proceeds - which will be realised as Pounds 5.2m in cash and the balance as interest-bearing loan notes - will eliminate debt and leave the group with pro forma net cash of Pounds 1.3m.
