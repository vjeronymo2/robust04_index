Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] Lately, the Azerbaijani Television and Radio Broadcasting Company has been broadening its ties with numerous foreign television companies. A delegation led by Andrew (Tayce), the head of the department for European broadcasts at the BBC, is currently visiting the Republic's national television company to exchange views on cooperation. Babesk Mamedov, head of the Azerbaijani Television and Radio Broadcasting Company, and (Tayce) were expected to sign a protocol of intent today on launching a cooperation program between the companies of both countries. The protocol envisages agreements on broadcasting BBC programs on the Republic's radio and Azerbaijani radio programs on BBC radio.
