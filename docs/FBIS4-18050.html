BFN [Untitled Russian Federation president's Directive No. 184-rp, signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin and dated 10 April 1994] [Text] 1. The Russian Foreign Ministry, jointly with the Russian Defense Ministry and other interested ministries and departments of the Russian Federation, is to continue its work to study the possibilities of setting up Russian Federation Armed Forces military bases or military facilities on CIS states' territory by agreement with them. 2. This directive comes into force the moment it is signed. [Signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin 10 April 1994
