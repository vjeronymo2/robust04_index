Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Rome, Dec 2 (AFP) -- Italy will aid Russia to dismantle its nuclear weapons, under an agreement signed here by Foreign Ministers Beniamino Andreatta and Andrey Kosyrev, the Italian Foreign Ministry said Thursday. The aid will be in the sectors of monitoring the environmental and health aspects and protecting the personnel carrying out the destruction of the weapons. The agreement, which will cost Italy 10 billion lire (six million dollars), has to be ratified by parliament. Kosyrev has been in Rome since Monday for the ministerial meeting of the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe (CSCE).
