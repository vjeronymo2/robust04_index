BFN [Text] Tripoli, Al Sayf [June] 27 JAMAHIRIYAH NEWS AGENCY -- Naji al-Ghatrayfi, the official spokesperson for the Egyptian Foreign Ministry described President Husni Mubarak's visit to the Great Jamahiriya demonstrated the depth of the relationship between the leader of the revolution and the Egyptian president. He said that the presence of President Husni Mubarak on the land of the Great Jamahiriyah at this time emphasises Egypt's solidarity with the great Jamahiriyah and at the same time was a [words indistinct] complete solidarity with the Libyan people against the background of the unjust measures which the Great Jamahiriyah is being subjected to through the so called Lockerbie crisis. In a statement to the JAMAHIRIYAH NEWS AGENCY's correspondent in Cairo, the spokesperson said that this visit reflects the care which the two countries sare giving to the process of co-ordination and consultation between them in the sphere of common interests in the Arab and international arena. He added that Libyan-Egyptian relations were blossoming uninterruptedly and he applauded the unique relationship between President Husni Mubarak of Egypt and his brother, Col. Mu'ammar Al-Qadhdhafi saying that their talks were not limited to simply the relationship between the two countries but also to the current Arab situation and the strengthening of joint Arab action for the benfit of the Great Jamahiriyah and Egypt but also for the entire Arab nation.
