BFN [Text] Durban March 31 SAPA -- Inkatha Freedom Party [IFP] President Mangosuthu Buthelezi on Thursday [31 March] critricised a group of visitng American congressmen for being misinformed about South Africa's political realities, SABC radio news reports. The mainly Democrats delegation is led by United States Congressman John Lewis. They will end their tour on Friday in Cape Town Mr Buthelezi said at a breakfast meeting he was grateful for past help, but asked the delegation to be more "even handed " in future. Delegation members urged the IFP to reconsider taking part in the election at the meeting. They said they hoped South Africa would not degenerate into civil war. Mr Buthelezi responded by saying the IFP would not support a "fatally flawed" constitution.
