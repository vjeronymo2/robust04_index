Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Hungarian Justice and Life Party considers that in our country human rights are violated only in that the administration of justice is slow and those who have fired the volleys are not brought in front of the judges. Therefore, Istvan Csurka, cochairman, considers the regular annual report of the U.S. State Department, which refers to shortcomings in Hungarian press freedom and the enforcement of minority rights, one-sided and biased.
