Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "NTC News" newscast] [Text] The guerrillas have tried in vain to thwart the democratic determination of hundreds of Colombians in an extensive area of Santander Department. The Efrain Pabon Front of the ELN [Army of National Liberation] kidnapped 31 [number as heard] delegates and voting officers in Carcasi, (Garcia Rovira). Meanwhile, efficient operations by the authorities have allowed voting to take place normally in the area. [Begin recording] [Reporter] The delegates and officers were traveling in five vehicles when they were intercepted by an ELN front in Carcasi Municipality, eastern Santander Department. The subversives are holding seven National Civil Registry Office delegates and 25 students who were going to (Toboson) to serve as voting officers. The guerrillas freed the driver of one vehicle, who subsequently informed the authorities. [Second Division Commander Manuel Bonnet; identified by caption] We have been helping the National Civil Registry Office and the Governor's Office since this morning. We have the material here at the brigade and are ready to transport it via helicopter. The troops are also ready to ensure that the elections are held in those areas. [Reporter] The kidnapping occurred on the afternoon of 12 March, and by that evening the National Civil Registry Office had dispatched new delegates to see that elections would be held in the place where the subversive incursion occurred. The military forces said the elections are progressing normally in that zone. [end recording]
