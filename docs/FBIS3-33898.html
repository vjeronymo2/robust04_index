Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Deputy Foreign Minister Yosi Beilin will meet next week with his Saudi Arabian and Tunisian counterparts. Our political correspondent Shlomo Raz reports that the meeting will take place when the heads of the delegations to the multilateral talks convene in Canada. At the convention, the delegates will discuss priorities in the implementation of joint projects in the Middle East, including communications networks and joint roads. The discussion of these issues will be held at Israel's initiative.
