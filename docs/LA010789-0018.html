January 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

SETTING THE STAGE FOR PEACE

In the span of two weeks:

-- The Reagan Administration finally agreed to speak with the PLO, which more than 90% of Palestinians support as their representative;

-- The federal judge in Los Angeles declared unconstitutional key provisions of the McCarthy-era McCarran-Walter Act being used in the government's attempts to deport eight resident aliens who espouse the Palestinian cause; and

-- The Times printed an article on its Op-Ed Page that is sympathetic with the Palestinians' just quest for a homeland and identity (Dec. 28).

As a Palestinian-American, I'm finally convinced: There IS a Santa Claus!

JAMIL RASHID

Torrance
