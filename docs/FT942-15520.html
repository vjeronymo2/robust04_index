940414 FT 14 APR 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Shares staged a firm recovery from a sharp fall on Tuesday. Bullion looked set to break back above the Dollars 380 an ounce level, while foreign interest was seen in mining stocks. The overall index put on 86 to 5,023, industrials 50 to 5,751 and the gold index improved 69 to 1,985. De Beers rose R4.50 to R109.50, Vaal Reefs R12 to R414 and Iscor 10 cents to R3.15.
