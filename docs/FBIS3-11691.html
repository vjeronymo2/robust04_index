Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Supreme Federal Court President Otavio Galotti criticized what he called improper government meddling in Judicial Branch accounts. When Supreme Court justices arrived for today's plenary session, they declined to talk about salaries. But before the session they held a meeting under the protection of guards. This delayed the beginning of the session for almost an hour. During the session, Galotti again read a note released on 21 March saying the Supreme Court rejects the crude and unacceptable insults against the court and its members. Galotti once again explained that salaries were converted to the URV [Real Value Unit] on 20 March because judicial branch members are paid their salaries on the 20th of each month. According to him, this measure caused no salary increase and served only to prevent losses from the conversion. Galotti harshly criticized the government decision to cancel the 10.9 percent adjustment of the salaries paid today. Attorney General Aristides Junqueira, who is attending the Supreme Court meetings, said after the session that the conversion is a 10-percent problem that is jeopardizing Brazilian institutions, and that this is unbelievable. Junqueira added that the solution is to modify the law and, to this end, representatives of the three branches of government are negotiating.
