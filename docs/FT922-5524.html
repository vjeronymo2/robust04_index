920530 FT 30 MAY 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Producer prices for spices remained unchanged as European grinders maintained hand-to-mouth buying, reports Man Producten. But since prices for some varieties were at historically low levels, buyers were showing more interest in covering forward requirements. Vietnamese cassia, broken, was quoted at USDollars 1,900 a tonne, cif, for June/July shipment, while Seychelles supplies were on offer at Dollars 1,350, cif, Europe and Madagascan cinnamom at FFr6.50 a kg, cif, for June/july shipment. Indonesian cassia prices were unchanged. Cloves, remain cheap, with spot Madagascan at Dollars 1,000 a tonne.
