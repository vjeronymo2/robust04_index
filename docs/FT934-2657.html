931213 FT 13 DEC 93 / The Business Traveller: Trouble spots - Travel Update Algeria: The British foreign office is advising against travel to Algeria in view of several recent attacks on foreigners, apparently by terrorists. A British resident and a Spanish visitor have been among those killed. Malawi: Following gunbattles between the army and armed Malawi Young Pioneers, the situation is quieter. The British foreign office is not advising against travel to Malawi, but it recommends visitors should exercise care. Lithuania: Several cases of diphtheria have recently been reported in Lithuania.
