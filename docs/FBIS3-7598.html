Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 30 KYODO -- The House of Representatives on Wednesday [30 March] approved a 50-day, 11.5 trillion yen stopgap budget for fiscal 1994 and sent it immediately to the second chamber, the House of Councillors, for further deliberations. The upper chamber is expected to pass the provisional budget on Thursday, putting it into law, coalition sources said. The stopgap budget will bridge a delay in Diet passage of the full fiscal 1994 budget of 73.08 trillion yen.
