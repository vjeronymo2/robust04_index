BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Taranov] [Text] Moscow, 22 Jun (TASS) -- The State Duma today adopted a law "On the Russian Federation's external borrowings and state credits granted to foreign states by the Russian Federation". The law defines the concepts of Russia's state external borrowings and state credits granted to foreign states by the Russian Federation. It establishes the procedure for the adoption of decisions on state external borrowing by the Russian Federation and the granting of state credits to foreign states by the Russian Federation. The law provides that international agreements of the Russian Federation on state external borrowings by Russia and the granting of state credits to foreign states by Russia are subject to ratification, if the amount of the funds being borrowed or granted under the agreement exceeds 100 million dollars.
