February 13, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

SCC PLAYS HOST TO FRESNO PACIFIC IN KEY BASKETBALL GAME TONIGHT

The Southern California College men's basketball team will play host to Fresno Pacific at 7:30 tonight in a key Golden State Athletic Conference game.

Both teams are 7-2 in the conference and trail leader Westmont by a game.

"The loser of this game is basically out of contention for the conference title," SCC Coach Bill Reynolds said. "It's a must win for both of us."
