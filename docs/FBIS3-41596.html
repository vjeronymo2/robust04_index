Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] There has been a first practical breakthrough in the multilateral talks on environmental issues. A joint Jordanian-Israeli mechanism was established at the Cairo conference in order to deal with sea pollution in al-'Aqabah and to protect the gulf's coral reefs. Our correspondent Shulamit Schmerling reports that the other delegations participating in the talks, including the World Bank, agreed to help finance the joint mechanism.
