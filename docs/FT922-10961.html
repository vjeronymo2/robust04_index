920428 FT 28 APR 92 / UK Company News: Com-Tek Resources Dollars 1.12m in the red Com-Tek Resources, the Denver-based oil and gas explorer, reported total revenue for the year to September 30 ahead 37 per cent, from Dollars 544,861 to Dollars 747,049 (Pounds 422,061). However, after taking account of costs and other expenses, the company incurred a loss of Dollars 1.12m (Dollars 118,335). Earnings came out at 3 cents per share.
