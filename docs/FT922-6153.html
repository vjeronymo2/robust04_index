920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Coca Cola adds Canada mixer Coca Cola & Schweppes Beverages has added Canada Dry to its range of mixer drinks, ending nearly 40 years during which Ginger Ale was bottled by Britvic Soft Drinks, the joint venture between Bass, Allied-Lyons, Whitbread and Pepsico. The new licensing arrangement will leave Britvic free to launch its own five flavour range of mixers in September.
