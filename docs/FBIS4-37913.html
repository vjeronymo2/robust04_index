BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' yesterday evening received a delegation from the U.S. national interests council, led by (Landrome Polling). Al-Shar' briefed the delegation on the latest developments of the peace process. He also lauded the council's position which enlightens the U.S. public opinion regarding the just Arab demands to achieve a just and comprehensive peace in the Middle East in implementation of both the UN resolutions and the land-for-peace principle.
