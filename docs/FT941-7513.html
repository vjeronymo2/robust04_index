940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Dunedin Income Net asset value per share of the Dunedin Income Growth Investment Trust stood at 784.6p at January 31, up from 645.4p six months earlier and 596.5p 12 months ago. Net revenue for the year totalled Pounds 8.7m (Pounds 6.92m) giving earnings per share of 27.39p (21.75p) and a final dividend of 17.35p is proposed, giving a 26.1p (25.4p) total.
