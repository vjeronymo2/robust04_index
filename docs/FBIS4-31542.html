BFN [Article by "r": "Chances of Possible Preelection Coalitions"] [Text] Bratislava--The FOCUS agency conducted a representative public opinion survey from 9 to 23 May 1994, in which 2,018 respondents were asked: "There is now much talk about the formation of preelection coalitions. Will you please say whether you would vote for each of the following possible coalitions?"
