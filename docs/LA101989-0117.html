October 19, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

General Manager Tom Flores and owner Ken Behring of the Seattle Seahawks have been served with subpoenas in connection with the California murder charge against former team official Mike Blatt.

"I was kind of surprised (about the subpoena) because of the fact that the only time I ever talked to him (Blatt) was for about a two-hour period way back in February," said Flores, who was interviewed by investigators about two months ago during training camp.

Behring couldn't be reached for comment.
