October 26, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: BUENA PARK;

MEMBERS SOUGHT FOR FINANCE COMMITTEE

The City Council is seeking qualified citizens to serve on the city's Finance Committee. The committee consists of residents or business people in the city who have a background in economic and financial matters.

The committee advises the council on short- and long-range financial planning and activity funding.

These positions are voluntary, and no monetary compensation is offered.

If interested, please submit an application letter to the Mayor/City Council, 6650 Beach Blvd., Buena Park, Calif. 90620.
