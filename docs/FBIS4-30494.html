BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, June 27 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The first issue of the revived newspaper "NOVOSTI MONGOLII" has appeared. The newspaper started appearing in Russian language from 1942, but in January in 1991 it was closed because of financial difficulties, well-known complications in the Mongolian-Soviet relations. Today "NOVOSTI MONGOLII" is revived rather as an economic commercial newspaper. In the new issue the majority of materials is devoted to problems of Mongolian-Russian economic relations. The newspaper publishes the interviews of the vice-premier C. Purebdorj, the ambassador of the Russian Federation to Mongolia S.S. Razov, an article by the well-known scientist-economist, Dr. P. Nergui devoted to Mongolian economy of the present day and tendencies for her development. The newspaper also publishes materials about cultural life and information for foreign tourists. On the occasion of the first issue of "NOVOSTI MONGOLII" the ceremony of presentation was held today at the House of Russian Science and Culture.
