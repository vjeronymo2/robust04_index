BFN [Text] As Interfax has learned from well-informed sources in Kiev, Leonid Kravchuk signed a decree on the representation of the Ukrainian president in Crimea. The decree is dated March 31. Valery Gorbatov, 39-year-old former president of a major agricultural firm, was appointed as the permanent representative of the Ukrainian president in Crimea. Gorbatov is a deputy of the new Ukrainian parliament. Observers in Kiev note that during the past four years Gorbatov has been working in one of the commissions of the Crimean parliament together with the present Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov.
