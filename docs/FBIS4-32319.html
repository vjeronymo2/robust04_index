BFN [Text] Petrovac, 23 Jun (TANJUG) -- Radio Velkaton of Velika Kladusa denied this morning's REUTER report that members of the Muslim army's Fifth Corps, loyal to Alija Izetbegovic, "deeply penetrated" the territory controlled by Fikret Abdic and that Abdic's "days are numbered." The radio said forces of the National Defense of the Autonomous Province of Western Bosnia [APZB] are firmly in control of all separation lines in the Cazin Krajina. This afternoon members of the Fifth Corps continued their ferocious attacks on civilian targets and positions held by APZB forces, Radio Velkaton said. According to the same source, heavy fighting is under way in the area of Pecigrad and the strategically important point Pivka. One member of the APZB army was killed in the fighting, while another three were wounded. Radio Velkaton claims that Izetbegovic's artillery units pounded the areas of Porcici, Veljkovici, and Liskovac, inflicting considerable material damage.
