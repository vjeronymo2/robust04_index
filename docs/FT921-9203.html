920213 FT 13 FEB 92 / International Capital Markets: ABN-Amro to advise Polish bank ABN-AMRO Bank of the Netherlands has signed a Dollars 3.6m agreement to provide management advice to the Industry and Commerce Bank in Krakow, one of Poland's largest state-owned banks. The two-year agreement is part of a 'twinning' scheme involving eight of Poland's largest state banks and western banks. It is financed by the World Bank through a Dollars 200m loan designed to help develop Polish banks. Midland Bank of the UK and Unibank of Denmark have signed similar agreements with two other Polish banks. ABN-Amro is already involved in Poland with an 18 per cent share in the International Bank of Poland, a new consortium bank capitalised at Dollars 20m. ABN-Amro and Australian Pacific of San Francisco have also established a small consulting company in Poland, Europe Pacific Investment.
