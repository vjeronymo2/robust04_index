BFN [Text] Vienna, June 18 (INA) -- Iraq's Oil Minister, Dr. Safa' Hadi Jawad, has reiterated Iraq's rejection of new conditions or changes on the Iraqi-Turkish accord to reoperate the Iraqi oil pipeline crossing the Turkish territories for cleaning and repairing it. In a press statement here on Friday [17 June], Dr. Jawad said that this topic was directly pertaining to the Iraqi and Turkish sides. Therefore, Turkey should begin implementing the accord without taking permission from the Security Council. The minister pointed out that both Iraq and Turkey had agreed to use the revenues to settle part of Iraq's debts to Turkey and the other part to purchase foodstuffs, baby milk and medicine from Turkey.
