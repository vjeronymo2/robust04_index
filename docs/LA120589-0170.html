December 5, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Art Fraud -- There have been no allegations of fraudulent prints or paintings being sold by the Triangle Gallery of Beverly Hills, as erroneously reported in Monday's Calendar. Stanley R. Lerner, owner of the Triangle Gallery, is a major witness against Lee Sonnier, former manager of the Rodeo Drive branch of the Upstairs Gallery in Beverly Hills, and Frank De Marigny of Manhattan Beach. Sonnier and De Marigny are both charged with conspiring to sell and distribute fraudulent artworks.
