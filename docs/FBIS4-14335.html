BFN [Text] Israel and China might cooperate on technologies to manufacture electricity, it was reported today during a tour by China's minister for electric power industry to Hadera's electric power station. Our correspondent Shulamit Schmerling reports that China is interested in selling coal to Israel, however Moshe Katz, director of the Israel Electric Corporation, told his Chinese guest that the quality of the coal he is offering is good, but too expensive.
