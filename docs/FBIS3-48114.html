Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 19 (KCNA) -- Production is on the constant increase in different sectors of the national economy in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The Songsan mine overfulfilled its January plan on all indices on January 14. Entering the new year, the Musan mining complex is topping its daily assignments by 30 percent in the production of iron headings. The Sinuiju textile mill has lifted the daily output of yarn 11 percent and textiles 12 percent up on the same period late last year by updating technical processes and operating all the equipment at full capacity. All the factories under the 727 general bureau and the Hamhung silk mill in South Hamgyong Province are topping their daily assignments by more than 20 percent in the new year.
