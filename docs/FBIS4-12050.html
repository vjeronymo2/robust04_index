BFN [Excerpt] In today's open Majles session, which was presided over by Deputy Speaker Movahhedi-Kermani, the bill for the amendment of Article 17 of the courts' appeal law, approved in Mordad 1372 [month beginning 23 July 1993], was approved after a second reading. According to a report, in a letter read out in today's open session, over 150 Majles deputies conveyed their condolences to the families and friends of the victims of the downing of an Iranian C- 130 aircraft in the Karabakh region, and described the Armenian Army as the main cause of the incident and voiced their protest over the silence of international organizations in response to this incident. In the letter, the Majles deputies called on the Armenian Government to give an explicit response on this issue and also called on our country's Foreign Ministry to pursue the matter in earnest.
