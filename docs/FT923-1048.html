920925 FT 25 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Improved margins buoy A&C Black Improved publishing margins and reduced interest charges helped A&C Black lift interim profits by 65 per cent. On turnover marginally ahead to Pounds 3.75m (Pounds 3.73m), the publisher's pre-tax line for the six months to June 30 amounted to Pounds 254,000 (Pounds 154,000). Net interest payable fell from Pounds 71,000 to Pounds 35,000. Earnings per share improved from 6.9p to 11p; the interim dividend is held at 4.25p.
