BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 26 (KCNA) -- A party workers delegation of the Communist Party of China led by Bai Keming, deputy head of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee, arrived here today. It was met at the airport by vice department director of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea, Kim Yang-kon, and a councillor of the Chinese Embassy here.
