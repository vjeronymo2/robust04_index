930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Albany Investment net assets ahead The net asset value of Albany Investment Trust stood at 139.77p per share at August 31 compared with 98.21p a year earlier and 127.75p at the trust's February 28 year-end. Net revenue for the half year fell to Pounds 208,400 (Pounds 247,500) for earnings per share of 2.08p (2.47p). The interim dividend is maintained at 1.25p.
