Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] A 25-member Thai lower house delegation led by a parliamentary [committee] chairman, Dr. Krasae Chanawong, arrived in Yangon [Rangoon] by special aircraft at 0930 today. The Thai parliamentary delegation was accompanied by the Thai ambassador, Mr. Wirasak Futrakun, and called on U Ko Ko Gyi, chairman of Union of Myanmar [Burma] Chamber of Commerce and Industry, and members at Bo Sun Pet Street, Pabedan Township, at 1100. They held talks on bilateral economic cooperation matters. Foreign Minister U Ohn Gyaw received Dr. Krasae Chanawong and the Thai delegation at Padauk Hall of the People's Park Restaurant and hosted a luncheon. The luncheon was also attended by the minister of development of border areas and national races, Lieutenant General Maung Thint; Vice Admiral Than Nyunt, health minister; and invited guests. The Thai delegation called on U Ba Htay, chairman of multiparty democratic general elections commission; members U Saw Kyar Doe, U San Maung, Saya Chai, and U Kyaw Nyunt; and Secretary U Aye Maung at the commission office at 1500. They held cordial and frank talks on improving goodwill relations.
