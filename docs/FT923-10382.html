920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: Nikkei continues fall Japanese finance ministry officials said there was 'nothing' the ministry could do to prop up stock prices as the Nikkei market average lost 330.69 to close at 15,095.99, its lowest level since April 1986. In London equities made sharp gains, inspired by Wall Street's new-found strength. The Dow Jones industrial average ended 45.12 up at 3,379.19, based on early and unofficial data.
