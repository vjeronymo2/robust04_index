BFN [Text] A ceremony was held today in Bishkek in connection with the establishment of the Kyrgyz State National Military Academy. The floor was given by Askar Kakeyev, minister of education of the Kyrgyz Republic, to Kyrgyz Republic President Askar Akayev for the inauguration. Major General Dayrakanov, director of the military school, expressed his gratitude for the Kyrgyz Government's support for the academy.
