940228 FT 28 FEB 94 / Stock Exchange director appointed Ms Christine Dann will be the new director of business operations at the London Stock Exchange from next month. This is a main board position. She will remain in charge of the information services department, which she has run since 1992, as well as the development of the new electronic trading systems. She also takes on responsibility for settlement and computer operations. The post comes about because Ms Jane Barker, who had combined the roles of finance director and chief operating officer, left last Friday to move to California.
