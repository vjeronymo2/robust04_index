940919 FT 19 SEP 94 / Company News (This Week): Hepworth - Other Companies The claypipes and refractories group is expected to report pre-tax profits of about Pounds 35.5m in the first half of 1994 on Friday. This compares with Pounds 27.5m. Analysts will be looking for the effects of stiff eastern German competition. The dividend is likely to be unchanged at 5.5p.
