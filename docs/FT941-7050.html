940226 FT 26 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Micklegate cuts deficit after stock writedown Micklegate Group, the USM-quoted industrial and commercial property developer, cut its pre-tax loss for the year to April 30 from Pounds 4.75m to Pounds 1.51m. Turnover was lower at Pounds 7.27m, against Pounds 9.79m. The result was after writing down its stock of developments by a further Pounds 1.74m (Pounds 2.28m). However, the company had been able to recover Pounds 2.07m through the settlement of two court actions. The shares were suspended in June; talks with a private company have been aborted.
