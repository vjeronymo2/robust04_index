Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] This afternoon, President Suleyman Demirel and Azerbaijani President Geydar Aliyev signed agreements and protocols envisaging cooperation between the two countries in various fields. Speaking at the signing ceremony at the Cankaya Mansion, President Demirel pointed out that the agreements which aim at developing multifaceted cooperation between the two brotherly countries and which cover all fields will also bring new life to the existing agreements. He stated that with his diagnosis and realistic assessments regarding various issues, the visiting president shed light on these issues. Demirel said: Aliyev's loyalty to the Azerbaijani ideal inspires his nation and stirs us. [Begin Demirel recording] Despite difficult conditions, Azerbaijan is forcefully trying to restructure itself as a state. It is trying to harmoniously carry out the process of its social change, and preparing the infrastructure necessary for a transition to a free market economy. Last, but not least, our Azerbaijani brothers, headed by Mr. Aliyev, are determined to establish a pluralistic, participatory, and democratic system. All these factors have led us to look to the near future together with hope. The fact that our two states are joined in the ideal of democracy will add a new power to the strong ties between our two peoples. [end recording] The Azerbaijani president said that the agreements will further advance the existing relations in all fields. After this ceremony, Azerbaijani President Aliyev gave a reception for the chiefs of foreign missions in Ankara.
