Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Georgian Internal Affairs Ministry has become a member of Interpol. By Georgian Interior Minister Shota Kviraia's order, Police Major Giorgiy Aleksidze has been appointed the head of the Georgian Bureau of Interpol National Center. Giorgiy Aleksidze was born in 1962. He graduated from Tbilisi State University. He has held the office of the head of a department in the Georgian Security Ministry. He has a perfect command of the French and English languages.
