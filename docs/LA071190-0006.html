July 11, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

L. RON HUBBARD'S CHURCH OF SCIENTOLOGY

I would like to thank The Times for the free publicity for the Church of Scientology. This advertisement must have cost several hundred thousand dollars.

I am a mechanical engineer working in a Fortune 100 company, and have been a Scientologist only a short while (just over two years). However, I have improved so much in just these few months that I wish I had at least heard of Scientology earlier in my life.

Some of my gains have been in the area of communication skills which is the backbone of everything that we do, everyday. I have also become very innovative and have filed several patents since that time. This is an ability that I did not think I had. My affinity for others has gone up tremendously. I can also see other people's point of view which makes me a very fair person indeed. I have become such a role model at work that people with four times as much experience as me come to me for advice, on not only work-related matters but also personal issues. I can think very clearly without any sidetracks. I feel great, and have never been happier in my life. I owe it all to Hubbard, his discovery of Dianetics and Scientology, and the correct application of his findings.

Your articles will have a positive impact by generating interest and curiosity among readers. Eventually they will want to know more and will stop by a Scientology organization and sign up for services.

JOSEPH ESKANDARI

Granada Hills
