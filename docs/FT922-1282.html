920623 FT 23 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Cost cutting helps Campbell Armstrong Aided by a programme of cost-cutting and stronger financial controls Campbell & Armstrong, the Manchester-based shop fitter, achieved a turnround into profits in the second half of the year to end-March. The company reported a first half pre-tax loss of Pounds 849,000 but after achieving a profit of Pounds 26,000 in the second half the loss for the year was reduced to Pounds 823,000. That compared with a loss of Pounds 344,000 last time and came from turnover up from Pounds 60.4m to Pounds 61.3m. Losses per share came out at 4.1p (1.4p). A same-again final dividend of 1p maintains the total for the year at 2p.
