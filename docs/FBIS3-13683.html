Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Arab League Secretariat has stressed its adherence to the resolutions of international legitimacy, foremost of which is UN Security Council Resolution 252 of 1968 which emphasized the legal status of the occupied Arab Jerusalem. In a statement issued yesterday, the Secretariat said any measures seeking to change the status of occupied Jerusalem are null and void. It notes that said resolution calls on Israel to immediately stop any acts that would change the status of Jerusalem.
