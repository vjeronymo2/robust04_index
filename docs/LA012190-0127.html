January 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

MALATHION CONTROVERSY

I have eradicated the Medflies from my navel orange tree in my back yard simply by using a fly swatter. A Medfly first appeared on my orange tree about three months ago.

I called the Department of Agriculture about them. They said they had traps in my area.

I then discovered that the Medfly is not as quick as a housefly. You could practically place your hand over the orange before they would fly away.

So each day I would go out and swat the flies. I must have killed about 50 to 100 or more and finally I got the last one. This took about two weeks. No more Medflies have appeared in the last three weeks.

ROBERT J. MESSER

Los Angeles
