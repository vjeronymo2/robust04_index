October 20, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

TRI-VALLEY LEAGUE

Oak Park 17, Moorpark 7 -- Quarterback Mike Stern scored all of Oak Park's points with two touchdown runs, two conversion kicks and a 20-yard field goal at Oak Park.

Oak Park (4-3, 1-1) scored on its first possession, marching 80 yards on 11 plays, with Stern going the final yard. Stern's other touchdown came on a 17-yard third-quarter run.

Moorpark (4-3, 1-1) scored its only touchdown on Toussaint Powdrill's four-yard run in the third quarter. J. SCOTT KLEIN
