October 8, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CHECK OUT B-52S

After reading Steve Hochman's review (Record Rack, Sept. 24) of the Sugarcubes latest album, may I suggest he listen to the B-52s for the "mischievous" quality he seeks?

It still amazes me that no one has heard this obvious rip-off of a sound created right here in the U.S.A. Good music doesn't have to come from an exotic-sounding place like: Reykjavik to be "brand-spanking new."

PAM TATE

Manhattan Beach
