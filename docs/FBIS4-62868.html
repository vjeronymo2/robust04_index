BFN [Text] Baku, 15 May (TURAN)--On 15 May, at midnight, the broadcasting of the independent television, BMTI, was stopped. On 14 May in the evening, the owner of the television company, President of Gunay company, the Chairman of Azerbaijan's Democratic Party of Businesspeople, Makhmud Mamedov, addressed viewers on television. He said that recently, such high-ranking officials in the country such as Rasul Guliyev, State Advisor for Humanitarian Matters, Gabil Guseinli, and the Chairman of State Television company "AzTV," Babek Guseinoglu, have been making attempts to close television company BMTI. The head of the television company called the authorities' activities illegal. "BMTI concluded an agreement with the Ministry of Communication concerning the lease of the television-channel for 25 years and paid the 1994 rent," said Mamedov. The independent television company BMTI began broadcasting in summer 1993. Other independent companies were not permitted broadcasting time for various reasons.
