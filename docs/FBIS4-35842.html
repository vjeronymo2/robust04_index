BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, Indian troops have martyred another 14 people during brutal operation. In Kupwara District alone, nine people were martyred by occupation forces. Indian soldiers rounded up more than 40 young Kashmiris during their siege and search operation and sent them to interrogation cells. In Srinagar, two bodies were recovered of people tortured to death during interrogation. Shiv Sena extremists in Kishtwar reportedly burned a Muslim woman to death. In Punch District, three people were killed in indiscriminate firing by Indian troops. In retaliation, mojahedin have stepped up their attacks on Indian military targets in the occupied valley. During these attacks many Indian soldiers were killed.
