Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 6 Feb--"We are outraged [my vozmushcheny] at the act of terrorism in Sarajevo that led to the deaths of many citizens of this city, which has suffered so much," says a statement of the Russian Federation Foreign Ministry issued today. The document stresses the need "to carry out a swift and objective investigation and to inform the world public about its findings". "Those guilty of this atrocity, whoever they are, must be severely punished", the Russian Foreign Ministry statement stresses. As already reported, about 60 people were killed and over 200 injured on Saturday when a mortar shell exploded.
