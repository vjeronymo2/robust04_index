BFN [Excerpts] The Israeli occupation forces have continued their acts of aggression in the south. From 1100 to around 1500 today, Israeli artillery continuously shelled Kafra, Yatar, Haris, Zibqin, Majdal Zun, al-Mansurah, Buyut al-Sayyid, al-Hinniyah and the outskirts of al-Qulaylah. This was accompanied by heavy machine-gun bursts and continual overflights by Israeli warplanes. A correspondent from the NATIONAL NEWS AGENCY reported that the villages of the western sector are now experiencing a state of economic, educational, and social paralysis as a result of the repeated Israeli aggression. Meanwhile, resistance groups carried out a strong attack against enemy forces near al-Ahmadiyah in Western al-Biqa'. [passage omitted] Hizballah Deputy Secretary General Shaykh Na'im Qasim stressed that the al-Ahmadiyah operation today is one in a series of attacks in the confrontation with the usurper Israeli enemy.
