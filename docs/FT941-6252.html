940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / International Company News: Ipsco shipments rise 24% Ipsco, western Canada's only basic steelmaker, ran at full capacity last year with shipments peaking at 1m tonnes, up 24 per cent from 1992. Demand was strong for sheet, oil and gas well tubing and fabricated products, but large diameter pipe business was weaker. Fourth-quarter net profit was CDollars 9m (USDollars 6m), or 39 cents a share, up from CDollars 6.1m, or 38 cents, on fewer shares outstanding a year earlier. Revenues were CDollars 168m, against CDollars 140.5m. For the year earnings were CDollars 28.7m, or CDollars 1.42, compared with CDollars 15.5m, or 98 cents.
