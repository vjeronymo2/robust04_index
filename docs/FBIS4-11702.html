BFN [Text] The prime minister has expressed the hope that enhanced cooperation between India and the United States will continue despite sharp differences in some areas of national importance. In an interview published in the latest issue of INDIA NEWS, Mr. Narasimha Rao described Indo-U.S. relations as multifaceted, complex, and replete with challenges and opportunities. He said U.S. is India's largest trading partner as well as the largest source of foreign direct investment. Referring to China, Mr. Rao said there has been steady improvement in Sino-Indian relations over the last few years. The uptrend in ties has facilitated attempts to resolve the difficult boundary question between the two countries.
