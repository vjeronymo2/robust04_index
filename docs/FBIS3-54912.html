Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] On Tuesday Belarusian Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kebich departed from Minsk for Moscow to meet with his Russian counterpart Viktor Chernomyrdin. The press service of the Belarusian premier reported to INTERFAX that the heads of the two governments intend to finally coordinate issues regarding unification of Russia's and Belarus' monetary systems. To this effect, our INTERFAX correspondent in Belarus reported that a joint statement of the two governments was expected to be voiced in late December, 1993.
