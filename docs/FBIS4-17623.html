BFN [Report by KHOVAR information agency correspondent Andrey Sidorin -- TASS] [Text] Dushanbe, 26 Apr -- The European Bank of Reconstruction and Development [EBRD] will invest in a number of economic projects in Tajikistan, such as the exploration of major deposits of minerals, hydro power engineering, and creation of joint enterprises for the processing of agricultural produce. The KHOVAR correspondent learned abut it today from Gani Dzhamalov, head of the foreign economic relations department of the Tajik Council of Ministers. This powerful international financial body has allocated 200,000 U.S. dollars to carry out a professional assessment of the republican agro-industrial complex. An agreement has also been reached to set aside currency funds for the development of small and medium businesses in Tajikistan.
