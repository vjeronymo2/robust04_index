BFN [Excerpt] Aden, 26 Jun -- A responsible source in Aden today accused the "rulers of Sanaa of violating the cease-fire they announced last night." At the same time, he called on the international community to adopt sanctions on the northern side in Yemen for continuing to violate the cease-fire. In a statement to KUNA here today, the source said that the northern forces have shelled the residential areas of al-Mansurah, Shaykh 'Uthman, and Khormaksar this morning, and pointed out the death and wounding of numerous people. The source called on the UN Security Council to adopt sanctions against Sanaa for continuing to violate the cease-fire. The northern artillery bombardment has resumed at an earlier hour this morning thereby putting an end to a cease-fire that lasted for few hours when Sanaa announced it would adhere to it from midnight last night.
