940108 FT 08 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: Changes to share price listings Shares listed in the FT are now arranged according to a revised list of industry sectors. Some securities have changed categories and some sectors have been merged, split, or given new names. A full alphabetical listing of stocks from these pages, together with the new sectors in which they can be found, is given on pages 22 and 23. The new sectors are those drawn up by the FT-SE Actuaries Classification Committee for the All-Share Index. End-1993 valuations for the index sectors are shown in this edition, under both the old classification and the new. Details, Weekend Page III
