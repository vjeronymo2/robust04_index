January 11, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: PASADENA;

CONSULTANT HIRING OKD

The hiring of a consultant to organize activities for the 20th anniversary of Earth Day, April 22, was approved Tuesday by the Board of Directors.

The consultant will be paid up to $15,000 to coordinate the city's activities with regional and national observances of the day, commemorating environmental awareness.

But the board declined to approve an additional $50,000 proposed for Earth Day activities, directing that other expenses be paid from regular budgets or from Earth Day fund-raising.
