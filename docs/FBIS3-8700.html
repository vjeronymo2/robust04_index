Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] Hungarian President Arpad Goncz has promoted Lieutenant-General Janos Deak, Hungarian Army commander and chief of staff, to the rank of colonel-general. On the occasion of the 15 March holiday, Arpad Goncz promoted 11 colonels, four of them to major general and seven of them to brigadier general. This was the first occasion that somebody was promoted to the rank of brigadier general, a rank established in the Defense Law passed in January. Defense Minister Lajos Fur and Interior Minister Imre Konya were also present at the ceremony.
