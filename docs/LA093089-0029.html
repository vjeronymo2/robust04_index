September 30, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP

Crescenta Valley 35, South Pasadena 9 -- Ryan Kadletz completed seven of nine passes for 142 yards and four touchdowns as Crescenta Valley (3-1) won handily at South Pasadena (0-4).

Kadletz completed his first four passes, two of which went for touchdowns. The senior quarterback hit Shane Cowsill for scoring passes of four and 17 yards as Crescenta Valley jumped to a 14-0 lead. Kadletz followed with a 20-yard pass to Javier Pimentel, and Pimentel added a two-yard run for a 28-0 halftime lead. JEFF RILEY
