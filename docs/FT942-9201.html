940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Chemring in New Zealand purchase Chemring Group has acquired Hutchwilco, a New Zealand supplier of lifejackets, buoyancy aids and flags, from Hargrave Holdings for NZDollars 4m (Pounds 1.5m). Hutchwilco made a pre-tax profit of NZDollars 413,000 in the year to April on turnover of NZDollars 6m. Net assets were NZDollars 2m.
