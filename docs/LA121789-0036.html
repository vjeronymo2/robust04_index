December 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

A HAVEN FOR GAY TEENS

I strongly agree that there should be some sort of program that accurately addresses teen-age sexuality. But it infuriates me that our tax dollars are being used to outright promote homosexuality. Virginia Uribe is not only a proud lesbian, but a gay rights activist as well. How can a program founded and operated by a lesbian activist be fair and unbiased? I am appalled that there is even a debate as to whether this program is converting confused individuals into proud homosexuals.

DAMON CARRON

Norwalk
