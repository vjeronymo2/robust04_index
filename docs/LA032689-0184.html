March 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

.. . CLOSING QUOTE

"It is troubling that . . . the agency charged with protecting victims of age discrimination has continued to allow over 200 age discrimination charges to die of old age."

-- Rep. Tom Lantos (D-San Mateo), chairman of the employment subcommittee of the House Government Operations Committee, commenting on the failure of the beleaguered Equal Employment Opportunity Commission to pursue cases before the statue

of limitations expires.
