920602 FT 02 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Scholl steps into herbs Scholl, the personal care products group best known for its health sandals, is stepping into the expanding field of herbal medicine. It is paying an undisclosed sum for Gerard House, a Bournemouth-based company which currently markets 30 licensed herbal medicine products. Gerard House had turnover of Pounds 1.9m last year and has net assets of Pounds 50,000.
