930113 FT 13 JAN 93 / UK Company News: M&W M&W, the food retailer and wholesaler, reported a decline from Pounds 2.15m to Pounds 2.03m in pre-tax profits for the year to September 27, due to a sharp fall in net interest receivable. Turnover rose 9.7 per cent from Pounds 66.1m to Pounds 72.5m and operating profits, including exceptional income of Pounds 35,000, from Pounds 1.82m to Pounds 1.97m. But net interest received was down from Pounds 338,000 to Pounds 60,000. Earnings per share worked through at 8.17p, against 9.21p, and the proposed final dividend of 1.5p raises the total for the year to 2.5p (2.25p).
