930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Andrews Sykes ahead 6% ANDREWS SYKES, the specialist industrial services group, reported a 6 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.2m to Pounds 1.28m for the year ended March 31 1993, despite redundancy and other exceptional costs of Pounds 844,000 (Pounds 208,000). The group showed losses of 0.5p per share (1.2p earnings) after a tax charge of Pounds 634,000 (Pounds 310,000), minorities of Pounds 70,000 and convertible preference dividends totalling Pounds 644,000. The dividend is unchanged at 4.4p, with a final of 3p. Operating margins improved from 6.4 per cent to 7.2 per cent on turnover down to Pounds 56.2m (Pounds 56.8m) including acquisitions which contributed Pounds 2.1m. Net interest payments fell by Pounds 340,000 to Pounds 1.9m.
