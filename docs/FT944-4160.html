941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Travis Perkins sells subsidiary for Pounds 11m Travis Perkins, the building materials group, has sold its Kennedys Garden Centres subsidiary for Pounds 10.8m cash in a management buy-out. A new company has been formed to make the acquisition. Mr George Bullivant, managing director of Kennedys, will be managing director of the new company, which will keep its original name. The consideration is subject to a revision of up to Pounds 1m, to be decided on completion.
