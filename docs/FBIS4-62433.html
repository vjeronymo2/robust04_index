BFN [Text] The Ukrainian Embassy in Moscow expressed gratitude to Russia and France for their active participation in the evacuation of Ukrainian citizens from Yemen. "The Russians and the French have done everything possible even risking their lives in order to ensure the security of civilians in the heart of the civil war in Yemen," the note of the Ukrainian Embassy underlined. The Ukrainian Embassy to Russia announced that some Ukrainians had found a refuge on Russian trade boats and others on the French International Legion base in Djibouti. On May 12 Ukrainian citizens arrived in Moscow by an special Aeroflot flight.
