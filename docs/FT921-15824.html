920108 FT 08 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Halma expands in US with Dollars 10m buy Halma, the Buckinghamshire-based safety and environmental technology group, is expanding in the US through the acquisition of Perma Pure Products of New Jersey. An initial cash payment of Dollars 6.05m (Pounds 3.32m), satisfied by a placing of new Halma shares, will be followed by further amounts totalling Dollars 4.2m by January 1996. In the 12 months to September 30 1991, Perma Pure achieved pre-tax profits of Dollars 220,000 on turnover of Dollars 3.24m. Asset value at acquisition was about Dollars 1m.
