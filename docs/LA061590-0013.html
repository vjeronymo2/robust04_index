June 15, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

TRIALS OF RECYCLING NOT TOO MUCH TO BEAR

Re "The Garbage That Ate Our Lives" (June 11): I find it less than cute and close to irresponsible to publish an article on the trials of making environmentally sound consumer choices at the market and the difficulties of separating recyclables.

Bottom Line: If my 2-year-old can separate the tin from the aluminum and my teen-ager can faithfully recycle the same plastic sandwich bag for days at a time, then presumably a household of college grads can do their parts as good citizens without whining about it.

SANDI SIGURDSON and STEVE McGRATH

San Luis Obispo
