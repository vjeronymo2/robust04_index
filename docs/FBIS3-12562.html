Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Higher Court Public Prosecutor Justice Munif 'Uwaydat has met Patriarch Sfayr. Following the meeting, 'Uwaydat said that investigation into the bombing at Sayyidat al-Najat Church has yielded some clues. He added: Such crimes are organized and well-planned. They are carried out in a manner that makes them hard to crack. 'Uwaydat disclosed that the investigating magistrate in the case will be named in the coming few days. Answering a question, 'Uwaydat said that similar crimes, such as the al-Balamand bombing, were solved in the past.
