September 30, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: FULLERTON;

ANTI-ABORTION GROUPS PLAN DAYLONG MEETING

Members of 12 local and national anti-abortion organizations will discuss "A New Agenda for the '90s" at the Orange County Pro Life Workshop from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. today at the Eastside Christian Church, 2505 E. Yorba Linda Blvd.

Several speakers -- among them Gregg Cunningham, a former legislative affairs official in the Justice Department during the Reagan administration -- will talk about developing anti-abortion presentations and about legal issues and church politics.

Further information may be obtained by calling (714) 952-9071.
