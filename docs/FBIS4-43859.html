BFN [Text] Ankara, June 21 (A.A.) -- Ankara State Security Court Chief Prosecutor Nusret Demiral said on Tuesday [21 June] that former Democracy Party (DEP) members Selim Sadak and Sedat Yurtdas will be taken into custody if they leave the Parliament. Demiral told reporters that eight former DEP deputies will not be judged at the same time with other DEP members who were earlier detained adding that another indictment will be prepared for these eight. Demiral said they will seek the death penalty for these eight according to the Constitutional Article 125 as they earlier did to five DEP and one independent deputies who were earlier detained. Article 125 says `an attempt to endanger the territorial integrity and future of the state will be punishable by death.'
