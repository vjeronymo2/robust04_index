Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] A previously unknown movement, the Rebels Movement [Tnu'at Hamordim], an extreme Jewish movement whose members apparently belong to or are affiliated in some way with the Kakh Movement, called IDF Radio's news studio a short time ago and assumed responsibility for the massacre in Hebron. The anonymous caller said that the Rebels Movement assumes responsibility for the massacre committed to avenge the slaying of Rabbi Me'ir Kahana, the late leader of the Kakh Movement.
