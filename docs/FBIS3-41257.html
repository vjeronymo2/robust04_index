Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] After three months of extensive searching, the various departments of the general administration for narcotics, in cooperation with security officers from Bani Suwayf and al-Minya, succeeded in seizing 20 kilograms of heroin amounting to 25 million pounds. An Egyptian belonging to the international mafia gangs was trying to smuggle the heroin to flood the governorates with the white poison. The car is a public sector car registered to an iron-casting company in Aswan. The operation to seize the vehicle took place after tracking and pursuing them for over 750 kilometers starting from Idfu in Aswan up to Cairo.
