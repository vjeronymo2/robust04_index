BFN [Report by UKRINFORM for TASS] [Text] Kiev 19 May TASS -- Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk and Russian President Boris Yeltsin had a telephone conversation on Thursday [19 May]. The Ukrainian president's press service reported that the presidents had a frank exchange of opinions on speeding up the implementation of the Ukrainian-Russian arrangements reached at the Moscow meeting on April 15. They agreed that the problems should be discussed in the next few days at the level of heads of governments for the subsequent signing of the documents by the Ukrainian and Russian presidents. Kravchuk and Yeltsin also touched upon the situation in the Crimea. Kravchuk spelled out his position on settling the Crimean problem in the framework of the constitutional regime. He stressed that Ukraine would take appropriate measures to preserve territorial integrity of the state.
