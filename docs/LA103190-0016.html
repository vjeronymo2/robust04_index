October 31, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

DIGEST: CSUN'S WALLACE HONORED BY WFC

Ken Wallace of Cal State Northridge has been selected defensive player of the week in the Western Football Conference. Wallace, a 6-foot-3, 240-pound senior, had a game-high 13 tackles, including 5 1/2 sacks, in CSUN's 10-7 win over Santa Clara last week.
