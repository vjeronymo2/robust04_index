BFN [Text] The three terrorists killed in Hebron approximately 10 days ago were involved in the murders of several Israelis in the territories. An IDF [Israel Defense Forces] spokesman reported that ballistic examinations of their weapons prove that the three men participated in the murders of Efrayim Ayubi, Shalom and Mordekhay Lapid, Me'ir Mendelovich, and Eliyahu Levin. They also participated in other terrorist attacks in which three Israeli citizens and six soldiers were wounded. An Arab youth was captured this afternoon at the entrance to the Cave of the Patriarchs in possession of a knife, a switchblade knife, and a leaflet calling for revenge against the Jews. Our correspondent Aviv Bushinsky reports that the youth confessed he had planned to stab Jews. Last night, the security forces discovered an arms cache containing a Kalashnikov rifle, a handgun, and a large quantity of ammunition, in Gaza city, near the home of a local resident, who is a Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] activist. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the man was detained for questioning.
