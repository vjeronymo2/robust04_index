BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 3 (KCNA) -- Minister of Foreign Affairs Kim Yong-nam sent a message of greetings to Boumber Alassounouma upon his appointment as minister of foreign affairs and cooperation of the Republic of Togo. Expressing the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries will continue to develop favorably, the message wished the Togolese minister success in his responsible work.
