940304 FT 04 MAR 94 / International Company News: Ardagh at IPounds 2.24m in difficult markets Ardagh, the Dublin-based glass products manufacturer, reported pre-tax profits of IPounds 2.24m (Pounds 2.14m) for the 26 weeks to December 28, against IPounds 898,000 struck after rationalisation costs of IPounds 1.17m. The company said the result was satisfactory in difficult trading. Turnover increased from IPounds 16.6m to IPounds 17.4m. Interest received fell to IPounds 242,000 (IPounds 592,000) resulting from lower interest rates after the devaluation of the punt. Earnings per share were 5.79p (2.05p) and the interim dividend rises to 0.76p (0.725p).
