BFN [Text] Asyut, 19 Apr (MENA) -- Four members of terrorist groups have declared their repentance and decided to return to the right path after having been misled by extremist leaders. They declared their acceptance of the judicial verdicts in the cases in which they were implicated. Stating this, Asyut Governor Samih al-Sa'id added that the four extremists surrendered to the popular departments in the governorate today of their own volition and were handed over to the competent security agencies.
