940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / International Company News: Burns Philp in stake disposal Burns Philp, the Australian food and hardware company which has been shedding non-core assets, yesterday announced that it had sold its 50 per cent interest in Five Star Shipping, which provides agency services for the Chinese national shipping line in Australia, to China Ocean Shipping Company, its joint venture partner, writes Nikki Tait. The sale, for an undisclosed sum, gives Cosco a 100 per cent interest in the business. Burns said that the sale, along with other recent disposals had brought in cash of ADollars 60m (USDollars 42m) so far in the current financial year.
