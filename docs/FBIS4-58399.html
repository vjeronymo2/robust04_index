BFN [Text] Our correspondent reports that tension is prevailing between the Amal Movement and Hizballah activists in 'Aytit and neighboring villages against a background of incidents involving the two parties in the past two days. The correspondent adds that the incidents took place after Hizballah elements provoked Amal Movement elements in the village. Tension also prevails in Qa'qa'iyat al-Jisr and other villages in the al-Nabatiyah district after Amal Movement elements accused Hizballah elements of killing Amal official Ni'mah Haydar about two weeks ago.
