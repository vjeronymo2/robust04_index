BFN [Text] A ceremony has been held in Quang Ninh Province on the opening of the border gate between Vietnam and China. The Bac Luong bridge, 135 meters long, links the Vietnamese district of Mong Cai with Tunhung [as heard] Province in China. The bridge was built by both sides in 1992. People from both Vietnam and China have been waiting for the opening of the border gate and the construction of Bac Luong bridge. After the ceremony, Vietnamese and Chinese peoples paid visit to each other in a friendly, peaceful, and cooperative atmosphere.
