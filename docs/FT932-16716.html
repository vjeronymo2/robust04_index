930403 FT 03 APR 93 / Bank of Japan engages in intervention in foreign exchange market THE Bank of Japan, the country's central bank, engaged in long-awaited intervention in the foreign exchange market in Tokyo yesterday, Reuter reports from Tokyo. It persistently bought US dollars below Y114 in afternoon trading. Earlier in the day it bought a small amount of dollars at around Y113.85 through several Japanese banks and a few foreign banks, after the US currency had hit an historic low of Y113.5 in mid-morning trade.
