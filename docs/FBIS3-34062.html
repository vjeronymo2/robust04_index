Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, the infamous Golboddin and Dostam militias again attacked Kabul residential areas with missiles. A military correspondent of the Bakhtar Information Agency reported that as a result of the missile and heavy weapons attack, a number of our citizens have been martyred and a number have been wounded. The wounded were transferred to hospitals and are under treatment. Also heavy material loss has been inflicted on Kabul citizens and the public property.
