940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Cranswick buys sausage business Cranswick, the supplier of grain, feed, livestock and meat products is paying up to Pounds 1.45m for a sausage making business and factory. The vendor is Tryton Foods, a division of William Jackson & Son, and Tryton has entered a three-year supply agreement with Cranswick for its sausage needs.
