920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Harry Ramsden's ahead to Pounds 76,706 Harry Ramsden's, the USM-quoted fish and chip restaurant group, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 73,559 to Pounds 76,706 in the half year to March 29. Turnover of Pounds 728,288 (Pounds 714,631) originated from the Guiseley restaurant only. Earnings per share rose to 0.9p (0.8p) and the interim dividend is held at 1p.
