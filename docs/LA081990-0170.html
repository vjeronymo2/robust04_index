August 19, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Metalclad Corp.: The Anaheim-based insulation and asbestos-abatement company reported a second-quarter loss of $177,000, contrasted with net income of $209,000 for the year-earlier quarter.

Revenue declined 30% to $6.5 million from $9.3 million. For the six-month period, the company lost $149,174, contrasted with net income of $515,488 for the same 1989 period. Revenue dropped 26% to $12.2 million from $16.5 million.
