Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Alija Izetbegovic, the president of the Bosnia-Herzegovina presidency, today received Victor Jackovich, the American ambassador to our country. They discussed the latest horrific massacre in Sarajevo and decided that it was necessary to establish all the relevant facts about the incident as soon as possible. The American ambassador gave assurances that his country and its allies would do everything to prevent such crimes from happening again, and would take the necessary steps in that respect.
