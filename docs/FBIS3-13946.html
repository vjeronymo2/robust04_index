Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, 22 Mar (MENA)--PLO Executive Committee member Samir Ghawshah has stated that the donor states supporting Palestinian self-rule in Gaza and Jericho have agreed to move their meeting, which was scheduled to take place in Paris Thursday, to Cairo. He told a MENA coprrespondent in Tunis that a Palestinian and an Israeli delegation will meet on the sidelines of the donor states' meeting to continue yesterday's talks in Tunis between PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat and an Israeli delegation led by Amnon Shahaq, deputy chief of staff and head of the Israeli team to the Taba negotiations. Ghawshah said the Israeli delegation promised that during the Cairo meeting it will reply to some Palestinian questions on the transitional international presence in the occupied territories, the status of the settlements, and protection for Palestinians in the occupied territories.
