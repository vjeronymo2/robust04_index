920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / Steelworkers in recognition row THE Iron and Steel Trades Confederation, the steel union, which was not derecognised for more than 100 years, is facing its second derecognition contest of this year. A bitter dispute at the Spartan Redheugh steel mill in Gateshead, Tyneside, has led to the sacking of all blue-collar staff and the recruitment of non-union workers.
