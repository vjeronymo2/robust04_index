November 2, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Hahn's Muddy Encounter: Jessica Hahn, the former church secretary whose sexual encounter with television evangelist Jim Bakker started the downfall of his PTL empire, will be hosting a pay-TV special Nov. 10 that features heavy-metal rock and female mud wrestling. "It's not 'Masterpiece Theater.' It's a little crazy," Hahn says of the show, "Thunder and Mud." "I've got mud in front of me and heavy metal behind me. The girls all look great -- I felt like their grandmother." Asked if her rock and mud show is denigrating to women, Hahn, 30, says no. "It's simply fun television. We're not claiming to be brain surgeons." ALEENE MacMINN
