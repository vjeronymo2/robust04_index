920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / UK Company News: ACT reduces stake in CMR to 25.9% Allied Combined Trust has reduced its shareholding in Cahill May Roberts Group from 38.4 per cent to 25.9 per cent via a placing of 3.3m CMR shares with institutions. ACT intends to retain its remaining 6.85m shares in CMR for at least one year.
