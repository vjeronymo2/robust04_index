930410 FT 10 APR 93 / UK Company News: Bullers trims loss to Pounds 450,000 Losses at Bullers, the manufacturer and marketer of giftware, fine art and decorative accessory products, were trimmed to Pounds 450,000 pre-tax for the half year to December 31. That compared with Pounds 497,000 for the same period of the previous year after stripping out losses of Pounds 838,000 for discontinued operations. Turnover of Pounds 1.32m compared with Pounds 1.81m.
