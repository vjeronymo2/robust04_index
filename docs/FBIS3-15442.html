Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] There is a complete absence of information in Akhalkalaki and Ninotsminda Rayons [south Georgia] due to lack of electricity. Information about current processes taking place in the republic reach the population in a biased form. The area is populated by non-Georgians. Not a single tonne of coal, fuel, or firewood has reached the population of the Javakheti region for a year, which is known for a severe climate. Neither are they getting gas. In addition, highways leading to most of the villages have been cut off due to heavy snow.
