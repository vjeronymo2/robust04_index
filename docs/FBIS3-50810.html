Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "This Morning" newscast] [Text] A large number of military activities have occurred in the northern part of the country between 22 December 1993 and 5 January 1994, according to the Nicaraguan Army's public relations office. Eleven clashes occurred between 22 December and 5 January, between irregular groups and the EPS [Sandinist People's Army], in the northern zone of the country. The irregulars have sustained 30 casualties -- four dead, 10 injured, and 16 prisoners. All are members of different groups, both recontras and recompas. Two civilians were injured, two kidnapped, and nine killed during that time.
