BFN [Text] The question of the Republic fully joining the ruble zone was one of the important issues discussed during [recent] talks between the leaders of Tajikistan and Russia. Abdujalil Samadov, chairman of the Council of Ministers, together with a group of experts, is now preparing the relevant documents in this regard in Moscow. However, reports on [Russian] central television allegedly say that Tajikistan is introducing its own national currency. These rumors are untrue.
