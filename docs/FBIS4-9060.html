BFN [Text] Litomysl, East Bohemia, April 15 (CTK) -- Polish President Lech Walesa, in his talks with President Vaclav Havel tonight, said he was afraid of a special formula for Russia within the Partnership for Peace plan, Pavel Seifter from Havel's office told journalists. He added that Walesa said this "special formula" for Russia could be dangerous to Europe. Havel voiced a similar apprehension, though not so strong as Walesa, Seifter said. Walesa said he was satisfied with today's meeting. Reacting to Hungarian President Arpad Goncz's invitation extended to the seven Central European presidents to come to a similar meeting to Hungary next year, Walesa said that he "will have" to invite the presidents to the next meeting.
