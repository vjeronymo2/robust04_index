Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Slovak National Bank Governor Vladimir Masar and Marian Jusko, vice governor of the bank, returned from Japan today. The main aim of their trip was signing an agreement with the Exim Bank of Japan about the loan to the value of approximately $42 million. The first installment of this loan will be transferred to Slovakia as early as 9 March. It will be used for the support of small-scale and medium businesses. The first repayment of the loan has been set for 15 February 1998. Representatives of the National Bank of Slovakia also held talks with the Japanese bank about the possibility of support in financing large investment projects. They include, above all, development of railway and river transport, development of the Bratislava airport, and transportation of gas and oil. [passage omitted]
