Hu] [Text] Washington, March 22 (CNA) -- The Republic of China [ROC] enjoyed a trade surplus of US$582 million with the United States in January 1994, down US$88 million from the same period of 1993, according to US Department of Commerce statistics released Tuesday [22 March]. During the period, the ROC imported US$1.56 billion worth of products from the United States, up US$72 million from December 1993, white exports were up US$19 million to US$2.14 billion.
