930628 FT 28 JUN 93 / Observer: Bottom line Talk about commercial logic. Sicilian police claim the Mafia have taken their murderous business to the ultimate conclusion by trying to corner the market in burials. It is alleged that in the past year a Palermo Mafia family has extorted control over 1,000 plots in one of the city's graveyards, demanding more than five times the standard price for them. A dozen people have been arrested, including city hall officials.
