September 2, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

LORD LOVE A DUCK

So Louie wears green, huh? As producer and writer of the animated feature "Duck Tales," I (along with my associates) went to great lengths never to differentiate between Huey, Dewey and Louie.

What made them unique was that they were one personality in three bodies.

However, I guess there are those like Kathryn Wafer who insists on structure and order for everything. If that's the case, don't I have the right to insist Louie's the one in red?

DAVID WIEMERS

Producer, story editor, writer, voice director of "Duck Tales"

Glendale
