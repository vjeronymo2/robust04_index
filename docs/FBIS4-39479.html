BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko, Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev 7 June TASS -- Ukraine has a reliable partner in the person of North Korea on the international scene, North Korean Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam told First Deputy Ukrainian Parliament Speaker Oleksandr Tkachenko here today. In his words, North Korea fully supports Ukraine in its building of independent democratic state and expresses the assurance it will take a worthy place in the international community. The North Korean foreign minister praised the Ukrainian decision to become a non-nuclear state. He highly estimated the foreign policy of Ukraine which stands for the solution of all problems at negotiations and on the basis of international norms and thanked the Ukrainian side for support to peaceful unification of North and South Koreas. In his turn, Tkachenko estimated the visit of the North Korean delegation is "the deepening of friendship and legal basis for the development of further mutually beneficial cooperation." He thanked the foreign minister for the invitation to visit North Korea and said "the date of this visit will be coordinated in the near future."
