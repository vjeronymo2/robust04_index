Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Text] In a letter to Herman Rigelnik, chairman of the State Chamber, Prime Minister Dr. Janez Drnovsek proposed that the State Chamber should relieve Janez Jansa of his duties as minister of defense on the basis of Article 112 of the Constitution of the Republic of Slovenia and Article 19 of the Law on Government of the Republic of Slovenia. The prime minister suggests that the State Chamber appoint Jelko Kacin as minister of defense. Prime Minister Drnovsek also suggested that the release of duty and appointment be carried out as soon as possible.
