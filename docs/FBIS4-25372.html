BFN [Text] Macao, June 9 (XINHUA) -- Macao Governor General Vasco Joaquim Rocha Vieira met here this morning a visiting delegation of members of the former Macao Basic Law Drafting Committee from China's mainland. Chen Ziying, deputy director of the Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office of the State Council, told this reporter after meeting that both sides expressed their satisfaction on the friendly cooperation between the two governments of China and Portugal over Macao issue. He added, the two sides were also optimistic about the development and future of Macao. At the invitation of the Association for Promotion of the Macao Basic Law, the delegation arrived in Macao on June 6. During their stay in Macao, they attended a symposium on the role of the Macao Basic Law in safeguarding the economic development in Macao and visited Macao Airport project and several large scale infrastructure projects here. The delegation will leave Macao for Beijing this afternoon.
