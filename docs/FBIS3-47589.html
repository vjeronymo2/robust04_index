Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, January 12 (KNS-KCNA) [dateline as received] -- Societies for the Study of Kim Chong-il's Works named "Sprouts," "Mountain Azalea" and "Seed" were recently organised in Tokyo and Osaka. At the inaugural meetings, reports and speeches were made and advisers, chairmen and executives of the societies elected. Letters to the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il were adopted at the meetings.
