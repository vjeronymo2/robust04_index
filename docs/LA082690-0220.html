August 26, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

HOME RENOVATION

Schweitzer's casual disregard for the profession of architecture is glib and irresponsible.

One cannot be a professional until he or she has been recognized by the profession. I would suggest that the only rationalization for eschewing a license stems from fear and insecurity rather than from a holier-than-thou arrogance.

Stick to picking paint and wallpaper, Mr. Schweitzer, or see if you can pass the test.

JAMES LECRON

Pacific Palisades
