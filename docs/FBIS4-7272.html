BFN [Report by correspondent Pak Chong-hyon from Paris] [Text] As an International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] inspection team's visit to Pyongyang has been delayed, the North Korean Embassy in Vienna reported on 26 April: "If an IAEA inspection team does not arrive in Pyongyang by 28 April, the deadline for witnessing the replacement of fuel rods, we will have no choice but to remove the seals and replace the fuel rods." In this way, North Korea strongly hinted that it would proceed with the replacement at all costs. Yun Ho-chin, a counselor at the North Korean Embassy in Vienna, said over the telephone: "We proposed that the inspection team observe the fuel rod replacement, and the IAEA presented eight preconditions." He also said: "These conditions were presented to ask us to abide by the Nuclear Safeguards Accord. Therefore, we cannot accept them."
