Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 19 Jan (TANJUG) -- At today's session chaired by Prime Minister Radoje Kontic, the Federal Yugoslav Government adopted the text of the joint declaration on the normalization of relations between the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia [FRY] and the Republic of Croatia. The declaration also envisages the opening of the two governments' missions -- an office of the FRY in Zagreb and an office of the Republic of Croatia in Belgrade, the Federal Information Ministry has disclosed. "The Federal Government wishes to point out that the signing of this declaration does not mean prejudging any solutions, but only the willingness to resolve all disputed issues in Serbian-Croatian relations in a peaceful way," the announcement says. "The Federal Government has charged the Foreign Ministry with the task of preparing the decision on the opening of a FRY office in Zagreb and has authorized Federal Foreign Minister Vladislav Jovanovic to sign the joint declaration in Geneva."
