BFN [Text] Norway and Sweden have concluded in Brussels the texts of the agreements on the conditions of EU membership. Norway managed to get its demands on fishing concessions included in the text of the agreement. The fishing questions concerning Sweden's agreement were also solved a little while ago. And the STT [Finnish Telegraph Agency] is reporting right now that the text of the Finnish membership agreement has now also been concluded. Austria concluded its negotiations last night. The negotiations were urgent because the texts of the agreements must be presented to the European Parliament today so that the Parliament can vote on new members on 4 May.
