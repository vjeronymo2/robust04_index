Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Hanover (DPA) -- The German Employees Trade Union (DAG) has accepted the wage compromise in Lower Saxony's metal industry. In a second strike ballot about 73 percent of the 2,050 people polled accepted the pilot agreement achieved over the weekend, the DAG announced in Hanover on Thursday [10 March]. Turnout was about 91 percent. Approval by 30 percent would have been sufficient to adopt the compromise. [passage omitted]
