Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, March 17 (KPL) -- Khamtai Siphandon, president of the LPRP CC [Lao People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee] and prime minister of the Lao PDR [People's Democratic Republic], has sent a message of thanks to Kim Il-song, general secretary of the Worker's Party of Korea and president of the DPR [Democratic People's Republic] of Korea for the best wishes extended to the Lao leader on the occasion of his 70th birthday. The Lao prime minister expressed thanks for the kind and meaningful words conveyed to the Lao party, state and people, as well as President Kim Il-song's assessment of the relationship and cooperation between the two parties, states and leaders of the two countries. "With goodwill and determination of the two sides, I do hope that the friendly relations, and the cooperation between the two parties, states and people based on the same ideal would be further developed," wrote the message. The message also wished the Korean leader good heath, long life, and success in the tasks of leading Korean people in the construction of socialism and in the reunification of the Korean peninsula.
