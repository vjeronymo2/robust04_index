940817 FT 17 AUG 94 / Merger values fall sharply in quarter The value of corporate mergers and acquisitions fell sharply to Pounds 1.63bn in the second quarter, from Pounds 2.71bn in the first three months. Figures published by the Central Statistical Office yesterday showed that the number of acquisitions continued to rise, with 189 deals concluded in the second quarter, up from 182 transactions in the previous three months.
