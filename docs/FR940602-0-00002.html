DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE Farmers Home Administration 7 CFR Part 1980 RIN 0575&hyph;AB69 Business and Industrial Loan Program AGENCY: Farmers Home Administration, USDA. ACTION: Interim final rule.

SUMMARY: The Farmers Home Administration (FmHA) amends its Business and Industry guaranteed loan program regulations, which are utilized by the Rural Development Administration (RDA), to provide procedures for interest rate buydown. This action is needed to implement provisions of the Supplemental Appropriations Act of 1993. The intended effect is to provide for payment by the Government of one percentage point of interest on certain guaranteed loans in areas affected by Hurricanes Andrew and Iniki and Typhoon Omar, thereby reducing the effective rate of interest to be paid by the borrower.
