BFN [Text] Talal al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs, has received at the Foreign Ministry the delegation accompanying his excellency the Austrian President in the presence of some officials from concerned ministries. The Austrian delegation includes representatives of the Ministries of Economy, Transport, and Foreign Affairs, as well as representatives of the private, tourism, and energy sectors. During the meeting, al-Hasan clarified Jordan's pivotal role in the peace process. He explained the economic difficulties Jordan is suffering as a result of the siege on the Port of al-'Aqabah and the grave annual losses it suffered despite its commitment to implementing resolutions of international legitimacy. For their part, members of the Austrian delegation praised distinguished relations between the two friendly countries. They also expressed their appreciation of the democratic life Jordan is enjoying which is represented by entrenching the state of law and institutions and respecting human rights. Four joint committees held separate meetings to discuss prospects of reinforcing bilateral cooperation in the fields of transport, technology, tourism, and energy.
