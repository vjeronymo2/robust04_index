Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Higher Jordanian-Palestinian Committee concluded its meetings at the Foreign Ministry today and issued a joint statement on these meetings. Details are in the following report from our correspondent for political affairs, Shakir Maharimah: [Begin recording] [passage omitted] [Maharimah] Information Minister Dr. Jawad al-'Anani, a member of the Jordanian team, responded to questions from journalists on the committee meetings. Responding to a question on a Jerusalem committee, al-'Anani said: [Al-'Anani] We had to confront what is taking place in Jerusalem and to discuss the Palestinian side's ability to negotiate it. Therefore, we agreed that the issue of Jerusalem is a major and sensitive one. It deserves a specialized committee to follow up all aspects of this subject and to try to prevent what is taking place inside the city, such as the erosion of property inside the walls and the growing settlement process in and around it.
