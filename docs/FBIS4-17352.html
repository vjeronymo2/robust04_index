BFN [Text] A new public organization has been established in Russia today, the Left-Wing Russia Party [Levaya Rossiya Partiya]. Steering Committee Chairman Vladimir (Burlyugov) said the party's main objectives were the establishment of a single multi-ethnic state, the renaissance of patriotism, and an international society of all working people. He described the actions that led to the collapse of the USSR as anti-constitutional. According to the organizers, the new party unites 1,000 members; there were 27 delegates at the congress.
