BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 19 (KCNA) -- Vice-President Yi Chong-ok today met and had a talk with the visiting delegation of the American Committee on Korea led by its Executive Director John M. Swomley at the Mansudae Assembly hall. The head of the delegation said Korea was built wonderfully in a short period after the war and that there are many monumental architectures in Korea. He also said he got deep impressions from the happy laughter of children and the diligence of the Korean people and their efforts for world peace.
