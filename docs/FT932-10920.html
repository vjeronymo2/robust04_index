930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / International Company News: Gains expected at Clark Oil HORSHAM Corp said yesterday a strengthening US economy would improve margins at its wholly-owned Clark Oil & Refining subsidiary this year. In addition, retail volumes were showing gains monthly. Horsham, a holding company controlled by Canadian entrepreneur Mr Peter Munk, owns 20 per cent of American Barrick Resources. First-quarter net profit was USDollars 6.8m, including special items, or 8 cents a share, against Dollars 8.2m, or 9 cents, a year earlier. Revenues were Dollars 591m, against Dollars 502m last time. Clark Oil, a US refiner and marketer, suffered a small loss, while the contribution from American Barrick doubled to Dollars 9m.
