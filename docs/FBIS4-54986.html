BFN [Text] Knin, 7 May (TANJUG) -- Discussing the situation around Brcko, the Supreme Defense Council of the Republic of Serb Krajina [RSK] said in Knin today that the defense of the corridor was vital for the Serbian nation west of the Drina River. In this context, the council has sent its full support to the people and the leadership of the republic of Srpska making it clear that the RSK was prepared to help in the corridor's defense, as it did when the corridor was established. The RSK Supreme Defense Council expressed support for the efforts to prevent clashes near Brcko by deploying UN monitors, pointing out that the corridor has to remain an integral part of the Serb ethnic territory because of its importance, and that this was not negotiable.
