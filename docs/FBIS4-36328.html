BFN [Text] Mr. Muhammad al-Amin Khalifah, the chairman of the Arab Parliamentary Union and the speaker of the Transitional National Assembly [TNA], has called on the brothers in Yemen to respect the cease-fire resolution to stop the bloodshed and to save the resources of the sons of Yemen. Mr. Khalifah expressed hope that the Yemenis will embark on an objective dialogue to arrive at a lasting solution to the crisis and an agreement acceptable to all the brothers in order to consolidate stability in their country and work toward reconstruction and development. Khalifah pointed out that the Arab homeland, which has been observing the strife in Yemen, aspired to see the role of a strong and united Yemen in supporting the march of the Arab and Islamic nations, in light of the stability which would be achieved by its sons. The chairman of the Arab Parliamentary Union called on the Arab parliamentarians to direct all their efforts towards containing the crisis, overcoming its negative effects, and supporting the peace and reconstruction efforts. He appealed to the brothers in Yemen on the need to heed the voice of reason and wisdom, for which they had been renowned, and to deny the opportunity to anyone intending to ambush the Arab and Islamic nation, endeavoring to squander its resources and destroy its unity.
