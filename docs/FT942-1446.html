940624 FT 24 JUN 94 / Medicine agency to Canary Wharf The European Agency for the Evaluation of Medicinal Products has decided to set up its headquarters in Canary Wharf, east London. The agency, which is responsible for co-ordinating drug licensing, is taking 45,000 sq ft at 7 Westferry Circus at Canary Wharf. It will expand into 65,000 sq ft by the end of 1998. The agency, which was advised by Erdman Lewis, considered another Canary Wharf building and one in Hammersmith, west London.
