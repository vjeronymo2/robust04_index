940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / Observer: Cue up A peculiar form of apartheid thrives on, deep in the heart of London, at the visa section of the French consulate situated in Kensington. Go hunting there for a French visa and you will find a nice little separate cubicle for citizens of Australia, New Zealand and South Africa; the citizenry of other African nations queues up at another booth. Or maybe it's just a subtle French joke about the British Commonwealth?
