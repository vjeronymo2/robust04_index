BFN [Text] Seoul, May 9 (YONHAP) -- Rep. Kim Tok-yong of the ruling Democratic Liberal Party (DLP) left for Germany last weekend to attend a seminar on German unification at the invitation of the Adenauer Institute and Freie (Freedom) University of Berlin together with Rep. Kim Ki-to. Kim, a close aide of President Kim Yong-sam, also visited London at the invitation of the British Foreign Ministry in March. He will be returning home around May 23 after meeting with German politicians. Although he has held no key party posts since leaving his state ministerial office last December, Kim is said to have played an active role in recent state and social Affairs.
