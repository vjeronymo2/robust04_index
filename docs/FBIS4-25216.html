BFN [Text] Beijing, June 7 (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), met on Tuesday [7 June] with officials of the Far East and the South Pacific Games of Disabled Persons (FESPIC) executive committee. Li, who is also the honorary chairman of the FESPIC organizing committee, extended warm welcome to Dr. Harry Fang and Dr. Kazuo Hataa, president and vice president of the FESPIC executive committee, and all the members who came to Beijing for the FESPIC executive committee meeting. Li said the Chinese Government pays great attention to the FESPIC and is making every effort to do the preparatory work for the FESPIC. Dr. Harry Fang from Hong Kong expressed gratitude for the support the Chinese Government has given to the FESPIC. He also gave a high appraisal of the preparatory work of the FESPIC. Dr. Harry Fang said the disabled persons in the Far East and South Pacific countries and regions were anxiously waiting for the beginning of the FESPIC. The FESPIC is to be held from September 4 to 10 in Beijing. It will be the first time for a capital city to host the games of the kind.
