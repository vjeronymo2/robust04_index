November 8, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

THE CORNER BAR: MORE BARS:

The Place (Mike's Sports Bar), 2920 E. Coast Highway, Corona del Mar.

Open daily, 10 a.m. to midnight. (714) 644-0210. Three satellite dishes, seven small TV screens, sports memorabilia on the walls and famous athletes dropping by for a visit. Make no mistake, this is an all-sports bar. ROBYN NORWOOD
