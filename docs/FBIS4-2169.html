BFN [Text] Beijing, April 7 (XINHUA) -- Mobil, a leading world oil company, is marking the 100th anniversary of its business in China by setting up a fund of 500,000 U.S. dollars to help with China's education. The corporation presented a 100,000 U.S. dollars check to the National Academy of Education Administration as the first instalment for the fund in support of the academy's efforts to upgrade the training facilities and programs for training education administrators. With its headquarters in the United States, Mobil started its Shanghai office 100 years ago and returned to China in 1974. Now it has three offices -- in Beijing, Shanghai, and Guangzhou. K. Yuan, chairman and managing director of the Mobil Corporation in Hong Kong, said that Mobil has been in China for a long time and intends to stay for a lot longer.
