930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: Finance lessons in schools urged SCHOOLS should teach personal finance as part of the national curriculum, a Conservative MP said yesterday. Mr Andrew Rowe, MP for Mid Kent, addressing a conference on money management and citizenship organised by Barclaycard, said: 'With 2m families currently facing debt problems it is evident that personal money management should be one of the life skills taught in our schools.'
