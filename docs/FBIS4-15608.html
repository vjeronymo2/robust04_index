BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Text] Sevastopol April 11 TASS -- It is impossible to contact Russian servicemen from the Black Sea Fleet unit which was stormed by Ukrainian commandos late on Sunday [10 April] as communications with the 46th Hydrographic Division of the fleet situated near Odessa have been disrupted. The fleet command is working to stabilise the situation, spokesman of the Black Sea Fleet Andrey Grachev told TASS on Monday. He did not elaborate.
