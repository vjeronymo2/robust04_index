BFN [Text] Oslo, May 10 (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the national committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), arrived here this afternoon for a three-day official goodwill visit to Norway. Norwegian acting prime minister and foreign minister, Bjorn Tore Godal, greeted the Chinese guest at a hotel where Li stays. Upon his arrival, Li said in a written statement that he was expecting to exchange views on bilateral relations and international issues of common concern with Norwegian leaders during his visit to the country. He also expressed the hope that his current visit would promote mutual understanding, cooperation and friendship between the China and Norway. Li started visiting the Nordic nation at the invitation of the Norwegian Government. It is the Chinese leader's third leg of his current five-European-nation tour which has already taken him to Finland and Sweden.
