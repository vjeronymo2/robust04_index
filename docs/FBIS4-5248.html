BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 7 -- A delegation of the Communist Party of Vietnam arrived in Tokyo on April 5 for a working visit to Japan at the invitation of the Central Committee of the Japanese Communist Party (JCP). On April 6, the delegation led by Dao Duy Tung, member of the Politcal Bureau and Secretariat of the party Central Committee held talks at the JCP's headquarters with a JCP delegation led by Kazuo Shii, chief of the Secretariat of the JCPCC [Central Committee] and executive member of its Presidium and member of the House of Representatives. The two sides informed each other of the situation of their respective party and country, exchanged views on international issues of mutual concern and discussed measures to strengthen the relations of solidarity, friendship and cooperation between the two parties in the present situation.
