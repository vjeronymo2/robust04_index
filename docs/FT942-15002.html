940418 FT 18 APR 94 / Business Travel (Update): Colombian tragedy At least 22 people were killed, most of them burned alive, in a fiery multiple traffic accident on a highway just outside the capital Bogota, police said Saturday. A cargo truck collided with a car and small bus that had crashed moments earlier along a highway near the town of Granada, about 20 miles southwest of the capital, police said in a statement.
