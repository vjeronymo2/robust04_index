December 14, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: SAN GABRIEL;

LAW FIRM FOR CITY SELECTED

After deciding earlier this fall to drop Brown, Winfield and Canzoneri as the city's law firm, the City Council last week selected Wallin, Kress, Reisman, Price and Dilkes of Santa Monica.

One of the firm's partners, Robert Kress, will serve as city attorney. A 1972 graduate of UCLA law school, Kress, 42, also functions in that role for for Rosemead and Bradbury, and as assistant city attorney for Bell Gardens.
