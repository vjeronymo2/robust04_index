921019 FT 19 OCT 92 / Construction Contracts: Office building in Poland BOVIS INTERNATIONAL has been awarded a Dollars 500,000 (Pounds 291,000) construction management project in Warsaw for Hewlett Packard SA of Geneva. The three month fast-track project involves the refurbishment and fit out of two floors of an office building, providing Hewlett Packard with 15,000 sq ft of accommodation to serve as a local headquarters for its operations in Poland. The project is being run by Mr Henry Liszka, Bovis International's operations manager in Poland, using local resources. Hewlett Packard's property consultant on the Warsaw project is Fuller Peiser of London.
