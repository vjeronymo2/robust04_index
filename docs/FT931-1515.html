930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / Polish business weekly launched RINGIER, the Swiss publisher and printing group, yesterday launched Cash, a business weekly, in Poland, Christopher Bobinski reports from Warsaw. The weekly is modelled on the original Cash first published in Switzerland in 1989 and on Profit, started in Czechoslovakia two years later. Profit now sells 130,000 copies a week while Ringier's Polish weekly aims at a 350,000 circulation by next year. Ringier owns 18 more daily and weekly papers in Slovakia and the Czech Republic.
