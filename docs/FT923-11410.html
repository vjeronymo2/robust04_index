920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Green Property improves to Pounds 1.01m Green Property, the Dublin-based development investment and trading group, lifted interim profits by some 20 per cent to IPounds 1.01m (Pounds 950,000) pre-tax. The increase from IPounds 847,000 for the six months to June 30 reflected net rental income ahead to IPounds 2.39m (IPounds 2.25m) and other operating income of IPounds 440,000 (IPounds 106,000). Interest charges were static at IPounds 1.3m. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.2p, payable from earnings of 4.39p (3.76p) per share.
