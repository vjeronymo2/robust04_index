CSO [Text] Events Service -- Police agents of the East Tehran region engaging in the antinarcotics campaign and the campaign against narcotics dealers seized 5.5 kg of opium at the home of a smuggler along with a large quantity of cans of beer. The Greater Tehran Police Precinct Public Relations Office reports: Following the receipt of a report that someone named Mehdi was procuring and distributing narcotics at his home on Sablan Street in North Tehran, East Tehran Police District Agents immediately began an investigation to confirm the truth of the report and realized that the aforementioned individual was engaged in extensive narcotics procurement and distribution activity in the area. The agents obtained a court order and at a suitable opportunity they searched the aforementioned party's home. While arresting him, they seized 5.5 kg of pure opium, 32 grams [g] of burnt opium residue, 11 gs of hashish, and 346 cans of beer. In this regard the defendant was turned over to the judicial authorities along with his file.
