940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Vymura maiden Vymura, the wallcoverings company which came to the market in May, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.53m for the first half of 1994 against Pounds 1.34m. Turnover advanced from Pounds 18.2m to Pounds 19.6m. Earnings per share came out at 4.36p (3.99p).
