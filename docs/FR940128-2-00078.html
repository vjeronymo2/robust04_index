[FE Docket No. 93&hyph;145&hyph;NG] Wisconsin Gas Co.; Long-Term Authorization To Import Natural Gas From Canada AGENCY: Office of Fossil Energy, DOE. ACTION: Notice of order.

SUMMARY: The Office of Fossil Energy of the Department of Energy gives notice that it has granted Wisconsin Gas Company (Wisconsin Gas) authorization to import up to 89,411 Mcf per day of Canadian natural gas beginning December 30, 1993, and continuing through November 1, 2003. This gas would be imported from Western Gas Marketing Limited pursuant to a contract dated October 20, 1993. Wisconsin Gas order is available for inspection and copying in the Office of Fuels Programs Docket Room, 3F&hyph;056, Forrestal Building, 1000 Independence Avenue, SW., Washington, DC 20585, (202) 586&hyph;9478. The docket room is open between the hours of 8 a.m. and 4:30 p.m., Monday through Friday, except Federal holidays. Issued in Washington, DC, January 10, 1993. Clifford P.
