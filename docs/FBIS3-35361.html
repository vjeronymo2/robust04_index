Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] IDF [Israel Defense Forces] Intelligence Branch officers today told the Knesset Foreign Affairs and Defense Committee that the Arab countries are facing serious economic difficulties. This state of affairs is taking its affecting both the military procurement and the civilian population's living condition. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that the officers also briefed the Knesset members of an improvement in Syria's economic situation following a rise in the production of oil. The intelligence officers also believe that the economic aspect will play an important role in the Palestinian ability to set up a self-government.
