BFN [Text] The Hungarian and Slovak prime ministers have met in Komarom. As planned originally, no agreement has been signed at the talks between Peter Boross and Jozef Moravcik. However, the two discussed the situation of the Hungarian minority in Slovakia, the Boes [Gabcikovo] Dam, the elimination of double taxation, and the opening of new border crossing points. At the joint news conference it was said that the greatest achievement of these Hungarian-Slovak prime ministerial talks is that a new phase will begin in bilateral relations. The meeting made possible the settlement of the disputed issues at a later stage, it was said at the press briefing.
