BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 12 (KCNA) -- Shuhachi Inoue, director general of the International Institute of the Chuche Idea, arrived in Pyongyang on April 11. On the same day a delegation of old officials of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) headed by Paek Chong-won, chairman of the Korean Physical Culture and Sports Federation in Japan, and a delegation of Korean Social Scientists in Japan headed by Pak Yong-kon, chairman of the Association of Korean Social Scientists in Japan, arrived in Pyongyang to visit the socialist homeland.
