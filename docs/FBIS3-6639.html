Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Datuk Sri Dr. Mahathir Mohamed hopes that that a new era will emerge in Sabah following the formation of the National Front Government in the state last week. He said the new era can be brought about considering that the appointment of Tan Sri Sakaran Dandai as the new Sabah chief minister be well accepted by all sides in the state. [sentence as heard] He told reporters this when asked about the setting up of the new Sabah cabinet. He had earlier launched the book: THE MAKING OF THE NATIONAL CAR -- NOT JUST A DREAM. Datuk Sri Dr. Mahathir said the number of members in the Sabah cabinet had yet to be determined and that the matter would be discussed at the meeting of the National Front Supreme Council to be held in Kuala Lumpur tomorrow night.
