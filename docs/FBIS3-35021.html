Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] As of today, Amman will witness an extensive political move to prepare in various fields for the historic reconciliation meeting to be held in Amman on Sunday, 20 February, which will bring together Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid. During the meeting, the Yemeni document of pledge and accord will be signed to mark the end of the political crisis and entrenchment of Yemeni unity. Within the framework of the signing ceremony, Mujahid Abu-Shawarib, Yemeni deputy prime minister and head of the Yemeni reconciliation meeting team, arrived in Amman yesterday. A high-level Yemeni media and political delegation is scheduled to arrive in Amman early this morning in order to prepare for Sunday's reconciliation meeting to be held under the auspices of His Majesty King Husayn. The delegation is led by Salim Salih Muhammad, member of the Yemeni Presidential Council, and comprises members of the Yemeni Socialist Party's Central Committee and Political Bureau. Ahmad Jabir 'Afif, head of the Yemeni Political Forces Dialogue Committee said that President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid, members of the Dialogue Committee, and top political and social figures representing the main Yemeni parties are expected to arrive in Amman on Sunday, 20 February, in preparation for signing the accord document in Amman of the Arabs. In a telephone conversation with AL-DUSTUR, 'Afif said that everything on the Yemeni side is ready, that all matters are proceeding in the right direction, and that all efforts are being made to travel to Amman on 10 Ramadan, corresponding to 20 February, to meet with His Majesty King Husayn and to sign the document. 'Afif reiterated the Yemeni people's eagerness to maintain their unity, stability, and security. He noted that unity exists and that it is the right of the Yemeni people, who insist on maintaining this unity whatever the circumstances. 'Afif said that His Majesty King Husayn's role has greatly helped reconcile the parties and restore amity due to the great respect and appreciation the Yemeni president, vice president, and all the Yemeni people have for his majesty.
