Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The POSTFAKTUM agency reports that rumors are being circulated in Moscow that on 20 March Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin urgently flew to Sochi to meet the president, that telephones in the House of the Russian Federation Government on Krasnopresnenskaya Naberezhnaya are receiving only incoming calls and it is impossible to phone from the White House, and that special purpose units were put on red alert and can enter Moscow any minute. The source was connecting the emergence of these rumors with the alleged aggravation of Boris Yeltsin's health. However, in Viktor Chernomyrdin's reception office and the Russian Federation Government press service, this information was categorically refuted. Answering a question by the POSTFAKTUM agency correspondent as to whether special purpose units have been put on red alert, a duty officer at the interior troops headquarters said that nothing of the kind had happened and nothing of the kind is expected.
