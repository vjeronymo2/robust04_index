920214 FT 14 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Modest fall at Trust of Property Shares The net asset value of the Trust of Property Shares showed a modest decline -from 79.75p to 78.43p per share - over the 12 months to December 31. Net revenue declined from Pounds 102,328 to Pounds 95,265 for earn-ings of 1.443p (1.55p) per 5p share. The proposed dis-tribution is raised to 1.432p (1.364p).
