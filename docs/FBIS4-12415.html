BFN [Text] Tehran, April 7, IRNA -- Foreign Minister of Yemen Muhammad Salim Basindwah arrived here Thursday with a message from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih for President Akbar Hashemi- Rafsanjani. He was welcomed by his Iranian counterpart 'Ali Akbar Velayati. Upon arrival at the airport Basindwah called for the strengthening of Tehran-Sanaa relations which he said date back to pre-Islamic era. He [word indistinct] confident that the two countries will enter into a new era of cooperation through further expansion of their brotherly ties. As for the differences between Yemeni president and the vice-president, Salih said the situation in Yemen was not so bad as reflected by foreign mass media outside that country. Commenting on the likelihood of Iran's mediation efforts, he said that Yemen welcomed all mediation efforts. However, he said, the issue of Yemen could be solved solely by Yemenis themselves.
