BFN [Report by Matanzas correspondent Manolo Garcia] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] The Antonio Guiteras thermoelectric plant in Matanzas is operating at full capacity and absolute efficiency. This plant resumed operations Saturday afternoon after a brief 72-hour down time period for maintenance. Engineer Jesus Valdez Torra, plant director, reported that all defects have been fully eradicated. Therefore, the plant will be fully operative until the next maintenance period, which will require six days. The engineer added that maintenance work will also be done on another 330 megawatt unit. This work will include regulating the valves and cleaning the boiler walls. The Antonio Guiteras plant has provided more than 500,000 megawatt-hours to date and expects to conclude the year with a total of 2.2 million megawatt-hours, 10 percent of the total amount of electricity generated by Cuba's Electric Union.
