Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] In response to an invitation from His Excellency [H.E.] Nouhak Phoumsavan, president of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR], H.E. Mongolian President Punsalmaagiyn Ochirbat, his wife, and a high-level Mongolian delegation arrived in Vientiane by special plane at 1630 today for a four-day official friendship visit to our country. H.E. Punsalmaagiyn Ochirbat, his wife, and the delegation were welcomed upon their arrival at Wattai Airport by H.E. President Nouhak Phoumsavan and his wife; H.E. National Assembly Chairman Saman Vi-gnaket; H.E. Khamphoui Keoboualapha, deputy prime minister and chairman of the Planning and Cooperation Committee; H.E. Vientiane Municipality Governor Boun-gnang Volachit; H.E. Phao Bounnaphon, minister attached to the Prime Minister's Office; H.E. Thongdam Chanthaphon, minister attached to President's Office; H.E. Trade Minister Sompadit Volasan; H.E. Thongsavat Boupha, deputy minister of foreign affairs; H.E. Khamphai Latsami, deputy minister of public health; and H.E. Chaleun Valinthalasak, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the LPDR to Mongolia, along with a number of dignitaries and Vientiane residents. [passage omitted] Accompanying H.E. President Punsalmaagiyn Ochirbat and his delegation on this visit to the LPDR are H.E. Foreign Affairs Minister Tserenpilyn Gombosuren; H.E. Tsebegmidiin Tsogt, minister of trade and industry; H.E. Toytigiyn Tomorhuleg, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of Mongolia to the LPDR [passage indistinct]. Later, H.E. President Punsalmaagiyn Ochirbat and his delegation met with H.E. LPDR Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon at the Prime Minister's Office. At 1900, H.E. Punsalmaagiym Ochirbat, along with his wife and delegation, attended a banquet in their honor hosted by H.E. President Nouhak Phoumsavan and his wife at the National Assembly Hall in the capital, Vientiane.
