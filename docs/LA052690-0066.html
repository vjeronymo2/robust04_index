May 26, 1990, Saturday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: OXNARD;

2 HELD IN BEATING ATTACK ON FAMILY

Two Oxnard residents were being held Friday on suspicion of attacking a family with weightlifting belts, police said.

Sabino Ortega, 21, and Angel Gonzales, 19, were being held in Ventura County Jail in lieu of $10,000 bail each.

They were arrested earlier this week in the 500 block of Cuesta Del Mar in Oxnard and charged with assaulting three residents with weightlifting belts they took from around their waists, said Oxnard Police Sgt. Frank Devorick.

Ann Marie Flores, 49; her son Jose Flores, 23, and daughter Marylena Flores, 27, were in their apartment when three men came to the door and challenged them to a fight, Devorick said. The men attacked the family with fists, then removed heavy leather weightlifting belts from their waists and used them as weapons, he said.

Marylena Flores lost several teeth when she was struck by a belt, Devorick said, and the other injuries were minor. Other members of the Flores family restrained two of the men until police arrived, but one man fled.

Motive for the attack is unknown, Devorick said. There is no indication that it was gang-related, he said.
