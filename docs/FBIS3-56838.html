Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] [First announcer] Admiral Igor Kasatonov, first deputy commander in chief of the Russian Navy, and Vladimir Yegorov, commander of the Baltic Fleet, met representatives of the Liepaja town council and Gaidis Zeibots, commander of the Latvian naval forces, in Liepaja [sea port in western Latvia] yesterday [19 January] to discuss the timescale for the withdrawal of the units of the Baltic Fleet. [Second announcer] Representatives of the town council put forward a proposal to the Russian Navy to leave Liepaja by 10 May 1994, thus creating favorable conditions for local elections scheduled for 22 May. Representatives of the Russian side did not give any specific answer to the proposals by the local government, but undertook, nevertheless, to work on that issue.
