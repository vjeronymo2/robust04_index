BFN [Text] Lima, 27 May (EFE) -- Congressional sources have reported that the Peruvian Congress today authorized the journey by President Alberto Fujimori to the Fourth Ibero-American Summit in Cartagena de Indias (Colombia), and to Japan and the PRC. In a full session the Democratic Constituent Congress (CCD) today approved President Fujimori's trip abroad: from 14 to 16 June to the Ibero-American summit, and from 2 to 13 June to Japan and the PRC. The visit to Japan will begin on 5 June, while he will be in the PRC from 8 to 12 June. Congressional authorization for the president's trips abroad is a requirement established by the Constitution.
