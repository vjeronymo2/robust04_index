November 25, 1989, Saturday, Southland Edition

DUBLIN OFFICIAL REVEALS HUGE COST OF IRA FIGHT

Fighting Irish Republican Army guerrillas has cost the government $3.5 billion over the past 20 years, Justice Minister Ray Burke said Friday.

But he pledged that the high security costs will be maintained "because of the duty of the government to do everything in its power to protect all of the people of this island from the men of violence."

In a direct message to IRA guerrillas, who have spent 20 years trying to oust Britain from Northern Ireland, he said: "Stop and stop now. Surely you must realize you cannot win militarily."
