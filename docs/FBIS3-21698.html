Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on drug seizures and arrests related to drug trafficking monitored through 3 March.

Argentina
National Border Police agents have arrested four members of a gang of drug traffickers and seized 38 kg of very pure cocaine during two operations conducted over the past few hours in the city of Salta, the capital of Salta Department. (Buenos Aires NOTICIAS ARGENTINAS in Spanish 2005 GMT 26 Feb 94) The Drug Prevention Programming Secretariat has reported that personnel from the Naval Prefecture, Customs, the Federal Police, and the Air Force seized 3,123 kg of cocaine, 2,250 kg of marijuana, 14 plants of marijuana, and 2,160 microdoses of LSD, and arrested 82 people, during the two-month "Sun Without Drugs" campaign in the city of Mar del Plata. (Buenos Aires NOTICIAS ARGENTINAS in Spanish 2133 GMT 26 Feb 94)
Peru
Six Canadian drug traffickers who are serving 10-15 year prison terms in Peruvian jails will be extradited to Canada in the next few days. They are Diane Deaudoin Bedard, 46; her husband Claude Descoteaux, 45; Sylvie Suzane Jones, 34; her husband Leopold Ladouceur, 57; Oscar Arturo Nieri Cavero, 44; and Olga Julia Quintana. (Lima EL COMERCIO in Spanish 23 Feb 94 p A11)
