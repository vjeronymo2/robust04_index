941215 FT 15 DEC 94 / Maxwell payout possible soon Creditors of Maxwell Communication Corporation could receive dividend payments from March or April after a legal victory by the MCC liquidators. Liquidators of Bishopsgate Investment Management, the main Maxwell pension fund managers, were yesterday refused leave to go to the Lords to contest a decision by the Court of Appeal that BIM did not have a prior claim to the first Pounds 235m of MCC assets. Price Waterhouse, the MCC liquidators, has recovered more than Pounds 680m, of which Pounds 500m is thought to be available for distribution.
