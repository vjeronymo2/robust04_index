940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Keller in Dollars 9m US purchase Keller Group, the international ground engineering specialist which is coming to market in the spring, has acquired Chicago-based Case International Company for Dollars 9m (Pounds 6m). The purchase of Case, a foundation engineer, will complement Hayward Baker, Keller's US subsidiary, and will lift the group's North American sales to in excess of Dollars 70m. Keller, a former engineering offshoot of GKN, was bought by its management for Pounds 26m three years ago. It will have a flotation value of Pounds 60m.
