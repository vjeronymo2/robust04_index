March 8, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: WEST HOLLYWOOD;

CITY HALL SITE ADVISERS

The West Hollywood City Council has invited representatives from a number of community groups to participate in the selection of a site for the new City Hall.

Representatives from groups such as the Senior Advisory Council, the Gay and Lesbian Advisory Council and the West Hollywood Community Alliance will be asked to make recommendations to the city on the new City Hall's design and location.

City Manager asked the council "to adopt a process that allows for the maximum amount of participation from the community."
