930517 FT 17 MAY 93 / Construction Contracts: London housing scheme BOVIS CONSTRUCTION, the P&O company, is to assist Waltham Forest Housing Action Trust to manage a Pounds 200m demolition and rebuilding programme in east London. Preconstruction work on the development of four large system-built housing estates in the borough of Waltham Forest is already under way. The government-sponsored HAT scheme involves demolition of 1960s/70s tower and slab blocks, to be replaced by low rise housing.
