930224 FT 24 FEB 93 / World Stock Markets: America AFTER a third consecutive decline in 1992, US corporate profits are forecast to hit new heights in 1993 and 1994. IBES, which tracks and collates analysts' forecasts, says that S & P 500 earnings on this basis are expected to increase by 23.4 per cent this year, to Dollars 28.76 a share, and by another 18.6 per cent to Dollars 34.12 in 1994. Gains for the Dow components are expected to be 31.1 per cent, and 22.7 per cent respectively.
