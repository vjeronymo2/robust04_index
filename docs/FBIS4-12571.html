BFN [Text] Reports from Iraqi Kurdistan indicate that the regime of the tyrant Saddam is in the process of moving hundreds of tanks and armored vehicles, thousands of troops, and dozens of missiles with a range of 150 km [100 miles] to an area bordering Iraqi Kurdistan. The reports add that the regime plans to hold large-scale military maneuvers in this area. Military planes and helicopters are expected to take part in the drills. According to our sources, 'Ali Hasan Majid, Saddam's defense minister and cousin, is in Mosul to supervise the deployment of the regime's forces and the military maneuvers.
