Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] [Mais Mamedov] A whole arms depot has been discovered by law enforcement bodies of Baku in the headquarters of the Azerbaijan People's Front. It consists of 42 projectiles for grenade launchers, about 200 bottles of inflammable liquid, 73 bullet proof vests, fuses for grenades, and smoke pots. Speaking today at a press conference, procurator-general of the republic Ali Omerov stated that according to information at his disposal the People's Front of Azerbaijan had been preparing a coup for 5 March. He also announced that in a number of districts of Azerbaijan today members of that organization had been detained and combat weaponry had been confiscated from them. Those arrested include citizens from Chechnya. Minister of Internal Affairs Vafig Novruzov [as heard] described as ridiculous claims of the People's Front leadership that the weaponry had been deliberately planted by policemen to settle scores with members of that organization.
