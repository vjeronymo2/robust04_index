February 8, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

LOCAL;

PATTI DAVIS' MARRIAGE ON ROCKS

Patti Davis, Ronald and Nancy Reagan's estranged daughter, is now estranged from her husband, it was reported today.

Davis, 37, has been living apart from yoga instructor Paul Grilley, whom she married Aug. 14, 1984, for about a month, the New York Daily News said. "They are divorcing," a friend of Davis told the newspaper. Davis' acting career failed to take off, and she turned to writing novels -- first with "Home Front," an unflattering parallel of her family, and then "Deadfall," a thriller about Nicaraguan policy. Neither pleased her parents, according to reports.
