EFFECTIVE DATE: February 23, 1994. Farmers Home Administration 7 CFR Part 1924 Limited Resource Loan Reviews AGENCY: Farmers Home Administration, USDA. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: The Farmers Home Administration (FmHA) amends its regulation with regard to the annual review of borrowers with limited resource Farm Ownership (FO), Soil and Water (SW), and Operating (OL) loans. The amendments are necessary to clarify annual review procedures for limited resource loan borrowers. The intended effect is to reduce the number of borrowers who are past due for limited resource loan reviews. EFFECTIVE DATE: February 23, 1994.
