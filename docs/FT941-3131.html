940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / UK Company News: WSP Group Pre-tax profits of WSP Group, the consulting engineer which merged with ABC Consulting last October, halved from Pounds 560,000 to Pounds 262,000 in the 12 months to December 31. Mr Peter Welch, chairman, said that considering the recession in the property and construction industry and the additional work required as a result of the merger the results compared favourably with the sector as a whole. Turnover moved ahead to Pounds 11.5m (Pounds 10m) with continuing operations at Pounds 9.43m. Earnings per share halved to 1.9p (3.8p) and the proposed final dividend is maintained at 0.9p for a 1.8p (2p) total.
