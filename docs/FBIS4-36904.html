BFN [Excerpt] Shaykh Zayid Bin Sultan al-Nuhayyan, president of the United Arab Emirates [UAE], today received brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the prime minister, who briefed him on the developments affecting the situation in Yemen and on the war imposed on our people by the al-Ahmar gang and its effects on our people. For his part, Shaykh Zayid Bin Sultan al-Nuhayyan expressed deep regret that the war was still continuing in Yemen, stressing the need for the implementation of the cease-fire to avert further bloodshed and destruction.
