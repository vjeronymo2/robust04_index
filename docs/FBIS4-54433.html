BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 29/4/1994 -- Romania's President Ion Iliescu received Thursday afternoon [28 April] the minister of heavy industry of the Islamic Republic of Iran, Nezhad-Hoseynian, currently in Bucharest to take part in a session of the joint Romanian-Iranian Commission. According to a statement made by the Iranian minister to a ROMPRES editor, the level of bilateral exchanges between the two countries has reached some $600 million, which was considered by Nezhad-Hoseynian as satisfactory. The Iranian minister also said that at present Romania was listed first on Iran's list of exchanges with Eastern Europe.
