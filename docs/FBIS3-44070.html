Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg Jan 24 SAPA--Democratic Party [DP] Transvaal leader Tony Leon has been nominated to stand for the national Parliament. The DP said on Monday its PWV [Pretoria, Witwatersrand, Vereeniging] electoral college unanimously chose Mr Leon, MP for Houghton, to head the party's regional list for the post-April 27 election parliament. Johannesburg City council managment committee chairman Ian Davidson defeated Brian Goodall, MP for Edenvale, to head the list for the provincial parliament. The DP said other names on both lists would be announced later in the week.
