BFN [Excerpt] President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali has ordered an increase in the number of Tunisian troops to the UN forces in Rwanda. This took place when the president received Defense Minister Abdelaziz Ben Dhia this morning in Carthage Palace. This decision gives substance to the determination of our country shown during the OAU [Organization of African Unity] summit in Tunis of assuming its responsibility in settling conflicts in Africa. President Ben Ali, the current chairman of the OAU, stressed that this initiative is a response to the need shown in the African summit for an effective contribution by African countries to the settlement of hot conflicts in the continent, within the framework of cooperation between the OAU and the United Nations.
