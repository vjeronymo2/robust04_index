920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / Australians giving up search for work MORE unemployed Australians are giving up the search for work. Figures released by the government yesterday show that the proportion of the work force actively seeking work fell 0.3 percentage points to 63.2 per cent in August, Emilia Tagaza writes from Canberra. This fall pulled the unemployment rate slightly down to 10.9 per cent last month from 11 per cent in July. This was the 12th consecutive month Australia's unemployment rate had exceeded 10 per cent.
