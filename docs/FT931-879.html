930327 FT 27 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Portmeirion up to record Pounds 3.7m Steady investment in modern and flexible production facilities helped Portmeirion Potteries (Holdings) lift pre-tax profits by more than 40 per cent, from Pounds 2.61m to a record Pounds 3.67m, in 1992. Sales grew 10 per cent to Pounds 22.5m. Earnings per share were 22.67p (16.68p) and the final dividend is 5.5p for a total of 7.75p (7.35p). Mr George Hesp, managing director, said 1993 had started encouragingly with the value of first quarter orders above that for the same period in 1992.
