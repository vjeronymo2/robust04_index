BFN [Text] Tehran, April 26, IRNA -- The 27th "Mohammad (PBUH) [peace be upon him] Rasulollah" Corps on Tuesday [26 April] announced its readiness within the framework of the government's policy, to march to the assistance of the defenceless people of Bosnia along with all facilities at its disposal. A press release by the corps, which saw service in the imposed war (1980-88), supported the recent statement of the leader of the Islamic revolution, Ayatollah Seyyed 'Ali Khamene'i, in this regard. In a separate statement, the Society of Imam Sadeq (AS) university graduates, also announced their readiness to join in the defence of aggressed Bosnia. The students' basij (volunteers) of the university, supported the statement, and said Gorazde is a picture of mass oppression. The minarets of mosques in Bosnia have become thorns for the "savage society of the West", it added. Army and IRGC [Islamic Revolution Guards Corp] forces over the past few days denounced the gory massacre of Bosnians at the hands of the Serbs and supported the call for despatch of peace-keeping forces to the Bosnia-Herzegovina.
