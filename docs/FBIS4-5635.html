BFN [Text] Aranyaprathet -- Khmer Rouge (KR)guerrillas last night managed to retake Pailin from Phnom Penh troops after a heavy battle with government forces. Military sources at the border said the Khmer Rouge forces had seized Highway Nos. 10 and 5 which were the main supply lines for the government troops to Pailin from Battambang and Banteay Meanchey before moving into Pailin yesterday evening. Details of the fighting remained sketchy last night. But the sources claimed the Phnom Penh force suffered heavy casualties and damage from the assault by the guerrillas. Several government tanks were seized by the guerrillas as ground troops were sent in "disarray" by the attacks, the sources claimed. Thai military officers claimed last night at least 15 Phnom Penh soldiers fled across the border into Ban Phakkat of Chanthaburi's Pong Namron district last night.
