BFN [Article signed MPK/RHR: "Claes and 'Arafat Discuss Difficulties Encountered by Middle East Peace Process"] [Text] Brussels, 13 Apr (BELGA) -- Belgian Foreign Minister Willy Claes spoke with PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat in Strasbourg on Wednesday, 13 April, of the difficulties encountered in the Middle East peace process, according to a communique from the Belgian Foreign Ministry. Mr. Claes stressed "the need to convince all parties to achieve rapid progress in this process," the communique stressed. The Belgian minister also reminded 'Arafat that he fully supports the idea of European Union (EU) funding for the Palestinian police force, a matter on which the EU ministers must soon decide. He also stressed the importance of a prompt and concrete implementation of projects benefitting the Palestinian population in the occupied territories which the EU has already agreed to finance, the communique added.
