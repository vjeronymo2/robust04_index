BFN [Text] Pakistan has been conveying its concern to the United States and other countries about the deployment of Prithvi and continued development of Agni missile by India. Answering questions by newsmen at a briefing in Islamabad today, a Foreign Office spokesman said Pakistan pointed out to member countries of Missile Technology Control Regime that this will be a serious development. India could deploy these missiles with its nuclear capability, raising the specter of nuclear arms race and endangering peace and security in the horn of South Asia. He said Pakistan still hopes that international community will prevail over India to stop its reckless move toward this new arms race.
