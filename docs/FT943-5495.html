940903 FT 03 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Readymix ahead to IPounds 1.36m at midway Readymix, the Irish building materials subsidiary of RMC, reported pre-tax profits up from IPounds 1.05m to IPounds 1.36m (Pounds 1.34m) for the half year to June 30. Turnover grew by IPounds 2.3m to IPounds 16.3m. Earnings came out at 2.98p (2.29p) per share and the interim dividend is maintained at 0.55p.
