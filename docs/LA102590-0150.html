October 25, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

CHRISTIAN ACTION: A new evangelical Christian group has targeted Orange County for political action, says Mother Jones magazine. . . . Jay Grimstead, head of the Mountain View-based National Coordinating Council, confirms the report, saying the county was chosen in the hope that "people who are bringing destructive influence to society will be defeated at the polls." Chris Woodyard and Len Hall
