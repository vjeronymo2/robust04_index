March 12, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

MTV En Espanol: MTV Internacional, a Spanish-language version of MTV's 24-hour English-language cable service, will go to a daily format today.

The 30-minute music video and interview show is aired at 4 p.m. every day except Saturday when it airs at 6 p.m. "I don't see this on Anglo TV," producer Barbara Corcoran said. "That's what I'm trying to do -- create TV for Hispanics in the United States." Shows on Thursday are in English, intended for Latino youths who speak English as a first language. The program airs locally on KVEA Channel 52. BETH KLEID
