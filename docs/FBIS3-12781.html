Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pakistan has welcomed the signing of agreements between Bosnia and Croatia for a bicommunal federation in Bosnia to be linked with Croatia in a confederation. A statement issued by the Foreign Office in Islamabad today said the government appreciates the role played by the United States in achieving this result and is confident that this accord, which follows closely the cease-fire concluded by the Bosnian Government and Croats last week, would bring about a lasting reconciliation between the two communities. The statement said the agreement reached in Washington between the Bosnian Government and the Croat community would mark a significant step toward a comprehensive peaceful settlement of the Bosnian conflict. Pakistan also welcomes the positive role played by the Russian Government in the reopening of the Tuzla airport, making possible humanitarian aid to the city.
