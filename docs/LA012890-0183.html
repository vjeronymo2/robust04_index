January 28, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

$900 RAISED FOR TOT LEFT BEHIND

About $900 has been raised by employees at Bob's Big Boy restaurants around Orange County for the 21-month-old daughter of a waitress killed Jan. 5.

Organizers collected the last of the donations last week, said Elliot Sousa, manager at the Huntington Beach Big Boy where Tammy Davis, 19, worked as a waitress before being shot just minutes after getting off work. The suspect in the case is Brian Keith Framstead, Davis' former boyfriend and father of their daughter.

Half the $900 will go toward savings bonds for the child, while the other half was given to Davis' grandmother, who is taking care of the girl. SHELBY GRAD
