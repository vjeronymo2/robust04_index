931222 FT 22 DEC 93 / EU Legislation In a legal assault meant to enforce the European Union's single market, the European Community said yesterday it would take member countries to court in 184 cases where they have failed to implement EU legislation, AP-DJ reports from Brussels. The offensive reverses an 11-month truce between the Commission, which is responsible for upholding Community rules, and member states which have been slow to take in Community laws to national legislation.
