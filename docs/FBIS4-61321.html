BFN [Text] Riga, May 04, BNS -- As many as 102,222 Latvian residents, or 6.6 percent of the country's economically active population, were looking for job on May 3, according to operative figures from the State Employment Service. From those, 88,136 have registered themselves as unemployed, 36,016 receive allowances, 31,443 have been seeking jobs for more than six months. In March, the joblessness rate was 6.5 percent, the service reported.
