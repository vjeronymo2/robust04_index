December 10, 1990, Monday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPORTS ET CETERA: GOLF

Tom Addis III was elected secretary of the Professional Golfers Assn. of America during meetings held in La Quinta.

Addis is the professional at Singing Hills Country Club.

BASKETBALL

Fans who deliver toys for the Marine Corps' Toys For Tots campaign at county Texaco service stations can request two-for-one coupons on tickets to the Dec. 21-22 Texaco Star Classic at the Sports Arena. Participating teams in the men's basketball tournament are San Diego State, Arkansas State, Lamar and Drexel.
