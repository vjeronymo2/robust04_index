BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 21 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song on April 21 received a Korean woman reporter, Mun Myong-cha residing in the United States, who is on a visit to the homeland. Present on the occasion was Kim Yong-sun, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea. Mun Myong-cha warmly congratulated President Kim Il-song on his birthday and wished him good health and a long life. President Kim Il-song expressed thanks for this and conversed with her in a cordial atmosphere overflowing with compatriotic feelings. He arranged a luncheon for her.
