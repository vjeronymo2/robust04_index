BFN [Excerpt] Although some ships fly the Honduran flag, this does not necessarily mean they are Honduran, President Carlos Roberto Reina said yesterday in reference to the report that Honduran-flagged ships have violated the embargo against Haiti. He considered it distressing and unfortunate that the United States and the inter-American system are about to adopt more drastic measures to reestablish democracy in Haiti. He also announced this topic will be addressed at the summit in Cartagena de Indias, Colombia. [passage omitted]
