BFN ["Own Information" report from the "Fax News" roundup under "Russkoye Oruzhiye" rubric: "Decision to Repay Debts to South Urals Military-Industrial Complex"] [Text] Chelyabinsk -- "Defense sector workers in the southern Urals, where there is a concentration of unique enterprises producing missiles, nuclear weapons, electronic warfare equipment, and many more items essential to a modern army today, are in serious need of help," Viktor Glukhikh, chairman of the State Committee for the Defense Sectors of Industry, believes. A Russian Federation Government commission working in the Urals has instructed that debts to military-industrial complex enterprises in the oblast pertaining to work carried out under the state order be paid by 1 July this year.
