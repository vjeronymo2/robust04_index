BFN [Report by Salamah Ni'mat: "King Husayn Expects Washington's Support for Demarcation of Borders With Israel"] [Excerpt] Amman -- Official political circles attach great importance to King Husayn's talks in Washington and link success to the possibility of making quick progress in the negotiations with Israel over the next three months. The official sources say that they do not rule out the possibility of several bilateral agreements on various issues on the agenda, including demarcation of international borders, water rights, and security arrangements linked to the border demarcation. The sources expect a military agreement to be concluded with Washington committing the United States to supporting Jordan's security and providing arms and advanced modern equipment for the Jordnaian security organs and Armed Forces.
