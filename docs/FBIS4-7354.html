BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 27 (KCNA) -- Papers here today dedicate articles to the 33rd anniversary of the independence of Sierra Leone. A by-lined article of MINJU CHOSON says that the Sierra Leonean people have worked to build a new independent and prospering society after independence. It notes: Sierra Leone is developing relations with many countries, pursuing a non-aligned policy externally. The Democratic People's Republic of Korea established diplomatic ties with Sierra Leone in October 1971. Since then travels and contacts have been frequent and friendly relations further developed between the two peoples. Believing that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries will grow stronger and develop in accordance with the idea of independence, peace and friendship, the Korean people wish the Sierra Leonean people new achievements in the efforts for the independent development of the country, the article says. NODONG SINMUN carries an article introducing the change of Sierra Leone.
