BFN [Text] Mmabatho June 2 SAPA -- The inauguration of North-West Premier Popo Molefe has been postponed indefinitely because of his ill health. A statement from the office of the premier on Thursday [2 June] said Mr Molefe had been ordered by his doctor to stay in bed. He is apparently suffering from influenza. A North-West legislature meeting scheduled for Monday has been postponed to June 20.
