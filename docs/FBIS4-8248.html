BFN [Text] Vidin, April 8 (BTA) -- Representatives of the Western European Union, the Customs Service and the local division of the State Shipping Inspectorate last night arrested the Hungarian ship "Vac" which, together with the "Egzsenek," tow five phosphate-laden barges. The cargo was found to lack the required UN permits. The ship has been detained until the case will be cleared up, said Vladimir Zubarev, chief of the State Shipping Directorate in Vidin.
