Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 16 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] sent a message of greetings to Eric Trevett upon his reelection as general secretary of the Central Committee of the New Communist Party of Great Britain. Expressing the belief that the relations of friendship and cooperation between the two parties would continue to develop on good terms, the message wished the general secretary greater success in his responsible work for defending socialism and strengthening the party.
