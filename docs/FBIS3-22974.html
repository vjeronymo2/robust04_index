Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A limited explosion took place in front of the international Kuwaiti-Alexandria bank in al-Muhandisin district here this evening. The blast smashed the bank's window and the windscreens of two cars parked nearby. The incident resulted in no fatalities or injuries. The security leaderships of al-Jizah governorate rushed to the scene and criminal lab experts as well as the prosecution were notified for investigations.
