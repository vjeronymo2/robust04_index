Neutron Bomb Using `Red Mercury'

Report `Absolutely Groundless'

LD1504104994 Moscow ITAR-TASS in English 0956 GMT 15 Apr 94 LD1504104994 Moscow ITAR-TASS English BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anatoliy Yurkin] [Text] Mirniy (Archangelsk region) April 15 TASS -- ``Reports about a `new Russian miniature bomb,' allegedly developed with the use of `red mercury,' are absolutely groundless,'' ITAR-TASS was told today by Lieutenant-General Yuriy Zhuravlev, chief of the state testing ground of the Russian Strategic Missile Forces. ``Russia is not making or testing new types of nuclear weapons,'' he stressed. The hullabaloo around the Russian ``top secret weapon,'' the general believes, was raised by those who disapprove of the partnership relations that are now being established between Russia and the West. We have seen more than once in the past that whenever some military-industrial complex needed new funds, reports immediately began to appear, claiming that this or that country ``is lagging behind the Russians,'' he recalled.
