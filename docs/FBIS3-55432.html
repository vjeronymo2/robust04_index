Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today Mircea Snegur, president of the Republic of Moldova, sent a message to heads of state and government of NATO member countries, RIA reports. Proceeding from the principles of the interdependence of states' security, the letter says, Moldova considers the transition to a quantitatively new level of cooperation to be timely and propitious. This cooperation will undoubtedly lead to a strengthening of security and stability throughout Europe, the Moldovan president's message says.
