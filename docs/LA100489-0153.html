October 4, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

DESIGNER KELLY TOO ILL TO SHOW WORK

Designer Patrick Kelly, the only American stylist accepted as a member of the French fashion establishment, has pulled out of this month's ready-to-wear collections because of an unspecified illness.

A press spokeswoman said today the Mississippi-born Kelly is convalescing and would be unable to present the spring and summer collection.
