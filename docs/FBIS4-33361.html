BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] The Baja California Attorney General's Office is investigating the possible entry of a large shipment of weapons through the state's border in view of the recent confiscation of 50 AK-47 rifles in Mexicali. This was disclosed by Baja California Governor Ernesto Ruffo Appel in Mexicali. [Begin Ruffo recording] Well, everyone knows that we have seized some weapons here. I think we recently seized approximately 50 of them. They were apparently part of a larger shipment. Therefore, we are investigating. [end recording] According to police, the AK-47 rifles, which were found in a clandestine workshop in Mexicali, were modified for automatic fire. Ruffo Appel said various police forces and the Army are conducting special operations in the state to detect the entry of weapons.
