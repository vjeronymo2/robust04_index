BFN [Text] As has already been reported, Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov personally dismissed Oleksandr Plyuta from the post of interior minister of this autonomous republic. Ukrainian Interior Minister Andriy Vasylyshyn sent a letter to the executive head of that autonomous area about this matter. The letter notes in particular that the Crimean president's decrees run counter to Ukraine's current legislation. The militia is a unified system of bodies, which are part of the structure of Ukraine's Interior Ministry and as such are subordinate to Ukraine's Interior Ministry. In Andriy Vasylyshyn's opinion, the Crimean president's decision regarding the interior minister should be reviewed. This was reported by the Interior Ministry's main public relations center.
