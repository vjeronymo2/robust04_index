January 20, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

49ER FANS GET LAST LAUGH AT COLUMNISTS

Something tells me that Jim Murray was eating quiche as the 49ers kicked the Rams' butts.

Like Murray's over-extended metaphors, the myth of the 49ers as a finesse team doesn't seem to fade for those sheltered ones who never see them play. Murray seems intent on coming up with a clever premise as a replacement for the facts.

Maybe if he started paying close attention to the thing he is purportedly writing about, his jokes would have more basis in truth, always the best source for humor as well as rational thought.

DARREN SHAW, Irvine
