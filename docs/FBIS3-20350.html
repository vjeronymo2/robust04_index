Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Achille Occhetto, PDS [Democratic Party of the Left] Secretary has said that the "electorate's response is clear" and that Silvio Berlusconi should be given the job of forming the government. He added: "Let us see if he is up to it." The election results will be discussed at a meeting in the secretariat tomorrow. Party leaders have laughed off speculation that Occhetto may resign. Claudio Petruccioli said it was "rubbish." Piero Fassino said, "It is not a problem. The center-right has a majority, but the Progressive Alliance was not routed."
