920828 FT 28 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Clondalkin up 10% despite weak dollar In spite of the weak dollar, Clondalkin Group, the Dublin-based printing and packaging company, lifted pre-tax profits 10 per cent to IPounds 6.17m (Pounds 5.8m) in the half year to June 30. Sales fell from IPounds 85.2m to IPounds 74.8m. Earnings worked through at 11.01p (10.47p) and the interim dividend is stepped up to 1.834p (1.698p).
