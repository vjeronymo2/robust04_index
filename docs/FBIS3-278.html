Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] As was the case yesterday, another meeting of the Cease- Fire Commission failed to take place in Maputo this afternoon, because of the absence of the Mozambique National Resistance's delegation. The meeting was scheduled to discuss four main points: the destruction of obsolete war materiel at assembly areas, the transfer of weapons from assembly areas to regional depots, the start of demobilization process, and incidents in some assembly areas belonging to Renamo.
