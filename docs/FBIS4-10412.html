BFN [Text] Argentine Foreign Minister Guido Di Tella travelled on 10 April to Tunis, where he will meet with Tunisian and PLO authorities. According to information obtained by LA NACION, Di Tella will discuss the status of the PLO's representation in Buenos Aires with his PLO colleague [Faruk] Qaddumi. The foreign minister will also meet with Argentine ambassadors posted in Middle East and North African countries in order to coordinate diplomatic actions that are to be undertaken. Di Tella will then travel to Morocco to participate in the GATT meeting that is to be held in Marrakech. After this meeting he will remain in Morocco for an official visit at the invitation of his colleague Abdellatif Filali.
