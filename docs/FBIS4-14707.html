BFN [Summary] The Georgian Parliament chaired by Deputy Speaker Rusudan Beridze resumed its work yesterday. It continued to approve nominations to the Cabinet of Ministers. The parliament approved the following nominations: Avtandil Jorbenadze -- minister of health; Murtaz Zankaliani -- minister of trade and material supplies; Aleksandre Chikvaidze - minister of foreign affairs; Davit Maghradze -- minister of culture; (Vladimer Kereselidze) -- minister of industry. The next sitting of the Georgian Parliament will be held on Monday 4 April.
