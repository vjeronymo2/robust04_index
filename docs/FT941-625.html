940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Dale Electric Dale Electric, the Yorkshire-based power systems group, plans to raise Pounds 4.46m through a 2-for-3 rights issue at 55p per share. The issue of 9.13m shares is underwritten by Beeson Gregory. Mr Iain Dale, chairman, said: 'The proceeds will be used to strengthen the balance sheet, reduce debt and enable us to continue our programme of investment in plant and machinery.'
