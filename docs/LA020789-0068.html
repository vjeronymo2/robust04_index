February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY ROUNDUP: KATERSKY FINANCIAL BUYS EL MONTE SHOPPING CENTER

Katersky Financial in Woodland Hills said it bought the 400,000-square-foot El Monte Shopping Center in El Monte for $33.5 million from The Alexander Haagen Co. in Manhattan Beach.

Katersky Financial owns and manages 19 Southern California shopping centers for investors.
