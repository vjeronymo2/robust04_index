931014 FT 14 OCT 93 / Guatemalan soldiers hunt rebels GUATEMALAN soldiers have been searching for leftist rebels around the town of San Marcos after a sudden flare-up in fighting, a military spokesman said, Reuter reports from Guatemala City. Soldiers were patrolling western Guatemala after the battles which occurred between Friday and Monday, he added. Three soldiers and a guerrilla were killed and 24 people injured in the clashes. From Mexico, a news agency believed close to the rebel Guatemalan National Revolutionary Unit said the clashes were part of a national offensive against the Guatemalan army. President Ramiro de Leon has accused the rebels of replying with bullets to a peace plan presented to them last week.
