BFN [Text] Fighting between the forces loyal to President Borhanoddin Rabbani and the joint forces of General Dostam and Afghan Prime Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar still continues in the northern province of Baghlan. Hekmatyar's forces have announced that they had repulsed an extensive offensive by the forces loyal the president on Baghlan city. Afghanistan's Defense Ministry also announced that a military transport aircraft belonging to General Dostam was fired at and crashed, killing all passengers.
