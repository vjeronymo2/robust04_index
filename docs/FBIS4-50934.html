BFN [Text] Beijing, May 23 (XINHUA) -- Keyum Bawudun, alternate member of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party (CPC), left here today for Morocco to attend the third plenary session of Morocco's Constitutionalist Union. Keyum Bawudun, also deputy secretary of the CPC Committee of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region, will call on some leaders of Morocco's major parties during his stay there.
