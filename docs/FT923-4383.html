920907 FT 07 SEP 92 / International Company News: Cons Venture net assets fall Net asset value per ordinary share of Consolidated Venture Trust declined from 275 1/2 p to 269 5/8 p over the 12 months ended July 31. For the six months to July 31 the trust incurred a net loss of Pounds 8,000 compared with available revenue of Pounds 115,000 for the same period of 1991. Losses per share emerged at 0.075p (earnings 1.08p).
