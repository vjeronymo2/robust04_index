940913 FT 13 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Biotrace Pounds 0.2m in loss after provision Biotrace International, the maker of microbiological testing systems which came to the market last November, announced pre-tax losses of Pounds 225,000 for the seven and a half months to end-June after an exceptional Pounds 565,000 provision for unrealised losses on fixed interest investments. For the year to July 31 1993 there were pre-tax losses of Pounds 113,000. Turnover amounted to Pounds 1.95m and losses per share came through at 0.7p. Mr Brian Levett, chief executive, said the company continued to trade ahead of plan.
