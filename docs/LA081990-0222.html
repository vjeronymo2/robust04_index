August 19, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY RECYCLING CENTERS

Here is a list of privately operated Orange County and Corona recycling centers certified by the state to pay refunds for beverage containers covered under California's recycling program. For containers that qualify, the refunds are: 5 cents for every two containers under 24 ounces, and 5 cents apiece for larger ones. Hours of operation for the centers vary. The abbreviations for materials accepted are:

AL -- aluminum; GL -- glass; PL -- plastic; OM -- other metals; and RF -- refillable bottles.

(Source: State Department of Conservation)
