POSTAL RATE COMMISSION Briefing by NAA February 10, 1994. Notice is hereby given that on February 28, 1994, members of the Commission and staff will be briefed by Newspaper Association of America on current trends in the newspaper industry. The briefing will be conducted at the Commission's Washington, DC office. Anyone seeking further information, or wishing to attend, should contact Charles L.
