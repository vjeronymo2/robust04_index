940604 FT 04 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Johannesburg ended the week generally firmer in line with improved domestic sentiment and demand for blue chips. The overall index added 27 to 5,500, industrials 14 to 6,578 and gold 18 to 1,958. De Beers rose R1.25 to R110.25 and Anglos 25 cents to R224.75.
