940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / UK Company News: F&C Pep lifts net asset value Foreign & Colonial Pep Investment Trust reported net asset value per share, taking prior charges at nominal value, of 121.3p as at March 31. The value compared with 107p a year earlier and 116.9p at the trust's September year-end. Available revenue for the six months amounted to Pounds 746,000 (Pounds 292,000) for earnings of 1.65p (0.99p) per share. As already announced, the interim dividend increases from 0.9p to 1p.
