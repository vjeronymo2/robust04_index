BFN [Excerpts] In a meeting with Edib Bukovic, the deputy prime minister of Bosnia-Herzegovina, Majles Speaker 'Ali Akbar Nateq-Nuri announced: The Iranian youth are ready to go to the republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina if the United Nations allows them to do so. [passage omitted] The Majles speaker stressed the need to solve the existing problems between Muslim leaders of Bosnia and Croatia and said that aggressor Serbs are the principal enemy. He further called for the removal of differences between Sarajevo and Zagreb. The Bosnian Deputy Prime Minister Edib Bukovic thanked the Islamic Republic of Iran's assistance and said Muslims' resistance against the Serbs will result in the growth of Islam.
