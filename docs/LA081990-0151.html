August 19, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ABOUT OLD HAWAII

Jerry Hulse's story July 15, "Hawaii, My Old Friend," was great for a lot of reasons, not the least of which was his listing of the inexpensive lodgings in out-of-the-way places. I'm a little tired of publications assuming we can all spend, or even want to spend, $250 a night on a room.

RICHARD SULLIVAN

Los Angeles
