Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Santa Fe de Bogota, 12 Mar (DPA) -- Julio Fabio Urdinola, a top leader of the Cali cocaine cartel, surrendered to Colombian authorities today, according to Radio Caracol, a private station in Bogota. The report indicates that Urdinola surrendered to a prosecutor in the western city of Palmira. Also surrendering were Hector Porras and Ancizar Ardila, who were sought by Colombian authorities on drug trafficking charges.
