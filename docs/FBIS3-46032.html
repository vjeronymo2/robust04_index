Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, January 24 (XINHUA) -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev left here today for China on a tour which will also take him to Kyrgyzstan. Upon his departure, Kozyrev told XINHUA that Russia hoped to develop long-term and all-round friendly relations with its biggest neighbor China. After his visit to the Central Asian republic of Kyrgyzstan, Kozyrev is expected to arrive in Beijing on January 26.
