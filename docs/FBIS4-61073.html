BFN [Text] Vilnius, 2 May, BNS -- A French-Lithuanian agreement on cooperation in the field of nuclear energy was signed in Vilnius Friday by Philippe Rouvillois, director general of the French Atomic Energy Agency, and Algimantas Stasiukynas, Lithuanian energy minister. A delegation of French nuclear energy experts, headed by Rouvillois, visited Lithuania last week to discuss safety at the Ignalina nuclear power plant. Vytautas Bieliauskas, head of the nuclear energy division of the Lithuanian Energy Ministry, said the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development allocated 33 million ECU to upgrade safety at Ignalina. The visiting French delegation said companies in France are ready to take an active part in the Lithuanian power plant's modernization.
