BFN [Text] Vladimir Zhirinovskiy intends to arrive in Croatia tomorrow, (Aleksey Vedemkin), Zhirinovskiy's envoy, has told HINA. The Russian Embassy in Croatia is in no way involved in Zhirinovskiy's visit to Zagreb, a representative of the embassy has stated, adding that the embassy knows nothing about Zhirinovskiy's reasons for the visit or its schedule, and does not know who invited him. The Foreign Ministry of the Republic of Croatia has announced that no official body has invited Zhirinovskiy to Croatia. Given that his possible visit is of a private nature, the Croatian state authorities will proceed in accordance with legal regulations determining the conditions of entry for foreign nationals and their stay in the Republic of Croatia.
