BFN [Text] Interior Minister Hasan al-Alfi has said that the security services have successfully brought back 29 wanted terrorists from abroad. Egypt and Pakistan are fully cooperating and exchanging information in this regard, al-Alfi said. An agreement between Egypt and Pakistan on the extradition of criminals and terrorists will be signed in Islamabad in 10 days, al-Alfi noted. The interior minister said that the state welcomes any terrorist who listens to reason and it promises to protect repenting terrorists and their families. He called on the extremists to return to the true path of God, for their sake and that of their country.
