910418 FT 18 APR 91 / UK Company News: Golden Vale up 22% Golden Vale, the Irish dairy group, turned in a 22 per cent increase, from IPounds 10.2m to IPounds 12.46m (Pounds 11.18m), in pre-tax profits for 1990 on turnover ahead from IPounds 192.8m to IPounds 209.7m. Earnings per share were shown as 7.47p (7.45p). The final dividend of 0.78p makes a total of 1.14p for the year.
