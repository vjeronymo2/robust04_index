Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 15 (XINHUA) -- China's Ministry of Finance has registered in the Securities Exchange Commission of the United States to issue one billion U.S. dollars-worth of global bonds. Led by Merrill Lynch and Co., Inc., some world major security companies and banks will underwrite the bonds in the United States, Europe and Asia. Jin Renqing, China's deputy finance minister, said that this was the first time for the Chinese Government to issue global bonds and enter the U.S. capital market.
