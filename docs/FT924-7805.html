921114 FT 14 NOV 92 / Citizen's Charter budget doubled THE government is to more than double the budget of the Citizen's Charter unit, the Cabinet Office section responsible for raising the quality of government services. The unit's budget will rise from Pounds 3.7m this year to Pounds 8.5m next year. The unit announced yesterday it will pilot a Charterline telephone helpline giving information on public services and how to complain when things go wrong. The pilot will be launched in the new year, with Price Waterhouse management consultants as project managers.
