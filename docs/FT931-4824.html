930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / International Company News: Shenzhen Tellus to offer 20m shares abroad SHENZHEN Tellus Machinery and Electricity, a mainland Chinese company, is to offer 20m B shares at Yn4.28 (Dollars 0.75) each to overseas investors by public subscription, Reuter reports from Hong Kong. The shares will be ready for listing on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange on June 18 and applications will start in Shenzhen from Thursday. The company said it would issue 21.8m A shares at the same price to Chinese citizens.
