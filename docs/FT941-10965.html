940207 FT 07 FEB 94 / Company News (This Week): Lloyds Bank - Other companies Announcing on Friday, Lloyds is thought to have raised full-year operating profits at best marginally as its income from securities and foreign exchange was limited. Analysts predict a dividend increase of at least 15 per cent. Pre-tax profits are expected to rise to about Pounds 1bn, against Pounds 801m, with the bad debt charge falling from Pounds 556m to about Pounds 350m.
