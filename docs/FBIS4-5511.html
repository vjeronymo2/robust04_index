BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 9 (KCNA) -- The Ministry of Posts and Communications has brought out new memorial stamps, postcards and envelopes on the threshold of the 15th of April. Printed on stamps are Mt. Paektu and "Song of General Kim Il-song" sung loudly by the Korean people and the world people in praise of the great leader President Kim Il-song's revolutionary exploits. Among them are stamps with kimilsongia and magnolia, the national flower, printed against the background of the old home in Mangyongdae, which reflect the best wishes of the Korean people and the world's revolutionary people. There are also postcards and envelopes depicting in the immortal flower kimilsongia in full bloom the deep reverence of the Korean people and the world's revolutionary people longing for the old home in Mangyongdae.
