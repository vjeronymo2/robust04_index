941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / Pounds 40m spent during D-Day anniversary Visitors to the D-Day 50th anniversary celebrations this year spent more than Pounds 40m, it was announced yesterday. The Southern Tourist Board said the region had more than 420,000 day visitors for the celebrations, including nearly 70,000 Americans who spent more than Pounds 10m. More than 160,000 visitors stayed a total of 550,000 nights in hotels and guest houses, while an estimated 60,000 stayed with friends or relatives.
