920428 FT 28 APR 92 / UK Company News: Molynx comes up to forecast Pre-tax profit from Molynx amounted to Pounds 1.93m for 1991, against Pounds 1.69m, and the final dividend is the promised 2.7p. Both matched forecasts made last October with the rights issue which helped fund two acquisitions, reduce borrowings and facilitate further expansion. The group makes closed circuit television and security systems and building energy management systems. Turnover rose from Pounds 15.6m to Pounds 22m and trading profit came to Pounds 2.34m (Pounds 1.94m). Earnings per share were 10.7p (11.8p) and the dividend total is 4p (3.75p).
