BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 3 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received Cuban woman lawyer Candelaria Rodriguez on a visit to Korea. Present on the occasion were Chairman Chong Chun-ki and Vice- Chairperson Chon Yon-ok of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries and Cuban Ambassador to Korea Jose Ramon Rodriguez Varona. President Kim Il-song conversed with the guest in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. He gave a luncheon for her.
