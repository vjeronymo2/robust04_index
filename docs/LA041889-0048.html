April 18, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

CSUN TO SIGN CERRITOS' HUMPHREY

Eugene Humphrey, a 6-0 guard who helped Cerritos College to the state junior college basketball championship, said Monday that he will sign a letter of intent with Cal State Northridge.

Humphrey averaged 12.5 points and four assists for Cerritos, which finished the season with a 31-4 record. He converted 51% of his field-goal attempts and made 46% from three-point range. He shot 80% from the free-throw line and played both point-guard and off-guard positions.

The CSUN baseball team has signed Tustin pitcher Monte Jones, a 6-1, 175-pound right-hander who leads Orange County in strikeouts with 77 in 45 innings pitched.

The Northridge softball team has signed Simi Valley's Denise Swank, a third baseman who has been an all-Marmonte League selection three times and was last season's team MVP for the Pioneers.

Signing with the women's basketball program is Haily Griffith, a 6-1 forward who led Eureka High to a 24-4 record last season. MIKE HISERMAN
