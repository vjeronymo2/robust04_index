December 28, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

SAN BERNARDINO;

SMALL QUAKE STRIKES BUT CAUSES NO HARM

A small earthquake struck seven miles northwest of San Bernardino, and there were no reports of damage or injury. The quake measured magnitude 3.2, said Caltech spokesman Hall Daily in Pasadena. A dispatcher at the San Bernardino Police Department said she had received several calls from residents asking about the quake, but there were no reports of trouble.
