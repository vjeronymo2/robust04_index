930717 FT 17 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Norweb chairman received 13% rise Mr Ken Harvey, chairman of Norweb, the electricity distribution company, received a 13 per cent pay rise last year, taking his total remuneration to Pounds 208,287. The company's annual report shows that most of the rise was accounted for by a performance related bonus for 1992 of Pounds 20,625; he received no performance bonus for the previous year.
