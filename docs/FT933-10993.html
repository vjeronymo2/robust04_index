930731 FT 31 JUL 93 / London Stock Exchange: Sharelink debut The first day's trading in Sharelink, the Birmingham-based execution-only dealer in equities and traded options, saw hefty activity with 3.1m shares traded. The shares, which came to the market at 250p, made their debut at 280p. They closed the day at 288p, after touching 299p. Nearly 4m shares were offered to the public which was 7.9 times oversubscribed. A similar amount of stock was placed with institutions.
