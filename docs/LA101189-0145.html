October 11, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

QUINCY JONES SUED FOR DIVORCE

Actress Peggy Lipton, star of television's "The Mod Squad," filed for divorce from music producer Quincy Jones after 15 years of marriage, according to court papers filed in Los Angeles.

Lipton cited irreconcilable differences for the breakup and is seeking unspecified spousal and child support in court papers filed Tuesday. She also seeks joint custody of the couple's two daughters, Kidada, 15, and Rashida, 13, who have been living with their mother since their parents separated three years ago, the court papers say.
