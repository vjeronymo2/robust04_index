920319 FT 19 MAR 92 / Election 1992 (The Manifestos): Raised eyebrows - Ballot Box There were raised eyebrows at Labour's headquarters. Eagle-eyed researchers had spotted that a UBS Philips and Drew 'shadow Budget special' tip sheet had named Tesco, Forte, British Steel and Royal Insurance as companies whose shares might benefit from a Labour victory. Their distinguished bosses, however, were among the 43 signatories of Tuesday's letter to The Times warning against the red peril. Although the letter concluded with a paragraph insisting it represented personal views, perhaps shareholders should be told.
