931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / International Company News: Spar acquires chain SPAR, one of Germany's main retailers, has acquired eastern Germany's former state-run retailing network from the Treuhand privatisation agency, writes Judy Dempsey in Berlin. The purchase of 221 outlets, the largest block sale of retailing outlets by the Treuhand, are likely to consolidate Spar's position in eastern Germany.
