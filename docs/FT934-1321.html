931220 FT 20 DEC 93 / Curriculum under rightwing attack Attacks on the framework of the national curriculum are made today by rightwing critics and a teachers' union. The Centre for Policy Studies warns that schools forced to work to a detailed national curriculum will be put in a 'straitjacket of dull mediocrity'. The opposition comes as Mr John Patten, education secretary, is due to receive the final report on the curriculum review by Sir Ron Dearing, chairman of the Schools Curriculum and Assessment Authority.
