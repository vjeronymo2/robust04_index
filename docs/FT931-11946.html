930130 FT 30 JAN 93 / Jordan wins Dollars 380m more aid International donors have found an extra Dollars 380m in 1993 aid for Jordan, rewarding its role in the Middle East peace process as well as its economic adjustment policies, officials from Jordan and aid agencies said yesterday, writes Reuter from Paris. Planning Minister Ziyad Fariz said after the end of a meeting called by the World Bank that the donors showed 'an appreciation of our role in the Middle East at large, of our role in the peace process.' They were also backing democracy in Jordan, he said.
