910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / UK Company News: BMSS declines sharply to Pounds 1.02m Profits of BMSS, the USM-quoted timber and building materials merchant, fell from Pounds 1.73m to Pounds 1.02m pre-tax for the year to January 31. Turnover improved to Pounds 22.51m (Pounds 13.1m). However, sales volume fell sharply in the latter months of the year from the already depressed levels of the opening six months. The cost of sales doubled to Pounds 16m as did distribution costs to Pounds 4.16m. Earnings per share emerged at 11.3p (16.2p) but a final dividend of 2.7p (2.5p) makes a 4.7p (4.375p) total. Group debt at year-end totalled Pounds 1.9m (Pounds 3.6m), representing 21 per cent of shareholders' funds. The shares closed 4p lower at 110p.
