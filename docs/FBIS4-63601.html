BFN [Text] Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev is setting off today for Tallinn to take part in a session of the Council of Baltic Sea States. According to RIA, the agenda for the forthcoming forum includes increasing economic cooperation among the Baltic countries and promoting democratic institutions, and several other points. The council will also approve the appointment of a commissioner for democratic institutions and human rights. Andrey Kozyrev will also hold consultations with the Estonian leadership on bilateral relations.
