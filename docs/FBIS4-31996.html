BFN [Text] Budapest, 16 Jun (MTI) -- The National Election Committee (NEC) has declared the official final results of the first and second rounds of the general elections, after the Supreme Court approved its decision on modifying the results in the sixth constituency -- Gyor-Moson-Sopron County (W Hungary) -- where Gyorgy Rasko (HDF) finished in first place after the re-count, instead of Gyula Varga (AFD). Thus, the Hungarian Democratic Forum receives one more seat (bringing the total to 38) in Hungary's 386-seat National Assembly, while the Alliance of Free Democrats has one seat less (total: 69).
