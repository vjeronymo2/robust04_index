920924 FT 24 SEP 92 / Observer: Unusual move Saudi Arabia's celebrations of the 60th anniversary of a unified state put a modern gloss on the old adage, plus ca change. A blitz of publicity material mailed to supposedly influential Brits rejoices under the slogan: 'Sixty years of progress, without change.' Riyadh watchers eagerly awaiting news of King Fahd's next move towards constitutional reform, take note.
