931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Mexican shares pared early losses on a late surge of buying by foreign investors. The IPC index, after the market's recent rise to record levels, finished only 2.06 points down at 2,384.08. Just before midday, the market troughed at 2,361.67, a setback of 24.47 points or 1.02 per cent. A total of 136.9m shares changed hands.
