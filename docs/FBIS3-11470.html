Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] At this very moment at Planalto Palace, President Itamar Franco and U.S. Vice President Al Gore are signing a letter of intent reactivating the science and technology agreement already signed between the two countries. This agreement will allow Brazil to purchase supercomputers specialized in nuclear energy calculations, and also in rocket manipulation. Since President Bill Clinton's installment a little over a year ago, Al Gore has been the highest U.S. official to visit Brazil.
