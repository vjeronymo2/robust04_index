Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Panamanian economic developments monitored through 22 March. Francia de Sierra, director general of the Mineral Resources Directorate at the Ministry of Commerce and Industries, revealed the mining industry has invested $24 million this year, whereas in 1993 the investment was $5 million. Sierra added that production at the Santa Rosa mine, Canazas jurisdiction, Veraguas Province, will begin in June; this is expected to increase investments. Referring to the mining industry's future, she said a geologic study and an infrastructure inventory will be carried out in April. The study will cost between $10 million and $15 million and the cost of its feasibility study -- $450,000 -- will be paid with a donation from the Inter-American Development Bank. (Panama City LA PRENSA in Spanish 9 Mar 94 p 11A) ``Delia de De Castro, director general of domestic trade,'' reported 556 licenses with an invested capital of $1,674,500 were issued in January. She added that this represents $20,401 in taxes. She said this is a 15 percent increase compared with January 1993, when the Ministry issued 480 permits for an invested capital of $1,909,650. (Panama City EL PANAMA AMERICA in Spanish 15 Mar 94 p 11A) The Panamanian Government has approved Executive Decree No. 19 to confirm a 23 cent discount in the tariff for each box of bananas exported in 1994. The report adds that 38,255,291 boxes of bananas were exported in 1993, thus allowing the discount anticipated in Article 1 of Law 16, dated 18 June 1993. (Panama City LA PRENSA in Spanish 20 Mar 94 p 2) Ricardo Duran, president of the Panamanian Chamber of Commerce, Industries, and Agriculture, has stated that growth levels will continue to rise although the country is engaged in an electoral process. A survey conducted by the chamber's Studies Center states the Panamanian economy registered stable growth levels, and the GNP attained a 6.6 percent growth rate. (Panama City EL SIGLO in Spanish 21 Mar 94 p 2)
