BFN [Text] The main purpose of the visit to Tajikistan by the President of the Russian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs is participation in a congress of the Tajik Union of Science and Industry on Wednesday [25 May]. He disclosed this to INTERFAX on Monday in Dushanbe where he arrived as the head of a 23 person delegation. Volskiy thinks the contacts of industrialists are now extremely necessary for both Russia and Tajikistan because there is a burning need to discuss further cooperation and begin seeking a way out of the crisis. "We cannot help it, because we would find life difficult without each other," Volskiy said. Asked about the expected results of the working visit Volskiy said an agreement with the Tajik union was drafted and Russian businessmen would gladly sign it, if Tajik colleagues approved it. The visit will last till 26 May. On Tuesday Volskiy is to meet the chairman of parliament Emomali Rakhmonov.
