941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Hydra-Co Enterprises, International Energy Partners, US Energy Corporation, Precursor Systems - Contracts and ventures Hydra-Co Enterprises, International Energy Partners, US Energy Corporation and Precursor Systems have closed financing for their 60MV slow-speed diesel generating facility to be located in Kingston, Jamaica. The Dollars 138m plant will supply more than 10 per cent of Jamaica's electricity needs and is the largest private foreign investment in Jamaica in more than a decade.
