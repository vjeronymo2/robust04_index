Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 14 TASS -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin has met heads of executive power bodies from republics within the Russian Federation, territories, regions, autonomous formations and the cities of Moscow and St. Petersburg, head of the government press service Valentin Sergeyev said here today. On proposal of Viktor Chernomyrdin, the meeting set up a commission to elaborate new principles of social-economic policy and forms of its implementation in Russian regions. The commission will be headed by Vice-Premier Yuriy Yarov and include Sergey Shakhray and representatives of all regional associations. The commission will work to elaborate efficient mechanisms of interaction between the government and federal and regional authorities on principle of greater independence and expansion of rights of administration heads. The commission's proposals will be considered by the government and reported to the Russian president. The meeting showed Federation Council deputies are ready for active cooperation with the government of Viktor Chernomyrdin.
