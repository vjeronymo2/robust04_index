931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / Observer: Mixed grille Spare a tear for the directors who waved goodbye to their company cars last year. An Institute of Directors and Reward Group survey on director's pay discloses that the number of directors with company cars is down this year to 63 per cent from last year's 79 per cent. As those who dream of Direct Line salaries kiss goodbyes to their four-wheeled friends, they can perhaps take comfort from the fact that some standards are not falling; the Jaguar is still the director's favourite, followed by Mercedes.
