940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Jackson up 32% Jackson Group, the construction concern, saw pre-tax profits rise by 32 per cent from Pounds 236,000 to Pounds 311,000 in the first half of 1994. Turnover on continuing operations grew 27 per cent to Pounds 33.5m. Earnings per share improved to 0.9p (0.7p) and an unchanged interim dividend of 0.5p has been declared.
