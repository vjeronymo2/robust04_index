Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Decree issued by President Leonid Kravchuk; date not given] [Text] With the aim of stimulating activity to provide the general population with fuel and lubricants and establish the rules of privatizing gas stations that sell fuel and lubricants specifically to the general population and the plots of land these stations are situated on, and in compliance with item seven of Article 114/5 of the Constitution of Ukraine, I decree: To carry out privatization of gas stations that sell fuel and lubricants specifically to the general population. Along with the privatization of the gas stations, the land on which they are situated will also be privatized. The right to the privatization of the gas stations and plots of land is vested in the citizens of Ukraine, foreign citizens, persons without citizenship, legal entities registered on the territory of Ukraine whose authorized fund contains no share of state property, and legal entities of foreign countries. A decision on the privatization of gas stations is taken by the State Property Fund of Ukraine, its regional branches whose representations, with the participation of the State Committee of Ukraine for Land Resources and its bodies in the regions. These decisions are, at the same time, decisions on the privatization of plots of land. The privatization of gas stations and plots of land will be carried out by auction.
