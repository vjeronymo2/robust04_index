BFN [Text] President Hashemi-Rafsanjani, in a gathering of the central council of the Islamic Coalition Society [ICS--Jam'iyyat-e Mo'talefe-ye Eslami], described action based on the axis of velayat-e faqih [guardianship of the jurisconsult] and following the guidelines of the exalted leadership [Khamene'i] at this juncture in society to be a duty for officials, the people, and various formations of the community. Praising the lofty character of the martyrs of ICS, the president said: What guarantees the correctness and soundness of the activities of a formation is its adherence to the principle of velayat-e faqih and giving preference to the interests of the Islamic system as opposed to personal and factional interests. The ICS has maintained its adherence to that important principle since its inception. Referring to the fact that Iran is known as a wealthy state in the world, Hashemi-Rafsanjani laid emphasis on the need for endeavor, planning, and sincere cooperation among each and every one of the people and said: Thus, we will prove to the world that a nation is able to make up for past underdevelopment and to correct past wrongful policies through reliance on its financial and human resources while preserving its holy, Islamic, noble, and national values and aspirations. The realization of that import by the end of the second development plan is not hard to expect.
