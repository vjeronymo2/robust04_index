Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] According to our station reporter's dispatch from the press conference sponsored by Xingtai City Lianhe Airline on 26 January, the Xingtai airport will open new flights to the cities of Suzhou and Wuxi of Jiangsu Province on 23 February. These new flights will establish a bridge between Xingtai and the middle and lower reaches of the Chang Jiang. The opening of new flights will exert active influence on broadening the economic, cultural, and technical exchanges among different regions and on improving the investment environment of south Hebei.
