September 15, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

TRAINS CALLED LOUD

Imust disagree with Debra Cohen ("Plug for Light Rail," Aug. 25) that a "light-rail system is engineered to be clean and quiet."

The Blue Line trains have been equipped with VERY loud air horns, which are sounded with traditional long-long-short-long blasts at each grade crossing.

Ms. Cohen is invited to try to carry on a conversation or watch television in a living room with Blue Line trains passing 12 times an hour. They are NOT quiet.

JERRY MARTZ

Long Beach
