940209 FT 09 FEB 94 / World News in Brief: Witold Lutoslawski dies Polish composer Witold Lutoslawski died in Warsaw aged 81. His work, which was once banned by the former communist government, includes symphonies, concertos for cello and for oboe and harp, and settings of French verse to music including Three Poems of Henri Michaux in 1963 and Les Espaces du Sommeil in 1975.
