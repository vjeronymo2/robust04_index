920916 FT 16 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Trafford Park edges ahead to Pounds 3.6m Trafford Park Estates, the Manchester-based property investment and development group, reported a near-10 per cent advance in annual profits. The pre-tax line for the 12 months to June 30 amounted to Pounds 3.63m, against Pounds 3.3m last time which included Pounds 743,000 from property sales. Gross income totalled Pounds 11.7m (Pounds 13.1m). An unchanged final dividend of 1.725p maintains the total at 2.575p, covered 1.6 times by earnings of 3.8p (3.6p). An extraordinary gain of Pounds 221,000 related to a pension refund following a restructuring of the company's scheme.
