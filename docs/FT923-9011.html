920807 FT 07 AUG 92 / Olympic News in Brief: Sweden wins table tennis title Sweden's Jan Ove Waldner kisses his gold medal after his crushing defeat of French world number one Jean-Philippe Gatien in the final of the men's table tennis singles. Waldner beat Gatien in three straight sets with a score of 21-10, 21-18, 25-23. The win by Waldner, who has won the world championship twice, gives Sweden its first gold medal.
