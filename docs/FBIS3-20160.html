Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Stephen Martin, speaker of the Australian House of Representatives, has arrived in Turkey as the guest of Husamettin Cindoruk, speaker of the Turkish Grand National Assembly. The Australian parliamentary delegation and Martin were received by Cindoruk, Istanbul Governor Hayri Kozakcioglu, and other officials at the state guest house of the Istanbul Ataturk Airport. Martin said that during their six-day visit in Turkey they will hold beneficial contacts to further strengthen the bilateral ties of friendship. He noted that there are over 100,000 Turks in Australia and that, therefore, Turkey is important for his country. Cindoruk said that a friendship that is being strengthened each passing day has been established between the two countries. He concluded by expressing the wish that the Australian delegation will leave Turkey with the best of impressions.
