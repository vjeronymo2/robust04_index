BFN [Text] Cuba's ambassador to the Philippines, Jose Luis Martin Portal, had been found dead at his Manila home, apparently from cardiac arrest. He was 49. The Philippines' Foreign Office said Portal's body was found on Sunday evening [19 June] by his household staff, who called the police. Portal's wife and children were in Havana, and the envoy was living alone in the exclusive North Forbes Park district in Metro Manila. Police said they found no sign of foul play. Portal assumed his Manila post in October 1990.
