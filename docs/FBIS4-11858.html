BFN [Address by Tunisian President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali to Arab Magreb Union summit in Tunis on 3 April -- live or recorded] [Excerpts] In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate; Your Excellencies, at a time when the proceedings of our council are about to be concluded, I am pleased to express to you once more our pride at hosting this Maghreb meeting in which we have changed viewpoints on our joint Maghreb action, on the most efficient means to give a boost to the march of our Arab Maghreb, and on the most prominent matters and international issues. [passage omitted] There is no doubt that any progress on the path of building a unified Maghreb is liable to add strength and competence to the economies of our countries for integration and positive interaction with the international economic community and achieve more equilibrium in the exchange relations with the large economic groups and particularly those with which we have traditional relations in our immediate neighborhood. [passage omitted] At the end of my speech, your excellencies, I am pleased to express on your behalf and in my name most sincere congratulations to His Excellency President Liamine Zeroual, the head of state of the fraternal People's Democratic Republic of Algeria, on his taking over the Presidency of the Arab Maghreb Union this year. We are convinced that his undertaking these supreme tasks will give our unionist march, thanks to the wisdom and conviction in the Maghreb unity that characterize him, more impetus and progress to the course of building our Arab Maghreb towards achieving the common aspirations of our peoples. May God's peace, mercy, and blessings be upon you. [applause]
