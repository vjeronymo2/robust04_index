920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / World News in Brief: Briton sentenced Briton Paul Ride, 33, from Walthamstow, north-east London, who disappeared on June 28 after leaving a hotel in al-Jahrah, west of Kuwait city, was sentenced in Baghdad to seven years' imprisonment for illegal entry into Iraq, the Foreign Office said. Britain had protested to Iraq 'in the strongest possible terms' and demanded his release.
