November 21, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

COLLEGE FOOTBALL REPORT;

WEEKDAY UPDATE;

AROUND THE NATION;

TV REPORT: KRAGTHORPE TO BE FIRED

Dave Kragthorpe will be fired from his job as head coach of the Oregon State football team, a Portland television station reported.

KOIN-TV, citing unnamed sources close to the university administration, said the firing would be announced today after school Athletic Director Dutch Baughman meets with Kragthorpe.

Baughman refused to comment. Oregon State went 1-10 this year.

Kragthorpe, 57, has a 17-48-2 record at Oregon State.
