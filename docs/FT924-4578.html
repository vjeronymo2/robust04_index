921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / International Company News: Arco to sell oil and gas sites ARCO, the US oil and gas group, has agreed to sell 146 onshore US oil and gas properties for a total of Dollars 362m. The property disposals are part of Arco's previously- announced programme to reduce overall costs and to focus on core holdings by selling non-strategic assets. Arco said the sales would be part of its plan to sell properties valued nearly Dollars 600m by year-end. The sales will be made in three separate transactions.
