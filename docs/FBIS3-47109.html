Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Phnom Penh AKP January 8 -- A French aircraft began regular commercial flights on Friday following an inauguration ceremony at the international airport of Pochentong. The commencement of flight by the French plane of A.T.R. 72, chartered by the Cambodian Aviation Company at a monthly cost of 238,000 U.S. dollars, is made in accordance with an agreement signed recently in Phnom Penh by the officials of the aviation companies of the two countries, Mr. Tep Hen and Daniel Tomasi. The 70-seat A.T.R. 72 will perform regular local flight from Phnom Penh to the town of Siem Reap where the famous temples of Angkor Wat is located and the other flight from Phnom Penh to Ho Chi Minh City (Vietnam), said Tep Hen.
