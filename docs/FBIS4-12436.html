BFN [Text] Cairo, 8 Apr (MENA)--Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat left Cairo this afternoon following a three-day visit during which he met President Husni Mubarak and discussed the latest developments in the Palestinian issue and consulted on the situation before the handover of self-rule in Gaza and Jericho. 'Arafat also met Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd- al-Majid and reviewed with him the latest developments in the Palestinian arena. The Palestinian president also held a series of meetings with members of the Palestinian delegation, Palestinian military commanders, and delegations from Hebron and Gaza.
