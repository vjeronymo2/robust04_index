930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Throgmorton 1000 net assets ahead Throgmorton 1000 Smallest Companies Trust, which came to the market in July last year, reported a net asset value of 118.4p per share at the end of June. Assuming exercise of warrants the figure was 115.3p. Directors said the undiluted net asset value had improved to 119.3p by July 31. Attributable revenue for the 64 weeks amounted to Pounds 1.06m, equivalent to earnings of 3.29p per share. A dividend of 2.5p is recommended.
