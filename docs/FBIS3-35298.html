Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In Sidon today, Palestinian Muhammad Sa'id al-Sayigh was killed and his daughters Ruwaydah and Rana wounded when explosives placed under the driver's seat in his Toyota went off. There was an estimated half a kg of explosives. Our correspondent learned that the crime was committed for moral reasons and that the deceased had distributed pornographic films that prompted opposition from Islamic groups in Sidon.
