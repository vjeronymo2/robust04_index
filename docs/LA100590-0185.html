October 5, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

PEREZ-ROLDAN UPSET AT ATHENS

Franco Davin of Argentina used an overpowering backhand to upset countryman Guillermo Perez-Roldan 6-3, 6-1 today and move into the semifinals of the Athens International Championship.

Marian Vadja of Czechoslavakia continued his surprising advance with a 6-3, 6-3 victory over Spain's Tomas Carbonell.

Mark Koevermans of the Netherlands and Jordi Arrese of Spain also advanced to the semifinals.
