931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / International Company News: Food pushes Hunt Leuchars ahead IMPROVED performances from its sugar and poultry investments helped Hunt Leuchars & Hepburn (HLH), the food and timber company in the Rembrandt group, record a 36 per cent increase in attributable earnings to R32m (Dollars 9.5m), compared with R23.6m a year ago, in the six months to September, writes Philip Gawith. Earnings per share rose to 21.5 cents from 15.8 cents and the interim dividend was maintained at 13.5 cents.
