July 7, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CHRISTOPHER COOGAN; YOUNGEST SON OF ACTOR

Christopher Coogan, 22, youngest son of actor Jackie Coogan. His father, who as a child appeared in Charlie Chaplin's 1921 film "The Kid" and later played Uncle Fester in television's "The Addams Family," died in 1984. Near Palm Springs June 29 in a motorcycle accident.
