BFN [Text] Gaza -- Dr. Mahmud al-Zahhar, yesterday denied having any knowledge of news agency reports that an agreement had been signed between Fatah and Hamas in the Gaza Strip. He accused Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin of raising such allegations in a bid to promote dubious aims. Al-Zahhar said that relations between Fatah and Hamas were normal and that the meetings between the two sides are merely aimed at resolving some outstanding problems. Regarding the arrival of the international observer forces to Hebron, al-Zahhar said: I still believe that this is an international trick to draw the Palestinians to the negotiating table. This will not change anything on the ground.
