BFN [Text] Odessa, 20 May -- Colonel Vyacheslav Voronkov, press secretary of the Odessa military district commander, refuted reports by the "Ostankino" TV company about Ukrainian troops movements, which took place in Crimea. He said information about the movement of Ukrainian tanks in the vicinity of the village of Tylovoye, about the deployment of a field hospital in the village of Chernobayevka, and about the movement of the special purpose troops do not correspond to reality. The 32d Army Corps also remains in its deployment site. A routine inspection is being carried out in the Odessa military district units. A similar inspection will get under way in Crimea on 23 May.
