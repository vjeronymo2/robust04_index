Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] We just received a report noting that, supported by tanks and various types of armored cars, Azerbaijani troops launched an offensive towards Fizuli in the southeastern sector of Karabakh war front at 0515 this morning. They managed to advance. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] Defense Army press service reported at noon that fierce battles were still continuing in this sector of the front. The NKR Defense Army command ordered its troops to take the necessary measures to foil the enemy's attacks and recover their former positions. The NKR Foreign Ministry has stated that all responsibility for the consequences of such actions fully rests with the Azerbaijani side, which is methodically violating the cease-fire agreement achieved through the mediatory efforts of the Russian Defense Ministry. The Armenian Defense Ministry press service reports that relative calm now prevails on the republic's borders.
