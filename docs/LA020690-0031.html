February 6, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Stephen A. Crisham has been appointed vice president, acquisition/information services for Geneva Business Services. Before joining Geneva in 1985 as marketing manager, Crisham was marketing manager of Scott Foresman & Co., a Glenview, Ill., publisher of educational materials. Geneva, based in Irvine, is a merger and acquisition services company.
