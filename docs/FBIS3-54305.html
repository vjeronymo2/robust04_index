Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Dr. Khalid al-Karaki, adviser to His Majesty King Husayn, today returned to Amman from Sanaa following a one-day visit to Yemen, during which he met with Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, the Yemeni Presidential Council chairman, and 'Ali Salim al-Bid, the vice chairman. He conveyed a message to them from His Majesty King Husayn concerning the brotherly ties between the two countries and the next stage in the implementation of the agreement reached by the national dialogue committee that represents the Yemeni political forces to guarantee an end to all differences and protect Yemen's unity and the march of national unity and democracy. The Yemeni president and vice president voiced appreciation for His Majesty King Husayn and his efforts to put an end to the crisis in Yemen.
