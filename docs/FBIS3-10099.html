Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] At a ceremony at Los Pinos, the official residence of Mexican President Carlos Salinas de Gortari, Salinas received the credentials of Cuba's new ambassador to Mexico, Abelardo Curbelo. In statements to the press, Curbelo stressed the Cuban Government's interest in expanding ties with Mexico. Curbelo said: We must work at finding formulas for doing this. He said, further: It is necessary to develop existing agreements between the two nations and seek ways to expand them, especially in areas of mutual interest such as tourism and industry, along with a greater presence of Mexican capital in Cuba.
