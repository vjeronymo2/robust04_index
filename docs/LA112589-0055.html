November 25, 1989, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

WITNESSES SOUGHT TO FATAL HIT-RUN

Police are looking for witnesses to the hit-and-run death of a 19-year-old San Diego man who was struck and killed by an unidentified driver late Thursday.

The body of Lemoe Tauanuu was found in the 8300 block of Skyline Drive by police at 9:10 p.m., police spokesman Bill Robinson said.

Police said the victim was lying in the road when he was struck. Authorities did not know how Tauanuu came to be in the road. Other drivers avoided the victim before he was hit by the third, who did not stop, traffic investigator Oscar Johnson said.
