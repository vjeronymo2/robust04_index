931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / UK Company News In Brief Adwest Group ADWEST GROUP has acquired Rousseau, a French maker of jacks for the automotive industry, for Pounds 1.1m in cash. ALVIS is paying an initial Pounds 600,000 cash for Morfax's worldwide business in Wheelbarrow explosive ordnance disposal robots. A further consideration, to a maximum Pounds 1.4m, is sales-related.
