Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias today met German Deputy Foreign Minister Helmut Schaefer. During the talks, they ascertained that they have similar views and the German deputy foreign minister described allegations of tense Greek-German relations as rumors. The two officials discussed the former Yugoslavia, particularly the Bosnian problem, and the Cyprus issue. Asked about Skopje, the German deputy minister said Greece and Skopje should talk to solve their problems. He also supported that sentimentality, as he described it, should at last be removed from this issue.
