930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Blockleys Blockleys, the Shropshire-based brick and pavior manufacturer, reported a 36 per cent decline in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 410,000 to Pounds 261,000, for the six months to end-June. The outcome was struck on turnover down by Pounds 391,000 to Pounds 4.75m and was bolstered by a Pounds 46,000 surplus from a property sale and a reduced interest charge of Pounds 467,000 (Pounds 626,000). Earnings per share fell to 0.71p (0.99p) but the interim dividend is unchanged at 0.5p.
