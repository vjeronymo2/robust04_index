June 6, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Burbank-based NBC correspondent Dan Molina, who worked at the network's Houston bureau until it was closed last year, has been reassigned again, this time as NBC's Chicago bureau chief, the network said Monday. Jim Cummins, who had held the Chicago post, will become NBC's Southwest Bureau chief and correspondent when NBC decides where that new bureau (formerly in Houston) will be located. ALEENE MacMINN
