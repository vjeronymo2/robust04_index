BFN [Text] Guangzhou, June 19 (XINHUA) -- Yi Meihou, renowned patriotic Overseas Chinese leader, vice president of the All-China Federation of Returned Overseas Chinese, and former vice chairman of the Guangdong Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee, died of sickness on 29 May at the age of 86. A ceremony to pay last respects to Yi Meihou's remains was held in Guangzhou on 18 June. Sending cables, letters, or wreaths to express condolences were Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, Qiao Shi, Liu Huaqing, Tian Jiyun, Li Lanqing, Li Tieying, Yang Baibing, Qian Qichen, Xie Fei, Yang Shangkun, Lei Jieqiong, Wu Jieping, Wang Zhaoguo, Henry Ying Tung Fok [Huo Yingdong], and Ma Man-kei. Attending the ceremony were a few hundred people including Ye Xuanping, vice chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference's National Committee, and celebrities from relevant departments of the central authorities, Guangdong Province, Hong Kong, Macao, and abroad.
