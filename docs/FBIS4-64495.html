BFN [Text] The consultations over the make-up of the cabinet will end on 6 May. On 7-8 May, "the situation will be examined by the members of the Freedom Alliance," and on 8 May, Prime Minister Silvio Berlusconi will break his silence. Berlusconi is being cautious over the timetable and is denying that there are obstacles concerning the possible make-up of the government: "I believe that there will not be any opposition." The government's program is "very advanced" and will only be revealed when it will be presented to the Chamber of Deputies. He also confirmed that there would be a meeting this evening at 2100 hours between the heads of the parties in the Freedom Alliance. Umberto Bossi will also be present at the meeting.
