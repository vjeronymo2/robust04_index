Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Tirol's SPOe Leaders Resign"] [Excerpt] On Tuesday afternoon [15 March] Hans Tanzer, chairman of the Tirolean Social Democratic Party [SPOe] and deputy provincial governor, announced his resignation during a meeting of the SPOe Diet group in Innsbruck. In the morning Walter Hengl, the second SPOe member of the Tirolean Government and provincial councilor for health and social matters, had already announced his departure from politics and had suggested Nationalrat deputy Walter Guggenberger as his successor. Tanzer resigned from all his posts during the meeting. Regarding a successor for Tanzer, the vote taken in the diet group between Provincial Party Secretary Herbert Prock and Nationalrat Deputy Walter Guggenberger led to a stalemate with both receiving seven votes. A definitive decision is to be made by the Provincial Party Executive Committee during a meeting on Monday evening. [passage omitted]
