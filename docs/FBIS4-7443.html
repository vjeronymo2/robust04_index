BFN ["Statement referring to the amendment of the Serbian Citizens Council" adopted by the Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly in Sarajevo on 30 March; read by Mariofil Ljubic, deputy chairman of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly -- live] [Text] Mr. President, Esteemed Deputies, I would like to read the text of a statement: The initiative of the deputies (?launched) at the session yesterday that the Assembly of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina support the declaration of the congress of citizens of Serbian nationality in Bosnia-Herzegovina was supported by all deputy caucuses of the parties represented in the parliament and the Constitutional Commission. The Assembly of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina is counseled to adopt the following statement: The Assembly of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina supports the positions of the congress of the citizens of Serbian nationality in Bosnia-Herzegovina adopted at the session held on 27 March 1994 and expressed in its declaration. The Assembly requests that the state delegations of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina, in the continuation of peace negotiations on Bosnia-Herzegovina, insist on their implementation when the final decision is made on the constitutional-legal set-up of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina at the peace conference on Bosnia-Herzegovina. I propose that we accept this statement.
