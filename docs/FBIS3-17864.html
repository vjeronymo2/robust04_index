Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Mar 29, BNS -- A Lithuanian consul and a vice consul on Tuesday [29 March] began work in the town of Kaliningrad, which is the administrative center of the Kaliningrad region. The Lithuanian consulate in Kaliningrad is headed by Sigitas Sileris, who until now worked as director of the Klaipeda seaport in Lithuania. Raimondas Sivys, Lithuanian vice consul in Kaliningrad, has been replaced by Algirdas Kasperunas, who previously headed the press center of the Lithuanian parliament.
