921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Observer: Blooming loo Whereas the French have Gabriel Chevallier's novel Clochemerle, the Brits have a Loo of the Year competition. Number One in the 1992 rankings, just announced is the lavatories at the M3 motorway service station at Fleet in Hampshire. In singling them out as 'a pleasure to judge', however, the awarding panel may have fingered the staff of attendants for a sex discrimination charge. One of the things they are commended for is 'putting fresh flowers in the ladies'.
