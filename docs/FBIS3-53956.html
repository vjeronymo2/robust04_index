Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan 20, IRNA -- The fourth congress of Hizballah of Iraq opened here Thursday on the fourth martyrdom anniversary of Iraq's prominent clergyman Hojatoleslam Mahdi Hakim. The congress is attended by a number of distinguished Iraqi political and religious figures who are to assess the present situation in Iraq and ways to help the Iraqi people. Addressing the session, head of the congress Shaykh Asad Silli, said that the objective is to discuss the latest developments in Iraq, propose ways of consolidating ranks of the Iraqi opposition and mobilize all resources to support the Iraqi oppressed people.
