940921 FT 21 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Wolstenholme Rink up 47% Wolstenholme Rink, the manufacturer and supplier of printing materials, saw pre-tax profits rise 47 per cent from Pounds 2.02m to Pounds 2.96m in the six months to June 30. The result came on the back of turnover up 23 per cent from Pounds 26.6m to Pounds 32.7m. Earnings per share were up 51 per cent to 25.3p (16.8p). The interim dividend is 7.8p (6.8p). The share price closed 38p up at 808p.
