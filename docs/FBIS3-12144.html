Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Kuwait, 28 Feb -- A security source at the Interior Ministry announced here this morning that a bomb has exploded at the entrance of Granada cinema in Khaytan area west of the city of Kuwait. In a statement received by KUNA the source explained that "the explosion resulted in the injury of two people and smashed glass in the building" The source pointed out that the competent authorities in the Interior Ministry have initiated investigation to learn the circumstances that led to the incident. The source appealed to whoever has any information about the incident to immediately contact the nearest police station to give information to facilitate the task of the security apparatus in identifying the perpetrators. The security source, at the end of its statement, expressed its indignation at this criminal act, which has targeted the lives of the innocent. The source expressed the hope that the security services, with the cooperation of the citizens and those who were at the cinema, would be able to apprehend the perpetrator as quickly as possible.
