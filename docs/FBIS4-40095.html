BFN [Report by Vladimir Berezko under the "KRASNAYA ZVEZDA and News Agency Correspondents Report" rubric: "Strong `Barrier' to Smuggler Will Be Erected"] [Text] Russian Federation Border Troops -- Colonel General Andrey Nikolayev, commander in chief of the Border Troops, indicated at a news conference: "Within the framework of Operation Putina [fishing] our state has shown that it has something to defend and the people to defend it with." In addition to Putina in the Far East, as of 20 June the border guards together with interested departments are commencing Operation Zaslon [barrier] in the Sea of Azov, the Barents Sea, the White Sea, the Baltic, the Black Sea, the Caspian, on the Amur and Ussuri Rivers, and on Lake Peipus. Col. Gen. Andrey Nikolayev also reported that the killers of border guard officers in Tajikistan have been detained.
