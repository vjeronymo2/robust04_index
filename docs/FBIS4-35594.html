BFN [Text] Ahmad Muhammad al-Mutawakkil, the new Yemeni ambassador to Lebanon, arrived in Beirut this evening from Yemen via Amman. He was welcomed at the airport by Ambassador Ramiz Dimashqiyah, protocol director of the Foreign Ministry, and some staff members of the Yemeni embassy. The new Yemeni ambassador is expected to submit a copy of his credentials to Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz next week, and to submit his credentials to President Ilyas al-Hirawi at a later date.
