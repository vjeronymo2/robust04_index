940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Brown & Jackson Pounds 7m stores deal Brown & Jackson, owner of the Poundstretcher discount retail chain, has exchanged contracts with British Aerospace Pension Fund for the sale and leaseback of 18 Poundstretcher stores and another shop for a total Pounds 7.1m. Five of the properties are in Scotland, 12 in northern England and two in southern England. The total rental commitment gives a net purchase yield of 10.2 per cent. Brown & Jackson will receive about Pounds 5.9m net on completion.
