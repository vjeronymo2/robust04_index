930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / Gestetner gives profit warning The cost of reducing its workforce and difficulty in letting surplus property would have a negative effect on short-term profits at Gestetner Holdings, Mr Basil Sellers, chairman told the annual meeting. He added that in the first quarter of the current year there had been increased pressure on machine sales in Europe, which accounted for 55 per cent of the total. Trading had been particularly disappointing in France, Italy and Spain.
