931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / UK Company News: F&C German net asset value at 137p Foreign & Colonial German Investment Trust had a net asset value of 137.2p per share at September 30, up from 121.5p at the March year-end and 105.2p at end-September 1992. Attributable revenue for the trust's first half dipped to Pounds 277,000 (Pounds 320,000) for earnings of 0.71p (0.91p) per share.
