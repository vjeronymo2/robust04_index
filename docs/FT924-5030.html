921128 FT 28 NOV 92 / Markets: New retirement guide - At a Glance A new edition of the Good Retirement Guide has just been published. It includes chapters on financial planning, pensions, healthcare and investment options. It also covers hobbies, how to start a business and how to look for paid or voluntary work. The Good Retirement Guide, Rosemary Brown, (Kogan Page, Pounds 13.99, 488 pages).
