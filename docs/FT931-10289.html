930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / UK Company News: NEI preference share decision Following a formal objection to Northern Engineering Industries' petition for confirmation by the Court of the cancellation and repayment of its 3 per cent, 5.375 per cent and 11 per cent preference shares the judge has reserved judgment. Accordingly, the timetable for the cancellation and repayment of the preference shares has been extended.
