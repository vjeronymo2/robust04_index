BFN [Text] Moscow April 5 TASS -- "I am convinced that the agreement on civic accord In Russia is possible and necessary for all of us, both politicians and Russian citizens," says a message of Russian President Boris Yeltsin sent today to the parliament speakers, possible signatories to the agreement and mass media bodies. "The agreement shows that our new political system is consolidating, we are turning more powerful and sincere and political ambitions and interests have less power on us. "However, the improvement, adoption and implementation of the agreement can be hampered: Mutual mistrust and striving for victories at the expense of the opponent are still great. This cannot be forgotten, if we really want to achieve accord and civic peace in Russia," stressed the message.
