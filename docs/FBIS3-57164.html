Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Russian Defense Ministry has held an expanded interdepartmental conference to discuss a concept of military-legal reform for the Russian Armed Forces. It was noted during the conference that the purposes, goals, and stages of a military-legal reform should fit the concept for the structure of the country's armed forces and the principles of a democratic, law-based state. Therefore, they will be drawn up in accordance with the Constitution and the Russian military doctrine. It is planned to complete the drafting of a concept for military-legal reform in June 1994.
