931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / Rolls-Royce to shed more jobs Rolls-Royce, the aero-engine and industrial power group, is to shed an additional 1,000 jobs in its UK aerospace operations next year because of continuing difficult trading conditions in both its civil and defence aero-engine businesses. The latest restructuring will increase the total number of job cuts in its aerospace activities to 3,000 next year on top of the 3,000 jobs it is losing this year.
