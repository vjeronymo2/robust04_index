Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] On 15 March at the Boyana residence, Evgeni Matinchev, deputy chairman of the Council of Ministers, and Industry Minister Rumen Bikov met Jovan Andonov, deputy prime minister for Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM]. The possibility of transit transportation of Macedonian goods through Bulgaria, cooperation between our enterprises and Skopje companies, and the possibility of granting help to Macedonia were discussed. The work of the two delegations continues in specialized commissions, where issues related to bilateral cooperation in transportation, industry, customs control, and road construction were specified.
