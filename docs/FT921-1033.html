920326 FT 26 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Town Centre Secs improves to Pounds 3.31m Town Centre Securities, the property investment and development group, increased pre-tax profits from Pounds 3.01m to Pounds 3.31m in the half year to December 31. Gross rental and investment income in the six months rose from Pounds 7.61m to Pounds 8.39m. Earnings were up from 2.01p to 2.21p and the interim dividend goes up from 0.9p to 1p. Mr Arnold Ziff, chairman, said that the Park Square office development in Leeds was now complete and 80 per cent let, and that work had commenced on the refurbishment of the Cookridge Street property in partnership with Leeds City Development.
