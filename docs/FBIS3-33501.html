Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Events" newscast] [Text] The government and El Chacal [Jose Angel Talavera] will hold a decisive meeting on Thursday in Caulatu. The preparations for the high-level meeting, which will determine the disarmament of El Chacal, were made yesterday afternoon by Sergio Caramagna, mediator and coordinator of CIAV-OAS [International Commission of Support and Verification-OAS] and Government Minister Alfredo Mendieta. The meeting with El Chacal is scheduled to be held at noon today [as heard] at Caulatu, the same place where the rearmed group last year kidnapped two Sandinist deputies and several government officials. Yesterday, the Army withdrew all of its troops from the area to facilitate the meeting. In addition to Government Minister Alfredo Mendieta, who leads the government delegation, the other people who will attend the meeting are Colonel Javier Carrion, on behalf of the EPS [Sandinist People's Army], while the Catholic Church has appointed Dr. Winston Betanco. Government sources reported the government delegation is fully empowered to solve the conflict with the rearmed men from the 3-80 Northern Front.
