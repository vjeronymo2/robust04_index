Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Another two right-wing extremists in the territories were arrested last night. Kakh spokesman No'am Federman, whom the authorities have decided to place under administrative detention, was captured hiding in a Jerusalem apartment. David Axelrod, the spokesman for the Kahana Hay group, was arrested in the Samaria settlement of Tapuah after refusing to turn in his IDF [Israel Defense Forces] issued weapon. He is also refusing to say where the weapon is hidden. The police are continuing to search for Barukh Marzel and Ben- Tziyon Gobstein. This was reported by our correspondents Aviv Bushinsky and Gil Litman.
