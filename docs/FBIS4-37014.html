BFN [Text] Aden, Jun 16--Eyewitnesses have reported that a northern fighter plane attempted this morning to shell the house of 'Ali Salim al-Bid, secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], in the suburb of Kuraitar, the largest suburb of Aden. The witnesses added that the plane fired one of its missiles at the house of al-Bid, who is currently in Hadramawt governorate. They said that ground defenses fired at the plane, forcing it to flee. The extent of damage to al-Bid's house as a result of the incident was not immediately known.
