BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 28 -- Indonesian President Suharto and Vietnamese President Le Duc Anh held talks in Jakarta yesterday morning. President Suharto warmly welcomed President Le Duc Anh's visit to Indonesia as a very important event marking a new step of development in the relations between the two countries. For his part, President Le Duc Anh highly appreciated the Indonesian Government's policy which attaches great importance to the strengthening of the multi-faceted cooperation with Vietnam and affirmed that Vietnam would closely cooperate with Indonesia in the interests of each country, for peace, stability, cooperation and prosperity in the region. Host and guest discussed orientation and measures aimed at further promoting the traditional friendship and multifaceted cooperation between the two countries. They were unanimous that Vietnam and Indonesia will soon male joint efforts in solving the remaining problems in the spirit of the neighbourly relations and cooperation between the two countries. President Le Duc Anh and his counterpart Suharto also exchanged view on the regional and international issues of common concern. The talks took place in an atmosphere of friendship and mutual understanding.
