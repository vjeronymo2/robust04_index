December 2, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

LOW DOWN PAYMENT

Bruss' article with its get-rich-quick overtone is great. However, the eagle has landed (i.e. nationwide real estate recession).

Whatever happened to the free and clear American dream? Your article and millions of people who believe in this theory are now the new co-stars of 1990's "American Nightmare."

Bruss states, "home buyers should avoid personal liability on their mortgage, just in case property values decline." Brilliant! JAMES D. LEUR

Avalon
