Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] In the wake of Labor Secretary General Nisim Zvili's remarks about a Palestinian state, NRP [National Religious Party] and Tzomet officials said today that it is preferable to influence the government policy from within the government, rather than through demonstrations and protests. There is talk in these parties about joining the government following the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] withdrawal from Gaza and Jericho. Minister Ya'aqov Tzur is liaising between the Labor and these parties. [passage omitted]
