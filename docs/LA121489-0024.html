December 14, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

FUR COATS

Everyone has a right to wear a fur coat. However, before purchasing that fur, everyone has an obligation to be fully informed of how a fur coat is made. I can assure you that after viewing, personally or by videotape (with sound intact), how one captures, raises and skins an animal, one would think twice about wearing a fur coat. After all, we are suppose to be intelligent beings.

Let's keep the fur where it belongs-on the animals.

KADY LANE

Pacific Palisades
