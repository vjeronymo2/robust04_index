BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Albert Kochetkov] [Text] Tbilisi April 26 TASS -- The Georgian parliament today appointed Lieutenant-General of the Russian Armed Forces Vardiko Nadibaidze, a Georgian by nationality, to the post of Georgian defence minister. Nadibaidze had formerly served as assistant commander of the group of Russian troops in Transcaucasia. His candidature was proposed by Eduard Shevardnadze, head of the Georgian state, and approved by the overwhelming majority of the georgian parliament. Nadibaidze was born in Georgia. He is 55 years old, thirty-eight of which were devoted to military service.
