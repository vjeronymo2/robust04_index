Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina arrived in Zambia as part of a tour of Africa. The foreign minister has already visited Ghana, Angola, and Zimbabwe. Robaina traveled to Lusaka from Harare, where he visited for three days and met with Zimbabwean authorities. During his stay in Zambia, Robaina plans to meet with Zambian President Frederick Chiluba and other government authorities. The foreign minister also plans to visit Windhoek, Namibia, where he will meet with President Sam Nujoma. Namibia is scheduled to be the last stop of his first tour of Africa, which will end on Friday.
