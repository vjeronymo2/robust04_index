BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 14 (KCNA) -- The Pyongyang-visiting group of former heads of state and government and politicians from different countries led by ex-President of Costa Rica Rodrigo Carazo Odio visited Mangyongdae Wednesday. At the old home in Mangyongdae where the great leader President Kim Il-song was born and spent his childhood, the guests saw the relics with keen interest, hearing the immortal story about this historical home. After going round the revolutionary sites on the Mangyong hill, former member of the parliament of France and member of the European Parliament Pierro Ceyrac said Mangyongdae is a very beautiful place. "The Korean people look up to, revere and follow President Kim Il- song because he has performed great feats for them, always finding himself among them," he noted. At the construction site of the Nungna suspension bridge which is being built by Korean People's Army soldiers, former President of Costa Rica Rodrigo Carazo Odio congratulated the soldiers participating in construction for the welfare of the people. He expressed the belief that the Korean people would certainly achieve progress. After inspecting the three-revolution exhibition, former Prime Minister of Egypt Aziz Sedky said Korea has built a wonderful independent national economy. He noted that Korea has attained self-sufficiency in nearly all things because its policy is correct. "Agriculture has developed and the people are well off as good policies for the people are pursued in your country," he said, adding: "All the people are evenly off. This is important." After making a round of the Mangyongade School Children's Palace, General of the Army Jeannou Lacaze, former chief of staff of the Armed Forces of France, member of the European Parliament and chairman of the union of independentists, said he had never seen such talented children. "I will remember till the end of my life everything I saw at the palace," he added. President of the Summit Council for World Strategy of Japan Hirogatsu Ota said it is wonderful to bring up the children, regarding them as "kings" of the country. The guests went round the May Day Stadium and Chongchun Street of the capital on the same day.
