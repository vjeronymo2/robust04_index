921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Coventry climbs 10% to Pounds 11.8m Coventry, the sixteenth largest building society, announced pre-tax profits up by 10.3 per cent, from Pounds 10.7m to Pounds 11.8m, in the half year to June 30. Total net income rose from Pounds 26.7m to Pounds 27.8m and the pre-tax result was after a reduction in provisions against loan losses from Pounds 4.42m to Pounds 3.57m, one of the best performances in the industry. Mortgage advances in the period fell from Pounds 257m to Pounds 241m. Total assets rose from Pounds 2.12bn to Pounds 2.5bn. During the half year the society converted Pounds 40m of subordinated debt into permanent interest bearing shares.
