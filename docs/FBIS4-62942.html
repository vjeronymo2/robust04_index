BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow May 19 TASS -- "Russia is in no hurry to join the Council of Europe, but should not be late either", Russian State Duma Speaker Ivan Rybkin said at a meeting with Miguel Martinez, chairman of the Interparliamentary Assembly of the Council of Europe, on Thursday. Martinez, in his turn, said he would like to see Russia as a fully-fledged member of the Council and added that only a few countries still doubt the necessity. He drew Rybkin's attention in this connection to the position of Poland and the Baltic states. Rybkin assured his guest that Russia would withdraw its troops from Germany and the Baltic states "on time".
