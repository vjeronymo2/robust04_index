BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] The Federation Council committee for agrarian policy has described the situation in the Russian agro-industrial complex in 1994 as extreme and catastrophic. In its statement it forecasts a production slump in agricultural enterprises of all types of ownership in 1994. It suggests that compared to average annual figures for 1986-90, production will fall by between 45 and 50 percent. The committee believes that in those conditions emergency economic measures are needed to support the agricultural sector.
