BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan June 13 TASS -- Azerbaijani parliament Chairman Rasul Guliyev called for peaceful settlement of the conflict with Armenia over the disputed enclave of Nagorno-Karabakh. In an interview with Armenian television on Monday, Guliyev said that all occupied territories must be freed and the sides involved should begin negotiations in order to determine Nagorno-Karabakh's status. Guliyev stressed the need to find ways of economic integration. "We are bound to live together. We must not fear each other or fight each other," Guliyev said. He believes that as soon as normal economic relations are restored, "our people will forget the war in six months."
