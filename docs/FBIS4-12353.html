BFN [Text] Cairo, 5 Apr (MENA) -- Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana arrived in Cairo today on an official two-day visit, during which he will head his country's delegation to the meeting of the Joint Higher Egyptian-Spanish Committee. Egyptian Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa will head the Egyptian side to the meeting, which will begin later today. Talks between the two sides began on the level of experts yesterday. During the visit, the Spanish foreign minister will meet with Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid to discuss Arab-Spanish cooperation. He will also meet with a number of Palestinian officials to review the latest developments in the peace talks and the role that Spain and the European Community can play to promote these talks.
