940804 FT 04 AUG 94 / Customs says drugs war is unwinnable The war against drugs can never be won in spite of the seizure of a record Pounds 75m worth of illegal substances at London's airports last year, HM Customs said yesterday. Mr David Chesters, assistant collector for Customs at Gatwick airport, said: 'There is a way to stop drug smugglers and that is stop every single passenger. It's not acceptable to the politicians or to the general public.'
