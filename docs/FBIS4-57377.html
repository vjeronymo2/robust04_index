BFN [Text] A Bhif Bank branch was damaged by a bomb last night. Those responsible for the attack are reportedly members of the MIR [Movement of the Revolutionary Left]. [Begin unidentified reporter recording] The tranquility of Sunday night was abruptly interrupted when a powerful bomb exploded at the Bhif Bank branch on Macul Street near the corner of Irarrazaval Streets. An area of 100 meters, including nearby houses, was damaged. Fortunately, there were no casualties. According to preliminary information, the explosion was caused by a plastic bomb with a high destructive power. The bank branch was considerably damaged. Unofficial reports state that an extremist organization is responsible for the attack. [end recording]
