Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Caracas, 26 Mar (DPA) -- President Rafael Caldera made official on Saturday, 26 March, the release of the main leader of the 4 February 1992 coup attempt, Lieutenant Colonel Hugo Chavez, who during the early morning hours of today also requested his discharge from active duty, Radio Caracas Radio (RCR) confirmed today. Chavez, regarded as the ideologue of a movement of midlevel officers who in 1992 executed two attempts to overthrow then President Carlos Andres Perez, was released after two years in jail when the president dropped the charges on the condition the officers more deeply involved in the coup attempts do not return to the Armed Forces. According to RCR, the lieutenant colonel and other officers will create a new political organization that will be based on the military movement of the nationalist inspiration Chavez created within the Armed Forces and which received the public's support.
