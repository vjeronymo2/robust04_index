920912 FT 12 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Elys (Wimbledon) suffers 40% fall Elys (Wimbledon), the department store, suffered a further setback in the first half with a 40 per cent fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 203,000 to Pounds 121,000. Turnover for the 26 weeks to August 1 was virtually static at Pounds 5.71m. The interim dividend is being maintained at 1.5p, payable from earnings per share of 6.7p (11.2p). The company said trading conditions in the first half had proved difficult, with the continuing recession coupled with job insecurity and falling house prices eroding customer confidence.
