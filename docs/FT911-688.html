910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Mangoes at 75p-Pounds 1.00 each (Pounds 1.00-1.50) are this week's best fruit buy, reports the FFVIB. Avocados from Spain and South Africa at 35-50p each (35-50p), and Cape Golden Delicious apples at 35-45p a lb (55-65p) are all plentiful. English asparagus at Pounds 1.30-1.40 a lb (Pounds 1.30-1.40) is superb quality this week, along with the Spania variety at Pounds 1.25-1.40 a lb (Pounds 1.25-1.40). Cherry tomatoes at Pounds 1.30-1.50 (Pounds 1.30-1.75) and beef varieties at 75p-Pounds 1.20 a lb (Pounds 1.00-1.25) are both abundant. Cucumber at 40-60p each (55-70p), Round lettuce at 20-35p each (25-35p) and Iceberg lettuce at 60-85p each (60-85p) are all good salad buys.
