941203 FT 03 DEC 94 / London Stock Exchange: Equity futures and options trading Stock index futures had a volatile session, ending sharply lower in by far the heaviest volume of the week, writes Jeffrey Brown. The FT-SE 100 December contract was 3,005 at the close of pit trading, down 36 points for a two-day decline of 88 points. On the week the contract is a net 31 points lower. Volume rose steeply from 1pm onwards after the latest US unemployment data. There were 15,919 contracts traded, up from 12,828 on Thursday. Stock option volume was 44,235 lots, compared to 32,365.
