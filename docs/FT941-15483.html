940114 FT 14 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Cap and Regional Capital and Regional Properties has acquired Greencoat House, Kingston upon Thames, from Chartwell Land Investments for Pounds 3.45m. The property, built in 1980, comprises seven shops and more than 16,000 sq ft of offices. Current annual rental income is Pounds 425,000, and is expected to rise to Pounds 450,000.
