BFN [Text] Beijing, June 2 (XINHUA) -- At the invitation of the Chinese National People's Congress Standing Committee, a delegation of the ASEAN Inter-Parliamentary Organization (AIPO) led by Jose de Venecia, speaker of House of Representatives of the Philippines and chairman of the current session of AIPO, will visit China from June 6 to 14. A Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman made the announcement here this afternoon at the weekly press conference.
