Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Major General Ulises Rosales del Toro, first vice minister of the Revolutionary Armed Forces (FAR), said today this corps is in optimum condition to fulfill its mission due to its high cohesion and combative readiness. In a meeting with journalists on the occasion of Cuban Press Day, which will be held on 14 March, Rosales del Toro said that the FAR are in their best stage of development, according to a National Information Agency report. The General Staff chief argued the Armed Forces have a commander in chief, Fidel Castro, who is capable enough to continue leading the revolution, a minister, Raul Castro, who has much experience, and a corps of officers forged by revolutionary struggles. Regarding the education of the new generations, the general cited journalists' role. Rosales del Toro, also a member of the Communist Party of Cuba Politburo, said the press must fight every formality and work for the defense of the country.
