BFN [Article signed UC: "Eight Kilos of Cocain Seized in Chiasso"] [Text] Chiasso, 15 May (ATS/ANSA) -- Eight kilos of cocaine worth 7.2 million Swiss francs were seized during the night from Saturday [14 May] to Sunday [15 May] onboard the Zurich-Rome train at Chiasso station. The drugs were found inside the suitcase of a 28-year-old Polish national, who claimed he did not know what was inside the suitcase. According to the customs service, the cocaine came from Argentina and was bound for Rome. On 24 March, 11 kg of cocaine from Argentina were seized. Three individuals, two Italian brothers and one Latin American national, were arrested in that seizure.
