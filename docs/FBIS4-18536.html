BFN [Text] Silvio Berlusconi has decided to call off negotiations with the Northern League. The meeting scheduled to take place tomorrow has been postponed. "From today, the leader of Forza Italia said during the course of a news conference, we will go neither to the Northern League nor to any other side. We will await the decision of President Oscar Luigi Scalfaro who is scheduled to appoint the next prime minister, and we will be at his disposal in order to form a new government."
