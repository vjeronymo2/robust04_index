February 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

PRESIDENT BUSH ON GUN CONTROL

President Bush is dead wrong in maintaining that the AK-47 is needed by the bigger game hunters. Our wild game is being decimated fast enough without the use of those infernal weapons. A hunter with a high-powered rifle should be satisfied with one shot at his game. If he wants to spray the target with a volley from an AK-47 he is no sportsman at all.

GEORGE L. PETERSON

Laguna Hills
