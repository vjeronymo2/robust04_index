CSO [Text] Bombay, March 2: Officers of the collectorate of customs, Sahar international airport, intercepted a Nigerian passenger departing for Accra via Addis Ababa and seized 1.30 kg of heroin valued at Rs 1.38 lakhs. The heroin was cleverly concealed in the false bottom of a suitcase and was given to him for smuggling by another Nigerian. He was paid of Rs 12,000 and a free air ticket.
