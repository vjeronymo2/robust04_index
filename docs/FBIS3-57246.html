Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 24 Jan -- An armed clash between a local self-defense detachment and a group of about 15 Afghan mojahedin who penetrated from the territory of adjacent Afghanistan, took place on 23 January in the region of the Tajik border village of Yamkun. After losing two men killed and several wounded the mojahedin retreated. During the battle the local population liberated two fellow villagers seized by the Afghans. The authorities of Gorno-Badakhshan and representatives of the headquarters of the command of the Russian border troops in Tajikistan protested to the Afghan side and demanded that the organizers of this provocation are brought to justice. The press center of the Russian border troops' command in Tajikistan reports that the situation in other sections along the Tajikistan-Afghanistan border remains complicated, but under control. According to the same source, no armed incidents in the Moskovskiy, Pyandzhskiy and other sections of the border have been registered in the last two days.
