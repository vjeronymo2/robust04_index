BFN [Excerpts] Listen to Peter Juhasz's report from Miskolc on Gyula Horn. [Begin Juhasz recording] Dr. Zoltan Katona, head physician at the Miskolc County Hospital, informed our editorial shortly before midnight about the condition of Gyula Horn, who suffered a car accident tonight. According to the report, the HSP [Hungarian Socialist Party] chairman has suffered a fracture of the cervical vertebra. Luckily the vertebra did not become dislodged and no nerves have been damaged. Apart from this, one of his wrists was broken and he has facial injuries. Gyula Horn lost consciousness at the moment of the crash but he has now come round and is well considering the circumstances. [passage omitted] The examination into the cause of the accident is continuing.
