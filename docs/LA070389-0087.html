July 3, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Proceeds from a sale of art by Andy Wing will help defray the cost of heart surgery for the Laguna Beach artist. The sale, Thursday at 7:30 p.m. at Piret's Bistro in South Coast Plaza, is being sponsored by arteffects of Irvine and the Art Resource Group of Newport Beach. Wing's work involves the addition of thinned pigment to old, castaway objects. Information: (714) 724-1460 or 497-8282.
