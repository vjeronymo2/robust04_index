920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Acquisitions help Lilleshall to Pounds 1.83m Acquisitions made over the past 18 months helped Lilles- hall, the building products, plastics and engineering group, to a pre-tax profit of Pounds 1.83m in the half year ended June 30 1992, a 46 per cent advance over the previous Pounds 1.25m. Sales rose 58.5 per cent to Pounds 25.5m (Pounds 16.1m). Operating profit rose to Pounds 1.85m (Pounds 808,000) with building products accounting for Pounds 1.21m (Pounds 1.09m), and plastics and engineering Pounds 1.04m (Pounds 282,000). Industrial consumables cut its loss to Pounds 123,000 (Pounds 286,000). The pre-tax outcome included Pounds 85,000 (Pounds 172,000) profit on the disposal of investment properties. Earnings per share came to 5.6p (4.4p) and the interim dividend is 1.6p (1.5p).
