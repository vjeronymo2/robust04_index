940226 FT 26 FEB 94 / Finance and the Family: Cuts in card interest rates - At a Glance Save & Prosper/Robert Fleming has cut the interest rate on its Visa and Mastercard credit cards to 0.95 per cent a month (APR 13.9 per cent, or 15.4 per cent for cash) from 1 per cent. It is the lowest rate of interest available on a UK credit card. The cards carry an annual fee of Pounds 12.
