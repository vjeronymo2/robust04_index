Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Malatya, Feb 3 (AA) -- Seventeen terrorists belonging to an outlawed leftist terrorist organization were captured following security operations in southeastern Malatya Province, it was reported on Thursday. A written statement from Malatya Mayor's Office said the terrorists were captured following joint operations by security headquarters and the gendarmerie forces to the terrorists safe-houses in the city. Firearms, ammunitions and money were seized following the searches of the safe-house, it added.
