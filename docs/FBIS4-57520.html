BFN [Text] Tourism Minister Osmany Cienfuegos stated at the Varadero-94 convention that in 1994, Cuba will invest 300 million pesos in tourism and will do whatever is necessary to ensure tourism development. Cienfuegos also pointed out that contracts have been signed with foreign companies to build 7,000 rooms in the next five to six years. He added that there are 1,200 rooms under joint management arrangements and that there are plans to have 30,000 rooms by 1995 and 50,000 in another five years. Regarding tourism prospects, Cienfuegos affirmed that in forthcoming years, tourism will surpass all other sectors of the national economy and make a significant contribution to the nation.
