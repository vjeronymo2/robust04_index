November 13, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWS WATCH

A FINE SUM: Crime doesn't pay, especially in the car-pool lane. . . . The Orange County Transportation Commission will try to figure out today what to do with $40,852 -- its share of fines from car-pool lane violators on the Costa Mesa and San Diego freeways. One idea: Pay CHP officers overtime to issue more tickets. Steve Emmons and Dana Parsons
