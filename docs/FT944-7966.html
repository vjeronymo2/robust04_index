941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / UK Company News: F&C Eurotrust Foreign & Colonial Eurotrust reported fully converted net asset value of 227.7p at September 30, against 212.5p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year to the end of September was Pounds 812,000 (Pounds 751,000) for earnings per share of 1.35p (1.26p). A single final unchanged dividend of 1.23p is proposed.
