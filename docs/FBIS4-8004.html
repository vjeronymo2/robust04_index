BFN [Text] Alija Izetbegovic, president of the Presidency of Bosnia-Herzegovina, today received General Michael Rose, the UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] commander for Bosnia-Herzegovina. Haris Silajdzic, the prime minister of Bosnia-Herzegovina, and General Rasim Delic, the Bosnia-Herzegovina army commander, were present at the talks. The difficult situation in Gorazde, the town which has been under a strong offensive by the Serb aggressor for over a week, was discussed. Gen. Rose was asked to do everything within his powers to bring an end to the offensive and to have the aggressor's army return to its pre-31 March positions. It is expected that Gen. Rose will again hold talks with President Izetbegovic tomorrow after his talks with representatives of the aggressor's side.
