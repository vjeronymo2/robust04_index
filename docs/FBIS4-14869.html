BFN [ITAR-TASS report from the "Yesterday, Today, and Tomorrow" column: "Does North Korea Have a Specific Program?"] [Text] Tokyo is unable to confirm the statement by U.S. Secretary of Defense William Perry that the DPRK has started implementing a program which will enable it to produce at least 12 nuclear devices a year within two to three years. An ITAR-TASS correspondent was informed of this by a senior Japanese Foreign Ministry official. He observed that he did not know the reasons which enabled the U.S. secretary of defense to make such a statement during an interview with a U.S. television company on Sunday [3 April].
