BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow, 27 Apr -- The agenda of today's plenary session of the Duma has been "disrupted" as a result of yesterday's murder of a deputy of the house, Andrey Ayzderdzis. Duma deputies went to lay flowers at the the site where their colleague died. According to the chairman of the State Duma [Ivan Rybkin], the leadership of the house will do its utmost to ensure that the Minister of Internal Affairs, Viktor Yerin, speaks to deputies at 1200. The State Duma has also adopted a political statement on the murder of a deputy of the house.
