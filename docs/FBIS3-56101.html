Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow January 12 TASS -- Twenty independent members of the State Duma (lower chamber of the new Russian legislature) have decided to join the centrist parliamentary faction of Sergey Shakhray's Party of Russian Unity and Accord (PRES). All the volunteers represent interests of Russia's new financial and industrial sector, Aleksandr Kotenkov, acting executive committee's chairman of the PRES, told ITAR-TASS on Wednesday. According to him, the PRES faction would enlist about 40 MPs and is due to be registered on Thursday.
