920530 FT 30 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Rank Organisation sells US theme park Rank Organisation is selling a water park in Williamsburg, Virginia, to Busch Entertainment, a subsidiary of US brewing giant Anheuser-Busch. A purchase price was not disclosed. The park, currently into its ninth season, had attendances of about 352,000 last year. The deal also includes 850 acres of undeveloped lands surrounding the water park, known as Water Country USA.
