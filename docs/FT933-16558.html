930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit and Vegetables Spanish plums at 45-55p a lb (60-90p) are this week's best buy, together with other stone fruits such as apricots at 45-60p a lb (50-60p), cherries at Pounds 1.00-1.20 a lb (Pounds 1.00-1.20) and peaches at 15-25p each (15-25p), reports FFVIB. Wimbledon favourites, strawberries, are 99p-Pounds 1.20 a lb (80p-Pounds 1.20). Homegrown vegetables are becoming more plentiful with peas at 35-45p a lb (50-65p), Broad beans at 30-40p a lb (35-40p), and cauliflower at 45-70p a head (40-50p). English Iceberg lettuce at 45-55p a head (45-55p), Round lettuce at 20-30p a head (25-30p) and Webbs at 30-35p each (30-35p) are good salad buys.
