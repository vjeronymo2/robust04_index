920418 FT 18 APR 92 / UK Company News: Burns-Anderson deeper in the red Burns-Anderson Group, the recruitment and financial services company, fell deeper into the red in 1991. Pre-tax losses amounted to Pounds 2.74m, against Pounds 1.97m for the previous 15-month period. Turnover was Pounds 64.7m (Pounds 98m). Losses per share worked through at 6.3p (4.3p).
