931204 FT 04 DEC 93 / Finance and The Family: Directors' transactions - The Budget and You Regalian Properties has been hurt by the property decline but business is picking up and three board members including the chairman and chief executive and finance director have all bought stock, increasing their stakes considerably. Kenwood Appliances came to the market in July 1992 and has performed strongly since. Following the interim results,,six board members have sold stock at 316p. Each retains a sizeable number of shares. Forward has been a strong performer over the past 12 months, outperforming the market by more than 158 per cent. Interim results showed good growth. In May, the chairman and chief executive sold stock at 223p. Since then, the shares have shown further increases to trade around 230p level. P.
