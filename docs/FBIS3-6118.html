Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] On 9 March, Do Muoi, general secretary of the Vietnamese Communist Party [CPV] Central Committee, received a delegation of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] headed by Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the WPK Central Committee. At the meeting, the head of the delegation conveyed greetings from the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to General Secretary Do Muoi. Expressing deep thanks for this, the general secretary asked the head of the delegation to convey his sincere and warm greetings to the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. The General Secretary said that the CPV will make efforts to strengthen forever the friendship between the two parties, which was originated by Comrade Ho Chi Minh and Comrade President Kim Il-song. He said that the CPV will always support the WPK's policy of and struggle for national reunification. He stressed that the WPK's reunification policy based on a confederal system is the most reasonable method. He pointed out that the Vietnamese party and people always remember the precious support extended by the WPK and Korean people for the Vietnamese people's struggle for independence and socialism.
