BFN [Text] Tehran, April 20, IRNA -- Chairman of the Security Commission of Iran Gholamhosein Bolandian said at the joint Iran-Turkey security committee here last night that the U.S. and the Zionist regime support terrorist groups and international drug traffickers. He offered documents to the committee in demonstration of the connection between international drug rings and the CIA for trafficking drugs through Iran and Turkey. The meeting of the commission in Tehran is in connection with security issues affecting the two countries. The Turkish side of the commission is headed by Gogistan Oughlu of the Turkish Ministry of the Interior.
