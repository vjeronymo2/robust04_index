930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / Inflation at 4.7% in Spain SPAIN'S year-on-year inflation rate has fallen to 4.7 per cent, its lowest level since June 1988, the consumer price index for January published yesterday showed, Tom Burns reports from Madrid. February figures, to be published next week, are expected to bring the 12-month inflation rate down to below 4.5 per cent. The lower inflation has fuelled hopes of a cut in the official intervention rate early next week at the Bank of Spain's repurchase tender for its certificates of deposit.
