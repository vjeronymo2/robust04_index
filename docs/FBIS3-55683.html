Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] [Latvian] Prime Minister Valdis Birkavs met with Lithuanian Prime Minister Adolfas Slezevicius at Nica [western Latvia] today. Valdis Birkavs admitted that the talks were productive. An agreement was reached on the joint construction of an oil import-export terminal in Liepaja. [Words indistinct] the prime ministers also discussed the sea border. Talks between the two prime minister will continue in Riga on 19 January.
