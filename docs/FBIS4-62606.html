BFN [Excerpt] Tallinn, May 17, BNS -- Any attempts to force a resignation upon Prime Minister Mart Laar will cause Estonia big damage both in the foreign as well as in the interior political aspects, the faction of Mart Laar's Pro Patria party in the parliament said in a statement today. The statement followed recent press reports that have predicted a change of the government to take place already within the next few days. Andres Herkel, adviser to the Pro Patria faction in the parliament, told BNS that the faction supports Mart Laar. [passage omitted]
