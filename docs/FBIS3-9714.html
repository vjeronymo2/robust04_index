Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Crvenkovski-Daskalov Meeting"] [Text] Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic on Macedonia -- FYROM] Foreign Minister Stevo Crvenkovski, together with his associates, met with the Bulgarian counterpart Stanislav Daskalov, who paused in Skopje on his way to Albania. The talks focused on future development of Macedonian-Bulgarian relations and cooperation. Both ministers stressed the need to intensify and regulate mutual economic and traffic relations, in spite of present sanctions and blockades.
