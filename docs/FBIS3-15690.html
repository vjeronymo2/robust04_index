Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Latvia's National Independence Movement (LNNK) faction proposed to cease broadcasting of some of the "Ostankino" programmes. The newspaper NEATKARIGA CINA reported. The programme "Sunday" was mentioned as one of the reasons for such a proposal, which said that the books distributed in Latvia as text-books express approval of how the Jews issue was solved during the World War Two. The faction proposed to conclude a new agreement with the "Ostankino" company on broadcasting only some of their programmes in Latvia.
