940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / International Company News: IFG advances to IPounds 403,000 at six months IFG Group, the Dublin-listed financial services company, reported a 24 per cent increase in pre-tax profits for the six months to June 30, from IPounds 324,000 to IPounds 403,000 (Pounds 396,575). Turnover rose 18 per cent from IPounds 4.75m to IPounds 5.6m. Earnings per share were 0.81p (0.69p). No interim dividend is proposed but Mr Richard Hayes, chief executive, indicated the intention to recommend an increased final dividend for 1994.
