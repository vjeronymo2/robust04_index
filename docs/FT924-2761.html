921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / World News in Brief: Voice of tennis Dan Maskell dies Dan Maskell, the gentlemanly BBC tennis commentator, whose genteel exclamations, such as 'That's a dream of a backhand' and 'Oh, I say', were part of the Wimbledon tournament coverage, died aged 84. For 42 years, he did not miss a day's play at the world's foremost tennis event.
