BFN [Russian Federation Government Decree No. 336, signed by Premier Chernomyrdin and dated 15 April 1994: "On the Procedure for Determining in 1994 the Regulated Amount of Expenditure on the Remuneration of Workers at Enterprises and Organizations"] [Text] In execution of Paragraph 3 of Russian Federation Presidential Edict No. 2270 of 22 December 1993 "On Certain Changes to Taxation and the Relationship Between Budgets at Different Levels," the Russian Federation Government decrees that: 1. It shall be laid down that in 1994 the regulated amount of expenditure on remuneration at enterprises and organizations shall be determined on the basis of the actual average monthly pay of workers included in the prime cost of the output, operations, or services (but not more than six times the minimum monthly wage laid down in the Russian Federation), and on the basis of the actual average number of workers employed. And this number includes workers who are part of the staff (including those doing more than one job), as well as those not on the staff (working under subcontract or other civil-law contracts). The regulated amount of expenditure on remuneration is being increased by the payments laid down by Russian Federation legislation (within the set norms), which are determined by: rayon regulation of pay; the pay value of collective catering provided free to members of seagoing and river vessels, air traffic controllers and members of air detachment crews, and seagoing members of the fishing fleet; the pay value of official clothing and uniforms issued free of charge; expenditure on the upkeep of servicemen and both rank-and-file employees and senior staff at internal affairs organs envisaged by the Russian Federation laws "On the Status of Servicemen" (VEDOMOSTI SYEZDA NARODNYKH DEPUTATOV ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII I VERKHOVNOGO SOVETA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII, No. 6, 1993, page 188) and "On Institutions and Organs Implementing Criminal Punishment in the Form of Imprisonment" (VEDOMOSTI SYEZDA NARODNYKH DEPUTATOV ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII I VERKHOVNOGO SOVETA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII, No. 33, 1993, page 1316) as regards that portion of the pay that has a bearing on prime cost of output, operations, and services as part of the labor remuneration fund; payment for travel to and from vacation areas for workers at enterprises and organizations located in regions of the Far North, equivalent areas, and remote parts of the Far East; supplementary payments for intinerant work and work involving travel; payments for work carried out by duty tour and for field work; payments from the federal budget for forming labor remuneration funds at enterprises and organizations undergoing conversion; payments to mothers (or other relatives who actually look after a child) who have been hired by enterprises and organizations irrespective of their form of ownership and who are enjoying child-care leave in accordance with Russian Federation legislation until the child attains the age of three. 2. It shall be laid down that the procedure for determining the regulated amount of expenditure on workers' remuneration stipulated in this decree shall cover all enterprises and organizations irrespective of the results of their activity or the existence of concessions on taxable profits (including exemption from profits tax), including enterprises wholly owned by foreign investors. [Signed] Russian Federation Premier V. Chernomyrdin [Dated] Moscow, 15 April 1994 No. 336
