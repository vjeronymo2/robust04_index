BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 23 (KCNA) -- A national symposium on medical science was held in Pyongyang on April 19 to carry through the tasks laid down by the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il in his famous work "On the Further Improvement of the Health Service". Read at the symposium were papers profoundly proving the validity and vitality of the famous work of the dear leader and showing excellent achievements of science and technology made in the course of implementing the tasks laid down in the work. More than 330 excellent papers showing achievements recently made in medical scientific researches were read at the first national festival of medical science and technique which took place till April 22 following the symposium.
