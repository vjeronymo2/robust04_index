931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / International Company News: Yamato up 7.6% at half-way stage YAMATO Transport, Japan's largest door-to-door parcel delivery service operator, reported parent pre-tax profit for the fiscal first half to September 30 7.6 per cent higher at Y8.25bn (Dollars 7.6m) compared with Y7.67bn a year ago, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. Sales were 3.4 per cent higher at Y235.83bn.
