January 26, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: FIGHT AT HIGH SCHOOL ENDS IN TEEN'S STABBING

A fight between two male students on the Culver City High School campus Thursday sent one of the teen-agers to Brotman Medical Center with a stab wound inflicted with a screwdriver, authorities said.

Principal Glenn Cook said the student's injury did not appear to be serious. Police arrested the other teen-ager for investigation of assault with a deadly weapon and took him to Juvenile Hall.
