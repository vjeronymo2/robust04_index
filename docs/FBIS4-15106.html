BFN [Text] Moscow, April 6 (SNARK) -- Both Georgia and Abkhazia should be pleased with the results of the negotiations. At this stage of the talks both sides were yielding to achieve desired compromise, said in his exclusive interview to SNARK Sandro Kavsadze, Shevardnadze's personal representative. Kavsadze is of the opinion that the UN representatives' participation in the negotiations and the Russia's positively activizing her position can be considered as the guarantee of the subsequent constructive dialog between the sides.
