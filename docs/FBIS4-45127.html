BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow June 14 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed today a resolution imposing a strict control on exports of gene materials which could be used in a biological warfare. The list of substances under control includes "pathogenes ... and their genetically changed forms, fragments of genetical material and equipment, which could be used in preparation of bacteriological (biological) and toxic weapons," the presidential press service reported.
