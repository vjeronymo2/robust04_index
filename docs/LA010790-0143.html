January 7, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

'TOUGH LOVE'

Obviously, P. Lucius Sladek Jr. and Frank L. Burke, whose Dec. 24 letters blasted Bill Cosby, have had radically different experiences with dishonest and/or addicted children than I have had.

Having seen "open-minded and understanding" parents give unlimited support and love in a frustrating and fruitless effort to help offspring salvage their lives, I feel I can comment knowledgeably on the subject.

My well-meaning parents' never-ending efforts to help those who "strayed from the mold" have drained and disrupted four generations; one after another, the children and grandchildren were affected by the tumultuous household.

It is not easy to use the "tough love" method, but the survival of a whole family must be considered.

M. ATTEBERRY

Torrance
