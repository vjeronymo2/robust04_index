January 13, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Savings Bond Sales Up 3%: Sales of U.S. savings bonds totaled $593 million in December, bringing to $7.64 billion the total amount of sales for the year, the Treasury Department reported. The 3% increase of sales over calendar 1988 also increased the amount of bonds held by the public to a record $117.7 billion. Savings bonds currently earn interest at a rate of 6.98% when held five years or longer.
