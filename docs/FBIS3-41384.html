Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Feb. 18 -- Only one Sejm deputy voted on Friday against the law allowing the Polish president to ratify the 1988 U.N. convention on combating the illegal trade in drugs. The law was passed with 383 votes for. During the discussion held earlier in the Sejm, the deputies pointed out that lenient penal regulations cause that Poland is becoming an important route for illegal drug trade and an interesting market for international gangs.
