BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent Leb Ryabchikov for TASS] [Text] Simferopol 5 May TASS -- The government of the Crimea received a bomb threat on Thursday [5 May] and the staff have been urgently evacuated from the building. An unknown person called the policeman on duty at the government offices in the morning and said the building had been mined. All employees were immediately evacuated and the building is being searched by special police units.
