BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Gennadiy Kochuk] [Text] Moscow April 8 TASS -- An agreement on principles of relations between the Russian Federation and Colombia was signed on Friday by the two countries' ministers of foreign affairs Andrey Kozyrev and Nohemi Sanin Posada de Rubio. The signing took place in the Russian Foreign Ministry's mansion in downtown Moscow within the framework of bilateral talks conducted by the Colombian minister in Russia. The agreement which the two ministers signed today is the first document of its kind in relations between Russia and Latin American countries. The agreement is most important for Russia's global and bilateral relations with the countries of the Latin American continent, Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev said after the signing. The Colombia minister of foreign affairs for her part stressed the fact that Colombia opens a new page in relations between Russia and Latin American countries. She expressed the hope that her country would soon become a stable partner of Russia's in a variety of sphere, including scientific, technological, cultural and trade cooperation.
