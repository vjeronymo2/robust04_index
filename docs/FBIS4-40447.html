BFN [Report by Major Vasiliy Likholeta under general heading: "Shot from the Corner; Those Are the Tactics Resorted to Increasingly Frequently by Gunmen from Tajik Opposition"] [Text]In the Last Hour Major Vasiliy Likholeta, chief of the Tajikistan Defense Ministry press center, has reported to the editorial office that on 15 June at 0930 hours as they were traveling from the Khait population center, at crossroads near the hamlet of Nimich two vehicles carrying servicemen of the Tajikistan Defense Ministry were destroyed by automatic machine-gun fire from an ambush set up by gunmen. Six people were killed and one wounded man is in a serious state. The dead include Colonel R. Radzhabov, Tajikistan Deputy Defense Minister, and Captain S. Kholov, a directorate officer. An operation has started to block off and destroy the gang.
