920203 FT 03 FEB 92 / Rescue goes on in Turkey Rescuers gather round the body of a soldier recovered from an avalanche yesterday in the village of Gormec, south-eastern Turkey, Reuter reports from Ankara. More bodies have been found in Kurdish mountain villages swallowed by avalanches, raising to 142 the death toll over the weekend. Some 150 other villagers have been missing after the series of avalanches which have hit south-eastern Turkey since Saturday. The European Community has sent a message to the Turkish government yesterday offering emergency aid for victims of the avalanches.
