Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Akimov and Amangaldiy Akhmetova] [Text] Almaty, 11 Feb--The press service of the president of Kazakhstan has today denied reports appearing in the Russian media on a meeting between the Russian and Kazakhstan presidents expected in the next few days. The dates given for the meeting in the majority of reports are either before or immediately after Nazarbayev's official visit to the United States, which is scheduled for 13-17 February. An ITAR-TASS correspondent was told by the press service of the Kazakh president that the exact dates for the meeting of the two leaders is not yet being discussed. The meeting can take place only after the documents on which agreement was reached during the recent talks between the foreign ministers of Russia and Kazakhstan in Almaty are prepared for signing.
