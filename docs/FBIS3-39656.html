Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Families of the diplomatic corps serving at the Turkish Embassy in Belgrade have left Serbia, two days before the NATO ultimatum expires. The group of 27 -- 17 children and 10 women -- left Belgrade and entered Turkey from the Kapikule border post. Other NATO member countries have already evacuated the families of their diplomatic corps. NATO countries with embassies in Belgrade have reportedly reduced the number of their diplomats.
