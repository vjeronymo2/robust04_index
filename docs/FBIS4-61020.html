BFN [Text] Riga, 2 May, BNS -- The Central Electoral Committee extended the deadline for accepting lists of candidates by 9 May in a number of regions. It also decided to convene a meeting of election coordinators, Budget Minister Janis Platais and MP's to tackle the financial problems of elections. As reported, the Central Electoral Committee requested the related institutions to increase the funding for the elections. Platais said the claim was ungrounded. Electoral Committee member Dzintars Rasnacs stressed that the work of regional commissions may be hampered by the lack of resources and the financial shortfall may even obstruct the organization of local elections. The committee plans to inform about the situation Prime Minister Valdis Birkavs if the resources are not allotted soon.
