BFN [Text] Major General Nasri Yusuf, commander of the Palestinian Police Force, has stated that his forces will refuse to hand over to Israel alleged perpetrators of operations against the Israeli state who seek refuge in the self-rule areas. At a news conference in Gaza, he said: We will not permit the Israelis to come and arrest anyone in the sectors that are under our control. Maj. Gen. Nasri Yusuf also said that the Palestinian police will issue arms licenses to the self-rule inhabitants, but will not permit weapons to be carried on the streets as they should be kept at home.
