940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Kleinwort High Net revenue for the year to end-June at Kleinwort High Income Trust rose from Pounds 2.37m to Pounds 2.41m, to give an increase in earnings per share from 7.89p to 8.05p. The fourth quarterly dividend is maintained at 1.875p to give a same-again total for the year of 7.5p. Net asset value for the zero dividend preference shares, designed for capital appreciation, was 137.64p and for the ordinary shares, 81.5p.
