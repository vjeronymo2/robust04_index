BFN [Report from Kuito by correspondent Abel Abraao] [Text] War continues in Kuito, Bie Province. Fifteen days have passed since clashes erupted, killing more people. Over the past days, government troops have made many prisoners among Jonas Savimbi's men and captured a quantity of war materiel. Our correspondent Abel Abraao has further details. [Begin recording] [Abraao] The atmosphere is still characterized by war here in Kuito. The Black Cockerel's men continue to attack the martyred city of Kuito. After 15 days of fierce clashes and heavy shellings, government forces killed [words indistinct] Jonas Savimbi's men and captured 42 AKM's, six [words indistinct], three RPG-7's, one M-79's, [words indistinct], 15 hand grenades, a quantity of rounds of ammunition, 25 clips, (?two) missiles, and two machineguns. Government forces also destroyed one vehicle. Brigadier Simeone Mukume, second commander of the FAA Operational Command in Bie, told our reporter that the political and military situation is worrying in the city of Kuito at the present moment. [Mukume] The situation is worrying because forces belonging to the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] continue to move men from the neighboring Province of Huambo, as well as war materiel from Cuando Cubango. [Abraao] At this moment, the situation is worrying, but it is under the control of government forces. [end recording]
