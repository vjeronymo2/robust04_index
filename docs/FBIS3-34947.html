Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Islamic revolution mujahidin have confronted a special unit of the regime's security forces known as the emergency company. The company was trying to arrest an innocent citizen from the area of (al-Sajiyah), which belongs to the district of (al-Shifayij) in the governorate of al-Nasiriyah. The operation resulted in the killing of the captain who commanded the company and two mercenaries. Other people were hurt in the operation. One of the vehicles used by the attacking force was burned. A group of mujahidin fighters dealt a punitive military blow to the headquarters of Saddam's forces in (al-Mudaynah) district in the governorate of al-Basrah using 60-mm mortars, hitting military locations and the (al-Fathiyah) checkpoint. It also killed or wounded regime men.
