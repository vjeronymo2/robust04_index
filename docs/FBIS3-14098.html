Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "News at Ten" program] [Text] Rival Yemeni parties are reported to have agreed to pull out their troops from the provincial capital 'Ataq in the southern oil-producing region of Shabwah. The Jordanian-Omani military committee helping to disengage the two sides' armies said an agreement had been reached to withdraw troops from and around the city. A statement by the committee said all groups around the city would return to their bases. Jordanian, Omani, and Yemeni officers, along with the American and French military attaches in Yemen, formed the committee after the country's two main political rivals signed a reconciliation agreement in Amman on February 20th.
