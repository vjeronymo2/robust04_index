BFN [Text] Baghdad, 21 Jun (INA)--Assembly Speaker Sa'di Mahdi Salih has received a Spanish delegation, headed by Marcuri Fija [name as received], official at the Middle East Department of the Spanish Foreign Ministry. They reviewed the parliamentary and political relations between Iraq and Spain and ways of promoting them. The head of the Spanish delegation referred to the Spanish Government's recent move in the Security Council to lift the unjust blockade, stressing that his country supports Iraqi efforts to lift the blockade. He also referred to current efforts to send a Spanish delegation to Iraq to acquaint itself with the Iraqi people's suffering as a result of the continued unjust blockade and then to explain to the Spanish people the tragedies that the blockade has created. Sa'di Mahdi Salih expressed the Iraqi people's thanks to Spain for the Spanish Government's positive positions toward Iraq. He also expressed hope that they will be further promoted to serve both countries' interests.
