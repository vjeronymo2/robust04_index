BFN [Text] Reliable sources in Aden have told AL-ITTIHAD that the Yemeni president yesterday telephoned southern leader 'Ali Salim al-Bid and asked for an unconditional cease-fire. Another source in the Socialist Party has also said that Mujahid Abu-Shawarib, the deputy prime minister in the Sanaa government, who is currently in Paris, the night before last telephoned some Socialist Party leaders in Aden asking them to approve a cease-fire and start immediate negotiations in Paris between Sanaa and Aden (three people from each party). The source said Aden does not object to a cease-fire and negotiations, on the condition that Sanaa withdraws all of its troops from the south.
