920909 FT 09 SEP 92 / GPT wins Pounds 25m cable TV order GPT, the largest UK telecommunications manufacturer, is to provide about Pounds 25m of equipment to Southwestern Bell, the US telecommunications operator, for its cable television franchises in the Midlands and the north-west. The cable television industry is making a drive into the telecommunications business and the cable TV companies have so far installed 50,000 telephone lines. GPT has set up a business unit, GPT Cablecom, to tackle the market, which it predicts will be worth Pounds 400m over five years.
