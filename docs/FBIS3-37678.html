Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Kondrashov] [Text] Belgrade February 21 TASS -- Two reinforced companies of the Russian peace-keeping force in former Yugoslavia were deployed Monday [21 February] morning along the Serb-Muslim line near Sarajevo. 400 paratoopers are now guarding positions in Sarajevo outskirts of Grbavica, Vrace and Nedzarici, as well as the artillery of Bosnian Serbs handed over under UN control.
