920208 FT 08 FEB 92 / Israelis fear clashes after death in custody ISRAELI security forces were braced for trouble in Jerusalem today at the funeral of a Palestinian whose death in custody on Wednesday was said by an American pathologist last night to have been partly caused by his treatment during interrogation, writes Hugh Carnegy. The case of Mustafa Abdullah Akawi, an East Jerusalem resident who complained in court the day before he died of being beaten by his Israeli captors, is the kind of volatile confrontation between Israel and the Palestinians that could upset their fragile peace talks. The belief is widespread among Palestinians that Mr Akawi was tortured to death.
