BFN [Text] Damaris Lisoba Gomes Sardinia, 28, was detained at Ataturk Airport in Istanbul for trying to smuggle 5 kg of cocaine into Turkey yesterday. Sardinia, who is a citizen of Brazil, arrived in Turkey from Spain. The police discovered 5 kg of cocaine in the pockets of her slacks in her suitcase after she passed through immigration control. She was handed over to the officials of the Narcotics Department after the police at the airport interrogated her.
