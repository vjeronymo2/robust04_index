BFN [Text] Mr. Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the prime minister, yesterday had a telephone call with Dr. Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid, the Arab League secretary general. During this call they reviewed the development of the situation in the Yemeni arena. The prime minister praised the Arab League's efforts to overcome the grave developments as a result of the military outbreak of the situation, which began with the attack on the 5th Brigade in Harf Sufyan, the 3d Brigade in 'Imran, Ba Suhayb Brigade in Dhamar, and later by launching the all-out war against our Yemeni people by the bloody secessionist al al-Ahmar dynasty with a view to undermining the covention document.
