August 24, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: ANAHEIM;

REDEVELOPMENT PLANS FOR MALL TO BE DISCUSSED

The city will hold a workshop tonight for residents and business owners who want to know more about redevelopment plans for the Anaheim Plaza mall.

Members of the Anaheim Redevelopment Agency will answer questions about plans to improve the mall at Euclid Street and the Santa Ana Freeway. A redevelopment plan and environmental impact report for the project are scheduled to be released this fall.

The meeting will be from 7 to 8:30 p.m. at the Anaheim Family YMCA, 1515 W. North St. For more information, call (714) 533-8750.
