940307 FT 07 MAR 94 / Company News (This Week): A new voice as Barclays rebounds - Other companies UK bank results culminate this week with Barclays and Standard Chartered. At Barclays' presentation on Thursday, Mr Martin Taylor, the new chief executive, will give his views after examining the bank. Analysts are predicting pre-tax profits of about Pounds 750m, against a Pounds 242m loss. The bank cut its 1992 dividend and no increase is expected. Standard Chartered should deliver its first unflawed set of results for several years on Wednesday, with analysts expecting pre-tax profits to double to about Pounds 400m.
