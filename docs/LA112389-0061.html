November 23, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: NAMES IN THE NEWS

The Broward County attorney's office filed two charges against Eric Kumerow, a Miami Dolphin defensive end, in connection with a brawl at a bar Nov. 5. Charges were also filed against Kumerow's sister, Cheryl Kumerow, and a friend, Robert Skelton of Chicago. The arraignment is scheduled in one to two weeks.
