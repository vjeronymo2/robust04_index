Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] President Yeltsin has issued an instruction on certain questions regarding the work of the commission for questions of appeals under the president of the Russian Federation. In accordance with the document, as the president's press service has reported, the commission is due to examine applications for appeals by persons condemned to capital punishment and to prepare a draft decree of the president on the pardoning of such persons. The criminal code says that in the event of the commutation of capital punishment on appeal to deprivation of freedom, it may be fixed for a period of up to 15 years or for life.
