BFN [Text] Outgoing Police Commissioner Rafi Peled says there is no Palestinian police activity in Jerusalem. Peled was reacting to East Jerusalem Arabic press reports submitted to the Knesset Internal Affairs Committee by chairman Yehoshu'a Matza. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that according to Peled, the pictures that appeared in the newspapers were staged for photographers. The commissioner added, however, that the activity of Palestinian groups organized for dealing with ethical and drug-related matters in Jerusalem has been stopped.
