CSO [Unattributed report: "International Economic Relations Are Expanding"] [Text] Our republic will be one of the participants in the first session of the International Committee for International Cooperation and Economic Reform, which is to take place on 12-14 May. On the agenda of the first session is discussion of the issue of the production and distribution of foodstuffs; cooperation in the fuel and energy and banking and finance spheres; ensuring social protection of the population during the period of economic reforms; and other issues. Our country will be represented at the session by a group of experts headed by Azerbaijani Minister of the Economy S. Sadygov. Let us recall that this organization was founded on 1 February 1994 and unites 18 countries of the former USSR, as well as Central and East Europe. Its headquarters are located in the capital of the FRG.
