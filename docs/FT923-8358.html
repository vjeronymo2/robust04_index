920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Holmes Protection offer shunned Holmes Protection, the US security company with a UK listing, said shareholders had taken up a very small proportion of new shares on offer as part of its debt restructuring. Of 25.5m shares on offer at a price, after share consolidation, of 62 1/2 p, only 825,355 were taken up. The balance has gone to institutions, the company's previous lenders and Sir Ian MacGregor, the chairman.
