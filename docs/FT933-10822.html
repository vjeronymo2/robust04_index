930802 FT 02 AUG 93 / Construction Contracts: Burke wins Pounds 14m BT order MP BURKE, part of the Southern Electric group, has clinched a Pounds 14m contract with BT. The Barnsley-based company is responsible for the construction and upkeep of BT's underground duct network in parts of north-east England. The contract area includes major towns and cities such as Newcastle, Sunderland, Hartlepool, Sheffield, Barnsley, Doncaster and Rotherham. Southern Electric, the regional electricity company which serves central southern England, acquired a 70 per cent stake in MP Burke's parent company, Rightmain, in May 1992.
