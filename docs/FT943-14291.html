940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / Prince urges corporate award The Prince of Wales yesterday urged a standard to recognise corporate involvement in the community. The prince, who is president of Business in the Community, a charity which promotes community involvement by business, said: 'More and more companies recognise that what happens to business matters to the rest of society and that what happens to society matters to business.' He was speaking in Birmingham at the annual conference of training and enterprise councils, the employer-led bodies which deliver government-funded training schemes in England and Wales.
