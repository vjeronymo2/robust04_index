BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Andrey Shtorkh] [Text] Paris April 30 TASS -- France is ready to promote the quest for a peace settlement in Nagorno-Karabakh if the Armenian and the Azeri sides wish so. French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe said this here on Friday during a meeting with his Armenian counterpart Vahan Papazyan. Juppe said France had always been in favour of solution to the Nagorno-Karabakh conflict in the framework of the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe (CSCE). The ministers noted with satisfaction that there are no contradictions in Franco-Armenian bilateral relations. Juppe confirmed that the draft law envisaging the ratification of the agreement on friendship between France and Armenia will be put on the agenda of the spring session of the National Assembly now underway in France.
