BFN [Text] Knesset Member [MK] Ari'el Sharon says he does not understand Likud members' attacks on Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres for his letter to the Norwegian foreign minister regarding the Palestinian institutions in Jerusalem. Mr. Sharon told the Knesset Foreign Affairs and Defense Committee that these institutions were established during the tenure of Yitzhaq Shamir's government. According to Sharon he used to raise this issue in almost every government session and demanded a halt to Orient House activity, but most of the ministers used to react by bursting out in laughter.
