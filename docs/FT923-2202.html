920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Baby products and dyes buoy Mayborn Improvements in its fabric dyes and baby products divisions enabled Mayborn Group to increase interim pre-tax profits 29 per cent. In the six months to June 30 the USM-quoted company reported profits of Pounds 1.66m (Pounds 1.29m) on turnover of Pounds 17.1m (Pounds 15.2m), a rise of 12 per cent. Earnings per share were 5.8p (4.4p) and the interim dividend is raised from 1.4p to 1.6p.
