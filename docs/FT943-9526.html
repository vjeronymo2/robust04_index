940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: Burundi's capital closed as 15 die in tribal clashes Strikes and clashes shut down Burundi's capital Bujumbura for a second day and authorities stepped up security as diplomats feared the country may follow neighbouring Rwanda into conflict. At least 15 people have been killed in Burundi in two days of clashes involving youths of the minority Tutsi tribe. Aid officials warned that their operations in south-west Rwanda and eastern Zaire could be affected.
