BFN [Text] Li Lanqing, vice premier of the Chinese State Council, met An Tong-sun, chairman of the Trade, Industry, and Resources Committee of the ROK National Assembly, and his entourage in Beijing on the afternoon of 26 April. Chairman An Tong-sun and his entourage arrived in Beijing for a visit to China on 24 April at the invitation of the Financial and Economic Committee of the Chinese National People's Congress. Prior to this, the ROK guests visited Hainan Province and Harbin at the invitation of the Hainan and Heilongjiang Provinces. At the meeting, Vice Premier Li Lanqing said that President Kim Yong-sam's visit to China, which was made sometime ago, has played an important initiative role in the development of the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries.
