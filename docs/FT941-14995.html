940118 FT 18 JAN 94 / 400,000 drawn into tax net About 400,000 low-income workers will be drawn into the tax net because of the decision to freeze the personal income tax allowance for two consecutive years, the Treasury said last night. Mr Stephen Dorrell, financial secretary, said the decision to keep the allowance at Pounds 3,445 would raise an extra Pounds 1.1bn for the Treasury.
