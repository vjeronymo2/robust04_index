BFN [Text] Unknown people have set fire to the house belonging to ANC Natal premiership candidate Jacob Zuma at Inkandla in Zululand. There were no casualties, and the extent of the damage is not known. The attack on Mr. Zuma's house followed rumors that he would no longer contest the premiership. However, Mr. Zuma dismissed the rumors and gave the assurance that he was still determined to participate in the elections. This is the second attack on Mr. Zuma's house in a month.
