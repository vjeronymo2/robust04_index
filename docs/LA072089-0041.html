July 20, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: COMING ATTRACTIONS

Although there are several holidays between now and then, Hallmark Cards has already booked the pre-Valentine's Day show for its "Hallmark Hall of Fame" on CBS. It will be "Face to Face," a romantic drama starring Elizabeth Montgomery and Robert Foxworth as two strong-willed people who fall in love in spite of themselves. The film was shot on location in Kenya. A Hallmark executive notes that the slot is important for the company because Valentine's Day is the second most popular greeting-card occasion of the year, behind Christmas. ALEENE MacMINN
