940326 FT 26 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Advisers to power sale appointed The Government has announced that Barclays de Zoete Wedd and Kleinwort Benson have been appinted joint advisers on the sale of the Government's 40 pc stake in National Power and PowerGen, expected next Feb. Kleinwort is broker to PowerGen. PowerGen said yesterday that Kleinwort would step down temporarily during the sale and a replacement appointed.
