BFN [Presidential Council Decree No. 11 issued on 9 May -- read by announcer] [Text] The chairman of the Presidential Council, cognizant of the Constitution of the Republic of Yemen; in view Prime Minister Engineer Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas' actions, which violate constitutional legitimacy, and his abuse of office by exposing the homeland's sovereignty and independence to threats, such as his conduct that was discussed by the House of Representatives, which issued recommendations to the Executive Authority to adopt the necessary measures in accordance with the Constitution; and in view of the requirements of public interest and after the approval of the President Council, decrees the following:

Article I.
Engineer Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the prime minister, shall be dismissed from his office and shall be referred to interrogation in accordance with Article 111 of the Republic of Yemen Constitution.
Article II.
This decree will be valid as of date of issue and will be published in the official gazette. Issued at the Presidency in Sanaa on 29 Dhu al-Qi'dah, 1414 of the hegira, 9 May 1994. [Signed] Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the President Council.
