BFN [Text] My visit to Minsk has confirmed that Yugoslavia has a good and reliable friend in Belarus. Soon after sanctions are lifted our two countries will become close partners in economic cooperation, declared FRY Foreign Minister Vladislav Jovanovic, on the outcome of his visit to Belarus. The Yugoslav minister said that during his negotiations with the Belarus leadership readiness was expressed for speedy establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries. We also reached agreement on drawing up documents envisioning accelerated development of cooperation in the economic sphere. We expressed mutual interest in the speediest settlement of the Yugoslav crisis.
