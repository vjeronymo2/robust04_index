Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Item from roundup of ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA, ITAR-TASS reports under the "Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow" rubric: "U.S. Minister Is Our Shareholder"] [Text] U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Lloyd Bentsen has set a good example for businessmen who still cannot make up their minds to invest their capital in Russia.
