BFN [Text] Potchefstroom May 3 SAPA -- The state of emergency imposed on 15 Western Transvaal towns last week was lifted on Tuesday [3 May] morning. Deputy regional commissioner of police Brig Izak Smalman said the state of emergency declared on April 28 in the magisterial districts of Potchefstroom, Swartruggens, Marico, Lichtenburg, Coligny, Ventersdorp, Delareyville, Schweizer-Reneke, Wolmeransstad, Bloemhof, Klerksdorp, Koster, Christiana, Oberholzer and Rustenburg had been lifted.
