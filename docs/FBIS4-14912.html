BFN [Text] Ingushi President Ruslan Aushev assessed with restraint the Ossetian-Ingushi summit in Nalchik last weekend. The talks continue and it is premature to speak about their results, he told Interfax Monday. Deputy Russian Minister for Nationalities and Regional Policies, Vyacheslav Mikhailov, who took part in the talks, believes that the main result is in the recognition by both sides of the fact of human rights violations in the zone of the Ossetian-Ingushi conflict. In an interview with Interfax he said that the first step towards the solution of the problem was made. Currently Aushev and President Akhsarbek Galazov of North Ossetia arrived in Moscow where the State Duma on Monday debates the draft address to the leaders of both neighboring republics due to the situation in the region.
