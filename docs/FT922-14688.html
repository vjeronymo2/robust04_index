920404 FT 04 APR 92 / Dalgety names chairman DALGETY, the foods and agribusiness group, has named Mr John West as its chairman when Sir Peter Carey retires in September. Mr West, aged 64, was chief executive of Reckitt & Colman between 1980 and 1988. He has been on the Dalgety board as a non-executive director since June 1990. Mr West is also chairman of Bridon and Christian Salvesen, although he is retiring from Salvesen later this year. Sir Peter has been non-executive chairman of Dalgety since 1986 and a director since 1983. In June Mr Bryan Fawcett, executive director responsible for agribusiness, retires aged 60 after 21 years at Dalgety. Sir Christopher Laidlaw, aged 69, also steps down after eight years as a non-executive director. Mr Charles Woler has been appointed chairman of SmithKline Beecham's European pharmaceutical operations with effect from June 1.
