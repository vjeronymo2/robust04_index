920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / Election 92: Business leaders THE survey's clear message is that business leaders believe a Labour government would be bad for the country - 88 per cent said it would be 'very bad' or 'fairly bad' - but not quite as damaging for their businesses (58 per cent). Respondents were equally unenthusiastic about any coalition which might be formed in the event of a hung parliament.
