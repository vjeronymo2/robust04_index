BFN [Text] Authorized by the president of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, the Ministry of National Defense yesterday, 25 June, held in Hanoi a solemn ceremoney to confer the Ho Chi Minh Order on Lieutenant General Le Tu Dong and Lieutenant General Dang Kinh, aka Le Trung Truc; and the Independence Order first class on Lieutenant General Le Linh and Lieutenant General Do Quoc Tuan. The orders were awarded for their valuable contributions to the protection and construction of the fatherland.
