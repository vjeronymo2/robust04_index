BFN [Text] Litomysl, East Bohemia, April 16 (CTK) -- The two-day informal summit meeting of seven Central European presidents ended this afternoon. In the courtyard of the Litomysl chateau, where the talks have been held, the heads of state of Germany, Austria, Slovakia, Poland, Hungary and Slovenia ceremonially took their leave of Czech President Vaclav Havel, initiator and host of the summit. The motorcade left the chateau grounds at 4:10 P.M.
