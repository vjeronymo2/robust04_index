Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] 'Allamah Muhammad Husayn Fadlallah called the bombing at Sayyidat al-Najat Church an atrocity and lamented the death of innocents. The bombing, he said, signals a new pattern of attacks on places of worship. He added that the crime is designed to disturb the country's security and political life and unsettle the public's renewed sense of civic peace. Israeli hands cannot be absolved of this crime, whose purpose was to divert attention from the massacre at the Ibrahimi Mosque and trigger a new cycle of violence in the country, Fadlallah stated. He called on the authorities to uncover the evil hands that carried out the crime.
