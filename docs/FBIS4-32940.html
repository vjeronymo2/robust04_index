BFN [Text] Commander in Chief Fidel Castro Ruz has met Pierre Cardin, the acclaimed French designer, entrepreneur, and artist, and two of his associates at the Palace of the Revolution. Cardin told Fidel that he was fulfilling a longtime dream by visiting our country for the first time and by meeting him personally. Cardin also conveyed to the head of the revolution his enormous appreciation for all he had been able to see since his arrival in Havana, the capital's architecturally beautiful historical areas, the friendliness of the Cuban people, and Cuba's excellent conditions for developing international tourism. Fidel thanked Cardin for his visit and held a cordial and extensive exchange of ideas on issues related to the multifaceted activities of the French entrepreneur.
