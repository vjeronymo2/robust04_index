921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / UK Company News: GWR more than doubles to Pounds 721,000 Pre-tax profits of GWR Group, the USM-quoted independent radio contractor, more than doubled from Pounds 339,000 to Pounds 721,000 in the year to end-September. The result was struck on turnover up by Pounds 1.14m to Pounds 8.64m. Earnings rose by 95 per cent to 15.6p per share and the final dividend of 4p (3p) raises the total for the year to 7p (4p).
