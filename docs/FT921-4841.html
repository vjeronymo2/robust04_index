920306 FT 06 MAR 92 / International Capital Markets: RJR notes offering RJR Nabisco, the large food and tobacco group which was subject to a Dollars 25bn leveraged buy-out in 1989, has filed a registration statement for two offerings of senior notes, totalling Dollars 1.2bn, agencies report. It expects that the proceeds will be used primarily to refinance bank debt, in turn used to retire high-cost bonds.
