BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Vladimir Nadashkevich, Vilor Niyazmatov] [Text] Tashkent, 4 Jun -- Kim Yong-sam, president of the Republic of Korea, arrived in Uzbekistan on a state visit today. The purpose of the visit is to discuss questions of developing bilateral relations further, first of all in the economy, and to become acquainted with the lives of over 200,000 Koreans living in Uzbekistan. Kim Yong-sam is accompanied on the trip by a group of ministers and heads of departments. The lofty guest was met at the capital's airport by Islam Karimov, president of Uzbekistan, members of the cabinet of ministers, representatives of the Tashkent public and by heads of diplomatic missions accredited to the Republic of Uzbekistan.
