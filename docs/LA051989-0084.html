May 19, 1989, Friday, Southland Edition

HONOLULU INVESTOR HOLDS 7.9% STAKE IN CHANTAL

Honolulu investor Garner Anthony said he acquired a 7.9% stake in Chantal Pharmaceutical Corp. for investment.

In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, Anthony said he holds 1.17 million shares of Chantal, including 727,723 shares he bought from the company May 2 for $2 million.

The stake also includes 415,000 shares purchased between March 13 and May 15 for about 1.3 million.

Chantal is a Los Angeles skin care products manufacturer.
