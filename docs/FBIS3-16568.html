Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] St. Petersburg, 18 Mar -- The fourth plenary sitting of the CIS Interparliamentary Assembly opens today in the Tauride Palace in the city on the Neva. There are several main issues on the preliminary agenda for the sitting: a list of recommendatory legislative enactments regulating economic relations within the framework of an Economic Union; drawing up legislative enactments for the fight against organized crime in the CIS; drawing up recommendations on a single educational space and on enhancing the social status of students, research, and doctoral students. The question of the role of the Assembly in strengthening the Commonwealth may also be considered. Vladimir Shumeyko, chairman of the Council of the Interparliamentary Assembly and head of the Russian Federation Council, has arrived in order to attend the sitting.
