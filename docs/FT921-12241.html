920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There were 17,765 packages for the day, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Demand was selective and less general. Landed lower priced Assams met fair support at easier rates, but mediums proved very irregular. A limited quantity of brighter liquoring east Africans remained about steady. All others declined by 3-8p and sometimes more. The small quantity of Ceylons attracted only fair demand. Offshore: limited enquiry at easier rates. The highest price realised this week was 165p for an Assam fbop. Prices: quality 140p ,good medium 108p, medium 88p, low medium 77p.
