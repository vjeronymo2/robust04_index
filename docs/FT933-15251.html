930709 FT 09 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Torex Hire cuts interim losses Torex Hire, the USM-quoted company which hires tools and catering equipment, reduced pre-tax losses from Pounds 290,000 to Pounds 139,000 in the six months to April 30. Turnover was little changed at Pounds 1.94m, against Pounds 1.92m. Losses per share were 0.99p (2.06p) and there is again no interim dividend. Last year's single final was 0.4p.
