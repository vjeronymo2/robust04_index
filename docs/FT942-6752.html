940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / UK Company News: South Staffs Water South Staffordshire Water Holdings lifted pre-tax profits by 17 per cent from Pounds 10.3m to Pounds 12m in the year to March 31. Turnover increased by 9 per cent to Pounds 56.3m (Pounds 51.7m). A final dividend of 35.5p (32p) is proposed for a 52p (47p) total. Earnings were 160p (159p) per share.
