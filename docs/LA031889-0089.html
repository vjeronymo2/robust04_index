March 18, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

OKLAHOMA STATE IS A 69-55 WINNER IN NIT

Corey Williams made key 3-pointers for Oklahoma State in the second half, and the Cowboys used free throws to beat Boise State, 69-55, Friday night in the first round of the National Invitation Tournament.

Oklahoma State (17-12) will play at St. John's Tuesday night.

Boise State (23-7) made 5 of 7 three-point attempts in the first half and 7 of 12 in the game. Wilson Foster was 9 for 9 for the Broncos, including four three-pointers. He had 26 points.

Williams led the Cowboys with 23 points.
