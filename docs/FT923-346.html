920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / The Labour Party at Blackpool: Conference business YESTERDAY: Conference agreed that 'the Maastricht treaty, while not perfect, is the best agreement that can currently be achieved'; Supported the aims and objectives of the European social charter Rejected motions calling for an immediate cut in the number of MPs needed to nominate candidates for the Labour leadership and deputy leadership. TODAY: Conference will debate environment, transport and water and party finances. Party leader Mr John Smith will addresses delegates.
