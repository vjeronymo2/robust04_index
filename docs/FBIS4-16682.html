BFN [Text] [Deputy Foreign Minister] Vitaliy Churkin has dismissed rumors of an alleged rift between him and [Foreign Minister] Andrey Kozyrev about a solution of the Yugoslav conflict. However, Churkin said he has witnessed the events in the Balkans and has something to say about it. On his return to Moscow on Monday, Churkin sharply criticized the Bosnian Serbs. He told journalists that Bosnian Serbs are using Russian policy as a shield.
