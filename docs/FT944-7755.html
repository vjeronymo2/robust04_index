941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Birkdale in black With all its principal operating companies showing profits, Birkdale Group, the advertising and marketing consultancy, returned to the black with pre-tax profits of Pounds 142,000 for the six months to September 30, against losses of Pounds 1.47m. Turnover was Pounds 7.47m, compared with Pounds 11.6m which included Pounds 4.97m from discontinued activities. Underlying turnover growth was 12 per cent. Earnings per share were 0.2p, compared with losses of 2.9p.
