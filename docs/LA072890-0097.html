July 28, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

AREA BABE RUTH TEAM REACHES STATE FINAL

The Van Nuys-Sherman Oaks Babe Ruth team defeated El Segundo, 21-3, Friday night in Culver City to advance to the final of the Babe Ruth state baseball tournament.

Van Nuys-Sherman Oaks will face Culver City at 10 a.m. today at Culver City Park. Van Nuys-Sherman Oaks must defeat Culver City twice to win the double-elimination tournament.
