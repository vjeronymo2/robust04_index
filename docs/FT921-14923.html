920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / Turks to make combat sights TURKISH defence firm Aselsan will start joint-production of target-finding night vision systems within two weeks under a Dollars 85m (Pounds 47.4m) deal with Texas Instruments, Aselsan's deputy general manager said, Reuter reports from Ankara. 'With the project, Aselsan will be one of the main competitors in its field in the world,' Mr Cengiz Ergeneman said. The systems, to be produced at the company's Akyurt plant near Ankara, will be installed on 650 armoured combat vehicles being made in Turkey by a FMC Corp partnership with Turkey's Nurol. Mr Ergeneman said the company is aiming for sales of Dollars 137m (Pounds 76.5m) this year, compared with 1991 sales of Dollars 60m.
