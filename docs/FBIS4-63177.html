BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Olga Svistunova and Igor Veksler] [Text] Moscow May 19 TASS -- "The signing of an agreement on civic accord which began 20 days ago gave a positive impetus to socio-political processes in the Russian Federation. The document changes the character of political relations in the country and creates a basis for the beginning of the unification of all strata of Russian society," said Chairman of the Federation Council Vladimir Shumeyko, opening a signing ceremony which took place in the St. Vladimir Hall in the Grand Kremlin Palace today. Representatives of creative associations, scientific organisations, workers of culture and science put their signatures under the agreement. The process of signing the agreement began by a ceremonial act in the Kremlin attended by the Russian president, prime minister, representatives of both chambers of the Russian parliament, representatives of the subjects of the Federation, leaders of a number of parties, public and religious associations. The agreement is open to representatives of any political forces coming out for peace and accord in the country, for strengthening state power and surmounting the socio-economic crisis.
