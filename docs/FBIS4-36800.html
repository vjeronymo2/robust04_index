BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 13 Jun (WAKH) -- Lakhdar Brahimi, the UN secretary general's envoy, arrived in Abu Dhabi from al-Mukalla tonight on a visit to the United Arab Emirates [UAE] as part of a tour of the countries in the region. He was welcomed on arrival by Shaykh Hamdan Bin-Zayid Al Nuhayyan, minister of state for foreign affairs; Sayf Sa'id Bin- Sa'id, acting Foreign Ministry under secretary; and Foreign Ministry officials. Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, envoy of 'Ali Salim al-Bid, and other Yemeni officials arrived on the same plane.
