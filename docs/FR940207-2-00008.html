[I.D. 013194F] North Pacific Fishery Management Council; Meeting AGENCY: National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS), National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA), Commerce. ACTION: Notice of public meeting.

SUMMARY: The North Pacific Fishery Management Council's Crab Consultation Committee will meet on February 18, 1994, in the Old Federal Building, 605 W. 4th Avenue, room G45, Anchorage, AK. The meeting will begin at 8:30 a.m. The Committee, composed of representatives of the North Pacific Fishery Management Council and the Alaska Board of Fisheries, will review crab stock data and management and provide guidance to the Council and the Board on pertinent crab issues. This meeting is physically accessible to people with disabilities. Requests for sign language interpretation or other auxiliary aids should be directed to Judy Willoughby, on (907) 271&hyph;2809, at least 10 working days prior to the meeting date. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Clarence Pautzke, Executive Director, North Pacific Fishery Management Council, P.O. Box 103136, Anchorage, AK 99510; telephone: (907) 271&hyph;2809. Dated: February 1, 1994. David S. Crestin, Acting Director, Office of Fisheries Conservation and Management, National Marine Fisheries Service.
