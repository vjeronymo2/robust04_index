August 16, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

BARGAINS: OFFICE SUPPLIES, NOT FOR MEMBERS ONLY

We've had many membership clubs the past few years that offer good deals after you pay an annual fee. But it's refreshing to come across a no-membership fee operation that can beat some of the major discounters.

HQ Office Supplies Warehouse is possibly the best deal in office supplies in Southern California. Starting with one store in Culver City in February, 1989, the chain now has 12 locations in the greater Los Angeles area.

Here's an example of the savings offered there. The Thomas Los Angeles/Orange County Map Book (with no ZIP codes), a publication that no one in Los Angeles should be without, usually lists for $22.95. Crown Books' discounted price is $20, but HQ offers it for $12.48 -- a savings of almost 45% off list. We don't know of any spot in town that offers this good a deal.

One of the best-selling word-processing programs, Word Perfect, lists for $495. HQ's price is $249.49, or 49% off.

On a smaller scale, a box of 25 letter-size Pendaflex hanging folders lists for $13.99. Quill, a discount office supply catalogue, has a price of $8.79, but HQ's price is $5.06 -- a dazzling 64% off.

Discouraged with the high cost of Dayrunners appointment books? The compact pocket edition in vinyl retails for $25 and is priced here at $17.86, or 28% off list. The 8 1/2-by-11-inch leather Entrepreneur edition lists for $175, but HQ's price is $125.96. The discount is even better on the vinyl Running Mate, which is $28.90 instead of the $40 list, and the Pocket Planner, which is $12.92 instead of the $20 list. If you're still into the Classic Dayrunner, you'll save 37% on the black leather model. Instead of $110, it's $68.96. You don't score as well on the refills, which are discounted from 12% to 14% for the Entrepreneur variety and 16% to 26% for the Classic edition.

Whether you need a copier (Sharp or Canon), a typewriter (Smith Corona, Brother or Canon), an answering machine (AT&T and Phonemate), file cabinets, banquet tables, microwaves (Emerson), coffee makers (Norelco), desk lamps or, of course, all kinds of paper, you'll do well with average price cuts of 30% to 60% off list.

There are toll-free 800 numbers for phone or fax ordering from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Monday through Friday. For phone order service, call (800) 765-6060; the fax number is (800) 765-3030. The delivery charge is $15 or 5% of the purchase price, whichever is greater. If you place your order before noon, it will arrive the following day.
