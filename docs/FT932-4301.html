930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / Cameras banned from Shinto ceremony at Japanese royal wedding Crown Prince Naruhito and his bride Princess Masako leave the imperial palace in Tokyo in a Rolls-Royce convertible after their marriage yesterday. Cameras were prohibited at the 15-minute ceremony in the palace's inner shrines, with their vows taken only in front of officials of the Shinto religion, of which the emperor is the figurehead
