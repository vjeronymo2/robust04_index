February 11, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Shortstop Jose Uribe of the San Francisco Giants cannot leave the Dominican Republic for spring training until he stands trial on a rape charge, a district attorney said.

A woman filed a complaint last month, accusing Uribe of drugging her beer at his discotheque, threatening her with a gun and forcing her to go to a hotel, where he raped her. Uribe denied the charge. A trial date has not been set.
