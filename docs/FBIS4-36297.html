BFN [Text] Russia today expressed the hope that the forthcoming visit of the prime minister, Mr. Narasimha Rao, to Moscow will give a boost to economic and political relations between the two countries. Addressing a press conference in New Delhi today, the deputy prime minister of Russia, Mr. Yuriy Yarov, said several agreements -- including investment guarantee pact and cooperation in science and technology -- will be signed during the visit. Responding to questions, he said Moscow gives priority to ensure uninterrupted supply of defense spares. On cryogenic engine technology, the Russian leader said experts from both the countries have reached an understanding for the supply of engines.
