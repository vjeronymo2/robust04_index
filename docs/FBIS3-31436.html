Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Liana Buligan item: "Liviu Luca, Vice President of the National Confederation of Romania's Free Trade Unions Fratia, Has Had a Serious Accident Following His Statements for Our Paper!"] [Text] On Thursday evening, 11 February, Mr. Liviu Luca, vice president of the National Confederation of Romania's Free Trade Unions [CNSLR] Fratia, granted an exclusive interview to our newspaper by making serious accusations against the current government. Only two days later, a car accident occurred in which Liviu Luca was seriously injured, while the "offender," SRI [Romanian Intelligence Service] Lieutenant Colonel Ion Buica, died. Although the interview, which was granted confidentially in Mr. Luca's office, was published only on Monday, 14 February 1994, by TINERETUL LIBER, we ask ourselves to what extent his bold statements and the accident that was caused by a SRI officer were just an ordinary coincidence.
