910504 FT 04 MAY 91 / Sunday bans lifted THE HIGH Court yesterday lifted Sunday trading bans on shops in eight local-authority areas. The court lifted an injunction and released Woolworths from undertakings that had closed its stores on Sundays at Plymouth, Milton Keynes, Norwich, Southend, Coventry, Rotherham and Greenwich in south-east London. B&Q, the DIY group, and the Co-op were released from undertakings not to open their Exeter stores.
