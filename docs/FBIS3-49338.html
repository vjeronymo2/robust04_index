Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [TA SR report: "Farewell Visit"] [Text] Bratislava--Deputy Prime Minister Jozef Prokes yesterday received Ahmad al-Mutlaq, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the Republic of Iraq to the Slovak Republic resident in Prague, for a farewell visit in Bratislava. During their talks, both sides expressed their interest in maintaining and developing mutual relations, particularly economic relations.
