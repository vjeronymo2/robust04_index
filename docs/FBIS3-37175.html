Smirnov: "Is Gamsakhurdia Alive?"] [Text] "Gamsakhurdia is alive. And he will soon head a Georgian Government in exile in a state somewhere outside the former USSR." This was announced quite categorically by a group of Georgian Zviadists on an unofficial visit to Stavropol to meet with kindred spirits, Russian nationalists, and the Georgian diaspora.
