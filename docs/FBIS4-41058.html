BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow, 22 Jun -- Returning after an interval to vote on the law on the federal budget for 1994 as a whole, the chamber's deputies again failed to adopted it. This time, however, 221 deputies voted for the 1994 budget, whereas this morning during the first vote on this issue, the budget was supported by 194 deputies. A total of 226 votes is needed for a positive decision to be taken. After a keen debate, deputies passed a decision to return to a vote on the country's budget at 1600 hours after the situation has been discussed in factions.
