BFN [Text] Baku, 26 May (AZERTAC)--Today, after a two-day debate, the parliament of Azerbaijan passed the Republic budget for 1994 year with a deficit of 23.6 per cent. The MPs considered this level of deficit more acceptable than a deficit level of 31.2 per cent stipulated by the previous draft budget. According to the passed budget, the Republic revenue will come to 138.5 billion manats and expenditure--181.4 billion manats this year. The Ministry of Finance proposes to cover a deficit of 43 billion manats by releasing domestic loan, increasing tax dues as well as by means of privatization, draft of which was approved by the president in April, however was not been passed by the parliament so far.
