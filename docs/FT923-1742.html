920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / Observer: Deja vu Invoices in the soup, computer discs in the bedlinen? Surely not. Tomorrow, just for the day, executives at London's Hyatt Carlton Tower will be sporting waiters' aprons and bell boys' jackets in an exercise designed to help managers understand the problems encountered by their staff. It is all part of parent Hyatt Hotels Corporation strategy for 'improving lines of communication'. Although the Chicago corporate HQ will be shut, Darryl Hartley-Leonard, president of Hyatt Hotels Corporation, will be on hand as a front desk clerk, the position in which he started his Hyatt career.
