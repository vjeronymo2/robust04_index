February 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ENTREPRENEUR KOONS

Koons combines the worst aspects of the art world with the worst aspects of capitalism.

Publishing the Koons article has alerted readers to the most despicable form of art fraud, but I question the Times decision to put the article in the Calendar section. The business section would have been more appropriate.

BOB KAPLAN

Rancho Palos Verdes
