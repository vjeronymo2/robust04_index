BFN [Excerpts] Belgrade, 15 Apr (TANJUG) -- The Chamber of Republics of the Federal Assembly today adopted a declaration vehemently condemning the NATO air strikes against the Serbs in former Bosnia-Herzegovina carried out under UN auspices that caused destruction and casualties among the civilian population. [passage omitted] The declaration was supported by 28 deputies; two voted against. The same document was adopted by the Chamber of Citizens of the Federal Assembly yesterday.
