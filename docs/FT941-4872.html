940309 FT 09 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Thorn EMI in German buy Thorn EMI, the UK music and rentals group, is to acquire Intercord Tongesellschaft, the largest independent German recorded music company owned by Verlagsgruppe Georg von Holtzbrinck. Intercord has an annual turnover of DM150m (about Pounds 58.5m) and about 5 per cent of the German market. Artists include Reinhard Mey, Pe Werner,Pur, Depeche Mode, Erasure and Captain Hollywood.
