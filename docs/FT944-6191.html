941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Budget 94 (Government Statements): Change to partial exemption rules The rules on the VAT partial exemption de minimis limit are to change, to help combat tax avoidance estimated to cost Pounds 20m a year. Customs and Excise said: 'From December 1 1994, to qualify for treatment under the de minimis limit, businesses must satisfy the additional condition that VAT incurred on the exempt supplies they make (exempt input tax) must be no more than 50 per cent of the VAT on all their purchases.'
