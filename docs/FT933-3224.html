930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / Tenders sought for Forth bridge THE SCOTTISH Office is to invite companies to register their interest in tendering to build the second Forth road bridge, which could be complete by the end of the century, James Buxton writes. Yesterday Trafalgar House and Balfour Beatty said they had formed a joint venture to tender for a design, construction and operating contract on the project. Two other consortia have been formed. The deadline for submissions is November 2.
