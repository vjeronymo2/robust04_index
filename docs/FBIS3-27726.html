Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Tokyo, Jan. 29 KYODO -- The Diet enacted the government-proposed political reform bills Saturday [29 January] whose key elements are set to be revised at the regular Diet session slated to convene on Monday in line with a compromise reached between Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa and opposition Liberal Democratic Party (LDP) President Yohei Kono. The enactment came on the last day of the current extraordinary Diet session, the deadline set by Hosokawa, who had staked his political fortunes on passage of the package. The House of Councillors approved the package in a plenary session after the House of Representatives passed it. Each chamber gave its blessing to the measures by a stand-up vote. [passage omitted]
