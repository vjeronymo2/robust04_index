September 18, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Spectradyne Inc. announced that Leonard Goldberg, former president and chief operating officer of 20th Century Fox Film Corp., has been elected to the board of Spectradyne, which supplies in-room video and data communication systems to hotels. Before joining Fox, Goldberg operated Leonard Goldberg Co. and Mandy Films. In 1972, he formed Spelling/Goldberg Productions, a partnership with Aaron Spelling, which spawned a string of hit TV shows. Spectradyne is owned by Marvin Davis, who previously owned 20th Century Fox Film.
