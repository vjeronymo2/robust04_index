February 1, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County P.M. Final

IN BRIEF;

WORTHY, 6 OTHERS JOIN ALL-STAR TEAM

James Worthy of the Los Angeles Lakers was among seven players added today to the NBA Western Conference team for the Feb. 12 All-Star Game.

The other reserves, chosen by the conference coaches, were guard Clyde Drexler and center Kevin Duckworth of Portland, guard John Stockton and center Mark Eaton of Utah, and forwards Tom Chambers of Phoenix and Chris Mullin of Golden State.

Previously selected as starters in fan balloting were guards Magic Johnson of the Lakers and Dale Ellis of Seattle, center Akeem Olajuwon of Houston and forwards Karl Malone of Utah and Alex English of Denver.

Pat Riley of the Lakers will coach the West team that will face the Eastern Conference team in the All-Star Game at Houston.
