Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 15 -- Party General Secretary Do Muoi received here this afternoon Mr. Ku Pun-mu, vice president of Lucky Goldstar Group of South Korea, on the latter's current visit to Vietnam. Speaking to his guest, the Vietnamese party leader said that Vietnam wished to learn experiences, receive capital and advanced technology, and promote the multifaceted cooperation with other countries, thus strengthening the construction of a prosperous country, contributing to the cause of peace, cooperation and development in the region and all over the world. Also present at the reception were Director General of the General Department of Post and Telecommunications Dang Van Than, deputy head of the party External Relations Commission Nguyen Van Son.
