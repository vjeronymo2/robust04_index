940309 FT 09 MAR 94 / UK Company News: USDC USDC Investment Trust, managed by GT Management, lifted net asset value by 30 per cent to 281.5p over the 1993 year, outperforming the gain of 22.6 per cent achieved by the FT-A World Index. The dividend is maintained at 4.5p with a final of 3.25p, paid from net revenue lower at Pounds 1.72m (Pounds 2.05m). Earnings per share were 4.69p (5.59p).
