BFN [Excerpt] The Ugandan Government has decided to open embassies in South Africa and Japan. This was disclosed today by the minister of finance and economic planning, Mr. Joshua Mayanja-Nkangi, when he was presenting the 1994-95 budget in the National Resistance Council. Mr. Mayanja-Nkangi disclosed that he was the one who announced the ban on trade with South Africa in 1964, when he was minister of commerce, in protest against the country's white minority regime's policy of apartheid.
