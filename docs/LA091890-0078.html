September 18, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: NEWPORT BEACH;

CITY TO HOLD TOUR OF BIKE TRAIL NETWORK

In an effort to reacquaint residents with the Newport Beach bicycle trail network, the city is holding its sixth annual Great Newport Beach Bicycle Tour on Oct. 7 at 10 a.m.

Participants will tour the approximately 9 miles of trails along the Upper Back Bay bikeway and Back Bay Drive and also have an opportunity to view demonstrations on bicycle maintenance and safety.

For additional information and registration, phone the city's Public Works Department at (714) 644-3311.
