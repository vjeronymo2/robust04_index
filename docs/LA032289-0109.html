March 22, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE: LOS ANGELES COUNTY

Michael B. McAllister has been named president of Bayley, Martin & Fay Inc. of Los Angeles, an insurance brokerage. McAllister previously was executive vice president and director of BMF Los Angeles' entertainment division.

Timothy N. Thalman, Michael J. Marsolek and Larry E. DeBoer have been named senior vice presidents. Richard R. Friese has been named manager of the entertainment division.
