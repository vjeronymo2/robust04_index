940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Cairn Energy cuts US holding Cairn Energy, the oil and gas exploration and production company, has placed 824,000 shares in Cairn Energy USA raising a net Pounds 3.9m. As a result, Cairn now holds about 54 per cent of CEUSA. Cairn expects to sell further CEUSA shares in a US placing dependent on the proposed acquisition of US oil and gas interests of Aeneas Group by CEUSA.
