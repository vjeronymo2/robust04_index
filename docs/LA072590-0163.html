NAMES IN THE NEWS;

MELVIN BELLI PROFESSES ALTRUISM

Celebrity attorney Melvin Belli says he went into law to help people, not to make money.

"If you're going into law to make money you're going to have a very miserable old age," Belli, who turns 83 on Sunday, said while in San Diego recently for a seminar.

"But if you're going into it to help people, what a life it is. I can't think of anything more rewarding."
