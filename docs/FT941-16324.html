940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / People: Ofgas Clare Spottiswoode, the new gas industry regulator, continues to beef up her staff ahead of the deregulation of the gas industry prompted by the recent monopoly inquiry. She has appointed Eileen Marshall to the newly created post of chief economic adviser where she will lead the in-house team of economists. Marshall, who joins Ofgas on April 1, is currently director of regulation and business affairs at Offer, the electricity regulator. She will be replaced by Tony Boorman, the director of consumer affairs.
