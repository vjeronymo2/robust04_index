BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow April 28 TASS -- The Russian State Duma registered a new deputy faction on Thursday, the Liberal Democratic Union "December 12." The faction will have its own office and representatives in the Duma Council. Under the regulations adopted by the Duma a parliamentary faction has to have at least 35 members to be officially registered. The new faction has 39 deputies, including former members of the reformist Russia's Choice bloc and the nationalist Liberal Democratic Party of Russia.
