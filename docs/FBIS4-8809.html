BFN [Text] Serb forces in Baranja, in east Croatia, have taken part of the heavy weapons which were under the control of the UN Protection Force, according to Belgian military spokesman Colonel (Fredi Van Weg). A group of 520 Belgian soldiers is deployed in Baranja, which has been under Serb occupation for two years. In early April, Croatia agreed on a cease-fire with the rebel Serbs and, under the agreement, the Serbs placed their heavy weapons under UN control in six warehouses. The fact that a certain number of weapons have been stolen should be condemned, because this will have a negative impact on the peace process which has taken off well, Colonel (Van Weg) said.
