941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / International Company News: SocGen to buy rest of Sogenal Societe Generale, one of France's largest banks, has announced a public offer to buy the 5.5 per cent of the share capital of Sogenal, a regional bank, it does not already own. The offer - which will apply to 800,000 publicly-held shares - is expected to be slightly in excess of the FFr127 at which the shares were trading yesterday before they were suspended.
