920630 FT 30 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Turnbull Scott turns in Pounds 570,000 In spite of continuing difficult trading conditions Turnbull Scott Holdings, the motor and property group, achieved a profit before tax of Pounds 570,000 in the year to March 31. This was struck after an exceptional charge of Pounds 135,000 and compared with a loss of Pounds 3.39m last time. The bulk of last year's loss related to Pounds 3.89m of exceptional charges covering property write-downs and the closure of the TS Frobisher property arm. The profit came on turnover halved from Pounds 26.8m to Pounds 13.1m. Earnings per share came out at 6.1p (37.5p losses). The Turnbull family intends to take the group private and is offering 130p a share in cash and loan stock for the outstanding equity.
