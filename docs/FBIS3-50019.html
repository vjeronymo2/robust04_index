Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 14/01/1994 -- With regard to the affirmation in the Bulgarian DENYAT daily alleging that Romania would have moved military units to the border with Bulgaria, the Defense Ministry declared: "The Romanian Army has good relations with the Bulgarian Army in full concordance with the climate of security, friendship, understanding and good-neighbourhood between our countries. The information published by the daily DENIAT is groundless. No movement of troops has taken place along the frontier with Bulgaria.
