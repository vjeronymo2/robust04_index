930306 FT 06 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: Skopje air crash Seventy-nine people were killed and 18 injured when a recently-built Fokker 100 airliner plunged to the ground just after take-off from Skopje, capital of Macedonia. The Fokker was bound for Zurich, Switzerland. Accident investigators from Fokker and the Dutch civil aviation authority arrived in Skopje last night.
