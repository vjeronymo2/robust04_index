930114 FT 14 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Recovery continues at Kelt KELT ENERGY, the independent oil and gas explorer, swung from losses of Pounds 4.13m to profits of Pounds 629,000 pre-tax for the half year ended September 30. The group returned to profits in the second half of last year and finished the 12 months with a profit of Pounds 163,000. First half turnover totalled Pounds 8.91m (Pounds 18.1m, including Pounds 6.95m from discontinued operations). Earnings per share of 0.4p compared with losses of 9.6p. Directors said the figures reflected the streamlining which took place at the end of 1991. They added that the results were 'free of the distortions' caused by the 1991 financial reorganisation.
