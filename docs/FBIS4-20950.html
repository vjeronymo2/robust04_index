BFN [Text] Fides News Agency reports that the La Paz 1st Controlled Substances Court [Juzgado Primero de Substancias Controladas] has ordered the opening of judiciary police proceedings against Oscar Eid Franco, Susana Seleme, and other aides of former President Jaime Paz Zamora, and has ruled that former Interior Minister Guillermo Capobianco and former Santa Cruz Mayor Rolando Arostegui may be tried by a court for their alleged involvement with drug trafficker Isaac "Oso" Chavarria.
