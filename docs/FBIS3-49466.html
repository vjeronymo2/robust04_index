Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] At the end of November 1993, the Czech Republic state budget showed a surplus of 2.7 billion Czech korunas. This transpires from the data of the Czech Statistical Office released yesterday [10 January]. The number of unemployed has increased to 175,800, while the number of job vacancies has dropped to approximately 55,000. Thus, the number of unemployed was three times higher in November than the number of job vacancies. Two-thirds of the unemployed are workers. The unemployment rate was 3.34 percent.
