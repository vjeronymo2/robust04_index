BFN [Text] Mr. 'Ali Salim al-Bid, the vice chairman of the Presidential Council and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, made a telephone call to Mr. Yasir 'Arafat, the president of the State of Palestine and chairman of the Executive Committee of the PLO. The talks dealt with the current military developmnets and the brutal war imposed on our Yemeni people by the al-Ahmar gang. The Palestinian president expressed concern of the events and affirmed that he is going to exert his efforts to stop bloodshed in Yemen. There was exchange of greetings on the occasion of 'Id al-Adha. The Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat wished the Yemeni people peace, stability, and progress and Mr. 'Ali Salim al-Bid also congratulated Mr. Yasir 'Arafat on this happy occasion, and wished the Palestinian people more success on the path of achieving their Palestinian state.
