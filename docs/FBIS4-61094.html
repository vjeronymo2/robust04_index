BFN [Text] A briefing has been held at Ukraine's Foreign Ministry to sum up the visit to Japan by the ministry's delegation led by Mykola Makarevych, first deputy foreign minister. The principal aim of the visit was to lay a contractual and legal foundation for Ukrainian-Japanese bilateral relations. It was emphasized at the briefing that Japan is one of our country's most promising partners. The issue of developing bilateral cooperation was discussed at Japan's Foreign Ministry and International Trade and Industry Ministry. It was decided that there should be an exchange of delegations of specialist lawyers to work out documents which would become the basis for relations to be enhanced.
