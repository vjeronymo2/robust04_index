941228 FT 28 DEC 94 / Cornhill cuts its home premiums Cornhill Insurance is the latest insurance company to reduce household insurance premiums. The company is introducing no-claims discounts of up to 20 per cent on buildings and contents insurance from January 1 and special discounts for older customers. Last week Halifax Building Society announced it was cutting buildings insurance premiums by an average of 20 per cent from the new year.
