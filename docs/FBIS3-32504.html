Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The U.S. Coast Guard arrested members of the anti-Cuban terrorist organization Alpha 66, who were traveling on board a boat on which agents found weapons, ammunition, and money. According to information from Miami, one of the detainees, Rodolfo Frometa, admitted that they had attempted to carry out an expedition in Cuba, supposedly to land men ashore, but had problems with their boat. The report also points out that these members of Alpha 66 may be accused of violating the U.S. neutrality law.
