BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondent] [Excerpts] Beijing, 27 May -- Jiang Zemin, chairman of the People's Republic of China, accepted Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin's invitation to make an official visit to Russia with great enthusiasm, Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin announced at a news conference in Beijing on the second day of his visit. [passage omitted] Chernomyrdin said: "We were unanimous in the view that relations between our countries are on a firm path towards constructive partnership on a broad range of bilateral and international problems." The prime minister believes that China can play an important role in opening up Siberia and the Far East. The possibility of Russia participating in the reconstruction and modernization of industrial enterprises built with the help of the USSR in the 1950's was discussed, along with the building of the "three gorge" hydroscheme power installations. The sides agreed to lift obstacles hindering joint enterprise, investment cooperation, and other forms of direct partnership. [passage omitted] The exchange of views during the talks and meetings with the Chinese leadership on the main issues in the world showed that the views of China and Russia on the main international issues coincide, or are close, the Russian prime minister said. Constructive inter- relations in regional and world affairs, he said, are an important factor for stability and security in Asia and general improvement of the situation in the world as a whole.
