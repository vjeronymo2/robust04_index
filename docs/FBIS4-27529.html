BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 27 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on May 27 sent a message of greetings to Bakili Muluzi on his election as president of the Republic of Malawi. Expressing the belief that the friendly relations between the two countries would continuously develop in the interests of the two peoples, the message wished the president success in the work for the prosperity of the country.
