July 4, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: CAPISTRANO BEACH

Goodwill site: Goodwill Industries has arranged for a donation station at Palisades United Methodist Church, 27002 Camino de Estrella. The station is open from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. seven days a week and is staffed by an attendant who can issue receipts for donations. Pickup of items too large to be taken to the station can be arranged by calling (714) 547-6300. Donations of reusable household goods support Goodwill's job-training and rehabilitation programs for physically and mentally disabled people.
