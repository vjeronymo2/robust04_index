BFN [PRAVDA and ITAR-TASS column report under the rubric "In Brief": "Enemies All Around"] [Text] "The task of building a new Russian Navy has to be resolved in extremely complex conditions. All the negative processes taking place in the Russian economy are also having an effect on service. We have lost a number of military bases in the Baltic, Black, and Caspian Seas, and attempts to play the naval card are continuing in various political circles," Viktor Chernomyrdin, Russian Federation Government prime minister, stated on Sunday, taking part in the ceremony of the commissioning in the Russian Navy of the first category destroyer Besstrashnyy.
