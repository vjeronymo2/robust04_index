940406 FT 06 APR 94 / Malaysia expels journalist Malaysia has expelled a journalist working for Inter Press Service (IPS), a news agency specialising in coverage of issues concerning developing countries, writes Jose Galang in Manila. The work permit of Ms Leah Makabenta, a Filipina based in Kuala Lumpur, was cancelled following 'an unspecified article deemed to be a threat to national security,' the agency's Manila-based regional office for Asia and the Pacific said.
