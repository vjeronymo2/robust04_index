BFN [From the "Face to Face" newscast] [Text] Today marks the 75th anniversary of Emiliano Zapata's death. There will be a number of ceremonies, including one in Morelos State. The president will attend this morning's ceremony in Cuernavaca, Morelos State. There will be another in Zacatepec this afternoon. Cuauhtemoc Cardenas, Party of the Democratic Revolution presidential candidate, will attend the ceremony in Zacatepec. As for the anniversary, there will be a demonstration in Mexico City. Some 70 peasant organizations will stage a march, which will end in El Zocalo, downtown Mexico City. They will declare a seven-day sitdown strike here to demand the repeal of Article 27 of the Constitution. Meanwhile, there will be some celebrations, quote, in Chiapas, unquote. A report in REFORMA newspaper adds: The Indian and Peasant State Committee will stage marches, conduct motorcades, seize land, hold regional sitdown strikes, take over public buildings, and hold regional gatherings in the municipalities of Tapachula, Palenque, San Cristobal de las Casas, and Comitan. This is how the 75th anniversary of Emiliano Zapata's death will be commemorated.
