BFN [Excerpts] Two soldiers have been stabbed and wounded, one moderately and one lightly, at the entrance to the Jerusalem mall in Mallaha. The assailant, an East Jerusalem Arab, was captured at the site. Mark Weiss reports. [Begin Weiss recording] The attack occurred just before 1700 [1400 GMT], during the busy afternoon shopping period. The attacker, an Arab from the East Jerusalem village of Jabal al-Mukabar, stabbed two soldiers close to a bus stop at the entrance to the mall. [passage omitted] The two soldiers were rushed to Hadassah-'En Kerem Hospital and both were treated for stab wounds in the shoulder. The condition of one was described as moderate and the other one was suffering from light wounds. The assailant was chased by police, security staff, and civilians and captured carrying the knife. [end recording] [Jerusalem New Channel 2 Television Network in Hebrew at 1700 GMT on 27 April adds that "the terrorist belongs to Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement]. A few months ago he was detained on suspicion of participating in riots and was later released until the beginning of his trial." Also, "An Arab woman was shot and critically wounded when she tried to stab a soldier in the center of Gaza. The soldier was not hurt."]
