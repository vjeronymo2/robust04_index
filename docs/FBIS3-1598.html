Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Dhaka, February 27 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen left here today en route to Sri Lanka via Bangkok after ending a four-day official goodwill visit to Bangladesh. The Chinese foreign minister was seen off at the Zia International Airport by Bangladesh Foreign Minister Mustafizur Rahman. On Sunday morning, Qian had brief talks with Awami League chief and leader of opposition in parliament Sheik Hasina and also paid a visit to the parliamentary building. Before his departure, Qian attended a news conference and replied questions on a wide-range international issues. Qian arrived here on Thursday from Pakistan and Bangladesh is the second leg of his five-nation tour which also includes the Maldives and Iran.
