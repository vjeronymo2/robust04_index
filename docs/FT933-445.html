930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / The Labour Party Conference: Renationalisation of water ruled out A STRENGTHENED regulatory regime rather than a return to public ownership would face the privatised water companies under the next Labour government. Mr Chris Smith, shadow environment secretary, promised to ban the disconnection of domestic consumers for non-payment of bills, as conference approved a resolution calling for the water industry to be brought back under public control at the earliest opportunity.
