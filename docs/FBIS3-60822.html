Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Damascus, 25 Dec (SANA)--The Palestine National Liberation Movement, Fatah [the Uprising], has announced that one of its groups has detonated explosive and incendiary devices inside the Israeli ship Jrush Shalom [name as received] while it was docked at Eilat port. A statement issued by the movement today said the explosion caused a fire in the ship and that heavy clouds of poisonous gases were leaked as a result of the fire in the ship's cargo of poisonous materials. The ship's cargo was due to be unloaded into the warehouses of the enemy's army. The statement said 11 sailors were injured, and the group that carried out the operation returned safely.
