BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Excerpt] Moscow, 9 Jun -- Russian State Duma chairman Ivan Rybkin thinks Boris Yeltsin's decrees on economic reform appeared because of "the known backwardness of Russian legislation." This is what he stated today while opening parliamentary hearings on the strengthening of cooperation between the federal authorities and local state administrative bodies. Ivan Rybkin reported that the important gap in the legislation should be overcome as soon as possible and key legislative acts needed to be adopted for this to happen, including civil and land codes as well as a number of laws on legislation.
