December 26, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: 1990 ORANGE COUNTY. CHALLENGE FOR THE 1990S;

COMMUNITY LEADERS WERE ASKED WHAT WILL BE THE BIGGEST SINGLE CHALLENGE FACING ORANGE COUNTY IN THE NEXT DECADES AND ALSO THEIR TOP FIVE ISSUES.

Housing: "Decent, affordable housing. The county's real estate continues to appreciate, making housing (that is) affordable to low-and-moderate-(income) families almost impossible to find. Local governments, responding to the demands of voters, appear timid about requiring low-cost housing in their jurisdictions."-Mary Anne Galdo, Housing specialist with the Orange County Human Relations Commission

Top 5 Issues

Homelessness/ affordable housing.

Health care.

Transportation.

Prejudice toward minority groups.

Immigration.
