BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow June 13 TASS -- The Russia's Democratic Choice Party has voted in favour of economist Yegor Gaydar being party chairman and Oleg Boyko, head of the National Credit Bank and chief of the Olby concern, chairman of the party's executive committee. Boyko promised to contribute to creating an efficient organizational structure attracting more entrepreneurs to its ranks and boosting the party's publicity activity.
