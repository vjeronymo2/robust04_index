Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 18 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received a delegation of the Communist Party of Canada (M-L) led by its national leader Hardial Bains on a visit to Korea. On hand were Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] Hwang Chang-yop and vice-director of a department of the C.C. [Central Committee] the WPK Pak Kyong-son and Vice-chairman of the State Education Commission Yo Won-ku. President Kim Il-song had a talk with the guests in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. He arranged a luncheon for the guests.
