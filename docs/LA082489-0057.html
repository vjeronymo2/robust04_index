August 24, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

CalFed Inc., Los Angeles, has named Peter Wyman senior vice president and director of internal audit. Wyman joined CalFed in 1987 as vice president and director of internal audit. CalFed's principal subsidiary is California Federal Bank, which has named two senior vice presidents. David C. Henri was named senior vice president and general counsel. Henri, who previously served as associate general counsel in the company's litigation/corporate department, joined California Federal in 1984 as a senior attorney. Stephen E. Sear was named senior vice president-property management. Sear joined California Federal in 1982 as a building manager.
