June 24, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ON GHOST SINGERS, LIP-SYNCING AND HIP-SYNCING

I went to the recent Milli Vanilli concert in Los Angeles, and I'm sure that the thousands of other mesmerized girls in the audience who paid to see the two best bods in the music business will agree with me: We were there to see Rob and Fab's hip-sync and could not care less about their lip-sync!

RHONDA WILLIAMS

Santa Monica
