941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Sleepy Kids deal Sleepy Kids, the animation and character merchandising group, has signed another 11 licensing deals for Budgie, The Little Helicopter, the character created by the Duchess of York, bringing its number of deals worldwide to 86. A second series of 13 cartoons featuring Budgie will be shown on children's ITV from January 1995, along with a repeat of the first series.
