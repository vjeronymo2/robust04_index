BFN [Text] Philippe Pentecost, a businessman of European origin, and Nidoish Naisseline, the Kanak separatist leader, yesterday established a new Caledonian political party that advocates the establishment of an "autonomous Caledonian state, a member of the Francophone community and the EC." The party is called "1999," which is the year following the referendum on self-determination provided for in the 1988 Matignon accords.
