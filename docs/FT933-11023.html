930731 FT 31 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Start-up costs hit Zetters The start-up costs for a division to supply telephone-interfaced information technology was the main factor behind a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.04m to Pounds 880,000 in the year to March 31 at Zetters Group. The costs led to increased operating losses of Pounds 155,000 (Pounds 21,000) in the competitions and marketing activities. The profits from pools fell to Pounds 812,000 (Pounds 844,000) after lower interest received. Group turnover was Pounds 22m (Pounds 22.9m) of which pools provided Pounds 21.5m (Pounds 22.4m). Earnings per share were 8.9p (10p). An unchanged final dividend of 4p is proposed for a maintained total of 8p.
