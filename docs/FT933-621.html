930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / UK Company News: GT Japan net asset value at 241.9p Net asset value at GT Japan Investment Trust improved from 149p to 241.9p over the year to June 30. Net revenue for the 12 months to the end of June was Pounds 1.48m (Pounds 851,000) for earnings per share of 2.38p (1.36p). A proposed final dividend of 0.85p plus a special of 0.5p makes a total for the year of 1.75p (1.4p).
