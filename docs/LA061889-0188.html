June 18, 1989, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

NOTRE DAME, SYRACUSE MAKE DATE IN BASKETBALL

A home game with Notre Dame and travel dates with Duke and Florida State are among the highlights of the 1989-1990 Syracuse basketball schedule.

Syracuse, which was 30-8 last season when it was one of the final eight teams in the NCAA tournament, will play 17 of its 27 regular-season games at home, starting with Rutgers on Nov. 27.

The Orangemen will host Notre Dame on Feb. 20 in a return-match and play Florida State in Orlando on Feb. 3.

Duke and Syracuse will meet in Greensboro, N.C., on Dec. 6 as part of the inaugural Big East vs. Atlantic Coast Conference week.
