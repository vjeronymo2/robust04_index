BFN [Text] Pretoria May 3 SAPA -- The Pan-Africanist Congress [PAC] has been admitted as a member of the Transitional Executive Council [TEC]. According to a proclamation by President F. W. de Klerk in a special government gazette of Tuesday, the PAC is now a participant. Although TEC sittings have finished -- its management committee may meet on Monday -- the move will give the PAC a seat on the joint military command council which is planning the new South African National Defence Force [SANDF]. The PAC will now be entitled to have its Azanian People's Liberation Army cadres included in the new SANDF.
