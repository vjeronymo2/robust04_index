Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin presented a proposal for a multilateral council of foreign and Russian bankers, investors, and entrepreneurs. He announced this at the world economic forum in Davos. Chernomyrdin believes this council could be convened this May-July in Moscow. Chernomyrdin thinks that Russia can use about $10-12 billion in foreign investments. He emphasized that conditions for foreign investors in Russia is gradually improving.
