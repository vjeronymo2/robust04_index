BFN [Text] Cape Town June 27 SAPA -- Minister of Sport Mr Steve Tshwete has been appointed acting minister of education while the minister, Professor Sibusiso Bhengu, recovers from a mild stroke he suffered last Thursday [23 June], President Nelson Mandela announced Monday. Mr Mandela said after visiting Prof Bhengu in a Cape Town clinic that he was making an "excellent recovery". "Professor Bhengu is able to walk around and communicate. His physicians have, however, advised that he should rest for a few weeks. The government of national unity and I wish Professor Bhengu a speedy recovery." Mr Tshwete would act until he recovered sufficiently to resume his duties, the president said.
