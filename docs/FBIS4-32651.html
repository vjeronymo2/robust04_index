BFN [Text] At the request of Peter Boross, President of the Republic Arpad Goncz has relieved the administrative state secretaries at the Ministries of Defense and Foreign Affairs and at the Prime Minister's Office. Rudolf Joo [administrative state secretary at the Ministry of Defense], will depart in the middle of July, Janos Martonyi [administrative state secretary at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs] will leave at the end of August, while Jozsef Kajdi [administrative state secretary at the Prime Minister's Office] will vacate his office on 10 July.
