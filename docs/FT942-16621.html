940408 FT 08 APR 94 / International Company News: Pechiney International to acquire Capolo stake Pechiney International, the packaging arm of Pechiney, the French state-owned aluminium group, yesterday announced that it would take a 40 per cent stake in Capolo, the Italian packaging group. Capolo, which has annual sales of L270bn (Dollars 163m), employs 800 people in seven factories in Italy and is one of the country's largest food packaging companies. Pechiney declined to state the amount it was paying for the stake in Capolo, which is currently owned 100 per cent by Europe Capital Partners, an investment fund. The current management team will remain in place, the French group said.
