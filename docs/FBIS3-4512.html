Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 31 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman today expressed the hope that all parties concerned in South Africa will take practical measures to end violence. Asked to comment on the recent aggravation of violence in South Africa at a weekly news conference here this afternoon, spokesman Wu Jianmin said that the recent aggravation of the violent conflicts in some regions of South Africa has aroused concern of the international community. "China always firmly supports the peace process of South Africa and hopes all parties concerned in South Africa will overcome their differences through negotiations, take practical measures to end violence, so as to ensure a smooth general election and join hands in working for a new South Africa in which all races are equal," Wu said.
