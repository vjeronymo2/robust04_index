January 10, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY ROUNDUP: SUPERIOR INDUSTRIES WINS WHEEL CONTRACT WITH FORD

Superior Industries said it has received a five-year contract to supply Ford Motor Co. with aluminum wheels for cars and trucks. The contract amount was not revealed.

The Van Nuys company said the contract extends from 1989 to 1993. Superior makes more than 24 wheel models for Ford, representing annual sales of more than $100 million.
