Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] President Glavkos Kliridhis held a meeting this afternoon with Gustave Feissel, the UN secretary general's alternate special representative in Cyprus, within the framework of the proximity talks. In a statement after the meeting, Mr. Feissel said that the proximity talks will continue on Monday but this time at a more intensive pace. He also noted that in the meantime the UN officials and the three experts who arrived in Cyprus will prepare ideas to submit to the two sides that would facilitate the process and aim at achieving an agreement on the confidence-building measures [CBM's]. Government spokesman Ioannis Kasoulidhis said that today's meeting has concluded this phase of the proximity talks, during which the two sides outlined their positions and views and exchanged views. This morning, Mr. Feissel had a meeting with Rauf Denktas.
