BFN [Text] The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] foiled an attempt by three terrorists to infiltrate the settlement of Neve Deqalim in the Qatif Bloc this morning. An IDF force pursued the terrorists out of the settlement. One terrorist was apprehended, another was shot in the leg and detained after he tried to attack a soldier, and the third terrorist managed to escape. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the IDF lodged a complaint with the Palestinian police and is continuing to investigate the incident.
