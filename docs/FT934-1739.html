931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / Red Star withdrawn from sale The government has withdrawn BR's Red Star parcels business from sale because no satisfactory offer has been received. Red Star is a small company in the parcels' delivery business with 1,200 staff and sales of Pounds 40m, on which it makes an annual loss of Pounds 9m. BR is to prepare a new management plan with a view to the business achieving financial viability and a sale being completed next year
