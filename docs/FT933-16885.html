930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Anglo St James Anglo St James, the commercial property developer, cut pre-tax losses to Pounds 943,000 in 1992, against Pounds 2.72m for the previous 18 months. Mr Jeffrey Green, chairman, said that virtually all group properties were now income producing. Rental income totalled Pounds 934,000 (Pounds 792,000 for 18 months). Losses were struck after exceptional charges of Pounds 504,000 (Pounds 1.63m). Losses per share were 5.8p (23.1p).
