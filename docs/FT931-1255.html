930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / World Trade News: Congo oil deal Technip, the French engineering group, has won an engineering and supply contract in the development of the N'Kossa offshore oil field off Congo, Alice Rawsthorn writes from Paris. The budget for the construction of the whole development, which will include two drilling platforms and a production barge, is FFr8.5bn (Dollars 1.5bn) and Technip has won one of the largest contracts in the project. The field is expected to provide a third of Congo's oil production by 1997.
