Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- German Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel will meet Bosnian Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic in Bonn later today. The German Foreign Ministry said in Bonn that after the meeting, Kinkel will hold talks with Lord Owen and Thorvald Stoltenberg, the two cochairmen of the Geneva conference on Yugoslavia. The meetings will focus on efforts aimed at ending the conflict in Bosnia-Herzegovina.
