June 13, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Fidel and Ted: Turner Broadcasting chief Ted Turner spent last weekend in Cuba talking to Fidel Castro. As a result, his interview with the Cuban chief of state, "A Conversation With Fidel Castro," is slated to air on CNN the night of June 25, preempting "Larry King Live" that Monday evening. BETH KLEID
