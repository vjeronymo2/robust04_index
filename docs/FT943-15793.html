940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Norwegian buy for Newman Tonks Newman Tonks, the building products group, has purchased Bergen Lasservice for a maximum consideration of NKr20m (Pounds 1.9m) cash. Bergen Lasservice, a locking system and electronic security specialist, is based in Bergen, Norway, and in the year to end-December 1993 made a pre-tax profit of NKr2.9m on turnover of NKr17m.
