920311 FT 11 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Consld Venture net assets rise Consolidated Venture Trust announced net asset value increased from 211.5p to 275.5p over year to January 31. At July 31 the figure was 263.5p. Pre-tax profits for the year to end-January were Pounds 412,000 (Pounds 8,000 losses) for earnings per share of 2.12p (losses of 0.45p. The proposed single final dividend is 1.5p (nil).
