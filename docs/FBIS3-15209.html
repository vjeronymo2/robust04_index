Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report on comment by Russian Federation Council Chairman Vladimir Shumeyko: "Vladimir Shumeyko: By the End of March the Federation Council Will Propose Specific Measures To Implement the President's Message"] [Text] Answering a question from a ROSSIYSKIYE VESTI correspondent, Vladimir Shumeyko, chairman of the Federal Assembly's upper chamber, said: After careful study of the president's message in the chamber's committees we will meet to sum up the proposals. This Federation Council sitting is planned for 15 March. Of course, all this will have to be straightened out, which will take at least another two weeks. After this the chamber will submit to the president its proposals regarding a number of draft laws and possibly presidential edicts translating the fundamental tenets into the language of specific measures. We don't intend to seek out things to criticize in the president's message, we are concerned exclusively with action.
