BFN [Text] DPRK leader Kim Il-song has sent the Cuban people a message of solidarity. The statesman received Pedro Margolles, director of the Latin American news agency PRENSA LATINA, at Government Palace in Pyongyang. On that occasion, Kim Il-Song took advantage of the interview on the situation in the peninsula and throughout the world to send greetings to the Cubans and their president, Fidel Castro.
