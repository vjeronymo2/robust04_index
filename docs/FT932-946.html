930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / International Company News: MCB in Sri Lanka MUSLIM Commercial Bank, Pakistan's first public sector bank, is opening its first foreign branch next month with a view to expanding internationally, writes Farhan Bokhari from Karachi. The MCB branch will begin operations in Colombo, Sri Lanka, next month as a first step to expanding operations in parts of southern and eastern Asia. Other branches are planned for the Maldives and Hong Kong.
