April 18, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

IDENTITY CRISIS: The feds have issued a list of firms whose names sound like financial institutions but are not banks, trust companies or S&Ls. On the list of sound-alikes is Great American Bank & Trust in Laguna Beach, easily confused with the local branch of San Diego-based Great American Bank -- "the legitimate one," according to a federal spokesman. "Somebody is trading on their name." Steve Emmons and David Reyes
