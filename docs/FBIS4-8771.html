BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 18 Apr (TANJUG) -- The office of president of the Srpska republic, Radovan Karadzic, stated today that -- following yesterday's meeting with UN Secretary General Butrus-Ghali's special envoy Yasushi Akashi and his appeal -- the authorities of the Srpska republic have decided, as a sign of good will, to immediately carry out or help in carrying out the following: 1. Serb forces will remain outside a three-kilometer exclusion zone, on the left bank of the Drina River near Gorazde, and will agree to the immediate deployment of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] troops. 2. Medical aid and the evacuation of the wounded and sick from Gorazde and other locations. 3. UN High Commissioner for Refugees, International Committee of the Red Cross and Handicap International may continue their humanitarian convoys. 4. Freedom of movement for all humanitarian workers on the entire territory of the Srpska republic. 5. Police units of the Srpska republic will escort all convoys, it is said in the statement released by the office of the president of the Srpska republic.
