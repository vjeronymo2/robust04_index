BFN [Excerpts] After Rome, Jeddah was the second stop in Prime Minister Mokdad Sifi's tour. He arrived today on a two-day official visit. The head of government was met by Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal, Saudi foreign minister. Our correspondent Faouzia Dati reports from Jeddah: [Dati] The head of government, Mokdad Sifi, arrived in Jeddah this morning accompanied by the ministers of foreign affairs, energy and industry, finance and equipment. Sifi was met by Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal. Later, Mokdad Sifi had a meeting with Prince Sultan Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, second deputy prime minister and minister of defense and aviation. The visit is within the framework of cooperation and consultation between the two countries in many spheres which concern the future of bilateral relations and others which concern the future of the Arab world in general. [passage omitted] The visit will provide an opportunity for Algerian and Saudi officials to discuss the most effective means of cooperation and investment in all areas, particularly in the economic sphere. Bilateral meetings were held this evening at the hospitality palace between Algerian ministers and their Saudi counterparts to discuss cooperation in specific areas. [passage omitted]
