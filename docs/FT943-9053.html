940813 FT 13 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Burlington Burlington Group, the investment holding company, reported a net asset value at June 30 of 17.71p per share, after taking its investments at market value, down from 20.45p at end-December and 18.78p at June 1993. Pre-tax profits for the six months to end-June improved to Pounds 72,000 (Pounds 64,000), helped by capital profits from a reduction in the fixed interest portfolio. Earnings per share emerged at 0.43p (0.39p). The directors said they expected a similar outcome for the full year and sufficient earnings to cover a maintained dividend of 0.6p.
