BFN [Text] Varna, June 22 (BTA) -- NATO General Antonio Milani, deputy commander-in-chief of Allied Forces Southern Europe, today met with Bulgarian Deputy Navy Commander Rear Admiral Khristo Kontrov. General Milani has been visiting Bulgaria since the start of this week. The sides reaffirmed agreements between the working groups of the Allied Forces Southern Europe and the Bulgarian Navy Staff. The General Staff of the Bulgarian Army will be asked to approve a plan for a Black Sea naval exercise under Partnership for Peace in June or July 1995. It will be attended by four to eight NATO ships deployed in the Mediterranean. The participants will practice rescuing a ship in distress, struggle with fires and flooding water, evacuation of wounded people, etc.
