930302 FT 02 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Gartmore American assets increase Gartmore American Securities, a split capital investment trust specialising in high yielding North American equities, reported net asset value per share of 43p at December 31 compared with 35.2p at March 31 1992. Net revenue for the nine months to end-December was Pounds 842,000 (Pounds 1.11m) for earnings per share of 2.28p (3p). A third interim dividend of 1p has already been declared, payable on April 2.
