920428 FT 28 APR 92 / International Capital Markets: Hammerson ratings cut by Moody's HAMMERSON, the UK property group, has had its credit ratings cut by Moody's Investors Service, the US credit rating agency, and may yet face another downgrading, writes Simon London. Moody's has cut the long-term credit rating from A2 to A3, a rating which applies to a recent Pounds 100m Eurosterling bond issue. The short-term credit rating for commercial paper is lowered from Prime-1 to Prime-2.
