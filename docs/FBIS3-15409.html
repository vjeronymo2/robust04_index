Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Uzbekistan President Islam Karimov has issued a decree under which most of the housing construction in the Central Asian republic is to be funded either by enterprises or private individuals. The state will finance the building of homes only for poorer people, large families, pensioners, and war and labor veterans. Under the decree, an individual building a house for himself does not have to produce an income declaration, may receive a credit from a bank for at least 10 years at 20 percent interest and buy building materials at reduced prices.
