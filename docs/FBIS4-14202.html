BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received Russian First Deputy Prime Minister Oleg Soskovets at 1300 today. The meeting was attended by Dr. Salim Yasin, deputy prime minister for economic affairs. During the meeting, Soskovets conveyed a letter to the president from Russian President Boris Yeltsin dealing with bilateral cooperation and the Middle East peace process. Talks centered on some international issues and the peace process. They also touched on bilateral relations and ways of promoting them for the benefit of both countries.
