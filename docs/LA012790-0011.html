January 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CRANSTON'S BILL TO AID LINCOLN BONDHOLDERS RECOVER FUNDS

I can't understand why Keating's five senators keep insisting they are honest. Of course they are; our system allows them to be bribed by anyone with a special interest.

Let us just focus on incompetence -- what corporation would not fire any executive who cost it $2 billion?

DONALD SPONZA

Los Angeles
