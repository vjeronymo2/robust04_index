October 4, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: AZUSA;

TOW-AWAY RATES INCREASED

Parking in a tow-away zone just got more expensive.

The City Council on Monday unanimously raised the rates.

Owners of impounded cars will pay $59 for towing, up from $53.50. Storage charges have been raised to $10 a day, up from $9.

Byron Nelson, interim police chief, recommended raising the tow-away rates to bring them into line with those in other areas of Los Angeles County.
