940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Ptarmigan International Capital Trust PTARMIGAN International Capital Trust: Net assets at May 31 1994 were 251.5p (286.7p at December 31). Net revenue for five months was Pounds 79,000 (Pounds 105,000 for year) and earnings per share 1.91p (3.52p). An interim dividend of 1.4p has already been declared.
