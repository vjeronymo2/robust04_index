BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 3 (KCNA) -- A symposium was held in Pyongyang on May 2 to commemorate the 20th anniversary of the publication of "On Some Tasks For Improving Education in Schools", a famous work of the great leader President Kim Il-song, on May 3, 1974. Speeches were made under the subjects "The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song's famous work 'On Some Tasks for Improving Education in Schools' is a highly important work indicating the way of developing education in schools to a new, higher stage in keeping with the requirements of modelling the whole society on the chuche idea" and "The idea of enhancing the sense of responsibility and role of instructors of the League of Socialist Working Youth of Korea and the Children's Union at schools is a great idea giving sure guarantee for bringing up students to be revolutionaries boundlessly loyal to the party and the leader". The speakers said the work of President Kim Il-song is a great ideological and theoretical weapon which gives comprehensive answers to theoretical and practical questions arising in developing education in schools to a new, higher stage in conformity with the requirements of modelling the whole of society on the chuche idea. With the exposition of the idea of combining education with organisational life in the socialist society by the great leader, a firm guarantee has been provided for rearing the youth and children to be staunch revolutionaries with perfect ideological and mental traits as communists of chuche type, the speakers stressed. They pointed out that the work indicates the matters of thoroughly establishing the party's monolithic ideological system among the teachers in keeping with the requirements of modelling the whole society on the chuche idea, improving their quality and bringing home to them how to educate students. Elaborating on the remarkable achievements made in the education in schools over the past 20 years, they said that all the educators must further strengthen and develop the education at schools and make an active contribution to the accomplishment of the cause of socialism, the revolutionary cause of chuche. The meeting was attended by Choe Tae-pok, alternate Politburo member and secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea.
