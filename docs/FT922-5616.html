920529 FT 29 MAY 92 / North Sea oil output recovers NORTH Sea oil output made a healthy recovery in April, according to the Royal Bank of Scotland's oil index, prompting hopes of improvement in the UK's next balance-of-payments figures. Output rose by 4.3 per cent to an average of 1.9m barrels per day. The value of North Sea production increased 10 per cent to Pounds 20.3m a day, boosted by the recovery in the dollar price of Brent crude.
