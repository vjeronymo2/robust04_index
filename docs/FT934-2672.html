931213 FT 13 DEC 93 / Charities fear rise in audit fees More than half of Britain's charities believe that their audit fees may rise as a result of new charities legislation, according to a survey by NGO Finance magazine. Fifty-five per cent said the new accounting requirements for charities, which are still awaited, were likely to increase costs. The survey covered nearly 600 charities representing total annual income of Pounds 3.4bn and with audit fees between them of Pounds 4.5m a year.
