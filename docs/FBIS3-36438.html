Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, February 5 (SNARK) -- Three war planes of the Azerbaijani Air Force have again bombed this morning a number of populated areas in Nagorno-Karabakh. This time, the Mardakertskiy region settlements in the northern Karabakh were attacked. Victims among civilians were reported to SNARK by the Karabakh Defense Army press-service. Details are revealed. Meanwhile, military activity continues already the third day on the eastern part of Martuninskiy region. This morning the Karabakh forces were able to reject the attack of Azerbaijani troops. After two-hour fight, the Azerbaijani side retreated to initial positions, bearing losses.
