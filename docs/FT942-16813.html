940408 FT 08 APR 94 / Minister responds to teachers' union Baroness Blatch, education minister, yesterday told the Association of Teachers and Lecturers that it had taken further cuts in the curriculum 'on trust' and that it would 'not be let down'. She was responding to threats by the traditionally moderate union that it might continue to boycott national curriculum tests if the review of the workload they created was 'cosmetic and expedient'.
