940615 FT 15 JUN 94 / World News in Brief: Rwandan children feared massacred Hutu militia in the Rwandan capital Kigali abducted up to 40 children in a church complex and almost certainly butchered them, a senior UN officer said. At the Organisation of African Unity summit in Tunis, the rebel Rwanda Patriotic Front envoy said he had agreed a ceasefire with government forces.
