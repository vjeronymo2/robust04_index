Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] North Korea has been allowing ROK-flagged vessels to enter its Chongjin port, which has been designated as a free trade port, since August last year, according to a high-ranking Western diplomatic source. The source said that since it designated Chongjin port as a free trade port, North Korea has been allowing ROK-flagged vessels to enter Chongjin port, reviewing the feasibility on a case-by-case basis. Accordingly, three large-size cargo ships carrying used entered Chongjin port flying ROK flags last August, and the used cars were exported to the Yanbian region in China via an overland route, the source added.
