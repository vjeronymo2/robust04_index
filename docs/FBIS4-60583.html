BFN [Text] The first deputy foreign minister of the Russian Federation, Mr. Anatoly Adamishin, called on the president, Mr. Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari, in Islamabad today. During the meeting he exchanged views on developments in the region and Pakistan-Russia bilateral matters with the president. The Russian deputy foreign minister said there existed a lot of scope for enhancing economic cooperaion between Pakistan and Russia. The president said that the two sides should explore the possibilities on cooperation in the field of science and technology.
