BFN [Excerpts] President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali held talks this morning at the Carthage Palace with President Sam Nujoma of Namibia within the framework of the official visit which the Namibian president is making to our country after the OAU summit. [passage omitted] The official spokesman [for the presidency] said that during these talks President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali and President Sam Nujoma discussed cooperation between the two countries and agreed to set up a joint commission to prepare integrated programs for this cooperation at the government and private levels. The two presidents gave instructions to the foreign ministers of the two countries to prepare a special program for the exchange of visits between the various bodies concerned with this cooperation. It was decided that the Tunisian side would move this cooperation forward by contributing its expertise in carrying out development programs in Namibia. On the other hand, the official spokesman said that the talks dealt with the situation in the Arab Maghreb and Southwest Africa as well as the challenges facing the OAU. [passage omitted]
