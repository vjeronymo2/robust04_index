920502 FT 02 MAY 92 / International Company News: Credit Lyonnais tender offer for Pathe shares PATHE Communications, the Beverly Hills-based film producer and distributor, and Credit Lyonnais Bank Nederland, said last night they had reached an agreement on the pending foreclosure by the bank on the common shares of MGM-Pathe Communications held by Pathe, Agencies report from New York. The agreement provides that immediately following the foreclosure, Credit Lyonnais will make a cash tender offer for substantially all publicly held, unencumbered common shares of Pathe at Dollars 1.50 a share. Credit Lyonnais also agreed to begin at the same time an offer to purchase for cash the outstanding 12 3/8 per cent notes and 12 7/8 per cent debentures of Pathe at 47 per cent of their face amount and the 8 7/8 per cent convertible debentures of Pathe at 42 per cent of their face amount. The bank will also provide funds to help Pathe meet its interest obligation on any debt securities outstanding after the tender offer through May 5, 1993. In light of the commitments paid by the bank, Pathe has agreed not to take any action with respect to the foreclosure auction on May 7.
