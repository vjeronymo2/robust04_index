BFN [Text] Agent Lahd's militia elements [South Lebanese Army--SLA] entered the village of 'Aramta this morning to commit a massacre. They killed citizen Jihad Jahjah on the road and assassinated citizen Fu'ad Jandal Yunus in his home. They also wounded Warrant Officer Nabil al-Haj and a woman named Umm-Sulayman. The agents evicted the village's inhabitants to Jazzin.
