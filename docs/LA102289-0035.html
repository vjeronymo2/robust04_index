October 22, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ROCK 'N' ROIL: DEBATES ON WHO'S NO. 1, RACISM, SEXISM AND CRITICISM

Yes, Guns N' Roses' song "One in a Million" uses racist remarks, but it uses them to show us how evil racism is!

The song does not reflect the band's opinions; they have explained that in many heavy-metal magazines. You have to show the ugliness of hate, just as you must show the ugliness of drugs.

ALEX ZARBANO

Los Angeles
