930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Berisford gets bid specialist MR DENIS MULHALL is leaving Tomkins, the conglomerate which among other things makes Smith and Wesson guns and gardening machinery, to join Berisford International, the property and agriculture group, as finance director. He is expected to take up the appointment in the summer. Mr Mulhall's most recent job within Tomkins was at Ranks Hovis McDougall, the milling, baking and grocery products company which Tomkins acquired at the end of last year. At Tomkins Mr Mulhall was one of the team which integrated the group's acquisitions. Berisford is currently bidding Pounds 184m for C&J Clark, the private shoe company. Berisford's proposals will be put to Clark's shareholders at a special meeting on Friday, May 7. Berisford is expected today to make a final appeal to Clark's shareholders to vote in favour of the deal. Mr Mulhall said the move to buy Clark was very exciting.
