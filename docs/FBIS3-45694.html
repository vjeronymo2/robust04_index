Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 24 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met with Sidney Taurel, director of Lilly, a company based in the United States, and his party here this morning. Taurel, who is also president of the pharmaceutical department of Lilly, and his party arrived here last Saturday as guests of China's State Pharmaceutical Administration. Apart from Beijing, they are scheduled to visit Shanghai and Suzhou.
