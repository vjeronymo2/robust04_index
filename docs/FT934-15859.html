931009 FT 09 OCT 93 / P&O announces 240 job cuts P&O European Ferries last night said it would cut about 240 jobs by end of the year on its routes between Felixstowe, Suffolk, and Zeebrugge and Rotterdam, and between Larne, Northern Ireland, and Cairnryan, Scotland. One third of the sea-going redundancies would be on the Irish route and two-thirds in Suffolk.
