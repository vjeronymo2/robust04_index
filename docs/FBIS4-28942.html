BFN ["National Conference Must Be Convened" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, June 11 (KCNA) -- The present situation makes it incumbent upon our compatriots to come out without hesitation to the plaza of the national conference which will open a new chapter of national reconciliation and unity, irrespective of residence and job, if they to not want to fall a victim to big powers but really want the peace and peaceful reunification of the country, says NODONG SINMUN in a signed article today. The great unity of the nation is the best way out for all the fellow countrymen to remove the danger of a nuclear war facing the nation, defend the peace of the country and open a phase of turnround for its independent and peaceful reunification, the paper notes, and goes on: All the fellow countrymen in the North and the South and overseas fully support the convocation of a national conference which will seek a short cut to reunification in the 90s by promoting the great unity of the nation and pooling the will and wisdom of the nation, surpassing differences in ideology, idea and political view. But the Kim Yong-sam group, which dislikes the peace and peaceful reunification of the country, is desperately opposing our proposal for the convocation of a national conference unreasonably branding it as "united front tactics" and is cracking down with the bayonet upon the South Korean students, political parties and organisations and individual figures aspiring after the opening of the national conference. The Kim Yong-sam group answered our proposal with its role of the shockbrigade in the "nuclear" row of the outside forces against the northern half of Korea, clamouring for "a tough response," "an international cooperation system" and "sanctions against the North." This reveals the vicious plot to block the great unity of the nation by frustrating the convocation of the national conference at any cost and ignite a nuclear war against the North together with outside forces. This is an unpardonable criminal act which can be done only by such rare traitors and warmaniacs as the Kim Yong-sam group which takes no notice of the country and the nation, peace and peaceful reunification. The Kim Yong-sam clique is trying to block the convocation of the national conference for national reconciliation and unity with sword-brandishing and war moves, but in vain. Unshakable is the will of the 70 million fellow countrymen to convene the national conference and tide over the present difficulties and open the road of the country's peace and reunification.
