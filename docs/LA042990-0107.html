April 29, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

MOTOCROSS DUEL BETWEEN BREWER, GRAY INTENSIFIES AT VENTURA

Josh Brewer of Oak View and Mike Gray of Ventura each won two races in the 125cc beginner class Saturday night at Ventura Raceway to keep their motocross season points battle close.

Brewer leads the division with 126 points and Gray has 123.

But while most of the points leaders in the other divisions failed to pick up victories Saturday, there was an abundance of double winners.

Mark Stuen of Simi Valley and Kyle Lewis of Simi Valley were double winners in the 125cc junior and pro divisions, respectively.

Other two-time winners included Gary Baker of Westlake Village and Mark Fildes of Ventura in the 250cc beginner division; Kenny Millsaps of Lompoc in the veteran pro division; Don Kadler of Santa Barbara in the 500cc beginner division; Frankie Politelli of Canyon Country in the quad 250cc division; and Lewis again in the 250cc pro division.

Ron Carter of Culver City outclassed the field to win the 12-lap Class 10 buggies main event. Randy Rinehart of Palmdale finished second and Richard Litton of Culver City took third.

Rinehart and Carter each won a heat race in the Class 10 buggies. Rinehart had the fast lap for the class at 19.633 seconds.
