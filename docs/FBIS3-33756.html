Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Basim Abu-Sumayyah dispatch from the occupied territories] [Excerpt] In the Gaza Strip, a previously unknown Palestinian faction, called the battalions of martyr Ahmad Abu-al-Rish, which is affiliated with the Fatah Movement, has announced its rejection of the peace negotiations. In its first statement, distributed in Khan Yunus and Rafah yesterday, it called for escalating armed struggle against Israel, saying that negotiations should be carried out through the muzzle of a gun and not across a peace table. The Abu- al-Rish faction claimed responsibility for eight armed operations against the Israeli Army in January. Palestinian sources in the Gaza Strip say that the Abu-al-Rish battalions were formed following the assassination of Ahmad Abu-al-Rish, a member of the Fatah Hawks, by the Israeli secret units last November.
