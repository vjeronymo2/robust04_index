BFN [Text] Doboj, 28 Jun (TANJUG) -- Muslim forces launched a multiple rocket launcher attack on Doboj from the direction of Tesanj at noon today, committing a most flagrant violation of the cease-fire agreement. The attack was repeated 10 minutes later when Doboj was hit by about 20 missiles, causing great damage to buildings. According to initial reports, many civilians suffered serious and light injuries and they have been admitted to Doboj hospital. A general alert has been sounded in Doboj and the citizens are in shelters.
