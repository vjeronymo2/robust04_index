920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / US to sell copters to Taiwan The US Defence Department said yesterday it would sell 12 anti-submarine helicopters to Taiwan despite strong protests from China over the recently announced sale of 150 US fighter jets to Taipei, Reuter reports from Washington. Congress has 30 days in which to veto the Dollars 161m (Pounds 90.9m) sale or allow it to proceed.
