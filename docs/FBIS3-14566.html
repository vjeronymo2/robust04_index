Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A 30 million Kuwaiti dinars loan agreement was signed at the State Planning Commission with the Arab Fund for Economic and Social Development [AFESD] at noon today. The loan will cofinance an electrical power plant in Zayzun, in the al-Ghab area. The plant comprises three gas units, with a capacity of 130 megawatts per unit. The agreement was signed for the Syrian Arab Republic by Dr. 'Abd-al-Rahim Subay'i, minister of state for planning affairs, in the presence of Kamil al-Baba, minister of electricity. The agreement was signed for the AFESD by 'Abd-al-Latif al-Hamad, the fund's director general and board chairman. [passage omitted]
