Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Bihac area is stoically resisting a heavy chetnik offensive. The enemy's long-range artillery has not been quiet today, the 28th day of the aggressor's offensive against the Bihac region. Artillery and tank attacks on the defense lines on Hacin Vrh and Vrsak and on Cazin have intensified since midday. The chetniks also launched fresh attacks on the Grmusa plateau, where fierce fighting is under way as I am filing this report. At the same time artillery attacks on the fringes of Cazin have been intensified. Several howitzer shells have fallen in the area of the town while the suburbs on the right bank of the river Una have been continuously shelled all day. According to unofficial reports there are several casualties. [passage omitted]
