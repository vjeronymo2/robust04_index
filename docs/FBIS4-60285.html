BFN [Text] Jeddah, 16 May -- King Fahd held talks this evening [16 May] with Egyptian President Mubarak. The two leaders discussed issues related to developments in the Arab and Muslim region in an attempt to resolve the worsening situation in a number of regional disputes. The beloved King Fahd and President Mubarak expressed their sincere wishes that brothers everywhere would take the occasion of these blessed days as an opportunity to stop the fighting, spare lives and bring about security and peace.
