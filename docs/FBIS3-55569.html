Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Lunch Hour Break" program] [Text] The military council of the Pacific Fleet discussed the results of fleet combat training. It was noted that despite the economic difficulties and insufficient financing of vessel repair, Pacific Fleet sailors accomplished their tasks successfully, with 95 percent of the combat training plan fulfilled. More than 30 various exercises were carried out during 1993; during several combined sea cruises, several hundred combat drills were carried out, most of which were evaluated as excellent and good.
