Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A military source has said that Israeli warplanes carried out an air raid on the al-Na'imah hills at 1430 today and that Lebanese Army antiaircraft guns stationed in the area confronted them. Our correspondent reports that an Israeli plane fired a missile at the headquarters of the Ahmad Jibril-led Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine--General Command in the al-Na'imah hills at 1430. The extent of the damage is not yet known. Shortly before 1445 today, the Iqlim al-Tuffah hills came under artillery bombardment originating from the Israeli occupation forces' positions in Sujud, Bi'r Kallab, Mlita, and al-Luwayzah. The shelling coincided with raking by large-caliber machine guns. A resistance group today carried out a machine-gun and rocket attack on the Sujud position, where the resistance and the occupation forces clashed for 10 minutes.
