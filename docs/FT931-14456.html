930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / SPD calls for contract probe Germany's opposition Social Democrats yesterday demanded clarification of the circumstances surrounding a DM3bn (Pounds 1.2bn) contract for a US-manufactured high-altitude reconnaissance system, which has been put on ice pending an investigation into possible corruption, writes Quentin Peel in Bonn. The signing ceremony for the Lapas system, made by E-Systems, of Greenville, Texas, was cancelled on Friday by Mr Volker Ruhe, the German defence minister.
