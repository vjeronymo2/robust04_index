940426 FT 26 APR 94 / UK Company News: Ferraris margins improve Ferraris Group, the specialist engineering and medical equipment company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 350,000 for the six months to February 28. The increase from the comparable Pounds 251,000 was achieved on turnover down from Pounds 5.43m to Pounds 5.18m, the later figure including a contribution of Pounds 121,000 from acquisitions. Earnings per share dipped to 2.3p (2.4p), but the interim dividend is raised from 0.75p to 0.85p.
