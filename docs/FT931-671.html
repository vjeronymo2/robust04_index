930329 FT 29 MAR 93 / UK Company News: GR in the red GR (Holdings), which has interests in property, leisure and fitness, and sheepskin processing, fell Pounds 124,753 into the red in the six months ended December 31. Last time there were pre-tax profits of Pounds 413,630. Turnover amounted to Pounds 2.63m (Pounds 3.45m). Although there were losses per share of 1.3p (1.5p earnings) the group is paying an unchanged interim dividend of 0.4p. Last time there was also a special interim of 25p.
