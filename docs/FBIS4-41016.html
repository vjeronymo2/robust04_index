BFN [Text] Ukrainian Prime Minister Vitaliy Masol today received Belarusian Ambassador to Ukraine Vitaliy Kurasyk. The two men discussed a wide range of issues concerning the (?development of traditionally good) relations between the two states. They emphasized that it is particularly important to broaden economic cooperation, in order to increase production of various goods to fill the Ukrainian and Belarusian markets. They voiced the mutual desire to deepen ties in science, culture, and other sectors.
