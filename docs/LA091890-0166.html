September 18, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

NATION;

RAPIST SINGLETON FREED IN FLA.

A man convicted in California of raping a 15-year-old girl and hacking off her arms walked out of a county jail today after serving 48 days on a petty theft charge.

Lawrence Singleton, 62, initially pleaded innocent to the second-degree misdemeanor charge of petty theft but changed his plea July 31, citing his past record and saying he did not believe he could get a fair trial.

He was released about 6 a.m. today, 12 days short of his maximum 60-day sentence.

Singleton was arrested by sheriff's deputies April 8 at a Brandon drugstore in rural Hillsborough County after a guard spotted him taking an instant camera from a display.

In California, he served eight years and four months of a 14-year sentence for raping the teen-ager and leaving her for dead along a roadside in central California in 1978.
