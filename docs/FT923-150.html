920930 FT 30 SEP 92 / Boost to unit trust funds UNIT trust business recovered slightly in August, before the sterling crisis, with a net inflow of Pounds 56m after net outflows in June and July. The Unit Trust Association said the rise showed private investors' confidence in the stock market had recovered. The net inflow to UK growth funds from private investors was Pounds 73m, while a net Pounds 59m came from institutions.
