March 22, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Distributed Logic Corp.: The Anaheim-based computer products company reported a loss of $1.7 million for the first quarter of fiscal 1990, contrasted with a loss of $376,933 for the comparable period last year. Revenue for the three months ended Jan. 31 declined 15% to $10.3 million from $12.1 million. The company said the loss resulted from a combination of factors, including lower revenues from U.S. operations, delays in deliveries to a major customer and costs related to a recent staff reduction.
