941031 FT 31 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Baris Holdings BARIS HOLDINGS has received applications for 3.72m of the 7.99m shares available in its recent 9-for-8 placing and open offer. The take-up represents about 66.4 per cent of the total offered, other than the 2.39m which were subject to an irrevocable commitment not to subscribe. Subscribers have been procured for the outstanding 4.27m shares.
