941017 FT 17 OCT 94 / Staff canteen 'perk' worth Pounds 300 a year The staff canteen is a 'substantial perk' worth at least Pounds 300 a year to workers, a report says today. The survey of 800 businesses by the Industrial Society and Touche Ross Management Consultants says more workers than ever use office restaurants because of the harsh realities of the economic climate. Most of those surveyed contracted out catering services, with companies paying an average subsidy of Pounds 328.90 a year per worker. Some paid up to Pounds 600. More than half the 500,000 workers covered by the survey said that they used the staff canteen. The Industrial Society said: 'We are seeing a new, competitive era for staff restaurants and employees are realising what good value they really are.'
