The NAFTA Article 1904 Panel Rules are intended to give effect to the panel review provisions of Chapter Nineteen of the Agreement by setting forth the procedures for commencing, conducting, and completing panel reviews. These rules are the result of negotiations among Canada, Mexico, and the United States in compliance with the terms of the Agreement, and are derived in large part from the Article 1904 Panel Rules under the United States-Canada Free Trade Agreement.
