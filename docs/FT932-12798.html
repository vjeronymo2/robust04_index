930427 FT 27 APR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Households to bear VAT burden THE Treasury estimates that only about 1 per cent of the additional yield from extending VAT to all fuel and power is likely to be paid by charities, Sir John Cope, the paymaster general, said. 'We expect almost all of the remaining additional yield will be in respect of private households, though there is no reliable information on the expenditure by non-private households on fuel and power,' he said.
