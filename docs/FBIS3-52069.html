Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Buenos Aires, 28 Jan (NA) -- Foreign Minister Guido Di Tella today stated that security and drug trafficking are on the agenda he will discuss with UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali on 31 January in New York. Di Tella has been in Washington since 26 January discussing several topics with State Department officials. The definition of the purchase of the A-4M planes equipped with the most up-to-date radars and the retooling of the Falda del Carmen plant are among the topics he is discussing. The Argentine foreign minister stated that he is aware of the trip by Chilean congressmen to the Malvinas Islands "for purely ecological reasons," adding that "they promised to pass on their impressions upon their return."
