July 15, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

RETURN TO THE BOOTH BY REAGAN PRODUCES DIVIDED CONSTITUENCY

Vin Scully must have been terribly embarrassed with his novice color man by the end of the first inning. I thought it would never end. If he got paid more than a free hot dog, it would be too much.

I hope in the future Scully's protege will stick to horseback riding, where he has a lot more experience.

JAMES HARRINGTON

Glendale
