BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 28 (KCNA) -- Yang Hyong-sop, chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly, sent messages to Carlo Scognamiglio and Irene Pivetti congratulating them upon their respective election as president of the Senate and president of the Chamber of Deputies of Italy. Expressing the belief that the friendly relations between the parliaments of the two countries will grow stronger and develop in the future, the messages wish them success in their responsible work.
