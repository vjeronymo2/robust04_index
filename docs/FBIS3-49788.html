Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The BSP [Bulgarian Socialist Party] and SDS [Union of Democratic Forces] are responsible for again creating a situation of political tension, states a memorandum adopted this evening at a special session of the Council for Cooperation. According to the members of the council, what is needed is for all the political forces in the country to rally around the national priorities. According to the memorandum, parliament must speed up its work on establishing the legislative basis for regulating the political, economic, and social reforms in Bulgaria. The government should adopt severe measures to bring the crime rate under control, eliminate crime, and speed up the economic and agricultural reforms. The president should consolidate his role as head of state and the personification of the unity of the nation, as stated in the Constitution. The memorandum is signed by 10 of the 11 parties that are members of the Council for Cooperation.
