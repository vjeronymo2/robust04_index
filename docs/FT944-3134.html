941215 FT 15 DEC 94 / World News in Brief: Modahl ban upheld after positive drug test The British Athletic Federation confirmed a four-year ban on former Commonwealth 800 metres champion Diane Modahl, who tested positive for drugs in Lisbon in June. Her urine was said to contain high levels of the male hormone testosterone. The BAF rejected her argument that the sample had been mishandled at a Lisbon laboratory, but last night Mrs Modahl said she would appeal against the decision.
