931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / International Company News: French ski equipment group ahead Skis Rossignol, the French ski equipment and sportswear manufacturer, almost doubled net profits, from FFr79.5m to FFr140.2m (Dollars 23.6m) in the six months ended September. The increase was achieved on sales of FFr983.6m, up about 17 per cent on the same period in 1992. Operating profit rose from FFr126.1m to FFr192.9m. The results include the acquisition of the Roger Cleveland Golf, the US sports group.
