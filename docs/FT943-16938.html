940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Gresham Telecom Gresham Telecomputing, the USM-quoted information technology concern, suffered a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 419,000 to Pounds 244,000 for the six months to April 30. Turnover was virtually unchanged at Pounds 3.07m and earnings per share were halved at 0.43p (0.86p). Profits this time were struck after Pounds 53,000 reorganisation costs.
