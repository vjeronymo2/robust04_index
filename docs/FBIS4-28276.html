BFN [Report by Sin Yong-su from Beijing] [Text] There have been some reports that Chinese President Jiang Zemin warned North Korea to accept negotiations on the nuclear issue when he met on 7 June with Choe Kwang, chief of general staff of North Korea's Army during his visit to China. However, it has been learned this is not true. One high-ranking official of the ROK Embassy in China said: I understand that President Jiang did not make a detailed mention of North Korea's nuclear issue, but only confirmed the traditional friendship between the two countries when he met with Chief of General Staff Choe.
