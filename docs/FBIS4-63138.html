BFN [Russian Ministry of Emergency Situations Press Service report under the "News" rubric: "Radioactive Waste Could End Up In Ocean"] [Text] This could happen, specialists think, unless urgent measures are taken at the "Zvezda" plant in Maritime Kray to set up facilities for the dumping, storing, and recycling of liquid radioactive waste. Looking for a way out from the current situation, the Russian government sent a number of instructions to interested ministries and departments. But as is always the case, the whole thing is held up for want of funds. Anatoliy Shumilov, deputy chief of the Russian Ministry of Emergency Situations' man-made and natural emergency situations administration, a regular meeting will be held in June of representatives of interested ministries and departments which will perhaps make the optimal decision.
