January 19, 1989, Thursday, Late Final Edition

LOCAL;

$32-MILLION LOTTO PRIZE EXPECTED

Nobody won the midweek Lotto jackpot, and state lottery officials estimated today that Saturday's grand prize could reach $32 million.

Wednesday marked the third drawing in a row that the Lotto jackpot went unclaimed. Eight tickets had five of six winning numbers plus the bonus to win the ticket-holders $323,596 each. The winning numbers picked Wednesday night by a Lotto machine for the twice-weekly "Lotto 6-49" game were: 4, 8, 11, 18, 24, 47 and the bonus number, 23.
