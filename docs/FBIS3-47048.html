Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 5 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Beginning since January 5 of the current year the prices for bread and confectionaries have been increased in the Mongolian capital. Now retail prices for a loaf of "atar" bread come to 77 tugrik and a loaf of the "ulaanbaatar" one costs 86 tugrik each.
