941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Samsung wins Thai order Samsung Engineering, a subsidiary of South Korea's Samsung Group, said it had won a Dollars 350m order from Thai Petrochemical Industrial to build an ethylene plant on a turnkey basis. Construction of the plant, capable of producing 300,000 tons of ethylene and 50,000 tons of butadiene a year, is due for completion by November, 1996.
