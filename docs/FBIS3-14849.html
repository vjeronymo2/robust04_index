Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] As Russia's Foreign Ministry reported to Interfax, on the initiative of the American side, Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and U.S. State Secretary Warren Christopher had a telephone talk on Monday [28 February] evening. The parties discussed the Bosnian settlement and the situation in the Middle East. In particular, they stressed the need to strictly for strict fulfillment of the UN Security Council resolutions by all Bosnian parties. Moscow and Washington intend to encourage striving for compromise solutions in the spirit of goodwill. Given the latest events in the Middle East, the importance was recognized of preserving the negotiating process and strengthening security guarantees for the population in the Gaza Strip and Jericho with an appropriate international presence.
