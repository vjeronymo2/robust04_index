Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Bosnian Army soldiers have achieved new successes on the Teocak front [eastern Bosnia]. Second Corps units have captured the Greben elevation. Many chetniks were killed in the operation and the bodies of eight of them were seized by our soldiers. A considerable amount of military equipment was also captured. The chetniks have continued their offensive on the Maglaj front in the zone of responsibility of the Seven-South Operational Group [Bosnia-Herzegovina Army]. The size of the chetnik losses at this front is illustrated by the fact that yesterday our soldiers killed 70 and seriously or lightly wounded dozens of chetniks. [passage omitted] Chetnik tank shells fired from Mount Majevica have killed four and wounded nine people in Tuzla. [passage omitted]
