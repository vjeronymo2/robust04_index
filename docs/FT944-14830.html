941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Deutsche Aerospace - Contracts and ventures Deutsche Aerospace, the Daimler-Benz unit, has been awarded a DM26m (Dollars 17.3m) contract by the Defence Ministry to produce 24 antenna towers for the Hawk surface-to-air missile. The contract will be carried out between 1995 and 1997 by the Dasa Information and Communication Systems division of its Dornier unit.
