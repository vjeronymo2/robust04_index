920930 FT 30 SEP 92 / The Labour Party at Blackpool: Conference business YESTERDAY: Conference approved plans for reforming party policy making; Defeated a motion citing clause four of the party's constitution as 'central to the philosophy of the Labour party'; Supported an environmental charter; Rejected a motion calling for the closure of nuclear power stations and the stopping of coal imports; Opposed the privatisation of Scottish water and called for those companies already priva tised to be brought back under public control. TODAY: Conference will debate party membership, the health service, housing, local government and Labour's trade union links.
