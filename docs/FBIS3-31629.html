Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] After stopovers in Moscow and Tashkent, a Slovak delegation led by Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar arrived in China today for an official visit. At Sheremetyevo airport in Moscow, the Slovak prime minister met Russian Deputy Prime Minister Yuriy Yarov. Vladimir Meciar said that an agreement with Russia on goods exchange is being drafted and will be signed during a visit by Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin to Bratislava. In Tashkent, Vladimir Meciar met Uzbek Prime Minister Abdulhashim Mutalov. According to him [as heard], Slovakia can participate in a water-management project and in the development of the gas and oil industry in the Republic of Uzbekistan. Tomorrow, Vladimir Meciar will meet Chinese Premier Li Peng and President Jiang Zemin.
