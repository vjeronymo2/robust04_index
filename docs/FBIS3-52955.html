Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Mr. 'Ali Salim al-Bid, the vice chairman of the Presidential Council and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, and Mr. Salim Salih Muhammad, the member of the Presidential Council and assistant secretary of the Yemeni Socialist Party, published a statement about the message of the Presidential Council to Mr. Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the prime minister, and the ministers. The statement said that the message which was sent in the name of the Presidential Council is an open and desperate attempt to distort the nature of the political and economic crisis in the country, and an attempt to look for a scapegoat with the purpose of canceling the real causes of the deterioration of the political, economic, and security situation. Its purpose is also to divert attention from those who are behind the misuse the [word indistinct] of the state funds, the devaluation of the local currency, and the wave of terrorism. The statement said that the message which was issued by some members of the Presidential Council took place at the time when the national dialogue commission is holding its meetings, and is about to complete its work and come out with a general agreement to find a solution to the political crisis. Mr. 'Ali Salim al-Bid and Mr. Salim Salih Muhammad affirmed that they are not responsible for the practices, and are putting responsibility on those who seek to escalate the situation.
