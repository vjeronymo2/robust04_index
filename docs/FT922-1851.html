920619 FT 19 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Portsmouth & S'land falls Pounds 0.9m PRE-TAX profits fell at Portsmouth & Sunderland Newspapers from Pounds 5.28m to Pounds 4.39m for the year to March 28 after an exceptional charge of Pounds 1.39m relating to redundancy costs. Group turnover advanced 12 per cent to Pounds 9.65m (Pounds 8.59m). The pre-tax figure was stuck after a net interest charge of Pounds 154,000, against income of Pounds 604,000. Earnings per share came out at 24.2p (30.2p). The proposed final dividend is 5.87p for an increased total payment of 8.6p (8.24p).
