BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 11 (KCNA) -- Stamps on the lapse of 30 years since the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il began to work at the Central Committee of the Workers' Party [WPK] of Korea were issued in Korea. Printed in stamps are pictures of Comrade Kim Chong-il wisely organising and leading the work of the party, the state and the Army as a whole with his tireless and energetic activities. The stamps well depict the leadership feats of Comrade Kim Chong- il in strengthening the might of the independent national economy and effecting a new turn in the improvement of the people's living standard, while defending and carrying forward the glorious revolutionary traditions of the WPK. The stamps impressively reflects the unanimous respect and reverence of the world revolutionary people for Comrade Kim Chong-il who gives perfect answers to the theoretical and practical questions arising in the accomplishment of the socialist cause at the present time and is wisely leading it.
