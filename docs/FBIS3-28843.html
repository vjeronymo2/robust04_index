Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] A Thai delegation led by Deputy Prime Minister Mr. Amnuai Wirawan paid a courtesy call on Vice Admiral Maung Maung Khin, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council and deputy prime minister, at the latter's office at the Secretariat Building at 1030 this morning. During the meeting, they held a frank and cordial discussion on Thai economic investment in Myanmar [Burma], banking, and other matters related to economic cooperation.
