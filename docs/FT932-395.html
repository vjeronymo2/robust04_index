930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / Observer: James capable Sticking out like a sore thumb as the only big agency broker left in London, James Capel can sometimes sound a trifle pompous about the merits of its independence. But it looks as if the realities of life (and the profit and loss account) are beginning to tell and it has recruited two Warburgs men to start making markets in the Pounds 19bn investment trust sector. Previously it has managed to argue the case for making an exception for options, convertibles, continental Europe and, er, HSBC stock. The longer and more diverse the list, the more porous the argument becomes.
