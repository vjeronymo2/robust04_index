BFN [Excerpt] Juba, 15 May (SUNA) -- The convention of Juba peace gathering affirms the southerners' extreme desire for peace, said Vice President Major General George Kongor. Addressing the opening session of the gathering, Kongor said that the gathering is a message unanimously reached by the internal front to condemn the acts of those who back the continuation of the war in the south. The revolution, he said, is keen to achieve the Sudanese peoples' full desire for a comprehensive peace and stability in southern Sudan. All the Sudanese people reject the continuation of the 14-year war, he added. [passage omitted]
