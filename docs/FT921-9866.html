920210 FT 10 FEB 92 / Construction Contracts: Water treatment LAING CIVIL ENGINEERING has received Pounds 10.8m worth of orders as part of an Anglian Water project to boost capacity and improve quality at Grafham water treatment works in Cambridgeshire. The contracts, awarded by Degremont GB, call for the fast-track construction of a series of reinforced concrete tanks and pipework. The largest tank will be 80 metres long, 50 metres wide and 10 metres deep.
