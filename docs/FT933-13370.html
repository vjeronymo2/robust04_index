930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / Full list of 10 alleged crimes: Substantial changes made to indictment The full list of charges against the six defendants are: 1. Credit Suisse That Kevin Maxwell, Larry Trachtenberg, Robert Bunn and Albert Fuller, between July 1 1990 and December 6 1991, conspired together and with Robert Maxwell to defraud Credit Suisse ('the bank') by falsely representing that Robert Maxwell Group plc (RMG) (formerly known as Mirror Group plc) was the beneficial owner of shares of Berlitz International Incorporated belonging to Macmillan Incorporated and shares belonging to First Tokyo Index Trust plc delivered to the bank as security under the terms of an agreement, dated September 7 1990, between the bank and RMG for a loan facility of Pounds 50m. 2. Baverische Vereinsbank That Kevin Maxwell, Robert Bunn, Albert Fuller and Ian Maxwell, between July 16 1990 and February 29 1991, conspired together and with Robert Maxwell to defraud Bayerische Vereinsbank AG ('the bank') by falsely representing to the bank that Mirror Group plc (subsequently known as Robert Maxwell Group plc) or Pergamon Holdings Ltd was the beneficial owner of shares delivered to the bank as security under the terms of an agreement, dated July 30 1990, between the bank and Mirror Group plc for a loan facility of Pounds 25m. 3. Swiss Bank Corporation That Kevin Maxwell and Robert Bunn between May 1 1991 and December 10 1991 conspired together and with Robert Maxwell to defraud Swiss Bank Corporation ('the bank') by selling shares belonging to First Tokyo Index Trust plc knowing that such sale was contrary to representations and warranties given to the bank. 4. Scitex Corporation Ltd That Kevin Maxwell between July 3 1991 and December 10 1991 conspired together with Robert Maxwell to defraud the trustees and beneficiaries of the pension schemes that participated in the Common Investment Fund managed by Bishopsgate Investment Management Ltd by: (i) falsely representing that Robert Maxwell Group plc was able to pay Bishopsgate Investment Management Ltd for 5,400,000 shares in Scitex Corporation Ltd; (ii) selling the shares and using the proceeds of sale to the benefit of Robert Maxwell Group plc knowing that there was a risk that it would not be able to pay Bishopsgate Investment Management Ltd the sum to which it was entitled. 5. Independent fund managers That Kevin Maxwell and Larry Trachtenberg between September 18 1991 and December 10 1991 conspired together and with Robert Maxwell to defraud the trustees and beneficiaries of the Mirror Group Pension Scheme and the AGB Pension Scheme by using shares which formed part of the assets of the schemes (and which were, on September 18 1991, managed by Lloyds Investment Management Ltd, Thornton Investment Management Ltd, Lazard Freres, and Morgan Grenfell Investment Management Ltd) as collateral to secure credit facilities granted by Credit Suisse and Swiss Volksbank to Robert Maxwell Group plc knowing that there was a risk that Robert Maxwell Group plc would default on the credit facilities and the shares would be forfeit. 6. MGN That Kevin Maxwell and Michael Stoney, between August 1 1991 and December 3 1991, conspired together and with Robert Maxwell to defraud MGN Ltd by obtaining from Bankers Trust Company in the name of MGN Ltd a revolving credit facility in the sum of Pounds 50m and using the proceeds of the same for purposes other than the purposes of MGN Ltd. 7. MGN That Michael Stoney, between October 21 1991 and December 2 1991, dishonestly falsified a document required for an accounting purpose, namely the general ledger of MGN Ltd, by omitting or concurring in the omitting from the general ledger a material particular, namely the receipt on October 21 1991 of Pounds 50m from Bankers Trust Company. 8. MGN That Michael Stoney, between October 21 1991 and December 2 1991, dishonestly falsified a document required for an accounting purpose, namely a group financial report by omitting or concurring in the omitting from the said group financial report a material particular, namely the liability of MGN Ltd to Bankers Trust Company in respect of the revolving facility in the sum of Pounds 50m. 9. Swiss Volksbank That Kevin Maxwell, Larry Trachtenberg, Robert Bunn and Ian Maxwell, between November 4 and 21 1991 conspired together to defraud the Swiss Volksbank ('the bank') by falsely representing: (i) that Robert Maxwell Group plc was then the recorded and beneficial owner of 2.4m shares in Berlitz International Incorporated ('the shares') to be delivered to the bank as security for the granting of a loan facility of USDollars 35,506,000 to Robert Maxwell Group Trading plc ('the loan'); (ii) that Robert Maxwell Group plc then had marketable title to the shares; (iii) that it was intended that the loan would be repaid to the bank from the proceeds of sale to Fukutake Publishing of shares of common stock in Berlitz International Incorporated owned by Robert Maxwell Group plc. 10. Teva That Kevin Maxwell, Larry Trachtenberg, Robert Bunn and Ian Maxwell between November 5 and 21 1991, conspired together to defraud the trustees and beneficiaries of the pension schemes that participated in the Common Investment Fund managed by Bishopsgate Investment Management Ltd by granting to National Westminster Bank plc a charge over 25,196,228 shares in Teva Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd, which formed part of the schemes' assets, as security for credit facilities granted by National Westminster Bank plc to Robert Maxwell Group plc knowing that there was a risk that Robert Maxwell Group plc would default on the said credit facilities and the shares would be forfeit.
