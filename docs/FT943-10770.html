940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / Observer: Wet one Perilous business, drying your hands. A reader reports that US federal law now prescribes the following notice be affixed to America's roller towels. 'Instructions 1. Pull towel gently with both hands. 2. Wipe hands and face. 3. WARNING: Do not attempt to hang from towel, or insert your head into the towel loop. Failure to follow these simple instructions can be harmful or injurious.'
