BFN [Text] Cairo, 18 May (MENA)--The central Cairo prosecution today started interrogation of 10 lawyers who were arrested early this morning for taking part in the events surrounding the march they were planning to hold yesterday, Tuesday. Among the 10 lawyers, three are members of the Lawyers' Union council. These are Mukhtar Nuh, Khalid Badawi, and Jalal Sa'd. The central Cairo prosecution had ordered that 39 people--among them 25 lawyers--be imprisoned for 15 days for interrogation. All those who were arrested have been interrogated about the crimes they are charged with, such as participating in criminal collusion to incite a riot, demonstrate, or organize protests in order to disrupt security and attack some police officers.
