941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: Moonstone Group Mr Yannic Mercier, chairman of the Moonstone Group, has asked us to make it clear that in the article about his company on December 2 his reference to USDollars 22m of expenditure in the Zhambyl region was drawn from comments by Mr Seribek Daukeev, Minister for Geology of Kazakhstan.
