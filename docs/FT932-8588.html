930518 FT 18 MAY 93 / International Company News: ABB Asea Brown Boveri improves first quarter ABB Asea Brown Boveri, the leading power engineering group, has improved first-quarter operating earnings by 1 per cent to Dollars 409m, writes Ian Rodger from Zurich. It expects pre-tax profit for 1993 as a whole to be about the same as last year's Dollars 1.11bn.
