January 31, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BUSH STATE OF UNION SPEECH ON TV

President Bush will deliver his first State of the Union address to a joint session of Congress at 6 tonight.

The speech will be broadcast live on many television and radio stations, and will be followed by a Democratic Party response by House Speaker Thomas S. Foley of Washington state.

Television coverage of the speech will be provided by ABC, CBS, NBC, CNN and C-SPAN.
