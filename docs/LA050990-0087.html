May 9, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE WATCH

Stallone Fights Off Italian Fans: Sylvester Stallone feared for his safety when he was mobbed by about 5,000 fans in Italy.

Stallone was leaving a Milan theater in a limousine when its escape route was blocked accidentally by two other cars, allowing a crowd to move in. Chanting "Rocky! Rocky! Rocky!" the fans pounded on the window before the car made its getaway. "He said it was the scariest experience he's ever had before in a crowd," Stallone publicist Paul Bloch said. GREG BRAXTON
