931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Silvermines in bid talks with Molynx The directors of Silvermines have confirmed that the Dublin-based electrical and property group is in talks with Molynx Holdings, the closed circuit television, security and building energy management systems maker, which could lead to a merger of the two companies. If agreed, the merger could take the form of an offer by Silvermines for Molynx or of offers for both companies by a new company.
