October 17, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

"Meeting of the Mouths," an all-day marathon gabfest, will air on KFI radio (AM 640) Friday from 9 a.m. to 1 a.m. KFI plans to fly in syndicated talk show hosts from New York (Rush Limbaugh, WABC) and San Francisco (Dr. Dean Edell, KNBR) to join its own talent (including Tom Leykis, Mother Love and Christopher Bartlett). The 11 hosts of the radio round table will discuss a range of issues as the hours go by.ALEENE MacMINN
