Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 14 (KCNA) -- "The Workers' Party of Zaire highly estimates the revolutionary economic strategy laid down by His Excellency President Kim Il-song as an epoch-making revolutionary step to increase the might and attraction of man- centred socialism", said Emile Kibala Bey a Nsien, national chairman of the Workers' Party of Zaire, when he met the DPRK ambassador to his country on January 8. The Zairean party leader wished the Korean people new success in the efforts to build socialism and reunify the country under the energetic leadership of dear Comrade Kim Chong-il. Narayan Man Bijukchhe, chairman of the Nepal Workers and Peasants Party, told the DPRK ambassador to his country on January 4 that the New Year address of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song indicates the road ahead of the world people and serves as a moral weapon for his party. He expressed full support to the position toward the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula which was clarified by the great leader.
