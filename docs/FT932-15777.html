930410 FT 10 APR 93 / American soldier, Somali boy reach out to each other AN American soldier and a Somali boy reached out towards each other at Kismayu in Somalia while Adm Jonathon Howe, a UN special envoy in Somalia, was launching there yesterday a new effort to find the assassin of a British aid worker murdered in the town. He appealed to clan elders to help the UN find who killed Mr Sean Devereux of the UN Children's Fund in January.
