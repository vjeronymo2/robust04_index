BFN [Text] A deputy spokesperson attached to the Prime Minister's Office this morning announced her resignation from the post together with the assistant secretary to the prime minister and a member of the group of whips for parties in the coalition government. All three persons are Phalang Tham Party MP's who endorsed the submission of a motion calling for the establishment of a special commission to redraft the Constitution. Mrs. Sudarat Keyuraphan, deputy spokesperson for the Prime Minister's Office, said that she decided to resign from the post because she wanted to fully perform her duty as a Member of Parliament mainly for the sake of the people and country.
