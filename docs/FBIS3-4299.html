Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 28 (XINHUA) -- The first Sino-ROK (the Republic of Korea) high-tech joint venture went into operation here today. Tian Jiyun, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of China's National People's Congress, and other senior Chinese and visiting ROK officials attended the opening ceremony. The Beijing-Kohap General Telecom Equipment Co. Ltd. was set up jointly by the Beijing Municipal Telecom Administrative Bureau and the Kohap Group Ltd of the ROK. The company specializes in the development, production and sale of digital telephone duplication devices and credit-card checking equipment.
