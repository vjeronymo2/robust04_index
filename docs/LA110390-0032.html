November 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NATION IN BRIEF;

NEW YORK;

IVANA TRUMP WILL FILE FOR DIVORCE

The long-anticipated Trump divorce became more than just gossip when Ivana Trump's lawyer announced that, "with deep regret," she was filing for a divorce from her developer husband, Donald.

It wasn't clear when the papers would be filed. The couple separated in February amid a flurry of publicity over Donald Trump's relationship with model-actress Marla Maples. "Mrs. Trump and I have discussed our situation and have mutually agreed that it would be appropriate for her to file for divorce," Trump said in a statement. The Trumps married 13 years ago and have three children.
