January 29, 1989, Sunday, South Bay Edition

14 CANDIDATES FILE IN INGLEWOOD RACE

Inglewood officials have provided the names of two additional candidates who filed last week for the April City Council election.

The candidates are Andrew Chapralis, 63, a businessman who is running in the 2nd District, and Leroy Fisher, 49, a community activist, running in the 1st District.

They bring the total number of candidates running for three council seats to 14.
