930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: Union reforms in place by 1994 THE MAJORITY of reforms contained in the Trade Union Reform and Employment Act, which received Royal Assent yesterday, will come into force well before the end of the year, the government said yesterday. Mr David Hunt, the employment secretary, said the government's industrial relations reforms had helped create a climate where strikes were at record low levels and Britain was the most attractive EC country for Japanese and US inward investment.
