940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Charter Kleinwort Charter Investment Trust reported net asset value per share of 239p at May 31 compared with 231.1p at the November 30 year end and 204.9p a year earlier. Net revenue for the six months to the end of May fell from Pounds 1.87m to Pounds 1.34m for earnings per share of 1.63p (2.28p). The interim dividend is unchanged at 1.25p.
