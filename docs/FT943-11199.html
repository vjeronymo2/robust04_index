940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / Business Travel: Nigerian strike A general strike is likely to start in Nigeria on Wednesday. The Nigeria Labour Congress (NLC), comprising 41 trade unions, decided last Friday that its 3.5m members would take action after failing to persuade head of state General Sani Abacha to free presidential claimant Moshood Abiola and other political detainees. Shell and other oil companies have been told by the unions to pull out their expatriate staff. The west African nation has all but shut down over the crisis prompted by the arrest of Abiola, the assumed winner of last year's annulled presidential election, on treason charges.
