BFN [Text] The Armenian Foreign Minister Vahan Papazyan began an official visit to Romania 19 April. He will meet with Romanian President Ion Iliescu and convey to him a message from Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan. The Foreign Ministry press and public relations service reported that Papazyan will also meet Adrian Nastase, speaker of the Upper House of the Romanian Parliament; Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu; and Foreign Minister Teodor Malescanu. A consular agreement will be signed between the two countries during the visit of the Armenian foreign minister. Mr. Papazyan will take part in the work of the congress of (?Trans-Moldavia) between 21-24 April.
