921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Allied Radio posts Pounds 1m loss Allied Radio, which operates local stations in Surrey, West Sussex and Hampshire, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.01m, against Pounds 954,000, for the nine months to June 30. Turnover slipped to Pounds 3.41m (Pounds 3.67m). The figures were issued to comply with Stock Exchange regulations following a change of year-end to September 30 to match the quoted radio sector. Despite difficult trading conditions, operating losses were reduced by 33.5 per cent from Pounds 683,000 to Pounds 454,000. The operating loss for radio activities alone was reduced from Pounds 406,000 to Pounds 127,000, on turnover of Pounds 2.31m against Pounds 2.45m. Losses per share were 2.20p (2.89p). No dividend is declared.
