Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Hundreds of trucks and tanker trucks carrying fuel remain stranded on both sides of the border checkpoint at Kakavyia. Furthermore, tension has risen in Albania following the arrest of businessmen from North Ipiros and four Albanian customs officers. Our correspondent in Ioannina, Yeoryios Kirousis has the following report: [Kirousis] The situation in Kakavyia has become unbearable today here as hundreds of trucks have piled up on both sides of the Greek-Albania border. The Greek customs checkpoint continues to allow passage to only five fuel-carrying trucks per day, but problems are now being caused by Albanian customs, which processes customs documents at an extremely slow pace. In addition, many Greek tanker trucks have been confiscated and are being held at the Albanian customs checkpoint. [passage omitted]
