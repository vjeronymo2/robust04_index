920108 FT 08 JAN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and cotton JUTE January/February: C and f Dundee; BTC Dollars 415, BWC n/o, BTD Dollars 380, BWD n/o; C and f Antwerp: BTC Dollars 400, BWC Dollars 400, BTD Dollars 370, BWD Dollars 370. COTTON Liverpool- Spot and shipment sales for the week ending 3 January amounted to 240 tonnes against 160 tonnes in the previous week. Trading was quiet with interest being shown in South American and African varieties.
