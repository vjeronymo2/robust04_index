BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, today met with leaders of political parties and organizations; unions; professional associations; and popular, creative, and professional organizations; and with shaykhs, ulemas, and representatives of the youth, women, and all national forces and figures in the entire Yemeni homeland. He delivered an important national speech which touched on developments on the national arena. He urged everyone to stick together in a single front to defend unity and Constitutional legitimacy and pool efforts to build the new Yemen under the banner of unity and democracy.
