Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Dubai, United Arab Emirates, Jan 18 (AFP)--President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih's party charged Tuesday that a southern Yemeni warplane bombed a northern army brigade, but the south denied the accusation. The Russian-made MiG warplane bombed the brigade at al-'Amaliqah in the southern province of Abyan on Monday, causing enormous damage but no casualties, the spokesman for the General People's Congress (GPC) said. He told AFP that the warplane returned to Badr air base, near the southern port city of Aden. However, a spokesman at the Badr base denied the charge, insisting the plane was on a routine training mission.
