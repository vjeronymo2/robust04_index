BFN [Text] About 20,000 rocket troops are at their duty stations around the clock. Military journalists were informed of this by Colonel-General Stanislav Kachumasov, the chief of the main headquarters of the Russian Strategic Missile Forces. This is done to maintain the combat potential of rocket troops at the required level. According to Colonel-General Kachumasov, by the year 2003 this potential is defined at 900-1,000 launchers.
