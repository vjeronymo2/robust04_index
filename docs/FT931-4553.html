930310 FT 10 MAR 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG analysts said that results from the diamond giant, De Beers, were at the top end of expectations and lifted the market. De Beers was R2.60 higher at R68.85, while the overall index advanced 34 to 3,425, industrials moved forward 28 to 4,449 and golds appreciated 14 to 1,012.
