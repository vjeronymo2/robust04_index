Office of the Secretary Industry Executive Subcommittee of the National Security Telecommunications Advisory Committee; Meeting AGENCY: National Communications System, DoD. ACTION: Notice of meeting. A meeting of the Industry Executive Subcommittee of the National Security Telecommunications Advisory Committee will be held on Monday, January 31, 1994 from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. at the Mitre-Hayes Building, 7525 Colshire Drive, McLean, VA 27006.
