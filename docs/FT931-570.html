930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Health of the Nation conference THE Department of Health yesterday held a conference to advance the paper outlining its health strategy, the Health of the Nation. Mrs Virginia Bottomley, the health secretary, marked the development at a North West Thames regional conference for the initiative in London. She was joined by agriculture minister Mr John Gummer. The conference covered the five key areas of the health initiative; coronary heart disease and stroke, cancers, mental illness, HIV/Aids and sexual health, and accidents. Mr Gummer criticised British eating habits, telling the conference that although people no longer ate together they should maintain balanced dietary habits.
