920506 FT 06 MAY 92 / Threat to Cuba investment ATTEMPTS by President Fidel Castro to rescue Cuba's battered economy through foreign investment was dealt a blow yesterday when 11 Cuban exile groups, representing all shades of exile opinion, said that were they to come to power 'these (foreign) investments would be considered as state property and disposed of accordingly', writes Damian Fraser in Mexico City. In an open letter to foreign investors, the 11 groups said the investments served to 'prolong the tragic situation' of the country and its people.
