November 19, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: HUNTINGTON BEACH;

COLLEGE FOUNDATION ELECTS 3 TO BOARD

The Golden West College Foundation has elected three new directors. Robert Polentz, vice president of finance for Hyundai Motor America and Walter Martynec, president of Walter K. Martynec accountancy firm, will contribute their financial expertise, officials said.

Jack Scholl, vice president of public relations for ICN Pharmaceuticals, will assist in managing the foundation's public affairs program.

The 17-member volunteer board provides guidance for the foundation's endowment and sponsors fund-raising activities to benefit Golden West College.
