Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] In Azad Kashmir, one woman was killed and two young men were seriously wounded as a result of shooting by Indian forces from across the Line of Control. According to reports, Indian forces started firing indiscriminately without provocation at Chhatri village of Kot Katahra Sector at 1400 on 9 March. Two bullets hit the woman in the stomach, killing her on the spot. In two separate incidents, Indian forces fired at Baiwal village of Nakial Sector and Jagal village of Kot Katahra Sector, seriously wounding two young men.
