930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / Frankfurt graves desecrated Police said yesterday five gravestones in a Jewish cemetery near Frankfurt had been overturned in another expression of anti-Semitism in Germany, Reuter reports. Neo-Nazis and other right-wing extremists damaged nearly 80 Jewish cemeteries and Holocaust memorials in Germany last year during a wave of xenophobic violence in which an estimated 17 people were killed.
