930619 FT 19 JUN 93 / Healthcare plant for Devon VIDAMED, a Californian healthcare company, is to set up a manufacturing plant at Plymouth, Devon, with an investment of Pounds 4.9m. The project will receive Pounds 750,000 in regional selective assistance from the Department of Trade and Industry and is expected to create up to 50 jobs by the end of next year. The plant, in Langage science park, will make dilation catheters used in the treatment of prostate problems.
