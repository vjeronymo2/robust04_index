BFN [Text] The Hungarian Socialist Party [HSP] calls on the leader of Hungarian Television to ensure the cleanness of political programs in the remaining part of the election campaign and to suspend from their jobs the editors of the "Newsreel" and "Panorama" programs. Imre Szekeres, executive deputy chairman of the HSP, notes in a letter addressed to TV Deputy Chairman Gabor Nahlik that the television documentary and news programs contain false allegations that fulfill the conditions for defamation and public slander. The HSP demands that the editors responsible for the programs apologize publicly to the party and the slandered politicians, and that Deputy Chairman Gabor Nahlik apologize for the false accusations by 2 May. The HSP is asking the National Election Committee to take a stance in connection with Hungarian Television's political programs; if no change occurs the party will appeal to the supreme state prosecutor.
