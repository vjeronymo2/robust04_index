940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Quayle Munro rises Quayle Munro Holdings, the financial services group, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 670,000 for the year to June 30 against Pounds 540,000 for the previous 10 months. Revenues more than doubled to Pounds 1.81m (Pounds 804,000). Earnings per share worked through at 11.78p (3.71p) and a proposed final dividend of 6p (3p) raises the total to 9p (6p). Net asset value per share rose by 48 per cent over the year to 234p (158p).
