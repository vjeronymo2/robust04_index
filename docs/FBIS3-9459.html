Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] During the talks being held in Prague by Dieter Kastrup, state secretary of the German Foreign Ministry, both sides made a great effort to take a step forward in their bilateral relations. This was said by Foreign Minister Josef Zieleniec, who observed that, although these relations are good and friendly, they nevertheless contain some problem areas. Both sides are working very hard to find a way to resolve them. Zieleniec also noted the importance of their having also discussed ways of organizing a dialogue with Germany.
