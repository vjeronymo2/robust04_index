Language: Estonian Article Type:BFN [Text] A regular consignment of the necessary means for dismantling nuclear reactors reached Paldiski today. According to information from the Estonian Foreign Ministry, this concerns the fourth goods wagon which arrived recently from Russia and which contains the necessary equipment for dismantling nuclear reactors. The load contains sprays for preventing the spread of nuclear substances during dismantling. Representatives of the Russian naval forces confirmed today that the removal of nuclear fuel from Paldiski will begin on 4 April. They also confirmed that the Russian naval training center will cease its operations as a unit by 30 June.
