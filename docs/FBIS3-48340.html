Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 21 (KCNA) -- The U.S. imperialists brought overseas-based fighter-bombers, pursuit-assault planes, VTOL [vertical take-off and landing] assault planes and tanker planes to the air above Wonju, Kunsan, Yongwol and Pyongchang, South Korea, on January 18 and 19 to stage an exercise of quick long-distance flight to the air above the operation area and strike at targets and a bombing exercise for striking the strategic interior of the northern half of Korea, according to military sources. Meanwhile, the South Korean puppets let formations of helicopters, each composed of three or four, make low and ultra-low flights above Yonchon, Paju and Chorwon on the same days in an exercise for supporting an attack operation of ground units to break through the Military Demarcation Line. Tensions persist on the Korean peninsula due to the war moves of the U.S. imperialists and the South Korean puppets.
