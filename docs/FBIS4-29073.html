BFN [Text] Seoul, June 15 (YONHAP) -- President Kim Yong-sam on Wednesday instructed the Foreign and Defense Ministries to provide daily press briefings on day-to-day changes in major international issues and the nation's security situation. Kim ordered the daily briefings in view of the need to alleviate the people's sense of insecurity amid growing uncertainty over the nation's security, Presidential Press Secretary Chu Ton-sik said. In accordance with the instructions, the Foreign Ministry will conduct press briefings on developments in the nuclear and other issues at the United Nations, as well as other major international affairs, while the Defense Ministry will brief the media on the military situation in North Korea and the nation's defense preparedness, Chu said.
