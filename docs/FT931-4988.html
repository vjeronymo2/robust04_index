930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / World Trade News: Iveco signs South Korean deal Iveco, the industrial vehicles subsidiary of Italy's Fiat automotive group, has signed a licensing agreement with South Korea's Halla Engineering and Heavy Industry company to produce off-road trucks for the local market, Haig Simonian writes from Milan. The deal envisages production of 250 heavy-duty Iveco trucks in South Korea this year.
