BFN [By reporter Wu Ping (0702 1627)] [Text] Copenhagen, 14 May (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, met staff of the Chinese Embassy in Denmark and representatives of the Chinese community and students here on 14 May, and had cordial conversations with them. Li Ruihuan called on embassy staff to make more friends and work harder to help create a sustained, fine, and peaceful international environment for China's economic development. He also expressed the hope that the Chinese community in Denmark will make use of its favorable conditions to do something for China. In his conversations with representatives of Chinese students, Li Ruihuan said that although Denmark is a small country lacking resources, it possesses many advanced technologies and strong points for us to study and master. After the meeting, Li Ruihuan also separately posed for photographs with the attendees.
