September 28, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

JURUPA VALLEY BECOMES CENTURY'S FIRST VICTIM

The Century High School football team got its first varsity victory by defeating Jurupa Valley, 23-6, here Thursday night.

The victory was only fitting because Jurupa Valley, like Century, is another school in its first season of varsity football.

Karlo Balderrama gave Century a first-quarter lead when he scored on a five-yard run. Stacy de la Motte caught a pass from Henry Ruvalcava to give Century (1-3) an 8-0 lead that it never relinquished.
