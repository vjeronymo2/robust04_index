Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Russian defense minister will not have fewer deputies, Interfax was told by the Minister's spokeswoman Yelena Agapova. She described as groundless the speculation on the alleged elimination of the air defense troops as an armed service. In this connection Agapova said that various sorts of rumors carried by the media about the Ministry's leadership are being spread to deceive the public and to interfere with the Ministry's activities.
