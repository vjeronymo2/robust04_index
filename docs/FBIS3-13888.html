Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Libyan National Organization Welcomes `Movement for Change and Reform'"] [Text] London, AL-HAYAH -- Bashir al-Rabiti, leader of the Libyan National Organization, has welcomed the announcement by dissidents from the National Front for the Salvation of Libya of the establishment of a new organization called the Libyan Movement for Change and Reform. He said that after thorough study of the new movement's founding statement, the Libyan National Organization has decided "it is necessary to support what is mentioned in it and bless the movement's steps." He wished "the movement and all honorable strugglers success."
