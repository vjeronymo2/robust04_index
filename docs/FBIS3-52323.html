Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali today received in his office at the Prime Ministry Qatar's Foreign Minister Shaykh Hamad Bin-Jasim Bin-Jabr Al Thani and his accompanying delegation. During the meeting, the two sides discussed distinguished relations between the two fraternal countries and means to develop and reinforce these relations in all fields, expressing concern for the need to rebuild Arab solidarity based on trust and mutual respect. They discussed the peace process in the region and stressed the importance of the comprehensiveness of the solution, taking into consideration the restoration of occupied Jordanian territories and Jordan's right to water. The guest minister stressed the uniqueness of relations between the two countries. He described these relations as ancient, reaffirming his country's support for Palestinian decisionmaking. The meeting was attended by Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs; Tirad al-Fayiz, Jordan's ambassador to Qatar and Nasir 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Nasir, Qatar's ambassador in Amman.
