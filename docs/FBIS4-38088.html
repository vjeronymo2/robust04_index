BFN [Text] According to reports, fierce fighting continues in southeast Afghanistan. A spokesman of Prime Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar has claimed that the combined forces of Hekmatyar and General Abdol Rashid Dostam have reoccupied one out of two strategic positions in the country's southern region. Independent sources, however, have not confirmed this claim. The reports say at least 35 people have been killed and 360 others injured in the latest flare up of fighting. Meanwhile, a meeting of the jehad leaders assessed the current situation in Afghanistan. A joint statement issued after the meeting calls for an urgent meeting of the Leadership Council to decide on the future government as the term of office of President Borhanoddin Rabbani and Prime Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar expires today. Among those who attended the meeting include former president Professor Mojadeddi, Shaykh Asef Mohseni, Pir Seyyed Ahmad Gilani, and Maulvi Mohammad Nabi Mohammadi.
