931013 FT 13 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Cooper Clarke to delist from USM Cooper Clarke Group, the builders' merchant which earlier this week reported interim profits up from Pounds 125,000 to Pounds 204,000, yesterday announced that it intended to delist its shares on the USM with effect from October 29. Following the cancellation of its quotation, the company will continue to trade as a plc, but its shares will no longer be traded on the USM.
