Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The security services have announced that four citizens, a woman, two war veterans, and a farmer were killed by members of armed groups in Tlemcen, Skikda, and Jijel during the past three days. The security forces have also denied today's report by L'INDEPENDANT that 12 soldiers have been killed in the suburbs of Algiers. The daily reported today that 12 soldiers were killed by cold steel weapons in the suburbs of Algiers sometime last week and that their bodies were taken to Ain Naadja hospital.
