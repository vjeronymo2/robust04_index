940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / Commercial rents 'increase 0.8%' Commercial property rents have risen for the first time since 1990, figures published by Hillier Parker, chartered surveyors, show. This is further evidence of a recovery in the rental market. Average property rents have risen by 0.8 per cent over the past three months. Office rents showed the strongest increase, with a rise of 1.6 per cent. Rents in the retail sector have edged up by 0.1 per cent.
