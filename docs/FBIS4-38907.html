BFN [Text] Despite the official exchange rate of 1,916 rubles [R] for one U.S. dollar, set by the National Bank of Tajikistan on 1 June, the rate for selling and buying has already passed the R2,000 mark in dealings by the business and state banks of Tajikistan. The buying and selling rate for the U.S. dollar set for individuals by the National Bank of Tajikistan is R2,000 and R2,095 respectively. The rate for the German mark [word indistinct] is R1,050 and R1,095. The Tajik Bank Business buys one U.S. dollar for R1,800, selling it for R,2063. Due to a lack of supply of rubles to the republic, some banks have temporarily halted selling rubles to the population. The Vneshekonombank buys one U.S. dollar for R2,050 and one German mark for R1,050.
