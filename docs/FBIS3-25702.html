Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Walvis Bay Feb 24 SAPA -- A fully equipped unit of the South African Police Internal Stability Division [ISD] arrived in Walvis Bay on Thursday, just four days before the enclave is handed to Namibia. The 94 troops from the Pretoria-based Unit 19 of the ISD off-loaded their bulletproof vests, ammunition, shotguns, R5 rifles and pistols from a Hercules C130 aircraft at the almost deserted Rooikop Air Base.
