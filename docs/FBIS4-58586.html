BFN [Text] An expanded Lebanese-Syrian summit is scheduled to be held in Damascus this morning involving President Ilyas al-Hirawi, Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, and Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz on the Lebanese side and Syrian President Hafiz 'al-Asad on the Syrian side. The Lebanese president, speaker, prime minister, and foreign minister left for Damascus at 1000 today. Before leaving Beirut, President al-Hirawi met U.S. Ambassador Mark Hambley and discussed local and regional developments.
