Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Electoral Commission has announced the extension of the voter registration period from today to 26 March. Making the announcement today in Blantyre, a spokesman for the commission said it was clear that extra time was needed to give voters a chance to register. The spokesman called on all Malawians who will be 18 or more on the ballot day to register their names before the close of the registration on the 26th of this month. [passage omitted]
