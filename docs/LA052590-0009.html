May 25, 1990, Friday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: VENTURA;

MAN, 31, ARRESTED ON DRUG CHARGE

Police arrested a 31-year-old Ventura man on suspicion of possessing narcotics for sale, authorities said Thursday.

Tom Ray Floyd was taken into custody at 10:15 a.m. Wednesday after Ventura police found an ounce of methamphetamines and a pound of marijuana in his room at the Pacific Inn at 350 Thompson Ave., Sgt. Steve Bowman said. Police also recovered drug paraphernalia, Bowman said. CAROL WATSON
