940425 FT 25 APR 94 / Faltering steps towards sell-offs July 29 1992: Mr Heseltine says he is considering privatising the Post Office. Further consultations began last week. July 1 1993: DTI announces review of Patent Office with a view to possible privatisation. No further announcements. July 12 1993: DTI publishes proposals to privatise Companies House. No further announcements. March 25 1993: DTI says it will launch a review of the nuclear power industry before the end of the year. On December 17, energy minister Mr Tim Eggar tells the Commons the review will be announced 'as soon as possible'. No further announcement. December 21 1993: Mr Heseltine promises detailed consultative document on gas-market de-regulation 'early in the new year'. Ofgas, the gas regulator, has sent proposals to the DTI. No action has resulted.
