Summary of Response Mission The Department of Justice (DOJ) is the lead agency for coordinating the Federal response to acts of terrorism in the United States and U.S. territories. Within the DOJ, the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) will manage the law enforcement aspect of the Federal response to such incidents. The FBI also is responsible for investigating all alleged or suspected criminal violations of the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, as amended. 2. Capabilities and Resources The FBI will coordinate all law enforcement operations including intelligence gathering, hostage negotiations, and tactical operations. 3.
