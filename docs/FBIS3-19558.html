Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Turkey and The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia [FYROM] have signed in Ankara an agreement regarding economic and trade cooperation. The agreement, which was signed by Mehmet Mogultay, labor and social security minister, and FYROM's Economy Minister Petrus Stefanov envisages cooperation between the two countries in the areas of transportation, communication, and banking.
