930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Broadgate net asset value at 119.82p Broadgate Investment Trust reported net asset value per share of 119.82p in its first set of results. The period is from the trust's inception on April 14 1992 to January 31 1993. Gross revenue was Pounds 200,986 and after administrative expenses of Pounds 62,334 and interest on equities index unsecured loan stock of Pounds 55,888 revenue before tax came to Pounds 82,764. Earnings per share were 1.24p.
