931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / World Trade News: Jakarta to buy Protons Indonesia plans to buy Malaysian-manufactured Proton cars in exchange for Indonesian-made aircraft, reports Kieran Cooke. Malaysia manufactures the Proton with Mitsubishi of Japan. Indonesia's heavily subsidised Industri Pesawat Terbang Nusantara manufactures the turboprop CN-235 aircraft with Casa of Spain.
