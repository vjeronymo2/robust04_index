921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Ipeco improves 5% to Pounds 1.82m Taxable profits at Ipeco Holdings, the Southend-based aircrew seat manufacturer, rose from Pounds 1.73m to Pounds 1.82m for the six months to June 27. The 5 per cent improvement was scored on the back of a 16 per cent rise in turnover to Pounds 10.12m. Operating profits rose by 16 per cent to Pounds 1.68m. Earnings emerged at 4.42p (4.14p) and the interim dividend is 1.3p (1.2p).
