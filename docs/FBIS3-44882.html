Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 8 (XINHUA) -- Japanese deputy prime minister and Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata arrived here this afternoon for a two-day official visit to China at the invitation of his Chinese counterpart Qian Qichen . Foreign Minister Hata, the most important Japanese official visiting China this year, is scheduled to confer with Qian Qichen later today. He is also expected to meet with Chinese President Jiang Zemin and Premier Li Peng. Their discussions will cover mainly Sino-Japanese ties as well as regional and international issues.
