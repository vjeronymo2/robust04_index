Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Mainayev] [Text] Bishkek February 4 TASS -- The president of Kyrgyzstan decreed on Friday to create the National Security Council which is to become a consultative body at the president [as received] and will draft security decisions for the head of state. The council is to consider strategic problems of state, economic, ecological and other types of security. It is also to expose internal and external threats to Kyrgyzstan, assist the drafting of major guidelines of the national security strategy, prepare recommendations for the president on urgent decisions to avert extraordinary situations and other problems. The council united the defence, interior, security ministers, the commander of the National Guard, several government members.
