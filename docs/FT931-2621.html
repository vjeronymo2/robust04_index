930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / UK Company News: English & Scottish Investors assets rise English & Scottish Investors reported net asset value of 106.7p at January 31, compared with 86.3p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year to end-January was Pounds 2.22m (Pounds 2.36m). Earnings per share came through at 1.36p (1.45p). An unchanged second interim dividend of 1.25p is recommended for a total of 1.75p.
