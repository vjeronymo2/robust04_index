921127 FT 27 NOV 92 / World Stock Markets: America WALL STREET was closed for Thanksgiving. Toronto stocks held on to early gains in quiet midday trading, bolstered by a partial recovery in the Canadian dollar and a drop in rates. The TSE-300 Index rose 10.5 to 3,272 with a volume of 25.9m shares valued at CDollars 214.5m. Advances led declines by 278 to 220 with 332 unchanged. Among active metals, Alcan Aluminium firmed CDollars 1 to CDollars 21 1/4 and Inco rose 7/8 to CDollars 27 1/4 .
