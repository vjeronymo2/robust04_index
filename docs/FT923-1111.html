920924 FT 24 SEP 92 / Societies announce rate cut THE first-ever building society 8.25 per cent five-year fixed-rate mortgage was offered yesterday as more medium-sized mortgage lenders broke ranks with the rest of the industry and cut mortgage interest rates. National & Provincial, the seventh-largest building society, reduced its mortgage rate for new borrowers to 9.99 per cent and launched a mortgage fixed at 8.25 per cent for up to five years. Royal Bank of Scotland, the fifth-largest banking group, cut its mortgage rate for new borrowers from 10.7 per cent to 9.95 per cent with immediate effect. Existing customers will not benefit for the time being. Skipton, the 15th-largest society, which raised its mortgage interest rate for existing customers to 11.25 per cent in August, said it was cutting it to 10.25 per cent. The same rate will apply to new borrowers from 19 October. Newcastle, the 26th-biggest society, cut its mortgage interest rate for new borrowers to 9.99 per cent from 10.65.
