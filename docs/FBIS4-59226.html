BFN [Text] Tripoli, 10 May, (JANA-Arab Maghreb Union Pool) -- In continuation of the efforts made by the great leader of the al-Fatih revolution to mediate between the parties to the crisis in Yemen, a telephone call was made between the brother leader of the revolution and brother 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council in Yemen, in an attempt to tackle the tense situation and to spare the blood of brothers.
