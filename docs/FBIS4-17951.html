BFN [Unattributed report under the "NEGA Reports" rubric: "Tatarstan"] [Text] Leaders of four public organizations -- the Tatar Public Center, the Tatarstan Communist Party, the Republican Party of Tatarstan, and the People's Front of Tatarstan -- have announced the creation of a new democratic opposition organization, the Anti-Bureaucratic Front of Tatarstan [AFT]. The front stands for the resignation of the bankrupt Sabirov government, cuts in and the reorganization of the government staff, a merger of the presidential staff with that of the Cabinet of Ministers, the abolition of the posts of vice president and deputy prime ministers, election of all administration chiefs, open decisionmaking by the government (no secrets from the people), the publication of all secret agreements, the freedom of the mass media, and demands that every step, every decision, and every thought of bureaucracy be placed under people's control.
