March 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

OSCAR SCHOLARSHIP

Martin Scorsese should not have been nominated for best director ("The Last Temptation of Christ"). He should have been nominated for best con man for displaying a caricature of Jesus Christ in 70 millimeter and fooling a lot of intelligent people into believing his film had accomplished something honorable.

If he thinks he has a chance of winning, he is indulging in still another fantasy.

GREG VELNER

Rowland Heights
