Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Prime Minister of Kazakhstan Sergey Tereshchenko received Turkish Transport Minister Makhmet Kostepen yesterday. [passage omitted] Makhmet Kostepen said Turkey is ready to begin to implement several of specific proposals. For instance, Turkey is ready to participate in the reconstruction of Kazakhstani airports and to install electronic equipment there. Cooperation in the field of telecommunications is also very promising. In February, Turkey, together with France, will launch a satellite which will substantially broaden the volume of Turkey's telecommunications with America, Western Europe, Central Asia, and Kazakhstan. The guest also expressed a wish that an additional air flight to Turkey be opened. Sergey Tereshchenko, having expressed hope that cooperation between the two countries will facilitate the speedy development of joint marine transportation, put forward the idea of setting up a joint venture for carrying freight by sea because each year about 7-10 million metric tons of oil have to be transported south and west.
