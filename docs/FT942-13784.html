940422 FT 22 APR 94 / World News in Brief: Comeback for legendary bullfighter Legendary Spanish bullfighter Manuel Benitez - 'El Cordobes' - said he was returning to the professional bullring after an absence of 13 years. The 57-year-old (pictured here during a charity bullfight in 1986) is expected to make the first of four appearances in the Catalan city of Tarragona on May 21. He told a news conference his fee would be Pta400m (Dollars 2.8m).
