930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil SAO PAULO advanced 8.1 per cent in heavy trading, the Bovespa index finishing 1,124 up at 14,974. Trading was nervous after press reports on Sunday that Mr Paulo Haddad, the former Brazilian economy minister, had planned a price freeze as a way to stabilise the economy. Mr Eliseu Resende, Mr Haddad's successor, has said that he might use elements of Mr Haddad's anti-inflation plan.
