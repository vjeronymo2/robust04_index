Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow February 28 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed a decree on Monday creating a new presidential financial-budget department within his administration. According to the presidential press service, the department will supply the president with information and analytical assistance in the sphere of state finances, budget, insurance, prices, credits and money circulation. The new body will also prepare practical recommendations on issues of finances and monetary policy. Igor Moskovskiy was appointed head of the presidential financial-budget department, the press service said.
