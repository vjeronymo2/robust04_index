930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / Observer: Watershed Is water polo dangerous? The risks clearly aren't confined to the one immortalised by Tony Curtis when, impersonating an aqua-sporting millionaire in Some like it Hot, he answered the question with: 'I should say so. Why, the last time I played, I had three ponies drown right under me.' The Red Stars water polo team from Yugoslavia have just been left high and dry in South Africa because, in the opposite of a case of the biter bit, the Pretoria government has endorsed the UN sanctions against their country. They did manage one game on Monday, but the rest of their programme has gone down the drain.
