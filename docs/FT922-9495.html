920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / The Queen's Speech: Lottery aims to raise up to Pounds 1bn -Main Points The new Department of National Heritage, under Mr David Mellor (pictured), will set up the lottery with proceeds going to sport, arts, heritage, charities and a new Millennium Fund to celebrate the year 2000. It is estimated that the lottery could raise up to Pounds 1bn a year. An independent board will be responsible for distribution of the proceeds, with day-to-day management undertaken by the private sector under contract. The long-awaited announcement of plans for a UK lottery was first made in March by Mr Kenneth Baker, then home secretary.
