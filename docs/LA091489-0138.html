September 14, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Dion Thurman, a former Bradley University basketball player, has been charged with possession of cocaine after a police raid that resulted in the seizure of four grams of crack cocaine, authorities said.

Thurman, 21, was arraigned Monday and bond was set at $5,000. He was also charged with possession of cocaine with intent to deliver, and resisting arrest.

If convicted, Thurman faces up to three years in prison.
