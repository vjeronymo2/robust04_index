941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / International Company News: Johnson Electric advances 10.7% Johnson Electric Holdings, one of the world's largest manufacturers of micromotors for a broad range of domestic, commercial and consumer applications, yesterday reported a 10.7 per cent improvement in its interim net profits, writes Our Financial Staff in London. The Hong Kong company, which has diversified production bases in China, Thailand and Switzerland, saw net profits for the six months to September 30, 1994 advance to HKDollars 168.45m (USDollars 22m) from HKDollars 152.15m in the corresponding period last year. Turnover rose to HKDollars 918.50m from HKDollars 770.50m.
