940323 FT 23 MAR 94 / Hungary sees record trade gap Hungary's current account saw a record deficit of Dollars 3.45bn or 9 per cent of gross domestic product in 1993, but Mr Peter Bod, president of the National Bank of Hungary, said yesterday the shortfall was an indicator of economic recovery and forecast growth of 3-4 per cent in 1994. Exports, hit by drought, Yugoslav sanctions and German recession, fell 17 per cent in 1993.
