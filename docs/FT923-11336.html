920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Former MAM chief takes post at Jupiter Tyndall MR LEONARD LICHT, former vice-chairman of Mercury Asset Management, has moved to the Jupiter Tyndall Group, another fund manager, where he will be group managing director and deputy chairman. Mr Licht's departure follows those of Mr James Dawnay, formerly in charge of MAM's unit trust business, to Baillie Gifford, and Mr Richard Bernays, a Mercury vice-chairman, to Hill Samuel. Mr Licht enjoys a strong reputation as a fund manager, and will work on the management of pension funds and other institutional clients. Mr John Duffield, chairman of Jupiter Tyndall, said: ' We have no doubt that as a fund manager Leonard is unsurpassed in his generation and he will enormously strengthen our team.' Mr Peter Stormonth Darling, chairman of MAM, announced that Mr Stephen Zimmerman, Mercury's deputy chairman, will take day-to-day responsibility for the group's specialist equity division with immediate effect following Mr Licht's departure.
