920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / International Company News: 3M breaks run of five declines MINNESOTA Mining and Manufacturing, the diversified US manufacturer known as 3M, broke a string of five quarterly declines in the three months ended June 30, writes Barbara Durr. The company reported net income of Dollars 322m, or Dollars 1.47 per share, up 7.6 per cent from Dollars 299m, or Dollars 1.36, a year ago. During the first half, net income rose 1.9 per cent to Dollars 610m, or Dollars 2.79, compared with Dollars 599m, or Dollars 2.73, a year ago. Sales were up 2.5 per cent to Dollars 6.9bn from Dollars 6.7bn last year.
