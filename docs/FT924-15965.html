921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Anglo Pacific incurs Pounds 174,000 deficit Anglo Pacific Resources, the USM-quoted coal mining and mineral exploration company, reported a loss of Pounds 147,000 for the six months to end-June. The outcome compared with a profit of Pounds 114,000 for the comparable period and with a loss of Pounds 352,000 at the December year end. Turnover fell to Pounds 948,000 (Pounds 1.54m) reflecting the sale of the Kurara Gold Mine. Losses per share emerged at 0.19p (earnings 0.15p).
