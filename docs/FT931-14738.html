930116 FT 16 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Chartwell incurs Pounds 137,000 loss Chartwell Group, the USM-quoted maker of carpet tiles and cubicles for the contract market, ran up a loss of Pounds 137,000 pre-tax for the half year to September 30 against previous profits of Pounds 41,000. The directors blamed the prolonged recession for the downturn and pointed out that economic conditions had become tougher during the current year. Turnover of continuing operations fell to Pounds 3.1m (Pounds 4.59m). Losses per share were 1.5p, against earnings of 0.4p.
