BFN [Report signed "kett": "Greens' No to European Union Is Now Set"] [Excerpt] Vienna -- Austrian People's Party [OeVP] Chairman Erhard Busek indicated on Saturday [9 April] that his party will not make a recommendation for the European Union [EU] referendum on 12 June. In contrast, Karl Krammer, chief of Chancellor Franz Vranitzky's cabinet, stated that a party has to take a clear position on such an important question. Yesterday, Sunday, the Greens set their "no." The party is to make a relevant "recommendation without patronizing" for the referendum at the end of April. Yesterday, the extended federal executive committee agreed unanimously on this. Federal Spokesman Peter Pilz stated that he had submitted the relevant motion to the committee. [passage omitted]
