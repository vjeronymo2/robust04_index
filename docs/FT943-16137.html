940706 FT 06 JUL 94 / Cathedral Employment in liquidation Cathedral Employment Enterprise, one of the biggest providers of training for the unemployed in London, yesterday went into voluntary liquidation with debts of nearly Pounds 1m. The joint liquidators said the chance of selling the whole concern was limited but constituent parts might be sold. Cathedral employed about 150 staff in 50 centres across London. Its annual turnover was Pounds 3m.
