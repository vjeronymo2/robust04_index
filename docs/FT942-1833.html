940622 FT 22 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Aukett A flow of new orders helped Aukett Associates, the design and architectural services group, swing back to the black with a first half pre-tax profit of Pounds 131,000, against losses of Pounds 2.35m. In the six months to March 31 work done totalled Pounds 3.5m, against Pounds 3.57m including Pounds 536,000 from a discontinued activity. Continuing activities showed a 15 per cent rise. Operating profits on continuing activities were Pounds 232,000 (Pounds 207,000 loss). Earnings per share were 0.86p (losses 16.91p).
