940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Burmah Castrol expands in India Burmah Castrol, through its Foseco chemicals subsidiary, is buying a controlling interest in its metallurgical chemicals joint venture in India for Pounds 155,000. It increased its stake from 50 per cent to 51 per cent by purchasing new shares in the venture. Greaves, Burmah's partner, has sold most of its shares to the public, the balance being reserved for preferential allocation to the venture's employees.
