BFN [Announcer-read report over still of regional map] [Text] Fifty-eight terrorists have been killed in operations launched by the security forces in the state of emergency region. Two terrorists have been captured in the operations. The State of Emergency Governor's Office has issued the following statement on the issue: Operations aimed at preventing the activities of the militants belonging to the illegal separatist terror organization are continuing in the region at large. The security forces on 17 May launched an operation in the Bezar Mountain region, some 20 km north of Adiyaman, after learning that there was a large group of terrorists in that region. Twenty-eight terrorists were killed in the operation and their weapons were seized. Meanwhile, other operations were also carried out by the security forces on 17 and 18 May. Seventeen terrorists were killed on the Kato Mountain west of Hakkari, four in the pursuit of terrorists who fired at buildings belonging to the security forces in central Siirt, three near the Ortabagi village of Sirnak's Uludere District, two around the Okcular village of Siirt's Pervari District, one in the Beybagi village of Batman's Kozluk District, one in the Kusluca village of Bitlis' Tatvan District, one in the Dallitepe region of Bingol, and one in the Akcay region of Sirnak's Cizre District. Rocket launchers and hand grenades were also seized. Two terrorists were captured in the Ortabagi village of Sirnak's Uludere District and in central Van.
