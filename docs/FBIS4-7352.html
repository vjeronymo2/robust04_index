BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 29 (KCNA) -- A meeting was held here on April 28 to mark the 34th anniversary of the independence of Togo under the co-sponsorship of the Committee for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries and the Korea-Togo Friendship Association. Present at the meeting were An Pok-man, deputy editor-in-chief of MINJU CHOSON and vice chairman of the Korea-Togo Friendship Association, and other officials concerned and working people in the city. A speech was made and a message of greetings to the Togolese president was adopted at the meeting.
