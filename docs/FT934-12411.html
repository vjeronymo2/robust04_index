931027 FT 27 OCT 93 / Euro-sceptic Tory MP may quit SIR Richard Body, a senior Tory Euro-sceptic MP, is to discuss his future with his local constituency association amid speculation that he may quit politics and create a vacancy in the Lincolnshire constituency of Holland with Boston. Sir Richard, widely believed to be among the Tory rebels recently described as 'barmy' by the prime minister, had a meeting yesterday with Sir Norman Fowler, the party chairman. Sir Richard had a majority of nearly 14,000 at the last general election.
