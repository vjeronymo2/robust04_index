930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Ernest Green Ernest Green and Partners Holdings, the USM-quoted structural, civil and environmental engineering consultancy, reported pre-tax profits 32 per cent ahead at Pounds 773,000 for the 12 months to end-June. The advance was achieved on slightly lower turnover of Pounds 7.66m (Pounds 7.74m). Earnings per share rose by 10 per cent to 6.6p (6p) and a same-again final of 4.25p maintains the total dividend at 7p.
