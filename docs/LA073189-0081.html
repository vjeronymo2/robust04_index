July 31, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

HOWELL IS NAMED ANGEL OF THE WEEK

Jack Howell, who hit four home runs and drove in eight runs last week, is The Times' Orange County Edition Angel of the Week.

Howell, who has hit 12 homers in his last 37 games, established a career high for homers in a month with seven in July.

The Times will donate $200 in Howell's name to the Boys and Girls Clubs of Orange County.
