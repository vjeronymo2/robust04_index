Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The press service of the headquarters of Nagorno-Karabakh's Defence Army has confirmed to Interfax that military actions in Kelbadjar District were stepped up. According to the headquarters, as a result of successful counter-offensives conducted in the north of the district on Saturday and Sunday morning Karabakh army units managed to recapture several strategically important heights. Their trophies included two armored vehicles, a tank and a personnel carrier. There are no reports on casualties. As the press service said, Sunday morning the Azeri troops launched an offensive in the Fizuli-Martuni direction at the south-eastern sector of the Karabakh front. At 12:00 on Sunday military actions in that direction continued.
