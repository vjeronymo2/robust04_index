BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Azerbaijani Deputy Prime Minister Abbas Abbasov, who is in Ankara, visited Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin and conferred with him for some time. Prior to the meeting, Abbasov said his visit was focused on activating the $250-million credit Azerbaijan is seeking from Eximbank. Explaining that as a result of Azerbaijan's transportation difficulties with other countries, Turkey is the only country from which it can obtain wheat, Abbasov announced that the first wheat consignment Azerbaijan will buy will total 300,000 tonnes.
