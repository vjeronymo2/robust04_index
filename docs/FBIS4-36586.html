BFN [Text] Haydar Abu-bakr al-'Attas, the prime minister, today visited al-Jazirah Hospital and al-Mafraq Hospital in the UAE, during which he inspected the injured of the Armed Forces and the injured citizens as the result of the brutal war launched by the Sanaa regime on our country. The prime minister praised the stance of the UAE and its assistance in alleviating the sufferings of the democratic Yemeni people. He thanked the hospital management and staff for their efforts to provide human services to the injured.
