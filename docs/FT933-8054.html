930818 FT 18 AUG 93 / ECGD strike STAFF in the eastern Europe department of the Export Credit Guarantee Department went on strike yesterday as part of a continuing campaign in protest against a proposed 25 per cent cut in staffing levels. The action, which may continue for several days, follows a one-day strike on Monday throughout the department by members of the two Civil Service unions, the NUCPS and the CPSA.
