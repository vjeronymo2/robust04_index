February 14, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

SANTA CLARA;

PARK FIRES 28 FOR THEFT, DRUG ABUSE

Great America amusement park has fired 28 of its 160 full-time employees, including some in management, for stealing and drug and alcohol use.

The dismissals came after an investigation of several months by a private firm. The investigation was prompted by an anonymous tip, park spokeswoman Lise Hurst said. The types of drugs involved and the amount of stolen property were not disclosed.
