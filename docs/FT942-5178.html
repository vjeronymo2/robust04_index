940606 FT 06 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Quadrant in black Quadrant Group, the photographic and video concern, moved from losses of Pounds 15.9m to profits of Pounds 786,000 pre-tax for the year to February 28. Turnover of continuing activities fell from Pounds 47.9m to Pounds 43m. Earnings per share of 3.36p against losses of 59.83p.
