BFN [Excerpts] The delegation of imams, preachers, teachers of the holy Koran and teachers of Arabic and Islamic studies continued their meetings with the parties to the conflict in Afghanistan. Thus, the delegation met in Peshawar today Mohammad Imam, representative of Afghan President Borhanoddin Rabbani, Qotboddin Helal, deputy prime minister and representative of Prime Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar, and a number of representatives of organizations and parties in Afghanistan. This falls within the framework of the efforts made by the Great Jamahiriyah to bring about reconciliation among the warring factions in Afghanistan and end their in-fighting. [passage omitted] During the meeting Deputy Prime Minister Qotboddin Helal hailed the stands taken by the Great 1 September Revolution towards the causes of Islam and Muslims. He talked about the prominent role played by the brother leader of the revolution [Mu'ammar al- Qadhdhafi] in uniting the Afghan organizations during the period of resistance. He also expressed his thanks and appreciation for the Great Jamahiriyah's initiative aimed at achieving reconciliation among the parties to the conflict, restoring peace to Afghanistan and ending the in-fighting among brethren. Mohammad Imam, representative of President Borhanoddin Rabbani, and Qotboddin Helal, representative of Prime Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar, expressed their readiness to offer all the facilities to ensure the success of the mission of the Libyan delegation. They also said that the Council of Coordination was prepared to declare a cease-fire during the mission of the Libyan delegation. The two men stressed their full desire to reach a fair solution which would satisfy all parties with a view to restoring security, stability and peace to Afghanistan.
