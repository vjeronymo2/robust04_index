920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / Australia to press on with airline sale THE Australian government has indicated it will go ahead with a sale of Qantas, the state-owned carrier, and Australian Airlines to raise up to ADollars 1.75bn, writes Emilia Tagaza from Canberra. The ruling Labor party had earlier decided to merge the two airlines and sell 49 per cent of the merged entity. Two overseas airlines - British Airways and Singapore International Airlines -have shown interest in bidding for the share but have indicated that the government's valuation was too high.
