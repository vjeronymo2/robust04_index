April 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

AND NOW, THE GOOD WORD ABOUT MADONNA

"Lewd behavior?" "Self-absorption"? "Marginally voiced"? "Screwed up"? These are the words used against Madonna by judge-and-jury letters writers (March 26). The word that comes to my mind is jealousy.

Madonna is beautiful, talented and laughing all the way to the bank. When was the last time Pepsi offered you $5 million?

JOYCE YOVANNONE

Woodland Hills
