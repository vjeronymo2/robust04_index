Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of Foreign Ministry Deputy Spokesman Ferhat Ataman] [Text] It was reported that Turkey expects the NATO Council in Brussels to take concrete and decisive measures as soon as possible regarding the Bosnia issue. In answer to a question at his weekly news conference, Foreign Ministry Deputy Spokesman Ferhat Ataman pointed out that it is obvious there is a general and strong will toward speedily implementing violence-preventing measures. Explaining that new plans for an air operation are not needed since NATO already has access to plans for an air operation, Ataman said they will reply favorably in case NATO headquarters have a request from Turkey.
