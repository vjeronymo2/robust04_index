BFN [Excerpt] On the occasion of the world antinarcotics day on 26 June, on the morning of 25 June the people's court of Vientiane Municipality held a ceremony to destroy seized narcotic drugs at the Justice Ministry in the presence of Souban Salitthilat, deputy foreign affairs minister and chairman of the National Committee for the Suppression and Control of Narcotic Drugs. Attending the ceremony were diplomats and foreign dignitaries as well as a number of Lao authorities concerned. The destroyed drugs, worth more than 45 million kips, included 10 kilos of heroin, 120 kilos of opium, more than 170 kilos of marijuana, and 14,000 packages of "Buat Hai" pain killers. These drugs were seized by Lao authorities along border areas and in post offices throughout the country. [passage omitted]
