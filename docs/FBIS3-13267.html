Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The delegation of the 1948 Palestinians met with several sons of the Musawi [Jewish] community in Syria yesterday. 'Abd-al-Wahhab Darawshah, head of the Palestinian delegation, said the Musawi community in Syria is part of the Syrian people. He said they met with them to clarify some Israeli political and public opinion issues, and to bring back a message of the true image of their life in Syria. Darawshah said the delegation will visit a synagogue and a Musawi community school today to prove to all who seek to distort the truth that Syria does not discriminate against the Musawi community. Yusuf Jajati, deputy head of the Musawi community in Syria, denounced the holy Ibrahimi Mosque massacre. He said: In the name of the Musawi community, we strongly denounce these actions. Jajati stressed that the Musawi community is living in Syria in peace. He expressed hope that a just and comprehensive peace will be achieved in the region.
