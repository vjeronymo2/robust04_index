BFN [Text] Belarus industrialists and entrepreneurs will support Vyacheslav Kebich, current head of republican government, in the forthcoming presidential elections. BELINFORM reports that this decision was adopted by the Belarus Confederation of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs at an extraordinary meeting. At the meeting, the confederation approved the government's activity aimed at creating a single economic space with Russia -- the treaty on merging Russia's and Belarus' monetary systems -- and proposed to submit it to a session of the republican Supreme Soviet for examination.
