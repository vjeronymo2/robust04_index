Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 17 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng held talks with Cambodia's First Prime Minister Norodom Ranariddh and Second Prime Minister Hun Sen here this afternoon. They exchanged views on bilateral relations and on regional and international issues. Ranariddh and Hun Sen, leading a government delegation from the Kingdom of Cambodia, arrived here this afternoon on a five-day official visit to China as Li's guests. During the talks, Premier Li welcomed them on their first visit to China since peace resumed in Cambodia and the country formed its new government. Li said China and Cambodia have close, friendly, cooperative relations, adding that it is significant to further such relations under the new situation. The Chinese Government is willing to work with the Cambodian Government to develop and strengthen bilateral traditional friendship and restore cooperation in various fields on the basis of the five principles of peaceful coexistence. Ranariddh said that the Cambodian people and Government have always regarded China as a sincere and great friend, and will never forget the important contributions that China made to Cambodia's peace process. At present, Ranariddh said, his country is at a crucial stage of restoration and reconstruction, and the Cambodian Government wants its friendly cooperation in all fields with China to be enhanced through the current visit, on the basis of mutual benefits and equality. Ranariddh expressed his thanks to the Chinese Government and the Chinese doctors for the excellent medical treatment for King Norodom Sihanouk. Touching on Cambodia's domestic situation, Premier Li said that China is glad to see the changes which have taken place in Cambodia, and its entering into a period of restoration and rebuilding. King Norodom Sihanouk had played an outstanding and irreplaceable role in this, he added. Li Peng said that the Chinese Government and people sincerely hope that Cambodia will become a neutral and non-aligned country. China appreciates Cambodia's efforts towards a proper handling of relations with its neighbor nations, Li said, adding that the more friends that Cambodia makes, the better. The Chinese premier said it is necessary to have a peaceful neighborly environment for Cambodia's reconstruction and development, and that China hopes that the Cambodian Government, under the leadership of King Norodom Sihanouk and in the spirit of national reconciliation, will solve its internal problems properly to maintain peace and stability in Cambodia for the country's reconstruction. Ranariddh said the Kingdom of Cambodia, led by King Sihanouk, is working for unity and reconciliation, and all parties in the Cambodian Government and Parliament can cooperate with each other closely. There are sound grounds for Cambodia's national reconciliation and its economy will certainly be restored and rebuilt with the help of the international community. During the talks, the Chinese premier briefed the guests on China's economic construction and its reforms and opening up. The two sides also exchanged views briefly on regional and international issues of common interest. Ranariddh invited Premier Li Peng to visit Cambodia at his convenience. Li expressed thanks and accepted the invitation. Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing was among those who took part in the talks. Meanwhile, Zhu Lin, Premier Li Peng's wife, met with Madame Ranariddh and Madame Hun Sen. After the talks, Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing and Norodom Sirivut, Cambodian deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs and international exchange and cooperation, signed an agreement of economic and technological cooperation between the governments of the two countries. Before the talks this afternoon, Premier Li presided over a ceremony to welcome Ranariddh and Hun Sen. This evening, Li hosted a banquet in honor of Ranariddh and Hun Sen and their party.
