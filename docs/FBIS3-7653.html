Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, March 26 (YONHAP) -- Vice Chairman of the National Assembly Hwang Nak-chu Saturday embarked on two-week-long tour of Australia, New Zealand, Malaysia and Japan. Hwang, accompanied by Reps. Pak U-pyong, Kim Han-kyu (Democratic Liberal Party) and Choe Uk-chol (Democratic Party), is scheduled to meet with parliamentary leaders of the countries to discuss ways to promote bilateral cooperation. He plans to return on April 8.
