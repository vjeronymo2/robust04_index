921217 FT 17 DEC 92 / Banks levied for BCCI payment BANKS were levied Pounds 80m by the Deposit Protection Board yesterday to cover payments to depositors in Bank of Credit and Commerce International. BCCI depositors have so far received Pounds 58m, leading to a growing deficit in the fund. The fund was in deficit by Pounds 28.9m in February this year and has covered payments from a Pounds 125m borrowing facility at the Bank of England. Payments to BCCI depositors are expected to total between Pounds 80m and Pounds 85m.
