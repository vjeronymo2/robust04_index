930525 FT 25 MAY 93 / Income support claims up 13% THE NUMBER of people claiming income support rose to more than 5m last year. Department of Social Security figures published yesterday showed an increase of 600,000, or 13 per cent, over the 12 months to May 1992. Numbers of claimants have risen every year since 1989, with unemployed people and single parents accounting for most of the increase during the 1990s. There were 1.7m unemployed claimants last May, and nearly 1m single parents.
