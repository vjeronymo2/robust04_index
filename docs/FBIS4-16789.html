BFN [Order No. 116 of the Russian Federation Minister of Defense "On the Drafting of Citizens of the Russian Federation for Military Service in April-June 1994, and the Transfer to the Reserve of Soldiers, Seamen, Noncommissioned Officers, Petty Officers, and Military Construction Personnel who Have Served the Designated Period of Military Service", dated Moscow, 15 April 1994 and signed by Russian Federation Minister of Defense, Army General Pavel Grachev] [Text] In execution of the Russian Federation president's Edict No. 784 of 15 April 1994, I order that: 1. Soldiers, seamen, noncommissioned officers, petty officers, and military construction personnel who have served the designated period of military service be transferred to the reserve from the ranks of the Russian Federation Armed Forces in April-June 1994. 2. Commanders of forces in military districts and military commissars ensure jointly with local administration heads the organization and implementation in April-June 1994 of the draft for military service in the Russian Federation Armed Forces and other forces and military units of citizens of the Russian Federation born between 1967 and 1976 who do not have the right to a deferment of and exemption from being drafted for military service. 3. The order be posted in all companies, batteries, squadrons, and on ships. [Signed] Russian Federation Minister of Defense Army General P. Grachev
