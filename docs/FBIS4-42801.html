BFN [Text] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos today said that Greece condemns every act of violence and terrorism but it does not accept becoming the victim of an orchestrated effort to export Turkey's domestic problems to Greece. Venizelos repeated that no office of the PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] operates in Athens, and once again described the Turkish claims as ridiculous, unfounded, and slanderous. Venizelos said that President Konstandinos Karamanlis will soon give his answer to Turkish President Suleyman Demirel's letter and the Turkish claims regarding the PKK action.
