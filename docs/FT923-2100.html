920919 FT 19 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Haslemere Ests climbs to Pounds 6.9m Haslemere Estates, the property investor and developer, reported pre-tax profits up from Pounds 5.27m to Pounds 6.9m in the first half of 1992. The result was helped by lower interest charges of Pounds 7.87m (Pounds 10.3m). Turnover was Pounds 22.5m (Pounds 23.9m) with a further Pounds 16.2m (Pounds 17m) net rental from investment properties. Earnings per share were 22.79p (15.3p).
