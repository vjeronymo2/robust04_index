920626 FT 26 JUN 92 / International Company News: General Mills lifts earnings GENERAL Mills, the US cereal and resturant group, reported after-tax profits of Dollars 495.6m in the year to end-May, compared with Dollars 472.7m in the previous 12 months. In the final three months, net profit was dollars 90.9m previously. Both final-quarter and full-year figures for 1992 are scored after a dollars 10m charge, relating to a potential lease adjustment being negotiated with R. H. Macy, the bankrupt US department store group.
