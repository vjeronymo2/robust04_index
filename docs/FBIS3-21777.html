Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Civil Guard have seized 60 kilos of high purity heroin in "Operation Bread" in Madrid. Three suspected members of an international drug-trafficking network were arrested. The drugs seized would have fetched over 1 billion pesetas on the market. In the town of Mojacar in Almeria province, the Civil Guard have seized 1,171 kilos of hashish resin, and arrested an Algerian national who is linked with the consignment.
