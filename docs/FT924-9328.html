921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / Italy to freeze share deal tax Italy's controversial capital gains tax on share dealings, is to be suspended for a year following a decision by the lower house of parliament writes Haig Simonian in Milan. The complex tax, introduced in February 1991 and levied at either 15 per cent or 25 per cent, has been strongly criticised by stockbrokers as an important factor penalising the country's stock markets. The suspension is to take effect later this month.
