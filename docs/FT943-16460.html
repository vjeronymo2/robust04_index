940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / China attacks EU import curbs China yesterday condemned recent quotas imposed by the European Union on Chinese exports and warned that the curbs would ultimately backfire. The warning from the Chinese foreign ministry came as Mr Li Peng, the Chinese prime minister, began a week-long trip to Germany designed to cement improving relations. Chancellor Helmut Kohl (pictured with Mr Li Peng at a welcome ceremony in Bonn) said Germany would use its presidency of the EU to improve trade between the EU and China. Report, Page 6
