931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / Council to canvass staff on leaving EXPECTED BUDGET cuts have led Birmingham city council, the biggest local authority in England, to ask each of its 11,000 administrative employees if they would be prepared to take redundancy, John Authers writes. The council said it feared it could lose Pounds 30m in the 1994-95 budget allocation, but stressed it did not have a target number of staff reductions.
