920204 FT 04 FEB 92 / Observer: Factinating Fans of useless trivia will be delighted by a new booklet produced by Boots entitled Some Fascinating Facts. Did you know, for example, that an elephant weighs as much as the frames of 339,286 pairs of sunglasses? Or that right-handed people use more deodorants than the left-handed because they sweat more? Or that it takes 15 inflated condoms to float a jeep? I thought not.
