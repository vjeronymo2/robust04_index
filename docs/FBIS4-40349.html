BFN [Text] Tbilisi, JUNE 15 (SNARK) -- The assembly of the commanding officers of the formations and detachments within the Group of Russian troops in Transcaucasia has started today in Georgia. The Commander-in-Chief Colonel-General Feodor Reut leads this muster. It is held on the basis of the Batumi garrison's 90th motor-rifle regiment. June 16 is supposed as the day of the arrival of the Georgian and Armenian Defence Ministers. They will take part in the measure. Our correspondent reports, that June 17 studies on the local battle direction are to start. The Black Sea Navy landing party will be drawn in these studies.
