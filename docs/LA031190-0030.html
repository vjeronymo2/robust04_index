March 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

LEAN TIMES FOR FOREIGN MOVIES

I am frustrated by the ever-shrinking availability of foreign-language films in Los Angeles.

Imagine how I felt when I experienced the same problem when visiting France a year ago. The triplex in Avignon offered a choice of (1) a Rambo movie, (2) Mickey Rourke and (3) Schwarzenegger. If you can't find a French film in France, where then?

ROXANE ALKASLASSY

Sherman Oaks
