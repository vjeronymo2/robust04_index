March 17, 1990, Saturday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: THOUSAND OAKS;

6TH YOUTH CONGRESS TO CONVENE TODAY

Self-esteem, substance abuse and gang violence are topics scheduled for a series of seminars and speakers at the sixth annual Youth Congress, which will be held today in Thousand Oaks.

Seventy-five students from eight area high schools and California Lutheran University will join the speakers and community leaders in round-table discussions.

The event will be held from 9 a.m. to noon at 1 GTE Place. CAROLYN KOPP
