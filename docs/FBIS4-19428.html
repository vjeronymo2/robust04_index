BFN [Text] Rome, April 16 (AFP) -- Prime Minister Carlo Azeglio Ciampi handed in his resignation to President Oscar Luigi Scalfaro on Saturday [16 April], the premier's office announced. Ciampi, 73, said last week he was ready to resign after the March 27-28 general election but would do so only after the election of the speakers of the upper and lower houses of parliament. Politicians from the right-wing Freedom Alliance [Carlo Scognamiglio and Irene Pivetti respectively] were voted into the posts earlier Saturday.
