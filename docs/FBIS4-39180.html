BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Kulagin] [Text] Almaty 6 June TASS -- During his meeting with visiting Indian Minister of State for External Affairs Salman Khurshid, who arrived in Almaty on Sunday [5 June], Speaker of Kazakhstan's Parliament Abish Kekilbayev displayed special interest in India's experience of resolving problems of local self-government. The two leaders also discussed questions of ecology, flood-prevention measures, and other topical problems. The two sides expressed the hope that their bilateral ties would progressively broaden on the international, as well as interparliamentary levels. Abish Kekilbayev handed over an invitation to the Indian parliamentary delegation to visit Kazakhstan in the second half of 1994.
