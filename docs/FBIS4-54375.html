BFN [From the "Intelligence" column] [Text] Elusive Khmer Rouge supremo Pol Pot has moved house again. Khmer Rouge [KR] defectors reported that after spending some time in a special residence in Phnum Chhat area near the Thai border in late 1993, Pol Pot moved to the safety of Thailand. He is believed to living in a well-guarded compound inside a durian plantation near the provincial capital, Chanthaburi. For a number of years in the late 1980s, Pol Pot had lived in a compound called "Zone 87" in Trat province bordering Cambodia.
