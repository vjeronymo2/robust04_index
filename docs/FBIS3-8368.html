Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The political department of the Croatian Ministry of Defense has issued the following statement: Shortly after 1200 today a Spanish UN Protection Force plane took off from Zagreb for Split. When it was above the Plasko area, which is under the control of Serb terrorists, two missiles were fired at it. One missed the target and the other exploded near the plane and damaged its tail. It can be reliably said that the missiles were fired by Serb terrorists, members of the so-called 39th Banija Corps. There were four crew members and five passengers on the plane. Three of them were slightly wounded. The missiles were fired at 1235. The plane made an emergency landing at Krk airport at 1252, the statement says.
