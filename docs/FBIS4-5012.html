BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 6 April Morning Edition] [Text] Masaru Hayami, chairman of Keizai Doyukai [Japan Association of Corporate Executives], commented on the economic situation at a news conference held on 5 April. He stated: "The economic situation will soon hit bottom, then take a turn for the better." With regard to the recent appreciation of the yen, he indicated there will be no further rise in the yen's value, saying: "The United States is not in a position to wish for the depreciation of the dollar." Concerning criticism about the Hosokawa administration in connection with the issue of Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa's loan, Hayami indicated his hope for the continuation of the Hosokawa administration, stating: "I think it is premature to say the Hosokawa administration's role is over."
