BFN [Text] The Portuguese Government is preparing an emergency plan to evacuate 600,000 Portuguese nationals from South Africa if the security situation deteriorates before or after the elections. Reports from Lisbon indicate that the returnees would be housed in disused buildings and military bases in northern Portugal after being airlifted from South Africa. Portuguese Prime Minister Anibal Cavaco Silva said he was extremely concerned about the violence in South Africa but that he did not think he should advise the Portuguese community in South Africa to leave the country yet.
