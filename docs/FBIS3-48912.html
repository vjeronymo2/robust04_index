Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sofia, December 30 (BTA) -- A prime-time address by Prime Minister Berov called upon Bulgarian people to unite. "Society is tired of political strife and wants a calmer life and more common sense," the prime minister said. "I will not promise you a bright future, but as a nation and a state, we have to break clear from our past," he went on to say. According to Prof. Berov, the National Assembly bears inscribed the secret for Bulgaria's headway: "Unity Is Strength." Prime Minister Berov summed up the 1993 performance of the government which he headed precisely an year ago. He thanked the cabinet, which he called "valiant," for its "courage, zeal and wisdom." "We worked to further advance the changes that had already been launched though there was nobody to applaud us. We did everything we could to take this country's ship into calmer waters," Prime Minister Berov said.
