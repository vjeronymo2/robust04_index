Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 7 (XINHUA) -- Procurator-general of China's supreme people's procuratorate Zhang Siqing met here this afternoon with Peter Kooijmans, visiting minister of foreign affairs of the Netherlands. Zhang briefed the guest on China's People's Congress system, legal construction, functions of procuratorial organs, and so on. He said that China pays much attention to the construction of its legal system and is speeding up legislation focused on the building of a socialist market economy. Kooijmans arrived here this morning at the invitation of Chinese vice-premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen . Qian will hold talks with Kooijmans tomorrow.
