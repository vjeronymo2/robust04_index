940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / International Company News: Armco to take Dollars 20m charge Armco, the US steel group, plans to close all its Empire-Detroit steel division's facilities, laying off 1,300 workers and taking a Dollars 20m, 19 cents per share charge, against first-quarter earnings, Reuter reports from Pittsburg. The company said it expected the shutdown to last until mid-1995, when its new thin-slab continuous caster is completed at Empire-Detroit's Mansfield, Ohio, plant.
