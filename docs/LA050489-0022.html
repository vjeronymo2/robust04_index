May 4, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHARITY SCORECARD

The art auction held at Ron's in Laguna on April 18 netted $4,310 for the Art Institute of Southern California. Arlene Froemmling and Lizbeth Reilly were co-chairwomen.

The "Centennial Ball" held at St. Catherine's Military School in Anaheim on April 22 netted an estimated $5,000 for the school. Kathi Lydon was chairwoman.

The "Celebrity Golf Classic" held at Los Coyotes Country Club in Buena Park on April 24 netted an estimated $106,000 for the Cystic Fibrosis Foundation. Bill Kendig was chairman.

The Interval House golf tournament held at Seacliff Country Club in Huntington Beach on April 24 netted an estimated $15,000 for Interval House Huntington Harbour League. Chris Frejlach and Sue Graham were co-chairmen.

A luncheon and fashion show held at the Four Seasons Hotel in Newport Beach on April 27 netted an estimated $6,000 for the Big Canyon Philharmonic Committee of the Orange County Philharmonic Society. JoAnne Mix and Ollie Hill were co-chairwomen.

The "Evening in Rio" dinner and dance held at the Red Lion Inn in Costa Mesa on April 29 netted $19,673 for the Easter Seal League of Orange County. Penny Hart was chairwoman.

A cocktail party held at the Newport Beach home of Mr. and Mrs. Richard E. Dillion on April 29 netted an estimated $7,000 for the Balboa Committee of the Orange County Philharmonic Society. Kellee Fitzgeorge and Becky Riddet were co-chairwomen.

The "Opus 2" dinner held at 5'-0" in Laguna Beach on April 30 netted an estimated $10,000 for the Laguna Beach Chamber Music Society's Young Artist Series. Roda Felden and Darryle Stafford were co-chairmen.
