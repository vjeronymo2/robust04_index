BFN [Text] Brother 'Abd-al-Rahman 'Ali al-Jifri, the deputy chairman of the Presidential Council, this morning inspected the air force and air defense headquarters, where he was briefed on the battle operations and the role of the pilots in implementing their responsibilities of defending the homeland and its achievements, and of foiling the plans of the enemy on the various fronts. The brother deputy chairman of the Presidential Council praised the pilots' efforts to protect democratic Yemen's airspace, and to prevent the enemy forces from achieving their military aims. Brother 'Abd-al-Rahman al-Jifri also visited the families of victims of the barbarous indiscriminate shelling suffered by a number of residential districts in the capital, Aden, which came from the forces of the military regime in Sanaa and resulted in the deaths and wounding of 30 women, children, elderly persons, and peaceful civilians. Brother 'Abd-al-Rahman al-Jifri, the deputy chairman of the Presidential Council, expressed his sorrow and condolences at these horrible massacres perpetrated against the peaceful residents of the capital, Aden. The brother chairman of the Presidential Council then visited the naval command, saw for himself the fighting morale of the naval officers and enlisted men, and observed their intrepid role in supporting their brothers in the Armed Forces in repulsing and defeating the invading forces.
