BFN [Text] Lesotho's information and broadcasting minister, Mr. Mpho Malie, has been released from detention by striking Lesotho policemen. Our correspondent in Maseru reports that Mr. Malie, who is also the acting finance minister, was released last night after being held for more than five hours in the city. He said that Mr. Malie had been interrogated about the 60 percent salary increase demanded by the police.
