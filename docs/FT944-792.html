941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Scottish Equitable Aegon, the Netherlands' second-largest insurance group, has agreed to inject a further Pounds 150m into its Scottish Equitable unit in line with its initial investment agreement. The money will be used to develop the company and pensions and investment business. Edinburgh-based Scottish Equitable, which was taken over by Aegon in 1993, said new business in 1994 was up 20 per cent, indicating 'substantial gains in market share'.
