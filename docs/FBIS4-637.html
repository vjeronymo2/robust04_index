BFN [Text] Heavy fighting is continuing in Tubmanburg, Bomi County, between Madingo and Krahn elements within ULIMO [United Liberation Movement for Democracy in Liberia]. Alhaji Koromah and Roosevelt Johnson recently issued a joint communique in the Sierra Leonean capital, Freetown, to stop the bloody conflict within the organization. People say the battle for Tubmanburg has escalated to other areas in Bomi County. A convoy of relief supplies for thousands of citizens in Cape Mount, Bomi, and Lower Lofa Counties was stopped at the Po River by ECOMOG [Economic Community of West African States Cease-fire Monitoring Group] soldiers. Roosevelt Johnson's faction of ULIMO has vowed to revenge the death of their people killed by the Koromah faction. People say ECOMOG soldiers in the Tubmanburg area are being disarmed by the Krahn elements of ULIMO.
