January 8, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Andrei Dundukov of the Soviet Union took the lead after the jumping event in the World Cup Nordic combined competition at Schonach, West Germany.

Dundukov had 224 points after 2 jumps of 89.5 meters.

Hubert Schwarz of West Germany held second place with a total of 218.3 points after jumps of 86.5 meters and 88 meters. He trails Dundukov by 38 seconds.
