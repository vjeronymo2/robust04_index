Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The brother leader received for the second time in 24 hours Mr. Viktor Posuvalyuk, the personal envoy of Russian President Boris Yeltsin, within the context of the continued Palestinian-Russian contacts to discuss the current developments following the Ibrahimi mosque massacre in Hebron. Brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] also discussed with the Russian envoy the ways and means that stress the need to recognize the resolutions on the al-Ibrahimi Mosque massacre, and also in connection with the international protection for our people and our Islamic and Christian sanctities. The current situation was also discussed at the different levels.
