940128 FT 28 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Continental Assets improvement Undiluted net asset value per share of Continental Assets Trust improved by 35 per cent over the 12 months to December 31 from 170.3p to 229.1p. The figure at June 30 was 189.8p. Revenue at the Ivory & Sime-managed trust moved ahead from Pounds 545,000 to Pounds 687,000 giving earnings per share of 3.16p against 2.51p. The single dividend is raised to 3p (2.4p).
