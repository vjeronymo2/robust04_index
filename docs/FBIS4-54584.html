BFN [Text] Skopje, 29 Apr (TANJUG) -- Albanian paramilitary units in Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] were in possession of "Stinger" missiles before they were disbanded by the republic's Ministry of Internal Affairs in November 1993. This was revealed today by VECER, a Skopje-based daily, which based its story on an account given by one of the main defendants, Hasan Agusi, former Yugoslav People's Army captain serving in the Marshall Tito barracks in Skopje until 1991. According to Agusi, the Albanian paramilitary headquarters, which was based in Gostivar, paid 200,000 German marks to import weapons from Albania through Debar. The shipment consisted of 200 Chinese-made Kalashnikovs.
