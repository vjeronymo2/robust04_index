Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Russian Federation Government Decree No. 15, signed by Chernomyrdin and dated 10 January 1994: "Questions of Ensuring Publication and Dissemination of the Mass Media and Printed Matter"] [Text] The Russian Federation Government decrees that: 1. Russian Federation Council of Ministers-Government Decree No. 1233 "On the Revaluation of the Fixed Capital (Funds) of Enterprises and Organizations" of 25 November 1993 regarding the revaluation of the fixed capital of printing enterprises, including those forming part of publishing and printing complexes, shall be suspended for three months. The Russian Federation Finance Ministry and the Russian Federation Economics Ministry in conjunction with the Russian Federation Committee for the Press shall elaborate within this period a mechanism for providing compensation to the print mass media for the additional expenditure incurred in implementing this decree. 2. The Russian Federation Committee for the Press in conjunction with interested ministries, departments, and organizations shall draw up within one month proposals: - on the favorable taxation of the print mass media, as well as publications of children's, teaching, and other socially important literature; - on the system of state subsidies for the print mass media. 3. The Russian Federation Ministry of Communications in conjunction with interested organizations shall prepare within one month proposals to improve the system for delivering and disseminating the print mass media. 4. The Russian Federation State Committee for the Management of State Property and the Russian Federation Committee for the Press in conjunction with interested ministries, departments, and organizations shall submit within two months to the Russian Federation Government proposals on the procedure for privatizing printing enterprises, including those forming part of publishing and printing complexes, these proposals being linked in with the participation of collectives at mass media editorial offices and book publishers in their flotation and privatization. [Signed] V. Chernomyrdin, chairman of the Russian Federation Government [Dated] Moscow, 10 January 1994
