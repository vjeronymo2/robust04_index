Final Action Accordingly, FGIS is revising Part 801 of the regulations, Tolerances for Near-Infrared Spectroscopy (NIRS) Analyzers, to incorporate by reference the AOAC International Method 992.23, Crude Protein in Cereal Grains and Oilseeds Generic Combustion Method (1992).
