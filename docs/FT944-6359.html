941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Howard aide is arrested A personal assistant to Mr Michael Howard, the home secretary, has been suspended after being arrested for allegedly breaching Scotland Yard security and taking a Special Branch car. Ms Janine Barnes is reported to have walked into the underground car park beneath Scotland Yard after a row with one of Mr Howard's bodyguards. She was not stopped when she drove an unmarked Special Branch car out of the building.
