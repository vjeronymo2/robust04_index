BFN [Text] The progress made in carrying out socioeconomic programs in Guantanamo Province was acknowledged today by a visiting ministerial committee headed by Division General Senen Casas Regueiro, who is also a member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Cuba. Casas Regueiro assessed as very positive the efforts that have been made by the workers of Guantanamo Province to eliminate as quickly as possible the consequences of the torrential rains of 1993. He also commented on the progress made in implementing guidelines left by a Politburo committee during a visit to the province two years ago, as well as the progress in reestablishing electric and telephone lines. The rebuilding of canefield roads, railway lines, fountains, and other socioeconomic works are the result of the intense work of the residents of Guantanamo Province, as the ministerial committee that visited this province today acknowledged.
