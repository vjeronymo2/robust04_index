920613 FT 13 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Enlarged Neotronics dips NEOTRONICS Technology, the measuring instrument manufacturer, reported profits of Pounds 1.14m pre-tax for the six months to March 31. The outcome, down from Pounds 1.51m at the same stage of the previous year, took in losses of Pounds 66,000 from Laser Monitoring, acquired last May, and Pounds 308,000 from Solomat Instrumentation, purchased at the beginning of this year. Exceptional charges of Pounds 106,000 related to redundancy and reorganisation costs at Solomat. Turnover of the enlarged group was Pounds 9.29m (Pounds 7.9m). Earnings per share dipped to 2.77p (4.05p) but the interim dividend goes up to 0.85p (0.8p).
