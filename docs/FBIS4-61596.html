BFN [Report by AZERTAC correspondent Dzhamilya Kuliyeva for TASS] [Text] Baku, 7 May -- It is reported by the press service of the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry that units of the Armenian armed forces, "after being on the point of halting their offensive because of the losses suffered, have again started bombarding peaceful populated localities in Azerbaijan's Terterskiy [Terter] rayon." On this same front, according to Azerbaijani military experts, one must expect a resumption of the offensive by the Armenian armed forces, since there is information of the transfer to the area of an additional military contingent, armored vehicles, and ammunition. The latest offensive by Armenia's armed forces, Azerbaijan's military department notes, has been going on since 1 May with nonstop, but unsuccessful attacks on positions in Geranboyskiy [Geranboy], Terterskiy, and Agdamskiy [Agdam] rayons.
