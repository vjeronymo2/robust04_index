930712 FT 12 JUL 93 / International Company News: Southern Union gas acquisition WESTERN Resources of the US is to sell its Missouri natural gas properties and operations to Southern Union for about Dollars 360m, Reuter reports. Southern Union will fund the purchase through a debt offering and a rights issue of common stock. The natural gas properties serve about 460,000 Missouri customers in areas including Kansas City, Joplin and St. Joseph. The deal hinges on approvals from the Missouri Public Service Commission and other regulatory agencies, Western Resources said.
