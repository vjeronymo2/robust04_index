931223 FT 23 DEC 93 / Grundig repays subsidies Grundig-Austria has repaid Sch67m (Pounds 3.7m) of subsidies totalling Sch100m from the city of Vienna, following a European Union decision to withdraw tariff concessions on imports from the Austrian company, writes Patrick Blum in Vienna. The EU decision also affects General Motors' Austrian plant, which faces punitive tariffs on EU imports of cylinder heads and gearboxes.
