BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, June 23. (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The secretary of the National Security Council of Mongolia J. Enhsaihan was on an official visit to the Russian Federation in June 19-23 at the invitation of his Russian colleague O.I. Lobov. During meetings and talks they have exchanged views on many aspects of Mongolian-Russian mutual relations. In view of J. Enhsaihan and O.I. Lobov priority importance in the Mongolian-Russian mutual relations should be attached to the issues of regional and international security, strengthening nuclear free status of Mongolia, economic security, struggle with criminality, international terrorism, illegal transit transportation of arms, narcotics. In the near future a visit of the secretary of the National Security Council of the Russian Federation to Mongolia is expected, as a result of which a document on cooperation in the national security sphere will be signed, informs today the press service of the Ministry for External Relations of Mongolia.
