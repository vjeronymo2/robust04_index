Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, March 1. (SNARK) -- Armenian Liberal Democratic Party called for faster ratification of agreement on legal status of Russian troops in Armenia, signed two years ago. Parliamentary representative of this party Petros Katsakhyan reminded the deputies that the Supreme Council of Russian Federation has already ratified this document exactly a year ago. According to the information made known by the Armenian financial minister Levon Barkhudaryan, nearly 8 billion rubles were allocated in the republican state budget to support the Russian armed contingent.
