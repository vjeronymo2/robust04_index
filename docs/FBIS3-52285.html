Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 4 Jan (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa held a meeting today with Chief of the PLO Political Department Faruq Qaddumi, Palestinian Ambassador in Cairo Sa'id Kamal, and Fatah Movement representative in Cairo Zuhdi al-Qidrah. The conferees discussed developments in the Palestinian issue and obstacles delaying the implementation of Gaza-Jericho Accord. They also assessed the situation in light of the Israeli stance on implementing the accord and the Palestinian position.
