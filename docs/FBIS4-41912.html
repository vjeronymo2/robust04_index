BFN [Text] Kiev -- The Central Electoral Commission on 28 June summed up the outcome of the presidential election in Ukraine. As the result of the ballot in 27 constituencies, Leonid Kravchuk secured 37.7 percent of the vote or 9,944,740 votes; Leonid Kuchma 31.27 percent or 8,244,840; Oleksandr Moroz 13 percent or 3,437,810; Volodymyr Lanovyy 9.3 percent or 2,455,830; Valeriy Babych 2.4 percent or 630,392; Ivan Plyushch 1.3 percent or 341,720, and Petro Talanchuk .5 percent or 142,996. Thus, it is Leonid Kravchuk and Leonid Kuchma who are through to the second round of the election.
