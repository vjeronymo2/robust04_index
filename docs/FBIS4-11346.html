BFN [Text] Tegucigalpa, 22 Apr (NOTIMEX) -- Fuel prices in Honduras changed twice in less than 12 hours, official sources have reported. Texaco changed its prices on the morning of 22 April, but in the afternoon it incorporated the changes that had been decided by the three other fuel distributors. Texaco had set the price of premium gasoline at 10.19 lempiras ($1.40), regular gasoline at 9.57 lempiras ($1.31), and diesel fuel at 7.27 lempiras ($1) a gallon. Nevertheless, the local enterprise DIPPSA [Petroleum Products Distributor Corporation] and the multinational companies Esso and Shell decided to change their prices effective Saturday.
