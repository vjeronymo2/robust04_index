July 21, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

MGM/UA Distributes Shareholder Payment: MGM/UA said it made the $4-per-share distribution to shareholders in connection with Pathe Communications Corp.'s proposed $1.3-billion acquisition of the movie studio.

Big beneficiaries included Kirk Kerkorian, who pocketed $180 million from his 45 million common and preferred MGM/UA shares. MGM/UA shareholders are to receive $21.50 a share. MGM/UA said remaining $17.50 per share is expected to be paid on or before Oct. 23, 1990. Pathe's purchase of MGM/UA is far from a done deal, however. Pathe President Giancarlo Parretti must still come up with $650 million in financing that evaporated when Time Warner Inc. pulled out of the deal earlier this month.
