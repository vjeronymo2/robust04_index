BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] In the General Procuracy in Moscow today, an expanded meeting of the collegium is taking place. It is discussing measures aimed at intensifying the fight against crime. Those taking part in the collegium sitting are the procurators of republics, krays and oblasts, heads of all the federal law enforcement bodies, security councils of Russia, senior staff of the president and the government, representatives of both houses of the Federal Assmebly, and law experts. As ITAR-TASS learned from competent sources, the General Procuracy will also try to stir the participants to seek compromise regarding the enforcement of the president's edict of 14 June, until the legislative bodies adopt necessary laws.
