July 3, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

U.S. ADMINISTRATION SUSPENDS DIALOGUE WITH ARAFAT'S PLO

A dialogue that achieved nothing was suspended because of a military raid that achieved nothing. It is amazing how non-events make the news these days.

The suspension did show U.S. double standards (non-killing of Israelis was "punished" while actual killing of Palestinians is constantly financed).

KHALID BAIG

Van Nuys
