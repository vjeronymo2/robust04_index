Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 5 Mar (XINHUA) -- PRC Presidential Decree No. 20 The "Law on the Protection of Investments of Taiwan Compatriots in the PRC," which has been passed by the Sixth Meeting of the Eighth National People's Congress Standing Committee on 5 March, is hereby promulgated and enters into force upon promulgation. [Signed] PRC President Jiang Zemin [Dated] 5 March 1994
