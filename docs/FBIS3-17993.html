Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] The press center of the Georgian Defense Ministry reports that military actions continued in the Lata Gorge last night. Using heavy artillery, the enemy opened fire on the positions of Georgian fighters and border troops. The enemy, who receives reinforcements of manpower and combat equipment every day, is attacking the gorge with a 2,000-strong Army. Despite numerous attempts by the Abkhaz separatists and the mercenaries, Georgians are retaining their positions. The evacuation of the civilian population will continue today. A representative of the press center also told us that the situation remains stable in Zugdidi and that no combat actions have taken place there.
