Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow March 18 TASS -- 32 people were killed when an Iranian C-130 transport plane crashed three kilometres north of Stepanakert, the capital of Nagorno-Karabakh, overnight. The plane took off from the Moscow International Sheremetyevo-2 Airport at 18.46 Moscow time /15.46 GMT/ bound for Tehran. There were 19 family members of the employees of the Iranian Embassy in Moscow and 13 crew members, TASS learned on Friday from the Ministry for Emergency Situations. The causes of the crash are being investigated.
