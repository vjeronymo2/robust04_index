940615 FT 15 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Amberley Amberley Group, the USM-quoted building services concern, has acquired Metacol, a supplier of liquid colourants for plastic processors, for up to Pounds 5.5m. In order to fund the acquisition an underwritten rights issue is proposed to raise Pounds 4.38m cash for the vendors and Pounds 1.94m to provide Pounds 1.1m for repaying Metacol's debts with the remainder for expenses and working capital. The issue, of 13.5m new ordinary shares, is on a 1-for-2 basis at 47p. Following a turnround at the interim stage, the group announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 403,000 for the year to March 31 against losses of Pounds 91,000.
