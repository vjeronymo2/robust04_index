920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / Iran sets a Dollars 19.8bn oil target Iran's parliament set a target yesterday of Dollars 19.8bn for oil and gas revenues for the year from next March 21, Tehran Radio said, Reuter reports from Nicosia. In a budget debate the parliament also authorised the sale of 279,000 barrels per day (bpd) of crude to finance the import of refined products.
