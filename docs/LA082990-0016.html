August 29, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Diane Directs: Academy Award-winning actress Diane Keaton will make her debut as a prime-time television director with an upcoming episode of "China Beach," it was announced Tuesday. The episode, "Fever," is about nurse Colleen McMurphy's (Dana Delany) life back home in the States upon her return from Vietnam. Production begins Sept. 5. BETH KLEID
