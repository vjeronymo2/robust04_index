Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 24 Jan (INA)--Iraq has again notified the Arab League of the continued provision of facilities by the rulers of Kuwait and Saudi Arabia for the U.S. British, and French aircraft that violate Iraqi airspace and threaten its security, sovereignty, and national unity. In a letter, Iraq's permanent representative to the Arab League said that U.S., British, and French aircraft continued to violate Iraqi airspace on 17, 18, 19, and 20 January. After taking off from bases in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait, the aircraft carried out 202 hostile air sorties. A list detailing these violations has been attached to the letter.
