Language: Dutch Article Type:BFN [Report signed "G.T.": "Senator Nelly Maes Confirms Massive Arms Export"] [Excerpts] Brussels, 30 Dec -- "The data published yesterday by DE MORGEN are correct," according to Nelly Maes, VU [People's Union] senator. For years she has been quietly battling weapons exports. After comparing DE MORGEN's articles with her own records based on numerous parliamentary questions, Senator Maes claims that the data perfectly match her information. [passage omitted] In her opinion, some blatant samples of hypocrisy are the delivery of mortars to Zaire on 4 December 1991, the delivery of guns and munition to Angola on 3 April 1991, or the export of 808 crates of kalashnikovs to Saudi Arabia on 19 July 1991.
