930428 FT 28 APR 93 / UK Company News: How tumbles Pounds 2.3m into the red How Group, the building services contractor, swung from pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.3m to losses of Pounds 2.28m in the year to December 31 1992. Turnover fell to Pounds 136m against Pounds 224m. Losses on discontinued operations amounted to Pounds 3.61m but profits on continuing operations improved 33 per cent to Pounds 1.16m. Losses per share came through at 3.96p (1.82p earnings) and the proposed final dividend is halved to 0.75p for a total of 1.5p (2.5p).
