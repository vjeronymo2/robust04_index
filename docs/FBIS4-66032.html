BFN [Text] New Democracy Chairman Miltiadhis Evert today held a news conference for the foreign and Greek press. During the news conference, he discussed our national issues, with particular emphasis on the increasing idealism of countries such as Albania. He also stressed the possible dangers that could result if the persecution against the Greek minority of Albania continues. Evert also referred to Turkey's aggressive role in the region. Finally, he accused the government of inaction on economic affairs.
