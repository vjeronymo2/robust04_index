January 21, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

CS FULLERTON SEEKS ANOTHER BIG WEST WIN

Cal State Fullerton, which snapped a 7-game losing streak Thursday night with an 82-60 Big West victory over San Jose State, plays host to Utah State in another conference game tonight at 7:30 at Titan Gym.

The Titans (6-8 overall, 1-4 in the Big West), are led by forward Cedric Ceballos, who scored 23 points and had a career-high 20 rebounds against San Jose State.

Utah State, which won the conference tournament last season and was one of the favorites -- along with Nevada Las Vegas -- to win the conference title this season, was routed by the Rebels, 102-80, Thursday night in Las Vegas. The Aggies (6-10, 4-3) are led by guard Reid Newey, who scored 22 points against Las Vegas.
