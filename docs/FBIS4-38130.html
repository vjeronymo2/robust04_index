BFN [Text] Al-Basrah, 26 Jun (INA)--The Greek ship Archimedes docked at the commercial jetty of the grain silo of Umm Qasr Port this evening, loaded with large quantities of Australian wheat imported by the Ministry of Trade. A correspondent of the IRAQI NEWS AGENCY in al-Basrah said that workers of the state enterprise for grain trading began the mechanical unloading of the ship as soon as it docked at the port, while scores of trucks owned by the State Enterprise for Land Transportation lined up at the jetty to transport the wheat to the silos of the governorates.
