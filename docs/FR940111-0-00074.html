In addition to the relatively low level of overall industry Rule compliance, the rulemaking record demonstrates that funeral providers have not provided the initial stimulus for ``increased competition'' on the basis of price considered necessary by the Commission. The few funeral home entrants that aggressively compete on price, documented in the record, appear to be former, more traditional funeral directors who reentered the market as ``discount'' providers, and who are considered to be ``mavericks'' by the funeral industry as a whole.
