BFN [Text] Cairo, 17 Apr (MENA)--Mustafa Najib, chairman of the board of directors and chief editor of MENA, has denied a report published in a recent issue of the magazine ROSE AL-YUSUF that the agency is preparing to establish an office in Tel Aviv. He affirmed that the agency has not sent any delegation to Tel Aviv for this purpose. He said the agency is considering establishing a presence in Gaza and Jericho in coordination with the PALESTINIAN NEWS AGENCY (WAFA) after Palestinian self-rule is established there.
