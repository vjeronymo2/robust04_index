Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] At the request of the Nagorno-Karabakh authorities, the Armenian Foreign Ministry is now handling the issues related to the internationalization of the Karabakh conflict and is preparing corresponding messages to the UN Security Council chairman and the head of the CSCE Minsk group. This was stated in a document of the Armenian Foreign Ministry's department for public relations and press released in Yerevan on Monday. The document says the reasons are the instances of Afghan mojahedeen involvement in the conflict on the side of Azerbaijan, the presence of a large quantity of Chinese-made arms in the Azerbaijani Army and British press reports, in particular, The Independent newspaper, about the possibility of British military aid to Azerbaijan. At the same time, according to the Armenian Foreign Ministry, the above mentioned facts have not yet been officially confirmed. During diplomatic consultations the British, Afghan and Chinese sides denied information about any involvement in the Karabakh conflict.
