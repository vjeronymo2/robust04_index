941221 FT 21 DEC 94 / Germany pledges to support UN Germany pledged yesterday to support Nato if UN peacekeepers have to quit Bosnia, Agencies report from Bonn. Mr Friedrich Bohl, chancellery minister, said Bonn had agreed to take part in any evacuation, but gave no details. Officials had earlier said that Chancellor Helmut Kohl's cabinet had agreed to offer Tornado fighter bombers, medical personnel, mine-sweepers and high-speed patrol boats in what, if approved by parliament, would be Germany's first foreign combat mission since the second world war. Meanwhile, German aid flights to Sarajevo, the besieged Bosnian capital, are to resume today.
