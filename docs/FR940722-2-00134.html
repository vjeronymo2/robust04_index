BOARD OF GOVERNORS OF THE FEDERAL RESERVE SYSTEM Time and Date: 10:00 a.m., Wednesday, July 27, 1994. Place: Marriner S. Eccles Federal Reserve Board Building, C Street entrance between 20th and 2lst Streets, N.W., Washington, D.C. 20551. Status: Open. Matters to be Considered: 1. (1) Proposed amendments to Regulation Y (Bank Holding Companies and Change in Bank Control) to permit discounts on traditional bank products and brokerage services for customers obtaining traditional bank products from affiliates (proposed earlier for public comment; Docket No. R&hyph;0832); and (2) publication for comment of a proposed amendment to Regulation Y to remove its restrictions on tying between nonbank subsidiaries when the packaged products are separately available. 2. Proposed 1995 Federal Reserve Bank budget objective. 3. Any items carried forward from a previously announced meeting. Note: This meeting will be recorded for the benefit of those unable to attend. Cassettes will be available for listening in the Board's Freedom of Information Office, and copies may be ordered for $5 per cassette by calling (202) 452&hyph;3684 or by writing to: Freedom of Information Office, Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, Washington, D.C. 20551.
