BFN [Text] The first constituent congress of the renewed Communist Party of Georgia was held on Saturday in Tbilisi. While opening the congress, retired general Panteleimon Georgadze, leader of the party, declared that the aims of the communists "remain unchanged -- creation of a free equal society and protection of the interests of the working people. We want to return achievements of socialism," he stressed. While addressing the congress, Filipp Makharadze, veteran of Georgia' Communist Party and former director of Lenin's museum in Georgia, died just on the rostrum. However, this tragic incident has not interrupted the congress. A representative of the Armenian communists who attended the congress declared that it "is a death worthy of a communist."
