940421 FT 21 APR 94 / UK Company News: Chloride Group CHLORIDE GROUP has bought the Ondyne companies for a maximum of Pounds 2.03m. The power supplies in which Ondyne specialises will help Chloride expand in the office equipment market. The consideration will be satisfied by an immediate Pounds 500,000 cash and three further profit-related payments, all to be paid out of Chloride's existing resources.
