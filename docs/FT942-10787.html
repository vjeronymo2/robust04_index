940509 FT 09 MAY 94 / Observer: Starck solution Moving offices from one side of town to the other? Philippe Starck, the superstar French industrial designer, has solved what can be a traditional corporate headache with some style. Having transferred his headquarters to Issy Les Moulineaux on the outskirts of Paris, he compensated his employees for any upheaval in their travel arrangements by giving each of them a shiny silver mobylette, or moped, so they can whizz through the traffic jams to get there.
