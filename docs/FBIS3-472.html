Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] One-hundred-seventeen members of the National Peacekeeping Force receiving training at the General De Wet training center outside Bloemfontein have resigned since the start of the weekend. A spokesman for the force said that reasons for the resignations varied from domestic problems to dissatisfaction with conditions at the base. About 70 members of the force resigned last week after a strike over salaries. However, the spokesman said that conditions at the base had returned to normal and that training had been resumed. He said a delegation from the Transitional Executive Council would visit the base this afternoon to help solve the problem.
