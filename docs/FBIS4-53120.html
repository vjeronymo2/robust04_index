BFN [Text] A ranking military official has confirmed the build-up of New People's Army [NPA] forces in Northern Negros through its "Balik-Masa [Return to Mass]" program. However, Lt. Col. Victor Ibrado, 11th Infantry Battalion commander, said the armed NPA Front Command 4, is still "temporarily dissolved." There are sightings of five to seven armed men but are avoiding soldiers, Ibrado said. [sentence as published] The NPA Front Command 4 was divided into several groups to give priority in recruitment land recovery of lost territories, Ibrado said. [sentence as published] NPA-initiated offensives have been reduced since early this year.
