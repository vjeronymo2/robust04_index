BFN [Text] Indonesia deeply regrets a plan for a conference on East Timor in Manila, the Philippines, to be held from 31 May to 2 June. The director for foreign information, Irawan Abidin, stressed that Indonesia is not pleased with the plan in view of good Indonesian-Philippine ties, particularly within the context of ASEAN. Local newspapers have reported that a conference on East Timor sponsored by a number of non-governmental organizations will be held at the state-run University of the Philippines.
