December 2, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NEW COLUMNISTS DRAW PRAISE FROM NEAR AND FAR

How marvelous to read Melvin -- formerly Mel -- Durslag in the Los Angeles Times. The more formal name can't presage a more elegant or sophisticated view of the sporting scene since he long ago scaled the heights in both those categories.

Congratulations and thanks for acquiring his unique talents.

E.C. BRESSLER, Los Angeles
