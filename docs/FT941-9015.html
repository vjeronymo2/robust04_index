940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / Bavarian right-winger resigns A leading German opponent of European union, Bavarian environment minister Mr Peter Gauweiler, resigned yesterday over a financial scandal, Reuter reports from Bonn. His Christian Social Union party was embarrassed by revelations that the populist Mr Gauweiler,44, had earned DM10,000 (Pounds 3,891) a month on top of his political salaries by 'leasing' clients from his law firm to other attorneys.
