BFN [Report by Aleksandr Pilat under the "In Brief" rubric: "The Grivna To Replace the Karbovanets"] [Text] NEZAVISIMAYA GAZETA has learned from sources close to Ukraine government circles that a new monetary unit -- the grivna -- is being introduced on all the territory of Ukraine as of 25 (or possibly 26) May to replace the Ukrainian karbovanets-coupons. It is assumed that the rate of exchange is 100 karbovanets for one grivna. The dollar rate has shot up at Ukraine's exchange offices. Whereas one dollar could be bought for 47,000 karbovanets on 23 May, it cost more than 60,000 karbovanets and between 66,000 and 70,000 in Kiev, Lvov, and Crimea on 24 May.
