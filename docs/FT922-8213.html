920514 FT 14 MAY 92 / UK Company News: James Wilkes sells offshoot for Pounds 1.17m James Wilkes, the Sheffield-based specialist engineer, has completed the sale of the business and certain assets of its Knightway Air Charter offshoot to Lambson Group for Pounds 1.17m cash. After repaying hire purchase debts of Pounds 580,000 on the company's Cessna aircraft, the balance will be used to reduce group borrowings.
