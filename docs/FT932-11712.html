930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Fairbriar suffers Pounds 8.37m deficit for 9 months FAIRBRIAR, the property development and housebuilding company, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 8.37m for the nine months to December 31, against Pounds 13.4m for the previous 12 months. During the period the company was in administration and the figures include exceptional costs of Pounds 1.48m (Pounds 1.29m) relating to professional fees and reconstruction, which was agreed in January this year. There was only limited trading, mainly concerning residential housing. Turnover was Pounds 5.08m (Pounds 8.52m). Interest costs were Pounds 7.21m (Pounds 13.2m). Losses per share worked through at 21.93p (35.26p). The company said that in line with the general improvement in the residential market it had seen an upturn in sales in the first quarter. Fairbriar is concentrating on the residential side of its business.
