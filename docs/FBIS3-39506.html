Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Berlin (ADN-BLN) [Berlin regional office] -- The Media Council today issued three more FM frequencies in Berlin and Brandenburg. The stations in question are Radio Concept, Radio 50 Plus, and Sender Freies Berlin (SFB), which intends to broadcast a schedule for foreign minorities living in Berlin. Radio Concept was allocated the frequency 98.2 MHz, which is at present being used by Deutschlandradio Cologne. The Media Council issued the station's organizers with the broadcasting permit for a "speech-oriented station with a cultural focus." The station Radio 50 plus, which is aimed largely at older listeners, will be available on 105.5 MHz. SFB was granted the frequency 106.8 MHz for its new station.
