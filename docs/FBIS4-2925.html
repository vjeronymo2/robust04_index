BFN [Text] Santiago, April 13 (XINHUA) -- Chilean President Eduardo Frei met here today with visiting Chinese Defense Minister Chi Haotian to discuss the promotion of relations between their two countries. Speaking at the meeting, President Frei said that Chile admires very much the great efforts of the Chinese people to develop their country and expressed the desire to strengthen the relations between Chile and China. The common aim of Chile and China is to promote the development of each country, and Chile wants to learn from China its experience in economic development, Frei said. Speaking on the same occasion, the Chinese Defense Minister expressed his wish to see the greatest accomplishments in Chile's national construction and the success of his current visit in promoting the relations of friendship and cooperation between the two countries and the two armed forces. Chi arrived here Tuesday night for a four-day official visit to Chile, the last leg of his current three-nation south America tour which has already taken him to Brazil and Uruguay. Chi is the first Chinese defense minister to visit South America since the founding of the People's Republic of China in 1949.
