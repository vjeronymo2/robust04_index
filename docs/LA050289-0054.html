May 2, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Showtime Networks Inc. has joined with Sylvester Stallone's White Eagle Enterprises Inc. and Carolco Pictures Inc. for pay television rights to 10 upcoming feature films from White Eagle. Five of the productions will be action-adventure films starring Stallone. The other five movies Stallone will produce for White Eagle. The first film, tentatively titled "Lock Up" is a prison action-drama centering around an inmate (Stallone) who rebels against a tyrannical jail warden. Claudia Puig
