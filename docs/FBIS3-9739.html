Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, March 27 -- The centre-right groupings are concerned over "Russian Defence Minister, General Pavel Grachev's statement about creating military fortified region around Kaliningrad and with a lack of reaction of Waldemar Pawlak's government to it," reads communique forwarded to PAP Sunday. The centre-right groupings appealed to the government to "take up, on the international forum, relevant activities leading to demilitarization of Kaliningrad and to ensure security to Baltic states." Grachev announced the creation of a "special defence region" around Kaliningrad on March 20, during his stay in the city.
