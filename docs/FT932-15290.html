930414 FT 14 APR 93 / UK Company News: ICI completes Canadian disposal Imperial Chemical Industries has completed the disposal of its Canadian-based nitrogen products business to Terra Industries, of Sioux City, Iowa, for CDollars 68m (Pounds 35.6m) cash. The sale included ICI's anhydrous ammonia plant and related upgrading facilities in Sarnia, Ontario, and its interest in 32 'agromart' farm service centres in Ontario, New Brunswick and Nova Scotia. Combined sales from ICI's Canadian nitrogen products and 'agromart' operations totalled about CDollars 250m in 1992.
