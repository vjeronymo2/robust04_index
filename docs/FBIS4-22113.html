BFN [Text] Baku, April 19, (TURAN) -- Azerbaijan's security agencies have prevented a new tragedy on Azeri railway from taking place. TURAN was told that this most recent attempted act of terrorism was planned for today in wagon number 5 along the Baku-Moscow route. During an inspection of wagons and passengers' belongings security employees found a cardbord box with a large timing devise. The timing devise weighed 13 kilos and consisted of 65 pieces of dynamite placed in soap weighing 200 grammes each. The box belonged to five Georgian citizens. Presently they are being detained by Azeri security bodies.
