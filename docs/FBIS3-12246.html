Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Brother Abu Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, has had a telephone conversation late this evening with the Egyptian President Husni Mubarak, during which they examined in length the U.S. President's invitation to the Palestinian and the Israeli sides to hold urgent talks in Washington. They also discussed the current emergency meeting of the UN Security Council to examine the Israeli horrible massacre carried out by Israeli settlers against Palestinian worshippers in the Ibrahimi shrine, and the need to take a decision on the provision of international protection to the Palestinian people as well as for the armed settlers.
