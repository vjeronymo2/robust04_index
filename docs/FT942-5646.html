940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / Observer: Young buffer Who says that the Swedes don't have a sense of humour? Lars-Olof Odlund, an executive VP with Swedbank, was so chuffed when his bank won the Swedish State Railways bank account that he felt it was worthy of announcing to the London Stock Exchange. The banker said it confirmed that Swedbank is 'moving in the right direction. Or perhaps I should say on the right track . . .' Ho, ho, Lars.
