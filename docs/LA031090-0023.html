March 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FANS IN FIGHTING MOOD ABOUT KINGS-OILERS BRAWL

So, owner Bruce McNall doesn't think that any fan who was at the Forum on Feb. 28 was disappointed. I hate to break it to you Bruce, but as one who came to see a hockey game and saw a boxing match instead, I was disappointed. I didn't pay thousands of dollars for my season seats to see the game I love deteriorate into a street brawl.

MICHELLE BUEHLER, Los Angeles
