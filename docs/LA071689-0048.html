July 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

LOOKING FOR A FEW GOOD HANDYMEN

Have you got a skilled, reliable handyman whom you'd be willing to share with our readers? Or tips on how to find a good one? Or advice on how to avoid hiring a bad one? We'd love to hear from you.

Please send your ideas, along with your name, address and telephone number, to Real Estate Editor, Los Angeles Times, Times Mirror Square, Los Angeles, Calif. 90053.
