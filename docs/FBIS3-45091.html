Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 12 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met here this afternoon with Mr. Chong Chun-mong, member of the National Assembly of the Republic of Korea, and they exchanged views on issues of common interest. The five-member delegation headed by Mr. Chong arrived here on January 9 at the invitation of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade (CCPIT).
