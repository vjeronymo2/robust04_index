December 2, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

I watched what I thought was the season premiere of "Wiseguy."

What I saw was a slow-moving, grossly mutated version of "Miami Vice." From the deaths of Sonny and Father Pete to the McPike/moose face-off in the Volchek family crypt, the series had never been dull. Until now. We don't need another "Miami Vice." What we need is Ken Wahl as Vincenzo Michael Terranova.

Anthony Alborini, Valencia
