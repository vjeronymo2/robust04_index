Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] A heavily attended and militant ceremony of solidarity with Cuba, organized by the executive secretariat of AALAPSO [Afro-Asian-Latin America People's Solidarity Organization], was held at the international press center's Havana location. Support for the Cuban Revolution was expressed by numerous representatives of national liberation movements, union and cultural organizations, and participants in the Fourth Latin American and Caribbean Meeting that begins tomorrow at the Convention Center. AALAPSO Secretary General Rene Anillo Capote gave the first of the speeches at this ceremony for solidarity with Cuba, which was presided over by Jaime Crombet, vice president of the National Assembly of the People's Government. This open forum for solidarity with Cuba denounced U.S. imperialism's unchanging and systematically aggressive policy against our country, and the criminal economic and financial blockade.
