Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A leading member of the Azanian People's Liberation Army [Azapo], Mr. Letsatsi Mosala, has been shot dead by unknown gunmen in Soweto, near Johannesburg. Mr. Mosala, a member of Azapo's strategy committee and a former national organizer for the organization, was shot at point blank range while driving through the Rockville area of Soweto last night. The gunmen escaped in his car.
