Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Skopje, 13 Jan (Tanjug) -- A delegation of the Japanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, led by Shunji Yanai, director general of the Foreign Policy Bureau, is visiting Skopje today. According to a statement by the UN Protection Forces [UNPROFOR] Command for Macedonia, the Japanese delegation will meet with representatives of the Macedonian Government and will also visit UNPROFOR members at an observation point near Tabanovci. The Japanese delegation is visiting Macedonia as part of its fact-finding tour of the former Yugoslav republics, on the basis of which it will decide on possible financial investments.
