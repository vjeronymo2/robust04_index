920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / Survey of Software at Work (16): Travellog Systems - Supplier Profile Travellog Systems was formed in 1983 and concentrates solely on reservation systems for tour operators. It claims to be the market leader in systems for ferry and motoring holidays. Customers: Its 25 customers include the AA, French Life Holidays, the Caravan Club, plus Sally Ferries, P&O Scottish Ferries and Eurotunnel, which has just signed for a system to handle its bookings. Status: Travellog became a private limited company two years ago. Financial Statistics: Turnover: Pounds 1.35m in 1991-92; Pounds 809,000 in 1990-91. Net profit: Pounds 81,000 in 1991-92; Pounds 34,000 in 1990-91. Staff: 34 staff members spread between head office in Haywards Heath, Sussex, and the sales office in Borehamwood, Hertfordshire.
