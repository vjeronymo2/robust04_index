940817 FT 17 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Green Property Green Property, the Dublin-based investment group, is buying Wanze Properties (Ireland) from Merchants Investor Assurance Company. The purchase consideration is IPounds 9.9m (Pounds 9.7m) and will be paid by way of the discharge of borrowings by Wanze to Merchants Investor. It will be met from Green's internal resources. Wanze - a subsidiary of Cornhill Insurance - owns four shopping centres in Ireland with a current combined rental income of about IPounds 1.3m.
