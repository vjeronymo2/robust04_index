BFN [Text] We have just received the following report: Fire was opened on an aircraft carrying General Bertrand de Lapresle, commander for the area of the former Yugoslavia as it was landing at Sarajevo airport, Richard Pernod, UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] spokesman in Sarajevo has said. Fire was opened from infantry weapons at the west end of the runway near a zone controlled by forces of the Bosnian Serbs, Pernod said and added that one of the wings had been hit. The Blue Helmets at the airport were immediately placed on high alert. They are expected to return soon to their regular level of readiness, the officer explained.
