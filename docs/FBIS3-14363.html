Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo--Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak today received Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' in the presence of Egyptian Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa. During the meeting, al-Shar' conveyed to President Mubarak greetings from President Hafiz al-Asad. The Egyptian president asked al-Shar' to convey his warm greetings to President al-Asad. Talks dealt with the regional situation, the peace process, and the current Arab League Council meetings.
