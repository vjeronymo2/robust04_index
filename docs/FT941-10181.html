940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / International Company News: Chairman of Air India resigns The chairman of state-run Air India, Mr Yogesh Chandra Deveshwar, has resigned, Reuter reports from Bombay. The civil aviation and tourism ministry said Captain Durga Mathur would take over as acting chairman. Mr Deveshwar, who took charge in November 1991, is returning to his former company, private-sector tobacco company ITC. Last month, the government issued an ordinance making the airline and state-run Indian Airlines, which runs domestic flights, public limited companies to enable them to tap equity markets.
