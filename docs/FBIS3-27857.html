Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 31 KYODO -- The Labor Ministry said Monday [31 January] that 12 manufacturing industries, including bag makers, will be eligible for the government's employment adjustment subsidy for one year from Tuesday. Under the existing system, employers will be provided with a subsidy to pay a portion of salaries and allowances to employees who are temporarily laid off because of the economic slowdown. The ministry said the 12 industries to be added to the list include offshore oil drilling, felt manufacturing, polyurethane making and processing, refrigerating and air conditioning regulators manufacturing and electric welding machine manufacturing. Seventeen other industries, including piano and its accessories manufacturing, were also re-designated as eligible, the ministry said. It said the list will cover 4,856,000 workers in a total of 233 categories of manufacturers.
