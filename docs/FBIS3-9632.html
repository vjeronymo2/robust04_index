Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 25 Mar (TANJUG) -- Vladimir Lukic, prime minister of the Srpska republic, this evening tendered his resignation to the post of prime minister. At the 39th session of the Srpska republic assembly, which was held behind closed doors this afternoon, Lukic left Radovan Karadzic, president of the Srpska republic, to decide about his mandate. His resignation followed a report on and an assembly discussion of the work of ministries. The assembly ended its two-day session this evening.
