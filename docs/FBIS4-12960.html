BFN [Text] In a major success in antiterrorist operations, the acting chief of the so-called Khalistan Commando Force, Punjab Singh, and an accomplice were killed this morning in an encounter with the security forces in Punjab's Tarn Taran District. He is responsible for more than 100 killings, including two bus massacres. Sixteen AK-47 rifles, one machine gun and one wireless set were recovered from the truck in which the militants were traveling.
