February 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FANS PUT TYSON, KING DOWN FOR THE COUNT

Invoking the "Don King Clause" was not the most embarrassing moment in the aftermath of the Tyson-Douglas fight. Seeing the Japanese judges' cards clearly was.

I'll make a deal with the Japanese: I won't eat your sushi, you don't judge heavyweight championships.

RICH BORDEN

Moorpark
