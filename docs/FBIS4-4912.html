BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 5 (KCNA) -- A delegation of Korean social scientists headed by Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and chairman of the Korean Social Scientists Association, left Pyongyang today to participate in the international seminar of the chuche idea on independence, peace and friendship scheduled in India. It was seen off at the airport by Kim Yang-kon, vice-director of a department of the WPK Central Committee, and Shehkholen Kipgen, Indian ambassador to Korea.
