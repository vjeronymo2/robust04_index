921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / Parliament and Politics: Customs to advise on tax avoidance HM CUSTOMS & EXCISE is to issue guidelines on what constitutes 'unacceptable tax avoidance', Sir Brian Unwin, chairman, told the public accounts committee yesterday. It has been delayed by the difficulty in distinguishing avoidance from legitimate 'tax mitigation', he said. Customs defined mitigation as attempts to reduce the tax burden within the spirit of the law. Avoidance is not, however, and may be challenged by officials.
