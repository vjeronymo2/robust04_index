920728 FT 28 JUL 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was more general demand, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Landed brightest liquoring and good medium East Africans well supported at dearer rates with lesser mediums also strong at 3-5p dearer. Plain descriptions and most Central African teas met limited interest while Ceylons attracted fair competition. Offshore Kenya cif teas were mainly firm, Central Africans met limited support. Fob teas from Mauritius remained about steady at last rates. The highest price this week was 161p for one Kenya and two Rwanda pf. 1. Quotations: quality 160p/kg, good medium 135p/kg, medium 115p/kg, low medium 83p/kg.
