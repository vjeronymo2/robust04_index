930223 FT 23 FEB 93 / Ansells announces Pounds 25m pub scheme ANSELLS, the pub owner which is a subsidiary of food and drinks group Allied-Lyons, is to invest Pounds 25m on the improvement of 100 pubs in the Midlands, west England and Wales, which could create up to 700 jobs. It is part of a national Pounds 65m pub improvement programme spread over three years.
