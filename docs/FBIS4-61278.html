BFN [Text] Another meeting of the bilateral expert group from the Russian and US defense departments began in Moscow on Wednesday. A spokesman from the Defense Ministry's information and press department has told Interfax that for three days experts will discuss issues associated with European security, the situation in Bosnia, and problems on Russia's joining the Partnership for Peace program. The two sides also intend to exchange views on nuclear safety cooperation and the reduction and further elimination of wapons of mass destruction.
