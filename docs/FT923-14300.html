920708 FT 08 JUL 92 / Parliament and Politics: Row over health funding settled MINISTERS have settled a row that was threatening to delay the switch to local authorities of responsibility for the government's care in the community policies for the elderly and mentally handicapped. In what is seen as a defeat for the Treasury, health secretary Mrs Virginia Bottomley is also close to winning her battle to ensure that money transferred to local authorities from the social security budget to fund the new system will be 'ring-fenced'.
