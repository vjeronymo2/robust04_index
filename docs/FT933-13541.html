930719 FT 19 JUL 93 / National Savings contribute Pounds 220m NATIONAL Savings contributed Pounds 220m to government funding in June, although Pounds 195m of this was due to accrued interest. Net sales were just Pounds 25m, with gross sales of Pounds 604m offset by repayments of Pounds 579m. Index-linked savings certificates made the highest contribution, with Pounds 86m, followed by Capital Bonds at Pounds 32m. The total amount invested in National Savings at the end of June was Pounds 44.8bn.
