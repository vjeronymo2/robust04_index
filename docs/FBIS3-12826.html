Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 6 Mar (MENA)--Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat arrived in Cairo this evening. He is due to hold talks with President Muhammad Husni Mubarak and make a complete assessment of the peace process and the latest developments following the Ibrahimi Mosque massacre. Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa received 'Arafat upon his arrival.
