930120 FT 20 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Rise in net assets at First Philippine Net asset value for the First Philippine Investment Trust increased from 39.1p to 65.44p over the year to October 31. Net revenue for the 12 months to the end of October was Pounds 119,500 (Pounds 266,000) for earnings per share of 0.24p (0.53p). A single final dividend of 0.2p (0.95p) is proposed.
