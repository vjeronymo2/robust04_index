Antitrust Division Notice Pursuant to the National Cooperative Research and Production Act of 1993_Petroleum Environmental Research Forum Project 92&hyph;16 Notice is hereby given that, on March 22, 1994, pursuant to section 6(a) of the National Cooperative Research and Production Act of 1993, 15 U.S.C. 4301 et seq. (``the Act''), the participants in the Petroleum Environmental Research Forum (``PERF'') Project 92&hyph;16 have filed written notifications simultaneously with the Attorney General and the Federal Trade Commission disclosing a change in its membership. The notifications were filed for the purpose of extending the protections of the Act limiting the recovery of antitrust plaintiffs to actual damages under specified circumstances. Specifically, Arco Exploration and Production Technology Company, Plano, TX, has become a member of PERF Project 92&hyph;16. No other changes have been made in either the membership or planned activity of PERF Project 92&hyph;16. Membership in this group research project remains open, and PERF Project 92&hyph;16 intends to file additional written notification disclosing all changes in membership. On August 13, 1993, the participants in PERF Project 92&hyph;16 filed its original notification pursuant to section 6(a) of the Act. The Department of Justice published a notice in the Federal Register pursuant to section 6(b) of the Act on September 30, 1993, (58 FR 51104). Constance K.
