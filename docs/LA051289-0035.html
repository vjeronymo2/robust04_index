May 12, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Davis Offers Assurances: Marvin Davis assured Minnesota officials that he wouldn't fire Northwest Airlines workers or seek wage concessions. Davis gave his assurance in an effort to settle litigation over a Minnesota commerce commission ruling that prevented him from conducting a tender offer in Minnesota for NWA, the parent of Northwest. Commerce Commissioner Michael Hatch said he would rule today on whether new details submitted by Davis satisfy Minnesota disclosure laws.
