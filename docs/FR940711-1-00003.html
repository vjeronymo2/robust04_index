Time accounts with a stated maturity greater than one year that compound interest less often than annually For time accounts with a stated maturity greater than one year that do not compound interest on an annual or more frequent basis, and that require (or permit) the consumer to withdraw interest at least annually, the annual percentage yield shall be equal to the interest rate.
