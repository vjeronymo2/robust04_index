BFN [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] Government spokesman Dieter Vogel said Honecker's death did "not come as a surprise." In an interview with Deutschlandfunk, Vogel added: "We all knew that he had been ill with cancer for a long time, and one had to expect this end." Vogel could not officially confirm Honecker's alleged wish that his ashes be buried in Germany.
