940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Chez Gerard at Pounds 800,000 at midway Chez Gerard, the restaurant group which is being floated at the end of this month, announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 800,000 for the half-year to December 26, compared with Pounds 942,000 for the previous 52 weeks. Mr Neville Abraham, chairman and chief executive, said that both sales and margins 'continued to move upwards' in the first three months of 1994. The flotation of the company, whose restaurants include Bertorelli's, Cafe Fish, Soho Soho and Chutney Mary, is expected to raise Pounds 14m-Pounds 16m by way of a placing, which would capitalise Chez Gerard at about Pounds 22m, including Pounds 4m of new money.
