940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / International Company News: Saab ready to meet Volvo management Saab Automobile is ready to discuss co-operation projects with Volvo, its rival Swedish car maker which last month scrapped a plan to merge with France's Renault, Mr Keith Butler-Wheelhouse, Saab chief executive, said yesterday. He said he was open for discussion on common development and production of car components, pointing out in an interview with a Swedish news agency that the two companies already shared a number of component suppliers in Sweden.
