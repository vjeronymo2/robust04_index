April 28, 1990, Saturday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: THOUSAND OAKS;

DRIVER DIES AFTER HITTING GUARDRAIL

A 22-year-old man was killed after he fell asleep at the wheel of his car and hit a guardrail on the Ventura Freeway in Thousand Oaks.

Arturo Castillo was traveling north Thursday on the Ventura Freeway near Westlake Boulevard when his car drifted onto the right-hand shoulder of the road and hit the guardrail, Officer Randy Klucker of the California Highway Patrol said.

Castillo, who was not wearing a seat belt, suffered arm fractures, broken ribs and internal injuries, Klucker said. He was taken to Westlake Medical Center in Westlake Village, where he died shortly afterward, Klucker said. CAROL WATSON
