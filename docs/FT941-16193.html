940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / Gun attack on Swiss banker Mr Claudio Generali, chairman of Banca del Gottardo of Lugano, was hit in the lungs by a pistol shot when an assailant burst into his office yesterday, writes Ian Rodger in Zurich. Mr Generali, also a vice chairman of the Association of Foreign Banks in Switzerland and a former finance minister of the Italian-speaking canton of Ticino, had emergency surgery and is not in danger. Mr Walter Canepa, a semi-retired Gottardo manager responsible for looking after the funds of several of the bank's wealthy private clients, is being held in custody.
