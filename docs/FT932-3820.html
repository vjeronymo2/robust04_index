930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / World Trade News: Corning sells optical fibre to Russians CORNING, the US speciality glass and materials company, yesterday announced a long-term agreement to supply optical fibre to Russia for use in its planned civilian telephone network, writes Andrew Fisher. The All Russian Scientific Institute of the Cable Industry and four Russian cable manufacturers will buy the optical fibre for use in making fibre optic cable. They will also consider manufacturing optical fibre using Corning's technology. Mr Izyaslav Peshkov, director of the institute, said Russia planned to build a telecommunications network compatible with those in western Europe.
