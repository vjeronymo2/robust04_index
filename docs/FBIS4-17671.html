BFN [By ITAR-TASS freelance correspondent Semyon Ivanov] [Text] Moscow, 26 Apr -- The "Cosmos-2279" spacecraft was launched today from Plesetsk cosmodrome by the Russian military space forces. The launch was carried out at 0500 hours 14 minutes and 15 seconds statutory Moscow time with the help of the "Cosmos 3M" light rocket carrier. ITAR-TASS has learned at the press center of the military forces that the launch, which was carried out for the Russian Defense Ministry, was successful. The "Cosmos-2279" is the 10th spacecraft launched from Russia's northern cosmodrome since the beginning of 1994.
