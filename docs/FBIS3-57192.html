Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Batyrev] [Text] Paris January 21 TASS -- Georgian State Head Eduard Shevardnadze, currently here on an official two-day visit, was received by French President Francois Mitterrand here today for one-hour negotiations and signing of a French-Germany [as received] treaty on accord, friendship and cooperation. The Georgian state head also signed the Paris Charter for new Europe -- the fundamental CSCE document.
