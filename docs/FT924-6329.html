921121 FT 21 NOV 92 / Finance & The Family: At A Glance - Savings scheme for Services The Ministry of Defence has launched the Services Home Savings Scheme for members of the Armed Forces. Those who save between Pounds 50 and Pounds 200 a month for 60 months will be entitled to an additional payment, called the Home Savings Allowance (HSA), when they buy a home. The HSA will be paid at a rate of Pounds 1 for every Pounds 3 saved in the account, including interest earned.
