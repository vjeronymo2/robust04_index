920122 FT 22 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Union Square sales Union Square is to concentrate on property investment following the sales to their managements of Glentree Estates and Goldschmidt Howland Glentree for Pounds 50,000 and Pounds 290,841 cash respectively. Both disposals are subject to shareholders' approval. Union's pre-tax losses were cut to Pounds 686,000 (Pounds 1.07m) in the six months to September 30, with the deficit per share at 1.06p (1.08p).
