Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] A draft cooperation agreement is being prepared between the military departments of the Russian Federation and the Chechen Republic. Journalists have been told by Colonel Musa Mirzhoyev, a spokesman for the president of Chechnya at the headquarters of the Chechnya armed forces, that the draft agreement envisages preserving the single defense space. According to this document, the Russian side has to provide Chechnya's troops with weapons, not to use its army against the Chechen Republic, train Chechen military experts and provide assistance in the maintenance of military equipment. If necessary, the Chechen side is obliged to give the Russian forces the use of their airfields, to take part in joint military manoeuvers and exercises and not to join any bloc opposing the Russian Federation.
