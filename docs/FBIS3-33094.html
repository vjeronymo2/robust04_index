Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Commander in Chief Fidel Castro has received a French parliamentary delegation, which is headed by Andre Santini, president of the Parliamentary Group of Friendship between France and Cuba and of the French Parliament's Commission for Production and Trade. This delegation is visiting Cuba at the invitation of the National Assembly of the People's Government. During the cordial and friendly meeting, the Cuban president and the French parliamentarians discussed and exchanged information on several important subjects of international interest. Present at the meeting were Jean Raphael Dufour, French ambassador to Havana; Carlos Lage, executive secretary of the Council of Ministers; and Alfredo Guevara, deputy and president of the Group of Friendship Between Cuba and France.
