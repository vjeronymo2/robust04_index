930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / International Company News: IBM downgraded MOODY'S, the US credit rating agency, said it had downgraded the long-term debt of IBM and its financially-supported wholly-owned subsidiaries, IBM Credit, IBM International Finance and IBM Japan, to Single-A1 from Double-A2, AP-DJ reports. About Dollars 18bn of debt is affected. Today, Mr Louis Gerstner, currently chairman of RJR Nabisco, is expected to be appointed chief executive of IBM.
