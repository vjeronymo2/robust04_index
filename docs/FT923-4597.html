920905 FT 05 SEP 92 / Markets: At a Glance - Small company shares fall Small company shares fell yet again this week - but at least the decline slowed. The County Small Companies Index fell 0.2 per cent to 797.46 over the seven days to August 3; the Hoare Govett Index (capital gains version) dropped only 0.02 per cent to 1004.6 over the same period.
