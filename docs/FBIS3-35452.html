Language: Persian Article Type:CSO [Text] Tehran, 6 Bahman [26 Jan] (IRNA) -- The Embassy of the Republic of Kazakhstan was officially opened in Tehran with the raising of the flag of that country in the presence of the ministers of foreign affairs of Iran and Kazakhstan. Tuleutai Suleymenov, the Kazakhstani minister of foreign affairs, said during the embassy inauguration ceremonies, which were also attended by several foreign ministers of other Asian republics: With the opening of the Kazakhstan Embassy in Iran, mutual relations between the two countries in political, cultural, commercial, and economic areas will be actively expanded. Dr. 'Ali Akbar Velayati also termed the inauguration of the Kazakhstan Embassy in Iran one of the fruits of the friendly relations between that country and the Islamic Republic of Iran. It is interesting that Myrzadai Zholdasbekov, the Kazakhstani Ambassador, submitted his credentials to President Hojjat ol-Eslam Hashemi-Rafsanjani last Azar 2 [23 November].
