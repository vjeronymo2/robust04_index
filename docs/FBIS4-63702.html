BFN [Text] Moscow, 24 May (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has issued an edict, setting up the Department for Liaison with Federation Council and State Duma Deputies [Otdel po vzaimodystviyu s deputatami Soveta Federatsii I Gosudarstvennoy Dumy] within the Russian president's administration. The department will be staffed with existing personnel of the administration. The presidential press service told ITAR-TASS today that the edict has been issued with the aim of maintaining liaison with Federation Council and State Duma deputies and their associations.
