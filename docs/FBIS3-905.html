Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The minister of information and culture, Professor Jerry Gana, has appealed for the support and understanding of friendly nations over the Bakassi Peninsula dispute with Cameroon. Receiving the Iranian deputy minister of culture and Islamic guidance, 'Ali Larijani, Prof. Gana described Cameroon as Nigeria's brother. He said France should allow both countries to settle their differences in a true African way. He was happy at the evolving relationship between Nigeria and Iran and hoped that when the cultural and education agreement between the two countries is signed, it will open up other areas of cooperation both in economic and technological fields.
