BFN [Report by Konstantin Parishkura from Kiev under the general heading: "Ukraine Has Decided To Play War With the Black Sea Fleet. Meanwhile, Yeltsin and Kravchuk Have Decided To Split the Fleet"] [Text] Meanwhile, on 15 April the press center of the Ukrainian Defense Ministry issued the following statement: "Vice Admiral Vladimir Beskorovaynyy, commander in chief of the Ukrainian Navy, has said that in accordance with the Ukrainian Naval organization plan, the creation of the Western Sea Area under Captain First Rank Dmitriy Ukrainets continues. Today, commanders of Black Sea Fleet units in Izmail, Ochakov, and Nikolayev will be introduced to their Ukrainian counterparts so as to organize cooperation. Military formations, command and control agencies, and their staffs are being set up in advance so that they will grow into the situation in the areas and prepare for taking over ships after the problem of splitting the Black Sea Fleet is resolved. The newly created formations will be tasked with interfering in the activity of the existing Black Sea Fleet units. [as published]
