940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Copymore ahead 46% to Pounds 1.1m Copymore, the USM-traded photocopying machine supplier, increased pre-tax profits from Pounds 758,000 to Pounds 1.11m for the six months to June 30, a rise of 46 per cent. Turnover rose 37 per cent from Pounds 17.2m to Pounds 23.5m. The company sold the distribution division of its Birmingham-based subsidiary, Mekom Computer Products, for Pounds 1.35m in August. Earnings per share were 6.5p (4.5p) and an interim dividend of 2.6p (2.5p) is declared. The share price rose 10p to 115p.
