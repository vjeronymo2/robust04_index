930416 FT 16 APR 93 / UK Company News: Aspen sells its 13% stake in GWR Aspen Communications has sold its 13 per cent stake in GWR Group for about Pounds 1.9m. The holding, of 386,838 ordinary 20p shares, has been placed with a number of institutions. The proceeds, of about Pounds 730,000, will be used for expansion of Aspen's core printing, marketing and media activities, and will be shown as an exceptional credit in Aspen's 1993 results.
