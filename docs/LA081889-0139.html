August 18, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Keye/Donna Wins Bridgestone Account: Los Angeles ad agency Keye/Donna/Pearlstein, which recently resigned the Suzuki automotive business, has picked up the estimated $10-million Bridgestone Tire account. Ads for the Japanese-owned tire maker, which were formerly created by the firm Lintas: Campbell Ewald, will now be created by Keye/Donna's year-old New York office; this single piece of business more than doubles the annual billings at that location.
