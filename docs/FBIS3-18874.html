Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Steven Oxman, U.S. assistant secretary of state, will meet Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou shortly. Oxman is now meeting his counterpart, Yeoryios Papandreou. In an arrival statement at Ellinikon Airport, Oxman said he is not bringing any special message on Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM]. He stressed that the U.S. Government and President Bill Clinton are interested in promoting Cyrus Vance's initiative and finding a solution that will secure stability in the area. The U.S. President will meet Greek Americans tomorrow to discuss Skopje. During the meetings with Oxman, the Greek side will explain its well-known positions on Skopje as well as all other issues that are of particular interest to Greece. [passage omitted]
