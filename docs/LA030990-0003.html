March 9, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

LESS TRASH

I have just recently returned from a camping trip in Anzo Borrego State Park and I am happy to say that for the first time in many years I have not had to pick up the trash of other careless campers. Usually when I go I bring a trash bag for my own refuse and another one for the garbage that other people have left behind.

I take it as a good sign that people are finally becoming aware of how delicate nature is and that we should live with nature, in harmony, and not against it.

PAUL JURGENS

La Mesa
