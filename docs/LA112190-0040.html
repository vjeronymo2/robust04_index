November 21, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

EMIR OF BAHRAIN HAS SURGERY IN CLEVELAND

The emir of Bahrain, Sheik Isa ibn Salman al Khalifa, has had an operation at a U.S. hospital noted for its heart surgery, according to a statement released Tuesday.

"Sheik Isa underwent a successful operation at the Cleveland hospital," the Gulf News Agency quoted the statement as saying. There were no further details.
