Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Ecuadoran economic developments monitored through 3 January 1994. Source is cited in parentheses following each item. The 1994 general state budget is 6 trillion sucres, 1 trillion of which is earmarked to pay the Ecuadoran foreign debt. The price per barrel of oil is estimated at $13. (Quito Voz de los Andes in Spanish 2300 GMT 28 Dec 93) An official report from the Institute of Statistics and Census indicates that the inflation rate for 1993 was approximately 31 percent compared to more than 60 percent at the end 1992. Prices increased 0.57 percent in December compared to 1.63 percent in November and 1.59 percent in December 1992. (Quito Voz de los Andes in Spanish 2300 GMT 30 Dec 93)
