BFN [Text] At 1300 today, Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam received a Chilean parliamentary delegation led by (Mario Hamwi), chairman of the Defense Committee. Shakir Is'ayyid, deputy chairman of the executive committee of the Syrian parliamentary branch, and the Chilean charge d'affaires in Damascus attended. Discussion revolved around the situation in the region, developments in the Arab-Israeli conflict, and bilateral ties.
