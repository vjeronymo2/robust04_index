940713 FT 13 JUL 94 / Channel 4 to fight watchdog's ruling Channel 4 was yesterday given leave to bring judicial review proceedings to challenge a ruling by the Broadcasting Complaints Commission that upheld a complaint by two former chancellors. The commission ruled in March that Lord Howe and Lord Lawson had been treated unfairly by a television documentary A Brief Economic History Of Our Time, broadcast in February last year. The programme, which was made by Fulcrum Productions, analysed economic policy making in the 1980s.
