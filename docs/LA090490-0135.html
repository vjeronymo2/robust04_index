September 4, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

CONSTRUCTION POSTS SLIGHT RISE IN JULY

Construction spending posted a slight 0.4% gain in July, the first advance in four months, the government reported today.

The Commerce Department said residential, non-residential and government spending totaled a seasonally adjusted annual rate of $442.2 billion.

Construction spending had not risen since a 0.4% increase in March. It had fallen 0.9% in both May and June and 1.9% in April.
