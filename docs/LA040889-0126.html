April 8, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

PREP BASEBALL;

EL TORO CATCHER TOPPO WILL ATTEND UC IRVINE

Scott Toppo, El Toro High School catcher, has made a verbal commitment to attend UC Irvine in the fall and plans to sign a national letter of intent on Wednesday, the first day a high school player is allowed to sign.

Toppo, batting .381 in his second season as a starter, plans to become a second baseman in college.
