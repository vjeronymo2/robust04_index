(c) Combustible Materials. Where an ATCT or part of an ATCT is required to be constructed of noncombustible construction, the use of combustible elements shall be permitted subject to the limitations of this section without altering the construction classification. (1) Roofs, Floors, and Walls. Combustible elements in roofs, floors, and walls are permitted to be used for the following components: A. Interior finish and trim materials as prescribed in section g.1. B. Fire retardant treated wood.
