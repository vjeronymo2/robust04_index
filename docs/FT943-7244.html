940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Fidelity Euro Values net assets static Fidelity European Values had a net asset value of 167.76p per share at June 30, a marginal increase on the December figure of 167.49p. The FT-A Europe (Ex UK) Index fell by 3.49 per cent in the same period. The trust, which seeks long-term growth through a continental European portfolio, reported retained revenue of Pounds 413,000 (Pounds 474,000) for the six months, equivalent to earnings of 0.79p (1.14p) per share.
