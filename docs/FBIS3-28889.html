Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 11 (KCNA) -- Yang Hyong-sop, chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly [SPA], sent a message of greetings to Al-Zinati M. al-Zunati upon his reelection as secretary of the general People's Congress of the great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriyah. The message expresses the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the supreme legislative bodies of the two countries will continue to develop on good terms and wishes him success in his responsible work.
