930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / IBM to pilot Charter helpline THE CONTRACT to run the pilot for the Citizen's Charter national helpline service has been awarded to IBM, the US computer manufacturer. Charterline will give callers information about the Citizen's Charter and how to complain about 1,400 public services. Accessible at local call rates, it will be piloted in the east Midlands from May for six months.
