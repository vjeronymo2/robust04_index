931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / UK Company News: River Plate General capital assets rise Net asset value per capital share of River Plate and General Investment Trust improved from 79.8p to 166.7p over the year to October 31. Available revenue was static at Pounds 4.81m (Pounds 4.84m), equal to earnings per income share of 8.95p (9p). A final dividend per income share of 5.9p makes a same-again 8.9p total.
