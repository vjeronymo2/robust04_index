BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Vladimir Solntsev] [Text] Moscow, 28 Jun -- PRC President Jiang Zemin is to pay an official visit to Russia at the beginning of September this year. An accord on this was reached during the visit to Moscow by Qian Qichen, vice premier of the PRC State Council and foreign minister. "We virtually only have to agree on dates with you. The month has already been set," Russian President Boris Yeltsin said, when he received the head of the Chinese Foreign Ministry in the Kremlin today. The Russian president suggested that the schedule proposed by the Chinese side be shifted by two days and that the summit begin on 2 September.
