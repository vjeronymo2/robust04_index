BFN [Text] Beijing, April 29 (XINHUA) -- Luo Gan, Chinese state councillor and secretary-general of the State Council, met with Golam Hafiz, Bangladesh minister of law and justice, and his party here today. Chinese Minister of Justice Xiao yang was present at the meeting. Hafiz, also president of the Bangladesh-China Friendship Association, and his party are here as guests of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship With Foreign Countries.
