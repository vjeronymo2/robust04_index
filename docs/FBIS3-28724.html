Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] There have been two new clashes between Papua New Guinea [PNG] security forces and the Bougainville Revolutionary Army [BRA] in which one person was reported to have been killed and three wounded. PNG Radio said the first clash occurred when the BRA attacked the government station at Boku in Southeast Bougainville yesterday morning. The radio said one of the rebels was killed in an exchange of fire with troops guarding the station. In the second clash, three civilians, including a senior public servant, were wounded when rebels ambushed two government vehicles travelling from Sovele to (?Koto). One of the vehicles was abandoned and later set on fire by the rebels. PNG Radio said the clashes are part of step-up attacks by the BRA in government- controlled areas of Bougainville.
