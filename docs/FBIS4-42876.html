BFN [Text] Erzincan, June 8 (A.A.) -- Fifty-four terrorists have been killed during operations carried out by security forces in the last five months in eastern Erzincan Province, it was reported on Monday [6 June]. Erzincan Governor Recep Yazicioglu told the ANATOLIAN AGENCY that the operations were carried out in the rural areas and in the town center, without interruption and that the organization was crippled in the region. `Twenty-five people who provided logistic and material support to a terrorist organization were also captured and arrested,' he added. He also said that handguns, machine guns, ammunition and prohibited publications were also seized, adding four security force members were also killed. Yazicioglu also stressed that the arrested terrorists' average age was 16 and this shows the organization is having problems recruiting new members.
