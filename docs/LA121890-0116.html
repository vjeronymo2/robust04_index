December 18, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

POLLY PECK BOSS STILL IN JAIL

Asil Nadir, who recently ranked among Britain's ultra-rich, spent a third night in jail Monday after failing to post bail of $3.9 million on charges of theft and false accounting stemming from the collapse of his Polly Peck empire.

Nadir, arrested when he returned Saturday from a business trip to Turkey, has until Jan. 28 to come up with the cash.
