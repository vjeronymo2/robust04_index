931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / Workers taken off North Sea rig More than 70 workers were evacuated from BP's Forties Delta production platform in the North Sea yesterday after an incident in the flare system. BP Exploration said there was an emergency shutdown after a suspected internal ignition in pipework. No injuries were reported. In all, 77 non-essential personnel were evacuated while 86 people stayed on the platform, 110 miles north-east of Aberdeen.
