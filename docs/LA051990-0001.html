May 19, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WALTON OBVIOUSLY HAS A LOT TO LEARN

Jerome Walton's comments about teachers were those of an ignorant and foolish man. The fact that The Times chose to print them reflects not only a certain insensitivity on the part of your editors, but also the perception that teachers are underpaid because their jobs are easy and unimportant.

The lopsided values of society can never be changed while so-called leading publications give any sort of credence to the comments of those whose only virtues lie in their physical skills.

WILLIAM WAXMAN, Ocean View High School, Huntington Beach
