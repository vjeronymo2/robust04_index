November 27, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

FarWest Financial -- A Nov. 24 story about FarWest Financial Corp. incorrectly stated the company's relationship with Michael Milken and Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc. FarWest and its subsidiary, FarWest Savings & Loan, had only a remote business relationship with Milken, the former head of Drexel's junk bond division. Also, FarWest Savings purchased about $300 million of junk bonds from Drexel, not $600 million as was incorrectly reported.
