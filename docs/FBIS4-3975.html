BFN [Text] Beijing, April 28 (XINHUA) -- The report about China sending additional 30,000 troops to Sino-Indian border is a "sheer fabrication", Wu Jianmin, a spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, said here today. "Spreading such lies is obviously out of ulterior motives," he said. He told a news briefing that the Governments of China and India signed an agreement on the maintenance of peace and tranquillity along the Line of Actual Control in the border areas last year and have begun talks on the reduction of troops along the Actual Control Line," he said.
