920912 FT 12 SEP 92 / Airport seeks loan approval EAST MIDLANDS International Airport at Castle Donington in Leicestershire is seeking government approval to borrow Pounds 23m for expansion. The airport - owned by Derbyshire, Leicestershire and Nottinghamshire county councils and Nottingham City Council - will use Pounds 7m of its reserves to complete the financing of a Pounds 30m plan.
