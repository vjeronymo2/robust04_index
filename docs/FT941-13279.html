940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Willoughby's falls after mining setback A decrease arising mainly from its mining operations, where production was at a reduced level, meant pre-tax profits of Willoughby's Consolidated declined from a restated Pounds 1.79m to Pounds 1.64m in the year to September 30. The result included a Pounds 34,000 profit (Pounds 714,000 loss) share from the Northchart Investments associate, attributable to a reduced loss on sale of investments. As a result of an accounting change, profit on disposal of investments is now calculated according to book value of investments rather than original cost. Turnover climbed to Pounds 11.8m (Pounds 11.1m). Earnings per share slipped from 16.2p to 14.8p and a final dividend of 1p makes a total for the year of 1.5p (2.5p).
