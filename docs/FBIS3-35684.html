Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riga, Jan 28, BNS -- The Riga city Vidzeme district council of people's deputies overwhelmingly reelected Andrejs Rucs as council chairman. Rucs gathered 53 votes in the ballot, while only 14 deputies supported other candidates. "Vidzeme district's local government is against the Cabinet -- let all Latvia learn it. Let all municipalities work as we work," Rucs exclaimed after the vote was announced. The conference rejected the request by the Prosecutor's Office to nullify Rucs' deputy's immunity. Consequently, Rucs cannot be tried for the Jan. 10 incident with Russian generals. Rucs described the incident as a political order of the Russian military intelligence service, in an interview with BNS before the vote. During the session, Rucs reiterated that he "acted correctly to prevent a possible conflict." Rucs argued that investigator Biruta Ulpe's comments on the lack of evidence on the groundedness of his actions. [sentence as received] Ulpe has not acquainted herself with all the materials, he said. The council's next session is scheduled for Feb. 11.
