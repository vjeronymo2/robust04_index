BFN [Text] Baghdad, 16 Jun (TANJUG) -- Milorad Unkovic, federal minister for foreign economic relations, arrived in Baghdad this morning. He is heading a Yugoslav delegation during its official visit to Iraq at the invitation of the Iraqi Government. In the course of his visit, scheduled to end on Monday, Unkovic will meet a number of high-ranking Iraqi officials. He is expected to discuss bilateral relations and particularly economic problems and outstanding debts owed to Yugoslav companies which have for years been involved in construction projects. The projects and the payments for them were suspended after Iraqi troops entered Kuwait in August 1991.
