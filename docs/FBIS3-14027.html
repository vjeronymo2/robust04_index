Language: Urdu Article Type:CSO [News Report: "Establishment of Kashmir Liberation Force Announced"] [Text] Peshawar, 2 February (PPI) -- Tribal leaders have decided to establish the Kashmir Liberation Force (KLF) to give moral and material support to the Kashmiri freedom fighters. Mohammed Rouf Afredi has been appointed chairman of this force. He said at a Peshawar press conference today that under the KLF, that not only seminars and rallies will be held, but training will also be given to volunteers in countering serious situations. It is said that Rouf Afredi is the brother of Aurangzeb, who was the first martyr in Kashmir. *
