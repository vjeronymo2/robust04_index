(c) Quarantine. If a quarantine has been imposed by a State or local agency on the planting of upland cotton or rice for harvest on farms in such State or County Office area, the following rules apply: (1) The State committee may recommend to the Deputy Administrator, State and County Operations, that deficiency payments be made without regard to the 50 percent planting requirement under the provisions of this section to producers in the area who were required to not plant upland cotton and rice. (2) In order for the quarantined acreage to be eligible for deficiency payments, the quarantined acreage must be devoted to eligible conserving use for payment acreage in accordance with §1413.61. §1413.43 Planting flexibility.
