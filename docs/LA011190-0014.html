January 11, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

KOVIC STATEMENT

I am writing in response to Ron Kovic's statement (Jan. 9) that he is " . . . asking people to write to me, to call me, to come up to me on the street. . . . "

I have not forgotten Vietnam or the connection of Vietnam to what is happening with the United States today, both in domestic and international arenas.

Robert Dornan is an epitome of the worst of this country. I thought your characterization of him as appealing to people's fears was exactly right. I also think it showed considerable political acumen about how you might pitch a campaign to defeat him.

NANCY PRESS

Laguna Beach
