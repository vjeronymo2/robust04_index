BFN [Text] Vienna, June 22 (XINHUA) -- Austria welcomes the forthcoming official visit by Premier Li Peng, the first by a Chinese chief of government, said Chancellor Franz Vranitzky here today when interviewed by XINHUA reporters. According to the chancellor bilateral relations have enjoyed smooth development since his visit to China last April and he stressed that economic cooperation is quite satisfactory. The chancellor said he feels glad that a large economic group will accompany Premier Li during his European visit. He suggested that the two countries have a future of cooperation in a variety of economic fields, such as energy, transport and sewage disposal, in which Austria has much experience. "The two countries will sign a series of economic accords during Li's visit to Austria," he said. He spoke highly of China's socialist market economy and its great economic achievements. He stressed that Austria will not limit its attention within Europe in spite of its formal EU membership from January 1, 1995, rather, it will attach more importance to the relationship with countries and regions outside the continent.
