940910 FT 10 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Headway Despite a low level of demand for garden furniture, Headway, the consumer and industrial goods specialist, raised pre-tax profits by 19 per cent from Pounds 272,000 to Pounds 323,000 for the year ended June 30. Turnover was 9.5 per cent higher at Pounds 27.3m. Operating profits grew 24 per cent to Pounds 406,000. The consumer products side made a slightly lower loss of Pounds 308,000 (Pounds 336,000) on turnover up 9 per cent to Pounds 19.6m, while industrial products raised turnover 11 per cent to Pounds 7.7m and profits by 7.5 per cent to Pounds 714,000. Earnings per share rose by 19 per cent to 1.9p (1.6p) and the recommended single final dividend is again 0.7p.
