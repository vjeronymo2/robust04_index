December 28, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

REBOUND? Freedom Bowl ticket sales have declined since 1986 (above) when hometown UCLA played. The turnout has been even smaller, for 20% of ticketholders typically do not attend. . . . But organizers think the trend will be reversed at Saturday's game in Anaheim. About 52,000 tickets had been sold, including 10,000 donated to local military bases, and officials say game-day sales could bring the game close to a sellout. Bob Schwartz and Len Hall
