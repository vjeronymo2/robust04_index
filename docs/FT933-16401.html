930703 FT 03 JUL 93 / Finance and the Family: House prices rise by 1.6%, says Nationwide - At a Glance House prices rose by 1.6 per cent in June, the fourth consecutive monthly increase and the biggest since May 1991, according to figures from the Nationwide building society. The Nationwide house price index has now risen 3.5 per cent since the start of the year, but prices are still down 3.5 per cent from June last year. The average house in the UK now costs Pounds 53,710. Brian Davis, Nationwide's operations director, said: 'A recovery in the housing market does appear to be underway, but it is still fragile with turnover still only slightly above last year's depressed levels.'
