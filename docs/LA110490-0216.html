November 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: BISHOP AMAT SENIOR PICKED FOR CONFERENCE

Maricel Sabillo of Baldwin Park, a senior at Bishop Amat High School, has been selected to attend the 1990 National Young Leaders Conference from Nov. 27 to Dec. 2 in Washington, D.C.

She will be among 350 high school students attending the conference for outstanding young leaders sponsored by the Congressional Youth Leadership Council.
