BFN [Editorial report] San Pedro Sula TIEMPO in Spanish 5 May 94 p 2 reported that Adolfo Facusse, president of the Honduran Council for Private Enterprise, has announced that electricity rationing has paralyzed one-fourth of all national production, causing a daily loss of 20 million lempiras, which, if not halted, will cause losses amounting to 5.5 billion lempiras by the end of 1994. Facusse said that among the immediate solutions contemplated is the establishment of an electrical connection with Panama and Nicaragua and repairing of two thermal plants in Tegucigalpa and San Pedro Sula.
