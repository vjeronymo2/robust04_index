Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Dumitru Iuga, the chairman of the Free Trade Unions of the Radio and Television Companies, is to go on hunger strike from 11 January at 0800 [0600 GMT], according to our national news agency, ROMPRES. In a press statement published today, Iuga points out that he will continue his hunger strike until [national television company head] Paul Everac is removed from that institution and parliament passes, in emergency procedures, the draft bill on the organization and functioning of the national radio and television companies.
