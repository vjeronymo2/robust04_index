930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Smaller Cos Inv Tst Net asset value per share of The Smaller Companies Investment Trust stood at 124.6p at the six months ended June 30, against 84.08p six months earlier and with 88.95p at the interim stage last year. Earnings were 1.31p (1.52p) per share. As already announced, the interim dividend is maintained at 1.2p.
