March 22, 1990, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

HIGH SCHOOL ROUNDUP

Baseball

Nonleague

El Capitan 10, Poway 6 -- Greg Rans weiler went two for four with two RBIs for El Capitan.

Poway fell to 4-2-1.

Milwaukie (Ore.) 8, Fallbrook 7 -- With the victory, Milwaukie improved to 2-3 on its tour of North County. Fallbrook fell to 4-3.

Ramona 15, Clairemont 13 -- First baseman Sean Kelly hit a two-run homer and a grand slam as Ramona took a 9-3 lead after two innings. Jesus Jauregui went two for four with four RBIs, including a three-run homer for Clairemont (4-4).
