November 9, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

POST-MORTEM: Orange County gay activists, losers Tuesday in Irvine (A1), are heading to Washington this weekend for a national conference to discuss gay politics. They'll "tell their story of how it didn't work," said National Gay and Lesbian Task Force spokesman Robert Bray. He said the task force is well aware of Orange County: "We're usually the first to get bashed by (Orange County congressmen) Dannemeyer and Dornan." Dana Parsons and Rick VanderKnyff
