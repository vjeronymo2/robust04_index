Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Rabat, Jan.07-(MAP)-Moroccan Premier Mohamed Karim Lamrani received here Thursday Senegalese Minister in Charge of Regional Economic Integration Maguette Thiam who handed him a message from President Abdou Diouf to King Hassan II. The audience was attended by Moroccan State Minister in Charge of Foreign Affairs and Cooperation Abdellatif Filali and Maguette's accompanying delegation comprising members from the Inter-State Standing Committee for Drought Control in the Sahel (CILSS). Talks focused on the fraternal and sound ties existing between Morocco and the Sahel countries, the problems suffered by these countries due to drought, desertification, locusts and their economic recovery programs. The two officials also tackled Morocco's assistance to the Sahel countries, hailing King Hassan II noteworthy moral and material assistance to the region.
