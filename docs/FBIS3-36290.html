Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report on statement issued by the Ukrainian Foreign Ministry] [Text] The Ukrainian Foreign Ministry press center issued a declaration saying that the Romanian mass media have recently intensified their dissemination of disinformation on Ukraine, often citing officials of that country. Those reports and declarations are an attempt to mislead the international community and distract its attention from internal problems currently facing Romanian society. The Foreign Ministry of Ukraine, the press center declaration said, reserves the right to react to these kinds of reports in a proper way.
