August 18, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The leader of the Texas House of Representatives vowed to use his political power to keep Texas and Texas A&M from leaving the Southwest Conference, defections other schools say would destroy the 76-year-old league.

House Speaker Gib Lewis (D-Ft. Worth) said he would go so far as to reduce legislative appropriations to the two public universities if they tried to leave.
