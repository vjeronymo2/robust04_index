January 26, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

GEORGE WILL

It was gratifying to read George Will's column "Information Differentials Deepen the Two-Tier Society" (Op-Ed Page, Jan. 8), gratifying because it is clear that he no longer believes in the "trickle-down theory."

Congratulations, George -- it's wonderful that even you finally understand!

BILLYE BROUSE

Upland
