930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: Canadian bank buys bulk of Royal Trustco Royal Bank of Canada is to buy the bulk of the operations of Royal Trustco, Canada's second-biggest trust company, for CDollars 1.65bn (USDollars 1.3bn). The acquisition will boost Royal Bank's assets by about CDollars 14bn, or 10 per cent, and give it access to a variety of fiduciary and investment management services. Royal Trustco suffered large losses on businesses it acquired during the 1980s in Europe and the US, as well as losses in its core Canadian mortgage market.
