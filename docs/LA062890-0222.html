June 28, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The World League of American Football has approved a franchise for Memphis -- a city that has sought a NFL team for 20 years.

The WLAF is a league designed to develop players for the NFL. It is to begin play next March.

Seven of the 12 franchises have been approved -- Birmingham, Ala., Orlando, Fla., San Antonio, Sacramento, Montreal, Barcelona, Spain, and Frankfurt, West Germany.
