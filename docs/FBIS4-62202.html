BFN [Text] Vilnius, 12 May, BNS -- Lithuanian Foreign Minister Povilas Gylys and the charge d'affaires of the Lithuanian mission to the Council of Europe [CE], Aurimas Taurantas, attended a session of the CE Committee of Ministers in Strasbourg Wednesday [11 May]. The meeting focused on ways of reforming human rights control and possibilities to expand the CE. The Lithuanian Foreign Ministry said Gylys later met with his Spanish and Slovakian counterparts for a discussion of bilateral cooperation issues.
