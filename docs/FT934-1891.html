931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Hunters Armley Hunters Armley Group, the printer, announced profits of Pounds 2.67m pre-tax (Pounds 2.03m), up 32 per cent, for the year to September 30 on turnover of Pounds 32.2m (Pounds 28.4m). Earnings were 9.32p (6.98p), and a final dividend of 2.5p is proposed for a 3.75p total.
