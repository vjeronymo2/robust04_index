BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] INTERFAX reports that a post of Russian border guards at the 12th outpost of the Moscow border detachment came under mortar fire on Saturday. The fire came from Tajik territory. There are no casualties among the Russian border guards. Up to 10 groups of militants have accumulated in the rear of the 11th and 12th outposts of the Moscow border detachment. The command of the Russian border troops in Tajikistan is taking urgent measures to repel a possible attack on border detachments and military settlements.
