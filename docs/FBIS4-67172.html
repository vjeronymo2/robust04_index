CSO [Excerpt] Hermosillo, Sonora, 20 April--A shipment of nearly a ton of cocaine was seized early this morning at the PGR [Attorney General's Office] checkpoint set up at Km 120 north of this town. The announcement was made by Miguel Angel Cortes Ibarra, the PGR's circuit representative. The federal official added that these drugs were concealed under a load of pine logs arriving from the southern part of the country, and were bound for a site in the state's northern section. The drugs were being transported on a gray Kenworth tractor trailer truck with Federal Public Service [SPF] plates 660-AT-1, hitched to a red flatbed carrying SPF plates 371-TXS. The information concerning the driver was not disclosed, so as to avoid interfering with the pertinent investigation [passage omitted]
