920108 FT 08 JAN 92 / London has top EC jobless rise LONDON has the fastest rising unemployment in Europe, according to figures from the European Commission released by the Labour-dominated Association of London Authorities. The commission found that unemployment remained higher in many European regions in Spain, Italy and the Ireland, but was not rising anywhere as fast as in Greater London. It rose by more than 25 per cent between April 1990 and April 1991. The association said the large number of redundancies in the finance sector had raised unemployment from 6.9 per cent to 10.4 per cent.
