Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Netherlands Foreign Minister Peter Kooijmans wants the problems in the Serbian province of Kosovo to get more attention from the international mediators for ex-Yugoslavia. At a congress Mr. Kooijmans called Kosovo a volcano that could erupt at any moment. According to the foreign minister, an explosion of violence there must be prevented through dialogue. Mr. Kooijmans places responsibility for the tense situation in the province primarily on the Serbs. Three years ago Belgrade ended the autonomous status of Kosovo, which has a majority population of ethnic Albanians.
