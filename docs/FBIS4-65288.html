BFN [Text] According to the Defense Ministry, two persons died when a Portuguese military plane crashed between the beaches of Almadrava and Cala Montjoi [in Catalonia]. A colonel and a captain who were taking part in the NATO "Dynamic Impact-94" maneuvers died. The plane had come down in a "black spot" where radio communications were difficult and there was a lot of fog. Another fighter plane, a Spanish Harrier came down in the sea this morning, and its pilot has been rescued unhurt near Palma de Mallorca [in the Balearic Islands]. The Harrier was based on the Principe de Asturias aircraft carrier.
