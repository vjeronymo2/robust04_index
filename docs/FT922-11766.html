920423 FT 23 APR 92 / UK Company News: Courtney Pope issue 34.52% taken up The Pounds 5m rights issue launched by Courtney Pope Holdings, the shopfitting group which is being refinanced, has been taken up as to 2.37m ordinary shares (34.52 per cent). Within this total 1.97m shares were the subject of commitments and agreements to subscribe by certain existing institutional shareholders, new directors and a trade investor. Of the 4.89m ordinary shares underwritten, applications were received in respect of 393,869. The balance of 4.49m shares will be taken up by the underwriters. The new management team, Mr George Duncan, Mr Tim Wightman and Mr Nino Allenza, are changing the company's name to Rubicon.
