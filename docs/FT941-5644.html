940305 FT 05 MAR 94 / Kravchuk success President Leonid Kravchuk of Ukraine yesterday won a promise of increased financial aid from the US during a White House meeting with President Bill Clinton, writes George Graham in Washington. The extra aid is a reward for Mr Kravchuk's agreement to a three-way pact with Russia and the US for Ukraine to eliminate around 1,800 nuclear warheads.
