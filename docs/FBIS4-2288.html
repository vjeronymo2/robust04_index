BFN [Text] Prime Minister Esko Aho, who is in Shanghai, has studied China's rapid economic development and new large projects. He was especially briefed on the Pudong special economic zone, which foreign enterprises are induced to invest in. Of Finnish enterprises, Nokia [telecommunications] is already represented there. The economic briefing by the Shanghai city leadership revealed that China will no longer establish any new special economic zones. Prime Minister Aho also attended a ceremony in Shanghai at which Kone Oy [elevator and crane company] signed its latest elevator deal with China. The deal is worth nearly 30 million Finnish markkas.
