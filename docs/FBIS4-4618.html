BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 4 (KCNA) -- A ceremony was held here Sunday to confer the title of Labour Hero of the DPRK on delegates to the national conference of party cell secretaries. Present at the ceremony were secretary of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] Kye Ung-tae and chief secretary of the South Pyongan Provincial party committee So Yun-sok who are Politburo members of the WPK Central Committee, officials concerned and the delegates to the conference. The title of Labour Hero of the Republic was conferred on Hong Chong-ok, Yi Kang-ha and An Pyong-ok who, working as party cell secretary for many years, had enhanced the militant function and role of party organizations and actively aroused party members to the revolution and construction with unfailing loyalty to the party and the leader, thus greatly contributing to strengthening the singlehearted unity of the whole society.
