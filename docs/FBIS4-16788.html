BFN [Edict No. 784 of the Russian Federation president "On the Draft of Citizens of the Russian Federation for Military Service in April-May 1994 and the Discharge of Soldiers, Seamen, Noncommissioned Officers, Petty Officers, and Military Construction Personnel Who Have Served the Designated Period of Military Service", dated Moscow, the Kremlin, 15 April 1994 and signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin] [Text] In accordance with the Russian Federation laws "On Defense" and "On Military Service Obligation and Military Service," I decree that: 1. A total of 216,000 citizens of the Russian Federation born between 1967 and 1976, including those who are studying in day departments of establishments of primary and secondary professional education and are not entitled to a deferment, are to be drafted in April-June 1994 for military service in the Russian Federation Armed Forces and in other forces and military formations. 2. Soldiers, seamen, noncommissioned officers, petty officers, and military construction personnel who have served the designated period of military service are to be discharged in April-June 1994. 3. The Russian Federation Government and organs of executive power in the republics, krays, oblasts, the cities of Moscow and St. Petersburg, the autonomous oblast, and autonomous okrugs are to ensure that measures are taken to draft citizens for military service. 4. The Russian Federation Ministry of Defense together with other ministries and departments where there is provision for military service are to submit for approval their proposals for the placement of citizens drafted for military service in April-June 1994. 5. Persons drafted for military service are to be transported as a rule in teams in the passenger cars of trains or in airplanes (ships) in accordance with a special schedule. The Russian Federation Ministry of Railways and the Russian Federation Ministry of Transport are to set aside the necessary quantity of rolling stock and airplanes (ships) or places in passenger cars and planes (ships) in accordance with requests from the relevant command to transport the said persons at given times, with provision for security measures; payment for the transport is to be charged at the present mutually agreed tariffs. 6. This Edict comes into force from the moment of its signing. [Signed] President of the Russian Federation B. Yeltsin [Dated]Moscow, The Kremlin, 15 April 1994 No. 784
