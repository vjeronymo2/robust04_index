BFN [Unattributed article: "Scalfaro To Be `Insistent' on Employment"] [Text] (ANSA) Cagliari, April 28 -- President Oscar Luigi Scalfaro said he would be "attentive and insistent" on employment issues during the term of the government expected to be ushered in by his meeting at 5 p.m. (1500 GMT) this afternoon with the leader of the conservative coalition that won the elections, media magnate Silvio Berlusconi. Talking to reporters at the inauguration of the Cagliari Fair, Scalfaro was asked if Berlusconi's word would be enough to allay conflict of interest concerns. "I cannot say before we have had a talk," was his reply.
