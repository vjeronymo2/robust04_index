940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / International Company News: Bankruptcy looms for Deckel Maho Deckel Maho, one of Germany's largest machine tool companies, is on the verge of declaring itself bankrupt. Mr Werner Schneider, appointed receiver to the Bavaria-based group after it filed for protection against creditors in April, said that a formal application for bankruptcy was the next legal step after it had proved impossible to work out a solution with creditors to guarantee the group's survival. Mr Schneider said, however, that he was hopeful that it would be possible to rescue the group - which owes banks DM530m (Dollars 325.1m) - through a sale to a third party.
