930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Chester Waterworks Chester Waterworks turned in pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.17m for the year ended March 31, against Pounds 2.45m for the previous 15 months. Turnover came to Pounds 5.64m, against Pounds 6.58m. Earnings per share amounted to 617.7p, while a recommended final dividend of 150p makes a total for the year of 225p.
