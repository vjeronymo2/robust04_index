BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 16 Apr (MENA)--The National Democratic Party panel today reiterated President Mubarak's eagerness to guarantee the success of the national dialogue by providing all the necessary resources and opportunities for the various views and national efforts to attain the highest possible level of concurrence on the political, economic, and social issues that concern society. In a statement issued following its meeting today, the panel announced that President Mubarak has decided to include all Egyptian party leaders in the committee in charge of preparing for the national dialogue. This will expand the membership of the committee, which will include representatives of the other categories announced earlier. The statement added that President Mubarak is expected to invite the committee to a meeting with him after he returns from a scheduled visit to China this month. [passage omitted]
