BFN [Text] According to Colonel-General Valeriy Patrikeyev, commander of the collective peacekeeping forces in Tajikistan, the situation in the Republic is gradually returning to normal. In the words of the new commander, he has come to this conclusion after a two-week acquaintance with the situation in the country and meetings with the Republic's residents and leadership. Incidentally, the Russian general described as expedient the decision on extending the mandate of the collective peacekeeping forces until 31 December 1994. In his words, the situation on the Tajik-Afghan border remains complex.
