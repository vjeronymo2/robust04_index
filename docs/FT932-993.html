930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Cranswick calls for Pounds 3.8m Cranswick, the supplier of grain, feed, livestock and meat products, announced a 29 per cent rise in profits and a call on shareholders for Pounds 3.8m. The issue, by a rights of 1-for-4 at 152p, is underwritten by Samuel Montagu with brokers James Capel. The shares fell 11p to 181p. Part of the proceeds will be used to refinance the cost of acquiring George Buckton of up to Pounds 1.15m. For the year ended March 31 turnover rose from Pounds 93.9m to Pounds 109.6m and pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.71m to Pounds 2.2m. Earnings per share were 13.7p (14.6p) and the final dividend is 5.6p for a total of 8p (7.5p).
