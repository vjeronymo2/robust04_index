BFN [Article by "zd": "The French Will Exercise in the Czech Republic"] [Text] Prague/Paris--At the beginning of June, military exercises will be conducted with a French company in the Czech Republic and at the same time a Czech company in France. It is also expected that French experts will be commissioned to Czech military schools. That is one of the results of the three-day visit by Defense Minister Antonin Baudys to France. The delegation also met with the representatives of a leading electronic concern, Thomson-CSF, that was seriously interested in Pardubice Tesla once. Minister Baudys admitted the possibility of cooperation with this firm in the development of the electronic equipment for the Czech light multipurpose L-159 aircraft currently in the works, the modernization of air defense, and the T-72 tank. After the talks, Baudys confirmed that the Czech Republic enjoys great credibility in the industrial world. This fact, according to him, makes it possible for the Republic in the modernization of the Army not to purchase only finished products but rather opt for supplies of state-of-the-art technology and utilize the capacity and possibilities of the Czech firms.
