February 27, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: FIRST OFF . . .

Frank Sinatra, Liza Minnelli and Sammy Davis Jr.'s concert Saturday in Tokyo grossed $1.8 million, the highest ever for a Japan concert in a 7,000-seat venue. Ticket prices for the concert at NK Hall were about $520, $320 and $200. The concert will air on the Tokyo Broadcasting System on Saturday as a two-hour prime-time special. Sinatra, Minnelli and Davis continue their world tour with four concerts in Australia and two in Honolulu before returning to Los Angeles for a three-week break. They play concert halls in Europe on a five-week tour in April and May. Shauna Snow
