Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Sarajevo, Jan 6 (AFP) -- Five French soldiers in the United Nations Protection Force (UNPROFOR) were wounded on Thursday at Sarajevo airport, UNPROFOR Guy de Battista said here. Two non-commissioned officers were seriously hurt, and underwent emergency operations but they would survive, and would be evacuated to France Friday, de Battista said. [passage omitted] French forces have lost 18 killed and more than 260 wounded, a third of them seriously, since the UN intervention began in April 1992. Half of the casualties have been caused by fighting. [passage omitted]
