BFN [Text] Alija Izetbegovic, president of the state presidency, and Army General Rasim Delic, commander of the General Staff of the Supreme Command of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina, met with the top commanders of the Supreme Command, comprising the chief of the General Staff, the commanders of the 1st, 2d, 3d, 4th, and 7th Corps of the Army of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina, and the commander of the Air Force and Air Defense at a command headquarters of the Army of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina in central Bosnia today. They discussed and analyzed the military situation on the Bosnia-Herzegovina fronts, adopted appropriate decisions, and issued tasks for the period ahead.
