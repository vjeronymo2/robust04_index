August 6, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Vestron Debt Holders to Consider Live Bid: Vestron Inc. said it will present Live Entertainment Inc.'s acquisition proposal to holders of its senior and subordinated debt.

The proposal calls for Van Nuys-based Live to acquire Stamford, Conn.-based Vestron in exchange for Live securities. Vestron said an agreement would be concluded if debt holders agree to the deal.
