BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow May 20 TASS -- Economic measures designed to combat the crisis in Russia were discussed by President Yeltsin and the State Duma speaker during their meeting in the Kremlin today, the Russian president told journalists. According to Yeltsin his conversation with Ivan Rybkin carried on the four-hour discussion the president had with industrialists and entrepreneurs on Thursday concerning a package of presidential decrees on economic reform which are being prepared now. Yeltsin stressed that the press misrepresented his statement on the possible collapse of the economy on Thursday. "The collapse may occur if we do not take steps to overcome the crisis. We must make all efforts not to let the worst to happen," the president said.
