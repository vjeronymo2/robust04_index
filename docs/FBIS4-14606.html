BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Kim Boldokhonov] [Text] Ulaanbaatar April 1 TASS -- Co-chairmen of the joint Russo-Mongolian commission on checking the border between the two countries met on Thursday to discuss verification and demarcation issues. The border is several thousand kilometers long, and only the last 500 kilometers now await demarcation. The commission members are to define more accurately geographical coordinates of the remaining stretch and to legalize relevant documents. We have agreed on all the technicalities, distributed the tasks and confirmed mutual readiness to accomplish the programme, Luvsandorjin Mundagbator, chairman of the Mongolian delegation, told ITAR-TASS on Thursday. We took on an important task: We must complete checking the state border in 1995 and to report to the respective parliaments about the results by the end of the same year, he said.
