940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / Race bias complaints against police rise Allegations of racial discrimination and serious assault against police officers rose sharply last year, but the total of complaints of all kinds fell, the Police Complaints Authority said yesterday. The authority considered 17,991 complaints last year, a fall of 7 per cent on the previous year. The number of complaints of racial discrimination rose from 67 in 1992 to 291. Disciplinary action was taken on 4 per cent of those complaints, compared with 10 per cent of all investigated complaints.
