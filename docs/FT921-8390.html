920218 FT 18 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Gartmore Emerging shows improvement At December 31 1991 net asset value of Gartmore Emerging Pacific Investment Trust had improved to 51.1p per share from 44.9p a year ear- lier, though it slipped from 58.9p at the halfway stage. Gross revenue for the year to December 31 increased to Pounds 1.12m (Pounds 851,000) and pre-tax profit more than doubled from Pounds 187,000 to Pounds 423,000. Net revenue came out at Pounds 306,000 (Pounds 121,000). A dividend of 0.13p (0.1p) is proposed. This, together with a special payment of 0.1p, makes a total of 0.23p for the year.
