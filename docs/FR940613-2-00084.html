Glass Ceiling Commission; Postponement of Open Site Hearing

SUMMARY: This notice postpones the June 24, 1994 Open Site Hearing of the Glass Ceiling Commission. The notice for this Open Site Hearing was announced in the Federal Register on May 17, 1994 (59 FR 25670). A new date for the Open Site Hearing will be announced in the future.
