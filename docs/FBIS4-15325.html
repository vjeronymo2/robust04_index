BFN [Unattributed report: "Distribution of Spheres of Influence in Abkhazia!"] [Text] Sukhumi -- According to information supplied by the IBERIA news agency, the spheres of influence between military formations located in the Abkhazian region have been distributed as follows: North Caucasians have mainly been deployed in Sukhumi; Cossacks in Gulripshi; Armenians in Gali; and Abkhazians in Gudauta and Tqvarcheli.
