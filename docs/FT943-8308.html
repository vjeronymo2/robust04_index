940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / World Trade News: Faulding buys Chinese drugs company F H Faulding, the Australian pharmaceutical company, is to buy a 90 per cent interest in a leading Chinese pharmaceutical company, writes Emilia Tagaza from Melbourne. F H Faulding is to buy all the issued capital of the Hong Kong company, Merrywise, for around ADollars 40m (Dollars 30m). Merrywise already has an agreement with the Foshan city government, in China's Guangdong province, to buy 90 per cent of Foshan Horizon, which manufactures, markets and and distributes pharmaceuticals. Foshan Horizon, which recorded sales of ADollars 20m in 1993, will manufacture both western pharmaceutical products and traditional Chinese medicines.
