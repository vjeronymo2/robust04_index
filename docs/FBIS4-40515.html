BFN [Unattributed report: "Unemployed on the Permission of Administration"] [Text] At the beginning of June, 101,500 unemployed with the corresponding status were registered in Ukraine. This means that, over a five month period, their number has increased by 18,500 persons, and Ukraine feels confident to be among those European countries, which enjoy the lowest level of unemployment. At the same time, the Ministry of Statistics maintains that about 2.5 million actually jobless citizens do not have official status and are on leave of absence on the permission of the administration.
