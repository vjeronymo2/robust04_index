BFN [Text] Cairo, 3 May (MENA)--Hasan 'Usfur, member of the Palestinian delegation to the Palestinian-Israeli Security Affairs Committee, has disclosed that the new obstacles that Israel suddenly raised to the agreement on self-rule in Gaza and Jericho result from its putting forth 25 new points on all issues--security, civilian, and legal. He told a MENA correspondent today that these points had been agreed upon earlier. He said: This is a known Israeli tactic aimed at preventing us from making any new proposals.
