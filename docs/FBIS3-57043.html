Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Sokhbet Mamedov under "From Our Correspondents and News Agencies" rubric: "Criminal Proceedings Brought in Baku Against Culprits of Black January Tragedy"] [Text] Baku -- Azerbaijani General Prosecutor Ali Omarov has stated that criminal proceedings have been brought against Abdurakhman Vezirov and Ayaz Mutalibov, who are considered the chief culprits of the tragedy on 20 January 1990, when Soviet Army units were brought into Baku. At that time they held the posts of first secretary of the Communist Party of Azerbaijan Central Committee and chairman of the republic Council of Ministers respectively. But the investigation is being made difficult because both defendants are currently outside Azerbaijan, in Moscow to be exact.
