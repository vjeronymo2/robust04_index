March 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SOUTHLAND'S TOP 10 BUILDING LEADERS

Key to Home Builder Roster

The name of each company is followed by its headquarters city and then by its principal officer. Sales figures are given for 1989, and have been limited to residential sales in Kern, Los Angeles, Orange, Riverside, San Bernardino, San Diego and Ventura counties.

Sales projections are for anticipated residential sales in the above-mentioned counties for 1990. The number of units constructed in 1989 includes units completed for sale during 1989 and is broken out by number and percent of each type of unit: single-family homes, apartments and condominiums (including units in planned-unit developments). Inventory includes any units completed for sale that were unsold by the end of the accounting period.

Note: Ranking is based on rounded sales figures. Subtotals may not add to 100.0% due to computer rounding. Firms with the same 1989 sales volume are ranked in alphabetical order.

* Firm reporting on a fiscal-year basis.

The following builders changed their names in 1989: The Casden Co./CoastFed Properties from CoastFed Properties; Century Homes Communities from Century Homes; Forecast Group from Forecast Mortgage Corp.; Forest City Properties Corp. from Forest City Development; The Grupe Company from Grupe Development Co. -- Southern California; McKellar Communities from McKellar Development of La Jolla; McMillin Communities Inc. from McMillin Cos.; Meeker Development Co. from California Communities Inc.; Richmond American Homes from Richmond American; Socaland Group Inc. from Socaland Development or Socaland Corp.; Sunland Communities from Sunland Consolidated or The Sunland Housing Group.
