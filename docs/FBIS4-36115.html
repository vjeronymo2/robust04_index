BFN [Text] Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz has said Iraq's relations with fraternal, friendly, and other countries are witnessing positive development in terms of backing Iraq's just issues. Attending an evening session of the annual diplomatic conference of Iraqi ambassadors and heads of diplomatic missions abroad, Tariq 'Aziz reviewed Iraq's relations with the world's countries, the development of its relationship with the Security Council, and the international attitude toward Iraq in general. During the conference, also attended by Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf, the deputy prime minister reviewed Iraq's diplomatic and political activity in the international arena in light of the growing international support for Iraq, even inside the Security Council, to reinforce its just demand for lifting the unjust blockade imposed on its people, after it has met all its obligations toward the Security Council's resolutions. Tariq 'Aziz also explained the means the inspection teams had adopted to fabricate crises and justifications to continue the aggression in all respects.
