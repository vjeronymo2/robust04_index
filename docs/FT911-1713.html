910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / Appointments: Chairman of Alliance & Leicester Mr Fred Crawley becomes chairman of the ALLIANCE & LEICESTER BUILDING SOCIETY from August 1, succeeding Mr John Baker who retires but remains on the board. Mr Crawley, who joined the Society's board in 1988, was a director and deputy chief executive of Lloyds Bank. He is chairman of Betta Stores, a director of Barratt Developments, and of Lloyds Development Capital, and deputy chairman of the Society's subsidiary, Girobank.
