920225 FT 25 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Throgmorton Dual The Throgmorton Dual Trust yesterday reported net asset value of 623.8p per capital share and 28.5p per income share at January 31. The figures showed advances from the respective 584p and 27.4p at the same stage of the previous year, but were down on the trust's year-end values of 652.7p and 30.4p. Net revenue for the six months to end-January dipped to Pounds 692,000 (Pounds 844,000). Earnings per share were 3.01p (3.67p). An unchanged second interim dividend of 1.75p makes 3.5p so far this year, partly satisfied from reserves.
