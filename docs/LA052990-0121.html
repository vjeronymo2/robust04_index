May 29, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

STATE COMMUNITY COLLEGE FINAL POSTPONED

The state community college championship baseball game between L.A. Harbor and Chabot was postponed because of wet grounds, and will be played at 1 p.m. today at UC Irvine.

Harbor (50-5) is undefeated in the tournament and needs only one victory to capture its first state title since 1984. Chabot (43-11) needs to win twice today to gain its first state baseball championship.
