920414 FT 14 APR 92 / UK Company News: Guernsey gas Group rises Pounds 1m to Pounds 4.2m Guernseygas Group, which includes Guernsey Gas Light Company and has core businesses in property, motor vehicles, energy and fuel, lifted pre-tax profits by Pounds 1m from Pounds 3.2m to Pounds 4.2m in the year to December 31. The company said that its activities 'all came good at the same time'. Turnover advanced to a record Pounds 103m (Pounds 99m). During the year Guernseygas sub-divided its shares from Pounds 1 to 10p, and the group achieved a 42 per cent rise in earnings per share from an adjusted 4.3p to 6.1p. A final dividend of 1.6p gross has been proposed for a total of 2.5p (2.25p).
