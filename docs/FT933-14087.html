930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / Draskovic leaves hospital Vuk Draskovic, leader of the largest Serbian opposition group, leaves hospital yesterday and immediately vowed to oust Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic and restore democracy in Serbia, which he called a dictatorship, writes Laura Silber. Mr Draskovic under Mr Milosevic. He said he would obey medical orders to avoid political engagement for the next two months. Mr Draskovic appeared wan after ending an eight-day hunger strike in protest against his detention. In apparent fear of mass unrest, Mr Milosevic last Friday issued a pardon for the opposition leader after Mr Draskovic was reported to be near death after a hunger strike. He had been jailed since June 1 after anti-government demonstrations
