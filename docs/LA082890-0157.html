August 28, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

MASERONI-FOREMAN BOUT SLATED

Walter Maseroni of Argentina today was named the opponent for former heavyweight champion George Foreman in a Sept. 25 fight at the London Arena.

Foreman, 42, has won 22 consecutive contests since coming out of retirement in 1987, with 21 of those victories by knockout.

Promoter Frank Warren announced last week that Foreman had agreed to a 10-round fight next month, but no opponent was named at the time.
