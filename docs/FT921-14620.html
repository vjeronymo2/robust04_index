920115 FT 15 JAN 92 / International Company News: MGM-Pathe incurs deficit of Dollars 233.2m MGM-PATHE Communications, the financially troubled Hollywood studio controlled by Credit Lyonnais, the French state bank, unveiled a Dollars 233.2m net loss for the 39-week period to September 28, writes Alan Friedman. The loss, struck on 47 per cent higher revenues of Dollars 703m, compares with a Dollars 65.2m loss in the nine-month period to August 28 1990. Mr Giancarlo Parretti, the former MGM chairman, was released from an Italian prison last week after being held on various tax and fraud allegations. Mr Gary Lieberthal, chairman of the television division of Sony's Columbia Pictures, has announced his departure. A successor has not yet been named.
