931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / Budget 93: Their Budget - The entrepreneur Geoff Cross, 47 Job: chief executive of Aerospace Systems & Technologies, near Consett, Co Durham. The company was founded in 1990 by Mr Cross and two colleagues. AS & T employs 70 and has annual turnover of around pounds 4m Earns: in excess of pounds 30,000. His wife, a part-time teacher, earns pounds 5,000 a year Politics: voted Liberal Democrat in the last general election and expects to vote LibDem next time Verdict: 'What I wanted from this Budget was long-term stability. His whole thrust was reducing the Budget deficit - that's quite significant. He is addressing it in a relatively creative way: because of that I'll applaud the guy. But the lack of any mention of capital allowances was a big disappointment.
