Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Rome, 14 Feb (TANJUG) -- The leader of the Serbian Renewal Movement [SPO], Vuk Draskovic, arrived in Rome this evening on a four-day visit to Italy. Draskovic is expected to meet Italian Foreign Minister Beniamino Andreatta, Senate President Giovanni Spadolini, and parliamentary lower house president Giorgio Napolitani. In the course of his visit to Italy, an SPO delegation including Vuk Draskovic, his wife Danica Draskovic, and Milan Komnenic, the party's deputy chairman, is also to meet representatives of Italian Red Cross and other humanitarian organizations.
