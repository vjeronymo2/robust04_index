Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 23 Jan (MENA)--Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat arrived in Cairo early this morning on a short visit to Egypt. 'Arafat said in an arrival statement that he will brief President Husni Mubarak on the results of his meeting with Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres in Oslo. 'Arafat emphasized the importance of persistence to reach an agreement with Israel for the meticulous implementation of the Declaration of Principles, which was signed in Washington on 13 September. He said that there are many obstacles that have prevented the implementation of the agreement until now. We expect the Palestinian-Israeli talks to continue to overcome the obstacles, he added. 'Arafat said that he is hoping that President Mubarak will make efforts to remove these obstacles. He noted that he will pay a visit to Saudi Arabia today to perform a mini-pilgrimage and meet with Saudi officials.
