BFN [Text] Thousands of workers and their families from the Free Algeria Agroindustrial Complex in Las Tunas were given moral and material rewards as a result of the outstanding work done in the current harvest tasks. Labor union leaders advised that 328 workers received bicycles; 325 received various articles from local shops; and 30 received paid vacations at tourist centers in Las Tunas Province. As a result of the sugar harvest special emulation, more than 1,800 workers received pay increases; 20 received new homes; and the homes of 25 workers were repaired. Manati Municipality is the best in Las Tunas Province regarding attention given to the sugar worker, which includes recognizing the best Committees for the Defense of the Revolution and cultural and sports activities for the workers.
