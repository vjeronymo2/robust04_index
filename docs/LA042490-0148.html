April 24, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: TODAY'S ELECTION

What: Voters in Cypress go to the polls to decide on a referendum that, if approved, would allow the rezoning of 75 acres next to Los Alamitos Race Course for development of a Cypress Business and Professional Center, which would include office buildings and a hotel.

When to vote: Polls are open from 7 a.m. to 8 p.m.

Information: For information about polling places, call the Orange County registrar of voters office at (714) 567-7600.
