920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / Bovis wins Dollars 234m deal BOVIS, the construction arm of Peninsular & Oriental Steam Navigation (P&O) of the UK, has won a contract to manage construction of a Dollars 234m (Pounds 122.5m) commercial property development in Lincoln Square, New York, Andrew Taylor writes. It continues the company's recent successes in winning large international contracts, including one to manage the main building works for the 1996 Olympic Games in Atlanta, Georgia. The latest deal, awarded to Lehrer McGovern Bovis by New York developers Millennium Partners, involves constructing a 47-storey building with 8,000 sq ft of residential and commercial space, cinemas and shops. Work should be finished by December 1994.
