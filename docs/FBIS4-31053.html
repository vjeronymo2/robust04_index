BFN [Text] In a letter addressed to the chairman of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly Miro Lazovic, the Serbian Civic Council proposed that the next session of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly includes a debate about the strategy toward the Serbian question and the constitutional position of the citizens of Serbian ethnicity on the territory of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Federation. The Serbian Civic Council proposes that the amendment to the constitution of the federation that was proposed at the last session of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly forms the basis for the debate at the next session of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly. The Serbian Civic Council believes that Legislation Minister Kasim Trnka, one of the authors of the Constitution of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Federation, and one representative of the Serbian Civic Council should speak about the position of the Serbs within the federation. It would be imperative to obtain the views and opinions of the constitutional commission of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly and Government prior to the assembly session, is stated in a letter of the Serbian Civic Council to Miro Lazovic.
