940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Fleming European possible placing Fleming European Fledgeling Investment Trust is considering a placing and offer of conversion shares to increase the size of the trust. The directors will assess the potential demand over the next few days. Any placing and offer will be structured to ensure there is no dilution of the net asset value of the existing shares and will be made available to all existing shareholders, including those in other Fleming trusts and schemes, and to the public.
