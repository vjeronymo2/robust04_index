Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, 8 Feb (SUNA) -- The Foreign Ministry yesterday issued a statement on the massacre recently perpetrated by the Serbs in Sarajevo, in which over 100 Bosnian Muslim citizens fell victim. The statement condemend the heinous crime and reaffirmed Sudan's (?denunciation) of the failure and conspiracies of the great powers. The statement condemned the UN Security Council's negative stance towards the massacre. It expressed Sudan's concern over the international community's silence in response to the violation of human rights in Bosnia. The statement said that what the Serbs were doing in Bosnia was unprecedented since what the Nazis did during the Hitlerite era and that the UN's failure was exactly like the League of Nations' failure to stop the crimes of the Nazis, which lost it the confidence of the peoples of the world (?at that time). The statement appealed to the peace-loving forces and all states to confront this inhuman (?whirlpool), to stand firmly by the Muslim people of Bosnia, to pressure the Security Council into discharging its duty to protect the Bosnian people's legitimate rights and lift the arms embargo imposed on the Bosnian people to enable them to defend themselves, strike at the Serbian forces to destroy their military concentrations and to immediately evict them from the Bosnian territories.
