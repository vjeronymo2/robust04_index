940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: CI Group factory sale to reduce debt CI Group, the Wolverhampton-based engineer, has sold a factory and offices for Pounds 900,000 and will be relocating its head office to premises nearby. Proceeds will be used to repay part of the group's medium-term borrowings. CI reported increased losses to Pounds 1.53m (Pounds 338,000) in the year to end-January, after selling its French subsidiary, Societe Metallurgique de Brevilly, and cutting the workforce by 11 per cent to less than 1,000.
