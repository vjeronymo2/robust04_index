941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Kingfisher Kingfisher, the retail group which includes the B&Q DIY chain, announced that the Paris Bourse quotation for Etablissements Darty, the electrical retailer it acquired last year, has been suspended as its Financiere Darty subsidiary is considering exercising its power to acquire the outstanding 2 per cent of shares which it does not already own.
