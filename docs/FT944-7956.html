941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Unigroup offers itself as reverse takeover vehicle Unigroup, the slimmed-down air curtain manufacturer and wind farm operator, is offering itself as a vehicle for a reverse takeover for a company looking for an alternative to a float. The group is seeking to acquire a UK-based business making pre-tax profits of between Pounds 3m and Pounds 5m. The group has cash resources of Pounds 13.6m available as part of the consideration and to invest in the business. 'The acquisition will be purchased using the group's cash resources together with new shares,' said Mr Jonathan Mervis, chief executive. Unigroup made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 364,000 in the year to end September, against Pounds 4.41m for the previous 15 months, from turnover of Pounds 6.59m (Pounds 38.5m). Continuing activities contributed a pre-tax profit of Pounds 333,000 (Pounds 1.21m loss). Losses per share were 0.1p compared with earnings of 8.5p.
