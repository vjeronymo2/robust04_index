940802 FT 02 AUG 94 / International Company News: Nova ahead 44% at CDollars 69m in second term Surging petrochemical prices enabled Nova, the Alberta energy group, to post second-quarter net profits of CDollars 69m (USDollars 50m), or 15 cents a share, up 44 per cent from CDollars 41m, or 10 cents, on the year-ago figure. First-half final net profit was CDollars 236m, or 52 cents a share, including a special CDollars 120m gain on the sale of a resource subsidiary. Without special items, second-quarter profit was CDollars 116m, or 26 cents, against CDollars 84m, or 21 cents.
