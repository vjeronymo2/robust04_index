BFN [Text] Commenting on the interview that AL-SHA'B had conducted with Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati, the Iranian interests section office in Cairo has clarified that Iran denies all the reports that it is sending weapons to Bosnia in violation of the international resolutions. The office reiterated Iran's declaration of support for the oppressed Bosnian Muslim people on all levels and added that the embargo on weapons to Bosnia to defend its rights is unfair and that Iran is seriously demanding the lifting of this embargo.
