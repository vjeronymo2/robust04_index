Article Type:CSO [Editorial Report] Managua EL SEMANARIO in Spanish of 3 to 9 February on page 9 reports that according to the Ministry of Economy and Development, 1994 Free Trade Zone exports will increase 82 percent over last year's, which totaled $14.1 million. The zone, which includes mostly Korean, Taiwanese, and U.S. clothing assembly plants, currently employs 5,300 people, up from 2,918 employees in 1992. Despite the success rate, some companies have had to close while others have faced workers' protests over the ban on unions and problems in labor-management relations, according to the article.
