941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Portsmouth Water Portsmouth Water, the subsidiary of Brockhampton Holdings, yesterday explained its reasons for seeking a referral last month to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission of the Director General of Water Services' proposals for regulation of its charges. It said the K factor, which determines prices over the next 10 years, was lower for Portsmouth than for any of the 10 privatised water companies and harsher than all but two of the 31 companies involved in the periodic review. The K factor of minus 1.5 per cent compared with an average of plus 0.6 per cent for all water companies and plus 1.4 per cent for the industry as a whole. It was therefore seeking a redetermination of its K factor from the MMC.
