January 29, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Revco to Sell 712 Drugstores: Revco said it plans to sell 712 of its nearly 1,900 drugstores in a bid to emerge from Chapter 11 bankruptcy proceedings. The privately held company, based in Twinsburg, Ohio, said it intends to sell stores in 17 Southeast and Midwest states and focus on its strongest operations of more than 1,100 drugstores in 10 Eastern states.
