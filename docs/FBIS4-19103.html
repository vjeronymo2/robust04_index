BFN [Text] This afternoon 60 Italians were evacuated from Rwanda, which is in the throes of a civil war. One of our air force Hercules C-130's took 40 Italians from Kigali to Nairobi. Another 20 Italians left Kigali onboard a Belgian aircraft. The C-130 that evacuated the 40 Italians has taken 80 Italian special boat squadron marines to Kigali, and they have already been deployed alongside Belgian and French paratroopers to defend the airport. Another Italian C-130 is flying from Nairobi to Rwanda.
