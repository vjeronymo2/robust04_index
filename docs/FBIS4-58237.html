BFN [Text] Sanaa, 2 May (MENA) -- An official Interior Ministry source in Sanaa has stated that an officer from the Central Security branch in Aden was the target of an assassination attempt this morning. The source said that Captain Salah al-Khayyat was hit by three bullets and taken to the hospital. He noted that the incident coincided with the imposition of a siege on the Central Security camp in Aden which cut its supply of food, power, communications, and transportation. The source said that the security authorities in Aden are responsibile for capturing the perpetrators and lifting the siege on the camp. He denounced what had happened, describing it as a criminal action, and drew attention to its serious effects on unity, democracy, and security.
