930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / International Capital Markets: FM250m for KOP KOP's 50-year subordinated perpetual bond issue raised FM250m. The loan is to be included in the Finnish bank's Upper Tier 2 capital, Reuter reports from Helsinki. The bank said that the bond would boost its capital adequacy ratio by 0.2 percentage points. At the end of April, KOP's capital adequacy ratio was 9.2 per cent under BIS rules.
