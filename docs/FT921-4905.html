920306 FT 06 MAR 92 / Gas workers offered 4.25% BRITISH GAS has offered its 28,000 craft and manual workers a 4.25 per cent pay increase plus a consoli-dation of bonuses that unions claim will add an extra Pounds 30 a week to the pay of a service engineer who now receives Pounds 295. The GMB general workers' union said the offer would be discussed at a national delegate conference. Mr Eddie Newall, GMB national officer, said the offer represented the best possible outcome of negotiations. It also includes four days' paternity leave, a career-break scheme and a childcare allowance.
