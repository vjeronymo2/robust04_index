930427 FT 27 APR 93 / UK Company News: Investors Capital assets improve Net asset value per share of Investors Capital Trust stood at 132p at March 31, an improvement of 18 per cent on the 111.8p standing at the September 1992 year-end. Available revenue for the half year to end-March amounted to Pounds 5.69m (Pounds 6.21m), equal to earnings of 2.301p (2.51p). The second interim dividend is maintained at 1.275p.
