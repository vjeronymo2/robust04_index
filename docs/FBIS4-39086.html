BFN [Text] The Coordination Council of the Supreme Council held its regular session chaired by Speaker Abish Kekilbayev today. President Nazarbayev participated in its work and spoke at the session. The head of state shared his thoughts with the parliamentarians about the most important foreign political and domestic problems of the Republic's life. Problems of relations between the Legislative and Executive Branches of power were also at issue, as well as the problem of organization of the Supreme Council's work, and its future as a law-making organ.
