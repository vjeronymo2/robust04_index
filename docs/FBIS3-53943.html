Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A 20-man foot patrol belonging to agent Lahd militiamen [South Lebanese Army, SLA] this morning advanced toward the Tallat Zaghlah post and the bank of earth that separates the liberated and occupied areas in the Mimis valley and raked the area indiscriminately for 90 minutes. Israeli helicopters overflew the al-'Urqub hills and the Shab'a township from 0730 for one hour.
