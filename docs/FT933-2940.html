930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / Letters to the Editor: A doubtful attachment Sir, The chief executive of Lloyd's of London likens the introduction of new rules on corporate membership to the attachment of stabilisers to a motorbike (Observer: 'City biker', September 14). Many may think that any rider who needs stabilisers should seek a vehicle more suited to his riding skills. John D Harris, The Green, Easton, Nr Winchester, Hampshire S021 1EG
