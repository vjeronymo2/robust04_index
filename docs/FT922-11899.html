920423 FT 23 APR 92 / UK Company News: Asprey purchases Scottish jeweller Asprey, the Bond Street jeweller which last year bought Mappin & Webb for Pounds 75m, yesterday announced the acquisition of Hamilton & Inches, the Edinburgh jewellers, for an undisclosed amount. Hamilton & Inches, which was founded in 1866 and holds a royal warrant, will have a close association with Garrard, the Crown Jewellers in London which is part of the Asprey group.
