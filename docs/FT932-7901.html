930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / WH Smith pays PM libel damages WH Smith, the newsagent chain, has paid libel damages to the prime minister over the recent New Statesman and Society article which linked him with Westminster caterer Ms Clare Latimer, it was confirmed last night. The out of court settlement of actions brought by Mr Major and Ms Latimer against the company as distributors and retailers of the magazine article was made six weeks ago, a spokesman for WH Smith said. Ms Latimer has also been paid damages.
