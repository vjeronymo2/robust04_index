BFN [Report by RTL-TVI newscaster David Oxley] [Text] A Sabena 737 Boeing is to leave Brussels airport in the hour for Bujumbura. The plane will be used as a shuttle between Bujumbura and Kigali so as to proceed with the evacuation of the Belgian nationals. A maximum of 150 people will be allowed on each flight. A Sabena DC-10 will also leave as soon as possible for Entebbe.
