940402 FT 02 APR 94 / UK Company News: Glenchewton A strong second half performance enabled Glenchewton, the toys, housewares and basketware company, to swing from losses of Pounds 2.2m to profits of Pounds 606,000 pre-tax for 1993. Turnover from continuing operations improved by 19 per cent from Pounds 16.2m to Pounds 19.2m. Earnings per share emerged at 1.89p (losses 9.25p).
