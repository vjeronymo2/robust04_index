930403 FT 03 APR 93 / Kodak to shed 125 jobs this year KODAK, the photographic company, is to shed up to 125 jobs. The company, a subsidiary of the Eastman Kodak Company of the US, said most of the cuts would be at its headquarters at Hemel Hempstead, Hertfordshire. Jobs will be lost 'across the board' in manufacturing, marketing and administration during the rest of the year. The total workforce at Hemel Hempstead is 1,300. The company said: 'In the current economic climate we have to keep costs down.'
