BFN [Text] Damascus, 11 Jun (SANA)--President Hafiz al-Asad received 'Abdallah Ghanim, minister of justice in the Republic of Yemen, at al-Rawdah Palace at 1800 today. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', Justice Minister Dr. 'Abdallah Tulbah, and Yemeni Charge d'Affaires 'Abdallah al-Iryani.
