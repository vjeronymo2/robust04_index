Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A street demonstration is planned to support the resolutions of the popular congress. A rally is planned for today in Port-au-Prince to prompt the Haitian legislators to adopt the resolutions of the congress held in the National Theater from 18 to 20 January. The congress had asked for the formation of a consensus government to solve the crisis. Let us listen to a spokesman for the popular congress: [Begin unidentified spokesman recording, in Creole] We are asking all citizens who are aware, as we are, of the seriousness of the situation to express solidarity with the Government of Public Security as an attempt to solve the crisis that we have been facing for too long. It is to undermine the bases of [word indistinct]. [end recording] That was the assistant secretary general of the popular congress held on 18, 19 and 20 January by the Group of the Great North. Another demonstration is due to be held this morning in Port-au-Prince in front of the parliament building to demand the lifting of the international sanctions and the embargo.
