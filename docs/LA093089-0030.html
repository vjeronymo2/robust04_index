September 30, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP

Moorpark 34, Nordhoff 7 -- The Musketeers rushed for 330 yards and five touchdowns at home to improve to 4-0, their best start since 1962.

Tony Upshaw gained 152 yards in 14 carries and scored two touchdowns -- his 11th and 12th of the season -- for Moorpark, despite playing in only the first half because of a slightly sprained ankle.

Upshaw was replaced in the second half by Chris Aguilar, who gained 114 yards in nine carries. LANCE O'FALLON
