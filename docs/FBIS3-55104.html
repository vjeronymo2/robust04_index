Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Zurab Argun] [Text] Sukhumi January 5 TASS -- A delegation of the Russian Federation led by Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Boris Pastukhov arrived in Sukhumi on Wednesday. In an interview with ITAR-TASS Pastukhov said that the delegation arrived in Abkhazia to familiarise itself with the situation in Abkhazia on the eve of Georgian-Abkhazian talks due to begin in Geneva on January 11. A meeting between Pastukhov and Vladislav Ardzinba was held in the residence of the Abkhazian parliament chairman. After the talks with Abkhazian leaders the Russian delegation met with the people of the Abkhazian capital Sukhumi and visited districts damaged during military operations. Pastukhov positively assessed efforts made by the Abkhazian government to eliminate the after-effects of the war and improve the social position of the Abkhazian population. Pastukhov expressed the hope for the speediest solution of problems connected with the return of refugees who fled the republic during the war. On Thursday, the Russian delegation is leaving for Moscow.
