Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The secretary general of the National Chemical, Mining, Energy Industry Workers Trade Union analyzed how the economic problems the country is facing have had an effect on the nickel industry and have limited its potential. At the same time, he acknowledged the sustained effort of the work collectives to place the industry on a path to steady recovery. During a meeting with representatives of the National Chemical, Mining, Energy Industry Workers Trade Union, he called for the prompt introduction of an important program of organizational, labor, and wage reforms from the administrative directives to boost the recovery of the industry and achieve a greater interest by workers in the results of their activity.
