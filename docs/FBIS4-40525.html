BFN [Report by Lieutenant Colonel Sergey Prangishvili and Captain Vitaliy Denisov: "Joint Studies for Russian and Georgian Military"] [Text] A course for commanders and deputy commanders of combined and other units began on 15 June at the Batumi garrison under the direction of Colonel General Fedor Reut, commander of the Group of Russian Forces in the Transcaucasus. A delegation from the Georgian Defense Ministry headed by Brigadier General Guram Nikolaishvili, first deputy defense minister, is attending the course. In conversation with your KRASNAYA ZVEZDA correspondent, Gen Guram Niklaishvili stated: "Our contacts with the Russian military-political leadership and the command of the Russian troops in the Transcaucasus are being strengthened constantly. The present event is by no means the first in a series of theoretical and practical courses based on the Group of Russian Forces in the Transcaucasus that our generals and other officers have taken part in."
