940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Lombard Insurance LOMBARD INSURANCE said that of its placing and intermediaries offer of 20.63m ordinary 10p shares at 160p apiece, 15.47m were placed firm and 5.16m were placed conditionally - subject to recall for the purposes of meeting valid applications under the intermediaries offer. By May 13, valid applications were received in respect of 327,286 shares, of which 164,200 had been received from intermediaries and 163,086 from priority applicants. Valid acceptances will be satisfied in full.
