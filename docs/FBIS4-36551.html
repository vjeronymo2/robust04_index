BFN [Text] Under the supervision of Attorney General Counselor Muhammad 'Arafah, the North al-Jizah prosecution office has begun investigations with Muhammad Hamid Abu-al-Nasr, general guide of the Muslim Brotherhood, in the case of the seizure of leaflets bearing his name which were distributed by some students during the 'Id al-Adha holiday. The leaflets carried incitements against the government and called for the overthrow of the regime and the changing of the Constitution by force.
