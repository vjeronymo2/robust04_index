BFN [Text] Russia says it is ready to provide India its technologies for space exploration, aviation, ship building, and transport sectors. The leader of the Russian Center for Scientific and Cultural Cooperation, Ms. Valentina Tereshkova, said this in New Delhi yesterday. She is now in India as part of a Russian delegation which has already visited Bangalore and held discussions with the representatives of the Indian aerospace industry. Ms. Tereshkova is the first woman in space. In an interview, she said the forthcoming visit of the prime minister, Mr. Narasimha Rao, to Moscow will give a new impetus to the development of bilateral relations, particularly in cultural and scientific fields.
