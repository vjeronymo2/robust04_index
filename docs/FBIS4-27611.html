BFN [Text] Senator Wigberto Tanada, a human rights advocate, has called on the Indonesian Government to stand for the people of East Timor. Tanada made the statement during his opening speech at the start of the four-day conference on East Timor held at the Malcolm Hall of the University of the Philippines. The senator stressed his support for the people of East Timor who are seeking independence from the Indonesian Government. In this regard, Tanada strongly criticized the weak national leadership which yields to Indonesia's wishes.
