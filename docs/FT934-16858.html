931004 FT 04 OCT 93 / Construction Contracts: Business centre in Budapest JAIDO (JAPAN INTER NATIONAL DEVELOPMENT ORGANISATION), NICHIMEN CORPORATION of Japan and UNIVERSALE BAU AG of Austria, have completed a joint venture agreement to develop the DM31m (Pounds 12.5m) Central Business Centre (CBC) in Budapest, Hungary. The mixed use development of about 8,000 sq metres comprises offices, serviced business suites, retail and residential areas. Located on Horvatutca in Budapest's second district (on the Buda side of the River Danube), construction is due to commence in February 1994 for completion in August 1995.
