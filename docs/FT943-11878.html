940728 FT 28 JUL 94 / Office demand in London sustained Increased demand for London office accommodation has been sustained in the first six months of this year although property yields remain flat, according to separate reports from commercial property estate agents published yesterday. Savills says 4.06m sq ft of London office space was occupied in the first six months of this year. A separate study by estate agents Hillier Parker said that commercial property yields had remained flat for the second month in succession.
