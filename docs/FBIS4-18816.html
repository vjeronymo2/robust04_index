BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] The Constitutional Court has decided to close the Greens Party. The Constitutional Court's decision published in today's edition of the OFFICIAL GAZETTE states that the Greens Party was closed because it did not submit the final records for the 1988 accounting period, although warned beforehand, within the statutory time period. According to the Constitutional Court's decision, the assets of the Greens Party will be transferred to the Treasury.
