921130 FT 30 NOV 92 / Observer: Suitable Still on the subject of bank-bashing, have you noticed Barclays Bank's latest advertising campaign - Starting a business this year? It promises 'all the practical, actionable advice you need to turn your dream into a reality'. The dictionary's definition of actionable is 'furnishing ground for an action of lawsuit'. Hasn't Barclays got enough lawsuits on its hands already?
