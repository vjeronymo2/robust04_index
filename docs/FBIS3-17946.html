Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Poltava, 28 March -- Elections were valid in all of the Poltava region's 16 constituencies. Votes were cast by 1,103,473 out of 1,315,593 of those registered, which represents 84.3 percent. The only one to have been elected outright was Colonel Hryhoriy Omelchenko, Security Service of Ukraine officer.
