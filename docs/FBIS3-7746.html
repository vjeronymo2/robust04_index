Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] There are 10.4 billion Slovak korunas [Sk] earmarked in the 1994 state budget for the Slovak Defense Ministry. The sum is Sk2.2 billion higher than in 1993. However, this year's expenditures include payments into the Health Insurance Fund, hospital care, and pension insurance, as well as a contribution of Sk30 million for Slovakia's top sports and financial support of Sk34 million for studies at nonmilitary schools.
