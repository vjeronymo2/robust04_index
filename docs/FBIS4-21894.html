BFN [Excerpt] Asyut, 7 Apr (MENA)--Asyut Governor Samih al-Sa'id has praised local leaders who helped in the surrender of a number of extremist leaders to security organs. He said that these extremists have repented and returned to the right path in society. His remarks came after six extremist terrorists voluntarily surrendered themselves to the security organs in Asyut Governorate after becoming convinced that the wrong ideas they believed in were corrupt. The six repentant terrorists are: Rushdi Sayyid Muhammad, Hasan 'Abd-al-Rasul, Hasan Ibrahim Mahmud, 'Abd-al-Badi' Rashwan, 'Abd-al- Halim 'Ali 'Abd-al-Halim, and al-Qadhdhafi 'Abd-al-Raziq 'Ali.
