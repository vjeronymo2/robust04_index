940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Embassy Property Following a year of change, Embassy Property Group, the development and investment company, reported a pre-tax loss of Pounds 852,000 for the year to March 31, against profits of Pounds 1.25m. Just before the end of the period the company completed a complex restructuring together with a Pounds 8.7m acquisition and a Pounds 3m Chinese investment. Net assets at March 31 were Pounds 18.1m. The company said that prospects were encouraging. Further acquisitions were planned. Turnover for the year was Pounds 9.12m (Pounds 11.9m). The pre-tax figure was struck after an exceptional gain on the restructuring of Pounds 489,000 (Pounds 6.72m) and lower net interest charges of Pounds 160,000 (Pounds 347,000). Losses per share were 0.7p, against earnings of 2.3p.
