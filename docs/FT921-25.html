920331 FT 31 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Murray Split net assets at 138.7p Murray Split Capital Trust, which began on July 31, reported net asset value at February 29 of 138.7p per capital share, 106.6p per zero dividend preference share, and 51.2p per income share. Net revenue for the 38 weeks to end-February was Pounds 560,384 for earnings per income share of 7p. A second interim dividend of 3.3p is declared, making 5.8p so far.
