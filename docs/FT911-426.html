910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / Greenpeace reports submarine problems THE ROYAL NAVY is having difficulty maintaining continuous patrols by submarines that provide the UK's Polaris nuclear deterrent, according to Greenpeace, the environmental pressure group. Greenpeace said one of the four submarines has had to double the normal eight-week length of its patrols and has been on patrol for 10 of the last 16 months. Another, HMS Revenge, has been in service for a record 8 1/2 years since its last refit.
