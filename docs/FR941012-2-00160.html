SUMMARY: Notice is hereby given that the United States Trade Representative (USTR) has submitted the report published herein to the Committee on Finance of the United States Senate and the Committee on Ways and Means of the United States House of Representatives identifying trade expansion priorities pursuant to Executive Order 12901 of March 3, 1994.
