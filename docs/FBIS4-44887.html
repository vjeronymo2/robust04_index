I. Main Points of the 1985 Telecommunications Reform
A. Establishment of the Constitution Which Makes Entry into All Fields Possible 1. Introduction of the Principle of Competition to Carriers Which Establish Their Own Telecommunications Circuit Facilities (Classified as Type I carriers) 2. Liberalization of Circuit Usage (Classified as Type II carriers) 3. Deregulation of Terminal Equipment (Liberalization of telephone sets) B. Improvement of Business Efficiency by the Privatization of NTT 1. Change of the Regulations on Tariffs (Decision of the Diet Approval by the Minister) 2. Abolition of Approval by the Minister for NTT's Budget 3. Elimination of the Regulation on NTT's Investment
