Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, Jan 13, IRNA -- Iran's Agriculture minister 'Isa Kalantari, currently in Manila, met with President Fidel Ramos Thursday and called for direct trade between Iran and the Philippines without intervention of a third party. Kalantari also underlined Iran's strategic status in the Persian Gulf particularly after the establishment of the newly independent Central Asian republics. Ramos welcomed the idea and expressed desire for further collaboration between Iran and the Philippines in all areas. He said Tehran and Manila could meet each other's needs through their recources and facilities. Kalantari also conveyed the greetings of President Akbar Hashemi- Rafsanjani to Ramos and briefed him on the agricultural developments in Iran.
