920916 FT 16 SEP 92 / The ERM and Maastricht: Odds slashed on Yes vote The British bookmaker Ladbrokes yesterday slashed its odds on a Yes vote in Sunday's French referendum on Maastricht after a flurry of betting that the treaty would be endorsed, Reuter reports from London. The odds for a Yes were cut from 2 to 5 to 1 to 5, while punters betting on a No vote could get odds of 3 to 1. 'We have been swamped with bets for a Yes vote since first thing Monday,' a Ladbrokes official said.
