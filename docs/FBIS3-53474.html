Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] This evening Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa met with Dr. Nabil Sha'th, head of the Palestinian delegation to the Taba talks. The meeting was attended by Palestinian Ambassador in Cairo Sa'id Kamal and Fatah Representative Zudhi al-Qidrah. Musa and Sha'th reviewed the results of the meetings of the Taba committee and the security committee which were held last week. They also discussed the obstacles still obstructing the implementation of the Palestinian-Israeli Declaration of Principles.
