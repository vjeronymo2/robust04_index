Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] China and Pakistan have signed a protocol on bilateral cooperation in the fields of science and technology. Twenty projects will be undertaken by the two sides under the protocol during this year. China will undertake 12 projects in Pakistan, while Pakistan will undertake eight projects in China in various fields. China has also offered training to Pakistani scientists and engineers in various fields. The protocol has been signed in Beijing in the wake of an agreement between the two countries for cooperation in science and technology reached during the recent visit of Prime Minister Ms. Benazir Bhutto.
