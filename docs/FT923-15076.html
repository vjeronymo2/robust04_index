920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / Parliament and Politics: Labour's Scots support surges LABOUR has surged to a 20 percentage point lead over the Conservatives in Scotland according to an opinion poll. The System Three survey for The Herald showed Labour on 44 per cent, 5 points up on the previous month, the Tories on 24 per cent, (down 2) the SNP on 22 per cent, (down 1) and the Liberal Democrats on 8 per cent, (down 2).
