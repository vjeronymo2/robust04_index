941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / International Company News: Moore reverses three-year sales slide in quarter New strategies, restructuring and changes at the top have reversed a three-year slide in sales at Moore, the Toronto-based international business forms and information services group. Third-quarter net profit was USDollars 30.5m, or 31 cents a share, on sales of Dollars 584m, against USDollars 16.5m, or 17 cents, on sales of Dollars 566m a year earlier. Moore has changed its traditional forms operations towards electronic-based systems, direct mail and labelling. It has eliminated 1,500 jobs worldwide. Nine-month net profit was USDollars 82.2m, or 83 cents a share, against USDollars 60.6m, or 61 cents.
