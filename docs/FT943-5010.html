940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Panther wins Etonbrook Props Mr Andrew Perloff has gained control of Etonbrook Properties. The offer by his Panther Securities, an investment and property dealer, has been accepted by holders of 397,351 shares representing 8.12 per cent of the voting rights taking the company's share of the voting rights to 50.45 per cent. The offer is now unconditional.
