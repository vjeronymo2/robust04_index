BFN [From the "Telepress" newscast] [Text] The National Council on Belize, which will be in charge of conducting a general and thorough evaluation of the dispute over that territory, was officially installed today. An agreement to update and reorganize the National Council on Belize specifies that the entity will do studies and planning and will advise the Foreign Ministry on all matters related to the territorial dispute with Belize. The Council will be headed by Foreign Minister Gladys Maritza Ruiz de Vielman and will be made up of the following persons, who will work without compensation: Fernando Andrade Diaz Duran, Roberto Bermejo Gonzalez, Eduardo Castillo Arriola, Erwin Fernando Guzman Ovalle, (Alberto Errarte Gonzalez), Roberto Herrera Ibarguen, Carlos Larios Ochaita, Jorge Lujan Munoz, Oscar Clemente Marroquin Godoy, Gabriel Orellana Rojas, Mario Quinonez Amezquita, Jorge Skinner Klee, and Francisco Villagran Kramer. The 8 June agreement states that the territorial dispute with Belize and the search for a solution within the framework of the Constitution and the principles of international law are matters of national interest. The document recognizes the appropriateness of the Foreign Ministry receiving the advice of qualified citizens who, based on their knowledge and capabilities, can help formulate an adequate policy to resolve the territorial dispute with Belize.
