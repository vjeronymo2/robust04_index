September 23, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

SUNNY HILLS ROLLS OVER LA ZERTE, 51-0

Brian Pizula scored three touchdowns Friday as Sunny Hills High School defeated La Zerte of Edmonton, 51-0.

"It's just a matter of experience," said Pizula, who enjoyed playing on the 65-yard wide Canadian field. "I like this field, I wish we had it back home."

Dan Wackerman added a pair of touchdowns for Sunny Hills, which defeated La Zerte, 28-6, last September at home.

"They hit much harder than they did last year," Sunny Hills Coach Tim Devaney said. "They shut us down early tonight."

Sunny Hills scored only a touchdown in the first period Friday, adding 15 points in the second quarter, 14 in the third quarter and 15 in the fourth quarter. MARK SPECTOR
