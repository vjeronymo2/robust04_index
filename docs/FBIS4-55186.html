BFN [Text] U.S. Vice President Al Gore this evening telephoned Alija Izetbegovic, president of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Presidency, to talk about the upcoming quadripartite meeting in Geneva with Bosnia-Herzegovina as the main topic. The second in command in the U.S. Administration appealed to President Izetbegovic for flexibility to ensure the latest efforts by the international community to find a political solution to Bosnia are successful. We shall do our utmost to bring about peace that will guarantee our people all their rights, Izetbegovic told U.S. Vice President Al Gore during this evening's telephone conversation.
