September 12, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

UCI MEN LOSE IN SOCCER TO SAN DIEGO STATE

David Quesada and Greg Cipolla scored in overtime for San Diego State, which defeated UC Irvine, 4-2, in a nonconference men's soccer match Tuesday at Irvine.

Jeff Wale scored early in the first half for San Diego State (4-1). Irvine (1-3-1) came back to take a 2-1 lead on first-half goals by John O'Brien and Erik Nasarenko.
