BFN [Text] Israeli warplanes conducted mock raids over the Iqlim al-Tuffah Heights at 1345 today. The mock raids followed earlier Israeli aggression on 'Arab Salim and Humin. The aggression coincides with an Israeli warning broadcast by Israeli radio today. The radio quoted a military spokesman as saying: Troops may carry out a large-scale attack against Resistance positions in the south and they are ready to carry out their mission the moment they receive their orders.
