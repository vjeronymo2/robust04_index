March 12, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

WEATHER FORECAST: IT'S IN THE (TIMES) BAG

Re meteorology-Orange County: I am bemused by the weather antics on television of Doctor George, Willard Scott, et al., as they are almost invariably "wrong."

For the 20 years I have lived in Anaheim, Orange, Irvine and Newport Beach, I have found a far more reliable forecaster of weather: my Los Angeles Times delivery person!

If, when I walk out to the driveway to retrieve my Times, it is in a plastic cover, I know we will have rain in the near future -- and dress accordingly.

If the good doctor and Willard forecast rain, and my Times is unwrapped, I dress for a fine and clear day.

It works. Try it!

WARREN ISHAM

Orange
