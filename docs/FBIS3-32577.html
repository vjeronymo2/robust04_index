Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Pedro Ross Leal, secretary general of the Cuban Workers Federation [CTC], called for the necessary measures to guarantee the current sugar harvest, during a meeting with the CTC's national secretariat. Ross underscored the importance of enlisting all organizations and entities, along with the workers of the sugar industry, in the solution of the problems that could arise from the resource deficit during the campaign. Another one of the topics analyzed was the assemblies of production and services, which should evaluate economic efficiency based on the suggestions made at the workers parliaments.
