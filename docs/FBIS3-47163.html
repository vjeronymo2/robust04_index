Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Approximately 800 students of the National Federation of General Student Councils [Hanchongnyon] staged a demonstration in front of the U.S. Embassy in Seoul to protest U.S. pressure to open ROK markets. After assembling at the City Hall subway station and other places, the students occupied the street in front of the embassy and staged a sit-in for 40 minutes. All the students were taken to the police station and are now under questioning.
