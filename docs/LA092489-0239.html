September 24, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

Norte Vista 14, Rancho Alamitos 7 -- Clint Jordan scored on a 22-yard pass from Ron Poindexter in the first quarter, and Frank Ruiz scored on a 1-yard run in the second quarter to lead the hosts at Riverside City College.

Dana Ruiz scored on a 14-yard run in the fourth quarter for Rancho Alamitos (1-2). Norte Vista improved its record to 2-1 with the victory.
