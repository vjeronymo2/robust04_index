931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / Warship yard workers walk out FIVE HUNDRED white-collar and supervisory workers at Yarrows, the Glasgow warship yard, yesterday walked out on indefinite strike in protest at a pay offer - said by the company to amount to a rise of 1.8 per cent - and further performance-related increases of up to 2.6 per cent. The company said the action would have no immediate effect on production.
