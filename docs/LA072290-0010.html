July 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: STALLONE'S CHALLENGE

Meanwhile, Brown has another project in development, "The Player," based on a novel by Michael Tolkin, that could send real chills down Tinseltown spines: Set in "the New Hollywood," it's about a screenwriter who stalks and threatens the life of a studio executive . . . for not returning phone calls. Daniel Cerone
