Proposed Commentary .05 provides that members and member organizations shall properly communicate, in accordance with Rule 6.24, final exercise decisions to the Exchange in respect of options positions for which they are responsible. 12 Additionally, Commentary .05 also will provide that member organizations may establish an internal processing cut-off time prior to the Exercise Cut-Off Time at which time final exercise decisions from their customers will no longer be accepted by them for expiring options. 13 12 See Amendment No. 2, supra note 3. 13 Id . Finally, proposed Commentary .06 provides that if a member submits or prepares an exercise instruction after the Exercise Cut-Off Time in any expiring option on the basis of material information that is released after the Exercise Cut-Off Time, then such activity is inconsistent with just and equitable principles of trade. 14 14 Id . The Exchange believes that the proposed rule change is consistent with Section 6 of the Act, in general, and furthers the objectives of Section 6(b)(5) of the Act, in particular, in that it is designed to prevent fraudulent and manipulative acts and practices and to promote just and equitable principles of trade.
