BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 May (MENA)--President Muhammad Husni Mubarak this morning received a telephone call from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih who explained the developments in Yemen over the past 24 hours. President Mubarak stressed the need to halt military operations between the two sides immediately, contain the worsening situation, and take measures to initiate a dialogue for the good of the Yemeni people. During the telephone conversation, President Mubarak warned of the Yemeni arena turning into an uncontrollable all-out war. Yesterday, President Mubarak addressed an appeal to the Yemeni leaders asking them to stop the military operations. He urged President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, as head of the country, to adopt decisions and initiatives to preserve the Yemeni people's interests.
