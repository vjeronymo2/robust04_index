BFN [Article by "ab": "What a Survey Showed"] [Text] Bratislava--According to a representative public-opinion survey carried out by the Focus Agency--the Center for Social and Marketing Analysis--33.9 percent of respondents definitely agree with the dismissal of Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar. Between 16 and 23 March, 1,066 respondents were asked their view of the dismissal of Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar by the National Council of the Slovak Republic. Another 17.5 percent "rather agree," 16.4 percent "rather disagree," 24.3 percent "definitely disagree" with the dismissal, and 7.8 percent of respondents are unable to assess the situation. The Focus Agency made the results of the survey available to our editorial office yesterday.
