Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Zou Aiguo (6760 1947 0948)] [Excerpts] Beijing, 1 Jan (XINHUA) -- The Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] National Committee held a new year reception at the CPPCC Auditorium this morning. Leading comrades Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, Qiao Shi, Li Ruihuan, Zhu Rongji, Liu Huaqing, Hu Jintao, and Rong Yiren joined some 400 people from all walks of life in the capital to celebrate the advent of the year 1994. Li Ruihuan, chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, officiated at the reception. In his speech, Jiang Zemin, general secretary of the CPC Central Committee, president of the state, and chairman of the Central Military Commission extended -- on behalf of the CPC Central Committee, the State Council, and the Central Military Commission -- warm solicitude and noble tribute to the democratic parties and patriotic personages without party affiliation; to workers, peasants, intellectuals and cadres of all nationalities across the country; and to Liberation Army commanders and fighters, Armed Police officers and men, and public security cadres and policemen; as well as sincere holiday greetings and best wishes to compatriots in Taiwan, Hong Kong and Macao, and overseas Chinese, and foreign friends who have concerned themselves with, supported, and assisted China's modernization construction. [passage omitted] Also attending the reception were leading comrades: Ding Guangen, Li Lanqing, Yang Baibing, Zou Jiahua, Wei Jianxing, Wen Jiabao, Wang Hanbin, Ren Jianxin, Ni Zhifu, Chen Muhua, Fei Xiaotong, Sun Qimeng, Lei Jieqiong, Li Ximing, Wang Guangying, Cheng Siyuan, Lu Jiaxi, Buhe, Li Peiyao, Wu Jieping, Luo Gan, Wu Xueqian, Yang Rudai, Wang Zhaoguo, Seypidin Aze, Hong Xuezhi, Qian Weichang, Hu Sheng, Qian Zheng, and Sun Fuling. Attending the reception were also officials from democratic parties and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, members of the National People's Congress Standing Committee, members of the CPPCC National Committee Standing Committee in Beijing, officials from people's organizations, and celebrities from various circles. Artists from the Central Nationalities Philharmonic Orchestra, the China Song and Dance Theatrical Company, and the Central Song and Dance Troupe staged colorful performances at the gathering.
