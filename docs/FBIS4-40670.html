BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe 20 June TASS -- Russia border guards foiled an attempt at an armed penetration of the territory of Tajikistan from Afghanistan by a small group of Tajik opposition in Shuroabad border sector guarded by the Moskovskiy Border Detachment on the night from Sunday to Monday. The border gourds opened artillery fire on the crossing area, forcing the intruders to withdraw to the contiguous bank. Foe losses are unknown. There were no losses among Russian servicemen, military sources in Dushanbe told ITAR-TASS. In all, over the past 48 hours, border guards thwarted six border crossing attempts. The situation remained relatively calm in the other sectors of the Tajik-Afghan border.
