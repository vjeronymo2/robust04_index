Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 13 (XINHUA) -- The Bank of China has recently issued 500 million U.S. dollars worth of Yankee Bonds in the United States as part of its effort to support domestic economic construction. The issue of the bonds, in terms of five years or twenty years, is the first attempt by the bank to raise funds on the U.S. securities market. These bonds are the largest total with the longest terms so far issued by the Bank of China overseas. The U.S. Morgan Stanley Investment Bank has been the main contractor for sale of the bonds. The money raised by issuing the bonds will be invested in the development of China's domestic projects for communications and energy resources.
