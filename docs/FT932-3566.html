930614 FT 14 JUN 93 / Audit abolition plan attacked ABOLISHING the requirement for small companies to have an annual audit would reduce standards and increase the rate of business failures, the Chartered Association of Certified Accountants warned yesterday. The association was responding to Department of Trade and Industry proposals published earlier this year to lift the requirement for a statutory annual audit for companies below the registration threshold for value added tax - presently Pounds 37,600 a year.
