August 28, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

BRONCOS' SMITH OUT FOR SEASON

The Denver Broncos today placed offensive lineman Monte Smith, who accidentally shot himself in the foot while cleaning a gun last Tuesday, on the "non-football injury" reserve list.

Smith, a second-year player, will be sidelined for the season. He underwent surgery to repair a fractured navicular bone in his right foot.

He was considered one of the most valuable backups on the team.
