940312 FT 12 MAR 94 / International Company News: US broker trades Norsk Hydro shares ahead of rights issue announcement A US broker made a large disposal of Norsk Hydro shares in New York on Thursday ahead of the rights issue announcement. A total of 721,900 Hydro shares were traded in New York, compared with normal daily trading volume of between 100,000 and 130,000 shares. The shares fell around 4 per cent to NKr243 in Oslo yesterday.
