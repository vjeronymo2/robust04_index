940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / World Trade News: Sweden agrees rail link contracts The Swedish government yesterday agreed to place contracts for a SKr4.5bn (Dollars 588m) private sector rail link to Stockholm airport, Reuter reports from Stockholm. The decision confirms contracts for five companies, three Swedish and two British-based, in the construction and engineering consortium Arlanda Link Consortium. John Mowlem Construction and GEC Alsthom will supply rails, signals and trainsets. Swedish builders NCC, Siab and Kraftbyggarna will do construction worth over SKr2bn.
