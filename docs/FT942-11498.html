940505 FT 05 MAY 94 / UK Company News: UK Estates Pounds 6m property purchase UK Estates has agreed to acquire Market Square House, a 63,000 sq ft freehold shop and office property in the centre of Nottingham, for Pounds 6m cash. The purchase increases the company's portfolio by Pounds 7m to Pounds 40m. It is its first large deal since its Pounds 8.1m fund raising and buy-out of its joint venture with Philips Pension Fund in March.
