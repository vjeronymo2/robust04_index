BFN [Text] Yasir 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, this morning received His Excellency Mr. Yahya al-Arashi, the Yemeni civil service minister and personal presidential envoy of President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, the head of the presidential council of the Yemeni Republic. The minister put the brother president completely in the picture regarding the situation in Yemen and developments in the military and political arenas. President 'Arafat stressed to the minister the importance of Yemeni unity and the need to halt the current fighting for the benefit of the Yemeni people and the region. President 'Arafat also emphasized to the minister that he was greatly concerned over what was happening in Yemen and that he was following the situation there closely. He expressed his deep regret over what was happening.
