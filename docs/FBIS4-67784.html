BFN [Text] Tehran, April 18, IRNA -- Head of Iran's Atomic Energy Organization, Reza Amrollahi, and secretary general of the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA), Hans Blix, discussed declaring the Middle East region a nuclear free zone (NFZ) Monday. At the meeting Blix welcomed Iran's proposal on promoting technical cooperation between Iran and IAEA. Blix arrived here Monday morning to hold talks with Iranian officials on joint cooperation. During his two day stay here Blix is expected to hold discussions with senior Iranian officials.
