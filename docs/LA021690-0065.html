February 16, 1990, Friday, Southland Edition

CARGO SHIP SINKS OFF FRANCE; 12 MISSING

A Maltese cargo ship carrying a load of cement apparently sank off western France and 12 sailors are missing, the coast guard reported Thursday.

It said the Scan Trader sent a distress signal Sunday night as 96-m.p.h. winds whipped through the Bay of Biscay. Searchers found no sign of the vessel, but lifejackets from the ship washed up on the beaches at Arcachon on Thursday, appearing to confirm fears that the vessel had gone down, the coast guard said.

The ship left Bilbao, Spain, on Saturday.
