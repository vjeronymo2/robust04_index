Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Staff General Husayn Kamil Hasan, minister of industry and minerals and supervisor of the Military Industrialization Organization [MIO], today inaugurated a plant for the production of compressors at the State Enterprise for Electrical Industries. The plant has an annual production capacity of 500,000 compressors. As he toured the plant's sections, the minister was acquainted with the progress of the production operations. He gave instructions for increasing production to serve the manufacturing of locally-made freezers and refrigerators, of which compressors constitute a basic part. Staff Gen. Husayn Kamil Hasan also inaugurated a comprehensive irrigation pumps fair at the State Enterprise for Mechanical Industries. During a conversation with workers at the enterprise, the minister said that the zealous personnel of the Ministry of Industry and Minerals, with the effective support of the MIO formations, are presenting this accomplishment as a gift to the leader of the march, leader President Saddam Husayn. He said that after the country's need for chemical and phosphatic fertilizers has been fully met in the service of the agricultural sector, this distinguished effort constitutes a new technological link in defiance of the circumstances of the unjust blockade. An Iraqi News Agency correspondent said that the State Enterprise for Mechanical Industries has put into action its productive plan for this year. This plan aims at producing and manufacturing thousands of pumps of various sizes through 100 percent Iraqi designs, molds, and technological methods.
