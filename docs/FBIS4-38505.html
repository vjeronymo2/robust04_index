BFN [Text] Moscow, May 30, RIA -- "Russia insists on an unconditional fulfilment in full volume of all resolutions of the UN Security Council with respect to Iraq", said the chairman of the State Duma, Ivan Rybkin, in the course of his meeting today with the chairman of the National Assembly of Kuwait, Ahmad 'Abd-al-'Aziz al- Sa'dun who is on an official visit to Russia. "The position of Russia is immutable since 1990 when Iraq started its aggression against Kuwait", Ivan Rybkin pointed out. For his own part, the chairman of the Kuwait parliament expressed gratitude for Russia's actions in the UN Security Council and the hope that they would continue to contribute to the final settlement of the problem. According to the chairman of the Kuwait National Assembly, "peace in the region can only be established after a final demarcation of the border between Kuwait and Iraq and the completion of the exchange of prisoners".
