BFN [From the "Diplomatic Panorama" feature: Reports by Diplomatic Correspondents Igor Porshnev, Vitaly Trubetskoy, and others] [Text] In the near term Russia should ratify the agreement on the leasing of the Baykonur cosmodrome signed with Kazakhstan. Early May the Russian Space Agency (RSA) and the Russian Foreign Ministry intend to prepare all the necessary documents related to the agreement on the leasing of the cosmodrome so that Russian President Boris Yeltsin would present them to the State Duma for consideration. A top-ranking employee of the RSA reported to Interfax that the text of Yeltsin's letter which explains the need to ratify the agreement as soon as possible should be prepared by Friday. The RSA representative underlined that "the presidents' signatures dated March 28 are not enough to hand over the cosmodrome for lease." "The sum totalling 115 million dollars (for the lease) is a large amount money and the decision on its allocation should be included into the budget of the Russian Federation," stressed he. "If this does not take place Russia will remain without Baykonur," he said.
