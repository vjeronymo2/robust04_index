Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Agreement on Russia's unfreezing last year's credit to Armenia, amounting to 24,000 million rubles [R], has been reached at a meeting between the two countries' prime ministers, Viktor Chernomyrdin and Grant Bagratyan. According to the SNARK agency, Moscow will also grant Yerevan credit totaling R10,000 million for rehabilitation work in the earthquake area. Additionally, the sides decided during the talks to consider, before 20 January, the question of the joint running of the Armenian nuclear power station.
