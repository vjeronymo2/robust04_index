September 25, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: HOLYFIELD-FOREMAN FIGHT IN THE WORKS

A heavyweight title fight between Evander Holyfield and George Foreman is in the works -- if Holyfield dethrones undisputed champion Buster Douglas on Oct. 25 and Foreman continues to beat his unheralded opponents, Newsday reported.

According to promoter Bob Arum, "an agreement in principle" for such a fight has been reached, as long as the two heavyweights keep up their ends of the bargain.

Tonight Foreman (68-2, 64 knockouts) will earn $250,000 to fight Terry Anderson (19-3, 17 knockouts) in a 10-round bout at the London Arena.
