April 14, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WINDS OF TRADE SWITCH DIRECTION

If there is still anyone who thinks the Kings made a horrible deal in trading Bernie Nicholls for Tomas Sandstrom and Tony Granato, it's evident they don't know a thing about hockey.

Contrary to what most of the critics have been muttering, it will probably go down as the second-best trade in Kings' history. (If you have to ask what the first was, then you probably don't have an opinion to begin with.)

DAN BURNETT, Glendora
