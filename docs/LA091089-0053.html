September 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NO FLOW

While I was thrilled to see my home state of Vermont pictured on the front of the Travel Section ("Autumn in New England," Aug. 20), I must point out an error in the caption. The Black River is that which is shown on the front page. The Williams River does not flow through Ludlow. At least it didn't flow through Ludlow the last time I saw it! Nonetheless, thank you for showing Californians the splendor of New England. I only hope you didn't encourage too many of them to visit.

STEPHEN J. FRAZIER

San Diego
