930518 FT 18 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Losses deepen at Jermyn Investment Pre-tax losses at Jermyn Investment, the property investor, grew by Pounds 58,000 to Pounds 831,000 in the year to end-December. However a lower tax charge of Pounds 42,000, against Pounds 72,000 left losses per share lower at 45.06p, compared with 49.75p. Net interest payable increased from Pounds 261,000 to Pounds 728,000, and there was a further write-down in the value of investments amounting to Pounds 302,000 (Pounds 650,000). There is no dividend this year. A final of 1.5p was paid last time.
