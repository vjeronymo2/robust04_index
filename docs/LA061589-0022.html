June 15, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: ART

The first major Georgia O'Keeffe exhibit in two decades, which will end a blockbuster national tour Sunday at the Los Angeles County Museum of Art, has become the museum's fourth most popular show ever. The 101-piece survey has already been seen here by 256,000 visitors, topping attendance figures for the museum's successful 1988 David Hockney retrospective, a spokeswoman said Wednesday.ALEENE MacMINN
