940207 FT 07 FEB 94 / Court rejects BCCI plea An Abu Dhabi criminal court has rejected pleas by defence lawyers to release former BCCI officials who have been held in the emirate since 1991 when the failed bank was shut down, Reuter reports from Abu Dhabi. The court trying 13 former Bank of Credit and Commerce International officials adjourned until February 19 the hearing in the biggest court case to stem from the bank's collapse.
