Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 28 (XINHUA) -- Two agreements between the governments of China and the Republic of Korea (ROK) were signed here today. They are an agreement on cultural cooperation, and another on the avoidance of double income taxation and the prevention of tax evasion, the 36th of its kind between China and foreign countries. According to the cultural agreement, the two countries will strengthen and promote exchanges and cooperation in the fields of education, science, culture and art, the press, broadcasting, film, television, publishing and youth activities as well as sports. Chinese President Jiang Zemin and visiting ROK President Kim Yong-sam were present at the signing ceremony.
