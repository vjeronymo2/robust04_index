920225 FT 25 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Firstland Oil Firstland Oil & Gas intends to diversify away from oil and gas exploration and production in North America and move into areas of distribution and specialist manufacturing. There will be a placing of 7m shares at 10p to raise Pounds 700,000 gross which will eliminate indebtedness and an open offer of 2.5m shares at the same price to raise Pounds 250,000.
