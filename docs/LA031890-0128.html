March 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

MALCOLM FORBES

For all those critics of the late Malcolm Forbes' birthday bash of last year: He celebrated his life on his last birthday in the grand fashion that he wanted.

Who said that he was going to deduct his expenses anyway? He'd only have been following the legal guidelines set by our Congress.

Forbes at least got twice the monetary value from the publicity throughout the world for his celebration.

Forbes will be sorely missed by those who realize that he did what everyone with or without a lot of money wishes he could do. He did what he loved.

Don't let anyone speak against those who know that life is for living and not just working.

CHRIS CONSIDINE

Altadena
