October 19, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Dow Chemical Co.: The Midland, Mich., maker of chemicals, plastics and pharmaceuticals said its third-quarter net income fell 52% to $282 million, despite the fact that revenue was up 16% to $4.92 billion. The company said skyrocketing costs of petroleum -- a key ingredient in many of its products -- was the reason for the profit drop.
