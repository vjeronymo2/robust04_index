921119 FT 19 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Sea Containers marginal increase Sea Containers, the Bermudan-based marine container lessor, ferries operator and hotel owner, announced pre-tax income of Dollars 28.5m (Pounds 19m) for the quarter ended September 30, against Dollars 27.6m. Mr James Sherwood, president, said that profits from marine container asset leasing were Dollars 10.3m, up 56 per cent. Ferries, however, fell from Dollars 24.9m to Dollars 23.6m. Turnover was Dollars 47.7m (Dollars 40.6m). Earnings per share were 1.92 cents (1.93 cents). The figures brought totals for the nine months to pre-tax profits of Dollars 33.3m (Dollars 33m) on turnover of Dollars 334.4m (Dollars 292.7m).
