December 21, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

CHRISTMAS BONUS: It may not be an official holiday, but Monday will be a day off with pay for many workers in Orange County, including those at Rockwell International in Anaheim, McDonnell Douglas Space Systems Co. in Huntington Beach and the main headquarters of Del Taco Inc. in Costa Mesa.

"It's the same (as) Thanksgiving," said a spokeswoman for Western Digital in Irvine. "It's more to give employees an extended weekend than anything else. And there's a chance people would take vacation time on that day anyway." Matt Lait and Anita Cal
