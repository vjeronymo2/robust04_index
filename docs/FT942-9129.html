940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Stocks finished a lethargic session nearly unchanged after investors moved to the sidelines on expectations of a rise in US interest rates. The IPC index of 37 leading shares ended a slight 0.33 point down at 2,239.84. Turnover was 51.43m shares, of which roughly 29m corresponded to Telmex. Telmex ADRs in New York ended unchanged at Dollars 56. In the local market Telmex 'A' eased 0.22 per cent.
