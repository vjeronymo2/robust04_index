941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / International Company News: SA foods group lifts payout C. G. Smith, the South African foods group, said it expected real growth in earnings per share in the year to next September, Reuter reports from Johannesburg. The forecast came as the company announced a 12.5 per cent rise in earnings per share to 106 cents for the past year, and raised its dividend to 36.8 cents from 32.7 cents. Attributable profit rose to R499.3m (Dollars 141.81m) from R443.3m. C. G. Smith said significant progress had been made in repositioning the group for the future.
