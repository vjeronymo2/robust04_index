BFN [Text] A ceremony to sign a trilateral agreement between the Governments of the Union of Myanmar [Burma], the People's Republic of China [PRC], and the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR] on the PRC-LPDR-Myanmar border tri-junction point was held today at the Foreign Ministry Annex Building in Yangon [Rangoon]. U Aye Lwin, director general of Consular Affairs, International Treaties, and Research Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; Mr. Liang Feng, ambassador of PRC, and Mr. Kideng Thammavong, ambassador of Laos, signed the agreement on behalf of their respective governments.
