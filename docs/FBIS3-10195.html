Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "National Events" newscast] [Text] According to all polls held in this country, the Nationalist Republican Alliance [ARENA] will win the 20 March elections. Rene Figueroa, of ARENA, has said that the voters' intentions to favor this organization are very clear and that this tendency is growing as elections day draws closer. [Begin Figueroa recording] Looking at our own polls and at those carried out by the University of Central America [UCA] we find that it is clear that the electoral preferences for the presidential ticket made up of Armando Calderon Sol and Enrique Borgo Bustamante indicates that the ARENA candidates are sure first round winners. The trend is clear, the backing of the Salvadoran people is clear, the intentions of the Salvadoran voters are clear. Another conclusion we have reached is that the Christian Democratic Party and the National Convergence-Farabundo Marti National Liberation Front [FMLN] coalition are competing for a distant, very distant, second place. We want to make this clear. Our polls and those of UCA indicate this. We have reached the conclusion that as we approach election day, the support for ARENA grows while the support for the Christian Democratic Party and for the National Convergence-FMLN coalition remains unchanged. [end recording]
