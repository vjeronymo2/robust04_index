Language: French Article Type:BFN [Article signed MVM/SVD: "Minister-President of the Walloon Government Resigns"; as released by Brussels BELGA Database] [Text] Brussels, 21 Jan (BELGA) -- Guy Spitaels, minister president of the Walloon Government, released the following statement to BELGA on Friday [21 January] afternoon: "Guy Coeme having resigned, I consider that he should not be the only individual responsible for the present situation. Therefore, I informed President of Parliament Willy Burgeon of my resignation as minister president of the Walloon Government."
