BFN [Text] Ukrainian-Russian negotiations are under way, aimed at coordinating the implementation of the Black Sea Fleet agreement signed by Boris Yeltsin and Leonid Kravchuk on 15 April in Moscow. Negotiations to this effect began in Sevastopol today, attended by the Ukrainian and Russian defense ministers. After the face-to-face talks between Defense Ministers Vitaliy Radetskyy and Pavel Grachev, the Ukrainian and Russian delegations began an expanded dialogue with the fleet's staff. After the personal meeting with his Russian colleague, Vitaliy Radetskyy told journalists that the dialogue was continuing in a constructive atmosphere.
