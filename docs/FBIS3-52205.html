Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Dezful, Khuzestan Province, Jan. 1, IRNA -- The number of Iraqi refugees entering Iran through Hur Al-Hoveyzeh border region reached 1,250, a provincial official said here Saturday [1 January]. Another 24 Iraqi families arrived in the region during the past few days, an official of the provincial Red Crescent Soceity, Seyed Ahmadi, told IRNA. According to him, the Red Crescent Society has provided the refugees with relief supplies but due to their increasing number more assistance is needed. The number of Iraqi refugees seeking shelter in Iran has increased during the past three months as Iraqi forces have stepped up attacks against southern marsh Arabs.
