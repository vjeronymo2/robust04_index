BFN ["Text" of Bulletin No. 1 issued by the Special Deputy Attorney General's Office conducting the investigation of the murder of Luis Donaldo Colosio, presidential candidate for the Mexican Institutional Revolutionary Party, in Mexico City on 31 March; from the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] This office believes it has made progress on essential aspects of the case. Given the nature of the investigation and taking into consideration Special Deputy Attorney General Miguel Montes Garcia's commitment to handle the information in such a way that the investigation will not be hampered or impeded, it has been regarded as advisable to withhold the information and release a report in a few days after all the important aspects of the investigation have been looked at. The work, which is being carried out in accordance with the outline of police analysis, comparison, and intelligence programs, has yielded positive results.
