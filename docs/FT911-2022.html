910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Scottish Cities Scottish Cities Investment Trust had a net asset value per stock unit of 925p at March 31, 8 per cent higher than the restated 856p reported for the same date last year and 28 per cent more than the 724p at the September 30 year-end. Post-tax profits for the six months to March 31 slipped to Pounds 365,000 (Pounds 375,000) and earnings to 10.9p (11.2p) per share. The interim dividend is lifted to 9p (7p) though directors stressed that this did not indicate an increased final.
