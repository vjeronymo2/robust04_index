Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Hizballah Secretary General Hasan Nasrallah has said that Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri's government was established according to the settlement plan of capitulation in the area. He noted that the government's projects are inspired by peace with Israel. He accused some state officials of seeking to cause a clash between Hizballah and the Lebanese Army over implementation of commitments, which he said the government gave to the U.S. secretary of state.
