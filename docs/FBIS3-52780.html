Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yasir 'Arafat has an interest in delaying the Israeli withdrawal from Gaza and Jericho: His security agencies are not yet complete. After establishing the armed force (police and public security), 'Arafat now wants to create security services for the presidency and national security (intelligence) to control any security problem from the opposition Palestinian forces. Arab sources told AL-SHIRA' that 'Arafat is deliberately procrastinating at the negotiations, setting conditions that are not mentioned in the Gaza-Jericho agreement he signed with Israel. He is buying time to prepare his own security.
