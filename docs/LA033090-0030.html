March 30, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MEETING STATE'S WATER NEEDS

Regarding the formula for possible water rationing: One article suggested rationing might be based upon 75-80% of previous consumption. It seems that such a formula would only benefit overusers while penalizing responsible homeowners who have voluntarily reduced their consumption.

The only fair plan would be to allot specific numbers of gallons of water per person per household.

J. SMITH

Moorpark
