BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 16 May (SNARK)--The Azerbaijani President Geydar Aliyev will visit Georgia on 18 May. SNARK was informed of it in the Georgian Foreign Ministry. It is supposed that there will be signed an agreement on trade-economic cooperation between two sides and a political communique. Georgian officials hope that the Aliyev's visit will promote to solve a number of problems, connected with the fuel delivery from Azerbaijan.
