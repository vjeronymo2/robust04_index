930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / UK Company News: FW Thorpe The start-up costs of Compact Lighting left interim pre-tax profits at FW Thorpe, the lighting equipment company, down at Pounds 402,000, against Pounds 630,000. Turnover for the six months to December 31 rose from Pounds 6.93m to Pounds 7.54m. Earnings per share came out at 2.11p (3.4p) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 0.8p.
