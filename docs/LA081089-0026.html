August 10, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

CLAIREMONT BANK ROBBED; SUSPECT TIED TO MORE

Police and the FBI are investigating the robbery Wednesday of a Union Bank branch in Clairemont.

The same suspect -- described as a tanned white male in his early 20s -- may have been involved in seven or eight other bank heists in the Clairemont area, police spokesman Bill Robinson said.

The bandit entered the bank about 2 p.m., told the teller he had a weapon and handed the teller a note demanding money, Robinson said. He received $1,525, placed it in a gym bag and escaped, the police spokesman said.
