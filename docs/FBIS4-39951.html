BFN [Text] A meeting of the committee organizing an international conference of political parties from the countries of the so-called Black Sea-Baltic area was held in Kiev. It has already been reported that this conference is being called on the initiative of the Ukrainian Republican Party, the Democratic Party [of Ukraine], and the Green Party [of Ukraine] with the aim of setting up a league of political organizations of the countries between the [Black and Baltic] seas. In particular, it was stated at the meeting that the Belarusian Popular Front and a number of parties from Lithuania, Latvia, Estonia, Moldova, Romania, and Hungary have already expressed a desire to take part in the conference.
