BFN [Text] Hanoi vna april 27 -- 'Vietnam is prepared to become full member of ASEAN to further promote the friendship and multifaceted cooperation with all countries, through contributing to pace, stability and development in the region', President Le Duc Anh told ASEAN Secretary General Ajit Singh during his official visit to Indonesia. In his courtesy visit paid to the Vietnamese Leader in Jakarta today, Mr. A. Singh expressed his pleasure at Vietnam's new development in national construction. He informed President Anh of ASEAN's willingness to extend full membership to Vietnam. President Anh thanked ASEAN member countries and Mr. Singh himself for their goodwill towards Vietnam.
