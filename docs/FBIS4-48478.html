BFN [Text] Niamey, 19 May (ANP/PANA) -- Five rebels were killed and one captured in clashes with the Army in Niger, the presidency announced on Thursday [19 May]. The presidential information office said in a statement that a detachment of the Army tracked the rebels after they attacked vehicles between Tillabery and Ayorou, north-east of Niamey, the capital, on Wednesday. It said the Army did not suffer any casualties during the clash which took place near the Malian border.
