940422 FT 22 APR 94 / Facelift for Blackpool Tower centenary Mark Smith begins work on painting Blackpool Tower gold yesterday to mark the landmark's centenary. The work will be carried out by a team of eight who will abseil down the side. They will slap on more than 220 gallons of paint during the facelift and are aiming to finish the job before the tower's centenary on May 14.
