931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables The good variety of grapes available at the moment make them this week's best fruit buy, reports the FFVIB. Greek Thompson seedless grapes are Pounds 1.00-2.00 a lb (Pounds 1.00-2.00), Italian White seedless grapes are 45p-Pounds 1.00 a lb (45p-Pounds 1.00), and Spanish pomegranates are also good value at 15-25p each (15-25p.) Root Vegetables are good value with English parsnips at 25-45p a lb (25-45p) and Dutch onions remaining good value at 15-25p a lb (15-25p). Best salad buys include: English Celery 30-60p per head (30-60p), English and Spanish cucumbers 40-70p each (45-60p) and English salad cress at 15-20p a punnet (15-20p).
