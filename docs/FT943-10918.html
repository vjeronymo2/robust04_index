940802 FT 02 AUG 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports, good general demand. Bright and good medium East Africans saw keen competition at fully firm to often dearer rates, but plainer Africans were irregularly easier. Selected brighter ceylons often advanced, others were irregular with plainer sorts difficult of sale by the close. Kenyas were sought after at dearer rates and ceylons came to a firm market. Quotations: best available 168p/kg. nom, good 158p/kg., good medium 140p/kg., medium 121p/kg., low medium 84p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 176p for a Kenya pd.
