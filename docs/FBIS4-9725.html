BFN [From the "Early Morning" newscast] [Text] At the time they welcomed Ernesto Zedillo into the electoral campaign as PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party] presidential candidate, the Socialist People's Party [PPS] and the Mexican Democratic Party [PDM] said they hope Zedillo will stick to the agreements signed by Luis Donaldo Colosio. [Begin recording] [PPS Deputy Leader Cuauthemoc Amezcua] We hope he will make a great and responsible effort to follow up and fulfill the democratic agreements signed by eight political parties. [PDM President Marcelo Gaxiola] Dr. Zedillo's taking up Colosio's banner and making a public commitment to follow the same course should be hailed and regarded as positive. [end recording] Both the PPS and PDM coincided by saying evidently, the new PRI candidate is at a disadvantage in relation to the other candidates and that, for this reason, he must make a greater effort to catch up and recover lost time.
