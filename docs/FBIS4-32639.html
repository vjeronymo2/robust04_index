BFN [Text] Split, Croatia June 28 (A.A.) -- The transfer of Turkish troops to Zenica, Bosnia-Herzegovina, was postponed today because of problems concerning the renting of living quarters for the troops. All preparations for the transfer have been concluded. The first group of Turkish forces which will join the UN peacekeeping forces arrived in Split June 22. The second contingent left for Split, on June 24. The first contingent brought with it 260 tons of weapons and equipment, 50 small vehicles, 139 large vehicles, armored personnel carriers, and 19 trailers.
