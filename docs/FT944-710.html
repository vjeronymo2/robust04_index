941224 FT 24 DEC 94 / Abbey predicts confidence boost Strong economic growth and falling unemployment will increase confidence among homebuyers, Abbey National bank predicted yesterday. It forecast a rise in average house prices of up to 4 per cent, although with significant regional variations. Turnover would rise to 1.3m transactions from about 1.25m in 1994. Abbey National also predicted that base rates would be about 7.25 per cent next December and that the standard variable mortgage interest rate could be above 9 per cent.
