November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

UCLA PLAYS GERMAN TEAM TONIGHT

UCLA, in its final tuneup before Friday night's season opener against UC Irvine in the Great Alaska Shootout, will play TTL Bamberg, a German club team, in an exhibition game tonight at 7:30 at Pauley Pavilion.

TTL Bamberg, coached by former Bruin Terry Schofield, is 0-6 on its U.S. tour, and has lost at Arizona and Arizona State.

The UCLA women's team will play the Bulgarian national team in a preliminary game at 5:15. JERRY CROWE
