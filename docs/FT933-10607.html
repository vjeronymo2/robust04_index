930803 FT 03 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Castle Cairn Net asset value per share at Castle Cairn Investment Trust stood at 57.34p at June 30, compared with 49.78p a year earlier. Available revenue for the six months to end-June improved from Pounds 55,000 to Pounds 69,000 for earnings per share of 0.57p, against 0.46p.
