940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / UK Company News: MY Holdings MY Holdings, the enlarged specialist packaging group, has acquired two more businesses, Cleanprint and Jensa Packaging, for a total cash consideration of Pounds 1.6m. The two companies supply the healthcare sector and for 1993 generated combined pre-tax profits of Pounds 240,000 on turnover of Pounds 2m. Net assets acquired were about Pounds 260,000.
