Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] His Royal Highness Crown Prince Hasan met British Prime Minister John Major in London tonight for discussion on the Middle East and bilateral ties. The meeting at 10 Downing Street centered on the political climate in the Middle East and the need for the respect of international law. His Royal Highness related the situation in the region to human needs and development within the framework of sovereignty of all states in the area. Prince Hasan and Prime Minister Major also discussed ideas for regional and intraregional economic and trade cooperation, particularly with Europe, once a just and comprehensive peace is established in the region. The meeting, which followed Mr. Major's return from the NATO summit, also discussed the situation in the Balkans and UN efforts to end the conflict as well as Jordan's contribution to peacekeeping and its keenness to put an end to the bloodshed there.
