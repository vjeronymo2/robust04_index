BFN [Text] Stepanakert ("LRAGIR", "AZG" dailies), 05/14/94 -- Tension at the RMK [Republic of Mountainous Karabakh] fronts persists. In spite of the new cease fire agreement, the Azeri units continue the attacks at the northern and eastern sections of the front. The RMK Defense Army headquarters report, military operations in the environs of the village of Yusifdzhanli in the Agdamskiy District continued in the evening of 12 May. The Azeri units attacked the Armenian defense positions, but were forced to retreat to the initial positions. About 50 Azeri soldiers were killed. The RMK armed forces repulsed the Azeri offensive in the Seisulan-Karmiravan section in the Mardakertskiy District. According to the RMK reconnaissance, the enemy continues concentrating new forces in this direction. The enemy shelled the village of Berdavan in the Martuninskiy District in the evening of 12 May, using "Grad" installations stationed in the Agdamskiy and Agdzhabediskiy districts. According to preliminary information, two women were killed, more than 10 peaceful inhabitants injured. Significant property damage is reported. The situation was rather calm on 13 May, only positional shellings are reported. An RMK Defense Army representative Serge Arushanyan said the Azeri side is violating the new cease-fire agreement, and thus challenging not only Karabakh but also Russia and CSCE, attempting to fail their efforts on peaceful regulation of the conflict.
