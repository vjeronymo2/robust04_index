941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Essex and Suffolk Essex and Suffolk Water, the merged supply company controlled by Lyonnais Europe, announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 15.3m for the half year to September 30, against a pro forma Pounds 12.8m. Turnover was Pounds 48.4m (Pounds 45.3m). Earnings per share were 119p and an interim dividend of 40.8p is declared.
