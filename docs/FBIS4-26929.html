BFN [Text] Beijing, June 28 (XINHUA) -- Top Chinese leader Li Ruihuan had an hour-long discussion here today with Kwok Ping-sheung, chairman of Sun Hung Kai, one of the biggest real estate companies in Hong Kong. Sun Hung Kai and the Beijing Dongan Group have decided to invest 300 million U.S. dollars to rebuild the Dongan market in downtown Beijing into a modern business center. The new market is scheduled to open in 1996. Li, a standing committee member of the Political Bureau of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee and chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, praised Sun Hung Kai for making investment in the mainland. Li also praised Kwok and other Hong Kong residents for their efforts in upholding Hong Kong's prosperity and stability, hoping that they will work together for Hong Kong's smooth transition and its prosperity and stability after July 1, 1997.
