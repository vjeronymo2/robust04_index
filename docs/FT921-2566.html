920319 FT 19 MAR 92 / Cossiga tours Mafia heartland In a calculated act of defiance to the Mafia, President Francesco Cossiga yesterday toured the Sicilian heartland of Italy's organised crime, Reuter reports from Palermo. 'The state can be ruthless too,' he told local police officials after last Saturday's murder by Mafia hitmen of Euro-parliamentarian Salvatore Lima.
