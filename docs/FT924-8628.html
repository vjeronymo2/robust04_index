921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Downturn at Personal Assets Available revenue of Personal Assets Trust fell from Pounds 117,000 to Pounds 90,000 over the six months ended October 31. Total income declined by Pounds 38,000 to Pounds 193,000. As already announced the interim dividend is being lifted to 0.85p (0.75p) from earnings of 0.6p (0.78p) per share. Shareholders are to be asked to authorise a resolution to consolidate the share capital on the basis of one new Pounds 12.50 ordinary share for every 100 12.5p ordinary held.
