BFN [Text] Prague, 19 May (XINHUA) -- A delegation from the Ministry of Supervision of China, led by Vice Minister Feng Tiyun, visited the Czech Republic from 15 to 19 May. During the visit, the Chinese delegation held talks with a delegation from the Czech Supreme Supervisory Council [zui gao jian cha yuan 2584 7559 4148 1390 7108]. The two sides briefed each other on the situation of the supervisory departments of their respective country, exchanged views candidly on how to improve the supervisory work, and agreed to further strengthen and expand the friendly and cooperative relations between the supervisory departments of the two countries.
