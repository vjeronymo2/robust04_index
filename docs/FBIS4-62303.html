BFN [Text] According to the press-service of the Russian border troops, action was taken on 12 May to clear the rear of forts 12 and 13 of the Moskovskiy border guard unit of opposition militants, as well as discover and destroy arms caches in that area. A total of 700 men, including troops of Tajikistan's Defense and Security Ministries and the Ministry of Internal Affairs are engaged in the mopping-up operation which will take several days. They use armor and artillery guns and are supported from the air. According to the press-service, Russian border patrols came under fire from positions in both Afghanistan and Tajikistan more than 90 times this year. The outpost Navranga at the meeting point of forts 12 and 13 came under fire more often than others. One border guard was killed and several -- wounded. All in all, more than 500 charges for recoilless guns and grenade-launchers, 130 rockets and 60 anti-tank mines were discovered in caches near Tajikistan's border with Afghanistan this year.
