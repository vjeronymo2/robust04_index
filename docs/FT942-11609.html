940505 FT 05 MAY 94 / 1994 David Thomas Prize winner Mr SA 'Juggy' Pandit, of London, winner of the 1994 David Thomas Prize, with Ms Jenny Rossiter, head of the prize's trustees, at the Financial Times headquarters where the presentation took place last night. The award was established in 1991 in memory of David Thomas, FT natural resources editor, who was killed on assignment in Kuwait. The theme for the 1994 prize was: 'Can the developing world become richer without the developed world becoming unemployed?' Mr Pandit is a general manager for Thorn EMI.
