920102 FT 02 JAN 92 / UN envoy heads for Somalia A United Nations special emissary arrived in Nairobi yesterday en route to Somalia's capital Mogadishu, where he will try to persuade rival clan factions to end nearly seven weeks of clashes, Reuter reports from Nairobi. Mr James Jonah was scheduled to meet rival warlords Mohammed Farah Aideed and Ali Mahdi Mohammed to discuss ending the carnage, which has killed and wounded up to 20,000 people, and getting emergency relief operations restarted.
