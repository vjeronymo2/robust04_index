Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Erfurt (DPA) -- On 31 August, Russian President Boris Yeltsin will come to Weimar to attend the farewell ceremony for the last Russian soldier. FRG Chancellor Helmut Kohl (Christian Democratic Union) is also expected to participate in the festive act in the national theater of the city, the state chancellery reported in Erfurt on Friday [25 March]. The German-Russian friendship of the last century is to be recalled and continued in the city of classical writers with this commemoration ceremony, which is accompanied by a cultural program. Kohl provided the initiative for the farewell ceremony in Weimar, it was stated.
