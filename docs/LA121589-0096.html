December 15, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEW WRITER

Christopher Knight, formerly the art critic of the Los Angeles Herald Examiner, is now writing art commentary and criticism for Calendar. His initial review is of a show at the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art that surveys the first significant movement in modern art indigenous to California. The exhibition, "Bay Area Figurative Art: 1950-1965," isn't daring in its approach, but it does shed a lot of light on a pivotal period in postwar American art.
