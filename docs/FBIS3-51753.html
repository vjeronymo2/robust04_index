Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Caracas, 21 Jan (PL) -- Following a discussion with President-elect Rafael Caldera, Gonzalo Garcia Bustillos, the Venezuelan ambassador in Cuba, today stressed that relations between Caracas and Havana are excellent and friendly. In statement to journalists, Garcia Bustillos said president Caldera's Government is willing to maintain friendly relations with Cuba. Garcia Bustillos also expressed his rejection of the economic blockade the United States has maintained against Cuba for three decades, and he said measures of this sort should not be implemented at this time. Garcia Bustillos recalled that President-elect Caldera views the theory of social justice as the basis for international relations, and he said this theory should go into effect in today's international community. Garcia Bustillos favored Cuba's reintegration into the OAS as part of the family comprising all of the states in the continent, and discussed the situation and the recent economic measures implemented in Cuba. Garcia Bustillos said he met with President-elect Caldera to exchange general views regarding foreign policy.
