Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn has received a cable of congratulations from His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, the amir of Qatar, on the occasion of the signing of the Yemeni document of accord, which was held under the auspices of His Majesty King Husayn in Amman on Sunday, 20 February. In the cable, his highness expressed his warmest congratulations to his majesty on the success of Jordanian efforts to reconcile the viewpoints of officials in the Yemeni leadership. He said: As we express our solidarity with your majesty's efforts toward cementing the rift in the Arab nation's facade and toward closing its ranks and uniting its word, we ask God the almighty to grant you continued health and happiness and your fraternal people more progress and prosperity. His Majesty also received congratulatory cables on this pan-Arab occasion from His Highness Hamad Bin-Khalifah Al Thani, Qatar's heir apparent, and from the Lebanese defense minister in which they expressed their appreciation for his majesty's efforts in reuniting the Yemeni family and reinforcing Arab solidarity at this critical stage in the history of the Arab nation.
