Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At 1200 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received the foreign ministers of the Damascus Declaration member states on the conclusion of their Damascus meeting. The ministers briefed the president on the proceedings of their meeting. Speaking on behalf of the ministers, [Kuwaiti Foreign Minister] Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad al-Jabir al-Sabah voiced satisfaction with the results achieved by the meeting and support for Syria's efforts to establish a just and comprehensive peace in the region. He said he hopes these efforts will realize their objective. Shaykh al-Sabah said the Damascus Declaration member states are committed to joint cooperation within the Declaration's framework in all fields to serve the national interests of the member states and Arab national issues. The ministers expressed their appreciation of Syria's policies and the president's pan-Arab stands. The president voiced satisfaction with the results of the foreign ministers' meeting and the support they expressed for Syria's pan-Arab policy. He said Syria will continue to work in accordance with its pan-Arab principles to defend Arab issues and rights and to achieve a just and comprehensive peace in the region.
