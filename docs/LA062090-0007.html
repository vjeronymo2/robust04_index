June 20, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NRA AND SECOND AMENDMENT

This is a letter to National Rifle Assn. members:

You keep talking about the Second Amendment.

I hate to tell you fellows, but none of today's guns are covered by the Second Amendment.

Now, if you have a single-shot cap and ball pistol or a single-shot, muzzle-loading rifle you're covered. Anything else is not covered.

These were the guns the Second Amendment was written for; that's all there were in those days. Too bad.

RON EKARD

Laguna Hills
