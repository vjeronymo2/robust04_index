Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Afghanistan prime minister has once again stressed the need for the resignation of President Borhanoddin Rabbani from his position and deemed it the sole means of resolving the country's crisis. According to the Central News Unit, Golboddin Hekmatyar declared his own willingness to step down from the post of prime minister in the event of Rabbani's resignation and said that an elected government chosen by all the Afghan groups should then take over the administration of the country's affairs. The Central News Unit reports that fighting among the hostile factions in Kabul seemed less intense yesterday compared to the preceding 48 hours.
