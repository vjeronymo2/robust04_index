Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 18 -- General Secretary Do Muoi received Malaysian Ambassador Cheah Sam Kip here today. Speaking at the reception, General Secretary Do Muoi thanked the government, the UMNO [United Malays National Organization] party and the people of Malaysia for their fine sentiments and precious support to Vietnam in the process of national construction, and reaffirmed the desire of the Vietnamese party, Government and people to increasingly foster the friendship, solidarity and cooperation between the two countries. For his part, the Malaysian ambassador conveyed the greetings of president of the UMNO party and Prime Minister Mahathir Mohamed to General Secretary Do Muoi, and expressed the wish of the Malaysian Government and people for strengthening friendship, and their readiness to share experiences in national development and promote close cooperation with Vietnam. The Malaysian diplomat said he believed that the coming Malaysia visit of general secretary will success, thus marking a new step of development in the relations between Malaysia and Vietnam.
