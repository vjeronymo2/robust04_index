Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] There are no differences regarding military policy between the leaderships of the Defense Ministry and the Armed Forces and the President of the Republic, Dr. Zhelev. This is the official stance of the Defense Ministry, which was announced at the Ministry's regular news conference held in Sofia today, in connection with certain reports in the press regarding the president's decree on setting up a military office. Tsvyatko Donchev, chief of the Defense Ministry Press Service, once again confirmed Defense Minister Aleksandrov's categorical refusal to comment on the letter published in the 24 CHASA daily.
