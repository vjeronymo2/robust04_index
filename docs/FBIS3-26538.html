Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video; from the "National News Hookup" program] [Text] Li Peng, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau Standing Committee and premier, who has just returned to Beijing after an inspection tour in other parts of the country, came to the residences of Comrades Peng Zhen and Wan Li this morning to wish them happy New Year and Spring Festival. [video shows medium close-ups of Li Peng and Peng Zhen exchanging greetings. Peng Zhen is seen sitting in a wheelchair pushed by a young man. Video then shows Ding Guangen and Luo Gan shaking Peng Zhen's hand] Premier Li Peng presented bouquets to Comrades Peng Zhen and Wan Li and wished them good health, a happy New Year, and success in everything. He also briefed the two veteran comrades on the domestic situation and the series of general and specific policies which the party Central Committee and the State Council have adopted for economic development, reform, and opening up. Li Peng also attentively listened to their views and suggestions on various projects. [video shows close-ups of Peng Zhen and Li Peng talking to one another across a small table. Peng's right hand appears to be immobile. Video then cuts to show medium close-ups of Li Peng visiting Wan Li and close-ups of them having a conversation across a small table] Comrades Peng Zhen and Wan Li expressed satisfaction about the work accomplished by the CPC Central Committee and the State Council. They were also pleased with China's reform, opening up, and economic development. They also indicated that they fully supported the requirements -- namely seizing the opportunity to deepen reform, open up wider to the outside world, expedite development, and maintain stability -- which the CPC Central Committee has set for this year's projects. They hoped the party Central Committee and the State Council will, on the basis of the successes they have accomplished, continue to work hard and lead people of all nationalities in the country to work with one heart and one mind and achieved even greater success in building a strong country. Ding Guangen, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and Secretariat; Luo Gan, state councillor, and responsible comrades of relevant departments also visited Comrades Peng Zhen and Wan Li.
