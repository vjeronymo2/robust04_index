BFN [Text] Brother Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-Husayn al-Ahmar, speaker of the House of Representatives, today received brother Badr 'Allam, assistant Egyptian foreign minister and President Mubarak's envoy. The brother speaker welcomed the Egyptian delegation to Yemen and thanked them for their country's sincere efforts on behalf of resolving our country's crisis. Al-Ahmar called for sustained efforts to ensure that the state institutions resumed their normal business and that the accord document is implemented. For his part, the Egyptian official said he was happy to see all parties show a positive response to the efforts being exerted to resolve the crisis. The meeting was attended by House member brother Jubran Mujahid Abu-Shawarib.
