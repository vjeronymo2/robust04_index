Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 5 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Friday [4 March] sent a message of greetings to James Fitz Allen Mitchell upon his reassumption of office as prime minister of Saint Vincent and the Grenadines. President Kim Il-song in the message wished the prime minister great success in his work for national unity and the country's prosperity, expressing the belief that the friendly relations between the two countries will continue to develop on good terms.
