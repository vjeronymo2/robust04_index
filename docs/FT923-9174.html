920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Rise in new premium income at Sun Life Sun Life Group's total new premium income for the first half of 1992 rose by 39 per cent to Pounds 886m, against Pounds 636m. The largest segment of this was Pounds 331m in the single premium with profits bonds. Total single premium business was 43 per cent higher at Pounds 844m (Pounds 588m) of which life income was almost doubled at Pounds 607m (Pounds 319m). As well as the with profits bonds there was also Pounds 167m from the distribution and deferred distribution bonds. Total annual premiums were down 13 per cent at Pounds 42.1m (Pounds 48.2m) although life premiums advanced 6 per cent to Pounds 13m (Pounds 12.3m). Mr John Reeve, managing director, warned that new business in the second half would be affected by the withdrawal of the with profits bond.
