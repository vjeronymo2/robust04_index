931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / Rome's ex-envoy to US probed Anti-corruption investigators yesterday ordered the arrest of Mr Rinaldo Petrignani, a former Italian ambassador to Washington, as part of Italy's corruption scandal, judicial sources said, Reuter reports from Milan. The sources said Mr Petrignani, 65, was wanted for questioning about the role he played as an adviser to US merchant bank Salomon Brothers after leaving the Washington embassy, his last diplomatic post, in 1991. Mr Petrignani was quoted as saying in Washington he was sure the matter would be cleared up very shortly.
