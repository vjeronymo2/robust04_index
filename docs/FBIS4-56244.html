BFN [Excerpt] Buenos Aires, 29 Apr (NA) -- Interior Minister Carlos Ruckauf has explained that he "will not act as a lone ranger" at the helm of the Buenos Aires branch of the Justicialist Party [PJ]. He said that "he will work with all the factions to make sure that the PJ will win in the capital in the 1995 elections." Ruckauf made these remarks on 28 April during his formal installation as head of the JP in the capital. Presidential Legal and Technical Secretary Carlos Corach; Juan Jose Minichillo, muncipal social promotion secretary and outgoing PJ metropolitan branch chief; and Deputy Patricia Bullrich also attended the ceremony.
