BFN [Text] Kinshasa, 13 Apr (AFP) -- Casimir Bizimungu, former Rwandan public health minister, and his higher education colleague arrived in Kinshasa today from Goma (eastern Zaire) after fleeing the trouble taking place in their country. This was learned from reliable sources today in the Zairian capital. According to eyewitnesses who saw them at the Ndjili airport, they were welcomed by Etienne Sigigera, the Rwandan ambassador in Kinshasa. The two former ministers, who were at the Kigali airport on 6 April waiting for the aircraft bringing back the Rwandan and Burundian presidents, had to flee towards Zaire when confusion followed the assassination.
