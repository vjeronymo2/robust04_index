931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / UK Company News: British Empire Trust British Empire Securities and General Trust saw a 53 per cent increase in net asset value over the year ended September 30, up from 60.47p to 92.82p basic. Fully diluted, the figure rose from 60.43p to 89.83p. Net income after tax rose from Pounds 1.29m to Pounds 1.55m, with earnings per share coming out ahead at 1.17p (0.98p). An increased final of 0.68p (0.64p) is recommended, to give a total of 0.93p (0.89p).
