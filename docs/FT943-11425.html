940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / Nirex files waste site plans Nirex, the nuclear waste disposal company, yesterday filed its long-awaited planning application with Cumbria county council for a Pounds 120m rock laboratory beneath the Sellafield site, a step towards its proposed Pounds 2bn underground waste repository. The laboratory is needed for a geological study of the site to enable Nirex to decide whether the repository should be built.
