Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Turkey has strongly condemned the savage attack carried out against Palestinians in the West Bank where there were a great number of casualties. A statement issued by the Foreign Ministry says that the violence which has been escalating in the occupied territories during the past few days causes great concern for the future of the Middle East peace process. The statement says: We hope that this was an isolated incident carried out by an insane person. We feel concern about the clashes that took place after the incident, and about the harsh measures taken by the police force during these clashes.
