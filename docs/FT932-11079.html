930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Minister concedes aerospace position BRITAIN has again been overtaken by France as the west's second-largest aerospace industry after the US and is also being challenged by Germany at the top of the European aerospace league. Mr Tim Sainsbury, the industry minister, yesterday told a Commons inquiry into the UK aerospace industry that the sector's Pounds 11bn turnover last year was slightly less than the Pounds 11.32bn turnover of the French aerospace industry.
