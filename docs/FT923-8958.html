920807 FT 07 AUG 92 / Nigeria scraps results of polls NIGERIAN plans for primaries to pick civilian presidential candidates were in disarray yesterday when both army-created parties scrapped the results of last weekend's flawed polls following allegations of rampant vote-rigging and intimidation, Reuter reports from Abuja. The Social Democratic party and the right-of-centre National Republican Convention also postponed indefinitely a second round of primaries set for tomorrow. The parties said their decisions followed meetings with government officials, including the electoral commission.
