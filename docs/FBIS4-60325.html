BFN [Text] A source in the Armed Forces Command has ridiculed the statement put out by a responsible source in the Transport Ministry broadcast by Sanaa radio this evening. The source said the al-Ahmar gang are in a difficult position now and are accordingly incapable of threatening Democratic Republic of Yemen [DRY] sea ports and airports. The DRY Armed Forces are in total control of all the Republic's sea ports and airports, and can fully guarantee the security of these seaports and airports and all the services they supply to all ships and aircraft.
