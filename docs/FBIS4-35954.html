BFN [Text] Cairo, 3 Jun (MENA) -- 'Abdallah al-Asnaj, who was recently appointed deputy prime minister and foreign minister of South Yemen, has appealed to President Husni Mubarak to act to save the Yemeni people from the continuing war. In a message to President Mubarak, he expressed his thanks to him and to the Egyptian people for the consideration given him during his stay in Egypt, which lasted for more than 10 years. Al-Asnaj also sent a message to the Gulf Cooperation Council foreign ministers expressing his utmost appreciation for their interest in developments in Yemen since the UN Security Council passed a resolution calling on the Yemeni parties to immediately halt the war and start negotiations.
