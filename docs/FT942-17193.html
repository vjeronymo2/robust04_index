940406 FT 06 APR 94 / UK Company News: Tamaris Tamaris plans to raise Pounds 2.5m, net of expenses, through a 5-for-4 rights issue to fund the purchase of additional nursing homes in Northern Ireland and East Sussex, and a potential development site in Bristol. A total of 151.26m new ordinary shares at 2p each will be issued, fully underwritten by Teather & Greenwood, the stockbroker. At the same time, Tamaris is disposing of its Westbury, Hampshire, nursing home for Pounds 750,000 cash. The company plans to pay Pounds 1.63m cash for the 63-bed Lisnisky nursing home in Northern Ireland and Pounds 875,000 in a cash and shares deal for the 30-bed Lydfords nursing home in East Sussex. The Bristol site is being acquired in exchange for 7m new ordinary shares.
