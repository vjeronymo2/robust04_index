Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 1 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese Society of Yellow River Culture and Economy was set up in Beijing today. Aimed at promoting patriotic spirit and economic reforms and development in the Yellow River Valley, the society will nurture ties among Chinese and international scholars, experts and noted figures and help collect suggestions and material on cultural and economic development. It will provide consultant services for local governments, conduct various forms exchanges, edit and publish books, magazines and picture albums, make TV and film programs and introduce the reforms and development in the Yellow River Valley to overseas and domestic viewers. Chai Zemin was elected president of the society. Peng Zhen, Bo Yibo, Liu Lantao, Ji Pengfei and Chu Tunan were invited to be honorary presidents.
