BFN [Text] A delegation from the Mori Kazak area [Xinjian Uygur Autonomous Region] has arrived in Almaty at the invitation of Kazakhstan's friendship society. It is led by Salem Rakhman-Ulu, the chairman of the people's government of the area. The goal of the visit is to establish friendship and business relations with Kazakhstan. Zhibek Omirkhanova, the chairman of the presidium of of the friendship society, briefed guests on the work of it. The guest will visit educational establishments, museums, and companies, and will meet activists of the Chinese-Kazakhstan friendship society.
