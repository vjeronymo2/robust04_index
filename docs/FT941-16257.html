940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Jersey Electricity shows downturn Despite an increase in turnover from Pounds 36.9m to Pounds 37.9m, Jersey Electricity suffered a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 7.72m to Pounds 6.02m for the year ended October 3 1993. After tax of Pounds 488,000 against Pounds 1.32m, earnings per share were 361p (418p) while the dividend is lifted from a gross 36p to 37p with a final payment of 24p.
