Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid today telephoned Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid regarding the latest developments in Yemen. Meanwhile, the Arab League Secretariat General issued a statement today in which it said that it is following with extreme concern the developments taking place in Yemen, which affect its national unity, security, and stability. The Secretariat General called on all the Yemeni national forces to safeguard the historic achievement in Yemen and overcome all the differences preventing national reconciliation.
