October 22, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

Marshall 48, Verdugo Hills 27 -- Mark Gutierrez passed for 247 yards and five touchdowns to lead Marshall in a Northern Conference game at Verdugo Hills.

Gutierrez connected with Miguel Osorio on scoring passes of 13, 27 and 41 yards, hit Ramon Altruz with a 63-yard touchdown pass and found Ron Carter for a 52-yard scoring pass.

Verdugo Hills quarterback Lance Novak completed 17 of 28 passes for 183 yards. Novak also caught a touchdown pass from backup quarterback Darryl Evans in the fourth quarter. HANDEL WHITMORE
