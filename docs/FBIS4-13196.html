BFN [Text] The negotiations between Israel and the Palestinians are continuing in Cairo today. Today's talks revolve around the release of the Palestinian detainees. A member of the Palestinian delegation said Israel has agreed to release all the detainees, including Hamas members, adding that the main argument revolves around the timetable for their release. Our envoy in Cairo Moti Amir was told by a Palestinian source that Egypt will open a quasi-consulate in Jericho which will serve the residents of the Palestinian autonomous areas until an independent Palestinian state is established. According to the source, the consulate will be located in a building belonging to the Coptic church, upon the approval of the head of the Coptic church in Egypt.
