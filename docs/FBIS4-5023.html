BFN [Text] The plenary session of the National Convention [PSNC] resumed at 0900 this morning in the Central Meeting Hall of the president's residence compound on Ahlone Road, Yangon [Rangoon]. The plenary session was attended by Lieutenant General Myo Nyunt, chairman of the National Convention Convening Commission, and commission members; U Aung Toe, chairman of the National Convention Convening Work Committee [NCCWC], and work committee members; Brigadier General Tin Aye, chairman of the National Convention Convening Management Committee, and committee members; national convention delegates; and local and foreign correspondents. At the plenary session, U Khin Maung Htoo from the public service personnel delegate group acted as alternate chairperson while Dr. Than Nyunt; U Saw Philip, alias U Philip San; U San Tha Aung; U Mahn Ohn Maung; U Kan Nyunt; U Soe Maung; U Tin Maung; and U Sai Aung Htun acted as members of the Panel of Chairmen, while U Khin Maung Myint, director general of the NCCWC Office and the official in charge of the meetings, acted as secretary. First, the secretary of the meeting announced the validity of the session as 645 out of 693 delegates attended. According to the agenda, the Panel of Chairmen of the National Convention presented a review and analysis of the suggestions submitted by the delegate groups at the PSNC to prescribe the fundamental principles related to the state, state structure, and head of state chapters which will be included in drawing up the constitution. Initially, Alternate U Khin Maung Htoo presented the review and analysis on the state chapter and Dr. Than Nyunt, member of the Panel of Chairmen, later continued the review and analysis on the same chapter. The plenary session recessed at 1035. The afternoon session of the PSNC is currently in progress at the Central Meeting Hall of the president's residence compound on Ahlone Road, Yangon.
