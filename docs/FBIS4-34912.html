BFN [Text] Mexico City, 24 Jun (NOTIMEX) -- Today, President Carlos Salinas de Gortari rejected the resignation of Interior Secretary Jorge Carpizo, who had sent a letter to the president stating his decision to resign and retire from political life. The resignation presented by Carpizo, a prestigious attorney and former university director, took place two months before the 21 August elections. Nine political parties have presented candidates for the country's presidential elections. Carpizo assumed the Interior Secretariat at the beginning of the year.
