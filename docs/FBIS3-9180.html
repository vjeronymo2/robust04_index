Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Interview with Prime Minister Jozef Moravcik by "zl" on 16 March in Bratislava: "Does the Government Have the Means or Not?"--in the "Hot Questions For" column] [Text] After the first meeting of the government on Wednesday [16 March], we asked the Slovak prime minister: [REPUBLIKA] REPUBLIKA is the only daily that is inconvenient for the government. Has the government adopted measures to liquidate this daily? [Moravcik] The government does not have the means to liquidate dailies. Or does it? The press ought to be independent, also economically. I believe that it can fulfill its role only when it is independent. [REPUBLIKA] In other words, you have not dealt with the liquidation of REPUBLIKA in the government? [Moravcik] No, not yet.
