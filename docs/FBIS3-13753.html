Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] Chaired by esteemed Mowlawi Arsalan Rahmani, the deputy prime minister, the Council of Ministers held a meeting this morning. The Cabinet's press office reported to the BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY that, at this session, esteemed Mowlawi Arsalan Rahmani spoke briefly about the country's military-political situation and progress of work of the commissions working for the restoration of peace and security in the country. According to the session's agenda, organizational issues and future of [words indistinct], as well as the country's plan and budget for 1373 [beginning 21 March 1994] were discussed. The session decided [word indistinct] that under the direction of esteemed Dr. Hamidollah Tarzi, minister of state for economic affairs in the Cabinet, (?a mixed commission from) the Ministries of Finance, Planning, Labor, and Social Affairs is to prepare the draft budget for 1373 and then present it to the Council of Ministers by 9 April after consultation with the ministries and departments and in consideration of the country's economic situation [words indistinct] and principles of administration.
