In assigning funds to each Region, Headquarters will designate an amount to be subassigned to each non-Indian Field Office, based on each Office's FY 1993 subassignment and adjusted as necessary. The FY 1993 subassignments reflected both relative shares of modernization needs within the Region and PHA capability to administer the modernization.
