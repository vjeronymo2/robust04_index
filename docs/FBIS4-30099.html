BFN [Text] Kim Yong-sop, ambassador of our country, on 14 June paid a farewell call on Yusuf Amin Wali, general secretary of the Egypt National Democratic Party. At the meeting, the general secretary expressed satisfaction with the fact that friendly relations between the Egypt National Democratic Party and the Workers Party of Korea and between Egypt and DPRK are developing well. He stressed that the independent policies implemented by His Excellency respected leader Kim Il-song and His Excellency dear leader Kim Chong-il are making the image of the DPRK brighter. He said he hopes that all problems, including the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula, will be peacefully resolved through dialogue between the DPRK and the United States.
