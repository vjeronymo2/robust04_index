920702 FT 02 JUL 92 / More tourists visit Britain 16 per cent up on the same month last year and a record for April. They also spent a record Pounds 570m in April 1992 - 20 per cent up on the previous year. The number of American visitors - which fell last year after the Gulf war - rose 18 per cent in April and those from western Europe by 16 per cent.
