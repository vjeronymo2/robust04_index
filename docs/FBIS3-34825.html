Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday, in Tunis, Brother President Abu-'Ammar received a troika delegation comprised of the ambassadors of Greece, Germany, and Belgium, along with the EC commissioner. Abu-'Ammar briefed the ambassadors on the Cairo document, which was signed within the framework of the Gaza-Jericho first agreement to advance a just and comprehensive peace in the Middle East. He pointed out the obstacles placed by Israel in the path of this agreement. Abu-'Ammar impressed on the ambassadors the need to urge the Israeli Government to expedite the implementation of the Washington agreement and meet its target dates. The Israeli side, he emphasized, should show credibility and implement the agreements it has signed.
