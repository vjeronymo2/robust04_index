BFN [Text] Cairo, 17 Jun (MENA) -- A Yemeni parliamentary delegation arrived in Cairo this afternoon on a visit of several days to Egypt. Delegation leader Yahya Mansur Abu-'Uthman stated that the visit falls within the framework of the efforts of the legitimate Yemeni authority to brief the brothers in Egypt on the current situation in Yemen. He added that the delegation will meet during this visit with Egyptian officials and discuss bilateral parliamentary relations, the situation in Yemen, and the dangers facing Yemeni unity. The three-member delegation will next visit Libya, Sudan, and Djibouti to present the Yemeni view of the ongoing war in Yemen.
