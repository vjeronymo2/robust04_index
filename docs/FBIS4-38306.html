BFN [Text] Sanaa, 29 Jun (INA) -- President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih today issued a decree naming Mr. Taha Ahmad Ghanim new governor for Aden. He replaces Salih Munassar al-Siyayli, one of the secessionist leaders. It is to be recalled that, except for Crater, al-Mu'alla, and al-Tawahi, where the secessionists are entrenched, the Yemeni legitimate forces are now in control of all of Aden's suburbs.
