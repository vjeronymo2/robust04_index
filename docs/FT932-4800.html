930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / Security tightens in Mogadishu after shooting of UN troops A Turkish United Nations soldier guards a bunker near the US Embassy in the Somali capital Mogadishu. Security has been doubled since gunmen shot dead at least 23 Pakistani UN troops in weekend ambushes. At least two gunmen were killed yesterday in a clash with Pakistanis, while Somali leaders loyal to Gen Mohammed Farah Aideed handed five Pakitanis back to UN troops.
