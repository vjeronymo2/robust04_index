Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 3 (XINHUA) -- At the invitation of President Jiang Zemin of the People's Republic of China, Geydar Aliyev, president of the Azerbaijani Republic will pay an official visit to China from March 7 to 11. According to an announcement made by a Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman here today, the two sides will exchange views on bilateral relations and other issues of shared concern.
