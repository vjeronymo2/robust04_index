BFN [Text] The leadership of the NPFL [National Patriotic Front of Liberia] has expressed satisfaction over the recent visit by two separate delegations from the LNTG [Liberian National Transitional Government] to Gbarnga. Speaking at a press briefing at the Executive Mansion in Gbarnga, former NPRA [National Patriotic Reconstruction Assembly] government president, Charles Gankay Taylor, said he believed the recent visit of the State Council and TLA members to Gbarnga were in line with the search for lasting peace. Mr. Taylor also congratulated the acting chairman of the State Council, Councilor Philip Banks; State Council first vice chairman, (Isaac Missiles); and TLA [Transitional Legislative Assembly] Speaker Morris Dukuly for braving the storm to come to Gbarnga, despite all the lies and deception.
