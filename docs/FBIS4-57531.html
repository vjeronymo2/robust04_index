BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities monitored through 19 May. The Association of Motor Vehicle Manufacturers (Adefa) reported today that record production was achieved in April, when 35,160 vehicles were manufactured, which represents a 4.1 percent increase as compared to March. (Buenos Aires NOTICIAS ARGENTINAS in Spanish 0013 GMT 14 Mar 94) It was officially reported today that house electricity rates were increased 8 percent on 1 May. (Buenos Aires NOTICIAS ARGENTINAS in Spanish 2322 GMT 13 Mar 94)
