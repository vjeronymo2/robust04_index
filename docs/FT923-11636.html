920723 FT 23 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Hemingway Props losses fall sharply Hemingway Properties reported a sharp reduction in losses in the six months to June 30. Turnover rose from Pounds 1.79m to Pounds 3.04m. The pre-tax deficit was cut from Pounds 2.15m to Pounds 332,000 and per share the outcome was reduced to a 0.61p loss, against 4.33p previously. Last August new management was installed at the company, formerly Marylebone Estates, under a reverse takeover.
