BFN [Text] The following is the content of the letter from the two-headed government's interior ministers to the Party of Democratic Kampuchea's [PDK] representatives in Phnom Penh: The Ministry of the Interior requests that all PDK representatives and their staff leave Phnom Penh immediately -- before 19 June 1994. The Ministry of the Interior will provide safe escort for your group's trip from its office to Pochentong Airport. You must inform the Ministry of the Interior at least one day before your departure, however. The Ministry of the Interior will not be responsible for your security beyond the deadline stated above. [Dated] 17 June 1994 [Signed] Sar Kheng and Yu Hokkri
