920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / UK Company News: REA Holdings falls A fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.23m to Pounds 883,000 was announced by REA Holdings, the plantation holding company, for 1991. Turnover improved to Pounds 76.9m (Pounds 66.4m) but increased cost of sales resulted in lower operating profits of Pounds 5.38m (Pounds 5.84m). Earnings emerged at 4.1p (10.8p) and the dividend is unchanged at 4p.
