BFN [Excerpts] The peace process in Western Sahara witnesses a new phase after it stumbled many times on account of the disagreement between Morocco and the Polisario on the criteria for determining the voters' identity. [passage omitted] The details of this file are with Hamza Wahib: [Begin Wahib recording] The UN mission in charge of organizing the referendum affirmed that it has received numerous registration applications from Western Sahara and the Saharan refugee camps in Tindouf. [passage omitted] Bachir Mustapha Sayed, Polisario's chief negotiator in charge of relations with the United Nations [title as heard], affirmed today that the UN secretary-general had a conception which was compatible with Moroccan interests on settling the Saharan issue. The Saharan official said that the criteria for determining the voters' identity, introduced by the former UN secretary-general, Peres de Cuellar, two days before his term in office came to an end, were reducing the hope of finding a just and fair solution to the issue. [end recording]
