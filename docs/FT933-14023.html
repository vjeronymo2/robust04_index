930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / North Shields housing scheme A FURTHER Pounds 5m Estate Action investment to improve housing on the Meadowell Estate, North Shields, was announced yesterday by Sir George Young, housing and inner cities minister. Sir George also announced a Pounds 11m development of 284 homes for sale on the adjacent Royal Quays site, a Tyne and Wear Development Corporation redevelopment project.
