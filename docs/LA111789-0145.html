November 17, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BOMB BLAST AT SCHOOL IN CHILE INJURES 15

A bomb exploded in a school in Santiago Thursday, injuring about 15 teen-age students, police said.

The bomb, which had been left in a bathroom, went off while classes were in progress in the technical school attended by 17- and 18-year-olds, local radio reported.

Nobody immediately claimed responsibility for the blast, in a northern working-class district of the capital.
