Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At around 1145 today, the Lebanese resistance shelled and launched several attacks on the Israeli positions in al-Ghizlan and Sujud. The Israeli forces retaliated with intensive artillery bombardment of several villages in Iqlim al-Tuffah. The clashes later developed into artillery duels between Tallat Sujud and the resistance positions in Iqlim al-Tuffah in which 155-mm and 120-mm artillery was used. Israeli aircraft flew intensive sorties over the central and western sectors and over Tyre, breaking the sound barrier more than once. According to our correspondent in al-Biqa', a South Lebanese Army [SLA] foot patrol of 12, backed by a tank, crossed the contact line in the Zhaghlah-Hasbayya sector at 1000 today, advanced 2 km north of the security zone toward Mimis valley, and stopped 1 km from the Lebanese Army positions. The Army personnel in the area were placed on alert and took up combat positions. The SLA patrol returned to its position at 1200. Our correspondent said that Israeli Merkava tanks were spotted moving along the Kawkaba axis and fired five artillery shells at the al-Dallafi-Jabal al-Dahr axis.
