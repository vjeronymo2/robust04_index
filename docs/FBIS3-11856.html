Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Along with pardoning Lieutenant Colonel Hugo Chavez Frias, President Rafael Caldera has dismissed charges against 10 other officers involved in the 4 February and 27 November 1992 coup attempts, all of whom were being held at the San Carlos garrison. At 1130 today, Saturday, the 10 officers who received the presidential pardon left the old military garrison. They are: Captains Antonio Rojas Suarez, Noel Martinez, Edgar Hernandez Berens, Guillermo Blanco Acosta, Alcala Cardones, and Aguilar Gomez who participated in the 4 February coup attempt. Also released were Air Force Lieutenants Jose Baltazar Nelo and Jessy Chacon Escanilla, Captain Jose Valera Rumbos, and Army Mayor Carlos Lugo Lopez, who had taken part in the 27 November coup attempt.
