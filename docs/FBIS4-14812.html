BFN [Text] A Tajik Government delegation left by plane for Moscow today for talks with the opposition, with Russian mediation. The head of the delegation, Labor Minister Shukurjon Zuhurov told journalists at Dushanbe airport that he did not know which of the opposition's representatives would be at the talks in the Russian capital. He expressed the hope that an agreement would be reached at the meeting on 5 April on beginning full-scale negotiations on a settlement for Tajikistan.
