920619 FT 19 JUN 92 / Observer: Abstract verb Why is it that most of us would 'extract' water but the National Rivers Authority warns about excessive 'abstraction'? Of the industry's several contradictory explanations, the one that makes the most sense is that 'extraction' is permanent while 'abstraction' implies that the water eventually will be returned to the river. Observer suspects that, unlike the hapless River Darent, this one may run and run.
