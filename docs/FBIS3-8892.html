Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Czech Government has discussed the expected visit by Zhirinovskiy, leader of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, to the Czech Republic, and has decided that it will not try to prevent it. [Begin Prime Minister Vaclav Klaus recording] Since there is no visa requirement for Russian citizens, the government does not have any legal means that would enable it to prevent Mr. Zhirinovskiy from entering the territory of the Czech Republic [words indistinct]. The government does not intend to find a way round our laws and therefore it will act in this way. Naturally, the government is ready to intervene if any of our laws are violated or if public order is disturbed, etc. [end recording] [passage omitted]
