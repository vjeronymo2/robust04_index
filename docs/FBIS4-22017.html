BFN [Text] The currently reorganized special force Vympel will combat nuclear terrorism, Commander of the Russian Interior Troops, Colonel General Anatoliy Kulikov announced on Tuesday. He said that Vympel would be directly controlled by the interior minister. He also pointed out that the tactics of the interior troops guarding nuclear power plants and nuclear defense facilities have become outdated in as they were mostly oriented against individual terrorists, not organized criminals. The interior troops also lack armored equipment and special security devices, said Kulikov. There are plans to equip servicemen with gas weapons, he stated. He also complained that the interior troops were 49 percent understaffed.
