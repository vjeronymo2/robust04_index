BFN [Text] Bonn -- Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel has promised Romania the support of the Federal Government on its path toward the European Union [EU] and the transatlantic security structures. During a meeting with the Romanian Parliamentary President Adrian Nastase in Bonn today, Kinkel encouraged Romania to continue the political and economic reform process. The chairman of the economic committee of the Bundestag, Friedhelm Ost, also promised the Romanian politician that he will try to ensure a stronger commitment by the German industry. Development Minister Carl-Dieter Spranger and the parliamentary state secretary in the Interior Ministry, Horst Waffenschmidt, will travel to Romania next week. Kinkel stated that he will also visit the country in the near future.
