BFN [Text] Croatian President Franjo Tudjman received international peace mediators David Owen and Thorvald Stoltenberg in the presidential palace this evening. After one hour of talks, Lord Owen said they discussed many issues of mutual interest, most important of which was the resumption of talks between the Croatian Government and the Serbs from the Croatian [UN] protected areas. Owen did not specify a date for the talks, but he expressed the hope that they will take place soon.
