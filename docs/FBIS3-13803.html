Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] has announced it rejects UN Security Council Resolution number 904 on the Hebron massacre because it does not denounce the Zionist entity. In a statement disseminated in the territories today, Hamas spokesman Ibrahim Ghawshah says the UN resolution does not suffice, because it does not call for the evacuation of the settlers from Hebron and for disarming them.
