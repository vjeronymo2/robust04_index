BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 19 May -- The press service of the Georgian Defense Ministry has informed Iberia news agency that, at the order of Georgian Defense Minister Lieutenant General Vardiko Nadibaidze, Colonel Davit Tevzadze, who is the holder of the Vakhtang Gorgasali Order 1st Degree, has been appointed head of the Defense Ministry's Foreign Relations Department. Before the appointment to this post, Col. Tevzadze worked as head of brigade No. 11. Col. Tevzadze is a full member of the New York Academy of Sciences. He is married with four children.
