941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Deutsche Babcock - Contracts Deutsche Babcock, the German engineering group, has won a DM150m (Dollars 97.4m) contract to modernise a Romananian brown coal fired power station. Deutsche ABB, the German arm of the Swiss-Swedish engineering group, will pick up DM60m of the contract, which is being funded by a loan from the Kreditanstalt fur Wiederaufbau, the state-owned bank which finances export contracts.
