940604 FT 04 JUN 94 / UK Company News: London Industrial Continuing operations' turnover increased from Pounds 5.8m to Pounds 6.9m at London Industrial, the provider of workspace for rent to small businesses in Greater London, while pre-tax profits were down at Pounds 933,400 for the year ended March 31, compared with Pounds 1.03m, despite a rise in fourth quarter figures. The company was floated in December and raised Pounds 14m from the share placing. Earnings per share were 12.5p (16.2p) and, as forecast, there is a final dividend of 5p for the year.
