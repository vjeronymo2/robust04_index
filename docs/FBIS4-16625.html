BFN [Text] Today, legislative speaker Mechyslaw Hryb and Head of the CIS Executive Secretariat Ivan Karatchenya held a news conference at the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Belarus, dedicated to the results of the regular conference of the CIS heads of state and government, held in Moscow last week. Mechyslaw Hryb told those present about the conference and voiced satisfaction over its results. In particular, he pointed out that 26 issues were addressed and as many documents signed. They were on the implementation of the treaty on the forming of the economic union, the realization of commissions issued by the Heads of State Council, the issues discussed at the initiative of states, statutory, and branch bodies of the Commonwealth, and so on. The Supreme Soviet chairman answered reporters questions about the agreement signed between the Government of the Russian Federation and the Republic of Belarus on the unification of monetary systems. The legislative speaker said that he was not an advocate of amending the Constitution and has no right to do this. This can be resolved solely by the Supreme Soviet.
