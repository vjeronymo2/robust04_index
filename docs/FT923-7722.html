920815 FT 15 AUG 92 / Kenya opposition figure dies A leading member of Kenya's squabbling opposition died yesterday, increasing the likelihood of a final split before the east African country's first multi-party polls in 26 years, Reuter reports from Nairobi. Mr Masinde Muliro, 72, interim vice-chairman of the main opposition Forum for the Restoration of Democracy (FORD) party, collapsed at Nairobi airport after returning from a private visit to Britain. 'Muliro stumbled and fell after his passport was stamped. We thought he was only slightly hurt. But airport doctors pronounced him dead soon after,' one witness said.
