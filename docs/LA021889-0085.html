February 18, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

COMETS' GOOSSENS GETS 400TH CAREER GOAL, AND LAZERS LOSE

Jan Goossens scored three goals, including the 400th of his career, to lead the Kansas City Comets to a 7-3 Major Indoor Soccer League victory over the Los Angeles Lazers Friday night.

Goossens' 400th was his first of the night, at 13:50 of the first quarter, and gave the Comets a 3-1 lead.

After scoreless second and third periods, the Lazers' Doug Neely scored his 28th goal of the season at 1:42 of the fourth quarter to make it 3-2.

Goossens' second goal came on a power-play goal at 7:45 of the fourth quarter, and Gary Heale scored for the Lazers one minute later to make it 5-3.

Goossens followed with his third goal of the night at 11:10. Greg Ion added an empty-net goal to finish the scoring.

The Comets have won four of their last five games and are 11-17. The Lazers are 14-15.
