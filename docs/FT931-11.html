930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / Outokumpu plans to boost share capital THE BOARD of Outokumpu, the Finnish metals and mining group, will propose to the shareholders' meeting on April 20 that the board raise the company's share capital by FM300m (Dollars 50.8m) from the current FM798.6m, the company said in a statement, Reuter reports from Helsinki. The rise could take place through one or more rights issues, or through issuing convertible bonds or bonds with warrants. The funds would be used to finance acquisitions, to enable the company to participate in joint ventures or other important projects, the statement added.
