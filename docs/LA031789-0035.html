March 17, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

DIGEST: BRAY TO FIGHT IRISH NATIONAL CHAMPION

Heavyweight boxer John Bray of Van Nuys will face Irish national champion Sean O'Reagan at Caesars Atlantic City tonight as part of the U.S.-Ireland amateur dual meet.

Bray, 18, was selected for the U. S. team as a result of his performance earlier this month at a training camp at the Olympic Training Center in Colorado Springs, Colo.

This is the second international competition for Bray, a 1988 graduate of Van Nuys High. Last year he split two bouts on a Scandinavian tour.

The 10-bout dual meet will be televised by cable station TBS at 3:05 p.m. Saturday.

Jamal Swinton of Montclair Prep has signed a letter of intent to play football next season at Colgate.

The 5-foot-10, 180-pound defensive back intercepted three passes and gained 348 yards as a running back last fall.
