940713 FT 13 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Emerald Energy Pounds 25,765 in profit Emerald Energy, the Isle of Man-based energy producer which obtained a USM quote last November, reported a pre-tax profit of Pounds 25,765 for the six months to end-March, against a loss of Pounds 15,738. The outcome was struck after an exceptional gain of Pounds 61,196 on the disposal of a subsidiary and came from turnover up from Pounds 23,600 to Pounds 122,200. Earnings per share came to 0.01p, against losses of 0.15p. In April this year, Emerald raised Pounds 660,000 through a placing of 25m shares to provide funds for acquisitions and its refurbishment programme. It also acquired three gas fields in the Appalachian Basin for Dollars 475,000 (Pounds 312,000).
