940831 FT 31 AUG 94 / International Company News: Roche bid for Syntex cleared Roche, the Swiss pharmaceuticals group, has won conditional clearance from the US Federal Trade Commission to complete its Dollars 5.3bn agreed bid for Syntex, a US drugs group, writes Ian Rodger. Under an agreement with the FTC, Roche will sell Syntex's Syva medical diagnostics business in the US. Syva had sales of Dollars 212m in the year to July 31 1993.
