910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Cakebread up almost fourfold to Pounds 282,000 Cakebread Robey & Co, the builders' and timber merchant, lifted pre-tax profits nearly fourfold from Pounds 74,000 to Pounds 282,000 in the year to December 31. Turnover rose almost 9 per cent to Pounds 27.01m (Pounds 24.89m) and gross profit by 15 per cent to just under Pounds 7m. Interest charges took almost twice as much as last time at Pounds 365,000. After a tax charge of Pounds 90,000 (credit Pounds 255,000), and an extraordinary credit of Pounds 484,000 (nil) - relating to profits from the disposal of two properties - attributable profits worked through at Pounds 676,000 (Pounds 329,000). Earnings more than doubled to 11.3p (5.5p) and the final dividend is lifted to a proposed 5.25p (4.75p) to make 7.5p (6.75p)
