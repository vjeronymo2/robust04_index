Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 25 (XINHUA) -- With the approval of the Central Committee of the ruling Communist Party of China (CPC), the "PEOPLE'S DAILY", [RENMIN RIBAO] China's leading national newspaper, is to publish local editions. The newspaper today quoted its director Shao Huaze as saying that, as a first step, the east China edition of the newspaper is to be published in Shanghai as of January 1, 1995. Publication of the local editions is aimed at publicizing in a more timely fashion the principles and policies of the CPC and transmitting information about local economic and social development more fully, Shao explained. He revealed that Jiang Zemin, general secretary of the CPC central committee, and other members of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee have all given important instructions on further improving the newspaper, the organ of the CPC Central Committee.
