April 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

TRIVIA CORRECTIONS

As a teacher of geography and an airline buff, I was disappointed to find several glaring errors in the "Can You Guess?" trivia quiz. They are: 1) The airline of Spain is IBERIA not Iberian; 2) it's Qantas not Quantas; 3) LACSA not LASCA (Lineas Aereas Costarricenses S.A.) and 4) Colombia not Columbia.

FRANK S. BROWNIE

Santa Barbara
