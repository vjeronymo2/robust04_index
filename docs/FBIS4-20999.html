BFN [Text] The Yen Bai provincial people's committee recently burned 38 kg of confiscated opium at the 19 May Square in Yen Bai City. This is the fourth time the province has destroyed a large volume of opium since its implementation of the government's program on preventing and opposing drug addiction. While striving to destroy opium, the province has actively detected and arrested those who engage in drug trafficking. Recently, the province monitored and arrested the driver of a car with 43.7 kg of opium.
