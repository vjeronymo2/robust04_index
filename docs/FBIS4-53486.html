BFN [Text] Seoul, May 17 (YONHAP) -- President Kim Yong-sam on Tuesday called upon the nation's scientists and science-related officials to redouble efforts to further develop science and technology. While presiding over a meeting of the State Advisory Council for science and technology at Chongwadae [presidential office], President Kim emphasized the need of using available science resources most effectively because he said such resources are limited in the country compared with advanced countries. The meeting was called to observe the first anniversary of the advisory council. The president also asked for the resolute removal of what stand in the way to the nation's internationalization. "The extent of our internationalization ranks 11th among developing countries, undermining our efforts to enhance the country's international competitiveness," Kim said. Following the meeting, President Kim hosted a luncheon at Chongwadae for the advisory council members led by chairman Yi Sang-ui.
