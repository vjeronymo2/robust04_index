920305 FT 05 MAR 92 / International Company News: Woodside surges to ADollars 116.5m WOODSIDE Petroleum, the Australian operator of the North West Shelf gas project which is 40 per cent owned by Royal Dutch/Shell, yesterday reported a surge in net profits to ADollars 116.51m (USDollars 89.6m) for 1991 from ADollars 49.69m a year earlier, Reuter reports. Earnings per share jumped to 17.5 cents from 7.5 cents and the year's dividend is rising to 8 cents a share from 5 cents. Sales rose to ADollars 446.22m from ADollars 367.51m. Net profits were struck after lower interest charges of ADollars 122.25m against ADollars 144.19m and there was again no tax charge.
