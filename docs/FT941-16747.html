940108 FT 08 JAN 94 / Mexican uprising spreads north A week-old peasant rebellion that began in Mexico's southernmost state spread north yesterday, with all airports on maximum alert and the stock exchange evacuated after a bomb threat, Reuter reports. The rebels' Zapatista National Liberation Army, which began its uprising in the hilly state of Chiapas, said it had toppled two 30-metre electricity towers on Thursday in towns south of the capital.
