BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] It has been announced that Turkey is ready to make every possible contribution of observers to the Caucasus within the framework of the CSCE and to meeting the personnel and other requirements of this mission. Foreign Ministry spokesman Ferhat Ataman, replying to a question at his weekly news conference, stressed that Turkey, as a responsible member of the international community, actively participates in efforts to secure world peace and stability. Recalling that Turkey contributed to the UN peacekeeping forces in Somali and Bosnia-Herzegovina, Ataman added that Turkey is willing to fulfill its other international responsibilities with the same conscientious attitude. [Begin Ataman recording] As a matter of fact, Turkey is ready to make every possible contribution of observers to the Caucasus within the framework of the CSCE and to meeting the personnel and other requirements of this mission in order to solve the dispute between Azerbaijan and Armenia peacefully through the Armenian withdrawal from Azerbaijani territory and in order to establish an atmosphere of stability in the Caucasus. [end recording]
