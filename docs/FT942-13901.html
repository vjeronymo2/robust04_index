940422 FT 22 APR 94 / UK Company News: IFG IFG Group, the Dublin-based specialist financial services company, reported a 39 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from IPounds 528,000 to IPounds 732,000 (Pounds 709,000) for 1993. This was after charging goodwill previously written off of Pounds 72,000 to comply with FRS 2. Turnover rose to IPounds 10.3m, against a restated IPounds 9.83m. Earnings per share were 1.35p (0.92p) and the single dividend is increased to 0.3p (0.25p).
