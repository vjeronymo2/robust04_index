BFN [Text] Berlin (DDP/ADN) -- Chancellor Helmut Kohl described the great unity with which the CDU/CSU [Christian Democrat Union/Christian Socialist Union] group voted for Roman Herzog in the presidential elections yesterday as very important. He was also very pleased that the majority of representatives of the FDP [Free Democratic Party] had also voted for Herzog, Kohl said in Berlin today. The chancellor leveled harsh criticism at the Social Democrats. He said he could not understand the complaints of several SPD representatives. The SPD has proved itself a bad loser.
