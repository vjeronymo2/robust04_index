Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received a message from Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto. The message was handed to Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' at a meeting this morning with Mr. Khan, the special envoy of the Pakistani prime minister. The meeting was attended by the minister's assistant, Ruslan 'Allush; the director of the Asia Desk, Walid Rajab; and the Pakistani ambassador in Damascus, Naimul Hasan. Discussion at the meeting dealt with the situation in Kashmir.
