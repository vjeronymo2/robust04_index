930401 FT 01 APR 93 / Vietnam jails eight intellectuals A VIETNAMESE court has jailed eight anti-government intellectuals for up to 20 years after a two-day trial, ending a case that has aroused concern in the west, Reuter reports from Hanoi. They pleaded guilty to 'activities aimed at overthrowing the government,' the official Vietnam news agency (VNA) said. The people's court in Ho Chi Minh City on Tuesday sentenced Mr Doan Viet Hoat to 20 years in prison. Two chief associates were jailed for 16 and 12 years. Five others convicted of belonging to an organisation called Freedom Forum were jailed for between eight months and seven years.
