BFN [Text] Russian peace envoy for Nagorno-Karabakh Vladimir Kazimirov says that there could be no cease-fire in the disputed territory unless peacekeepers have been sent there. In an interview with Interfax, he spoke of no other alternative being envisaged for the time being. At the same time, Kazimirov stressed the forces that the military officials of Armenia, Nagorno-Karabakh and Russia had agreed on sending to the area of conflict were not intended to disengage troops. Instead, they were designed to ensure the security of military observers including one common and three regional headquarters and a large number of stationary observation posts and mobile observer groups.
