Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The talks between the Israeli and Palestinian delegations in Cairo have ended. Amnon Shahaq and Nabil Sha'th, heads of the two delegations, expressed satisfaction with the progress made in the current round of talks and expect further progress to be made when they meet again in Cairo next week. Our correspondent Karmela Menashe reports the delegations discussed three main issues: security problems, the transfer of civilian powers, and what is known as confidence-building measures such as the release of prisoners. The delegations also discussed the preparations for and the size of the Palestinian police force. The Civilian Affairs Committee discussed archaeological issues, the electronic media, and land and population registration.
