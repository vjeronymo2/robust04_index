941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Philip Green in Owen Owen deal Mr Philip Green, former head of Amber Day (now WEW) and present chairman of discount chain Xceptions, is going into department store retailing with the purchase of Owen Owen for an undisclosed sum. Together with Mr David Thompson, WEW's former finance director, he has acquired the 13-store group from SPP-LET International. Owen Owen's turnover last year was Pounds 175m, and although it made a loss of about Pounds 2m, Mr Green said he believed it could be made profitable by sharing distribution and administration costs with Xceptions.
