BFN [Text] Baku, June 13 (TURAN) -- The employee of the "Yeni Azerbaijan" Party's Central Apparatus Akhliman Tagiyev told TURAN, that head-quarter of the party was attacked at night on June 12. The entrance of the head-quarter was shelled by machine guns by unknown people. Tagiyev noted, that Rafael Allakhverdyev, major of Baku, the Head of Police Office and the heads of legal force bodies of Yasamal district in Baku visited the place of incident. According to him, the case was brought to trial and investigation is being held now. According to Tagiyev, the watchman of the building is under suspicion (a student of Baku State University). Some hours before the incident he left his working place. According to Tagiyev "it was the act of diversion against the president of the country, Geydar Aliyev, the chairman of the Party".
