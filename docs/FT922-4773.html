920603 FT 03 JUN 92 / Swiss petition campaign succeeds against fighter order THE Swiss cabinet has proposed delaying a Dollars 2.3bn order of 34 McDonnell Douglas F/A-18 fighters following the success of a petition campaign against it, writes Ian Rodger in Zurich. Many Swiss business leaders, who are also top army officers, are appalled at the unprecedented popular intervention in procurement decisions. But others think the country's security needs have changed, and a sophisticated fighter like the F/A-18 is no longer needed.
