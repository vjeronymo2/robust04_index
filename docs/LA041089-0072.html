April 10, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Trump Wants Tower in Brazil: New York real estate magnate Donald J. Trump said he wanted to construct a Trump Tower-style building in Brazil. "What I enjoy most is building great buildings. I think my most likely investment in Brazil would be building a Trump Tower-type building . . . a glamorous, super-quality building," Trump told a weekend news conference in Sao Paulo. He said the tower would be built there or in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil's biggest cities. The Trump Tower is a Fifth Avenue skyscraper where Trump has his headquarters. Trump, in Brazil to make a donation to a hospital, said, "It is very likely I will make an investment here. Brazil is one of the few places in this troubled world I feel confident to make an investment in."
