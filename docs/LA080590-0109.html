August 5, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: COUNTYWIDE

San Diego Sheriff's deputies are investigating an apparent murder-suicide in Cameron Corners near Campo on Saturday.

An unidentified citizen flagged down Sheriff Patrol Deputy William C. Smith at about 5 p.m. after the residents of the home located in the 1700 block of Buckman Springs failed to keep a morning appointment, said Lt. John Tenwold.

On entering the home, Smith discovered the body of a white man who appeared to be in his 60s and a white woman in her 50s.

The couple were not identified pending notification of relatives. Sheriff's deputies are continuing their investigation.
