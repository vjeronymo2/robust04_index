August 4, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COSTA MESA;

WORKSHOP SLATED ON RE-ENTERING COLLEGE

Orange Coast College will hold a free workshop for people who want to re-enter college after a break in their academic careers.

The two-hour workshop is designed for people who want to make career changes or learn new job skills. It will be held Aug. 15 from 6 to 8 p.m. in the Re-Entry Center, behind the college's Counseling and Admissions Building.

The college is at 2701 Fairview Road. For information, call (714) 432-5162.
