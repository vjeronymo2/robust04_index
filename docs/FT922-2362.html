920617 FT 17 JUN 92 / Union merger passes hurdle THE formation of a 1.5m-strong public-service union was yesterday approved by the annual conference of the Confederation of Health Service Employees. Plans for the union, comprising Nalgo, Nupe and Cohse, will face a stiffer test today when Nalgo's conference discusses the merger. At Cohse the vote was 2,133 in favour, 918 against.
