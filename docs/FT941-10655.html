940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed a strong demand, reports The Tea Brokers' Association. Coloury assams advanced by several pence with mediums firm while plainer types sold more readily though at slightly easier rates. Best liquoring and good medium East Africans generally gained 4 to 6p, sometimes more, with mediums firm to dearer but plainest sorts again tended lower. Ceylons were again well supported and frequently put on 4p to 8p by the close. Offshore offshore teas met good demand at firm to dearer rates. Quotations: quality 185p/kg, good medium 140p/kg, medium 112p/kg and low medium 86p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 238p for a Rwanda pf.
