Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Thirteen local residents were wounded as soldiers fired on rioters in Jabalyah in the Gaza Strip. In Janin, a Molotov cocktail was hurled at a Border Police patrol. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the policemen gave chase and detained the thrower, who had another Molotov cocktail in his possession. [Tel Aviv IDF Radio in Hebrew at 1500 GMT on 16 March adds: "Palestinian students in Judaea, Samaria, and Gaza today went back to school, after an extended interval which started after the Hebron massacre. In Hebron, students were prevented from going back to school by the ongoing curfew." [The report further states that "a young Arab woman armed with a knife today attempted to stab soldiers near the Ramallah Police building. One of the soldiers fired at the assailant's feet. She was wounded and taken in for questioning."] The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] has started building an outpost in Qiryat Arba' designed to enhance settlers' security. The outpost will be situated on the Qiryat Arba' municipal land between Qiryat Arba' and the Giv'at Haharsina neighborhood. Our correspondent notes that the construction of the outpost is being done in concert and coordination with Qiryat Arba' leaders.
