Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Damascus, 10 Jan (KUNA) -- First Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad has held a meeting with Syrian Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam. Kuwaiti Ambassador to Syria Ibrahim al-Musa, who attended the meeting, said that the discussion dealt with the firm fraternal relations between the two fraternal countries Kuwait and Syria and ways of developing them in various fields. In his statement to the KUWAITI NEWS AGENCY, Ambassador al-Musa added that Shaykh Sabah and Khaddam expressed satisfaction with the development of relations between the two countries and stressed their desire to bolster them. He pointed out that the talks dealt also with the subjects discussed by the Damascus Declaration states, the situation in the area, and the peace process in light of the planned summit between Presidents al-Asad and Clinton in Geneva in the middle of this month.
