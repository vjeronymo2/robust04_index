March 13, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: LEGAL FILE

'Most Wanted' Show Prompts Arrest: A murder suspect was in custody in Los Angeles on Monday after an anonymous caller telephoned police following Sunday night's episode of "America's Most Wanted." Based on information provided by the caller, Segundo Matias, 27, was arrested about 10:30 p.m. Sunday by officers on a murder warrant issued nearly two years ago in Honolulu, said Sgt. Mike Evers. BETH KLEID
