July 8, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COAST'S CORDOVA SIGNS WITH THE TWINS

Marty Cordova, a third baseman from Orange Coast College, has signed with the Minnesota Twins, the team announced Friday.

Cordova, a 10th-round selection in the June free-agent draft, will be sent to the Twins' Class-A affiliate in Elizabethton, Tenn.
