Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] According to Israeli radio, the PLO and the Israeli Government are holding contacts about a possible meeting today or tomorrow between brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] and Shim'on Peres, who will try to talk him into resuming the negotiations. Peres sent a message to brother Abu-'Ammar regarding expediting the withdrawal from the Gaza Strip and the Jericho area, boosting the size of the Palestinian police force, taking over immediate [as heard] responsibility for the Strip and Jericho, and the deployment of international observers in the projected Palestinian self-rule area.
