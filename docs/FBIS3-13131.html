Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 Mar (MENA)--Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih left Cairo for home tonight after a short visit to Egypt that lasted a few hours. During the visit, he held talks with President Husni Mubarak on developments in the internal situation in Yemen and on the future of bilateral relations. President Mubarak led the send-off party of the Yemeni president at Cairo Airport.
