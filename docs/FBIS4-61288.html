BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 4 May -- A further meeting of the interdepartmental commission on Tajikistan has been held at the Russian Foreign Affairs Ministry. Cooperation between the two countries was discussed in the light of the visit to Moscow, starting tomorrow, by Emomali Rakhmonov, chairman of the National Assembly of Tajikistan. Considerable attention at the meeting, which was chaired by Anatoliy Adamishin, first deputy head of the Russian Federation's foreign policy department, was devoted to the situation on the Tajik-Afghan border and to the complex of problems connected with a political settlement of the situation in Tajikistan.
