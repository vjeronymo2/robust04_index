BFN [Unattributed report: "'Udayy Dismisses Five Journalists"] [Text] Amman, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- 'Udayy Saddam Husayn, son of the Iraqi president, has prevented five Iraqi journalists from exercising their profession and decided to dismiss them from the the Journalists Association he heads. Among these journalists are Lutfi al-Khayyat and Ahmad Sabri, both members of the association's council. The decision came the day after the announcement in the Iraqi press that 'Udayy had been chosen as chairman of the Youth Union. This is his latest post, as he also heads the Students Union, the Journalists Association, the Olympic Committee, the Football Association, and the cultural grouping supervising the Writers Union and the Artists Union. He also heads the Saddam Engineering College and supervises Baghdad University, al-Mustansiriyah University, the Saddam Medical College, and the Scientific Research Center. He is also chairman of the meat company in al-Dawrah and the meat company in al-Mahmudiyah. In addition to the declared 13 posts he also assumes undeclared political and security tasks.
