Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [From Tokyo NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 27 Feb 94 Morning Edition] [Text] On 26 February Terusuke Terada, the Foreign Ministry's director general for Public Information and Cultural Affairs, issued a comment on the massacre of Palestinians by an Israeli immigrant, saying: "It is truly regrettable and we firmly denounce it." In addition, he expressed hope it would not have a negative impact on the Middle East peace talks.
