941114 FT 14 NOV 94 / Equity Markets: Amsterdam - Other markets The final price for EVC International, the PVC maker which is being floated off by Enichem of Italy and ICI of the UK, is expected to be set today. On Friday, the market fields the third-quarter results from the paper and packaging group, KNP BT. Hoare Govett expects earnings per share of 63 cents for the quarter, compared with a loss of 11 cents in the year earlier period and a gain of 71 cents in the second quarter of 1994.
