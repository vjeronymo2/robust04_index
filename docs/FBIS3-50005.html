Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, Jan 19 (CTK) -- Pavel Tigrid is to be new Czech culture minister to replace Jindrich Kabat, who resigned on Monday [17 January], Jan Kasal (KDU-CSL), deputy chairman of the Czech Parliament's Chamber of Deputies, told CTK today. Kabat is a KDU-CSL member while Tigrid is without party affiliation. Tigrid, 77, lived in exile after the communist coup of February 1948. For 35 years he was editor-in-chief of the quarterly SVEDECTVI (TESTIMONY) published in Paris. After the fall of communism in Czechoslovakia in late 1989, he returned to Czechoslovakia and became adviser to Vaclav Havel, who was then Czechoslovak president.
