940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / UK Company News: EFM seeks Pounds 75m for Latin trust - Issue News Edinburgh Fund Managers is hoping to raise more than Pounds 75m with the launch of a new investment trust specialising in Latin America, writes Bethan Hutton. The Edinburgh Inca Trust will invest 70 per cent in medium and larger companies, and 30 per cent in smaller companies, initially in Mexico, Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Columbia and Peru. The institutional placing will start this week, and the public offer is expected to open on March 11 and close after two weeks. Shares will be issued at 50p with one warrant attached to every five shares. EFM launched a Latin American unit trust in 1992, which is the third best performing unit trust in the international equity growth sector over the year to February 1, according to Hardwick Stafford Wright.
