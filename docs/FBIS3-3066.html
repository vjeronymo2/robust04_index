Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Qingdao, March 12 (XINHUA) -- The 11th Qingdao Foreign Trade Fair opens today in Qingdao, a scenic coastal city in east China's Shandong Province, three months earlier than usual. More than 600 overseas businessmen from Japan, Hong Kong, the United States, Germany, the Republic of Korea, Russia, Italy, Singapore and Taiwan attended the ceremony. It is estimated that more than 3,000 overseas businessmen will attend the fair. Shandong, one of the leading economic powers in China, held the first Qingdao Foreign Trade Fair in 1982. This year the local government advanced the date of the fair and cut short its duration from the usual 10 days to seven days. A delegation from northwestern Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region is attending the fair.
