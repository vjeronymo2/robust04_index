Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Zhirinovskiy Offers Military Aid"] [Text] At the press conference in Podgorica, Montenegro, Russian ultra nationalist Vladimir Zhirinovskiy said if Macedonia asks for a Russian military aid, it will easily be given to it, "just like to any other Slavic country." MAKPRESS informs Zhirinovskiy, asked "why he never includes Macedonia in his list of Orthodox Christian countries," answered "we have no reason to argue with Orthodox Greece." Referring to Zhirinovskiy's statements in general, Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev said Vladimir Zhirinovskiy is "not a political but a medical case."
