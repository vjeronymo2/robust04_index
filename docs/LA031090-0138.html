March 10, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COSTA MESA;

WHEELCHAIR ATHLETES IN BASKETBALL BENEFIT

Campus Colleagues, a disabled-students organization at Orange Coast College, will hold a wheelchair basketball tournament and benefit concert today to raise money for its scholarship program.

The tournament will feature the Casa Colina Condors, one of the nation's top wheelchair basketball teams. Winners will advance to the Western regional championships later this month in Pomona.

Games will begin at 10 a.m. and 2 p.m. in the college's Peterson Gymnasium. Admission is free.
