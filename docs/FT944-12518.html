941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Rackwood advances Pre-tax profits at Rackwood Mineral Holdings rose from Pounds 29,000 to Pounds 111,000 for the half-year to June 30, helped by a Pounds 283,000 profit on the sale of its Old Leyland Green Remainder site. The opencast mining group had operating losses of Pounds 130,000, compared with profits of Pounds 250,000, on turnover down 44 per cent from Pounds 2.97m to Pounds 1.67m. Earnings per share came out at 0.44p (0.18p), and there is no interim dividend.
