BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 27 May -- "The purpose of my visit to Tajikistan is to have talks with the republican leadership on further steps by the UN with regard to participating in finding a settlement to the inter-Tajik conflict," Marrack Goulding, UN under secretary general for political questions, told a group of Russian correspondents on arrival in Dushanbe. The second round of inter- Tajik talks, he said, is due to take place in Tehran on 7 June. Asked by a correspondent if the question on enhancing the status of the Dushanbe official delegation at the talks would be raised during the meetings, Marrack Goulding stressed that he would not like to go into details at this moment. He promised to speak in detail about the results of his meetings with the Tajik leadership, including his meeting with Emomali Rahmonov, at a news conference. Marrack Goulding reaffirmed that he is bringing with him a note from UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali for Emomali Rahmonov, chairman of the Tajik Supreme Soviet. However, he said, he would not wish to reveal the contents of this note just now. A meeting will take place today between Marrack Goulding and Tajik Foreign Minister Rashid Alimov, Labor and Employment Minister Shukur Zukhurov, and Security Minister Said Amir Zukhurov. During his visit the UN under secretary general will be received by Tajik Premier Abdulmadjid Samadov.
