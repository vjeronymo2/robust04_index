BFN [Text] Asked to comment on a statement by Turkish Cypriot leader Rauf Denktas that President Glavkos Kliridhis did not accept a proposal to meet to discuss defense and armament issues, government spokesman Ioannis Kasoulidhis said there had been no proposal by Denktas for a meeting with Kliridhis. Kasoulidhis reiterated that President Kliridhis' demilitarization proposal remains valid.
