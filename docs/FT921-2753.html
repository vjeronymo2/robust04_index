920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / UK Company News In Brief BRITISH DREDGING, through its builders' merchant subsidiary, J Thomas Edwards and Sons, has paid about Pounds 1.5m in cash to acquire 604 Building Supplies from the receivers. ***** CANNON STREET Investments has sold Stalbridge Kwick Klean Laundry to a new company set up by Stalbridge's management team in a deal which cuts CSI's net borrowings by about Pounds 5m. Cash consideration was Pounds 4.13m while Stalbridge's net borrowings were Pounds 844,000 at end of 1991. ***** CAPITA GROUP has acquired the international consultancy division of the Royal Institute of Public Administration for Pounds 430,000 in cash. RIPA is a leading independent supplier of public and government sector training and consultancy courses for senior officials. ***** CAPITAL RADIO has sold The Duke of York's Theatre to a newly-formed company controlled by, among others, Mr Eddie Kulukundis and London TurnStyle (DOY). Consideration was Pounds 2.73m in cash and the intercompany loan account of Pounds 0.32m was repaid. ***** HEADLAM GROUP has announced results of its placing and open offer: 8.3m new ordinary (71.5 per cent) taken up by shareholders; 2.47m (100 per cent of entitlement) taken up by directors; 3.3m otherwise placed. ***** HILL & SMITH Holdings is paying up to Pounds 3.35m in loan notes for Duct and Access Covers, a Gwent-based maker of steel manhole covers, frames and associated drainage products. Duct had net assets of Pounds 1.05m at September 30 1991. ***** PADANG SENANG Holdings made pre-tax profit of Pounds 97,832 (Pounds 70,166) for 1991 on turnover Pounds 406,472 (Pounds 325,590). Dividend of 0.8p (0.6p) on earnings 1.12p (0.85p). ***** PORVAIR has received acceptances in respect of 2.98m new ordinary shares (96.4 per cent) offered by way of rights at 115p each. ***** UNILEVER has bought the outstanding 50.1 per cent interest in Jalostaja Oy, Finnish food company with annual turnover of about Pounds 60m. The terms were not disclosed. ***** WILKES (JAMES) announced that it has exchanged contracts with Electronic Data Processing for the sale of the freehold properties, Beauchief Hall and Pegges House, Sheffield. The consideration is Pounds 1.65m cash at completion, which is expected to take place on April 10.
