BFN [Text] Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] leader Afonso Dhlakama registered as a voter at the Polana Secondary School today. Asked whether emigrants would vote, Dhlakama said that was a different matter and what was important at the moment was for him to register as a voter. Dhlakama once again accused voter registration teams of failing to carry out their work properly, but pointed out that the situation was not alarming. The Renamo leader goes to Maringue today where he will leave for Beira, Chimoio, and Tete.
