Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 7 -- Do Muoi, general secretary of the Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee (CPVCC) received here on March 5 a visiting high-level military delegation of Laos led by Defence Minister Sen. [Senior] Lt. Gen. Choummali Sai-gnason. Present at the reception were Gen. Doan Khue, Politburo member of the CPV CC and minister of national defence. Lieut. Gen. Nguyen Thoi Bung, member of the CPVCC and deputy minister of national defence and Do Van Tai, deputy head of the party's external relation commission. Sen. Lieut. Gen. Choummali Sai-gnason expressed his pleasure at the further consolidation and development of the friendship and fraternal solidarity between the two armies. He affirmed that the party, the state, the army and people of Laos are determined to defence and foster the special friendship, fraternal solidarity and multi-faceted cooperation between Vietnam and Laos. Sen. Lieut. Gen. Choummali Sai-gnason conveyed to General Secratary Do Muoi best regards from Khamtai Siphandon, chairman of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party and prime minister, and Nouhak Phoumsavan, president of Laos. For his part, General Secretary Do Muoi clearly pointed out that Vietnam and Laos are close neighbours, having tradition of solidarity and mutual assistance in their struggle for independence of each country. Nowadays, the two parties and states are unanimous to continue consolidating and developing such precious tradition in their national defence and construction in the interests of the two peoples and for peace, stability and cooperation in the region and the world as a whole. On this occasion, General Secretary Do Muoi asked the delegation to convey his best regards to Khamtai Siphandon, Nouhak Phoumsavan and other leaders of Laos.
