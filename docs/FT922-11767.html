920423 FT 23 APR 92 / UK Company News: Cookson pays Dollars 18m to complete US deal In accordance with the terms of its 1988 agreement, Leach & Garner has exercised its put option for Cookson Group, the industrial materials company, to acquire Leach's 50 per cent stake in Stern Leach for Dollars 18m (Pounds 10.1m). As a result, Stern Leach has become a wholly-owned subsidiary of Cookson.
