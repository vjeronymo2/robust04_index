Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, received in Aden today brother Yusuf Bin-'Alawi, minister of state for foreign affairs of the fraternal Sultanate of Oman, who conveyed a message to the vice chairman of the Presidential Council from his brother His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of fraternal Oman. The message deals with the brotherly relations and issues of interest to both countries. During the meeting, they discussed areas of cooperation between our country and the fraternal Sultanate of Oman and ways to promote them. They also exchanged views on current developments. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, voiced his appreciation for Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id's position, as well as the eagerness of the brothers in the Sultanate of Oman to support the march of unity and democracy. He also welcomed all honest Arab efforts to help our country overcome its complications and difficulties, to contribute to the march of unity and democracy, and to maintain security and stability in our country. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, stressed the brotherly relations between our country and the Sultanate of Oman, as well as the need to promote them in the services of the joint interests of the Yemeni and Omani peoples and to meet the two people's aspirations. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid asked his brother Yusuf Bin-'Alawi to convey his greetings to His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id, as well as his best wishes of further progress for the Omani people on the path of good, happiness, and development. The meeting was attended by brother Dr. Yasin Sa'id Nu'man, member of the Political Bureau and head of the Secretariat of the Yemeni Socialist Party Central Committee; Salih Abu-Bakr Bin-Husaynun, minister of oil and mineral resources; Muhammad Sa'id 'Abdallah, minister of housing and urban planning; and Salih Munassar al-Sili, governor of the Aden Governorate. On the Omani side, the meeting was attended by Yusuf al-Hariki, head of the Arab Department at the Foreign Ministry; and 'Awad Mahmud Bakathir, ambassador of the Sultanate of Oman to our country.
