940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / World Trade News: Rolls-Royce plant order Rolls-Royce, the UK aero-engine and power equipment group, has won its first full turnkey power station order in Indonesia through a Pounds 60m contract from PLN, the national electricity utility, writes Andrew Baxter. The UK company will supply all the electromechanical equipment and handle civil works for a 60MW combined cycle power station at Samarinda in East Kalimantan.
