Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, March 24 (ATA) -- Unknown persons have put explosives into several houses of the former politically persecuted who have taken part in the democratic changes. Bashkim Kodhima, head of the Association Former Political Persecuted of the Saranda district branch, and Minella Boci, the head of the former economically persecuted and kulaks of the district, issued yesterday a protest note addressed to the Ministry of Public Order, Ministry of Justice, the National Information Service (SHIK), through which they urge to find the terrorists. Mr. Kodhima and Mr. Boci blame leftist extremists to have done this act.
