Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Thailand and China have agreed on cooperation with civil lawsuits and arbitration between the two countries. Minister of Justice Sawai Phatthano says he had reached the agreement with his Chinese counterpart during his recent visit to China. Minister Sawai says according to the agreement, Thai nationals in China will be under the justice authority designated under the same category and at the same level as the Chinese authority. In addition, Thai nationals in China will now have the right to fight a lawsuit against individuals in China. He says Thailand is the 18th country to sign such an agreement with China. Earlier Thailand and China have signed an extradition agreement. The minister says he and his Chinese counterpart have agreed to promote arbitration as a means to settle disputes over marine trade and transportation between the countries. The minister of justice says that for the 19 years of Thai-Chinese diplomatic relations, their cooperation in laws should be made to support other ties.
