BFN [Excerpt] Brother Mahmud 'Abbas, also known as Abu-Mazin, member of the PLO Executive Committee, arrived in Abu Dhabi from Cairo last night on a visit of several days to the United Arab Emirates [UAE]. During the visit, brother Abu-Mazin will hold talks with senior UAE officials. The talks will deal with the Middle East peace process and the hurdles facing the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho accord. [passage omitted]
