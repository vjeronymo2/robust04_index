Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- SPD [Social Democratic Party] leader Rudolf Scharping has promised PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat his "unlimited support" in efforts toward a solution for peace in the Middle East. In Bonn today in a letter Scharping conveyed to 'Arafat his sympathy for the relatives of the Palestinians who were killed in a massacre in Hebron on Friday [25 February]. The SPD leader expressed his view that the attack meant a need for the speedy continuation of the peace negotiations in the Middle East.
