940304 FT 04 MAR 94 / French in Cameroon Togolese President Gnassingbe Eyadema flew to Nigeria and Cameroon yesterday to mediate in a growing border row between the two countries, and a French frigate docked in Cameroon, Reuter reports from Yaounde. A French embassy official in Yaounde said the surveillance frigate La Vendemiaire and its 90-member crew arrived in the port of Douala on Wednesday night on a 'routine visit'. Nigeria's military government had already protested on Wednesday after France sent a squad of paratroopers and two attack helicopters to Cameroon.
