931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables Spania satumas are this week's best fruit buy reports the FFVIB. They are currently available for 4-12p each, depending on size. Spania Navel oranges are also good value at 15-30p each depending on size. English and French apples are also good value. English spartans are 25-45p a lb and French Braeburns are widely available for 40-60p a lb. English and Dutch cauliflowers are 30-60p each depending on size and there is a number of cabbage varieties available; English and Dutch white cabbages are 15-25p a lb. English Brussels sprouts are available this week for 19-29p a lb making them this week's best vegetable buy.
