BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 6 (KCNA) -- Vietnamese Ambassador to Korea Duong Chinh Thuc hosted a film show and a cocktail party at his embassy Thursday evening to mark the 40th anniversary of the victory in the Dien Bien Phu battle. Invited there were the vice department director of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Kim Yang-kon, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Cho Kyu-il, Vice-Chairman of the State Education Commission Yi Kwan-su, Vice-Chairperson of the Committee for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries Chon Yon-ok, and other officials concerned. The participants saw a Vietnamese documentary film. Speeches were exchanged at the cocktail party that followed.
