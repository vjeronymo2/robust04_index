940804 FT 04 AUG 94 / World Trade News: Indian private airlines face import ban India has frozen imports of large aircraft by private airlines and decided to move slowly in permitting new airlines, Reuter reports from New Delhi. Civil aviation minister Ghulam Nabi Azad told parliament the freeze was due to congested air corridors and airports. He said imported smaller aircraft with a capacity of up to 30 seats would be encouraged to operate on short routes.
