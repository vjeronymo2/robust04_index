920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / French lose out in Kiev The French embassy in Kiev said yesterday it had lost FFr600,000 (Pounds 61,000) in the Russian takeover of Vnesheconombank, writes Chrystia Freeland in Kiev. Mr Philippe Pegorier, commercial attache, said the account opened by the Kiev embassy last July had been frozen by Vnesheconombank's Moscow headquarters. The embassy's plight is shared by hundreds of Ukrainian enterprises, said to have lost Dollars 500,000 deposited with Vnesheconombank. Their accounts were frozen in December when Russia suspended Vnesheconombank operations. Recovery of these assets and collective control over Vnesheconombank are key Ukrainian demands in negotiations over dividing Soviet debts and assets.
