921208 FT 08 DEC 92 / Observer: Upside down Even though Midland Bank is under new ownership, it cannot throw off its accident-prone image. Take the latest issue of its Meridian magazine. The editor has written a guide to help Midland customers understand financial jargon and notes that a price-earnings ratio is calculated by 'dividing the earnings per share by the price of one share'. An easy mistake to make, and Midland says it was 'purely a printing slip'.
