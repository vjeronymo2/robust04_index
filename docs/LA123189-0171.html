December 31, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

BASKETBALL ROUNDUP

MEN

The Master's 83, Grace College (Ind.) 65 -- The Master's outshot visiting Grace College, 61% to 33%, to win its second consecutive game since point guard Damon Greer joined the lineup.

Greer led Master's (9-6) with eight assists and scored nine points. Tom Bruner of Master's led all scorers with 24 points.

WOMEN

Valley 83, Ohlone 46 -- Karrie Parsons (six for eight) and Ericka Miller (five for eight) each scored 12 points in the fifth-place game of the Fullerton tournament to pace the Monarchs.

Forward Claudia Rocher, who was named to the all-tournament team, scored six points and had 10 rebounds for Valley (14-3).
