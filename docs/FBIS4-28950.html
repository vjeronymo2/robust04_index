BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 12 (KCNA) -- Members of the National Alliance for Democracy and Reunification (Chongukyonhap) and the South Korean Federation of General Student Councils (Hanchongnyon), more than 4,000 in all, held an all-people memorial service for those who fell while fighting for the nation and democracy at the amphitheatre of the Hanyang University on June 11, a Seoul-based radio reported. In his opening address, Yi Chang-pok, permanent chairman of Chongukyonhap, charged that "the present Kim Yong-sam `government' is totally blocking democratisation and reunification" and called for an all-people movement to check "the parliamentary ratification of the final act of the Uruguay Round world trade accord" and demanded a peaceful solution to the nuclear issue of the Korean peninsula. Then, a rally marking the June resistance anniversary was held to inherit the spirit of the martyrs. The ralliers vowed to fulfil the tasks to realise a genuine democracy and independent reunification on the occasion of the 7th anniversary of the resistance.
