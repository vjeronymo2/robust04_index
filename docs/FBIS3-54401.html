Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Iraq has agreed to supply Jordan with all its needs of crude oil and refined products in 1994. Minister of Energy and Mineral Resources Walid 'Asfur also said on his return to Amman after talks in Iraq that he also discussed with Iraqi officials the prospect of setting up a pipeline between Jordan and Iraq after the lifting of UN sanctions against Iraq. Mr. 'Asfur said he also discussed the possibility of setting up an oil refinery in the Port of al-'Aqabah after conducting a feasibility study on the project. Iraqi oil exports to Jordan are exempt from UN trade sanctions. During the visit, Mr. 'Asfur also discussed with the Iraqis other aspects of oil industry cooperation, including drilling and exploration.
