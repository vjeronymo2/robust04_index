930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: Irish reform of extradition halted THE IRISH government yesterday halted progress towards the reform of controversial procedures for the extradition of terrorist suspects to the UK. Mr Albert Reynolds, prime minister, stressed the hold-up was due to problems uncovered during the final drafting of a bill aimed at closing loopholes in the extradition legislation. He said the bill would now be tabled in the autumn.
