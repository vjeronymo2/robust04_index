920523 FT 23 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Enlarged Castings turns in Pounds 4.46m Profits of Castings, which has interests in ferrous metal foundries and the iron and steel industry, amounted to Pounds 4.46m pre-tax for the year ended March 31. Although that was an 8 per cent improvement over last time's Pounds 4.12m the results of William Lee, acquired a little over 12 months ago, were included. Turnover of Pounds 35.08m compared with Pounds 23.69m. Earnings emerged at 14.66p (13.35p) and a final dividend of 2.77p makes a 4.07p (3.72p) total. The directors said that in general, the level of business had improved over the last two months and added that the group should benefit from this during the coming year. The shares rose 10p to 162p.
