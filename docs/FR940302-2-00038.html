[Docket No. RP93&hyph;36&hyph;000] Natural Gas Pipeline Company of America; Notice of Informal Settlement Conference February 24, 1994. Take notice that an informal settlement conference will be convened in this proceeding on Wednesday, March 2, 1994, at 10 a.m., at the offices of the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, 810 First Street, NE., Washington, DC, for the purpose of exploring the possible settlement of the above-referenced docket.
