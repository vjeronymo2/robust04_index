940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Aran Energy Dollars 6.9m oil and gas field buy Aran Energy, the Dublin-based oil and gas exploration and development company, is buying a 100 per cent working interest in a Louisiana oil and gas field for Dollars 6.9m (Pounds 4.7m) satisfied by loan notes. The field covers mature leases and Aran believes there is a potential for redevelopment which could result in significant additions to existing reserves.
