920529 FT 29 MAY 92 / Survey of Coventry (13): The city's four MPs Robert Ainsworth, Labour, Coventry North East; electorate, 73,027; majority: 11,676, showing a swing from Labour to Conservative of 0.2 per cent. Geoffrey Robinson, Labour, Coventry North West; electorate, 64,787; majority: 6,432, showing a swing from Conservative to Labour of 1 per cent. Jim Cunningham, Labour, Coventry South East; electorate, 48,796; majority: 1,311, showing a swing from Labour to Conservative of 7 per cent. John Butcher, Conservative, Coventry South West; electorate, 63,474; majority: 1,436, giving a swing from Conservative to Labour of 1.7 per cent.
