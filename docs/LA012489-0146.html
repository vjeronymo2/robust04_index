January 24, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County P.M. Final

NATION;

40 ABORTION FOES SEIZED AT CLINIC

Forty anti-abortion activists were arrested today as they blocked the entrances to a health clinic, police said. An estimated 100 others participating in Operation Rescue marched and carried signs on the sidewalk in front of the Feminist Health Center. They cheered as protesters blocking the doors were escorted by police to two waiting buses.
