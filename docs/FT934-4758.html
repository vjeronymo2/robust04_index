931202 FT 02 DEC 93 / Observer: Rising sap George Bush's honorary knighthood may cement British relations with Texas - Mark Thatcher, Steve Wyatt and so on - but Ann Richards, the salty Democratic governor of the Lone Star State, shows much less respect for the former first family. She could well battle against the ex-president's oldest son, George Walker Bush Jr, in next year's governor's race. Getting her knives in early, she has started referring to junior as 'shrub'.
