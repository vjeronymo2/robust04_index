920807 FT 07 AUG 92 / Observer: Saving trees? Should we sympathise with the parlous state of Labour's finances or should we admire the party's parsimony? A press release put out yesterday by Frank Dobson, shadow employment secretary, carries on the masthead the name of John Underwood, its former communications director. For the record, Underwood resigned in June 1991. Will documents coming from the party leader's office continue to bear Neil Kinnock's imprimatur until stocks are exhausted?
