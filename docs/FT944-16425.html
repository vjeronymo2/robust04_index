941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Wates sells property Wates City of London Properties has sold a lease on one of its prime City of London sites for Pounds 4.1m to Axa Equity and Law Investment Managers. The 12,000 sq ft building was developed by Wates 10 years ago. Nearly half of the tenant leases expire next year, although Wates said that the principal tenant had recently extended its stay to 2009.
