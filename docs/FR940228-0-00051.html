SUMMARY: This document contains corrections to the final regulations (TD 8491), which were published in the Federal Register for Thursday, October 14, 1993 (58 FR 53125). The final regulations relate to the timing of income and deductions with respect to notional principal contracts. EFFECTIVE DATE: October 14, 1993. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Alan B. Munro, (202) 622&hyph;3950 (not a toll-free number).
