921022 FT 22 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Ensor Motors Pounds 2.55m buy-out Ensor Motors, the Mercedes truck dealership, has been bought from Ensor Holdings by Enza Motors, a company formed by its management, for Pounds 2.55m. With help from their advisers, Mr Alec Brown, managing director, and Mr Keith Webb, finance director, put together a finance package involving 3i, International Factors, Bold International Properties and Mercedes Benz group.
