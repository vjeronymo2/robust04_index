Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Algiers, Jan 6 (AFP)--Algerian security officials have identified the man behind last month's massacre of 12 Croats at Tamezguida south of the Algerian capital as Sayeh Attia, the EL WATAN daily reported Thursday [6 January] [see page 20 of 21 December 93 Terrorism Report]. Security forces have arrested Attia's father-in-law, named only as Bouchakor in the report, charging him with sheltering the armed gang after the December 15 killings, and have recovered stolen goods. Attia was reported to have led an armed band for more than a year and to be responsible for more than 200 attacks. He has been implicated in an assault on the Boughezoul barracks, 150 kilometres (90 miles) south of here, which left 19 soldiers dead, EL WATAN said. [background passage omitted]
