DATES: Comments must be file on or before July 11, 1994, and reply comments on or before July 26, 1994. 47 CFR Part 73 [MM Docket No. 94&hyph;37, RM&hyph;8449] Radio Broadcasting Services; Lihue Kauai, HI AGENCY: Fedral Communications Commission. ACTION: Proposed rules.

SUMMARY: This document requests comments on a petition by Linohau Entertainment seeking the allotment of Channel 251C to Lihue Kauai, Hawaii, as that community's third local FM transmission service. Channel 251C can be allotted to Lihue Kauai in compliance with the Commission's minimum distance separation requirements without a site restriction. The coordinates for Channel 251C at Lihue Kauai are North Latitude 21&hyph;58&hyph;48 and West Longitude 159&hyph;22&hyph;30.
