January 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

MISSING IN HOLLYWOOD

On Dec. 26, there was a very interesting article in Calendar surveying "the men and women who shape Los Angeles' cultural scene" ("Dear Santa . . . ," by Times writers).

While you chose to represent organization after organization in theater and through music, and though performance, painting and dance were all represented, not one of the excellent local film or video organizations was included. In the film and video capital of the world, this is a major omission.

I have enclosed a list of some of the organizations you should have included:

Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences, American Cinematheque, American Film Institute, Black American Cinema Society, Director's Guild of America, EZTV, FilmForum, Gay & Lesbian Coalition, Independent Documentary Assn., Independent Feature Projects, KCET, Los Angeles Contemporary Exhibitions, Plaza de la Raza, Sundance Institute, UCLA Film & TV Archives, Visual Communications, Women in Film.

GARY ESSERT

Founder/Artistic Director

American Cinematheque

Hollywood
