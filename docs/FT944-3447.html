941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / UK Company News: MS International MS International reported interim pre-tax losses of Pounds 918,000 following 'exceptional and difficult trading' in two of its subsidiary companies, MSI-Transportation Systems and Ernst Wilhelm. Last year, there were pre-tax profits for the six months to October 29 of Pounds 462,000. The interim dividend is passed (1p) while payment of a final will be considered when the full-year results are known. Losses per share emerged at 2.7p, compared with earnings of 1.1p. The shares, which fell sharply last month following a profits warning, closed yesterday at 26p. Turnover was flat at Pounds 14m (Pounds 13.9m), including Pounds 210,000 from acquisitions.
