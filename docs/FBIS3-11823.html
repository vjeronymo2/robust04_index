Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Mario Espinoza] [Excerpt] Concerning the cabinet crisis, the Council of Ministers today tendered their collective resignation to the president of the Republic. At 1100 today, the foreign minister summoned all of the ministers. We can see the images showing the arrival of all the ministers at the Foreign Ministry to actually tender their resignations. Within the next few hours, most certainly this afternoon, the new Council of Ministers will be installed at Government Palace. [passage omitted]
