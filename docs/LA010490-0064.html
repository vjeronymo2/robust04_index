January 4, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: COLLEGES;

CAL POLY ADDS PROGRAM

Cal Poly Pomona has launched an off-campus degree program in business administration.

Classes will be held at the General Dynamics Valley Systems Division in Pomona. They will be taught by instructors from Cal Poly's College of Business Administration. The degree program is a cooperative effort between the university and General Dynamics.

The General Dynamics program is one of several outreach programs being offered in the Pomona Valley. This month, Cal Poly Pomona's Office of Continuing Education will open an off-campus site at the Ontario Public Library, where credit and non-credit courses will be offered.

Classes taught at the new location will include landscape technology, management and human resources and teacher education.
