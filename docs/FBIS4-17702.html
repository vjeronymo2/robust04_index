BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Fedor Angeliy] [Text] Kishinev [Chisinau], 26 Apr -- The deputies of the Moldovan Parliament today ratified the CIS charter. There were 76 votes in favor and 18 against. The charter was signed by Moldovan President Mircea Snegur at the most recent meeting of the heads of CIS states in Moscow.
