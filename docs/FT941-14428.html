940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Division losses increase Division Group yesterday announced an increased loss of Pounds 503,000 for the year to October 31 on sales up Pounds 1m at Pounds 2.08m. The group, which came to the market in May and which makes 'virtual reality' software and hardware for professional users, had expected the increased loss, which compared with a Pounds 107,000 deficit in 1992. Shares fell by 6p to 89p. Operating loss was Pounds 593,000 (Pounds 94,000) and the deficit per share jumped to 2.1p (1p).
