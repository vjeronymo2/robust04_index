Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Lower House of Parliament resumed its debate of the Arab-Israeli peace negotiations behind a closed door. Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali made a statement last Wednesday [29 December] at the start of the debate on developments in the negotiations. His statement followed a vote in favor of a closed door meeting. Several representatives today demanded that their meeting should be held open and they argued the government had made its statement and that many things became clear after yesterday's speech by his majesty the king. After a discussion of the move the house speaker decided that the debate should again proceed behind closed doors.
