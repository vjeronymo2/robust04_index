931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / World Trade News: Ciba in Shanghai venture CIBA, the Swiss pharmaceuticals and chemicals group, has initiated a SFr34m (Pounds 15.8m) animal health joint venture near Shanghai, writes Deborah Hargreaves in Shanghai. The plant, which will produce drugs for treating parasites in animals, is part of the group's SFr50m annual investment programme in China over the next three years. Ciba aims to become the first western chemicals business to set up its own Chinese holding company; it has 20 projects in the country and is setting up a further seven joint ventures, including a Dollars 100m (Pounds 66.2m) plastic additives plant in Shanghai.
