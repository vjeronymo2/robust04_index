Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Summary From Poor Reception] The secretaries of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] organizations in the Yemeni governorates held an expanded meeting yesterday evening. Brother 'Ali Salim al- Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council and secretary general of the YSP, and other party officials attended the meeting. The conferees discussed and reviewed the current political developments in Yemen. Brother al-Bid asserted that safeguarding Yemeni unity and democracy is a major task. He pointed out that the YSP will proceed on the path of unity and democracy and of progress for the Yemeni people. He urged the YSP organizations to show vigilance and responsibility toward the fate of the people and the homeland. Al-Bid also expressed the YSP's conviction that the national dialogue can achieve its objectives. He voiced confidence that the Yemeni people can distinguish between one thing and another and know where their interest lies. Al-Bid added that our people are looking forward to building a modern state.
