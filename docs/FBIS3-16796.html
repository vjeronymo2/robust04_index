Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yuriy Biragov, chairman of the North Ossetia Supreme Soviet, and Georgiy Kozayev, first deputy chairman of the republican government, have sent a telegram on behalf of the Presidium of the North Ossetia Supreme Soviet to President Boris Yeltsin of Russia, chairman of the Federation Council Vladimir Shumeyko, and State Duma chairman Ivan Rybkin. The telegram states, inter alia, that the proposal made by a number of Russian deputies to introduce federal rule on the territory of North Ossetia has given rise to indignation among the people of the republic. Such proposals are regarded in North Ossetia as an attempt by certain forces -- I quote -- to play the national card on the Yugoslav model.
