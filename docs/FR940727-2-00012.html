SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: Section 4314 (c) (1) through (5) of Title 5, U.S.C. requires each agency to establish, in accordance with regulations prescribed by the Office of Personnel Management, one or more Senior Executive Service (SES) PRBs. The Board shall review and evaluate the initial appraisal of a senior executive's performance along with any comments by senior executives and any higher level executive and make recommendations to the appointing authority relative to the performance of the senior executive. The PRB is also responsible for providing recertification recommendations for career SES appointees in accordance with section 3393a of Title 5, U.S.C. and §317.504(f) of Title 5, Code of Federal Regulations.
