BFN [Text] Seoul, April 1 (YONHAP) -- Trade, Industry and Energy Minister Kim Chol-su and Canadian International Trade Minister Roy Maclaren held the 12th trade ministers' meeting here Friday and agreed to form a working-level team to discuss further development of a "special partnership." The ministers said their countries first developed a special partnership at the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) summit in Seattle last November. They agreed to hold the first meeting of the working-level team headed by assistant ministers this month. Kim called on Maclaren to extend Generalized System of Preferences (GSP) status for Korean exports to Canada, scheduled to expire at the end of June, and Maclaren vowed to consider the matter positively. The ministers also decided to form a Korea-Canada industrial technology cooperation committee to boost bilateral links in these areas.
