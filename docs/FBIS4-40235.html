BFN [Text] Prime Minister Dzhumagulov signed a resolution "On dismissing the heads of rayon state administrations" in accordance with which Torebekov Orozbek is relieved of his post as head of the Karabura rayon state administration of Talas oblast for shortcomings in his work and lack of exactingness to the personnel and for the economic situation in his rayon; Sisenov Dzolboldy is relieved of the post of head of the Suzak rayon state administration of Dzhelalabad oblast for serious shortcomings in implementing the land and agrarian reforms; Bekishev Bekbola is dismissed from the post of head of the Uchterek rayon state administration of Dzhelalabad oblast for discrediting the idea of land and agrarian reform which was expressed in many complaints about his incompetent approach to the creation of a pluralistic economic structure in the rural area. These resolutions of the government were submitted to the president of the Kyrgyz Republic for confirmation. President Akayev issued an order confirming the prime minister's resolution.
