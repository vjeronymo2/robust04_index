920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / International Company News: Burlington Northern has profit reverse BURLINGTON Northern, one of the large US railroad companies, yesterday reported fourth-quarter after-tax profits of Dollars 40m, down from Dollars 44m in the same period a year earlier, writes Nikki Tait. Income before extraordinary credits or charges stood at Dollars 54m, compared with Dollars 38m, while revenues rose from Dollars 1.16bn to Dollars 1.19bn.
