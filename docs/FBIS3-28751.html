Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Damascus, 9 Feb (SANA)--Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' this evening received Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati on the sidelines of the consultative meeting held by the foreign ministers of the nonaligned states in Jakarta. The meeting was attended by Syria's ambassador in Jakarta 'Abd-al-Salam 'Aqil and the Iranian ambassador in Jakarta. Talks covered the peace process in light of the outcome of the Geneva summit, as well as bilateral relations. The two sides discussed issues on the agenda of the Nonaligned Movement's consultative meeting. Faruq al-Shar' earlier met with Pakistani Foreign Minister Asaf Ahmad Ali in the presence of Syria's ambassador in Jakarta, 'Abd-al-Salam 'Aqil. The two sides discussed bilateral relations and issues of common interest.
