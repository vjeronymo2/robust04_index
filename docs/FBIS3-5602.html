Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] On 5 March, Comrade Carlos Lage, member of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of Cuba and vice president of Council of State and standing secretary of the Council of Ministers received our Ministry of Construction delegation led by Comrade Nguyen Manh Kiem, deputy minister of construction, who was on a working visit in Cuba from 25 February to 6 March. Comrade Carlos Lage affirmed that Cuba is ready to create all favorable conditions for the development of the cooperative relations with Vietnam. He also expressed hope that the VTC Company, the first joint venture between the two countries, will flourish.
