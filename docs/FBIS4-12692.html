BFN [Text] An Arab woman was fatally shot by an Israeli truck driver in the territories today. The incident occurred near the village of al-Jib, south of Ramallah. Our correspondent in the territories Golan Yokhpaz reports that the man was driving a Coca-Cola truck along the Pisgat Ze'ev road in the direction of al-Ram junction when local residents apparently threw stones at the truck. The driver fired several shots from his handgun, killing a local woman on the spot. The commander of the Judaea Region police reported that searches to find the driver are currently under way.
