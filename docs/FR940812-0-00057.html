DATES: Effective date: July 31, 1994. Expiration date: July 31, 1995. 41 CFR Part 101&hyph;41 [FPMR Temp. Reg. G&hyph;57, Supp. 1] RIN 3090&hyph;AF53 Use of Cash for Official Travel AGENCY: Federal Supply Service, GSA. ACTION: Temporary regulation.

SUMMARY: FPMR Temporary Regulation G&hyph;57 grants agency heads or their designated representatives authority to approve all cash purchases of passenger transportation services. This supplement extends the expiration date of the temporary regulation from July 31, 1994, to July 31, 1995, to allow sufficient time for conversion to a final rule.
