Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Exchange of rocket and heavy weapons fire between the forces of Afghan President Borhanoddin Rabbani and Prime Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar shook Kabul once again today. According to a report from Kabul, quoting Afghan Defense Ministry sources, the attacks took place in eastern areas of Kabul. In addition to inflicting heavy casualties and damage, the attacks caused some damage to the television station. The sources added: Hekmatyar forces were trying to seize the Pacha Bridge and eastern areas of Kabul, but government forces repelled them. The attacks come at a time when various local commanders are trying to establish a cease-fire. [passage omitted]
