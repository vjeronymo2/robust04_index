921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / Survey of Software at Work - Autumn 1992 (18): A world leader in reinsurance - Company Snapshot MERCANTILE and General Reinsurance claims to be the biggest reinsurance company in the UK and the sixth biggest in the world. Turnover and profit: premium income Pounds 988.8m and profit Pounds 25.2m in 1991; Pounds 840.5m and Pounds 35m in 1990. Staff: 750 in the UK, based in the City of London and at Cheltenham. Human resources systems: Delphi for payroll, personnel, pensions and training administration, running on the company's central Amdahl IBM-type mainframe, serving a network of IBM-type personal computers. System cost: Pounds 25,000 initial charge for Delphi, plus Pounds 6,300 per quarter for rental.
