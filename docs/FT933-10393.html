930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Spanish lace buy for Courtaulds Textiles Courtaulds Textiles is acquiring for cash the share capital of Galler Iberica of Spain and a sister company, Texicolor. Galler is the market leader in Spain and Portugal in raschel and jacquardtronic laces, and exports about half its production, mainly to other European countries. The aggregate turnover of the acquisitions will be about Pounds 14m in 1993 and net assets at December 31 1992 were about Pounds 7m. The consideration is not material in relation to Courtaulds Textiles' net assets.
