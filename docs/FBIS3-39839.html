Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis returned this evening from Germany, where he had talks with his German counterpart Klaus Kinkel. The talks between Mikhailidhis and Kinkel centered on the Cyprus issue, specifically on the confidence-building measures, as well as on Cyprus' application for accession to the European Union. Mikhailidhis will give a news conference tomorrow morning at the Foreign Ministry on the results of his contacts in Germany.
