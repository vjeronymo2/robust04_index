July 4, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

FOURTH OF JULY CLOSINGS

Today is Independence Day, a celebration of the signing of the Declaration of Independence on July 4, 1776. The following will be closed in observance of the holiday:

All city, county, state and federal offices.

Schools, including colleges and universities.

County, state and federal courthouses.

City and county libraries.

Banks and savings and loans.

All post offices. Mail deposited in mailboxes will be picked up according to the posted holiday schedule.

TRASH: There will be no trash pickup today, and refuse collection throughout the county will be a day late the rest of the week.

ORANGE COUNTY TRANSIT DISTRICT: Buses will run on holiday schedules today. There will be no dial-a-ride or car-pool services available today.

PARKS: All city- and county-run parks and recreation facilities will be open regular hours today.
