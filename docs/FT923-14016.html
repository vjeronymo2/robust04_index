920710 FT 10 JUL 92 / Ex-PM of Bulgaria arrested MR Andrei Lukanov (pictured left in glasses), Bulgaria's former communist prime minister, was arrested yesterday on charges of misappropriating state funds and mismanaging the economy, Reuter reports from Sofia. Mr Lukanov, 54, is alleged to have diverted up to Dollars 500m (Pounds 261.7m) of state funds for aid and credits to left-wing Third World states between 1986 and 1989. 'Lukanov was arrested and charged with taking illegal decisions which contributed to the country's economic crisis,' said Mr Mihail Doychev of the prosecutor's office, Mr Lukanov is one of 60 senior communist officials from the ousted government of hardline leader Todor Zhivkov, who will face charges.
