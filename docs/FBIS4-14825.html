BFN [Excerpt] It has been reported by the government press service that the government has decided to satisfy the request from the Estonian EVTV and Reklaamitelevisioon [RETV] television companies to jointly use the television channel vacated by the Russian Ostankino Television and Radio Company. The government has also decided to propose that the Kanal Kaks television company use the whole of the former Russian television channel. According to the government's resolution today, the Ministry of Culture and Education will have to issue temporary licenses for relays of local television programs in the areas around Sillamae and Narva. EVTV and RETV have decided to split the use of the channel between them for the time being. Mart Siiman, RETV's managing director told the Estonian Telegraph Agency that there is a possibility of a merger in the future. [passage omitted]
