Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by N. Patterton] [Text] Pretoria March 11 SAPA -- State President F.W. de Klerk discussed events in Bophuthatswana with African National Congress [ANC] President Nelson Mandela on Thursday [10 March] night and Friday morning. Asked whether more defence force units would be sent into the homeland to add to those already guarding the South African Embassy in Mmabatho, Mr de Klerk's spokesman Richard Carter said events in Bophuthatswana would be monitored through the day. Decisions would be made if and when necessary. Mr Mandela, who on Friday morning was at the Pretoria showgrounds to address a workers' meeting, confirmed to reporters he was in touch with Mr de Klerk and Minister of Defence Kobie Coetsee.
