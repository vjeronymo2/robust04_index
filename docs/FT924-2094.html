921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / Plan to tackle water pollution THE underground water reserves of England and Wales face an increasing threat from industrial pollution and chemicals used in agriculture, according to a report published yesterday by the National Rivers Authority. The environment watchdog was launching its Policy and Practice of Groundwater, with the aim of monitoring protection zones around the groundwater which is stored in natural underground reservoirs called aquifers. About 75 per cent of underground water goes into the public supply.
