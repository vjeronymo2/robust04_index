BFN [Text] Iraqi opposition sources report that the revolutionaries of the south have attacked the headquarters of the district governor and security department in (al-Ghdayliyah), al-Nasiriyah Governorate, killing and wounding several Iraqi regime personnel. A statement issued by the Kurdistan Bureau of the Supreme Assembly of the Islamic Revolution in Iraq said that following the attack, the regime arrested many people in that district and a number of its agents on charges that they did not prevent the attack. The statement added that the regime launched a large attack on Abu Saddah and al-Mazra' in (al-Mudaynah) District, al-Basrah Governorate, displaced the residents, and destroyed their homes with dynamite. The statement said the Iraqi regime is about to complete preparations for a large offensive on (al-Jakka) marshes in al- 'Amarah Governorate.
