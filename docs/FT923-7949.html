920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Newmarket Venture assets decline In the six months to June 30 the net asset value per share of Newmarket Venture Capital fell from 60p to 56p, a 7 per cent decline. The company has a portfolio of venture capital investments which are in the process of being realised. Newmarket does not take realised gains on its successful investments into its profit and loss account, and does not normally expect to pay ordinary dividends.
