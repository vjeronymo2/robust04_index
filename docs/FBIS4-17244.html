BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Text] Moscow, 21 Apr -- Fighting in Nagorno-Karabakh has intensified markedly. The press service of the Nagorno-Karabakh Defense Army told ITAR-TASS by telephone that Azerbaijani forces mounted a big, two-pronged offensive today. In the Fizuli sector, attacks by Azerbaijani tanks are being supported by combat planes. One Azerbaijani tank had been knocked out by midday. In the north of the Mardakert area, where Karabakh subunits are also containing the offensive, another tank has been destroyed. There are no reports as yet on manpower losses sustained by either side. According to the latest information, a woman was killed in yesterday's Azerbaijani air raid on Stepanakert, and two children were injured. Some damage was caused. The planes were in the air again today.
