BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS free-lance correspondent Vladimir Novikov] [Text] Murmansk 25 May TASS -- A two-day Murmansk visit of Finnish Prime Minister Esko Aho, accompanied by a large group of Finnish businessmen, has been completed. The Finnish prime minister told correspondents at a final news conference his meeting with the business community of the Kola peninsula were very useful and fruitful. The news conference was also attended by head of the regional administration Yevgeniy Komarov. Late at night the premier left for Helsinki.
