Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Acting Prime Minister Yukhym Zvyahilskyy voted in Donetsk. Speaking on Ukraine's relations with the CIS countries, he told journalists that Ukraine must join the economic union, and said: There is no other way. We must take part in the Commonwealth on equal and mutually beneficial terms, and work to the mutual benefit of all CIS countries, Yukhym Zvyahilskyy said.
