930420 FT 20 APR 93 / International Company News: Chazen to join Reichmann Intl REICHMANN International, the property investment joint venture between the Reichmann family and Mr George Soros, the billionaire fund manager, said yesterday it had recruited Mr Leonard Chazen as deputy chief executive, writes Alan Friedman. Mr Paul Reichmann, chairman of Reichmann International, said he had worked with Mr Chazen when the latter had served as an adviser to Olympia & York, the troubled property company founded by the Reichmann family. Mr Chazen, who will be president of Reichmann International's US division, was previously a managing director of Tishman Speyer Properties, a property developer. Mr Chazen is expected to lead Reichmann International's team in advising Mr Soros's separate Dollars 500m Quantum Realty Fund.
