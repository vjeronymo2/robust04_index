Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, February 15 (ATA) -- The president of the Republic of Kosova, Mr. Ibrahim Rugova, met on February 14 in Prishtina with Dr. Michael Venincer, the charge d'affaires of the Embassy in Belgrade, and Mr. Hejsek Joseph, the charge d'affaires of the Embassy of Czechia. President Rugova informed the diplomats about the present situation in Kosova, which is aggravated as a result of the everyday repression. Mr. Rugova spoke also on the harsh economical and social situation there. Mr. Rugova presented to the Austrian and diplomats the political project for an independent and neutral Kosova as a more optimal solution. He stressed the necessity of an international protectorate for Kosova as a transitory phase which will make possible the normalization of life and will create the necessary conditions for negotiations.
