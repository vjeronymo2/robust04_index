S5.4.1.1 Load Tests One, Two and Three. Compliance with S4.4.1.1, S4.4.1.2, and S4.4.1.3 shall be demonstrated in the same manner as specified in S5.1.1.1, except that the loads shall be in the directions specified in S4.4.1.1, S4.4.1.2, and S4.4.1.3. S5.4.1.2 Inertia Load. Compliance with S4.4.1.4 shall be demonstrated in the same manner as specified in S5.1.1.2. S5.4.2 Door Hinges. Compliance with S4.4.3.1, S4.4.3.2 and S4.4.3.3 shall be demonstrated in the same manner as specified in S5.1.2, except that the loads shall be in the directions specified in S4.4.3.1, S4.4.3.2, and S4.4.3.3. S5.5 Sliding Back Doors. Compliance with S4.5 shall be demonstrated by applying an outward longitudinal load of 8,900 Newtons (2,000 pounds) to the load bearing members at the opposite edges of the door (17,800 Newtons (4,000 pounds) total). The demonstration may be performed either in the vehicle or with the door retention components in a bench test fixture.
