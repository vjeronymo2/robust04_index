BFN [Text] Beijing, May 13 (XINHUA) -- Luo Gan, China's state councillor and secretary-general of the State Council, met with Kham-ouan Boupha, Laotian minister of justice, and his party here this afternoon. Chinese Minister of Justice Xiao Yang took part in the meeting. Boupha and his party arrived here this morning as guests of the Chinese Ministry of Justice. After Beijing, they are scheduled to visit Shenzhen and Kunming.
