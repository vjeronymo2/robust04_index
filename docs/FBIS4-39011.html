BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Ostanin] [Text] Moscow, 3 Jun (ITAR-TASS) -- An extra-budgetary fund of social support for Russian military servicemen has been created at the Main Directorate for the Military Budget and Financing of the Russian Defense Ministry. An ITAR-TASS correspondent was told of this today by Colonel Aleksandr Goncharov of the Defense Ministry's information and press department. He said that the money will come from the activities of the military insurance company. According to a Russian Federation Defense Ministry directive, it will be used to stage military- patriotic and cultural events and to organize meetings with the participants of wars and veterans of the armed forces. It is also envisioned to use the money from the extra-budgetary fund to pay for the treatment of servicemen and their families, to provide gratuitous financial assistance to large families, and particularly servicemen in need, and as subsidies to public catering enterprises located in officers' houses and officers' clubs.
