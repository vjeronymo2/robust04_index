Section 92.52 would be amended to revise the heading, redesignate the existing text as paragraph (a), add a heading and a sentence at the beginning of newly redesignated paragraph (a), and to add a new paragraph (b), to read as follows: §92.52 Formula allocations. (a) Notification of allocation. HUD will notify each participating jurisdiction of its HOME formula allocation amount. *␣*␣* (b) New participating jurisdictions. HUD will notify each jurisdiction that has not previously participated in the HOME program of its formula allocation. Within 30 days of that notice, a newly eligible jurisdiction must notify HUD of its intent to become a participating jurisdiction. Within 90 days of the jurisdiction's submission of the notice of intent, it must submit a new or revised consolidated plan, which must be approved by HUD before funding is forthcoming.
