November 30, 1990, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

San Diego police are searching for other victims of an extortion ring operating in East San Diego after the arrest of three suspects.

Three men were arrested Wednesday on suspicion of extorting money from a number of businesses operated by Southeast Asians, authorities said.

The suspects allegedly demanded money in exchange for protection and threatened to harm the victims or their families if they did not comply.

The suspects are being held at the County Jail downtown in lieu of bail set at $100,000 each. They are: Chhiv Chhuon, 20, of the 4100 block of Euclid Avenue; Serey Siev, 19, of the 10,000 block of Westchester Avenue and Chan Um, 21, of Long Beach.

The investigation is continuing and other arrests are expected, police said.
