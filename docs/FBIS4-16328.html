CSO ["Election Results in Chernihiv Oblast"] [Text] In Chernihiv's Desnyanskyy electoral district [E.D.] No. 439 and Novozavodskyy e.d. No. 440, O. Stepanov and I. Symonenko, both representatives of the Interregional Reform Bloc [MBR], defeated two Rukh members in the election on 10 April. In Nizhyn e.d. No. 441, Chumachenko (CPU), a collective farm chairman, defeated Labor Minister M. Kaskevych; in Bakhmach e.d. No. 443, Chumak (CPU), deputy chairman of the rayon state administration, defeated a collective farm chairman; in Bobrovytsi e.d. No. 444, P. Sheyko, chairman of the rayon state administration, defeated the chairman of the rayon council; in Kozelets e.d. No. 446, former Prime Minister Vitaliy Masol defeated a CPU member; in Mena e.d. No. 447, Oleksandr Borovyk (CPU), chairman of the All-Ukrainian Council of Collective Farm Chairmen, defeated Otrosh, a Ministry of Foreign Affairs secretary; in Ripky e.d. No. 449, Anatoliy Dron, chairman of the State Committee on Municipal Services, defeated a CPU member; in Chernihiv e.d. No. 450, Henadiy Dolzhenko, first secretary of the oblast committee of the CPU, defeated the director of the Nova Joint-Stock Company. The election in Pryluky e.d. No. 442 was invalid because voter turnout was lower than required by law.
