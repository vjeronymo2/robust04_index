941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Mirror purchase Mirror Group has paid Pounds 27.3m for a long leasehold on its former headquarters at 33 Holborn, London. It will be held for either resale or redevelopment. Planning permission has been granted for a new office building on the site. Under an agreement with the estate of Mr Robert Maxwell, the group was committed to paying an annual rent of Pounds 6.1m until 1998.
