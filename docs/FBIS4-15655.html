BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow April 10 TASS -- Thirteen people, four of them chance passers-by, were injured in a grenade blast near Profsoyuznaya metro station at 22.20 here on Saturday. Nine people were hospitalised. An F-1 grenade was hurled from a dark-coloured Mercedes car without plate numbers in the direction of a group of Caucasians who stood near kiosks run by the Raketa Limited Partnership, a spokesman in Moscow's South Western District prefecture, told ITAR-TASS.
