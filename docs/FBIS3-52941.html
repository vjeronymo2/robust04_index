Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riyadh, Jan 8, SPA -- Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal, the foreign minister held a meeting here tonight with his French counterpart, Alain Juppe who is accompanying Prime Minister Edouard Balladur in his current visit to the Kingdom. During the meeting, matters of mutual interest including situation of Bosnia-Herzegovina and the peace process in the Middle East were discussed. The meeting was attended by 'Abd-al-Rahman Mansuri, undersecretary of the Foreign Ministry for political affairs, Jamil al-Hujaylan, the Saudi ambassador to France and a number of senior officials of the Foreign Ministry. From the French side, the meeting was attended by Dennis Bochard director of the Middle East Department at the French Foreign Ministry Emi Bertard, advisor at the Office of the Foreign Minister and John Pierre deputy head of the Middle East Department.
