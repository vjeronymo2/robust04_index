920623 FT 23 JUN 92 / Nadir trial THE TRIAL of Mr Asil Nadir, former chairman of Polly Peck International, and Mr John Turner, group chief accountant, will begin at the Old Bailey on March 8 next year. Mr Nadir faces 20 counts of theft for amounts totalling Pounds 30m. Mr Turner faces 10 counts of false accounting. A further preparatory hearing will be held in September.
