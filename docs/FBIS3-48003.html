Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Jan. 21 (YONHAP) -- President Kim Yong-sam is scheduled to make a state visit to China in late March at the invitation of Chinese President Jiang Zemin, Chongwadae [presidential residence] announced on Friday afternoon. The announcement made by the office of the presidential spokesman said China extended invitation to President Kim in the first signed letter from a Chinese president since the two countries entered full diplomatic relations in August 1992.
