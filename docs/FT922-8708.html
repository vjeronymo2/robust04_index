920512 FT 12 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Sterling Industries Pounds 1.58m acquisition Sterling Industries, the engineering group, has acquired Thermo Engineers for Pounds 1.58m cash from Spectrum Group (Holdings). Thermo makes heat control equipment and reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 323,000 in the year to May 31 at which date net assets were Pounds 3.32m. At completion they are expected to be Pounds 1.73m.
