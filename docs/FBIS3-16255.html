Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] The head of the Georgian state today met Colonel General Andrey Nikolayev, the commander in chief of the Russian Border Troops; Major General Lamov, the deputy commander in chief of the Russian border troops; Lieutenant General Zemtsov, the commander of the North Caucasian Border District; and Vladimir Zemskiy, the Russian ambassador Georgia. Colonel Valeri Chkheidze, the commander of the Georgian border troops, was also present at the meeting. Implementation issues of the agreement signed earlier on the status of the Russian Army deployed in Georgia, as well as the issues of the Russian-Georgian border were discussed at the meeting.
