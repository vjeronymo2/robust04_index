940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Java Trust net assets almost doubled Net assets per share of EFM Java Trust stood at 61.39p at December 31, almost double the 32.88p standing 12 months earlier. The after-tax deficit came out at Pounds 28,000 (Pounds 54,000), and losses per share worked through at 0.09p (0.18p).
