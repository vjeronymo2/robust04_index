BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 18 (KCNA) -- Kim Pyong-sik, chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Social Democratic Party, on April 17 met and had a friendly conversation with A.D. Vengerovsky, vice-chairman of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, and his entourage. The Russian guests said that it was their great honor to visit Korea at the most pleasant time when the birthday of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song was celebrated. Though our stay is not long, we saw at first hand the achievements made by the Korean people under the guidance of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song, they said. They said they would strive to develop the friendly relations between Russia and Korea.
