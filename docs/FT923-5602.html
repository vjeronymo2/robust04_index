920829 FT 29 AUG 92 / International Company News: Movenpick passes annual dividend MOVENPICK, the Swiss restaurants and hotels group in which Mr August von Finck of Munich bought the controlling stake last December from the founding Prager family, reported a 1991 loss of SFr3.6m (Dollars 2.90m) and passed its annual dividend for the first time in its history, writes Ian Rodger. In 1990, net profit was SFr22.5m, and a SFr110 per bearer share dividend was paid.
