May 14, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Leading scorer Terry Cummings could return for the Milwaukee Bucks today in Game 3 of their National Basketball Assn. Eastern Conference playoff series against the Detroit Pistons at Milwaukee.

Cummings, who has missed the last three playoff games, had a cast removed from a sprained right ankle Friday.

The Bucks, who trail the series, two games to none, also are without Paul Pressey, who has a separated shoulder, and Paul Mokeski, who has two broken ribs.
