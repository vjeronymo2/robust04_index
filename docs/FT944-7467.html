941124 FT 24 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Emerald Energy Emerald Energy, the USM-quoted oil and gas explorer, is raising Pounds 485,000 through a placing of 25m shares at 2p with funds managed by GFM International Investors, the investment arm of Metropolitan Life. GFM will become Emerald's second largest shareholder. The shares rose 1/4 p to 2 1/4 p. The proceeds will be used in the development of its interests in the US.
