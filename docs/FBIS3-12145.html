Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Muscat, Feb 26, SPA -- The Sultanate of Oman has condemned the massacre perpetrated by Israeli settlers inside the al-Ibrahimi Mosque. The OMANI NEWS AGENCY quoted an official Foreign Ministry spokesman as describing this terrorist act as a heinous crime and holding the Israeli Government responisble for it. Oman urged that this act would not affect the peace process in the Middle East as the only way out of the whirlpool of violence engulfing the region.
