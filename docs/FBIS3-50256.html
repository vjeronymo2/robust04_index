Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The labor conflict begun by the Rovinari Federation of Free Trade Unions has spread, practically speaking, throughout the coal mining region administered by the Targu Jiu Autonomous Lignite Public Corporation, our correspondent Titus Stoichitoiu reports. Over 65,000 employees have stopped work, the only departments still functioning being those that transport personnel inside the pits and deliver coal to the population. Another warning strike and a rally was announced today by employees of the Targu Jiu [mining equipment] Repair Plant. Similarly, the Lignite Mining Trade Union Confederation, which is affiliated to the union confederation headed by Miron Cosma [union leader of the Jiu Valley miners], requested permission to organize a rally at Rovinari, but the local police and mayor's office refused, suggesting that it should be postponed until after the general strike in the region.
