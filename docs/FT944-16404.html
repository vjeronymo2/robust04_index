941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Fine Decor advances to Pounds 1.4m Fine Decor, a manufacturer of printed wallcoverings, increased pre-tax profits by 5 per cent from Pounds 1.33m to Pounds 1.4m in the six months to July 31. Sales came to Pounds 18.8m (Pounds 19.2m), made up of Pounds 10.1m (Pounds 11.7m) from the UK and Pounds 8.69m (Pounds 7.48m) from overseas - mainly the US. The interim dividend is increased to 2.7p (1p) on earnings per share of 7.3p (8.1p). Mr Roger Reagan, chairman, said yesterday that the second half had started well and export growth was still strong. However, conditions in the UK decorative market remained difficult.
