EFFECTIVE DATE: May 19, 1994. FEDERAL EMERGENCY MANAGEMENT AGENCY 44 CFR Part 2 RIN 3067&hyph;AC22 Organization, Functions and Delegations of Authority AGENCY: Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA). ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: This rule revises FEMA regulations on Organization, Functions and Delegations of Authority to reflect the recent reorganization of FEMA. It leaves the listing of OMB Control Numbers unchanged. EFFECTIVE DATE: May 19, 1994. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Joseph Flynn, Office of the General Counsel, Federal Emergency Management Agency, 500 C Street, SW., Washington, DC 20472, (202) 646&hyph;4105. SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: As this document relates to agency management it is not subject to the requirements for notice and public comment and may be made effective immediately.
