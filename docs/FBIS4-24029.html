BFN [Text] The South African National Defense Force troops have been deployed around Umtata to contain the mutiny by Transkeian soldiers. A column of defense force armored vehicles has also been spotted traveling toward the northern Transkei where the Maluti Army Base is situated. More than 300 Transkeian soldiers have been detained for mutinous activities following the incident last week when troops based in Port St. Johns and Umtata protested about salaries and took a senior officer hostage. The officer was freed by troops from Eastern Province Command.
