931108 FT 08 NOV 93 / Taiwan to ease China policy TAIWAN plans reforms of its economic policy towards China that will accelerate Taiwanese investment there and let Taipei play an active role in promoting it, Chiang Pin-kung, economics minister said, Reuter reports from Taipei. The Taiwanese government's system of screening investment in China would be revised to remove restrictions in many industries, newspapers quoted Chiang as saying over the weekend. The government would also change its current policy of 'not encouraging' Taiwanese investment in China to one of 'actively guiding' it in directions beneficial to Taiwan, he said.
