BFN [Unattributed report from the "Inform" column: "Dzhokhar Has No Enemies Any Longer"] [Text] Four citizens of Chechnya suspected of an attempt on the life of President Dzhokhar Dudayev were arrested in Groznyy in the early hours of Thursday morning. As Sultan Geliskhanov, head of the Chechen State Security Department, informed INTERFAX, this group is suspected of preparing a series of attempted assassinations of a number of top officials of the republic. Remote-controlled plastic explosive devices and firearms were seized from the arrestees, INTERFAX was informed at the State Security Department. The attempted assassination of Dudayev occuurred on 27 May, when a Zhiguli packed with explosives parked by the side of the road was detonated as a convoy of vehicles, including the president, approached Groznyy. The internal affairs minister and his deputy were killed as a result. Dudayev himself was not hurt.
