930329 FT 29 MAR 93 / Construction Contracts: RAF headquarters TILBURY DOUGLAS CONSTRUCTION has won orders worth Pounds 20m. The Ministry of Defence has appointed Tilbury Douglas as project manager for a Pounds 5m headquarters building at RAF High Wycombe. The building will provide office facilities for defence staff, together with auditorium and conference rooms with simultaneous translation facilities. The London Borough of Islington has placed a Pounds 4m order for general improvement works at the Packington Estate.
