February 10, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

CONSERVATIVES KEEP POWER IN BERMUNDA

Premier John Swan's conservative United Bermuda Party, which has ruled the island for 20 years, held onto power Thursday but its majority in the 44-seat House of Assembly was cut from 31 seats to 23.

The left-leaning Progressive Labor Party increased the number of its seats from 7 to 15, official election results showed.
