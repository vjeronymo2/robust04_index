920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Belgians buy Cresta Holidays for Pounds 8.42m Cresta Holidays, the UK short holiday operator, has been bought by Sun International of Belgium for about BFr500m (Pounds 8.42m). Mr Mark Vanmoerkerke, Sun's general manager, said Cresta's management would remain in place and the UK company would continue to function independently. Mr Vanmoerkerke will join Cresta's board. Cresta had turnover last year of Pounds 30m and carried over 100,000 passengers.
