Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Zhang Huanli (1728 3562 0448)] [Text] Osaka, 27 Feb (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice Premier Zhu Rongji attended a dinner given in his honor by Ichizo Kimura, president of the Japan-China Trade Center, in Osaka this evening. Speaking at the dinner, Zhu Rongji said: "Japan Kansai area's trade with China accounts for 40 percent of Japan-Chinese trade, occupying a decisive position. I hope my current visit will further promote the development of Kansai's economic relations and trade with China." During the dinner, Kimura spoke highly of the remarkable successes China has achieved in reform and opening up. He said: "The course China follows in carrying out economic reform is one no one has ever followed. Despite difficulties, I am convinced China will certainly be able to surmount them, and Japan can also develop economic relations and trade with China without worries." Attendng the dinner were He Chunlin, deputy secretary general of the State Council, and other principal members of the vice premier's entourage, as well as persons in charge of the Japan-china Trade Center and economic circles in Japan's Kansai areas. Zhu Rongji arrived in Osaka this afternoon to continue his visit to Japan after concluding his visit in Tokyo.
