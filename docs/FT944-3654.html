941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / Emerging Markets: Tibet The world's highest stock exchange in Lhasa, capital of China's Tibet region, has seen its highest daily turnover of Yn5m (Dollars 588,000) since it opened in January. The stock exchange has more than 2,000 members in a city of just 180,000. The exchange is a member of China's two stock exchanges in Shanghai and Shenzhen and trading is linked. Edited by John Pitt.
