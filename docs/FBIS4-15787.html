BFN [Text] Baku (April 11) TURAN -- Over the week-end President Aliyev visited Imishli, Beylagan and Barda regions of the republic. The goal of the trip was to gain an up-to-date understanding of the present situation of refugees and the over-all social and economic situation in those regions. During the visit, President Aliyev met with commanders of units located in those front regions. Towards the end of the visit he has with local regional heads, law enforcement bodies and military personnel the town of Beylagan which is the regional center of the region with the same name. During meeting President Aliyev said that all levels of leadership should coordinate their efforts in dealing with the refugees' problems. He mentioned that the front regions are the primary grape and cotton producing regions in Azerbaijan. President Aliyev said that the presence of refugees in the region must not be used as an excuse for the fall in production.
