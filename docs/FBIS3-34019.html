Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A responsible source at the Interior Ministry has said that at 1355 [1155 GMT] today an explosion took place at the Cinema Salwa in al-Zarqa'. The source said citizen 'Id Salih al-Jahalin was seriously injured in the incident and is in bad condition. Hanafi Muhammad Sa'id, an Egyptian citizen, sustained medium injuries. The cinema sustained material damage. The security organs immediately began an investigation to determine the causes of the explosion.
