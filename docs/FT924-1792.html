921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Creighton's 9% ahead at Pounds 0.62m Creighton's Naturally, the USM-quoted creator and maker of natural health and beauty products, achieved a 9.2 per cent improvement in profits to Pounds 617,000 pre-tax for the half year ended September 30. The interim dividend is lifted to 2.1p (2p) from earnings of 9.1p (8.4p) per share. The group also announced the acquisition of 50 per cent of Fine Fragrances for Pounds 875,000 in cash and shares. The purchase will help the group in both the domestic and export markets. The improvement in profitability arose from actions taken to reduce operating costs. Sales fell from Pounds 5.23m to Pounds 4.6m. The company exported more during the half year than for the whole of the previous 12 months.
