Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] President Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada arrived on 15 January in Laguna de los Patos Airport in the Uruguayan city of Colonia. There he was met by the Colonia mayor and then talked to the press. [Begin recording] [Sanchez de Lozada] Well now, we hope for informal and frank bilateral approaches and approaches with Mercosur [Common Market of the South]. We hope we will be able to approach this market that is so important for Bolivia and that will become more important in the future. [Unidentified reporter] The Uruguayan press announced during the past few hours that Presidents Lacalle, Menem, and Wasmosy [of Uruguay, Argentina, and Paraguay, respectively] reportedly have virtually accepted Chile's and Bolivia's entry in the near future. [Sanchez de Lozada] Well now, we have some problems because we are still part of the Andean market. We do not want to quit this pact. Nevertheless, we would like to become a hinge and actually be received by this agreement. If there is such a political will, it would be something good, but we will wait for this to become official. If it becomes official, we will consider it with much interest. [passage omitted] [end recording] [passage omitted]
