940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Aberforth Split ABERFORTH SPLIT Level Trust: Net asset value per unit share stood at 280.8p at December 31, an improvement of 12.2 per cent over the 250.3p standing at July 1. Available revenue for the half year to end-December rose from Pounds 804,000 to Pounds 829,000. Earnings per income share slipped to 5.02p (5.36p). A second interim dividend of 2p makes 4p (same) to date.
