BFN [Text] Colombian Agriculture and Livestock Minister Jose Antonio Ocampo arrived today in Havana on an official visit at the invitation of his Cuban counterpart, Alfredo Jordan Morales, who welcomed him. In remarks to the NATIONAL NEWS AGENCY, Ocampo said that he is coming to establish cooperation agreements between the two ministries and cooperation in the field of biotechnology. He also expressed the interest of Colombian companies in establishing trade and investment relations in Cuba, especially in rice and poultry farming.
