940905 FT 05 SEP 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Ivory Coast The Ivorian state is to sell its 35 per cent stake in the cable company, Societe Ivoirienne de Cables, which is 51 per cent owned by Italy's Pirelli group. Applications for shares are expected to be taken by local banks from next week and the company should be listed on the Abidjan Stock Exchange before the end of this year.
