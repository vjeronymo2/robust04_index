920518 FT 18 MAY 92 / World News In Brief: Mansell drives into the history books Britain's Nigel Mansell (left) led from start to finish in the San Marino Grand Prix at Imola yesterday, becoming the first driver to win the first five races of a formula one season. Mansell has a maximum 50 points in the drivers' championship, 26 clear of second-placed Italian Riccardo Patrese.
