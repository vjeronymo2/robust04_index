BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres, on a visit to Bucharest, said he will arrive in Cairo in the next few days, where he might meet with PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat. Peres told reporters he hoped the Gaza and Jericho autonomy negotiations with the Palestinians will end within a week. Peres also said he will come to Cairo on 2 May again to participate in the autonomy's donors conference. Today Peres held meetings in Bucharest with the presidents of Lithuania and Cyprus, Romania's prime minister and its parliament speaker. Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas said his country will open an embassy in Israel in the near future. Sources in Peres' entourage denied rumors according to which Minister Peres will hold a meeting with Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati, who is scheduled to participate in the economic conference in Bucharest tomorrow.
