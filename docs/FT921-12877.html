920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Derby Trust net asset value ahead Derby Trust reported net asset value per capital share of 343p at December 31 compared with 304p a year earlier. Net revenue for 1991 was Pounds 2.18m (Pounds 2.08m) for earnings per income share of 18.43p (17.65p). A second interim dividend of 10.0919p is declared for a total of 18.4348p (17.653p). Gross revenue was ahead at Pounds 3.27m (Pounds 3.16m).
