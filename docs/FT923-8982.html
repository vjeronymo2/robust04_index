920807 FT 07 AUG 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit and Vegetables Discovery apples, the first of the new season English apples, are in the shops at 35-50p a lb, reports FFVIB. Other good apple buys include English Bramleys at 40-50p a lb (40-50p) and Cape Golden Delicious at 65-70p a lb (65-70p). Top quality homegrown sweetcorn is superb at 30-40p a cob (42-50p). Other good vegetable buys include Runner beans at 70-80p a lb (80p-Pounds 1.00), broccoli at 60-80p a lb (60-80p), and beetroot at 30-35p a lb (30-35p). Tomatoes from England, Holland and Channel Islands are a good salad choice at 30-45p a lb (40-45p). Beef tomatoes remain good at 50-60p a lb (55-70p).
