920212 FT 12 FEB 92 / Observer: Speedy brothers Professional sport has historically provided a ladder for workers to climb out of their class, but not necessarily out of trade unionism. Nor are the rungs always cushioned with rich cash rewards. So the innovative GMB union is busily extending its influence. Besides already representing the Scottish Professional Footballers' Association and the British Rugby League Players' Association, it has just wheeled in the 300 members of the Speedway Riders' Association.
