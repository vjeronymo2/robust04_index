The heading of subpart C is revised to read as follows: Subpart C_Requirements for Documented Vessels That Operate Beyond the Boundary Lines or With More Than 16 Individuals On Board, or for Fish Tender Vessels Engaged in the Aleutian Trade 6. Section 28.200 is revised to read as follows: §28.200 Applicability. Each documented commercial fishing industry vessel must meet the requirements of this subpart in addition to the requirements of subparts A and B of this part if it: (a) Operates beyond the Boundary Lines; (b) Operates with more than 16 individuals on board; or (c) Is a fish tender vessel engaged in the Aleutian trade. 7.
