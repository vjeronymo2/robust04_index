940905 FT 05 SEP 94 / VW may set up plant in India Volkswagen plans to set up a car factory in India, one of the world's fastest growing vehicle markets. Volkswagen, Europe's biggest and the world's fourth leading car maker, has selected Indian tractor manufacturing group Eicher Motors as its partner in conducting a six-month feasibility study, newspapers reported. The study will help VW decide which models to launch in India, according to the reports, which quoted VW Asia-Pacific chairman Martin Posth. Volkswagen's foray into India comes 12 years after it set up its first Asian joint venture in China. Volkswagen is the latest in a string of foreign companies to target the Indian car market since the government relaxed restrictions on foreign investment last year.
