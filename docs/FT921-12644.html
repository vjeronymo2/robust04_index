920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Cardiff Property tumbles 56% Although gross rental income improved by 12 per cent to Pounds 558,731, pre-tax profits of Cardiff Property for the year to September 30 fell by 56 per cent from Pounds 216,640 to Pounds 94,390. Earnings per share dropped to 2.9p (6.8p) but the proposed final dividend is held at 1.55p for an unchanged 2.4p total.
