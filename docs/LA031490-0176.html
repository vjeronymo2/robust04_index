March 14, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

DIAMOND LIL LOOKS FOR A FOURTH WIN

Geraldine McClure's Miss Diamond Lil will be looking for her third win in four starts when she tackles John Stephensen's Kitty Spirit and seven others in the $4,000 distaff pacing feature Thursday night at Los Alamitos.

Eligible fillies and mares are non-winners of five races or $12,000 lifetime.

Miss Diamond Lil crushed a similar field last Thursday night and Kitty Spirit beat an easier field eight nights ago in strong fashion.
