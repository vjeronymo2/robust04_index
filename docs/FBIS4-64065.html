BFN [Text] The Civic Accord Pact in Russia was signed in the Kremlin on Thursday. As the head of the President's office, Sergey Filatov, said that now the ceremonies for signing the agreement will be held in the country's republics, regions and territories. According to him, in the month that has passed since the process of signing began on April 28, 765 representatives of government bodies, Federation members, parties, associations and organizations signed the document.
