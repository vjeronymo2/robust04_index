930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Avonmore shows 53% advance New acquisitions gave a sharp boost to Avonmore Foods, the Irish dairy and meat processing group, which reported a 53 per cent rise in pre-tax profits to IPounds 25m on turnover up 45 per cent to IPounds 831.4m for the year ending January 2. The main expansion was in the meat division, where the acquisition of 10 new processing facilities in Ireland, the UK and Germany boosted the division's sales from IPounds 109m to IPounds 302m. Some 36 per cent of group turnover, and 30 per cent of operating profits now come from meat processing. Earnings were 12.11p (10.83p) and a final dividend of 1.85p makes a 3.3p total.
