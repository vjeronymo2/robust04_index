Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Vladimir Skosyrev: "Geydar Aliyev Has Informed Beijing of Conflict with Armenia"] [Text] Beijing -- One of the main aims of the first visit to the PRC by the president of independent Azerbaijan was to inform the Chinese leadership of the reasons for the war with Armenia. G. Aliyev stated this at a press conference in Beijing. The Chinese leaders listened politely to their guest and expressed sympathy but went no further than calling for a peaceful settlement of the conflict. China, as a permanent member of the UN Security Council, prefers to maintain neutrality. Correspondents were interested to know whether China had promised to render economic aid to Azerbaijan. It follows from the president's reply that relations between the two countries will be built on a mutually advantageous basis and there was therefore no talk of China's economic aid.
