BFN [Text] PRC Vice Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei and his delegation left Damascus this morning at the end of a four-day visit to the Syrian Arab Republic. He was seen off at Damascus International Airport by Assistant foreign minister Raslan 'Allush, a number of ranking Foreign Ministry officials, the Chinese ambassador in Damascus, and embassy staff. Tian Zengpei made a statement to the correspondent of the Syrian Arab News Agency, SANA, in which he described his visit to Damascus as successful and one which contributed to bolstering bilateral relations of friendship and cooperation. He said: During the visit, we had the honor to meet with President Hafiz al-Asad. We were also received by the foreign minister, and held a round of talks with the assistant to the foreign minister. He added: We discussed several issues, bilateral relations, and international topics of mutual concern. The two countries' viewpoints on these issues were identical or close. Tian Zengpei expressed satisfaction with the development of bilateral relations during the 40 years of diplomatic relations between the two countries and their efforts to further them. The Chinese minister called for implementing UN resolutions on the Middle East crisis, especially Resolutions 242 and 338, realizing the legitimate rights of the Palestinian Arab people, and restoring all occupied Arab territories. He said: We appreciate Syria and its efforts to restore its occupied territories through peaceful means. We also greatly value the progress and achievements witnessed by Syria under the leadership of President Hafiz al-Asad, in the field of building the state, economic development, and boosting the people's standard of living. He added: The international situation is witnessing a quick change, and Syria and China believe that there is a need for bilateral consultation. He also said: During my visit to Damascus, I conveyed a message, wishes, and greetings of President Jiang Zemin to President Hafiz al-Asad. I am also carrying a reply message from President al-Asad to President Jiang Zemin. He said: In the message, the Chinese leadership expressed its readiness and desire to develop bilateral relations of friendship and cooperation. President al-Asad also expressed similar feelings and desire. Concluding his statement, Tian Zengpei expressed thanks for the warm reception accorded to him, and wished Syria, under the leadership of President Hafiz al-Asad, more achievements, progress, and prosperity.
