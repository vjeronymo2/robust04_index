BFN [Text] Riga, April 6 (LETA) -- Wednesday [6 April] the cabinet of members [as received] headed by prime minister, Valdis Birkavs, will finish the two-day discussion of state national issues. The leader of each program, who could be mister [as received] or any other official, is responsible for its elaboration. The discussion also included the issues of macroeconomic stabilization, advanced power industry in Latvia, development of national transport, environment protection in the Baltic Sea, national program of development of the Latvian rural areas and the programs "Education and Science" and "Latvian Residents."
