BFN [Text] Yesterday afternoon, Xie Fei, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and secretary of the Guangdong Provincial CPC Committee, met with Mr. Lee Shau-kee, Hong Kong affairs adviser and president of Hong Kong Henderson Investment Ltd., in Guangzhou's Zhudao Guesthouse. Xie Fei appreciated the action of loving China, Hong Kong and one's native place of Hong Kong Henderson Investment Ltd. in actively participating in China's economic construction, donating funds to run schools, and training various kinds of management personnel for China. He added: As long as the inland economy develops well, Hong Kong will achieve greater development. Both sides can thus give full play to their own strong points. Xie Fei also urged the accompanying responsible persons of Guangzhou City to pay attention to formulating plans well to integrate in a still better way the transformation of the old urban area with the adjustment of the distribution of industry to speed up the pace of transforming the old urban area. Mr. Lee Shau-kee and his party came to Guangzhou to discuss with the city authorities the second phase of investment and cooperative projects, including transformation and expansion of Guangzhou Oil Gas Plant, and others.
