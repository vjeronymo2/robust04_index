921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / Construction Contracts: Waste treatment SIMON-HARTLEY has gained orders worth over Pounds 2.5m for waste treatment equipment. The contracts include: Pounds 1.5m for a number of stainless steel sluice gates for Beckton Sewage Treatment Works, London; and Pounds 700,000 for the supply of electrical and mechanical equipment for a sewage pumping station in Hong Kong.
