931218 FT 18 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Losses deepen to Pounds 595,000 at Unit Pre-tax losses at Unit Group widened from Pounds 83,000 to Pounds 595,000 in the six months to end-September. Turnover fell from Pounds 10.1m to Pounds 8.51m. Comparisons were restated to conform with FRS 3. Directors of the USM-quoted maker of timber pallets blamed the fall on a further decline in volumes, coupled with an increase in timber prices which could not be passed on to customers. In spite of the trading losses, the group continued to reduce borrowings, reflected in a fall in interest charges to Pounds 157,000 (Pounds 224,000). Losses per share came out at 11p (1.6p).
