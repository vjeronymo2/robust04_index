BFN [Text] Zugdidi, 28 [Jun] -- The press center of the Group of Russian Forces in the Transcaucasus told IBERIA that yesterday Russian Federation peacekeeping troops, which are deployed along the Inguri River, set up three defended points [dasakrdeni punkti] and 12 observation posts. The mine-clearing operation continues. Repair work on the Inguri bridge is expected to be completed on 29 June.
