930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Chloride in black Chloride Group, the loss-making electronics and batteries company, reported higher sales and orders for the first quarter of the current year. The company announced at its annual meeting that it had traded profitably in the first quarter and expected this to continue. Chloride turned in an increased loss of Pounds 1.58m (Pounds 1.41m) for the year ended March 31.
