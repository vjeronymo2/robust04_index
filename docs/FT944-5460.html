941203 FT 03 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Blick purchase Blick, the electronic and electrical equipment group, is paying a maximum Pounds 3.2m cash for TR Services (Proprietary), a telephone equipment distributor in South Africa. TRS reported sales revenue for the 11 months to September 30 of R30m (Pounds 5.4m) and net assets total R15.1m. Of the consideration there is a deferred rental income related payment of up to Pounds 700,000.
