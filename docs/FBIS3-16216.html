Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The meeting of Russian and Central Asian foreign ministers that was held behind closed doors ended in Dushanbe Tuesday at noon. A communique on the meeting and numerous bilateral agreements between the foreign ministries are expected to be signed in the afternoon. Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev left Dushanbe by plane, probably for the 12th border post of the Pyanj unit on the Tajik-Afghan border.
