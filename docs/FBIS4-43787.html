BFN ["SN"-signed report: "Zhirinovskiy Back in Austria?"] [Text] From 5 to 8 July, Vienna will host a conference of the CSCE Parliamentarian Assembly. Representatives of 52 states, including 20 parliamentary presidents, are expected to take part. The Russian Liberal Democrats have nominated nationalist leader Vladimir Zhirinovskiy as delegate. Whether he will really try to enter Austria is still unclear.
