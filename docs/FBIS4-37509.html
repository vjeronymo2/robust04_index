BFN [Text] Head of State Liamine Zeroual today received Yahia Husayn al-Arshi, who conveyed to him a message from the Yemeni president, 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The meeting was evaluated by Mihoub Mihoub, the presidency's official spokesman: [Begin Mihoubi recording] His Excellency Liamine Zeroual, head of state, today received the Yemeni envoy and told him of Algeria's great concern over the development of the situation in fraternal Yemen. The head of state also told him that he is following the situation and reiterated to him our concern to work and exert concerted efforts to stop the fratricide and create suitable conditions to proceed with the dialogue in order to resolve pending problems through peaceful means. [end recording] After his meeting with the head of state the Yemeni envoy made the following statement: [Begin Arshi recording] I was happy to meet his excellency the head of state: I conveyed to him a message from His Excellency Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, president of the Republic of Yemen. Undoubtedly the meeting was a good opportunity [as heard]. I would like to reiterate my thanks and appreciation to his excellency the head of state for Algeria's principled and firm stance supporting Yemeni unity and the Yemeni internal situation. I have noticed the full support for the Yemeni people, and that the Algerian people are following the development of the situation in Yemen. I would like to reassure our brothers in Algeria, who have supported the Yemeni revolution and everything else in the interests of the Yemeni and pan-Arab causes, that events in Yemen, which are regrettable, are within the framework of protecting Yemeni unity and the Yemeni people's national gains. The Yemeni people are determined to endure any sacrifice to protect unity and achieve victory, and are determined to respond positively to any effort which falls within the framework of the sacrosanct national principles of the Yemeni people. [end recording]
