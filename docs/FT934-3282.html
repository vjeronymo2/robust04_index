931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / UK Company News: TGI benefits from restructure Restructuring benefits flowed through at TGI, the loudspeaker manufacturer, with pre-tax profits for the half year ended September 30 improving by 44 per cent to Pounds 501,000. Turnover totalled Pounds 17.3m (Pounds 16m). Earnings picked up to 2p (1.5p) and the interim dividend is raised to 0.6p (0.5p). Bank borrowings at period end had been reduced to Pounds 2.5m (Pounds 4.7m).
