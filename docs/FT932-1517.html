930623 FT 23 JUN 93 / UK Company News: First Spanish repurchase offer First Spanish Investment Trust is to invite holders of its 1.5p per unit convertible unsecured loan stock 1997 to tender for its repurchase by the company. The price will be equal to 95 per cent of the net asset value attributable to each unit of convertible loan stock, to be determined as at the closing date, July 7.
