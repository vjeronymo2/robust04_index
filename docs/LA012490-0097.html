January 24, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

FAITHFUL DONOR WINS RED CROSS RECOGNITION

The American Red Cross on Tuesday honored a man who has been sending weekly donations of $1 or less from his Social Security benefits to the Hurricane Hugo relief effort in South Carolina.

At the personal care home where he lives, Dalbey Marsh, 51, was given a certificate of appreciation and a South Carolina-shaped Hugo pin from the Red Cross chapter in Charleston, S. C. Marsh, who is mentally handicapped, moved to the home 3 1/2 years ago.
