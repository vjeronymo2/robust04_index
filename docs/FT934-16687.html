931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Avon joint Pounds 3m Welsh investment Avon-S&H, a joint venture between S&H Fabricating and Engineering, of Sandford, Florida, and Avon Rubber, is to go ahead with a Pounds 3m investment at Williamstown, South Wales. This establishes a second production unit for Avon's hose and coupling assemblies for vehicle air conditioning systems which is expected to create nearly 160 jobs over the next three years.
