BFN [Excerpt] As of tomorrow, the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] units in the Gaza Strip will start operating on the basis of new directives especially formulated for the interim period. Our military affairs correspondent Alon Ben-David has learned that tomorrow the IDF forces will start the overlapping with the first Palestinian policemen. According to the new directives, the IDF will be responsible for the safety of the policemen. Soldiers are forbidden to open fire at suspicious vehicles or cars which run over roadblocks unless it is clear that it is a terrorist attack. [passage omitted]
