July 21, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MEMORIAL CONCERT FOR SLAIN SOCIAL WORKER

A memorial concert for slain social worker Robbyn Panitch will be held Thursday at the University of Judaism. Pianist Mona Golabek, violinists Andres Cardenes and Mitchell Newman as well as cellist Jeffrey Solow will perform at the free concert.

Panitch, a 36-year-old social worker, was stabbed to death in February at the Santa Monica West Clinic by a patient who entered through an unguarded door. Information: (213) 514-4370.
