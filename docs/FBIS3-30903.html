Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [TA SR report: "V. Cech Was Also Killed"] [Text] Bratislava -- We carried a report yesterday on the car accident and death of Democratic Party Chairman Anton Duris. We received a more detailed agency report after our news deadline. It says that the party's deputy chairman, Vladimir Cech, who was probably driving the car, was also killed in the accident not far from Plesivec. V. Cech died on his 44th birthday.
