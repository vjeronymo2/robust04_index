921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / Ethiopia reforms 'depend on aid' Ethiopia's central bank governor Mr Leikun Berhanu appealed yesterday for urgent foreign aid to help the country cope with economic reforms and the legacy of Marxist rule and civil war, Reuter reports from Addis Ababa. 'Timely and adequate financial support from multilateral and other donors is very critical if the reform package is to succeed,' Mr Leikun said.
