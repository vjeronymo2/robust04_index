BFN ["Text" of the Azerbaijani Ministry of Defense Press Service communique; date not given] [Text] The enemy forces, which have been reinforced with additional units from Armenia, have launched a new attack in the Fizulinskiy sector of the front line. Azerbaijan's military subunits in that area have forced the Armenians to withdraw. They have killed nearly 100 enemy troops and destroyed a quantity of their military equipment. Armenia's military units also attacked the vicinity of the Omar Pass in Kelbadzharskiy Rayon. Combat operations are taking place in that area at the present time. Meanwhile, the Armenian enemy forces have fired on our positions in Gorenboyskiy, Terterskiy, and Agdamskiy Rayons.
