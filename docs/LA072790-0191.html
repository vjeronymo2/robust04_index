July 27, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

HIGHLAND TALK PREVAILS

Highland Talk, ridden by John Velasquez, collared the leader entering the stretch and then drew away to beat Etoile Eternelle by three lengths in the $47,000 Lamb Chop Purse for fillies and mares 3 and up at Belmont Park on Thursday. Etoile Eternelle finished two lengths ahead of Gottagetitdone in the field of six.
