BFN [Text] At a meeting today, representatives of Crimean radio and television protested against [Crimean President] Yuriy Meshkov's decree discharging Krym Broadcasting Company President Valeriy Astakhov from his duties. Participants in today's meeting emphasized that the decree was an insult to the company staff and ignored their opinions. There was a unanimous decision to lodge a protest against Yuriy Meshkov's decree. The protest has been sent to the Ukrainian president, the president of the Ukrainian Broadcasting Company, and the Ukrainian and Crimean Supreme Councils.
