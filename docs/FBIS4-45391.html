Arrests AU0906134694 Hamburg DPA in German 1042 GMT 9 Jun 94 AU0906134694 Hamburg DPA German BFN [Excerpt] Munich (DPA) -- In the struggle against organized drug trafficking, the greatest ever success in Europe was achieved. In several European countries, more than 400 kilograms heroin with a black market value of approximately 100 million German marks [DM] were seized. In the operation, which was led by the Bavarian Land Office of Criminal Investigation [LKA], the "laundering of money" totalling DM50 million was reconstructed, of which DM2.5 million was confiscated, the LKA said in Munich on 9 June. In a large-scale police operation on 6 June, 25 persons were arrested in Germany and Italy. In the Federal Republic, 21 banks and 20 other buildings were searched. [passage omitted]
