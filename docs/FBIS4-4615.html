BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on proceedings at the Workers' Party of Korea cell secretaries meeting held from 31 March to 2 April at the Pyongyang Gymnasium in Pyongyang. Pyongyang Korean Central Broadcasting Network in Korean at 0500 GMT on 31 March carries a 168-minute recorded report on the first day morning session of national party cell secretaries meeting. The announcer says O Chin-u, Kang Song-san, Yi Chong-ok, Pak Song-chol, Kim Yong-chu, Kim Yong-nam, Choe Kwang, Kye Ung-tae, Han Song-yong, So Yun-sok, Kim Chol-man, Choe Tae-pok, and others are on hand. Kim Ki-nam delivers a opening speech, noting the significance of the first opening of a party cells meeting. Kye Ung-tae reads a congratulatory letter from Kim Il-song to the delegates of the meeting. Kim Ki-nam delivers a report, noting the meeting is convened by Kim Chong-il and the significance of party cells. The first session ends after children's union members read a congratulatory message in which Kim Chong-il is admired as a warmhearted parent of party cell secretaries. Pyongyang Korean Central Broadcasting Network in Korean at 1000 GMT on 31 March carries a 90-minute recorded report on first day afternoon session of national party cell secretaries meeting. Party cell secretaries speak about achievements made by each party cell in indoctrinating party members and masses in fulfilling tasks assigned to each unit and attribute those successful achievements mainly to Kim Chong-il's wise leadership, and stresses the importance of people holding the spirit of self-reliance and fortitude. They express their will to protect party, leader and to raise party members as revolutionaries loyal to party. Pyongyang Korean Central Broadcasting Network in Korean at 0500 GMT on 1 April carries a 78-minute recorded report on second day morning session of national meeting of party cell secretaries. Party cell secretaries recall Kim Chong-il's love for people and report on their achievements. One party secretary resolves to strengthen party cells' role in implementing party's policy. Pyongyang Korean Central Broadcasting Network in Korean at 1000 GMT on 1 April carries a 75-minute recorded report on second day afternoon session of party cell secretaries meeting. Party secretaries continue to discuss Kim Chong-il guidance for effectively carrying out revolutionary tasks and encouragement and benevolence extended to cell secretaries. Pyongyang Korean Central Broadcasting Network in Korean at 0500 GMT on 2 April carries a 65-minute recorded report of the final session of national meeting of party cell secretaries. Party cell secretaries continue discussions and adopt a letter of resolution to Kim Il-song and Kim Chong-il. Details of the letter are not given. Kye Ung-tae delivers a closing speech in which he describes the success of the meeting. He says this was a meeting in which party cell secretaries' will to devote themselves to the fatherland was displayed and urges cell secretaries to thoroughly implement party's military policies.
