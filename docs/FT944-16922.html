941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / UK Company News: DK to put 20th Century on CD-Rom Dorling Kindersley Holdings, the publisher of illustrated reference books and multimedia products, is to create CD-Rom versions of the best-selling Chronicle publications from Bertelsmann, the German-based media group. The UK group has acquired rights to publish the Chronicle titles as books in the UK and US and in CD-Rom form worldwide. Mr Peter Gill, DK's finance director, said the first title to appear in multimedia form would be the Chronicle of the 20th Century, a factual encyclopaedia which is updated annually. DK recently launched five of its own CD-Rom titles in the US, Canadian, UK and Australian markets.
