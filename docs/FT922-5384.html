920530 FT 30 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Acatos & Hutcheson meets expectations Acatos & Hutcheson, a processor and marketer of edible oil products, met its own expectations by returning profits of Pounds 3.63m pre-tax for the half year to March 29. The 30 per cent improvement on last time's Pounds 2.8m was scored from a turnover of Pounds 104.64m (Pounds 115.74m). Interest charges were cut from Pounds 1.9m to Pounds 826,000. An interim dividend of 2.5p (1.75p) is being paid from earnings of 7.1p (5.1p).
