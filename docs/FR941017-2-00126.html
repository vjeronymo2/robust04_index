Determination: Disapproved. The Clearfield-Jefferson Counties Regional Airport Authority has not demonstrated that it can overcome those technical difficulties which led to the FAA's decommissioning of its NDB. In addition, the FAA is not aware of any strong desire by the aviation community to reestablish an NDB at DUJ. Therefore, the FAA is unable to conclude that this project will meet the requirements of section 158.15(a) Decision Date: September 29, 1994.
