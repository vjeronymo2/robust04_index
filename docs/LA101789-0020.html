October 17, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Reynolds Metals Co.: The Richmond, Va., company said third-quarter profit fell almost 10% due to lower prices for its aluminum products and increased material and labor costs. Earnings for the period were $123.7 million on revenue of $1.52 billion. In the same quarter a year ago, the company reported earnings of $137.2 million on revenue of $1.48 billion.
