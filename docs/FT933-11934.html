930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Independent Inv Independent Investment lifted net asset value by 14 per cent to 68.3p per share over the year to June 30. Available revenue amounted to Pounds 916,000 (Pounds 587,000) for earnings of 0.98p (0.63p) per share. An interim dividend, to be paid in lieu of a final, is 0.6p (0.5p).
