940825 FT 25 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Coal Investments takes Scottish stake Coal Investments has acquired a 20 per cent stake in Mining (Scotland), by exercising an option to acquire from Edwards Energy its 40 per cent shareholding in The Consortium Coal Company, which owns half of Mining (Scotland). Edwards Energy is owned and controlled by Mr Malcolm Edwards, chairman of Coal Investments. The company is to allot 64,054 shares to Edwards Energy in settlement of the Pounds 60,000 consideration. Coal Investments will also assume responsibility for a funding call made by Mining (Scotland), under which Pounds 20,000 is payable to Mining (Scotland) for a further issue of shares. This issue will maintain Coal Investments' interest at 20 per cent. Mining (Scotland) was formed to bid for all British Coal's assets in Scotland.
