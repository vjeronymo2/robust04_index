930306 FT 06 MAR 93 / Ashdown calls on government to boost recovery prospects Mr Paddy Ashdown, leader of the Liberal Democrats, called on the government to boost the prospects for recovery by adopting his party's pounds 4bn 'action for jobs' programme. He told Welsh Liberal Democrats in Brecon that a programme of public investment would put 350,000 people into work in a year and provide thousands of contracts for small companies, particularly in local construction projects.
