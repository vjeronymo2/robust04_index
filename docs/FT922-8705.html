920512 FT 12 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Leveraged Trust net assets ahead Net asset value per share of the Leveraged Opportunity Trust stood at 113.1p at March 31 1992. This represented a 25 per cent improvement on the 90.7p at the October 30 year-end and a 35 per cent rise from 84.1p a year previously. Net revenue for the interim period was Pounds 22,000 (Pounds 20,000). Earnings per share grew to 0.29p (0.27p).
