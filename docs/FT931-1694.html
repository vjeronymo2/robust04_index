930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / UK Company News: F&C High Income Foreign & Colonial High Income Trust reported a net asset value of 62.3p per share as at December 31. The figure compared with values of 56p at end-June and 52.9p at December 1991. Attributable revenue for the six months amounted to Pounds 395,000 (Pounds 468,000) reflecting lower interest rates on the trust's cash deposits. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.2p, payable from earnings of 1.32p (1.56p).
