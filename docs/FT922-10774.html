920429 FT 29 APR 92 / Engineering pay rises slow THE RATE of increase in pay in the engineering sector continued to slow slightly in the first quarter of this year according to the latest figures from the Engineering Employers Federation. It says there has been an increase in the number of freezes and deferred settlements, In the three months to the end of February the median pay rise was 4.5 per cent, falling to 4 per cent at the end of March thanks partly to an increase in freezes.
