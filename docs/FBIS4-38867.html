BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow June 2 TASS -- The Russian Federation Council, which approved the key figures of the draft budget on Thursday, increased defence appropriations in 1994 from initially envisaged 37 trillion rubles to 55 trillion, according to First Deputy Defence Minister Andrey Kokoshin. He told TASS by telephone that lawmakers adopted the decision mostly because the 37-trillion appropriations would mean a closure for many defence enterprises and mass unemployment.
