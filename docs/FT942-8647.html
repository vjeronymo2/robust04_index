940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Evans Halshaw adds three dealerships Evans Halshaw, the Birmingham-based motor distribution and vehicle management group, has added three new car dealerships taking its chain of retail dealerships to 58. Evans has paid Pounds 1.25m for certain trading assets and goodwill of Westbury Garage (Salop), giving it the Citroen franchise in both Telford and Shrewsbury, Shropshire. In addition, Evans has paid Pounds 26,000 for Lionel Smith, which holds the Rover franchise for Macclesfield, Cheshire.
