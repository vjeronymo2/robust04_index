August 25, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

THE PETE ROSE DECISION;

OTHER VIEWS;

NATION'S PRESS SHOWS LITTLE EMPATHY FOR ROSE;

(THE REACTION TO THE LIFETIME SUSPENSION OF PETE ROSE IS STRONG. HERE IS HOW SOME OF THE COUNTRY'S TOP SPORTS COLUMNISTS SAW THE DECISION.)

How dare Pete Rose insult us. How dare he stand before TV cameras, his stupid spiked hair sticking straight up, and evince no real remorse, take next to no responsibility for his actions and then, blithely and chillingly, say he hopes to be back in baseball posthaste.

Having dishonored himself and his sport, all Pete Rose wanted to talk about was how soon he might be reinstated. Gall, thy name is Pete.
