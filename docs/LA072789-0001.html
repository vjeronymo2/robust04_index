July 27, 1989, Thursday, Nuestro Tiempo Edition

COMMUNITY;

IN BRIEF

Flex-appeal advocate Rachael Elizondo McLish, four-time world body building champion, was among recipients of the Latino athlete awards presented by the Hispanic Public Relations Assn. . . . At UCLA, Los Angeles Deputy Mayor Grace Montanez Davis and Times Editorial Writer Frank del Olmo received Alumni Awards for Excellence. . . . On that same campus, the UCLA Latino Alumni Assn. has been organized.
