Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belarusian Supreme Soviet Chairman Stanislav Shushkevich has confirmed that his meeting with Russian President Boris Yeltsin will take part this week. Speaking to a BELINFORM correspondent, the legislative speaker said that a wide spectrum of bilateral relations will be discussed at the meeting. According to Shushkevich, Belarus and Russia are planning to coordinate their standpoint on the eve of the scheduled visit of President Clinton to Moscow and Minsk.
