April 3, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BUCHANAN ON FUNDING FOR ART

Buchanan's column was a breath of fresh air for the millions of us who are sick to death of funding the destruction of our cultural roots through our tax dollars. If we allow our Christian heritage and the morality it engenders to be destroyed by those with a pagan world view, we will see the United States destroyed.

Defund, deflate and discipline the National Endowment for the Arts!

VINCE ZEIMIS

Redondo Beach
