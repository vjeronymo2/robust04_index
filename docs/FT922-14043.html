920408 FT 08 APR 92 / UK Company News: Dunloe House Dunloe House Group, the quoted Irish property development company, incurred a pre-tax loss of IPounds 1.05m (Pounds 980,000) for 1991, compared with profits of IPounds 107,000. Turnover was IPounds 396,000 (IPounds 10.4m). Losses came out at 4.15p (earnings 0.79p).
