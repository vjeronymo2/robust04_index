941217 FT 17 DEC 94 / World News in Brief: Clinton's press secretary to resign Dee Dee Myers, the first woman to serve as White House press secretary, said she would resign at the end of the year. 'It is time for me to move on,' Myers said, adding that she had received several job offers. Myers' decision to leave the White House follows a failed attempt by chief of staff Leon Panetta earlier this year to replace her as part of a staff overhaul.
