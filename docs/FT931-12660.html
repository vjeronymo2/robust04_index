930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / UK Company News: British Fittings sells Astbury British Fittings Group, the loss-making valves, tubes and non-ferrous metals distributor, announced the sale yesterday of Astbury & Madeley, its brass rod operation, and the closure of British Fittings (Non-Ferrous Metals). Mr Cecil Buckett, finance director, said the group was refocusing on its core businesses. The sale price for Astbury had not been settled, while the company's year-end results would reflect extraordinary provisions for the closure of the metals operation.
