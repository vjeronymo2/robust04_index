940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Scottish Asian Inv The Scottish Asian Investment Company had a fully diluted net asset value of 354.9p at January 31 1994 against 170.6p a year earlier and 213p at the July 31 year end. There was an attributable loss of Pounds 76,000 (Pounds 90,000) for the half year resulting in losses per share of 0.43p (0.52p).
