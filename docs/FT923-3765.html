920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Hunter Saphir ends disposal programme Hunter Saphir, the fresh and speciality foods group, is to sell its Emile Tissot frozen meals offshoot to Cavaghan & Gray for about Pounds 3.75m cash. The sale of the operation, which had net assets of Pounds 3m and achieved operating profits of Pounds 570,000 on turnover of Pounds 7.2m in the year to February 29, completes Hunter Saphir's Pounds 15.5m disposal programme.
