May 3, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

FINDING COMMON GROUND TO END MIDDLE EAST TENSIONS

How hopeful it is to find side-by-side articles by (former) Israeli Gen. Harkabi and high-ranking Palestinian Hassan -- and to find them in such close agreement! But Hassan made one disturbing statement.

Defining justice he wrote, "Justice for us is to have a homeland; justice for the Jews is to live in peace." To my senses this difference is more significant than mere nuance. Why not say squarely that justice for the Palestinians is to have a homeland and live in peace; justice for the Jews is to have a homeland and live in peace!

JAMES SCHERR

Studio City
