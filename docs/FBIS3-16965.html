Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report under the "NEGA Reports" rubric: "Crimea"] [Text] An election meeting of the Officers Union of Crimea comprising 500 servicemen of a pro-Russia orientation was held in Sevastopol on 20 March. The Officers Union of Crimea nominated 11 candidates for the Supreme Soviet of Crimea who include Black Sea Fleet servicemen. The Officers Union turned to Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin and the Russian Federation State Duma with a request to speed up the solution of the question on placing Sevastopol and the Black Sea Fleet under Russian jurisdiction. An appeal to Republic of Crimea President Yuriy Meshkov contains a call to create on the basis of the Officers Union a National Guard of Crimea and, in this connection, to eliminate on the peninsula's territory the National Guard of Ukraine.
