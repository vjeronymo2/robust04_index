930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: New rules ahead on patient rights NEW draft rules governing the privacy of NHS patients and how the service should deal with the media are to be issued within the next two weeks, junior health minister Baroness Cumberlege said last night. She stressed she wanted to tighten safeguards on patient confidentiality, but said the government would consider responses to the proposals before circulating guidelines to the health service.
