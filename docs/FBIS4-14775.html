BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Tigran Liloyan and Sergey Ostanin] [Text] Yerevan, 2 Apr -- The Armenian president welcomes the involvement of CIS parliamentarians in the quest for ways to achieve a settlement in Nagorno-Karabakh. This was stated by Levon Zurabyan, the Armenian leader's press secretary. He told the ITAR- TASS correspondent some details of Levon Ter-Petrosyan's Friday evening meeting with the visiting CIS Interparliamentary Assembly delegation. The presidential spokesman said that the participants in the conversation had discussed the role that the CIS can play in achieving peace in this disturbed region. In this connection, the president stated that Armenia pins great hopes on the Russian plan for a settlement, and in his view it would be exceptionally important therefore for the CIS to assist Russia's peacemaking efforts, the press secretary stressed. The presidential spokesman said that direct and indirect contacts between CIS parliamentarians were exceptionally important and useful.
