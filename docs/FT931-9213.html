930216 FT 16 FEB 93 / World News in Brief: Roy of the Rovers hangs up boots Roy of the Rovers is hanging up his boots after 39 years at the top of comic book soccer. The weekly comic, named after the player manager of Melchester Rovers, is to close next month. Roy launched his career in the Tiger in 1954 but transferred to Roy of the Rovers in 1976. The comic, which has seen its circulation fall sharply, was sold with the rest of Fleetway Publications to Danish publisher Gutenberghus when the Maxwell empire collapsed.
