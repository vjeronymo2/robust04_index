March 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

CHARITY SCORECARD: ADL RAISES $70,000 FOR BETTER UNDERSTANDING

The Anti-Defamation League of B'nai B'rith raised about $70,000 at a luncheon Jan. 26 at the Regent Beverly Wilshire Hotel. The ADL does extensive outreach work in the field of education to promote better understanding between diverse societal groups.

Honorees were. U.S. Rep. Barbara Boxer (D-Greenbrae) and Dr. Sonya Friedman.

* The Antique Seminar by Gwen Znerold on Feb. 13 raised $2,148 to be divided equally between the Crippled Children's Society and Children's Home Society to help in their ongoing programs to aid children.

* On HOPE (Helping Other People Eat) Day, Feb. 14, 200 members of the Los Angeles County Chiropractic Society treated 12,400 patients throughout the county free in exchange for food donations for LIFE (Love Is Feeding Everyone) and collected 60 pounds of nonperishable food, as well as cash donations of $2,000 given by chiropractors and their patients. The food and money were distributed to more than 70 local social service agencies. BEA MAXWELL
