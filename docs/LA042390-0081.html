April 23, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE WATCH

Tell Me, Kate: Acting legend Katharine Hepburn's memoirs are coming out next year, according to the Alfred A. Knopf publishing house. The four-time best acting Oscar winner and star of the "Philadelphia Story" and "The African Queen" -- and other classics -- will reflect on her childhood and family, her early days in New York, her long on- and off-screen association with Spencer Tracy and working with the leading actors, directors and producers of the past 50 years. Describing her decision to write the book now, the 80-year-old Hepburn said: "It's later than you think!" GREG BRAXTON
