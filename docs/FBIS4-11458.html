BFN [Text] Croatian Foreign Minister Mate Granic arrived today in the country for a 48-hour official visit. He will meet President Carlos Menem and Foreign Minister Guido Di Tella. Granic is the first Croatian official to visit Argentina since the establishing of diplomatic relations between the two countries. Granic also will sign the first overall cooperation agreement with his Argentine colleague, and he will discuss a bilateral agreement regarding the drafting of an agenda containing topics of common interest.
