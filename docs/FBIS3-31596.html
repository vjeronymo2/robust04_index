Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] The Slovak National Party [SNS] has been purified after the departure of Cernak and his colleagues. In the future, it will again have a national orientation, as its name indicates. Jan Slota said this at a briefing in Zilina after his being elected chairman of the SNS. According to Slota, the SNS will continue to cooperate with the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia as a coalition partner, while supporting programs of other parties that have the good of Slovak citizens in mind.
