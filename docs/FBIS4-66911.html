BFN [Text] All nuclear warheads will be pulled out of Ukraine within three-four years, Ukrainian Defense Minister Vitaliy Radetskyy told journalists in Kiev on Monday. He said the arrangements for one train with warheads takes about one month. As of today, Radetskyy said, three trains with 180 nuclear warheads have been sent from Ukraine to Russia. "Everything is going according to schedule," Radetskyy assured journalists. According to information available, at the end of 1993 Ukraine had approximately 2,000 nuclear warheads. Radetskyy also said that Ukraine will be controlling the process of re-targeting American strategic missiles earlier targeted against Ukraine. With this purpose a Ukrainian delegation is due to visit the USA soon. By May 30 the USA is to change the flight program of its missiles so that they are not aimed against Ukraine any more.
