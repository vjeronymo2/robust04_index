Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Lome, 14 Feb (AFP) -- The Action Committee for Renewal [CAR], one of the main Togolese opposition, claimed today that three of its supporters, including a deputy, have been "assassinated." The burnt bodies of the victims, including that of Gaston Edeh Aziandouvo, elected on 6 February as deputy for Yoto (70 km northeast of Lome), were found today at the outskirts of Lome, Mr. Yao Agboyibor, president of CAR, told AFP.
