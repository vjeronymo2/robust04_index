Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Heavy fighting raged yesterday between Phnom Penh Government forces and Khmer Rouge soldiers near the historic Preah Vihear temple in Cambodia, Second Army Region Commander Lt. Gen. Anuphap Songsunthon said. Lt. Gen. Anuphap said the fighting was the result of an all-out offensive by Phnom Penh forces to capture the Khmer Rouge stronghold in Preah Vihear Province. The fighting is between 10 to 20 kilometers away from the Thai border and at present there has been no spillover to Thailand.
