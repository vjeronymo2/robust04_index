Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vatican, Jan 11 (AFP) -- The Vatican said Tuesday [11 January] the international community had committed "the crime of failing to help" end the war in the former Yugoslavia. Europe was "dying" and a collective lack of action was "the most shameful cowardice," said the Vatican's Council for Justice and Peace, in an appeal made to mark a World Day of prayers for peace in the Balkans scheduled for January 23.
