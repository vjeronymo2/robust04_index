October 11, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STRIKE UPDATE

Three Las Vegas showrooms that have been dark because of a musicians strike are opening for business this week. The performers involved will be crossing picket lines established by Musicians Local 369. Frankie Valli, the Four Seasons and the Four Tops were scheduled to begin a one-week engagement Tuesday at the Las Vegas Hilton. David Copperfield is scheduled for two weeks at Caesars Palace starting tonight, and Sheena Easton is scheduled for one week at Bally's beginning Thursday. ALEENE MacMINN
