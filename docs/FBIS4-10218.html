BFN [Text] We have a last-minute cable announcing that five deputy ministers and other high-ranking Colombian officials from divers sectors are coming to Cuba tomorrow [10 April] to participate in the first meeting of the mixed binational commission that will be meeting 11-15 April. The deputy ministers who will take part in this meeting are: Deputy Minister of Education Antonio Lizarazo, who heads the delegation; Deputy Minister of Health Eduardo Alvarado; Deputy Foreign Minister Andelfo Garcia; Deputy Minister of Development Camilo Penalosa; and Deputy Minister of Communications Jaime Uride. Also in the delegation are the directors of the following institutes: Luis Munoz, Sports; Juan Luis Mejia, Culture; (Fernando Sarama), Civil Aviation; and (Miguel Ricaurte), of the National Training Service [Servicio Nacional de Aprendizaje].
