940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / International Capital Markets: Indosat plans US or UK float PT Indosat, Indonesia's international telecommunications company, plans a flotation of its shares in London or New York, Reuter reports from Jakarta. This will be the the first state company to make such a move, according to finance minister, Mar'ie Muhammad. He said that Indosat would be listed in Jakarta but most of the shares would be offered overseas. No date was given for the offer but 'Indosat . . . will be given the first opportunity to go international,' he said. Other state companies likely to be listed overseas include Garuda Indonesia, the national airline, and PT Telecom, the domestic telecommunications company.
