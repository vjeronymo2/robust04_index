Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Fierce fighting has been raging between resistance men, on one hand, and Israeli troops and the agent Lahd militia [South Lebanese Army] on the other, along the Zummarayya combat line since 0800 today. Artillery and rockets are being used in the clashes. A group of resistance men attacked a Lahdist patrol on the Hasbani-Zummarayya road this morning. Resistance men also pounded Lahdist posts in 'Ayn Qinya. Israeli forces bombarded several southern areas at 0700 today, including the Mimas axis, Marj al-Zuhur, Mazra'at 'Ayn Jabbur, Jabal al-Zahr, and Dallafah. A column of 12 Israeli armored vehicles escorted by some 50 troops advanced toward the Zummarayya crossing point. Meanwhile, the area between al-Hamra' and Haddathah came under fierce attacks amid Israeli overflights today.
