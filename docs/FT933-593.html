930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / International Company News: Siemens joins Australian pay-TV venture AWA, the Sydney-based elect-ronics group, is joining forces with Siemens of Germany to develop pay-TV and interactive video services which use existing copper telephone wires for delivery. Earlier this month, Telecom Australia, the government-owned telecommunications carrier, awarded a contract to AWA to provide a demonstration system based on this technology. Under the partnership, AWA will provide the switching and transmission equipment, with Siemens supplying the networking 'know-how'. The two companies claim the equipment market could be worth ADollars 500m (USDollars 327m) between 1995 and 2000.
