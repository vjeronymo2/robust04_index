940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Dewhurst improves to Pounds 935,449 Dewhurst, the electrical component and control equipment maker, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 935,449 for the 53 weeks to October 3, against Pounds 849,801, a rise of 10 per cent. Turnover rose 17 per cent to Pounds 10.7m (Pounds 9.12m). Earnings per share were 5.62p (5.1p) and an increased final dividend of 1.4p makes a 2.06p (1.89p) total.
