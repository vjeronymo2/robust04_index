September 13, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

THE CORNER BAR: MORE BARS:

Heritage Brewing Co., 24921 Dana Point Harbor Drive, Dana Point. Open Sundays through Thursdays from 11:30 a.m. to midnight; Fridays and Saturdays till 2 a.m. (714) 240-2060.

Currently the only pub in Orange County that makes its own beer on the premises: three menu-listed ales daily plus a different specialty brew every couple of weeks. All are rich, flavorful and heads above many English beers. Plus, there's even reggae music on Sundays. FRANK MESSINA
