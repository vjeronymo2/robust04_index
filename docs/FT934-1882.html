931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Richmond Oil Richmond Oil & Gas has called an extraordinary meeting to warn shareholders that net assets had fallen to less than half the company's called up share capital of Pounds 9.1m. Richmond also announced the disposal of its outstanding interest in a coal bed methane property, valued in the books at Pounds 3.5m. The company, which has been seeking to sell the properties since 1991, is disposing of the San Juan Basin interest for Dollars 6.2m (Pounds 4.2m). However, this price could be revised downwards by as much as Dollars 1m. Richmond has the option to buy back 50 per cent of the properties for a minimum of Dollars 3.1m.
