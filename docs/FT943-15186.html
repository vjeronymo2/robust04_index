940711 FT 11 JUL 94 / Arts (Openings): Los Angeles The Dodger Stadium is the venue of the Three Tenors' first concert together in the US. Jose Carreras, Placido Domingo and Luciano Pavarotti will perform on Saturday in a stadium tranformed for the night into a neo-classical amphitheatre complete with columns, waterfalls and exotic flora. The concert, in association with World Cup USA 1994, is staged on the eve of the soccer final, which will be held at the nearby Pasadena Rose Bowl.
