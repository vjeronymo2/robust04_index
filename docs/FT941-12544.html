940129 FT 29 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Markheath Markheath, the property company, cut pre-tax losses from Pounds 4.71m to Pounds 2.37m in the six months to September 30. The company said that restructuring talks, begun last March, were continuing. Mr Michael Rendle, chairman, said that reducing overheads enabled rental income, at Pounds 1.74m (Pounds 1.57m), to make a contribution towards interest costs of Pounds 2.55m (Pounds 2.4m). Losses per share were 1.94p (4.03p). The company is controlled by Adelaide Steamship, the Australian company and its associates.
