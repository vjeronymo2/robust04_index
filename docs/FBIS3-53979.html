Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' this morning received a mixed Democratic and Republican Congressional team led by Mr. Tom Lantos. Dr. Majdi al-Jazzar, chief of the U.S. Desk, and the U.S. charges d'affaires in Damascus were present at the meeting. They reviewed the Geneva summit between Presidents Hafiz al-Asad and Bill Clinton and its impact on the chances for a just and comprehensive peace in the Middle East. They also explored ways to bring about an improvement in relations between Syria and the United States.
