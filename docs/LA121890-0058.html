December 18, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

ACLU WILL SALUTE ACTOR GREGORY PECK

Actor Gregory Peck will be the honoree at the ACLU of Southern California Bill of Rights dinner on Jan. 16 at the Bonaventure.

Peck will receive the Bill of Rights Award for contributing to the growth and understanding of civil liberties.

ACLU Foundation board member Laurie Ostrow will receive the Eason Monroe Courageous Advocate Award, given each year to an individual who has dedicated his or her life to the cause of civil liberties.

Tickets are $80 and $150 per person. Information: (213) 487-6614.
