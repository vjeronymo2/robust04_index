March 6, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

'Miss Saigon' Tops Olivier Nominations: "Miss Saigon" led the nominations for Britain's Laurence Olivier awards, England's equivalent of the Tony Awards.

The show, now in its seventh month on the West End and due to bow on Broadway early next year, earned five nominations, including best musical, best actor in a musical and best director of a musical. Andrew Lloyd Webber's "Aspects of Love," expected to be its principal rival, earned no nominations. The winners will be announced April 8. BETH KLEID
