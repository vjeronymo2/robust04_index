Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Jan. 13 (YONHAP) -- International telephone rates will be lowered at least 3 percent and paging rates reduced more than 5 percent in the second half of the year, when they are unified, Communications Minister Yun Tong-yun said Thursday. Companies with their own communications networks, like Korea Electric Power Corp. (KEPCO) and Korea Highway Corp., will be able to take part in exclusive circuit projects such as cable television from next January, Yun told reporters after reporting his communications agenda to the president earlier in the day.
