BFN [Report by BELINFORM correspondent Nikolay Nazarovich for TASS] [Text] Minsk 24 June TASS -- "What has happened came as a sensation only for those who refused to face the truth," said Belarusian presidential candidate and presidential race leader Aleksandr Lukashenko commenting on his success in the first round of the elections. "The poor and deprived people for the first time had a chance to elect their like to the supreme state post, and the people said its word," Lukashenko told BELINFORM over the telephone today. Speaking of his further plans against corruption, Lukashenko said "all the high-ranking officials accused by the parliamentary commission against corruption remain on their posts. They are ready to do everything to finish the construction of their villas." As for his election program, the presidential candidate noted they have "outlined three areas: To keep in check the inflation and price increase, work against corruption and establish close economic relations with Russia." Lukashenko's team has elaborated "a block of proposals to solve the economic problems, stop the production recession within six months, resume the work of enterprises, bring people back to their jobs and create more jobs." He said they hope to do it with the assistance of Russia.
