Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 11 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received telegrams from foreign party leaders on the New Year. The telegrams came from General Secretary of the Central Executive Committee of the Bangladesh National Socialist Party Hasanul Huq Inu, convenor of the Central Committee of the Socialist Party of Bangladesh Khalequzzaman Bhuiyan, Chairman of the Nepal Workers and Peasants Party Narayan Man Bijukchhe, National Chairman of the Workers' Party of Zaire Emile Kibala Bey A Nsien, General Secretary of the Congolese Socialist Party Leon Nzokene, Secretary General of the Party of Revolution of Benin Ali Houdou, General Secretary of the Movement for Peace and Socialism of Italy Roberto Gabriele, National Secretary General of the Socialist Political Action Party of Peru Gustavo Mohme Ryona, General Secretary of the Socialist Party of Peru Luis Mateo Munos, General Secretary of the Sandinist National Liberation Front of Nicaragua Daniel Ortega Saavedra and Secretary General of the National Direction Committee of the Sudanese Union-African Democratic Rally in Mali Amadou El Becir Gologo. They hoped that in the new year, too, the Korean people would register a great success in the efforts to carry on the cause of socialism and reunify the country under the wise leadership of Comrade Kim Chong-il, braving the threats of the imperialists and reactionaries. They wished Comrade Kim Chong-il a long life in good health.
