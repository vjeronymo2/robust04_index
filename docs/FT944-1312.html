941221 FT 21 DEC 94 / Businesses reduce overseas mergers UK companies scaled back overseas mergers and acquisitions activity in the third quarter, according to figures released by the Central Statistical Office yesterday. The total spent by UK companies on overseas acquisitions in the third quarter was Pounds 3.5bn spread among 79 deals, down from Pounds 4.9bn and 109 deals in the previous three months. The biggest single third-quarter deal was the Pounds 1.9bn purchase of Sterling Winthrop by SmithKline Beecham.
