930112 FT 12 JAN 93 / Hatton trial starts today MR Derek Hatton, the former deputy leader of Liverpool City Council, goes on trial today charged with conspiracy to defraud the Labour local authority. Mr Hatton of Childwall, Liverpool, denies three charges of conspiracy to defraud the council, relating to car parks at Manesty's Lane and Brownlow Hill, Liverpool and a land site at Stonedale Crescent, Croxteth, Liverpool. Two other former Labour councillors and two businessmen have also denied conspiracy charges.
