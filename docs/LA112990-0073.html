November 29, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

ZEE BEST IS JUST THAT AT AQUEDUCT

Zee Best edged Senor Cielo by a head after a long stretch battle in the seven-furlong featured race at Aqueduct on Wednesday. Senor Cielo, who led to mid-stretch, finished second in the five-horse field, eight lengths ahead of Pok Ta Pok.

The victory was the fifth in 13 starts this year for the winner, who paid $6.20.
