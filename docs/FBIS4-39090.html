BFN [Text] The Kyrgyz government delegation, led by President Akayev, has concluded its official visit to France. Key issues discussed in the talks were the prospects of economic and culture ties between Kyrgyzstan and France. The [words indistinct] visit in Paris, Akayev signed an agreement on scientific and technical cooperation between the two countries and an agreement to protect investments. On behalf of the Republic, Akayev signed the Paris charter for a new Europe. At the World Bank's Paris headquarters Akayev discussed with World Bank officials and representatives of the IMF the implementation of structural reforms in the Republic's economy and the possibility of receiving low-interest credits. The Kyrgyz president also took part in a conference on "Kyrgyzstan is a Central Asian Switzerland" organized by the French Institute of International Relations. Members of the delegation returned to Bishkek 4 June.
