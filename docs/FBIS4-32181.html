BFN [Text] Bratislava, June 17 (CTK) -- Some 29 percent of Slovaks would vote for former premier Vladimir Meciar's Movement for a Democratic Slovakia (HZDS), a poll conducted by the Slovak Statistical Office in June revealed. About twenty percent of Slovaks favor the governing Democratic Left Party (SDL) and 8 percent the Christian Democratic Movement (KDH). The five-percent border necessary for entering the parliament would be also crossed by the coalition of ethnic Hungarian parties Egyutteles and Hungarian Christian Democratic Movement (MKDH) with 8 percent and Premier Jozef Moravcik's Democratic Union with 6 percent of votes. The voting turnout would be 77 percent, while 10 percent of voters have not made up their mind.
