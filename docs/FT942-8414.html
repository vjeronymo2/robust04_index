940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: EOP raises Pounds 6.6m for acquisition English & Overseas Properties is to buy Keswick Holdings for up to Pounds 5.3m and is to fund the acquisition by way of a placing to raise about Pounds 6.6m. Keswick is a privately owned property holding company which owns five properties - comprising two office properties, two industrial and one retail -in the south of England. Together their net annual rental income is about Pounds 1.26m and they have been valued on an open market basis at Pounds 15.5m. At March 24 Keswick's consolidated net assets totalled Pounds 5.07m and, in the year to March, pre-tax profits were Pounds 46,000 on turnover of Pounds 1.24m. The consideration is to be satisfied as to Pounds 1m in ordinary EOP shares at 35p and the balance in cash from the placing. An open offer is to raise a further Pounds 1.7m on a 1-for-2 basis. In 1993, EOP returned to the black with pre-tax profits of Pounds 312,000 (losses Pounds 707,000) on turnover up at Pounds 5.98m (Pounds 986,000).
