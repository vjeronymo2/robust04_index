Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Information from Rio de Janeiro states that Cuba and Brazil have signed a contract for the sale of $30 million worth of generic medicines to Brazil, to be delivered in four months. The contract does not include vaccines and refers to a specific area of generic medicine. Jose Ignacio Goicochea, Cuban vice minister of public health, and Brazilian Minister of Health Henrique Santillo signed the agreement. The entire ministerial cabinet of Brazil and (Saulo Pinto Moreira), special adviser to the president, were present at the signing of the contract.
