Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] A military delegation led by Bulgarian Defense Minister Valentin Aleksandrov arrived in Bucharest today. The delegation had talks with a military delegation led by Lieutenant General Niculae Spiroiu, Romanian minister of national defense. The sides discussed issues pertaining to NATO's initiative on the Partnership for Peace, the situation in the former Yugoslav territory and its impact on the Balkans, and measures taken thus far to restructure and modernize the military bodies of the two neighboring countries. The sides also pointed out their joint desire to develop the traditional relations of friendship and cooperation between the two armies, concludes a fax received from the Army's Press Bureau.
