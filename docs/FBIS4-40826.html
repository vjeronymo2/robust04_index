BFN [Text] Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev said that his country is interested in a just solution to the Cyprus issue and is studying the channeling of its efforts in this direction. Kozyrev's statement was made today during a meeting with House of Representatives President Alexis Galanos and the members of the parliamentary delegation visiting Moscow. Galanos asked for Russia's support for convening an international conference, for an investigative visit to Cyprus by the UN Security Council and for the proposal on disarmament in Cyprus. Galanos noted that Russia can play a counterbalancing role on the Security Council.
