December 12, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

NATURAL DISASTER: The gusty winds buffeting Orange County (A1) may seem threatening, but they're lightweights compared to the county's No. 1 natural enemy of the last few years: winter's cold. . . . Last winter, the county sustained more than $30 million in crop damage, mostly the result of cold weather. Dana Parsons
