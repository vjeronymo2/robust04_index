Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tajikistan has endorsed the new structure and composition of the country's Council of Ministers. Henceforth, the republic will have 23 ministries and three state committees. Four deputy heads of government have been deprived of their posts; however, most ministers have kept their portfolios. A Ministry of Security has been formed. It has been decided that the decree of the Presidium of the Tajik Supreme Soviet will be submitted to the next session of the national parliament.
