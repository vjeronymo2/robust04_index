Language: English Article Type:BFN [By CRIME PRESS correspondent Yuriy Shcherban] [Text] Moscow February 24 TASS--Another hearing on the case of those involved in the abortive coup, fixed for February 25, has been put off till March 1. This decision is not connected with the State Duma's resolution on amnesty for participants in the August 1991 events, adopted on Wednesday, CRIME PRESS learned from the military collegium of the Russian Supreme Court. The hearing was put off because of the continued illness of the wife of defendant Vasiliy Starodubtsev. She is in an intensive care unit of a hospital after a stroke.
