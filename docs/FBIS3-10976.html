Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The foreign ministers of Cuba and Cambodia today reaffirmed the profound ties of friendship between their two countries and spoke in favor of expanding ties of cooperation in all spheres where it may be possible to do so, especially economics. During the official talks, Prince Norodom Sirivut, younger brother of King Norodom Sihanouk, reiterated that ties of friendship unite Cambodia with Cuba. The premier [title as heard] also emphasized his government's unalterable position that condemns the economic, trade, and financial embargo imposed on Cuba by the United States for over 30 years. Robaina expressed satisfaction at finding himself in a Cambodia that is right in the middle of a process of national reconciliation and that is busy rebuilding the country, supported by the new climate of political stability achieved already last year.
