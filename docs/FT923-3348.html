920912 FT 12 SEP 92 / World News in Brief: London stocks end week on higher note The latest UK inflation statistics and strength in the US dollar which boosted the big overseas earning stocks helped the FT-SE 100 Index gain 30.3 points to close at 2,370.9. The increase reversed what had been a loss over the week to show a net gain of 8.7 points since last Friday's close. London stocks, Page 13; Lex, Page 22
