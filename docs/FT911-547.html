910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Kelt Energy moves on restructuring The prospects of restructuring the finances of Kelt Energy, the debt-laden oil exploration group, appear to have improved following the tabling of proposals to transfer certain non-producing international assets to a private Netherlands Antilles-registered company controlled by Mr Hubert Perrodo, Kelt's chairman. Effecting the transfer was a pre-condition for continuing the restructuring negotiations with the group's bankers. A month ago, the eight banks agreed to Kelt deferring payments of principal and interest on its borrowings, amounting to more than Pounds 150m. 'We are going to resume negotiations and are trying to resolve matters as fast as possible,' Mr Perrodo said.
