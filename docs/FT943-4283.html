940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Manweb buy-back Manweb has become the fifth of the regional electricity companies to buy back its shares. It bought 1m yesterday at 870p each and 500,000 the day before. The share closed yesterday at 866p, up 4p. The other companies which have bought back the shares are Eastern Electricity, Seeboard, and Sweb.
