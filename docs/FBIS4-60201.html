BFN [Excerpt] A responsible military source at the armed forces command in the Democratic Republic of Yemen has denied what has been reported by the French news agency. The agency, quoting a military spokesman in Sanaa, said that the forces of the al-Ahmar gang shot down a southern aircraft over the Abyan area. The source also denied that the northern forces had seized any equipment or war materiel in this governorate. The source said that the forces of the adventurous al-Ahmar gang in the Governorate of Abyan were not in a position to shoot down aircraft or seize any other military equipment. What was reported was no more than cheap media reporting and feverish psychological warfare. [passage omitted]
