BFN [Text] Brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and PLO Executive Committee chairman, has demanded the release of Palestinian prisoners in Israeli jails and deployment of international observers in the West Bank and Gaza Strip before 4 July in accordance with the agreement signed by the PLO and Israel on 4 May. At a meeting last night with ambassadors of the European Union states in Tunis, brother President Abu-'Ammar called for lifting the restrictions imposed by the Israeli forces on the movement of Palestinian officials on the crossings that link the West Bank with the Jericho area and Gaza. Informed Palestinian sources said brother President Abu-'Ammar emphasized the importance of realizing these demands before his return to the Palestinian self-rule areas, and the importance of expediting the assistance promised by the donor countries for reconstruction and construction in these areas. He said he cannot return to Gaza and Jericho before receiving this assistance. The source said the ambassadors of the European Union promised, at the meeting which was held at their request, to convey the demands of brother President Abu-'Ammar regarding assistance to their governments.
