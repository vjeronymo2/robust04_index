Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Robert Raymond Appointed Director General of European Monetary Institute -- EMI"; as released by Brussels TEXTLINE database] [Text] The Council of the European Monetary Institute, which met on Tuesday [8 February], appointed Mr. Robert Raymond director general of the new institute. Mr. Raymond, age 60, is presently director general for credit at the Bank of France. The EMI Council also appointed the directors of three of the five departments set up in the EMI. Dutchman Mr. G.P.J. Hogeweg, director-coordinator at the Netherlands' Central Bank, was appointed Director of the Department of Economics and Statistics; Mr. Hanspeter K. Scheller, of the Secretariat of EC governors, will be responsible for the Secretariat General; Mr. J.S. Etherington, of the Bank of England, will head the Communications Systems and Information Department. The EMI Council is made up of the twelve governors of the European Union and its president, Mr. Alexandre Lamfalussy.
