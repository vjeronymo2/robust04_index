Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Sun Maoqing (1327 5399 1987)] [Text] Beijing, 24 Dec (XINHUA) -- Central Military Commission [CMC] Chairman Jiang Zemin; Vice Chairmen Liu Huaqing and Zhang Zhen; members Chi Haotian, Zhang Wannian, Yu Yongbo, and Fu Quanyou; and leaders from the PLA [People's Liberation Army] General Staff Department, General Political Department, and General Logistics Department met today at the Great Hall of the People with representatives who are attending the Air Force's Eighth Party Congress. At around 1400, Jiang Zemin, along with other CMC leaders, stepped into the Great Hall amid warm applause. Jiang Zemin elatedly and warmly greeted Air Force's party congress representatives. He wished the congress success. He also conveyed warm greetings to all who work at the forefront of the Air Force's modernization construction and wished all a happy new year.
