BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 28 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to the Changgwang Export Garment Factory and the Okryu and Munsu Garment Companies. The officials and employees of these units increased the output of export goods by keeping a high and steady rate of production in response to the party's revolutionary economic strategy and sincerely manufactured various goods for the Pyongyang maternity hospital. Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, also sent thanks to Yi Ki-pom and Hwang Tong-kyu, political workers of the Kim Tae-song unit of the Korean People's Security Forces, and four demobilised soldiers for giving full play to communist virtues.
