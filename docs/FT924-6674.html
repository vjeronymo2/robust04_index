921120 FT 20 NOV 92 / Students Protest Students from New Delhi colleges shout slogans against the so-called 'Mandal report' yesterday. The report sets a reservation quota of 50 per cent for lower castes and tribal groups in university admissions and government jobs. Other protesters set up road blocks in the capital, while students in north India attacked trains and post offices and disrupted traffic, stepping up their opposition against the ruling on job quotas, local news agencies said.
