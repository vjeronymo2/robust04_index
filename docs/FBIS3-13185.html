Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghad, Mar 7, INA--Engineers and technicians of the Telecommunications and Post Department, Ninawa Province, have recently completed reconstruction work on the telecommunications installations hit during the US-led aggression in 1991. Quoted by AL-THAWRAH daily head of the department, Engineer Qutaybah Muhammad Sa'id said his staff were able to reconstruct the television and broadcasting complex, Gharb Dijlah mircowave project and Abu-Tammam electronic exchange.
