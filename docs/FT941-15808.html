940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / People: Non-executive directors - Alliance Resources Alliance Resources, the North American-based oil and gas company, yesterday announced the appointment of William Kennedy, a Canadian citizen, as a non-executive director. The appointment follows the cancellation in September of a rights issue in Canada when it was discovered the offer had not complied with local security laws. In June, the company had launched a Pounds 2.8m placing and rights issue at 5p to fund expansion.
