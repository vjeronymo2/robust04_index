BFN [Text] Today, Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali received at the Prime Ministry Wang Zhaoguo, vice chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC], and his accompanying delegation. Senate Speaker Ahmad al-Lawzi attended the meeting. Dr. al-Majali reviewed the development in Jordanian-Chinese relations, which both sides are seeking to develop in all fields. He stressed Jordan's continuous commitment to achieving a just, lasting, and comprehensive peace which restores the legitimate rights of the Palestinian people. He said that Jordan appreciates China's stands on various Arab issues. For his part, the CPPCC vice chairman stressed the importance of reinforcing Jordanian-Chinese relations. He also expressed his support for Jordan's commitment to peace.
