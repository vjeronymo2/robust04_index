920428 FT 28 APR 92 / UK Company News: Jas Smith Estates shows rental growth James Smith Estates, the property company, lifted pre-tax profit from Pounds 1.16m to Pounds 1.19m in the year to March 24. Mr Stephen Mulliner, chairman, said gross rents were Pounds 2m (Pounds 1.63m), the increase attributable to properties held throughout the year being Pounds 350,000, or 21.5 per cent. Earnings per share came to 5.1p (5p). The final dividend is 2.5p for a total of 3.75p (3.3p).
