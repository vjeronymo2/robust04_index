September 21, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: LAKE WOHLFORD

A badly decomposed body was found Monday about 1 mile northeast of Lake Wohlford, the Sheriff's Department reported.

Investigators believe the victim is male, according to Sgt. Danny Goodrich. The partially buried body was discovered at 5:10 p.m., he said.

Goodrich could not estimate when the victim died or the cause of death. An autopsy is pending.
