920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / World Trade News: Greek mobile phone awards GREECE yesterday awarded two licences to set up competitive mobile telephone networks to Stet, the Italian state telecoms company, and Panafon, an international consortium led by Vodafone of the UK. The total investment in the networks will be over Dollars 500m, writes Kerin Hope. Each group's network must be available in 85 per cent of Greek towns and cities and along 75 per cent of main roads by 1998. The two consortia will have exclusive operating rights for eight years.
