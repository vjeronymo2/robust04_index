930504 FT 04 MAY 93 / People: National Association of Probation Officers Joining the tiny band - now three - of female trade union general secretaries from today is Judy McKnight, who becomes leader of the 7,000-strong National Association of Probation Officers. She succeeds Bill Beaumont who did the job for seven years. McKnight has made her career in public sector unions; her last job was as assistant general secretary at the National Union of Civil and Public Servants. Before that, she worked for the Civil and Public Service Association.
