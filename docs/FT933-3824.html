930911 FT 11 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Maritime Transport financial package Maritime Transport Services, the company developing the new Thamesport container facility at the Isle of Grain in Kent, has signed a restructuring package involving a Pounds 95m conversion of loans and debt to equity. The arrangement with its bankers reduces MTS's debts to Pounds 75m, with the first of the capital repayments not due to begin until 1996 and interest payments starting from January 1995.
