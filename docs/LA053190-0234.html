May 31, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

NATION;

PTL NETWORK SALE IS APPROVED

A federal bankruptcy judge today approved the sale of PTL's lucrative satellite network for $52 million to a Jewish-born evangelist from San Diego who became a born-again Christian.

Evangelist Morris Cerullo, once a guest on imprisoned television preacher Jim Bakker's PTL show, did not appear at a 3 1/2-hour hearing in U.S. Bankruptcy Court, but his lawyer threatened to drop an offer to buy all PTL assets unless the sale was approved immediately.

"It is our hope as a Christian organization to come back and achieve what the Bakkers failed to do," attorney Timothy Treadwell told Judge Thurmond Bishop.
