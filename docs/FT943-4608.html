940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / Reject Shop broke copyright The Reject Shop and one of its former directors have been found guilty of a criminal breach of the Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988. In a case brought before magistrates the company and Mr Gordon Black were convicted of infringing the copyright of Mr Robert Manners, a tile designer who runs the Life Enhancing Tile Company. The Reject Shop had sold notebooks and tins based on Mr Manners' designs. Sentencing will be on September 28.
