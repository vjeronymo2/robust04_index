Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Established Diplomatic Ties With Slovakia"] [Text] Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] Foreign Minister Stevo Crvenkovski met in Trieste, March 4, with the deputy president of the Republic of Slovakia Government Jozef Prokes, exchanging notes on establishing diplomatic relations between the two countries. Minister Crvenkovski and Deputy Prime Minister Prokes pointed out the mutual desire and readiness for a future intensive development of relations and co-operation between the Republics of Macedonia and Slovakia.
