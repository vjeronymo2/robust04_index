BFN [Text] The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song received Former U.S. President Jimmy Carter. The great leader DPRK President Comrade Kim Il-song today received Former U.S. President Jimmy Carter and his entourage who are visiting our country. Kim Yong-nam, vice premier and foreign minister, and Kang Sok-chu, first vice foreign minister, were present. The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song welcomed Jimmy Carter to our country and conversed with him in a warm atmosphere. The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and his wife arranged a luncheon for Jimmy Carter and his entourage today. Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam and his wife, Secretary of the Party Central Committee Hwang Chang-yop and his wife, Secretary Kim Yong-sun and his wife, and First Vice Foreign Minister Kang Sok-chu joined the luncheon. The luncheon was held in a warm atmosphere.
