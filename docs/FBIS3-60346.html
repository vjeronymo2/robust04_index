Language: Japanese Article Type:CSO [Complete text of article obtained through NIKKEI BIZ DATABASE] [Text] On 1 September, Nagano City introduced an electric vehicle and held a ceremony at city hall for its initial operation. The vehicle is a lightweight van manufactured by Daihatsu and is equipped with lead accumulators. This is the first electric vehicle to be introduced in the prefecture. The city plans to use it for monitoring pollution in the city and to promote recycling. The vehicle is called "E-Car," named after the initial English letter for electricity, ecology and environment. The white body has an oval design of moss green, pink and light blue stripes. Charging facilities have been established in the waste processing plant and city hall. The van is capable of driving about 130 kilometers for each 8- hour charge. It costs ¥3.162 million, and was partially financed by the Environment Agency. Mayor Tsukada looked satisfied, saying, "It has been introduced to promote an environment-friendly community. The van is quiet and stable."
