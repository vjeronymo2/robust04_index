920306 FT 06 MAR 92 / UK Company News: French Property asset value lower Net asset value per share of French Property Trust declined by 12.4 per cent to 67.67p over the year to December 31. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 383,608 (Pounds 178,716 for the period April 6 to December 31 1990). The dividend is 1p (0.47p). Earnings amounted to 1.53p (0.71p).
