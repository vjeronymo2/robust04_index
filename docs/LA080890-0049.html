August 8, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Bank of America, San Francisco, has named Ed Hall senior vice president and head of consumer marketing for the Consumer Banking Group.

Hall, 48, will be responsible for all consumer marketing, including advertising, in California. He replaces Shelly Porges, who left the bank to start her own business. Hall joins the bank from First Constitutional Bank of New Haven, Conn., where he was responsible for marketing, advertising and public relations.
