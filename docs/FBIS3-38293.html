Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] France has expressed its satisfaction after having heard of the plans made by UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali for the rotation of blue helmets in Srebrenica and the reopening of Tuzla airport in Bosnia-Herzegovina. UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] has been given the duties which we wanted to give it, said the Foreign Ministry deputy spokesperson, adding that the report makes explicit provision for close air support.
