930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News: Bostrom An 11.4 per cent increase in 1992 pre-tax profits from Pounds 825,000 to Pounds 919,000 was announced by Bostrom, the specialist engineer. Turnover was slightly up from Pounds 33.3m to Pounds 34.3m. Earnings per share emerged at 9p against 6.4p. The total dividend is maintained at 6p with a proposed final of 3.5p.
