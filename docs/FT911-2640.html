910429 FT 29 APR 91 / UK Company News: Folkes restructures and lifts profits to over Pounds 4m FOLKES GROUP, the property, engineering and building products group, lifted pre-tax profits by 12 per cent from Pounds 3.6m to Pounds 4.03m in 1990. The result was achieved on lower turnover of Pounds 47.71m against Pounds 58.51m. Mr Constantine Folkes, chairman, said the group had changed more significantly during the year than at any time in its history, enhancing its profit potential and its position in both open die forging and property. The balance sheet remained strong. Earnings improved to 7.49p (6.77p) and the recommended final dividend of 1.75p makes a total of 2.3p (2.05p).
