BFN [Excerpts] Some 39 percent of the public believe the government should pursue the political process with the PLO in spite of the recent terror attacks, 35.5 percent think the talks should be halted, 22.5 percent claim they should not be suspended, and 3 percent have no opinion on the matter. [sentence as published] These findings emerged in a public opinion poll commissioned by MA'ARIV from the Shiluv Institute directed by Yisra'el Olynik. Some 53 percent of the respondents support the evacuation of Israeli Army troops from Gaza and Jericho, 34 percent object to it, and 13 percent have no opinion on the matter. [passage omitted] The poll was conducted among 502 men and women over the age of 18, according to a nationwide random representative sample of the Jewish population.
