940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Kenmare Resources Kenmare Resources, the Dublin-based natural resources company, announced pre-tax profits of IPounds 21,238 (Pounds 20,400), for the six months to October 31 against losses of IPounds 84,027. Earnings per share were 0.03p, compared with losses of 0.12p. The company has been concentrating on its Ancuabe graphite project in northern Mozambique and is already producing usable graphite to industrial grade.
