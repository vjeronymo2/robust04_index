October 22, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

South Africa -- In Sheila Benson's Oct. 15 commentary, the director of "Mapantsula" was identified as a black South African. Oliver Schmitz is a white South African who collaborated on the screenplay with Thomas Mogotlane, the black South African who starred in the film. The two used a mixed crew to shoot the movie in the black township of Soweto and in Johannesburg.
