Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A statement by the Ministry of Interior and Local Authorities discloses that successive investigations have enabled the security services to find a quantity of weapons and various items which were hidden in an Algerian freighter by the sailor Hacene Mialet. His confessions and other clues indicated that this equipment was to be delivered to him in the German port of Hamburg by Djamel Lounici who is wanted for involvement in a number of terrorist activities. An unforeseen event happened and prevented the delivery of the equipment at the German harbor, but in the end the weapons were delivered to Hacene Mialet in the Belgian port of Antwerp. Investigations are continuing and have led to the arrest of several people involved including the above-mentioned sailor. The weapons seized are: 20 rifles, 15 automatic pistols, a considerable quantity of ammunition, two-way radios, and optical equipment.
