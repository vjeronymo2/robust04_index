Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 22 (XINHUA) -- A five-member delegation of the Communist Party of China (CPC) left here this afternoon for a goodwill visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK). As guests of the Worker's Party of Korea, the delegation is led by Li Shuzheng, head of the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee. It is Li's first trip to the DPRK since she took charge of the International Liaison Department. Pae Yong-chae, charge d'affaires ad interim of the DPRK Embassy here, was among those to see Li and her party off at the airport.
