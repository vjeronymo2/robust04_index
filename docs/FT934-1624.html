931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Southend Property in Pounds 20.75m deals Southend Property is selling Meridian House, Farringdon Street, London, to Pillar Property Investments for Pounds 14.3m. The property is let on a lease expiring in December 2005 at a current annual rent of Pounds 1.27m, producing a net initial yield of 8.75 per cent. In a separate deal, Southend is buying Thorncliffe Park Estate, Sheffield, for about Pounds 6.5m from the receivers of South Riding Estates.
