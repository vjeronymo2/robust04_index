BFN [Text] The first representatives of the Palestinian Authority will arrive in Jericho next week. This was said by sources in Jericho to our correspondent Moti Amir. Among those arriving will be Abu-al-'Ala' [Ahmad Quray'] and Nabil Sha'th. Meanwhile, 18 offices for the use of the Palestinian Authority members -- including an office for Yasir 'Arafat -- have been completed in Jericho. Our correspondent notes that the Palestinians will also set up a special office in Jericho for handling the intifadah victims. [Earlier on the same newscast, it is noted that "the closure imposed on al-Ram yesterday -- after the security forces killed the Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] activist who has killed Shin Bet officer No'am Kohen -- was lifted today."]
