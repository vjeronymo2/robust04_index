930812 FT 12 AUG 93 / Tajik government asks Afghanistan to mediate between it and Islamic rebels THE Tajikistan government yesterday asked Afghanistan to mediate between it and Islamic rebels attacking the former Soviet republic from northern Afghanistan, Reuter reports from Dushanbe. Afghan Foreign Minister Hidayat Amin Arsala agreed to act as an intermediary. Earlier the Tajik government said four Russian soldiers and a Kazakh officer guarding the frontier with Afghanistan had been seized by guerrillas from across the border.
