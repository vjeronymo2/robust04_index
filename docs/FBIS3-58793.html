Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] There was an assassination attempt on Mikhail Vranopoulos, former governor of the National Bank, at 0910 today on 46 Solon Street. An unidentified man wearing a suit fired four shots at Vranopoulos, hitting him in the abdomen. The gunfire also wounded an unidentified passerby. After the shooting, the culprit escaped on a motorcycle driven by an accomplice. Mr. Vranopoulos was taken to Evangelismos Hospital.
