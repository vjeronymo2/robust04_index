Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa called on parliamentary vice ministers to come up with deregulation measures and remedies for such exclusionary business practices as keiretsu [interlocking corporate groups]. He made the remarks at a meeting of parliamentary vice ministers held at the second office building for lower house members on 17 March. Hosokawa then stressed his intention to strengthen enforcement of the Antimonopoly Law. Also, he called for the vice ministers' positive attitudes toward narrowing disparity between Japanese and international prices, saying: "I want all of you to work on the subject without exceptions because this is closely related to the consumers' interests."
