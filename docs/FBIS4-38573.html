BFN [Text] Today Armenian Vice President Gagik Arutyunyan received Mir Ahmad Sadat, head of the Standards and Industrial Research Institute of the Islamic Republic of Iran; [name indistinct], deputy head of the metal production department of the institute; and [name indistinct], of the [word indistinct] department. In greeting the guests, the republic's vice president attached major significance to the agreements to be signed soon between the Iranian Standards and Industrial Research Institute and the Armenian Department of Standardization, Measurements, and Certification. Mr. Arutyunyan hoped that the document slated for signing and the mutual recognition of standards and certificates will contribute to the development of close cooperation between Iran and Armenia in the trade and industrial sectors. During the meeting the sides stressed the need to organize scientific symposiums and seminars and exchange information on standardization and measurements. Gukas Megrapyan, head of the Armenian Department for Standardization, Measurements, and Certification, and Iranian Charge d'Affaires in Armenia Ahmad Sobhani were present at the meeting.
