920702 FT 02 JUL 92 / UK Company News: London Share Service Monday's list of forthcoming changes to company classifications in the London Share Service should have stated that companies classified in Stock Exchange category 98 will be shown with effect from July 4 under Investment Trusts. The category includes investment trusts approved under Section 842 of the Income and Corporation Taxes Act but either registered outside the UK or denominated, accounting or quoted in a foreign currency. The placing of individual companies will be confirmed in the normal weekly list of changes to the London Share Service.
