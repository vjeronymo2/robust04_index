BFN [Text] The House of Representatives multiparty delegation led by House President Alexis Galanos, now on an official visit to Israel, today began high-level contacts with Israeli officials. The delegation was received this morning by Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin and then met with Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres. Both Mr. Rabin and Mr. Peres stressed the importance they attach to the visit and added that it will contribute to the development of Cypriot-Israeli relations. They also stated that the visit opens doors in the right direction. Referring to the Cyprus issue, Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin wished for an early reunification of the island and stressed that it is important for there to be one state entity in Cyprus, where the principle of rule by the majority should prevail. Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres stressed the need to safeguard the unity of the Republic of Cyprus through intercommunal talks. However, he added that Israel is interested in its relations with Turkey and that it should preserve and safeguard them. In a statement to the Cyprus Broadcasting Corporation, Shim'on Peres said that Cyprus has a bright future. He also expressed the conviction that there is enough prudence on the island for the existing dispute to be resolve soon. Peres further noted that Cyprus can become a significant center for the exchange of ideas and trade.
