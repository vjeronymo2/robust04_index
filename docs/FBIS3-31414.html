Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pristine, February 16 (ATA) -- Representatives of the Serbian authorities located forcibly in Kosova went again to the Academy of Sciences and Arts of Kosova with new threats and demanded to the Albanian academics to give the Academy building up and to handle the keys. The Academy's refusal was determined. Based on the decisions of the Extraordinary Serbian Assembly, the Serbian authority takes the Kosova Academy building and its property. Many personalities of the state, politics, and science who have protested against the Serbian warnings to abolishing the Kosova Academy, are expecting the international support for protecting the highest Albanian science institution in Kosova.
