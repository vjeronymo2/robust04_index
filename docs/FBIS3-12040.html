Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 28 Mar (NA) -- Lieutenant General Mario Candido Diaz, the chairman of the Armed Forces' Joint Chiefs of Staff, today installed Sea Captain Carlos Carbone as the new head of the Antarctic Joint Command in a ceremony held on the "Uruguay" corvette, which is riding at anchor at the Vuelta de Rocha in the Buenos Aires district of La Boca. The new commander, who replaces Sea Captain Ricardo Corbetta, will be in charge of organizing the 1994-1995 Antarctic Campaign. In his prior post as commander of the Argentine Navy's "Admiral Irizar" Antarctic vessel he commanded operations to resupply Argentina's Antarctic bases.
