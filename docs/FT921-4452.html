920310 FT 10 MAR 92 / Letter: 'No enormous salary' for Branson Sir, You quote me as saying, in your Thorn/Virgin article ('Thorn EMI pays Pounds 510m for Virgin Music', March 7): 'I get a really enormous salary, but I won't tell you what it is.' If I had been getting a really enormous salary, I hope it would have been out of character to have said so. As it is - Thorn shareholders will be pleased to know - I chose to take no salary, and my statement was made, glancing at Colin Southgate, with a twinkle in my eye] Richard Branson, chairman, Virgin Group, 120 Campden Hill Road, London W8
