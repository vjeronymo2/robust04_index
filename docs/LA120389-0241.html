CALIFORNIA ASSESSMENT PROGRAM TESTS IN PHYSICAL EDUCATION;

HOW TO READ CALIF. ASSESSMENT PROGRAM -- PHYSICAL EDUCATION TEST SCORES

Grade indicates grade level of students tested last spring. Other columns indicate the number of tests that students in a particular grade group passed.

The data in the chart lists the percentage of students in each grade that passed that number of tests. Thus, 26% of Orange County fifth-graders passed two of the fitness tests, and 24% passed only one test.

Students passing four or five of the fitness tests are considered physically fit by the state.

Ten percent of the county's fifth-graders passed none of the tests, which include pullups, sit-ups, a one-mile run/walk and a flexibility test called the "sit-and-reach." A fifth test, which measures skin folds on a student's arms and legs, was optional.

Note: Yorba Linda Elementary district became part of Placentia Unified School District in the fall of 1989.
