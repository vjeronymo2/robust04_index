BFN [Text] Dissatisfied with the mild statement of the State Duma on the situation in Bosnia, approximately 20 radically inclined deputies -- Aleksandr Nevzorov, Anatoliy Lukyanov from the Russian Way group, Vyacheslav Marychev of the Liberal Democratic Party, and others -- decided to hand in their own appeal to the U.S. ambassador in Russia on Wednesday evening. Expressed in it is, and I am quoting: profound indignation at the barbaric bombing by NATO aircraft approved by President Bill Clinton. The statement also contains the demand, and I am quoting again, that: The illegal operation be investigated immediately and NATO troops be withdrawn at once from the territory of the former Yugoslavia.
