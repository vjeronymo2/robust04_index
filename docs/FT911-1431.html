910504 FT 04 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Prudential chief gets 43% pay increase MR MICK NEWMARCH, the chief executive of Prudential Corporation, received a salary almost twice that of the average for other insurance industry chiefs in 1990. According to Prudential's latest annual report, he received Pounds 543,673 in 1990, a 43 per cent increase on the previous year's Pounds 379,651. The pay rise was decided by a committee of non-executive directors and includes bonus payments based on the company's performance in 1989, when profits rose by 9 per cent. The increase makes Mr Newmarch, who also has more than 600,000 share options and receives other benefits, one of the best paid insurance managers in the country.
