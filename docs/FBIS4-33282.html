BFN [Text] The implementation of revolutionary measures against the so-called money hoarders continues nationwide. On 3 June, the 5 DE SEPTIEMBRE weekly of Cienfuegos carried an article, entitled, Act of Justice, regarding the first nine cases being processed by the prosecutor's office and other entities in accordance with Decree Law 149. Cienfuegos authorities have seized from these people who attained their wealth illegally 19 cars of diverse makes, including two Moscovits, two Ladas, and three Jeeps, as well as six motorcycles. Authorities also seized 13 houses and [passage indistinct] of rice and 2,595 of beans. The article adds that the vehicles seized from the money hoarders will probably be allocated for use as ambulances and for other social services. The 13 homes will be used as schools and daycare centers among other uses, and the seized food will be distributed to hospitals.
