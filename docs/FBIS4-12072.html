BFN [Excerpt] His Excellency 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council in fraternal Yemen, left the country at noon today at the end of a two-day visit to the Sultanate. During the visit, His Excellency 'Ali Salim al-Bid met with His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Said to review the overall developments in Yemen and the ongoing efforts to contain the Yemeni crisis. They also discussed fraternal relations and ways to boost and develop these relations. Al-Bid was seen off at Salalah Civil Airport by His Highness Thuwayni Bin-Shihab Al Sa'id, personal representative of his majesty the sultan. [passage omitted]
