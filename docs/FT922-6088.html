920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / Polish President Walesa challenges centre-right minority government to resign PRESIDENT Lech Walesa yesterday challenged Poland's centre-right minority government to resign, writes Christopher Bobinski in Warsaw. In a letter delivered to parliament during the closing hours of a day-long visit to Warsaw by Mr John Major he declared he had lost 'confidence' in the cabinet led by Prime Minister Jan Olszewski. The prime minister, appointed in December, showed no immediate sign that he was thinking of resigning.
