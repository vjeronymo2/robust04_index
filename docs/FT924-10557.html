921030 FT 30 OCT 92 / Detailed terms of the Tomkins offer THE terms of the Tomkins offer for Ranks Hovis MacDougall are either 2.29 Tomkins shares plus Pounds 5.20p in cash for every four RHM shares, or 260p in cash writes Maggie Urry. The share offer values each RHM share at 251.4p, with Tomkins shares closing last night at 212p, down 49p. RHM shareholders will also receive a dividend of 9.54p net from RHM, enabling Tomkins to buy shares in the market up to 269.54p cum dividend. The cash offer values the ordinary shares at Pounds 925m, and there is an offer for the preference shares at par, worth about Pounds 10m in total. Hanson's cash offer of 220p a share for RHM plus its preference offer values RHM in total at Pounds 790m. Tomkins is making a rights issue to help finance the bid. This is of one new share for every two held at a price of 200p, to raise Pounds 653m net. It will be in two instalments, the first of 120p a share raising Pounds 384m net, which will proceed whether or not the bid succeeds. The second instalment of 80p, raising Pounds 269m, depends on the offer being accepted.
