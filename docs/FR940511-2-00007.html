Foreign-Trade Zones Board [Docket 79&hyph;91] Foreign-Trade Zone 72, Indianapolis, IN; Withdrawal of Application for Subzone Status for Hurco Machine Tool Plant Notice is hereby given of the withdrawal of the application submitted by the Indianapolis Airport Authority, grantee of FTZ 72, requesting special-purpose subzone status for the machine tool manufacturing facility of Hurco Companies, Inc., located in Indianapolis, Indiana. The application was filed on November 6, 1991 (56 FR 65040, 12/13/91). The withdrawal is requested by the applicant because of changed circumstances, and the case has been closed without prejudice.
