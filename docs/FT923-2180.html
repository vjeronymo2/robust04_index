920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Greater competition leaves MTL lower Increased competition for scarce orders left interim pre-tax profit at MTL Instruments, the maker of electronic measuring devices, lower at Pounds 2.2m, compared with Pounds 2.35m. Turnover in the six months to June 30 for this USM-traded company was Pounds 9.29m (Pounds 9.19m). Mr Ian Hutcheon, chairman and chief executive, said the result was disappointing and reflected poor trading in the UK and US, where sales were down almost 20 per cent. There was an extraordinary credit of Pounds 630,000 (Pounds 552,000) and earnings per share were 8p (8.5p). The interim dividend is raised to 1.6p (1.5p).
