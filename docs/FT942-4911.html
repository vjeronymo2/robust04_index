940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Foreign investor demand for bellwether stock Telefonos de Mexico, the telephone monopoly, led the market into higher ground. The IPC index ended 29.86, or 1.21 per cent, up at 2,494.84. Telmex 'L' series shares rose 22 centavos, or 2.13 per cent, to 10.54 new pesos.
