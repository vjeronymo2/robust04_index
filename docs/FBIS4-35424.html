BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 31 May (MENA) -- [passage omitted] President Husni Mubarak also received Kim Yong-nam, foreign minister of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (North Korea). Kim Yong-nam said after the meeting that he conveyed to President Mubarak the greetings of Leader Kim Il-song and the leaders of North Korea, and noted that President Mubarak asked him to convey his greetings to the leader Kim Il-song. He affirmed that President Mubarak greatly appreciates his excellent personal relationship with leader Kim Il-song. Asked whether he discussed with the president North Korea's crisis at the United Nations, he said: We did not discuss this subject. He noted that he came to Cairo to attend the nonaligned foreign ministers conference. The North Korean foreign minister affirmed that his country is looking forward to the visit by President Mubarak, noting that there is a long-standing invitation to his excellency to make this visit.
