BFN [Text] Baku (March 31) TURAN -- Today's session of Milli Majlis is to discuss socio-economic situation in the Republic. According to independent experts, since January 1994 Azerbaijan's economy was enveloped by hyperinflation. For three months of 1994 the inflation totalled 50-60 per cent, prices increased by more than three times. State budget has yet to be adopted. At the beginning of March, 1994, Milli Majlis adopted the resolution to hear the account of the government. According to several sources, the Cabinet is not ready to Parliamentary hearings.
