BFN [Text] A responsible source at the Defence Ministry of the Democratic Republic of Yemen said that with unique courage and under the command of the liberation war strugglers the popular resistance at al-Shu'ayb locality in Ad Dali' Province yesterday confronted the attacks of the invaders form the aggressive al-Ahmar gang which targeted the citizens' villages and their possessions. The source said in a statement to the Yemen News Agency, Aden, that the popular resistance have succeeded in repulsing the enemy's attacks and incurred heavy casualties on it, among which was the destruction of a military group and the killing of Staff Lieutenant Colonel Salih 'Ali Salih Zahir al-Sanhani and seized a quantity of drugs and important documents on him. A number of enemy officers and elements were also killed, and advanced communications equipments have been seized.
