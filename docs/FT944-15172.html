941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada With nearly all of the sub-groups on the Toronto market flat to lower, the TSE 300 composite index posted a decline of 11.04 points at 4,315.96. Falls outpaced rises at the close by 461 to 297 after moderate volume of 48.9m shares. Ten of the 14 sub-indices were down, led by transportation, energy, forestry and industrial stocks. Financial services, merchandising, pipelines and utilities made gains. The banking sector, the most heavily weighted in the composite index, rose 1.2 per cent.
