March 8, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

DAR: The Daughters of the American Revolution, now convening in Costa Mesa (N1), count some familiar names among their members: First Ladies Barbara Bush, Rosalynn Carter and Bess Truman. . . . And while some members, such as conservative activist Phyllis Schlafly, may fit the DAR stereotype, others from the entertainment community may not. . . . These include actresses Lillian Gish and Ginger Rogers, talk show host Tawny Little and Laguna Beach's Harriet Nelson. Kristina Lindgren and Mark I. Pinsky
