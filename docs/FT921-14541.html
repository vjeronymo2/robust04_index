920115 FT 15 JAN 92 / Lion Brewery to cut 225 jobs UP TO 225 jobs are to go at the Lion Brewery in Hartlepool, Cleveland, its new owner Wolverhampton & Dudley Breweries announced yesterday. The job losses, which will reduce the workforce of 329 by about two thirds by May, are part of a reorganisation at the brewery by the newly established Camerons Brewery Company. The company bought Lion Brewery, 51 north-east public houses and associated free trade businesses from Brent Walker last month for Pounds 18.7m.
