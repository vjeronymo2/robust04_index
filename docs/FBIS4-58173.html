BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn today visited al-Anbar Governorate and met with several tribal chieftains in the presence of al-Anbar Governor Mani' 'Abd Rashid and the secretary and members of the al-Anbar branch of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party. Speakers from the residents and tribal chieftains in al-Anbar Governorate reiterated their absolute love for and loyalty to the leadership of the leader president, may God preserve him, until total victory over the enemies is achieved, God willing. In speeches and poetry they delivered before the president, several chieftains voiced their great pride in the interest his excellency takes in the city of al-Ramadi and the rest of the governorate's cities, as well as his personal interest in the projects that are implemented. The leader president, may God preserve him, delivered an important speech in which he praised the position of the residents of al-Anbar Governorate, pointing out that it was historic, that future generations will remember it with pride, and that history will record the militant position and steadfastness of all the sons of great Iraq.
