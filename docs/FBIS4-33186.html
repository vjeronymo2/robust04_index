BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] Changes were made today in two precincts [delegaciones] of the Federal District. Today, the Benito Juarez Precinct delegate was changed. Enrique Jackson, government secretary of the Federal District Department, presided over the ceremony, in which authorities bid farewell to Roberto Ortega Lomelin and welcomed Jose Ramon Martel as new delegate. Ortega Lomelin thanked residents of Benito Juarez Precinct for their continued support for over five years. [Begin Ortega recording] What we were able to accomplish was made possible thanks, in large part, to an effective, enthusiastic team, a team with a great service calling. Thank you very much to my team of collaborators. [end recording] The new Benito Juarez Precinct delegate pledged to continue security, grounds use, and public service programs. [Begin Martel recording, broadcast in progress] ...is to continue work initiated by authorities of this precinct. We will continue to be meticulous and careful with the use of grounds. We will continue to support public security. [end recording] Martel served as delegate in the Tlahuac Precinct until yesterday. The ceremony was attended by members of the Benito Juarez Precinct Residents Board.
