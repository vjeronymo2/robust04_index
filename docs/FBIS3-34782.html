Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sidon, Lebanon, Feb 15 (AFP) -- Assailants killed a military commander of the pro-Syrian Amal movement and wounded his brother in a grenade attack in south Lebanon early Tuesday, security sources said. They knocked on Muhammad Yaghi's door and when he opened it tossed in the grenade, killing him and wounding his brother, the sources said. The assailants, who were not identified, escaped after the attack which took place shortly after midnight in Zawtar al-Gharbiyah, just north of Israel's security zone.
