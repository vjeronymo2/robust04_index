941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Inoco declines to Pounds 250,000 Inoco, the USM-quoted property group, reported a fall from Pounds 834,000 to Pounds 250,000 in pre-tax profits for the six months ended June 30. Turnover, however, improved from Pounds 4.81m to Pounds 5.44m. There was a Pounds 2.8m loss (Pounds 11,000 profit) on investments and a profit of Pounds 1.22m, against losses of Pounds 4,000, on the disposal of investment properties. Earnings per share at this Norwich-based company declined from 0.4p to 0.12p.
