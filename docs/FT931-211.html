930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Kirklees Tory guilty of theft A FORMER parliamentary candidate and former mayor of Kirklees was yesterday convicted of stealing Pounds 8,750 from the Huddersfield Conservative Association. York crown court heard that Mr John Holt used a Jersey-based company to divert to his own account part of a Pounds 10,000 bequest by a party supporter. He was found guilty of six charges of theft. Judge John Cotton, who called for a report on Mr Holt before sentencing, remanded him on bail.
