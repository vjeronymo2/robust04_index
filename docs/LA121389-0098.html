December 13, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Wimberly Allison Tong & Goo: Alan Kiji Murakami has been appointed controller of Wimberly Allison Tong & Goo, an architecture and planning company in Newport Beach. He succeeds Sun Hee Choi, who left to obtain an MBA at the University of Hawaii, Manoa. Murakami was previously auditor for the Defense Contract Audit Agency.
