BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 24 KYODO -- Japan's Overseas Economic Cooperation Fund (OECF) announced Tuesday [24 May] it will extend up to 10,332 million yen in loans to El Salvador. The fund will finance El Salvador's road improvement project to ease traffic congestion in its capital area, the fund said. The credit will carry an interest rate of 3.0 percent and will mature in 30 years, OECF said.
