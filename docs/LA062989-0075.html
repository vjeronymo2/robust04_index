June 29, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: HOLLYWOOD;

RESPITE CARE OFFERED

The Assistance League of Southern California's Hollywood center is providing weekend respite care for the frail elderly on the last weekend of every month between 10 and 4 p.m.

The Hollywood Senior Multipurpose Center is located at 6501 Fountain Ave. For more information, call Director Emma Kelly at (213) 465-2158.
