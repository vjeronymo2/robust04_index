October 13, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The Amateur Athletic Foundation's Board of Directors has authorized grants totaling $431,678 to 15 Southland youth organizations.

The largest of the grants was to fund the AAF/Ladies Professional Golf Assn. junior golf program for a second year. A $76,778 grant was authorized for Reviving Baseball in Inner Cities, which last year provided teams for almost 200 boys.
