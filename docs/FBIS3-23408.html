Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Citing Resistance sources, our security correspondent has reported that at 1235 today, the Resistance detonated an explosive device in a Mercedes car belonging to the Israeli intelligence as it drove along the al-Jarmaq-al-'Ayshiyah road. According to the same sources, the device killed everyone inside the car. Security sources reported that two explosive devices went off. The first went off on the al-Jarmaq-al-'Ayshiyah road targeting an Israeli military vehicle. The second targeted a foot patrol on the al-'Ayshiyah-al-Rayhan road. Using field artillery, the Israeli army shelled the al-Nabatiyah-Kafr Rumman area. The shelling also targeted Iqlim al-Tuffah, in particular the Mlita and al-Luwayzah hills, from the al-Zaffatah positions. Israeli warplanes also overflew the area. [passage omitted]
