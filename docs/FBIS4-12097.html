BFN [Text] Cairo, 5 Apr (MENA)--Major General 'Uzi Dayan, the Israeli Army's chief of planning, and the delegation accompanying him left Cairo this evening. Over the past two days, Dayan participated in the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations in Cairo. The talks revolved around ways of expediting the deployment of Palestinian forces in the areas that Israel will withdraw from in Gaza and Jericho and other security arrangements.
