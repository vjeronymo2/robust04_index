DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE Forest Service Newspapers To Be Used for Publication of Notice on Proposed Actions for Southern Region; Alabama, Kentucky, Georgia, Tennessee, Florida, Louisiana, Mississippi, Virginia, West Virginia, Arkansas, Oklahoma, North Carolina, South Carolina, Texas, Puerto Rico AGENCY: Forest Service, USDA. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: Responsible Officials in the Southern Region will publish notice of proposed actions under 36 CFR part 215 in the newspapers that are listed in the Supplementary Information section of this notice. As provided in 36 CFR 215.5(a), the public shall be advised, through Federal Register notice, of the principal newspapers to be utilized for publishing notices on proposed actions. DATES: Use of these newspapers for purposes of publishing notices of proposed actions under 36 CFR part 215 shall begin on or after January 5, 1994.
