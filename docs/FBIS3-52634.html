Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] A representative of Hekmatyar's Hezb-e Eslami in Islamabad announced that General Dostam and Golboddin Hekmatyar have given President Borhanoddin Rabbani 48 hours to resign. According to the Central News Unit from Islamabad, the statement by the opposition groups asked the political groups in Afghanistan to meet to decide on a replacement for Borhanoddin Rabbani and to implement an immediate cease-fire. Kabul and some other cities in Afghanistan have been the venue of intense internecine fighting in the past few days.
