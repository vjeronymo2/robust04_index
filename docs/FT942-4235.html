940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Lyons Irish down at IPounds 8.85m Lyons Irish Holdings, the tea and coffee wholesaler ultimately owned by Allied-Lyons, suffered a fall in pre-tax profits from IPounds 9.43m to IPounds 8.85m, or Pounds 8.63m, for the year to March 5. Interest income fell from IPounds 3.78m to IPounds 3m. Earnings per share declined by 2.55p to 24.86p while a final dividend of 6.95p makes a 10.4p (9.45p) total. Turnover of IPounds 25.9m compared with IPounds 24.9m.
