931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / Rousseau sold for Pounds 2.97m Henri Rousseau's portrait of the picture dealer Joseph Brummer, painted in 1909, sold for Pounds 2.97m at Christie's in London last night. The price, paid by a European dealer, was a record for Rousseau whose works rarely appear on the market. The auction of Impressionist and Modern Art made Pounds 14.28m and was 84 per cent sold by value, the highest sold percentage since the winter of 1989
