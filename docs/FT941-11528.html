940203 FT 03 FEB 94 / Telecommunications consortium under review The European Commission yesterday said it was reviewing a planned telecommunications consortium between Mannesmann, the main competitor to Deutsche Telekom in the German mobile phone market, RWE, the energy-based conglomerate, and Deutsche Bank, Germany's largest bank, Reuter reports from Brussels. The joint venture, which initially plans to offer fixed-wire services to corporate clients, needs Brussels approval under merger control regulations. The Commission has about a month to approve it or open an in-depth inquiry if it believes the alliance could hurt competition in any European Union market.
