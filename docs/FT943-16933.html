940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Brunner Net asset value per share of Brunner Investment Trust stood at 259.1p at May 31. That compared with 221.2p at end-May 1993. Available revenue for the six months to end-May totalled Pounds 1.29m (Pounds 1.68m) and earnings emerged at 2.01p (2.63p). The interim dividend is lifted to 2.5p (2.4p).
