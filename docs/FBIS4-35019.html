BFN [Text] President Carlos Menem emphasized today that Argentina will not participate in a probable military intervention in Haiti outside UN resolutions. The Argentine president guaranteed that the government will ask Congress for authorization if the United Nations makes a decision for our country to send military forces to that Caribbean island where a coup overthrew a democratic president in 1991. President Carlos Menem also urged the United States to increase political and economic measures against Haiti's de facto government in order to restore the democratic system in that country.
