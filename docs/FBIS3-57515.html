Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report under the "Military Review" rubric] [Text] The Russian Federation minister of defense has issued a directive on re-forming the former Main Military-Political Directorate, and over 150 officers and generals will have to look for a new job. There will now be no more deputy commanders specifically for educational work above corps and division level. This work has been given to deputy commanders. The sorest point in the Russian Army -- discipline -- will be the concern of chiefs of organs for troops' conditions and service.
