940823 FT 23 AUG 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports, there was again good general demand. Brightest and good medium East Africans sold well at about firm rates. Medium Kenyas were a strong feature and prices often advanced 2 to 3 pence. Plainer descriptions were irregularly easier. Brighter Ceylons met keen competition at much dearer rates. Plainer types were about steady. Brighter Kenyas gained several pence while plainer Ugandas were neglected. Quotations: best available 166p/kg. nom, good 150p/kg., good medium 140p/kg., medium 122p/kg., low medium 80p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 166p for a Kenya pf. 1.
