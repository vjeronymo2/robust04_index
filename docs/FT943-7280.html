940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / Observer: Chequered history The good news is that the Russian government has finally decided to cough up for all the loot appropriated by the state in political repressions since the 1917 Bolshevik revolution. But the maximum compensation will be no more than 100 times the current minimum Russian monthly wage - totalling 2.05m roubles - or the princely sum of Dollars 953. Romanovs of the world unite; you have nothing to lose but your claims.
