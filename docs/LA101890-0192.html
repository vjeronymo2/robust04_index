October 18, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: SAN MARINO;

SCHOOL SPEED ZONES URGED

The City Council has asked the California Department of Transportation to approve "school speed zones" for five schools -- four public and one private -- along Huntington Drive.

If approved by Caltrans, the speed limit would be reduced from 40 m.p.h. to 25 m.p.h. while schools are in session.
