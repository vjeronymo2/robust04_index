930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Acquisition for Bromsgrove Inds Bromsgrove Industries, the Birmingham-based specialist engineer, has acquired Cinpres from Jordan Group for 2.5m new shares. Cinpres, based in Staffordshire, makes and licences specialist patented gas injection control systems to more than 100 licensees worldwide. For the year to March 31 its pre-tax profits were Pounds 806,000 on sales of Pounds 2.02m, some 90 per cent of which were overseas. Net assets, excluding intangible patent assets, were Pounds 1,000, and at acquisition Cinpres had cash in hand of Pounds 223,000 and no borrowings. The vendor has undertaken not to sell the shares in the market for at least one year.
