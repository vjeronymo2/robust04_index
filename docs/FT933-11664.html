930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Clayform placing gets approval Shareholders and warrantholders of Clayform Properties have agreed the placing and open offer to raise Pounds 27.1m and the name change to Development Securities. The company will be placing 80.3m shares at 35p with a 2-for-1 clawback. Proceeds will be used to repair the balance sheet.
