940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / International Company News: Export sales at Audi rise 14% Audi, owned by Volkswagen, said its sales to export markets rose 14 per cent in the first six months of 1994, to 114,000 units, Reuter reports from Frankfurt. 'Sales on principal European markets totalled some 84,000 cars, almost 15 per cent more than in the first half of 1993,' the group said. Audi's largest individual market was Italy, where 25,000 cars were delivered.
