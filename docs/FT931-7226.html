930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Primadona assets show improvement Net asset value per share of Primadona stood at 207.14p at December 31, against 181p at the June year-end and 175.51p a year ago. Net revenue for the six months declined to Pounds 58,012 (Pounds 86,622) after tax of Pounds 22,178 (Pounds 32,513). The interim dividend is maintained at 2p, despite a fall in earnings from 1.92p to 1.29p.
