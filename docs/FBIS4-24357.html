BFN [Text] The federal office of the Union of Independent Democrats [UDI] at Matonge in the Kalamu Area was the target of a terrorist attack in the night of 24 June. A locally made bomb set in the building exploded. There was no loss of human lives but property damage has been reported. This was disclosed by Mr. (Tambo Mwamba), UDI national chairman. He was speaking this morning at a news briefing he held before reporters of state-owned and private media organs. The speaker said this is the fourth time his party has been the victim of acts of violence. He observed that his party has sufficient material means to react, but in a democracy people must express views and discuss or advance convincing arguments. It is not advisable to resort to violence in the event of disagreement on a viewpoint, UDI Chairman (Tambo Mwamba) concluded, adding that he is looking forward to the findings of the inquiry on the bomb attack [words indistinct].
