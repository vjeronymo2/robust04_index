[Docket No. ER94&hyph;204&hyph;000] Pennsylvania Power & Light Co.; Notice of Filing June 30, 1994. Take notice that on June 24, 1994, Pennsylvania Power Light Company (PP&L), tendered for filing with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission supplemental material relating to the above docket. Any person desiring to be heard or to protest said filing should file a motion to intervene or protest with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, 825 North Capitol Street, N.E., Washington, D.C. 20426, in accordance with Rules 211 and 214 of the Commission's Rules of Practice and Procedure (18 CFR 385.211 and 18 CFR 385.214). All such motions or protests should be filed on or before July 11, 1994. Protests will be considered by the Commission in determining the appropriate action to be taken, but will not serve to make protestants parties to the proceeding. Any person wishing to become a party must file a motion to intervene. Copies of this filing are on file with the Commission and are available for public inspection. Lois D. Cashell, Secretary.
