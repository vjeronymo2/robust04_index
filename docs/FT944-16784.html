941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / The Conservatives in Bournemouth: Gummer to brush aside 'Nimbys' Mr John Gummer, environ-ment secretary, yesterday promised to sweep aside unreasonable objections to new roads and housing and other developments needed to strengthen the economy. He told a fringe meeting that the Nimby (not in my backyard) attitudes of those who oppose any development in their area, whatever its merits, derived from an antagonistic view of growth and a misunderstanding of the nature of conservation.
