EFFECTIVE DATE: February 23, 1994. DEPARTMENT OF STATE [Public Notice 1949] Garnishment of Federal Employees Wages for Payment of Debt AGENCY: Department of State. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: The Department of State is giving notice that all requests for payments pursuant to court-ordered garnishments as authorized under section 9 of Public Law No. 103&hyph;94, Hatch Act Reform Amendments of 1993, for all employees of the Department of State shall be submitted to the Executive Director (L/EX), Office of the Legal Adviser, Department of State, room 5519A, 22nd and C Streets NW., Washington, DC 20520, (202) 647&hyph;8323. This is the same agent designated for service of garnishments under 5 CFR part 581. The Executive Director of the Office of the Legal Adviser, Department of State, is not authorized to accept requests for payments pursuant to court-ordered garnishments for employees of any other Federal agency, and any such requests submitted will be returned. EFFECTIVE DATE: February 23, 1994.
ADDRESSES: Comments regarding this notice should be sent to the Assistant Legal Adviser for Legislation and General Management (L/LM), Office of the Legal Adviser, Department of State, room 5425, 22nd and C Streets NW., Washington, DC 20520.
FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Ms. Sheila McCoy at (202) 647&hyph;7359. SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: Congress has authorized the garnishment of Federal civilian employees' wages for debts pursuant to section 9 of Public Law 103&hyph;94, Hatch Act Reform Amendments of 1993. The applications and orders and legal issues related thereto will be reviewed by the Office of the Legal Adviser. Dated: February 9, 1994.
