Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] A news conference about privatization took place in Prague today. Jitka Kucerova has more details. [Kucerova] According to Roman Ceska, first deputy minister for the administration and privatization of the national property, 90 percent of the property earmarked for privatization has already undergone the approval procedure at the ministry and has been handed over to the National Property Fund. Revenues from both small- and large-scale privatization are estimated at 54 billion korunas [Kc] so far. Mr. Ceska also said the small scale privatization was completed at the end of 1993. Any property remaining will be privatized by large-scale privatization methods. [passage omitted]
