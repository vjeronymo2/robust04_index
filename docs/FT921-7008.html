920225 FT 25 FEB 92 / Nationalists claim victory MALTA'S ruling Nationalist Party of Prime Minister Eddie Fenech Adami yesterday declared itself the winner of the country's general election, Reuter reports. A government spokesman said the Nationalists won 51 per cent of Saturday's vote against 47 per cent for the Labour party, giving them an expected three-seat majority. Full results are expected today. Previously Mr Fenech Adami had a single-seat majority.
