BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Datuk Abdullah Ahmad Badawi said Malaysia is not ready to establish diplomatic relations with Israel, although the latter wants to do so. This was decided by the Cabinet at its last meeting. He said the prime minister, Datuk Sri Dr. Mahathir Mohamed, mentioned Israel's request but the Cabinet wants to wait and see how sincere Israel is in adhering to the peace agreement it signed with the Palestine Liberation Organization before making a decision. Datuk Abdullah told reporters this at a diplomatic corps night organized by the National Press Club in Kuala Lumpur.
