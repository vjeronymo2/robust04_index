Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov signed a decree Wednesday night [23 March], according to which Army service in the Crimea will be confined to this peninsula. By the document, the Crimean Army Commander Aleksandr Volkov is instructed to carry out the drafting this spring of young men born in 1976. He has reportedly been trying to get in touch with Meshkov to discuss the matter. "I do not obey the Crimean President Meshkov and will comply with instructions from the Ukrainian president," he stressed and added that the spring draft would tally with earlier planned targets.
