BFN [Excerpt] A Canadian peacekeeper was killed in Croatia today [21st June] and two others were wounded. Thirty-one year old Corporal Mark Robert Isfield of Chillawack, British Columbia, died when a landmine exploded under his armored vehicle. Thirty-one year old Sergeant Paul Macmillan of Calgary, Alberta, and 35-year old Sergeant Gregory Paul James, also of Chillawack, were injured in the explosion. [passage omitted]
