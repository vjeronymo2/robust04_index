920514 FT 14 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Guinness expands in South Africa Guinness has established a subsidiary in South Africa to expand its distribution in the continent's biggest beer market. From later this year Guinness stout will be brewed and distributed under licence by South African Breweries. For almost two years, limited distribution in South Africa has been handled through Namibia Breweries, which brews and distributes the product in a number of African markets. Mr Alfons Walser, managing director of the new subsidiary, said yesterday: 'We believe the timing is right for us now to become directly involved in the marketing of the Guinness brand in South Africa.'
