Language: Finnish Article Type:CSO [Unattributed article: "Unemployment of Local Government Employees Rose 55 Percent"] [Text] The number of local government employees receiving unemployment benefits rose 55 percent during the year. The unemployment fund of the local government employees' trade union KTV paid unemployment benefits to over 77,000 of its members. The union has about 230,000 members in all. The KTV fund paid about 1.5 billion markkas in benefits last year. Sixty percent of the recipients were completely unemployed. Benefits were paid for 139 days on the average. Women made up 72 percent of the benefit recipients. Nearly 3,000 local government employees lost benefits tied to earnings because their unemployment lasted over 500 days.
