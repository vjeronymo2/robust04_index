BFN [Text] Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri returned to Beirut from Damascus at 2030 today after holding more than three hours of talks with Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad. Before his departure from Damascus, Prime Minister al-Hariri said: It is natural for us to visit President al-Asad at every phase of our deadlines and to consult with him on matters of interest to the two brotherly countries. He added: Syria takes great credit for Lebanon's stability, a credit that is recognized by every Lebanese who is interested in national detente. Al-Hariri added: My meeting with President al-Asad today was to assert these feelings and to thank him for his initiatives for Lebanon. It was also an opportunity to review the march toward stability in Lebanon and to coordinate the two countries' stances on the peace process.
