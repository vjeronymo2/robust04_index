930316 FT 16 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: 14% rise expected in NHS drugs bill THE NATIONAL Health Service drugs bill is expected to increase by more than 14 per cent this financial year, Dr Brian Mawhinney, health minister, said yesterday. Such a level of increase could not be sustained, he said. The drugs bill accounted for about 10 per cent of NHS expenditure and it was unacceptable to 'let it run free'.
