920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG remained nervous, although the gold index recovered slightly from an intraday low of 845 to close down 33 at 857 as some overseas investors returned to the market. The overall index fell 36 to 3,049, off a 12 month low of 3,038, and industrials shed 37 to 3,991. Vaal Reefs lost R5 to R118.
