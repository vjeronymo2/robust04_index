941203 FT 03 DEC 94 / UK Company News: B Elliott expands B Elliott, the specialist engineer, is buying Flight Link Control and Crane Control Systems for a maximum Pounds 1.1m. In addition, it is contributing Pounds 600,000 to a joint venture with Ohta Press of Japan to manufacture its range for the European market. It is also setting up a 55 per cent joint venture in China to make its measurement and digital read-out systems.
