930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / US bank expected to invest in Britain A LARGE US BANK is expected to lead a fresh wave of US inward investment projects in the UK which together could create more than 2,000 jobs, Mr David Hunt, employment secretary, said yesterday. Mr Hunt is touring the US looking at training schemes and talking to companies about investment in the UK. He said that Britain's 'opt-out' from the social chapter of the Maastricht treaty was contributing to 'record' investment from US companies.
