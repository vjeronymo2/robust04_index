920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / Heads roll over Escobar escape The head of Colombia's air force was sacked yesterday for being too slow in flying in troops to search for escaped cocaine boss Pablo Escobar, AP reports from Medelln. The prison governor was sacked too, as was the commander of troops at the prison who refused to enter the jail with three officials who were taken hostage before Escobar escaped.
