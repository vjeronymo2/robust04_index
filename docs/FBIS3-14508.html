Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The FLN [National Liberation Front] has sent a message to Lamine Zeroual, the head of state, in which it says that it rules out participation in any nominal dialogue, but remains committed to serious and deep dialogue with those who represent the authority of the state and all effective political forces. The FLN said that the invitation of parties that emerged through the latest elections, regardless of differences, to a joint and open meeting was pressing and urgent. This was an excerpt of the message addressed by the FLN to the head of state.
