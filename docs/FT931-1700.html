930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Part sale of project boosts Ex-Lands Ex-Lands, which is involved in a number of projects in Europe for the construction and development of golf courses in conjunction with Mr Mark McCormack's International Management Group, recorded a pre-tax profit of Pounds 822,000 for the six months to December 31, against Pounds 33,000. Operating losses from continuing operations was up from Pounds 567,000 to Pounds 768,000 and administrative expenses from Pounds 891,000 to Pounds 1.12m, but included was a profit of Pounds 1.03m relating to the part sale of the group's interest in the Vichy project to Ex-Lands Uno Rilldale, a new joint venture company. Mr David Marshall, chairman, pointed out that adjusted net assets, after incorporating the unrealised valuation surpluses on projects at June 30 1992, amounted to about Pounds 31m or 45p per share. Earnings were 1.19p (0.05p) per share.
