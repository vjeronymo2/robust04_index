March 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

LOUD BUT DECENT

Sure, the writing on shows such as "Growing Pains" and "Who's the Boss?" hovers between mediocre and insufficient ("Tales of Two Sitcoms," Feb. 26). But if I had kids, I wouldn't mind them spending one hour per week with a couple of "loud, dumb relatives" if it meant lessons in societal values and human decency.

JOE DUNGAN

Van Nuys
