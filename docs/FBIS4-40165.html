BFN [Text] The Russian troops' peacekeeping operation in the zone of the Georgian-Abkhazian conflict has begun. As INTERFAX reports, this morning sappers of the Russian 345th Airborne Regiment started clearning mines from territory in Abkhazia's Galskiy [Gali] Rayon. Colonel General Georgiy Kondratyev, Russian deputy defense minister who arrived in Sukhumi on Wednesday, said the direct introduction of Russian forces on the lines of contact of the combatant sides along the Inguri River will be effected in the perdiod from 16 to 21 June after the mines have been cleared from the territory. He said the batallion of the 345th Airborne Regiment, which regiment is stationed at Gudauta, would act as disengagement forces at the initial stage. In this same period, Kondratyev noted, the combatant sides should start pulling back their armed formations 12 km from the river.
