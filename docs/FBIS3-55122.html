Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] Your problems are also our problems: With these words, Danish Defense Minister Hans Haekkerup today ended his lecture at the Latvian National Defense Academy. He also ends his visit to Latvia today. In the lecture at the National Defense Academy, the Danish defense minister was explaining resolutions that will probably be passed at the summit meeting of the NATO member states in Brussels on 10 January. At the end of his lecture, Haekkerup promised that Denmark would also be granting to Latvia all kinds of assistance in the future. Haekkerup also held a news conference this morning about his visit to Kurzeme province yesterday. He visited the Russian military facility--the Skrunda radar station and the 45th Kuldiga battalion of the Latvian Home Guard. Haekkerup stressed that the radar station should not be left in Latvia and that the dismantling of the radar should be agreed on at Latvian-Russian talks. Haekkerup also positively assessed the Latvian Home Guard.
