August 15, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The United States overcame a 13-point deficit and injuries to Kenny Anderson and Billy Owens to beat Australia, 79-78, in the World Basketball Championships at Buenos Aires.

The U.S. clinched a semifinal berth when Puerto Rico beat Argentina, 92-76. The United States and Puerto Rico meet today for the top seeding spot.

In another game, Yugoslavia routed the Soviet Union, 100-77. Vlade Divac of the Lakers had eight points.
