930806 FT 06 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Tory popularity rating falls to 23% The Conservative party's popularity rating has slumped to 23 per cent, a level last reached in December 1981, according to a Gallup poll published in today's Daily Telegraph. The survey puts the Tories in third place on 23 per cent, behind the Liberal Democrats on 27 per cent and Labour on 44 1/2 per cent.
