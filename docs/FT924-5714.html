921125 FT 25 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Costs attack boost Guardian to Pounds 14.7m Mainly as a result of progress made in reducing the cost base, The Guardian and Manchester Evening News doubled its half time pre-tax profit. From turnover Pounds 1.67m ahead at Pounds 94.2m, the six months ended September 26 1992 produced a profit of Pounds 14.7m, against Pounds 7m last time. Results for the rest of the year were expected to reflect a continuation of the severe recessionary conditions.
