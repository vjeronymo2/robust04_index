BFN [Text] Beijing, 28 Apr (XINHUA) -- To further arouse the enthusiasm of people of all nationalities throughout the country in promoting reform, opening up, and the construction of socialism for better fulfillment of the various tasks forwarded by the 14th CPC National Congress, the State Council has decided to hold a rally on the eve of "1 May," international Labor Day, 1995 to commend national model and advanced workers who have made outstanding contributions in various fields and fronts amid reform and construction. The State Council issued a circular to this effect today to the people's governments of various provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government; and various ministries, commissions, and organs directly under the State Council. According to the State Council circular, 3,000 national model and advanced workers will be commended, covering workers, peasants, professionals, technicians, management personnel, government workers, and others who have made outstanding contributions toward reform, opening up, economic construction, industrial and agricultural production, and various social undertakings since 1989. The circular stated that the State Council has decided to form the National Model and Advanced Workers Recognition Rally Organizing Committee to lead preparations for the occasion, with Luo Gan, state councillor and secretary general of the State Council, as chairman and the following as vice chairmen: Zhang Dinghua, vice president of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions and first secretary of its secretariat; Song Defu, personnel minister; Li Boyong, labor minister; Wang Zhongyu, minister in charge of the State Economic and Trade Commission; Zhu Kaixuan, minister in charge of the State Education Commission; Zhu Lilan, vice minister in charge of the State Science and Technology Commission; Liu Jiang, agriculture minister; Hu Guangbao, deputy director of the CPC Central Committee General Office; and Li Shuwen, deputy secretary general of the State Council. An organizing committee office will be set up with Li Shuwen, deputy secretary general of the State Council, serving concurrently as director. The State Council circular pointed out that selecting and commending national model and advanced workers will be a major event in the political lives of the Chinese people of all nationalities. To ensure success, the people's governments of various provinces, autonomous regions, and municipalities directly under the central government as well as relevant departments must strengthen leadership and be conscientious in organizing the recognition rally. Grass-root level and front lines of economic construction will be the main focus for selecting national model workers and advanced work personnel. Advanced figures known for their persistence in upholding the party's basic line, in consistently working hard without being upset by criticism, and in promoting reform with new creations and outstanding deeds should be selected. Minority women and people should be selected according to fixed proportions. Through selection activities, we should widely publicize the exemplary deeds and lofty spirit of advanced individuals from various trades and professions, in a bid to mobilize the broad masses to take them as example when striving for new success in reform, opening up, and modernization drive. Meanwhile, the National Model and Advanced Workers Recognition Rally Organizing Committee held its first meeting in the afternoon of 27 April, presided over by its chairman, Luo Gan.
