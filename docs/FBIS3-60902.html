Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Unal Erkan, state of emergency region governor, has declared that 1,700 terrorists were killed and 540 were captured alive during operations conducted this year. Erkan added that 240 organization members surrendered to the security forces. In a statement to TRT in Diyarbakir, Erkan announced that 17,000 persons were caught and referred to judicial authorities during operations against the collaborators of the terrorist organization in populated areas. Noting that a total of 1,200 citizens, 135 of them women and 151 children, were killed in the organization's attacks against innocent and defenseless people, the regional governor remarked that 1,564 others were injured in these incidents. According to Erkan, this year security forces seized 4,000 weapons, 3,000 of them rifles, approximately 500,000 rounds of ammunition of various types and calibers, 164 rocket launchers, 1,500 rockets, 2,000 hand grenades, as well as large quantities of explosives, antiaircraft ammunition, and mortars.
