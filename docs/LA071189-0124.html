July 11, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

Tony Paradiso has been named vice president of marketing for Kofax Image Products Inc., Irvine. Earlier this year, Paradiso was a consultant for the company. He was previously director of marketing at AST Research and has also held marketing management positions at Emulex Corp., Contel/CADO Business Systems Corp. and MSI Data Corp. Kofax makes document imaging products for personal computers.
