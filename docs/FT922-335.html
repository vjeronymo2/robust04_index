920629 FT 29 JUN 92 / Arts: Lugano jazz The southern Swiss town of Lugano holds two jazz festivals in the summer, both free to the public. First is the 14th Estival Jazz on July 1 to 3, followed by the fourth Lugano Blues to Bop Festival on August 28 to 30. Both events will be held in the town's Piazza Riforma. Details from the Lugano Tourist Office, Riva Albertolli 5, CH-6901, Lugano.
