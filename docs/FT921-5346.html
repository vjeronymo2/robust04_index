920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / Arts: Jazz at Canary Wharf Olympia & York's Arts & Events programme at Canary Wharf, launched in 1991, this year includes free jazz concerts in the Cabot Hall. Highlight of them is the appearance by American guitarist John Scofield in the only British date (March 24) of his quartet's European tour. The series, which began last month, continues with Courtney Pine on March 12, Danny Thompson's Whatever on March 19, and Django Bates' Delightful Precipice April 2.
