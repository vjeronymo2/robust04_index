Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Latest Reorganization"] [Text] We learned from informed sources that the government has prepared a draft decree "On the Functioning of the Russian Federation Agro-Industrial Complex in 1994," whereby the Ministry of Agriculture and Food is to become the central federal executive organ in charge of agriculture and the agro-industrial complex. The reorganized ministry is assigned the functions of forming state food and raw material resources of grain and oil-seed crop seeds, and new departments for the food and processing industry, the mixed feed industry, and for procurement and produce shipment are to be set up within the ministry. What is more, there are plans to set up a number of sector state, state/cooperative, and state/joint-stock formations under the ministry with shared participation, delegating to these formations certain state management functions with regard to fundamental specialized sectors.
