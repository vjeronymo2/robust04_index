BFN [Excerpt] President 'Arafat continued his consultations this morning in Tunis within the PLO and with Palestinian figures from the occupied territories. These consultations are aimed at forming the transitional government of the self-rule in Gaza and Jericho. An official Palestinian source told AFP that this government will be installed in Jericho on 25 May in accordance with the final agreement on self-rule. [passage omitted]
