Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] 'Abd-al-Rahim Malluh, PLO Executive Committee member and Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine [PFLP] Political Bureau member, has denied the rumor that tried to link the movie theater bombings [in Jordan] to the search of PFLP Secretary General George Habash's car, in which, the rumor alleges, weapons and silences were found. In a statement to SHIHAN, Malluh said such rumors seek to harm Palestinian-Jordanian relations and to revive the past. Malluh said that tendentious parties are trying to link the bombings to the Palestinian factions in general to cast doubts on Palestinian-Jordanian relations, which are now being established on strong foundations. He said Dr. Habash came to Jordan on 23 February on a private visit and was not searched. He was treated with all the respect befitting a pan-Arab struggler, Malluh added.
