BFN [Text] Caracas, 14 Apr (DPA)--The Venezuelan Senate today approved the so-called Special Powers Act, granting special powers to President Rafael Caldera to decree economic and financial measures. This law will give Caldera special powers to legislate, a function that basically belongs to the Congress. The Special Powers Act was proposed to implement legislative initiatives pertaining to income tax, general sales and excise taxes, tax reform, bank debit tax, the Stamp Tax Law, and the Concessions Law.
