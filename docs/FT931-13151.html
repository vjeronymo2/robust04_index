930126 FT 26 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Parkland Textile in Pounds 0.6m disposal Parkland Textile (Holdings), the Bradford-based woollen yarn and worsted cloth manufacturer, has disposed of its garment marketing and overseas manufacturing operation to Albion for Pounds 631,453. The consideration comprises a cash payment of Pounds 131,435 and a secured loan note of Pounds 500,000. The sale completes Parkland's exit from garment manufacture.
