921113 FT 13 NOV 92 / World News in Brief: Journalists fined Associated Newspapers, publisher of the Mail on Sunday, was fined Pounds 30,000 in the High Court for contempt after publishing details of the secret deliberations of the Blue Arrow fraud trial jury. The newspaper's former editor, Stewart Steven, was fined Pounds 20,000 and its City editor, Clive Wolman, Pounds 10,000. The journalists said they would appeal.
