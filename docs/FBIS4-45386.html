U.S. NC0806130694 Beirut Voice of Lebanon in Arabic 1115 GMT 8 Jun 94 NC0806130694 Beirut Voice of Lebanon Arabic BFN [Text] This morning customs officials at Beirut Airport foiled an attempt to smuggle drugs abroad. A man named Agub Atashian, born in Allepo in 1941, was planning to travel to the United States via Paris with 1.3 kg of heroin hidden inside his shoe sole and plastic belt.
