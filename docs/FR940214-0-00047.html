(c) *␣*␣* (1) Interim financing. When the amount of the loan exceeds $50,000, the applicant may obtain interim financing from commercial or public sources for the construction period if it can be obtained at reasonable interest rates, fees, and terms, and in the best financial interests of the Government. Interim financing will be obtained to preclude the necessity for multiple advances of FmHA funds. The interim lender must be authorized to operate in the State in which the project will be located and must have an established record of providing financing to entities other than FmHA-financed projects. Since the interim lender is responsible for inspecting construction along with FmHA, the borrowing entity (including any of its identity of interest entities) cannot provide interim financing to its own project.
