BFN [Text] His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, the president, this morning telephoned his brother Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak. This telephone call comes within the framework of continuous consultations and praiseworthy efforts to contain the crisis in Yemen. During their conversation, the two presidents stressed the need to continue intensified efforts to immediately stop the fighting and avoid bloodshed among the sons of the one country as well as the need to return to true brotherly dialogue in order to reach a political solution to the Yemeni crisis based on the vision of the Yemeni leadership.
