June 20, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Dinah Shore, who headlined a popular variety show on NBC from 1956-63, followed by a daytime magazine series on that network and then a syndicated talk show, is heading back to TV land. She will host "A Conversation With Dinah," a new daytime talk show, this fall on the Nashville cable network. Although TNN is based in Nashville, Shore will do the show from the KCET studios here. ALEENE MacMINN
