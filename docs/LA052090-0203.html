May 20, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

HOME BUYERS FAIR;

MAKING THE DREAM COME TRUE...

Purchasing your first home, even in the current buyer's market, is still the adventure of a lifetime.

It can be fun and exciting if you're well-prepared; it can be an expensive disaster if you're not.

Come hear from dozens of experts on every phase of home buying, and ask them your questions, at Saturday's Home Buyers Fair.

And if you've become frustrated trying to sell your home to move up, participate in two-hour, small-group discussions with real estate experts on better ways to market your home.

DATE: Saturday, May 26.

TIME: 9:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m.

PLACE: Anaheim Convention Center.
