DIGEST: HARVARD'S MORAN RECEIVES HONOR FOR WATER POLO

David Moran, a Harvard High junior, has been named to an All-American water polo team sponsored by a national insurance company.

Last season Moran, a striker, scored 15 goals and was an All-Southern Section selection for Harvard, the Southern Section 2-A Division champion.
