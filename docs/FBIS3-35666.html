Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow January 29 TASS -- A Georgian high-ranking official has reported the failure of the four-party agreement on the return of refugees and displaced persons to Abkhazia. The announcement was made by Sandro Kavsadze, Georgian leader Eduard Shevardnadze's envoy, after the talks with Abkhazia held at the Russian Foreign Ministry and mediated by a representative of the U.N. High Commissioner for Refugees on Friday. "The Georgian side categorically refuses to differentiate between the refugees as to who took part in the war and who did not because the bloody conflict has involved almost the entire population, including Georgians, Abkhazians and other nations," Kavsadze said.
