January 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: TEMPLE CITY;

CITY MANAGER TO RETIRE

City Manager Karl Koski has announced he will retire June 30 from a public service career that spans three decades.

Koski, 59, joined the city staff as an administrative assistant in 1961, a year after incorporation. He said he decided to retire to spend time with his family, travel and enjoy the outdoors.

The City Council probably will begin with an in-house search for a new city manager, Koski said.
