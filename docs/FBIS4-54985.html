BFN [Text] The Croatian delegation headed by Mate Granic, deputy prime minister and foreign minister, left for Vienna today for the resumption of the talks on the implementation of the Washington agreement. The talks at the U.S. Embassy will also be attended by the Bosnian Muslim delegation headed by Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic and a delegation of Bosnia-Herzegovina Croats headed by Kresimir Zubak, president of the presidential council of the Croat Republic of Herzeg-Bosna. Prior to his departure for Vienna, Minister Granic said he hoped agreement would be reached on the further implementation of the Washington agreement. Before leaving for the talks, Minister Granic met with Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati in Zagreb this morning. The talks centered on Croatian-Iranian relations and the situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina. After a brief visit to Zagreb, Minister Velayati will leave for Sarajevo where he will meet Bosnia-Herzegovina officials and attend the opening of the Iranian Embassy.
