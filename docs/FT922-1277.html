920623 FT 23 JUN 92 / UK Company News: S Wales Electricity moves into gas South Wales Electricity is entering the gas market via the formation of a new subsidiary, South Wales Energy, which has signed an agreement with the gas marketing arm of Amerada Hess Gas, a large North Sea oil and gas producer. Mr David Jones, managing director of SWE, said the new company was created to take advantage 'of the opportunities which now arise from the progressive opening of the gas market to competition.' Initial marketing and supplies will be focused on commercial and industrial customers in south Wales and the gas will be transported through existing British Gas pipelines.
