BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz May 13 TASS -- "The multinational people of the North Ossetian Republic cannot imagine its present and future outside Russia. Two hundred and twenty years ago our wise ancestors decided that their people will live within Russia," says an appeal to the North Ossetian people signed today by North Ossetian President Akhsarbek Galazov and Parliament Speaker Yuriy Biragov on behalf of the North Ossetian Government and the Supreme Soviet. The appeal lays down the basis to implement the Civic Accord Agreement signed in Moscow on April 28, 1994. "Today we call on our compatriots to work together to achieve comprehensive accord and stop devastating processes in our multinational society, put Ossetia on the path of peace and creation, consolidation of unity and might of great Russia," says the document. The president told the signing ceremony that the appeal is the first step to implement the Civic Accord idea in the multinational republic.
