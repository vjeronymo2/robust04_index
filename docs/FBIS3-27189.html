Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 18 (XINHUA) -- Qiao Shi, chairman of the Standing Committee of the Chinese National People's Congress (NPC), had a cordial talk here this evening with Giovanni Spatolini, president of the Italian Senate. They agreed that in recent years, the all-round relations between China and Italy had been satisfactory. Qiao said China is willing to continuously expand her economic cooperation with Italy and that China welcomes more Italian entrepreneurs to come and invest here. He also voiced the hope that the bilateral ties would gain further development on the existing basis. Spatolini praised China for its role in maintaining world peace and said that many Italian entrepreneurs want to invest in China. They expressed concern over the latest situation of Bosnia-Herzegovina and voiced the hope that the United Nations would help settle the conflict by peaceful means. They also exchanged views on other regional and international issues of common interest. Spatolini visited China nine years ago when he was the Italian defense minister. Qiao today extended a warm welcome to Spatolini, saying that the visit would help enhance the mutual understanding and friendship between the two countries. Wu Jieping, vice-chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, took part in the meeting. Afterwards, Qiao hosted a dinner in honor of Spatolini, who arrived here this morning as guest of the NPC Standing Committee.
