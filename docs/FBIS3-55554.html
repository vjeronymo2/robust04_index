Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Representatives of the State Duma's influential factions intend to demand a detailed report on government spending on repairing the White House. Deputies of a major parliamentary faction told Interfax that the rubles and hard-currency reserved for buying building materials, equipment and furniture had been mishandled. According to some estimates, spending on the restoration and construction work alone amounts to $75 Mn-$85 Mn. The work is done round the clock by Russia builders and their Turkish colleagues from the companies Enka and Gama.
