910426 FT 26 APR 91 / International Company News: Eaton reveals Dollars 12m deficit EATON Corporation, the Cleveland-based engineering group, reported a net loss of Dollars 12m for the first three months of 1991, down from a Dollars 54m profit in the period a year earlier, writes Nikki Tait in New York. Eaton, which sells products to the automotive, industrial and defence industries, revealed that sales for the first quarter slumped to Dollars 818m from Dollars 942m. There was a Dollars 39m restructuring charge; without this, Eaton would have made a Dollars 13m profit.
