BFN [Text] Ukraine considers the Kashmir issue as purely an internal problem of India. This was stated by the visiting Ukraine foreign minister, Mr. Anatoliy Zlenko, in New Delhi yesterday. Talking to newspersons, he said his country is opposed to internationalization of the issue. Mr. Zlenko said Ukraine is prepared to work with India in converting the military industrial complexes in the former Soviet state into nonmilitary units. Over 45 percent of the military units of the former Soviet Union are located in Ukraine.
