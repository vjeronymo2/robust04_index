BFN [TA SR report: "Next Time Specifically"] [Text] Bratislava -- Provisions in the sphere of arms, supplies of materials and equipment, and services for the armed forces were the central issues discussed by delegations of the Slovak and Ukrainian Ministries of Defense in Bratislava yesterday. Possibilities of cooperation between military repair plants were also discussed. As regards technical provisions, the Slovak side expressed interest in, for example, purchasing spare parts for tanks, infantry combat vehicles, and radar systems. The Slovak Army also needs special fuel for missiles, lubricants for the air force, and other special supplies that used to be manufactured in the states of the former USSR. The two sides agreed to work out lists of offers that they will reassess under their own conditions. At their next meeting, they will discuss specific requirements and proposals on the basis of the two armies' potentials.
