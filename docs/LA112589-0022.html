November 25, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

USC TOUCHDOWN CONJURES UP BITTER MEMORIES

In 1969, to Dickerson. In 1987, to Affholter. In 1989, to Wellman. Three times Bruin fans have endured "judgment calls" in the corner of a Coliseum end zone, which decided the outcome of games to the benefit of USC. Is instant replay in college football what it would take for UCLA to get a call against USC?

TONY BARRETO, Culver City
