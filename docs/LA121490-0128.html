CORONA DEL MAR'S CARLSEN ALL-AMERICAN IN VOLLEYBALL

Lara Carlsen of Corona del Mar High School, The Times' Orange County girls' volleyball player of the year, has been named to Volleyball Monthly magazine's All-American first team, the magazine announced Thursday.

Carlsen, a 5-foot-11 senior middle blocker, averaged 14 kills a match and led the Sea Kings to the State Division I and Southern Section 5-A championships. She has made an oral commitment to play at USC next season.

La Habra's Missy Clements, a 5-11 senior middle blocker, was named to the magazine's All-American second team. Clements, who is undecided on a college, led the Highlanders to their second consecutive State Division II and Southern Section 3-A titles this season.

Carlsen and Clements also were among several Orange County players named to the all-Southern Section teams, which were released Thursday.
