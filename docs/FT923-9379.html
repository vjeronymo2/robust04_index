920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Admiral makes 18% advance ADMIRAL, a Camberley-based computing services company, made sound progress in the first half of 1992 despite tough trading conditions. Pre-tax profits were 18 per cent ahead at Pounds 1.96m while turnover advanced 17 per cent to Pounds 13.66m. Earnings rose to 11.6p (10.3p) and the interim dividend is 1.7p, (1.5p). The results included a contribution from Computer Business Consultants, which Admiral acquired recently, but Mr Clay Brendish, chairman, said the results represented essentially organic growth.
