941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Care UK recovers Care UK, the USM-quoted nursing home operator, reported pre-tax profits for the year to September 30 of Pounds 6.92m on turnover of Pounds 13.4m, including Pounds 3.57m from discontinued activities. The result compared with losses of Pounds 7.02m on turnover of Pounds 17.6m of which Pounds 10.9m was from discontinued businesses. Earnings per share were 2.19p (losses 4.76p). However, the company said that because of its restructuring in March the figures were not strictly comparable.
