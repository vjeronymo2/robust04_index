931110 FT 10 NOV 93 / People: Insurance moves John Sidwell, formerly group chief internal auditor, has been appointed finance director, ROYAL Life Holdings; he is replaced by David Lewington, who moves from Nationwide Building Society. Gary Richards, formerly group controller of Enskilda Corporate, has been appointed finance director, Royal Insurance Global. David Heather is appointed finance director of Royal Life Estates on the resignation of Ron Way.
