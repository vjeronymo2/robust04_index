BFN [Text] Russian Federation troops will not be brought into Azerbaijan, President Geydar Aliyev of Azerbaijan has declared. He denied opposition accusations that the country might lose its sovereignty. According to Aliyev, political parties should consult and interact with the official bodies instead of holding rallies and mass protest actions. The Azerbaijani leader reiterated that the authorities acted correctly in signing the Bishkek protocol on a cease-fire. Meanwhile, the situation in the conflict zone remains relatively calm. The Azerbaijani Defense Ministry reports only rare cases of shelling of its territory from Armenia. The headquarters of the Azerbaijani National Independence Party has been raided. The premises were blockaded by 200 policemen, who announced they intended to find weapons. However, the press service of the republican Interior Ministry refuses to comment on this report, saying it knows nothing about the incident.
