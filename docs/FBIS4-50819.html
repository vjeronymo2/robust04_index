BFN [Announcer-read report over video; from the "Xinjiang News" program] [Text] The Xinjiang Military District held a discussion meeting on building up the party committee leading groups of division-level and regiment-level units on the morning of 3 May. Fu Bingyao, (Zhou Lianjiang), and other leaders of the military district attended the meeting. Political Commissar Pan Zhaomin spoke on how to adapt to the new situation of army development and to constantly improve the overall qaulity of the leading group of division and regiment party committees. He said: The leading groups of division-level and regiment-level party committees should presistently deem it a task of prime importance to study Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics and his thought on army building in the new period. It is necessary to improve the political quality and overall ability of the leading groups of division and regiment party committees. At the same time, we should improve our leading style and working methods and provide more scientific and effective work quidance. We should improve the party's workstyle, promote ethical integrity, and improve the image of leading groups.
