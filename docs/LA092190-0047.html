September 21, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Thomas F. Bradac, artistic director of the financially troubled Grove Shakespeare Festival, got a bundle of good news Wednesday afternoon when his wife, Anne, gave birth to a 9 lb., 8 oz. daughter at Kaiser Permanente Medical Center in Anaheim. They've named her Caitlin. Bradac was called out of a meeting Tuesday morning about the Grove's cash problems to take his wife to the hospital.
