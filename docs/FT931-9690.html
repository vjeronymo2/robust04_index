930212 FT 12 FEB 93 / World Trade News: Indonesians in UK steel talks THE Indonesian company negotiating to buy plant and equipment from British Steel's closed Ravenscraig steelworks in Lanarkshire was yesterday named as PT Gunawan Dianjaya, a family-run steel producer, Andrew Baxter writes. According to the Herald newspaper in Glasgow, experts from Gunawan visited Ravenscraig recently, and a report is being studied at the company's headquarters in Surabaya.
