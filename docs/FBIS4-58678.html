BFN [Text] The parliamentary bloc of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] issued a statement in which it expressed its regret and resentment over the statement issued today by the House of Representatives which represented the secessionist congress and al-Islah blocs, and has no connection with the Constitution. The statement said that the House of Representatives is to be used to justify the war declared by 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, the motives of which hide the secessionist lenience [as heard] of the forces which rejected the convention document and the national dialogue, and instead declared the war by attacking a number of brigades in the northern governorates. The statement said that the declaration of the war by 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and his military dynasty, supported by the House of Representatives, is a disaster to our people. The statement declared the boycott of the meeting of the House of Representatives and suspended the membership until it rectifies its stand.
