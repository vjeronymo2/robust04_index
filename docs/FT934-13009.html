931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The pepper market suffered from lack of activity throughout the week as a consequence of which prices tended slightly easier, reports Man Producten. More offers of black pepper from brazil appeared in the market at slightly reduced prices. Other origins were not active at all. Black pepper for prompt delivery in Europe was valued between USDollars 1,600 and Dollars 1,675 a tonne, ex-warehouse. White pepper offers were scarce at unchanged prices of about Dollars 3,300 a tonne, cif, and Dollars 3,375-Dollars 3,400 for spot material. Demand was negligible.
