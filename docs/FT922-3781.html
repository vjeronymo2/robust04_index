920609 FT 09 JUN 92 / International Company News: Axa unit acquires life portfolio AXA Insurance, a unit of French insurance group Axa, said it had acquired the group life insurance portfolio of Federation de l'Age d'Or du Quebec, a senior citizens group, from the provisional liquidator of Les Cooperants, AP-DJ reports from Montreal. Les Cooperants was forced into liquidation by Quebec regulators earlier this year.
