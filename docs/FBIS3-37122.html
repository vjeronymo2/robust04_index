Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 14 Feb -- IBERIA reports that Georgian head of state E. Shevardnadze, regards Major General Joni Pirtskhalaishvili as one of the candidates for the post of defense minister. He was appointed Georgia's defense minister in Gamsakhurdia's government. E. Shevardnadze has recently invited J. Pirtskhalaishvili from Russia and had a private conversation with him. A second candidate for the post of defense minister is Maj-Gen V. Nadibaidze, currently first deputy commander in chief of the Russian troops in the Transcaucasus.
