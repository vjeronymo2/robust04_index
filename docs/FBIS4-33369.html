BFN [From the "NTC News" newscast] [Text] Colombian Foreign Minister Noemi Sanin de Rubio has characterized as biased and unfair the gruesome images shown by "NTC News" from the video in which Amnesty International denounces human rights violations in Colombia. The foreign minister said the situations presented in the video cannot be considered the result of a premeditated government policy. [Begin Sanin de Rubio recording] I cannot, under any circumstance, allow the government to be blamed for this violence, these murders, and this so-called social cleansing. I cannot agree with that, nor can I believe that everyone in the police force is corrupt. [end recording] The foreign minister said that the government's doors are open to visits from the international community and that she has issued specific instructions to Colombia's ambassadors to invite nongovernment human rights organizations to Colombia.
