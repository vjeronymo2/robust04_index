920109 FT 09 JAN 92 / Cuban capture CUBAN security forces have captured three armed 'counter-revolutionary terrorists' trained in the US, who had landed by dinghy on the northern Cuban coast to launch attacks, Reuter reports from Havana. The Interior Ministry stated yesterday that the three, all resident in Miami, were seized during the night of December 29 after they had landed at El Jucaro near Cardenas in Matanzas province, about 90 miles east of Havana.
