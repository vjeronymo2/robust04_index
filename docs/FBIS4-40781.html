BFN [Text] Vladikavkaz, June 13, RIA -- The president of the Assembly of Democratic Forces of the Northern Caucasus, Schmidt Dzoblayev, left Vladikavkaz for Narzan to meet the president of the Ingushetian republic, Ruslan Aushev. According to Dzoblayev, he will go to Groznyy tomorrow, in order to hold a preliminary meeting as the official representative of the Russian president, with the president of the Chechen republic, Dzhohar Dudayev. Dzoblayev noted that Ruslan Aushev may well fulfill an intermediary role for the preparation of negotiations between Russia and Chechnya.
