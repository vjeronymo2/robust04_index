BFN [Text] Fair play with Russia in the world weapons market would not hurt U.S. strategic interests, Sergey Yushenkov, chairman of the State Duma Defense Committee said today. He told INTERFAX that Washington's obstruction of Russian weapon export, as in the case of the failed sales of MiG-29 fighter planes to Venezuela, forced Russia to deal with undesirable partners. As a result, Yushenkov said, odious dictatorial regimes obtained weapons through third or fourth countries and created tension for the entire world community.
