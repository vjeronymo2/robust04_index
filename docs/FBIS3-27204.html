Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Report: "XINHUA Official Discloses Namelist of Over 200 District Affairs Advisers To Be Announced Early Next Month"] [Text] Zhang Xiting, director of the XINHUA Hong Kong Branch's office in the New Territories, disclosed that the New Territories will account for a certain proportion of the more than 200 district affairs advisers to be announced early next month. After attending the opening ceremony of the Tsuen Wan Basic Law Promotion Carnival, held yesterday, Zhang Xiting was asked by reporters about the proportion of district advisers from the New Territories and Tsuen Wan in the namelist to be announced next month. Zhang Xiting reiterated that the namelist will include figures from various strata and circles. The main task of the district advisers is to offer advice to the Chinese side on district affairs. On the same occasion, Cheng Kai-nam, secretary of the Democratic Alliance for Betterment of Hong Kong, told reporters that the alliance will discuss at its central standing committee meeting tomorrow the question of its members being invited as district affairs advisers. Cheng stressed that the duties of the district affairs advisers and third group of Hong Kong affairs advisers to be appointed next month should not involve administration of the current Hong Kong government, but they can offer advice to China on post-1997 affairs.
