930427 FT 27 APR 93 / UK Company News: Dickie shows first half recovery James Dickie, the USM-quoted manufacturer of engineering components reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 250,000 for the six months to February 28, against losses of Pounds 292.000. There was an exceptional profit of Pounds 68,000, against Pounds 194,000 loss, previously treated as an extraordinary. Turnover rose from Pounds 6.9m to Pounds 7.86m and earnings per share were 3.03p (losses 4.73p).
