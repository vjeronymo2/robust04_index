Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yefim Shvartsman] [Text] Simferopol March 28 TASS -- According to preliminary results, a majority of Crimeans who came to the polls on Sunday voted to support the policy of their president Yuriy Meshkov by saying "yes" at a plebiscite which he had initiated, TASS learned from reliable sources in the Crimean capital. Official results are expected to be announced Wednesday. The referendum included questions on whether the population favours relations with Ukraine on the basis of agreements, on dual citizenship on the peninsula and on providing the force of law to a number [of] Meshkov's decrees. Local observers note that the affirmative answer to the questions means support to the first Crimean president elected in January and to his anti-crisis programme.
