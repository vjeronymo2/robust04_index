920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / Construction Contracts: Further education facilities in Sheffield Two contracts totalling over Pounds 5m for further education facilities in Sheffield have been awarded to the northern construction division of MJ GLEESON GROUP. At the Pond Street campus of Sheffield City Polytechnic, Gleeson will undertake construction of a six-storey block incorporating a lecture theatre, and also refurbish a five-storey block, on behalf of Cormorant at a cost of Pounds 3.3m. At Regent Street, Gleeson is to build a Pounds 1.9m five-storey management building for Sheffield University.
