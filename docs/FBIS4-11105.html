BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on developments related to El Salvador presidential run-off elections scheduled for 24 April 1994. Armando Calderon Sol, Nationalist Republican Alliance, ARENA, candidate for president, said on 16 April that, if he comes out the winner in the elections, he will combat government corruption even more strongly than Alfredo Cristiani's administration. (San Salvador Canal Doce Television in Spanish 0300 GMT 16 Apr 94) Reacting to Ruben Zamora, candidate for president for a coalition of three leftist parties who said Calderon Sol's eventual election victory would be "very dangerous," the ARENA candidate on 19 April asserted that he will comply with the pending peace agreements because they "are a national commitment and must be complied with to consolidate a true state of law." (Paris AFP in Spanish 2316 GMT 19 Apr 94)
