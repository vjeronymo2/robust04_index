May 9, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

American Isuzu Motors Inc. has named John E. (Jack) Reilly chairman.

In addition, Reilly will serve as senior adviser to Isuzu Motors America and senior consultant to Isuzu Motors Ltd. (Japan). Reilly had previously held the position of senior vice president and general manager. E. F. (Fritz) Kern, a 28-year veteran of the automotive industry, succeeds Reilly.
