Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 29 Dec (XINHUA) -- A Decision of the National People's Congress Standing Committee on Interim Regulations for Value-Added Tax, Consumption Tax, Business Tax, and Other Taxes Applicable to Foreign-Funded Enterprises and Foreign Enterprises, adopted by the Fifth Session of the Eighth National People's Congress Standing Committee on 29 December 1993. The Fifth Session of the Eighth National People's Congress Standing Committee examined a bill submitted by the State Council concerning Interim Regulations for Value-Added Tax, Consumption Tax, Business Tax, and other Taxes Applicable to Foreign-Funded Enterprises and Foreign Enterprises. To unify the taxation system, fairly distribute tax burdens, improve China's investment environment, and meet the needs of establishing and developing socialist market economy, a decision is hereby made as follows: 1. Pending the enactment of relevant taxation laws, the interim regulations on value-added tax, the interim regulations on consumption tax, and the interim regulations on business tax promulgated by the State Council will be applicable to foreign- funded enterprises and foreign enterprises beginning on 1 January 1994. "The Regulations (Draft) of the PRC Governing the Unified Industrial and Commercial Taxes" adopted in principle by the 101st Session of the National People's Congress Standing Committee on 11 September 1958 and promulgated for trial implementation by the State Council on 13 September 1958 will be abolished on the same date [1 January 1994]. The value-added tax on offshore oil and natural gas extracted by Chinese-foreign joint ventures shall be levied in kind. The rate and collecting measures of such tax shall be separately stipulated by the State Council. 2. Foreign-funded enterprises, the establishment of which was approved before 31 December 1993, may have to bear additional tax burdens after they are required, in accordance with Item 1 of this decision, to pay value-added tax, consumption tax, and business tax. In this case, they may apply for reimbursement of the additional taxes paid within their approved business period, with the maximum period of five years; and their application is subject to the approval of the taxation authorities. If their business period is not specified, they may apply for reimbursement of the additional taxes paid within a period not exceeding five years; and their application is subject to the approval of the taxation authorities. The concrete measures shall be stipulated by the State Council. 3. In addition to value-added tax, consumption tax, and business tax, foreign-funded enterprises and foreign enterprises shall pay other taxes in accordance with relevant laws, if any; or in the absence of such laws, in accordance with the regulations of the State Council. Foreign-funded enterprises referred to in this decision denote Chinese-foreign joint ventures, Chinese-foreign cooperative enterprises, and wholly foreign-owned enterprises established within the boundaries of China. Foreign enterprises referred to in this decision denote foreign companies, enterprises, and other economic organizations which have established organizations or workshops within the boundaries of China to engage in production and business, as well as foreign companies, enterprises, and other economic organizations whose incomes originate from within the boundaries of China even though they have no organizations or workshops in China. This decision will become effective on the day of its promulgation.
