921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / International Company News: Cray Research cuts CRAY Research, the US supercomputer manufacturer, is to restructure its operations with the loss of about 650 jobs, writes Louise Kehoe in San Francisco. It will take a Dollars 40m fourth-quarter charge but expects to remain profitable for the year. It expects to save about Dollars 50m in 1993 as a result of the cost-cutting.
