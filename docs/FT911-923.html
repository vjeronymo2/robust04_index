910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / Appointments: Inchcape shipping services Mr Simon Morse is to become shipping services director for INCHCAPE when the present director, Mr Peter Bainbridge, retires at the end of the year. All shipping services activities will be brought together as one global organisation from the start of next year. Mr Morse is currently responsible for shipping services activities in the Far East, based in Tokyo. Mr Bainbridge will continue on a consultancy basis. ***** CITY SITES ESTATES, Glasgow, has appointed Mr Neil Munro as development director. He was managing director of London & Metropolitan Scotland. ***** Mr Ken Maud has been appointed executive chairman of PEEK, Abingdon. He was chief executive. Viscount Slim has retired as chairman, but remains on the board as non-executive deputy chairman. ***** Mr Ian Forrest has been appointed to the new post of senior executive director, deal origination and marketing, at MIDLAND MONTAGU VENTURES. He was with Bankers Trust Co. ***** LANDIS & GYR COMMUNICATIONS (UK) has appointed Mr John Butcher, MP for Coventry South West, as a non-executive director. ***** Fletcher Challenge, New Zealand-based parent company, has appointed Mr Monty August as chief executive of UK PAPER. He will replace Mr Tom Wilding who continues as non-executive chairman. Mr August was managing director of Sittingbourne Paper Co.
