931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / Extremists kill 12 in Algeria Members of a radical Islamic group have stabbed to death 12 Bosnian and Croatian technicians in Tamezguida, the town of Blida, south of Algiers, writes Francis Ghiles. The security services said the dead were engineers for the Hydro-Electra company which is involved in building dams and pumping stations, part of the water master plan for the region of Algiers.
