930623 FT 23 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Expansion at West Bromwich Society The West Bromwich Building Society increased pre-tax profit by 56 per cent, from Pounds 11.1m to Pounds 17.4m, for the year to March 31. The rise was helped by a fall in provisions for bad and doubtful debts to Pounds 7.4m (Pounds 12.6m). Assets grew by Pounds 130m to Pounds 1.24bn and the capital rose to Pounds 88.9m (Pounds 77.1m).
