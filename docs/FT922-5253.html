920601 FT 01 JUN 92 / Sexual harassment 'frequent' BRITISH women face sexual harassment in most workplaces and all sectors of employment, according to IRS, a independent employment research group, Catherine Milton writes. More than half the 132 employers in the survey published today in IRS Employment Trends journal said complaints of sexual harassment had been made at their workplaces, with 46 per cent saying cases had been reported during the past year. Almost nine out of 10 harassment complaints are upheld after internal investigations. Sexual Harassment at the Workplace. IRS, 18-20 Highbury Place, London N5 1QP. Pounds 350 annual subscription.
