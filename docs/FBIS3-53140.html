Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Dr. Nabil Sha'th, head of the Palestinian delegation in the Taba negotiations, today denied reports that an agreement has been reached between the Palestinian and Israeli delegations regarding the size of the Jericho area, estimated at 55 kilometers. In statements to correspondents, Sha'th described such reports as fabricated, noting that no agreement will be announced until a settlement acceptable to the two parties is achieved.
