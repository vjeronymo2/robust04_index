Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 24 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received messages of greetings from foreign party leaders on his birthday. The messages came from President Marian Donnelly and General Secretary Pat Quearney of the Workers' Party of Ireland, Chairperson Betty Frydensbjerg Carlsson of the Communist Party of Denmark, Chairman Preben Moller Hansen of the Danish Workers' Party, Common Cause, General Secretary Anthony Vassalo of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Malta, General Secretary Khaled Bensmain of the Movement for Democracy in Algeria, General Secretary Dilip Barua of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Bangladesh (Marxist-Leninist), National Chairman Lambert Mende Omalanga of the Lumumba Congolese National Movement-Original of Zaire, National Chairman Otete Gaston Mboyo of the National Movement of the Genuine Lumumbist Combatants of Zaire, National Chairman Emile Kibala Bey A Nsien of the Workers' Party of Zaire, and Secretary General Dr. Mamadou El Bechir Gologo of the National Direction Committee of the Sudanese Union-African Democratic Rally in Mali. The messages extend warmest congratulations and greetings to Comrade Kim Chong-il and wish him good health and a long life. They express their parties' firm solidarity with the Workers' Party of Korea in the efforts to build socialism and accomplish the cause of national reunification. Comrade Kim Chong-il received a message of greetings from the Central Committee of the Mozambique Liberation Front Party on his birthday.
