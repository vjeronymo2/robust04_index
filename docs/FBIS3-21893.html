Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "`Gifts' From Kyrgyzstan"] [Text] On Thursday officers of the Internal Affairs Main Administration Criminal Investigation Administration arrested a group of opium pushers. The two men, 30 and 38, citizens of Kyrgyzstan, together with an 18-year-old girl from Novosibirsk, were living in an apartment they were renting on Krasnobogatyrskaya Street [Moscow]. The threesome was busted in the actual apartment, where 1.2 kilograms of opium were stored. The same day a 24-year-old inhabitant of Voronezh was arrested on Uralskaya Street and 600 grams of opium and a revolver were found in his pocket.
