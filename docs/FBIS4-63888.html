BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Yudmila Aleksandrova, Grigoriy Arslanov, and Vyacheslav Tomilin] [Text] Beijing May 26 TASS -- Russian Premier Viktor Chernomyrdin who has arrived at the Beijing Airport, said in an interview with ITAR-TASS on Thursday that the main aim of his Chinese visit is "to consolidate what was achieved by Russian President Boris Yeltsin in December 1992 and to develop further relations with China". The programme of the premier's four-day visit provides for talks with Chinese Premier Li Peng, a meeting with the Chinese republic's Chairman Jiang Zemin and visits to Shanghai and Dalian. According to Chernomyrdin, there are good grounds and prospects for expanding Russian-Chinese relations. He expressed hope that the sides will take important decisions and sign documents during his visit, which will give a new impetus to the development of mutually advantageous bilateral relations. "We should have good, normal and friendly relations with China," the premier stressed. Apart from the official welcoming ceremony and the first talk with Li, the premier's programme for Thursday provides for a sight- seeing tour of Beijing and a visit to the great Chinese wall.
