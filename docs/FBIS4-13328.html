BFN [Text] Cairo, 19 Apr (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has announced that arangements are being made to convene an emergency meeting soon of the Islamic Conference Organization to discuss the developments in the city of Gorazde in Bosnia. 'Amr Musa affirmed at a news conference today that what is happening in Gorazde is just another piece of evidence of the failure of international politics to deal with this issue.
