BFN [Text] Major General [previous two words in English] Nasr Yusuf, commander of the Palestinian police force in Gaza, said today that the police has no intention of clashing with the Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] military arm, 'Izz-al-Din al-Qassam. He made the remark in response to a question on whether the Palestinian police would prevent acts of terror by Hamas. Nasr Yusuf added that he had reached full understanding with the leaders of Hamas whom he met over the past several days. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that Yusuf made the remarks at a news conference held at the Ansar-2 detention facility which was handed over to the Palestinians this morning.
