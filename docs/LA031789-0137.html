March 17, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: REAL ESTATE

Nexus U.S.A. Equities, an Irvine developer, sold the 11.8-acre Nexus Towne Center in Buena Park to Fullerton property owner and developer JVD Inc. for $14 million.

Nexus bought the 22-year-old center -- whose stores total 150,000 square feet -- in 1986. It was recently renovated.
