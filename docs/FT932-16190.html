930407 FT 07 APR 93 / Giant boost for Ulster power sale THE promotional campaign for the sale of shares in Northern Ireland Electricity was launched in Belfast yesterday by Sir Patrick Mayhew, the Northern Ireland secretary (main picture, left) and Sir Desmond Lorimer, NIE chairman. The campaign will be spearheaded by an image of Finn McCool, the legendary Ulster warrior giant (far left). Leading banks will operate share shops for the issue, the first sell-off specifically targeted at the Ulster public. It will also be marketed in Britain and the Irish Republic.
