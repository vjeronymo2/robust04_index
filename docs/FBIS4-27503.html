BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 1 (KCNA) -- The book "The 21st Century and the Leader" and other books praising the chuche idea founded by the great leader President Kim Il-song are being distributed among people of different strata and families in South Korea, according to a radio report from Seoul. The chairman of the Democratic Liberal Party, upset by this, called "an enlarged meeting of party officials" on May 30 and cried that a measure must be taken to check it.
