931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / International Company News: Compaq opens Polish unit Compaq Computer of the US, the world's second-largest manufacturer of personal computers, opened its Polish subsidiary yesterday, AP-DJ reports from Warsaw. Compaq hopes to become a 'strong number one' in brand computers in Poland and win between 10 and 15 per cent of the local market. The subsidiary will build on Compaq's network of resellers in Poland.
