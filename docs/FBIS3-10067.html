Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Article by Jorge de Freitas from Rio de Janeiro] [Excerpts] Former President of the Republic Joao Baptista de Oliveira Figueiredo, who was in office from 1979 and 1985, said on 24 February during a barbecue at the Freight Companies Union in Rio de Janeiro, that the country is risking a social upheaval. "When the people are strong, no Armed Forces can resist them," he said. Figueiredo criticized Finance Minister Fernando Henrique Cardoso's economic plan, saying there will be huge loss of purchasing power. [passage omitted] Figueiredo said he will support General Newton Cruz' candidacy for Rio de Janeiro governorship. Gen. Cruz headed the National Intelligence Service central agency during Figueiredo's presidency. Cruz will run for Rio de Janeiro governorship as a Democratic Social Party candidate.
