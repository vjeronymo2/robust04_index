940615 FT 15 JUN 94 / Heseltine launches Business Links Michael Heseltine, trade and industry secretary, pictured yesterday beneath a sign for Business Links, which bring together the advisory, information and financial services offered by the government. Mr Heseltine told about 500 public and private sector executives at a conference in London: 'An effective Business Links network is an important contribution to our ability to compete and win in world markets.' Thirty-five Business Links have been set up, a further 47 have received DTI approval and a network of 200 is planned by the end of next year. Photograph: Tony Andrews
