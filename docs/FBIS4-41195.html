BFN [Text] Representatives of North Ossetia and Ingushetia have both turned down options for the return of refugees to their former homes, which had been put to both sides. The RIA news agency was informed of this today by negotiators representing the interim administration in the state of emergency zone. The interim administration has drawn up its own proposals, which are now being worked on by state structures in both republics. The details of the plan have not been divulged so far. However, RIA has learned from reliable sources that the interim administration intends to abide by the timetable and principles for the return of refugees set out in the Russian president's decree of 30 May this year.
