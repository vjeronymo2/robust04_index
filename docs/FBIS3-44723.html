Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Summary] Beginning on 10 January, the Hebei Provincial People's Broadcasting Station will extend its newscast from the present 20 minutes to 30 minutes. The first newscast will still take place at 0700 [Beijing standard time] to 0730 every morning and the newscast will be repeated from 0800 to 0830 every morning. The contents of the newscast will change somewhat with the addition of new columns.
