March 16, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

REAGAN PLANS TRIP TO BERLIN

Former President Ronald Reagan, inspired by the tearing down of the Berlin Wall and talks on reunification of East and West Germany, will make a trip to Berlin in coming months, his aide said.

"He has followed the events with great interest and he hopes to visit Berlin soon," spokesman Mark Weinberg said Thursday. No date was set.

Weinberg said Reagan wasn't making the trip on behalf of any particular party or candidate in coming elections.
