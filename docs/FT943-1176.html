940926 FT 26 SEP 94 / Part-time jobs gaining ground More people are taking two part-time jobs because they cannot find one satisfactory full-time one, says the GMB general union. The union's analysis of the employment department's Labour Force Survey also found that almost all new jobs being generated are part-time. Of the 68,354 new jobs in the second quarter of the year 65,219 were part-time. The majority are in distribution and hotels and catering. The survey shows there are 1,123,742 people in the workforce with two jobs of whom 43 per cent are part-time in both jobs.
