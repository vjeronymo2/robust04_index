BFN [Text] Eritrean President Isayas Afwerki arrived here in Addis Ababa today on a brief working visit to Ethiopia. On arrival at Bole Airport, the Eritrean leader was welcomed by President Meles Zenawi and other senior government officials. The two heads of state are expected to discuss issues of mutual concern. President Isayas will fly to the Zimbabwean capital, Harare, tomorrow to attend an international summit meeting called Global Coalition With Africa.
