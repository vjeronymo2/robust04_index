August 13, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Wedding bells are on hold again for Laker star Magic Johnson, who said his wedding, which had been scheduled for Sept. 1, was indefinitely postponed.

Johnson is engaged to Earletha Kelly, 31, of Detroit.

"We're still together and everything, but we're just going to wait," Johnson said. "We're still going to get married, probably in Lansing."

He didn't say why the wedding was put off, but added the plans had been postponed before.
