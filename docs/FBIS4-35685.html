BFN [Text] Jabin Bin-Hayyan Establishment, one of the units of the Military Industrialization Organization [MIO], has produced millions of rubber pieces and parts for several industrial firms in Iraq as its contribution toward beating the unfair blockade. The director general of the establishment said these pieces have been sold to the General Leather Industries Establishment for use in the production of bags. He also said that large quantities of plastic containers have been sold to the General Medicines and Medical Equipment Company. The director general added that millions of other rubber pieces have been sold to the Oil Ministry for use in repairing and maintaining gas cylinder valves, which used to be imported with hard currencies. He said that large quantities of school rulers of different sizes were sold to the Ministry of Education. He affirmed that Establishment teams have succeeded for the first time in manufacturing and producing rubber lids for oil tanks belonging to the Oil Ministry according to high standard specifications to meet the ministry's needs.
