Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Judicial officials in Egypt who are involved in indictment of a number of Muslim revolutionaries of that country announced that they have documentary evidence that indicates that the revolutionary Muslims had intended to kill the Zionist regime's ambassador in Cairo as well as nine Egyptian government officials. According to ASSOCIATED PRESS, these judicial officials stated yesterday that the Muslims who have been charged with trying to kill the Egyptian prime minister revealed 10 other names of people who had been targeted, including the Zionist regime's ambassador David Sultan, the Egyptian prosecutor general, some military judges, and the former chief of Egyptian intelligence. The trial of the Egyptian revolutionaries, who number 15, has been postponed to 5 February.
