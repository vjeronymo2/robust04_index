March 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

THE WORD IS OPA

We were very pleased by Sylvia Steinberg's Reader Recommendation ("Jerry Hulse's Travel Tips," Feb. 18). However, the name of our company is OPA Tours Greece, not OTA Tours. The word opa is Greek; it's what the Greek people say when they are having a wonderful time.

JOYCE HELFAND

OPA TOURS GREECE

Tujunga
