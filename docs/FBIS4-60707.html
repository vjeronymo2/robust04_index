BFN [Excerpts] President Hashemi-Rafsanjani, heading a high-ranking delegation, arrived in Mashhad this morning to meet the president of Turkmenistan. [passage omitted] During a meeting held at midday today, the two countries' presidents and their delegations met and reviewed ways of expanding ties and cooperation between Iran and Turkmenistan in the fields of transport, customs, banking, growth of commercial transactions, provision of facilities for goods transit, and cross border visits by the two countries' citizens. [video shows arrival and meeting with Saparmurad Niyazov] During these talks, referring to the large and well-equipped Razavi commercial center currently under construction in Sarakhs, President Hashemi-Rafsanjani expressed the hope that when this trade center opens, the volume of transactions would increase between the merchants in both countries, among other neighboring countries and the rest of the region. Niyazov too, during his remarks, in part referring to the growing volume of trade between the two countries, agreed with the export of cotton from Turkmenistan to Iran. In another part of his remarks, referring to the policy to expand road and railway networks between the two countries, Niyazov added: Good progress is made in the feasibility study phase of the project to transport Turkmenistan gas to Europe via Iran. It is worth mentioning that at the conclusion of the talks between the presidents of the Islamic Republic of Iran and Turkmenistan it was agreed that ministers and expert teams from the two countries should study and investigate ways of strengthening bilateral cooperation and should inform their respective presidents on the outcome of their discussions.
