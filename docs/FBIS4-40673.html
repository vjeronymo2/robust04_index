BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksey Yerovchenkov] [Text] Nicosia 20 June TASS -- "Turkmenistan is the motherland of our ancestors, and the Turkish Republic is the second home for our esteemed guests from Turkmenistan. We are happy to see the relationship between the two fraternal states developed successfully over the past two and a half years," Turkey's President Suleyman Demirel said in welcoming President Saparmurad Niyazov of Turkmenistan for an official visit to Turkey at Ankara international airport on Sunday. President Demirel later said in the presidential palace in Ankara that relations between the two countries were developing on the basis of equality, non-interference in each other's internal affairs, mutual benefit and respect. Cooperation between Turkey and Turkmenistan make an important contribution to ensuring peace and stability, as well as prosperity of all nations of the region, he said. Of course, we want to take our relations to a higher level and are displaying a political will necessary to do so. We will discuss with Mr. Niyazov a variety of problems, including the key issues of international character, the Turkish president said. President Demirel expressed the hope that Turkmenistan will make ample use of Turkey's experience and potential to overcome specific difficulties which arose in the process of its development and will soon become a highly developed state. Turkey for its part, will render all possible assistance and support to the fraternal people.
