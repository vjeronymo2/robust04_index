Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Aleksandr Kharchenko and Andrey Chytov] [Text] Dushanbe March 15 TASS -- The foreign ministers of Russia, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan are conferring with the commander of the collective peacekeeping force in Tajikistan, Colonel-General Boris Pyankov and the commander of the Russian contingent of border troops in Tajikistan, Lieutenant-General Anatoliy Chechulin. The plenary meeting, which began in the government residence in Dushanbe on Tuesday morning, has heard reports of General Pyankov and Chechulin about the situation on the Tajik-Afghan border, in and around the republic. The plenary session was followed by talks in a narrower format, including between Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and Tajik Foreign Minister Rashid Olimov. A Russian delegation member told ITAR-TASS the ministers have not yet decided whether or what documents should be adopted as a result of their meeting. Generals Pyankov and Chechulin told journalists that the possibility of a intra-Tajik dialogue was discussed in general, but he did not rule out the possibility of talks between the Dushanbe government and the opposition in March and April. Andrey Kozyrev is due to fly to the 12th border post of the Pyanj border group, where two attempts of penetrating Tajikistan from Afghanistan were reported last night. Later today Kozyrev is due to meet Tajikistan's leader Emomali Rakhmonov for one-to-one talks.
