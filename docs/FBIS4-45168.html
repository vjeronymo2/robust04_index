Battle PY2705142894 La Paz Radio Fides Network in Spanish 1100 GMT 27 May 94 PY2705142894 La Paz Radio Fides Network Spanish BFN [Text] In referring to bank secrecy and the tapping of telephone conversations, President Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada stated in Sucre: Nobody is thinking about lifting the bank secrecy. There is no plan to this end, and still less to (violate) the specific rights of Bolivian people living in democracy. An authorization issued by a prosecutor or a judge is necessary to do this. Speaking about the militarization of the Chapare zone, the president said: There is no intention of militarizing the struggle against drug trafficking. I was never in favor of that when I was a member of the opposition, and still less so now that I am in government.
