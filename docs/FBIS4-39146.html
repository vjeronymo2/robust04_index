BFN [Text] Regional branches of the Democratic Party of Russia, the Party of Russian Unity and Accord, the Social Democratic Party, and the Social Democratic People's Party of Aleksandr Rutskoy are hoping to set up a united democratic center in Moscow. This issue was tackled today by the steering committee of the united center. Ilya Roytman, one of the center's organizers, said that the center will become a coordinating body, able to identify and support united democratic candidates in the run-up to the next election.
