940716 FT 16 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Moorgate Inv Trust below benchmarks Moorgate Investment Trust reported net asset value per share up from 140.1p to 162.1p over the year to May 31. The rise of 15.7 per cent was lower than the 17.6 per cent increase by the benchmark Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index. However the FT-SE SmallCap Index showed a rise of 16.2 per cent. Net revenue for the year slipped to Pounds 1.41m (Pounds 1.43m) for earnings per share of 4.98p (5.09p). A lower final dividend of 2.55p is proposed for a total of 4.25p (5.5p).
