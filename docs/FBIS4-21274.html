U.S. PA2404022494 Santa Fe de Bogota Inravision Television Canal A in Spanish 0130 GMT 24 Apr 94 PA2404022494 Santa Fe de Bogota Inravision Television Canal A Spanish BFN [Report by Oscar Galvis from the "AM-PM" newscast] [Text] Upon arriving in the United States from London, Prosecutor General Gustavo de Greiff said President Cesar Gaviria's administration is subservient to the United States. De Greiff called several U.S. officials liars and accused President Gaviria of unnecessarily criticizing him for his fight against drug traffickers. De Greiff said the president wanted to calm the U.S. Government's temper by attacking him. The prosecutor general said of the Colombian Government: They wanted to make themselves seem subservient to the U.S. Government by attacking me. As for the accusations that Colombia is a narcodemocracy, de Greiff said it is an exaggerated perception and once again called Senator Kerry and Assistant Attorney General Harris liars.
