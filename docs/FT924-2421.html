921212 FT 12 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Unit well down at Pounds 14,000 halfway Reflecting reduced volumes and margins, first half pre-tax profits at Unit Group fell from Pounds 122,000 to Pounds 14,000. This maker of timber pallets and property investor saw its turnover fall from Pounds 11.3m to Pounds 9.79m in the six months ended September 30 1992. Earnings per share were 0.2p (1.4p). Devaluation of sterling had increased raw material costs and added to the pressure on margins. The directors were continuing to reduce the cost base of the group.
