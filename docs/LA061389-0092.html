June 13, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: EXIT LINES

Ex-model and restaurateur Marilyn Lewis, who was once part of the Andy Warhol clique and is now making a documentary about the artist, believes Warhol was "one of the most unusual men of our times. He was terribly religious. He was a voyeur regarding drugs, sex and other elements of life but not a participant." Lewis suspects the world hasn't seen the last of Warhol. "I believe Andy is an afterlife person. He spent his life preparing for a reincarnation." ALEENE MacMINN
