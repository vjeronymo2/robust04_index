BFN [From the "Names and Notes" section] [Text] The chief of the U.S. Southern Command, General Barry McCaffrey, discussed the new defense strategy for South America with Brazilian military officers. After a meeting with the Armed Forces chief of staff, Admiral Arnaldo Leite Pereira, Gen. McCaffrey said the main objective of his visit to Brazil is "to listen and study" the Brazilian military leadership's point of view. According to McCaffrey, the Brazilian military chiefs' opinions will help define future relations between the U.S. and Brazilian Armies.
