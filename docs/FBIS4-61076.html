BFN [Text] Riga, May 03, BNS -- The Baltic states' presidents, Guntis Ulmanis, Lennart Meri and Algirdas Brazauskas, discussed Tuesday [3 May] Russian Army withdrawal from Baltics, and Latvian -- Russian recently signed agreements, President's Office told BNS. [sentence as received] The presidents met in Latvian president's residence in Dubulti. No documents were signed during the meeting, the Latvian presidential office said. Officials refused to give any commentaries to press. The talks were confidential and no interpreters participated in them.
