930504 FT 04 MAY 93 / Man charged over London bomb MR JOHN Gerrard Mathews, a 22-year-old unemployed man, was yesterday charged with causing an explosion hours after the City of London bomb, Scotland Yard said. The charge is believed to relate to a mini-cab bomb which exploded in Holborn, central London, but which was bound for Downing Street. He will appear today at Arbour Square magistrates' court, east London.
