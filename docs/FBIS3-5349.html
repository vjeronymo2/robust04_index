Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Pomminnyon Will Be Defended, Says Chairman of Preparatory Committee for Formation of South Side Headquarters of Pomminnyon"-- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 6 (KCNA) -- Kang Hui-nam, chairman of the Preparatory Committee for the Formation of the South Side Headquarters of the National Alliance for the Country's Reunification (Pomminnyon), in an interview with the magazine MAL declared that they would defend Pomminnyon even under any suppression, according to KNS from Tokyo. He announced the plan to expand organisations under Pomminnyon in different parts of South Korea. The "reunification policy" of the Kim Yong-sam "regime" is little different from that of the preceding military "regimes", he said.
