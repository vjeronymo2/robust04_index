BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Guatemalan guerrilla activities monitored through 25 May. Guatemalan National Revolutionary Unity, URNG, guerrillas on 16 May attacked a farm in Malacatan, San Marcos, and stole 14,000 quetzales. Farmers in that area have recently reported that guerrillas are demanding "war taxes" from them. (Guatemala City LA REPUBLICA in Spanish 17 May 94 p 8) A presumed guerrilla was arrested on 22 May when the police and Army raided a house in Pujujil, Solola, discovering an arms cache containing weapons, uniforms, and medicines. (Guatemala City PRENSA LIBRE in Spanish 23 May 94 p 127)
