910419 FT 19 APR 91 / UK Company News: Aviva moves back into the black Aviva Petroleum, the Texas-based oil and gas company, swung from losses of Dollars 715,061 to profits of Dollars 739,061 (Pounds 416,000) pre-tax for 1990. Sales rose from Dollars 5.78m to Dollars 12.29m. Profits benefited from a currency gain of Dollars 2.1m (loss Dollars 1.42m) and net interest income of Dollars 980,000 (Dollars 350,000 charge). After deducting tax of Dollars 1.35m (Dollars 80,220) and adding in extraordinary income of Dollars 1.15m net earnings emerged at Dollars 537,390 (losses Dollars 795,281).
