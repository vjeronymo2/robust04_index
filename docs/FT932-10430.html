930508 FT 08 MAY 93 / Finance & The Family: New BES from Govett - At a Glance John Govett has launched an assured tenancy Business Expansion Scheme, Govett Assetbuilder IV. The scheme aims to raise Pounds 12.75m to develop a mill and surrounding properties in Ashton-under-Lyne. There is an option to sell the properties to the Sanctuary Housing Association at 117p in five years time; this equates to a 13.38 per cent per annum return for top rate taxpayers and a 9.08 per cent return for basic rate taxpayers. Sanctuary is putting up cash collateral worth 60 per cent of the buy-back price.
