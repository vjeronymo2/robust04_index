BFN [By reporter Wang Laixi (3769 0171 0823)] [Text] Harbin, 21 May (XINHUA) -- The ninth session of the Eighth Heilongjiang Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee on 21 May appointed Tian Fengshan [deputy secretary of the provincial party committee] vice governor and acting governor of the provincial people's government. Tian was nominated for the post by the chairmanship meeting of the eighth provincial people's congress standing committee. At the same time, the ninth session approved Shao Qihui's resignation from the post of governor of the provincial people's government.
