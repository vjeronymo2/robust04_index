930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / UK Company News: P&S advances 50% to Pounds 6m Portsmouth & Sunderland Newspapers achieved a 50 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 4.01m to Pounds 6.01m for the year ended March 27. Excluding rationalisation and exceptional costs, there was a 13 per cent rise in underlying pre-tax profit. Turnover of Pounds 60.1m (Pounds 58.1m) came from newspaper publishing and printing operations, while retailing contributed Pounds 42.4m (Pounds 37.9m). Retail trading profits were Pounds 1.3m (Pounds 954,000) while newspaper publishing and printing produced Pounds 5.2m (Pounds 5.3m). The group's net cash position improved over the year by Pounds 3.2m, leaving it with Pounds 1.1m. Earnings were 39.3p (31.9p). The final dividend of 6.62p makes a total of 9.46p (8.6p). The shares added 7p to 675p.
