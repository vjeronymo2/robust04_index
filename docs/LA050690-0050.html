May 6, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

REPLACING QUAYLE WITH BAKER

So, Republican strategiests are considering taking Vice President Dan Quayle off the 1992 ticket, and moving him to secretary of defense! If this item were datelined April 1, it would be more appropriate (Part A, April 24).

Here is a man who turned heaven and earth to keep from going to Vietnam -- shoehorning himself into the Indiana National Guard and into law school over those ahead of him in line. How would our valiant men and women of the armed forces regard Quayle as their boss?

Here's another idea that makes about as much sense: Move William Bennett out of his job drug czar and put in Timothy Leary.

JERRY COWLE

Pacific Palisades
