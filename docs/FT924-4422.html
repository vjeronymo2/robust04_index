921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Huntingdon declines to Pounds 14.4m HUNTINGDON International Holdings, the engineering and environmental services group, announced a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 16.9m to Pounds 14.4m in the year to September 30. Profits in the final quarter amounted to Pounds 4.22m against Pounds 4.79m. Revenues for the year improved from Pounds 123m to Pounds 150m and to Pounds 40.6m (Pounds 34.6m) in the final three months, with subcontract costs at Pounds 9.8m (Pounds 7.69m) and Pounds 3.19m (Pounds 2.63m) respectively. Earnings per share worked through at 11.5p (14.6p) and 4.3p (3.4p). An unchanged final dividend of 1.9p is proposed to maintain the total at 2.775p.
