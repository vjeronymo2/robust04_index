930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / Milken joins drugs fight MR Michael Milken, the former Drexel Burnham Lambert financier who once earned more than Dollars 500m (Pounds 324.6m) from the investment banking firm in a year, is to help inner-city school children combat drugs, Nikki Tait writes from New York. He was ordered by a New York court to do 1,800 hours' community service in each of the next three years as part of his sentence for violating securities laws. He will spend the time with the Los Angeles office of DARE America, an anti-drugs organisation, it was disclosed yesterday.
