930202 FT 02 FEB 93 / UK Company News: SEP expands in fasteners SEP Industrial Holdings, the Surrey-based maker of engineering products, has acquired Jaton Holdings, a distributor of industrial fasteners. Under the terms of the deal SEP is paying Pounds 1 for Jaton plus a further Pounds 1.87m in respect of claims by Jaton's former parent, Tollgate Holdings, now in liquidation, against Jaton.
