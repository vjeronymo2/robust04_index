CSO [Unattributed report: "Fuel--For Grain"] [Text] As has already been reported, the United States Government has arranged to make available to the Ministry of Agriculture of the Republic of Armenia $1 million for the purchase of fuel, which the Ministry intends to offer farms in exchange for grain. The funds will be delivered through the intermediary effort of the United States Agency for International Development and monitored by the Armenian Assembly of America. As Ashot Voskanyan, minister of agriculture of the Republic of Armenia, stated to a Noyan Tapan agency correspondent, some of the fuel has already been purchased. The fuel will be afforded in exchange for grain, barley, and spelt wheat turned over to the state. This measure is already being successfully carried out in Gorisskiy, Sisianskiy, Araratskiy, and Artashatskiy Rayons of Armenia.
