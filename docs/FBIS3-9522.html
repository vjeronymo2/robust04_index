Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Two British soldiers have been wounded in the Serb shelling of Bugojno, a British UN forces spokesman in Bugojno has announced. Around 0630 this morning, Serb artillery fired a number of shells on Bugojno, one of which hit the local UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] base. Serb artillery shelled Travnik throughout the night. The [Croatian Defense Council] Stjepan Tomasevic brigade has said that Serb artillery was intensely shelling Muslim parts of Travnik. The cease-fire has been observed by the Croat and the Muslim sides.
