941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Reckitt & Colman Reckitt & Colman has announced an 86.8 per cent take-up of its rights issue to fund the purchase of L&F Household. The company has received acceptances in respect of 40.8m of the 47m units of non-interest bearing convertible loan stock. These were offered to qualifying shareholders at a price of 500p per unit, payable in two instalments.
