BFN [Text] Today, the Kazakh cabinet of ministers held a joint session with the union of entrepreneurs and industrialists to discuss updating and correcting the government's anticrisis economic program. First Deputy Prime Minister Akezhan Kazhegeldin gave a report on the issue. A new mechanism for management of the economy based on making the [word indistinct] system healthier and improving mass privatization and investment policy was welcomed by the participants. They commented on the report sharing their views on ways to boost reform and make half-way proposals. They focussed on what is impeding the speed of the reforms. The result of an open and interesting discussion on possible ways to bring the economy out of the crisis was summed up by Prime Mininster Tereshchenko. He said that all valuable advice expressed by the participants would be taken into account during the further elaboration of the anti-crisis program. The head of the government answered numerous questions. President Nazarbayev participated in the work of the session.
