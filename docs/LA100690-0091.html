October 6, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

NONLEAGUE

Cantwell 26, Paraclete 24 -- Carlos Zarate ran for four touchdowns and Cantwell (4-1) hung on for the victory at home.

Paraclete (3-2) had a chance to win the game with 14 seconds left, but a 35-yard field-goal attempt failed.

Paraclete's Jorge Ordaz rushed for 151 yards in 27 carries and scored two touchdowns. Teammate Mike Pokorny carried the ball 14 times for 114 yards and scored one touchdown. CAROLYN BRYANT
