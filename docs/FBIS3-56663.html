Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow January 17 TASS -- The State Duma will continue its work today. Its session will begin at 14.00. The lower chamber of the Russian parliament is expected to discuss issues dealing with the election of deputy chairmen of the Duma and heads of parliamentary committees. Last Saturday those issues were tentatively discussed at a conference of chairman of the State Duma Ivan Rybkin and heads of parliamentary groups, held behind closed doors. The planned number of deputy chairmen and the names of candidates to those posts remain a secret. The names of candidates to the posts of heads of parliamentary committees (there are going to be 18 of them) are not known either. In the opinion of observers, the main struggle will develop over the posts of heads of the committees on international affairs, defence and security.
