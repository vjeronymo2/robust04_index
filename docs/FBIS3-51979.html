Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] New registrations in activities such as producing light foodstuffs, collecting raw materials and agricultural products, and serving as video operators are among the five or six kinds of self-employment occupations that have been suspended. Nationwide, there is a total of 135 occupations in which workers are permitted to engage as self-employed persons. Over 137,000 such licensed workers have been approved. This information came from (Alfredo Diaz Urbay), director of the Labor Research Institute [Instituto de Investigation del Trabajo] of the State Committee for Labor, who commented that the rate of increase in requests has already begun to decline. (Diaz Urbay) pointed out that approximatelyy 11,000 people have requested self-employment permits.
