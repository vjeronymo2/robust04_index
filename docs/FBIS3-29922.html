Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 24 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received Li Shuzheng, head of the International Liaison Department of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Communist Party of China, and her companions on a visit to Korea. Present on the occasion were Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the C.C., the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK], Chu Chang-chun, Korean ambassador to China, Kim Yang-kon, vice-director of a Department of the WPK Central Committee, and Qiao Zonghuai, Chinese ambassador to Korea. President Kim Il-song conversed with the guests in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. He was presented with a gift by Li Shuzheng.
