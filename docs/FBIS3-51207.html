Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] PRC Foreign Ministry spokesman (Hu Hiangming) has affirmed that it is up to the Chinese people alone to comment on the human rights situation in the PRC. The Chinese Government, in this way, responded sharply to reports from officials in Washington who link trade between the two countries to the internal situation of the PRC. The United States has reduced to 35 percent its imports of textiles from China, and now there is talk at the White House of withdrawing China's trade status as a Most Favored Nation, according to a report from PRENSA LATINA. Business is business, stressed the Foreign Ministry spokesman, recalling that the PRC has achieved remarkable successes since it was founded in 1949, especially with the reforms put into practice in the past 15 years -- which has been acknowledged internationally.
