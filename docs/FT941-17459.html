940104 FT 04 JAN 94 / International Company News: Safra group acquisition Republic New York Corporation, the US holding company in the banking group controlled by Mr Edmond Safra of Geneva, has completed its acquisition of Mase Westpac, one of five members of the London Gold Fixing, from Westpac, the Australian bank. Mase, with capital of Pounds 100m, (Dollars 148m) is to be known as Republic Mase Bank.
