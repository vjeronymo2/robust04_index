Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, December 29 (SNARK) -- Today the Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan has a phone talks with Eduard Shevardnadze, the Head of the Georgian state. As SNARK was informed at the press-center of the Armenian president, a problem of cargo conveyance for Armenia and the restoration of gas pipe-line damaged in the Marneul region in Georgia was discussed. In the opinion of most observers, transit Armenian cargo's plundering has got in Georgia a kind of official character. As a result, hundreds of railroad cars and trucks don't get [to] their destination places in Armenia. Georgia takes the most part of gas from Turkmenistan, for which Armenia has already paid in gold. Vazgen Sargisyan, the Armenian state minister, has said recently that "the Armenian government has to feed not only Armenia but also Georgia."
