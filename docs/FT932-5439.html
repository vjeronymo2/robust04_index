930603 FT 03 JUN 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil No entry yet for this page. SAO PAULO closed as 5.5 per cent higher. There was heavy early trading encouraged by news that Telebras, the state-owned telecommunications group, plans to launch a Dollars 500m American Depositary Receipt. The Bovespa index reached reached 39,252, following a 5 per cent rise on Tuesday.
