November 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SUPPORT FOR CRISIS PREGNANCIES

Hunnicutt's letter makes judgmental statements as to compassion shown by pro-lifers outside the delivery room.

She specifically stated how wonderful it would be if the demonstrators would adopt unwanted children, etc. My wife and I and three children, including two adopted twin boys, now Eagle Scouts, stood in that line. Our daughter adopted three unwanted, children, two of which came from druggie mothers. Our sister-in-law and husband and other members of our expanded family have adopted five others. Let Hunnicutt be less judgmental. Let her rather talk by the example she has set in doing any of the things she judges us pro-lifers to have been remiss in doing.

GRANT A. STUCKI

Rancho Palos Verdes
