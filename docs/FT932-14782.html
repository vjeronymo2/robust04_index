930416 FT 16 APR 93 / International Company News: Brooke Bond India acquisition BROOKE Bond India, a subsidiary of Unilever, the Anglo-Dutch food-to-detergents combine, is to acquire Kissan Products, an Indian food products company, from UB Group, a local conglomerate formerly known as United Breweries, for Rs250m (Dollars 8m), writes RC Murthy in Bombay.
