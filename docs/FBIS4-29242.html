BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 16 (KCNA) -- A meeting was held on June 15 at Changdok school, Mangyongdae District, Pyongyang Municipality, to welcome the delegation of Jilin Yuwen Middle School, China. The great leader President Kim Il-song conducted revolutionary activities, studying at Jilin Yuwen Middle School from January 1927 to Autumn of 1929. Kang Chun-ho, principal of Changdok school said in his speech at the meeting that Jilin Yuwen Middle School with a long history is a proud school which actively struggled against the Japanese imperialist aggressors and reactionary warlords in the past and trained excellent revolutionaries of China in this course. The teachers and students of the school have better tended the statue of respected Comrade Kim Il-song erected at their school out of a noble desire to convey forever the undying revolutionary relics of Comrade Kim Il-song, the great leader of the Korean people, and develop the traditional Korea-China friendship down through generations, he said. Zhao Zhongxin, secretary of the Party Committee of the school, who is heading the delegation, speaking next, referred to the fact that President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il sent precious gifts to the school several times with deep concdrn. He said that they consider the school a symbol of China-Korea friendship. The friendship between Yuwen school and Changdok school is all the more precious because the two schools are the historical places where President Kim Il-song studied. Saying that Changdok school with modern educational equipment has a wealth of precious experience in instruction and education, he laid stress on deepening friendship between the two schools. Souvenirs were exchanged at the meeting.
