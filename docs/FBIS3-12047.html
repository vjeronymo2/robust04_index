Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The second scientific meeting on Latin American integration called Inter-Lat-94 began today at the Cienfuegos Superior Technical Institute with the participation of approximately 100 delegates, among them Mexican and Ecuadoran guests. At the opening session, Dr. Eduardo Cruz Gonzalez, dean of Cienfuegos University, said the objective of these meetings, the second of which is being held here in coordination with the Center for Research of World Economy, is to study how universities, in the midst of the difficulties daily affecting the world, can contribute to maintaining the concept of support to the integrationist process in America. Several working groups are analyzing such topics as Latin American integration in history; the Latin American university in today's world; research on housing; and science, technology, and sustainable development. Two important seminars will close the international scientific research meeting on integration tomorrow.
