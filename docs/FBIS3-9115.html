Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Knin, 18 Mar (TANJUG) -- Nineteen T-55 tanks for the Croatian Army arrived in the Croatian port of Bakar on 14 and 15 March this year. TANJUG has learned from military sources in Knin that Croatia acquired the tanks from weapons arsenals of the army of the former East Germany. Military circles in Knin interpret the import of the tanks as the reward to Croatia for its acceptance to form the Croatian-Muslim confederation.
