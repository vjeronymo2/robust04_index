BFN [Text] This morning, the brother President 'Arafat received a U.S. Senate delegation, in the presence of the U.S. Ambassador to Tunisia Mr. John McCarthy. The delegation comprises Senator Paul Simon, Senator Russel Fengold, and Senator Harry Reid. At the meeting, they reviewed the latest developments of the peace process and the stages of implementation of the self-rule agreement in Gaza and Jericho, as well as the importance of the donor countries meeting their commitments to bolster the Palestinian National Authority. During the meeting, brother 'Arafat entrusted the U.S. Senate delegation with a letter for U.S. President Bill Clinton on the implementation of the Declaration of Principles agreement signed in Washington last September in the interest of the peace process in the Middle East. The meeting stressed the strengthening of bilateral relations between the U.S. Senate and the U.S. Congress and the PLO in the interest of the U.S. and Palestinian peoples, and in the service of a just peace. The meting was attended by brothers: Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, member of the PLO Executive Committee and 'Abd-al-Latif Abu-Hijlah, director-general of the PLO Political Department.
