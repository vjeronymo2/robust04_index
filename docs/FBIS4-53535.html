BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 20 (KCNA) -- More than 3,000 students and citizens out of the participants in a commemoration ceremony and a people's rally on the 14th anniversary of the May 18 Kwangju popular resistance which was held in Kwangju, the city of resistance, on May 18 went over to a demonstration from 11:00 at night, according to a report from Seoul. The demonstrators thronged to the Kwangju and South Cholla Provincial branches of the "Democratic Liberal Party" and strongly demanded a probe into the truth behind the Kwangju massacre which was still kept in the dark and the punishment of those responsible for it. They continued the demonstration till past midnight, fighting the riot police. That day another group of participants in the people's rally mourned those who fell in the Kwangju resistance, marching in a candle procession toward the Kwangju railway station along Kumnan road, the spot of the historical resistance.
