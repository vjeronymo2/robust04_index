Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Another 200 UN election observers have arrived in South Africa and are being taught about South African conditions, the political scene, the media, and voter education. The observers are also being trained in security matters by the South African Defense Force and the South African Police, and in first aid by the Red Cross. They will be deployed throughout the country on Monday to monitor the election campaign. Besides the 500 observers already in the country, an additional contingent of at least 1,300 will be deployed in mid-April and will stay until 2 days after the election.
