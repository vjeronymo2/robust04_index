BFN [Text] The cabinet met yesterday under the chairmanship of Prime Minister Mokdad Sifi, according to a statement released by the prime minister's department. The statement says the cabinet studied the preliminary plan for the transitional program, which will form the basis for government activity during the next three years. The statement adds that following a fruitful discussion on the aims of the program and the conditions for achieving it, the cabinet agreed to hold further discussion on some subjects. The goal is to incorporate measures necessary for the mobilization of the greatest potentials and the rational use of all resources, thus allowing a genuine revival of the national economy and answering the preoccupations of the people.
