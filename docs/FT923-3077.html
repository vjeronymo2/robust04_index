920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Second Alliance net assets off 6% The Second Alliance Trust had a net asset value of Pounds 12.79 per stock unit at July 31 - a decline of some 6 per cent on the Pounds 13.61 of 12 months earlier. Available revenue amounted to Pounds 7.15m (Pounds 6.93m), equivalent to earnings of 37.22p (36.1p) per stock unit, the relatively modest growth reflecting a slower rate of dividend increases and lower interest accrued from the trust's cash balances. A proposed final dividend of 24.5p brings the total for the year to 36.5p (35p).
