920603 FT 03 JUN 92 / Accountants raise subscription MEMBERS OF the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales yesterday approved an increase in subscriptions by Pounds 15 to Pounds 115 next year. The rise follows two successive annual deficits for the institute. The special meeting did not ratify proposed additional increases for the next two years. Members also backed the speeding up of disciplinary actions against members.
