BFN [Text] A parliamentary delegation from the Republic of Turkey led by His Excellency Husamettin Cindoruk, speaker of the Turkish Grand National Assembly, arrived in Tbilisi yesterday. The esteemed guest was met at Tbilisi airport by Eduard Shevardnadze, chairman of the Georgian Supreme Council and head of state, Supreme Council Speaker Vakhtang Gogudze, and Foreign Minister Aleksandre Chikvaidze. The delegation will take part in the work of a meeting of the Assembly of Countries of the Black Sea Basin, which opens today at the Metechi Hotel in Tbilisi and will last for two days. Nodar Komakhidze, Georgian ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to the Republic of Turkey, accompanies the delegation. Yesterday the guest had a meeting and discussion with head of state Eduard Shevardnadze.
