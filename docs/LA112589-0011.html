November 25, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

UNDERTAXING THE WEALTHY

Why don't we adopt Kuttner's call to increase taxes on the "small minority of (wealthiest) Americans" to balance the federal budget and to pay "for some of the (unspecified) public needs that have been shortchanged during the past decade." By exercising majority rule, we can enrich ourselves. There is no limit to what we can buy! But whether this will please or upset the "opinion-leader class" is not as clear.

BILL MOSELEY

Los Angeles
