BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] President Suleyman Demirel has stated that the Turkish people give special importance to furthering Turkish-German relations. Demirel sent a congratulatory message to Roman Herzog on his election as Germany's president. According to a statement issued by the Press Center of the President's Office, Demirel wished Herzog success in his new appointment. The statement further notes: The Turkish nation attaches special importance to furthering relations with Germany because of the deep-rooted historical ties between the two countries and the 2 million Turkish citizens living in Germany. Expressing his belief that the traditional Turkish-German cooperation and friendliness will be furthered during Herzog's term, Demirel added that he is always ready and willing to cooperate on this issue.
