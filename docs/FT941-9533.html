940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / World News in Brief: Heavier snowfalls expected across country Heavier snowfalls were expected to spread throughout the UK today after snow and bitter cold caused chaos for travellers in south and west England and Wales yesterday. The number of road accidents rose by 50 per cent, the RAC reported, with 449 incidents in the London area alone.
