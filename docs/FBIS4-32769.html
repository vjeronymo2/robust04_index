BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 28/06/1994 -- The censure motion against the Vacaroiu Cabinet laid by the Democratic Agrarian Party of Romania [PDAR] is "a very big error," said Adrian Nastase, executive president of the ruling Party of Social Democracy [PDSR], at a press conference on Tuesday [28 June]. The PSDR leader said that the PDAR Leader Victor Surdu was attempting "to draw the electorate of his party onto a certain zone in order to justify the PDAR shift to opposition." Anyway, the ruling PSDR will treat the PDAR "as an opposition party with all the consequences resulting thereof," said Adrian Nastase.
