930402 FT 02 APR 93 / Observer: On the record Hard-bitten media bosses with a jaundiced eye for journalists' expense claims may be sceptical of stories by reporters in the Balkans that they've been robbed of cars, equipment and large amounts of cash by gunmen at Serb checkpoints near Sarajevo. But the hacks have corroborating evidence. For one thing, their complaints have been taken up by the United Nations Protection Force with the Bosnian Serb military command. For another, in many cases the gunmen gave them receipts for what was stolen.
