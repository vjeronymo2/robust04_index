Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] On 29 December 1993, the prime minister issued a decision on the exportation of timber and forestry products. The decision contains the following main points: -- Timber products including raw (?and processed) timber and forestry products entitled to export must include complete products, which are processed in accordance with required technical standards. -- The names, categories, shapes, and measurements of these products are defined in the list attached to this decision. -- To export these products, exporters must provide the correct names of products, their unit quantities, nature, measurements, qualities, and unit prices. They must also provide pictures of products or product samples. As for those products with complex shapes, there must be descriptive color photographs. -- All export contracts and attached photographs should be original ones with signatures and seals of the sellers and the signatures and seals, if any, of the buyers. The decision also sets forth a number of measures to deal with the exportation of timber and forestry products other than those cited in the attached list.
