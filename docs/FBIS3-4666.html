Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Phnom Penh 28 Feb (AKP) -- A delegation led by Prince Kromkhun Norodom Sirivut, deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs and international cooperation, will soon pay a visit to Israel and Egypt at the invitations of the two countries' foreign ministers. This is the first visit by a Cambodian delegation to the two countries since the formation of the Royal Government of Cambodia in September 1993.
