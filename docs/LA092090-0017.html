September 20, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Hosts With the Most: Exiled New York Yankees owner George Steinbrenner, "All My Children" star Susan Lucci, musician Paul Simon and film actors Patrick Swayze, Christian Slater and Tom Hanks will be among the guest hosts this season for "Saturday Night Live," producer Lorne Michaels announced. . Steinbrenner's date on the show is Oct. 20. Kyle MacLachlan, who plays FBI agent Dale Cooper on ABC's "Twin Peaks" will host "SNL's" 16th season premiere Sept. 29, with Sinead O'Connor the musical guest. BETH KLEID
