BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Jun (INA) -- Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf, head of the Iraqi delegation to the meetings of the Nonaligned Movement [NAM], met here today with Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah. Basindwah explained to al-Sahhaf the plots being hatched against Yemen's unity with the aim of undermining the people's achievements. The Yemeni minister stressed his country's rejection of any interference in its domestic affairs by any party because what is happening in Yemen is an internal affair and because the legitimate authority and the people are determined to preserve unity and to side with the constitutional government to foil the objectives of the separatists. For his part, Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf reiterated Iraq's firm position in support of the legitimate government in fraternal Yemen and preserving its unity. During the meeting, they also discussed the current Arab situation, ways of enhancing joint Arab action, and the items on the agenda of the nonaligned meeting in Cairo. The meeting was attended by Dr. Nabil Najm, Iraq's permanent envoy to the Arab League.
