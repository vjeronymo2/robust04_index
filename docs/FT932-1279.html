930624 FT 24 JUN 93 / Observer: Another place Tired but still good humoured after a hard day, former UK foreign secretary Lord Carrington got into the car waiting to take him to a function at Madame Tussaud's. 'Now to the Chamber of Horrors,' he told the chauffeur, then dozed off in the back. Waking up as the car stopped with a jerk, he found he was at the House of Lords.
