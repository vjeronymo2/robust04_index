BFN [Text] A source close to the General Headquarters of the Haitian Armed Forces [FADH] points out that transfers have been carried out within the military institution. Thus, Lieutenant Colonel Erwin Mehu has been transferred from the General Headquarters to occupy the post of commander of the Croix-des- Bouquets garrison, replacing Col. Mondesir Beaubrun who has been transferred to the General Headquarters. The source pointed out that Warrant Officer Lamour of the fourth company and another sergeant of the same garrison have been dismissed from the FADH and will be handed over to the justice department very soon. By the way, the source dismissed reports of the death of Warrant Officer Lamour following possible physical abuse, saying that the warrant officer is just fine.
