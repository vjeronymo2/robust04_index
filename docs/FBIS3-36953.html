Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yevgeniy Belovitskiy] [Text] Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, 10 Feb -- The residents of Sakhalin and the Kurils found themselves "switched off" from Russia's information space today. The local telecommunications workers who are owed 200 million rubles just for last year by the "Sakhalin" television and radio company have expressed complete solidarity with their colleagues on the mainland. The specific nature of the remote island region is that central newspapers take at least a week to get here. The only way of receiving up-to-date news is on the television.
