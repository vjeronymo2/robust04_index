BFN [Text] Rabat, 1 Jun (MAP) -- Moroccan Prime Minister Abdellatif Filali has begun consultations with leaders of the various political parties represented in parliament in accordance with the requirements of the Constitution. Mr. Filali met with Maati Bouabid, leader of the Constitutional Union Party, followed by Mohand Laensar, secretary general of the Popular Movement. On 1 June, the prime minister will meet Mohamed Boucetta, secretary general of the Istiqlal Party; Ahmed Ousman, leader of the National Rally of Independents; and Mohamed Yazghi, deputy secretary general of the Socialist Union of Popular Forces.
