940117 FT 17 JAN 94 / Business Travel: Dearer France - Travel Update Travellers to France face a series of price hikes on cigarettes, petrol and telephone calls, leaving almost no one in the country exempt. From this month, smokers will have to pay 13 per cent more for cigarettes and tobacco on Monday, petrol is up by 2.3 per cent, and domestic telephone calls will be invoiced on the basis of a three-minute chat, instead of the previous six-minute base rate.
