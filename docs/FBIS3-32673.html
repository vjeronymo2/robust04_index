Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Bolivia through 10 February: The Andean Development Corporation, CAF, and Bolivian Government Oil Deposits, YPFB, signed a $20 million loan agreement on 28 January. This credit, which will not have to be secured by government collateral, is the first of its kind and will be earmarked for the purchase of supplies and drilling equipment to step up production. (La Paz Television Boliviana Network in Spanish 1700 GMT 28 Jan 94) National Statistics Institute Director Luis C. Jemio Mollinedo reported that the January inflation rate was 0.83 percent, the lowest rate in the past few years. The cumulative inflation rate was 9.53 percent in 1993. (La Paz HOY in Spanish 2 Feb 94 p 5)
