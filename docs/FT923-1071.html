920925 FT 25 SEP 92 / International Company News: Westpac issue under-subscribed WESTPAC, the leading Australian financial services group, yesterday announced that its ADollars 1.2bn (USDollars 867.67m) share issue had closed 72.6 per cent under-subscribed. The bank announced that only 111.28m of the 405.62m shares offered had been taken up. AMP Society, Australia's biggest insurance group, took up its full subscription, but other shareholders took up only 50.44m shares. Westpac shares fell 9 cents to ADollars 2.90 on Australian stock exchanges yesterday.
