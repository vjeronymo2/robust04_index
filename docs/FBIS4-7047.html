BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 23 (KCNA) -- High citations of the state and gifts were awarded to the participants in the national meeting of coal industrial workers which was held in Pyongyang from April 20 to 22. The title of labour hero of the DPRK were awarded to nine participants, citations of the great leader President Kim Il-song to 16, citations of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to 86, the title of merited coal miner to 31, the Order of National Flag first class to 62, the Labour Order to 480, the Order of National Flag second class to 821 and the Order of National Flag third class to 814 at the awarding ceremony on April 22. Testimonials of President Kim Il-song were awarded to 390 and gifts of Comrade Kim Chong-il to the entire participants of the meeting. The speakers at the awarding ceremony said that the commendation was a token of deep political trust and benevolent love of President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il who always reared the coal miners in their warm bosoms and put them in the foreground. They vowed to overfulfil the coal production plan for the period of adjustment in order to repay this solicitude with loyalty.
