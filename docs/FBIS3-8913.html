Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sarajevo, March 17 (AFP) -- The Bosnian Government and Bosnian Serbs on Thursday signed an agreement to open roads and partially lift the siege of the Bosnian capital Sarajevo, officials announced. Under the UN-sponsored agreement, Sarajevo will be linked by road to central Bosnia-Herzegovina via the Moslem majority town of Visoko (25 kilometres) to the west and other road corridors will be opened.
