BFN [Text] Vietnam and Tunisia have signed a trade agreement for cooperation in investment, tourism, agriculture, culture, education, and other fields. The agreement came during a visit by Vietnamese Deputy Prime Minister Nguyen Khanh, who was received by Prime Minister Hamed Karoui. The Tunisian prime minister said: The Dien Bien Phu Victory won by the Vietnamese people has strongly inspired the struggle for national independence in north Africa and Tunisia in particular.
