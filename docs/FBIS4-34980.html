BFN [Text] Clinics continue to distribute Neovitamin II pills for free in the province, following the arrival of new shipments of this product. The vitamin's sporadic distribution over the past two months has been corrected. Health personnel want to maintain a steady supply of this preventive complex, as has been done for several months. The people are reminded of how important it is to take Neovitamin II, which is rich in B complex and other vitamins the body needs.
