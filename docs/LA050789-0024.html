May 7, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WHERE'S GETTY?

I wish to protest the fiction, which the Los Angeles Times connives at, that the J. Paul Getty Museum is in Malibu. It is in Pacific Palisades, and therefore in the city of Los Angeles.

In the interests of truth and accuracy, to which I believe the Times is committed, I ask that this erroneous designation be rejected in the future.

H. A. KELLY

Pacific Palisades

Lori Starr, head of public information for the Getty, says the museum has a Malibu address because several hundred feet of the property facing the ocean is in Malibu. The much larger portion of the acreage is in the Pacific Palisades area of Los Angeles.
