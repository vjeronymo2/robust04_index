Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ministry of Defense has confirmed that Minister Kobie Coetsee is engaged in talks with a PAC [Pan-Africanist Congress] delegation in Pretoria. Earlier the PAC said that members of its Executive Committee, including Mr. Benny Alexander and Mr. Barney Desai, were to have talks with the government today. The talks follow the PAC's announcement that it has suspended its armed struggle. The agenda for today's talks is expected to include possible amnesty for APLA [Azanian People's Liberation Army -- PAC military wing] members, joint command of the security forces, and PAC participation in the Transitional Executive Council.
