Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Long lines of tanker trucks carrying fuel have formed at the Kakavyia border checkpoint with Albania following the recent Finance Ministry decision to allow only five tanker trucks from Kakavyia and one from the checkpoint at Kristallopiyi to enter Albania. The Finance Ministry made its decision after the number of tanker trucks crossing the border to Albania increased following the adoption of the measures against Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM]. There are many signs that these trucks used Albanian territory as a route to Skopje. According to the Finance Ministry decision, Albania's daily fuel needs are fully covered by the six tanker trucks that go through every day.
