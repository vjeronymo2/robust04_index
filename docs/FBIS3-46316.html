Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [By Nobuyasu Takayama] [Text] Beijing, 20 Jan -- King Sihanouk, who is currently undergoing cancer treatment in Beijing, will probably be able to return to Cambodia in early April because of smooth progress in chemotherapy. This was disclosed on 20 January by the Sihanouk Office in Beijing. Since negotiations between the government and the Pol Pot faction have been deadlocked, expectations for the King's return to break the deadlock have been growing in Cambodia.
