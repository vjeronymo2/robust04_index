Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, March 15 (SNARK)--As a manifestation of "political idiotism" characterized Zurab Achba--the Chairman of Legislation Committee in Abkhazian Parliament, staying now in Moscow--the resolution of Georgian Supreme Council on dissolution of Abkhazian Parliament. In his opinion, this is an attempt to torpedo the peace process. "Instead of starting a peace dialogue we are being abolished", told Achba.
