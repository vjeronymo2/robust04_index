BFN [Text] According to the 28 April edition of LAO DONG newspaper, Customs officers in Ho Chi Minh City recently discovered a container sent via the United Kingdom by an overseas Vietnamese living in America to a Vietnamese citizen with registered residence in Ho Chi Minh City. Various items in the container include air guns, hunting guns, ammunition for hunting guns, 4.5 caliber ammunition, grenades, two pairs of binoculars, two sets of walkie-talkies, one helicopter rotor, one diver's suit, two diver's air tanks, one mine-clearing machine, several other items, and 19 packs of books and papers, the contents of which are being examined by authorized agencies. All the exhibits and related documents have been transferred to Ho Chi Minh City police for further investigation.
