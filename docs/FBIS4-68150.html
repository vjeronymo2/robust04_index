BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on insurgent activities in Peru compiled from Peruvian media monitored through 10 May. The police have arrested SL political leader Elmer Nery Otivo Manturano, 28, aka Comrade Pedro, who operated in the Mantaro Valley. His operations center was located in the township of Chupaca, 9 km west of Huancayo. (Lima EL COMERCIO in Spanish 9 May 94 p A10) On 8 May SL "terrorists" shot to death Belisario Castro Huaman, owner of a restaurant in La Molina District, Lima. The subversives left subversive propaganda behind. (Lima EL COMERCIO in Spanish 9 May 94 p A11) A column of 50 SL "terrorists" invaded the township of Cachicoto, 30 km from Tingo Maria, killed a businessman, and kidnapped two residents, who were probably murdered half an hour later. (Lima EXPRESO in Spanish 10 May 94 p A4)
