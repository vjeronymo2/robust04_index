Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] Tahir Ahmadov, one of the leaders of the working group dealing with the return of the Tajik refugees from the Islamic State of Afghanistan held talks in Sherkhan Bander district, lower Pyanj, with Haji Farid Farukh, acting head of this port [Sherkhan Bander, in Afghanistan]. During the talks, questions related to the return of the Tajik refugees from Afghanistan and political problems in Afghanistan that are hindering the return of the refugees. Currently, more than 600 refugees have gathered in the port wanting to return home.
