941202 FT 02 DEC 94 / Ford to suspend Dagenham output Ford of Britain is to suspend output at its Dagenham plant in Essex, which makes Fiesta cars and vans, for seven days this month, taking nearly 4,000 vehicles out of production. Further cuts are also expected at Ford's Escort plant on Merseyside, where output of 20,000 Escorts has already been lost through short-time working since October.
