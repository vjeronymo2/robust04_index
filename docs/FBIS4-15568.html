BFN [Text] Members of the All-Union Communist Party of Bolsheviks today picketed the U.S. Embassy in Moscow. In the opinion of the pickets, the authorities in the United States, Japan, and South Korea are conducting a provocative policy toward the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The party members believe socialist Korea is sticking in the throats of various reactionary forces in the world, and so all progressive people on the planet should show solidarity with the heroic people of North Korea.
