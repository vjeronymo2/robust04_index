March 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

KINGS HAVE NO DEFENSE FOR THEIR POOR DEFENSE

Once again, the Kings have made the trade (they think) will bring the Stanley Cup to Los Angeles.

I think that's seven years in a row now!

Rogie Vachon should know, better than anyone else, what makes a goaltender and his team successful: defense.

A goaltender is only as good as the people playing in front of him. Unfortunately for the Kings' goaltenders, the people in front of them are really good. The problem is that they are always wearing the wrong jerseys.

THOMAS FOURNIER

Torrance
