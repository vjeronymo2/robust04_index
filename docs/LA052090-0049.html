May 20, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

TO THE RESCUE

To all the people who worked on "Cartoon All-Stars to the Rescue," the anti-drugcartoon for children, I would like to say a big thank you! My children loved the show and learned a lot.

ItUs so nice to see everyone pulling together for the children. They are our future. HereUs to the children, for a bright and drug-free future!

Judy Ingels, Culver City
