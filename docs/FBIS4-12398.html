BFN [Text] At 1230 today, His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad received Hojjat ol-Eslam Hasan Ruhani, Iranian deputy Majles speaker and chairman of the Majles Foreign Relations Committee, and the delegation accompanying him. The meeting was attended by Dr. 'Adil Jamus, deputy speaker of the People's Assembly; Shakir Is'ayyid, chairman of the Arab and Foreign Relations Committee of the People's Assembly; and Iranian Ambassador in Damascus Mohammad Hasan Akhtari-Foruqi. Ruhani conveyed to the president two messages. The first message is from Ayatollah 'Ali Khamene'i, leader of the Islamic revolution in Iran. The second message is from Hojjat ol-Eslam Akbar Hashemi- Rafsanjani, president of the Islamic Republic of Iran. Talks dealt with the situation and developments in the region and bilateral relations.
