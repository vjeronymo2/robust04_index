930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / Algiers extends emergency Algerian leaders have extended indefinitely the state of emergency, imposed a year ago after suspended elections which the Islamic Salvation Front was poised to win, writes Francis Ghiles. The country's five-man presidency, meeting under head of state Mr Ali Kafi, said at the weekend that it had extended the emergency, in what was interpreted as a demonstration of its determination to crush Islamic fundamentalism.
