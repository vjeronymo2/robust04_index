940708 FT 08 JUL 94 / Goldstar plant will create 431 jobs Goldstar Electric is to create up to 431 jobs at a new factory making colour televisions and microwave ovens in Washington, Tyne and Wear. The Pounds 26m project will receive Pounds 4m Regional Selective Assistance from the Department of Trade and Industry. The company, a wholly owned subsidiary of Goldstar Corporation of Korea, set up a microwave oven plant at Jarrow, Tyne and Wear in 1989. The 44 staff there will transfer to the new site. The plant is expected to be operational within a year and by 1999 be producing 579,000 televisions a year and 600,000 microwave ovens.
