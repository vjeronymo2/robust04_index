BFN [Text] Tirana, April 11 (XINHUA) -- Albanian Parliament Speaker Pjeter Arbnori left here today for China for an official visit at the invitation of the Chinese National People's Congress (NPC). Arbnori, who is making the first visit of its kind in about 20 years, will be the highest-ranking Albanian official to travel to China since the Albanian Democratic Party came to power in 1992. In a recent interview with XINHUA, Arbnori said the visit will offer him a chance to learn more about the NPC's work, China's experience in building the legal system and the progress in China's economy.
