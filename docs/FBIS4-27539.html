BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 1 (KCNA) -- Relays of members of the Korean Children's Union (KCU) carrying red neckerchiefs to the great leader Generalissimo Kim Il-song started in cities and counties throughout the country on the threshold of the 5th KCU Congress slated for early June here. Starting ceremonies took place there. The relay groups were entrusted with red neckerchiefs carrying the firm resolve of the KCU members to continue forever on the road of the march starting from Mt. Paektu following the dear leader Mr. Kim Chong-il like revolutionary forerunners who had fought bloody battles for long years for the liberation of the country and for the freedom and emancipation of the people, flying high the red flag of revolution with President Kim Il-song in the van of their ranks. KCU members and people of broad strata warmly sent off the relay groups on streets and villages, playing wind instruments, waving bunches of flowers and scattering confetti.
