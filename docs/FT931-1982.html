930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Trace Computers halved to Pounds 105,000 Trace Computers, a supplier of computer software, hardware and related services, reported a drop in pre-tax profits from Pounds 221,000 to Pounds 105,000 in the half year ended November 30. Turnover fell from Pounds 9.72m to Pounds 8.84m. Earnings fell to 0.78p (1.52p) per share and the interim dividend is held at 0.55p.
