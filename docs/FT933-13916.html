930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Second-half fall at Jurys Hotel A second-half shortfall at Jurys Hotel Group left the Irish hotel operator with pre-tax profits down from IPounds 2.51m to IPounds 2.32m (Pounds 2.17m) in the year ended April 30, on turnover little changed at IPounds 26.3m, against IPounds 26.1m. Earnings per share dropped from 8.3p to 7.6p. The final dividend is maintained at 3p for an unchanged total of 5p. Profits in the first half had risen 4 per cent to IPounds 2.16m.
