Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Experts from the Council of Heads of Administrations attached to the Russian president have suggested to Boris Yeltsin that he should set up commissions to bring the constitutions of the republics within Russia in line with the constitution of the Russian Federation. Analysis carried out recently has revealed that the constitutions adopted in Tatarstan, Bashkortastan, and Tyva not long ago contradict the Russian Constitution in a number of norms.
