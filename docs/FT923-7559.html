920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / Health reforms are criticised FUNDAMENTAL flaws in the government's Care in the Community reforms are set to plunge the health service into chaos, claims a report by the Kings Fund Institute, an independent health policy think-tank. Changes in who pays for the care of frail, elderly people are likely to result in a shortage of hospital beds says the report, written by Ms Melanie Henwood, a fellow of the institute.
