BFN [Text] Beijing, June 22 (XINHUA) -- Beijing is to hold a large electronics products investment and trade fair here June 28-30 to promote overseas investment in the sector. The city will offer more than 70 investment projects for discussion at the fair. The projects include mobile telecom equipment, color monitors, satellite television receiving systems, optical user terminals and automatic tellers. Wang Dishan, deputy-director of the Electronics Industry Office of the Beijing Municipal Government, said that the coming fair has received active responses from over 70 international companies and agencies in Beijing from about 20 countries and regions including the United States, Japan and Canada. A famous brands and high quality electronics products exhibition will be held at the same time. The exhibition will display more than 100 models of 20 kinds of products. The electronics will be a pillar industry in Beijing. More than 80 foreign-funded enterprises have been founded in the sector. Wang said that Beijing will offer preferential policies for foreign investors.
