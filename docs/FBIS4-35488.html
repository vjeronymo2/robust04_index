BFN [Excerpt] Toward the implementation of Articles 27 and 28 of the Majles' internal regulations, the third annual elections for the Majles Presidium during the current fourth term of the Majles were held today. According to our correspondent, 'Ali Akbar Nateq-Nuri was reelected as the Majles speaker, receiving 193 votes. Hasan Rowhani and Hoseyn Hashemian were also reelected as deputy speakers, with 164 votes and 122 votes respectively. Habibollah Askarowladi-Mosalman, Gholam Abbas Za'eri, and Seyyed Reza Taqavi were elected as commissioners [karpardaz] in charge of supplies and administration. According to the election results, Hoseyn Sobhaninia; Mohsen Yahyavi; Mohammad Reza Bahonar; Mohammad Karim Shahrzad; Javad Angaji; and Akramis [name as heard] were elected as secretaries of the Majles Presidium.
