941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / Howard launches identity card plan Proposals for identity cards will be set out in a green paper in the spring, home secretary Michael Howard told the conference, raising the prospect that citizens may be forced to carry ID cards for the first time since 1952. Mr Howard (pictured on a video screen above the conference platform) focused on a voluntary card but would not rule out making it compulsory. Report, Page 18; Editorial Comment, Page 17
