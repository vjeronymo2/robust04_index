920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / UN and Iran envoys arrive in Azerbaijan Mr Cyrus Vance, the UN special envoy flew to Azerbaijan yesterday to try to find a way to end the republic's bloody conflict with Armenia over the disputed territory of Nagorno-Karabakh, Reuter reports from Baku, Azerbaijan. More than 1,500 people have died in four years of fighting over the enclave, mainly populated by Armenians but located inside Azerbaijan. The Iranian deputy foreign minister, Mr Mahmoud Vaezi, left for Baku yesterday to follow up Tehran's mediation in the Nagorno-Karabakh conflict, the Iranian news agency, Irna, reported, Reuter reports from Nicosia. Mr Vaezi will try to build on a draft truce signed in Tehran last Sunday.
