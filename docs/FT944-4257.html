941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / BMA seeks talks with Bottomley British Medical Association leaders yesterday sought a meeting with Mrs Virginia Bottomley, health secretary, in an effort to avert a dispute over performance-related pay. Leaders of hospital consultants have prepared plans for sanctions if the government tries to implement a shift from national pay arrangements without agreement. After a meeting of the BMA's consultants' committee yesterday Mr Jim Johnson, its chairman, said details of sanctions planned would not be divulged until later.
