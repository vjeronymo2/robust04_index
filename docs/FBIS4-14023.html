BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Apr (MENA) -- PLO Executive Committee member Mahmud 'Abbas, alias Abu-Mazin, left Cairo for Tunis at noon today at the end of a two-day [as received] visit to Egypt. Palestinian Ambassador in Cairo Zuhdi al-Qidrah, who saw Abu-Mazin off, said that during his visit to Cairo the Palestinian official was informed of the progress in the Palestinian-Israeli talks. He also met members of the Palestinian delegation to the negotiations and gave guidance regarding some issues on the negotiating table. Al-Qidrah said Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat would arrive in Cairo in the next few hours to meet President Muhammad Husni Mubarak and U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher during his visit to Egypt tomorrow. The talks will deal with developments in the peace process.
