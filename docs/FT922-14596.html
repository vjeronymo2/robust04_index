920406 FT 06 APR 92 / Construction Contracts: Overseas orders for Bovis Group Hard on the heels of the Atlanta Olympics contract, BOVIS companies in the US have won three major contracts, one in Indianapolis, one in Chicago and the third in Tokyo. In Indianapolis, Lehrer McGovern Bovis will manage the construction of a new maintenance operations centre for United Airlines. Occupying a 300 acre site at Indianapolis International Airport, the USDollars 1bn (Pounds 588m) project includes building and fitting out hangers, airframe repair shops, engine repair shops, a central utilities plant, support facilities and site works. The recently-acquired Schal Associates is to begin work soon in Chicago on the redevelopment of the city's 76 year-old Navy Pier. As general contractor on the 30-month project, Schal Associates will manage all aspects of construction involved in the creation of an 'urban playground', with five parks, a Ferris wheel, ice skating rink, Festival Hall and family Pavilion Building. In Tokyo, Schal Associates is the sole foreign member of a joint venture headed by Kajima to build the east wing of the Tokyo Telecom Center, a 21-storey 792,000 sq ft office building on the city's waterfront. The total project value is over USDollars 450m (Pounds 265m).
