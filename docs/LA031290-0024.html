March 12, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BUCHANAN ON COLLABORATORS

Buchanan's column is the worst poison-pen writing I have read for some time.

To equate the efforts of "liberals" to obtain decent living and working conditions for American citizens with Nazi collaboration to murder millions of civilians is not only incredible, it is satanic.

Surely the American right to free speech allows any citizen to talk and to work politically for fair and decent living conditions without the kind of moral slander and smearing Buchanan expresses. Why did The Times allow such toxic material to be printed?

JANE OLIVER-ALLEN

Lucerne Valley
