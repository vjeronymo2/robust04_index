BFN [Text] The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army [KPA], visited a KPA unit and congratulated officers and soldiers of the People's Army on the occasion of the 62d anniversary of the founding of the KPA. Comrade Kim Il-song, the great leader of our party and people, and Comrade Kim Chong-il, chairman of the DPRK National Defense Commission and supreme commander of the KPA, today visited the 564th Unit of the KPA on the occasion of the 62d anniversary of the founding of the heroic KPA, and congratulated officers and men of the People's Army. Soldiers of the unit enthusiastically welcomed the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the supreme commander Comrade Kim Chong-il, shouting thunderous cheers. Bouquets containing the sense of boundless reverence of all officers and men of the People's Army were presented to the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the supreme commander Comrade Kim Chong-il. The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander, were greeted on the spot by Comrade O Chin-u, first vice chairman of the DPRK National Defense Commission and minister of People's Armed Forces; Comrade Choe Kwang, vice chairman of the DPRK National Defense Commission and chief of the General Staff of the KPA; Comrades Yi Ul-sol, (Kim Pong-yol), and Kim Kwang- chin, members of the DPRK National Defense Commission and vice marshals of the KPA; Comrades Choe In-tok, Paek Hak-nim, and Kim Ik- hyon, vice marshals of the KPA; Comrades Yi Ha-il, Kim Tu-nam, Yi Pong-won, and Kim Myong-kuk, generals of the KPA; and other KPA generals and Army unit commanders. The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and supreme commander Comrade Kim Chong-il warmly congratulated officers and men of the People's Army who are marking the 62d anniversary of the founding of the KPA, and comprehended the military life of the soldiers by meeting them. The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the supreme commander Comrade Kim Chong-il expressed great satisfaction for the fact that our People's Army, which achieved perfect harmony [honyon ilche] with the party and the leader, is excellently fulfilling its honorable mission and duties as a reliable defender of the chuche revolutionary cause--the socialist cause--and inspired and encouraged soldiers of the unit who are preparing themselves to be one-a-match-for-one-hundred guards. Soldiers of the unit were filled with firm determination to tenaciously defend and protect the party's revolutionary cause with force of arms by further strengthening combat capabilities of the unit. The great leader Comrade Kim il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander, posed for a picture with officers and men of the unit.
