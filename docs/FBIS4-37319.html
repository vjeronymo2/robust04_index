BFN [Text] National Assembly Speaker Sa'di Mahdi Salih last night received a delegation of the Yemeni House of Representatives headed by Muhammad 'Abdallah al-Kibsi, who is currently visiting Iraq. During the meeting, they discussed the fraternal bilateral parliamentary ties and the means to promote them. They also reviewed the current state of affairs on the Arab and international scenes, as well as the conspiracies being hatched against the Arab nation to undermine its unity. Muhammad 'Abdallah al-Kibsi addressed the developments in Yemen, and the imperialist conspiracies concocted against it in an attempt to undermine its security, sovereignty, and national unity. Several National Assembly members attended the meeting.
