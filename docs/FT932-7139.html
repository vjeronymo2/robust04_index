930525 FT 25 MAY 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa GOLD stocks put on a further 5 per cent in active trading with most of the interest again coming from foreign investors. The index advanced 91 to 1,885 with some of the biggest gains coming from smaller mining groups. Doorns, for instance, jumped by R1.20, or 24.4 per cent to R6.15. The industrial index rose 15 to 4,526 and the overall gained 46 to 4,032.
