Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Jan (MENA)--PLO Political Department Chief Faruq Qaddumi left Cairo for Geneva this morning after a two-day visit to Egypt, during which he signed an agreement for economic cooperation between Egypt and the PLO. It is worth noting that Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres will meet in Davos, Switzerland, on Saturday.
