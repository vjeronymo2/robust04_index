930406 FT 06 APR 93 / Fall in French GDP forecast Rexecode, an economic forecasting institute, said France's gross domestic product could fall by 0.5 per cent in 1993, Reuter reports from Paris. It also said the unemployment rate would continue rising, averaging 11.2 per cent over 1993 and 11.7 per cent in 1994. It ended 1992 at 10.5 per cent.
