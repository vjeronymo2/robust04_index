October 15, 1989, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

MOTHER TERESA TO LEAVE HOSPITAL AFTER A MONTH

Nobel Peace Prize winner Mother Teresa will soon be able return home after more than a month in the hospital with a heart condition, her doctors said Friday.

Mother Teresa, 79, was hospitalized Sept. 5 and fitted with a temporary pacemaker a few days later to help an irregular heartbeat caused by a slightly blocked artery.
