920212 FT 12 FEB 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG was held back by the weak financial rand. The JSE all-share index gained 6 to 3,666 in low volume. The all-gold index put on 3 to 1,287, with Vaal Reefs unchanged at R224 and Southvaal shedding R1 to R73. Industrials rose 9 to 4,490.
