930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / International Company News: Jardine motor subsidiary ahead 145% JARDINE International Motor, the car distribution arm of the Jardine group, yesterday surprised the Hong Kong market with a 145.5 per cent rise in net 1992 earnings to USDollars 59.4m, from USDollars 24.2m in 1991. Turnover advanced 44 per cent to USDollars 910.9m. The directors recommended a final dividend of 4.95 cents per share, making 6.01 cents for the year - nearly 52 per cent up on 1991. Mr Simon Keswick, chairman, said the record profits resulted from 'excellent sales' of Mercedes-Benz passenger cars in Hong Kong and China. The results for these markets reached record levels, led by outstanding sales of the new Mercedes-Benz 'S' class models.
