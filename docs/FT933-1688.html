930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / World News in Brief: Train crashes into alligator swamp At least 41 people were killed and 12 were missing after a passenger train crashed into an alligator-infested swamp near Mobile, Alabama. Most of those missing were in a submerged carriage. The Foreign Office said it knew of 15 Britons on the train, all of whom were accounted for.
