BFN [Report by Nikolay Semena: "Washington's Stance on Crimea"] [Text] Simferopol -- The U.S. Embassy in Kiev has issued a press statement saying that the "United States supports the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine." When asked by your IZVESTIYA correspondent to inform journalists of the content of Bill Clinton's letter to the Crimean president, [Crimean President] Yu. Meshkov said: "I have not received U.S. President Clinton's consent to publish this kind of correspondence."
