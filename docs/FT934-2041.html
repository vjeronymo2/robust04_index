931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / Croats release 500 Moslems Bosnian Croats yesterday freed over 500 more Moslem prisoners of war, pressing ahead with a promised unilateral release of some 4,300 before Christmas, Reuter reports from Zagreb. There was no sign that the Moslem-led government army and rebel Serbs would reciprocate by freeing their detainees. The International Committee of the Red Cross said it was negotiating with both.
