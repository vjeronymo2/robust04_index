930427 FT 27 APR 93 / UK Company News: German buy for Senior Engineering Senior Engineering has paid DM10.6m (Pounds 4.3m) for Polenz, a Hamburg-based distributor of air conditioning equipment. An additional payment of up to DM2.75m is dependent on profits. Polenz has 11 branches and operates in both east and west Germany. Sales in 1992 amounted to DM36m and profit before tax was DM1.8m.
