921023 FT 23 OCT 92 / UK Company News: James Smith Estates ahead 22% James Smith Estates, the USM-quoted property investment group, lifted interim profits by 22 per cent despite bad debts and increased interest charges. With gross rental income ahed to Pounds 1.18m (Pounds 934,000), pre-tax profits for the six months to September 24 advanced to Pounds 689,000 (Pounds 567,000). Administrative expenses of Pounds 169,000 (Pounds 139,000) included Pounds 20,000 for bad debts. The interim dividend is increased from 1.25p to 1.4p, on earnings of 2.99p (2.47p) per share.
