940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Purchases lift Scott Pickford Two acquisitions helped Scott Pickford, the USM-quoted petroleum consultant, more than double pre-tax profit and turnover for the year to March 31. Pre-tax profits advanced to Pounds 498,000 (Pounds 231,000), on turnover ahead from Pounds 2.33m to Pounds 4.78m. The figures included an eight-month contribution from Gaps Geological Consultants and three months from Samuel Denison, a manufacturer of materials testing equipment. Mr Tony Burch, chairman, said the higher oil price since the year end had increased confidence and would work through to improved trading in the current year. Earnings per share rose from 0.93p to 5.25p, and the single final dividend is 0.9p (0.8p).
