940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Restructuring helps YJ Lovell YJ Lovell (Holdings), the builder and property developer, reported reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.13m for the six months to end-March, against a deficit of Pounds 3.93m in the comparable period. The outcome was struck on turnover down from Pounds 106m to Pounds 102m, of which continuing operations contributed Pounds 96.4m (Pounds 99.9m). Net on-balance sheet gearing at the period end was reduced to 43 per cent, against 180 per cent in 1993. On and off-balance sheet borrowings totalled Pounds 57m, giving total gearing of 97 per cent, against Pounds 125m and 302 per cent respectively last year. Losses per share came out at 6.4p (47.2p).
