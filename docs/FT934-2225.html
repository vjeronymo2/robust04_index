931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / Major agrees to discuss GCHQ The prime minister has agreed to meet civil service union leaders soon to discuss the government's ban on unions at its intelligence gathering centre in Cheltenham. The meeting will be the first time since he came to office that Mr John Major has met a high-level union delegation. Mr David Hunt, the employment secretary, said that the government's position over the ban on unions at GCHQ had not changed.
