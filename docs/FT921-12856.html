920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / International Capital Markets: Tokyo broker to acquire affiliate MITO Securities, a medium-sized brokerage house listed on the second section of the Tokyo Stock Exchange, is to acquire its affiliate, Kojima Shoken, as of May 1, Reuter reports from Tokyo. Mito Securities said one Kojima share would be exchanged for 0.8 of a Mito share. Mito, capitalised at Y10.83bn, currently holds 5 per cent of Kojima, which is capitalised at Y73m and is unlisted. Mito Securities said the merger was aimed at expanding its sales network and improve efficiency. There had been market speculation on the merger since last week that it may trigger a string of mergers between small affiliated securities houses.
