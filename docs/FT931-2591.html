930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables This weeks best fruit buys are Spania strawberries at 90-99p per 8oz punnet, reports the FFVIB. seeded grapes at 99-Pounds 1.20 a lb and seedless varieties at Pounds 1.40-1.60 a lb remain top quality along with Kiwifruit at 8-12p each and bananas at 40-55p a lb. William Bon Chretien pears at 50-55p a lb, oranges at 10-20p each and white fleshed grapefruit at 15-25p each round of this weeks best fruit buys. Breakfast mushrooms at 50-60p a 1/2 lb and buttons at 40-60p a 1/2 lb. English onions at 18-20p a lb, potatoes at 1-14p a lb, and carrots at 18-20p a lb.
