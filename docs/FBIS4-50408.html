BFN [Text] A provincial land management meeting opened in Fuzhou yesterday [12 May]. The main agenda of the meeting is: Summing up and exchanging our province's experiences in land management, and probing the way to further strengthen land management, speed up the establishment of land system, cultivate and develop a standardized real estate market, and so on. Governor Chen Mingyi made an important speech at the meeting. Zou Yuchuan, director of the State Land Administrative Bureau, made a special trip to Fuzhou to attend and address the meeting. Other Fujian leaders who attended the meeting included You Dexin and Liu Yongye, and responsible persons from relevant departments directly under the provincial authorities, as well as leaders in charge of land management in various prefectures, cities, counties and districts. At the meeting, Governor Chen Mingyi and other leaders presented awards to 79 advanced units that had been commended by the provincial government for doing well in 1993 in opening up cultivated land, and serving the project of developing cultivated land.
