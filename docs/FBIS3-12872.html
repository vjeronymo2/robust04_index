Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin said that the Gaza-Jericho agreement can be implemented within six weeks. Our political correspondent Shlomo Raz reports that Mr. Rabin made the remarks to an EC delegation led by the Greek foreign minister. The prime minister also said in the meeting that he would be ready to accept an international presence only in Gaza and Jericho after the Palestinians take over those areas. He also told the guests that Israel is interested in the resumption of the negotiations with the PLO as soon as possible, but he would not be prepared to reopen agreed upon accords.
