Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa in a luncheon meeting with top leaders of the four major economic organizations on 12 January talked about the outlook on the House of Councilors' deliberations of the political reform bills, and said, "Enactment of the bills on 19 January will be difficult." Following the luncheon meeting, an official of the four organizations told reporters, "the prime minister said he wants to have the bills passed on 19 January and to have the next Diet session revise them." But in answer to reporters' question at the prime minister's mansion in the evening, Hosokawa denied the story, saying: "That is not what I said. They asked me if I expected the bills to be passed on 19 January. I told them that will probably be difficult."
