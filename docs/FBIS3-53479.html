Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sidi Barrani, 17 Jan (MENA)--Information Minister Safwat al-Sharif arrived in Sidi Barrani this morning on his way to Tripoli. Al-Sharif is expected to meet Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, leader of the Libyan revolution, tonight. The meeting is part of the consultations between President Husni Mubarak and Col. al-Qadhdhafi on topics of common concern.
