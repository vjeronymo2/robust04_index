BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Novikov] [Text] Murmansk June 2 TASS -- Greek Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias, currently paying an official visit to Russia, arrived in Murmansk today. He will meet Yevgeniy Komarov, head of the regional administration. They are expected to discuss the possibility of greek participation in the development of the economy of the Kola Peninsula. The agenda of the Greek minister's brief stay here includes a sightseeing tour of Murmansk and a visit to the Annunciation Church in the ancient town of Kola. The Greek foreign minister will return from Murmansk to Moscow today.
