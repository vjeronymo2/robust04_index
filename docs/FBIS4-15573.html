BFN [Text] Moscow, April 11 (AFP)--An Azerbaijani air raid on Stepanakert, main city of the Armenian-dominated Nagorno-Karabakh enclave, killed three people and wounded nine, all of them women and children, defence officials there said. The attack came Sunday. A second attempted raid Monday was driven off by anti-aircraft fire, the officials said. They said ground fighting was continuing in the north-east of the enclave, now in its sixth year of war, especially around Mardakert after Azerbaijani forces mounted an offensive.
