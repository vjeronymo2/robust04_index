BFN [Text] This morning in Sarajevo talks began between the top level delegations of the Bosnian and the Croatian part of Bosnia-Herzegovina, led by Bosnia-Herzegovina Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic and Kresimir Zubak. The talks concern the constitution of the bodies of authority and institutions of the Federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina. Charles Redman, special envoy of the U.S. President, is expected to attend. Apart from Kresimir Zubak, the Croatian delegation also consists of Jadranko Prlic, Mariofil Ljubic, Mile Akmadzic, Ivan Bender, and Zoran (Bunjdi).
