921119 FT 19 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Shani ahead and prospects improve Shani Group, the USM-traded maker of womens' and childrens' fashion separates, lifted pre-tax profit by Pounds 51,000 to Pounds 1.96m in the year to July 31. And the current year 'should prove to be much better', following a good start, said Mr Martin Hollis, chairman. The recent acquisition of the Lampert and Admyra coat and suit business complemented the existing operation and enhanced the product range and customer base. 'It has already shown great promise', Mr Hollis stated. The final dividend is raised to 2.8p, bringing the total for the year to 4.4p, against 4p last time. Turnover declined to Pounds 12m (Pounds 13m). Earnings per share rose to 9.2p (8.9p).
