920323 FT 23 MAR 92 / UK Company News: FT-SE Eurotrack 100 Index The FT-SE Eurotrack steering committee has agreed to make the following changes to the FT-SE Eurotrack 100 Index constituent list with effect from Wednesday, April 1: Additions: Alleanza (Italy); Union Electrica Fenosa (Spain); Pirelli SpA (Italy). Deletions: Commerzbank (Germany); Swiss Bank participation certificates (Switzerland); Viag (Germany). The indicative reserve list now comprises: Thyssen (Germany); Elsevier (Netherlands); Michelin B (France); Nestle participation certificates (Switzerland).
