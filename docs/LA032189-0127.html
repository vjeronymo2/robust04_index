March 21, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

GARDEN GROVE;

OPEN HOUSE THURSDAY AT NEW PRINGLE OFFICE

Republican Assemblyman Curt Pringle will have an open house Thursday to celebrate the opening of his new district office in Garden Grove.

The event, which is open to the public, will be from 5:30 to 7:30 p.m. at the new headquarters at 12822 Garden Grove Blvd., Suite A. For more information, call Tony Russo at (714) 537-4477.

Pringle, who was elected last November, represents the city of Stanton and parts of Garden Grove, Santa Ana, Westminster and Anaheim.
