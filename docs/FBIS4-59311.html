BFN [Text] A responsible source has said that His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, the president, continues to follow with concern the developments in Yemen and that his highness is holding continuous sincere contacts and exerting genuine intensified efforts concerning the Yemeni crisis with his brothers the Arab leaders, urging Yemeni officials to immediately stop fighting, exercise self-restraint, and avoid more bloodshed among the sons of the one country. The source added that his highness the president last night received a telephone call from 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice president of the Yemeni Republic, during which he explained to his highness the latest developments in Yemen. During the telephone conversation, His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan expressed the United Arab Emirates' willingness to continue its mediation to restore stability in Yemen on condition there is an immediate cease-fire and a return to true brotherly dialogue in order to reach a political solution to the Yemeni crisis based on the vision of the Yemeni leadership. The responsible source added that his highness the president's appeal to the leaders of Yemen truly reflects his highness's concern to prevent the situation in Yemen from becoming a tragedy with dire results and to prepare the atmosphere for dialogue and understanding among the brothers in Yemen. The appeal also reflects his highness's concern that the brothers in Yemen should fully shoulder their national responsibilities, not widen their differences, and to put the interest of the Yemeni people above all interests because history does not (?forgive).
