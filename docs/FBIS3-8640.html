Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] At its weekly meeting yesterday, the government adopted a decision on the altering of the nationwide minimum monthly gross salary, the payment of which is guaranteed by the government, setting it at 60,000 lei for 178 worked hours [per month]. Among the draft bills that were examined and adopted yesterday, we would like to mention the one referring to Romania's adherence to the General Accord on the Immunities and Privileges of the Council of Europe and the one proposing that parliament ratify the European Convention on the Prevention of Torture and Inhuman or Degrading Punishment or Handling of Persons. The aforementioned documents are intended to make Romania adjust to the institutional standards of the Council of Europe, a communique released by the government says. [passage omitted]
