BFN [From 20 April PAEVALEHT, EESTI SENUMID, p 1, 3; "News Bulletin" No. 75/94] [Text] Wednesday, April 20 -- Commander in Chief of the NATO Northern group of forces General Garry Johnson met President Lennart Meri yesterday. Commander in Chief of the Estonian Defence Forces General Aleksander Einseln, General Johnson's assistant Colonel Bob Hendicott and aide Captain Tim Warrington attended the meeting. General Johnson and President Meri discussed the development prospects of the Estonian Defence Forces. General Johnson assured that the NATO will further support Estonia. He also described the results of his recent visit to Russia. The Russian military leaders assured General Johnson that the troops will be withdrawn from Estonia in an orderly way and no military bases will be established here, the NATO general said. President Meri held a dinner in honour of General and Mrs Johnson. The NATO general also met Foreign Minister Juri Luik and Defence Minister Indrek Kannik yesterday. They discussed Estonian-Russian relations, security in the Baltic region and Estonia's cooperation with NATO. General Johnson is to leave Estonia today.
