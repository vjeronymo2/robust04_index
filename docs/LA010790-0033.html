January 7, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SHADOW OF A DOUBT

Maybe we jump to conclusions too quickly without carefully thinking about the possible damage to the accused, if innocent.

But our intentions are to save the children and catch the guilty. It would not be easy to take the standpoint I am taking now if it were my child that was molested. I feel sorry for the accused, also for the parents of the child, and for the child, who will be damaged for the rest of her life. And I am hateful for the guilty who are still out there hurting more and more innocent victims.

CHELSEY CHAMPION

Encinitas
