920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / European HDTV 'a nightmare' THE European approach to high-definition television (HDTV), which is being developed for improved picture quality, was strongly criticised yesterday as 'a consumer marketing nightmare, doomed from the outset'. Mr John Flaherty, senior vice-president for technology at the US media group CBS, told the FT Television of Tomorrow conference that the European decision to adopt a step-by-step introduction of full HDTV would confuse the consumer. The choice of an HDTV standard compatible with the existing system would not optimise HDTV performance.
