March 8, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

A living room set that was owned by composer Ludwig Von Beethoven until his death in 1827 and was part of his estate brought about $8,333 at an auction Tuesday in Paris. The five-piece ensemble was purchased by an unidentified American. The set consisted of a love seat with a curved back and arms in solid walnut, three fan-backed chairs covered in green felt and a small laminated table. ALEENE MacMINN
