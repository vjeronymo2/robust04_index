941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Julian Hodge rises 12% Julian Hodge Bank, the family-owned Cardiff concern specialising in commercial and industrial lending, improved pre-tax profits by 12 per cent to a record Pounds 9.16m in the year to end-October, compared with Pounds 8.13m. This included a surplus of Pounds 2.69m on the sale of investments. Operating profits rose 36 per cent to Pounds 6.46m (Pounds 4.75m). Mr Jonathan Hodge, chairman, said that despite a difficult environment for new business, the bank had increased its asset base by nearly 15 per cent to Pounds 127m while maintaining a high degree of profitability. Each of the core businesses had continued to make a significant contribution.
