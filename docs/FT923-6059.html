920827 FT 27 AUG 92 / Iran pipeline deal Iran and Turkmenistan have agreed to build a pipeline to deliver the Central Asian republic's gas to Europe via Turkey, the Iranian news agency (IRNA), reported, agencies report. Turkmen President Saparmurad Niyazov signed the agreement with President Akbar Hashemi Rafsanjani during a visit to Tehran on Tuesday, the agency said.
