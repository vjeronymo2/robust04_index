941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports only fair demand. Better liquoring Assams met selective enquiry and prices dropped 3 to 8p following quality. Mediums also came to an easier market, losing 2 to 5p. Plainer descriptions were much lower where sold. Bright Kenyas opened at irregular rates but closed generally 4 to 8p lower, while mediums lost 2 to 4p. Burundis met better competition and prices were fully firm to dearer. Ceylons also came to a slightly lower market. Brighter teas were firm but plainer types again lost ground. Quotations: best available 190p/kg. nom, good 135p/kg., good medium 120p/kg., medium 112p/kg., low medium 84p/kg. nom. The highest price realised this week was 190p for an Assam pd.
