931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / Labour announces tax and employment offensive Labour yesterday announced a tax and employment offensive designed to increase pressure on the Conservatives in the run-up to local and Euro-pean elections, David Owen writes. The campaign will aim partly at maximising the expected adverse public reaction to recently-announced tax increases, some of which will begin to take their toll from April - a month before local government elections and two months ahead of European elections. Mr John Smith, the Labour leader, told his parliamentary colleagues that the Budget was 'unravelling by the hour'. He predicted that when employees received their payslips next April their 'anger' would create 'an immense political reaction against the government'.
