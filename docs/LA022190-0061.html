February 21, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Sale of Zenith Unit in Dispute: Zenith Electronics said it is embroiled in a "major dispute" with Paris-based Groupe Bull over the final sale price of Zenith's computer business. The previously announced deal, completed Dec. 28, called for Zenith to receive $91.9 million plus the adjusted net book value of the assets sold. That value is in dispute.
