February 5, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Santa Ana's Way Off Broadway Theater, a basement troupe going into its third year, has announced six plays for its 1990 season: "Women of Manhattan" by John Patrick Shanley (Feb. 14-March 10); "A Higher Source . . . " by Allison Gappa (April 5-29); "Hunting Cockroaches" by Janusz Glowacki (June 7-30); the tentatively titled "A State of Mind," an audience-participation drama, also by Gappa (Aug. 2-Sept. 2); "Countess Dracula" by Neal DuBrock (Oct. 11-31), and "Oh, Fudge" by Monk Ferris (Dec. 6-Jan. 6, 1991). Information: (714) 547-8997.
