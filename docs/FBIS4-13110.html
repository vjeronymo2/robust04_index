BFN [Text] Phalangist Party leader Dr. George Sa'adah has stated that the government's recent decision regarding the information media has harmed the security and judicial measures and has caused misinterpretations. He stressed his absolute trust in the security services and judicial authorities. He voiced his support for all forms of controls to safeguard the security and judicial measures while preserving the freedom of expression and views. He said: We are concerned about the return of refugees to their villages safe and sound. He expressed support for the General Labor Federation's moves and the right to stage demonstrations.
