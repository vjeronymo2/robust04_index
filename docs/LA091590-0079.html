September 15, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

Channel Islands 30, Hueneme 19 -- A fourth-quarter comeback by Hueneme fell short and Channel Islands' Pharoh Milner scored on a 40-yard run on the last play of the game at Channel Islands.

Channel Islands (2-0) used a strong ground game to build a 24-7 lead after three quarters, but Hueneme quarterback David Andrade completed 13 of 18 passes for 113 yards and a touchdown in the second half to lead his team back.

The Vikings cut the deficit to 24-19 when Rex Barrios scored on a seven-yard run after taking a pitch from Andrade with 3:20 left before Milner put the game away with his long touchdown run.

Renard Carn of Channel Islands, who rushed for 197 yards and three touchdowns in last week's season-opener, left the game late in the second quarter with an ankle injury after gaining 46 yards in seven carries. MIKE BARTLETT
