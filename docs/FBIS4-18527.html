BFN [Text] Ankara--Turkish officials told Robert Lamb, U.S. coordinator for Cyprus Affairs, in Ankara yesterday that the Turkish Cypriot side will not make any concessions. Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin held talks with Robert Lamb yesterday morning and informed him that the changes made in the confidence-building measures [CBM's] are unfair. He also informed Lamb that it is impossible to agree to the changed CBM package. Meanwhile, Ambassador Tugay Ulucevik, deputy under secretary of the Turkish Foreign Ministry who is also responsible for Cypriot affairs, said that the Turkish Cypriot side must not be expected to make additional sacrifices. He said that the Greek Cypriot side is the one that should be approached.
