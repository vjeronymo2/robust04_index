BFN [Text] Beijing, May 12 (XINHUA) -- Zhang Ruiyu, who was convicted in August 1990 of having conspired to overthrow the government by means of religious activities, has been released on parole, sources said here today. Zhang was sentenced to a term of imprisonment by the Putian city Intermediate People's Court of Fujian Province. The court recently ruled according to law that as she had abided by prison regulations and had behaved well during her imprisonment, she was qualified for release on parole.
