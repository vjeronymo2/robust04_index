930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / Algerian militants murder Frenchmen Two French surveyors kidnapped by armed men were found dead yesterday from gunshots to the head, the first foreigners killed in a 20-month-old Moslem fundamentalist insurgency, AP reports from Algiers. Francois Bertelet, 32, and Emmanuel Didion, 25, were found dead yesterday morning outside Sidi bel Abbes, 440 kms southwest of Algiers, a government official said. The two were kidnapped a day earlier while driving to work near the city of Tlelat, where they were involved in a high-tension line project for the French company Herkiq.
