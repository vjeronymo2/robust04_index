931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / Australia jobless rate 11% THE Australian jobless rate jumped to 11.2 per cent last month despite a strong rise in total employment, official figures released yesterday show, Nikki Tait reports from Sydney. This is highest level since December 1992 and the jump is certain to propel the federal government into addressing Australia's long-term unemployment problem. Seasonally-adjusted, the jobless rate rose from 10.9 per cent in September, to stand at 982,000. Only Western Australia posted a fall in unemployment to 9 per cent.
