BFN [Text] The prime minister, Ms. Benazir Bhutto, arrived today in Kathmandu on a three-day official visit to Nepal. She was received at the airport by the prime minister, Mr. Girija Prasad Koirala. Ms. Benazir Bhutto said she was delighted to be in Nepal. She said this is her first official visit to any SAARC [South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation] member country. It shows special importance that Pakistan attaches to developing its relations with Nepal. She said we will try to identify areas where we can cooperate so that we can consolidate our historic friendly relations with Nepal.
