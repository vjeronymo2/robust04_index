930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / London Stock Exchange: ACT shares placed Credit Lyonnais Laing placed 5.88m of the nil-paid new ordinary shares in computer software group ACT in a bought deal. The broker purchased the shares from Singer & Friedlander, the merchant bank, which decided not to take up its rights. ACT launched a four-for-13 rights issue at 123p a share to raise Pounds 50.9m as part of a Pounds 93.5m deal to buy the rival BIS Group. It is thought that the broker bought the shares at around 32.5p apiece and sold them to a variety of institutions at around 35.5p. The nil-paid closed 3 up at 36p. The ordinary shares finished 8 off at 159p ex-dividend.
