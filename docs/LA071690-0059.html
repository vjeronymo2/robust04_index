July 16, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

CHRISTIAN ACTION: Pat Robertson, above, has a new, grass-roots political organization -- the Christian Coalition -- and is gearing up for work on the West Coast.

A local official of the group has been discussing an Oct. 6 rally at Anaheim Stadium that would feature Robertson. Even at a reduced price, the rent would range anywhere from $10,000 plus expenses to a percentage of the gate. "This is a very expensive facility to open up," says the stadium manager. James S. Granelli and Mark I. Pinsky
