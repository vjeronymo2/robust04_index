Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Guatemala City, 23 Feb (DPA) -- Chilean Ambassador to Guatemala Christian Bisquert and Paraguayan Ambassador to Guatemala Gilberto Caniza are being held hostage in the Government Palace in Guatemala City after the palace was occupied today by a group of union members demanding that President Ramiro de Leon Carpio's government increase their wages. Along with the two diplomats, about 1,000 others are also being held hostage at the palace. Some 700 of these people are employees of various government ministries that have offices in the palace, and the rest were people visiting the various offices. The Chilean and Paraguayan ambassadors were at the Guatemalan Foreign Ministry, which is housed on the first floor of the Government Palace, when the siege began. [passage omitted]
