941121 FT 21 NOV 94 / Arts (Openings): Paris The Kirov Opera is in residence at the Theatre des Champs-Elysees for the next three weeks, with staged performances of Tchaikovsky's 'The Queen of Spades', Mussorgsky's 'Khovanshchina' and two Rimsky-Korsakov rarities - 'Sadko' and 'Legend of the Invisible City of Kitezh'. The casts include Kirov favourites Olga Borodina and Galina Gorchakova. All performances are conducted by Valery Gergiev.
