Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, March 3 (YONHAP) -- The Defense Ministry confirmed Thursday that Air Force Chief of Staff Cho Kun-hae, his wife and four others have died in a helicopter crash. All six people aboard the ill-fated craft died after the UH-60 chopper crashed into a small mountain near Yongin County, Kyonggi Province, the ministry said. Cho and his wife left Seoul at 2:25 PM and the helicopter is presumed to have gone down around 10 minutes later. They were on their way to watch the graduation ceremony rehearsal at the Korea Air Force Academy in Chongju. The 56-year-old chief of staff graduated from the Air Force Academy in 1961 and was appointed to the top post last May 27.
