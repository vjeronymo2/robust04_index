BFN [Text] Phnom Penh, June 2 (XINHUA) -- Cambodian Government troops on May 30 began launching attacks against the Khmer Rouge in the southwest of the country, said the Khmer press agency here today. The press agency quoted General Touch Kim Siem [spelling of name as received], commander of the Sihanouk Ville military operation, as saying that the government troops had conducted a three-pronged operation against guerrilla positions in Talan, Chraing Kraham and Khal Chhay, killing three Khmer rouge soldiers, wounding seven and taking 15 others prisoner. After the Pyongyang round-table talks on May 27 and 28 proposed by King Norodom Sihanouk failed to reach an agreement on an immediate ceasefire. [sentence as received]
