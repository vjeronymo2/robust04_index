December 18, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: LA HABRA;

VALLE NEW PRESIDENT OF EDUCATION BOARD

Antonio Valle Jr. is the new president of the La Habra Board of Education, succeeding Nancy S. Zinberg, who was named vice president for 1990.

"Nancy deserves a lot of credit for the parenting goal that has been established for the district," said Supt. Rich Hermann. Zinberg has served 10 years on the board and the 1989 term was her second as president.

Assistant Supt. Ruth Fehr said that Zinberg "does whatever she can to ensure quality education for every student in the district."

Valle was elected to the board in 1980 and begins his second stint as president.

"He is a hard-working board member; he is around the schools and he is around the community," Hermann said. "He's a valuable resource to the district."

Zinberg teaches special education classes for the Fullerton Joint Union High School District. DAVID F. BURKE
