BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 23 Apr (WAKH) -- His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin- Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the United Arab Emirates, met here this evening with Egyptian President Husni Mubarak. The EMIRATES NEWS AGENCY said talks between the two leaders dealt with all joint issues, including the situation in the Gulf, the latest Arab developments, and all that serves the Arab and Islamic nations and their causes. The agency said the two presidents will hold a round of talks tomorrow, Sunday.
