BFN [Text] Iranian Parliament Speaker 'Ali Akbar Nateq-Nuri has underlined the need for establishment of a common trade market of the Economic Cooperation Organization [ECO] member-countries. Speaking at a banquet in his honor hosted by Senate Chairman Wasim Sajjad in Islamabad yesterday, he said pooling the resources of the ECO member-countries and increasing mutual cooperation in all fields are very essential. Nateq-Nuri said bilateral fraternal relations have stood every test of time and the two countries maintain identical views on several regional and international issues. Speaking on the occasion, Senate Chairman Wasim Sajjad called upon Iran to use its influence to persuade India to fulfill its pledge of granting the right of self-determination to the Kashmiri people. He said the peaceful settlement of the Kashmir problem will pave the way for peace and meaningful cooperation among all countries of this region. Sajjad stressed the need for regular consultations and cooperation between Iran and Pakistan, so that the legitimate rights of the Muslims of Kashmir, Palestine, and Bosnia can be protected.
