Language: Finnish Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "IVO Delivers Simulator to Kola Power Station"] [Text] IVO, a subsidiary of the Imatran Voima company, is to deliver a simulator to Russia's Kola Nuclear Power Station for technical failure and emergency management training. The simulator will improve the ability of the operators to control the power station in case of a breakdown or an accident. The simulator can also be used to test the effects of the plans to transform the plant's operations. The delivery, worth about 1 million Finnish markkas, is financed by the Commerce and Industry Ministry and the Foreign Ministry. The delivery has been made within the framework of the safety improvement program for nuclear power stations in Russia. The simulator, which will be shipped to Kola in April, is scheduled to be operational in September.
