930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / UK Company News: LGW 23% ahead with Pounds 538,000 LGW, the USM-quoted marketing and luxury goods group which distributes Pierre Cardin Sun Care products, lived up to its projections with a 23 per cent increase in pre-tax profit for the six months ended June 30. The result was achieved by close attention to margins, overheads and stock levels, the company said. Profit for the first half was Pounds 538,000 (Pounds 438,000) on turnover slightly lower at Pounds 6.76m to Pounds 6.74m. Earnings per share came out at 6.3p (5.2p) and there will be an increased interim dividend of 1.6p (1.125p).
