941103 FT 03 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Jermyn declines to Pounds 55,000 Jermyn Investment, the property investor, reported pre-tax profits for the half year to June 30 lower at Pounds 55,000, against Pounds 77,000. Net property income rose from Pounds 371,000 to Pounds 380,000. The figures related to the period before the recent Pounds 12.5m placing and open offer and acquisitions as part of the company's expansion plans. It said yesterday that a number of transactions were being considered which might lead to further purchases before the year-end. Earnings per share were 1.8p (2.75p) or 1.19p (1.83p) fully diluted. The Pounds 300,000 arrears on the preference shares have been declared and the payment of a dividend, which has not been paid since 1991, will be considered at the year-end.
