920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / UK Company News: BICC lifts stake in Spanish cable maker BICC has increased its holding in Grupo General Cable, the Spanish cable maker, from 55 per cent to 67 per cent. The remaining 33 per cent of the equity continues to be held by GGC managers. BICC however, has granted the GGC management options under which these shares may be sold to BICC over the next eight years, at a price based on the original 1990 arrangements.
