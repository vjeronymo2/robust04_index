February 8, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Hammer Teams with HBO on Miniseries: Russian history in the 20th Century will be the subject of a 10-part fictional HBO miniseries set to be co-produced by industrialist Armand Hammer, whose interest in and influence in the Soviet Union has spanned several decades.

The project, tentatively titled "Mother Russia" and set to air in 1992, will be based on fact. Historical events will provide the backdrop for its focus on individual fictional lives. It will be filmed entirely on location in the Soviet Union, with pre-production scheduled for this fall and shooting to start in February, 1991. CLAUDIA PUIG
