941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / Company News (this Week): Sharp recovery for insurance sector - Other Companies Commercial Union and Royal Insurance: Two of the UK's largest insurance companies, Commercial Union and Royal Insurance, are expected to report sharply higher profits for the first nine months of 1994. reflecting the strength of recovery in the sector. CU results on Wednesday are forecast to show pre-tax profits of about Pounds 285m compared with Pounds 144m last time. RI is expected on Thursday to report pre-tax profits of about Pounds 310m, against Pounds 113m last time.
