BFN [Text] The nationalist conservative group within the Fatherland National Party is convinced that Mart Laar, prime minister and chairman of the Fatherland Party, will no longer be able to continue to be in charge of the government or the party under the present circumstances. This was reported to ETA by the Board of the nationalist conservative group. They have proposed Enn Tarto for the post of chairman of the Fatherland Party. Tarto is the chairman of the nationalist conservative group and a member of the Riigikogu. The Fatherland's Tallinn branch has already proposed [parliamentary Speaker] Ulo Nugis for the job.
