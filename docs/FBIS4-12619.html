BFN [Text] Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam received Salim Salih Muhammad, member of the Yemeni Presidential Council and assistant secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], and Dr. 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Dali, member of the YSP Political Bureau and secretary of foreign relations, at 1800 today. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' and Yemeni Ambassador in Damascus Muhammad 'Abduh Shutfah attended the meeting. Talks dealt with the situation in Yemen. The vice president indicated Syria's eagerness to maintain Yemeni unity and stressed the need to resolve the Yemeni crisis through brotherly dialogue that averts the dangers of bloody conflicts.
