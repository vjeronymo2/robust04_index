Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 1 Feb (TANJUG) -- Representatives of the Socialist Party of Serbia (SPS), Depos, and the Democratic Party (DS) have failed to reach an agreement on the election of the chairman of the Serbian parliament. Dragan Tomic (SPS), Vuk Draskovic (Depos) and Zoran Djindjic (DS) informed parliamentary deputies about this today and gave their explanations. Two new candidates have been proposed -- Dragan Tomic (SPS) and Jorgovanka Tabalovic (Serbian Radical Party). Neither of the initial candidates -- Zoran Arandjelovic (SPS) and Gvozden Rosic (Depos) -- received the necessary majority of votes.
