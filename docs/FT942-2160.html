940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / BBC withdraws from racing talks BBC Television yesterday withdrew from negotiations to renew the contract to broadcast horseracing at Cheltenham, which includes the National Hunt Festival. The BBC, which has covered racing from the course since the 1950s, said Cheltenham had asked for a five-fold increase in its fee. Cheltenham Racecourse said it was still in discussion with Channel 4.
