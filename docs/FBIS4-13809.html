BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres' bureau announced that there is total agreement between Mr. Peres and Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin on the tactics and path to be taken in the negotiations with the PLO. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that this was said in response to the report that there are differences of opinion between the two on the negotiations with the Palestinians, which are focused on the issue of whether Israel must sign an agreement within two weeks.
