BFN [Text] Malaysia cannot accept the statement by Britain which wants the Bosnian Government to accept defeat in its war against the Serbs. Foreign Minister Datuk Abdullah Ahmad Badawi explained that if the statement is accepted, it will mean that Malaysia and the whole world agree with the policy of ethnic cleansing in Bosnia-Herzegovina. According to the minister, the statement made by Britain indicates that the country will not do anything to bring about peace in Bosnia.
