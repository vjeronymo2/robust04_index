Language: Thai Article Type:BFN [Text] General Tea Chamrat, deputy defense minister of Cambodia [title as heard], called on Defense Minister General Wichit Sukmak at the Defense Ministry this morning. After the meeting, Wichit told reporters that they had discussed Cambodia's request for Thai assistance in national development. In particular, Cambodia wants Thai soldiers to provide agricultural training and assistance with military uniforms. The two sides also discussed security along the border. The two sides agreed to cooperate in maintaining peace along the border.
