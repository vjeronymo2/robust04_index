January 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $25 Saturday when the front horse in his parlay, Monsieur Maison, ran second in the second race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,097.

Bob's bets today are $20 daily doubles keying Jetting Home in the first with Zoe Akins and Truly Naughty in the second and a $10 double to Akitori from Jetting Home.
