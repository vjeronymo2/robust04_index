March 23, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

PEREZ DEFENDS TITLE IN IRVINE FIGHT

Tommy Perez of Santa Ana will defend his California super welterweight title tonight against Ryan Hammond of Vallejo in a 12-round fight at the Irvine Marriott.

Perez has a 20-3 record. Hammond is 16-2.

The first bout in the five-fight card begins at 7 p.m.
