Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, December 31 (KCNA) -- A floral basket and a congratulatory letter were sent to the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il by the economic and commercial councillors' corps here on the occasion of the New Year 1994. Yevgeniy Bilim, commercial representative of the Russian Embassy and doyen of the corps, conveyed the floral basket and the letter to an official concerned on December 31.
