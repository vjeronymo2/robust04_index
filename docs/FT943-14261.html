940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / Arts: Today's Television It was, of course, entirely predictable that if two series about sport and health should suddenly turn up, the broadcasters would ensure that they would clash head on so that those really interested would have to use the VCR to record one or the other. Those involved in The Contenders on BBC1 at 7.00 include hurdler Sally Gunnell and cyclist Chris Boardman. Those involved in Body Heat at 7.00 on ITV include Chris Boardman and Sally Gunnell. The BBC programme is concerned today with the effect of diet upon performance. The ITV programme is described as 'a competitive fitness series'. BBC Proms 1994 begins on BBC2 and Radio 3 at 8.00 with Andrew Davis conducting the BBC Symphony Orchestra, the BBC Singers, the Philharmonia Chorus, and the BBC Symphony Chorus, all participating in Schoenberg's cantata 'Gurrelieder'. The soloists include soprano Karita Mattila, mezzo Ann Murray, and tenors Neil Breeden and Philip Langridge. For those who prefer their culture less highbrow, Fancy A Bit celebrates the humour of the seaside postcard (8.45 Radio 2).
