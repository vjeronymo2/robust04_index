November 28, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

NATION;

BUSH, SHAMIR TO MEET DEC. 11

President Bush will meet with Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Shamir for the first time in 10 months on Dec. 11 at the White House, spokesman Marlin Fitzwater announced today. He said that Shamir will be in Washington on a private visit and that Bush will receive him for talks.
