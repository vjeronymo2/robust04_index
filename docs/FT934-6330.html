931125 FT 25 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Falcon Hldgs Pre-tax profits at Falcon Holdings, the valve and pipeline equipment distributor formerly known as Walker & Staff, declined from Pounds 237,000 to Pounds 175,000 in the six months to September 30. Turnover at both Falcon and Walker & Staff, its trading company, fell 14 per cent to Pounds 3.15m (Pounds 3.67m). Operating profits emerged at Pounds 144,000 (Pounds 187,000). Earnings shrank to 5.3p (7.3p) per share and an interim dividend of 3p (nil) is declared.
