Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Due to the spate of reports pertaining to the IDF's [Israel Defense Forces] rules of engagement in the territories and in the wake of the Hebron massacre, the cabinet today adopted a resolution reiterating the Israeli Government's responsibility, through the IDF and the police, for the security of all the residents of the territories, both Jews and Arabs. The cabinet reiterated its commitment to provide maximum security for all the residents of the territories. The resolution stated that the IDF and the security forces had been instructed to exhaust all legal and security measures in order to fulfill this goal. The cabinet's resolution stipulated that the Palestinian police would be responsible for the security of Palestinian residents in the areas it was deployed in. Until the Palestinian police was established, Israel would take all necessary measures to prevent any security vacuum.
