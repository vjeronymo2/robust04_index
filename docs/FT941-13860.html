940122 FT 22 JAN 94 / Finance and the Family: Bradford & Bingley Bradford & Bingley has relaunched its 30-day Direct Notice postal account. Interest rates are 6.35 per cent gross on Pounds 1,000-Pounds 9,999; 6.85 per cent gross on Pounds 10,000-Pounds 24,999 and 7.1 per cent on balances of Pounds 25,000 and above. There is also a monthly income option of 6.65 per cent on balances of Pounds 10,000 and above. The rates are 6.65 per cent gross (Pounds 10,000-Pounds 24,999) and 6.9 per cent gross on Pounds 25,000 and above.
