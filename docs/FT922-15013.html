920403 FT 03 APR 92 / UK Company News: Home Counties pays uncovered dividend Pre-tax profits at Home Counties Newspapers Holdings declined sharply, from Pounds 2.89m to Pounds 1.06m, in the year to December 31, though turnover expanded by Pounds 1m to Pounds 21.5m. Following a proposed final dividend of 5.5p, the unchanged 8.25p total is uncovered by earnings of 7.15p per share. Earnings in 1990 came to 18.5p. Profits of this printer and publisher, already halved at the operating level to Pounds 735,000 (Pounds 1.4m), were further affected by a fall in investment income to Pounds 778,000 (Pounds 1.49m) and by exceptional charges, mostly relating to redundancy costs, of Pounds 458,000.
