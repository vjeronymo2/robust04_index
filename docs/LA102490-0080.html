October 24, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SLAM: Buzz, the new hyperhip, L.A.-based magazine debuting this month, slams Yorba Linda as "this nothing sort of burg" that became "home of the library extolling the ability of one man to make an entire nation ashamed of itself."

Mayor Pro Tem Mark Schwing brushed away the barbs. "There are always extremists. . . . People either love Richard Nixon or they hate him." Writer/commentator Harlan Ellison did include something nice about the county: San Clemente, which rejected the library, "opted for slow growth and being able to look in a mirror without seeing the Swamp Thing." Dennis McLellan and Danny Sullivan
