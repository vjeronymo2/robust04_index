Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi, Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri, and Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri returned to Beirut this evening after taking part in the funeral ceremony of the Syrian president's son, Staff Major Basil al-Asad. The three leaders plus a large number of ministers, deputies, religious and political figures, and popular delegations shared with Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad, the Syrian leadership, and the Syrian people their sadness and pain over the loss of Staff Major Basil al-Asad.
