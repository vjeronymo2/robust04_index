Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] In Ciego de Avila, the first Youth Labor Army [EJT] brigade to cut 1 million arrobas of sugarcane in Cuba has received a letter of congratulations from Army General Raul Castro. The average age of the brigade members is barely 18 years. However, they have been capable of the labor feat of cutting 1 million arrobas in slightly less than 60 workdays. This result places them at the head of the EJT forces participating in the sugarcane harvest and is a repeat of last year's success. [passage omitted] They have once again raised high their battalion flag -- the 531st -- of the Batalla del Ubero Camp in Venezuela Municipality. The brigade members were happy to receive the letter of congratulations from Army Gen. Raul Castro for being an outstanding unit. [passage omitted]
