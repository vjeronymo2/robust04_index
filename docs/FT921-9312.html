920212 FT 12 FEB 92 / International Capital Markets: S-E Banken to open Tokyo office SKANDINAVISKA Enskilda Banken, Sweden's leading banking group, said yesterday it would become the first Nordic bank to open a branch in Tokyo, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. Subject to government approval, the branch is expected to commence activities during the second half of 1992. Initially, it will focus on foreign exchange, and money and capital market transactions.
