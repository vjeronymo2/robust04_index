Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 27 Feb (XINHUA) -- President Jiang Zemin sent a cable message to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat on 26 February to express his strong condemnation of the serious atrocity of killing innocent Palestinian civilians on West Bank on 25 February and expressed his deep sympathy to the families of the victims. Jiang Zemin reiterated that the Chinese Government will always support the just cause of the Palestinian people for restoring their legitimate national rights and promotion of peace process in the Middle East.
