May 6, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

'SMASH-MOUTH PEST CONTROL' METHOD TO ERADICATE MEDFLIES

Jordan really knows how to make a point! As if Medflies weren't enough of a problem, we also have a plague of bungling bureaucrats and pseudo-scientists -- and now, aphids, aphids everywhere. Meanwhile the "experts" are telling us to just spray more malathion on the aphids. Don't we ever get to try something sensible?

FRANCES RUSSELL

Burbank
