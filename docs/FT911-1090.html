910508 FT 08 MAY 91 / Parliament and Politics: Community charge capping used in Scotland MR IAN LANG, yesterday used community charge capping powers for the first time in Scotland to reduce poll tax bills for Lothian region, which includes Edinburgh. He said the budgeted spending by the Labour-controlled council - which levies the highest poll tax in Scotland - was 'excessive and unreasonable' and should be cut by Pounds 25m.
