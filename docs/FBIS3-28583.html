Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata is expected to hold talks with Vice President Al Gore when he accompanies Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa on a visit to the United States starting 10 February. This was disclosed by a Foreign Ministry source on 7 February. Moreover, the foreign minister is scheduled to meet with influential U.S. legislators, including both Senate floor leaders -- Democrat George Michel and Republican Robert Dole. It is expected that Japan's domestic issues, such as political and administrative reforms and economic pump-priming measures, which the Hosokawa administration is currently tackling, will be brought up during the meetings.
