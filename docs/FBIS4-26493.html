BFN [Text] Beijing, June 22 (XINHUA) -- Chen Muhua, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of China's National People's Congress (NPC), met with a Jordanian Government delegation here today. Chen, also president of the All-China Women's Federation (ACWF), exchanged views with the group on the Fourth World Conference on Women to be held here next year and other issues of common interest. The delegation, headed by Kamel Abu-Jaber [name as received], former minister of foreign affairs, senator and member of the Senate's Foreign Affairs Committee, arrived here on June 19 upon acwf's invitation. After the meeting, Chen hosted a dinner for the delegation.
