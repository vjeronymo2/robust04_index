BFN [Report by Konstantin Parishkura from Kiev: "The Crimea President Has Demanded That the Ukraine Deputy Internal Affairs Minister Be Recalled From the Peninsula"] [Text] In a telegram to Andrey Vasilishin, head of the Ukraine Internal Affairs Ministry, Crimea President Yuriy Meshkov objects to interference in the peninsula's affairs: "Your deputy, Colonel General Nedrigaylo, is undertaking actions aimed at demoralizing the officers of the Crimea Ministry of Internal Affairs, which manifest themselves in an attempt to encourage them to abandon their duties. I demand that Col. Gen. Nedrigaylo and his entourage be recalled immediately."
