Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday morning the provincial party committee and government held a meeting for the leaders of city party committees to discuss family planning work and arrange measures for controlling this year's population growth. At the meeting, Governor Zhu Senlin signed responsibility agreements with city mayors on controlling 1994 population growth. Xie Fei, a member of the CPC Central Committee's Political Bureau and secretary of the provincial party committee, addressed the meeting, stressing: All levels of party committees and governments should have a clear idea of the grim population situation, carry out family planning work in a down-to-earth manner, pay particular attention to false reports on family planning, do a good job in improving grass-roots organizations in the rural areas, ensure the implementation of family planning at the grass-roots level, improve the family planning service network, and gradually form a system which will lead to the systematization of family planning.
