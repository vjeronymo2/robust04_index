Language: Russian Article Type:BFN ["NG" report under the "In Brief" rubric: "Where Shall Duma Sit?"] [Text] It has become known to NEZAVISIMAYA GAZETA from a confidential source in the State Duma apparatus that most probably, the question of moving the lower chamber from the building of the former Council for Mutual Economic Assistance to a new location will be decided today. The same source has made it known that the most probable destination for the Duma is in southeast Moscow, in the building of Moscow's Academy of Management. Incidentally, this option was discussed during the meeting between President Yeltsin and Speaker Rybkin last week after Mr. Rybkin came in for criticism from the leaders of Duma factions for his excessive pliancy.
