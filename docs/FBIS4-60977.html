BFN [Text] Max van der Stoel, CSCE supreme emissary for national minority issues, began a visit to Ukraine today. It is expected that he will hold meetings with Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk, Ukrainian Deputy Prime Minister Mykola Zhulynskyy, Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko, National Minorities and Emigration Minister Oleksandr Yemets, and other officials and public figures. The CSCE supreme emissary is scheduled to visit Donetsk Oblast and the Crimean Republic.
