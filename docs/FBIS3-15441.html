Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Georgian head of state's edict on determining lawfulness of creating military commandant's offices in Georgia and on examining their activities. In order to determine lawfulness of creating commandant's offices in the Republic of Georgia, the chief military prosecutor's office will examine and determine lawfulness of commandant's offices existing in towns and rayons of the Republic of Georgia in one month's time. Suggestions on stopping the activity of commandant's offices that have been established through the violation of requirements of the Georgian Defense Ministry regulations on garrison and guard services will be drafted and presented. The state of implementation of paragraph three of the 22 November 1993 decree by the commander-in-chief of the Republic of Georgia on the organization of garrison services in towns and rayons of the Republic of Georgia will be studied. A report on the results of the work done and examination carried out will be submitted before 1 April 1994.
