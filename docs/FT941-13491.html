940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports, landed there was improved, widespread demand. Better liquoring Assams met good competition at dearer rates, mediums remained steady, while plainer types attracted more support than of late with prices about firm. Brightest and good medium East Africans were well supported and often dearer but lesser mediums were irregular and tended easier. Plainer africans were firm. Ceylons also met improved demand and closed dearer, particularly brighter types. Offshore good demand with prices for brighter teas fully firm, others were irregular. Quotations: quality 165p/kg, good medium 140p/kg, medium 106p/kg, low medium 90p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 206 for a Rwanda pf.
