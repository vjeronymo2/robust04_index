920225 FT 25 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Hewitt Hewitt Group, a manufacturer of industrial ceramics and refractories, lifted pre-tax profits nearly 7 per cent, from Pounds 585,000 to Pounds 625,000, in 1991. Continued enhancement of profit margins, up from 9.3 to 9.9 per cent, was achieved against a backdrop of severe price cutting in all markets. Turnover fell to Pounds 7.41m (Pounds 8m). Earnings per share were 11.4p (10.9p) and the dividend is again 2.5p, with a 1.5p final.
