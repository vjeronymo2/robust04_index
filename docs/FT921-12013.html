920129 FT 29 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Ladbroke sells stake in Berlin Hilton Hilton International, a subsidiary of Ladbroke Group, has sold a 60 per cent stake in the Berlin Hilton, formerly the Dom Hotel in east Berlin. Ladbroke, which bought the hotel last month, refused to name the buyer. It said, however, that it was a non-UK company. Ladbroke is thought to have paid Pounds 100m for the hotel and is believed to have sold the 60 per cent stake for Pounds 60m.
