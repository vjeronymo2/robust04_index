910427 FT 27 APR 91 / International Company News: Bols set to buy 45% stake in sherry producer LUCAS BOLS, the Dutch spirits and liqueurs group, expects to reach agreement soon on acquiring a 45 per cent stake in Williams & Humbert, the producer of Dry Sack sherry, from Antonio Barbadillo of Spain. Barbadillo is to sell a further 10 per cent in Williams & Humbert to Luis Paez, a Spanish venture between Bols and Ahold, the Dutch food retailer. Williams & Humbert, based in Jerez de la Frontera has annual sales of Fl 40m (Dollars 20.3m).
