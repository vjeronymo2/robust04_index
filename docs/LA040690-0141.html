April 6, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

TONIGHT: Knott's Berry Farm's new roller coaster Boomerang opens tonight at 8. The ride turns travelers upside down six times -- three forward and three backward -- in less than a minute. It replaces the park's old Corkscrew roller coaster, credited as the first to turn riders upside down. Ann Conway and Steve Emmons
