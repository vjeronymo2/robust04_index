BFN [Text] The Council of the State Duma has instructed the Duma legal department to turn to the media court of arbitration following a complaint by the Liberal-Democratic faction about a news analyst of Russian television Nikolay Svanidze. This was disclosed to Interfax by the deputy speaker of the Duma Mikhail Mityukov. In a TV show on May 13 Svanidze said that according to his knowledge the leader of Liberal-Democrats Vladimir Zhirinovskiy paid each member of his faction 500 U.S. dollars for supporting the draft of the federal budget at the first reading. In an interview with Interfax Alexey Mitrofanov of the Liberal- Democratic faction said the council did not make a final decision on the suggestion of the faction to temporarily ban Svanidze from appearing on television. Mitrofanov said that faction members are going to sue Svanidze for misinformation.
