Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Feb 19 (A.A) -- President Suleyman Demirel on Saturday [19 February] condemned Friday's bomb attack on Democracy Party (DEP) headquarters which killed one and wounded sixteen people. "Our country needs peace and quiet. I am calling on everyone to stand against the shedding of blood for any reason," Demirel said in his message. He also sent a message to DEP party leader in which he expressed sorrow over the attack and condemned the incident. A bomb that exploded in the headquarters of the Democracy Party (DEP) early in the evening on Friday killed one and wounded another sixteen people. The attack was later claimed by the Islamic Jihad organization.
