BFN [Text] The Higher Council for Economic and Oil Affairs met today under Acting Prime Minister Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar. At the outset of the meeting, the Council reviewed its agenda, and then discussed the supply policy under the current circumstances and made the necessary decisions to guarantee the availability of foodstuffs in all the republic's governorates for the set prices, especially since these commodities are available in sufficient quantities to meet the republic's needs.
