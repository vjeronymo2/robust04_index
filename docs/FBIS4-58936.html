BFN [Text] Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih last night telephoned President Husni Mubarak. The two presidents reviewed the latest developments in the conflict between the parties to the conflict in fraternal Yemen. President Mubarak reiterated Egypt's desire to end the fighting between the brothers and to avoid the random attacks on vital installations and peaceful civilians. President Mubarak told the Yemeni president that Egypt is continuing its efforts to end the crisis quickly to protect the Yemeni people's interests and respect its will.
