930911 FT 11 SEP 93 / Briefcase, Q&A: Paying for a wife's help WHAT IS the amount (weekly or annually) which I could have paid my wife for her help with my business in tax year 1992/93 which would still be below the amount at which NIC (employer's stamp) became payable? The maximum amount you can pay your wife in any one week in the present tax year (without the payment attracting class 1 NIC) is Pounds 55.99. The corresponding figure for 1992-93 was Pounds 53.99.
