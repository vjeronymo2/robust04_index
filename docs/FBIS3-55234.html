Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belarusian Foreign Minister Petr Kravchanka has held talks with Magdalene Phoff, head of a Europarliament delegation for cooperation with CIS states. In the course of their conversation, the officials highlighted issues pertaining to the widening of Belarusian cooperation with the Europarliament and the European Council. Magdalene Phoff spoke in favor of expanding dialogue between the Europarliament and Belarus, and continuing negotiations on forming a reliable legal footing for mutually advantageous cooperation. The officials also discussed the problem of conventional arms reduction and nuclear disarmament. Speaking of economic relations between Belarus and the EC, Petr Kravchanka underlined that the Republic is counting on the EC lifting its antidumping duties imposed on certain Belarusian products.
