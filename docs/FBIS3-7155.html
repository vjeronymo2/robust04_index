Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 25 -- Russian Ambassador to Vietnam Mr R.L. Khamidulin held here yesterday a regular press briefing on the recent developments in the Russian Federation. Speaking about the friendship and cooperation between the Russian Federation and Vietnam, Mr Khamidulin stressed that the friendship and mutual beneficial cooperation between the two countries would be continously developed. The Russia-Vietnam cooperative projects are regularly carried out, and expected to be put into operation on schedule. An economic delegation of the Russian Federation Government is expected in Vietnam to attend the third session of the Russia-Vietnam Inter-Governmental commission to discuss the issues of common concern to promote the coming cooperation between the two countries, he added.
