Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, February 17 (XINHUA) -- Yemeni Prime Minister Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas received Chinese Ambassador to Yemen Li Liugen in Aden today. XINHUA learned that the Yemeni prime minister and the Chinese ambassador discussed some issues related to economic cooperation between the two countries. Al-'Attas also expressed his profound concern over the three kidnapped Chinese experts who were working on the Jahanna-Maareb Road construction project. The three experts, who were kidnapped on February 12, include the head of a Chinese technical team executing the road building project, the chief engineer and the assistant manager of the project. The security situation in Yemen has been deteriorating in the recent years, and there has been recurrent abductions of accredited foreign diplomats, foreign tourists and employees of foreign companies operating in Yemen.
