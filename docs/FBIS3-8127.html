Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague March 3 (CTK) -- Eighteen percent of Czechs are fully satisfied with the Czech foreign policy, according to a poll conducted by the Centre for Empirical Research among 1,623 adults. Fifty-eight percent of those polled described the Czech foreign policy as quite correct, while the rest of the population held the opposite opinion. Asked whether or not they were satisfied with the domestic political situation, 38 percent of people answered positively, and 25 percent said they were quite satisfied. The remaining 34 percent of those questioned answered negatively. On both counts, men proved to be more satisfied than women. People aged over 60 expressed their anxiety regarding the internal political situation.
