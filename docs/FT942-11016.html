940507 FT 07 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Hartstone sells Leeds hosiery side Hartstone Group has sold the business and certain of the assets of Hartstone Hosiery, comprising its hosiery distribution business based in Leeds, to its senior management, for Pounds 1.82m. The consideration includes a deferred amount of Pounds 300,000. Hartstone sold its other ladies' hosiery interests to Courtaulds in January.
