930406 FT 06 APR 93 / IMF-Egypt debt talks Talks took place in Cairo yesterday between the International Monetary Fund and Egyptian officials on a programme of economic and financial reform which Egypt needs to win Dollars 3bn (Pounds 2.1bn) of western debt forgiveness, Reuter reports from Cairo. A spokesman said the IMF team in Cairo was discussing a successor agreement to Egypt's stand-by facility due to run out in May. Western creditors have agreed to forgive Egypt about Dollars 3bn, part of a Dollars 10bn total forgiveness package, once it starts a new IMF programme.
