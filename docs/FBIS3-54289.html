Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Presidential Council Chairman Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih today received brother Dr. Khalid al-Karaki, adviser to His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal of the fraternal Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. Al-Karaki relayed to the brother president a message from his brother His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal on fraternal bilateral ties, issues of common interest, and the efforts made by His Majesty King Husayn to address the current challenges posed to our country. The message said that the fraternal Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan blesses the document of accord designed to build a modern Yemeni state agreed to by the Political Forces Dialogue Committee, and deems it a positive accomplishment. The message also invited the brother Presidential Council chairman to visit the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. The brother president expressed his thanks and appreciation for the efforts made by His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal in this regard, as well as the heartfelt fraternal sentiments expressed by the fraternal Jordanians toward their brothers in Yemen and their concern for seeing our country cope with all the challenges posed to it so that unity and democracy may be safeguarded. The brother president asked the Jordanian official to relay his greetings to his brother His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal, as well as his wishes of continued good health and happiness to him, and of further progress and prosperity to the fraternal Jordanian people. The meeting was attended by brother Brigadier General Mujahid Abu-Shawarib, deputy prime minister; Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani, member of the General Committee of the General People's Congress; 'Ali Muhammad al-Anisi, director of the Presidential Council chairman's office; and Dr. Fayiz al-Rabi', Jordanian ambassador to Yemen.
