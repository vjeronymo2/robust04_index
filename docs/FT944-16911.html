941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Goldsmiths loss reduced A 13 per cent increase in turnover from Pounds 18.8m to Pounds 21.2m helped Goldsmiths Group, the retail jeweller with 116 branches in the UK, cut pre-tax losses from Pounds 1.34m to Pounds 817,000 for the six months to July 30. Sales were up 10.9 per cent in the nine weeks since the start of the second half. Losses per share were reduced to 2.49p, compared with 4.09p and interim dividend payments have been resumed with a 0.8p distribution.
