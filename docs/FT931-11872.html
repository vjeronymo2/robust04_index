930201 FT 01 FEB 93 / Captain Scarlet competes with video games at Earls Court Captain Scarlet, hero of a 1960s children's television series, is slugging it out with more modern toys such as video games systems at the British International Toy and Hobby Fair. The fair, which opened yesterday at Earls Court, west London, is the industry's annual showcase when more than 400 exhibitors seek to convince retailers and manufacturers that their product is set to be a favourite among children next Christmas.
