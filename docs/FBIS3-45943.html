Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, January 24 (XINHUA) -- Tomur Dawamat, vice chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China left here for home today after a one-week goodwill visit to Malaysia. During the visit, Malaysian Prime Minister Mahathir Mohamad, speaker of the lower house of Parliament Mohamed Zahir, president of Senate G. Vadiveloo met with Tomur respectively and exchanged views on the further enhancement of bilateral relations, including economic and trade links. Supreme Head of State Sultan Azlan Shah and his wife hosted a luncheon today for Tomur and his wife. Tomur and his party toured Malaysia's national car plant in Shah Alam near here, the industrial zone of Penang and other economic institutions.
