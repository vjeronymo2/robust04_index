Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Van, Feb 3 (AA) -- 2.5 tonnes of hashish, hidden in 91 sacks were seized in Baskale township in eastern Van Province on Wednesday. AA was told that security forces acting on a tip off staged an operation in Sinova region in Ekecek village in Baskale township. The market value of the hashish was 50 billion Turkish lira [approximately $2,500,000]. An investigation has been launched into the incident.
