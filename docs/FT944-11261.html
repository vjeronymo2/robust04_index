941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / Equity Markets: Other markets - Hong Kong The market is set for another US-led week, with last Friday's non-farm payroll and job data providing a key signal for the direction of trade today, writes Louise Lucas. Investors will be looking for any inflationary sparks which could feed through into a further rise in interest rates - initially in the US, but followed through the currency link into the colony. Domestically, there is little on the corporate front this week, although China Light and Power, the electricity utility, reports final results today. Friday's signing of the long-awaited airport financing deal was received by investors with a degree of nonchalance, with many analysts believing that the deal is already fully factored into the market.
