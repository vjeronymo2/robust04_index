June 13, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

SIDELINES;

TAC CHIEF HURT IN AUTO ACCIDENT

Ollan Cassell, executive director of The Athletics Congress, was hospitalized today with five broken ribs and a possible broken collarbone after being injured in a two-vehicle accident.

TAC spokesman Pete Cava said Cassell was hurt Tuesday night when his car was struck by a truck.

"The truck came around a corner and hit him on the blind side," Cava said. "The car was totaled."
