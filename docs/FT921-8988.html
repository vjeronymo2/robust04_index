920214 FT 14 FEB 92 / International Company News: Carolco sells rights CAROLCO Pictures, the troubled independent Hollywood film company, will receive Dollars 64m from Spelling Entertainment for some television rights to Carolco's library, writes Karen Zagor. Carolco, maker of Terminator 2 and Rambo movies, said the deal was valued at Dollars 64m, including some assumed obligations. The company, along with other independent film-makers, has been caught in a financial squeeze.
