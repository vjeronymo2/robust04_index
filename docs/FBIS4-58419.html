BFN [Text] AL-MANAR has learned from a senior Palestinian source that the Palestinian National Authority will comprise 25 members and will be headed by PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat. Among the names that will be announced next week are: Nasr Yusuf, Muhammad Ghunaym, Ahmad Quray', Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, Yasir 'Amr, Samir Ghawshah, 'Ali Ishaq, Muhammad Zuhdi al-Nashashibi, Sulayman al-Najjab, Ilyas Frayj, Mahir al-Masri, Zakariya al-Agha, Dr. Hanna Nasir, Jamil al- Tarifi, Furayh Abu-Middayn, Sa'ib 'Urayqat, and Faysal al-Husayni. The Palestinian source said that the complete list of names will be released soon, because consultations are still continuing and no agreement has been reached yet regarding the final, precise list. Consultations are under way within the Palestinian leadership and between the factions that support the peace process. The source added that this composition of the National Authority is transitional and for a defined period. AL-MANAR has learned that all the members of the National Authority are supporters of the peace process and the Palestinian-Israeli agreement.
