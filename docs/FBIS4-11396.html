BFN [Excerpts] Lima, 23 Apr (EFE) -- Peruvian President Alberto Fujimori asserted that now that Congress has approved the proposal that the National Elections Board (JNE) organize the elections, nobody can suspect any fraud. [passage omitted] Fujimori on 22 April told journalists in Arequipa, 1,000 km south of Lima, that with this decision "now absolutely nobody can question the clear and transparent development of the electoral process." He added that reports stating that "the government upheld electoral fraud" have thus been dismissed, and said the executive branch will not have any problem promulgating the law approved by Congress. Fujimori indirectly referred to the possible presidential candidacy of former UN Secretary General Javier Perez de Cuellar by stating: "The road is clear and nobody can condition his candidacy to the establishment of clear and clean rules."
