BFN [Text] An aircraft carrying 90 members of the 92d infantry regiment of the French Army from Clermont-Ferrand landed at the Kladne airport near Ceske Budejovice this afternoon. Another 40 French soldiers arrived at Boletice military training ground in Cesky Krumlov district shortly after midnight. Until 10 June they will be training in Boletice together with 120 members of the second company of the 23d Mechanized Battalion of the Czech Army, which is based in Strasice in Rokycany district. This is the Czech Army's first major contact with the West within the context of the Partnership for Peace initiative, during the course of which soldiers of a NATO member country will be for the first time training with their own weapons on the Czech Republic's territory.
