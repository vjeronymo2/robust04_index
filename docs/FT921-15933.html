920107 FT 07 JAN 92 / Observer: Recharged Miffed at being refused entry to a trendy nightspot for having no tie, a City reveller got the battery jump-leads from his car, knotted them round his neck, and returned to the door, snorting: 'Will that do?' 'OK,' said the bouncer grudgingly. 'But don't try to start anything, that's all.'
