940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / International Company News: Armco sells off insurance operations Armco, the US steel group, is selling its on-going insurance operations to Vik Brothers Insurance, a privately held, property and casualty insurance holding company, AP-DJ reports. The transaction is valued at about Dollars 85m. Armco recorded a Dollars 45m charge against 1993 fourth-quarter earnings in connection with the proposed sale.
