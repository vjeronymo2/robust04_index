Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, 24 Jan (SUNA) -- The secretary-general of the Islamic Conference Organization [ICO], Dr. Hamid Algabid, has arrived here this morning. He was received by Foreign Minister Husayn Abu-Salih. While here, Dr. Algabid will attend the inauguration ceremony of the Islamic Institute for Translation in Khartoum and will attend also the opening session of the Seventh Conference of Arabicization on Tuesday morning. The secretary-general of ICO will meet during his stay here (?some) Sudanese officials.
