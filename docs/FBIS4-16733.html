BFN ["Directive" issued by Givi Lominadze, Republic of Georgia state minister, chairman of the Interim Committee for Emergency Situations in Western Georgia] [Text] 1. To approve the Statute on Temporary Commissions for Reconciliation With Supporters of the Ex-President Attached to Bodies of Local Administration for Emergency Situations in Western Georgia that has been submitted. 2. The statute comes into force on the day of its publication. 3. To entrust bodies of local administration with forming these commissions before 10 March and submitting information on the composition of the commission to the Temporary Committee for Emergency Situations. [Signed] G. Lominadze, Republic of Georgia state minister, chairman of the Interim Committee for Emergency Situations in western Georgia.
