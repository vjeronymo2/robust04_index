Language: French Article Type:BFN [Article signed SH: "After Arrest Ticino Policeman Confesses To Trafficking 500 Kg of Cocaine"; as released by Bern ATSA/SDAA database] [Excerpt] Bellinzone, 22 Feb (ATS) -- The Ticino police corporal arrested last Friday for drug trafficking has confessed to importing, transporting, storing, and selling 500 kg of cocaine, as the canton Public Ministry revealed on Tuesday evening. This would have paid him some 90,000 Swiss francs [SFr], in addition to the SFr100,000 still owed him.
