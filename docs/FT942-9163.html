940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / International Company News: Telecom NZ ahead 15.4% Telecom New Zealand, controlled by Bell Atlantic and Ameritech of the US, reported net earnings up to NZDollars 528. lm (USDollars 308.8m). Mr Roderick Deane, chief executive, said strong progress had been made on all fronts with operating revenue rising by NZDollars 22.7m to NZDollars 2.49bn. Telecom claimed success in countering competition, increasing operating efficiency and raising call volumes. Domestic call volumes rose 9.9 per cent, while international calls rose 11 per cent. Operating expenses fell 3.9 per cent excluding abnormal restructuring costs.
