September 23, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BUILDING TRADE SHOW SCHEDULED

Hundreds of exhibits, panel discussions and educational seminars will highlight the second annual Building Industry Show, to be held Oct. 24-26 at the Los Angeles Convention Center.

The trade is sponsored by the Building Industry Assn. of Southern California and co-sponsored by The Times and the National Assn. of Home Builders.

New products, services and technologies will be on display at the convention's 45,000-square-foot exhibit hall on Oct. 24 from noon to 6 p.m.; on Oct. 25, from 10:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. and on Oct. 26 from 8:30 a.m. to 2 p.m.

Building professionals and the general public are invited to the show. One-day passes to the exhibition are $10 for members and $15 for non-members. Preregistration fees to attend both the trade show and the seminars range from $63 to $125, depending on how many days each visitor attends.

More information can be obtained from the Building Industry Assn. of Southern California's headquarters in Diamond Bar.
