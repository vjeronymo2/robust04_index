Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] General Ahmet Corekci, deputy head of the Turkish General Staff, departed the TRNC [Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus] at the end of his three-day visit, during which he inspected the Turkish Peace Force units. A military ceremony was held at Ercan Airport to see him off at 1700. Olgun Pasalar, deputy speaker of the TRNC Republican Assembly; Cahit Bayar, Turkey's ambassador in Nicosia; Lieutenant General Necati Ozgen, commander of the Turkish Peace Force units in the TRNC; Major General Attila Kurtaran, commander of the 28th Division; Major General Kadri Ozer, commander of the 39th Division; Brigadier General Yasar Spor, commander of the TRNC Security Forces; Gural Tanova, under secretary of the TRNC President's Office; Munir Muhtaroglu, under secretary of the Prime Minister's Office; and other military officials attended the ceremony.
