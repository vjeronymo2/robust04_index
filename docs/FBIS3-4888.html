Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, March 1 (KPL) -- Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon yesterday afternoon received Mongolian president Punsalmaagiyn Ochirbat, who is on a current visit to Laos from February 28 to March 3. The Lao prime minister and the visiting Mongolian president talked with each other in a frank and cordial atmosphere, in which they exchanged views on developing relationship and cooperation between Laos and Mongolia. The sides expressed satisfaction over their relations over the past years and hoped that these good relations would be further strengthened in the interest of the two people, Lao and Mongolian. Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon praised the visit to the country by the Mongolian president as an important step and historic landmark in the relations between the two countries at the new stage. He expected that the Mongolian president's visit would be successful. On his part, the Mongolian president expressed satisfaction and thanks to the Lao prime minister as well as the Lao Government and people for giving him and his delegation a warm hospitality during their current visit here. He also welcomed the achievements the Lao Government and people had (?yielded) in various fields through implementing the renovation policy in the country.
