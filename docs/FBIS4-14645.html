BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 470] [Text] Vilnius, April 1, ELTA -- European Union [EU] hails the initialing of the agreement on friendship and cooperation between Lithuania and Poland. European Union expressed hopes that this agreement would be a great contribution to the stability in the whole region, corresponding the EU efforts to realize the European stability pact. EU hails the efforts to open a new page in the history of the both countries. This act of showing mutual trust will be useful for both countries, which chose the way of closer cooperation with the European political and economic structures.
