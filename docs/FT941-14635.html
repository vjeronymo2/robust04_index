940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / International Company News: Sun Micro surges 80% Sun Microsystems, the US market leader in computer workstations, yesterday reported an 80 per cent rise in second-quarter net income. The company made Dollars 43.8m, or 46 cents a share, up from Dollars 24.1m, or 23 cents, a year before, on revenues up 8 per cent at Dollars 1.13bn. The earnings were broadly in line with market expectations. Mr Kevin Melia, chief financial officer, said: 'Sun's successful new product offerings and the resulting strength in the high-end product mix, as well as our continued focus on cost management, helped increase gross margins from last year's level.' For the six months, Sun reported net income of Dollars 60.4m, or 62 cents a share, up from Dollars 28.9m, or 28 cents, on revenues 10 per cent higher at Dollars 2.09bn.
