920905 FT 05 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Thompson Clive asset value up Over the six months ended June 30 1992 net asset value of Thompson Clive Investments rose 6.1 per cent to 175p a share. A year earlier it stood at 166.7p. Revenue fell from Pounds 718,000 to Pounds 584,000 and net profits were Pounds 166,000 (Pounds 262,000) for earnings per share of 1.3p (2.05p). During the period Pounds 565,000 was invested in three companies, two of which are in the US and one in France.
