920211 FT 11 FEB 92 / Graduate hiring set to increase COMPANIES ARE planning to employ 4 per cent more graduates this year than last year, although prospects for graduates are bleak, according to a survey of company intentions published today. For those who do get jobs, the mean starting salary is likely to be just under Pounds 12,700 - about 5 per cent higher than the mean for last year.
