920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / Halifax offers Peps to companies HALIFAX, the largest building society, is offering businesses a single-company Pep (personal equity plan) for employees. The scheme, which is not available through branches or to individual consumers, will enable employees of companies to set up single-company Peps using shares acquired under employee share-option schemes. Employees will be able to transfer Pounds 3,000 worth of shares directly from share option schemes into the Pep in which capital gains will be exempt from tax liability.
