BFN [Text] Moscow, April 1, RIA -- From April 1 considerable changes will be made in the principles used in drawing up weekly schedules of Russia Television programmes. Due to financial difficulties the length of broadcasting will be cut back by 2.5 hours daily -- at the expense of repeating feature films and political programmes in the daytime. The children's "Holiday Every Day" evening fairy-tale is moved from 19.45 to 18.40. Theatre programmes that appeared on Saturdays at 18.15 are to be shown on Tuesdays at 21.25.
