Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Anna Melnikova and Ivan Ivanov] [Text] Moscow, 2 Mar -- Boris Yeltsin has officially welcomed Islam Karimov in St. George's Hall in the Grand Kremlin Palace. The Uzbekistan president arrived this morning in Moscow to strengthen cooperation and integration between the two countries. The anthems of the two countries were played at the official welcoming ceremony. Having exchanged brief welcoming speeches, Boris Yeltsin and Islam Karimov proceeded to the green drawing room where they are to talk tete-a-tete.
