ACTION: Interim rule.

SUMMARY: The statutory requirements that relate to the feed grains, rice, upland and extra long staple cotton, and wheat programs were amended by the Agricultural Reconciliation Act of 1993 (the 1993 Act), which was enacted on August 11, 1993. This interim rule sets forth amendments: to conform to the provisions of the 1993 Act; to make certain technical corrections; to delete references to obsolete provisions; to add references relating to current policy; to set forth the provisions for The Options Pilot Program (OPP) and Voluntary Production Limitation Program (VPLP); and to improve the operations of these programs for the 1994 through 1997 crop years. DATES: Interim rule effective November 16, 1994. Comments must be received on or before December 16, 1994 in order to be assured of consideration.
