Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 29 (KCNA) -- A national seminar on the chuche idea was held in Kigali on Jan. 15 on the threshold of the birthday of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. Mbonigaba Stanislas, chairman of the Rwandan Committee for the Study of the Chuche Idea, made a keynote report on the subject "philosophical principle and socio-historical principle of chuche idea." He said the chuche idea founded by his excellency the great leader President Kim Il-song raised the fundamental question of philosophy with man in the centre and expounded the philosophical principle that man is the master of everything and decides everything, thus establishing a man-centred new outlook on the world. The reporter said: "The imperialists and international reactionaries find themselves powerless before socialist Korea because the DPRK has an ideological and moral weapon -- singlehearted unity -- which is mightier than A-bomb." "This ideological and moral weapon of the Korean people is noble and invincible because it was prepared through arduous trials in history," he added. Rudahunga Ignace, vice-chairman of the Rwandan Committee for the Study of the Chuche Idea, in his report on the subject "guiding principles of chuche idea" said the guiding principles of the chuche idea indicate the fundamental principles in maintaining the independent and creative stand and successfully carrying out the revolution and construction with the enhanced role of ideology. "If the chuche idea is to be applied in the revolution and construction, its guiding principles must be observed," he said. The speakers at the seminar dwelt on the validity of the philosophical principle of the chuche idea and stressed the need to apply the guiding principles of this idea in conformity with the specific conditions of Rwanda. A congratulatory message to Comrade Kim Chong-il was adopted at the seminar.
