930105 FT 05 JAN 93 / Clinton and Yeltsin hold telephone conversation President-elect Bill Clinton and Russian President Boris Yeltsin held a 20-minute telephone conversation yesterday, Reuter reports from Little Rock. They discussed a wide range of subjects, including the Start 2 arms control agreement and Yugoslavia, a spokesman said. He added that US and Russian officials would begin setting up an early meeting between the two after the January 20 inauguration, but no date had been set.
