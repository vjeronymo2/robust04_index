930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / International Company News in Brief: Ten Group TEN GROUP, the Australian commercial television network, made a net profit of ADollars 17.3m (USDollars 11.3m) for the 39 weeks ended June, before ADollars 5.0m interest paid on subordinated debentures, Reuter reports from Sydney. The result compares with a profit of ADollars 15.2m in the 50 weeks ended September 1992.
