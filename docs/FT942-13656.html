940423 FT 23 APR 94 / Finance and the Family: Small companies hold steay - At a glance The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) was virtually unchanged over the week to Thursday April 21. Over the same period the FT-A-All Share index fell by 1 per cent. So far this year smaller companies have held up comparatively well - up 3.3 per cent compared with a 9 per cent fall for the FT-SE 100 index and 6.6 per cent for the All-Share.
