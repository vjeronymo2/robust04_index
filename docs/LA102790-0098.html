October 27, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP;

TRI-VALLEY LEAGUE

Moorpark 20, St. Bonaventure 19 -- Toussaint Powdrill scored on an 87-yard run and intercepted a pass in the end zone with less than a minute to play as Moorpark hung on at home.

Powdrill led the Musketeers with 191 yards rushing in 18 carries. Moorpark built a 20-7 lead early in the third quarter on a four-yard touchdown run by Chris Aguilar. DAVID DONNELLY
