920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / World Trade News: Record Suez earnings The Suez Canal earned Egypt a record Dollars 1.85bn in fiscal 1991-92 against Dollars 1.77bn the year before, its chief executive said in an interview due to be broadcast at the weekend, Reuter reports from Cairo. Mr Ezzat Adel, chairman of the Suez Canal Authority, told the London-based Middle East Broadcasting Centre that last year's revenue was the highest in the canal's history. The canal is one of Egypt's biggest foreign currency earners.
