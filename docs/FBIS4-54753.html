CSO [Report by unnamed Bratislava correpondent: "Slovak Republic Wants $350 Million From IMF"] [Text] Bratislava--The Slovak Government will try to obtain a so-called standby loan from the IMF in the amount of $350 million, even though the restrictive measures in the form of the loan conditions will hit the public before the parliamentary elections in the fall and will obviously affect the prospects of the government parties. This was announced yesterday in Bratislava by Prime Minister Jozef Moravcik. The prime minister also confirmed that the condition for obtaining the IMF loan is to keep the deficit of the state budget under 4 percent of the GDP.
