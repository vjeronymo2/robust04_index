April 28, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

CS NORTHRIDGE SIGNS FORWARD FOR BASKETBALL

Jody Bittner, a 5-foot-10 forward from North Monterey County High, has signed a letter of intent to play for the Cal State Northridge women's team.

Bittner averaged 16 points and 11.9 rebounds a game in earning All-Salinas Valley honors for the second year in a row.
