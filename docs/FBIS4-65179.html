BFN [Unattributed article: "Maroni Names His Chef de Cabinet"] [Text] (ANSA) Rome, May 12 -- Prefect Claudio Gelati, 55, was today named chef de cabinet at the Interior Ministry by Minister Roberto Maroni, who confirmed Gelati at the post he was moved to earlier this year by former Interior Minister Nicola Mancino. Before taking on the portfolio with his swearing in yesterday, Maroni said he intended to keep on the job the senior civil servants who had been managing the affairs of the ministry's departments. Still to name are the officials who usually become the minister's closest aides, the chief of the secretariat, the personal secretary and the head of the ministry's press office. The chef de cabinet served earlier, at the end of last year, as special commissioner for Milan when the great northern city was temporarily without a mayor.
