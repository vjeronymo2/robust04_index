March 1, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHARITY SCORECARD

The "Casino Night" held at the Crystal Court at South Coast Plaza in Costa Mesa on Feb. 24 raised about $50,000 for the Cabaret chapter of the Guilds of the Orange County Performing Arts Center.

Emily Rogan was chairwoman.

"Winefest '90," held at the Radisson Plaza on Feb. 25, raised about $10,000 for the Town and Country Committee of the Orange County Philharmonic Society. Nancy Cox and Sally Shaw were co-chairwomen.
