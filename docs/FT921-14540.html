920115 FT 15 JAN 92 / Gas-fired power station approved MR JOHN WAKEHAM, the energy secretary, yesterday gave planning consent to the Energy Supply Company for a 118MW combined cycle gas-fired power station at King's Lynn, Norfolk. Energy Supply, a small private company, will build the Pounds 45m station with a significant shareholder, Associated Energy Projects, a subsidiary of the French company Generale des Eaux.
