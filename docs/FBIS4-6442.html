BFN [Text] Vientiane, April 20 (KPL) -- A delegation of the Organisation Board of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee led by its head Thongsing Thammavong, Party CC [Central Committee] Politburo member, yesterday left here for Hanoi on an official visit to the Socialist Republic of Vietnam. The visit, which is to last ten days, was made at the invitation of the Organisation Board of the Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee. The Lao delegation will meet with its Vietnamese counterpart to discuss and share experiences concerning organisational affairs and party cadre training at the new stage. Seeing the delegation off at the airport were Thongsavat Khaikhamphithoun, Party CC member, head of the Party CC Office; and senior officials of the Organisation Board. Vietnamese Ambassador to Laos Bui Van Thanh was also present.
