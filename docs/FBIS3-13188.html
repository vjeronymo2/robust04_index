Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by 'Irfan Rashid: "Vatican Spokesman Tells AL-HAYAH: Pope Does Not Plan To Visit Iraq in the Near Future"] [Excerpt] Rome -- A spokesman for the Vatican has denied reports that Pope John Paul II has accepted an invitation to visit Iraq "in the near future." He said: "The pope's program does not include anything that indicates the possibility of his visiting that country." The spokesman did not confirm whether the pope had received an invitation. He told AL-HAYAH: "At any rate, sending an invitation officially or unofficially does not necessarily mean its acceptance and inclusion in the program of the pope's religious visits."
