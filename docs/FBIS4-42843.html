BFN [Text] A special commission of the Bavarian Land Office of Criminal Investigation [LKA] has crushed an international drug ring. A spokesman for the LKA said in Munich today, that in a large-scale operation in Munich, Hamburg, Duesseldorf, and Nuremberg earlier this week, police apprehended 14 Germans and Turks. They are suspected of having imported tonnes of heroin to Spain, Italy, and Germany. The spokesman added that another 10 persons were arrested in northern Italy and Spain. In the operation, the officials also detected comprehensive money laundering practices.
