921224 FT 24 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Caledonia Inv sells De Morgan stake Caledonia Investments, in which the Cayzer family has a 48 per cent stake, has sold its holding in De Morgan, to Mr Patrick Cooper, De Morgan chairman, for Pounds 258,000. The holding of 11.5m ordinary shares carries 24.3 per cent of the voting rights. The bulk of the consideration has been satisfied by a Pounds 208,000 interest-free loan to Mr Cooper. The remaining Pounds 50,000 is satisfied by the granting to Caledonia of the right to share in any profits on the sale of the shares within the next five years. Caledonia's shares yesterday added 13p to 450p.
