Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hangzhou, March 5 (XINHUA) -- Construction of the Tianhuanping pumping station, China's largest in Zhejiang Province, is in progress. Located in Anji county, the project, which will pump and store water at night in order to generate electricity during the day time, has been listed as one of China's key capital construction projects scheduled for completion next century. With a combined investment of at least 2.3 billion yuan, the pumping station will have a total installation capacity of 1.8 million kilowatts. Its first generating unit is expected to go into operation in 1997. The whole project is being built jointly by the State Energy Investment Corporation, Shanghai and Jiangsu, Zhejiang and Anhui Provinces.
