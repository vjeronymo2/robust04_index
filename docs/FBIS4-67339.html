BFN [Text] Kocaeli, May 19 (A.A) -- Sixty-five kilo of heroin were seized in operations carried out by narcotics teams on Thursday in Kocaeli with a market value of 30 billion lira, it was reported. Two people were detained in this connection. A statement from Kocaeli Security Department said that narcotics teams acting on a tip-off searched a truck which was on its way to board a ferry in Derince, in Izmit Gulf. 65 kilos of heroin were found hidden in secret compartments of the truck. The detainees said the heroin was given to them by a man of Iranian origin and made an agreement to carry it to Romania, where they would hand it back to him. Police said they will start initiatives towards Romania for cooperation in finding the Iranian believed to be presently there.
