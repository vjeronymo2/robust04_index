September 19, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Talk, Then Rap: On Nov. 8, the 2 Live Crew pay-per-view show on TV will include more than one kind of rap.

A debate on censorship will precede the show, which costs $19.95 to watch. Arguing against censorship will be the chairman of the Southern California American Civil Liberties Union, Danny Goldberg. On the censorship side will be Jack Thompson, a lawyer from Florida. BETH KLEID
