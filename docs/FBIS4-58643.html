BFN [Text] Amman, May 5 KYODO -- Japanese Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa toured the West Bank town of Jericho on Thursday [5 May], a day after he attended the signing ceremony in Cairo of a historic agreement on limited Palestinian self-rule for Gaza and Jericho. He visited a local hospital in Jericho to which Japan has offered to supply medical equipment. Kakizawa visited Jericho before arriving in Amman, where he paid a courtesy call on King Husayn and conferred with other Jordanian leaders. Kakizawa is visiting Jordan during a tour of the Middle East which has already taken him to Egypt and Israel.
