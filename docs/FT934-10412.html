931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / Observer: Sic A bad attack of the metaphors seems to have struck UBS' economics team in their latest assessment of the UK economy headed, incidentally: 'UK Eocnomics'. It concludes: 'The chances are, then, for all his tough talk, that Mr Clarke will offer this November a mere teaspoon to hold back the tide. The end result this side of the millennium could well be a roller-coaster ride for the economy carrying the ultimate payload of another burst of inflation. If so it would be a fitting, if familiar, retribution for Britain's chronic fiscal incontinence.'
