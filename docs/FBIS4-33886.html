BFN [Text] Cartagena de Indias, 13 Jun (NOTIMEX) -- Cuban President Fidel Castro arrived on the evening of 13 June in Cartagena de Indias, Colombia, to attend the Fourth Ibero-American summit, which will gather 22 leaders from Latin America, Spain, and Portugal for two days. Castro arrived at 2315 (0415 GMT) and was greeted at the Rafael Nunez Airport by Colombian Foreign Minister Noemi Sanin and writer Gabriel Garcia Marquez. Fidel Castro was the last, among the chiefs of state and government, to arrive in Cartagena de Indias to attend the summit which will start on Tuesday.
