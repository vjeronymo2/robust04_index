March 8, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

SP to Acquire Digital Signal: Southern Pacific Telecommunications, a subsidiary of Southern Pacific Transportation of San Francisco, has signed a definitive agreement to acquire the Southfield, Mich., company.

Terms of the deal were not disclosed. SP provides optical-fiber communications between Houston and San Francisco and arranges for installation of similar systems for long-distance carriers and companies along its 15,000 miles of railroad. Digital Signal provides private-line service for long-distance carriers between 88 cities.
