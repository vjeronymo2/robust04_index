940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / European Commission agrees on share out-of Ecu14bn The European Commission yesterday agreed on the share-out of the Ecu14bn (Pounds 11bn) social funds for 1994-99, aimed mainly at combating long-term and youth unemployment, writes David Gardner from Strasbourg. The UK share will be Ecu3.4bn; France gets Ecu3.2bn; Germany Ecu1.9bn; Spain Ecu1.8bn; and Italy Ecu1.7bn.
