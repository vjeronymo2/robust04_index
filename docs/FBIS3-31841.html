Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] An agreement on the exchange of prisoners has been reached at a meeting held in the HQ of UNPROFOR's [UN Protection Force] Spanish Battalion in Medjugorje between HVO [Croatian Defense Council] and Bosnia-Herzegovina army representatives. One thousand prisoners from Mostar, Jablanica, Prozor, Bogojno, Capljina and Stolac are to be exchanged. It was also agreed that the time and method for the exchange will be determined over the next few days in cooperation with the International Red Cross in Medjugorje.
