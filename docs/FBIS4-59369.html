BFN [Text] Pursuant to instructions from brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, Shaykh 'Abd-al-Majid al-Zandani yesterday visited the families of the victims of the spiteful missile aggression. He extended condolences on behalf of the brother president. Shaykh 'Abd-al-Majid al-Zandani had earlier inspected the situation of the people at the site of the ugly, odious crime committed by the forces of apostasy and secession when a missile fell on the homes of secure citizens. The Yemeni News Agency, SABA', has learned that brother Lt. Gen. 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih has instructed the competent authorities to reconstruct the houses that were destroyed at the expense of the state, and to give 100,000 riyals in aid to each affected family.
