BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 15 (KCNA) -- Secretary of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] Hwang Chang-yop Thursday met and conversed in a friendly atmosphere with A. D. Vengerovsky, vice-chairman of the Liberal Democratic Party and deputy speaker of the state Duma of the Federation Council of Russia, and his entourage. Chi Chae-yong, vice-director of a department of the WPK Central Committee, was on hand.
