BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received solidarity messages supporting the struggle of the Korean people to defend national dignity and sovereignty of the country from general secretary of the popular force organized in Costa Rica Jose Francisco Aguilar Bulgarelli, chairman of the People's Front of Workers, Peasants and Students of Peru Genaro Ledesma Izquieta, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Peru (Red Motherland) Alberto Moreno, acting leader of the February 18th Movement of Trinidad and Tobago Vincent Cabrea, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Dominican Communist Pary (Marxism-Leninism) Leopoldo Grullon and federal secretary of the Communist Party of Austria Walter Bayer. The great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received a solidarity message from chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Denmark (Marxist-Leninist) Klaus Riis. The solidarity messages say the just stand maintained by the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea is a guideline of the world people's struggle to defend freedom and independence against aggression and interference of the imperialists and fully support it. Noting that the struggle of the Korean people is one to defend national sovereignty and socialism and to reunify the country and nation in a peaceful way, the messages state that they would extend firm solidarity to the struggle of the Korean people in the future, too.
