941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Aminex Aminex, the Dublin-listed oil and gas exploration group, turned post-tax losses of IPounds 124,860 into profits of IPounds 481,778 (Pounds 476,159) for the half-year to June 30. Oil and gas revenue was boosted from IPounds 55,764 to IPounds 3.88m, largely by Amkomi, the company's joint venture in the Komi Republic of Russia.
