BFN [Statement issed by International Mediation Forum on the SAPA PR Wire Service on 14 April] [Text] After an evening of separate sessions with the three parties, the mediators are currently meeting to determine the way ahead. The mediators are still in contact with the parties. The mediators would like to make it clear that queries have been raised regarding fees the mediators may have been paid in order to participate in the mediation process. No fees have been asked for and none have been paid. The mediators are participating on the basis of a commitment to a peaceful settlement of the constitutional differences of the parties and a sense of goodwill. You will be advised of further developments during the day.
