Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Knesset Member [MK] Gonen Segev was elected chairman of YI'UD [Independent and Democratic Israel] last night in the first meeting of the movement's secretariat. MK Ester Salmovitz was elected chairman of the Central Committee and the movement's president. MK Alex Goldfarb was elected faction chairman. The secretariat will be made up of 20 members.
