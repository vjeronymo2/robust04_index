BFN [Edict No. 659 of the Russian Federation President "On Suspending the Validity of Edict No. 63 of the Republic of Ingushetia President `On the Republic of Ingushetia Minister of Security' Dated 21 March 1994"; dated Moscow, the Kremlin, 5 April 1994 and signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin] [Text] In connection with the publication of Edict No. 63 of the Republic of Ingushetia President "On the Republic of Ingushetia Minister of Security," dated 21 March 1994, which contravenes Article 71 of the Russian Federation Constitution and the Russian Federation President's Edict No. 19 of 5 January 1994 "On Ratifying the Statute on the Russian Federation Federal Counterintelligence Service," I decree that: 1. Guided by Articles 80 and 85 of the Russian Federation Constitution, the validity of Edict No. 63 of the Republic of Ingushetia president "On the Republic of Ingushetia Security Minister" dated 21 March 1994 is to be suspended. 2. A proposal shall be made to Republic of Ingushetia President R.S. Aushev that he bring the aforementioned edict into line with the Russian Federation Constitution. 3. This edict comes into force from the moment of signing. [Signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin Moscow, the Kremlin 5 April 1994 No. 659
