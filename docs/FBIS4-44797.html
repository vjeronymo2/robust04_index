BFN [Unattributed article: "GEC Favourites To Acquire Ferranti's Defence Activities"; as released by Brussels Textline database] [Text] In the United Kingdom, the legal administrators of Ferranti have designated GEC [General Electric Co] as a "favourite candidate" for the takeover of Ferranti's main defence activities. An agreement in principle has been concluded with GEC on the takeover of the divisions Ferranti Defence Systems Integration and Ferranti Simulation and Training which employ 1,100 salaried workers. Negotiations are expected to be concluded before the end of the month.
