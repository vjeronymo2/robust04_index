September 7, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

Heeeeere's Leno: Jay Leno is the first performer confirmed to be heading to the Persian Gulf.

The USO announced Thursday that Leno will do two concerts for U.S. troops in Saudi Arabia over the Thanksgiving weekend. A spokeswoman for Leno said the comedian will do his usual act during the two-hour volunteer shows. "When he was asked by the USO, he said, 'They sent the audience there, I better follow them,' " she said. BETH KLEID
