930401 FT 01 APR 93 / World Trade News: Turks and Romanians in banking venture A PRIVATE Turkish group is to set up a banking venture with one of Romania's largest state banks to help promote growing trade ties between the two Black Sea neighbours. Turko Romano Bank, a joint venture between Bayndr Holding, a Turkish construction and tourism group, and Banca Commerciale Romana will be Turkey's first commercial bank in Romania. The bank will provide trade finance for an estimated 1,600 small and medium-sized Turkish companies, which have invested about Dollars 30m in joint ventures in Romania.
