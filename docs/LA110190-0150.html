November 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: MALIBU;

HEARING ON POLLUTION SET

A state Senate committee will conduct a fact-finding hearing Friday in Malibu to deal with pollution in Santa Monica Bay and Malibu Lagoon State Beach.

The hearing by the Senate Committee on Toxics, chaired by Sen. Art Torres (D-Los Angeles), will be held from 9:30 a.m. to 1 p.m. at the Malibu Community Center, 9566 Fernhill Drive, a committee spokesman said.
