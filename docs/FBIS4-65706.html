BFN [Text] Ankara (DPA) -- Turkish Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin on 19 May deplored the decision of a number of laender governed by the Social Democratic Party of Germany (SPD) to halt the deportation of Kurds. No one should doubt that, like Germany, Turkey was a state that is ruled by laws, he said. "Friendship and mutual confidence between Turkey and Germany are the strongest guarantee for solving these problems."
