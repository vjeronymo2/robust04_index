930602 FT 02 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Blacks agrees sale of LA Gear business Blacks Leisure Group has agreed the sale of its LA Gear distribution business, including certain fixed assets and stocks, to LA Gear Inc for Pounds 1.65m. Of the purchase price Pounds 1.4m will be paid on completion. The consideration will be subject to adjustment following a post completion stocktake. In addition, the distribution agreement between LA Gear and Blacks' wholly-owned subsidiary, All American Footwear Corporation, will be cancelled in exchange for a payment of Pounds 700,000.
