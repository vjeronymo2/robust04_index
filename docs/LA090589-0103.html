September 5, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

As the U.S. Open tennis tournament approaches its climax, former Swedish star Bjorn Borg was drawing a different kind of crowd in Milan, Italy.

His five-minute wedding ceremony with Italian pop singer Loredana Berte at the city hall was preceded by a brawl between the couple's private security guards and reporters and photographers who were barred from the ceremony. An Italian weekly magazine was given exclusive rights to the story.
