November 20, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

COLEMAN SCORES 23 TO LEAD TROJANS OVER BRAZILIANS

Ronnie Coleman had 23 points and nine rebounds to lead USC to an 83-78 victory over Nosso of Brazil in an exhibition game Sunday night at Rancho Santiago College.

Robert Pack added 20 points and seven assists while Chris Munk had 12 points and seven rebounds for the Trojans.

USC led, 41-31, at halftime, but Nosso tied the game at 63 with 8:56 to play on a three-pointer by Carlos Romano, who led his team with 23 points. Munk tipped in an offensive rebound with 44 seconds remaining to put the Trojans in front for good.
