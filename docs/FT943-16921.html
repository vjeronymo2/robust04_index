940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Alvis cuts its Avimo stake and raises Pounds 8m Alvis, the defence contractor, has raised Pounds 8.4m cash by reducing its stake in its Avimo Singapore subsidiary from 51.3 per cent to 40 per cent. Alvis placed 11.7m ordinary shares in AVS, which makes precision optics and electro-optical instruments, on the Singapore Stock Exchange at SDollars 1.72. It will realise an exceptional profit after tax from the disposal of about Pounds 4.5m. Last year Alvis restructured its business by grouping all its electro-optical subsidiaries under AVS.
