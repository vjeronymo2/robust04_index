BFN [Text] Baku, May 26 (TURAN)--Yesterday police from Baku's Yasamaly District Station detained the chairman of Baku's Ismir District Branch of the PFA [People's Front of Azerbaijan], Agagusein Aliyev, and three members. TURAN's correspondent attempted to learn of the reasons for the detention but the police refused to release any information. However, a police official said that they are looking for two additional members of the Ismir region branch of the PFA who are at large.
