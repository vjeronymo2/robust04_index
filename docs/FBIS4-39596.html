BFN [Text] The government has no clear budget policy today, while the draft budget it has submitted is simply a statement of expenses, Federation Council chairman Vladimir Shumeyko told journalists today. He believes the draft budget has no purpose. Shumeyko compared it to porridge spread thinly on a plate. Answering a question about the Federation Council's proposal to increase military expenditure, Shumeyko said that this is a demand put forward by life itself.
