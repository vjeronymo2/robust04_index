BFN [Excerpt] After taking part in the funeral of the martyrs of the Israeli raid at Rawdat al-Shahidayn Mosque at 1100 today, Hizballah Secretary General Hasan Nasrallah stressed that the confrontation with the enemy will continue until the land is liberated. He called for withdrawing from negotiations with the enemy and for not relying on the UN Security Council. He called for making the resistance a general national plan. He said that the resistance is the rock on which the occupation will founder and the homeland will be unified. He declared that the resistance has been born anew with the fall of the martyrs yesterday. He stressed that any peace given to the killers of prophets and people and the usurpers of land and sanctities is a disgrace no matter what the justifications and circumstances. [passage omitted]
