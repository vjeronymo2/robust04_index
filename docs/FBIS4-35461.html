BFN [Excerpt] Irbil -- Regrettably, sporadic armed clashes took place in some quarters in Irbil on the evening of Sunday, 29 May 1994. As a result of these clashes, a number of people from both parties [the Kurdistan Democratic Party, KDP, and the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan, PUK] were killed or wounded, some houses were damaged, and wheat fields in the northern and eastern quarters of Irbil were set ablaze, inflicting huge losses on the owners of these fields. The leadership of both parties, the KDP and PUK, issued a joint statement in which they called for an immediate cease-fire. The events were contained thanks to the intervention and efforts exerted by all parties, including the Operation Room and the joint security committee in Irbil. [passage omitted]
