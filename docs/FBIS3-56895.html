Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the self-proclaimed Dniester Republic has outlawed any activity aimed at the organization of elections to the new parliament of Moldova in the Dniester Republic. In accordance with the resolution adopted on Wednesday, the elections to the parliament of Moldova in the Dniester Republic have been outlawed and the persons guilty of organizing the election campaign will be prosecuted. Observers in Tiraspol link this decision of the Dniester Republic parliament with the decree of the president of the Dniester Republic, Igor Smirnov, on the introduction of a "special situation" in the region. Parliamentary elections in Moldova will be held on February 27.
