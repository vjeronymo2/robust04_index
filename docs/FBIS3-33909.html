Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Liamine Zeroual, head of state and minister of national defense, today sent a message of condolences to the Rally for Culture and Democracy [RCD] following the assassination of RCD National Secretary (Ahmed Ramdane Tigziri). In his message, Liamine Zeroual stressed that he received with great sorrow the news of (Ahmed Ramdane)'s assassination. Liamine Zeroual reaffirmed his determination to do his utmost to stop the vicious circle of violence in our country.
