Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 25 (KCNA) -- A Chinese art troupe staying in Korea to participate in the 12th April spring friendship art festival gave its premiere at the Pyongyang Grand Theatre Thursday before the festival. The performance was seen by Chang Chol, vice-premier of the Administration Council and minister of culture and art, who is chairman of the organizing committee for the festival, officials concerned and Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Zonghuai and embassy officials. The performers put on the stage colorful numbers portraying the traditional customs and happy life of the Chinese people and beautiful landscape. They also put on the stage a Korean song "Long Live Generalissimo Kim Il-song" and a Korean dance "Kimilsongia in Full Bloom all Over the World". A floral basket was conveyed to them in congratulation of their successful performance.
