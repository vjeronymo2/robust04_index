940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Baring Emerging oversubscribed The placing and open offer for shares in the recently-launched Baring Emerging Europe Trust raised Dollars 124m (Pounds 83.7m) and was oversubscribed. Under the placing, interests from placees were received for 92.5m ordinary shares with warrants while the offer for subscription for the remaining 31.5m shares was subscribed some 2.2 times. Applicants for the latter will receive a scaled down allocation.
