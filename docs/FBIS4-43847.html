BFN [Text] Government Spokesman Ioannis Kasoulidhis confirmed that the government has sent letters to the leaders of the twelve European Union [EU] member states regarding the EU summit in Corfu. Kasoulidhis did not reveal the contents of the letters. On Thursday evening [23 June], President Glavkos Kliridhis will meet Greek Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou in the presence of European Commission President Jacques Delors on the sidelines of the EU summit, which will begin on Friday. Kliridhis will also meet British Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd at the latter's request. The government spokesman said the government's efforts in Corfu will focus on promoting Cypriot positions on political issues and also on the reasons why the Corfu summit must take a step forward in Cyprus' EU membership application. The president will leave for Corfu tomorrow afternoon. Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis, the government spokesman, and other officials will accompany him.
