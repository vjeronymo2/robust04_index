BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 30/5/1994 -- Indian President Shankar Dayal Sharma arrived on Monday, 30 May, morning in Bucharest for a formal visit at the invitation of Romania's President Ion Iliescu. The distinguished guest was welcomed at the International Bucharest-Otopeni Airport by a honorary mission set up on the occasion. The four-day visit of Indian President Shankar Dayal Sharma includes several rounds of talks with the Romanian head of state and the sealing of bilateral documents, as well as visits to several units in Bucharest and in Romanian counties.
