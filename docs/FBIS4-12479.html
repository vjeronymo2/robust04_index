BFN [Text] Presidential Council Chairman brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih received at his office yesterday evening brothers Safwat al-Sharif, Egyptian minister of information and envoy of President Muhammad Husni Mubarak, and Rashid 'Abdallah al-Nu'aymi, minister of foreign affairs in the United Arab Emirates [UAE] and envoy of His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan. The two envoys relayed to the brother president messages from his brothers His Excellency President Muhammad Husni Mubarak and His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan on the two countries' joint efforts and endeavors to contain the crisis. The messages affirmed that both Egypt and the UAE are committed to promoting and safeguarding Yemeni unity, given that it is a pan- Arab gain that must be protected in the interest of pan-Arab objectives and enhanced regional security and stability. The messages also underscored the need to resort to dialogue to resolve all differences in order to safeguard Yemeni unity and protect Yemen from mischief, God forbid. The two messages also reiterated that Egypt and the UAE are eager to make every possible effort to approximate views, close Yemeni ranks, and pool resources to safeguard unity through the implementation of the accord document. They also indicated that all Arabs support Yemeni unity, and that they will not accept anything that could undermine this unity. They added that any party that believes otherwise is mistaken. The two messages underlined the importance of halting media campaigns, and any step that could lead to infighting or violence. They also stressed the need to promote trust in order to create the propitious climate for dialogue in order to foster unity and safeguard stability in Yemen. For his part, the brother Presidential Council chairman highly appreciated the good offices made by the Egyptian and UAE brothers, headed by brothers President Muhammad Husni Mubarak and His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan. He commended the noble pan-Arab positions they have taken to support their Yemeni brothers and foster Yemeni unity, which is not a gain for only Yemen, but for the entire Arab nation. He affirmed that Yemeni unity is the destiny and fate of the Yemeni people. He added: We will never abandon this unity, regardless of the costs. Any path other than unity will cause a catastrophe in Yemen. Such a catastrophe is bound to affect the region and the Arab nation. He affirmed his renunciation of war, and his adherence to dialogue as the best means of addressing the situation and meeting the challenges posed by the political crisis and its regrettable repercussions. The brother Presidential Council chairman asked the two envoys to relay his greetings to his brothers President Muhammad Husni Mubarak and His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, and to also communicate to them his wishes of continued progress and prosperity for the Egyptian and UAE peoples.
