Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 14) TURAN--Today, Armenian military units attempted once more to heighten tension at in the front. [sentence as received] In the northern section of Agdamskiy, Azeri positions were shelled by artillery. At the same time, three Armenian tanks launched an attack against Azeri positions in the direction of the region of Fizulinskiy. In addition, Armenian mobilization can be observed in the Terterskiy region which is located in the northern part of Karabakh. According to the press-center of the Defence Ministry of Azerbaijan, the Azeri army has yet to take any retaliatory measures despite the provocation made.
