940804 FT 04 AUG 94 / North Sea field deal announced British Petroleum and Shell yesterday announced the first big contract connected with their accelerated development of the Foinaven field west of the Shetland Islands. A consortium of McDermott International and Golar-Nor Offshore of Norway was awarded an interim contract to begin converting a Finnish-made cargo vessel into a floating production platform. The full contract could be worth Dollars 750m over five to 10 years and BP and Shell will relinquish responsibility for operating the field to the consortium.
