941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Fitch loss halved to Pounds 0.15m Fitch, the architectural and graphic design services group, saw pre-tax losses fall from Pounds 381,000 to Pounds 151,000 for the six months to June 30. Turnover slipped from Pounds 6.28m to Pounds 6.15m. Operating profit from continuing operations was Pounds 49,000 (Pounds 149,000 loss), struck after a Pounds 233,000 exceptional charge relating to the financial restructuring of the UK business, where trading losses have reduced slightly. Losses per share fell from 2p to 1.1p.
