BFN [Text] President Hashemi-Rafsanjani today received Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah. The president described the strengthening of cooperation between Iran and Yemen as being important in helping to safeguard the security and stability of the region. Stressing the need for strengthening the unity of the two Yemens, the president announced the Islamic Republic of Iran's readiness to help in this matter. At the start of the meeting, the Yemeni foreign minister offered the warmest greetings from the president, government, and people of his country to the government and people of Iran. He described relations between the two countries as deep-rooted and historic. He expressed the interest of the government and people of Yemen in strengthening these relations. After reporting on the latest developments in Yemen, on behalf of the Yemeni president, he invited Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani to visit Yemen.
