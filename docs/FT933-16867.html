930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / UK Company News: City Site A cut of Pounds 300,000 in interest charges enabled City Site Estates, the property investor, to record a pre-tax profit of Pounds 170,000 for the half year ended March 31 1993, compared with a previous Pounds 199,000. Rental income was again Pounds 5.6m but property outgoing more than doubled to Pounds 518,000. Earnings per share were 1p, against losses of 2p after preference dividends. The company completed the disposal of Stockley House, London, for Pounds 26.6m.
