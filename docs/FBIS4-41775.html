BFN [Text] According to RID, the constituent congress of the Party of All-Belarusian Unity and Accord [Partyya Usebelaruskaha Adzinstva i Zhody] has been held in Gomel. The organizing committee of this party includes those who participated in the first stage of presidential elections but failed to secure a sufficient number of signatures to continue with their campaigns -- Mikalay Shylyahovich, Viktar Tsyareshchanka, and Uladzimir Byalow. It also includes well-known Belarusian journalists, businessmen, and scientists.
