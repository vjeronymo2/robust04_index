921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / UK Company News: British Empire Securities assets fall British Empire Securities & General Trust reported net asset value of 60.47p basic at September 30, against 64.54p a year earlier. The fully diluted figure was 60.43p, against 64.12p. Net income for the 12 months to end-September fell 8 per cent to Pounds 1.29m (Pounds 1.4m) for earnings of 0.98p (1.05p). A proposed final dividend of 0.64p makes a total of 0.89p (0.86p).
