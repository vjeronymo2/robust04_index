March 17, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Hollywood Pictures, a newly formed division of Disney Studios, has signed Goldie Hawn in its first major talent agreement. Hawn will star in seven films for Hollywood, and may produce some or all of them as well. The deal also allows her to produce other projects developed by her company. Also upcoming on Hawn's schedule is a picture for Universal -- "Bird on a Wire," an action comedy in which she stars with Mel Gibson. ALEENE MacMINN
