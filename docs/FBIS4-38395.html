BFN [Text] Sevastopol, 26 May -- The Crimean security service personnel have declared their support for the Ukrainian president's edict disbanding the service. They confirmed their subordination to the Ukrainian leadership and expressed their willingness for close cooperation with the leaders of Crimea "for the sake of solving vital problems in the region." The appeal by the personnel of the Ukrainian security service directorate in Crimea to the Crimean president and parliament was published in the Crimean press.
