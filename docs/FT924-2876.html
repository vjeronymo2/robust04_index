921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / UK Company News: NMC benefits from strong push in US Good results from the US enabled NMC Group, which serves the paper and plastic packaging and the printing industry, to improve pre-tax profit by 24.5 per cent in the half year ended September 30. Mr Norman Gordon, chief executive, said the strong performance of the core business - Universal Packaging of the US, which provides folding cartons for high volume branded food items - together with the favourable dollar enabled the second half to be faced with confidence. Sales increased 20 per cent to Pounds 52m, with the US accounting for almost Pounds 43m (Pounds 30.8m); profit came to Pounds 2.86m (Pounds 2.29m) after increased interest charges of Pounds 738,000 (Pounds 320,000). The company returned to dividends with an interim of 0.5p; last year there was no final after an interim of 1.25p. Earnings per share worked through at 2.01p (1.01p).
