BFN [Text] After a long period of truce, the Serbs launched another artillery attacks on Mostar. Ankica Posavljak has a report: [Posavljak] The information center of Mostar reports that a general alert was sounded at 1259 in the town. We were informed by the Information and Political Service of the Croatian Defense Council for the Mostar operational area that 10 tank shells were fired from Serbian positions located at the foot of Mt. Velez. The Mostar suburbs of Cim, Orlovac, Ilici, and Zovnica in the western part of Mostar, which are under Croatian Defense Council control, were targeted. A base of the Spanish UNPROFOR [UN Protection Force] battalion in Mostar was also hit and several vehicles were damaged. There have been no reports of casualties, but considerable material damage has been caused.
