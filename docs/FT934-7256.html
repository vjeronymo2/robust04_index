931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Black & Edgington in profit year-end Despite a second half loss, Black & Edgington, the USM-quoted group which provides portable buildings, tents and other structures for sporting occasions and exhibitions, ended the July 31 year with Pounds 20,000 pre-tax profits against Pounds 2.9m losses. Turnover for the 12 months fell from Pounds 9.48m to Pounds 7.6m and included Pounds 425,000 (Pounds 1.8m) from discontinued activities. Pre-tax profits were after interest payments down from Pounds 698,000 to Pounds 571,000. Earnings per share were 0.01p, compared with 0.57p losses. The directors stated that there was a decrease in bank borrowings of Pounds 508,000 over the year.
