920529 FT 29 MAY 92 / UK Company News: TDS Circuits losses cut to Pounds 0.92m Improved cost control to reduce the break-even point helped TDS Circuits, an electronics component manufacturer, to cut year-end losses before tax to Pounds 921,000 compared with Pounds 2.14m. The result was achieved with turnover kept broadly constant at Pounds 6.02m against Pounds 6.23m. Export value rose by Pounds 726,000 to Pounds 2.78m. Orders remained strong, particularly in the second half.
