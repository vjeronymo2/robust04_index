BFN [Editorial Report] PRC media monitored by Okinawa Bureau carried the following reports on PRC Central leadership activities from 15 April to 21 April 1994. The source is noted in parentheses after each report. Qiao Shi, Li Ruihuan, Hu Jintao Condole -- Upon hearing news of the death of Chu Tunan, honorary chairman of the China Democratic League and formerly vice chairman of the Sixth National People's Congress Standing Committee, party and state leaders Qiao Shi, Li Ruihuan, Hu Jintao, Rong Yiren, Tian Jiyun, Li Tieying, Wang Hanbin, and others telephoned his family to express condolences and sympathy. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0504 GMT 16 Apr 94) Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, Others Mourn -- The remains of Chu Tunan, honorary chairman of the China Democratic League Central Committee, was cremated on 19 April. "When Comrade Chu Tunan was seriously ill and after he passed away, Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, Qiao Shi, Li Ruihuan, Hu Jintao, Rong Yiren, Tian Jiyun, Li Tieying, Wang Hanbin, Song Renqiong, Chen Muhua, Fei Xiaotong, Sun Qimeng, Lei Jieqiong, Wang Zhaoguo, Zhao Puchu, Qian Weichang, Su Buqing, Wang Guoquan, Huang Hua, and leading comrades of Yunnan Province either visited him at the hospital, sent messages of condolence or called his relatives to express their sympathy." (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0631 GMT 19 Apr 94) Wu Bangguo Receives Japanese -- On 15 April, CPC Central Committee Political Bureau Member and Shanghai CPC Committee Secretary Wu Bangguo met with a delegation from the Japan Association of Corporate Executives (Keizai Doyukai), and called on Japanese business circles to seek projects in Shanghai. (Shanghai People's Radio Network in Mandarin 2300 GMT 15 April 94) Wu Bangguo Receives Balladur -- On 9 April, CPC Central Committee Political Bureau Member and Shanghai CPC Committee Secretary Wu Bangguo met with French Prime Minister Edouard Balladur and his party in Shanghai, and briefed them on Shanghai's reform, opening up to the outside world, and economic construction. He attributed Shanghai's economic achievements to the central government's leadership and Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics, and called for increased cooperation and exchanges between Shanghai and France. (Shanghai JIEFANG RIBAO in Chinese 10 Apr 94 p 1) Peng Peiyun Attends Ceremony -- The China Organizing Committee for the Fourth World Women's Congress received a donation of 1.5 million yuan from the Jiamei Group Company of Kunming, Yunnan, on 19 April. Peng Peiyuan and Xu Zhijian attended the donating ceremony. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0836 GMT 19 Apr 94) Li Ruihuan, Others Head Groups -- The Sixth Far East and South Pacific Games of the Handicapped will be held in Beijing from 4-10 September this year. Li Ruihuan and Chen Xitong are honorary chairmen of the organizing committee for the games, while Li Tieying and Peng Peiyun are honorary heads of the Chinese sports delegation to the forthcoming athletic meeting. The State Council's Coordination Committee for Handicapped Persons was briefed on the preparations for the games at its second plenary meeting in Beijing on 19 April. "The meeting was chaired by Peng Peiyun, state councillor and chairman of the Coordination Committee for Handicapped Persons. She called on members of the coordination committee to strengthen cooperation and take the initiative in providing allout support to make the games a success." (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1107 GMT 19 Apr 94) Rong Yiren Visits Exhibition -- An exhibition of photographs on the landscape of the Chang Jiang is being held in the China Art Gallery. Vice President Rong Yiren attended its opening ceremony and viewed the exhibition on 19 April. (according to Shanghai People's Radio Network in Mandarin 2300 GMT 19 Apr, Rong also inscribed the name of the exhibition) The exhibition is scheduled to close on 24 April. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0627 GMT 20 Apr 94) Tian Jiyun Writes TV Series Title -- A discussion meeting on the premiere of the 16-episode television series "Chinese Peasants" was held in the Great Hall of the People on 20 April. The title of the series was inscribed by Vice Chairman Tian Jiyun. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1035 GMT 20 Apr 94) Jiang Zemin, Li Peng Inscribe -- "China Mainland Orphan Assistance and Rescue Social Welfare Program" sponsored by the China Society for Promoting Social Services was inaugurated on 23 October 1993. Its main purpose is to help orphans by seeking donations from the public. Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, and Qiao Shi recently inscribed for the "program." Jiang Zemin's inscription is: "Let the entire society show concern about the orphans' growth." Li Peng's inscription is: "The entire society should be concerned about the growth of orphans." Qiao Shi wrote: "The entire society should display a humanitarian spirit and show a common concern about and help the healthy growth of orphans." (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1106 GMT 21 Apr 94) Peng Peiyun Addresses Jiangsu Meeting -- A Jiangsu provincial family planning work meeting was held in Nanjing on 15 and 16 April. Peng Peiyun, state councillor and minister of the state Family Planning Commission, attended and addressed the meeting. She pointed out: "Jiangsu is one of the 10 provinces in China which have done a better job of family planning work. However, Jiangsu is a province with a large base figure and high density of population so it must not relax its efforts in family planning even a little bit." The meeting studied Governor Chen Huanyou's speech on family planning delivered at a recent meeting of city party secretaries and mayors. (Nanjing Jiangsu People's Radio Network in Mandarin 2300 GMT 16 Apr 94) Li Ruihuan, Li Tieying Head Committee -- The organizing committee for the 43d world table tennis championship tournament was formed recently in Tianjin. Li Ruihuan serves as honorary chairman of the organizing committee, while Li Tieying serves as honorary vice chairman. (Beijing Central People's Radio Network in Mandarin 1030 GMT 17 Apr 94) Li Tieying Inspects Shenzhen -- "Comrade Li Tieying, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and state councillor, successively inspected the Stock Exchange and some companies and enterprises in Shenzhen on 15 and 16 April. He also conducted an investigation and study there on how to establish a modern enterprise system. He fully affirmed the useful explorations made by Shenzhen on the management of state property and the establishment of a modern enterprise system." (Beijing Central Television Program One Network in Mandarin 1100 GMT 17 Apr 94) Wei Jianxing Addresses Training Class -- "Wei Jianxing, member of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee and secretary of the Central Commission for Discipline Inspection, on 20 April called on all comrades attending a training class for discipline inspection and supervision cadres to study basic knowledge on securities. He said: The discipline and supervision cadres should pay attention to studying the theories and basic knowledge of socialist market economy during the course of studying Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics." (Shanghai People's Radio Network in Mandarin 2300 GMT 20 Apr 94)
