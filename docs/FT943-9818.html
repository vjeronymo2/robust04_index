940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / Observer: Well balanced No one can say that former Soviet enterprises released from the shackles of communism are not setting themselves ambitious targets. Passengers on Uzbekistan Airways' regular service between London and Tashkent must be reassured to read a new year wish from Gani Rafikov, the company's director-general: 'That the number of take-offs be equal to the number of landings.'
