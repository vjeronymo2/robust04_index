October 29, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: HUNTINGTON BEACH;

MAN SERIOUSLY HURT IN PARKING-LOT FIGHT

A 19-year-old Westminster man suffered a skull fracture during a fight in the parking lot of a 7-Eleven store early Sunday.

Police said they answered a call about a fight at Newland Street and Warner Avenue at 1:20 a.m. and found Robert Rounsefell unconscious.

He was listed in critical but stable condition Sunday at Humana Hospital-Westminster.

Detectives interviewed witnesses and people involved in the fight, but no arrests were made Sunday. Information on the cause of the fight was not available from police.

A clerk at the store said that two people were involved and that they were beating and kicking each other, but he didn't know what the fight was about. MARLA CONE
