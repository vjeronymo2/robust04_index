BFN [Text] Over 30 local residents were wounded in riots in Hebron today. Of these, 25 people were wounded by rubber bullets; they were treated and sent home. Six others were wounded by live gunfire: one seriously, one moderately, and four slightly. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that an IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldier who was hit by a stone suffered moderate injuries. Two other soldiers and a border policeman sustained slight injuries. Our correspondent notes that an investigation into the incident in which a grenade was thrown at an IDF unit this morning has revealed that six local residents were wounded by shrapnel. There were no IDF casualties.
