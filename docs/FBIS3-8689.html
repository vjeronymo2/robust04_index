Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] At today's meeting in Bratislava, the Slovak Government has unanimously decided to end its activity. According to another decision, no member of Vladimir Meciar's government will accept the president's request to exercise his duties until the formation of a new cabinet. The government has also agreed to recall all state secretaries [deputy ministers] and all heads of ministerial offices by 18 March. Meciar announced that he received two letters from the president. In one of the letters, the president notified him of his dismissal from the office of the prime minister and in the second one, he was asked as the chairman of the strongest political party to nominate a representative of the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia [HZDS] who would attempt to form a new government. Meciar emphasized that the HZDS was not going to enter into any government and was going into opposition.
