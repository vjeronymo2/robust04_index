Appendix C to §1910.266_Comparable ISO Standards (Non-mandatory) The following International Labor Organization (ISO) standards are comparable to the corresponding Society of Automotive Engineers (Standards that are referenced in this standard.) Utilization of the ISO standards in lieu of the corresponding SAE standards should result in a machine that meets the OSHA standard.
