940104 FT 04 JAN 94 / UK Company News: GrandMet agrees terms over Absolut US rights Grand Metropolitan, the food, drinks and retailing group, said it had agreed terms over the early transfer of its US distribution rights for Absolut vodka, the Swedish-owned premium brand, to Seagram, the North American drinks group. Vin & Sprit, the Swedish wine and spirits company which makes Absolut, shocked GrandMet's IDV drinks division in October by announcing the transfer of the brand from September 1994. The reason for the switch appears to have been growing doubts at V&S about the future development of Absolut alongside GrandMet's own Smirnoff and Popov brands. IDV yesterday said it had agreed to bring forward to February 1 the transfer of its distribution rights to Absolut in the US, Puerto Rico and the US Virgin Islands. A confidential settlement between the three parties compensated IDV for all the profits foregone between February and September. Mr John McGrath, chairman and chief executive of IDV, said: 'We consider it in everyone's best interest to agree the early transfer of the Absolut brand in the US to Seagram.'
