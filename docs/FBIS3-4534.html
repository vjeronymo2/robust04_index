Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 30 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin met with C.H. Llewellyn Smith, director of the European Organization of Nuclear Research (EONR), and Professor C.C. Ting, nobel prize winner, here today. During the meeting, the Chinese president expressed his hope that exchanges and cooperation between China and EONR in high-energy physics will be further enhanced. Llewellyn Smith briefed Jiang on cooperation links between EONR and scientists from various countries, especially its cooperation with Chinese scientists. Song Jian, state councillor and minister in charge of the State Science and Technology Commission, and Xu Zhihong, vice-president of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, took part in the meeting.
