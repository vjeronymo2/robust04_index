940214 FT 14 FEB 94 / World News in Brief: American wins Olympic downhill title American Tommy Moe, left, won the downhill skiing title on the first day of competition at the Winter Olympics at Lillehammer, Norway. Moe, 23, unheralded before the race, won by four-hundredths of a second ahead of Kjetil Andre Aamodt of Norway - the narrowest margin of victory in Olympic Alpine history.
