BFN [Text] Brother Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-Husayn al-Ahmar, [Yemeni] House of Representatives speaker, arrived in Abu Dhabi last night heading a delegation on a two-day visit to the sisterly United Arab Emirates [UAE]. He was received on arrival at Abu Dhabi Airport by His Excellency al-Hajj Bin-'Abdallah (al-Muhaydi), chairman of the Federal National Council, a number of UAE officials, and brother Muhammad Hatim al-Khawi, our country's ambassador to the UAE.
