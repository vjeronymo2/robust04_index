May 5, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

4 SOCCER TEAMS PLAY AT COLISEUM

The national soccer teams of El Salvador, Guatemala, Chile and Paraguay will compete in a tournament this weekend at the Coliseum. Chile will play Guatemala tonight at 7, and El Salvador will take on Paraguay at 9. Sunday, Guatemala will face Paraguay at 4:30 p.m., and El Salvador will meet Chile at 6:30. CHRIS BAKER
