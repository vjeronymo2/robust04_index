Internal Revenue Service OMB Number: 1545&hyph;1225. Form Number: IRS Form 5310&hyph;A. Type of Review: Revision. Title: Notice of Merger, Consolidation, Spinoff, or Transfer of Plan Assets or Liabilities; Notice of Qualified Separate Lines of Business. Description: Plan administrators are required to notify IRS of any plan mergers, consolidations, spinoffs, or transfers of plan assets or liabilities to another plan. Employers are required to notify IRS of separate lines of business for their deferred compensation plans. Form 5310&hyph;A is used to make these notifications.
