BFN [Report by Levente Suekoesd] [Text] The majority of the new 22-member KDNP [Christian Democratic People's Party] parliamentary group elected Tibor Fuezessy head of the deputy group at their founding session in Dobogoekoe. They also decided that in addition to the new parliamentary group head, [outgoing] state secretary Tamas Isepy and Janos Latorczai, [outgoing] minister of industry and trade, will represent the KDNP at the six-party preliminary coordination talks on the distribution of the parliamentary and parliamentary committee seats, former parliamentary group head Bela Csepe told the "Chronicle."
