April 29, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

A CASE OF TWO PLAYERS IN THE WRONG PLACE WITH THE WRONG SKIN?

On a day that was supposed to be Troy Aikman's, the coverage given to Rodney Peete and Erik Affholter (both middle picks) far outweighed that given to the nation's No. 1 pick and highest-paid rookie.

HARPER LELAND

CLAUDE O'BRIEN
