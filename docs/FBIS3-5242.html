Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, March 7 (YONHAP) -- The government is speeding up its work on administrative reorganization, with an eye to finishing the job by month's end. As the state council approved the Trade, Industry and Energy Ministry's reorganization plan at a meeting on Monday, the Government Administration Ministry will urge other ministries to submit their restructuring plans as soon as possible. The schemes of the Economic Planning Board and the Korean National Railroad have already been approved by the state council, and those of the Justice, Labor, Transportation, Communications and Environment Ministries and of the National Tax Administration, the Military Manpower Administration and the Korean industrial property office are now in the Government Administration Ministry's hands and are ready for examination by the state council. The government will finish the reorganization by month's end without making any changes in civil service strength from the present level, Government Administration Ministry officials said.
