BFN [Excerpt] Prime Minister Edem Kodjo's Togolese Union for Democracy, UTD, today stated in a communique that the prime minister had "in no way" decided to resign in spite of the crisis that has broken out within the opposition, which has a majority in parliament. Last night, Mr. Kodjo had, in an interview with the AFP, envisaged this possibility if allies persisted in their refusal to participate in the government. [passage omitted]
