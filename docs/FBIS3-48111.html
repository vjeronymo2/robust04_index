Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 14 (KCNA) -- The final damming project on the Chongsu Islet tideland was completed successfully in Korea in the new year. The nature-remaking project is to reclaim 1,000 hectares of land by building a dam linking Sikyo-ri, Chongsu Islet and Tungam-ri in Kangyong County, South Hwanghae Province. The project has increased the area of cultivated land and changed the coastline and looks of the country. The builders of the South Hwanghae Provincial Chongdan General Tideland Reclamation Enterprise who were in charge of the project had overfulfilled the daily plan by more than 70 percent on an average in the whole period of the project by boosting the utility rate of transport means and actively introducing new construction methods.
