BFN [Excerpts] The Alliance for Democracy, Aford, has today congratulated the president-elect, Mr. Bakili Muluzi, for winning the presidential vote, and wished him success in his new position. The president of Aford, Mr. Chakufwa Chihana, spoke to reporter (Francis Chicucuzeni) today, saying he was concerned about the regional fragmentation of this country in the elections. [Begin Chihana recording] You are bringing a technical issue. I am concerned, I am worried that the country has been fragmented. Let me be very ordinary with you -- I was rather technical. Say, he has been voted overwhelmingly by the people of the south, southern region. And it seems the MCP [Malawi Congress Party] are holding the (?differential), and to my disappointment the people of the north have voted for AFORD. Regardless of these factors which have been created by the result of the election, if I were Bakili I would definitely call on the MCP, the Alliance for Democracy for a get-together meeting to discuss this problem first before going to [word indistinct] [passage omitted].
