BFN [Text] Peru and Romania today signed six important bilateral cooperation agreements, mainly on business and commercial investment. The documents were signed by Peruvian Prime Minister Efrain Goldenberg and Romanian Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu. [Begin recording] [Unidentified reporter] Six important bilateral agreements between Peru and Romania were signed in the Foreign Ministry today. The agreements include investment promotion, economic and commercial agreement, and the creation of consultation mechanisms between the two countries' foreign ministries. The agreements also refer to the reciprocal elimination of visas for diplomatic use, agrarian projects, and agreements between the Romanian Chamber of Commerce and Adex [Exporters Association]. The documents were signed by Romanian Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu and his Peruvian counterpart, Efrain Goldenberg. [Vacaroiu, in Romanian with simultaneous translation into Spanish] I am convinced the results of the agreements will be positive. I am thinking of the two countries' economic potential, the two governments' will in supporting this action for developing bilateral economic relations. [end recording]
