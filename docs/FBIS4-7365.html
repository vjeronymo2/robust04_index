BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 29 (KCNA) -- Syrian Ambassador to Korea Yasir Farah and his wife on April 28 met with Yi In-mo, a former war correspondent of the Korean People's Army, who is known as an incarnation of faith and will. The ambassador said the Syrian people were struck with admiration at Yi In-mo who did not abandon his creed as a communist while being kept behind bars for a long time, not yielding to the barbarous suppression by the enemy. Recalling that the great leader President Kim Il-song called on Yi In-mo in his sickbed last year, so busy as he was, and showed deep solicitude for him, the ambassador said he was deeply moved by the president's loving care for his revolutionary soldiers. Yi In-mo said that the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il are pursuing benevolent politics and independent politics and that they are the life and soul of the Korean people. The guests presented souvenirs to Yi In-mo.
