November 15, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: CITY'S BUILDING PERMITS TO BE ISSUED WITH A CATCH

Developers willing to assume all legal liability may apply for building permits from the city of Lawndale while its officials try to resolve a dilemma that has halted major development in the city.

Voters last week invalidated Lawndale's General Plan, which is required under state law before a city issues building permits. The City Council voted Monday to ask state officials to approve an interim permit process in the South Bay city.
