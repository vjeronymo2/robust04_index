920415 FT 15 APR 92 / UK Company News: London St Lawrence net assets down Net asset value of London and St Lawrence Investment stood at 138.24p at February 29, down from 140.02p a year earlier. Net revenue for the six months to end-February amounted to Pounds 520,684 (Pounds 493,338) for earnings of 2.74p (2.6p) per share.
