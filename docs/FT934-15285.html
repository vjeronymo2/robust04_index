931013 FT 13 OCT 93 / Maastricht clears last obstacle THE LAST legal obstacle to the ratification of the Maastricht treaty on European union was removed by the German federal constitutional court yesterday. The court in Karlsruhe rejected a series of challenges to the treaty as either inadmissible or unsubstantiated. The ruling by the eight judges, four of whom are pictured above, was greeted with relief in European capitals, allying fears that the German judicial authorities might impose conditions incompatible with the treaty.
