August 30, 1989, Wednesday, Southland Edition

THE STATE

A car dealer from Hayward took out a newspaper advertisement apologizing for any displeasure he may have caused by hiring a stripper to entertain some policemen during a party he gave for them in his luxury box at the Oakland Coliseum before an April 26 Oakland Athletics game. The Coliseum and the team warned Dale Bridges that "any future actions in violation of the Skybox code of conduct will result in immediate termination of his Skybox contract."
