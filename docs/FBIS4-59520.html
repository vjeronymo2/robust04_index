BFN [Text] Field Colonel 'Ali al-Jayfi, commander of the northern Yemeni al-'Amaliqah Brigade, has said that his troops, backed with tanks and artillery units, are currently stationed at a distance of 40 km northeast of Aden, having inflicted successive defeats on the southern forces and overrun their strongholds in Abyan. Speaking to an international media delegation touring the areas of the fighting, Col. al-Jayfi said that some northern troops have arrived at the al-'Alam area 20 km north of Aden. He pointed out that President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih ordered the siege tightened on Aden, stronghold of the Yemeni Socialist Party. The northern commander added that his troops are repositioning themselves around the city and waiting for a political decision to capture it. An official military source in Sanaa has said that the northern troops advanced in the al-Dali' sector and regained control of the southern al-Musaymir town.
