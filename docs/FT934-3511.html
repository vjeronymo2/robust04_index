931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / International Company News: HK travel group changes hands Malayan United Industries' Hong Kong unit, Firstway International Investment, has bought 52.8 per cent of Morning Star Holdings for HKDollars 229.91m (USDollars 19.23m) and will make a general offer for the Hong Kong-based travel company, Reuter reports from Kuala Lumpur.
