October 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NOVAK ON AMERICANS MOVING UP

Your headline said that the "rich" were to be taxed by the proposed House budget. After reading the article, I found that the "rich" were those people making more than $50,000. Just whose definition of rich is this? The idea that anyone making above $50,000 a year is rich is a lie.

TYE RUBINS

Beverly Hills
