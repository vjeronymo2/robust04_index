940422 FT 22 APR 94 / UK Company News: British Empire Secs British Empire Securities and General Trust reported a basic net asset value of 107.88p per share as at March 31, up from 92.82p at end-September and 78.66p at end-March 1993. The fully diluted figure was 103.57p per share, against 89.83p and 76.97p respectively. A substantial increase in interest payments following October's debenture stock issue left net revenue at Pounds 374,144 (Pounds 771,589), equivalent to earnings of 0.28p (0.59p). The interim dividend is maintained at 0.25p.
