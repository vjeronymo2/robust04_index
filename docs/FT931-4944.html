930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / Business and the Law: Senior salaries - Legal Briefs SENIOR in-house lawyers at the Fortune magazine top 100 US companies received an 11 per cent pay increase last year taking the average salary package for these lawyers to Dollars 469,500 (Pounds 330,600). Their UK counterparts enjoyed an average 3.2 per cent rise according to a survey by legal recruitment consultants Chambers & Partners. Average salary for senior in-house lawyers reached Pounds 89,960. The top 10 per cent in the 30-34 and 40-44 age groups fared better, with remuneration of Pounds 149,000 and Pounds 171,250 respectively.
