930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Edinburgh Oil projects advance Edinburgh Oil and Gas continued its steady progress in the first half of 1993, the highlight being the consent received to develop the North Yorkshire Power Project. Agreement had been reached with Scottish Power to finance construction of the project and to purchase the gas production expected to flow at the end of 1994. All three gas projects made significant progress in the last few months, as it was also agreed that Scottish Power will buy the uncommitted gas reserves in the Hatfield Moors field, and planning permission was received for the Albury gas development in southern England. Turnover came to Pounds 1.17m ((Pounds 851,000), operating profit to Pounds 74,000 (loss Pounds 28,000) and pre-tax profit to Pounds 103,000 (Pounds 6,000). Earnings per share were 0.6p (0.02p).
