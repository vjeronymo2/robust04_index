October 31, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Bank Employee Arrested: Swiss police said they arrested Konstantin Phacos, 39, a former Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co. investment banker in connection with the alleged embezzlement of $2.2 million from the bank's Geneva office. A Manufacturers Hanover spokesman, John Stefans, confirmed in New York that "irregularities concerning customer accounts were discovered in our Geneva office" and that Swiss officials were investigating.
