October 18, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Diaper Maker to Change Environmental Claims: Yielding to pressure that its "green marketing" is deceptive, American Enviro Products of Placentia said it has agreed to tone down claims that its Bunnies biodegradable disposable diapers benefits the environment.

The firm entered into the agreement following a probe of the environmental claims by a group of attorneys general in 10 states, including California, on behalf of consumers. The company agreed to redesign its diapers and packaging, and pay $50,000 in settlement costs. It admitted no wrongdoing in making the settlement.
