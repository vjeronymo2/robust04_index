April 20, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

JANET JACKSON TICKETS GO ON SALE SUNDAY

Tickets for Janet Jackson's June 23 and 24 stand at the Pacific Amphitheatre go on sale Sunday at 9 a.m. at the amphitheater box office, Ticketron outlets, and via Teletron. Outlets will distribute priority wristbands starting at 7 a.m.

Tickets go on sale Saturday at 10 a.m. for Pacific shows on May 26 by Tracy Chapman and Johnny Clegg & Savuka and July 7 by UB40. Information: (714) 634-1300.
