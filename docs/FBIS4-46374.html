BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] The Shin Bet several days ago uncovered an assassination attempt against Jibril al-Rujub, head of the Palestinian preventive security service. Al-Rujub, who was in Tulkarm that day, was informed he had better cancel a planned visit to Nabulus because he can expect an unpleasant surprise there. The tension between the Palestinian police and Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] in the Gaza and Jericho autonomy areas is increasing.
