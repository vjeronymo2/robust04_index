June 14, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

My Sister Pam: Pam Dawber, who made her television series debut with Robin Williams in the old "Mork & Mindy" sitcom, will star in a new half-hour series for CBS-TV. Dawber signed a development deal with the network to star in the as yet untitled series to be produced by Pony Productions in association with Grant-Tribune Productions. The actress' most recent series was CBS' "My Sister Sam." BETH KLEID
