Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] In 1993, the industrial production in this province, on the basis of the previous year's fast and efficient growth, maintained a strong momentum of development. The industrial output value generated throughout the year was 87.89 billion yuan, up 38.2 percent over the previous year. The net increase of output value was 24.4 billion yuan. The combined industrial and agricultural output value was 121.6 billion yuan, up 27.1 percent. Last year witnessed deepened reform, expanded opening up, and accelerated development in this province. The broad ranks of cadres and workers in the industrial and communication sector provincewide further implemented the spirit of the important talks of Comrade Deng Xiaoping, the 14th National CPC Congress, and the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee. They emancipated their minds, forged ahead despite difficulties, and scored a victory in the third campaign of the effort to tackle key problems in industrial adjustment with conscientious efforts. They devoted much energy to enterprise reform, structural adjustment, strengthening of management, and acceleration of technical transformation, and succeeded in maintaining synchronized fast growth of industrial production speed and efficiency. The characteristics of industrial production last year are as follows: First, strenuous efforts were made to implement the "Regulations on Converting the Operational Mechanisms of State-Owned Industrial Enterprises" and the implementation measures made by the provincial authorities. The number of joint-stock industrial and commercial companies rose to 26 and that of enterprises undergoing reform experiments conducted in light of the policies for Chinese- foreign joint ventures expanded to 230. There are now 36 standard enterprise groups and 110 small state-owned industrial enterprises run by local people all over the province. By the end of the third quarter of this year [as heard], there were over 33,000 township and town enterprises under the cooperative shares system. Second, the internal management of enterprises, energy conservation, and cutting down of consumption were strengthened. In the industrial and communication sector, an extensive production and economy drive was carried out. The rate of energy conservation and consumption reduction province-wide was about 10 percent.
