920605 FT 05 JUN 92 / International Company News: JAL unveils revenue plan JAPAN Air Lines, the Japanese international carrier which last month announced a loss for the year to the end of March, yesterday unveiled a cost-cutting and revenue-boosting programme, writes Stefan Wagstyl in Tokyo. By increasing income from domestic operations, JAL hopes by the 1996-97 financial year to increase net revenues by Y100bn (Dollars 782m) a year. It is planning a Y25bn pre-tax profit in 1996-97.
