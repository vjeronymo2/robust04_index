Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 11 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- For ensuring a usual activity of the public transport service of the Mongolian capital with nearly 600 thousand residents there is required more than 490 buses and trolley-buses. According to the rough calculations for a partial renovation of the motor fleet and purchasing 120 buses it is needed over 3 billion tugrik and it is necessary to render urgently proper aid to the transport servicemen on the part of appropriate organizations in finding such financial resources. Last year in the capital on 21 routes there run about 160-180 buses and on 7 routes some 80-85 trolley-buses. The public transport service goes on to be the matter of concern of the communal service of the Mongolian capital.
