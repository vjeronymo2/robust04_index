June 23, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

RYAN GIVES HOPE TO AN OLDER CROWD

I've read that Joe DiMaggio's hitting streak was fed to computers and predicted to last 520 years (as a record). Let's do the same to six no-hitters and I'll bet you have a record for 1,000 years. After all, Nolan Ryan has beaten Sandy Koufax by 50% and the other greats by 100%.

STEVE LEONARD

Los Angeles
