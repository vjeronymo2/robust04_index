941221 FT 21 DEC 94 / International Company News: ConAgra sees further growth ConAgra, the US diversified foods group, said it expects fiscal 1995 to be its 15th consecutive year of record earnings, Reuter reports from Omaha. Mr Philip Fletcher, chairman, said: 'We believe it also will be a year in which ConAgra creates substantial shareholder value for the future.' Net income for the second quarter ended November 27 was Dollars 0.63 a share, up from Dollars 0.56 a year ago. In the first half, ConAgra earned Dollars 0.94 a share, up from Dollars 0.83. 'Our company's first-half results were excellent, but our actions to drive future results were more important,' Mr Fletcher said. He said ConAgra had acquired several businesses that fit tightly with core operations and add quickly to earnings. It began an accelerated programme to divest non-core businesses and continued to refresh branded foods, including Healthy Choice, he added.
