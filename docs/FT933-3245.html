930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / World Trade News: French farmers may stand alone French farmers, who will today try to blockade Paris, risk 'isolation' within French society if they disrupt the life of their fellow citizens, Mr Jean Puech, the country's farm minister, warned yesterday, writes David Buchan from Paris. But Mr Philippe Arnaud, a leader of the hardline Co-ordination Rurale which is mounting today's protest, claimed yesterday his organisation was trying to bolster, not thwart, government demands for a better deal for French farming. 'We just want to show that there are some Robin Hoods determined to defend their way of life,' he told Le Figaro newspaper.
