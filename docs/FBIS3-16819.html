Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Following talks that lasted nearly two years, Lithuania and Poland have initialled a bilateral treaty on good neighborliness and cooperation. Upon initialling the treaty in Warsaw on Friday [18 March] the Lithuanian and Polish foreign ministers stressed that the treaty was of vital importance to both sides while [words indistinct]. According to Polish Foreign Minister Andrzej Olechowski the treaty opened a new page in bilateral relations between Lithuania and Poland. Lithuanian Foreign Minister Povilas Gylys said that some time would still be required to understand the importance of this document. The text of the treaty will be made public as soon as it is signed by the presidents of the two states. The signing of the document is expected to take place in [word indistinct] April.
