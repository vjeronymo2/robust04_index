920317 FT 17 MAR 92 / UK Company News: US behind asset rise at Alliance Trust The Alliance Trust reported a net asset value of Pounds 15.39p at January 31 -an advance of some 24 per cent over the year. Directors attributed the rise to the comparative strength of US markets where the largest proportion of the trust's portfolio outside the UK is centred. Net revenue edged ahead to Pounds 21.9m (Pounds 20.5m), for earnings of 43.5p (40.66p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 29p lifts the total for the year by 3p to 43p.
