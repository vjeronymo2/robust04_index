BFN [Text] This morning a new group of Palestinian police left Algeria by air. In implementation of the articles of the self-rule agreement, which stipulate for the creation of a Palestinian police force in the two areas of Gaza and Jericho, a new group of Palestinian police left at dawn today by air from Laghouat military airport, heading for Rafah on the Egyptian-Palestinian border. Brigadier-General Fodil Saidi, commander of the Fourth Military Zone, was at the farewell of the group. This group is composed of 256 soldiers, most of them are young, who received training at Algerian military academies. This group will enter the Palestinian territories tomorrow, Sunday evening, to be deployed in the city of Gaza.
