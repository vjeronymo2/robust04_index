BFN [Report by Mirza Sadikovic from Bihac] [Text] Throughout last night and this morning chetnik artillery has been pounding the Bihac area. From their strongholds in Veliki (Radic), and Grabez, the chetniks have been shelling parts of this UN protected zone with howitzers and mortars, and trying to conceal this behind the [words indistinct] conflict caused by Abdic's traitor clique. Dozens of shells were fired overnight at the outskirts of Bihac, while the defense lines on the Grmusko-Srbljanski plateau, Hasin Vrh, and Grabez have been under continuous mortar fire. In decisive counterattacks, the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army 5th Corps has recaptured some of the territory on the plateau, inflicting heavy losses on the chetniks in manpower, and capturing a large amount of ammunition and equipment. This must have been the reason for the start of the chetniks' bestial shelling of civilian targets, which also served as a sign to the Kladusa traitor paramilitaries to intensify their own attacks on the areas of [word indistinct], Pecigrad, (Liskovac), and Todorovo.
