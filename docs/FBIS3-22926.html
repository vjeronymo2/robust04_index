Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Sandra Vergara on the "National Newscast"] [Text] Good afternoon. At this very moment, in the presence of Fabio Villegas, government and interior minister, the governor of Antioquia Department, the mayor of Medellin, and representatives of the People's Militia, a pre-agreement for the negotiation of a real peace process between the People's Militia and the Colombian Government is being signed. The members of the People's Militia have said that a cease fire will begin with the signing of this pre-agreement and that in the next 30 days, approximately 400 armed militiamen will surrender their weapons.
