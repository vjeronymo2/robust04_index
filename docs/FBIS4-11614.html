BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Peru monitored through 21 April. The Eximbank of Japan on 13 April reported in Tokyo that it will grant $22.5 million to Peru to finance electricity projects. The agreement was signed during the Inter-American Development Bank annual meeting held in Guadalajara, Mexico. (Lima LA REPUBLICA in Spanish 14 Apr 94 p 2) The Lima stock market showed an increase of 24.3 percent in the year's first three months, when it traded $948 million compared with the $253.1 million negotiated in the same period in 1993. (Lima EL COMERCIO in Spanish 17 Apr 94 p A16) The State Petroleum Agency reported that the new oil well in the Pavayacu oil field, 235 km west of Iquitos, will begin operation on 19 April. It will produce 6,000 barrels of oil daily. Oil production went from 1,500 barrels per day in February 1993 to 14,885 barrels in March following the discovery of 11 oil wells in the past 12 months. (Lima EL COMERCIO in Spanish 18 Apr 94 p 1)
