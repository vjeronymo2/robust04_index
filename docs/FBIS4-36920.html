BFN [Text] Mohammad Nawaz Sharif, leader of the opposition in the National Assembly, has filed a constitutional petition with the Lahore High Court in which he requested that the provincial government be restrained from filing cases against him. A spokesman for the Governor's House in Lahore has rejected reports on the possible arrest of opposition leader Mian Mohammad Nawaz Sharif. He said that the provincial governor had given no interview to the GULF NEWS as was reported; therefore, the reports are baseless, fabricated, and speculative.
