930605 FT 05 JUN 93 / Recruitment agency takeover NRL, a national technical recruitment consultancy, has bought MSM Holdings and its network of offices in Aberdeen, Glasgow, Edinburgh, Newcastle, Manchester and Watford. The price is not being disclosed. NRL - which is based in Egremont, Cumbria, with branches in Newcastle, Falkirk and Swindon - will have a staff of about 800 in the combined operation, with turnover of Pounds 15m. The deal, arranged by KPMG Corporate Finance in Manchester, where MSM is based, includes the purchase of three smaller recruitment agencies. NRL was bought out from John Laing with help from 3i last year for Pounds 1.5m by Mr Andrew Redmayne and his management team.
