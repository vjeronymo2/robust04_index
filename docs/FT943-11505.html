940729 FT 29 JUL 94 / International Company News: Taiwan bank rises State-run International Commercial Bank of China, one of Taiwan's leading commercial banks, posted provisional pre-tax profits of NTDollars 1.81bn (USDollars 68m) for the year to June 30, up 5.8 per cent on the previous 12 months, writes Laura Tyson in Taipei. Revenues rose 22.8 per cent to NTDollars 11.8bn.
