Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Dr.'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali received at his office in the Prime Ministry today Dr. Haydar 'Abd-al-Shafi, head of the Palestinian delegation to the peace negotiations. The meeting reviewed the latest developments of the peace process, especially in the Palestinian arena. Dr. 'Abd-al-Shafi emphasized the importance of deepening Jordanian-Palestinian coordination regarding the peace negotiations, and said this coordination should take place any time and anywhere. He emphasized the importance of a unified Arab position and the continuation of coordination by the two sides. On the memorandum he is carrying to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, Dr. 'Abd-al-Shafi said this memorandum deals with the difficult and sensitive situation the Palestinians are facing concerning the interpretation and implementation of the Declaration of Principles agreement. He said the Palestinians are facing great challenges, and the memorandum and the meeting [with 'Arafat] concern improving the Palestinian performance in facing these challenges.
