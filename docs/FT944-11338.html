941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / People (Names in the News): Harry Schwartz goes home Harry Schwartz, South Africa's ambassador to the US and a longtime friend of President Mandela, is going back into legal practice and will devote himself to getting foreign investment for 'the new South Africa', writes Nancy Dunne. He is setting up shop in Johannesburg representing the prestigious Washington law firm of Akin, Gump, Strauss, Hauer & Feld, which is headed by two Democratic party stalwarts. He also will join the Johannesburg-based law firm of Hofmeyr van der Merwe, which he is merging with his own former firm -Schwartz-North. 'I would like to devote my efforts to sustaining democracy by assisting in the development of economic growth and job creation,' he said in his farewell statement.
