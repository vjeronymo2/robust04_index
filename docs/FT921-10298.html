920207 FT 07 FEB 92 / Denmark will bring forward CFCs ban DENMARK'S environment minister, Mr Per Stig Moller, said yesterday that chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs) would be banned in the country from 1995, five years earlier than planned, writes Hilary Barnes in Copenhagen. His statement follows reports this week that the ozone layer over Scandinavia is exceptionally thin this winter. The Energy Agency yesterday advised Danes spending a lot of time out of doors this winter to use protective cream against the risk of sunburn.
