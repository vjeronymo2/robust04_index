920203 FT 03 FEB 92 / Construction Contracts: Aircraft servicing WIMPEY CONSTRUCTION (UK) has been awarded a contract worth Pounds 5m by Travers Morgan Project Management, acting on behalf of the Ministry of Defence, for the design and build of an aircaft servicing hanger at RAF St Athan. The project, which is to be completed by the end of August, will provide facilities for servicing VC10 aircraft.
