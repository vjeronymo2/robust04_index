920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / US producer prices rise US producer prices for finished goods rose 0.1 per cent last month and by 1.7 per cent in the year to July, indicating that inflationary pressures remain subdued, the Labour Department reported yesterday, Michael Prowse reports from Washington. The figures were in line with forecasts in financial markets and followed a 0.2 per cent increase in producer prices in June.
