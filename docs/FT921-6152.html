920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Eleco First half profits at Eleco Holdings, the building products and contracting group, fell from Pounds 2.34m to Pounds 1.16m pre-tax. Turnover slipped by Pounds 3.19m to Pounds 26.41m. Earnings amounted to 2.6p (4.9p) and the interim dividend is a same-again 2.3p.
