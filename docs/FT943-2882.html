940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Swallowfield doubles Pre-tax profits doubled at Swallowfield in the 24 weeks to June 18, from Pounds 522,000 to Pounds 1.04m. Turnover at the aerosols, toiletries and cosmetics manufacturer increased 24 per cent from Pounds 12m to Pounds 14.9m. Earnings per share more than doubled to 5.6p (2.6p) and the interim dividend has been raised to 2.7p (2.2p).
