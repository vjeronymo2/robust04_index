BFN [Text] Yesterday, His Excellency [H.E.] Tan Sri Datuk Mohamed Zahir bin Haji Ismail, speaker of the House of Representatives of Malaysia [HRM], and the high-level HRM delegation paid a courtesy call on H.E. Nouhak Phoumsavan, president of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR], at the Hokham presidential palace. The high-level HRM delegation is currently paying a friendship visit to the LPDR at the invitation of H.E. Saman Vi-gnaket, chairman of the National Assembly. H.E. the HRM speaker expressed pleasure and profound thanks to H.E. Nouhak Phoumsavan for according him and his delegation a warm and honorable welcome. He also informed the LPDR president of the outcome of the talks between the high-level delegations of the HRM and the LPDR National Assembly. He said the two legislative organizations unanimously agreed on all issues raised by the two sides for consultation at the talks, thereby further strengthening the relations of friendship and good neighborliness. The Lao and Malaysian peoples always maintain a feeling of affection, normal relations, and good cooperation with one another. The two countries frequently exchange visits by delegations at various levels [words indistinct]. The relations between the legislative organizations of Malaysia and Laos have also been developed with improved quality. LPDR President H.E. Nouhak Phoumsavan hailed the friendship visit to the LPDR by the high-level HRM delegation, noting that the visit will contribute to strengthening relations of friendship and good cooperation between the peoples and legislative organizations of Laos and Malaysia. He pointed out that the visit constituted a good omen for the relations of friendship and served to further strengthen the existing mutual cooperation and assistance. Present with H.E. Nouhak Phoumsavan in welcoming the HRM delegation was H.E. Saman Vi-gnaket, chairman of the National Assembly. H.E. Abdul Wahab B. Harun, ambassador of Malaysia to Laos, was also present at the meeting.
