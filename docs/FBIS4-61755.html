BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan May 10 TASS -- Azeri troops heavily pounded the positions of Karabakh forces with artillery at the eastern section of the front at 06:00 this morning and mounted an offensive against the village of Bashkarvend, ITAR-TASS learnt at the press service of the Karabakh army, which stressed that the actions by the Azeri side run counter to the protocol adopted by the Bishkek meeting of legislators of the Commonwealth of Independent States on a Karabakh settlement, providing for a ceasefire along the entire front. According to the same source, following artillery preparation fire, Azeri troops supported by armour launched an attack at 10:15 at the north-eastern section of the front against the village of Yarmdzha in the Mardakertskiy district, Nagorno-Karabakh. A report received at 11:00 says that Karabakh units are successfully repulsing enemy attacks. A tense situation persists at other sections of the front, Stepanakert reports. As for the Armenian borders, it was quiet here last night and this morning, ITAR-TASS learnt at the press service of the republican Defence Ministry.
