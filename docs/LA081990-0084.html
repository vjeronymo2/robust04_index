August 19, 1990, Sunday, Southland Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

OAKLAND;

INJURED PARACHUTIST ESCAPES CHARGES

Authorities have decided a broken back is punishment enough and will not file charges against a man who parachuted from an unfinished high-rise. John Carta, 44, a former Green Beret whose hobby is parachuting from skyscrapers and bridges, fractured his back in three places when his parachute failed Thursday.
