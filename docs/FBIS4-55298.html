BFN [Text] After a stormy meeting behind closed doors, the parliamentary faction of the Union of Democratic Forces [SDS] rejected by a large majority, with four abstentions, the draft decision proposed by Edvin Sugarev on the self-dissolution of the National Assembly if Professor Berov's cabinet remains in power. This draft decision does not conform to the Bulgarian Constitution and would deceive the country. This was stated by Stefan Savov, chairman of the SDS parliamentary faction, after the meeting. The proposal of Aleksandur Yordanov was not discussed because he withdrew it. Edvin Sugarev described that proposal as a joke.
