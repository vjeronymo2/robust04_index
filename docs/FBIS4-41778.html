BFN [Text] Tiraspol, ROMPRES, 23/06/1994 -- Informed sources from the 14th Army told BASA that a group of 30 officers of the Dniester Security Ministry had left for Crimea. The group is assigned to take part in preparing the soldiers of the Crimean Interior Ministry to carry out certain military operations. Sources from the Dniester security told BASA that such information is totally unfounded. The same sources stated that the spreading of such rumors is part of the campaign to discredit Tiraspol officials. Earlier, it was said that a group of eight officers of the "Delfin" detachment and "Dniestr" battalion of the Dniester Interior Ministry were sent in 1993 to Abkhazia to organize diversions in the rear of the Georgian Army. Sources from the 14th Army said that the Abkhazian defense minister sent a letter to the Tiraspol Interior Ministry recommending promotion of the officers of the "Dniester" battalion who took part in military operations in Abkhazia.
