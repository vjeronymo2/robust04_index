Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Azeri troops attacked Karabakh positions in the north of the Mardakertskiy District Friday, officials in the Karabakh army press center told Interfax. They said that the Azeris had retreated after losing two tanks and nearly 40 troops. On the day before Karabakh units stood their ground against enemy attacks in the north of the Mardakertskiy District and in the east of the Martuniskiy District of the Nagorno Karabakh Republic. No data on casualties was immediately available. A Karabakh army spokesman said that six Azeri tanks and four armored personnel carriers (APC) had been knocked out and five tanks captured and repaired in recent days, over 700 Azeri soldiers had been killed. The Karabakh forces lost three tanks, two APCs and 42 men.
