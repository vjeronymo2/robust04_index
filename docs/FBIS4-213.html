Peter, are you there? Can you please tell us what is the situation at present? [Scott] At the moment thousands of IFP supporters are marching down John Ross Highway on their way to the magisterial building in the center of town. Now, earlier a senior police officer on the scene, Major Margaret Kruger of the police legal department, confirmed that permission was not granted for the marchers to carry any weapons during the procession.
