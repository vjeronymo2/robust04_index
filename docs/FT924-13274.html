921016 FT 16 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Tudor moving into profit Tudor, a tiles and crystal glassware company, recorded a pre-tax loss of Pounds 117,000, after a Pounds 59,000 exceptional charge in the six months to June 30. That followed the Pounds 71,000 deficit in the second half of 1991, when the final dividend was passed. The company was now said to be profitable. There is no interim dividend, against 0.5p paid from a profit of Pounds 94,000. Sales in the half year came to Pounds 7.8m (Pounds 6.42m). Losses per share were 1.06p (earnings 1.24p).
