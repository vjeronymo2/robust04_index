BFN [Text] The city of Baghlan, north of Kabul, was captured by forces of Borhanoddin Rabbani. According to a report by the Central News Unit, fierce fighting continues in Baghlan province between forces loyal to the president and the joint forces of General Dostam and Afghan Prime Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar. Sources opposed to the government, while confirming that intense battles are continuing in the Baghlan province, blame the government forces for starting the fighting. Another report indicates that fighting is intense in the vicinity of the town of Pol-e-Khomri in Baghlan province, and forces loyal to the president are trying to capture this town.
