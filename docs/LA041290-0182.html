April 12, 1990, Thursday, Ventura County Edition

BEST BETS: RELAXING GETAWAY;

WHERE YOU CAN BE SURE OF AROMA WITH A VIEW

A $2 cup of cappuccino and a million-dollar view can be found at Lorenzoni's in Ventura Harbor Village.

Looking out over a sailboat-filled marina with rolling hills in the background makes for a nice break from the daily grind. It offers beverages and an assortment of gourmet takeout sandwiches and desserts. Lorenzoni's is at 1575 Spinnaker Drive, No. 104, Ventura, 658-2228.
