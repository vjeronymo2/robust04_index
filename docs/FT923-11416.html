920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Grahams Rintoul net assets rise Grahams Rintoul Investment Trust, which invests mainly in smaller companies and is ultimately controlled by American Express Bank, reported a net asset value of 133.4p as at June 30 - an increase from 121.6p a year earlier and from 123.8p at its financial year-end in December. Net revenue for the six months to end-June totalled Pounds 427,000 (Pounds 294,000) but after a distribution of 5.25p (4.875p) per share to holders of the stepped preference capital, losses per share emerged at 0.96p (1.92p).
