921016 FT 16 OCT 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit and vegetables English Spartan apples are this week's best fruit buy at 35-45p a lb (35-45p), reports FFVIB. Comice pears at 45-55p a lb (45-65p), and pineapples at 80p-Pounds 1.00 each (80p-Pounds 1.50) remain good value. Carrots at 10-15p a lb (10-15p) are excellent, along with most other root vegetables - parsnips at 25-30p a lb (25-30p), and swede at 15-25p a lb (15-25p). Add a splash of colour to Autumnal salads with the many different exotic salad leaves available. Choose from: Lollo Rossa at 60-70p each (60-70p), Radicchio at 65-80p each (65-80p), Oak Leaf and Frise at 50-60p each (Pounds 1.10-1.20).
