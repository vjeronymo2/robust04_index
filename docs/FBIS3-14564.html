Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] People's Assembly Speaker 'Abd-al-Qadir Qaddurah today received a Pakistani parliamentary delegation, led by Maulana Fazlur Rahman, chairman of the Pakistani National Assembly Standing Committee on Foreign Affairs. The meeting was attended by Shakir Is'ayyid, head of the Arab and Foreign Affairs Committee of the Syrian People's Assembly; Hasan Sibahi, member of the Syrian People's Assembly; and the Pakistani ambassador in Damascus. The discussions dealt with parliamentary relations, cooperation and coordination, exchanges of parliamentary delegations, and means to reinforce parliamentary ties to serve the interests of the two fraternal peoples. Talks also covered the peace process and the issue of Kashmir. Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi also received the Pakistani parliamentary delegation, led by Maulana Fazlur Rahman, at 1300 today. The meeting was attended by Shakir Is'ayyid, Hasan Sibahi, and the Pakistani ambassador in Damascus. Discussion dealt with existing bilateral relations and issues of common interest.
