Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yusuf Bin-'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah, Oman's minister of state for foreign affairs, today arrived in Amman on a brief visit to Jordan during which he will relay an oral message to His Majesty King Husayn from his brother Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of Oman. He was received at Amman Civilian Airport by Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs, senior Foreign Ministry staff, and the Omani ambassador in Amman. Yusuf Bin-'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah spoke to our correspondent Muhammad al-Hajaya, and said: [Begin Bin-'Abdallah recording] I have an oral message from his majesty the sultan to his brother His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal. The message concerns issues of joint interests and consultations over various issues. Oman, like Jordan, is concerned about all dimensions of the Arab interests and we hope the joint effort of the two kings will, God willing, be a blessed effort and bear fruit to benefit the Arab and Muslim nations. Consultations between the leaders are continuing and I believe that circumstances are now better than what they were in the past. We hope that the efforts will, God willing, bear fruit for the benefit of common interests. [end recording]
