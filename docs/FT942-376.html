940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Dawson International DAWSON INTERNATIONAL has paid Pounds 700,000 for a 15 per cent stake in Luda JiLi, a Mongolian cashmere processor. A further investment of about Pounds 500,000 is planned - increasing its stake to 25 per cent - along with an investment of about Pounds 4m to acquire 25 per cent of Luyuan Cashmere Group.
