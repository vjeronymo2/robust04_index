May 17, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

The Centennial Group Inc., Orange, reported earnings of $1.6 million for its third quarter ending March 31, compared to earnings of $6,000 a year ago. Revenues were $19.8 million, compared to $13.7 million last year. For the first 9 months of its fiscal year, Centennial reported earnings of $1.8 million, compared to $8.2 million a year ago. Revenue was $39.5 million this year, compared to $69.4 million last year. Centennial builds and develops houses and commercial property and manages and owns property.
