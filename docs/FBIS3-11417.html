Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Army General Raul Castro Ruz, second secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba and Revolutionary Armed Forces minister, presided over the Minfar's [Ministry of the Revolutionary Armed Forces] expanded military council, at which a final assessment was made of all the activities carried out during 1993 by the three armies and of all the weapons that defend the national territory. This account led to the conclusion that a gigantic and fruitful effort had been made, which shows that the capabilities and combat readiness of our Armed Forces have been strengthened. Another important point analyzed was the FAR's [Revolutionary Armed Forces] agricultural-livestock activity toward the goal of supplying all the troops with food; a 30-percent increase toward achieving this objective is expected this year. During the Minfar's expanded military council, on the occasion of his 50th anniversary as a combat pilot, Enrique Carrera [last name as heard], hero of the Republic of Cuba, was promoted to the rank of division general.
