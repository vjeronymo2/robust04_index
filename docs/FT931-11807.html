930201 FT 01 FEB 93 / International Company News: Sheafbank better For the 15 months ended June 30 1992 Sheafbank Property Trust, the property investor and financial services group, incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 333,656. That compared with a deficit of Pounds 992,778 for the year to end-March 1991. Exceptional charges for the 15 months were Pounds 83,637 (Pounds 950,000) and net interest accounted for Pounds 469,550 (Pounds 14,070 received). Share of associates' profits was Pounds 101,592 (Pounds 14,948). Losses per share amounted to 1.58p (6.19p) or 0.97p (5.09p) fully diluted. A proposed final dividend of 0.1p makes a same-again 0.2p total.
