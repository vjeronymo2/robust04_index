BFN [Unattributed report: "The Registration of Refugees Has Not Ended"] [Text] The IBERIA news agency has been informed by the Committee for Refugees and Forcibly Displaced Persons that there are currently about 295,000 refugees in the Republic of Georgia of which 60,000 are in Tbilisi. They receive monthly assistance amounting to 70,000 coupons [per person]. This amount increases according to inflation. As regards humanitarian aid, this is distributed directly in the rayons according to local registration. Preparations are being made to register anew refugees and forcibly displaced persons; this should take place on 18-19 June. Refugees are to be given new identification cards, which have already been printed and sent to the rayons.
