930717 FT 17 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Greenfriar asset value up to 434.7p Over the six months ended June 30 1993 net asset value per ordinary share of Greenfriar Investment rose from 395.4p to 434.7p, while the warrants grew from 61.4p to 100.7p. A year earlier the values stood at 351.1p and 17.1p respectively. For the 1993 half year income totalled Pounds 713,000 (Pounds 670,000) and net revenue came to Pounds 418,000 ((Pounds 362,000) for earnings of 3.62p (3.14p) per share. The interim dividend is stepped up to 2.15p (2.1p) and the directors are forecasting at least maintenance of the 4.25p final.
