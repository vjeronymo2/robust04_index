BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn and Her Majesty Queen Nur left Amman before noon today on a several-day private visit to the United Kingdom. His majesty was seen off at the airport by His Royal Highness Prince Muhammad, the king's personal representative; Princess Taghrid; Prince 'Abdallah Bin-al-Husayn; and Prince Ghazi Bin-Muhammad. Also present at the airport to bid farewell to his majesty the king were the prime minister, speakers of both Houses of parliament, the chief chamberlain, and top civilian and Army officials. Meanwhile, His Royal Highness Prince Muhammad, the king's personal representative, took the constitutional oath before his majesty and in the presence of Cabinet members as regent during his majesty's absence abroad. His majesty the king is accompanied by Princess Basmah and the chief of the Royal Court al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir.
