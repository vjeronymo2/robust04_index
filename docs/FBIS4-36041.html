BFN [Text] Mr. 'Ali Salim al-Bid, chairman of the Presidential Council, today made a telephone call to the Syrian president, Hafiz al-Asad, and discussed with him the grave situation in the current war in our country and the military escalation by the Sanaa rulers after the Security Council's resolution taken yesterday concerning the situation in our country and the immediate response. He acquainted al-Asad with the bombardment by the Sanaa regime of vital military installations in the capital, Aden, and its desperate attempts to bombard Aden refinery.
