930417 FT 17 APR 93 / Finance and the Family: Govett cuts minimum investment - At a glance John Govett is reducing the minimum investment on its Futures & Options Funds to Pounds 2,000 for postal dealings. Among the 18 funds are indexed funds on the US, UK, Japanese and European markets and bear funds which profit when those markets fall. Bull and bear funds are available on currency and bond markets. Charges vary from 4.5 per cent to 0.5 per cent initial and 1 to 0.5 per cent annual.
