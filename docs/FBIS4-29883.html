BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 22 KYODO -- The Social Democratic Party (SDP) [of Japan -- SDPJ] and the ruling coalition will agree on a policy platform to pave a way for the SDP's return to the coalition, a senior SDP official said Wednesday [22 June] night. SDP Chairman Tomiichi Murayama and Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata will hold a meeting as early as Thursday and strike a deal, the official said.
