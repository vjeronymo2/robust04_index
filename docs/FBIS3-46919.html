Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, December 30 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The Ambassador of the PRC to Mongolia Pei Jiayi said in an interview to the MONTSAME correspondent that 1993 was the year of further normal development of relations between China and Mongolia in the direction of strengthening of good-neighbourly friendship and cooperation. Parliaments, governments, some army organs and public organizations of the two countries have achieved expansion of friendly exchange, extension of mutual understanding and mutual confidence, stregthening of friendship, development of cooperation. In 1993 the number of trade points has increased, and that of the opened points has reached 11. The border trade is developing on a large scale as never before. According to the present data, 103 joint enterprises of China and Mongolia are registered in Mongolia. Relations between the two countries, said the ambassador, have wide perspectives for development, as China and Mongolia as the neighbouring countries are pursuing a friendly policy of good-neighbourliness. The peoples of the two countries have traditional friendship, the two states are developing trade relations, having many years history. And plus mutual supplement of economy of the two countries. Pei Jiayi expressed confidence that owing to mutual efforts relations of good-neighbourly friendly cooperation between the two countries will develop and strengthen from day to day continuously, being based on five principles of peaceful coexistence.
