940831 FT 31 AUG 94 / Sri Lankan peace move Mrs Chandrika Kumaratunge, the new Sri Lankan prime minister, will meet the army chief and other service commanders tomorrow to discuss a ceasefire in the 11-year long war against the separatist 'Tamil Tigers', Mervyn de Silva reports from Colombo. They will also discuss a partial lifting of the economic embargo imposed on the northern peninsula of Jaffna, by the previous United National party government regime. The UNP was defeated by Mrs Kumaratunge's People's Alliance, a left-inclined coalition in parliamentary elections on August 16.
