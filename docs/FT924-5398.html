921127 FT 27 NOV 92 / Treuhand agency suspects fraud The Treuhand privatisation agency has uncovered a case of suspected fraud by a west German buyer of one of the agency's larger east German companies, writes Leslie Colitt in Berlin. Mr Wolfram Froeb of the Treuhand's control department said the agency lost between DM20m and DM30m (Dollars 18.8m) on the sale last December of the Leipzig-based Agrotechnic agricultural implements company to the Eurata company in Hamburg. Criminal charges had been brought against the majority shareholder in the West German company, the agency said.
