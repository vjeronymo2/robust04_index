BFN [Text] Universo, a new company in the Cubanacan, Inc. group established a $20-million goal for this year. The company will have 76 stores, where tourists are offered a vast array of products. Abraham Maciques and Carlos Garcia, president and vice president of that tourism corporation, attended the ceremony for the official presentation of this enterprise, which has become one of 18 new companies subordinate to Cubanacan, Inc. Julio Cesar Mas Zavala, marketing manager of Universo, provided explanations on the new company and said its units are located in nine shopping arcades at the Comodoro Hotel, old Havana, Varadero, the central part of the island, Ciego de Avila, Camaguey, Holguin, Granma, and Santiago de Cuba. He said the goal is specialized marketing of local and imported articles.
