BFN [Text] Warsaw, 27 Apr--In a letter on 27 April to Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak and Industry Minister Marek Pol, the Trade Union Federation of Brown Coal Mining said that its members are suspending their participation in the strike and are ready for negotiations with the government on demands put forward by the union. "Because Decision No. 30 has been annulled, the federation strike committee is suspending its participation in the strike and obliging enterprise commissions to withdraw from strike committees in enterprises after internal demands are met. When the strike has been suspended, we are open to negotiations with the government on the union's demands", federation miners said.
