920622 FT 22 JUN 92 / Observer: Continental drift The front cover of WPP Group's annual report talks of 'the management of the imagination'. Imagination is clearly necessary to read the report of the marketing services group, currently in the throes of a refinancing. A map showing the company's worldwide operations has lines of longitude and latitude, an equator and two tropics, but no land masses. There are just numbers locating the offices - 64 of them from Argentina to Vietnam - floating on a white page.
