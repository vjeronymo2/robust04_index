BFN [Excerpts] Kuwait, 12 Jun -- Finance and Planning Minister Nasir 'Abdallah al-Rawdan announced today that the Ministry of Finance had received $1.397 million from the UN Reparation Committee. The money is the first installment for victims of Iraqi aggression and is in compensation for physical and psychological damages under the terms of damages/group B as defined by the Geneva-based agency. Al-Rawdan told KUNA that the Ministry of Finance, in coordination with the public commission in charge of estimating losses resulting from the Iraqi aggression, would determine payment procedures to those who deserve them. [passage omitted] The first installment sum is $2.74 million dollars [as received] which will be disbursed among 670 victims of the Iraqi invasion. They are victims as defined under group B terms, which includes cases of death and serious injuries. [passage omitted]
