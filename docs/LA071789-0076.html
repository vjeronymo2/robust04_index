July 17, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: DANA POINT;

SERVICE GROUP PLANS DINNER, FUND-RAISER

Laguna Shanti will celebrate its second anniversary with a Spirit of Service awards dinner and fund-raiser Sunday at Michael's Supper Club in Dana Point Harbor.

The event, which will also honor its volunteers, will include a photo exhibit and an auction. Proceeds will benefit the group's assistance efforts for people with acquired immune deficiency syndrome. For information, call (714) 494-1446.
