December 4, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

BEATRIX POTTER DRAWINGS FOUND

Three watercolor drawings by children's writer Beatrix Potter have been discovered in a Gloucestershire farm house and will be offered at auction Dec. 14. The 1896 pictures of Cecily Parsley the Rabbit, a character in her Peter Rabbit books, have lain undiscovered in a picture album once owned by the writer, auctioneer Simon Bruton said. Potter died in 1943.
