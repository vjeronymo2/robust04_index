Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The training of the first of three special forces battalions to be integrated in the future Mozambique Defense Armed Forces [FADM] is expected to begin this week in the former Nacala Air Base in Nampula Province. This was revealed by DOMINGO newspaper. On 3 March 40 troops from the government and the Mozambique National Resistance began training as navy officers and noncommissioned officers at the Catembe Naval Base in Maputo. The training is being given by Portuguese military officers and noncommissioned officers.
