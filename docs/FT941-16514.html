940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / Business Travel (Travel Update): Floating down The planned opening of the Channel Tunnel next May has sparked the expected price cutting by ferry operators. Eurotunnel, the tunnel operator, will announce its fares on Tuesday. They are likely to range between Pounds 160 and Pounds 260, depending on the season. Last week Stena Sealink simplified and cut its charges. A standard round trip for a car and five passengers will start at Pounds 126, rising to a peak of Pounds 220, with extra charges for weekend sailings. The previous peak price was Pounds 294, Stena said.
