March 22, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Bridgford Foods Corp.: The Anaheim-based convenience food producer posted a 20% gain in net income for the first quarter of its 1990 fiscal year, to $831,176 from $694,882 for the like period a year ago. Sales for the quarter ended Feb. 2 rose 16%, to $20.4 million from $17.5 million.
