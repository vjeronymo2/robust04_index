September 21, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Tish Johnson of Panorama City rolled strikes in 10 of 12 frames in the championship match to win the AMF Cobra tournament at Chesapeake, Va., with a 270-214 victory over Aleta Sill.

The title was Johnson's ninth on the Ladies Professional Bowling Tour and first since the end of 1989.

The 28-year-old left-hander, who ended the game with nine consecutive strikes, earned $20,000 of the $100,000 purse.
