Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali has said His Majesty King Husayn was keen on Yemen's unity as a model for Arab world. He told the Yemeni magazine ADWA' AL-YAMAN that his majesty had strong belief that the Arab nation at large was destined to achieve unity and reconciliation. Dr. al-Majali said his majesty had sincerely extended a hand of reconciliation to all Arabs, particularly at the current stage when the Arab world was facing a critical time on the new world order and regional order. The prime minister said the Hebron mosque massacre was a heinous and unprecedented crime whereas unarmed victims were murdered in cold blood at the sacred shrine. Dr. al-Majali said peace was a strategic Arab quest which should not be abandoned.
