June 17, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

HOW ABOUT A PARADE TO SHOW APPRECIATION FOR FINE LAKER EFFORT?

I'm sure the Lakers are not, and they shouldn't be, ashamed of their play during these last four games. I watched every moment of every game and felt proud of the Lakers.

Are there parades only for winners? There should be an appreciation parade for the Lakers.

PAUL A. KLEME

Los Angeles
