941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / UK Company News: On Demand losses increase On Demand Information, the electronic publishing company, yesterday announced an 80 per cent slide in pre-tax losses from Pounds 1.45m to Pounds 2.62m for the year to July 31. ODI had been expected to report losses for 18 months after its December flotation as it invested in operational infrastructure for its new products, and demerged its marketing activities. Shares, which floated at 78p, were unchanged at 102p yesterday. Staff had increased from 97 to 197 by the end of October. Group turnover in the 12 months fell by 24 per cent to Pounds 14.5m (Pounds 19m), however, input from continuing operations advanced to Pounds 5.23m (Pounds 2.98m). Losses per share emerged at 5.7p (3.9p).
