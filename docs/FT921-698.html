920327 FT 27 MAR 92 / Election 1992: Standard ad - Ballot Box Labour's qualms about the influence in London of the staunchly Tory Evening Standard did not deter it from placing a full-page ad. 'Someone is killing 600 businesses in London every week,' it says. 'We all know the guilty party.' A party official said: 'We feel it is the only way we can achieve balance in London's only daily newspaper.' The Standard was unlikely to reject the money.
