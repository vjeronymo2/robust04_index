CSO [Text] Bombay, March 5: Four kg of heroin, foreign and Indian currency totalling Rs 5 crores were seized by customs officials from four persons, two foreigners and two Indians, here on Thursday. On following a tip-off, officials went to the Breach Candy swimming pool and arrested Jackson, a Finn, as he was passing on a packet to a British national, Martin. The packet contained 30 grammes of heroin, officials said. Interrogation of the duo led the police to a flat in Venus Apartment, Altamount Road, revealed that Jackson had allegedly stashed away large amounts of currency and a trans-receiver. Even as officials searched the apartment, an Indian, Manoj Singh, walked in. He too was interrogated and he revealed that he had come accompanied by his mother, Rajmani Singh alias Mishra, to deliver heroin to Jackson. The Singhs further led the officials to another flat in Savitha Apartments at Versova, from where some more heroin and currency was seized by the officials. Further investigations are on.
