BFN [Text] Port-au-Prince, 10 May (EFE) -- A group of senators, who were elected on 18 January 1993 in elections described as fraudulent, are planning to appoint Judge Emile Jonassain, former Supreme Court president, as temporary Haitian president tomorrow, 11 May. The legislators, led by Bernard Sansaricq, who leads one of the Senate Board of Directors, made the decision today during a session described as irregular. Nevertheless, Jonassain said he is officially unaware of the decision. Sansaricq sent Jonassain a copy of the decree adopted during the session, which has been described as illegal. He also sent a copy to General Raoul Cedras, Armed Forces chief, and Frantz Robert Monde, president of the Chamber of Deputies.
