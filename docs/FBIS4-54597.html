BFN [Text] Budapest, 29 April (MTI) -- "Government control of the public-service media has continued to strengthen, with the television newscast `Hirado' and political TV magazine `A Het' (The Week) using unacceptable terms in their reporting. This style must change between the two election rounds, from May 8-29," Alliance of Free Democrats (SZDSZ) officials Alajos Dornbach and Balint Magyar told the press today in Budapest. At the press conference, Magyar also voiced the party's satisfaction over the firm police and National Security Office action against the far-right Hungarist Movement.
