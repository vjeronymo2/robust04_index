931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / International Company News: LTV edges into the black in third quarter By Agencies in New York LTV, the third biggest US steel producer which in June emerged from seven years of bankruptcy protection, has turned in third-quarter profit of Dollars 400,000, against Dollars 632.4m a year earlier, agencies report from New York. Revenues rose to Dollars 1.05bn from Dollars 944.2m. Net earnings for the first nine months totalled Dollars 4.07bn against Dollars 727m with revenues rising to Dollars 3.04bn from Dollars 2.88bn. The third-quarter results include a gain from reorganisation items of Dollars 1.3m, against a loss of Dollars 13.1m. The latest nine-month returns include a gain of Dollars 143.2m against a loss of Dollars 97.1m a year ago. Results for last year's quarter and nine months include income from discontinued operations of aerospace and defence businesses of Dollars 65m and Dollars 96.8m respectively. They also include a gain on disposal of discontinued aerospace and defence businesses of Dollars 582m and Dollars 732m respectively. Results for the latest nine months include an extraordinary gain on debt discharge of Dollars 3.93bn.
