Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Vladimir Dlouhy, minister of industry and trade of the Czech Republic, and Vladimir Radkevich, head of the Belarusian Government's Foreign Trade Committee, have signed an agreement in Prague on trade and economic relations between Belarus and the Czech Republic. The document provides for the liberalization of foreign trade, settlement of hard currency payments, and direct economic links between the partners. Czech experts believe that the agreement will allow other agreements to be signed in specific areas of cooperation, such as support and protection of investments and the prevention of dual taxation.
