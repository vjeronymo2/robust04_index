BFN [Excerpt] Holy Mecca, 10 May (SPA) -- The General Secretariat of the Islamic World League [IWL] in Mecca has urged the leaders of the Yemeni Republic to exercise self-restraint and wisdom and end armed fighting in that dear country, as no one will benefit from such a conflict except the enemies of the Islamic nation. The league's official spokesman added that the IWL's General Secretariat would like to express its greatest regret at seeing the dispute turning into armed conflict between brothers in Yemen. He added that the IWL, which represents all Muslim peoples, is urging all political leaders and forces in Yemen to end the fighting, resolve their dispute, put an end to the bloody events, and spare Muslim lives.
