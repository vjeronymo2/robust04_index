BFN [Text] Beijing, June 19 (XINHUA) -- Wei Jianxing, member of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC), met with and feted a delegation from the Communist Party of Israel (CPI) here this evening. On behalf of General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee Jiang Zemin, Wei extended a welcome to the delegation headed by CPI General Secretary Muhamed Naffa, saying that the relations between CPC and CPI have been developing well since the two parties resumed their ties in 1987. Wei briefed the visitors on the current political and economic situation in China. He said that the major reform measures which China has taken since the beginning of this year have been going smoothly and produced better results than expected. He said China, enjoying a good political and economic situation, must handle well the relations between reform, development and stability. The CPI delegation arrived here yesterday on a visit to China at the invitation of the CPC.
