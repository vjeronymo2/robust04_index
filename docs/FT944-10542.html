941110 FT 10 NOV 94 / Post workers ballot over franchise plan The UCW postal workers union is balloting more than 9,000 Post Office Counters clerks over a call for industrial action to protest against the conversion of post offices to privately run franchises in shops. Post Office Counters said industrial action would be 'pointless and misguided' and that it aimed to 'ensure change without compulsory redundancies'.
