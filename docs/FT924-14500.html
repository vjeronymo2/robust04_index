921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / International Company News: Abbott Laboratories up ABBOTT Laboratories, a large US health care products group, yesterday turned in third-quarter earnings of Dollars 278.8m, or 33 cents a share, against Dollars 251.6m, or 29 cents, a year earlier. Sales rose to Dollars 1.97bn from Dollars 1.65bn. For the first nine months, net earnings increased 15 per cent to Dollars 890.1m from Dollars 774m.
