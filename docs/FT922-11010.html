920428 FT 28 APR 92 / Mortgages move ONE OF the measures the government devised to try to ease the repossessions crisis in the housing market was laid before parliament yesterday. The regulations will allow the mortgage interest element of social security income support to be paid directly to lenders. Mortgage lenders will have to decide by May 22 whether they want to opt out of the scheme. If they do not opt out, direct payments will begin on May 25.
