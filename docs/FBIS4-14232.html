BFN [Text] Sanaa, April 28 (AFP) -- Fighting between Yemeni Army factions has killed more than 100 people and wounded 200 in the past two days, a team of Yemeni journalists who visited the scene said Thursday [28 April]. The dead included 18 civilians. More than 85 tanks had been destroyed in the fighting between northern and southern armoured units north of Sanaa, according to the team, who included journalists from the independent Yemen Times.
