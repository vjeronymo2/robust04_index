920204 FT 04 FEB 92 / International Company News: Pathe names new executives PATHE Communications, the parent company of the financially crippled MGM Hollywood studio, yesterday named new executives, including Mr Alan Ladd, Jr, as a co-chairman, writes Alan Friedman. The board meeting was the first since a Delaware court gave Credit Lyonnais, the French state bank, the right to expand the board and suspend members who had been appointed by Mr Giancarlo Parretti, the Italian financier who bought MGM in 1990.
