940510 FT 10 MAY 94 / International Company News: Royal Bank of Scotland buys Banesto stake Royal Bank of Scotland is to acquire 2 per cent of the share capital of Banco Espanol de Credito (Banesto) from Banco Santander, the Spanish bank which last month won the auction to take a controlling stake in Banesto, writes John Gapper, Banking Editor. Royal Bank, which has had a strategic partnership with Santander since 1988, when the Spanish bank acquired 9.9 per cent of its equity, said it would pay Pounds 46m (Dollars 67.16m) to acquire 12.25m shares at a price of Pta762 each.
