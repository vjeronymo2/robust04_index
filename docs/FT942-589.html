940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Equities moved ahead strongly after Friday's losses on news that Mr Jorge Carpizo, the interior minister, had agreed to withdraw his resignation. Sentiment was also boosted by the rebound on Wall Street. The IPC index ended with a gain of 51.92, or 2.37 per cent, at 2,239.19 in light volume of 54.01m shares.
