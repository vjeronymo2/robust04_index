November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

American Pacesetter: The Newport Beach parent company of San Clemente Savings Bank reported net income of $233,000 for the third quarter of 1990, compared to net income of $634,000 for the corresponding 1989 quarter. For the first nine months of the year, the company posted net income of $263,000, contrasted with a year-earlier loss of $372,000.
