Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] While one waits for peace from Lusaka, in Angola the war continues. Over the weekend, National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] rebels strengthened their positions in the outlaying areas of the city of Cuito, Bie Province. Local sources say UNITA reinforcements have come from Soyo. UNITA plans to mount a large-scale attack on the city of Bie. Savimbi's men have attacked the town of Limbombos in Bengo Province, and Quijenje and Ukuma in Huambo Province. UNITA's heavy artillery fire caused a substantial number of dead and wounded among civilians.
