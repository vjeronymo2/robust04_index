BFN [Excerpt] National Highway One between In Salah and Tamanrasset was the scene of a series of armed attacks during the last few months. The attacks were carried out by gangs against trucks belonging to the National Road Haulage Company. Faced with such a situation the lorries of the aforementioned company did not have any other solution but to cut short their journey at In Salah, thus starving the region from being supplied with most commodities. In an attempt to contain the situation, the local authorities at Tamanrasset have taken a number of security measures. [passage omitted]
