June 11, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

KLESKO'S 2-RUN DOUBLE NOT ENOUGH FOR SOUTH

A team of Northern area all-stars defeated the South, 9-6, Saturday at Clover to even the three-game baseball series at one game each.

Westminster High School first baseman Ryan Klesko highlighted the South's six-run-sixth inning with a bases-loaded double that scored two runs.

In Friday's game, which the South won 18-2, right fielder Chris Sheff of Laguna Hills went three for three with three runs batted in and four runs scored.
