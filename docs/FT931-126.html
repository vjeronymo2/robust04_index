930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Philip Harris calls for Pounds 5m Philip Harris, a supplier of laboratory equipment materials and pharmaceutical products, is to raise about Pounds 5m net via a rights issue of 2.69m new shares on a 1-for-3 basis at 200p each. The board expects to recommend a final dividend of 4.65p to be paid on the enlarged capital for a total of 6.85p (6.25p). Harris also announced the acquisition of the general laboratory equipment supply business from Scotlab for Pounds 500,000 plus stock at valuation. A further Pounds 100,000, related to sales figures, may be payable. The shares fell 2p to 238p.
