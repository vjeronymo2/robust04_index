March 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

BROWN

I suspect that Brown has spent the past years doing fundamental social research and, like many of us who have taken a good, long look at where the United States is going, has found that the 1990s will see a strong return to values, ethics, the family and productivity. And so, Brown is back with a new image of values, ethics, the family and productivity.

It is all an act, and if we are not willing to see through it, we'll have to put up with his same old weaknesses and phony social programs.

JOE M. TURNER

Vista
