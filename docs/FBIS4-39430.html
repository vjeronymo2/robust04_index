BFN [By unidentified ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow June 8 TASS -- There was a telephone talk between Russian President Boris Yeltsin and President of the Republic of Korea Kim Yong-sam today, the press service of the Russian president reports. The report says that the "heads of the two states have expressed satisfaction with the results of their summit talks in Moscow and discussed some possible measures to implement the achieved agreements, including those linked with the settlement of the North Korean nuclear problem. Yeltsin and Kim Yong-sam agreed to continue regular contacts to discuss bilateral relations and the situation on the Korean peninsula."
