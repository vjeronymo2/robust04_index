BFN [Text] Cairo, 11 Apr (MENA)--Dr. Usamah al-Baz, Foreign Ministry first under secretary and director of the President's Office for Political Affairs, returned to Cairo this afternoon after a week-long visit to the United States. During his visit al-Baz discussed the current peace efforts and Egyptian-U.S. ties with U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher. He also discussed with senior White House officials the recent developments of the Middle East peace operation.
