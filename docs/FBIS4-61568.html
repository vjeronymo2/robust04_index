BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchegoleva] [Text] Sochi, 6 May -- During meetings between Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Boris Pastukhov, Russian Deputy Defense Minister Georgiy Kondratyev, and the leadership of Abkhazia, an understanding was reached that peacekeeping forces should be introduced along the confrontation line and not on the entire territory of Abkhazia, as the Georgian side was insisting. Today, the Abkhazian Information and Press Ministry informed an ITAR-TASS correspondent about this and noted that these consultations pursued the goal of agreeing the positions of the sides on the eve of a regular round of the Georgian-Abkhazian talks, which should be held in Moscow on 11 May.
