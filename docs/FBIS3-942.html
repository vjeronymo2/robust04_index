Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pretoria March 16 SAPA -- The South African Defence Force [SADF] will hold bilateral talks with the Azanian People's Liberation Army (APLA) in the week running up to the elections, an SADF spokesman said on Wednesday [16 March]. "The talks will be exploratory in nature, aimed at determining whether there is scope and potential for further discussion," Maj N. C. Sendall said in a statement. He declined to comment on the agenda of the talks which will be held during the week of 21 to 25 March. The venue of the talks is still to be agreed upon.
