940307 FT 07 MAR 94 / Company News (This Week): TI - Other companies Profits on the disposal of non-core activities are likely fuel a sharp improvement at TI, the specialist engineering group, which is expected to post pre-tax profits of up to Pounds 130m on Thursday, against a restated Pounds 87.4m. But with deliveries for the aerospace industry still heading downwards, chairman Sir Christopher Lewinton will again face questions over the Pounds 500m paid for Dowty.
