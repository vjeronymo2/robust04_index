Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Summary] The Bank of China Group is continuing to finance mainland infrastructure projects in the energy, transport, telecommunications, and raw material sectors as "supply shortages" in these areas were slowing economic development. According to the group's general manager in Hong Kong, the Bank of China is now directly involved in over 2,500 projects on the mainland and has advanced $7 billion in loans, 39 percent of which are directed to energy projects such as the Daya Bay Nuclear Power Plant.
