BFN [Text] Brother president Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] received at noon today Mr. Harashima, Japanese ambassador to Tunisia. During the meeting, the latest developments concerning the peace process, the current negotiations in Cairo regarding the implementation of Gaza-Jericho accord, and the implementation of the Security Council's Resolution 904 concerning the protection of the Palestinian people under occupation were discussed. Brother President Abu-'Ammar also briefed the Japanese ambassador on the obstacles created by the Israeli Government and its evasion from the commitment to implement the withdrawal from Gaza and Jericho at the fixed dates.
