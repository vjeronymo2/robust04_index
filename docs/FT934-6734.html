931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Gold shares maintained their early strength and the index rose 44 or 2.3 per cent to 1,972. However, industrials retreated amid weakness on world markets, off 17 at 4,884 and the overall added 14 to 4,253. De Beers rose 75 cents to R84. Elsewhere Remgro lost 85 cents to R29.75 and Richemont lost a R1 to R38.75.
