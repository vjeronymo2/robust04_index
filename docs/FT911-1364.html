910507 FT 07 MAY 91 / Construction Contracts: Kent highway improvements worth Pounds 5.6m MONK CONSTRUCTION has been awarded contracts for highway construction and for a sixth form college, together worth over Pounds 10m. The highway work is for Kent County Council and involves the dualling of 4.2km of the existing A299 single carriageway to Thanet Way, Ramsgate, along with 2.4km of side roads and additional bridges. The contract is worth Pounds 5.6m.
