Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Just Like Totally Independent"] [Text] The presidents of Tatarstan and the Crimea have agreed to conduct consultations on issues of bilateral relations and to contribute to the development of contacts between state institutions and public, national, and other organizations of their respective autonomous republics. An accord was also reached on opening trade and economic and cultural centers in Kazan [capital of Tatarstan] and Simferopol. This is stated in a communique issued by the Crimean presidential administration based upon the results of the Crimean president's trip to Tatarstan on 18 and 19 March. The document also points out that, during the negotiations, [Crimean President] Meshkov and [President of Tatarstan] Shaymiyev expressed their mutual interest in the further development of economic, scientific and technological, cultural, and humanitarian ties.
