930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / Dutch retail sales rise RETAIL sales in the Netherlands rose 2.7 per cent in 1992, buoyed by gains in both food and non-food sectors, according to preliminary figures released by the Dutch Central Bureau for Statistics, AP-DJ reports from Amsterdam. The data, which was not corrected for price increases, also showed sales at the country's department stores grew just 0.8 per cent last year. Department store sales rose 1.4 per cent in 1991. The 1992 Dutch retail sales continued a declining trend over the past two years.
