BFN [Text] Prime Minister Silvio Berlusconi has criticized RAI. According to the government leader, every year RAI spends billions of lire making programs which could serve as propaganda for the Communists. He added that it is always the citizens who end up paying for this wasteful use of their money. According to agency reports, Berlusconi made his harsh comments during the course of a meeting of Forza Italia deputies which was held today. The prime minister was talking about the lack of efficiency in the public sector.
