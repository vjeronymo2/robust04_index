930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Beales Hunter Pounds 1.5m purchase Beales Hunter is extending its refrigeration business into southern England through the acquisition of the business and certain assets of JT Herbert, a refrigeration and mechanical business based in Kent, for a maximum Pounds 1.5m. The initial consideration is Pounds 240,000 cash, and further payments depend on profits over the next three years.
