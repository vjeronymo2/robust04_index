940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Cardiff Property shows recovery With gross rental income up from Pounds 615,457 to Pounds 730,643, Cardiff Property turned in a pre-tax profit of Pounds 119,445 for the year to September 30, against a Pounds 917,716 loss last time. At the attributable level, there was also a substantial recovery with a Pounds 173,027 profit (Pounds 922,243 loss). Earnings per share were 6.5p (35.7p losses) and the recommended final dividend is 1.65p for a total up from 2.4p to 2.5p.
