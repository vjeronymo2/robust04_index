Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] An Interior Ministry statement pointed out that it has been confirmed that the hijackers of the Air Algerie plane to Alicante in Spain were young policemen in general security working in Tindouf. The statement pointed out that these policemen, who were employed less than two years ago, had been in an illegal administrative condition for 10 days. As a consequence, the statement added, they were considered dismissed.
