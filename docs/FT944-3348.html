941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / China in the black China expects to post a 1994 trade surplus of Dollars 6bn (Pounds 3.8bn), returning to the black after a Dollars 12.19bn deficit in 1993 ended three years of trade surpluses. The customs department, in a report quoted in the Economic Daily, forecast total 1994 exports at Dollars 120bn, up from Dollars 91.76bn in 1993, and imports at Dollars 114bn, up from Dollars 103.95bn.
