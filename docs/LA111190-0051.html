November 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GUIDEBOOK;

KENYA LUXURY CAMPING

Getting there: We traveled to Kenya independently, although many safari tour operators can arrange package trips in luxury accommodations such as the ones we experienced. We flew to Nairobi via Amsterdam (there are no direct flights from the United States); it's about $2,000 round trip per person.

Our safari arrangements were booked through Kenyan friends by Rhino Safaris: Box 48023, Nairobi, local telephone 28102. Total cost of our trip (excluding Amsterdam stopover) was approximately $8,500 for 15 days, including air fare.

Accommodations:

The following are approximate daily rates per couple based on double occupancy, meals included:

Mara Safari Club: $205; c/o Lonrho Hotels, Kenya, P.O. Box 58581, Nairobi, local telephone 723776.

Sarova Shaba: $165; P.O. Box 30680, Nairobi, 333233.

Treetops: $175; c/o Block Hotels, P.O. Box 47557, Nairobi, 540780.
