Language: Thai Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Chuan Likphai and his delegation returned home last night after paying a successful official visit to the Socialist Republic of Vietnam. The prime minister held a news conference upon his arrival at the Air Force Airport. Recalling the warm reception accorded him and his delegation by Vietnamese leaders during the visit, the prime minister said political, investment, and trade relations between the two countries were discussed during his courtesy calls on the general secretary of the Communist Party of Vietnam and the SRV president. He noted that the two sides had reached an understanding to a certain extent on fishing problems.
