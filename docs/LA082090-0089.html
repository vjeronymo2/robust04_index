August 20, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

PARK'N PAY: All-day parking rates at John Wayne Airport will climb from $12 to $14 if the county supervisors approve a county staff proposal Tuesday. The price would drop to $7 on the third day and the $1 hourly rate would remain the same. . . . The new prices are scheduled to go into effect on Sept. 16, when the Thomas F. Riley Terminal opens. . . . "We're providing more service for the money," an airport spokeswoman says. "People are getting covered parking, and it's very convenient." Lanie Jones and Frank Messina
