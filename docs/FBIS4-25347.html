BFN [Excerpts] The '94 Hunan New Technology and Products Fair closed as a success. Provincial party and government leaders including Wang Maolin [provincial party secretary] and Chen Bangzhu [provincial governor] visited the fair. [passages omitted] During the fair, which was sponsored from 25 to 28 May, Wang Maolin, secretary of the provincial party committee, Governor Chen Bangzhu, Executive Vice Governor Wang Keying, and Yin Haitao, vice chairman of the Standing Committee of the provincial people's congress, visited the fair. Li Ximing, vice chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress [NPC] and Xiong Qingquan, member of the NPC Standing Committee also visited the fair. The 4-day fair had 300 outlets or so occupying a floor space of 4,000-plus square meters. During the fair, more than 400 technological contracts and 1,500 new-products trading contracts were signed.
