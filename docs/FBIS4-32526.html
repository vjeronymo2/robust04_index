BFN [Excerpt] Doboj, 26 Jun (TANJUG) -- Muslim forces have resumed their intensive attacks on Serbian positions and residential areas along all fronts in the Doboj area, the information service of the republic of Srpska army's 1st Krajina Corps said this evening. The fiercest Muslim attacks are taking place on the Ozren-Vozuca front. In addition to several unsuccessful attempts to break through Serb defense lines in the southwestern part of the Ozren battlefield, the Muslim units have also launched fierce attacks on the Serbian villages of Stanic Rijeka and Karanovac on the right bank of the river Spreca and on Alica Naselje and Badinovici in the eastern part of Ozren.
