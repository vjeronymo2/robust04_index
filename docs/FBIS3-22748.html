Article Type:CSO [Text] A special issue of the JPRS Series SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY/KOREA entitled `White Paper on Nuclear Energy Safety' (JPRS-KST-93-001, 22 Dec 93) Published by the Korea Institute of Nuclear Safety, Ministry of Science and Technology presents information on Korea's nuclear energy safety management system. The report also provides the status of selected Korean R&D projects in the field of nuclear energy technology for 1991. This is the premier issue of a new FBIS report covering Korean Science and Technology Issues. Consumers not already receiving copies may call (703)-733-6346.
