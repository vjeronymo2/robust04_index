931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / Rural agency to cover wider area The Rural Development Commission, a government agency for development of rural areas of England, has secured a slight increase in the areas on its existing map. It will cover about 35 per cent of England and about 2.75m people next year. Its budget has been increased from Pounds 38.5m in the current year to Pounds 42m for 1994-95.
