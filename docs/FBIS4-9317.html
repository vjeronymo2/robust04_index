BFN ["Pool" item] [Text] Brussels, April 22 (TANJUG) -- The North Atlantic Council late on Friday night [22 April] extended its ultimatum to Bosnian Serbs to cmver the other U.N.-designated `safe havens' in Bosnia-Herzegovina. A statement released from the session said that Bosnian Serb armament would be targeted if they attacked the `safe havens' of Gorazde, Bihac, Srebrenica, Tuzla and Zepa from heavy weapons or in other ways. The towns were designated `safe havens' in spring of 1993.
