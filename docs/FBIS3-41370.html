Language: Urdu Article Type:CSO [News Report: "Three Smugglers Arrested for Attempting To Smuggle Narcotics in Shoes; Those Arrested Include a Brother and Sister; the Police Have Charged the Accused and Started Investigaton."] [Text] Peshawar (Staff Reporter): The Narcotics Control Board carried out raids in Peshawar Bannu and foiled attempts to smuggle narcotics worth large sums of money. Three smugglers were arrested including a brother and sister. The suspects have been charged in the police stations concerned. According to details received, Nizamud din, a resident of Tehkal Payan, hid a half kilogram of heroin in his boots and arrived at Bara bus stand on GT road with the intention of travelling to a certain destination. An informant tipped off the Narcotics Control Board and the Board agents arrived at the bus stand and surrounded the suspect. They searched the man and found heroin hidden in his boots. Agents of the Bannu narcotics control board raided a bus bound for Laki and seized 2 kilograms of opium and four kilograms of marijuana from a brother and sister, named Rozena and Mohammad Ayaz, both residents of Char Sada.
