BFN [Text] Because of poor flying conditions, the delegation of the joint peace-making mission of the CSCE and Russia was today once again unable to fly out to Stepanakert for talks with the Karabakh leadership. As an ITAR-TASS correspondent learned, a decision was taken today to hold the meeting between Jan Eliasson, chairman of the CSCE Minsk Group and Vladimir Kazimirov, plenipotentiary representative of the Russian president, and the leadership of the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic in Yerevan.
