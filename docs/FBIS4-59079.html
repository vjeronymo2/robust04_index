BFN [Text] The House of Representatives met today in the presence of the parliamentary bloc of the Yemeni Socialist Party. It discussed the issue of the illegitimate and unconstitutional moves and activities abroad by brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the prime minister. The conferees supported the issuance of a circular to all embassies and official and diplomatic quarters in the sisterly and friendly countries and the international bodies and institutions, asking them not to officially deal with brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, currently abroad, because he was not on an official mission. He was not entrusted to undertake such a mission by the legitimate and constitutional political leadership nor has this mission been approved by the Council of Ministers.
