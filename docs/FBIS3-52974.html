Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Jeddah, 10 Jan (KUNA) -- Faysal al-Husayni, head of the Steering Committee of the Palestinian delegation to the Palestinian-Israeli peace talks, met here this afternoon Dr. Hamid al-Ghabid, Islamic Conference Organization's [ICO] secretary general. A press statement issued by the ICO General Secretariat said that al-Husayni acquainted Dr. al-Ghabid with the recent developments of the Palestinian-Israeli peace talks and the conditions through which the Palestinian people, and particularly in holy Jerusalem, were going. Al-Husayni affirmed the important role played by the Islamic Conference Organization in supporting and backing the Palestinian people, working diligently for the return of holy Jerusalem to Palestinian sovereignty and confronting Israeli expansionist plans. For his part, the organization's secretary general affirmed the organization's principled and constant stance towards the Palestinian cause and the Islamic nation's support to the Palestinian people in order for them to regain their just rights and establish their state with holy Jerusalem as its capital.
