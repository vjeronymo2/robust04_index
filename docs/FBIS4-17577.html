BFN [Text] Russian Vice Premier Yuriy Yarov categorically dismissed the April 22 report of the ITAR-TASS news agency that he had allegedly prepared an order for the Finance Ministry to grant 4 billion rubles to the newspaper SOVETSKAYA ROSSIYA, INTERFAX learned from the press service of the Russian government. "The reliable source referred to by the ITAR-TASS correspondent, is spreading a blunt falsification."
