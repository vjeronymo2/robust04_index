930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Mountview Estates Reduced interest charges enabled Mountview Estates, the property concern, to raise pre-tax profits from Pounds 6.56m to Pounds 6.79m in the year ended March 31. Turnover dropped from Pounds 13.7m to Pounds 13.4m and operating profits were marginally lower at Pounds 7.16m (Pounds 7.22m). Interest took Pounds 365,895 (Pounds 661,954). Earnings per share came to 99.3p (95.8p). The recommended final dividend is raised from 10p to 12p for a total of 20p (18p).
