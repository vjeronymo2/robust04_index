Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers has approved the so-called Giugni package. This is the legal decree containing a series of measures aimed at extending the framework in which social shock absorbers [money set aside for helping workers and communities through the recession] are applied. Further provisions have been added to the decree, concerning early-retirement for ILVA [Industry for the Production of Steel and Aluminium] and Alitalia personnel. The Giugni package for employment plans to set aside 4.2 trillion lire over three years; 1.6 trillion in 1994, and 1.3 trillion in each of the next two years.
