940510 FT 10 MAY 94 / International Company News: Transamerica in share buy-back Transamerica, the San Francisco-based financial services group, is planning to buy back 4.5m of its shares in a deal worth more than Dollars 200m, writes Richard Waters. Like other financial services stocks, Transamerica has been hit by rising interest rates in recent weeks. Its shares closed at ended last week at Dollars 51 1/8 , down from a high of Dollars 62 3/8 . However, news of the buy-back spurred a rise of Dollars 1 1/4 in the stock in New York yesterday to Dollars 52 3/8 .
