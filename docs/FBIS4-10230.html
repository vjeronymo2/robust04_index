BFN [Text] The required levels of manual sugarcane weeding were not met by every province during the January-March quarter. This process does not require any type of imported resources, but relies fundamentally on manpower and the organization and mobilization of the necessary work force. Holguin and Granma where the only provinces that surpassed their weeding goals; Havana and Camaguey Provinces followed close behind. However, Pinar del Rio, Villa Clara, and Ciego de Avila Provinces did not reach even 50 percent of the areas completed in their best quarters.
