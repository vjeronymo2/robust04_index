May 8, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

PHOENIX TECHNOLOGIES REORGANIZES

Phoenix Technologies Ltd., a software design company, today said it is closing a facility in Scotts Valley, Calif., and consolidating its activities with one in San Jose.

The company also said President Lance E. Hansche has assumed Neil J. Colvin's duties as chief executive officer. Colvin remains chairman.
