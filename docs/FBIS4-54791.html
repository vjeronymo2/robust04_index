BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 4/5/1994 -- The parliamentary secretary of state of the German Ministry of the Interior, Horst Waffenschmidt, in charge with the affairs of the Germans abroad, started on Wednesday a three-day visit in Romania accompanied by a delegation comprising deputies in the Bundestag, the presidents of the associations of the Swabians and the Saxons of Germany, representatives of charity institutions, government dignitaries, said Romanian Foreign Ministry spokesperson Simona Miculescu at a press conference on Wednesday. The visit is meant to assess the aid granted by the German Government to the German nationals of Romania in order to decide over new measures of aid in the economic, cultural, social and education fields. The German delegation will be received by Romanian President Ion Iliescu and will have talks with members of the Romanian Government. The Foreign Ministry spokesperson said also that the German minister for economic cooperation Carl-Dieter Spranger would visit Romania on May 6-7 at the invitation of the Romanian Government in order work out new actions of German support to the economic restructuring process in Romania especially in the industrial sector. On that occasion, a governmental frame-agreement will be signed concerning the technological cooperation between Romania and Germany.
