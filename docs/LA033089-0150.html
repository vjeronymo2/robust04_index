March 30, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Consolidated Freightways Inc. has extended its cash tender offer for all common shares, including the associated rights, and all preferred shares of Emery Air Freight Corp. until midnight EST Friday . . . San Leandro, Calif.-based Liquor Barn, the nation's leading liquor, wine and beer retail chain, has received a $7.5-million loan from Barclays Bank PLC that will enable the chain to maintain a full range of products throughout the year . . . Polaroid Corp., Cambridge, Mass., announced that the trial for the damage phase of the patent suit against Rochester, N.Y.-based, Eastman Kodak has been delayed until May 1.
