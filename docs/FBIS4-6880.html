BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 22 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The situation concerning the political hunger strike has not been settled down yet. For nine days goes on the hunger strike of members and supporters of the Mongolian Democratic Association, the Union of Mongolian Students and Union for New Progress demanding resignation of the government. The counter strike for support of the government is lasting for six days. Yesterday rallies were held called by fighting political forces. The meeting at the central square of the Mongolian capital was ended in scanning by its participants the appeals for resignation of the government and marching around the state residence. As for the meeting called by the public organization "281 Union" at the Erh Choloonii Square it has gone off rather quietly with a few participants.
