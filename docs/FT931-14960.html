930115 FT 15 JAN 93 / Maxwell pensions mediation urged Mr Peter Lilley, social security secretary, acting on recommendations from the all-party committee on social security, has asked Sir John Cuckney to continue to encourage mediation among liquidators and advisers to the Maxwell company pension schemes. 'If rapid progress cannot be made in the Common Investment Fund by agreement then I hope all parties will consider mediation,' Mr Lilley said.
