BFN [Text] Police Minister Moshe Shahal confirmed in the Knesset that 362 Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] supporters were detained in the Gaza Strip last night. Some will be put on trial and others will be held in administrative detention. The minister said the detentions were carried out following the investigation into the car bombs in 'Afula and Hadera. Shahal noted that at least one investigation of the eight car bombs recently discovered uncovered components the security forces have found in Lebanon.
