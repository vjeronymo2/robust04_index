BFN [Text] Yerevan ("LRAGIR" daily), 06/21/94 -- Baku radio reported that the head of the Nakhichevan delegation refuted the information, spread by the Armenian media, that an agreement was reached on organization of border trade at the Armenian-Nakhichevan border. He declared that the Armenian side suggested to open trade exhibitions at the border, but the Nakhichevan side abruptly refused, saying this is impossible between warring countries, when Armenia occupied significant Azeri territories.
