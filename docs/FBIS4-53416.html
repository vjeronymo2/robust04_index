BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 19 (KCNA) -- The Korea-Jordan Friendship Association held a commemorative meeting here Wednesday on the occasion of the 48th anniversary of the independence of Jordan. Attending the meeting were Yi Chin-su, vice-chairman of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea and chairman of the Korea-Jordan Friendship Association, and officials concerned and working people in the city. A message of greetings to King Hussein the First of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan was adopted.
