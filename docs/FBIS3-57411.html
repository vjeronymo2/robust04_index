Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Talalayev] [Text] Moscow, 26 January -- Boris Fedorov is leaving the post of finance minister. His press secretary Olga Leonova told ITAR-TASS that at a meeting with Russian President Boris Yeltsin this morning, Boris Fedorov rejected the proposal to take the post of finance minister in the reformed government on the conditions offered to him. At the same time, according to the press secretary, conditions put forward by Boris Fedorov were not accepted. ITAR-TASS has learned that Boris Fedorov is preparing a statement for the press.
