BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 10/5/1994 -- The Tuesday, 10 May, afternoon schedule of President Sali Berisha of Albania, during the first day of his official visit to Romania, included a meeting with Adrian Nastase, speaker of the Chamber of Deputies of the Romanian Parliament, and a call on the Parliament Palace. Accompanied by the other members of his delegation, President Berisha was shown the National History Museum in Bucharest. Earlier today President Berisha had laid a flower wreath at the tomb of the Unknown Soldier in Bucharest. After the ceremony, the Albanian delegation made for the Albanian embassy in Bucharest for a meeting with representatives of the Albanian Cultural Union of Romania.
