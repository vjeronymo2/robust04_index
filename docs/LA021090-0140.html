February 10, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COUNTYWIDE;

SCOUTS WILL COLLECT CANNED FOOD TODAY

Thousands of Boy Scouts will go door-to-door today, hoping to find the orange-and-white bags that they dropped off last weekend filled with canned goods.

Last year, the Scouts collected more than 810,000 cans of food. This year, the goal is a million cans.

Scouting officials believe their food drive is the largest ever held in Orange County.
