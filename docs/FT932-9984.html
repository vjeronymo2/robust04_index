930511 FT 11 MAY 93 / International Company News: Brambles shares plunge on poor estimate for half SHARES in Brambles Industries, the Australian transport group, fell yesterday to an 18-month low after a forecast of sharply lower second-half profits. The shares fell 96 cents to ADollars 12.98 (USDollars 9.20), their lowest since November 1990, after directors said earnings for the half to June faced a marked fall. They cited recession in Europe as the main reason, but added full-year earnings should not fall more than 15 per cent on the earlier ADollars 180.2m.
