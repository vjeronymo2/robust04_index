Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The blockade of the besieged town of Maglaj and the Croatian community of Usora [northeast Bosnia] was lifted at 1500 today. According to Colonel Ivo Lozancic, the commander of the [Bosnian Croat] 111th Brigade, following an ultimatum issued by the 111th Brigade Command, the Srpska army today withdrew all their military equipment from their lines near Maglaj and the roads to Zepce and Usora. They have already reached the vicinity of Teslic. The Usora-94 operation was thus successfully completed. After several months of blockade the Croatian community of Usora is at last linked to the Croats of Zepce.
