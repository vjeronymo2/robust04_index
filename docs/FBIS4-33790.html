BFN [From the NTV newscast] [Text] Politburo members Jose Ramon Balaguer and Carlos Lage bid farewell to the CPC delegation at Jose Marti Airport on 12 June. Wu Banngou said he was very satisfied with his visit to Cuba and underscored the importance of having met President Fidel Castro on two occasions. In a statement for Cuban television, he said: [Begin Wu recording in Mandarin with passage by passage translation into Spanish] We are greatly impressed with some things. Cuba made it through 1993, a most difficult year. Cuba now has a very clear development strategy. Cuba has adopted a series of achievable economic measures. We sincerely hope that Cuba can continue developing every day. [end recording]
