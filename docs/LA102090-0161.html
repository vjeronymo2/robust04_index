October 20, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

MEMORIAL SERVICE FOR DANCE MANAGER

A memorial service for Tom Corcoran, who died at home Sunday at the age of 41, will be held Oct. 26 at 4:30 p.m. at the Old Globe's Lowell Davies Festival Theatre in Balboa Park. The former manager of Three's Company & Dancers was also an executive director of the San Diego Area Dance Alliance until early 1990, when he retired for health reasons.

Three's Company and Dancers will perform during the service.
