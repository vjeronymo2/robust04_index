DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE Office of the Secretary Notice of Availability, Department of Defense Pollution Prevention Written Strategy AGENCY: Office of the Secretary, DOD. ACTION: Notice of availability.

SUMMARY: As part of the requirements under Executive Order 12856 ``Federal Compliance with Right-To-Know Laws and Pollution Prevention Requirements,'' this notice announces the availability of the Department of Defense written pollution prevention strategy.
ADDRESSES: Requests for copies of the strategy and comments on the strategy may be submitted to the Office of the Deputy Under Secretary of Defense (Environmental Security), Attn: Andy Porth, OADUSD (PP), Skyline 6 suite 310, 5109 Leesburg Pike, Falls Church, VA 22041.
