940830 FT 30 AUG 94 / More InterCity trains set to break strike today British Rail plans to operate services from most major cities to London in spite of industrial action by signalmen from noon today until noon on Thursday. In all cases, passengers should check the hours of operation locally. Services may be subject to alteration at short notice.
