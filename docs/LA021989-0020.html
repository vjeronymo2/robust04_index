February 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE BIG MIX

As a member of the National Task Force on Presenting & Touring the Performing Arts, I can say the issues raised in "The Big Mix" (Feb. 5) are the cultural issues being discussed in think tanks across North America. I would add, however, it is important to go beyond ethnicity to acknowledge the art of lesbians, gays, the elderly, the homeless.

TIM MILLER

Santa Monica
