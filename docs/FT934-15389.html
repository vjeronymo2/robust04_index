931012 FT 12 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Unilabs acquires JS Pathology Unilabs Group, an independent European pathology laboratory company, is acquiring JS Pathology, which claims to be the UK's largest private clinical testing laboratory. Following the acquisition, Unilabs will have an annualised turnover in excess of Pounds 50m. JS was a quoted UK company which was floated in November 1985 with a market capitalisation of Pounds 19m. It was acquired by a subsidiary of Corning in September 1992 for Pounds 23.1m.
