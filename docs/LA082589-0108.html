August 25, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Thrifts Approve Anti-Takeover Shields: Two California thrifts, Coast Savings in Los Angeles and HomeFed Corp., parent of Home Federal Savings in San Diego, announced separately that they have approved anti-takeover shields to make it more expensive for hostile suitors to take them over. Both measures would be triggered when a suitor buys a stake of 15% or more.
