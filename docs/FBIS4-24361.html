BFN [Text] Beijing, May 27 (XINHUA) -- Gertrude Mongella, secretary general of the Fourth World Conference on Women, arrived here today at the invitation of Liu Huaqiu, Chinese vice-foreign minister and vice-chairman of the Chinese organizing committee of the conference. Gertrude Mongella has come to inspect China's preparations for the conference. Greeting her at the airport was Wang Shuxian, vice-chairman of the All-China Women's Federation.
