931011 FT 11 OCT 93 / Textile industry attacks Gatt plan TRADE rules proposed under the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade 'are a grossly unfair handicap for British textile and clothing exporters', the industry's trade body says in a report published today. The Apparel, Knitting and Textiles Alliance says the barriers result in a higher balance of payments deficit for the industry, which last year had record exports of Pounds 4.8bn. Letters, Page 14
