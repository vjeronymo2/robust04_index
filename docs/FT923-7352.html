920819 FT 19 AUG 92 / Lufthansa agrees pay cut The six management board directors of Lufthansa, the stricken German airline, have agreed to a 10 per cent pay cut this year. The move, expected to save up to DM300,000, (Pounds 106,382) was intended as a clear signal that all employees must tighten their belts, the company said. It followed Monday's announcement that the DAG white-collar union was prepared to accept pay reductions and longer working hours to help protect jobs.
