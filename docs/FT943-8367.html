940817 FT 17 AUG 94 / World Stock Markets: De Beers falls on results De Beers fell heavily in Johannesburg after the diamond group reported disappointing half-year results, and its shares lost R2.75 at R109.50. Brokers said that the results were worse than expected and forecast that the shares could move lower. The market was generally softer, although activity remained muted and volume was low for the second consecutive session. The overall index declined 26 to 5,763, industrials shed 11 to 6,569 and the gold shares index dipped 16 to 2,110. Anglos eased 50 cents to R251.50 and Johnnies receded R1 to R110, but Gold Fields edged up 25 cents to R126.25 following improved annual results. Among gold issues, Western Areas added 50 cents at R64 and Vaal Reefs retreated R2 to R390. Elsewhere, Iscor put on 3 cents at R4.03, but Highveld Steel slipped back 25 cents to R32 after recent gains, while Barlows relinquished R1 to finish at R32.
