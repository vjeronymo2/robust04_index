BFN [Excerpts] Santa Fe de Bogota, 29 May (NOTIMEX) -- Official sources have reported Colombian President Cesar Gaviria today canceled his trip to El Salvador where he was to attend the swearing in ceremony of Salvadoran President-elect Armando Calderon Sol. Presidential Palace spokespersons have said the Colombian president will not attend the swearing in ceremony, because the doctors who performed surgery on his right foot 10 days ago have recommended rest. [passage omitted] Gaviria underwent surgery on 19 May to relieve pressure in his posterior tibial nerve. Using crutches, the Colombian president was the first to vote in Sunday's presidential elections, in which 18 candidates sought to become president of Colombia for the next four years.
