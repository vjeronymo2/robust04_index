BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS special correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Sevastopol 3 June TASS -- 127 Ukrainian paratroopers arrived on two transport An-26 planes in the Crimea on Friday [3 June]. TASS learned from local military sources that the planes landed at the "Bilbek" airfield situated practically within the city of Sevastopol, which is the major base of the Black Sea Fleet. The paratroopers are expected to form an airmobile company which will guard the airfield and secure airlifting of troops in case of necessity, according to the sources. Earlier over 350 Ukrainian national guards were airlifted to Sevastopol from Ukraine. According to the Crimean Interior Ministry, thay are protecting public order in the city.
