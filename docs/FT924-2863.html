921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / International Company News: Compromise on trading switch in Amsterdam THE EUROPEAN Options Exchange in Amsterdam is to combine its traditional open-outcry trading system with screen-based trading following a large vote in favour of the proposed changes at a general meeting of members. Under the proposed system, public-order members will be allowed to choose whether to have their clients' orders executed through an electronic system, or on the open-outcry trading floor.
