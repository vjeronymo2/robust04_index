Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] In Qalqilyah, a border policeman was slightly injured by a stone. In clashes with security forces in the territories, 19 residents were hurt: 10 in Nabulus, three in Hebron, two in Tulkarm, and four residents of the Jabalyah refugee camp in the Gaza Strip. In Gaza City, border policemen fired at a suspect car, wounding one of the passengers. A Carl Gustav was found in his possession. He was detained for questioning. The other passengers in the car fled on foot. Two senior members of the Arab Liberation Front, a rejectionist organization, were captured in the Gaza Strip. The two wanted people, Khalid al-Afgani from the al-Burayj refugee camp, and al-Nasir Abu-Madin from Gaza, were detained for interrogation. A gun was found in their possession. In Salah-al-Din Street in East Jerusalem, several Arabs rioted and threw stones at an Israeli car. The driver escaped unharmed, but his car was set on fire and burned.
