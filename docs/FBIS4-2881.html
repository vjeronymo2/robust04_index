BFN [Text] Beijing, April 14 (CNS) -- Spokesman of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mr. Shen Guofang, said at today's news briefing that the incident at Qiandao Lake was an accidental event involving tourism which should not have a negative influence on the relations between the two sides of the Taiwan straits. The spokesman said that the Mainland side also deeply grieved over the accident, and related departments there were conducting a full investigation into the event and were willing to hear the viewpoints and suggestions on the matter from Taiwan compatriots. Mr. Shen said that the further strengthening of personnel exchange and contacts in various sectors between the two sides was in conformity with the interests of the Chinese nation.
