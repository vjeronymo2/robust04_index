BFN [Text] Crude oil is now being produced at Changcheng Oil Field at the south end of Horqin Grasslands. This indicates that Liaohe Oil Field has made a new breakthrough in prospecting oil and gas deposits in Horqin Region and also indicates that the oil-deposit region of Horqin as a whole will become an important area of Liaohe Oil Field in developing its oil resources. Throughout the year of prospecting, five wells were drilled in Changcheng Oil Field and gushed with varying degrees of oil and gas. Of these wells, the daily output of two exceeds 30 tonnes.
