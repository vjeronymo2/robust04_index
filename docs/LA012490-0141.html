January 24, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Donor organ flights -- Because of an editing error, a story in some editions Saturday incorrectly identified Harbor-UCLA Medical Center as a hospital that uses Van Nuys Airport for flights to retrieve donor organs for transplants.

The hospital is UCLA Medical Center. Also, a photo caption accompanying that story misidentified a heart transplant coordinator with Loma Linda University Medical Center. The coordinator, Kay Ogata, was correctly identified in the story.
