930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / Nato forces head due to be named THE US is expected to name General George Joulwan as Supreme Allied Commander of the North Atlantic Treaty Organisation's forces in Europe, writes George Graham from Washington. Gene Joulwan, who now heads the US Southern Command in Panama, would succeed General John Shalikashvili, who has been nominated to become chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff, the top military position in the US. The position of Saceur is traditionally in the gift of the US, but the appointment would be subject to approval by the Nato allies.
