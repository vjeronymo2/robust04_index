Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] [No dateline as received] -- A UNIAN correspondent learned today that heating in Ukraine's residential buildings and at enterprises will be switched off before the end of March rather than on 15 April as was the case previously. Commenting on the situation, Oleksiy Sheberstov, Ukraine's first deputy minister of power engineering and electrification, said Ukraine's fuel supplies are rapidly diminishing. At the same time, consumers owe 8,000 billion karbovantsi to the ministry, which is the reason why it lacks the money to buy oil and gas for heating.
