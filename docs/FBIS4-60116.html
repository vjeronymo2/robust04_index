BFN [Text] Speaking in Gaza, Nabil Sha'th said that joint patrols by the Palestinians and the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] would begin today. He noted that the main mission of the Palestinian police right now is to launch an investigation into yesterday's attack. We are very concerned, Sha'th said, because the Palestinians are responsible for whatever happens within the autonomous areas. He was speaking with reporters in Gaza after meeting Deputy Chief of Staff Amnon Shahaq and the head of the Shin Bet.
