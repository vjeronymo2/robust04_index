941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Equities finished mildly easier after investors shadowed the movements of Mexican blue chips traded on Wall Street. The IPC index shed 21.72 or 0.78 per cent to 2,755.97. Turnover totalled a light 518.3m new pesos on 37.4m shares traded. Brokers said they expected stocks to make gains during the week in anticipation of a flow of third-quarter results before the month's end. The market had risen 6 per cent in the previous four sessions.
