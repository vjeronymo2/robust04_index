March 26, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BATTLING THE MEDFLY

Los Angeles County will conduct aerial spraying of malathion in two separate areas tonight from 9 p.m. to 3 a.m., weather permitting.

Area: One area covers Azusa, Bradbury, Duarte and parts of Glendora, Irwindale, Monrovia and San Dimas in the East San Gabriel Valley. The other area covers parts of Los Angeles and Orange counties, including parts of La Habra Heights, Rowland Heights, Brea and La Habra.

Precautions: Stay indoors; keep animals indoors; wash animal dishes and toys left outside; cover cars; keep doors and windows closed.

Information: Toll-free numbers for the Agricultural Commissioner: (800) 356-2894; (800) 225-1346.
