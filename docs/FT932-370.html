930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Approval for enlarged GBE Shareholders of Downiebrae Holdings have approved the reversal of the company into GBE International, the machinery manufacturer. The enlarged group will be known as GBE International. Mr Graham Peacock, Downiebrae chairman, and Mr Robert Newman will leave the board; Mr Gerald Edwards, chairman and chief executive of GBE, will take that office in the new group, and Mr John Ormston becomes finance director. Over 36.6m shares were issued for the acquisition, valuing GBE at Pounds 22m. Dealings in the new shares should start today; dealings in existing shares were suspended on May 24 at 77p.
