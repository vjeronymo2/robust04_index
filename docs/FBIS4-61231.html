BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yelena Gorelova] [Text] Moscow May 4 TASS -- Chief of staff for military cooperation between CIS member-states, Viktor Samsonov, 53, was seriously injured in a traffic accident late on May 2, ITAR-TASS learned from the press service of the traffic police. The accident occurred within the distance of one kilometer as Samsonov's car "Volga" with his family entered the Ilyinsk highway, returning from Barvikha in the Moscow countryside. A woman-driver in the oncoming Zhiguli car failed to keep the wheel, drove into the opposite side of the highway for the oncoming traffic and the Zhiguli collided with Samsonov's car. As a result of the collision Samsonov received numerous injuries and bone fractures, his wife was badly injured in the face and his son, a student of the Suvorov Military School, had a concussion and his nose broken. The woman-driver responsible for the accident was taken to hospital, suffering from numerous bone fractures, and her companion riding in the same car was instantly killed.
