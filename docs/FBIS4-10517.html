BFN [Report by Annerys Gonzalez; from "The News" newscast -- live relay] [Text] Disip [Directorate of Intelligence and Prevention Services] members arrived at San Martin Avenue at 0910 [1310 GMT] this Tuesday to defuse an alleged explosive found near a Banco Latino automatic teller machine. Well-protected members of the Disip Explosive Division are proceeding to defuse the pipe bomb. An official is signaling that the situation is under control. As you can see, people were ordered out of the building to avoid any possible mishap. Disip officials picked up a polyethylene bag--apparently containing an explosive device--and put it inside this truck to transport it to a location where it can be detonated. Members of the Special Action Brigade of the Judicial Technical Police [PTJ] and officials of the Disip Explosive Division arrived at the location to take pictures of the pipe bomb before it was detonated. As you can see, all security measures are being taken to carry out this operation. Additionally, vehicle traffic in the area was stopped to prevent injury to people near the explosion site. We expect the detonation of the pipe bomb found at Banco Latino any minute. [loud explosion heard in background] Vehicle traffic was finally resumed, and Disip officials departed the area. The Disip and PTJ will investigate this situation to determine who planted this explosive at Banco Latino in San Martin.
