941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / Company News (This Week): Chubb Security - Other Companies The electronic alarms and locks group, is forecast on Wednesday to report pre-tax profits of between Pounds 38m and Pounds 41m for its first six months. Analysts are looking for better revenues as further improvements in margins are unlikely. The company will be quizzed on its strategy of introducing new product ranges and expanding its sales force. The City is also keen to see how markets in the US and continental Europe have recovered.
