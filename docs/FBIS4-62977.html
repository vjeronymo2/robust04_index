BFN [Text] Yerevan, 16 MAY (SNARK)--Armenian Ministry of Foreign Affairs appealed today to all parties involved in the Karabakh conflict "to refrain from hostile actions and announcements, to demonstrate good will in order to end the tragedy and make possible the solution of Nagorno-Karabakh problem through negotiations at the Minsk Conference." In the statement issued today, the ministry expressed its hope that "collaboration between the Russian Federation and the CSCE Minsk group will continue, and all possible and required mechanisms will be effected, including disposition of observers, in order to transform the truce into a stable cease-fire as soon as possible."
