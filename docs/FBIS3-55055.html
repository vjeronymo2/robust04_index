Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] It is very likely that Russian special agencies stand behind the Kebich government; this was declared by Vladimir Novik, Belarusian Supreme Soviet deputy and secretary of the legislative standing commission on the economy, in an interview with SVABODA [Freedom] on 5 January. "You can be absolutely sure," he said, "that stabilizing the Russian ruble or the U.S. dollar means undermining our economy and robbing our people. This would strengthen the economies of the countries whose currencies would be allowed in Belarus. Who is devising these kinds of policies? The government of an independent state or a special agency? The latter, is the most likely answer."
