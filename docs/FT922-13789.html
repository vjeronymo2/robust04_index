920409 FT 09 APR 92 / UK Company News: Magnolia Magnolia Group, the Southend-based picture frame moulding and publishing company, saw pre-tax profits dive from Pounds 1.05m to Pounds 401,000 over the 1991 year. The outcome - struck after exceptional restructuring costs of Pounds 101,000 (Pounds 106,000) - came on turnover down to Pounds 21.8m (Pounds 23.5m). Interest charges took Pounds 304,000 (Pounds 312,000). Gearing at the year-end was cut by 1 percentage point to 17 per cent. Earnings per share declined from 11.16p to 4.65p, but the total dividend is maintained at 5.4p with a same-again final of 3.65p.
