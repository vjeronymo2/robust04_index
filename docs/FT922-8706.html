920512 FT 12 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Avon Rubber sets up joint venture Avon Rubber, the automotive components company, has agreed to a joint venture with Clevite Elastomers, the US vehicle suspension systems group. Clevite is paying Pounds 4.9m for a 49 per cent stake in Avon-Clevite which will operate Avon's new components factory in Chippenham, due to open soon.
