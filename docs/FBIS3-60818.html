Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Shaykh Ahmad Yasin, the Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] spiritual leader, said this morning that his movement would accept a cease-fire with Israel provided that the use of firearms against Palestinians stops. Yasin added that Hamas will not stop its activities until the Jewish settlements in the Gaza Strip are evacuated. Shaykh Yasin made the remarks at a meeting this morning with Knesset Member Talib al-Sani' at the Ramla Prison hospital where Yasin is being imprisoned. [passage omitted with al-Sani' remarks] At the same time, more than 1,000 Hamas and Islamic Jihad leaflets were found last night in a large bag on Temple Mount urging the termination of the peace process, attacks on the settlers, and exacerbating the security situation in the territories. The Jerusalem Police launched an investigation.
