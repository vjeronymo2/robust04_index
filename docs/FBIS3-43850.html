Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The Angolan Government has already responded to Jonas Savimbi's communique. A source of the Angolan Government delegation to the Lusaka talks said that they were unaware of any air bombardment. According to the source, what happened on Wednesday in the geographical area between Pungi and the city of Cuito was a clash that originated in a dispute over air-dropped food. During the incident, some Angolan Armed Forces and UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] soldiers were killed, as well as several civilians.
