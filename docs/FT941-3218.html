940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / Environment fears 'exaggerated' Fears about global warming and many other environmental threats are exaggerated, economists claim today. The main cause of warming of about 0.5`C in the earth's temperature over the last century is unlikely to be an increase in 'greenhouse gases' in the atmosphere, say the authors of a book from the Institute of Economic Affairs, the free-market think-tank.
