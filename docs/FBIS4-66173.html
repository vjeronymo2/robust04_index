CSO [Summary] As of 15 March, Anhui Province had procured 3,320,000 dan of cotton, fulfilling 73.8 percent of the procurement plan, and was a decline of 680,000 dan from the same period in 1993. In 1994 Anhui's cotton production tasks are to plant 6 million mu, to have a gross output of 6 million dan, and to procure 5 million dan.
