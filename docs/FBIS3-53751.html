Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] An expanded Lebanese-Syrian summit will be held in Damascus to assess the Geneva summit and discuss how to coordinate the two countries' positions. President Ilyas al-Hirawi, Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz, and the head of the Lebanese delegation to the Arab-Israeli talks, Suhayl Shammas, left for the Syrian Capital this morning.
