Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 25 Jan--On 25 January, President Lech Walesa welcomed to the Belweder Slovak President Michal Kovac, who is on his first official visit to Poland. The Sejm and senate speakers and the prime minster took part in the ceremony. After the welcoming ceremony, both presidents began face-to-face talks. After this meeting, plenary talks will be held between the two delegations. A PAP journalist has learned unofficially that the agreements on regional cooperation and environmental protection will most probably not be signed. Representatives of the Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs had announced before that such agreements were to be signed.
