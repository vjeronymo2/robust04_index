930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Pounds 40m aid package for jobless launched A POUNDS 40m SCHEME to provide week-long workshops for those out of work for 12 months or more was launched by the government yesterday. Jobplan workshops, announced in the Autumn Statement, will be mandatory - people refusing a place or failing to complete the training could lose some benefit. Mrs Gillian Shephard, the employment secretary, said she was keen to do more to help and motivate the 1m people who have been out of work for more than a year. 'Attending a Jobplan workshop will give them a better chance to compete more successfully for the opportunities that are available,' she said. 'It is not unusual for people who have been out of work for over a year to be unclear about their job goals and the options open to them,' she said. 'Jobplan will give them the maximum help to overcome their difficulties and get back to work as quickly as possible.'
