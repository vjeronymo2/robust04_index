920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / International Company News: Inquiry into Tomson Pacific AN investigation has been launched into the activities of Hong Kong's Tomson Pacific Group, headed by Macao casino entrepreneur Mr Stanley Ho, writes Simon Davies. The financial secretary appointed an inspector yesterday and trading in the three group listings was suspended. The Securities and Futures Commission recommended the investigation into 'a number of specific transactions' carried out by Tomson Pacific and its 33.4 per cent owned associate World Trade Centre (WTC), since 1990. WTC was formerly Mr Alan Bond's Hong Kong investment company Bond Corporation International (BCIL). 1990.
