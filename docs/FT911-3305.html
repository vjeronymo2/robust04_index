910425 FT 25 APR 91 / International Capital Markets: Japan Airlines plans listings JAPAN Airlines (JAL) is planning stock market listings in London, New York and Paris to help finance new aircraft purchases and other capital expenditure totalling nearly Dollars 12bn over the next 10 years, writes Paul Betts, Aerospace Correspondent. JAL executives confirmed the plans in a report to be published today in the latest edition of Business, the magazine jointly owned by the Financial Times and the Conde Naste group. JAL is also considering acquiring a stake in either a US or European carrier.
