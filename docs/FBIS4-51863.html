BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 2 (KCNA) -- Signature campaigns have been conducted in Peru and Jamaica in support of the "10-point Programme of the Great Unity of the Whole Nation for the Reunification of the Country" put forward by the great leader President Kim Il-song. The signature campaign which began in Peru on April 6 according to a call of the Peruvian Initiative Committee of the Signature Campaign for supporting the "10-point Programme of the Great Unity of the Whole Nation for the Reunification of the Country" involved the president and the general secretary of the National Executive Committee of the Left Revolutionary Union of Peru, the chairman and the organisational secretary of the People's Front of Workers, Peasants and Students of Peru, the general secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Peru (Red Motherland), the general secretary of the Socialist Revolutionary Party of Peru, the general secretary of the Socialist Party of Peru, the chairman of the Peruvian Front for National Liberation, the general secretary of the Central Committee of the Peruvian Communist Party, the general secretary of the General Confederation of Peasants of Peru, and other political and public figures and broad segments of people. More than 2.75 million people had participated in the signature campaign as of April 20. In Kingston, Jamaica, participants in a meeting which was held in celebration of April 15 conducted a signature campaign on April 16.
