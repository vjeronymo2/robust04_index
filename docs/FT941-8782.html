940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / UN inspectors in Baghdad United Nations inspectors yesterday arrived in Baghdad to visit military plants to prepare for long-term UN control and monitoring of Iraq's arms industry, Reuter reports from Manama. The 14 UN experts will visit a dozen military production facilities around Baghdad during their nine-day stay. The UN Special Commission dismantling Iraqi weapons under 1991 Gulf ceasefire terms is stepping up pressure to enforce UN resolution 715 preventing Baghdad from re-establishing its previous nuclear, chemical, biological and long-range missile potential.
