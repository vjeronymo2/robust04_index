940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Daejan jumps to Pounds 19.5m Daejan Holdings, the property company, yesterday ann-ounced a 17 per cent rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 16.7m to Pounds 19.5m for the year ended March 31, writes David Blackwell. Net rental income rose from Pounds 16.4m to Pounds 19.1m and there was a Pounds 7.89m (Pounds 6.42m) surplus on sale of trading properties. Operating profits from continuing operations improved from Pounds 18.5m to Pounds 22.4m. The surplus on the sale of investment properties improved from Pounds 727,000 to Pounds 1.28m. Earnings moved ahead from 81p to 85.7p. The dividend is up from 27p to 29p with a final of 17p. Net asset value per share was estimated at Pounds 17.
