BFN [Text] Dr. Muhammad Bayt-al-Mal, secretary of the General People's Committee for Planning, Trade, and Finance in the Libyan Jamahiriyah, arrived in Damascus this evening on a visit that will last several days. He was received at Damascus International Airport by Dr. Muhammad Khalid al-Mahayini, minister of finance; Humayyid Abu-Bakr Jallud, secretary of Libyan Relations [Bureau] in Damascus; and ranking Finance Ministry officials. In a statement to a SANA correspondent, Dr. Bayt-al-Mal expressed his happiness at visiting Syria and said: We always look forward to meeting with brothers in Syria in order to exchange ideas on all issues of common concern. He hoped his visit will develop relations of fraternal cooperation between the two sisterly countries and peoples.
