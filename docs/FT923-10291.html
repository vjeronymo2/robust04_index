920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Harry Ramsden's ahead to Pounds 76,706 Harry Ramsden's, the USM-quoted fish and chip restaurant group, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 73,559 to Pounds 76,706 in the half year to March 29. Following the success of the Glasgow and Heathrow franchises, Mr John Barnes, chairman, said the company was on track to open up to 15 restaurants in the UK and the Republic of Ireland by 1995 and there were plans to open franchised outlets in the Pacific Rim and the Middle East. He said the highlight of the second half would be the first international opening in Hong Kong, which forms the basis of the expansion in the Pacific Rim. Turnover of Pounds 728,288 (Pounds 714,631) originated from the Guiseley restaurant only. Earnings per share rose to 0.9p (0.8p) and the interim dividend is held at 1p.
