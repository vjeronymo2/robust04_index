BFN [Text] On 25 April, our forces attacked a company position of the communist Vietnamese puppets and totally liberated Kompong Chrey township in Kirivong District of Takeo Province. We killed three enemy soldiers, including a major, and wounded a first lieutenant; destroyed two military trucks and two military motorcycles; and seized five AK rifles, a drum-magazined machine gun, a pistol, and a quantity of ammunition and war materiel.
