Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 17 KYODO -- The Finance Ministry said Thursday [17 March] it will raise the base interest rate on loans from its fund management bureau to government institutions by 0.65 percentage point to 4.30 percent per annum. Following the move, the government-backed Housing Loan Corp. is expected to raise its base housing loan rate from the record low 3.6 percent per annum to around 4.0 percent, probably effective late April. The Fund Management Bureau, which invests public funds from postal savings and other sources, offers the loans to the Housing Loan Corp., Japan Road Corp. and other government-backed organizations. The base housing loan rate applies to loans for houses with a floor space of up to 125 square meters.
