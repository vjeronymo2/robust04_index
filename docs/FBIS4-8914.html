BFN [Text] Geneva, April 19 (AFP) -- Bosnian Serb artillery units pounding Gorazde scored two directs hits Tuesday afternoon [19 April] on the building sheltering four UNHCR aid workers and five UN Protection Force observers, the UNHCR said here. The UN High Commissioner for Refugees here said the shells struck at around 5:50 p.m. (1550 GMT), when communications with the agency's team in the Bosnian enclave were cut off after a radio message confirming the two hits.
