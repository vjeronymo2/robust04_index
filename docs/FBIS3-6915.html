Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the Voice of America on 22 March, the U.S. Department of Defense said that a U.S. Patriot missile battalion would move to South Korea from the U.S. state of Texas. It was reported that the Patriot missile battalion is made up of approximately 40 missile launchers and is composed of 800 troops. After breaking up the DPRK-U.S. talks, U.S. President Clinton has ordered the deployment of Patriot missiles to South Korea. He is also leading the tension on the Korean peninsula to the situation of war by babbling that the missiles, which will be deployed to South Korea, contribute to the interest of the United States and South Korea.
