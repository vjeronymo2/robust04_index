BFN [Text] Moscow, May 6 (XINHUA) -- The promotion of good neighborly relations with China is in keeping with Russia's long-term national interest, Vladimir Podoprigora, chairman of the committee on foreign affairs of the federation council said here today. At a press conference following a parliamentary hearing on the development of Sino-Russian relations, Podoprigora said that promoting relations will be beneficial to the security and stability of the Asia-Pacific region and the world. Mutually beneficial economic and trade relations with China is an important element in Russia's economic reform and for the integration of the Asian-Pacific region, he added. Addressing the problems of the development, Podoprigora said the process now lacks coordinated long-term strategy, and that the import-export system must be perfected.
