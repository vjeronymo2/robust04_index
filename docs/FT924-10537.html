921030 FT 30 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Rowe Evans doubles to Pounds 926,000 Continuing firmness in the palm oil price and increasing crops in the developing Indonesian estates enabled Rowe Evans Investments to double its pre-tax profit. For the first half of 1992 profit rose from Pounds 443,000 to Pounds 926,000 on turnover ahead from Pounds 1.73m to Pounds 2.42m. Earnings were 1.13p (0.62p).
