Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] A group of demobilized soldiers from the government army revealed a few days ago that Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] militants have had contacts with government soldiers and policemen in Gaza Province, inciting them to react against the minimum salary established for the soldiers of the future unified army. This was revealed during a session of the provincial government of Gaza, held in Xai-Xai on 18 March. The meeting heard that Renamo militants were headed by Antonio Muchanga, Renamo deputy representative in Gaza.
