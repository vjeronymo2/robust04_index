930921 FT 21 SEP 93 / UK Company News: ICI sells Victrex to management Imperial Chemical Industries has agreed in principle to sell its Victrex polyetheretherketone (peek) business, based at Hillhouse, Lancashire, to a management buy-out backed by a consortium of financial institutions led by CVC Capital Partners. Victrex is involved in high temperature, high performance plastics sold into the automotive, aerospace, industrial and electronics industries. The value of the transaction represents less than 1 per cent of ICI's net assets.
