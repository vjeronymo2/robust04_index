Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman, (PETRA)--Their Majesties King Husayn and Queen Nur received at their residence here Monday [24 January] Arab ambassadors to the United States. The King briefed the envoys on his meeting with President Bill Clinton, Jordan's support for the peace process and the necessity to bring about a comprehensive, just and durable peace in the Middle East. He stressed the need for a unified Arab position and a high level of coordination among the Arab countries. Present at the meeting were Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al- Majali, Royal Court Chief al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, Minister of Information Jawad al-'Anani, Minister of Finance Sami Qammuh and Jordanian ambassador to the U.S. Fayiz al-Tarawinah.
