BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Vyacheslav Kebich, chairman of the Belarus council of ministers, has said in an IMA-Press interview that the fate of the treaty on a merger of the Republic's monetary system with that of Russia entirely depends on the outcome of Belarus's first presidential elections. During his working visit to Grodno, Kebich also revealed the size of the loan granted by Russia to Minsk. It was 200 billion rubles. The prime minister stressed that it would allow the Republic to reduce its debt for gas and other fuels.
