930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / Directors accused of Pounds 12m fraud A DOUBLE-GLAZING company defrauded customers and creditors of more than Pounds 12m, Birmingham Crown Court was told yesterday. Mr Martin Wilson QC said that Therm-a-stor and its holding company Thermal Securities collapsed in 1990 with the loss of 600 jobs. When the Peterborough-based company collapsed secured creditors were owed Pounds 8m, suppliers Pounds 2.5m and customers Pounds 1.6m, the court heard. Mr John Morris, former chairman of Thermal Securities, and Mr Anthony Bonnar, former managing director of Therm-a-stor, both deny one charge of defrauding customers and one of defrauding creditors. The trial continues today.
