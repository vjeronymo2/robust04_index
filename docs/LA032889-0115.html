March 28, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Benedictine Health System of Duluth, Minn., and Pasadena-based Beverly Enterprises Inc. announced an agreement whereby BHS acquires six North Dakota nursing homes from Beverly for an undisclosed price . . . Walt Disney Co. said it needs to fill 2,500 new jobs at its Florida theme park because of the May 1 opening of a studio tour attraction and the completion of a 2,112-room resort.
