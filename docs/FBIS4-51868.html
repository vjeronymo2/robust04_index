BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 3 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Il-song, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and president of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, received on his birthday a message of greetings from Obiang Nguema Mbasogo, president and head of state of the Republic of Equatorial Guinea and founder-chairman of the Democratic Party of Equatorial Guinea. Messages of greetings to him also came from A. Moroz, chairman of the Political Council of the Ukrainian Socialist Party; Madhav Kumar Nepal, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Nepal Communist Party (United Marxism-Leninism); Rashed Khan Menon, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Bangladesh; Chairman Argelia Raya and International Secretary Jorge Valero of the Movement to Socialism of Venezuela; Chairman Rakutuvau Andriantiana and General Secretary Gisele Rabesahala of the Party of the Congress for the Independence of Madagascar; Otete Gaston Mboyo, national chairman of The National Movement of the Genuine Lumumbist Combatants of Zaire; Emile Kibala Bey a Nsien, national chairman of the Workers' Party of Zaire; Robert Corbin, general secretary of the People's National Congress of Guyana; Kamba Raymond, general secretary of the Congolese Communist Party; and Leon Nzokene, general secretary of the Congolese Socialist Party. The messages extend warmest congratulations to President Kim Il- song and wish him good health and a long life. The messages express full support and solidarity for the struggle of the Korean people for socialist construction and national reunification by means of confederacy.
