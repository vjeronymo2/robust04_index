920226 FT 26 FEB 92 / UK Company News: JD Wetherspoon seeks listing JD Wetherspoon, the London pubs operator which yesterday announced first half pre-tax profits ahead from Pounds 432,000 to Pounds 1.01m, is to seek a Stock Exchange listing towards the end of the year. It has appointed Kleinwort Benson to float 35 per cent of the company to raise about Pounds 15m. Mr Tim Martin, the managing director who founded the company in 1979, said the flotation would help to accelerate expansion. During the past 13 years, the company has grown from one free house to an estate of 37 pubs with a turnover in the six months to January 25 of Pounds 9.6m.
