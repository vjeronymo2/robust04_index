940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Bogod makes progress Bogod Group achieved some progress in the second half of the year to March 31, enabling it to report a small rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 232,000 to Pounds 242,000 on sales up from Pounds 5.72m to Pounds 6.77m. In the first half the group, which sells sewing machines, parts and accessories, had seen profits fall from Pounds 105,000 to Pounds 74,000 pre-tax. The dividend for the year on the A shares is maintained at 0.7p, via a final of 0.5p. Earnings came to 2.92p (2.58p).
