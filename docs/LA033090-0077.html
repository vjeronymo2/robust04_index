March 30, 1990, Friday, Southland Edition

LITHUANIA'S INDEPENDENCE BID ASSAILED IN CUBA PRESS

Cuba's official Communist Party newspaper Granma said Thursday that Lithuania's bid for independence was an attempt to re-establish "bourgeois power" and crush Soviet socialism.

"Enough weeks have gone by to be able to judge without fear of making a mistake what is really happening in Lithuania," wrote Granma's Moscow correspondent Nidia Diaz.
