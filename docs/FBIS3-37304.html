Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, February 15 (SNARK)--Today there was a telephone talking between the Armenia's Foreign Minister Vahan Papazyan and the Turkish head of Foreign Office Hikmet Cetin. As SNARK was informed at the press-center of Armenia's Foreign Ministry, in the course of the talking the last events of the region were discussed. The same source said that there was an exchange of opinions on possible steps to the Karabakh conflict's settlement. Other details of this talking are not divulged.
