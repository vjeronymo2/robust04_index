930619 FT 19 JUN 93 / Finance and the Family: Halifax increases Maxim charges - At a Glance Halifax building society is raising charges on its Maxim current account but has avoided imposing charges for those in credit. From July 1, account holders who have an authorised overdraft of over Pounds 50 will have to pay a flat fee of Pounds 5 a month in addition to an interest rate of 17.5 per cent EAR. Those with an unauthorised overdraft will be charged Pounds 10 a month in addition to the interest rate currently of 28.9 per cent EAR. The transaction charge on an unauthorised overdraft will be Pounds 10 for the first transaction each month and Pounds 3 for each subsequent transaction instead of Pounds 10 per transaction.
