941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / The Labour Party in Blackpool: Howard's law and order condemned Labour accused the government of turning one of the safest countries in the world into a land plagued by crime. Mr Jack Shaw, shadow environment secretary, said yesterday: 'The so-called party of law and order has turned much of Britain into a no-go area.' He condemned Mr Michael Howard, the home secretary, over security lapses at high security prisons, mocking his claim that they would be made 'no picnic' for criminals. He said: 'As the lobster thermidor lunches at Whitemoor prison showed, in Britain's prisons today life for our notorious criminals and terrorists is not a picnic, it's a banquet.'
