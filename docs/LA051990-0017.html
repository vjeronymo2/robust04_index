May 19, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

GERRYMANDERING IN CALIFORNIA AND MERITS OF PROPS. 118, 119

Probably one of the most important votes on the June 5 ballot will be Proposition 119.

A yes vote will stop the politicians from insulating themselves from the voters by drawing safe districts, will create a commission to draw fair boundaries for legislative districts, will give freedom-loving Californians an opportunity to vote out of office politicians with whom they disagree.

Proposition 119 was written for the people for a change, not for the benefit of the politicians. For this reason you will find many politicians are working hard and putting a lot of money behind defeating it.

It's time the voting public took control of our government. Let them know they are working for us.

DODIE MURSET

Temple City
