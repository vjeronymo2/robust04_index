BFN [Text] Vientiane, April 2 (KPL) -- Paul Keating, prime minister of Australia, will visit Lao PDR [People's Democratic Republic] very soon, at the invitation of his Lao counterpart Khamtai Siphandon, according to a press release issued today by the Prime Minister's Office. The press release indicates that the official visit to Laos by the Australian prime minister will be from April 5-6.
