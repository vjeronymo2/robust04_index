931012 FT 12 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Azlan plans stock listing listing this year Azlan Group, a distributor to the network computer market, is planning to seek a listing before the end of the year. Azlan, which also provides complementary technical services including support and training, employs 180 people and conducts its activities primarily from premises in Wokingham and York. It this year commenced operations in Germany and Denmark. The company, founded in 1984, was acquired by Logitek in 1989. Two years later, Mr David Randall, who had been managing director since 1986, led a Pounds 6.5m management buy-out, supported by a group of institutional investors led by CINVen. Sales have grown by a compound 47 per cent a year over the last five years and in the year to March 31 1993 reached Pounds 41m, with pre-tax profits of Pounds 3m.
