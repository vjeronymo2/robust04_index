Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [See Selected Video Reports section for worldwide TV coverage of killing] [Excerpt] We have Ubaldo Diaz of the newspaper OVACIONES, from Tijuana. Go ahead Ubaldo. Diaz: This afternoon at 1908 Luis Donaldo Colosio, presidential candidate for the ruling Institutional Revolutionary Party [PRI], was the target of an attack. He apparently sustained two bullet wounds -- one in the head and one in the chest. The political rally was being held in the Lomas de Tijuana neighborhood, better known as Lomas Taurinas. Colosio was leading a political rally here for 3,000 persons. At the end of the rally, as he was leaving the podium, two individuals, a young person and an older one, approached him with pistols and managed to shoot the PRI candidate. [passage omitted]
