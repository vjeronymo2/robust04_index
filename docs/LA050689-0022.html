May 6, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

THE PREVIN-FLEISCHMANN CONTROVERSY

Bernheimer is at his brilliant best when he asks the basic question: Who should be the director of the Philharmonic -- a world-famous conductor-composer-performer or an autocrat who doesn't even hold an MBA degree ("Previn Wanted to Run the Philharmonic -- So Does Fleischmann," April 26)? If anyone is to go, it should be Fleischmann.

CHARLOTTE GEORGI

Culver City
