Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Workers of the Sinuiju Shoe Plant and the Tongdaewon Textile Plant held rallies on 4 January to implement thoroughly the militant tasks that the great leader Comrade Kim Il- song presented during his New Year address. Kim Hak-pong, chief secretary of the North Pyongan Provincial Party Committee and chairman of the North Pyongan Provincial People's Committee; (Kang Sun-hui), vice chairman of the Light Industry Committee; Kim Kuk-sam, vice chairman of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea Central Committee; pertinent officials; and plant workers were present at the rally of the workers of Sinuiju Shoe Plant. (Kim Chun-paek), secretary of the primary-level party committee of the plant, delivered a report at the meeting. [passage omitted] Chu Song-il, chairman of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea Central Committee; Yi Pyong-nim, vice chairman of the Light Industry Committee; (Pak In-kun), secretary of the Pyongyang Municipal People's Committee; pertinent officials; and plant workers were present at the meeting of the workers of Tongdaewon Textile Plant. (Kang Sung-yong), secretary of the primary-level party committee of the plant, delivered a report at the meeting. [passage omitted]
