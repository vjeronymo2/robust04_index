Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Following the devaluation of the CFA franc, the industry, commerce, and mines minister would like to inform the public and traders that price increases may only be effected with prior approval from the Ministry of Industry, Commerce, and Mines. Anybody found violating this measure will be prosecuted and punished since price, profit margin, and stock controls were instituted yesterday. The standardization of prices and proceeds of locally manufactured goods has also been instituted.
