Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 15 Jan (MENA)-- President Husni Mubarak this morning received a written message from the custodian of the two holy mosques, King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz of Saudi Arabia, delivered by Muhammad Ibrahim Mas'ud, Saudi state minister and King Fahd's special envoy. The meeting was attended by Saudi Ambassador in Cairo As'ad Abu- al-Sa'd.
