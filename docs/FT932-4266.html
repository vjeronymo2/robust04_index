930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / Pre-letting deal agreed in London LEO BURNETT, an advertising agency, has agreed to move offices in 18 months time to an 65,000 sq ft office building in South Kensington, London, that is being developed by John Mattson Fastighets, a Swedish investor. The deal is one of the first pre-letting agreements in London since the property industry went into recession.
