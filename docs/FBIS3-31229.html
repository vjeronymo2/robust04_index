Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] This evening President Ion Iliescu received Nicolae Andronati, first deputy prime minister of the Republic of Moldova, at Cotroceni Palace. The meeting was attended by the ministers of economy and trade of the Republic of Moldova, Sergiu Certan and Andrei Cheptene, by Marcel Dinu, secretary of state at the Romanian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and by other high-ranking state dignitaries from the two countries. During the meeting President Ion Iliescu was briefed on the proceedings of the Intergovernmental Commission. The sides also exchanged views on the stage of the national economies of the two countries. They also discussed matters pertaining to a more profound integration of their economic units, with the sides pointing out the increase in the number of joint companies, as well as financial-banking issues, Nicolae Andronati told the press at the end of the meeting.
