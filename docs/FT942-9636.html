940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Overseas Inv Overseas Investment Trust raised fully diluted net assets per share to 416.2p at March 31, against 344p a year earlier and 390.3p at September 30 1993. The undiluted figures were 426.3p, 350.8p and 390.3p respectively. After-tax earnings dropped from Pounds 452,000 to Pounds 296,000 for the half year giving earnings per share of 0.78p (1.19p). The interim dividend is maintained at 0.85p.
