Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The POSTFACTUM agency, referring to sources close to the leadership of the IMF, has circulated a report that talks between the IMF leadership and the Russian Government have reached an impasse. The report also says that the IMF leadership is examining the question of the closure of its mission in Moscow. The Moscow mission of the IMF told a Mayak correspondent that this report by the POSTFACTUM agency is misinformation. Talks between the IMF delegation and Russian representatives are continuing at present. It is expected that Michel Camdessus, the fund's managing director, will meet with Viktor Chernomyrdin, head of the Russian Government again tomorrow.
