920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Malvern UK The net asset value of Malvern UK Index Trust was 108.13p per share at the end of the 15-month period to December 31. This represented a rise of some 23 per cent on the previous period which covered the seven months to end-September 1990. Earnings per share emerged at 4.54p, against 1.84p for the previous seven months. A proposed final dividend of 2p brings the total for the 15 months to 4.5p.
