Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Former Supreme Soviet Chairman Stanislav Shushkevich in an interview to INTERFAX has spoken in favor of creating an electoral bloc of democratic forces in the republic. According to the ex-speaker, this bloc could consist of various social and political organizations, including the oppositional Belarusian Popular Front. Having suggested that he will actively participate in the formation of such a bloc, Shushkevich stated that for the time being, he does not intend to create a party of his own. It is necessary to urge early parliamentary elections, which were promised by the Supreme Soviet to the people some time ago, noted the ex-speaker.
