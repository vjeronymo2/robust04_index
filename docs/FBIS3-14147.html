Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Jordanian side, led by His Majesty King Husayn, and the Omani side, led by His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of fraternal Oman, held a meeting this afternoon and exchanged views on various current Arab and international issues. The two Arab leaders also discussed the special relations between the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan and the fraternal Sultanate of Oman. His majesty the king and His Majesty Sultan Qabus then held a closed meeting.
