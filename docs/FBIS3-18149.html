Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Switzerland's defense and foreign ministers have come out in support of Swiss participation in peacekeeping missions by United Nations blue helmet troops. Kaspar Villiger and Flavio Cotti said such participation would be in line with Switzerland's tradition of supporting peaceful solutions for political conflicts. [Defense Minister] Mr. Villiger, speaking ahead of a national vote on the issue in June, said all Swiss blue helmet personnel would take part on a purely voluntary basis. He said the Swiss contingent would only take part in noncombat missions, that all parties in a conflict would have to agree to a UN deployment, and that Swiss troops could be withdrawn at any time.
