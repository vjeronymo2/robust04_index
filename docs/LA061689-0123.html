June 16, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

SHARP TAKES OVER AS MAGNOLIA COACH

John Sharp, who coached Magnolia High School's junior varsity baseball team to an Orange League championship this year, has been named the school's varsity coach, said Bob Rishel, Magnolia athletic director.

Sharp, who will be a walk-on coach, replaces Paul Daly, now an assistant baseball coach at Fullerton College. Sharp has coached the Magnolia junior varsity for two years.
