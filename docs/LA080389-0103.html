August 3, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

CALTECH GETS $1 MILLION FOR SCIENCE FELLOWSHIPS

The Weingart Foundation has donated $1 million to Caltech to establish the Dr. Lee A. DuBridge Postdoctoral Prize Fellows Award Fund, which will be used for postdoctoral fellowships in physical sciences research. Each award will provide a stipend of $30,000 a year for three years. DuBridge served as president of Caltech from 1946 to 1969.
