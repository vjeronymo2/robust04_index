940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Rubicon makes Pounds 4.8m acquisition Rubicon Group has made one acquisition and two disposals. It has conditionally agreed to buy Beeley Wood Holdings, a manufacturer of safes for automated teller machines, for Pounds 4.84m. This acquisition will complement High Speed Production (Holdings), the precision metal components and assemblies company, which Rubicon bought in July 1993 for Pounds 9m. At the same time it is shedding some of its core shop fitting operations with the sale of Chiltern Retail Systems, which makes moving belt check-out systems, to Wagon Industrial Holdings for Pounds 4m.
