BFN [Text] Phnom Penh 14 Jun (AKP) -- Mr. Chin Siat Yoon, Singaporean ambassador to Cambodia, emphasized the need to faciliate the investments of Singaporean companies in Cambodia. Singapore's diplomat was received by Samdech Hun Sen, second prime minister of the Royal Government, in Phnom Penh on 10 June. Bilateral cooperation and especially Singapore's investments in Cambodia were at the center of the discussions. Mr. Chin Siat Yoon disclosed the commitment of his government to help Cambodia achieve economic development. "Cambodia must strengthen its cooperation with Singapore, declared Samdech Hun Sen, particularly in the construction of an airport and a port." Scholarships on management training will be given by Singapore to future personnel of these projects. The second prime minister informed his guest of the current situation in Cambodia, assuring him that the government is capable of maintaining security in the country.
