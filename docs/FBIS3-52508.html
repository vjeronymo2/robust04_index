Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Jammu and Kashmir governor, General K.V. Krishna Rao, says security forces are maintaining a strict vigil all along the Line of Control to foil any attempts to push more militants into the state from across the border. Speaking at a high-level meeting in Jammu yesterday, he said that the pressure on militants is continuing, and security forces are achieving continuous successes against the militants in the Kashmir Valley, as also in Doda District of Jammu region. Gen. Rao said people are also extending their full cooperation to the security forces in their anti-insurgency operations in the state.
