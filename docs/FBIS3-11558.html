Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina today said in Hanoi that Vietnam is a nation of daring people and that within that virtue the children are also capable of challenging the U.S. economic, financial, and trade blockade against Cuba. Robaina is paying an official visit to Vietnam during which he met with several Vietnamese officials, including Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet and Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam. In a solemn ceremony at the fatherland's building, Robaina handed over awards to the three children winners of the "On the Way to Cuba" contest sponsored by the Vietnamese Pioneers organization. Robaina said that together with the tremendous material value of the rice and school supplies donated by the Vietnamese people to Cuba, this Asian country is an endless source of incentive with its work and spirit of sacrifice.
