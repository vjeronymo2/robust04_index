BFN [Text] Panmunjom, June 28 (YONHAP) -- Seoul proposed exchange visits between South and North Korean capitals in arranging inter-Korean summit sometime mid-July at preparatory contact Tuesday, informed sources said. South Korean Deputy Premier Yi Hong-ku, chief representative for the Seoul side, proposed that the leaders of the two Koreas exchange visits between Seoul and Pyongyang for mutuality. Seoul reportedly suggested July 25-26 for the summit date and showed flexibility on which city should host first as long as the summit is arranged next month. North Korea's top delegate Kim Yong-sun is said to have proposed an August meeting in Pyongyang.
