931018 FT 18 OCT 93 / Downturn in exports 'offset' caused almost entirely by downturn in western Europe - has been offset by improving home markets, the third-quarter survey of the nine north-west England chambers of commerce says. The chambers warn, however, that any recovery is 'still quite delicate' and next month's Budget must support it by avoiding 'any measures which might put further economic improvement at risk'.
