Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Veronika Romanenkova] [Text] Plesetsk (Arkhangelsk Oblast), 2 Mar -- The automatic universal orbital station "KoronaS-I" was launched from the Plesetsk cosmodrome toward the sun by Russian Federation Military Space Forces at 0625 Moscow standard time. This satellite will help scientists conduct studies of the sun.
