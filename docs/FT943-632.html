940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Wiggins reduces loss Wiggins Group, the property developer, reduced pre-tax losses from Pounds 3.88m to Pounds 1.58m in the year to March 31 after exceptional costs of Pounds 971,000 against Pounds 2.29m. Wiggins also announced the sale for Pounds 1m of six acres of the 175-acre Kent International Business Park, of which 30 acres are owned and the remainder optioned by the group. Turnover in the period was a minimal Pounds 28,000 (Pounds 1.41m). Losses per share worked through much lower at 0.7p (24.4p).
