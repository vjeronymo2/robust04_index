BFN [Text] Georgian formations eliminated an Abkhazian reconnaissance group of five men in the Kodor Ravine, the Georgian Defense Ministry told Interfax. Georgia has not sustained any losses. According to the same source, helicopters evacuated about 60 civilians from the high-mountain villages of Abkhazian Svanetia (the Kodor Ravine). Since fighting began in the Kodor Ravine last March, more than a thousand Georgians have abandoned their native villages. No information about the situation in the Kodor Ravine has arrived from the Abkhazian Defense Ministry yet.
