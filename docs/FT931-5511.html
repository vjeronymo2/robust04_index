930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / International Capital Markets: Canadian securities industry sees profit fall The Canadian securities industry saw operating profits fall 10 per cent to CDollars 676m in 1992, Reuter reports from Toronto. The Investment Dealers Association of Canada said total revenues for the Canadian securities industry rose to CDollars 3.4bn from CDollars 3.1bn. Canadian dealers earned an average 18 per cent return on shareholders' equity. Bethlehem Steel has sold 5m shares of Dollars 3.50 cumulative convertible preferred stock at Dollars 50 per share, Reuter reports. The private placement was arranged by Salomon Bros and JP Morgan Securities. The shares are convertible into Bethlehem common stock. Visa International said its market share in the Dollars 880bn worldwide market for all purpose cards rose to 52 per cent in 1992 from 51.5 per cent in 1991, Reuter reports. Worldwide card sales volume rose by 15.8 per cent to Dollars 457.9bn in 1992, while the number of Visa cards issued rose by 9.5 per cent to more than Dollars 304.3m.
