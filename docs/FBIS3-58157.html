Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Everybody agrees that there is a need to reexamine the issue of the presence, and particularly the role, of the blue helmets in Bosnia. [French] Foreign Minister Alain Juppe, who wants NATO member countries to discuss this in Brussels on Monday [10 January], met today in Paris with the mediators, Owen and Stoltenberg, who have for months now been leading with difficulty the peace negotiations on the former Yugoslavia. They all agree that if a negotiated solution seems impossible, conclusions should be drawn and the current peacekeeping operation should be reexamined.
