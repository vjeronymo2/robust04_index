Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Summary] "Dear friends: At the invitation of President Le Duc Anh of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, Mongolian President Punsalmaagiyn Ochirbat and his wife arrived in Hanoi for an official visit to Vietnam. Accompanying the president were Excellencies Tserenpiliyn Gombosuren, minister of foreign relations; (Sanin Yamborger), chairman of the parliamentary Justice Committee; Tsebegmidiyn Tsogt, minister of trade and industry; and other high-ranking Mongolian officials. "A solemn state reception was held for the Mongolian president, his wife, and entourage at the Presidential Palace Square. Greeting the Mongolian honorable guests with President Le Duc Anh were: Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam; Head of the President's Office Nguyen Viet Dung; Ha Manh Tri, chairman of the National Assembly's Justice Committee; and Le At Hoi, chairman of the Hanoi People's Committee. Also present were other high ranking officials and representatives of foreign missions in Hanoi." The Mongolian president and entourage arrived at 1100 local time. He was invited by President Le Duc Anh to inspect the honor guard and meet the guests. "After the reception ceremony, President Le Duc Anh and President Ochirbat held a friendly meeting. President Le Duc Anh delivered a speech welcoming the Mongolian president, his wife, and the other Mongolian guests to Vietnam. President Ochirbat thanked President Le Duc Anh for his invitation and for the solemn reception. The president also expressed his wish for his delegation to learn Vietnam's renovation experience through this visit."
