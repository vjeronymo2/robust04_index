940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: FBD falls to IPounds 5.4m FBD Holdings, the Dublin-based insurance and financial services group, saw pre-tax profits fall from IPounds 7.3m to IPounds 5.44m (Pounds 5.36m) in the first half of 1994. Underwriting losses fell from IPounds 3.53m to IPounds 233,000. Insurance activities contributed IPounds 4.6m (IPounds 6.6m) after realised losses of IPounds 1.63m (IPounds 2.57m gains) on investments. Earnings per share dropped to 8.89p (11.25p) and the interim dividend has been stepped up to 2.575p (2.3p).
