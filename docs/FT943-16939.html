940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Fairbriar Fairbriar reported a pre-tax profit of Pounds 6.99m for the 12 months to December 31 1993, which the property development group attributed to an exceptional item of Pounds 10.4m, representing a write-off of creditors under a voluntary arrangement within its reconstruction terms. Sales in the year were Pounds 11.8m and earnings per share were 39.28p. In the previous nine-month period a pre-tax loss of Pounds 8.37m was incurred on sales of Pounds 5.08m. The deficit per share was 73.35p.
