Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA February 1 -- A delegation of the Ministry of Education and Training led by its Deputy Minister Tran Chi Dao has recently visited Czech and Slovakia. On Jan. 26, the Vietnamese deputy minister and Mr. J. Vodika, minister of Labour and Social Affairs of Czech signed an agreement on training jobs for Vietnamese and protocol on the implementation of the above-said agreement for 1994. Another agreement was signed by Mr. Dao and Mrs. O. Kentosava, minister of labour and social affairs of Slovakia, for the same purpose. Under these agreements, some 250 Vietnamese will attend classes at schools for job training in Czech and Slovakia this year.
