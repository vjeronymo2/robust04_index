Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Combatants of the 41st Sarallah Army of the Islamic Revolution Guards Corps' [IRGC] ground forces carried out a successful operation during which six drug convoys were destroyed in Kerman's Anduhjerd region and in the hard to reach heights. The IRGC public relations office reports that during these operations, which were code named "The Birth of Mahdi, May God Hasten His Advent," the IRGC's 41st Sarallah Army engaged in several hours of clashes with drug smugglers and disbanded the convoys of 'Isa, Javad, and Shamshir Shahbakhsh and also inflicted heavy blows on three other convoys that belonged to the Shahbakhshi's. The report adds that the operations were supported by the Kerman Army Air Corps and that six of the traffickers were killed and 10 were wounded in the clashes. Also, during the operations 32 sacks of drugs weighing 241 kg were found and five vehicles, 30 motorbikes, and 40 light weapons and ammunition were confiscated.
