Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, January 10 (TURAN)--The Defense Ministry of Azerbaijan says that yesterday's fighting in the front continues. Units of Armenian Armed Forces are attempting to intercept the initiative. Last morning they launched an offensive towards the north of the city of Agdam. Earlier they bombarded positions of the Azeri Army from "Grad" power-plants. The Armenian's attack was repelled with return firing. Having sustained losses, Armenian units retreated to their positions. Fighting in the southern section of the front continues. The Azeri Army reached the Goradiz-Fizuli Highway. Yesterday the Armenians abandoned a number of important positions and Azeri Army units were able to reach the city of Fizuli. The enemy has withdrawn from the village of Lulasaz and a predominant hill-top in the northern section of the frontline. The fighting near the Sarsang Reservoir continues. For the past two days the Armenian Army lost 3 tanks, 2 BMPs and approximately 50 soldiers, say military sources.
