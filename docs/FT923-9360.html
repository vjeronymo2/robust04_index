920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / Israel attacks refugee camp Israeli helicopter gunships yesterday attacked Palestinian guerrilla positions in south Lebanon, wounding four people, security sources said, Reuter reports from Sidon. The air raid was the fourth attack on guerrilla targets in Lebanon in four days. The sources said three helicopter gunships fired eight air-to-surface missiles at the refugee camp of Rashidiyeh in Tyre, 85km south of Beirut, drawing heavy anti-aircraft fire.
