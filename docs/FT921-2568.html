920319 FT 19 MAR 92 / French industrial production rise French industrial production rose by 1.9 per cent inJanuary from December, but the output index was just 0.4 per cent higher than in January 1991, according to seasonally adjusted data from national statistics office INSEE, Reuter reports from Paris. The fall in December production compared with November was revised to 1.2 per cent from 0.9 per cent. That represented a 2.6 per cent increase over December 1990. INSEE pointed to a 1 per cent rise in manufacturing output in January over the average index level of the last three months of 1991 as evidence of a pick-up in activity.
