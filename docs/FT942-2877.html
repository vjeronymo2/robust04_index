940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / Lamont warns on Europe president Mr Norman Lamont, former Tory chancellor, warned yesterday that if Mr Jean-Luc Dehaene, Belgian prime minister, became European Commission president it would give impetus to the federalist cause. He called on Mr John Major to maintain the government's support for Sir Leon Brittan, who was Britain's 'excellent candidate' with a brilliant negotiating track record.
