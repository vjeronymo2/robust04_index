940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / UK Company News: MTL buys Telematic MTL Instruments has acquired Telematic Systems for up to Pounds 2.4m. An initial payment of Pounds 1.2m will be satisfied by Pounds 400,000 cash, Pounds 400,000 in shares and Pounds 400,000 in loan notes. There will be a further turnover-related payment of up to Pounds 1.2m in loans notes. In the year to the end of March, Telematic made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 5,000 (Pounds 67,000) on turnover of Pounds 893,000 (Pounds 1.04m). Net assets at the period end were Pounds 287,000.
