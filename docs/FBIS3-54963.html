Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to a report by the press center of the Russian military space forces, on 8 January a Soyuz TM-18 rocket complex will be launched from the Baykonur cosmodrome. The crew consists of the commander, Colonel Viktor Afanasyev, the on-board engineer, Yuriy Usachev, and Doctor (Valeriy Polyakov). The program of the flight has been arranged in such a way that Viktor Afanasyev and Yuriy Usachev will spend 179 days in orbit, and Doctor (Valeriy Polyakov) is to spend 429 days in orbit -- no one has spent that long of time in space before.
