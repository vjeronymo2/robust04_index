BFN [Text] The National Electoral Commission has held a meeting in Accra with all the registered political parties in connection with the provision of identity cards for Ghanaian voters. The political parties agreed that identity cards with thumbprint and special security marks should be provided for all voters throughout the country. However, in order to tackle the problem of impersonation in the big towns, photo identities will be provided in all the 10 regional capitals. The political parties also agreed that failure to raise adequate funds to undertake the exercise photo identities should be provided in only four selected urban centers. [sentence as heard]
