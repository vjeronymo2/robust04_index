920926 FT 26 SEP 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Pepper prices continued to rise following the firmer trend in almost all countries of origin reports Man Production. Especially white pepper has become more expensive on shipment from indonesia at about usd 1475/1500 cif. Since available stocks in Europe are small the price of spot goods is about usd 100/mt higher. Black pepper prices have remained very steady as well. Demand,if any, was mainly for ready goods priced at about usd 1100/mt ex warehouse. Offers on shipment basis at 950/1000 cif were mostly neglected.
