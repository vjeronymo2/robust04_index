BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 12 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received gifts from the participants in the international chuche idea seminar on independence, peace and friendship. The gift book "Kimilsongism, Banner of Independence, Peace, Progress and Prosperity" was handed to the head of the delegation of the Korean social scientists staying in India by Vishwanath, secretary general of the "International Kim Il-song Prize" Council and secretary general of the Chuche Idea Study Society of India, on April 10. Gifts of the Asian Regional Institute of the Chuche Idea, the Italian delegate Dino Fiorot and the British delegate Keith Bennet were handed on the same day.
