RELEVANT ACT SECTIONS: Order requested under section 6(c) of the Act for an exemption from sections 2(a)(32), 2(a)(35), 18(f)(1), 18(g), 18(i), 22(c) and 22(d) of the Act and rule 22c&hyph;1 thereunder.

SUMMARY OF APPLICATION: Applicants request an order that would permit the Funds to issue multiple classes of shares representing interests in the same portfolio of securities and to assess, and under certain circumstances waive, a contingent deferred sales charge (``CDSC''). The order would supersede a prior order (``Prior Order'') and would permit the Funds to impose CDSC schedules that may be different from the one described in the Prior Order.
