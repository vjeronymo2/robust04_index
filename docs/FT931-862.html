930327 FT 27 MAR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Nutmeg and mace prices were unchanged this week, but can be expected to come under pressure from the peak harvest season in Indonesia, reports Man-producten. Bwp spot Dollars 800 a tonne, shipment Dollars 700; shrivels spot Dollars 1,200, shipment Dollars 1,025; abcd spot Dollars 1,350, shipment Dollars 1,150. Cassia unchanged, but ramadan ends next week, and Indonesian farmers will start cutting again. Madagascar cloves Dollars 770 cif, Dollars 850 spot; Zanzibar cloves spot Dollars 900. Mexico pimento unchanged at Dollars 1,700 spot, shipment Dollars 1,650. Jamaica spot easier at Dollars 2,175, shipment Dollars 2,140 and Guatemalan spot Dollars 1,900.
