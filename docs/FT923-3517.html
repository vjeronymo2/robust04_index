920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Severfield-Reeve dives into the red Severfield-Reeve said that unrealistic pricing levels within the construction industry had forced the structural steelwork group into the red in the first half. For the six months to June 30 a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 398,000 was reported on sales of Pounds 8.66m. That compared with a profit of Pounds 505,000 on sales of Pounds 8.84m. Losses per share amounted to 2.65p (earnings 3.24p) and the USM-quoted group is passing its interim dividend - 1p was paid previously.
