940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / World Cup '94: Second round almost complete Fourteen teams have now booked second-round berths: Romania, Switzerland, the US, Brazil, Sweden, Germany, Spain, Argentina, Mexico, Ireland, Italy, Belgium, Holland and Saudi Arabia. The last slots will be confirmed today, at the end of first-round action. Second round: July 2: Switzerland vs Spain (Washington, 9.30pm; all times British Summer Time). July 2: Germany vs a 3rd-place finisher (Chicago, 6pm). July 3: Romania vs a 3rd-place finisher (Los Angeles, 9.30pm). July 3: Saudi Arabia vs Sweden (Dallas, 6pm). July 4: Brazil vs a 3rd-place finisher (San Francisco, 8.30pm). July 4: Holland vs Ireland (Orlando, 5pm). July 5: Group D winners vs a 3rd-place finisher (Boston, 6pm). July 5: Mexico vs Group D runners-up (New Jersey, 9.30pm). Quarter-finals: weekend of July 9-10, in Boston, Dallas, New Jersey and San Francisco; semi-finals: Wednesday, July 13 (New Jersey and Los Angeles); final: Sunday, July 17 (Los Angeles).
