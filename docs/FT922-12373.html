920418 FT 18 APR 92 / Taiwan announces deposit rules Taiwan's Finance Ministry published regulations governing the issue of global depository receipts (GDRs) by Taiwanese companies yesterday, paving the way for China Steel's Dollars 360m (Pounds 203m) international share offering to be made by July this year, writes Luisetta Mudie in Taipei. The use of GDRs is the first by a Taiwanese company, and constitutes the next stage in the privatisation of China Steel, which is 88 per cent government-owned. Fifteen per cent of China Steel's shares will be floated.
