920429 FT 29 APR 92 / International Company News: USBancorp up slightly to Dollars 1.7m USBancorp said first-quarter net income rose to Dollars 1.7m, or 53 cents a share, from Dollars 1.6m, or 52 cents, in the year-ago quarter, Bloomberg reports. Net interest income rose to Dollars 8.7m from Dollars 8.2m. Net loans as of March 31 rose to Dollars 677.5m from Dollars 439.8m. Deposits rose to Dollars 994.1m from Dollars 663.7m. Non-performing assets rose to Dollars 9m from Dollars 4.1m, and total assets rose to Dollars 1.1bn from Dollars 757.7m.
