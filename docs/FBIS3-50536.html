Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The people of Pinar del Rio Province have sent Commander in Chief Fidel Castro a message reaffirming their commitment to further the development of the Revolution and the preservation of the accomplishments of socialism. The letter was read by Fidel Ramos, first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba [PCC] in Pinar del Rio, during the plenum of the provincial PCC Central Committee. This occasion, in addition to being a forum for analysis of the province's 1993 performance, was the right moment to read the message sent to the province by Army General Raul Castro. Raul congratulated the people of Pinar del Rio for their work and dedication during the 35th anniversary year of the triumph of the Revolution.
