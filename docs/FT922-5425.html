920530 FT 30 MAY 92 / Afghan leader under fire Afghanistan's new president escaped unhurt yesterday when a rocket hit the airliner flying him back to Kabul from a visit to Pakistan, agencies report from Kabul. The aircraft's nose cone was blasted away and one pilot was injured, but the other pilot got the aircraft down safely. Airport guards believe a rocket was fired from on or behind a hill overlooking the runway as the aircraft carrying President Sibghatullah Mojadidi was at a height of 300ft.
