September 10, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Toyota Hikes Prices: Toyota Motor Corp. said 1991-model prices will rise by 2.3%, or about $318, from 1990. The 1991 models include new standard equipment, such as larger tires, full wheel covers and dual manual remote mirrors. The prices of the least expensive Camry will rise 2.3% to $11,948 and the least expensive Corolla 2.1% to $8,998.
