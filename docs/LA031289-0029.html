March 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TV VIEWERS RISE UP TO DEFEND THE 'MARRIED' LIFE

Rakolta, the one-woman bombardier, does not speak for my four-member traditional family. We have watched the show religiously (irony intended).

Those cynically inclined might suspect the lady of being in the employ of the show she attacks. The hilarious show needed more attention than the fledgling FOX would normally get. Voila, it has found it.

FLOYD A. OLIVER

Los Angeles
