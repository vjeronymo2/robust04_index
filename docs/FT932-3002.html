930616 FT 16 JUN 93 / UK Company News: High Gosforth Park losses cut to Pounds 21,000 Pre-tax losses at High Gosforth Park, operator of the Newcastle racecourse, were more than halved - from Pounds 46,000 to Pounds 21,000 - in the 1992 year. Last year there was an exceptional charge of Pounds 60,000. The outcome was struck on turnover up Pounds 99,000 to Pounds 1.13m. However, the cost of sales increased by 13 per cent from Pounds 892,000 to Pounds 1.01m and at the operating level there was a loss of Pounds 62,000 against Pounds 29,000 last time. Losses per share came out at 27.7p (82.9p) and there is no dividend this year - last year a final of 15p was paid.
