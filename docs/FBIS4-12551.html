BFN [Text] A session of talks between the Syrian side, led by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', and the Austrian side, led by Austrian Foreign Minister Alois Mock, was held at the Foreign Ministry building this evening. Attending on the Syrian side were Hani Habib, head of the Economic Desk, and Muwaffaq Nassar, head of the West Europe Desk. Attending on the Austrian side were (Alexander Kristiani), head of the Middle East Desk at the Austrian Foreign Ministry, and Robert Karas, Austria's ambassador in Damascus. Talks dealt with the international situation, the situation in the region, developments in the peace process, bilateral relations, and means to develop them in various fields.
