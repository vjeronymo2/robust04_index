May 3, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

TRIO COMMITS TO CAL LUTHERAN

Three high school basketball players said Wednesday that they have made oral commitments to play for Cal Lutheran.

They are: Hank Hou, a 5-foot-10 guard from Marshall High who averaged more than 18 points a game and was a three-year starter.

Kevin Richberg, a 6-2 guard who averaged 15 points and was first team all-league for Moreau High in the Bay Area.

Joe Cohen (6-4), a two-year varsity starter for Woodbridge High in Irvine.
