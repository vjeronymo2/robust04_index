September 4, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

UCI BEATS LOYOLA IN SOCCER, 3-0

Kevin Smith scored a pair of first-half goals to help UC Irvine to a 3-0 victory over Loyola Marymount Sunday in a nonconference men's soccer match at Irvine. It was the season opener for both teams.

John Gunderson added a second-half goal.
