Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 19 (XINHUA) -- The Bank of Tokyo of Japan recently decided to provide 960,000 yen as scholarships for two institutes in Beijing. This morning a ceremony was held for signing an agreement on the scholarship, attended by Beijing Mayor Li Qiyan and president of the Bank of Tokyo. The two institutes are Beijing Institute of Economics and Beijing Institute of Finance and Trade. The annual scholarship will be granted to 32 students from the two institutes. Li Qiyan noted that this year marks the 15th anniversary of the establishment of sister relationship between Beijing and Tokyo. He said that the scholarship set by the Bank of Tokyo will certainly promote the friendly relations between the two cities. He also expressed welcome for the Bank of Tokyo to set up office in the Chinese capital.
