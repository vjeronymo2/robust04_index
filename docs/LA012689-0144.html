January 26, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

The projected sale of Turner Broadcasting System's CNN Building in Atlanta for $170 million has encountered a hitch. A letter of intent for an acquisition of the office-hotel-shopping complex by 66 Properties Inc., which was announced last Nov. 7, "expired without consummation," Turner said. However, Paul D. Beckham, senior vice president of finance and administration, said talks with 66 Properties have been continuing and Turner still intends to sell the building "on the appropriate terms."
