BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, June 11 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The Mongolian civil aviation "Miat" company has set new air route flights to Kazakhstan and Singapore. The air flights on the route to Almaty from Ulaanbaatar with a stop-off in the Bayanulgii in the western part of the country where are residing mainly Mongolian Kazakhs, are to be functioning every Thursday. What about Singapore, the Miat is to perform charter flights in accordance with the agreement concluded between the Mongolian State Tourist "Juulchin" company and Singapore's tourist "Universal Travellers" company.
