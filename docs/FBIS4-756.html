BFN [Text] From news just coming in, the head of state, General Sani Abacha, has ordered the immediate release of the three NEWSWATCH editors, Ray Ekpu, Yakubu Mohamed, and Dan Agbese. According to a statement just reaching us from the office of the chief press secretary to the head of state, the order was given after representations to the government by newspaper proprietors, the Nigerian Press Organization, and the NUJ [Nigerian Union of Journalists] on the media press briefing of Gen. Abacha in Abuja today. According to the statement, the order marks a new beginning in government-media relations built on mutual respect, understanding, and the pursuit of positive national goals and objectives.
