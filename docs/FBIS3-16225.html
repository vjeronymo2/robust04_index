Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Daugavpils, Latvia, Mar 14, BNS -- The Russian army Command and the Latvian Daugavpils region administration signed the handover-takeover papers of a former Soviet military airfield at Lociki. The parties agreed on all paragraphs of the document except on the material value of the airfield compound. They decided to view the issue separately. The Russian military persons assess the total value of all installations at 9 billion Russian rubles, or around 5.2 million U.S. dollars. Latvia, however, prices the compound at 4 billion Russian rubles, or 2.3 million dollars. The sums have to be coordinated by the Northwest Army Group command and the Latvian Finance Ministry. The local authorities claim that the Lociki airfield was the last Russian military installation in the Daugavpils region.
