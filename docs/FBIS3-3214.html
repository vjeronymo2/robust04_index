Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 16 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice Premier Zou Jiahua met here this evening with Anand Panyarachun, former prime minister of Thailand, and his party. Zou noted that Sino-Thai relations in every field have been good, adding that China welcomes entrepreneurs from Thailand to invest here. He said that with its fast economic growth, China has a great need for energy supplies, in which its cooperation with foreign enterprises will enjoy a broad prospect. Anand, who is now chairman of Thailand's Saha Union, said that it was his firm's present and future strategy to engage in economic cooperation with China, especially in the field of power generation. Saha Union is now discussing with many Chinese cities to jointly set up power plants in the country.
