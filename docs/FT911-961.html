910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Glasgow Income net assets dip 5% As at March 31 1991, net asset value of Glasgow Income Trust amounted to 45.98p per share. The figure showed a modest decline of some 5 per cent on the 48.57p of 12 months earlier, but represented a gain of 22 per cent on the trust's year-end value of 37.64p. Net revenue for the six months to end-March rose to Pounds 436,000 (Pounds 385,000) for earnings of 1.5p (1.33p) per share. A second interim dividend of 0.6p (same) is declared making 1.2p so far this year.
