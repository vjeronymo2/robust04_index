BFN [Text] Moscow, 23 May (ITAR-TASS) -- Today in the Kremlin Russian President Boris Yeltsin held a meeting with representatives of the State Duma deputies' group "Liberal-Democratic Union of 12 December," a report by the Russian president's press service, circulated here today says. The participants in the conversation exchanged opinions on a number of topical issues in the country's political and economic life and State Duma activities. Assessing the first results of the work of the lower chamber of the Russian Federal Assembly, Boris Yeltsin expressed concern about its insufficiently high effectiveness. Incidentally, the Russian president expressed confidence that the Duma will continue to raise the effectiveness of its activities. The Russian president stressed that for his part he firmly intends to continue the course toward constructive cooperation with the Federal Assembly. During the meeting a great deal of attention was paid to a package of "economic" decrees signed by the Russian president today. Deputies from "the Union of 12 December" expressed their support for the measures they contain and that are aimed at the further development of economic reforms.
