BFN ["U.S. Imperialists and South Korean Puppets Stage Surprise Strike Exercise and Gunfiring Against North" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, April 2 (KCNA) -- The U.S. imperialists and the South Korean puppets on March 31 hurled fighter planes of various types including F-15s and FA-18s into the skies above Nyongwol, Wonju and Chunchon and staged an exercise of surprise strike at targets in the midst of electronic jamming, according to military sources. In separate action that day, large-calibre artillery groups and heavily-armed bandits of the puppet army took firing positions in Cholwon County, South Korean Kangwon Province, Phaju and Ryonchon Counties, Kyonggi Province, and kicked up a war frenzy, firing over 60 105mm shells and 1,000 large-calibre machinegun bullets into spots near the Demilitarized Zone along the Military Demarcation Line. On the same day, the puppets deployed scores of large-calibre guns in Cholwon County, South Korean Kangwon Province, and brought some 500 armed bandits into areas near Rinje and Ryonchon Counties. Some ten helicopter gunships and transport helicopters of the puppet army flew into the skies above Ryonchon and Phochon and Cholwon, areas under the very nose of the North side's portion for a frantic exercise of support to ground attack and war materiel airlifting.
