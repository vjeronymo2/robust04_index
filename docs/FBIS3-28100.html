Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 3 KYODO -- Japan will send 8.77 million yen worth of emergency relief materials to Myanmar [Burma] for the victims of a large fire in Yangon [Rangoon] last month, the Foreign Ministry said Thursday [3 February]. The government will send the goods, which include blankets, sleeping mats and soap, through the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA), at the request of the Myanmar government, the ministry said. A large fire broke out in Yangon on January 24, burning more than 900 houses and affecting over 6,530 people, it said.
