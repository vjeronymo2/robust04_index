940523 FT 23 MAY 94 / Sport: Main events TODAY:The French Open begins its two-week run in Paris. Steffi Graf, who has won four straight grand slams and should again sweep through the women's singles. Her path is clear of young challengers. Jennifer Capriati (18) entered drug rehabilitation last week and even though Monica Seles (20) seems to have recovered physically from her stab wounds, she shows little interest in returning to the sport. SUNDAY: Formula One motor racing resumes its gloomy season with the Spanish Grand Prix at Jarama, after the deaths of Ayrton Senna and Roland Ratzenberger at Imola in Italy and Karl Wendlinger's crash in Monte Carlo. The F1 teams are struggling to modify their complicated cars to meet new safety rules by Sunday. The comparatively low-tech US Indycar teams have no such worries as they prepare for the main event of their season. A crowd of 400,000 will gather at the Brickyard for the Indianapolis 500, one of America's great traditional sports events.
