As a result of the founding of the Korean People's Revolutionary Forces, our people's long-cherished desire to have their own powerful armed forces was fulfilled brilliantly, and, from that time, the proud history of the heroic KPA started. Our revolutionary forces have walked along an honorable road of struggle since declaring their birth throughout the world. They were also brilliant annals in which it has brought about immortal achievements for the fatherland and the nation and the era and the revolution.
