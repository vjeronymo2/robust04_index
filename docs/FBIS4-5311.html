BFN [Text] Hanoi, April 8 (BTA) -- A Bulgarian Government economic delegation paid a visit to Vietnam at the invitation of Vietnam's Government on April 2 through 7. The delegation was led by First Deputy Agriculture Minister Rumen Khristov who chairs the Bulgarian side of a Bulgarian-Vietnamese Intergovernmental Committee on Economic, Trade, Scientific and Technical Cooperation, the Bulgarian Foreign Ministry said today. The Bulgarian Government officials attended the 15th session of the joint committee and met their Vietnamese counterparts, headed by Vietnam's Deputy Minister of Agriculture and Food Processing Industry, to discuss the prospects of promoting the bilateral cooperation in the 1994-95 period, the Foreign Ministry also said.
