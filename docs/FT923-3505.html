920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Acquisitions and exports lift PCT A good export performance, together with the benefits of its acquisition programme, helped PCT Group advance 65 per cent in the first half to June 30. Pre-tax profits at the USM-quoted group jumped from Pounds 560,200 to Pounds 925,000 on sales ahead from Pounds 9.39m to Pounds 10.7m. The group is involved in the marketing, hire and development of power tools and welding equipment. The interim dividend is maintained at 2.5p on earnings per share of 7p (7.6p).
