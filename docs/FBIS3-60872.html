[passage omitted] Suffice it to say that the gang's ringleader has three criminal convictions, and one of its members was tried for rape. Oleg Soskovets also noted the courage and endurance of the pilots. The main responsibility for dealing with the gang lay with those subordinate to First Deputy Internal Affairs Minister Mikhail Yegorov. However, all the heads of oblasts and the rayons in the North Caucasus, even of those regions where a state of emergency is currently in force, took part in resolving the situation. [passage omitted]
