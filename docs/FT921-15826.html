920108 FT 08 JAN 92 / UK Company News: M&W passes Pounds 2m mark over year M&W, which operates convenience stores, cash and carry and distribution warehouses, saw its pre-tax profit rise from Pounds 1.99m to Pounds 2.15m in the year ended September 29 1991. Turnover moved up from Pounds 60.6m to Pounds 66.1m. Operating profit showed little change at Pounds 1.78m, exceptional income fell to Pounds 35,000 (Pounds 207,000) but net interest received rose to Pounds 338,000 (Pounds 37,000). Earnings were 9.21p (9.74p) and the final dividend is 1.25p for a total of 2.25p (1p).
