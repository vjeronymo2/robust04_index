September 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE JIM BAKKER SAGA

May I suggest that Garry Abrams was stretching it a little when he reported on the Jim Bakker saga? He reported that the water slide at Bakker's amusement park, Heritage USA, is still open. But come on now: The children carrying blue inner tubes to the top of the slide looked "like penitents cimbing to a shrine."

Gimme a break.

MICHELLE LENTZ, Northridge
