BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 21 April Morning Edition] [Text] With regard to the question of Japan's permanent membership on the UN Security Council [UNSC], a top Foreign Ministry official said on 20 April: "If we do not join the UNSC, there are various kinds of information that we cannot obtain. In order to play an active role, Japan should attain permanent membership on its own initiative rather than wait for other nations' recommendations." He thus indicated that with the inauguration of the new administration, Japan should demonstrate an even more positive attitude than that of the Hosokawa administration.
