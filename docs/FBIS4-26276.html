BFN [Text] Beijing, June 20 (XINHUA) -- General Xu Huizi, deputy chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, hosted a dinner in honor of P.K. Musukwa, standing secretary of the Zambian Defense Ministry, here this evening. Zambian Ambassador to China P.L. Kasanda was present at the dinner. It is learned that Musukwa and his party arrived here June 19 for an 8-day official goodwill visit to China as guests of the Chinese Defense Ministry.
