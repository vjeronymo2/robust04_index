910426 FT 26 APR 91 / International Company News: Allied-Signal ALLIED-SIGNAL, the US industrial conglomerate, saw its first-quarter earnings halved as a result of weak markets in its core automotive, aerospace and engineered materials businesses, writes Bernard Simon. Net earnings tumbled to Dollars 63m, or 47 cents a share, from Dollars 129m, or 90 cents, a year earlier. Sales fell to Dollars 2.9bn from Dollars 3.1bn.
