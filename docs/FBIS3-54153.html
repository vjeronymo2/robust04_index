Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The resistance forces detonated an explosive device under an Israeli patrol on the road between Hula and Shaqra at 0930 today. They also fired more than 10 mortars shells at the Bra'shit and Shaqra positions and later carried out an attack on them. The Israeli army bombarded Wadi al-Saluqi and the al-Litani River basin with tank guns and other heavy artillery. [passage omitted] In another development, security forces said that the Israeli forces began military exercises at al-Majidiyah Camp in the central sector of the border strip this morning. The sounds of tank fire and heavy and medium machine-gun fire from those exercises can still be heard.
