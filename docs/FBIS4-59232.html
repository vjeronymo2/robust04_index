BFN [Text] Baghdad, May 7, INA -- AL-JUMHURIYAH daily has stressed that the current events and the killing in Yemen have been arranged by the United States of America and its satellites from the Arab reactionary countries. In its editorial today, AL-JUMHURIYAH said that America wanted form the killing in Yemen to seize control the oil in the north and the south of the country at the same time and the current fighting and the plan of its expansion and rooting is a direct American act which cannot be implemented by the reactionary countries which move within the American orbit only. The paper added that the current American plan in Yemen is to preserve the formal Yemeni unity to secure the control on all Yemeni oil. It went on to say that the regretted events in Yemen were no more than implementing a general American-Zionist plan. The paper strongly denounced the double standered of the American stand towards the Yemeni unity. It also pointed out to the Saudi conspiracy against the Yemeni unity following Sanaa joining to the Arab Cooperation Council.
