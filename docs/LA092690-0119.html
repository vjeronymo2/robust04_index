September 26, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

NAME GAME: The new name on the Big A outside Anaheim Stadium -- Great Western Bank -- will cost GW at least $125,000 a year. But that's nothing like the estimated $1 million a year GW pays to have Inglewood's sports arena called the Great Western Forum. . . . Could there be a "Great Western Anaheim Stadium" in store? Both sides say it's tempting, "but the name Anaheim Stadium is the only identity the city has for its investment here," a stadium spokesman says. "If the teams were named after Anaheim, it would be different." Marcida Dodson and Jeffrey A. Perlman
