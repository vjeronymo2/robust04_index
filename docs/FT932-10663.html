930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / UK Company News: 22% rise at James Smith Estates James Smith Estates, the USM-traded property investment group, lifted pre-tax profit by 22 per cent in the year to March 24, from Pounds 1.19m to Pounds 1.45m. That was despite a provision for bad debts of Pounds 39,000 included in administrative expenses; without that those expenses rose only 2 per cent. Earnings per share moved up from 5.1p to 6.3p, and the final dividend is 2.8p for a total of 4.2p, against 3.75p. Properties have been revalued by the directors at Pounds 24.1m, a decline of 12 per cent over the year.
