921030 FT 30 OCT 92 / Parliament and Politics: More NHS trusts set for Wales A FURTHER 13 national health service trusts, covering hospitals and ambulance services, will come into operation in Wales on April 1 next year, Mr David Hunt, Welsh secretary, told the Commons yesterday. Mr Hunt said he had invited applications from 14 further hospitals, community units and ambulance services to become trusts in April 1994.
