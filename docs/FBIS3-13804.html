Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine [DFLP] regards the new U.S. attitude toward the UN Security Council resolution as a disturbing retreat from the stands adopted by previous U.S. Administrations. The front called Resolution No. 904 weak and colorless with no firm decisions on protecting the Palestinian people. The Islamic Resistance Movement, Hamas, rejected the resolution and considered it insufficient and incomplete. Hamas voiced its disappointment with the stands of the Arab sides, including that of the PLO.
