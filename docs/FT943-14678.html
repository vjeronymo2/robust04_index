940713 FT 13 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Real Time Control well ahead Real Time Control, which develops software and supplies and services systems for epos applications, announced a jump in pre-tax profits from Pounds 492,000 to Pounds 1.39m in the year to March 31. The result was achieved on turnover up 40 per cent at Pounds 8.53m (Pounds 6.11m) and included interest receivable of Pounds 240,000 (Pounds 309,000). Earnings per share advanced from 4.9p to 13.3p and the proposed final dividend is lifted to 4p (3p). The company's shares trade on the USM.
