Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Mar (MENA)--Sudanese Foreign Minister Dr. Husayn Sulayman Abu-Salih has stated that the issue of supporting Sudan and preserving its territorial integrity has occupied a prominent place in discussions at the meeting of the Arab foreign ministers today. In a statement he made today on the sideline of the Arab League Council meeting, Abu-Salih said that he held bilateral consultations with both the Iraqi and Kuwaiti foreign ministers. He added that his meeting with the Kuwaiti foreign minister is the first such meeting since the Gulf war, noting that this is a good gesture to clear the atmosphere and achieve inter-Arab reconciliation.
