BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Vladimir Kazimirov, the Russian special representative in Nagorno-Karabakh, has arrived in Turkey for talks. On arrival at Ankara Esenboga Airport, Kazimirov said he will hold talks at the technical level, within the framework of consultations being held regarding the issue of Nagorno-Karabakh, concerning finding a solution to the problem. He said he did not bring a special message to Turkey.
