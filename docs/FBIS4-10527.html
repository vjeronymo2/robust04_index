BFN [Text] Gunfire was exchanged during the night of 7 April between armed strangers and soldiers at the outpost of Borgne in northwest Haiti, sources reported in Port-au-Prince on 12 April. According to the sources, two military detachments from Cap-Haitien, 260 km north of Port-au-Prince, were sent to the area. Neither side suffered casualties. The unidentified attackers are being sought, according to a Haitian military source. Weapons of unrevealed nature and number were abandoned by the attackers, according to the same source. A similar incident took place the night of 2 February in Chantal, near Les Cayes, 200 km south of Port-au-Prince. Two M-1 rifles abandoned on that occasion by the attackers were recovered by soldiers.
