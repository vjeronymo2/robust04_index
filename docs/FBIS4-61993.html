BFN [Vitaliy Denisov report from Tbilisi under the "News" rubric: "Georgian Guys Like Russian "Green Caps"] [Text] Conscription of Georgian personnel for the Russian border troops on Georgian territory has begun according to an agreement between Georgia and Russia on the joint protection of borders. Colonel Vladimir Berezin, deputy chief of the "Gruziya" ["Georgia"] group of forces of the Caucasus Special Border District, said that at the initial stage Georgian citizens will account for 10 percent of the group's strength -- in May-June 350 young Georgians will be conscripted into the Russian border units. In the future, the entire length of the Georgian-Turkish border will be protected by Georgian units.
