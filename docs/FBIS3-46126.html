Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Summary] Liaoning Province increased its gold output in 1993, which has topped the previous peak. By the end of last year, the province overfulfilled the annual production plan and its output showed a 7.07 percent increase over 1992. The province's output of gold finished products in the year showed a 24.53 percent increase over 1992. The province's gold production front realized 150 million yuan of profits and taxes, which showed a 580 percent increase over 1992. The volume of gold deposits newly prospected in the year reached 31.8 metric tons.
