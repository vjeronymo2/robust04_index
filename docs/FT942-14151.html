940421 FT 21 APR 94 / UK Company News: Wm Jacks recovery continues William Jacks, the car distribution, servicing and rental company, produced a turnround from pre-tax losses of Pounds 514,000 to profits of Pounds 383,000 for the year ended January 31, after lower interest payable of Pounds 501,000, against Pounds 789,000. At halfway profits amounted to Pounds 65,000 (Pounds 1,000). Turnover expanded from Pounds 38.9m to Pounds 52.4m for this Berkshire-based concern whose ultimate holding company is Johan Holdings of Malaysia. Earnings per share were 1.44p, against losses of 6.37p, while the resumption of dividends 'will be considered when the distributable reserves in the parent company have been fully restored after the losses incurred in the preceding three years', directors stated. After refurbishment and extension of freehold premises purchased in January, the Wessex Land Rover franchise in Hampshire started trading this month. Preliminary agreement has been reached with AC Cars to represent it in certain of the home counties.
