BFN [Text] Kokshetau Oblast published data on its political and social situation with regard to the first quarter of this year. Production output is declining. All rayons reduced milk production. Kzyl-Tu rayon produced 385 tonnes of milk; Zerendy -- 432 tonnes. Every 100 cows produced 26 calves, and every 100 sheep -- 73 lambs. Valikhanov, Kzyl-Tu, and Lenin rayons fulfilled only 50 percent of the state order on procurement of agricultural products. Seventy two enterprises failed to fulfill their production plans. Goods worth 128.03 million tenge have not been produced.
