930511 FT 11 MAY 93 / UK Company News: JO Walker loss cut to Pounds 402,000 JO Walker, an importer of timber, wall boards and plywood, reduced its loss in 1992 from Pounds 467,000 to Pounds 402,000. After a lower tax credit of Pounds 103,000 (Pounds 165,000) the net loss came out almost unchanged at Pounds 299,000 (Pounds 303,000), giving losses per share of 37.4p (37.8p). Turnover was Pounds 12.2m (Pounds 13.87m).
