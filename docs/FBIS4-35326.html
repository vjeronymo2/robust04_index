BFN [Text] Presidential Council Chairman Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid had a telephone conversation last night with his brother, His Highness al-Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the fraternal state of the United Arab Emirates [UAE]. They discussed the Yemeni situation and current efforts to tackle it. Brother 'Ali Salim al-bid expressed his appreciation for the sincere efforts and honest initiatives of al-Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan to stop the bloodshed and infighting between brothers and to achieve peace and stability in all parts of the Yemeni homeland. He expressed our people's feelings of pride and appreciation of His Highness al-Shaykh Zayid's stand and his good efforts to preserve Yemeni stability and security.
