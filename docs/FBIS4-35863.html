BFN [Excerpts] Elyaqim Rubinstein, chief Israeli delegate to the talks with Jordan, will leave tonight for Washington at the head of a delegation that will hold an informal meeting with the Jordanians on the border and water issues. This was reported by our political correspondent Shlomo Raz. [passage omitted] Our correspondent adds that the discussions of the tripartite Israeli-Jordanian-U.S. conference on economic issues will also be launched on 6 June. The decision to hold the encounter was made following Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres' meeting with Crown Prince Hasan at the White House. The meeting will deal with the impact of the Israeli-PLO economic agreement on Jordan. [passage omitted]
