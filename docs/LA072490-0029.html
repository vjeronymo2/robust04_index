July 24, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: EXPANDED SMOKING BAN PROPOSED FOR SCHOOLS

The Los Angeles Board of Education next week will vote on a proposal to ban smoking and other tobacco use on all district property -- including cars.

The all-encompassing ban, proposed by board President Jackie Goldberg, would apply to people at everything from sporting events to faculty lounges to meetings of non-school groups at district facilities.

"Allowing adults, but not students, to smoke gives students the message that smoking is for adults, providing a powerful incentive for children to smoke," Goldberg said in introducing the proposal.
