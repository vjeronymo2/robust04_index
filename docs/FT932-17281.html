930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News: Stewart U'writing Stewart Underwriting has acquired MFK Underwriting Agencies through the issue of new shares in a move which will make it one of the larger groups in the Lloyd's market. MFK manages three syndicates with a combined capacity of Pounds 86m and also manages the affairs of 30 direct names. The enlarged Stewart & Hughman Managing Agency will manage syndicates with more than Pounds 240m in capacity, and Stewart Members Agency will manage more than Pounds 300m of names premium income, based on 1993 figures.
