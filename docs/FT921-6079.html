920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Courtyard Leisure Courtyard Leisure, the USM-quoted restaurant and wine bar operator, has been notified by TW Consultants that, together with other shareholders 'deemed to be associates and acting in concert', it had a beneficial interest in 27.64 per cent of the issued ordinary capital. Courtyard directors are investigating this interest, including the nature of the associates and concert party relationships, and are seeking to clarify the intentions of TW Consultants.
