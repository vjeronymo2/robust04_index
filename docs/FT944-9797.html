941114 FT 14 NOV 94 / Company News This Week: Buoyant times for British insurers - Other Companies General Accident: Results for the first nine months of 1994 are expected tomorrow to show pre-tax profits of about Pounds 330m, against Pounds 206m last time, highlighting the buoyancy of the UK insurance sector. Willis Corroon, the insurance broker, is expected to produce less impressive results on Thursday. Pre-tax profits for the first nine months of about Pounds 55m are expected, down from Pounds 70m last time, largely because of problems in the US.
