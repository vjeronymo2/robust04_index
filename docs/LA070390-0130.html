July 3, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ROW THE BOAT: Orange Coast College's rowing team has yet to make the finals, failing in seven previous tries at the Henley Royal Regatta near London (C12). . . . This year, its first race is on the Fourth of July. . . . David A. Grant, OCC campus president and crew coach, says the date is coincidence: "I don't think the British have any interest in celebrating that holiday. In fact, there is some talk of black armbands." Mark I. Pinsky and Martin Beck
