Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Italian foreign minister, Professor Beniamino Andreatta, arrived in New Delhi on a three-day visit last night. He visited Rajghat this morning and laid a wreath at the samadhi [mausoleum] of Mahatma Gandhi. Professor Andreatta is to call on the external affairs minister, Mr. Dinesh Singh, and the president, Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma, today.
