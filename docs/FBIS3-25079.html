Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A military helicopter has crashed near Gulu town killing seven people. (?All those) on board including the pilot, Major William Andama, died in the crash. Three children in a house died instantly when the helicopter crashed into the house a few minutes after take-off. Entebbe Airbase Commander Lieutenant Colonel Joshua Natara said the helicopter which was on normal operation caught fire in mid-air and crashed into the house as it tried to land. The helicopter was coming to Kampala. The cause of the fire is not yet known, and experts have been despatched to the scene to carry out an investigation.
