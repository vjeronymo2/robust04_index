Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Hainan has made marked achievements in electric power development. Last year the province generated 1.9 billion kwh, an increase of 33.4 percent over the previous year. The gross output value amounted to more than 500 million yuan, an increase of 63.1 percent over the previous year. Last year electric power departments in the province made great efforts to increase electric power generation. [words indistinct] Since funding is guaranteed, the Nanshan electric power station can smoothly proceed with all its construction projects. Its first generating unit is expected to go into operation at the end of this February. To guarantee the fulfillment of state key construction projects in the province, the Dagongba hydropower station has gone into operation earlier than scheduled. Electric power departments in the province have opened up all avenues to ensure power supply. The Xiubian electric power project, with a total investment of 73.95 million yuan, was completed and put into operation last October after one year of efforts. [words indistinct]
