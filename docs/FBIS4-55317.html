BFN [Text] Warsaw, 12 May--Sejm Speaker Jozef Oleksy today received General Zhang Wannian, chief of the General Staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, a Sejm Chancellery communique reads. "While striving toward NATO and European structures' membership, we will not forget our good cooperation with China. Our countries have always respected their individual characteristics and their adopted solutions," the Sejm speaker said. General Wannian, who is in Poland at the invitation of General Tadeusz Wilecki, chief of the General Staff of the Polish armed forces, shared his impressions of the visit. He stressed the good combat training of the Polish Army and the warm reception he encountered. He expressed the hope that Polish-Chinese relations will continue and that the visit of a Sejm delegation to China, scheduled for the end of May, will strengthen these relations, the communique reads.
