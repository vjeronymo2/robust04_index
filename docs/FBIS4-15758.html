BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Grigoriy Arslanov] [Text] Beijing April 12 TASS -- Colonel General Mikhail Kolesnikov, chief of the General Staff of Russia's armed forces, who is currently here on a four-day visit, has held talks with his Chinese counterpart, Colonel-General Zhang Wannian. The two military leaders discussed the course of negotiations on a reduction in armed forces in the area of the Russian-Chinese border, confidence-building measures in the military field and matters connected with exchanges of delegations between the two countries' defence ministries. Kolesnikov set out the main provisions of Russia's military doctrine. On Tuesday Kolesnikov had a meeting with Liu Huaqing, member of the standing committee of the Political Bureau of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee and deputy chairman of China's Central Military Council. The two sides expressed satisfaction with the development of contacts between the armed forces of the two states, which promotes stronger friendship, good-neighbourliness and trust between Russia and China, and peace in the Asia-Pacific region and throughout the world. Kolesnikov visited the guards division of Beijing garrison where he familiarised himself with the combat training of Chinese servicemen. On Tuesday afternoon the Russian general left by air for Shanghai for a meeting with the command of Shanghai garrison and a tour of the one [as received] of defence-industry plant being converted to civilian production. Kolesnikov will also visit the Pudong economic development area.
