Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "This Morning" newscast] [Text] Monday, the Nicaraguan Government condemned the indiscriminate bombing of Sarajevo civilians and at the same time reiterated it categorically rejects the use of violence to settle differences in that strife-torn nation. In an official statement, the government of Violeta Barrios de Chamorro called on the warring sides in Bosnia-Herzegovina to talk so peace can return to that strife-torn region. Last Saturday, a 120-mm mortar shell killed 58 people and wounded another 142 at a Sarajevo market.
