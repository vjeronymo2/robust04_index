BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 14 (KCNA) -- A high-level military delegation of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam arrived here by air today. The delegation led by General Doan Khue, Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Vietnam and minister of national defence, consists of Lt. Gen. Phan Thu, vice-minister of national defence; Lt. Gen. Le Hai, deputy head of the General Political Department of the Vietnam People's Army; Lt. Gen. Tran Hanh, deputy chief of the General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army, and other members. It was met at the airport by O Chin-u, member of the Presidium of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and minister of People's Armed Forces; Vice Marshal of the Korean People's Army Kim Kwang-chin; Col. Gen. Pak Chae-kyong; Lt. Gen. Pak Sung-won and other generals and officers of the Korean People's Army. Vietnamese Ambassador to Korea Duong Chinh Thuc was also present there to meet the delegation.
