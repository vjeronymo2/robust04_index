September 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

LOVE OF COEUR

I have found (Jerry Hulse's) articles on western Colorado and the recent Coeur d'Alene (Aug. 6) truly wonderful to read. They capture the true atmosphere and feeling of those places. I grew up in the mountains of Colorado and get homesick for them at times. Thank you.

GERTRUDE R. PEARSON

Santa Monica
