Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Rangoon Radio Burma in Burmese at 1330 GMT on 14 March provides several reports on the activities of Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina Gonzalez during his visit to Burma. Senior General Than Shwe, chairman of the State Law and Order Restoration Council, SLORC, and commander in chief of the Defense Services, at 1000 this morning received the visiting Cuban delegation led by Mr. Roberto Robaina Gonzalez, foreign minister of the Republic of Cuba, at the Defense Ministry's Dagon House. Also present were Lieutenant General Maung Aye, deputy commander in chief of the Defense Services and Army commander in chief; SLORC Secretary-1 Lt. Gen. Khin Nyunt; SLORC Secretary-2 Lt. Gen. Tin U; Foreign Minister U Ohn Gyaw; and Protocol Department Director General Thura U Aung Htet. At 1100, the Cuban delegation paid a courtesy call on Deputy Prime Minister Vice Admiral Maung Maung Khin at the latter's office and held frank and cordial talks on matters relating to foreign investment, economic cooperation, and promoting bilateral goodwill. At 1430, the visiting delegation called on Trade Minister Lt. Gen. Tun Kyi at the Trade Ministry and held talks on bilateral economic and trade matters. At 1330, the Cuban foreign minister and delegation met and held cordial talks on social and economic matters with Burmese traders and entrepreneurs at the Union of Myanmar Chamber of Commerce and Industry on Bo Son Pet Road. The Cuban foreign minister and delegation this afternoon left Burma for Cambodia. They were seen off at the airport by Foreign Affairs Minister U Ohn Gyaw, Deputy Foreign Affairs Minister U Nyunt Swe, and other ranking officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
