BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow May 16 TASS -- Former Soviet President Mikhail Gorbachev has no chances to become the Russian president as people do not support him, according to an influential lawmaker. "Statements by Mikhail Gorbachev about his possible participation in presidential elections in 1996 are completely groundless. He has no social basis", Viktor Mironov, deputy chairman of the Duma Committee on Security told TASS on Monday. "With all my respect to the former Soviet president, I believe he has exhausted his resources of a head of state", he added.
