910419 FT 19 APR 91 / UK Company News: Mid-States soars Reflecting a full-year contribution from its principal operating subsidiary in the US, 1990 pre-tax profits at USM-quoted Mid-States, the automotive parts distributor, soared from Pounds 215,000 to Pounds 5.27m. The increase was struck on turnover almost quadrupled to Pounds 41.88m. A final dividend of 2.25p makes a total of 3p - 0.75p was paid for the nine months to December 31 1989.
