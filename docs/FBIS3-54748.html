Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Leaders of Russia's electoral blocs and political parties on Friday held a new meeting in the Kremlin to discuss candidacies to the posts of chairperson and deputy chairpersons of the State Duma, the lower house of the new parliament. Well-informed sources told Interfax the meeting was attended by Yegor Gaydar (Russia's Choice), Grigory Yavlinsky (the Yavlinsky-Boldyrev-Lukin bloc), Vladimir Zhirinovsky (the Liberal Democratic Party), Nikolay Travkin (the Democratic Party), Yekaterina Lakhova (the Union of Women of Russia), Mikhail Lapshin (the Agrarian Party), and Gennadiy Zyuganov (the Communist Party). Nobody from the Party of Russian Unity and Concord was present, however. The next meeting has been scheduled for January 4.
