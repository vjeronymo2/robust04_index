BFN [Report by Vitaliy Denisov from Tbilisi under the "News" rubric: "New Minister Starts With Reshuffles"] [Text] Lieutenant General Vardiko Nadibaidze, Georgian defense minister, has issued orders on new appointments to senior posts at the Republic's Defense Ministry. Major General Paata Datuashvili, first deputy minister, has been removed from office. He will be replaced by Maj. Gen. Guram Nikolaishvili, who has until the present day been chief of the General Staff. The General Staff, in turn, will be headed by Maj. Gen. Nodar Tatarashvili, who has until recently been in command of the Georgian peacekeeping forces in South Ossetia.
