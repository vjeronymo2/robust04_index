931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / Slovakia forms coalition A NEW coalition government has been formed in Slovakia between Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar's Movement for a Democratic Slovakia and the Slovak National Party, led by Mr Ludovit Cernak, a former economy minister, writes Patrick Blum in Vienna. The agreement follows months of wrangling over positions between the two parties after their earlier coalition collapsed last spring. It will end a period of uncertainty overshadowed by calls from the left-wing opposition for early elections.
