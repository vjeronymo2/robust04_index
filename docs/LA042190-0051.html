April 21, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

SOUTHLAND;

2 MEDFLIES FOUND OUTSIDE SPRAY AREAS

Agriculture officials announced the discovery of two Mediterranean fruit flies -- one in the city of Cerritos and the other in the San Bernardino County community of Rancho Cucamonga.

The Cerritos fly was trapped Thursday in a fig tree about an eighth of a mile outside a treatment area. The Medfly in Rancho Cucamonga was found in a grapefruit tree, also just outside a malathion treatment area.

Both flies were identified as immature females. No decision has been made whether the areas will be sprayed with malathion.
