BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 26 (KCNA) -- "President Kim Il-song who has been active as an outstanding statesman for many years, performing great exploits in the struggle for the freedom and independence of the country, is a great leader of the present time who is famous worldwide," said Pedro Margolles Villanueva, director general of the PRENSA LATINA of Cuba, who visited Korea. Giving his impressions of Korea here, he said, "When I had an audience of President Kim Il-song, I could deeply feel once again comrade president's unshakable revolutionary will to defend the cause of socialism with no retreat before the imperialists and his strong and resolute position never to pardon any invading enemy." "Under the wise guidance of the great Comrade Kim Il-song and dear Comrade Kim Chong-il, enormous achievements have been made in socialist construction of Korea and the Korean people have wrought miracles which no one else can effect over the 40 odd years since the ceasefire," he said. The director general of the PRENSA LATINA added that wonderful and magnificent grand monuments have taken shape in different parts of the country, Pyongyang has turned into the most beautiful city and all people lead a happy life with stable jobs. "Now the West is alleging that the DPRK is stepping up `war preparations.' But it is quite contrary to the fact," he said, adding that Korea is promoting peaceful construction, not war preparations.
