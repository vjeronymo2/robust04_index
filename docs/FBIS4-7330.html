BFN [Declaration No. 2/94 of the State Law and Order Restoration Council; issued on 27 April 1994] [Text] Appointment of vice chairman of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC]. The SLORC has appointed General Maung Aye, SLORC member, deputy commander in chief of the defense services, and Army commander in chief, as the vice chairman of SLORC. By the authority vested in me, Signed; Khin Nyunt, Lieutenant General, secretary-1 of the SLORC.
