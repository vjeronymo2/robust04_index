BFN [Unattributed report in the "Provincial Chronicle" column] [Text] Kazan -- A program envisioning the restoration of the USSR on "a renewed organizational-legal basis" and the republic's accession to the Union has been adopted at the first congress of the Tatarstan Communist Party. The program notes that the Tataria leadership has managed to "cushion the blows by Russian authorities on the working people by supporting the agro-industrial complex, regulating prices, crime control, and targeted social protection programs."
