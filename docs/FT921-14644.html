920115 FT 15 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Nobo cut to Pounds 62,000 in line with warning In line with forecasts and as a result of some of the poorest trading conditions in its history, Nobo Group, the office products company, reported sharply reduced interim profits, down from Pounds 1.29m to Pounds 62,000 pre-tax. The figure for the six months to October 31 was hit by a Pounds 227,000 exceptional charge for compensation to a senior director who left the group. Turnover was down 23 per cent from Pounds 12.9m to Pounds 9.92m, with office furniture being worst affected. Operating profit was down at Pounds 658,000 (Pounds 1.86m), and interest charges were reduced from Pounds 513,000 to Pounds 369,000. Earnings per share came out at 0.06p (7.93p). There is a reduced interim dividend of 1p (2.42p). The company also announced that Mr Reg Barr, executive chairman, was to become non-executive chairman.
