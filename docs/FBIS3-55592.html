Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Zaytsev] [Text] Delhi, 5 Jan -- President Islam Karimov of Uzbekistan left for home today on completion of a three-day official visit to India. The visit by the president of Uzbekistan, which is being described here as "most successful and productive," was crowned with the signing of an agreement on the principles of development of economic ties and all-round cooperation. Five other agreements and memoranda setting out the thrust of Indian-Uzbek cooperation in various fields were also concluded.
