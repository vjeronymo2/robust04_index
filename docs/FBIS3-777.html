Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The leader of the Conservative Party [CP] and co-leader of the Afrikaner Volksfront [National Front], Dr. Ferdi Hartzenberg, has declined to comment at this stage on the decision by General Constand Viljoen's Freedom Front to contest the election. Dr. Hartzenberg said that the possibility of participation had been raised by various organizations, and that the CP and Volksfront had discussed the matter. The Freedom Front submitted its list of candidates to the Independent Electoral Commission minutes before last night's midnight deadline.
