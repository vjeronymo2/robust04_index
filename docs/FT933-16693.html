930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: SNP support rises after Rosyth loss SUPPORT for the Scottish National party has risen 2 per cent in the past month, in the wake of the decision not to award the Rosyth dockyard the Trident submarine refit contract, a poll for The Herald newspaper in Glasgow shows. Compared with last month, the poll puts Labour on 46 per cent (up 1 per cent, and up 7 per cent on its general election performance), the SNP on 25 (up 2, up 4), the Tories at 16 (no change, down 10) and the Liberal Democrats at 12 (down 4, down 1).
