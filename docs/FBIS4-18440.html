BFN [Excerpt] Ankara, April 4 (A.A) -- U.S. special envoy for Cyprus, Robert Lamb on Monday [4 April] started his contacts in Ankara after concluding talks in Lefkose [Nicosia] and Athens. Speaking to reporters before his meeting on Monday morning with Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin, Lamb expressed hopes that talks will resume for finding a common ground for Cyprus talks. Answering a reporter's question on the confidence-building measures he said the document is a "dynamic one" whose contents may change. He added that what matters is to keep the balance during the process. The confidence-building measures of the United Nations involves the opening of the Lefkose Airport to international traffic and the inhabited Maras [Varosha] section to the UN monitoring. UN-sponsored intercommunal talks on Cyprus were interrupted last July. [passage omitted]
