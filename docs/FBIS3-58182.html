Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Turkey has expressed deep concern about the resumption of the intensified armed clashes in Afghanistan which claim many lives and cause great destruction. Turkey says that it is a great misfortune for the friendly and brotherly Afghan people that clashes continue when domestic peace should be established and when all efforts should focus on rebuilding the country. A Foreign Ministry statement on the issue says Turkey insistently defends the need to establish a national [word indistinct] that will comprise all the groups in Afghanistan. It says: We urge all Afghan groups to stop this fighting among brothers and to make the necessary efforts to sign a lasting cease-fire as soon as possible.
