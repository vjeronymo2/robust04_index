April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DIANNE FEINSTEIN

I can't tell you how infuriating it was to see the caricature of Feinstein on the cover.

While San Francisco's former mayor may not be popular with some Southern Californians, that does not warrant such a display of contempt.

Would you have dared to paint such an unflattering portrait of our two male gubernatorial candidates? I think not. Yet you chose to portray our one female contender in a pseudo-Leona Helmsley pose.

MARINA BAROFF

La Jolla

Editor's note: This illustration of gubernatorial candidate John K. Van de Kamp, also by Edward Sorel, appeared on the cover on Jan. 14.
