BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 9 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il extended thanks to soldiers of the So Chang-sin unit of the Korean People's Army and the Pak In-yong and Im Ung-song units of the Korean People's Security Forces, who had taken an active part in building up and caring for the revolutionary sites with a wholehearted desire to defend and honor the glorious revolutionary traditions of the Workers' Party of Korea. Those soldiers have so far planted many trees of different species around the Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery, the Grand Monument on Mansu Hill and other revolutionary sites and dedicated many facilities to the sites. Comrade Kim Chong-il also sent his thanks to the Central Committee of the League of Socialist Working Youth, the Speed Campaign Youth Shock Brigade, the Taean heavy machine complex and the Komdok mining conglomerate which had contributed to the construction of the Sangsulryong youth power station.
