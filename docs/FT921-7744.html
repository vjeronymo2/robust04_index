920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Wickes proposes to convert loan stock Wickes, the DIY and timber group, is proposing to convert the outstanding Pounds 19.1m of its 5.5 per cent subordinated convertible unsecured loan stock into ordinary shares on the basis of 97.333 new ordinary for every Pounds 100 of stock held. There is a cash alternative of 73p per Pounds 1 nominal of stock held, underwritten by SG Warburg Securities. Irrevocable undertakings in favour of the proposal have been given by holders of 77.3 per cent of the stock.
