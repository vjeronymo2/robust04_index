June 14, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: SANTA MONICA;

RENT BOARD OKS BUDGET

The Rent Control Board has approved a $4.3-million budget -- a 3% increase over last year -- for the fiscal year beginning July 1.

The board also decided not to raise the annual fee of $144 a unit charged to landlords.

The majority of the budget -- $3.2 million -- is for salaries, including seven new staff positions that will increase the total staff to 59.
