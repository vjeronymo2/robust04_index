930807 FT 07 AUG 93 / Finance and the Family: Higher interest for bigger savers - At a glance NATIONAL Savings has increased the interest payable to larger investors on two of its products. Those who invest more than Pounds 25,000 in income bonds will now receive 7.25 per cent, up from 7 per cent; similarly, those who have a balance of more than Pounds 25,000 in an investment account will earn 6.5 per cent, up from 6.25 per cent. In both cases, the rates paid to investors with less than Pounds 25,000 are unchanged.
