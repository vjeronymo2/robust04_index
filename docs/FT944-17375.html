941008 FT 08 OCT 94 / UK Company News: BZW Commodities placing BZW has raised almost Pounds 70m from the placing of shares in the BZW Commodities Trust, a Jersey-incorporated, London-listed investment company. The trust aims to match the performance of the Goldman Sachs commodity index by using derivative instruments. A public offer for the trust closes on October 20, and dealings in shares and warrants are due to start on October 27. Hambros has raised Dollars 42m (Pounds 26.5m) with a placing of shares in its Hambros Smaller Asian Companies trust. Dealings started yesterday.
