Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the press center of the Georgian Interior Ministry, a powerful explosion shook the headquarters of the Georgian Saviors' Corps in Tbilisi. The corps formed of members of the paramilitary Mkhedrioni group is led by Jaba Ioseliani. Part of the building was destroyed. According to preliminary reports, two persons were killed and several wounded. More victims can be hidden under the debris. The Georgian Interior Ministry has launched an investigation. This was not the first act of terrorism in Georgia this year. On January 28 in Rustavi to the south of Tbilisi the chief of a local branch of Mkhedrioni Temur Talakhadze was killed in a barber shop. A bomb was planted under his chair. On February 3 the deputy defence minister Niki Kekelidze died in an explosion in his own house in Tbilisi. The same day defence minister Georgi Karkarashvili was shell-shocked by another blast. On February 8 a ministry officer Vasil Koberidze was shot dead outside his house in Tbilisi. None of the assassins has been captured.
