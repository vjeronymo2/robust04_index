930206 FT 06 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Improvement for Continental Assets Undiluted net asset value per 75p ordinary share of Continental Assets Trust improved from 165.2p to 170.4p over the 12 months ended December 31. Diluted, the figure was up from 154.6p to 158.9p. Available revenue for the year amounted to Pounds 544,000 (Pounds 338,000), equal to basic earnings of 2.51p (1.56p). The single dividend is being lifted from 1.25p to 2.4p. Earnings benefited, to an extent, from high interest rates and the impact of the pound's devaluation. Yesterday its shares rose 7p to 160p.
