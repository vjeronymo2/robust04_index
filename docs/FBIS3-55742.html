Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by special correspondent Yelena Kuznetsova from the Federation Council] [Text] At the proposal of Boris Nikolayevich Yeltsin, the first session of the Federation Council following the elections is being chaired by senior deputy Yuriy Chernichenko, who announced that the first question on the standing orders would be a report by Nikolay Ryabov. The chairman of the Central Electoral Commission reported on the results of the elections and called on the deputies to justify the high trust of the people. The next issue on the standing orders was the election of the credentials commission and recognition of the authority of the deputies. The main question on the agenda is the election of working bodies and of the chairman of the Federation Council and his deputies, as well as the formation of committees. The house will elect yet another commission today from among the deputies to conduct the secret nomination of candidates for the post of chairman of the Federation Council. They will be responsible for the preparation of the voting paper. Each deputy will be given one voting paper which will be filled in in a booth for secret voting. In accordance with the rules, only one name may be entered for the post of chairman of the Federation Council. As a result of the secret nominations, the tellers' commission will draw up a report which will be signed by all members of the tellers' commission and confirmed by the Federation Council.
