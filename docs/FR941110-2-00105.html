DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR Fish and Wildlife Service Receipt of Application(s) for Permit The following applicant has applied for a permit to conduct certain activities with endangered species. This notice is provided pursuant to Section 10(c) of the Endangered Species Act of 1973, as amended (16 U.S.C. 1531, et seq .) PRT&hyph;676811 Applicant: Regional Director, Region 2, U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, Albuquerque, New Mexico. The applicant requests an amendment to the Regional Director's Blanket Endangered Species Permit to include take activities for the Louisiana black bear (Ursus americanus luteolus) for the purpose of scientific research and enhancement of propagation and survival of the species as prescribed by Service recovery documents. Addresses: Written data or comments should be submitted to the Assistant Regional Director, Ecological Services, U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, P.O. Box 1306, Albuquerque, New Mexico 87103, and must be received by the Assistant Regional Director within 30 days for the date of this publication. Documents and other information submitted with this application are available for review, subject to the requirements of the Privacy Act and Freedom of Information Act, by any party who submits a written request for a copy of such documents to the above office within 30 days of the date of publication of this notice.
