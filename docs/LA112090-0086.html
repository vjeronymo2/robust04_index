November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Baby, You're a Rich Man: A 26-year-old college student shelled out $18,000 for Paul McCartney's birth certificate at an auction in Houston Sunday.

Brian Taylor, a self-described "Beatles freak," said that he didn't necessarily buy the document as an investment but would sell it if the price is right. Bids came from as far away as Australia and Japan, and started at $10,000. "It's ridiculous," Taylor said. "People are just ridiculous. I'm ridiculous for buying it. . . ." CLAUDIA PUIG
