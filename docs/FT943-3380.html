940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Bloomsbury loss cut Bloomsbury Publishing, the book publishing company floated in June, cut its seasonal pre-tax losses from Pounds 563,000 to Pounds 478,000 for the first half of 1994. The company typically trades at a loss in the first half because of its orientation towards the Christmas book market. Turnover for the period grew 30 per cent to Pounds 3.13m and losses per share were 1p lower at 6.02p.
