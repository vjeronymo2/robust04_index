940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / Company News (This Week): Westinghouse Electric - Other companies On Tuesday Mr Michael Jordan, chairman of the troubled conglomerate, will brief Wall Street analysts for the first time since his appointment last June. He is expected to spell out his strategy for turning the company around. Mr Jordan, formerly a senior executive with PepsiCo, replaced Mr Paul Lego, who resigned from the chairmanship under boardroom pressure a year ago because of Westinghouse's poor performance.
