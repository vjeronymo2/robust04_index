931011 FT 11 OCT 93 / World News in Brief: Poll shows backing for Yeltsin Ninety per cent of Moscow people backed President Boris Yeltsin's imposition of emergency rule in the Russian capital and 62 per cent supported his use of the army to storm the Russian parliament, according to an opinion poll.
