Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [INPRINDA news agency report under the rubric "According to Reports From Correspondents and Information Agencies": "Once More About Gamsakhurdia"] [Text] As the agency's correspondent was informed in the republic's Ministry of Security, the Georgian government has consented to the reburial of the body of ex-president Gamsakhurdia and its removal from western Georgia without there being a forensic examination. Regarding the composition of the delegation from Groznyy [Chechnya] that is dealing with matters concerning the reburial, the Georgian side has officially stated that proceedings have been instigated against his closest associates Avtandil Rtskhiladze and Merab Kiknadze and, therefore, it is suggested that they be excluded from the list of persons accompanying the body of the ex-president; otherwise, they will be arrested.
