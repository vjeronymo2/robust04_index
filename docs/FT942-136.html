940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / Observer: A to Zoo Standard Chartered had its own codename for the inquiry into its misdemeanours in the Hong Kong securities markets which led to a ban on its taking part in flotations until next April. The name was Hippo, after the Initial Public Offerings (IPOs) which it was helping to ramp. Given its tendency to get into this sort of scrape, which exotic species will we hear of next?
