Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 18 (XINHUA) -- The Standing Committee of the Eighth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) approved draft resolutions and lists of candidates for some new CPPCC leaders at its sixth meeting held here this morning. The resolutions and the lists for candidates for vice-chairmen, Standing Committee members and secretary-general of CPPCC National Committee will be put to a vote at the closing meeting of the second session of the CPPCC National Committee tomorrow. Li Ruihuan, chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, presided over today's meeting. Vice-Chairman Wang Zhaoguo briefed participants on discussions of the CPPCC National Committee members. He said that the members basically approved the draft amendment of the Constitution of the CPPCC and proposed some revisions on it. The members expressed satisfaction about all draft resolutions. Wang said that the members expressed their appreciation of the government work report delivered by Chinese Premier Li Peng to the current session of the National People's Congress, saying it sums up last year's achievements and experience and outlines this year's principles and work. The CPPCC members also raised some suggestions on the report concerning price rise control, state-owned large and medium-sized enterprises, anti-corruption campaign, agriculture and social ethical progress. The meeting also approved the draft political resolution of the second session of the CPPCC National Committee and a draft resolution on the work report of the standing committee of the CPPCC National Committee. At today's meeting participants also examined and approved a report by the subcommittee for handling proposals of the CPPCC National Committee on examination of the proposals raised by the current session of the CPPCC National Committee.
