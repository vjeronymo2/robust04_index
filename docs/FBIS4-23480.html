BFN [Text] Dr. Brazao Mazula, chairman of the National Electoral Council [CNE], has acknowledged [words indistinct] that logistical and transportation problems are hindering the smooth registration of voters. Mazula said that as a result of the CNE appeal, the government, and the UN Operations in Mozambique have already provided vehicles, light aircraft, and helicopters which are scheduled to arrive in various provinces this week. Mazula was speaking after he registered at Maputo's 1 May Industrial School for the October elections.
