July 8, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

PERHAPS, DOWNEY COULD CHANGE HIS SUBJECTS

Mike Downey writes about kid's games for an adult readership. Now that is something truly meaningful and courageous to do with one's life.

Writing about sports in the scheme of things ranks somewhere between flagpole sitting and cherry-pit spitting.

It sure doesn't rank with cheerleading.

WILLIAM ASH

Santa Monica
