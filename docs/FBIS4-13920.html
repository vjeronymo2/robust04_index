BFN [Text] Beirut, 23 Apr (MENA)--Investigations by Lebanese judicial and security authorities with 16 members of the Abu-Nidal-led Fatah Movement--the Revolutionary Council [FRC] arrested a few days ago have revealed that eight of them were involved in the assassination a few months ago of Jordanian diplomat Na'ib al- Ma'aytah. The number of persons arrested in connection with the crime has thus risen to 11, after charges were pressed against three others. The Lebanese Army troops on Thursday stormed one of the Abu-Nidal group's barracks in al-Rawdah, Western al-Biqa', and seized a large amount of weapons, ammunition, vehicles, and missiles estimated at 25 truck loads. The army arrested 16 FRC members.
