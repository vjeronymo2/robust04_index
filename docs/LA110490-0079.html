November 4, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Michael Russell has been appointed senior vice president of Lusk Interiors, an Irvine commercial design firm. Russell, who has been with the company since 1987, will direct the commercial activities of the company's satellite offices in San Diego, Menifee, Pleasanton and Honolulu. Before joining Lusk, Russell worked with the architectural firm of Charles Kober Associates.
