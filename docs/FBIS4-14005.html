BFN [Text] Cairo, 25 Apr (MENA) -- An official source in the Arab League has stated that the League's Secretariat is awaiting the member states' response to the Libyan request to hold an emergency meeting of the foreign and defense ministers council. The official explained that holding the meeting requires the consent of 12 Arab states. However, he added, if two states agree within 30 days of the request, the meeting will be held at the level of permanent delegates. Libya called that extraordinary meeting to discuss the U.S. sophisticated weapons deal to Israel, in addition to other topics.
