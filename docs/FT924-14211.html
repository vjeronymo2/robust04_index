921010 FT 10 OCT 92 / World News in Brief: China The US and China reached tentative agreement on a long-running dispute over access to the Chinese market, pending approval on the terms from Beijing. The deal comes the day before expiration of a US deadline for retaliation. The US, which has long complained about non-tariff barriers in China, had published a list of potential sanctions totalling Dollars 3.9bn (Pounds 2.19bn).
