930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Bimec sells five subsidiaries Bimec Industries, the loss-making waste treatment and engineering company, has sold five subsidiaries in its electrical engineering division to Romac Group, the parent of a management buy-in team backed by 3i, the investment capital group. The five companies, Billora Electrical, Ace Electrical, Panelmatic Systems, Talbotbest and Earlswood Environmental, specialise in the installation and maintenance of electrical systems, control panel assembly and manufacture of industrial pipework and ductwork.
