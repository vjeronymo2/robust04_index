910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Spanish link for United Biscuits United Biscuits has agreed a joint venture with Grupo Larios, the Spanish spirits group. The venture, KP Larios, is aimed at creating a new force in the expanding Spanish snacks market. Grupo Larios will initially hold a 25 per cent stake with an option to increase this to 50 per cent over the next three years.
