920215 FT 15 FEB 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices India abolished export cess (3.5 per cent) on hard currency sales of cassia and cinnamon, reports Man-producten. Demand evident for spot and nearby, especially for better grades. Cloves: Madagascar resellers USDollars 1,425-Dollars 1,400 a tonne for afloat and Feb/Mar shipment. Spot cloves hardly available. Ginger: good demand from North Africa and Europe. Nigerian split ginger Dollars 950, Cochin ginger Dollars 975. Nutmegs/mace: buyers looking for offers to cover their needs for positions as far as 1994. Pimento: Mexico not available until new crop Jul/Aug. Jamaican spot Europe traded Dollars 2,500, shipment Dollars 2,425 cif.
