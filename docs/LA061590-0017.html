June 15, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MEMORIAL SET FOR CARNATION OFFICIAL E.S. HARTWICK

A memorial service for Elbert S. Hartwick, retired head of the legal department and a director and senior vice president for the Carnation Co., will be held Tuesday at 10:30 a.m. at All Saints Episcopal Church in Beverly Hills.

Hartwick, a former member of the Republican State Central Committee and first chairman of the board of the John Thomas Dye School, a private school in Bel-Air, was 86 when he died in Los Angeles on Tuesday.

Survivors include his wife, Joy, a son, Ronald, and a grandson. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Dye School, 11414 Chalon Road, Los Angeles 90049.
