January 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WEEKEND TV;

SUPER BOWL HYPE STARTS ON SATURDAY; OTHER WEEKEND SHOWS ON DRUGS, EL SALVADOR

For those who are committed to the Super Bowl and are looking for a head start on Sunday's hype, CBS (2)(8) has "Super Bowl Saturday Nite" tonight at 9. Hosted by Don Johnson, this two-hour gala from New Orleans will include appearances by Terry Bradshaw, Gallagher, Linda Ronstadt and Harry Connick Jr. STEVE WEINSTEIN
