BFN [Text] Mr. Nguyen Quang Ha, minister of forestry of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam [SRV] currently visiting Myanmar [Burma], called on Lieutenant General Chit Swe, minister of forestry, at 1550 today at the International Business Center on Prome Road. The ministers held cordial and frank talks on the promotion of bilateral friendship, development of forestry sector, the exchange of forestry technology, cooperation in the development of timber-based industry, and forest conservation.
