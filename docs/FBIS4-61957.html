BFN [Text] Baku, May 10 (AZERTAC)--According to the Azerbaijani Defence Ministry, today Armenian troops launched a large-scale offensive in Agdam. Despite the statement of unilateral cease-fire, the Armenians continue their offensive in the Geranboyskiy, Terterskiy and Fizulinskiy Districts beyond Nagorno-Karabakh. The Azeri Army's positions in Mourovdag [as received] range, in the north of the district of Kelbadzharskiy, and border villages in Kedabek are subject to the enemy's shelling.
