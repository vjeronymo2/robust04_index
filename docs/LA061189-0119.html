June 11, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

TOMAC TOP U. S. RIDER IN CYCLING'S MILK RACE

John Tomac of Chatsworth was the top U. S. finisher and 15th overall in cycling's Milk Race, which concluded Saturday. Tomac completed the 13-day, 1,150-mile race through Britain in 43 hours, 36 minutes, 43 seconds -- 2:02 behind winner Brian Walton of Vancouver, Canada.

Tomac also was the best American in Saturday's final stage, a 68-mile race from Leicester to Birmingham, England, finishing 10th in an 85-rider field.
