BFN [Text] PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat, who arrives in Moscow today for talks with the Russian leadership, hopes to reach agreement on intensifying Russian mediation in the Middle East settlement, a high-ranking representative of the Palestinian embassy in Moscow told a RIA correspondent. He said that the PLO head hopes that Russia could play the main role in the resumption of the bilateral Palestinian-Israeli talks on Gaza and Jericho, broken off as a result of the tragic events in Hebron. The Russian Foreign Ministry told RIA that 'Arafat's visit will last until 20 April. The itinerary envisions talks with the leadership of the foreign policy department, meetings with State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin, Internal Affairs Minister Viktor Yerin, and also with Aleksey II, patriarch of All Russia.
