BFN [Text] Constitutionalist Bloc senators are now working on the amnesty law. According to parliamentary sources, the Senate office of President Jean-Louis was able to reach the quorum of 13 senators to discuss this law. Alliance [for Parliamentary Cohesion] senators, notably including Franck Leonard and Luc Fleurinord, allegedly participated in those sessions. Senate President Firmin Jean-Louis, who has just returned from a tour of North America, where he met with President Aristide and State Department officials, is working feverishly to finalize the works. The Senate will hold a session this morning in the presence of the press under the presidency of Honorable Firmin Jean-Louis.
