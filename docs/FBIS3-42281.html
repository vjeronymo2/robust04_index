Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 Feb (MENA)--An explosive device went off this evening near the Egyptian Central Bank, opposite the al-Fath Mosque, on Ramsis Street in Cairo. According to preliminary reports, the explosion caused no casualties. However, there was some damage to the bank's outside windows. Meanwhile, Interior Ministry explosives experts have defused two bombs--one placed near the Muhammad Faris branch of the Bank of Egypt, and the second near the National Bank on Sharif Street, in central Cairo. Security commanders and criminal laboratory experts went to the site of the explosion to investigate.
