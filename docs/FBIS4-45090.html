CSO [Text] According to the deputy minister of economic and financial affairs, 40 percent of the nation's tax revenues is used for development projects. Speaking Sunday in Mashhad, Daryush Iranbandi added that the project to use tax revenues in the provinces for regional projects is being studied. At a get-acquainted meeting with the new Khorasan general manager of economic and financial affairs, he said: The government's policy is that oil revenues should be invested in infrastructure affairs and on national projects. He explained: To provide means to investors, henceforth investors in regional projects will be exempt from taxes for five years. Iranbandi said: With the expansion of tax sources and a view to society's economy, an office for deputy governor-general of economic affairs will also be created in the provincial general offices of economic and financial affairs. He cited the hiring of 1,500 new personnel in the tax auditing department as another program in the Ministry of Economic and Financial Affairs. At this meeting 'Ali Zarif-Golzar was introduced as the new Khorasan general manager of economic and financial affairs.
