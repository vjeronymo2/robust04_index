940711 FT 11 JUL 94 / Public-sector pay deals 'up to 3%' Most public-sector groups have settled for pay rises of between 2.5 per cent and 3 per cent, according to Incomes Data Services, the pay analysts. In local government the 2.9 per cent Pay Review Body award to teachers has been a strong factor in pay decisions. Other groups have been made opening offers of 1.75 per cent, which unions have rejected, IDS says. In the private sector many increases are at or above 3 per cent, with many of the highest pay rises in financial services, the analysts add.
