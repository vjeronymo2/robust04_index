September 20, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

PRO FOOTBALL REPORT / WEEKDAY UPDATE;

AROUND THE LEAGUE;

MAJKOWSKI WILL START FOR PACKERS

Quarterback Don Majkowski, who missed 45 days in a contract holdout, will make his first start of the season Sunday for the Green Bay Packers against the Kansas City Chiefs. Anthony Dilweg started the first two games as the Packers beat the Rams and lost to the Chicago Bears.
