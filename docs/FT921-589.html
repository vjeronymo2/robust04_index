920328 FT 28 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Ricardo Interim pre-tax profits for the six months to December 31 from Ricardo International, the engineering consultancy, halved from Pounds 2.5m to Pounds 1.21m. Turnover was slightly down at Pounds 28.6m, compared with Pounds 31.9m. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.9p from earnings of 2.48p (5.24p) per share.
