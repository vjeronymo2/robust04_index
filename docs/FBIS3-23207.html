Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Investigative Police on 9 March at noon finally arrested Rosa Ester Vargas, the original mujer metralleta [submachine gun woman], who is regarded as the most dangerous subversive in the Lautaro group. She was arrested in the country's southern section with two other people. She was transferred today to a Civilian Police post under strict security measures. [passage omitted] Vargas participated mainly in attacks on banks. Police reported her arrest to Special Judge (Arnoldo Ereise), who investigates the subversive group's actions. She will be placed at his disposal in 10 days.
