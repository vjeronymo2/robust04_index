930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Safeland Pre-tax profits of Safeland, the property group, advanced by 45 per cent, from Pounds 167,000 to Pounds 242,000, in the year to March 31. The outcome was achieved on turnover up by Pounds 5.25m to Pounds 13.8m and after reduced interest payable of Pounds 652,000 (Pounds 923,000). A final dividend of 0.44p (0.14p) makes an improved 0.5p (0.28p) total, payable from earnings per share of 1p (0.6p).
