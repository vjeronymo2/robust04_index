920121 FT 21 JAN 92 / International Company News: Salomon back in the black SALOMON, the French sporting goods group, returned to profit in the six months ended September, 1991, Reuter reports from Paris. The company plans to seek a listing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange for its Japanese subsidiaries. The company said JAFCO, a leading Japanese venture capital company, had acquired 10 per cent of Salomon's Japanese subsidiaries. JAFCO will partner Salomon in the listings project. Salomon said the listing was expected over the next three to five years. Net profit for the six months amounted to FFr10.7bn (Dollars 1.98bn), compared with a loss of FFr190.2m in the same period a year before. The accounts for the full year will also be in the black, it said.
