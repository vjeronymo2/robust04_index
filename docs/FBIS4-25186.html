BFN [Text] Beijing, June 7 (XINHUA) -- The China Southern Airlines passenger plane, hijacked to Taiwan yesterday, returned to Guangzhou at 00:30 today. A spokesman for the Civil Aviation Administration of China said that the B-737/2542 plane was hijacked to Taiwan yesterday at 19:57 on flight CZ3572 from Fuzhou to Guangzhou. All the passengers and crew members, except for the lone hijacker, returned with the plane, the spokesman said.
