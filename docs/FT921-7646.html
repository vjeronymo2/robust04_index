920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Exploration Co and El Oro decline The Exploration Company, the principal activity of which is dealing in investments and which owns about 45 per cent of El Oro Mining and Exploration, reported a 22 per cent fall from Pounds 3.18m to Pounds 2.47m in pre-tax profits for the 1991 year. Earnings per share were 14.15p (18.25p) and the dividend is maintained at 12p. El Oro, which now holds just over 6 per cent of the Exploration Company, also incurred a setback in pre-tax profits for the same 12 months, from Pounds 2.5m to Pounds 1.97m. The dividend is a repeated 24p from earnings of 30.37p (37.69p).
