940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Fairbriar Fairbriar, the contract management and property development group, has acquired Ilford Park Developments from the receivers of Fairfield Park Development for Pounds 2 cash - its net asset value. Ilford has not traded, and its sole asset is 18.4 acres of residential land at Ilford, Essex. It has indebtedness of Pounds 10.75m, which will be repaid from the sales proceeds of the development, without any further recourse to Fairbriar.
