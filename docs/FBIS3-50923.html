Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Labor Minister Walter Barelli has joined the PSDB [Brazilian Social Democracy Party], stating that he is not a candidate and he will remain in the government as long as President Itamar Franco wants. Barelli also defended alliances among parties that follow the same political path, promoting the alliance between the PT [Workers Party] and the PSDB. The main Sao Paulo PSDB leaders participated in the ceremony marking Barelli's affiliation with the PSDB at the party's headquarters.
