November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

New Rumors on the Block: Is Tiffany going to turn out to be the Yoko Ono of the '90s? The Boston Herald reports that the teen singing queen is involved with Jonathan Knight, 21, of New Kids on the Block, and the other New Kids want him to break it off with her because their fans want them romantically unattached. "His fellow band members have told him bluntly that if he continues with the romance, his career could be over -- as well as Tiffany's and the rest of the New Kids," the Herald quoted an insider as saying.

Knight is quoted as saying: "I love Tiffany a hell of a lot more than I love the New Kids on the Block. I don't want to break up the group but if they don't leave me and Tiffany alone, I will." CLAUDIA PUIG
