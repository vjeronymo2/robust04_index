931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Hillsdown completes poultry restructure Hillsdown Holdings, the food group, has completed the reorganisation of its UK poultry business via the sale of North Wales-based Cymru Chicken. The sale, to Grampian County Food, was not material in relation to Hillsdown's net assets. Meanwhile, Corporate Foods, a bakery group controlled by Maple Leaf Foods, the Canadian subsidiary of Hillsdown, has acquired the assets of a frozen bagel business located in Chicago with annual sales of Dollars 14m (Pounds 9m). Terms were not disclosed.
