931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Sharp recovery at CH Bailey CH Bailey, the engineering, shiprepairing and leisure group, recorded a pre-tax profit of Pounds 446,379 in the year ended March 26 1993, against a Pounds 1.09m loss previously. Turnover increased from Pounds 3.54m to Pounds 3.71m. Earnings per share were 0.74p (losses 1.8p).
