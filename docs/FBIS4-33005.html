BFN [Excerpt] The Senate has suspended the impeachment of [Supreme Court Justices] Edgar Oblitas and Ernesto Pope. Senate President Juan Carlos Duran has stated that the impeachment was suspended in compliance with a decision by the Congress stating that the two houses cannot work separately. A few days ago the Chamber of Deputies declared a recess until 6 June, and the Senate had to follow suit and thus suspend the impeachment process. [passage omitted]
