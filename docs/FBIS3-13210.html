Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At 1230 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received the delegation of the 1948 Palestinians led by 'Abd-al-Wahhab Darawshah. Members of the delegation offered their condolences on the loss of the nation of Staff Major Engineer Paratrooper Basil al-Asad. The president thanked members of the delegation for their noble sentiments and sincere condolences.
