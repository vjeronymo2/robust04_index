930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / UK Company News: TR Technology TR Technology, the investment trust, reported net assets per ordinary share of 172.28p at April 30, against 111.85p 12 months earlier. For the zero dividend preference shares the figures were 176.81p and 157.71p and for the stepped preference shares 126.92p and 120.99p. Net revenue for the year to the end of April was Pounds 3.19m (Pounds 3.26m) for earnings per share of 2.7p (3.18p). The proposed single final payment is maintained at 1.75p.
