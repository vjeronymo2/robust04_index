BFN [Unattributed report: "Will Achille Become President in Strasbourg?"] [Text] Brussels -- Will [outgoing Democratic Party of the Left, PDS, Secretary] Achille Occhetto become president of the European Parliament [EP]? In the presidential contest which has just started in Strasbourg, the PDS' outgoing secretary's name is among six leading candidates, together with the two Belgian former Prime Ministers Leo Tindemans and Wilfried Martens (who are considered the favorites), Social Democrat John Hume from Northern Ireland, French center-right leader Dominique Baudis, and Spanish millionaire Abel Matutes. The election of the president will take place on 19 July. Occhetto's candidacy was suggested by a number of left-wing exponents, as the standard-bearer of the European Left against "neofascism" and "the victory of the Right in Italy."
