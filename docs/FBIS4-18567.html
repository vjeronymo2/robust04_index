BFN [Text] A new party will be set up in the political center. The New National Center [Uusi Kansallinen Keskusta], UKK, intends to contest the next Diet elections with full candidate lists in all election districts. UKK calls for the implementation of a policy independent of European Union legislation and the pursuit of an independent foreign and security policy based on neutrality and the Finnish desire to defend the country. The chairman of the temporary board which is preparing for the registration of the party is Ilkka Hakalehto from Helsinki. Pentti Kettunen, a former Diet deputy of the SMP [Finnish Rural Party] from Kajaani, is deputy chairman. Both men also belong to the leadership of the Finnish People's Independence Front, which opposes membership in the European Union.
