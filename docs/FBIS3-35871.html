Language: English Article Type:BFN [By BELINFORM correspondent Valentina Menshikova for TASS] [Text] Minsk January 31 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin congratulated on Monday [31 January] Myechyslaw Hryb with his election to the top state position of the parliament chairman. "The peoples of Russia and Belarus always lived in peace, friendship and accord", the message said. "They are united by multi-century joint history, close culture, traditions of cooperation in all walks of life. I express conviction that Russia and Belarus will act jointly in solving complicated problems on the basis of equality, mutual understanding and benefit", the Russian president said.
