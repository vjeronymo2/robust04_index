921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / Councils to get Pounds 1.9bn for homes LOCAL authorities will get Pounds 1.9bn for housing investment programmes in 1993-94, Sir George Young, the housing minister, said in a Commons written reply yesterday. The allocation for the Estate Action programme aimed at inner cities will total Pounds 356m. Sir George also set out guidelines for council rents in England, which suggest a rise of 5 per cent in real terms.
