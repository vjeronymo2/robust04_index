930428 FT 28 APR 93 / Yeltsin no protection for Serbs RUSSIAN President Boris Yeltsin warned Bosnian Serbs yesterday they could expect no protection from Russia if they continued to defy an international peace plan for the fragmented former Yugoslav republic, Reuter reports from Moscow. Mr Yeltsin described a plan drafted by mediators Lord Owen and Mr Cyrus Vance and rejected on Monday by the Bosnian Serbs as the only prospect for peace in the region. 'The time has come for decisive measures to quell the conflict,' he said.
