941109 FT 09 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Greenway down Greenway Holdings, the waste oil recycling business, saw a fall in pre-tax profit for the six months to September 30 from Pounds 1.91m, which included a Pounds 1.5m exceptional disposal profit, to Pounds 915,000. Operating profit was ahead at Pounds 912,000 (Pounds 427,000) from increased turnover of Pounds 5.17m (Pounds 4.59m). The company said the improvement reflected the acquisition last year of BCS into Orcol Fuels. Earnings per share were 3.32, against 10.63p or 1.8p adjusting for the exceptional profit on the disposal of the US oil and gas operations. There is an interim dividend of 1p (nil).
