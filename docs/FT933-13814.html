930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / World News in Brief: European central bank 'to be sited in Frankfurt' The German business newspaper Handelsblatt reports in today's edition that EC heads of government have decided to locate the European central bank in Frankfurt. It said the decision would be announced at an autumn summit in Brussels. The UK Treasury said last night: 'No decision has been made.' The finance ministry in Bonn said it had nothing to add to finance minister Theo Waigel's recent comment that most EC leaders favoured Frankfurt.
