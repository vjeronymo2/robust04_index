Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At 1030 today President Ilyas al-Hirawi, Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri, and Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri -- together with a large number of ministers; deputies; and political, spiritual, economic, military, and trade union figures -- left Beirut for Syria on board three aircraft to attend the funeral of Major Basil al-Asad.
