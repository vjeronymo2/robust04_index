920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / Poles endorse finance minister The Polish parliament yesterday withheld approval for the coalition government's economic policy but approved the appointment of Mr Andrzej Olechowski, an experienced trade and central banking expert, as finance minister, writes Anthony Robinson. After three days' debate a large majority of the 460-seat parliament rejected an attempt by the opposition Democratic Union to throw out the government's economic policy but called for further amendments.
