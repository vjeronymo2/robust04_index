910427 FT 27 APR 91 / UK Company News: Joseph Holt rises by 24% to Pounds 5.7m Joseph Holt, the Manchester-based independent brewer, lifted pre-tax profits 24 per cent from Pounds 4.6m to Pounds 5.72m in the year to December 31. The increase was achieved on turnover which was ahead - less strongly at 18 per cent - from Pounds 15.16m to Pounds 17.82m. After tax of Pounds 2m (Pounds 1.7m), earnings worked through at 123.99p (96.52p) per share. The final dividend is a proposed 23p (19p) to make a total of 31p (26p) for the year.
