941008 FT 08 OCT 94 / International Company News: Ameritech lifts job cuts total Ameritech, the Chicago-based regional Bell telephone company, is to take a Dollars 392m pre-tax charge in its third quarter to September 30 to cover another 4,000 job losses, AP-DJ reports. The company said its forecast of 6,000 job cuts by 1995 had been revised to 10,000. It said that net of one-off gains and after tax, the charge would be Dollars 168m. Last year's third - quarter earnings were Dollars 425m after a one-time gain of Dollars 62m.
