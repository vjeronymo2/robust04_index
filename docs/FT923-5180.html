920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Societe Generale sells its CHL stake Societe Generale, the French banking group, has withdrawn from the UK residential mortgage market by selling its 51 per cent stake in Capital Home Loans to Credit Foncier. The deal, for an undisclosed sum, gives Credit Foncier full control of CHL, a joint venture set up by the two banks in 1989. CHL currently has a mortgage book of Pounds 200m.
