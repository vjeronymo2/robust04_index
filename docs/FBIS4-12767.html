BFN [Text] Two Israelis were hurt in a stoning incident in the village of al-Jib north of Jerusalem. In Jericho, four residents were wounded by rubber bullets fired by soldiers after stones were hurled at Israeli cars. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that rumors were disseminated in Jericho saying that nine locals were wounded in a shooting incident. It has been learned that all the casualties had been wounded by soldiers' gunfire.
