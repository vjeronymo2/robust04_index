Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid today sent urgent messages to the five permanent members of the UN Security Council prior to the council's meeting later today to adopt a resolution on the Israeli massacre of Palestinians in the al-Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron. In the messages, 'Abd-al-Majid voiced all Arab states' concern at the council's delay in adopting a resolution condemning the massacre 10 days after it was committed.
