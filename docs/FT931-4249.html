930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / Scottish estate sold for Pounds 2m THE 16,500-acre Knoydart estate in the west Highlands of Scotland has been bought for Pounds 2m by Titaghur, which claims to be the world's biggest jute manufacturer, employing 18,000 people in India. The estate includes eight miles of coastline, a lodge, salmon and trout fishing, woodland and three mountains. Titaghur has also bought sporting rights to a further 8,000 acres. The estate was placed on the market in 1991 by a property company headed by Mr Phillip Rhodes.
