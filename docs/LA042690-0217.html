April 26, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: CLAREMONT;

PRESECAN RESUMES AS MAYOR

Nicholas Presecan, who became mayor last December after the death of Alexander Hughes, has been given a new one-year term as mayor.

Presecan, 49, senior vice president of an engineering company, was reelected to the council April 10.

Councilwoman Diann Ring, who also was reelected to the council this month, will continue serving as vice mayor.
