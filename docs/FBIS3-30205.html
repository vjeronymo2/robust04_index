Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Foreign Ministry has informed the Russian Embassy in Ljubljana that the escorts of Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, Russian Deputy and leader of the Liberal Democratic Party, grossly violated public order in Bled last night. [passage omitted]. Zhirinovskiy has, in the meantime, already rebuffed the accusations of the Slovene authorities and said that he would not leave our country early. The Russian Foreign Ministry in Ljubljana [title as heard] has stated that it would not get involved in the matter and our authorities should, thus, treat the offenders against public order in accordance with internal Slovene legislation and international norms. They have also expressed belief in the correctness of the incumbent Slovene organs regarding the punishment of the Russian citizens.
