BFN [Text] Zambia National Broadcasting Corporation [ZNBC] acting director general Mr. Dunken Mbazima has confirmed that ZNBC is to introduce a pay channel on television. Mr. Mbazima said he has received many queries today about the pay channel following the announcement published in today's TIMES OF ZAMBIA by the minister of information of broadcasting and services, Mr. Keli Walubita. The government has already approved the project and other formalities are now being worked out. Mr. Mbazima said ZNBC has been strapped for cash and this venture is expected to improve the corporation's financial standing. Viewers who will subscribe to the channel in areas outside Lusaka, the Copperbelt, and Livingstone transmitter coverage range will be serviced through the cellular network.
