930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / More women trade unionists THE NUMBER of women joining unions in Britain rose last year as union membership fell to less than a third of the employed workforce, statistics published yesterday in the Employment Gazette show. In 1992 female union membership rose by 20,000, taking it to 3.7m. male union membership fell 382,000 to 5.8m. There has been a fall of 3.7m in the number of trade unionists since 1979.
