930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / World Trade News: Westinghouse joins Finnish group to bid for power contracts WESTINGHOUSE Electric of the US and Finland's Haka-Stroi have formed a co-operation agreement to identify, develop and supply power generation projects in the Baltic countries and the Commonwealth of Independent States. Westinghouse, one of the world's main suppliers of power generation plant, will supply the power island and related equipment for each project under the two-year deal. Haka-Stroi, a civil engineering company, will manage plant construction activities. It is a subsidiary of Haka, a big Finnish construction company which has been active in CIS markets for 20 years. Babcock International has won contracts worth about Pounds 30m for boiler repair work in Kuwait and ship unloading and bulk materials handling equipment from customers in Germany, Italy, Sweden, Taiwan and Thailand. The deals include a Pounds 10m order from Kuwait's Ministry of Electricity and Water for the reconstruction of three boilers at Shuwaikh Power Station, which were extensively damaged during the Iraqi invasion. GEC Alsthom Electromecanique, a Quebec-based subsidiary of the Anglo-French engineering group, has won an order from Korea Heavy Industries & Construction to supply a nuclear reactor calandria for the Wolsong IV power station in the south-east of South Korea.
