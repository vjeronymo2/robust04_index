931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Alphameric back in black with Pounds 178,000 A turnround from pre-tax losses of Pounds 252,000 to a profit of Pounds 178,000 was yesterday reported by Alphameric, the information technology group, for the six months to end-September. The recovery was achieved on a 75 per cent increase in turnover from Pounds 3.02m to Pounds 5.28m. After a minority charge of Pounds 33,000 (Pounds 8,000 credit) the retained profit for the period was Pounds 145,000 (Pounds 244,000 loss). Earnings per share came out at 0.4p (1p losses). Mr Alan Benjamin, chairman, said that although no dividend was proposed, it was the board's intention to restore dividends as soon as it was prudent to do so.
