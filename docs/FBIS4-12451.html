BFN [Text] The Army continues its raids and searches for arms caches and ammunition. This morning it discovered one arms cache in Fatqa and another one in Ghidras that is only around 200 meters from the former headquarters of the Lebanese Forces [LF]. A large quantity of weapons, ammunitions, and war material were confiscated, which filled four trucks and included 640 rifles of various types, 40 antiaircraft missiles, and a military radio transmitter in addition to various types of spares parts, weapons, and ammunition.
