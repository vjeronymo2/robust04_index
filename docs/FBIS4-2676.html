BFN [By reporter Zhang Huanli (1728 3562 0448)] [Text] Tokyo, 11 Apr (XINHUA) -- At a meeting at the prime minister's residence today with Wang Xiaoxian, vice president of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries [CPAFFC] and of the China-Japan Friendship Association, Japanese Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa said his recent visit to China was a great success. According to the Japanese prime minister, he was very pleased with his fruitful talks with President Jiang Zemin, Premier Li Peng, and Vice Premier Zhu Rongji during his recent visit to China. Today, Tsutomu Hata, Japanese deputy prime minister and concurrently minister of foreign affairs, met with Wang Xiaoxian at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Hata said: We must ensure good Sino-Japanese relations because they are very important for peace and stability in Asia and the world as a whole. Hata disagreed with the so-called theory of Chinese threat, which was spread by some people. Wang Xiaoxian and the CPAFFC delegation he led also met separately with Koshiro Ishida, director general of management and coordination; Satsuki Eda, director general of science and technology; Ryoko Akamatsu, minister of education; Kanju Sato, minister of home affairs; Yohei Kono, president of the Japan Liberal Democratic Party; and some Diet members. The CPAFFC delegation arrived in Tokyo on 6 April on a visit to Japan at the invitation of Japan's Soka Society.
