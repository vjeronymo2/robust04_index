April 24, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

RIGAMAJIG TRIUMPHS IN TOP ROUND PURSE

Rigamajig took the lead approaching the stretch and went on to carry Jacinto Vasquez to a 1 1/4-length victory Monday in the $34,000 Top Round purse at Aqueduct.

Same, with Jean Cruguet up, was runner-up in the field of 10 fillies and mares 3 and up, five lengths ahead of Color Blue.
