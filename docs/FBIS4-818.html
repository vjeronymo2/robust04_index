BFN [Text] Mbabane April 17 SAPA -- A tribal Swazi chief has claimed that Afrikaner Volksfront [National Front--AVF] cadres are being trained in northern Swaziland. Chief Prince Mnikwa told SAPA's correspondent in Mbabane he had informed the Swazi military of the activities of the AVF, but so far nothing had been done. Chief Mnikwa did not say how he come to the conclusion that the people he had "seen in the mountains" were AVF cadres, but he feared these people would attack his subjects.
