920606 FT 06 JUN 92 / Tebbit setback LONRHO can proceed with its multi-million-pound damages claim against the Department of Trade and Industry and Mr Norman Tebbit, the former trade secretary, for alleged negligence during the 1985 takeover battle for House of Fraser, the Court of Appeal ruled yesterday. An appeal by Mr Tebbit and the DTI to have the case struck out was dismissed.
