Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Commenting on the 21 January remarks by a North Korean Foreign Ministry spokesman carried by the North Korean Central News Agency, ROK Nuclear Ambassador Kim Sam-hun has said that "it is not time to express our clear-cut position on North Korea's attitude." He also said: "The statement by a Foreign Ministry spokesman was intended, in part, to make the negotiations between North Korea and the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] difficult and, in another part, to pacify people at home." He also said: "Given North Korea's attitude toward negotiations with the United States, the statement must have been intended for domestic consumption." He added that the "government will closely monitor the negotiations between North Korea and the IAEA."
