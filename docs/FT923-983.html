920925 FT 25 SEP 92 / House prices fell in summer A SURVEY of almost 150 estate agents conducted last month before the recent cuts in interest rates shows how deeply depressed house prices and sales had become. More than 60 per cent of agents questioned by the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors said house prices had continued to fall during the three months to the end of August. Other agents said prices had remained static over the summer.
