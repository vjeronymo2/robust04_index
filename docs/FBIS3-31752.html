Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Article by "sta": "KOVO Trade Union Did Not Accede"] [Text] Bratislava -- The General Agreement between trade unions, employers, and the government for 1994 was signed without the KOVO [metal workers] trade union acceding to the document. V. Stevko, vice president of the Confederation of Trade Unions, said that the KOVO trade union does not feel bound by the agreement because its council placed it under a different obligation. "Unfortunately, at the time of negotiations, when the terms [of the agreement] had already been agreed upon, it is very difficult to speak about new talks," Stevko said, commenting on the decision of the KOVO Council.
