BFN [Text] Beijing, May 25 (CNS) -- According to the State Planning Commission, the inspection staged in the whole country has achieved the initial success with more than 61,300 cases involving illegal rises in prices discovered in 19 provinces from March 10 to April 15, about one-third of cases involving daily necessities. Among these cases, over 47,000 cases have been dealt with to date involving illegal income of RMB 53.89 million [renminbi] and a total fine of RMB 28 million has been imposed. Nearly 20,000 cases related to illegal rises in prices of daily necessities, 471 cases related to production means, over 1,100 cases involving prices of public services and over 1,200 in prices of management fees.
