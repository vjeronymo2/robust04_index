March 22, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

POLICE INVESTIGATING SHOOTING FATALITY

Anaheim police late Tuesday were investigating the shooting death of a woman in the 1600 block of East Redwood Street earlier in the evening, police said.

Details were not immediately available, but Anaheim Police Lt. Vince Howard said the shooting occurred about 8 p.m.

There was no one in custody late Tuesday, and the investigation was continuing. Howard added that "it looks like (police detectives) know who the suspect is."
