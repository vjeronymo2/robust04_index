Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] President Hafiz al-Asad received His Highness Shaykh Sa'ad al-'Abdallah al-Sabah, Kuwait's heir apparent and prime minister, and the delegation accompanying him at 1300 today. [passage omitted] Shaykh Sa'ad offered his condolences and those of His Highness Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah, the amir of Kuwait, and the government and people of Kuwait to President al-Asad on the death of paratrooper engineer Staff Major Basil al-Asad. Talks at the meeting dealt with the Arab situation. Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam held a dinner banquet in honor of His Highness Shaykh Sa'ad and his delegation. [passage omitted] Shaykh Sa'ad and his delegation left Damascus at 1700 today. [passage omitted] Before leaving Damascus, Shaykh Sa'ad said in a statement to a SANA correspondent: I came to my second country, the sisterly Syrian Arab Republic, to offer my sincerest condolences to His Excellency brother Hafiz al-Asad on death of Staff Major Basil al-Asad, and to convey to his excellency the sympathy of Kuwait--its amir, government, and people--which shares this painful occasion, and to emphasize the unity of feelings and sentiments between our two sisterly states in both good and bad times. I pray that God will grant mercy to the dear deceased, and patience to His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad and his family. I also pray that God will preserve sisterly Syria and its honorable people from any harm.
