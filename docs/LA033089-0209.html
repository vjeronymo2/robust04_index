March 30, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

LOCAL;

3 SPLIT $14.5-MILLION LOTTO JACKPOT

Three gamblers picked all six winning numbers in the mid-week Lotto 6/49 drawing to share the $14.5-million jackpot, state lottery officials said today.

The winning numbers in Wednesday's drawing were 7, 12, 16, 24, 28 and 39. The bonus number was 33. The three winners will each collect $4.8 million after they submit their claims for verification at lottery offices. In the five-of-six plus bonus category, nine players each won $220,721.
