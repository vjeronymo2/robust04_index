940903 FT 03 SEP 94 / London Stock Exchange: Equity futures and options trading Continuing worries over inflation cut back an earlier advance in stock index futures in an otherwise lacklustre trading session, Boosted by good early buying, the September futures contract on the FT-SE 100 ended the week at 3,226, 5 points ahead of its previous close and at a small premium to cash. Volume was fairly good reaching 13,898 lots. In traded options, turnover fell back to 23,072 contracts against 34,233 on Thursday. The FT-SE 100 option volume traded 7,175 lots and Glaxo was the busiest stock option at 2,057 contracts.
