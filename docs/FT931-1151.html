930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Trafford Park rises Trafford Park Estates, the property group, improved pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.62m to Pounds 1.74m in the half year to December 31 1992. Turnover declined to Pounds 4.87m against Pounds 4.95m. Earnings per share amounted to 1.72p (1.672p) and the interim dividend is held at 0.85p.
