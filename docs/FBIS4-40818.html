BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Following the two hour long talks and a dinner reception at the sumptuous villa of the head of the Italian Government today, Premier Viktor Chernomyrdin told journalists: Silvio Berlusconi is the most interesting man I have ever met. The conversation between the two men was very cordial and fruitful. Berlusconi accepted the invitation to come to Moscow on an official visit. In the course of the visit Russia and Italy will sign a friendship and cooperation treaty. According to Italian diplomats, the treaty envisages the kind of political and economic cooperation which Russia has only with Germany. Berlusconi assured Chernomyrdin that he would support Russia in her effort to join the group of seven developed nations, and generally do his very best to make Russia a leading country in the world scene. In reply to the flattering remarks by Chernomyrdin, Berlusconi said that while engaging in personal cultural development he read more Russian than Italian writers. Today the Russian prime minister, who is now staying in Milan at the invitation of the Italian state oil company Eni, also took part in the 19th world congress on natural gas.
