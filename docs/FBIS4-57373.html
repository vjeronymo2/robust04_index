BFN [Text] President Alberto Fujimori held a 45-minute meeting with Romanian Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu in Government House today. After the private protocol meeting, President Fujimori gave a news conference to Romanian reporters. This is part of the news conference. [passage indistinct] The news conference given by the president to the Romanian reporters referred mainly to two issues: Andean integration and foreign debt. President Fujimori also said the Romanian official's visit will help develop electric energy in Peru. He added that Romania has long experience in electrical power, especially hydroelectric and thermoelecric plants. Fujimori said that in the last five years important steps have been taken for Andean integration, although he noted there are still barriers that can be overcome united. This is part of the news conference given by President Fujimori to the Romanian reporters who are part of the delegation headed by Romanian Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu.
