931103 FT 03 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Finsbury Growth Tst Net asset value per ordinary share of Finsbury Growth Trust improved from 85.6p to 112.9p over the 12 months ended September 30. Net revenue fell to Pounds 1.37m (Pounds 1.41m), equal to earnings of 2.94p (3.14p). The 2p final maintains the total at 2.9p.
