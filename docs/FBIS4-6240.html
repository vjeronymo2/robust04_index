BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 19 (KCNA) -- Greenpeace, the international organisation of environmental movement, urged the puppet authorities of South Korea to stop the construction project of the atomic power station now underway, according to a radio report from Seoul. Some 20 members of the organisation raised this demand at an anti-nuke rally they held in Samchok on April 14 with about 1,000 members of environmental protection and anti-nuke organisations of South Korea. Sounding a warning against the danger of radioactive leakage from the South Korean atomic power station, Greenpeace is strongly demanding a stop to its construction project, said the radio.
