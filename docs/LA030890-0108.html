March 8, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

HUNTINGTON BEACH;

2 REPUBLICANS ENTER RACE FOR ASSEMBLY

Moving swiftly to capitalize on his sudden visibility from last month's oil spill, Huntington Beach Mayor Thomas J. Mays, 36, announced that he will seek the 58th Assembly District seat to be vacated by conservative Dennis Brown (R-Los Alamitos). Long Beach City Councilwoman Jan Hall also announced that she will seek the Republican nomination. Hall is chairwoman of the California Commission on the Status of Women.
