Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 16 (KCNA) -- The regional headquarters of Koreans in Canada of the National Alliance for the Country's Reunification (Pomminnyon) in a recent statement urged the United States to immediately withdraw the nuclear weapons of its forces in South Korea and solve the "nuclear issue" through dialogue with the DPRK. The statement demanded that the United States no longer obstruct the denuclearization of the Korean peninsula and the realization of the nation's desire to peacefully reunify the country divided into North and South.
