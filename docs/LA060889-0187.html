June 8, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: SANTA MONICA;

PLACEMENT SERVICE REOPENS

The Placement Service for Older Workers has reopened its Santa Monica office, 612 Colorado St., Room 103.

The federally funded agency offers free job counseling and placement to residents 55 or older.

Hours are Monday through Friday, 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.

For more information, call (213) 399-0998.
