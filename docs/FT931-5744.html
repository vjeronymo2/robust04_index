930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Metal Bulletin at record Pounds 1.9m Profits of Metal Bulletin, which has interests in trade journals, directories and books and conferences, rose from Pounds 1.7m to a record Pounds 1.9m pre-tax for 1992. Turnover of Pounds 12.7m compared with Pounds 11.5m. Earnings per share rose to 13.8p (12.5p) and a final dividend of 5.3p makes an 8p (7.4p) total.
