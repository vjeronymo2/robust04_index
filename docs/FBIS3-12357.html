Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri has stated that the purpose of this abominable crime against worshipers in Sayyidat al-Najat Church was to sabotage general stability in Lebanon. He said this crime, which coincided with an Israeli escalation in southern Lebanon, is an attempt to divert world attention from the Ibrahimi Mosque massacre. He noted that Israel is behind all this. Speaker Birri called on the Lebanese to be vigilant and responsible so that we will all emerge from this test with more faith in Lebanon.
