920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Castle Cairn net assets edge ahead Net asset value of Castle Cairn Investment Trust edged ahead to 43.86p at June 30, against 43.58p six months earlier. In the first half of 1992 gross income was Pounds 5,000 lower at Pounds 112,000, while net profits slipped from Pounds 62,000 to Pounds 55,000, equivalent to earnings of 0.46p (0.52p) per share.
