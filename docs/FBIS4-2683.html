BFN [Text] Marrakech, 11 Apr (XINHUA) -- A Chinese delegation, led by Gu Yongjiang, vice minister of foreign trade and economic cooperation, arrived here this evening to attend a global trade ministers' conference, organized by GATT's Uruguay Round of Trade Talks Committee, and to sign a package of agreements of the Uruguay Round. The conference will open on 12 April morning and Gu Yongjiang will deliver a speech at the conference. On 15 April, he will sign a package of agreements of the Uruguay Round on China's behalf.
