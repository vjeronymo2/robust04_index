920609 FT 09 JUN 92 / Brewery to head Romania sell-off A regional brewing company is to be Romania's first candidate for privatisation, the National Privatisation Agency announced yesterday, writes Virginia Marsh in Bucharest. Ursus Sa, which had net sales of Dollars 2.18m in 1991, would be privatised through a public offer for sale, said Mr Adrian Severin, president of the NPA.
