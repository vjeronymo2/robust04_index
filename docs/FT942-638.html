940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Pounds 808,000 turnround at Christie Christie Group, the business services company, reported pre-tax profits for the year to March 31 of Pounds 106,000, against losses of Pounds 602,000. Turnover was slightly higher at Pounds 15.2m. Earnings per share came out at 0.45p (losses 2.75p). The company said there had been a strong result at Christie & Co, but J Pinder and Venners Computer Systems incurred losses.
