BFN [Text] Brigadier General Doron Almog, the commander of the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] in the Gaza Strip, met today with Muhsin Abu-'Ayta, one of the senior Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] officials in the Gaza Strip. The meeting was also attended by the adviser on Arab affairs at the Gaza Civil Administration. Several issues were raised at the meeting, including the agreement between Fatah and Hamas and the latter's position toward the Palestinian autonomy. Brig. Gen. Almog stressed that the IDF would continue to fight against Hamas while Abu-'Ayta, for his part, asserted that the agreement between Hamas and Fatah was an internal agreement which did not relate to political matters.
