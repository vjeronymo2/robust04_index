April 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITICS' CHOICE

BLACK LIVES, WHITE LIVES Three Decades of Race Relations by Bob Blauner (University of California Press: $25; 350 pp.)

"A sociological study with a vivid face and a warm heart. Blauner was clearly won over by the men and women who shared their lives with him and his readers -- and so was I." Jonathan Kirsch
