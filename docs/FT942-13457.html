940425 FT 25 APR 94 / Observer in South Africa: Nat's anthem Dropped from the Cape Town weekend finale of the FW de Klerk roadshow was the stirring hymn Nkosi Sikelel i'Afrika - God Bless Africa. Long the unofficial anthem of black southern Africa and recently adopted as one of South Africa's two official national anthems, the hymn brought last week's Johannesburg NP rally to a poignant conclusion. But that otherwise impeccably orchestrated event ended on an embarrassing note - President de Klerk clearly did not know the words.
