930220 FT 20 FEB 93 / China's HK stance puzzles UK China's reluctance to commit itself to talks with Britain about Hong Kong's political future has left British diplomats in the colony groping for an explanation, Simon Holberton writes from Hong Kong. News that Britain and China were talking about talks was leaked to the Chinese press in Hong Kong at the beginning of the week. An announcement that the two had agreed to negotiations was expected by the end of this week. One British diplomat said: 'The general presumption is that the Chinese have differences of opinion within their own camp. Whether they are fundamental or confined to presentation and tactics we just don't know.'
