Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Crvenkovski Gives Statement"] [Text] Following the talks with European Commissioner, Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] Foreign Minister Stevo Crvenkovski stated his appreciation of the EU [European Union] and Mr. van den Broek's efforts. He pointed out the only positive sides of the whole matter are the EU engagement in the dispute and Mr. van den Broek's permanent contacts with the main mediator Cyrus Vance. "We are ready to discuss all issues, but we are not able to continue the negotiations under such a pressure of the embargo," Crvenkovski said.
