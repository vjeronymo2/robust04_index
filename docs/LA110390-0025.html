November 3, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

HEAVY CRITICISM UNFAIR TO 'CALIFORNIA LIGHT'

In spite of Cathy Curtis' flippant "Ho-Hum" critique, I went to see the exhibit and I found it beautiful!

A good art critic puts aside his or her personal preferences when judging art. Curtis does not do this. She uses ridicule and should stop her silly comments about "expecting a broadly sociological look at the connection between art and sun-dazzled land."

You owe an apology to these fine artists of another era, when beauty was not ridiculed.

MRS. ALBERT AUKERS, Laguna Niguel
