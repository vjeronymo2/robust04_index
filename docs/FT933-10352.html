930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / International Company News: GE builds up mortgage side GE Capital, the financial services subsidiary of the US conglomerate General Electric, has agreed to buy the mortgage subsidiary of Wall Street investment bank Lehman Brothers for about Dollars 70m, writes Patrick Harverson. The California-based Lehman unit, Shearson Lehman Hutton Mortgage Corporation, will add to GE Capital's existing mortgage business. The GE financial services business currently holds a portfolio of mortgages worth Dollars 42bn.
