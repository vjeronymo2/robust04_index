December 11, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: LEGAL FILE

Feldman on Probation: Actor Corey Feldman was sentenced Monday to four years' probation and fined $5,000 in a plea bargain related to two drug possession arrests.

The 19-year-old pleaded no contest to two charges of heroin possession and one of cocaine possession. In exchange, prosecutors agreed to dismiss felony charges of heroin possession for sale and transportation of heroin. Feldman agreed to finish a seven-month drug rehabilitation program, which he entered in October. While in the program, the actor will be barred from working in the entertainment industry. BETH KLEID
