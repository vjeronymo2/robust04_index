July 8, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: NAME IN THE NEWS

Louis (Bill) Daddio, a 1937 and 1938 All-American end at the University of Pittburgh, died of a heart attack at 73 in Pittsburgh. Daddio, who played professionally for the Buffalo Bills and the Chicago Cardinals, also scouted for the Denver Broncos from 1969 until 1987.
