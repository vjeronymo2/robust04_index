910419 FT 19 APR 91 / International Company News: Westinghouse revives link-up IN ANOTHER sign of the technological advance of Japanese industry, Mitsubishi Electric, a leading Japanese electronics group, and Westinghouse of the US have revised a 70 year-old cross-licensing agreement. Under the old arrangement, Westinghouse passed to Mitsubishi information about a very wide range of its products, including turbines, electrical motors and consumer appliances, for which Mitsubishi paid Y500m (USDollars 3.7m) a year in royalties. 'Westinghouse was our teacher,' said Mitsubishi Electric yesterday. The new agreement casts the two companies as equal partners.
