941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / World News in Brief: Wall Street sharply down Program selling helped push US share prices down to their lowest levels since August 22 amid uncertainty about future interest rate increases by the Federal Reserve. At the close, the Dow Jones Industrial Average was down 45.75 at 3,769.51. The Nikkei average lost about 200 points in early trading in Tokyo today, sinking below 19,000 for the first time in more than seven months. World stocks, Page 43
