November 13, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL PEOPLE

Live Entertainment Inc., a Van Nuys home video company, said it appointed Carrie DeLong as director of operations analysis.

DeLong was formerly a financial consultant at General Mills Inc.

Also, Live said it named Rasmus Van der Colff to the post of internal auditor. Prior to joining Live, Van der Colff was an accountant with an international affiliate of Peat Marwick.
