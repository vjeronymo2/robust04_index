Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Qian Qichen, Chinese vice premier and foreign minister, has said that Pakistan-China friendship is useful for and conducive to the maintenance of peace and stability in Asia. Talking to newsmen on his arrival in Islamabad yesterday evening, he expressed the hope that his visit will further promote the existing friendship between Pakistan and the PRC and expand the understanding between the two countries. He said that the Chinese Government sincerely wishes further expansion of cooperation between the two countries. Foreign Minister Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali, who received the Chinese foreign minister at the airport, stressed the need for regular exchange of views between the two countries so as to further strengthen their bilateral relations.
