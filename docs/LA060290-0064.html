June 2, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SCHULLER'S PLEA

Regarding the May 24 Morning Report item on the Rev. Robert H. Schuller saying he will end his "Hour of Power" TV ministry unless viewers send in $3.2 million to cover debts: I am reminded of the boy who cried wolf.

I have been watching "Hour of Power" since 1974 and was a telephone counselor for the ministry for five years.

This is at least the 20th time Schuller has conducted a five-week fund-raising drive in which he has threatened to pull the plug. Maybe after 1,056 telecasts, it's time that plug was pulled. Either that or let him sell commercial spots to Chevrolet, etc.

Schuller wrote a book with his son on principles of money management. Why can't he balance his own budget?

RICK ROFMAN

Van Nuys
