November 5, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

L.A. Baptist 37, Marshall Fundamental 0 -- L. A. Baptist quarterback Chris Robinson gained 120 yards in 11 carries, scored a touchdown and threw for another to lead the Knights to an Alpha League victory at Monroe High.

Four running backs each gained more than 50 yards as L. A. Baptist (7-2, 3-1) rolled up 364 rushing yards. Tailback Colyn Van Buren paced the attack with 117 yards in 12 carries and one touchdown. DANIEL NEHORAI
