BFN [Report attributed to the Ukrainian Defense Ministry Press Service] [Text] On 3 April 1994, Admiral Eduard Baltin, commander of the Black Sea Fleet, was brought to the Sevastopol Navy hospital with a bullet wound to the thigh. Due to the fact that the Black Sea Fleet has issued no official communique on this accident, various rumors have started to circulate. To avoid this, the Ukrainian Defense Ministry Press Service reports that as has become known, Admiral Baltin was wounded at his summer house as a result of careless handling of his gun.
