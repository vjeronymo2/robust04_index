920718 FT 18 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: Damaged ferry limps home The French Channel ferry Quiberon docked safely at Roscoff early today with more than 1,100 passengers after a crossing from Plymouth lasting more than 12 hours during which a crewman was killed in an engine room fire. Meanwhile in the Strait of Malacca, about 65 miles south of Singapore, one crewman died when the oil tankers Radwan and Argo Hebe collided and caught fire.
