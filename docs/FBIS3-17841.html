Language: English Article Type:BFN ["News Release"] [Text] Tallinn, March 29 -- Estonia's government thinks Herkki Haldre, the director general of state-owned Estonian Radio should be dismissed, Prime Minister Mart Laar told a news conference on Tuesday [29 March]. The government supports the bill presented by the parliament's culture commission on dismissing the director general of the Estonian Radio, said Laar. The chairman of the parliamentary commission presented the bill to Riigikogu [parliament] last Thursday after state auditors found "violations of finance discipline" in the radio.
