Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The secretary of the General People's Committee for Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation has summoned the ambassadors of the UN Security Council permanent members, accredited in the Great Jamahiriyah; he told them of the Great Jamahiriyah's outrage at the wanton massacre carried out by the Zionist occupiers inside the al-Ibrahimi holy shrine against worshippers, the sons of the Palestinian Arab people. The brother secretary of the General People's Committee for Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation has asserted the need for the UN Security Council to impose harsh sanctions on the Zionist entity, not furnish it with any weapons and to impose a blockade against it. He stressed, moreover, that if the Council failed to take any of the abovementioned measures, such a failure would be seen an encouragement for the Israelis to perpetrate further massacres and annihilate the Palestinian Arab people. The brother secretary has asked the ambassadors to convey the abovementioned protest to their respective governments.
