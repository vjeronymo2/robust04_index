920424 FT 24 APR 92 / Japanese guilty on Iran arms A Japanese company and four former officials were found guilty yesterday of illegally exporting missile parts to Iran, Reuter reports from Tokyo. Japan Aviation Electronics Industry was fined Y5m (Pounds 21,140), a company spokesman said. The former officials, including ex-president Yukio Kaito, received two-year prison sentences suspended for three years. The company was accused both in Japan and the US of repairing US-licensed missile parts for Iran during its 1980-88 war with Iraq.
