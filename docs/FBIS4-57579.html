BFN [Text] The National Civil Defense General Staff announces that, as a result of the rainfall in the eastern provinces, which intensified yesterday, rivers and streams have risen and are flooding low areas. According to information received at 1100 GMT on 20 May, the Provincial Defense Councils of Santiago de Cuba, Granma, and Guantanamo remain alert, as do a number of municipal and area defense councils in the eastern provinces. As of this hour, 5,468 people have been evacuated to the homes of friends and relatives and 343 people are in shelters. Also, 6,540 animals have been moved to safer areas. Thirty-four houses were completely destroyed and another 300 are partially damaged. In Taguasco Municipality, Sancti Spiritus Province, five houses were partially damaged by the intense rainfall. The damage to crops has yet to be evaluated. No loss of human life has been reported. Flood gates of some dams in this territory have been opened. The National Civil Defense General Staff reminds the population in the affected provinces of the need to keep abreast of the situation through the weather forecasts and to comply with the measures taken by the defense councils in each territory.
