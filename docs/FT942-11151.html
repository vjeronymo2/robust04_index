940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Investors took profits in subdued trading as political nervousness continued to dominate activity ahead of the delayed final election result. The overall index fell 22 to 5,231, industrials 26 to 6,349 and golds 40 to 1,819. Anglos bucked the trend, adding R1 at R220, while De Beers slipped R1.75 to R105.25.
