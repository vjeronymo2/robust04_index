931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Keystone assets jump Net asset value per share of the Keystone Investment Company surged over the 12 months to September 30 1993 from 434.87p to 589.74p. Net revenue increased to Pounds 2.92m (Pounds 2.16m), giving earnings per share of 20.49p (15.14p). The dividend is stepped up to 15.5p (15p) with a final of 10.5p (10p).
