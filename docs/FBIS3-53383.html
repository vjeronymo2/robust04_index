Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas received in the economic and commercial capital, Aden, this morning the ambassadors of the EC member states to our country. They reviewed our country's relations with the EC member states, efforts by the political forces dialogue committee to deal with the current crisis, the positive and important results of the committee, the appropriate solutions to all the problems that led to the crisis, ways to prevent similar crises in the future, and efforts to go ahead with building the institutions and bodies of the Yemeni state. The EC member states' ambassadors stressed their countries eagerness to reinforce Yemeni unity and democracy, as well as the importance of solving the crisis through dialogue. They also praised the important results that the political forces dialogue committee has achieved. The prime minister then expressed his appreciation for the EC member states' position, their support for Yemen's unity, and their eagerness to achieve security and stability. He also praised the EC's appreciation for current efforts in the dialogue among the political forces and stressed the importance of promoting cooperation between Yemen and the EC member states in order to achieve mutual goals.
