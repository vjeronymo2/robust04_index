BFN [Text] Moscow, April 29, RIA -- Yuriy Yarov and Brigita Smengerova, vice-premiers of Russia and Slovakia and co-presidents of the Intergovernment Commission for Trade, Economic, Research and Technological Cooperation, met in Moscow today to discuss payment prospects for Soviet and Russian debts to Slovakia, Russian oil deliveries and nuclear research and industrial cooperation. The conferees exchanged opinions on the political and economic situation and specified both countries' stances on bilateral trade and economic cooperation. They also discussed organisational matters for the nearest session of the intergovernment commission, due in Moscow.
