930108 FT 08 JAN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables SEVILLE ORANGES are this week's best buy at 35-45p a lb, along with other citrus fruit such as lemons at 15-25p each (15-25p) and white-fleshed grapefruit at 22-28p each (22-28p), reports FFVIB. Apples remain plentiful, chose from English Cox at 25-35p a lb (40-45p), French Granny Smith at 35-40p a lb (35-40p) and homegrown Bramley cookers at 28-35p a lb (30-40p). Supplies of onions are ample with English varieties at 15-20p a lb (15-20p) and Dutch pickling onions at 20-25p a lb (20-25p). Abundant supplies of tomatoes are priced at 60-70p a lb (75-85p).
