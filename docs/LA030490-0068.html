March 4, 1990, Sunday, South Bay Edition

MORNINGSIDE HAS A POSITIVE SIDE

I am a very concerned freshman who attends Morningside High School in Inglewood.

Although I did not participate in the protest, I feel very frustrated that the media has brought primarily negative attention to our school.

I understand that our school has many problems. They shouldn't be ignored, but the media makes it seem that Morningside is so bad that students fear walking from class to class. I'm sure Los Angeles Times readers think bad things about us. The school has many positive points too.

Many parents may have considered sending their children to Morningside. However, I'm sure they've lost interest now. Please present both sides of the picture in further newspaper articles about our school.

TANISHA BAISLEY

Inglewood
