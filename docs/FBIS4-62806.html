BFN [Text] Oil production has gone down here in Russia by close to 16 percent as compared to the first four months of last year. The January through April output amounted to 98.4 million tonnes. Primary refining went down by 14.9 percent and the output of gasoline by 12 percent to 8.8 million tonnes, but oil exports to countries outside the Commonwealth increased by 7.9 percent to reach 28 million tonnes. There was 12 percent less coal extracted and 1.5 percent less of natural gas, but 2.6 percent more gas was exported to the West.
