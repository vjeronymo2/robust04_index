Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] on 28 March, UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] armed forces repelled yet another attempt by Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola-Labor Party troops to occupy Amboiva, a UNITA-controlled area southeast of Vila Nova do Seles. The commune was defended during clashes that went on throughout the night. The enemy suffered heavy human and material losses. Our correspondent in the area reports: War materiel captured by UNITA includes one RPG-7, one 60-mm mortar, thousands of rounds of ammunition, and hundreds of shells. The enemy, which abandoned 21 People's Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola soldiers killed, fled in disarray.
