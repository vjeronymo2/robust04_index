August 15, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

SIDELINES;

NCAA PENALIZES U. OF COLORADO

The University of Colorado has been penalized by the NCAA for a minor recruiting violation that will reduce the school's football recruiting manpower for six weeks.

Coach Bill McCartney said one of his assistants signed a player nine minutes too early last February. Under the NCAA rules, high school players are permitted to begin signing national letters of intent at 8 a.m.
