November 17, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

LITTLE FROM LITTLE

Jess Bravin's comedy review ("Rich Little Makes a Weak Impression," Nov. 13) was "right on target."

With an audience composed of citizens who worked diligently to raise funds for some 40 charities, he should be ashamed of his performance.

In his rote presentation of "old" material, not once did he seem in contact with his audience. His eyes were closed most of the time. I sincerely doubt he could find a valid excuse for such a dismal performance.

Bravin was indeed correct about Julie Budd -- she was absolutely marvelous and, in addition, donated funds from the sale of her new album to the buyer's charity choice.

Rich Little should have stayed home.

LYNN QUIGLEY

Monarch Beach
