December 7, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

GABOR UNSURE HOW TO PLAY VEGAS

Actress Zsa Zsa Gabor has announced plans to play Las Vegas over the New Year's holiday, but she's unclear as to what she'll be doing.

"Don't expect me to sing, don't expect me to do anything and then you'll be happy," she told a news conference Thursday. "Really, I'm too rich to work too hard."

Gabor teamed with Vegas World Hotel owner Bob Stupak to announce her appearance at the resort's Galaxy Showroom Dec. 28-Jan. 2.
