June 17, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

FUROR OVER GOP MEMO ON FOLEY

It was my belief that with the dirty tactics used by the Republican National Committee officials, which were so successful in getting Bush elected, that they would be satisfied with their accomplishment.

It now seems that the chairman of the committee is obsessed with innuendo and rumormongering type of political tactics.

Bush would do a great service to the party and country by firing the GOP chairman who thinks that scumbag tactics are what politics is all about.

FELIX CHALDU

North Hollywood
