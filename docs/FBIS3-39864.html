Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of Husamettin Cindoruk, speaker of the Turkish Grand National Assembly] [Text] Husamettin Cindoruk, speaker of the Turkish Grand National Assembly, has declared that the European Parliament demand to set up a mission of foreign observers to follow the election campaign in a certain region in Turkey is unacceptable. Cindoruk sent a letter to the president of the European Parliament following the Parliament's decision on the subject. In his letter, Cindoruk noted that the decision by the European Parliament blatantly discriminates against the various regions in Turkey and ignores the maturity of democratic processes. Cindoruk remarked that there is no logical reason whatsoever to rely on observers to secure a just and free election atmosphere during the upcoming local elections.
