Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn received Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat upon his arrival in Amman this afternoon on a short working visit for talks on the peace process in light of the latest developments. His excellency was also received by Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, prime minister; al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, Royal Hashemite Court chief; his majesty the king's political adviser; and his majesty's advisers. He was also received by Palestinian Ambassador in Amman al-Tayyib 'Abd-al-Rahim and PLO Executive Committee member Yasir 'Amr. 'Arafat is accompanied by Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, PLO Executive Committee member, and Akram Haniyah, adviser to President 'Arafat for occupied territories affairs.
