920619 FT 19 JUN 92 / Menem pushes for second term THE campaign to amend Argentina's constitution and allow President Carlos Menem to seek a second term formally begins today in his home province of La Rioja, with a conference of Peronist party provincial legislators, John Barham reports from Buenos Aires. Mr Menem, who is near the middle of his six-year term, has forcefully instructed Peronist party leaders to fight for an amendment, while claiming in public that he is not particularly interested in re-election.
