BFN [Text] Wuhan, June 27 (XINHUA) -- China has started survey and design projects for the construction of its first express railway, to run 1,300 km from Beijing to Shanghai. The survey and design work is being done by the No.4 Survey and Design Institute of the Ministry of Railways, and is expected to be completed at the end of this year. The institute is now working on the plans for the 631 km section from Xuzhou in Jiangsu Province to Shanghai.
