931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Davenport Knitwear advances by 62% Davenport Knitwear, the Leicestershire-based knitted fabric and garment manufacturer, yesterday reported a 62 per cent rise in interim profits. On turnover of Pounds 4.47m (Pounds 3.54m), the pre-tax line for the half year to June 30 rose to Pounds 607,000 (Pounds 374,000). Earnings per share were 23.45p (14.46p).
