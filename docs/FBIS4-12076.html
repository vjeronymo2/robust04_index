BFN [Excerpts] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, the Yemeni Presidential Council chairman, arrived in Doha Airport at 1230 today on a visit to the sisterly State of Qatar. His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of the State of Qatar, led those who received brother President Salih. [passage omitted on reception ceremony] President Salih made a statement to the press in which he said: I am pleased to visit the sisterly State of Qatar and to meet with my brother His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of Qatar. During this visit, we will discuss several issues that concern our warm fraternal relations and the spheres of cooperation between our two sisterly countries. I am satisfied with the continuous progress in these relations, which reflects the aspirations of the two fraternal peoples. Discussions will also deal with regional developments and issues that concern the region and the Arab nation. During this visit, I will brief my brother Shaykh Khalifah on developments in our country and the efforts made to overcome the political crisis that is endangering unity and stability in our country. We appreciate the efforts the brothers are making toward bolstering Yemeni unity in its capacity as a pan-Arab achievement that should be preserved in order to entrench peace and stability in the region. I express the appreciation of our country -- its leadership, government, and people -- for our brothers in Qatar, led by His Highness Shaykh Khalifah, for their fraternal positions toward our country, and their pan-Arab eagerness to safeguard the Yemeni unity. We appreciate the Qatari brothers' support for development in Yemen, which is something that demonstrates the depth of warm fraternal bilateral relations. [passage omitted]
