940219 FT 19 FEB 94 / Spain's jobless rate hits 23.9% Spain's jobless total rose by 630,000 last year to give an unemployment rate of 23.9 per cent at the end of December, according to the national statistics institute's labour survey for the fourth quarter of 1993, Tom Burns writes from Madrid. The labour ministry said last week the number applying for unemployment benefits increased by 63,000 in January.
