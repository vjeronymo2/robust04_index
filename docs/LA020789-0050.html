February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

A showdown between milers Said Aouita and Marcus O'Sullivan was quashed when it was announced that Aouita would run 3,000 meters at Friday night's Meadowlands Invitational.

According to Aouita, the decision was made by meet director Ray Lumpp. But Lumpp said that the decision had been Aouita's.

O'Sullivan won the Meadowlands Mile last year in a time of 3 minutes 50.94 seconds, the third-fastest indoor clocking in history and only a little more than a second slower than the world record of 3:49.78, set by Eamonn Coghlan on the Meadowlands track in 1983.
