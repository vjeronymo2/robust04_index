Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Yesterday morning, the provincial party committee and government jointly held a broadcast teleconference at which they discussed major problems of Hubei's publications market and planned a province-wide crackdown on pornography and hooliganism during the Lunar New Year Festival period. The provincial party committee and government decided to unfold a two-month crackdown on pornography and hooliganism across the province during the Lunar New Year Festival period with a view to resolving problems on Hubei's publications market. [passage omitted] Provincial Vice Governor Han Nanpeng briefed the conference participants on major problems in Hubei's publications market and made arrangements for the upcoming province-wide crackdown. Wang Zhongnong, provincial party committee standing committee member and propaganda department director, laid down requirements for the upcoming provincewide crackdown, saying: Under the new situation, we should heighten understanding of the importance, urgency, and complexity of the struggle against pornography and hooliganism. Under no circumstances should we try to seek economic growth at the expense of cultural growth. We should properly handle relations between the central task of economic construction and the struggle against pornography and hooliganism, between building a socialist market economy and opposing pornography and hooliganism, as well as between building two civilizations and opposing pornography and hooliganism. We should view the publications market from a high, political, and strategic plane, and strive to push ahead with the struggle against pornography and hooliganism with perfect assurance.
