931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / Welsh export growth slows THE GROWTH in Welsh exports has slowed sharply, the latest quarterly survey by the Wales Chamber of Commerce showed yesterday. The number of manufacturers showing an increase in export sales dropped from 42 per cent in the previous quarter to 32 per cent, while the number seeing a decline increased from 19 per cent to 27 per cent. The service sector reported a similar reversal. The first projects for a Pounds 350m a year programme to regenerate the south Wales valleys were announced yesterday by Mr John Redwood, Welsh secretary. It is the second five-year programme for the valleys. Spending on the programme totalled Pounds 773m.
