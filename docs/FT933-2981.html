930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / Ofgas seeks to reassure users OFGAS, the gas industry regulator, has rejected suggestions that it has not paid enough attention to consumer interests in advocating greater competition. Sir James McKinnon, director general, told a conference in Manchester that consumers need not fear higher costs, reduced safety or a lower level of service if the government decides to end the British Gas monopoly in the 'tariff market', which includes most households.
