910507 FT 07 MAY 91 / Arts: Opera North's 1991-92 season The 1991-92 Opera North season will open with Chabrier's comic L'Etoile (in English), conducted by Jean-Yves Ossonce and produced by Phyllida Lloyd. Three other new productions are of Mozart's Don Giovanni by Tim Albery, of Schreker's Der ferne Klang (in English) by Brigitte Fassbaender - both conducted by Paul Daniel - and of Rossini's The Thieving Magpie, by Martin Duncan with Ivor Bolton conducting. In November comes the premiere of Robert Saxton's Caritas to Arnold Wesker's libretto, with Diego Masson conducting and Patrick Mason as producer. Revivals complete the season: Mozart's La finta giardiniera, Paul Griffiths' Mozart confection The Jewel Box, Nielsen's Masquerade, Madama Butterfly, Rigoletto and Boris Godunov with John Tomlinson in the title role.
