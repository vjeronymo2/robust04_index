Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Our correspondent in al-Nabatiyah reports that the Nab' al-Safi basin, Wadi al-Akhdar, and the outskirts of 'Arab Salim came under intermittent artillery fire at dawn today. The shelling was accompanied by bursts of heavy-machine-gun fire from al-Suwayda' hill. The shelling took place following an attack launched by a resistance group against al-Suwayda' post in which rockets and automatic weapons were used.
