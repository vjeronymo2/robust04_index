BFN [Unattributed report: "Students Arrested in Iran"] [Text] London, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- Iranian universities have witnessed a number of sit-ins and protests staged by students over the past two days. Security forces have arrested a number of students accused of organizing the unrest. Hundreds of students from Tehran University began a sit-in at the Faculty of Economics in northern Amirabad Street the day before yesterday. The sit-in continued until yesterday morning. A statement issued by the Iranian opposition movement "Mojahedin-e Khalq" said that "the mullahs' repressive forces have arrested a number of students in the belief that they are the organizers of the sit-in."
