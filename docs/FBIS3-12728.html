Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres said following his talks with U.S. Undersecretary of State Peter Tarnoff that Israel is ready to come to terms with the Palestinians and supports the establishment of a strong Palestinian police. Our political correspondent Shlomo Raz says Peres' statement apparently means that Israel is prepared to restrict the number of Palestinian policemen to more than 8,000. The prime minister has recently said that this is the figure that Israel has agrees to. Regarding foreign presence in the territories, the foreign minister said Israel agrees to this according to the Declaration of Principles. In the foreign minister's opinion, it is possible to successfully conclude the negotiations with the Palestinians within one month. Our correspondent adds that the situation in Russia was also discussed in the meeting.
