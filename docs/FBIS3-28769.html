Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 9 (KCNA) -- The brief biographies of the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il have evoked lively responses worldwide. In 1993 alone, they were reported by newspapers, news agencies and radios in scores of countries including India, France, Poland, Burundi and Nepal. Foreign publications including the Afghan paper ANIS, the Jordanian paper AL CHAAB, the Egyptian paper EL MASSA and the Peruvian paper EXPRESO carried the full texts of the brief biographies, and radios and televisions of many countries including Pakistan, Lebanon, Italy and Nicaragua reported their brief biographies at a time drawing a largest number of viewers. Press media said the brief biographies of President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il are chronicles of the greatest leaders and must books for politicians in the present period. Seminars, lectures, reading, explanatory and other meetings on the brief biographies were held by chuche idea study groups and organizations for friendship and solidarity in Madagascar, Tanzania, Guyana, Uganda and other countries. Last year the brief biographies in nine languages were widely disseminated among figures of broad strata in some 80 countries including the Zimbabwean, Iranian and Burkinabe presidents and prime ministers. The Nigerian head of state who is concurrently commander-in-chief of Armed Forces and political, public, academic and press figures of different countries, upon reading the brief biography of President Kim Il-song, highly praised him as a great thinker and theoretician who founded the immortal chuche idea, as a legendary hero, a distinguished leader of the present era and a veteran leader of the international communist movement. After reading the brief biography of Comrade Kim Chong-il the general secretary of the People's Unity Party (Unionist) of Jordan and other figures of various countries highly lauded him as a great man of the century, a genius of creation and construction, the great sun with the noblest human love and ever-victorious iron-willed brilliant commander.
