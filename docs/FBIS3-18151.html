Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Finland's minister of foreign affairs, Heikki Haavisto, has deplored the massacre of Muslim worshippers in Hebron in the occupied West Bank. In a statement issued on Friday [25 February], Foreign Minister Haavisto called the massacre a shocking act and expressed the hope that the act of violence would not endanger the Middle East peace process. He said that Finland expressed its deep condolences for the loss suffered by the Palestinians. Mr. Haavisto also said that he joins in the widespread hope that the shocking act will not encumber the peace process or the negotiations between Israel and the PLO.
