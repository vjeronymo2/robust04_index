941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / International Company News: Industrie Natuzzi Industrie Natuzzi, the fast-growing manufacturer of Italian furniture, is buying 40 per cent of Creazioni Ellelle, a maker of leather and fabric upholstered furniture, for about L3bn (Dollars 1.9m), Reuter reports. The acquisition is expected to close in January and new shares will be issued in connection with a resulting capital increase. Creazioni Ellelle recorded net sales of about L7bn in 1993 and has about 70 employees.
