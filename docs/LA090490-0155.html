September 4, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

JOHN WAYNE AIRPORT;

TRAVEL TIPS

"Preparing for your flight as you would an important meeting can help you get a grip on your worst fears."

Jeffrey Kasdorf, PhobiaCare Treatment Center.

"One blouse works with two or three outfits. resist the temptation to throw in something at the last minute."

Kitty Leslie, Fashion Director.

"When using the airport, be aware of what's going on around you. The most common crimes are 'distraction thefts.' ".

Lt. Ken Lohrey, Sheriff Department's Airport Bureau.

"To reduce that sluggish feeling you can do stretching exercise in your airplane seat that won't bother your neighbors."

Dr. Geoffrey Ricchio, Chiropractic Rehabilitation Center.
