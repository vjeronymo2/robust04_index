930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Ex-MP to fight Christchurch seat MR Robert Hayward, the former MP for Kingswood who lost his seat in last year's election, was last night selected as the Conservative candidate for Christchurch. The by-election, in the seat which is under threat in spite of the Tories' 23,000 majority last year, is expected in July.
