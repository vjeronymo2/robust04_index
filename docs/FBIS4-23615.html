BFN [Text] We have learned that in Kinshasa seven candidates--seven candidates have presented themselves for election as prime minister of the transition. Of these seven candidates, the name of Etienne Tshisekedi wa Mulumba does not appear on the list. All candidates were given 48 hours from 11 June to campaign for their election. The elections themselves will take place tomorrow, 13 June 1994, at the level of the High Council of the Republic, the transitional parliament, which will hold a collective plenary session (?for) the seven [word indistinct] candidates from the moderate opposition.
