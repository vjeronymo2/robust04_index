941221 FT 21 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Kay's Pounds 4.8m for Corridor deal Kay's Food Group, the meat processor, is paying up to Pounds 4.84m in its reverse takeover of Corridor Group, the food manufacturer and distributor. An initial payment of Pounds 4.07m will be satisfied by the issue of 81.4m shares, representing 47 per cent of the enlarged equity. There will be a further profit-related payment to a maximum of Pounds 775,000 satisfied by up to 30m shares, or 14.7 per cent of the enlarged equity. Kay's is also raising Pounds 615,000 gross by a placing of Pounds 12.3m shares at 5p. The shares are suspended at 4 1/2p.
