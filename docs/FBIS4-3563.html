BFN [Text] Beijing, April 22 (XINHUA) -- Wan Guoquan, vice-chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, met Sa'd-Al-din Wahbah, vice-president of the Egyptian Writers Union, here this afternoon. Wan briefed him on China's reform, opening and economic development. Sa'd-Al-din, a famous Egyptian playwright and political writer, is a guest of the Chinese Writers' Association.
