921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Allied-Lyons sells Muswell's chain Allied-Lyons is to sell its chain of Muswell's continental style cafe-bars to Priorywood Holdings. Terms of the deal were not disclosed. A total of 28 Muswell sites will be sold. Allied will retain eight outlets and convert them to other brands. Mr Tony Trigg, chief executive of Allied-Lyons Retailing, said that the company had decided after a strategic review to focus resources on two core areas: pubs and mainstream branded restaurants. Mr Bob Sneesby, chairman of Priorywood, which operates a chain of restaurants, said that Muswell's was 'a very good fit with our outlets and trading activity'.
