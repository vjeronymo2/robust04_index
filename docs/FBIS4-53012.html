BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 15 (KCNA) -- Papers here today dedicate articles to the 20th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Kingdom of Nepal (May 15, 1974). NODONG SINMUN says that over the past two decades, the friendly and cooperative relations between the two peoples have developed favourably, which has been conducive to the building of a new life in the two countries. The paper further says: Today the Nepalese people are working hard to defend the sovereignty and territorial integrity of the country and develop the national economy. The Kingdom of Nepal is striving to strengthen international cooperation, maintaining an independent stand and the principle of the non-aligned movement in external relations. The Korean people are sincerely rejoiced over the achievements made by the Nepalese people in the building of a new life. Further developing the friendly and cooperative relations between Korea and Nepal is beneficial not only to the building of a new society in the two countries but also to the building of a new Asia independent and prospering. MINJU CHOSON says the Korean people will as ever make strenuous efforts to develop friendship and cooperation with the Nepalese people.
