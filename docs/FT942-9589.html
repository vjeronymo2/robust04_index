940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / Finance and the Family: Fidelity's cash withdrawal card - At a glance Fidelity is adding a cash withdrawal card to its cash unit trust, which invests in the money markets where banks and other financial institutions lend and borrow. The card is provided by Clydesdale bank and can be used in Midland, NatWest, TSB and Link cash withdrawal machines. It allows daily withdrawals of up to Pounds 200 with no minimum. The card is only available to those with Pounds 5,000 or more invested in the fund. The initial charge of Pounds 5 is being waived until May 31.
