BFN [Text] Damascus, May 26, IRNA -- Iran and Syria Wednesday [25 May] discussed ways to upgrade bilateral ties and reviewed matters of mutual interest. The talks were held here between Iran's Ambassador to Syria Hojjat ol-Eslam Mohammad Hasan Akhtari and Syrian Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi. During the meeting Hojjat ol-Eslam Akhtari submitted a message to the Syrian premier from Iranian First Vice-President Hasan Habibi for an official visit to Iran.
