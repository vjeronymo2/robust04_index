940512 FT 12 MAY 94 / Date set for Cleveland change Cleveland's four district councils are to become unitary authorities on April 1 next year, leaving elections until the following month, Mr David Curry, local government minister, announced yesterday. The decision is subject to the outcome of Cleveland County Council's application for a judicial review of the proposals. Mr Curry said the arrangement reflected the 'particular circumstances in Cleveland' and did not set a precedent for any future cases.
