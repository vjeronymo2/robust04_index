921231 FT 31 DEC 92 / New PM for Bulgaria Bulgaria's parliament ended a two-month political crisis yesterday by electing Mr Lyuben Berov as prime minister and approving his cabinet, Reuter reports from Sofia. Mr Berov, 67, an economic advisor of President Zhelyu Zhelev, was nominated by the Movement for Rights and Freedoms (MRF) representing Bulgaria's 1m ethnic Turks, after two previous attempts to elect a prime minister had failed.
