930227 FT 27 FEB 93 / Russian shipping chief is arrested The head of one of Russia's biggest state-owned shipping companies has been arrested in the first high-level crackdown since President Boris Yeltsin declared war on crime and corruption last month, reports Leyla Boulton in Moscow. Mr Viktor Kharchenko, head of the Baltic Shipping Company in St Petersburg, is accused of stealing at least Dollars 30,000 from the company.
