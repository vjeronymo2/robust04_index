BFN [Text] Swiss Foreign Minister Flavio Cotti met his Greek counterpart, Karolos Papoulias, and discussed the situation in the former Yugoslavia, The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia [FYROM], Cyprus, and bilateral relations. Cotti, who is officially visiting Greece as the head of a diplomatic delegation, met Alternate Foreign Minister Theodhoros Pangalos this morning. The Swiss foreign minister described the Greek Presidency's preparation for the accession of the four countries to the European Union [EU] as excellent and outstanding. He said he hopes negotiations for his country's accession to the great European family will begin while Greece is holds the presidency.
