920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / UK Company News in Brief AUTOMATED SECURITY (Holdings) has acquired Pounds 2.4m nominal of 9.5 per cent convertible capital bonds 2006 of ASH Capital Finance (Jersey). The bonds will be cancelled, leaving Pounds 56.59m in issue. ***** BELLWINCH: Of the 12.7m shares available under the open offer to shareholders, 2.2m have been applied for. ***** CHEMEX INTERNATIONAL: Of the 58.9m ordinary shares offered by way of rights, 41.5m (70.4 per cent) were subscribed for by shareholders or their renouncees. ***** INCHCAPE reported that 89.3 per cent of its recent rights offer had been taken up. ***** REED INTERNATIONAL subsidiary IPC Magazines has formed a joint venture publishing company in Germany with Burda, the German publisher. The new company, MK Verlag, in which both parties have a 50 per cent holding, will be based in Munich. ***** SUTCLIFFE SPEAKMAN has sold the engineering fabrication division of Sutcliffe Croftshaw for about Pounds 688,000. The division incurred a loss of Pounds 500,000 for the nine months ended December 31 1991, principally because of lack of orders. ***** YOUNG GROUP: Of the offer of convertible preference shares, applications were received for 73.6 per cent. Mr Robert and Mr Alan Young renounced entitlement to 3.02m shares, so allowing 3i Group and Hambro European Ventures to become shareholders.
