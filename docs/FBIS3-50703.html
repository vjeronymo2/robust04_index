Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Tuxtla Gutierrez (Mexico), 5 Jan (EFE) -- The Mexican Government today asked the rebel Zapatista National Liberation Army (EZLN) to establish a dialogue and end the armed struggle, but the guerrillas have refused. Eloy Cantu Segovia, the sole spokesman for the Mexican Government in Chiapas, today reiterated the authorities' resolve to establish a dialogue on the condition that the rebels lay down their weapons. In response,the guerrillas sent a brief message to the local television station rejecting any type of negotiation with the government and announcing that the armed movement will continue.
