921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / Observer: Biter bit It's not even a dog's life any longer for poor old Ben, erstwhile chief barker at Wern Gounsel Farm in Pandy, Wales. His kennel has been taken over by a ewe called Daisy who, having been adopted as a pet lamb a year ago, has grown up convinced she is a fellow-hound. 'Now she won't stay in the field with the other sheep,' says her owner Jayne Shields. 'When it rains she grabs Ben by his ear and pulls him out of his kennel so that she can shelter.'
