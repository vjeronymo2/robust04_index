940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / Pounds 2.16bn capital issues in August New capital issues worth Pounds 2.16bn were announced by the financial and corporate sector last month, with a total of Pounds 851m denominated in sterling, Bank of England figures showed yesterday. Gross issues by UK borrowers totalled Pounds 2.52m, the Bank said. After redemption net issues totalled Pounds 1.59bn. The largest issue announced was Dollars 999m for Aircraft Lease Portfolio Securitisation in nine tranches of fixed-rate bonds and floating-rate notes. The biggest share issue was the last instalment for Pounds 200m for Kleinwort European Privatisation Investment Trust. Halifax Building Society and HMC Mortgage Notes 12 announced floating-rate notes of Dollars 500m and Pounds 210m respectively and fixed-rate bond issues were made by British Telecom Finance for Pounds 300m and Dollars 375m, and by Halifax Building Society and European Investment Bank for Pounds 200m each.
