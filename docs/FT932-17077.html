930402 FT 02 APR 93 / Technology: Giving computer staff a boost - Worth Watching With the proliferation of personal computers the role of the traditional data processing department is increasingly coming under scrutiny. One particular problem, says Bob Zawacki, professor of management and international business at the University of Colorado, is that computer staff are highly motivated, but poor at the social skills needed to handle customers. Zawacki's research is being used by KPMG Management Consulting as a basis for its People Performance service, which aims to improve the performance of computer staff by up to 30 per cent by assessing their performance against a list of indicators.
