BFN [Text] Cairo, 6 Apr (MENA)--Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat today conferred with Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana shortly before Solana left Cairo. The meeting dealt with developments in the peace process and the position of Israel, which is placing obstacles before the process. The meeting also touched on the Spanish role in implementing Security Council Resolution 904. The meeting was attended on the Palestinian side by PLO Executive Committee member Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh; Sa'id Kamal, Palestinian ambassador in Cairo; and Zuhdi al-Qidrah, the Fatah representative in Cairo. On the Spanish side, the meeting was attended by the Spanish ambassador in Cairo and some advisers.
