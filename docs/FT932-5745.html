930602 FT 02 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Redland completes Pounds 10m disposal Redland has completed the sale of Kent-based Tilmanstone Brickworks to Desimpel, the Belgian industrial company, for Pounds 10m. The disposal completes all the divestments required by the Office of Fair Trading at the time of Redland's acquisition of Steetley last year.
