931222 FT 22 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Art market shows big sales rise The international art market improved significantly in 1993 judging by figures from Sotheby's and Christie's, the world's leading auction houses. Sotheby's registered sales during the year of Dollars 1.32bn (Pounds 879m), a 17 per cent gain in dollar terms and 31 per cent in sterling. Business improved in the autumn, with sales of Dollars 833m ,a 24 per cent improvement in dollars and 32 per cent in sterling. Christie's reported 1993 sales figures of Dollars 1.1bn ,a 14 per cent sterling rise and a 3 per cent gain in dollars during the year. Its business also picked up in the autumn, with a turnover of Dollars 531m (Pounds 357m), 14 per cent better in sterling but a 9 per cent improvement in dollars, the international currency of valuable works of art.
