940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Chinese move for Schroders Schroders, the UK merchant bank, has received permission to set up an office in mainland China, to be located in Shanghai, writes Norma Cohen. Schroders thus joins the growing list of UK securities firms and merchant and commercial banks which are seeking a toehold in the rapidly growing business of financing China's industrial expansion. Barings, Smith New Court and NM Rothschild are also establishing offices in China.
