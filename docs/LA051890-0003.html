May 18, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

LUJAN AND SQUIRRELS

There they go again. The environmentalists have placed another animal on the endangered species list.

It is called the red squirrel. I wonder how much longer it will be before the mosquitoes, flies, ants and cockroaches are on their list. It seems like the environmentalist won't be satisfied until the animals and insects are in complete control and we are all out of work.

FREDERICK D. MULLEN

Upland
