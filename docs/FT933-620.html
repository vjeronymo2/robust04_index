930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / UK Company News: British Empire Secs Pounds 15m debenture British Empire Securities and General Trust is raising Pounds 15m via the issue of debenture stock 2023. Investors may subscribe for the stock at a margin of 0.8 per cent over the gross redemption yield of Treasury 8.75 per cent 2017. The issue price will be payable in full on issue.
