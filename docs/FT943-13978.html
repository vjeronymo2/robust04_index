940716 FT 16 JUL 94 / Finance and the Family: Smaller companies sluggish - At a Glance Smaller company shares responded sluggishly to the general recovery in the UK stockmarket. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) was 0.6 per cent higher at 1616.38 over the week ending July 14. Over the same period the FT-SE-A All Share Index has risen 2.6 per cent. But smaller companies have still held up better over the year to date.
