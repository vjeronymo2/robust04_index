940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / Minister quits after wife's death Lord Caithness, minister for aviation and shipping, resigned from the government last night. His wife, the Countess Diana, was found dead at the family home in Oxfordshire on Saturday. He said last night: 'Following the death of my wife, I have told the prime minister that I wish to resign from the government in order to devote more time to my children.' Thames Valley Police said that officers called to the family home by Lord Caithness discovered his wife's body in a bedroom. 'No one is being sought in relation to this tragic death,' a police statement said.
