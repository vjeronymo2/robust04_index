Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Petrovac, 10 Jan (TANJUG) -- Fierce clashes in Cazin Krajina continued last night and today between members of the Muslim Army loyal to Izetbegovic and supporters of Fikret Abdic, local Muslim sources have announced. The same sources add that the forces of the [pro-Abdic] autonomous province of western Bosnia [APZB], using all available means, have taken the village of Trzac [northwest of Cazin] and inflicted great losses on the army loyal to the Sarajevo authorities. Izetbegovic's forces, using infantry and artillery weapons, are trying to break through the demarcation lines and infiltrate inside the APZB, but are not succeeding. The [pro-Abdic] Western Bosnian news agency, ZBIA, reports that both sides have suffered a large number of dead and injured.
