BFN [By ITAR-TASS string correspondent Marina Ryklina] [Text] Moscow May 16 TASS -- Russian Ministry for Emergencies sponsored a flight of an IL-76 aircraft from Moscow to Yerevan with humanitarian aid for Armenian Russian-speaking population. Several Armenia-based Russian communities applied earlier to the Russian Government for help. The ministry prepared for delivery 10 tonnes of flour, five tonnes of baby food and 12 tonnes of canned meat. After the plane landed at the Zvartnots airport in Yerevan, the humanitarian supplies were taken to the Russian Embassy in Armenia, where they will be distributed to the needy via the Russian communities' structures.
