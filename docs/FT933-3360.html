930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / International Company News: Cashflow slides 12% at Constantia CONSTANTIA, the diversified Austrian packaging and board group, said that its cashflow slid 12 per cent to Sch440m (Dollars 39m) in the first half on sales up 1.7 per cent to Sch6bn, writes Ian Rodger in Vienna. It forecast a cashflow of Sch800m for the year, the low end of a range indicated in May, and 16 per cent lower than last year.
