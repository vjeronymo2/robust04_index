920822 FT 22 AUG 92 / Markets (At A Glance): New postal account from C&G Cheltenham & Gloucester launches a new postal account today, the London deposit account, which is a tiered version of the London Share Account. Investors can choose to have interest paid monthly though this will be at a lower rate than annual interest. The minimum transaction amount is Pounds 250. The account is advertised as instant access but withdrawals will be subject to a penalty of seven days interest. The minimum deposit is Pounds 5,000, compared with Pounds 2,500 in the Share Account. There are three tiers. Interest rates start at 9.6 per cent gross (annual) and 9.11 per cent (monthly) and the top rate is 10.2 per cent gross on Pounds 25,000 or more (annual) and 9.66 per cent (monthly).
