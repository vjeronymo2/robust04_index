940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Fleming Intl High maintains pay-out The net asset value per share of the Fleming International High Income Investment Trust stood at 46.8p at the year ended May 31, a 10.6 per cent improvement on its level of 42.3p a year ago but marginally down from the 48.5p at which it stood six months previously. A same again final dividend of 2.5675p maintains the total at 3.5675p on earnings of 3.22p (3.99p) per share. Available revenue fell from Pounds 4.45m to Pounds 3.6m.
