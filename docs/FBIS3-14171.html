Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The United States assistant secretary of state, Ms. Robin Raphel, today held discussions with her Indian counterpart, Mr. H.S. Puri, joint secretary in the Ministry of External Affairs. According to official sources, the whole gamut of Indo-U.S. relations were discussed during the 90-minute meeting. Ms. Raphel arrived yesterday on a five-day visit to New Delhi.
