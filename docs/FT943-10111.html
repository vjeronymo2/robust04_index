940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / Books: The Damned 'The Damned': Felix Nussbaum's bleak depiction of life in Nazi Germany was completed in 1944; months later he was on board the last deportation train to succeed in leaving occupied Brussels ahead of the approaching Allies to his death at Auschwitz.
