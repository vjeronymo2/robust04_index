Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] At a report meeting held yesterday [27 February] by the provincial party committee on inspecting the work to support the impoverished in the Mashan and Yaoshan areas, provincial party Secretary Liu Fangren stressed: It is necessary to take the three actions of racking brains, cherishing deep affection, and setting to work in earnest to solve the problem of escaping poverty and becoming rich in the Mashan and Yaoshan areas. At the report meeting, a videotape on the situation in Mashan and Yaoshan was shown. Responsible comrades of the provincial committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and the provincial nationality affairs commission made a report on the history of and the practical conditions in the Mashan and Yaoshan areas, and submitted proposals on changing the conditions of these two areas. In his speech, Liu Fangren stressed: Helping the Yaoshan and Mashan areas escape poverty and become rich is of great significance. It is an issue of strategic and overall importance. It is necessary to adopt a special policy and measures, and to concentrate our efforts on helping the Yaoshan and Mashan areas escape poverty and become rich as early as possible. Those who attended the meeting included: provincial party and government leaders Chen Shineng, Long Zhiyi, Wang Siqi, Wang Guangxian, (Wang Suting), Hu Yunsheng, Huang Yao, Liu Yulin, (Wu Xueyong), Yao Jiyuan, Wang Siming, (Dong Sufen), Zhang Chaolun, Qiu Yaogao, An Diwei, (Li Yandong), (Wang Demao), (Wu Ruoqiu), Jiang Qiwen, (Mao Yupu), and Cheng Tianfu; the responsible persons of the relevant provincial departments, of the Qiannan and Qianxinan autonomous prefectures, and of Anshun prefecture, as well as of Huishui, Changshun, Wangmo, Ziyun, Pintang, and Libo counties; and others.
