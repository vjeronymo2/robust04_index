BFN [Excerpt] Deidesheim (DPA) -- On Thursday [12 May] at noon, Chancellor Helmut Kohl (Christian Democratic Union) and Russian President Boris Yeltsin arrived for a private visit to the chancellor's Palatinate homeland. In the wine town of Deidesheim, they were welcomed by a cheering crowd. When the last Russian soldier withdraws from Germany on 31 August, real reconciliation between Russia and Germany will be initiated, said Yeltsin, who entered his name together with Kohl in the Golden Book of the town of Deidesheim.
