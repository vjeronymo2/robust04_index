BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] The Council of Ministers has decided to submit a letter to the Turkish Grand National Assembly [TGNA] requesting the extension of the Poised Hammer mandate for another six months from 30 June. Council of Ministers held a meeting under the chairmanship of Prime Minister Tansu Ciller for approximately two hours. In a statement issued after the meeting, Yildirim Aktuna, state minister and government spokesman, said only the Poised Hammer issue was discussed at the meeting. Aktuna stated that Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin gave detailed information on the Poised Hammer situation and on the latest developments in northern Iraq. [Begin Aktuna recording] Later, as a result of the assessments during the meeting and upon the recommendation of the National Security Council, it was decided to submit a letter to the TGNA requesting extension of the Poised Hammer mandate for another six months from 30 June. [end recording]
