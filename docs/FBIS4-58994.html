BFN [Text] India has expressed its desire to develop closer and stronger bilateral relations with Israel and Palestine. This view was expressed in separate meetings the vice president, Mr. K.R. Narayanan, had with the Israeli president, Mr. Weizman, and the PLO chief, Mr. Yasir 'Arafat, in Johannesburg yesterday. In his meeting with Mr. 'Arafat, Mr. Narayanan offered India's support in the reconstruction and development of Palestine after the situation settles down in the region. India has already pledged $1 million in kind during the international donors meeting in Cairo. New Delhi has also offered 15 additional training positions for paramedical staff from Palestine.
