BFN [Text] In issuing a statement, the Harakat-e Eslami-ye Afghanistan [Islamic Movement of Afghanistan] has said: This party will not remain indifferent and will take necessary action via-a-vis those individuals who for their self-interest intend to disrupt peace and security in the west of Kabul and attempt to stir up the fighting once again. Highlighting the tragic and painful events of the past two years in the west of Kabul, resulting in insecurity, destruction, and fratricide, and bringing various calamities to the people, the statement denounces every terrorist and adventurist action. The statement adds: The Harakat-e Eslami emphasizes its peace-seeking policy and supports the atmosphere of peace and understanding, and regards talks and understanding as the only way to a solution of the existing issues in the country. This statement has been issued after sporadic clashes started two days ago in Kabul city by irresponsible individuals. The responsible officials of the belligerent sides also denounced these clashes and have managed to stop this fire.
