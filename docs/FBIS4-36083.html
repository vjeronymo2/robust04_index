BFN [Text] Our correspondent reports that al-Nabatiyah residents are tense following rumors that terrorists belonging to the Faithful Resistance and Hizballah have arrived in the Iqlim al-Tuffah region from the al-Biqa' during the past two days. The residents think that the terrorists have arrived to carry out operations against the Israeli army and the South Lebanese Army [SLA] in retaliation for the kidnapping of Mustafa al-Dirani and the raid against the Hizballah training center near Ba'labakk, knowing that this will expose the residents to danger. Informed security sources told our correspondent that the Israeli army and the SLA will respond with force to any terrorist operation carried out by Hizballah terrorists and their followers.
