940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / Congress hearings open into Whitewater affair White House counsel Lloyd Cutler swears in before the House banking committee which is holding hearings on the Whitewater affair. Mr Cutler said President Bill Clinton's close aides had breached no ethical standards in discussions with government regulatory officials in connection with the savings and loan institution at the heart of Whitewater.
