940321 FT 21 MAR 94 / Company News (This Week): Banque Nationale de Paris - Other companies Banque Nationale de Paris chairman Mr Michel Pebereau will today announce the French banking group's first set of annual results since its privatisation last autumn. Investors are already braced for a fall in net profits to around FFr875m for 1993 from FFr2.17bn in 1992 and will be more interested in Mr Pebereau's comments on the prospects for BNP's recovery this year.
