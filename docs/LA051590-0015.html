May 15, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

PREP POLLS;

TUSTIN FINISHES ATOP BASEBALL POLL

Tustin High School is the top-ranked team in the final Orange County Sportswriters' Assn. prep baseball poll, becoming the seventh team in seven years to win the county's mythical championship.

Tustin (22-3) follows Laguna Hills (1989), El Dorado (1988), Esperanza (1987), Sonora (1986), Mater Dei (1985) and Capistrano Valley (1984) as county champion.
