940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / Delays found in London tax appeals More than half of all appeals against council tax bandings in Greater London had still not been resolved 16 months after the tax was introduced, a survey by the Labour-controlled Association of London Authorities has found. At the end of July 57.2 per cent of appeals across London were outstanding. Hounslow and Barking and Dagenham were the only boroughs to have resolved more than half of their appeals. The boroughs with the highest proportions of appeals outstanding were Bexley (66.3 per cent), Tower Hamlets (66 per cent) and Lambeth (65.9 per cent).
