930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Recovery at Clinton Cards A SHARP recovery was mounted by Clinton Cards, the retailer of greetings cards and associated products, in the year to January 30 with pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.31m, compared with a loss of Pounds 163,000. Group turnover rose to Pounds 80m (Pounds 70.5m) and underlying sales showed an increase of 8.2 per cent. Earnings per share emerged at 9.76p (0.96p losses) and the total dividend is increased by 13 per cent to 4.25p (3.75p) with a proposed final of 2.75p.
