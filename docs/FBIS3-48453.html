Language: English Article Type:BFN ["`Comrade Kim Chong-il Is an Illustrious Leader With Literary and Military Accomplishments and Loyalty and Filial Piety'" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, January 25 (KCNA) -- Foreign visitors to Korea speak highly of the feats and personality of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. Mady Ndao who had visited Korea in the capacity of special envoy of the Senegalese president said he keenly felt that His Excellency Kim Chong-il is the greatest thinker and theoretician of the present era and ever-victorious iron-willed brilliant commander with literary and military accomplishments. "His Excellency Kim Chong-il has steadily developed and enriched the immortal chuche idea and strengthened and developed the Korean People's Army into steel-like Armed Forces that can defeat any aggressors at one blow," he noted. Alhaji Adam Bukhari Ziblim, general secretary of the National Convention Party of Ghana, had this to say: "The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il is a great man possessed of courage, tested organising ability and iron will. I am struck with admiration at his precious feats and energetic guidance in strengthening the Korean People's Army into matchless ranks and building many monumental edifices. "The looks of the Korean people who are marching forward in soaring spirits, full of confidence in victory, convinced me that no one on earth is a match for this people." Toyohide Araki who led a delegation of the Japan Society for the Study of Kimilsongism is quoted as saying: "Dear Comrade Kim Chong-il is a great man of the great men with both literary and military accomplishments. "The world is unstinting in praising him for humbling the U.S. imperialists." P. Mabiti, an MP of Tanzania, said he was struck with wonder at the phenomenal development of Korea. "The proud achievements gained by Korea are all a shining fruition of the independent politics of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the tested, veteran leadership of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il with literary and military accomplishments and loyalty and filial piety, who is leading the revolutionary cause to victory with his matchless grit and rock-firm faith," he stressed.
