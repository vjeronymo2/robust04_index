921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Ldn & Strathclyde asset value down At August 31 net asset value per share of London & Strathclyde Trust stood at 191.7p, compared with 246.3p a year earlier. Revenue totalled Pounds 1.71m (Pounds 1.77m) and earnings per share were unchanged at 5.74p. The final dividend is 4.25p for a same-again total of 5.75p.
