931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / Observer: Empty excuse Sir Paul Nicholson, the Old Harrovian chairman of Vaux - 'we're in the booze and snooze business' - wins Observer's prize for understatement of the day. Explaining away a Pounds 116m write-down in the value of his hotels, he says that recent revelations at the troubled Queens Moat Houses, which used to be one of Vaux's biggest shareholders, 'have shown valuation as an inexact science'.
