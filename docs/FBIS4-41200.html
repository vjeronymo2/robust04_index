BFN [Unattributed report: "Three Rayons Instead of Seven"] [Text] A reorganization of the organs of executive power is taking place in St. Petersburg. It is aimed at improving the work of the rayon link and reducing the number of officials in these structures. The city's administrative division has changed: Three rayons -- Admiralteyskiy, Kurortnyy, and Tsentralnyy -- have been organized on the basis of seven rayons. Work is under way on the formation of the concept of local self-management. For this a directive from the mayor has formed a working group consisting of workers from the city hall and rayon administrations and representatives of the public.
