September 3, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

FALL SPORTS ROUNDUP;

MASTER'S BLASTS PEPPERDINE IN SOCCER MATCH, 11-0

The Master's College thumped overmatched Pepperdine, 11-0, Saturday at The Master's in a men's soccer opener for both schools.

Midfielder Randy Whitfield and forwards Chris Bingman, Rick Abasial and Chris Palm each scored two goals for Master's, which led, 5-0, at halftime.

Master's begins the season ranked among the top 30 in the National Assn. of Intercollegiate Athletics preseason poll.

WOMEN'S VOLLEYBALL

Westmont d. Master's, 15-8, 15-8 -- The Master's finished fourth in the six-team Grand Canyon Court Classic in Phoenix after suffering a two-game loss to Westmont (Santa Barbara) College.

Master's is 2-3.
