Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] [Defense Minister] Francois Leotard says France will continue to sell motorized armored vehicles and nonoffensive secondary military materiel to Taiwan. Taiwan has said it has no doubt that France will respect the contracts for deliveries that have already been signed, but it is worried about future deliveries of spare parts. Jean-Marie Le Pen says France has acted dishonorably by abandoning Taiwan to normalize its relations with China.
