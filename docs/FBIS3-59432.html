Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:CSO [Article by M.M.: "Muslims Drafted in Zagreb?"] [Text] Banja Luka -- Although yesterday Croat authorities officialy denied reports about the forced drafting of Muslims, reliable sources from the First Krajina Corps Headquarters report that 2,000 Muslims were arrested in Zagreb during the New Year's holiday and sent directly to the front lines. Arrested were men without residence papers and immigration cards. The same action was carried out in other larger Croatian cities.
