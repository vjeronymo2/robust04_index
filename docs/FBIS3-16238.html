Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Russia and the United States continue to be partners in spite of all the trials and tribulations that bilateral relations have been subjected to of late. This was stated in Vladivostok today by Foreign Minister Kozyrev after his talks with U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher. The problem of a settlement in Bosnia-Herzegovina and the situation in the Near East were also discussed during the meeting. Russia is no longer insisting on the immediate holding of a second international conference on the Near East, Andrey Kozyrev disclosed. He said that the most important thing now was to complete work on the implementation of the agreement on Gaza and Jericho.
