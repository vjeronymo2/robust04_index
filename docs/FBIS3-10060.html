Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Bolivia through 24 February: Jack Keenan, National Oil Engineering Directorate manager, has reported that proven natural gas reserves total 5 trillion cubic feet, which guarantees its supply to Brazil, Argentina and other markets. Keenan also said current oil reserves guarantee the domestic supply for the upcoming 15 years. (La Paz LA RAZON in Spanish 11 Feb 94 p A16) Pablo Zavaleta, state general accounting under secretary, has reported that 76 percent of the economic area budget is earmarked for paying the foreign debt. The Finance Ministry allocated 896.4 million bolivianos to meet the debt service in the first 10 months of 1993. (La Paz LA RAZON in Spanish 12 Feb 94 p B2) The Multilateral Investments Fund, under the jurisdiction of the Inter-American Development Bank, will grant a $2.3 million loan to various Bolivian state-owned companies to finance programs designed to promote private investments. (Madrid EFE in Spanish 2154 GMT 15 Feb 94)
