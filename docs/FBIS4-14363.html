BFN [Text] Minutes of meetings held by the General Assembly of the Syrian-Jordanian Maritime Navigation Company and the General Assembly of the Syrian-Jordanian Land Transport Company were signed at the Ministry of Transportation this evening. The minutes were signed for the Syrian side by Minister of Transportation Dr. Mufid 'Abd-al-Karim and for the Jordanian side by Minister of Transport Adib Halasah. After the signing, Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim announced the conclusion of the meetings. He said that the two companies are true witnesses of the importance of joint Arab action which President Hafiz al-Asad and King Husayn support and seek to achieve, develop, and entrench for the benefit of all the Arabs. For his part, Jordanian Transport Minister Adib Halasah expressed his hopes to continue joint action and wished the people in Syria and Jordan continuous growth and strength under the leaderships of President Hafiz al-Asad and King Husayn.
