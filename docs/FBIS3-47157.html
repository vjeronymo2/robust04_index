Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Jan. 7 (YONHAP) -- The Defense Ministry on Friday denied as "groundless" the North Korean charge that South Korea and the United States have been staging a joint war exercise threatening North Korea. The North KOREAN CENTRAL NEWS AGENCY, monitored here, claimed earlier in the day that South Korea and the United States were using more than 360 F-16 and other war planes in a bombing practice with major facilities deep in North Korea as potential targets.
