941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Creston Land Creston Land and Estates, property development and investment company, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 202,000 for the year to June 30, against Pounds 642,000 last time. Turnover rose from Pounds 6.25m to Pounds 6.46m. These are the first full-year results since the reverse takeover of Creston by Co-ordinated Land and Estates in June 1993.
