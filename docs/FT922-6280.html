920526 FT 26 MAY 92 / World News in Brief: EMS chart The chart shows the member currencies of the ERM measured against the weakest currency in the EMS's narrow 2.25 per cent fluctuation band. In practice, currencies in the EMS narrow band cannot rise more than 2.25 per cent from the weakest currency in that part of the system. Sterling, the Portuguese escudo and the Spanish peseta operate within 6 per cent fluctuation bands.
