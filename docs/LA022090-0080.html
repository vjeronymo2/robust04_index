February 20, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SIGN HERE: Want to rewrite the state Constitution? An Orange County firm now advertises that if your initiative is reasonable, it will gather the signatures to put it on the ballot within 45 days -- or your money back. . . . Bill Butcher and Arnold Forde, longtime and often controversial campaigners, say they can do it with 12 years' worth of direct mail and computer lists. . . . But at considerably more than $1 per valid signature, it can cost a bundle for the almost 600,000 signatures needed. Dana Parsons and Kristina Lindgren
