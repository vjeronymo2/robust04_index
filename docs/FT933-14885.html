930712 FT 12 JUL 93 / Observer: Pint taken Even if Manchester's bid to host the Olympics fails owing to lack of sun, the local nightlife is clearly bidding for intellectual acclaim. In September, Manchester's Institute for Popular Culture is hosting two days of egg-head discussions on the economic impact of the evening economy of cities, in which the star attraction will be a chance to 'experience Manchester's night-time economy at first hand'. Do they mean a pub crawl, by any chance?
