Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Paris, Jan 6 (AFP) -- The Bank of France's foreign exchange reserves increased by 3.4 billion francs during the last week of last year, according to information from the central bank's weekly statistics released on Thursday [6 January]. That made a total of 10 billion francs on December 30, against 6.6 billion on December 23. The reserves plunged deep into the red last summer during the European monetary crisis.
