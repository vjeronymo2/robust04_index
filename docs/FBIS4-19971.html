BFN [Text] In a message of condolences sent to his eldest daughter, President Francois Mitterrand has mentioned the "great role" played by Nixon "on the international scene." Former President Valery Giscard D'Estaing commemorated Nixon by stressing the fact that "under his presidency relations between France and the United States were excellent." Jacques Chirac, mayor of Paris and former prime minister, said that "Nixon was the man who put an end to the war in Vietnam and who started a dialogue with the communist world."
