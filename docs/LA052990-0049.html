May 29, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Steve M. Binnix has been appointed general manager of Ketema's Rodan division in Anaheim, which manufactures products for industrial and military applications. Binnix joined Ketema, a Pennsylvania-based company, in 1989 as Rodan's vice president of operations and engineering. Previously, he was president of Microcap Inc., a San Diego manufacturer of electronic components.
