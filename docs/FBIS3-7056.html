Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 23 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to the officials and employees of the Pang Ho-nim Institute and the Pyongyang general weaving equipment plant for their manufacture of new high-class plastic goods. Receiving the thanks, the employees of the units are making innovations in developing the nation's science and technology and producing light industrial goods needed for people's life.
