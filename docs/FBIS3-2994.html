Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Qiao Shi, chairman of China's National People's Congress Standing Committee, met with a delegation of the Foreign Affairs and Unification Committee of the ROK National Assembly in Beijing on 9 March. Saying that the overall Northeast Asian region can maintain its development only in a peaceful and stable environment, Qiao Shi stressed that China hopes the situation of Northeast Asia, particularly of the Korean peninsula, will be eased and stable.
