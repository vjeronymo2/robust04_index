Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 18 (KCNA) -- The New Year address of the great leader President Kim Il-song has evoked great repercussions among South Korean people of all walks of life, according to Seoul-based radio Voice of National Salvation [VNS]. Choe Ung-mo, an ex-journalist in Chongno District, Seoul, said the New Year address of respected President Kim Il-song gives a new hope and confidence to the people in the South who are yearning after the North under the politics centring on the masses as a beacon of hope. The group of traitors is resorting to vicious slanders and smear propaganda against the North in a bid to stop the minds of the people in the South from turning toward the North, but the people in the South stubbornly fight for the day when they will live under the administration of the president, looking up to the North, an earthly paradise of the working people, as a beacon of hope, he added. Yi Myong-chol, a peasant in Puchon, pointed out that the peasants in the South failed in farming because of man-made calamities and, still worse, had to give up farming because the "president" opened rice market to the Yankees. I heard the news that last year a bumper harvest visited the North under the sagacious agricultural policy of President Kim Il-song and, no doubt, this year, too, a bumper harvest will visit the North thanks to the agriculture-first policy set forth by the president, he noted, adding that the earnest wish of the peasants in the South is to do farming to their heart's content under the care of respected President Kim Il-song who is pursuing the best agricultural policy in the world. A dissident Yi Sang-o in Kwangju said the proposal for achieving reunification by means of confederacy reclarified by President Kim Il-song in his New Year address is a banner for the reunification movement that the Korean nation should hold aloft. There is no alternative but reunification by means of confederacy, the way of reunification which is acceptable to anyone under the present situation in Korea and this formula of reunification is the most fair and aboveboard and most realistic, best one, he said. He vowed to devote his wisdom and energies to the struggle to reunify the country by means of confederacy as taught by President Kim Il-song.
