Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpts] The Supreme Military Council met in an extraordinary session under the chairmanship of Prime Minister Tansu Ciller in Ankara today. In an opening statement, Ciller expressed the belief that decisions that will contribute to the development of the Armed Forces will be adopted at the meeting. She remarked that the Turkish Armed Forces fulfill their task with regard to internal security in a spirit of self-sacrifice and discharge the duties they assume abroad with a view to contributing to world peace. [passage omitted] [Begin Ciller recording] The Armed Forces constitute the most important guarantee of the country's security and our independence. Certain circles that are well aware of this fact are engaged in speculations concerning the Turkish Armed Forces -- either knowingly, because they are not friends of this country, or unknowingly. This saddens us all. Nevertheless, we know very well and we believe that our lofty nation is assessing the events in the most correct way on the basis of its boundless common sense and is aware of the motives behind them. [end recording] [passage omitted] In a statement about the Supreme Military Council meeting, Staff Colonel Dogu Silahcioglu, spokesman of the General Staff, declared that the political and military situation in light of developments in surrounding countries was assessed during the meeting. According to the statement, proposals for changes in the legal procedures concerning the Turkish Armed Forces were also discussed at the meeting.
