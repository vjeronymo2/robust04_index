September 5, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY PEOPLE

IHOP Corp., the North Hollywood owner and franchiser of more than 470 International House of Pancakes restaurants, said it named Frederick Silney vice president/finance and treasurer. Silney formerly was division manager and assistant general manager of Carnation Co.'s dairies division. IHOP also appointed J. Ken Masuda to vice president/development. Masuda, who has more than 20 years experience in real estate, will oversee IHOP's real estate and construction functions, the company said.
