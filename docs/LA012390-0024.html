January 23, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY EARNINGS: BANK REPORTS 54% INCREASE IN 4TH-QUARTER PROFITS

Santa Clarita National Bank in Valencia said its net income for the fourth quarter that ended Dec. 31 jumped 54% to $1.2 million from $778,000 a year earlier. As of Dec. 31, the bank's total assets were $258.2 million, up almost 4% from $248.1 million a year before.

Full-year profits for the bank increased 31% to $4.2 million from $3.2 million in 1988.
