January 20, 1989, Friday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Senior Bowl officials said that players in Saturday's all-star game will not be paid, for the first time in the 40-year history of the event.

Players on last year's winning team received $1,750, and the losers received $1,500. The players will continue to receive room and board and $75 a day for expenses, a game official said.
