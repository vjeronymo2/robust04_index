May 20, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

$9.2 MILLION FOR UNITED WAY

The San Gabriel Valley Region of the United Way has raised a record $9.2 million in its 1989-90 fund-raising drive.

The total represents a 7.3% increase over last year. Dr. Larry Papay, campaign chairman, said special credit goes to 400 businesses and individuals, many of them Latino and Asian Pacific, who contributed this year for the first time.

The San Gabriel Valley Region's campaign was part of the Greater Los Angeles United Way effort that raised more than $95 million. The San Gabriel Valley Region of United Way supports a local network of 65 health and human service agencies, funding programs to fight illiteracy, substance abuse and AIDS, and to help children and the elderly.
