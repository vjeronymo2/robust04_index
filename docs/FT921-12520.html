920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / Conoco gas find in North Sea CONOCO, the oil and gas arm of the Dupont group, said yesterday it had found gas in North Sea block 44/17. It estimated that the well, 110 miles off the Lincolnshire coast, might flow at a stabilised rate of between 10m cu ft and 15m cu ft a day. The company said it was evaluating further the commercial viability of the prospect. Conoco has 60 per cent of the block, Ranger Oil 20 per cent and Cairn Energy and Union Jack 10 per cent each.
