921015 FT 15 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Richmond Oil & Gas joint venture The rising gas price has helped Richmond Oil & Gas, loss-making natural resources company, forge a joint venture to revive Texas properties. The group said yesterday that two US energy companies had agreed to invest Dollars 2.2m in developing Richmond's Panhandle, Texas, operations, Richmond receiving a 30 per cent working interest through completion in 20 new wells. Earlier this year, the SFO launched an inquiry into aspects surrounding Richmond's 1989 flotation.
