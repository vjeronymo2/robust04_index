930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Regent Inns Regent Inns, the public house operator which came to the market in April, reported pre-tax profits up 47 per cent at Pounds 1.34m, against Pounds 913,000, in the year to July 3. Turnover rose to Pounds 13m (Pounds 11.9m). Earnings per share came out at 10.5p (7.8p) and a final dividend of 2.25p is proposed for a final of 4.5p. Mr Patrick Moorsom, chairman, said the results were encouraging given that the pub retail sector continued to experience tough trading.
