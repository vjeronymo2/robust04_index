941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Shaftesbury jumps Shaftesbury, the property concern, more than doubled full year pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.01m to Pounds 2.35m, helped by lower interest charges and an exceptional credit of Pounds 433,000 this time on the termination of an interest rate swap agreement. Turnover for the year to September 30 reached Pounds 9m (Pounds 8.86m), while net profits grew to Pounds 2.32m (Pounds 963,000) after a tax refund of Pounds 465,000 (Pounds 49,000 charge). Earnings per share were 5.8p (3.5p) and a dividend of 1p has been declared. The group's investment properties were valued at Pounds 77.1m - a net surplus of Pounds 4.67m. Shareholders' funds at the year end totalled Pounds 48.6m (Pounds 41.7m).
