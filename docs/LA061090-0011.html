June 10, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

EARTH FIRST

It was outstanding that the magazine would publish such an open-minded article about Earth First! It is distressing, though, to learn how much effort was put into pinning a case on Dave Foreman. I'm sure he was targeted only because he is an effective spokesman for a popular cause threatening moneyed interests. I hope nearly as much effort is being invested in investigating fraud and criminal activity in all the savings-and-loan association failures.

THOMAS E. PLISKA

Long Beach
