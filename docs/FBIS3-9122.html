Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Representatives of six political groups in Slupsk--Solidarity, the Christian-National Union, the Polish Democracy Party, the Movement for the Republic, Center Accord, and the Polish Peasant Party-Peasant Accord--have created an electoral bloc under the name "Christian-Independence Forum." The alliance is opting for the development of local self-governments and is expressing itself in favor of the decentralization of the state.
