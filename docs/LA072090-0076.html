July 20, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MIXED REVIEWS ON NIXON AND WATERGATE

Until I viewed the shining, clean-shaven image of Richard Nixon in your View section today, I had not gained full appreciation of the ancient Greek practice of political exile.

Let's find a media-proof island for the guy who brought new meaning to White House corruption and has left a legacy of mistrust which has undermined the Presidency of the United States.

PAUL R. GURIAN

Hollywood
