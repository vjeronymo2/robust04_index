940903 FT 03 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Exceptional leaves Eclipse at Pounds 0.8m Eclipse Blinds, formerly Ashley Group, announced pre-tax profits down from a restated Pounds 1.37m to Pounds 787,000 for the half year to June 30 on turnover from continuing operations of Pounds 18.4m against Pounds 16.8m. The result was after an exceptional Pounds 615,000 loss on disposal of the French operations. Operating profits of the blinds division were Pounds 2.39m - a margin on sales of 11.9 per cent and 13.7 per cent excluding discontinued businesses. The plywood and timber side achieved Pounds 179,000 (Pounds 112,000). The French disposal reduced net borrowings at the half year from Pounds 18.5m to Pounds 14.5m. Mr Hamish Grossart, chairman, said trading in the current year was good. Losses per share amounted to 0.16p (0.31p earnings).
