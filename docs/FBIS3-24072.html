Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Text of Postfaktum note] [Text] Moscow. The government of Moscow intends to allocate 14.5 million dollars in 1994 for the needs of the city law enforcement organs. This was reported at the meeting of the capital's government where, among other things, questions of the fight against crime and measures to provide material-technical assistance to the city internal affairs organs were discussed. The mayor of Moscow, Yuriy Luzhkov, spoke at the meeting and defined in this sphere "the main task for the city's government: to create a material-technical base for the GUVD [Main Administration of Internal Affairs] which will impose order in the capital."
