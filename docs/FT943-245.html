940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / Gooda Walker ruling expected Judgment in the Gooda Walker case, the biggest legal action by loss-making Lloyd's Names, is expected on Tuesday. A group of 3,095 Names, the individuals whose assets support the insurance market, is suing 71 Lloyd's agencies for Pounds 629m in losses sustained by syndicates managed by the Gooda Walker agency. Damages are likely to be the largest ever awarded in a UK legal action if the Names win their action.
