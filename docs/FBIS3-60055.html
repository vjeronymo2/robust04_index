Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Istanbul, Dec.7 (AA) -- In an operation here, narcotic teams have seized 31 kilos heroin. Five persons were detained in relation to the narcotic. A statement by the narcotics department of the Security Directorate said today that, teams acting on a tip-off, carried out an operation to the home of Aziz Celik in the Gungoren district of Istanbul. The narcotic was found in Celik's home. His interrogation led to the seizure of Makhial Altan, Kamil Yakut, Celimhan Capa and Naif Bayhan. Investigation into the case is expanded.
