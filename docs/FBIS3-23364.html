Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Security sources said that in Constantine, five terrorists who were searched for surrendered today. Security sources also said that eight terrorists had been eliminated and three others arrested between last Sunday [20 March] and Tuesday [22 March] in the capital, Tipaza and Boumerdes. The same sources announced that three armed people have attacked the National Bank of Algeria in Thenia in the Boumerdes province and run away with more than a billion centimes and over 100,000 French francs.
