930624 FT 24 JUN 93 / International Company News: Corning in agreed bid for Costar CORNING Inc, the US high-technology group, yesterday launched an agreed takeover bid for Costar, a manufacturer of laboratory products, valued at around Dollars 180m in stock. Corning, which has reached an agreement to buy 30 per cent of the Nasdaq-quoted company's stock from current shareholders, said the deal was a logical expansion of its laboratory products business. 'Costar's marketing and technology strengths are in areas that complement Corning's current plastic science products business,' said Mr Roger Ackerman, Corning's President. Costar, based in Cambridge, Massachusetts, makes a variety of disposable plastic products, filters and filtration equipment and had 1992 sales of Dollars 75m.
