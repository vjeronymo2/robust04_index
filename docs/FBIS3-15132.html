Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] As of 1 January, the Russian Armed Forces officer corps was over 84 percent up to strength. This was stated by Colonel General Yevgeniy Vysotskiy, head of the Russian Defense Ministry's main personnel administration. According to him, 34,000 fewer officers left the Army last year than in 1992. Yevgeniy Vysotskiy pointed out that at present the decline in competition when applying to enter a military educational establishment gives cause for concern. Last year, the competition ratio was 1.4 applicants for every place.
