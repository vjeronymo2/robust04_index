April 5, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COSTA MESA;

MEMBERS SOUGHT FOR DEVELOPMENT PANEL

The City Council is requesting applications from residents who want to be on the city's new ad hoc community development committee.

The seven-member panel will review how the city spends federal Community Development Block Grant money. It will also recommend new programs.

Interested residents may call Carol Jacobs at (714) 754-5140 for an application. The deadline for returning applications is April 25.
