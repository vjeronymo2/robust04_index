BFN [Text] Tirana, April 9 (ATA) -- The speaker of the Albanian Parliament, Pjeter Arbnori, met on April 8 with the head of the Turkish delegation to the Parliamentary Assembly of the Black Sea Economic Cooperation [BSEC], Mr. Ibrahim Atvinli. "It is a fact that Albania and Turkey, as two friendly peoples, have the same views regarding BSEC, Mr. Arbnori said expressing the need of a greater cooperation between the two parliaments, especially in the field of legislation. Mr. Ibrahim Atvinli said that the visit to a friendly country as Albania is of special delight to every Turk, especially now that it is following the path of democracy with devotion. In every international forum, Mr. Atvinli said, Turkey supports the foreign policy of Albania, which is an actor of peace and stability in the region. The strengthening of our parliamentary relations, he said in conclusion, will surely help the promotion of cooperation in economy and other sectors.
