Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] A confrontation has developed in the last few minutes between settlers, who had blocked the Qalqilyah-Nabulus road near the Tzofim settlement, and IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers. Our correspondent Gil Litman reports that members of the security forces have started forcibly removing Tzofim settlers who are sitting in the road, blocking it to protest what they term the deteriorating state of security in the area. Our correspondent adds that several settlers have been detained, and the IDF is now proceeding with an evacuation. A traffic jam has been created by the fracas. The IDF has declared the site a closed military zone.
