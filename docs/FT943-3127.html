940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / UK Company News: F&C High Income Net assets of Foreign & Colonial High Income Trust dropped by 7 per cent from 70.7p to 65.8p over the year to June 30. Net revenue dropped from Pounds 825,000 to Pounds 653,000 and earnings per share came to 2.18p (2.75p). The recommended final dividend is 1.5p for a total of 2.7p (2.62p).
