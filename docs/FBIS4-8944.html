BFN [Text] Belgrade, April 19 (TANJUG) - The Yugoslav Government on Tuesday [19 April] adopted its third report on war crimes committed in the lands of former Yugoslavia, which lists 186 fully and meticulously documentad new cases of gross violations of internaional law governing conduct and humanitarian issues in times of war. The Information Ministry said that the government would submit the report to the United Nations, in keeping with the Security Council's Resolution 780. Reports on other war crimes will be made as facts are established, the ministry said.
