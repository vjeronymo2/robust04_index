921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / Irish PM criticises minister's role Mr Albert Reynolds, Irish prime minister, has accused Mr Des O'Malley, his industry and commerce minister, of being 'reckless, irresponsible and dishonest' during evidence at a parliamentary inquiry into the beef industry, writes Tim Coone in Dublin. Mr Reynolds was in the witness box defending decisions he took as industry minister in providing export insurance to a number of beef companies selling to Iraq.
