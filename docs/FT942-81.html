940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Metrotect ends year with Pounds 2.36m In its first set of results since floating in June last year Metrotect Industries, the pipeline protection company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.36m on turnover of Pounds 21.5m for the 12 months to end-March 1994. The outcome compared with a profit of Pounds 2.28m on turnover of Pounds 22.6m. A proposed final dividend of 2.3p makes a total of 3.45p for the year, payable from earnings per share of 7.35p (7.89p).
