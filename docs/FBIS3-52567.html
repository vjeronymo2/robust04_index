Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, the head of state, today received a letter from his brother His Highness Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah, the amir of Kuwait, dealing with bilateral relations and issues of common concern. The letter was handed to Shaykh Sultan Bin-Zayid Al Nuhayyan, the deputy prime minister, when he met with Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad al-Sabah, Kuwaiti first deputy prime minister and foreign minister, who is visiting the United Arab Emirates [UAE] within a tour of the Gulf Cooperation Council [GCC] states. Following the meeting, Shaykh Sabah said that the letter expresses thanks and appreciation to His Highness Shaykh Zayid for UAE efforts during the GCC summits and during the occupation of the State of Kuwait and the UAE's support for the Kuwaiti people. His highness expressed the Kuwaiti amir's and people's appreciation and gratitude for the UAE, which has stood by the Kuwaiti people. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Rashid 'Abbdallah and Kuwait's ambassador Ibrahim 'Abdallah al-Mansur.
