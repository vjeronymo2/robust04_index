941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Mitsubishi - Contracts and ventures Mitsubishi yesterday said it expects to sign an agreement today with the Philippines' state-owned power concern for a 300,000KW coal power plant order. The Dollars 376m plant in Masinloc, about 150 miles north-west of Manila, is expected to be completed in 1998 with funds from the Asian Development Bank and the Export-Import Bank of Japan.
