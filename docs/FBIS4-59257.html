BFN [Unattributed report: "Algeria: Ben Bella Completes His Mediation Between Authorities and Salvation Front in Washington"] [Excerpt] Algiers, AL-HAYAH -- Reliable sources in Algiers have said that former President Ahmed Ben Bella, leader of the Movement for Democracy, has been in Washington for a week and that he met with senators and Western and Algerian figures close to the Islamic Salvation Front [FIS]. En route to the United States, Ben Bella stopped over in London, where he met with Arab and foreign figures who encouraged him to mediate between the FIS and the authorities. Well-informed sources have told AL-HAYAH that the former president's "secret mission" is represented in continuing contacts with some FIS leaders, which began in Algeria three weeks ago on orders from President Liamine Zeroual.
