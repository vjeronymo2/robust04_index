Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Frankfurt/Main (DPA) -- The Deutsche Bahn AG [German railway company] was established in Frankfurt today. Company Director Hanz Duerr and board member Diethelm Vack, as well as a representative of the German Government signed the foundation document at a lawyer's office in Frankfurt. The public limited company is owned outright by the German Government and will replace the Bundesbahn [in the west] and the Reichsbahn [in the east], which thereby cease to exist after operating for 44 and 56 years respectively. Under the agreement, the railway company will also transfer existing debts of 70 million German marks, which will now be assumed by the German Government.
