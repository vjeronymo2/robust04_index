BFN [Text]In order to prevent the possibility of future clashes in the western sections of Kabul city, a peace delegation consisting of representatives from the Islamic State of Afghanistan and representative from the Hezb-e Wahdat-e Eslami of Afghanistan [Unity Party of Afghanistan] have decided to implement a total cease-fire as from today in the western sections of Kabul city. The commission supervising cease-fire is to control the cease-fire and make sure that the roads leading to the western sections of Kabul city are open. The agreement says that the transgressors will be questioned and punished. Quoting eye-witnesses, the Bakhtar Information Agency correspondent reports that the enforced cease-fire is being fully observed today and the roads leading to western section of Kabul are open to traffic and people. The people continue to commute unimpeded.
