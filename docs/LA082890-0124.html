August 28, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: FOUNTAIN VALLEY;

WILEY TO SERVE ON CITIZENS COMMITTEE

Commissioner Betty Wiley was unanimously approved by the Planning Commission as its representative to serve on the Citizens Advisory Committee, which will help to revise the city's General Plan.

The committee will consist of 15 members from existing city boards and commissions, as well as one citizen at-large from each of the city's five geographic areas. Other members are expected to be selected in September.

The committee will meet five times, beginning in November through summer of 1991, making recommendations on land-use, conservation, safety and other issues. ROSE APODACA
