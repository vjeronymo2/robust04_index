BFN [Text] India has reiterated that lifting of UN sanctions against Iraq should be phased in accordance with Baghdad's compliance with the relevant resolutions of the UN Security Council. This was conveyed by the external affairs minister, Mr. Dinesh Singh, when the Iraqi foreign minister, Mr. Muhammad Sa'id Kazim al-Sahhaf, called on him in New Delhi yesterday. Mr. al-Sahhaf is on a two-day official visit to India. He is visiting as a special envoy of the president, Saddam Husayn, to three Asian countries including India.
