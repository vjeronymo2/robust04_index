930917 FT 17 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Britton turns in Pounds 0.52m BRITTON Group, the expanding packaging group formed last October, yesterday announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 520,000 for the first half of 1993 from a turnover of Pounds 6.39m. Mr Harry Westropp, chairman, said the results were as expected. The second half had started satisfactorily with the group benefiting from improved working capital control resulting in lower than expected interest charges. Since the period end Britton has acquired TACO, a polythene extruder, funded by a Pounds 31.4m placing and rights issue, making the group the second largest polythene extruder in the UK. Earnings per share for the period were 0.28p. An interim of 0.06p has been declared.
