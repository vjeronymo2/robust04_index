930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Soundtracs falls to Pounds 350,000 Profits of Soundtracs, the professional audio equipment specialist, fell from Pounds 545,699 to Pounds 350,385 pre-tax for the 12 months to November 6. Directors blamed the setback on pressure on margins. Turnover improved by 6.5 per cent to Pounds 4.11m. About 85 per cent of production is exported to North America, the Far East and Europe. Earnings emerged at 2.45p (3.79p). A final dividend of 1.35p makes a same-again 2.2p total. Along with its results Soundtracs announced its first acquisition since joining the USM in 1986. It is paying Pounds 300,000 cash for Sussex-based Spendor Audio Systems. For the year to April 30 1992 Spendor returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 28,000 and had net assets of Pounds 413,000.
