940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / International Company News: Samsung to offer 3.3m new shares Samsung Electronics, South Korea's largest electronics manufacturer, is to offer 3.3m new shares via a rights issue in mid-August, Reuter reports from Seoul. The issue will increase paid-in capital by 17bn won to 332bn won (Dollars 411m). The closing date is July 11 and the subscription will end on August 25. 'The price of the issue has yet to be decided but it will be fixed at around 60,000 won,' the company said. At this price, the issue would raise around 198bn won. Shares have changed hands recently at around 91,300 won. Both common and preferred stocks will have the same rights as the new issue.
