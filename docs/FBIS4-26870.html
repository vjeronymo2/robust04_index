BFN [Text] Beijing, June 27 (XINHUA) -- The Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries hosted a reception here this evening to commemorate the 40th anniversary of the proclamation of the five principles of peaceful co-existence. Qi Huaiyuan, president of the association, made a speech at the reception. Qian Zhengying, vice-chairwoman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee and president of the China-India Friendship Association, Indian Ambassador to China Chandra Shekhar Dasgupta and Myanmar Ambassador to China H.E. U Set were present at the reception.
