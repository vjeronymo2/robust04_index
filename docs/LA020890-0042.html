February 8, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

ST. MARY'S STOPS UC IRVINE IN BASEBALL

Gil Orta went two for three with two runs scored and two runs batted in to lead St. Mary's to a 7-4 victory over host UC Irvine in a nonconference baseball game Wednesday.

Orta had a two-run single to key St. Mary's three-run second inning and Randy Cooper and Mike Zanski had run-scoring singles in the seventh inning to lead St. Mary's (1-2).

Cooper went three for five with two RBIs.

Osmar DeChavez went two for four with one run scored and Brian Young homered in the fifth for host UC Irvine (2-3).

Tom Shildemeyer was the winning pitcher and Bill Christ had the save. David Bladow (1-1) took the loss.

In another nonconference game:

Southern California College 6, UC San Diego 1 -- Centerfielder Paul Plyler drove in two runs and scored a third for SCC (2-0). Three pitchers combined for a two-hitter, with starter Ralph Garcia pitching five innings for the victory.

In nonconference men's volleyball:

Pepperdine 3, UC Irvine 1 -- James Felton had 17 kills and six digs, but it wasn't enough as Pepperdine defeated host UC Irvine, 15-10, 15-3, 11-15, 15-10, in a Western Intercollegiate Volleyball Assn. match.
