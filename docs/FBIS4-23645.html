BFN [Text] Armed villagers attacked soldiers near Burundi's capital, Bujumbura, and four people were killed when a grenade was thrown into a crowded cafe in the city. Reports said men armed with automatic weapons attacked the Army post in Kaidi at Nyambuye, 20 km from Bujumbura. Diplomats said the attackers had not been identified and the motives were unclear. The government had recently been conducting searches for illegal weapons, apparently fearing attacks on government forces. They had no information of casualties in the attack and official sources in Bujumbura declined to comment.
