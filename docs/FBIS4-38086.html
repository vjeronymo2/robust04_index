BFN [Text] According to a report, the Golboddin-Dostam militia resorted to a failed attack on the positions of the Islamic Army from the direction of Kata-e Naw. They were forced to retreat after leaving 20 dead. Sources in the Armed Forces report that Golboddin's headquarters in Charasiab was seriously damaged during air attacks by the Islamic state's jets. According to another report, at around 0900 hours on 27 June an aircraft belonging to Dostam's militia appeared over Kabul and targeted the residential areas of Shar-e Naw and Shirpour with two bombs. As a result of this savage attack, seven Kabul city civilians were killed, 12 others were injured, and three houses were destroyed.
