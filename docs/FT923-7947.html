920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Batleys advances 19% to Pounds 8.33m Batleys, the private Huddersfield-based chain of cash and carry wholesalers, reported a 19 per cent rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 7m to Pounds 8.33m in the year to May 2. Turnover advanced 7 per cent to Pounds 454.9m (Pounds 426m). Profits included an exceptional credit this time of Pounds 876,000 relating to provisions against stock made in earlier years no longer needed. Earnings per share were 35.54p (30.85p).
