Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Representatives from the Ministries of Construction of Vietnam and Cuba have signed a cooperation agreement in Havana. The document establishes that Cuban and Vietnamese construction forces will work on infrastructure projects in Vietnam. The Vietnamese delegation is headed by Professor (Nguyen Manh Kin), vice minister of construction. [Begin (Nguyen Manh Kin) recording, in Vietnamese with superimposed translation into Spanish] This will be a multinational enterprise between Cuba and Vietnam. They will carry out construction work in Vietnamese territory. In the future, they will also be able to do more work in other countries in that region. [end recording] The Vietnamese delegation also held a meeting with Politburo member Carlos Lage and Commander of the Revolution Ramiro Valdes. During the meeting, the experience of Cuban construction workers in building projects abroad was highlighted. [passage omitted]
