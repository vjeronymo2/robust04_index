BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 69/94] [Text] A Chinese business delegation is on a two-day visit to Estonia, Foreign Ministry Spokesman Marko Kadanik announced. The delegation met Economy Minister Toivo Jurgenson yesterday, who explained the functions of the Economy Ministry and the economic-political background of Estonia. The delegation is interested in gathering information and making business contacts, particularly concerning paper and cellulose industry.
