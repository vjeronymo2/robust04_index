EFFECTIVE DATE: January 1, 1995. NUCLEAR REGULATORY COMMISSION 10 CFR Parts 30, 32, and 35 RIN: 3150&hyph;AD69 Preparation, Transfer for Commercial Distribution, and Use of Byproduct Material for Medical Use; Correction AGENCY: Nuclear Regulatory Commission. ACTION: Final rule: Correction.

SUMMARY: This document corrects a final rule, published in the Federal Register on December 2, 1994 (59 FR 61767), that amends the regulations for the medical use of byproduct material. This action is necessary to correct language in the preamble to reflect regulatory text, remove an obsolete cross-reference, and specify a date certain in document text. EFFECTIVE DATE: January 1, 1995.
ADDRESSES: Copies of the public record, including the final regulatory analysis and any public comments received on the proposed rule, may be examined and copied for a fee in the Commission's Public Document Room at 2120 L Street, NW. (Lower Level), Washington, DC.
