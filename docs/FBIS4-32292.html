BFN [Excerpt] The outgoing prime minister and the HSP [Hungarian Socialist Party] and SZDSZ [Alliance of Free Democrats] leaders have agreed that the current economic directors of Hungarian Television and Hungarian Radio should be entrusted with the management of the two bodies on a temporary basis. The HSP and the SZDSZ are preparing to form a government coalition. The three politicians' explain their decision by the need to avoid a leaderless state of affairs in the two national media institutions. This is contained, among other things, in the government communique published after the morning meeting of Peter Boross [outgoing prime minister], Gyula Horn [HSP candidate for the post of prime minister] and SZDSZ Chairman Ivan Petoe.
