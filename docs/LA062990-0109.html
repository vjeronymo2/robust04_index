June 29, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

North Carolina point guard King Rice was ordered by a judge in Chapel Hill, N.C., to perform 75 hours of community service stemming from an incident in which he was charged with resisting arrest.

In addition, Rice must pay court costs and restitution of about $30 to the town for damaging a jail wall he reportedly punched after his arrest.

Rice, 21, was arrested last month and charged with assault on a female, resisting an officer and damage to property.

The 3 a.m. incident involved Rice's girlfriend, Natalie Nester.
