930511 FT 11 MAY 93 / First evidence heard at Nissan UK tax fraud trial The first evidence from an executive of Nissan Japan, the carmaker, was heard at the Nissan UK tax fraud trial at the Old Bailey yesterday. Mr Masahiko Ikeda (left), the finance department manager of Nissan Motor Corporation Carriers, the company's freight subsidiary, is the first in a series of senior Nissan Japan executives expected to give evidence at the trial of Mr Michael Hunt, the Nissan UK director who denies being party to a Pounds 97m corporation tax fraud
