940407 FT 07 APR 94 / UK Company News: Powell Duffryn waste collection buy In line with its strategy of developing its share of the growing European environmental engineering market, Powell Duffryn has acquired Kiggen Beheer, the Netherlands-based designer and manufacturer of waste collection systems. Powell Duffryn, which has interests in distribution and specialist engineering, said Kiggen would complement its European refuse collection vehicle business. Kiggen has annual sales of Fl 18m (Pounds 6.2m) and net assets of Fl 4.4m.
