Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Podkrepa National Strike Committee held a meeting today at the headquarters of the Podkrepa Labor Confederation, which was attended by representatives of the strike committees of 15 federations and 21 regions throughout the country. The participants adopted a draft decision, which notes that a strike situation exists in the country. The General Assembly proposes to the Podkrepa Executive and Federative Councils to complete the preparations for national strike activities by 1 February. Those activities will be aimed, I quote, against the antihuman and antisocial policy of Professor Berov's government. The draft decision will be submitted to the Podkrepa Federative Council, which is scheduled to meet on 16-17 January in Blagoevgrad.
