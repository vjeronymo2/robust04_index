Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Brasilia, 25 Feb (EFE) -- In an official note, the Brazilian Government demanded today that Israel speed up the peace process with the Palestinians in repudiating the killing of dozens of Palestinians by an Israeli settler in a Hebron mosque. The Brazilian Foreign Ministry expressed "its indignation and strong condemnation of that act" and called on Israel "to adopt the necessary measures to prevent new violence from occurring" in the region.
