BFN [Text] His Excellency Carl Bildt, prime minister of the Kingdom of Sweden, arrived in Ho Chi Minh City yesterday on an official friendship visit to Vietnam at the invitation of SRV Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet. Prime Minister Carl Bildt and his entourage arrived in Hanoi from Ho Chi Minh City today. An official ceremony to welcome the Swedish prime minister was solemnly held at the Presidential Office in Hanoi this morning.
