941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Abu Dhabi expected to win shipyard contract - Contracts & ventures Abu Dhabi is soon expected to award a contract to build a shipyard, a deal that could indicate which western shipbuilder would win a multi-billion-dollar order to supply frigates. Abu Dhabi has drawn up a shortlist to build the shipyard at a cost of about Dollars 200m to service warships and commercial vessels.
