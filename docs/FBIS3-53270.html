Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 12 Jan (MENA)-- French Minister of Foreign Affairs Alain Juppe arrived in Cairo this evening on a two-day official visit, which will be followed by a three-day private visit. While in Cairo, the French minister will meet with President Husni Mubarak, Prime Minister Dr. 'Atif Sidqi, Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid, and Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa. Juppe's talks with Musa will discuss ways to bolster bilateral relations, the situation in the Middle East in light of the ongoing peace negotiations in Taba, France's interest in the Middle East issue, the situation in Somalia and the Horn of Africa, the French role in curbing tension in Africa, and boosting cooperation between Egypt and France in the economic and cultural spheres.
