BFN [Report by UKRINFORM-TASS] [Text] Kiev 12 May TASS -- The Ukrainian Government has issued a resolution on measures aimed to stabilize energy supplies to the national economy. According to the resolution, new generating units will be put into action at several nuclear power plants at the nearest future and new power transmission lines will be constructed to deliver the electric power. The Ukrainian State Committee for Use of Nuclear Power and Ministry for Power Engineering and Electrification are instructed to modify existing tariffs for electric energy at an early date. Relevant organizations are instructed to report on available opportunities and terms for increasing generating capacities of the active nuclear power plants and to map out a programme for developing nuclear power engineering and industry in Ukraine.
