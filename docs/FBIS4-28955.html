BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 14 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, received a gift from Tomiichi Murayama, chairman of the Central Executive Committee of the Japan Social-Democratic Party (JSDP). The gift was handed to an official concerned by Director of the General Affairs Department of the Central Executive Committee of the JSDP Kazo Watanabe on a visit to Korea.
