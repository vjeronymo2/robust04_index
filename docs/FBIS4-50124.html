BFN [Text] Moscow, May 13 (XINHUA) -- Russian State Duma (lower house) Speaker Ivan Rybkin expressed hope today that the parliaments of Russia and China will cooperate with each other for the happiness of the two peoples. Rybkin, who heads a State Duma delegation on the first visit of the legislature to China since its founding four months ago, was speaking to reporters at the airport before his departure for China. China has things for Russia to learn, he said. Rybkin said he paid great attention to the trip and he hoped to see how reforms are being carried out in China's industrial and agricultural sectors. The successes China has made in its reforms in industry and agriculture are just what Russia is trying to achieve, he added. Chinese Ambassador Wang Jinqing went to the airport to see Rybkin off.
