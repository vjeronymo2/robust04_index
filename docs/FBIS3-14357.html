Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Mar (INA) -- Iraqi Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf met here today with Yusuf Bin-'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah, the Omani minister of state for foreign affairs. The two ministers discussed points on the agenda, a number of current Arab issues, and Iraqi-Omani ties and ways to promote them. Al-Sahhaf also met with Qatari Foreign Minister Shaykh Hamad Bin-Jasim Al Thani and discussed with him developed bilateral relations. Al-Sahhaf briefed Bin-'Alawi and Al Thani on Iraq's efforts to lift the embargo and the change that occurred in the Security Council for the benefit of Iraqi causes, demonstrating understanding of Iraq's sound and positive position aimed at closing all the files between Iraq and the United Nations. The two meetings were attended by Sa'di 'Abd-al-Majid al-Faysal, Foreign Ministry under secretary, and Nabil Najm, Iraq's Arab League's permanent representative.
