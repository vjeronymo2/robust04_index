August 5, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ASTRO PITCHER GETS OFF SCOTT FREE IN EYES OF HERSHISER, DODGERS

It seems to me that calling this year's Cy Young Award winner a cheater is a bit of sour grapes by last year's Cy Young Award winner, Orel Hershiser.

If Hershiser never complained before, why now?

Is it because he's not on top of his game?

CHARLES COSS

Hollywood
