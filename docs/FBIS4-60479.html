BFN [Text] The Atomic Energy Regulatory Board, AERB, has ordered the suspension of all construction work of the inner container structures of the proposed nuclear reactors in Karnataka and Rajasthan. The AERB order was issued following preliminary analysis of the collapse of a large concrete portion of the inner container dome in the (Kaiga) unit-one project in Karnataka. The board has decided to review the construction procedure in use. The AERB chairman, Dr. A. Gopalakrishanan, has said that a high-level expert committee has been constituted to probe into the incident. He clarified that the (Kaiga) project is still under construction, and no nuclear fuel or heavy water has been transferred to the site. As such, there is no question of any radioactive release or radiological impact due to the incident, he added.
