Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Deputy Foreign Minister Yosi Beilin and a special Omani envoy discussed the upcoming water affairs conference scheduled for April in Oman. Our correspondent in Washington Arye Golan reports that the conference will be attended by Israeli diplomats and journalists. Deputy Minister Beilin also dined with Qatar's ambassador in Washington. The dinner was attended by Israeli Ambassador Itamar Rabinovich. The Qatari ambassador said his country is interested in relations with Israel. It also wants to do business with Israel and to receive Israeli know-how on desalination and irrigation methods.
