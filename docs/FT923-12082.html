920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / Theatre staff back strike call MEMBERS of the entertainment union Bectu voted in favour of strike action at the Royal National Theatre in London over a 4.5 per cent pay rise and a dispute over guaranteed minimum breaks. The union said staff had voted 4-1 for action. But the Royal National Theatre said that fewer than one in four of the 300 union members had taken part in the ballot.
