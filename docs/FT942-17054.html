940407 FT 07 APR 94 / Court recommends extradition A magistrate yesterday said Mr Ewan Quayle Launder, former chief executive of the Wardley investment arm of Hongkong and Shanghai Bank, should be extradited to Hong Kong to face accusations of receiving more than Pounds 4m in bribes. Mr Launder of Abingdon, Oxfordshire, must return to London's Bow Street Magistrates Court today for the formalities to be completed. The home secretary will make the final decision. Mr Launder's counsel indicated he would appeal against the magistrate's decision.
