940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Net assets rise at Geared Income Net asset value at Geared Income Investment Trust improved from 76.15p to 115.15p over the 12 months to December 31. Net revenue for the nine months to the end of December was Pounds 1.36m (Pounds 1.34m) for earnings per share of 6.16p (6.11p). The third interim dividend is maintained at 2p.
