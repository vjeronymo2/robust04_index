December 9, 1990, Sunday, South Bay Edition

NOMINATIONS SOUGHT FOR WOMAN OF YEAR

The Torrance YWCA is accepting nominations for the 1991 Woman of the Year awards.

The awards, which honor the achievements of women from all social and economic levels, will be given at the Women of the Year luncheon June 8, 1991, at the Torrance Marriott Hotel.

Deadline for nominations is Jan. 31, 1991.

For more information, call 320-2255.
