Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Slovak prime minister [Vladimir Meciar] said that he is not willing to hold talks on the Hungarian minority's collective rights, because the rights of the Hungarian minority in Slovakia, as he said, are already excessive. At a Bratislava rally of his party, the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia, Meciar said, we quote: There is complete freedom in Slovakia, therefore, those who want to leave can do so at any time. He added that the Slovak Government will not pursue a policy to suit certain circles of the Hungarian Republic.
