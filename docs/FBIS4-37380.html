BFN [Text] His Eminence Shaykh Muhammad Mahdi Sham-al-Din, chairman of the Higher Islamic Shiite Council, has stated that the continued occupation of south Lebanon is like having the capital itself occupied, in addition to the Presidential Palace, the Prime Minister's Office, the Chamber of Deputies, and all Christian patriarchial seats in the country. His eminence remarked that he opposes the establishment of an Islamic republic in Lebanon because the country is comprised of Muslims and Christians. He said an Islamic republic would not be conducive to the security of either the Muslim or Christian communities in Lebanon.
