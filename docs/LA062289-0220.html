June 22, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

MARRIOTT MAY SHIFT CATERING UNIT

Marriott Corp. today confirmed it is discussing the possible transfer of its airline catering division, Marriott In-Flite Services, to a group of investors led by the division's senior management.

Under the proposal, Marriott would have a continuing financial interest in the business. Marriott spokesman Robert T. Souers said the company is not disclosing the magnitude of its intended financial interest or a price for the transfer.

In-Flite Services division had revenues of approximately $800 million in fiscal 1988.
