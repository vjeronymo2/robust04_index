Language: English Article Type:BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 428] [Text] Vilnius, Feb 2, ELTA -- Speed of the agricultural reform is not satisfying neither to the government nor landowners, Prime Minister Adolfas Slezevicius told the national radio on Wednesday. But he added that land was being returned quicker at the moment. He said in 1991-1992, the documents returning the property were handed to 12.6 thousand former landowners and only in 1993 -- 25.2 thousand. Slezevicius said the reform would be speeded up when local authorities hire enough officials working with the land issues.
