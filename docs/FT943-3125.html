940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Cortecs losses Cortecs, the Australian biotechnology company which recently gained a London listing, reported net losses of ADollars 10.5m (Pounds 5m) for the year to end June, against a deficit of ADollars 10m. Operating revenue was ADollars 10.6m (ADollars 11.9m). Losses per share were unchanged at 13 cents.
