Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 17 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The Mongolian-Russian border line continues to be violated in reference to its Mongolian-Tuvinian part with the purposes of smuggling and rustling. Only for the first half of the month since beginning of the current year the state frontier line has been crossed illegally from the Mongolian side by 26 residents of the Uvs Aymag in the western part of Mongolia. Nine of them being residents of the Tes Somon who were crossing the border for the purpose of searching their cattle stolen were given a terrible beating by Tuvinians and arrested in the Oshina settlement of the Tesham Region, Tuva. From the Tuvinian side a searching group consisting of representatives of competent organs including police and securities did not behave themselves properly during their stay on the Mongolian territory in particular, they have beaten some residents of the Zuungobi, Baruunturuun Somons of the Uvs Aymag and then run away back through the border. This is reported to the MONTSAME correspondent in the Uvs Aymag. Such cases of crossing illegally the Mongolian-Tuvinian border line has been going on for a many years.
