920826 FT 26 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Ecclesiastical Ins Office back in black Ecclesiastical Insurance Office, owned by Allchurches Trust and responsible for insuring 95 per cent of Anglican churches, returned to the black with a Pounds 3.65m pre-tax profit for the half year to June 30 against a Pounds 2.16m loss last time. General premium turnover was up from Pounds 58.5m to Pounds 64.3m although life premium business fell from Pounds 15.6m to Pounds 9.16m.
