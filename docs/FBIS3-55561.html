Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By M. Tumanova; from the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] The interminable trial of the SCSE [State Committee for the State of Emergency] members consists of a multitude of separate sittings, most of which begin and are immediately adjourned. Like today. It was all over in five minutes. Two of the accused, Lukyanov and Pavlov, were absent. Pavlov is recovering after an operation, while Lukyanov is at home and being attended by doctors. The court ruled that doctors should provide findings on the health and ability to attend the trial for both men. It was adjourned until 12 January.
