February 1, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

A $1,000 reward is being offered by the Stroh Brewing Co. of Detroit for the return of surf rocker Dick Dale's guitar, reported stolen last week from the Huntington Beach International Surfing Museum. Anyone with information on the instrument's whereabouts can contact Detective Chuck Alquist of the Huntington Beach Police Department, (714) 536-5968. For information about the reward, call Natalie Kotsch of Huntington Beach at (714) 536-9326. Kotsch is offering an additional reward of $50.
