BFN [Excerpt] Doboj, 10 Apr (TANJUG) -- Muslim units have intensified their offensive and provocative attacks against Serb defense positions and settlements on all parts of the front in the Doboj area, the Information Service of the first Bosnian Krajina corps of the Srpska republic army said this evening. Reinforced by units from Zavidovici, the enemy launched two fierce combined artillery-infantry attacks in an attempt to break through Serb positions at the southwestern part of the Ozren defense line.
