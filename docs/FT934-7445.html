931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Bett Bros in the black with Pounds 4.4m BETT BROTHERS, the Dundee-based property, building and leisure group, returned profits of Pounds 4.36m pre-tax for the year to end-August. That compared with a restated deficit of Pounds 9.73m. The results benefited from the release of a property provision amounting to Pounds 2.4m, property gains of Pounds 207,000 (losses Pounds 1.9m) and a reduction in interest charges to Pounds 749,000 (Pounds 2.49m). Earnings emerged at 7.63p (losses 46.2p). A final dividend of 1.5p makes a 2p (nil) total. Year-end borrowings stood at Pounds 7.2m (Pounds 8.9m), giving gearing of 52 per cent (89 per cent). Shares closed 6p up at 101p.
