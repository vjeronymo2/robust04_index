BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] The inflation rate for May has been announced as 10 percent. The yearly inflation rate thus reached 117.8 percent. According to figures released by the State Statistics Institute, the consumer price index rose by 10 percent in May, while the wholesale price index rose by 9 percent in the same period. The yearly consumer price index is now 117.8 while the yearly wholesale price index is 138.6.
