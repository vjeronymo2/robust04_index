920624 FT 24 JUN 92 / World News in Brief: Mafia leader sentenced to life in prison Mafia leader, was sentenced in New York yesterday to life in prison on murder and racketeering convictions. Gotti, 51, was convicted in April of murdering his predecessor, Paul Castellano, to take over the Gambino crime family, the largest crime syndicate in the US. After the sentencing, 500 Gotti supporters tried unsuccessfully to storm the courthouse. Seven were arrested.
