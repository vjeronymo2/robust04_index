May 25, 1989, Thursday, South Bay Edition

CHILDREN'S ORCHESTRA WILL PERFORM TODAY

An honor orchestra made up of 67 elementary school students in the Palos Verdes Peninsula Unified School District will perform today at 7:30 p.m. in the Norris Theatre, Crossfield Drive and Indian Peak Road, Rolling Hills Estates. The program will range from baroque to contemporary music.

Youngsters were chosen for this year's orchestra -- the 14th since the program started -- during auditions in February.
