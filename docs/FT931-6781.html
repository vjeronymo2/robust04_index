930227 FT 27 FEB 93 / UK Company News: North of England Building Society North of England Building Society achieved a rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 15.4m to Pounds 16.7m in 1992. Total income amounted to Pounds 34.1m (Pounds 29.7m). The pre-tax result was after higher management expenses of Pounds 16.4m (Pounds 13.9m) and increased provisions of Pounds 1.05m against Pounds 434,000. Group assets grew by 16 per cent to Pounds 1.42bn.
