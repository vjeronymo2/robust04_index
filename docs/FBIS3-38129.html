Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Budapest/Essen (ADN-WDO) -- The first cooperation project in the gas industry between Germany and Hungary has been signed in Essen by MOL Magyar Olaj-es Gazipari R.T., Budapest, and Ruhrgas AG. The two companies announced today that they will cooperate primarily in the purchase of natural gas from the West and its transport across Germany to Hungary. It has also been agreed that Ruhrgas will provide auxiliary supplies via a planned pipeline from Austria to Hungary, thereby improving the country's ability to ensure a constant and steady flow. As part of the cooperation there are also plans to expand the infrastructure of the gas industry in Hungary and to introduce energy saving and environmentally friendly technology, including the installation of gasflow controlling and measuring equipment.
