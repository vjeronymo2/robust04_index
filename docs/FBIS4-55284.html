BFN [Text] Croatian President Franjo Tudjman has expressed satisfaction with the Vienna agreement. He was informed about it by Mate Granic and Kresimir Zubak upon their return from Vienna. Haris Silajdzic, head of the Muslim delegation, described the agreement as the most specific step toward peace, the implementation of the Washington agreement, and the constitution of the Bosnia-Herzegovina federation. President Alija Izetbegovic said that the agreement is a good one, but that the Muslims will continue to insist on a unitary state and on preserving its external borders.
