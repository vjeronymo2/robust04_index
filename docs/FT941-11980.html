940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / International Company News: Lippo Securities Indonesia's Lippo Securities, a venture between Lippo group and Swiss Bank Corp, will be Indonesia's first securities company to go public by offering 13.5m shares, 31.03 per cent of the enlarged share capital, Reuter reports from Jakarta. The initial public offering price will range from Rp7,500 to Rp8,500.
