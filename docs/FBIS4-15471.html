BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Vladimir Taranov] [Text] Moscow 7 April, TASS -- Russia welcomes strict observance of the cease-fire agreement signed in Zagreb on 29 March by delegations of the Republic of Croatia and Serbs living on territories under U.N. control, spokesman for the Russian Foreign Ministry Grigoriy Karasin told a briefing on Thursday. The fact that the warring factions are precisely following the schedule of troop withdrawal to new disengagement lines, set by the cease-fire agreement, makes it possible to hope that both sides will fulfill their commitments in full and on time, the Russian diplomat noted.
