Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi, February 22 (XINHUA) -- China and Vietnam today signed an executive plan for improved cultural exchange between the two countries. Under the plan, they will further strengthen their cultural cooperation and exchange in 1994. About 30 groups including writers, artists, and those involved in the film, opera, photography and dancing sectors will exchange visits. Meanwhile, film weeks and painting exhibitions will also be staged in the two countries. The chief of the Bureau for Relations With Foreign Countries of the Chinese Culture Ministry, Wu Chunde, and the Vietnamese Culture and Information Vice Minister Vo Khac Lien signed the plan on behalf of their respective governments. Wu Chunde, who was leading a government cultural delegation, arrived here on Monday for a week-long visit to Vietnam.
