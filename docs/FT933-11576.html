930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / Nobili released from jail Mr Franco Nobili, 67, former chairman of Iri, Italian state holding company, was yesterday released after 77 days in a Milan jail on corruption charges. His release follows the suicide in prison of Mr Gabriele Cagliari, former head of Eni and also accused of corruption, and criticism of magistrates' use of preventative detention.
