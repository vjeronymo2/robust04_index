940104 FT 04 JAN 94 / Company News (This Week): Carlton Communications - Other companies The UK television company, which holds the London weekday ITV licence, will hold an extraordinary general meeting for shareholders at Hambros Bank tomorrow to obtain formal permission for the agreed takeover of Central Independent Television. The meeting also has to approve changes arising from the bid, which valued Central at Pounds 758m, such as an increase in share capital and the issuing of a new class of shares - 5.5p net convertible preference shares.
