Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "News Patrol" newscast] [Text] Unsitragua [Guatemalan Workers' Trade Union] leader Byron Morales today stated that Guatemalan workers do not care about the results of the referendum; instead, what they really want is the resignation of the deputies. Morales, who participated in a demonstration across from the Congress building, indicated that the constitutional reforms do not attempt to resolve public problems. Rather, they attempt to resolve the private problems of politicians who want to remain in power forever.
