931125 FT 25 NOV 93 / Vauxhall plant rejects strike The threat of company-wide industrial conflict at Vauxhall faded yesterday when members of the TGWU general union at the company's main plant in Luton, Bedfordshire, voted by two to one against striking over pay. This follows last week's start of industrial action by the union at Vauxhall's Ellesmere Port plant on Merseyside.
