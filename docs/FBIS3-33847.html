Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] A single bench of the Lahore High Court headed by Mr. Justice Amin Malik has declared as illegal the notification issued by the former Punjab caretaker government dissolving the local councils. The court has asked the provincial government to hold the new elections of the local councils by 31 March this year. The court said that if the government fails to hold election by that time, the dissolved local councils will be restored by 1 April.
