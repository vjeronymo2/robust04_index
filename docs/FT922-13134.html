920414 FT 14 APR 92 / UK Company News: Touche Ross issues unmuddied facts about Muddy Fox owners Touche Ross yesterday clarified the identity of the two new owners of Muddy Fox, the privately-owned UK mountain bike company which came out of administrative receivership earlier this month. The UK accountant said it had been advised that the actual co-owners of Muddy Fox are - as originally stated - Sitac, a UK-based engineering and property group, and Parry Murray, and not TI Cycles of India as announced on April 5. Touche said Muruguppa Group of India had interests in both Tube Investments of India (of which TI Cycles is part) and Parry Murray.
