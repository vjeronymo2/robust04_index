910424 FT 24 APR 91 / Ratner comes clean on the secret of success MR GERALD RATNER, the chief of Britain's biggest retail jeweller yesterday gave the secret of his company's success - it sells 'total crap'. Mr Ratner, chairman and managing director of Ratners told the Institute of Directors in London that an imitation book with curled up corners were in the worst possible taste. 'But we sold a quarter of a million last year.' He added: 'We also do cut glass sherry decanters complete with six glasses on a silver-plated tray that your butler can serve you drinks on, all for Pounds 4.95. 'People say how can you sell this for such a low price. I say because it is total crap. On Monday Ratners announced annual pre-tax profits had risen to Pounds 110.05m from Pounds 108.22m.
