920908 FT 08 SEP 92 / Management (In a Nutshell): Piece of the action in the Italian job Four hundred small and medium-sized Italian companies will be looking for links with businesses in other parts of the European Community at Europartenariat Mezzogiorno, in the southern Italian city of Bari on December 3 and 4. Non-Italian companies will be able to book private meetings with local companies seeking links including distribution agreements and technology transfer deals. Introductory seminars will be held around the UK later this month and in October. The event is organised by the European Commission. Contact Business Briefing Consultants. Tel 0444 416678.
