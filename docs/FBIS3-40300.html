Language: Swedish Article Type:CSO [Unattributed article: "Increased Aid to Bosnia"] [Text] (TT)--The needy in Bosnia are going to get more Swedish aid. The contribution to the UN refugee organization--the UNHCR [UN High Commissioner for Refugees]--is being increased from 40 million to 275 million kronor. Support for the UN agency for Palestinian refugees--the UNWRA [UN Relief and Works Agency for Palestine Refugees in the Near East]--is being increased from 16 million to 130 million kronor.
