BFN [Text] Ankara, April 20 (AA) -- Interior Minister Nahit Mentese said today that a new organization named the 'Resalvation Union' which was planning to assassinate ranking officials and politicians, has been revealed. During a meeting of the parliament's interior commission, Mentese said that police were working under great risk and are revealing many incidents. Mentese said that the 'Resalvation Union' was revealed when police teams Tuesday caught five members of the organization, five minutes before their attempt to rob a bank in the Zeytinburnu township of Istanbul. The men were caught with three automatic guns, two revolvers, three hand grenades.
