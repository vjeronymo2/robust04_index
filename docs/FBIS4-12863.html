BFN [Text] The Military Committee today concluded its 10-13 April meetings in the economic and commercial capital, Aden, led by brothers Brigadier General Haytham Qasim Tahir, the defense minister, and Brigadier General 'Abd-al-Malik al-Sayyani, the chief of the general staff. Over the four days, the committee discussed the best ways to implement Council of Ministers' decision number 39 on the basis of the accord document concerning the military forces. The Yemeni News Agency, SABA', learned that a comrade-in-arms spirit, sincere Yemeni brotherhood, and a unionist spirit prevailed in the committee's meeting. All felt the importance of their meetings and their continuation in order to achieve the tasks assigned to the committee, which are based on the accord document's implementation, as emphasized by the meeting of the Council of Ministers. SABA' learned that the committee will continue its meetings in the Ta'izz Governorate early next week to complete what is left of these tasks, to work out a plan for military movements, and to eliminate any military tensions. The committee will present the Defense Ministry's plan to the Council of Ministers during its next meeting. The Military Committee meetings were attended by a member of the Jordanian military committee, and the U.S. and French military attaches.
