BFN [Text] The Ministry of International Trade and Industry [MITI] will hold the first regular vice ministerial meeting with the Chinese Economic and Trade Commission [SETC] in Tokyo on 25 and 26 April. The purpose of the meeting is to exchange opinions on such issues as economic situation and policies in the two countries. Among the main topics for the upcoming meeting are: the issue of the GATT membership for China; Japan's investment in China; and industrial cooperation between the two countries. MITI Deputy Vice Minister Sozaburo Okamatsu and other officials are scheduled to attend the meeting. Participants from the Chinese side will include SETC Standing Vice Chairman Yang Changji.
