Language: French Article Type:BFN [Article signed DC/AL: "Ciba Expands Its Activities in China"; as released by Bern ATSA/SDAA database] [Text] Basel, 7 Feb (ATS) -- The Basel-based chemicals and pharmaceuticals group Ciba is extending its plant protection and pigment activities to China. According to a statement released Monday evening, a contract was signed Monday for the creation of the first Chinese subsidiary of Ciba's plant protection division. In addition, Ciba's pigments division has concluded a joint venture with a Chinese company. Ciba is one of the most active foreign investors in China. With a capital base of 18 million francs, the new plant protection company, Ciba Agro Ltd., has been established in Kunshan [name as received] (province of Kiangsu). It will employ some 240 people once its activities, which are planned for 1996, begin. Ciba holds 95 percent of the capital of Ciba Agro (Kiangsu) Ltd., according to the statement. Ciba's pigments division concluded an agreement on the production of organic pigments as part of a joint venture with the Chinese company Qingdao Dyestuffs Factory. The plant should be commissioned in Qingdao [name as received] (province of Shantung) in late 1996. Qingdao Dyestuffs Factory is one of China's leading producers of dyes, while Ciba's pigments division is the world's largest producer of pigments.
