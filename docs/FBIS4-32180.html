BFN [Text] Jedlove Kostolany, west Slovakia, June 17 (CTK) -- A coalition agreement on election cooperation was signed by the chairmen of the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia (HZDS) and the Slovak Farmers' Party (RSS), Vladimir Meciar and Pavel Delinga, today. The agreement provides for nine RSS representatives to be placed on the HZDS list of candidates. The two parties have agreed to form a joint deputies' group in the new parliament, Meciar reported. The elections are scheduled for September 30-October 1. First and foremost, it is necessary to ensure that the elections are held, Meciar said. After them, Meciar wants to acquire such a position which would make it possible for him to undertake fundamental changes in the Constitution and economic structure of society, he said without specifying. According to the latest polls, the HZDS would reap 29 percent of votes, thus leading popularity ratings.
