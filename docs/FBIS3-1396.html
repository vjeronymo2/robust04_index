Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Durban March 28 SAPA -- The Inkatha Freedom Party's [IFP] Ed Tillett claimed on Monday that the African National Congress [ANC] had planted gunmen among Zulu marchers in Johannesburg to discredit Zulus in Transvaal. Mr Tillett, an IFP spokesman, said: "We've just received information from intelligence sources that the ANC planted snipers in the crowd. There are deliberate plans to discredit Zulus in the Transvaal. The ANC will claim this is an IFP march and this will probably be an attempt to discredit the IFP as well." He stressed it was not an IFP march but rather one organised by "indunas" [chiefs].
