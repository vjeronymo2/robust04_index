November 22, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BUSH THANKSGIVING ADDRESS ON RADIO, TV

President Bush will address the nation in a live broadcast airing tonight at 6 on ABC, CBS, NBC and cable's CNN.

The Thanksgiving speech, originating from Camp David, will express gratitude for the nation's bounty and for democratic reforms taking place in Eastern Europe.

On radio, the speech can be heard on KFWB (980), KNX (1070) and KCRW-FM (89.9).

The networks also may preempt regular programming to cover the scheduled 4:30 p.m. launch of the space shuttle Discovery.
