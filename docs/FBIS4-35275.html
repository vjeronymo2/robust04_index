BFN [Excerpt] His highness the amir of Kuwait, God safeguard him, today received Mr. Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the envoy of 'Ali Salim al-Bid, the secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party. Al-'Attas briefed his highness about the fighting and the development of the conflict in Yemen, and the reactions to this crisis at the Arab and regional level. His highness, God safeguard him, was keen to express Kuwait's firm and principled stance, which calls for tackling problems according to the logic of national interest and by brotherly dialogue without the use of violence. Resorting to violence and the use of arms increases disputes and complicates problems. It would also deepen wounds, waste energy and result in destruction. In this context, His highness the amir, God safeguard him, expressed his concern that the Yemeni conflict could have a bad effect on the Arab situation. This is a matter that worries all leaders and officials, especially as the Arab nation needs to close ranks and reunite to regain its dignity and influence. At the end of the meeting his highness the amir affirmed that Kuwait will support all honest and beneficial efforts to stop the bloodshed and restore understanding and reconciliation between the two parts of Yemen.
