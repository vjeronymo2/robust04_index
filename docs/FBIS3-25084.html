Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Yamoussoukro, 7 Feb (AFP) -- French President Francois Mitterrand left Yamoussoukro (220 km northwest of Abidjan) today at 2115 GMT after attending the funeral ceremony of Ivorian President Felix Houphouet-Boigny and participating in the minisummit of countries of the franc zone. The Concorde took off for Paris with former French President Valery Giscard d'Estaing and former Prime Ministers Pierre Messmer and Jacques Chirac. Mr. Chirac arrived in Yamoussoukro yesterday with French Prime Minister Edouard Balladur. Mr. Balladur is to leave Yamoussoukro aboard a Falcon-900 plane a few minutes after Mr. Mitterrand.
