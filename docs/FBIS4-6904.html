BFN [Text] Accepting our Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam's invitation, Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Maksymovych Zlenko and his wife paid an official visit to Vietnam on 21-23 April. Accompanying Foreign Minister Zlenko were high-ranking officials of the Ukrainian foreign and economic ministries and many businessmen. During their stay in Vietnam, Minister Zlenko, his wife, and the entourage visited Ho Chi Minh's mausoleum and his living and working quarters. Minister Zlenko was cordially received by State President Le Duc Anh and Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet. He handed the Ukrainian President Leonid Makarovych Kravchuk's letter to President Le Duc Anh and solemnly conveyed the former's invitation to President Le Duc Anh and Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet to pay an official visit to Ukraine at an appropriate time. President Le Duc Anh and the prime minister thanked him and happily accepted the invitation. Minister Zlenko held a working session with Defense Minister Doan Khue. His entourage also met with leaders of some Vietnamese ministries and sectors to exchange views on practical cooperation possibilities. The Ukrainian delegation visited some economic and cultural establishments in Hanoi. Minister Nguyen Manh Cam and his wife received and entertained Minister Zlenko, his wife, and the visiting guests. He welcomed the Vietnam visit by Minister Zlenko and considered it an important event in the development and acceleration of the traditional friendship and cooperation relation between the two countries in the new situation. He expressed his sincere and profound appreciation to the Ukrainian Government and people for their precious support to Vietnam. Minister Zlenko expressed joy at his chance to visit Vietnam and sincerely thanked his hosts for the solemn and wholehearted reception and hospitality given to his delegation by the Vietnamese leaders and people. He highly evaluated achievements scored by the Vietnamese people in their current renovation undertaking. Minister Zlenko held talks with Minister Nguyen Manh Cam, and they expressed their contentment at the recent active development steps in many fields between the two countries. They exchanged views on directions and measures to accelerate cooperation between the two countries in the coming period. They agreed to quickly prepare a document outlining the principles of the relations between the two countries and agreements that will be signed at the upcoming summit. They also exchanged views on international and regional issues of mutual concern. Minister Zlenko invited Minister Nguyen Manh Cam to pay an official visit to Ukraine. Minister Nguyen Manh Cam thanked him and accepted the invitation. On 22 April, Minister Zlenko held a news conference on the result of his official visit to Vietnam at the Government Guest House. The two sides also issued a joint communique on this visit. On 23 April, the Ukrainian foreign minister and his wife left Vietnam, successfully ending an official visit here.
