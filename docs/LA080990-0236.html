August 9, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

STATE;

NO WINNERS FOR LOTTO JACKPOT

No ticket had all six winning numbers picked Wednesday night in the California Lottery's "Lotto 6-53" game, leaving the $31.9-million jackpot unclaimed, lottery officials said today.

The jackpot will roll over to an estimated $40 million in Saturday night's drawing.

One ticket had five of six numbers plus the bonus number, winning $936,031.

There were 158 tickets with five of six numbers, each earning $5,924. They share a prize pool of $936,031.
