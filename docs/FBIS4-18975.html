BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] After a news conference, Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres was received by President Suleyman Demirel at the Cankaya Mansion. According to a statement issued by the Presidential Press Center, Peres conveyed to Demirel messages of friendship from Israeli President Ezer Weizman and Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin. The statement added that at the meeting, which was also attended by Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin, Peres briefed Demirel on the developments in the region and the Israeli-Palestinian peace talks. Peres told Demirel that progress has been achieved at the peace talks. At the meeting views were also exchanged on regional and international issues concerning both countries. Peres was also received by Prime Minister Tansu Ciller.
