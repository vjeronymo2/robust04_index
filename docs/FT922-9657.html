920506 FT 06 MAY 92 / ABB wins turboset contract Asea Brown Boveri, the Swedish-Swiss engineering group, announced yesterday that it had secured a Dollars 210m contract from the German company VEAG to design and supply two turbosets with a total output of 1,600MW for a power plant to be built at Boxberg in Saxony, writes Robert Taylor in Stockholm. The first turboset is scheduled to supply electricity from mid-1996 and the second from six months later. The order for ABB follows one worth Dollars 50m a few weeks ago for the same Saxon plant. The contract will be carried out with ABB Bergman Borsig of Berlin.
