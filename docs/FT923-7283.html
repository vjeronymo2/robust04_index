920819 FT 19 AUG 92 / World Trade News: Puerto Rican deal for ABB ABB Asea Brown Boveri, the Swedish-Swiss engineering group, has won a Dollars 133m (Pounds 69.6m) deal to design and build the first big power generating plant in Puerto Rico in 20 years, Ian Rodger reports from Zurich. ABB's US gas turbine power division will build the 248MW Cambalache station near Arrecibo for Puerto Rico Electric Power Authority.
