BFN [Text] PLO Executive Committee sources have disclosed the list of names of members of the Palestinian government which the Executive Committee is studying in its meeting which started on Friday [6 May]. The sources said the government is expected to be announced today or tomorrow. According to the sources, the names suggested for the government which the Executive committee is discussing are as follows: --Yasir 'Arafat, head --Jawid al-Ghusayn, financial affairs --Samir 'Abdallah, development --Jamil al-Tarifi, civil status --Furayh Abu-Middayn, justice --Shaykh Karim Sabri, religious affairs --'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Hajj, health --Muhammad Ishtayyah, planning and construction --Ibrahim Sha'ban, housing --Antun Sansur, culture --Yasir 'Amr, education --Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, information --Intisar al-Wazir, social affairs --Major General Nasr Yusuf, police --Muhammad Zuhdi al-Nashashibi, relations with Jordan --Faysal al-Husayni, Jerusalem affairs --Ahmad Quray', economic affairs --Nabil Sha'th, foreign affairs The sources said there are other candidates whose portfolios have not been decided yet. These are: Samir Ghawshah, 'Abdallah al-Ifranji, Zakariya al-Agha, Mahir al-Masri, Hisham 'Abd-al-Razzaq, and Ahmad Bayyud al-Tamimi.
