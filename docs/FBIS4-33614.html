BFN [From the "First Thing" program] [Text] The first well drilled by oil companies working with Cupet [expansion unknown] in the search for new deposits has yielded positive results. A deposit has been discovered with research currently underway to determine its dimensions. GRANMA reports that the discovery was made at Cardenas Bay, Matanzas Province, in the area leased by Canadian companies Canada Northwest Energy and Fortuna Petroleum, Inc. Tests reveal an estimated daily production of 3,750 barrels of 16 API -- the international unit to measure the oil's density -- oil. The oil is considered heavy but of better quality than the oil extracted at Varadero.
