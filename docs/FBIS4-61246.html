BFN [Text] As BELAPAN has learned, the 1,336th central tank reserve base in Chavusy, Mahilyow Oblast, has been liquidated four months ahead of a previously established deadline. Around 1,000 pieces of tank equipment have been transported by 24 trains to either the Barysaw tank repair plant for dismantlement or temporarily to the 969th central tank reserve base in Urechcha, Minsk Oblast. Although the Chavusy base was initially scheduled to be liquidated by 1 September 1994, the last three temporary-service soldiers were released to the Reserves on 30 April.
