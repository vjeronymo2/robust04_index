941118 FT 18 NOV 94 / Economic Watch: Wholesale prices edge down West German wholesale prices fell 0.3 per cent in October from September, but were up 2.7 per cent from a year earlier, the federal statistics office said. Figures for the previous month were rises of 0.1 per cent and 2.8 per cent respectively. Wholesale prices of fish products jumped 26.1 per cent in October from the previous month. Coffee extract prices rose 11.5 per cent, aluminium 6.4 per cent, organic chemicals and basic products 3.4 per cent, plastics 3.3 per cent and heavy heating oil 2.4 per cent. By contrast, the wholesale price of raw coffee fell 13.4 per cent, fresh fruit 12.3 per cent and fresh vegetables 9.9 per cent. Turkey's foreign trade deficit narrowed to Dollars 207m (Pounds 132m) in September against Dollars 1.141bn a year earlier, according to official figures. For the first nine months, the deficit fell to Dollars 3,793bn from Dollars 10,440bn in the same period of 1993.
