Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riyadh, Dec 30, SPA -- Custodian of the two holy mosques, King Fahd Bin-'Abd al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud received last night a cable from the U.S. President Bill Clinton over the inauguration of the Shoura Council. President Clinton expressed pleasure on the occasion and welcomed the developments concerning the establishment of the Shura Council and regional councils as important steps that cope with the history and traditions of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. He congratulated King Fahd, the royal family and the Saudi people on the political reforms. Replying the cable, King Fahd thanked President Clinton on the friendly contents of the cable and expressed good wishes to President Clinton and the American people. King Fahd expressed hope that the Saudi Government would continue the welfare march of development and construction.
