BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow May 26 TASS -- A working group consisting of representatives of the president, members of the government and Russian lawmakers suggested a single concept of adopting laws aimed at the formation of civic society in Russia. In an interview with ITAR-TASS presidential aide Georgiy Satarov pointed out that laws on elections, referendums, parties and local government "should be adopted in a single package which would meet comprehensive principles of interaction between society and the state." For the realisation of the proposal members of the working group expressed support to the initiative suggested by the Russian president, the Federation Council and the State Duma to set up a joint commission for coordinating legislative activities of the branches of power.
