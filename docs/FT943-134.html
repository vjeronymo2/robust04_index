940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Pochin's rises 64% A fall in operating costs helped Pochin's, the building and civil engineering company, to report pre-tax profits 64 per cent ahead at Pounds 2.52m for the year to May 31, against Pounds 1.53m. Turnover was Pounds 35m (Pounds 46.6m), of which Pounds 1.49m (Pounds 764,000) related to acquisitions. Earnings per share were 167.8p (100.2p) and the proposed final dividend is raised to 24p for a total of 32p (29p).
