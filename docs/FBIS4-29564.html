BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 18 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received the visiting high-level military delegation of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam headed by General Doan Khue, Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Vietnam and minister of national defence. Present on the occasion were O Chin-u, member of the Presidium of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and minister of the People's Armed Forces, Vice Marshal of the Korean People's Army Kim Kwang-chin, and Colonel General of the KPA Pak Chae-kyong. Vietnamese Ambassador E.P. [extraordinary and plenipotentiary] to Korea Duong Chinh Thuc and his embassy officials were on hand. The head of the delegation conveyed a personal letter of President Le Duc Anh to President Kim Il-song. President Kim Il-song had a talk with the guests in a cordial and friendly atmosphere.
