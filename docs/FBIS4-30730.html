BFN [Report by Gyori Szabo from Hungarian Socialist Party headquarters] [Excerpts] We are in the middle of an international news conference held by the deputy chairmen of the Hungarian Socialist Party [MSZP]. [passage omitted] Gyorgy Janosi has said that the possibility exists for the MSZP to be the leading force in a would-be coalition government. Several journalists asked about the coalition issue and Gyorgy Janosi is replying just now. He says that they are thinking in terms of the broadest possible coalition. [passage omitted] Imre Szekeres, the other deputy chairman of the MSZP, said that the Hungarian people's participation in the election means that they want to make this democracy, in which we live, work.
