BFN [Report by Silviya Velikova] [Text] Today Vaclav Havel, president of the Czech Republic, ended his brief working visit to our country. He held talks in private with President Zhelev. At the subsequent news conference, both presidents expressed satisfaction with the development of Bulgarian-Czech relations, while stressing that work in that direction should be pushed forward by various means and at various state forums. Presidents Havel and Zhelev also discussed the situation in the Balkans, the course of the democratic processes in both countries, and the development of democratic institutions in them. Prominent Bulgarian intellectuals, Bulgarian and foreign journalists, and of course both presidents attended an official presentation to launch Vaclav Havel's book "Power of the Powerless."
