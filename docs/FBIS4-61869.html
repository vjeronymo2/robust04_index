BFN [Unattributed "Chronicle" report] [Text] The Federation Council of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly has approved the composition of Federation Council delegations in the following international parliamentary organizations: the Parliamentary Assembly of Black Sea Economic Cooperation headed by Federation Council Deputy Chairman V.N. Viktorov; the Parliamentary Assembly of the CSCE headed by Federation Council Chairman V.F. Shumeyko; the North Atlantic Assembly headed by A.V. Vorobyev, member of the Federation Council Committee for Security and Defense Questions. The Federation Council has made changes to the composition of several Federation Council committees in connection with the formation of the Federation Council Committee for Affairs of the North and Minority Peoples and the approval of its composition.
