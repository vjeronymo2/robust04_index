BFN [Report by KHOVAR news agency correspondent Valeriy Zhukov for TASS] [Text] Dushanbe 19 April TASS -- A draft new Tajik constitution circulated here today by the KHOVAR news agency proclaims Tajikistan a sovereign, democratic, law-governed, secular and integral republic. The Majelis Milli (National Assembly -- parliament) is the supreme representative and legislative body of the Republic, and the president elected on the basis of universal, equal and direct electoral right in secret voting for a five-year term is the head of state and the executive power. The text of the document will be sent to specialized international organizations for an examination.
