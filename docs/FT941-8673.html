940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / Observer: Off limits Fresh evidence that France's car thieves are not going to be cowed by government interference. Less than a week after Edmond Alphandery, the French economy minister, summoned the creme de la creme of France's motor and insurance industries to discuss the growing problem of car theft, Nicolas Sarkozy, France's super-efficient budget minister, has had his official motor nicked.
