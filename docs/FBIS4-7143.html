BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 23 (KCNA) -- The opening ceremony of the Kim Chong-il Library took place at a senior middle school in Antananarivo, Madagascar, on April 13. The principal of the school in his address said dear Comrade Kim Chong-il is not only wisely leading the Korean people along the road of victory despite the complex international situation but also clearly indicating the road ahead of the revolutionary people of the world and showing great favours for them. He said the students must learn from dear Comrade Kim Chong-il, a great man. To this end, the Kim Chong-il Library was opening, he added. After the ceremony the participants saw a DPRK photo exhibition and an art performance.
