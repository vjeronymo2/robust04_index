BFN [Text] Interested Diet members of the ruling coalition parties will start a new policy study group, "Study Group on Japan's Security and Future Course [Nihon no Anzen Hosho to Shinro wo Kangaeru Kai]," on 6 April. Yukihisa Yoshida, Democratic Socialist Party member of the upper house, will be the chairman of the group, and members will include Tatsuro Matsumae, member of the Socialist Democratic Party of Japan upper house; Hideaki Tamura, member of Shinseito [Japan Renewal Party] upper house; and Natsuo Yamaguchi, memebr of Komeito lower house. Moves, mostly initiated by Shinseito, to form study groups across party boundaries on specific policy issues have been quite active after a plan to form a "Policy Study Group" of all ruling coalition parties to discuss overall policy failed to take off. However, this will be the first time a policy group on defense will be formed.
