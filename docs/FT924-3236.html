921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / Parliament and Politics: Switch announced in Faraday scheme THE Faraday programme, aimed at bridging the gap between academia and industry, will not create separate institutions as originally proposed, Mr Michael Heseltine, the trade and industry secretary, said yesterday. He told the Lords science and technology committee the DTI would apply to existing projects the scheme's principles of encouraging innovation and forging links between universities and companies.
