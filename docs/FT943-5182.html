940905 FT 05 SEP 94 / Company News (This Week): Flat profits expected at Carnaud Metalbox - Other companies The Franco-British packaging group is due on Thursday to report first-half profits which are expected to be substantially unchanged from the FFr509m (Dollars 95m) net profit it recorded in the first six months of 1993. CMB has already announced that turnover rose 3.6 per cent in the first half to FFr12.3bn. Last month it admitted that it remained under continued pressure on prices from its customers and from significant increases in some raw material costs.
