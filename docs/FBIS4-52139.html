BFN [Text] Dr. Sawit Phothiwihok, minister in the Thai Prime Minister's Office, along with his delegation paid a courtesy call on Lieutenant General Kyaw Ba, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council and minister of hotels and tourism, at the latter's guest room at 1530 this afternoon. During the meeting, Dr. Sawit Phothiwihok showed great interest by asking questions concerning investment in the Myanmar [Burma] hotel business, the situation with the arrival and departure of tourists, and Myanmar's tourism situation. Dr. Sawit Phothiwihok also said he hopes Myanmar and Thailand will be able to cooperate in tourism in a more friendly manner than at present. Lt. Gen. Kyaw Ba said he knew the extent of the success of Thai tourism and added that Myanmar is also making plans to promote tourism. He also expressed his desire for bilateral and mutual cooperation to promote tourism.
