920615 FT 15 JUN 92 / International Company News: Job losses after Thyssen units merge THYSSEN Stahl said its merger with Thyssen Edelstahlwerke would lead to 5,000 job cuts, Reuters reports from Dusseldorf. Mr Ekkehard Schulz, management board chairman, said the merger would lead to considerable savings. The supervisory board of Fried. Krupp has approved a merger contract with steel group Hoesch, Reuter reports from Essen.
