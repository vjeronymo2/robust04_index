BFN [Text] Jean-Bertrand Aristide solicits the renewal of the mandate of the civilian mission in Haiti. In a letter addressed to the UN secretary general, the Haitian president-in-exile asked for the renewal of the mandate of the International Civilian Mission in Haiti, according to the terms of reference already negotiated with Dante Caputo. Referring to a deterioration of human rights and democracy in Haiti, Aristide hoped that the presence of the mission is contributing to an improvement in the human rights situation and helping create a favorable environment for political dialogue in the country.
