Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Iyad al-Hinawi, 20, of Jabalyah in the Gaza Strip, was shot dead by the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] an hour ago. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the incident occurred near the tent where the Rihan family, whose relative was killed two days ago by soldiers, is mourning. When an IDF patrol was stoned, soldiers opened fire, killing one of the stone throwers. Our correspondent notes that today security forces captured three wanted men, members of a Fatah Hawks cell. They were questioned and released.
