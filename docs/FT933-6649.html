930826 FT 26 AUG 93 / UK Company News: LWT managers to share Pounds 70m More than 40 senior managers at LWT (Holdings), parent of London Weekend Television, will be able to reap the rewards of their substantial bonus scheme next Tuesday. They stand to share some Pounds 70m. LWT said yesterday the unlisted management shares and listed preferred shares will convert automatically on Tuesday into ordinary shares. Executives are expected to sell immediately at least some of their new holdings.
