BFN [Article by "bj": "Martinez Set Free; Cuban Charge d'Affairs on Yesterday's Demonstration"] [Text] Bratislava--"This is a needless politicization and ideologization of the problem, which cannot have a positive influence on bilateral relations between Slovakia and Cuba," Juan Sanchez Monroe, charge d'affairs at the Cuban Embassy in the Slovak Republic, told a news conference about yesterday's demonstration for the release from prison of dissident Joel Duenas Martinez, which was organized by the Bratislava branch of Amnesty International. Out of respect for the Slovak public and following a personal conversation with Marta Simeckova, the Bratislava representative of Amnesty International, J.S. Monroe, pledged already last year that he would personally plead for the revision of the verdict. This happened in March of this year and in April Martinez was released from prison. "Cuba did this as a friendly gesture toward the Slovak Republic," J.S. Monroe said. That is why he considers yesterday's demonstration, as well as the previous March demonstration, inappropriate interference in the Slovak-Cuban dialogue.
