BFN [Text] Beijing, May 15 KYODO -- A delegation of Japan's second largest opposition Social Democratic Party (SDP) [of Japan, SDPJ] arrived in Beijing on Sunday [15 May] for an eight-day visit marking the 10th anniversary of exchanges with the Chinese Communist Party. The mission led by SDP General Secretary Wataru Kubo will stay in China until May 22. Kubo and his party will meet with Ding Guangen, chief of the party's Propaganda Department, on Sunday and with General Secretary Jiang Zemin on Tuesday. The delegation will visit Dalian and Changchun.
