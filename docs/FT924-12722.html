921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Birmingham dips its toe in the water Birmingham, meanwhile, has launched its own version of the 'one-stop shop', called The First Step Shop, to provide a single contact point for business advice. The Birmingham advice centre is a joint venture between Birmingham City Council, Birmingham Training and Enterprise Council, the DTI, the City Action Team and Birmingham Chamber of Industry and Commerce. Contact First Step Shop, Chamber of Commerce House, PO Box 360, 75 Harborne Road, Birmingham B15 3DH. Freefone 0800 626462.
