Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' today received a Pakistani parliamentary delegation, led by Maulana Fazlur Rahman, chairman of the Pakistani National Assembly Standing Committee on Foreign Affairs. Attending the meeting were Shakir Is'ayyid, head of the Arab and Foreign Affairs Committee of the Syrian People's Assembly, Walid Rajab, director of the Asia Desk at the Foreign Ministry, and the Pakistani ambassador in Damascus. The talks dealt with the Arab-Israeli conflict, the Palestine question, and the Jerusalem issue. The talks also dealt with the situation in Kashmir and relations between the two friendly countries, Syria and Pakistan.
