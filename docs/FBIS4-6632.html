BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 22 (KCNA) -- Nanterre City, France, presented its commemorative medal to the great leader President Kim Il-song on his birthday. On behalf of the mayor of the city, the deputy mayor handed the medal to the chief of the general mission of the DPRK in Paris. The deputy mayor said his city deemed it the highest honor to offer the medal to President Kim Il-song, the outstanding leader of the Korean people who is making a distinguished contribution to the independence and sovereignty of Korea and its reunification cause, on his birthday.
