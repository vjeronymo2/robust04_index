BFN [Text] Beijing, April 20 (XINHUA) -- The authorities of five universities in Beijing have recently decided to cooperate in making use of their resources so as to improve education quality. At a forum on cooperation between these five, Vice-Premier Li Lanqing said that the cooperation in running institutes of higher learning is an experiment in China's educational reforms. He said it will promote the development of higher education. The five universities are the University of International Business and Economics, Beijing Chemical Engineering Institute, Beijing University of Traditional Chinese Medicine and Pharmacy, Beijing Garment College and China Institute of Banking. The five institutions are administered by different ministries, but they are located close to one another. They have decided to jointly hire teachers, share their libraries, computer labs and other laboratories. The students at each are being encouraged to select some courses at the other four. The five will also jointly undertake scientific research programs and run some enterprises.
