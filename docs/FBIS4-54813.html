BFN [Article by M.J.: "Ukraine's General Staff Head in Poland"] [Text] At the invitation of General Tadeusz Wilecki, head of the General Staff of the Polish Army, on 4 May General Anatoli Vasilievich Lopata, head of the General Staff of the Ukrainian Armed Forces, will visit Poland. The Ukrainian commander is accompanied by a group of high-ranking officers representing, among others, the air defense forces, communication forces, and logistics services. The visit is an important element of developing military contacts between both armies. During their visit to our country, the Ukrainian delegation will be received by National Defense Minister Piotr Kolodziejczyk; it will also hold working talks in the General Staff of the Polish Army. It is expected that such problems as technical cooperation and issues related to the "Partnership for Peace" program will be discussed. In line with the program of their stay in Poland, the Ukrainian guests will also become familiar with the scientific achievements of the Military Technical Academy, will listen to the observations of the marine servicemen of the Third Flotilla of Ships, and will visit the Higher Air Force Officers School in Deblin. The delegation of the General Staff of the Ukrainian Armed Forces will also place a wreath at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier.
