CSO ["Election Results in Zhytomyr"] [Text] According to preliminary results, Volodymyr Pataridze (secretary of the oblast committee of the SPU) and Svyatoslav Vasylchuk (chairman of the regional organization of Rukh) have moved on to the second round of elections in the Korolovskyy ED No. 155 of the city of Zhytomyr. In Bohunskyy ED No. 154 -- Heorhiy Mozer (general director of the Krok Production and Trade Footwear Association) and Vyacheslav Dekhtiyevskyy (chairman of the oblast organization of the Congress of National Democratic Forces [KNDS]).
