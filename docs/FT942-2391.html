940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / Talks continue on Severn dispute Talks between management and the Ucatt construction union and the GMB and TGWU unions will resume today in the dispute which has halted work on the Pounds 300m second Severn crossing between England and Wales. The main contractors, Laing-GTM, dismissed more than 700 workers on Thursday when they held a 24-hour strike seeking higher bonus payments.
