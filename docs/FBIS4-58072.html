BFN [Text] Tomorrow, Monday [2 May] the House of Representatives will resume its meetings under Brother Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-Husayn al-Ahmar, the House speaker. The people's deputies will discuss a host of issues on their agenda, as well as the latest developments in the country. In a related development, the house committee made up of the Defense and Security Committee and other House members continued its meetings this morning. Conferees discussed the initial reports on the field missions carried out by the committee in the past three days. The committee visited the camps of the 1st and 3d Armored Brigades in the 'Imran area. The committee also met with the Joint Military Committee and listened to statements by shaykhs and citizens in the 'Imran area and by some officers and personnel on the circumstances of the regrettable events and their grave results. The committee also reviewed the outcome of its visit to the military hospital to see the wounded personnel from the 1st and 3d Armored Brigades. It reviewed the outcome of its visits to personnel from the 3d Brigade and the Signals Corps.
