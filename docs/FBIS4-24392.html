BFN [Text] Beijing, May 29 (XINHUA) -- The delegation from the Communist Party of China (CPC) headed by Wei Jianxing, member of the Political Bureau and also member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, wound up its official goodwill visit to Egypt and Syria and returned here today. The delegation was met at the airport by Li Shuzheng, head of the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee, and embassy officials of the above two countries in Beijing.
