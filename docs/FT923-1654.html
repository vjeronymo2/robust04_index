920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Alexander Russell declines by a third Pre-tax profits of Alexander Russell, the Glasgow-based quarrying and coal company, fell by 32 per cent in the six months to end-June as recession continued to affect demand for building materials. Despite a 3 per cent increase in turnover, from Pounds 18.2m to Pounds 18.7m, profits before tax were down by Pounds 287,000 from the Pounds 621,000 reported at the half-way stage last year. Earnings per share emerged at 0.68p (1.4p) and an unchanged interim dividend of 1p is declared.
