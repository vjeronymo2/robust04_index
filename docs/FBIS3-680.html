Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg March 11 SAPA -- Two Amcoal Colliery workers were killed and 26 injured when gunmen opened fire on the bus taking them home to Thuthukani township near Standerton after the nightshift early on Friday [11 March] morning. Standerton police said the motive for the attack was not yet known. An Anglo American Corporation statement on Friday said six of the injured were treated on site and 20 were taken to hospital. Two are seriously ill in Rand Mutual Hospital. The statement said the morning shift had not gone underground and mine management was trying to restore calm. The names of the dead will be released when their next of kin have been notified.
