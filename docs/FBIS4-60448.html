BFN [Text] Viktor Posuvalyuk, personal envoy of Russian President Boris Yeltsin and director of the Middle East Department at the Russian Foreign Ministry, arrived in Damascus today. He was received at Damascus International Airport by Ghassan Raslan, director of the East European Department at the Foreign Ministry. Posuvalyuk told journalists upon arrival that his visit was aimed at activating the peace process and holding consultations and exchanging views with Syrian officials on the peace process and cooperation between the two countries.
