Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March (XINHUA) -- Chinese women from all walks of life are called on to make even greater contributions to peaceful unification and the prosperity of the country. Lei Jieqiong, vice-chairwoman of the Standing Committee of China's National People's Congress, made the call at a forum held by China's eight democratic parties and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce in celebration of the coming International Women's Day. Wang Zhaoguo, vice-chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, said at the forum that Chinese women have played a role equal to men's in the country's opening up and reforms as well as the modernization drive. Chinese women are expected to play an even more important role in the course of building socialism with Chinese characteristics and expanding and strengthening the patriotic unification front, Wang said. Meanwhile, more than 1,000 select women and grassroots units engaged in women's work nationwide were cited by the All-China Federation of Trade Unions for their outstanding contributions to China's modernization drive.
